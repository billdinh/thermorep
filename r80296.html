Turkey patties

Ingredients

  • 60 g wholemeal bread (approx. 2 slices), roughly torn
  • 1 sprig fresh rosemary (approx. 10 cm), leaves only
  • 2 sprigs fresh sage, leaves only
  • 2 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves only
  • 4 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 90 g brown onion, cut into pieces
  • 500 g turkey mince
  • 1 egg
  • ¼ - ½ tsp sea salt, plus extra to seaosn
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper, plus extra to seaosn
  • oil, for frying

Preparation

  1. Place bread, rosemary, sage, thyme and parsley into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place onion into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7.
  3. Add reserved bread and herb mixture, turkey mince, egg, salt and pepper and mix 3-4 sec/speed 5, or until just combined. With damp hands, shape mixture into 8 flat patties, then transfer onto a plate, cover and set aside.
  4. Place a frying pan over medium-high heat and cook patties until cooked through and golden brown on either side (approx. 3-4 minutes each side) or until cooked to your preference. Season to taste and serve immediately.

Useful Items

plastic wrap, frying pan, bowl, plate, turner/fish slice, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve patties in a bread roll to make a burger or with a side salad.
  • Make your own fresh Spelt bread rolls to enjoy with these burgers.
  • To mince your own meat: cut 500 g turkey meat into pieces (2-3 cm) and place in a single layer in the freezer until partially frozen. Place meat into mixing bowl and mince 7-10 sec/speed 7, or until a mince consistency is achieved. Use minced meat immediately or place into the refrigerator until ready to use.
  • Lamb patties: replace the turkey mince with lamb mince. Proceed as per recipe.
