Sour Cherry and Coconut Cookies

Ingredients

  • 120 g unsalted butter, diced
  • 90 g light brown sugar
  • 90 g granulated sugar
  • 250 g plain flour
  • 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
  • 1 medium egg
  • 80 - 100 g dried sour cherries
  • 40 - 50 g desiccated coconut
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
1100 kJ / 263 kcal
Protein
2.9 g
Carbohydrates

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 170°C and line two baking trays with baking paper then set aside.
  2. Place butter, light brown sugar, granulated sugar, flour, bicarbonate of soda, salt and egg in mixing bowl then mix 30 sec/speed 5.
  3. Add sour cherries and desiccated coconut then mix 20 sec//speed 3.
  4. Shape into a long log shape (approx. Ø 6 cm) then slice into 12 and place rounds onto prepared baking trays about 5 cm apart. Bake for 10-12 minutes (170°C) until golden and still slightly soft.
  5. Leave to rest on trays for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely. Once cool, serve or store in an airtight container.

Useful Items

2 baking trays and baking paper, wire rack, long sharp knife, airtight container, oven
