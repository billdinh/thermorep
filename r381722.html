Chicken, wild rice and coriander purée

Ingredients

  • 1000 g filtered water, plus extra as needed
  • 50 g wild rice (see Tips)
  • 300 g skinless, boneless chicken thighs, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 150 g broccolini, cut into pieces (2-3 cm)
  • 3 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only

Preparation

  1. Place water into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket, weigh rice into it and cook 25 min/100°C/speed 4.
  2. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh chicken into it. Insert Varoma tray and weigh broccolini onto it. Secure Varoma lid and steam 12-14 min/Varoma/speed 4 or until chicken and broccolini are cooked. Remove Varoma and set aside. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula. Transfer cooking liquid into a jug and set aside.
  3. Place rice, cooked chicken and broccolini, coriander and 100 g of the reserved cooking liquid into mixing bowl and blend 40 sec/speed 7.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and blend 10 sec/speed 6, to desired consistency.
  5. Set aside 1 serving, then freeze the remainder in individual portions for future use. If needed, stir through a little more water or your baby's milk to get the consistency just right before serving.

Useful Items

jug, ice cube trays, freezer

Hints & Tricks

  • Wild rice is not a true rice but actually a species of grass. It's a source of fibre, potassium, zinc and antioxidants and makes a nutritious alternative to white rice.
  • If your baby is not keen on this purée at first, you might like to try adding some puréed apple, pear or pumpkin (or another favourite flavour) to help them accept the taste.
  • You can replace the wild rice with basmati rice in this recipe. Reduce cooking time to 7 min/100°C/speed 4 when cooking rice (before steaming chicken and broccolini). Proceed as per recipe.
  • You can replace the broccolini with frozen peas in this recipe.
