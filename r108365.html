Tiffin slice

Ingredients

  • 150 g pistachio nuts
  • 120 g hazelnuts, without skin, roasted
  • 5 dried figs, cut into halves
  • 50 g butter, cut into pieces
  • 50 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 300 g dark chocolate, broken into pieces
  • 2 tbsp golden syrup
  • 2 tbsp brandy (optional)
  • 100 g digestive biscuits broken into pieces (approx. 3 cm)
  • cocoa powder, for dusting

Preparation

  1. Line a square cake tin (20 cm) with plastic wrap, extending plastic approximately 5 cm over the edges and set aside.
  2. Place 100 g of the pistachios into mixing bowl and mill 1 min/speed 10, until a fine dust consistency is achieved. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Without cleaning mixing bowl, place hazelnuts and figs into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5 . Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place butter, cream, chocolate, golden syrup and brandy (optional) into mixing bowl and cook 3 min/80°C/speed 2, until smooth.
  5. Add biscuits, remaining 50 g pistachios and reserved hazelnut mixture and combine 15 sec//speed 1.
  6. Transfer mixture into prepared tin, press into corners and smooth surface, ensuring that there are no air pockets. Cover with plastic wrap and place into refrigerator for 4 hours or until set.
  7. Remove slice and allow to stand for 10 minutes. Turn slice out onto a chopping board and remove plastic wrap. Dust half with reserved pistachio dust and half with cocoa powder then cut into thin slices or small squares and serve.

Useful Items

square cake tin (20 cm), plastic wrap, bowl, wooden chopping board, refrigerator
