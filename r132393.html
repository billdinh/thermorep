Orzo Pasta with Chicken and Pesto

Ingredients

Pesto

  • 40 g Parmesan cheese, cut in pieces
  • 10 g pine nuts
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 20 g fresh basil leaves
  • 40 g olive oil

Chicken Orzo Pasta

  • 150 g carrots, cut in pieces
  • 150 g leeks, cut in pieces
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 400 g chicken breasts, skinless, cut in cubes (3 cm)
  • 1 heaped tbsp chicken stock paste, homemade
  • 500 g water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 100 g frozen green peas
  • 250 g dry pasta, type orzo, or similar short dried pasta

Preparation

Pesto

  1. Place Parmesan cheese in mixing bowl and grate 15 sec/speed 10.
  2. Add pine nuts, garlic clove, basil leaves and olive oil then chop 10 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Chop again 10 sec/speed 7. Transfer pesto to a bowl and set aside.

Chicken Orzo Pasta

  1. Place carrots, leeks and olive oil in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl using spatula.
  2. Sauté 5 min/120°C/speed 1.
  3. Add chicken breasts, chicken stock paste, water and salt then cook 10 min/100°C//speed .
  4. Add green peas and orzo pasta then cook time indicated on packet/100°C//speed .
  5. Add reserved pesto and combine gently using spatula. Serve immediately.
