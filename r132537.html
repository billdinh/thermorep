Chicken with Herb Salsa and White Bean & Rocket Pesto Salad

Ingredients

Baked Chicken Legs with Herb Sauce

  • 4 chicken legs, skin on
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • ½ tsp dried chilli flakes (optional)
  • 1 - 2 lemons, preferably organic, zest and juice
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 5 g fresh parsley
  • 10 g fresh coriander, leaves only
  • 10 g red wine vinegar
  • 60 g shallots

White Bean Salad with Rocket Pesto

  • 30 g flaked almonds
  • 25 g Parmesan cheese
  • 100 g rocket leaves
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 20 g lemon juice, freshly squeezed
  • 40 g olive oil
  • 800 g tinned butter beans

Preparation

Baked Chicken Legs with Herb Sauce

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C. Line a baking tray with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place chicken legs in a casserole dish.
  3. Place salt, garlic cloves, chilli flakes, zest of 1 lemon and 30 g lemon juice in mixing bowl then chop 10 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl, rub the chicken legs with lemon-garlic mixture and bake for 35-45 minutes (200°C) until golden. Meanwhile prepare the herb sauce.
  4. Place olive oil, parsley, coriander, red wine vinegar and shallots in mixing bowl then chop 2 sec/speed 7. Transfer herb sauce to a bowl and set aside, then continue with recipe.

White Bean Salad with Rocket Pesto

  1. Place flaked almonds on prepared baking tray and bake for 5 minutes (200°C) or until dark golden.
  2. Place Parmesan cheese in mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 10.
  3. Add 20 g rocket , garlic clove, salt, lemon juice, olive oil and 15 g toasted slivered almonds then blend 15 sec/speed 7. Transfer rocket pesto to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place tinned butter beans, with liquid, in mixing bowl and warm up 5 min/80°C//speed . Drain white beans through simmering basket, add to rocket pesto and mix using spatula.
  5. Add remaining 80 g rocket salad and reserved 15 g toasted flaked almonds to white bean salad. Serve baked chicken legs with herb sauce and warmed white bean salad.

Useful Items

baking tray and paper, casserole dish, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • You can use any large white beans in the salad - e.g. butter beans, cannellini beans.
  • Chicken thighs with the skin on are also suitable for this dish.
