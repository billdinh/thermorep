Hot cacao fudge sauce

Ingredients

  • 100 g pitted fresh (Medjool) dates
  • 80 g cacao butter, broken into pieces (see Tips)
  • 30 g raw cacao powder
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract
  • 200 g milk (see Tips)
  • 3 tsp pure maple syrup
Nutrition
per 1 total recipe

5367 kJ / 1278 kcal

Preparation

  1. Place dates into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add cacao butter and melt 3 min/80°C/speed 2 or until melted. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add raw cacao, vanilla and milk and cook 3 min/90°C/speed 2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add syrup and mix 10 sec/speed 7.
  5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Mix 30 sec/speed 4 or until smooth. Serve warm with fresh fruit or over ice cream.
