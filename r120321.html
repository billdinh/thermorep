Immune Booster

Ingredients

  • 2 lemons (approx. 90 g), flesh only, no white pith and deseeded
  • 10 - 20 g piece fresh ginger (approx. 4 cm), peeled, to taste
  • 30 - 60 g honey, to taste
  • 200 g chilled water
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
411 kJ / 97 kcal
Protein
0.17 g
Carbohydrates
25.11 g
Fat
0.04 g

Preparation

  1. Place all ingredients into mixing bowl and blend 2 min/speed 10. Transfer into a jug and place into refrigerator to chill for 20 minutes (optional).
  2. Strain juice through a fine mesh sieve into 2 serving glasses. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

jug, 2 serving glasses, fine mesh sieve, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Lemon is an excellent source of Vitamin C, which can boost your immune system, while the ginger aids digestion.
  • You can store this juice overnight in the refrigerator. Place any leftover juice into an ice cube tray and freeze to use in future recipes. 
  • You can replace the lemon with lime flesh in this recipe.
  • You can replace the honey with pure maple syrup in this recipe.
