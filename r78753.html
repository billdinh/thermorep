Tomato and red lentil soup

Ingredients

  • 120 g red lentils
  • 5 whole black peppercorns
  • 900 g ripe tomatoes, cut into halves
  • 1 brown onion (approx. 150 g), cut into halves
  • 75 g tomato paste
  • 1 tsp white wine vinegar
  • 1 - 1 ½ tsp raw sugar
  • 5 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only, plus extra chopped for garnishing
  • 750 g water
  • 2 tbsp Vegetable stock paste (see Tips)
  • 1 - 2 pinches sea salt, to serve
  • 1 - 2 pinches ground black pepper, to serve
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
554 kJ / 131 kcal
Protein
8.4 g
Carbohydrates
17.2 g
Fat
2.1 g

Preparation

  1. Place red lentils and peppercorns into mixing bowl and mill 1 min/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add tomatoes, onion, tomato paste, vinegar, sugar, parsley, water and stock paste and chop 5 sec/speed 5, then cook 25 min/90°C/speed 1. Blend 1 min/speed 5-9, increasing speed gradually from speed 5 to speed 9.
  3. Season with salt and pepper, to taste then sprinkle with chopped parsley to garnish. Serve hot.
