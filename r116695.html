Ricotta and Sun-Dried Tomato Cheesecake

Ingredients

Pastry

  • 110 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 60 g butter, cold, diced
  • 35 g water, cold
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
  • ¼ tsp dried thyme

Filling

  • 30 g butter
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 140 g onions, quartered
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 20 g fresh basil, leaves only
  • 3 medium eggs
  • 2 egg yolks, from medium eggs
  • 500 g ricotta cheese
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper, or to taste

Topping

  • 200 g sun-dried tomatoes in oil, plus 30 g of their oil
  • ½ tsp sweet paprika
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt

Preparation

Pastry

  1. Place all pastry ingredients in mixing bowl then mix 15 sec/speed 4. Tip out onto a floured surface and roll out thinly to 2 cm larger than a Ø 23 cm spring form tart tin. Line tin with pastry, prick base with a fork then place in fridge for 30 minutes. Meanwhile, preheat oven to 170°C.
  2. Line pastry base with baking paper and fill with baking beans then bake blind for 15 minutes (170°C). Remove beans and paper then bake for a further 10 minutes (170°C). Meanwhile, make filling.

Filling

  1. Place butter, oil, onions and garlic in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then sauté 15 min/100°C/speed . Transfer to a bowl and set aside to cool. Wash mixing bowl and rinse with cold water to cool.
  2. Place basil in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add eggs and egg yolks then mix 20 sec/speed 5.
  4. Add ricotta and mix 20 sec/speed 3.5.
  5. Add salt, pepper and reserved softened garlic and onions then stir 10 sec/speed 3. Pour over pastry base then bake for 45-50 minutes (170°C) until just set. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl and prepare topping.

Topping

  1. Place sun-dried tomatoes and 30 g of their oil, paprika and salt in mixing bowl then blend 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then blend again 5 sec/speed 7.
  2. Spread paste over surface of tart then bake for 5 minutes (170°C). Leave to cool for 10 minutes then remove from tin and serve warm or cold.

Useful Items

rolling pin, spring form tin (Ø 23 cm), baking beans, baking paper, refrigerator, oven
