Sicilian iced coffee

Ingredients

  • 100 - 180 g coconut sugar
  • 700 g ice cubes
  • 200 g strong brewed espresso coffee, cooled
  • 300 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • coffee beans, for garnishing (optional)

Preparation

  1. Place coconut sugar into mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 9.
  2. Add 350 g of the ice and blend 20 sec/speed 9, with aid of spatula.
  3. Add remaining ice and espresso coffee and blend 1 min/speed 9, with aid of spatula. Transfer into a freezable bowl or container and place into freezer for a minimum of 4 hours. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Insert butterfly whisk. Place cream into mixing bowl and whip until soft peaks form/speed 3.5.
  5. Distribute iced coffee into 6 serving bowls or glasses and top with whipped cream and coffee beans (optional).

Useful Items

6 serving bowls or glasses

Hints & Tricks

  • The whipping time in step 4 will depend on how fresh your cream is. The fresher the cream, the longer it will take to form soft peaks. Watch your cream carefully through the hole in mixing bowl lid to ensure you don't overwhip it.
