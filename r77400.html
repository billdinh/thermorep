Blackberry Jam

Ingredients

  • 500 g blackberries
  • 500 g jam sugar
  • 20 g lemon juice
Nutrition
per 1 total recipe

9418 kJ / 2251 kcal

4.6 g

Preparation

  1. Place all ingredients in mixing bowl. Place simmering basket instead of measuring cup on mixing bowl lid then cook 15 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  2. Test setting point (see tip). Once set, transfer to warm sterilised jars and seal immediately. Serve or label and store in a cool, dry place.

Useful Items

sterilised jam jars

Hints & Tricks

  • Setting point test: place a saucer in the freezer for 5-10 minutes. Place 1 tsp jam onto the saucer. When cool, push jam with your finger. If it wrinkles, it is ready. If not, boil jam for a further 2 min/110°C/speed 3, then test again.
  • Blackberries make a really lovely autumn jam, especially if you’re able to make it using the huge glut of fruit found at this time. It’s always extra satisfying, too, to make a jam out of fruits you have picked yourself!
