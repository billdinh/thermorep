Blue Cheese Soufflés

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 oz unsalted butter, plus extra to grease
  • 2 oz all-purpose flour, plus extra to dust
  • 10 ½ oz whole milk
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 5 oz blue cheese, crumbled
  • 3 ½ oz whipping cream
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1385 kJ / 331 kcal
Protein
13 g
Carbohydrates
11 g
Fat
26 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease and lightly flour 6 ceramic soufflé dishes.
  2. Place eggs, butter, flour, milk, nutmeg, salt and black pepper into mixing bowl and heat 3 min/195°F/speed 3.
  3. Add blue cheese and cream and mix 10 sec/speed 6. Transfer into prepared soufflé dishes and bake for 30-35 minutes (350°F) or until golden brown. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

ceramic soufflé dishes (approx. 3 in. x 2 in.), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Prepare the soufflé with the cheese of your choice.
  • Add some fresh herbs or spices. Some tasty combinations are feta cheese and oregano, Gouda cheese and caraway seeds. Be creative and find your favorite combination.
  • Serve with a small green or mixed salad.
