Vegetarian Chilli

Ingredients

  • 200 g red onions, quartered
  • 20 g garlic cloves
  • 1 fresh red chilli, deseeded if desired
  • 10 g olive oil
  • 750 g tomatoes, cut in pieces
  • 200 g mixed peppers (e.g. red and green), cut in thin strips (approx. 2 cm long)
  • 1 - 1 ½ tsp fine sea salt, to taste
  • 150 g tinned sweetcorn, drained
  • 500 g tinned kidney beans, drained (approx. 2 x 400 g tins)
  • 6 vegetarian sausages (450-600 g)
  • 3 - 4 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1745 kJ / 418 kcal

Preparation

  1. Place onions, garlic, chilli and oil in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then sauté 5 min/120°C/speed .
  2. Add tomatoes and chop 3 sec/speed 4.
  3. Add mixed peppers and salt then cook 20 min/100°C//speed 0.5.
  4. Add sweetcorn and kidney beans to mixing bowl. Place sausages in Varoma dish, cover with Varoma lid, set Varoma into position then steam 15 min/Varoma//speed 0.5. Remove Varoma. Transfer chilli to a serving dish, top with sausages, garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.
