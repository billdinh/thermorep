Apple, leek and sage sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 leek, white part only, cut into pieces (approx. 200 g)
  • 3 - 4 fresh sage leaves
  • 20 g butter
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 green apples, unpeeled, cut into quarters and cores removed (approx. 250 g)
  • 1 tsp Chicken stock paste (see Tips)
  • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
Nutrition

Preparation

  1. Place leek and sage into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add butter and brown sugar and cook 3 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Add apple and chop 5 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add stock paste and cook 8 min/90°C/speed 2.
  5. Add vinegar and blend 30 sec/speed 8.
  6. Transfer into a sealable jar or container and place into refrigerator until required or serve immediately.

Useful Items

sealable storage jar or container, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Please refer to The Basic Cookbook or Everyday Cookbook for the Chicken stock paste recipe.
  • This apple sauce goes well with roasted or grilled pork, especially a festive glazed ham.
