Chocolate Hazelnut Milkshake

Ingredients

  • 10 g hazelnuts
  • 200 g cream, 35-37 % fat, chilled
  • 190 g hazelnut cocoa spread (e.g. Nutella)
  • 200 g whole milk
  • 200 g vanilla ice cream
Nutrition
per 1 glass
Calories
3244 kJ / 775 kcal
Protein
9 g
Carbohydrates
58 g
Fat
56 g

Preparation

  1. Place hazelnuts in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Place cream in mixing bowl and whip without measuring cup speed 3, without setting a time, until desired stiffness is achieved, watching carefully to avoid overwhipping.  Remove butterfly whisk, transfer whipped cream to an icing bag and cool in refrigerator. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Place hazelnut cocoa spread (e.g. Nutella) and 50 g milk in mixing bowl then warm 2 min/50°C/speed 1. Transfer 2 tbsp liquid hazelnut cocoa spread to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Add vanilla ice cream and 150 g milk to liquid hazelnut cocoa spread in mixing bowl then mix 30 sec/speed 8. Transfer milkshake to glasses. Cut one edge of the whipped cream icing bag off and top shake with it, then decorate with reserved liquid hazelnut cocoa spread and chopped hazelnuts. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

icing bag, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Replace the vanilla ice cream with 200 g raspberry ice cream for a hazelnut raspberry milkshake.
