Lemon and ginger Prosecco cocktail

Ingredients

Lemon and ginger syrup

  • 1 tsp Sichuan peppercorns (see Tips)
  • 100 g raw sugar
  • 20 g fresh ginger
  • 80 g lemon juice (approx. 2 lemons)

Lemon and ginger Prosecco cocktail

  • 1 bottle (750 mL) Prosecco
  • lemon peel, cut into thin strips, for garnishing (optional)

Preparation

Lemon and ginger syrup

  1. Place Sichuan peppercorns, sugar and ginger into mixing bowl, then mill 10 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add lemon juice and heat 4 min/Varoma/speed 2, without measuring cup. Strain through a fine-mesh sieve into a jug and set aside to cool (approx. 30 minutes), then place into refrigerator until ready to use.

Lemon and ginger Prosecco cocktail

  1. To serve, place 2-3 teaspoons of the Lemon and ginger syrup into the bottom of 4 champagne glasses, top up with Prosecco and garnish with thin strips of lemon zest (optional) to serve.

Useful Items

fine-mesh sieve, jug, champagne glasses, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Sichuan (also known as Szechuan) peppercorns can be found in spice shops, specialty food stores and Asian supermarkets.
  • You can store this syrup in a sealable container in the refrigerator for up to 2 weeks.
