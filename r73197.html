Quinoa tabouli

Ingredients

  • 100 g white quinoa
  • 700 g water
  • 60 g fresh flat-leaf parsley leaves, washed and well dried
  • 20 g fresh mint leaves, washed and well dried
  • ½ lemon, zest only, no white pith
  • 50 g red onion, cut into pieces
  • 80 g shelled and unsalted pistachios
  • 4 plum tomatoes, deseeded and finely diced
  • 20 g lemon juice, (approx. ½ lemon)
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 pinch sea salt, to taste
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper, to taste

Preparation

  1. Place a ceramic or glass bowl onto mixing bowl lid, then weigh quinoa into it. Add enough water to cover quinoa and set aside to soak overnight.
  2. Drain quinoa through simmering basket and discard soaking water.
  3. Place water into mixing bowl, insert simmering basket containing quinoa and cook 12 min/Varoma/speed 1. Transfer quinoa into a large serving bowl and set aside to cool. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Place parsley and mint into mixing bowl and chop 4-8 sec/speed 6, or until a roughly chopped consistency is achieved. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  5. Place lemon zest into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  6. Repeat grating 10 sec/speed 8 , until a fine consistency is achieved. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  7. Add red onion and pistachios and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  8. Add all remaining ingredients, including reserved parsley and mint and combine 3 sec//speed 3.
  9. Transfer into large serving bowl with the quinoa and gently combine using the spatula before serving.

Useful Items

ceramic or glass bowl, large serving bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve quinoa tabouli as a side dish or as part of a Middle Eastern mezze plate.
