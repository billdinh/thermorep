Chocolate Cream Pie

Ingredients

Pie Crust

  • 1 homemade pie crust, chilled (9 in.)

Filling

  • 6 oz bittersweet chocolate, broken into pieces, plus extra to garnish
  • 9 oz sugar
  • 2 oz corn starch
  • 1 oz cocoa powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 24 oz whole milk
  • 9 egg yolks, from large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Whipped Cream Topping

  • 15 oz heavy whipping cream
  • 1 oz sugar
  • chocolate shavings, to garnish

Preparation

Pie Crust

  1. Adjust oven rack to lower middle position. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. Line chilled pie crust with parchment and baking weights and blind-bake for 15 minutes (375°F), remove parchment and weights and bake a further 10 minutes (375°F) until lightly golden. Transfer pie pan to a wire rack to cool completely (approx. 1 hr.). Meanwhile make filling.

Filling

  1. Place a large bowl onto mixing bowl lid, weigh in chocolate and set aside.
  2. Place sugar, corn starch, cocoa powder and salt into mixing bowl and mix 5 sec/speed 4.
  3. Add milk and egg yolks and cook 9 min/195°F/speed 3. Place a sieve over bowl of reserved chocolate and strain milk mixture into chocolate. Add vanilla and stir through with spatula. Allow to cool to room temperature.
  4. Pour the chocolate filling into cooled pie crust and chill until completely set (approx. 4 hr.). Clean mixing bowl.

Whipped Cream Topping

  1. Insert butterfly whisk. Place whipping cream and sugar into mixing bowl and without setting a time whip speed 3, until desired stiffness is achieved, watching carefully to avoid overwhipping. Remove butterfly whisk. Top pie with whipped cream, garnish with chocolate shavings and serve.

Useful Items

baking weights, cooling rack, parchment paper, fine-mesh sieve, large bowl, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Add 1-2 tsp espresso powder with the dry ingredients before mixing the filling.
  • Baking weights can be replaced with 16 oz dry beans.
