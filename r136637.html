Tomato basil chicken stew

Ingredients

  • 40 g Parmesan cheese, crust removed and cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 150 g brown onion, cut into quarters
  • 200 g carrot, cut into pieces (3-4 cm)
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 3 - 4 sprigs fresh basil, leaves only (approx. 20 g), plus extra for garnishing
  • 2 celery stalks, cut into pieces (3-4 cm)
  • 400 g canned tomatoes
  • 40 g white wine
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tbsp dried oregano
  • ½ tsp sweet paprika
  • 800 g skinless chicken thigh fillets, cut into cubes (4 cm)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper, to season
  • 800 g canned cannellini beans, rinsed and drained (approx. 500 g after draining)

Preparation

  1. Place Parmesan into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place oil, onion, carrot, garlic, basil and celery into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5
  3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then sauté 5 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  4. Add tomatoes, white wine, tomato paste, oregano and paprika and cook 5 min/100°C/speed 1, then blend 30 sec/speed 1, increasing speed gradually from speed 1 to speed 7. Scrape down side of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add chicken, salt and pepper and cook 10 min/100°C//speed , placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  6. Place cannellini beans into thermal serving bowl or other large bowl. Add chicken stew and stir to combine. Cover to keep warm and set aside for 2 minutes. Transfer into serving bowls and garnish with reserved Parmesan cheese and extra basil leaves before serving.

Useful Items

bowl, thermal serving bowl or other large bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • You can replace the chicken thigh fillets with chicken breasts in this recipe.
