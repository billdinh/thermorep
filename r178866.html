Fresh Pasta Dough

Ingredients

  • 200 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 2 medium eggs
  • 1 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • water, for cooking pasta
  • salt, for cooking pasta
Nutrition
per 1 portion

Preparation

  1. Place flour, eggs and oil in mixing bowl then knead 2 min/. Dough will appear crumbly. Bring dough into a ball, wrap in cling film and leave to rest for 15 minutes.
  2. On a floured surface, either with a rolling pin or with a pasta machine, roll out dough to thin (2-3 mm) rectangular shapes. Dust pasta lightly with flour to prevent it from sticking. Cut pasta in desired shapes according to the type of pasta you are making: Lasagne or ravioli: Cut dough in sheets (approx. 20 cm x 10 cm). Tagliatelle: Cut in strips (2 cm x max. 30 cm). Farfalle: Cut in small rectangles (2 cm x 5 cm) and pinch centre of each rectangle to form a butterfly shape.
  3. Cook pasta in a large quantity of salted boiling water (approx. 1 tsp salt per litre of water) for 3-4 minutes, depending on shape and thickness of pasta. Serve hot, with sauce as desired.

Useful Items

cling film, large saucepan, rolling pin or pasta machine, hob

Hints & Tricks

  • The hard work typically required to make fresh, egg-enriched pasta is minimised in your Thermomix® thanks to the kneading function. This simple recipe can be altered to create three different flavour and colour combinations.
  • Serve pasta as a side or main dish with tomato sauce, pesto or your favourite sauce.
  • Roll out small amounts of dough at a time and cover remaining dough with a damp kitchen towel to prevent it from drying out.
  • For best results, after rolling and cutting, leave pasta to dry for 1-2 hours on a floured cloth
  • To increase quantity, add 1 extra egg per added 100 g flour. The maximum quantity at any one time is 500 g flour and 5 eggs. Increase kneading time to 3 minutes.
  • Green pasta dough: use basic recipe with added 100 g flour and 70 g spinach leaves (fresh, clean and dry; or frozen, defrosted and water squeezed out). Place 300 g flour and spinach leaves into mixing bowl and mix 20 sec/speed 9. Add eggs and oil and knead 2 min/.
  • Red pasta dough without eggs: instead of 2 eggs, use 100 g tomato juice and proceed as directed by the recipe.
