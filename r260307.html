Gingerbread Men

Ingredients

Gingerbread Men

  • ½ inch fresh ginger
  • 5 oz sugar
  • 6 oz unsalted butter, cut into pieces
  • 4 oz molasses
  • 2 oz maple syrup
  • 3 tbsp ground ginger
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • ½ tsp ground nutmeg
  • ¼ tsp ground cloves
  • 18 oz all-purpose flour, plus extra to dust
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 1 large egg

Royal Icing

  • 14 oz sugar, divided
  • 1 egg white, from a large pasteurized egg
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp hot water
  • food coloring (e.g., red, green, silver, blue, gold)

Preparation

Gingerbread Men

  1. Line baking sheets with parchment and set aside.
  2. Place fresh ginger and sugar into mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add butter, molasses, maple syrup, ground ginger, cinnamon, nutmeg and cloves and melt 4 min/185°F/speed 1.
  4. Add flour, baking soda, salt and egg and mix 15 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix a further 5 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl, cover with cling wrap and refrigerate for 1 hour. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  5. Lightly flour work surface and rolling pin. Roll out dough to ⅛ inch thickness. Cut out 30 gingerbread men with a cookie cutter (approx. 3 in.) and place on prepared baking sheets. Bake for 8-10 minutes (350°F).
  6. Allow to cool 5 minutes then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely. Meanwhile make the icing.

Royal Icing

  1. Place 7 oz sugar into mixing bowl and grind 1 min/speed 10. Set ground sugar aside.
  2. Place remaining 7 oz of sugar into mixing bowl and grind 1 min/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add reserved ground sugar, egg white, lemon juice and water and mix 1 min/speed 4. Divide icing into small ramekins and add food coloring, mix through until completely emulsified and desired colors are achieved. Transfer to piping bags and decorate cookies as desired. Allow to dry completely before storing in an airtight container.

Useful Items

baking sheets, parchment paper, rolling pin, gingerbread man cookie cutter, wire rack, piping bags, small ramekins, airtight container, cling wrap, oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Pasteurized egg white can be replaced with unpasteurized egg white or 5 tbsp of meringue powder in royal icing.
