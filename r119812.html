Feta and Olive Loaves

Ingredients

  • 10 ½ oz water
  • 3 oz olive oil
  • 2 tsp dried yeast
  • 25 oz bread flour
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 6 oz feta cheese, cut into small pieces
  • 2 oz yellow onions, quartered
  • 3 ½ oz mixed olives, pitted, drained and patted dry with a paper towel
Nutrition
per 1 slice
Calories
331 kJ / 79 kcal
Protein
2 g
Carbohydrates
11 g
Fat
3 g

Preparation

  1. Place water, olive oil and yeast into mixing bowl and warm 1 min 30 sec/100°F/speed 2.
  2. Add flour and salt and knead 3 min/. Transfer onto floured surface and divide dough in 2 equal pieces.
  3. Place one dough piece and feta cheese into mixing bowl, knead 30 sec/ with aid of spatula and transfer onto a floured surface.
  4. Place onion into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5.
  5. Add second dough piece and olives and with aid of spatula knead 30 sec/. Transfer onto a floured surface.
  6. Preheat oven to 400°F. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  7. Form each dough piece into a loaf shape, place both loaves on prepared baking sheet and cover with a kitchen towel. Let rise in a warm place until doubled in size (approx. 40-60 min.) and bake for 45 minutes (400°F). Allow to cool on a wire rack before slicing.

Useful Items

kitchen towel, baking sheet and parchment paper, wire rack, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Before baking the bread, brush loaf with water and top with a sprinkling of sesame seeds.
  • You can prepare the loaves with feta only or just with olives.
  • Proofing time can vary due to temperature and altitude. Best is to watch for dough to double in size. It's also ready to bake when, if poked with your finger, the dough bounces back slowly but steadily.
