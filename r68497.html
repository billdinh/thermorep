Poached eggs

Ingredients

  • 1600 g water
  • 30 g white vinegar
  • oil, for brushing simmering basket
  • 2 eggs
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
713 kJ / 170 kcal
Protein
14 g
Carbohydrates
0.3 g
Fat
13 g

Preparation

  1. Place water and white vinegar into mixing bowl. Brush simmering basket with oil, insert simmering basket and heat 12 min/100°C/speed 1.
  2. Crack eggs into two separate containers and set aside.
  3. Water should now be boiling. Poach 3 min/100°C/speed 3, adding eggs one at a time through hole in mixing bowl lid into simmering basket. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and lay poached eggs onto paper towel to drain well. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

paper towel

Hints & Tricks

  • Additional portions: crack 2 eggs into separate containers. Re-oil simmering basket and reinsert simmering basket into mixing bowl. Reheat water 3 min/100°C/speed 1. Once water has returned to the boil, cook 3 min/100°C/speed 3, adding eggs one at a time through hole in mixing bowl lid into simmering basket. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula, drain eggs and serve straight away.
  • Serve poached eggs on toasted bread with Hollandaise sauce.
  • The water must be boiling before pouring in the eggs in step 3.
