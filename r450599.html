Sun-dried Tomato Tear and Share Bread

Ingredients

Dough

  • 220 g water
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 tsp dried instant yeast (8 g)
  • 500 g strong white bread flour
  • 90 g sun-dried tomatoes in oil, drained
  • 30 g olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt

Baking

  • 1 egg yolk, from medium or large egg

Preparation

Dough

  1. Preheat oven to 50°C (see tip) and grease a large baking tray.
  2. Place water, sugar and yeast in mixing bowl then mix 20 sec/speed 2.
  3. Add flour, sun-dried tomatoes, oil and salt then knead 2 min/. Tip out and divide dough into 12 equal-sized pieces, then roll each into a ball. Arrange side by side in rows (6 x 2) on prepared tray so rolls are just touching. Place in preheated oven and leave to prove for 20 minutes (50°C, quick rise).

Baking

  1. After this time, remove rolls from oven and increase oven temperature to 180°C. Brush rolls with egg yolk then return to oven and bake for 18-20 minutes (180°C) until rolls sound hollow when tapped underneath. Transfer to a wire rack and leave to cool before serving.

Useful Items

large baking tray, pastry brush, wire rack, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • If the oven does not have a 50°C setting, preheat oven to 100°C then turn off before placing loaves in warm oven to prove.
