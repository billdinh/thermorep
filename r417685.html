Mexican Stuffed Peppers

Ingredients

  • 4 mixed peppers, halved
  • 80 g onions, quartered
  • 20 g olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 - 2 fresh jalapeño chillies, to taste, halved, deseeded if desired
  • 5 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only, plus extra for garnishing
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp chilli powder, mild
  • 250 g turkey mince, 5% fat or less, broken in pieces
  • 200 g tinned black beans, rinsed, drained
  • 150 g tinned sweetcorn, drained
  • 30 g tomato purée
  • ½ - 1 tsp fine sea salt, to taste
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 125 g mozzarella, reduced fat, sliced (1 cm)
  • 2 spring onions, finely sliced
  • fat-free plain yoghurt, for serving
  • mixed salad leaves, for serving

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C and grease a large baking tray.
  2. Place peppers on prepared tray, cut side up. Bake for 15-20 minutes (200°C) until softened but still holding their shape. Meanwhile, make filling.
  3. Place onions, oil, garlic, jalapeños and coriander in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add cumin, paprika and chilli powder then sauté 5 min/90°C/speed 1.
  5. Add turkey mince, black beans, sweetcorn, tomato purée, salt and pepper then sauté 8 min/100°C//speed .
  6. Divide mixture between roasted pepper halves and top with mozzarella. Bake for a further 3-5 minutes (200°C) until cheese is melted.
  7. Top with spring onions and extra coriander then serve with yoghurt and salad.
