Honey Cereal Bars

Ingredients

  • 500 g muesli
  • 50 g sesame seeds
  • 120 g honey
  • 100 g sunflower oil
  • 50 g unsalted butter, at room temperature, plus extra for greasing
  • 2 egg whites, from medium eggs
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
561 kJ / 134 kcal
Protein
2.1 g
Carbohydrates
13.2 g
Fat
7.8 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C and grease a baking tray (30 cm x 40 cm).
  2. Place all ingredients in mixing bowl and mix 30 sec/speed 3. Tip mixture onto baking tray and use the back of a spoon to level surface.
  3. Bake for 15-20 minutes (180°C) until lightly browned.
  4. Remove from oven and, while hot, cut into bars (approx. 4 cm x 10 cm).
  5. Leave to cool and serve or store in an airtight container.

Useful Items

baking tray (30 cm x 40 cm), airtight container, long sharp knife, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Nutritional comment: These bars are suitable to eat at any time of the day, in combination with dairy or fruit, contributing to the 5 a day rule recommended however they contain a high level of carbohydrates, fibre and fats, so should be consumed in moderation.
