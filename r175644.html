Persian Flatbread with Herbs and Walnut Dip

Ingredients

Persian Flatbread

  • 430 g water
  • 20 g fresh yeast, crumbled
  • 10 g honey
  • 510 g strong white bread flour
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • polenta, coarsely ground, for dusting
  • oil, for greasing
  • bread flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 2 Tbsp sesame seeds
  • 2 Tbsp poppy seeds

Herbs and Walnut Dip

  • 100 g walnuts, halved or chopped
  • 10 g fresh basil leaves
  • 5 g fresh tarragon leaves
  • 10 g fresh mint leaves
  • 10 g fresh coriander leaves
  • 10 sprigs fresh chives, cut in pieces
  • 200 g feta cheese, cut in pieces
  • 50 g olive oil

Preparation

Persian Flatbread

  1. Place 350 g water, yeast and honey in mixing bowl then warm 2 min/37°C/speed 2.
  2. Add 160 g bread flour and mix 30 sec/speed 4. Leave in mixing bowl to rise for 20 minutes.
  3. Add remaining 340 g bread flour and salt then knead 1 min 30 sec/. Meanwhile, line a baking tray with baking paper and dust with polenta. Grease a large bowl and spatula with oil.
  4. Using oiled spatula, transfer dough to prepared bowl, cover with cling film and allow to rise in a warm place until doubled in size (approx. 1 hour).
  5. Transfer sticky dough to a very well floured surface and divide into 4 pieces. Carefully, using floured hands, shape each piece into an oval (approx. 18 cm long), being careful not to remove too much air from dough. Place on prepared baking tray, cover then set aside to rest for a further 30 minutes. Meanwhile, preheat oven to 200°C.
  6. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid, weigh in 80 g water and 10 g bread flour, add bicarbonate of soda and mix using a fork.
  7. Brush loaves with the soda-flour glaze, dip spatula in soda-flour glaze and then use to form 4 lengthwise indents. Sprinkle two loaves with sesame seeds and two with poppy seeds then bake for 25-30 minutes (200°C). Set aside to cool for 20 minutes. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl.

Herbs and Walnut Dip

  1. Place walnuts in mixing bowl and toast 4 min/120°C/speed 1.
  2. Add basil, tarragon, mint, coriander and chives then chop 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add feta cheese and olive oil then mix 30 sec/speed 4. Serve flatbreads with the dip.

Useful Items

cling film, baking tray and paper, pastry brush, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Try sprinkling with fennel seeds for a different flavour.
