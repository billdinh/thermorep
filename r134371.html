Lentil Soup with Bulgur Dumplings

Ingredients

  • 130 g dried green lentils
  • water, for soaking
  • 400 g pumpkin flesh, diced
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 1 ½ tsp cumin seeds
  • olive oil, for drizzling

Dumplings

  • 100 g onions, quartered
  • 5 g fresh mint, leaves only
  • 5 g fresh coriander, leaves only
  • 5 g fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 30 g salted butter, soft
  • 80 g bulgur wheat
  • 75 g plain flour
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt

Soup

  • 1 tsp vegetable stock paste
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 1200 g water
  • 150 g fresh spinach leaves
  • ½ lemon, finely grated zest and juice
  • 50 g pine nuts

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C.
  2. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in lentils. Cover with water then set aside to soak for 30 minutes.
  3. Arrange pumpkin in a roasting tin, season with salt, pepper, cumin seeds and a drizzle of olive oil then bake for 30 minutes (200°C). Meanwhile, make dumplings.

Dumplings

  1. Place onions, mint, coriander and parsley in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add butter, bulgur wheat, flour and salt then mix 15 sec/speed 3. Form into dumplings (approx. Ø 2.5 cm) then distribute between Varoma dish and Varoma tray.

Soup

  1. Drain soaked lentils then place in mixing bowl.
  2. Add half the roasted pumpkin, stock, salt, oil and water in mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position and cook 30 min/Varoma//speed 1. Set Varoma aside.
  3. Add spinach, lemon zest and juice, remaining roasted pumpkin and steamed dumplings then stir with spatula.
  4. Serve garnished with pine nuts.

Useful Items

roasting tin, medium mesh sieve, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • These dumplings contain bulgur wheat which provides a rich source of protein, similar to that found in the popular quinoa.
