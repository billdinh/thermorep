Pasta with Prawns, Courgettes and Lemon

Ingredients

  • 1 lemon, thin peelings of skin and 30 g juice
  • 30 g Parmesan cheese, cut in pieces (3 cm)
  • 1200 g vegetable stock
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt, to taste
  • 200 g prawns, raw, large
  • 300 g courgettes, sliced
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 250 g tagliatelle
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 200 g crème fraîche
  • 1 tsp fresh chives, chopped
  • 1 tsp fresh dill
  • 1 - 2 pinches ground black pepper, to taste

Preparation

  1. Place lemon peel and Parmesan in mixing bowl then grate 10 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place vegetable stock and salt in mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish into position then weigh in prawns and pour 15 g lemon juice over the top. Insert Varoma tray then add courgettes and garlic then steam 10 min/Varoma/speed 1. Set Varoma aside.
  3. Add tagliatelle through hole in mixing bowl lid and cook 1 min 30 sec/100°C//speed . Place Varoma into position then cook select time indicated on packet/100°C//speed 1. Remove Varoma. Drain pasta through simmering basket reserving cooking liquid, then place in a serving bowl, stir through oil, cover and keep warm.
  4. Place 100 g cooking liquid in mixing bowl. Discard garlic cloves, add half the cooked courgettes, crème fraîche, remaining 15 g lemon juice, chives, dill and pepper then blend 20 sec/speed 8. Reheat courgette sauce 2 min/90°C/speed 1.
  5. Add remaining cooked courgettes to pasta in serving bowl then add prawns and courgette sauce. Mix well. Sprinkle with grated lemon-Parmesan mixture before serving immediately.

Hints & Tricks

  • Sauté the courgettes in a pan with olive oil before garnishing the pasta for an even more delicious flavour.
