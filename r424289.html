Strawberry and Peach Frozen Yoghurt Bites

Ingredients

  • 100 g fresh strawberries, hulled, halved, plus 3 extra diced (1 cm)
  • 100 g peaches, ripe, cut in pieces, plus 30 g extra diced (1 cm)
  • 20 g sugar
  • 200 g light cream cheese
  • 150 g fat-free plain yoghurt
  • ½ orange, finely grated zest only
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
104 kJ / 25 kcal
Protein
1 g
Carbohydrates
3 g
Fat
1 g

Preparation

  1. Line a 24 hole mini muffin tin with mini muffin cases (see tip).
  2. Place 100 g strawberries, 100 g peaches and sugar in mixing bowl then blend 20 sec/speed 5-10, increasing speed gradually. Scrape down sides and lid of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add cream cheese, yoghurt, orange zest and vanilla extract then mix 10 sec/speed 4. Divide mixture between prepared muffin cases and top each with a piece of strawberry and a piece of peach. Place in freezer for at least 2 hours or overnight (see tip).
  4. Serve from frozen.

Useful Items

24 hole mini muffin tin, mini muffin cases, freezer

Hints & Tricks

  • This recipe uses low GI fruits combined with low-fat, low-GI dairy to make a simple dessert or summery snack that can be made in advance.
  • Mini silicone muffin cases can be used instead of a muffin tray lined with paper cases.
  • Once frozen, these bites can be removed from the tray and stored in a sealed freezer-proof container in the freezer.
