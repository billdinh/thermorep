Pork Tortas

Ingredients

Pork

  • 42 oz pork loin, cubed (1½ in.), fat removed
  • 5 oz orange juice (approx. 3 lg. oranges)
  • 2 tsp salt, coarse, to taste
  • 1 sprig fresh thyme
  • 1 tsp fresh marjoram (approx. 2 sprigs)
  • 1 tsp dried Mexican oregano
  • 2 dried bay leaves
  • 2 oz vegetable shortening

Chili Sauce

  • 2 oz fresh 'chile de árbol' chilies, to taste
  • 21 oz water
  • 6 oz tomatoes, halved
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 4 ½ oz apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tsp salt

Tomato Sauce

  • 25 oz tomatoes, quartered
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 3 oz yellow onions
  • 10 oz water
  • 1 tbsp dried Mexican oregano
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp cumin seed

Tortas

  • 9 oz yellow onions, quartered
  • 3 tbsp dried Mexican oregano
  • 2 oz lime juice (approx. 1 lg. lime)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 6 bread rolls, cut in half
  • 1 can refried beans (approx. 16 oz)

Preparation

Pork

  1. Place pork, orange juice, salt, thyme, marjoram, oregano, bay leaves and vegetable shortening into mixing bowl and cook 70 min/212°F//speed . Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.

Chili Sauce

  1. Place chilies, water, tomatoes and garlic into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5, then cook 15 min/212°F/speed 1.
  2. Add vinegar and salt, and mix 1 min/speed 5-10, increasing speed gradually. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.

Tomato Sauce

  1. Place tomatoes, garlic, onions, water, oregano, salt and cumin into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 4, and then cook 15 min/212°F/speed 1. Blend 1 min/speed 5-10, increasing speed gradually. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.

Tortas

  1. Place onions, oregano, lime juice and salt into mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Spread bread rolls with refried beans, fill with reserved pork and pour Tomato Sauce over top of the bread. Serve with reserved onions and Chili Sauce.
