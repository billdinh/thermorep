Nectarine Tart with Hazelnut Frangipane

Ingredients

  • 320 g puff pastry, ready made
  • 2 medium eggs, one beaten
  • 100 g hazelnuts, roasted
  • 80 g sugar
  • 50 g plain flour
  • 100 g butter, diced
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 750 g nectarines, peeled, stoned, cut in slices (total weight)
  • 200 g apricot jam

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C and line a baking tray with baking paper.
  2. With a rolling pin, roll out puff pastry into a rectangle approx. 25 cm x 40 cm x 3 mm. Place in fridge to chill.
  3. Place hazelnuts, sugar and flour in mixing bowl then grind 10 sec/speed 8.
  4. Add butter, 1 egg, reserved remaining beaten egg and vanilla then mix 15 sec/speed 4. Spread onto chilled pastry base leaving a border of 1 cm unfilled, then arrange overlapping layers of nectarines on top.
  5. Bake for 30-40 minutes (180°C) until golden brown. Remove from oven then brush surface with apricot jam. Serve warm or cold, cut into portions.

Useful Items

baking tray and paper, rolling pin, pastry brush, oven, refrigerator
