Chocolate Cherry Muffins

Ingredients

  • 85 g cocoa powder
  • 150 g plain flour
  • 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • ¼ tsp baking powder
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • 225 g caster sugar
  • 170 g olive oil, light
  • 2 medium eggs
  • 240 g water, hot
  • 260 g tinned pitted black cherries, in syrup, (1 x 400 g tin, drained)

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 170°C. Line a 12 hole muffin tin with paper cases.
  2. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid then weigh in cocoa, flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt. Remove bowl from lid, combine using a spoon then Set aside.
  3. Place sugar, oil and eggs in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 3.
  4. Add hot water and mix 10 sec/speed 3.
  5. Set speed 3 and add reserved dry ingredients through hole in mixing bowl lid until combined. (The mixture may be slightly lumpy which is okay).
  6. Fold in drained cherries using spatula then divide between paper cases. Bake for 25-35 minutes (170°C) or until a cocktail stick inserted in the centre comes out clean.
  7. Leave to rest for 5 minutes then remove from tins to cool completely before serving.

Useful Items

12 hole muffin tin, cocktail stick, muffin paper cases, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Sweet cherries contain vitamins C and A, as well as calcium and iron. Antioxidant anthocyanins, which give cherries their red colour, can reduce the inflammation that can come with exercise.
  • Top these beautifully moist muffins with cream cheese frosting for a delicious snack or dessert.
