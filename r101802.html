Gluten Free Lemon Cupcakes

Ingredients

  • 130 g sugar, plus a little extra for sprinkling
  • 1 Tbsp vanilla sugar
  • 3 - 4 lemons, thin peelings of skin only
  • 4 medium eggs
  • 120 g sunflower oil
  • 125 g plain yoghurt, lemon flavoured
  • 20 g lemon juice
  • 180 g gluten free plain white flour
  • 50 g cornflour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp dried instant yeast
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt

Preparation

  1. Line two 12-hole muffin or cupcake tins with paper cases.
  2. Place sugar, vanilla sugar and lemon zest in mixing bowl then grind 30 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add eggs and beat 3 min/37°C/speed 5. Mix 3 min/speed 5.
  4. Add oil, yoghurt and lemon juice then beat 15 sec/speed 5.
  5. Add flour, cornflour, baking powder, yeast and salt then mix  6 sec/speed 6. Fill muffin cases until ¾ full and chill in fridge for 30 minutes. Meanwhile, preheat oven to 200°C.
  6. Sprinkle cupcakes with a little sugar then bake for 12-14 minutes (200°C) or until browned. Leave to cool then serve.

Useful Items
