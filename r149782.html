Allergen Friendly Mushroom Risotto

Ingredients

Stock

  • 230 g onions, quartered
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 30 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 1200 g water, hot
  • ½ Tbsp black peppercorns
  • 2 dried bay leaves
  • 2 sprigs fresh thyme

Risotto

  • 10 g fresh parsley leaves
  • 100 g onions, quartered
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 40 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 50 g dried porcini mushrooms, soaked in boiling water for 30 minutes then squeezed dry, soaking liquid reserved
  • 350 g risotto rice
  • 2 Tbsp nutritional yeast (optional)
  • 1 ½ tsp fine sea salt

Preparation

Stock

  1. Place onions, garlic and oil in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then sauté 4 min/100°C//speed .
  2. Add water, peppercorns, bay leaves and fresh thyme then cook 10 min/100°C/speed 1. With simmering basket in place of measuring cup, cook 30 min/105°C/speed 1.
  3. Strain stock liquid through a fine sieve into a jug, discarding contents of bowl. Set stock aside. Clean mixing bowl.

Risotto

  1. Place parsley in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place onions and garlic in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add oil and mushrooms then sauté 3 min/100°C/speed 1.
  4. Add rice and 100 g reserved stock then cook 3 min/100°C/speed 1.
  5. Add 800 g remaining reserved stock (top up to 800 g with water if necessary), 50 g reserved mushroom water (if using dried mushrooms), nutritional yeast flakes (if using) and salt then cook 13 min/100°C/speed 1. Serve hot sprinkled with reserved chopped parsley.
