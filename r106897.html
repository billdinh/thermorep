Fig and Maple Bourbon Fizz

Ingredients

Fig-infused Bourbon

  • 9 oz bourbon
  • 10 fresh figs (approx. 7 oz), halved

Bourbon Fizz

  • 9 oz ice cubes
  • 2 tsp pure maple syrup
  • 1 oz lemon juice (approx. ½ lemon)
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters
  • 21 oz ginger beer

Preparation

Fig-infused Bourbon

  1. Place a clean glass jar on top of mixing bowl lid. Weigh bourbon into jar, add figs into jar and seal. Set aside for 5-7 days to infuse, giving the jar a gentle shake each day. Once infused, strain bourbon through a fine mesh sieve into a clean glass jar. Reserve 4 bourbon-infused fig halves and store remaining for future use.

Bourbon Fizz

  1. Place ice cubes into mixing bowl and crush 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add 4 oz Fig-infused Bourbon, 4 reserved bourbon-infused fig halves, and all remaining ingredients and combine 10 sec//speed 2. Divide between four glasses, placing half a fig in each glass to serve.

Useful Items

fine mesh strainer, sealable storage jar, lowball glasses, airtight jar or container

Hints & Tricks

  • If fresh figs are not in season, replace with dried figs cut into halves.
  • Use leftover strained figs in cakes or jam.
