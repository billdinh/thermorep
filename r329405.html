Vegan Turkish delight cheesecake

Ingredients

Soaking

  • 200 g raw cashews (see Tips)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • filtered water, for soaking

Base

  • 2 tsp coconut oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 60 g pecans
  • 40 g shredded coconut
  • 1 tsp vanilla paste (see Tips)
  • 1 tbsp raw cacao powder
  • 1 tsp rose water
  • 1 tsp filtered water
  • 1 tbsp pure maple syrup
  • 4 pitted dates

Filling

  • 80 g lemon juice
  • 150 g coconut cream
  • 100 g pure maple syrup
  • 1 tsp agar agar powder
  • 2 - 4 tsp rose water, to taste
  • 1 tsp beetroot powder (see Tips)

Raspberry rose jelly

  • 60 g lemon juice
  • 200 g fresh raspberries (see Tips), plus extra for decorating
  • 1 tbsp pure maple syrup
  • 1 ½ tsp agar agar powder
  • 3 - 4 tsp rose water, to taste

Preparation

Soaking

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh cashews into it. Add salt, cover with water and set aside to soak for 3-6 hours.
  2. Using simmering basket, drain and rinse cashews.

Base

  1. Grease and line a loose-based rectangular tart tin (34 x 13 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place coconut oil into mixing bowl and melt 1 min/90°C/speed 1.
  3. Add all remaining base ingredients and blend 10 sec/speed 8.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then blend 5 sec/speed 5 or until mixture is combined and sticky. Using damp hands or wearing gloves, evenly press mixture into prepared tin. Place into refrigerator while preparing filling (approx. 15 minutes). Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Filling

  1. Place drained cashews into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7.
  2. Add all remaining filling ingredients and blend 1 min/speed 8, until smooth.
  3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and heat 5 min/100°C/speed 4. Pour filling over chilled base and tap on kitchen bench to remove any air bubbles. Cover and place into refrigerator while preparing jelly (approx. 5 minutes). Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Raspberry rose jelly

  1. Place all jelly ingredients into mixing bowl and cook 6 min/100°C/speed 2, without measuring cup. Pour jelly mixture over filling, then cover and place into refrigerator to set completely (approx. 2 hours). Decorate with fresh raspberries to serve.

Useful Items

baking paper, bowl, loose-based rectangular tart tin (34 x 13 cm), plastic wrap, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • If you don't have time to soak/activate your cashews, soak nuts in boiling water for 30 minutes, then drain and use as directed.
  • To make your own vanilla paste, refer to the recipe on Cookidoo® (www.cookidoo.com.au).
  • Agar agar yields the best results when combined with hot liquids, then transferred into the refrigerator to set. You can find agar agar powder at health food stores, some Asian food stores and online.
  • You can find beetroot powder at specialty food shops, health food shops and online.
  • You can replace the fresh raspberries with frozen raspberries, if desired. If using frozen raspberries, increase the cooking time to 10 min/100°C/speed 2, without measuring cup.
