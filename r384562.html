Pastéis de Nata (Custard Pastries)

Ingredients

Custard

  • 180 g sugar
  • 50 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 250 g double cream
  • 250 g milk
  • 5 egg yolks, from medium eggs
  • lemon peel, 1 strip
  • 1 cinnamon stick

Pastry

  • 500 g puff pastry, ready-made
  • 1 medium egg, beaten, for brushing

Preparation

Custard

  1. Place sugar, flour, cream, milk and egg yolks in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 4.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Add lemon peel and cinnamon then cook 15 min/90°C/speed 1.5. Remove butterfly whisk.

Pastry

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C.
  2. On a lightly-floured surface, roll out puff pastry then cut out 12 discs and use to fill a 12 hole muffin tin, making sure that pastry comes up the sides of each hole.
  3. Remove lemon zest and cinnamon then pour custard into pastry cups to just below the top of the pastry.
  4. Brush edges of dough with beaten egg. Bake for 12-15 minutes (200°C) until browned. Make sure pastry is cooked by lifting one tart out to check it is browned underneath.
  5. Transfer to a wire rack to cool completely or serve warm.

Useful Items

12 hole muffin tin, rolling pin, pastry cutter, pastry brush, wire rack, oven
