Chinese greens with XO sauce

Ingredients

XO sauce

  • 40 g dried scallops (see Tips)
  • 150 g boiling water
  • 200 g peanut oil
  • 4 dried red chillies
  • 60 g dried shrimp
  • 80 g eschalots
  • 60 g garlic cloves
  • 40 g piece fresh ginger, peeled and cut into slices (1 cm)
  • 100 g smoked bacon, cut into pieces (1 cm)
  • 6 long red chillies, deseeded
  • 1 fresh bird's eye chilli, deseeded if desired
  • 40 g light soy sauce
  • 40 g Shaoxing wine
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar

Asian greens

  • 100 g snow peas
  • 100 g frozen green peas
  • 500 g water
  • 2 baby bok choy, leaves separated
  • 6 stalks broccolini, cut into halves lengthways
  • 1 gai choy, leaves separated (8 leaves)
  • 3 spring onions/shallots, cut into slices, for garnishing
  • 2 long red chillies, cut into slices, for garnishing

Preparation

XO sauce

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh dried scallops and boiling water into it. Set aside for 30 minutes.
  2. Place oil and dried chillies into mixing bowl and cook 8 min/90°C/speed 1. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place dried shrimp and soaked scallops with soaking water into mixing bowl and blend 10 sec/speed 8. Transfer into a separate bowl and set aside.
  4. Place eschalots, garlic, ginger, bacon, red chillies and bird's eye chilli into mixing bowl and blend 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add reserved oil and chillies and heat 10 min/Varoma/speed 1. Scrape base of mixing bowl with spatula to loosen mixture.
  6. Add reserved shrimp and scallop paste and cook 30 min/100°C/speed 1.5. Scrape base of mixing bowl with spatula to loosen mixture.
  7. Add soy sauce, Shaoxing wine and sugar, then cook 20 min/70°C/speed 1.5, without measuring cup. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Asian greens

  1. Insert simmering basket and weigh snow peas and green peas into it. Add water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position and place bok choy, broccolini and gai choy into it. Secure Varoma lid and steam 5 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  2. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula if peas are cooked to your liking. Using tongs, turn vegetables in Varoma then secure Varoma lid and steam 2 min/Varoma/speed 1, or until vegetables are cooked, but still crisp.
  3. Arrange greens onto a large serving platter and spoon over some of the XO sauce. Garnish with spring onions/shallots and sliced chillies before serving.

Useful Items

bowl, serving platter

Hints & Tricks

  • Any greens can be used in this dish.
  • Vegetable oil can be used instead of peanut oil.
  • Scallops can be omitted and dried shrimp quantity increased to 100 g. Dried shrimp can be purchased at Asian food stores.
  • Transfer any leftover XO sauce into a sealable jar or container and place into refrigerator. It will last for 1 month in the refrigerator.
