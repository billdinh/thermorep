Brisket Burrito

Ingredients

Meat and Rice

  • 3 ½ oz long-grain rice
  • water, to soak rice
  • 16 oz beef brisket, untrimmed, cubed (1 in.)
  • 24 oz water
  • 2 pinches ground cumin
  • 1 pinch chili powder
  • 1 dried bay leaf
  • 2 ½ oz white onion
  • ½ tsp salt

Beans

  • 1 can black beans (approx. 15 oz), rinsed and drained
  • 5 oz canned sweet corn kernels, drained
  • 2 pinches ground cumin
  • 2 pinches chipotle chili powder (optional)
  • 4 ½ oz canned tomato sauce
  • ½ tsp salt (optional)

Burrito

  • 10 large flour tortillas
  • salad greens, to serve
  • pico de gallo, to serve

Preparation

Meat and Rice

  1. Place simmering basket onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in rice. Remove simmering basket with rice and rinse under running water until water runs clear, then submerge in a bowl of water and let rice soak for 35 minutes.
  2. Drain rice through simmering basket. Place brisket, water, cumin, chili powder, bay leaf, onion and salt into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket with rice and steam 40 min/Varoma//speed . Transfer cooked rice into a bowl, cover to keep warm and set aside.
  3. Strain cooked meat over a bowl. Discard broth and bay leaf. Return meat to mixing bowl and shred 8-10 sec//speed 4. Transfer shredded beef to a bowl, cover and keep warm.

Beans

  1. Place beans, corn, cumin, chipotle powder, tomato sauce and salt into mixing bowl and cook 5 min/250°F//speed . Transfer to a bowl, cover and set aside.

Burrito

  1. Warm tortillas in a hot cast iron pan (approx. 10 in.) and transfer to a plate. Fill each tortilla with reserved shredded meat and rice. Using a slotted spoon, add beans. Wrap the burritos by folding two opposite sides into the center and then rolling up with the remaining opposite sides. Serve with salad greens, pico de gallo or your favorite salsa.

Useful Items
