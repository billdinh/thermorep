Zesty avocado with crab

Ingredients

  • 500 g avocados, flesh only (approx. 350 g flesh)
  • 1 tsp Vegetable stock paste (see Tips)
  • 80 g crème fraîche
  • 1 lime, zested and juiced
  • 200 g water, plus extra to taste
  • flaked salt, to taste
  • 70 g celery stalks, cut into small pieces (5 mm), reserve leaves for garnishing
  • 100 g cucumber, peeled, deseeded and cut into small pieces (5 mm)
  • 120 g crab meat, cooked and chilled
  • freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Preparation

  1. Place avocado, stock paste, crème fraîche, lime zest and juice, and water into mixing bowl and blend 20 sec/speed 8.
  2. Mixture should have a thick, purée consistency. If desired, mix 30 sec/speed 4, adding extra water through hole in mixing bowl lid to attain desired consistency.
  3. Add salt to taste and mix 10 sec/speed 3.
  4. Divide purée between 8 small, shallow bowls or small cups. Arrange celery, cucumber and crab meat on top. Garnish with celery leaves and pepper to serve.

Useful Items

8 serving bowls

Hints & Tricks

  • Please refer to The Basic Cookbook or Everyday Cookbook for the Vegetable stock paste recipe.
  • Fishmongers sell cooked, picked crab meat.
  • You can substitute crème fraîche with yoghurt or sour cream.
  • You can substitute lime with lemon.
  • Soup can be made up to 1 day in advance; store in a sealed container in the refrigerator with plastic wrap resting on the surface of the soup.
