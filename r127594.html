Oat slice

Ingredients

  • 150 g unsalted butter, cut into pieces, plus extra for greasing
  • 180 g plain flour
  • 120 g oat flakes
  • 110 g raw sugar
  • 70 g shredded coconut
  • 2 tbsp golden syrup
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • 2 tbsp water, boiling
Nutrition
per 1 slice
Calories
671 kJ / 159 kcal
Protein
1 g
Carbohydrates
17 g
Fat
8 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C. Grease and line a square baking dish (21 cm) with baking paper, allowing a 2 cm overhang at each end. Set dish aside.
  2. Place flour, oats, sugar and coconut into mixing bowl and combine 10 sec//speed 3. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place butter and golden syrup into mixing bowl and melt 2 min/60°C/speed 1, or until butter has melted.
  4. Add baking soda and boiling water and mix 5 sec/speed 3.
  5. Add reserved oats mixture and combine 5 sec//speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then repeat combining 5 sec//speed 4. Transfer mixture into prepared baking dish.
  6. Using the back of a spoon, press mixture evenly into dish, then bake for 15-30 minutes (160°C) (see tip).
  7. Allow to cool in dish before cutting into squares and serving.

Useful Items

baking paper, square cake tin (21 cm x 21 cm), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • The baking time in step 6 will determine the end result. For a soft and chewy slice, bake for 15 minutes; for a firmer slice bake for 20-25 minutes and; for a very firm slice bake for 30 minutes.
