Roses with Love

Ingredients

  • 250 g fresh strawberries, chilled, washed, hulled
  • 750 g sparkling wine, chilled (e.g. Muscato)
  • 6 ice cubes
  • ½ - 1 tsp rose water, to taste
  • rose petals, dried, for garnish (optional)
Nutrition
per 1 glass
Calories
370 kJ / 88 kcal
Protein
0.5 g
Carbohydrates
6.7 g
Fat
0 g

Preparation

  1. Place strawberries and 375 g wine in mixing bowl then blend 15 sec/speed 7.
  2. Add remaining 375 g wine, ice cubes and rose water then mix 3 sec/speed 7.
  3. Strain cocktail through a fine sieve into a jug. Divide between 8 cocktail glasses and serve garnished with dried rose petals.

Useful Items

cocktail glasses, fine sieve or nut milk bag, jug
