Beef Empanadas

Ingredients

Pastry

  • 13 oz all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 ½ oz vegetable shortening
  • 6 - 6 ½ oz warm water, divided

Beef filling

  • 19 ½ oz flank steak, semi-frozen, thinly sliced, divided
  • 3 oz pitted black olives
  • 4 oz red bell pepper, de-seeded and de-stemmed
  • 2 oz white onion, cut into pieces
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • ½ oz olive oil
  • 5 oz fresh Roma tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • ½ tsp ground cumin
  • ¼ tsp oregano
  • ¾ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper

Assembly

  • all-purpose flour, to dust
  • 1 large egg, beaten for egg wash

Preparation

Pastry

  1. Place flour, salt, shortening and 6 oz warm water into mixing bowl and mix 15 sec/speed 4. Knead 3 min/, until dough is soft and easy to work with. If necessary, add more water (0.5 oz) and knead a further 30 sec/. Transfer into a bowl, cover with cling wrap and set aside.

Beef filling

  1. Place 10 oz semi-frozen beef slices into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 6, if necessary chop a further 10 sec/speed 6. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Place 9.5 oz semi-frozen beef into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 6, if necessary chop a further 10 sec/speed 6. Transfer into bowl with reserved chopped beef.
  2. Place black olives and red pepper into mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 4. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place onion, garlic, olive oil, tomatoes, Worcestershire, cumin, oregano, salt and pepper into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and sauté 5 min/240°F/speed .
  4. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line a baking sheet with parchment and set aside.
  5. Add chopped meat, reserved olives and peppers. Place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 7 min/212°F//speed 0.5. Transfer beef filling into simmering basket and drain, discarding juice. Transfer drained beef into a bowl, set aside and let cool completely.

Assembly

  1. Divide dough into 12 pieces and form small balls. Roll out dough on a floured surface into thin circles (⅛ in.). If pastry shrinks, roll out again just before filling.
  2. Place 1-2 heaping tablespoons of reserved beef filling in the center of each pastry circle. Lightly brush edges with egg wash and fold over to form a semi-circle. Seal edges together, making sure to remove any air bubbles. With a fork or fingertips crimp the edges. Continue until all pastry and filling is used.
  3. Transfer empanadas to prepared baking sheet, brush with egg wash and bake for 20 minutes (350°F) or until lightly golden. Serve warm.

Useful Items

cling wrap, rolling pin, baking sheet, parchment paper, oven
