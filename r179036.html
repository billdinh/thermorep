Pumpkin and pomegranate quinoa salad

Ingredients

  • 400 g butternut pumpkin, peeled and cut into pieces (2 x 5 cm)
  • 1 - 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 ½ tbsp pure maple syrup
  • 1 - 2 tbsp sesame seeds
  • 700 g water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 200 g quinoa
  • 30 g raw almonds
  • 30 g cashews
  • 40 g avocado oil
  • 1 spring onion/shallot, trimmed and cut into thirds
  • 2 sprigs fresh mint, leaves only
  • 20 g lemon juice
  • 50 g fresh baby spinach leaves
  • 1 pomegranate, arils only (see Tips)

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Line a baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper.
  2. Place pumpkin onto prepared baking tray, drizzle with olive oil and 1 tablespoon of the syrup, then sprinkle with sesame seeds and toss to coat. Roast for 25-30 minutes (180°C), until pumpkin is soft. Meanwhile, proceed with recipe.
  3. Place water, salt and pepper into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket, weigh quinoa into it, then cook 10 min/100°C/speed 4 .
  4. Stir quinoa with aid of spatula, then cook 10 min/100°C/speed 4. Set aside in mixing bowl for 5 minutes. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and transfer cooked quinoa into a large bowl. Stir with spatula to loosen and set aside for 10 minutes to cool. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  5. Place almonds and cashews into mixing bowl and crush 2 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a small bowl and set aside.
  6. Place avocado oil, spring onion/shallot, mint, lemon juice and remaining 2 teaspoons syrup into mixing bowl and combine 5 sec/speed 7 .
  7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and combine 5 sec/speed 5 .
  8. Add dressing to cooled quinoa and stir to combine. Place bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh spinach into it. Add pomegranate arils, reserved chopped nuts and roasted pumpkin. Stir gently to combine before serving.

Useful Items

baking tray (30 x 40 cm), baking paper, large bowl, small bowl , oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Go to the Thermomix in Australia YouTube channel to see a video on how to easily remove the arils from your pomegranate in your Thermomix®.
  • If you don't follow a strict vegan diet, you can replace the maple syrup with 1½ tablespoons honey.
