Cheddar and Walnut Scones with Onion Jam

Ingredients

Onion Jam

  • 16 oz red onions, cut into half moons (¼ in.)
  • 12 oz sugar
  • 3 ½ oz red wine vinegar
  • 1 oz white wine
  • 1 oz raisins
  • &frac18; tsp ground bay leaves (optional)
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • 3 whole cloves
  • ¼ tsp salt, to taste

Cheddar and Walnut Scones

  • 3 oz cheddar cheese, cut into pieces
  • 2 oz walnut pieces
  • 1 tsp dried rosemary
  • 8 oz all-purpose flour, plus extra to dust
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp cayenne pepper
  • ¼ tsp mustard powder
  • 1 large egg
  • 3 oz whole milk, plus extra to brush
  • 1 oz extra virgin olive oil

Preparation

Onion Jam

  1. Place onions, sugar, vinegar, wine, raisins, bay leaves, cinnamon and cloves into mixing bowl, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 45 min/212°F//speed 1.
  2. Add salt, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 15 min/Varoma//speed 1. Transfer to a bowl and cool completely.

Cheddar and Walnut Scones

  1. Preheat oven 425°F. Line a baking sheet with parchment and set aside.
  2. Place cheese, walnuts and rosemary into mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place flour, baking powder, salt, cayenne, mustard powder, egg, milk and olive oil into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add reserved cheese mixture and mix a further 5 sec/speed 5.
  5. Transfer dough to a lightly floured surface and pat or roll into a circle about 1½ inches thick. Cut dough into 6 equal wedges. Place scones onto prepared baking sheet.
  6. Brush scones with milk and bake 12-15 minutes (425°F) or until golden brown. Serve warm with reserved Onion Jam.

Useful Items

parchment paper, pastry brush, baking sheet, oven
