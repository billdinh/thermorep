Banana berry mini loaves

Ingredients

  • oil, for greasing
  • 100 g raw sugar
  • 80 g raw almonds
  • 30 g cornflour
  • 3 egg whites, room temperature
  • 1 pinch cream of tartar
  • 40 g unsalted butter
  • 6 strawberries (3 strawberries, cut into quarters; 3 strawberries, finely sliced for garnishing)
  • 40 g banana, cut into slices (1 cm)

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Grease 4 holes of an 8-hole mini loaf tray or 4 individual mini loaf tins and set aside.
  2. Place sugar into mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer icing sugar into a large bowl and set aside.
  3. Place almonds and cornflour into mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer into the bowl with the sugar and set aside. Thoroughly clean and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg whites and cream of tartar into mixing bowl and whip 3 min/50°C/speed 3.5, until stiff peaks form. Transfer egg whites into a separate bowl to the sugar and set aside. Remove butterfly whisk.
  5. Without cleaning mixing bowl, place butter into mixing bowl and melt 30 sec/80°C/speed 3.
  6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and repeat melting 30 sec/80°C/speed 3. Allow to cool for 2 minutes in mixing bowl.
  7. Transfer half of the reserved egg whites into the bowl with the icing sugar and almond mixture. Gently fold using a metal spoon. Add remaining egg whites and repeat folding.
  8. Add melted butter, 3 strawberries, cut into quarters and banana and gently fold through until well combined.
  9. Transfer into prepared tray or tins and bake for 10 minutes (180°C) on the bottom of the oven. Remove loaves from oven and garnish by placing 3 strawberries, finely sliced down the centre of the loaves.
  10. Place loaves onto the middle shelf of the oven and bake for 15-20 minutes (180°C) or until a wooden skewer inserted into the centre of each loaf comes out clean. Cool loaves in tray for 15 minutes. Remove from tray and transfer onto a wire rack to cool completely (approx. 30 minutes), before serving.

Useful Items

8-hole mini loaf tray or 4 individual mini loaf tins, wooden skewer, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Wrap portions in plastic wrap and store in the freezer to enjoy another day.
  • Dust loaves with a little icing sugar before serving if desired.
