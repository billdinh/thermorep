Quick Tomato and Basil Gnocchi

Ingredients

Breadcrumb Topping

  • 2 slices wholemeal bread, cut in pieces (approx. 90 g)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 5 g fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 75 g grated mozzarella

Gnocchi

  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 3 - 4 shallots, halved (approx. 100 g)
  • 10 g olive oil
  • 750 g passata
  • 1 ½ Tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • ½ tsp dried basil
  • ½ tsp dried chilli flakes
  • ½ tsp caster sugar
  • 2 tsp fine sea salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 150 g fresh spinach
  • water, for cooking
  • 1000 g gnocchi
  • 75 g grated mozzarella

Preparation

Breadcrumb Topping

  1. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in mozzarella, then set aside. Place bread, garlic and parsley in mixing bowl then grate 6 sec/speed 8. Transfer to bowl with mozzarella, stir with spatula to combine, then set aside.

Gnocchi

  1. Place garlic, shallots and oil in mixing bowl, chop 4 sec/speed 5 then sauté 2 min/100°C/speed 1.
  2. Add passata, balsamic vinegar, basil, chilli flakes, sugar, salt and pepper then cook 10 min/100°C//speed 2. Add spinach and stir with spatula until wilted.
  3. Bring a large saucepan of water with 1 tsp salt added to the boil. Cook gnocchi for 2 minutes less than the pack instructions, drain using a colander and transfer to a large oven-proof dish.
  4. Preheat grill to medium heat. Cover gnocchi with reserved tomato sauce, fold in mozzarella and finish with breadcrumb topping.
  5. Place gnocchi under grill for 3-5 minutes, until cheese is bubbling and golden. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

large saucepan, colander, oven-proof dish, grill
