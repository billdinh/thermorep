Goat's Cheese Pie

Ingredients

  • 500 g water
  • 160 g potatoes, peeled, thinly sliced (2 mm)
  • 2 pinches fine sea salt
  • 25 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 tsp fresh thyme, leaves only
  • 170 g red onions, thinly sliced (2 mm)
  • 1 Tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • plain flour, for dusting
  • 400 g puff pastry, ready-made
  • 200 g cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 150 g goat's cheese, hard or soft
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 1 egg, beaten

Preparation

  1. Place water in mixing bowl.
  2. Place Varoma dish and tray into position. Weigh potato slices onto Varoma tray and sprinkle with 1 pinch salt. Steam 12 min/Varoma/speed 1. Set Varoma aside to cool. Empty mixing bowl.
  3. Place oil, garlic and thyme in mixing bowl then chop 2 sec/speed 5.
  4. Add onions and balsamic vinegar then heat 7 min/Varoma//speed . Meanwhile, preheat oven to 200°C and line a baking tray with baking paper.
  5. On a lightly floured surface, roll out puff pastry to approx. 40 cm x 60 cm. Transfer to prepared baking tray.
  6. Lay steamed potato slices over half the pastry leaving a 2 cm border. Spread onions over potatoes. Scatter tomato halves over evenly. Crumble over goat's cheese or place in slices. Sprinkle over 1 pinch of salt and 1 pinch of ground black pepper. Brush edges of pastry with beaten egg then fold over other pastry half and press edges together to seal. Brush all over with remaining beaten egg.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes until golden brown. Allow to rest for 5 minutes then serve.

Useful Items

baking tray and paper, rolling pin, pastry brush, oven
