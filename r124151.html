Mini Spinach Calzone

Ingredients

  • 30 g extra virgin olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 220 g water
  • 1 ½ tsp sugar
  • 2 tsp dried instant yeast
  • 450 g strong white bread flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 1 ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 160 g chopped spinach, frozen, thawed
  • 160 g ricotta cheese
  • 120 g grated mozzarella
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • 3 pinches ground black pepper

Preparation

  1. Lightly grease a large bowl with oil and set aside.
  2. Place water, sugar and yeast in mixing bowl then warm 1 min 30 sec/37°C/speed 0.5.
  3. Add flour, oil and 1 tsp salt then knead 2 min/. Transfer dough to prepared bowl then cover with cling film and leave to prove in a warm place until doubled in size (approx. 1-2 hours). Towards end of proving time, preheat oven to 200°C and line a baking tray with baking paper then make filling.
  4. Place spinach, ricotta, mozzarella, basil, garlic powder, remaining ½ tsp salt and pepper then mix 3 sec/speed 3.
  5. Transfer dough to a lightly floured surface, cut in half then roll one half out to 3 mm thick. Use a 10 cm round cutter to make 24 circles of dough, re-rolling any trimmings. Place 1 heaped tsp spinach filling in centre then fold in half pressing edges together tightly, tucking edges inwards to form a firm seal. Place on prepared baking tray then repeat with remaining dough. Bake for 8-10 minutes (200°C) until golden brown.
  6. Transfer to a serving plate and serve while still warm.

Useful Items

rolling pin, large bowl, baking tray and paper, cling film, round pastry cutter, serving plate, oven
