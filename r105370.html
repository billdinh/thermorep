Orange, hazelnut and coconut balls

Ingredients

  • 40 g dark chocolate
  • 15 hazelnuts, roasted and skins removed
  • 80 g blanched almonds
  • ½ orange, zest only, no white pith, and cut into 3 strips (1 x 4 cm)
  • 150 g shredded coconut
  • 30 g coconut oil, melted
  • 20 g pure maple syrup
  • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste
  • 40 g desiccated coconut, for coating

Preparation

  1. Line a plate with baking paper and set aside. Line a baking tray (20 x 30 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place chocolate into mixing bowl and grate 6 sec/speed 8.
  3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then melt 2 min/50°C/speed 3.
  4. Add hazelnuts and stir 4 sec//speed , until nuts are coated in chocolate. Transfer coated hazelnuts onto lined plate, keeping each nut separate. Place into refrigerator to set (approx. 10 minutes). Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  5. Place almonds and orange zest into mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 7.
  6. Add shredded coconut, coconut oil, maple syrup and vanilla and mix 40 sec/speed 6, until combined.
  7. Place desiccated coconut onto a plate. Roll 2 teaspoons of coconut mixture around each chocolate-coated nut to form a ball. Roll in desiccated coconut to coat and place onto prepared baking tray. Repeat with remaining chocolate-coated nuts and mixture (makes 15 balls) and place into refrigerator until firm (approx. 30 minutes). Serve immediately.

Useful Items

1 plate, baking paper, baking tray (20 x 30 cm), baking paper, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • To store, place balls into a sealable container and keep in the refrigerator until ready to serve.
