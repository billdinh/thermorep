Brie, speck and walnut risotto

Ingredients

  • 30 g eschalots
  • 30 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 70 g speck, cut into cubes (1-2 cm)
  • 320 g arborio rice
  • 50 g white wine
  • 700 g water
  • 1 - 2 tsp salt, to taste
  • &frac18; - ¼ tsp freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 110 - 125 g Brie cheese, softened and cut into cubes (2-3 cm)
  • 20 g walnuts, broken into pieces

Preparation

  1. Place eschalots into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Add oil and speck and cook 3 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Add rice and cook 3 min/Varoma//speed 1, without measuring cup.
  4. Add wine and deglaze 1 min/100°C//speed 1, without measuring cup.
  5. Add water, salt and pepper and mix with aid of spatula. Cook 13 min/100°C//speed 1, placing simmmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  6. Add Brie and walnuts and cook 1 min/100°C//speed 1, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid. Allow to rest in mixing bowl for 1 minute, then transfer into serving bowls and serve warm.
