Crumbed fish nuggets

Ingredients

  • 60 g Parmesan cheese, crust removed and cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 500 g water
  • 350 - 450 g boneless skinless firm white fish fillet
  • 500 g full cream milk
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • 20 g salted butter
  • 250 g fine semolina
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 80 g panko breadcrumbs
  • oil, for frying
  • tomato sauce (optional)

Preparation

  1. Place Parmesan into mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh fish into it. Secure Varoma lid and steam 13-15 min/Varoma/speed 1 or until fish is just cooked. Set Varoma dish aside to allow fish to cool. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Place milk, salt, nutmeg and butter into mixing bowl and heat 8 min/90°C/speed 2.
  4. Add semolina and egg yolk and cook 3 min/90°C/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Break cooled fish into pieces. Add fish and reserved grated cheese to mixing bowl and combine 1 min/90°C//speed 2.
  6. Place a silicone bread mat or a piece of baking paper onto a clean work surface. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh panko breadcrumbs into it. Place half of the panko breadcrumbs onto silicone bread mat or baking paper and spread out into an even layer. Transfer fish mixture onto crumbs and spread out to a 1.5 cm thickness. Coat top of fish evenly with remaining panko breadcrumbs, pressing down gently. Allow to cool completely (approx. 20 minutes).
  7. Line a large plate with paper towel and set aside.
  8. Using a cookie cutter (approx. 4 x 9 cm - see Tips), cut out nuggets. Coat sides of nuggets with panko breadcrumbs. Continue to shape, cut and coat until all fish mixture is used (approx. 30 pieces).
  9. Place a large frying pan over medium heat and add 2 tablespoons of oil. Working in batches, carefully fry nuggets for 3-4 minutes on each side, until crisp and golden brown. Transfer onto prepared plate to drain. Add extra oil to frying pan as needed and repeat frying with remaining nuggets. Serve nuggets warm with tomato sauce (optional).

Useful Items

bowl, silicone bread mat, baking paper, large plate, paper towel, cookie cutter, large frying pan, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • We used a fish-shaped cookie cutter (approx. 4 x 9 cm), but you can use any size or shape you desire to cut out nuggets.
  • Nuggets can be cut out, crumbed and frozen for up to 1 month. Remove from the freezer and thaw for 10 minutes before frying.
  • Nuggets can be pan-fried the day before the party. Transfer fried nuggets onto a plate lined with paper towel and cover with plastic wrap. Place into refrigerator overnight. To reheat, place onto a baking tray lined with baking paper and bake for 10-15 minutes (180°C) or until warmed through.
