Pumpkin chicken purée

Ingredients

  • 350 g pumpkin, peeled and cut into pieces
  • 200 g apple, peeled and cut into pieces
  • 20 g extra virgin olive oil (see Tips)
  • 40 g filtered water, plus extra if needed
  • 350 g skinless, boneless chicken thighs, cut into cubes (3 cm - see Tips)
  • 3 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only (see Tips)

Preparation

  1. Place pumpkin and apple into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add oil, water and chicken and cook 8 min/90°C/speed 2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add parsley and cook 2 min/90°C/speed 2.
  4. Blend 1 min/speed 4-8, increasing speed gradually from speed 4 to speed 8 or until desired consistency is achieved. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Set aside 1 serving, then freeze the remainder in individual portions for future use. If needed, stir through a little more water or your baby's milk to get the consistency just right before serving.

Useful Items

ice cube trays, freezer

Hints & Tricks

  • You can replace the olive oil with butter in this recipe or use half of each. Butter tends to be preferred by many babies, but it's also worth getting your child accustomed to the more peppery flavour of olive oil.
  • Chicken thigh fillets have twice the iron and zinc of chicken breast, so they're a better choice to use in your baby's purées
  • Parsley is a great addition to any of your baby's purées - it's a good source of beta-carotene and folate and gram-for-gram it has more than twice the vitamin C of oranges.
