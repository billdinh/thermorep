Quinoa puddings with raspberry sauce

Ingredients

Raspberry sauce

  • 300 g fresh raspberries
  • 100 - 150 g sugar, to taste
  • 30 g lemon juice
  • butter, for greasing

Quinoa puddings

  • 300 g orange juice, freshly squeezed (4-5 oranges)
  • 300 g coconut milk, fresh or canned
  • 50 g pitted dates
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon, adjust to taste,
  • 160 g quinoa
  • ½ vanilla pod
  • 1 ripe banana (approx. 100 g), cut in slices
  • 500 g water
  • 50 g mixed fresh berries

Preparation

Raspberry sauce

  1. Place raspberries, sugar and lemon juice in mixing bowl and cook 6 min/90°C/speed 4. Meanwhile grease 6 ramekins (Ø 8 cm, height 4 cm, 140 ml capacity, see tips) with butter and set aside. For a smooth sauce, filter fruit coulis through a medium mesh sieve into a bowl and set aside to cool.

Quinoa puddings

  1. Place orange juice and coconut milk in mixing bowl and heat 4 min/100°C/speed 1.
  2. Add dates and blend 10 sec/speed 8.
  3. Add cinnamon and quinoa and cook 20 min/100°C/speed 1.
  4. Split vanilla pod lengthwise and scrape out seeds. Add vanilla seeds and banana to mixing bowl and mix 15 sec/speed 3. Spoon mixture into prepared ramekins, arrange 4 ramekins in Varoma dish and place 2 in middle of Varoma tray. Rinse mixing bowl.
  5. Place water in mixing bowl. Set Varoma in position and steam 35 min/Varoma/speed 1. Set puddings aside and allow to cool a little (approx. 30 min). Serve warm or cold, drizzled with raspberry coulis and garnished with fresh berries.

Useful Items

medium-mesh sieve, ramekins (Ø 8 cm, height 4 cm, 140 ml capacity)

Hints & Tricks

  • Is it breakfast? Is it dessert? Is it an after-school snack? No matter how you choose to serve them, these puddings are made with delicious ingredients that will make you feel great about giving your loved ones - and yourself - a treat.
  • The puddings can be steamed in other small dishes or containers, such as dariole moulds, espresso cups, glasses, verrines or silicone muffin cups, provided they are heat-resistant. The steaming time may have to be increased by 5-10 minutes if the containers are larger than 140 ml each. If using ramekins or moulds no taller than 4 cm, place them in both the Varoma dish and tray as described in the recipe. If using taller moulds, place them in Varoma dish and if they don’t all fit, steam them in 2 batches, adding water as needed to mixing bowl.
  • Leftover puddings can be stored in the refrigerator for 2-3 days. Once steamed, allow puddings to cool to room temperature, then cover and refrigerate. Serve cold, or warm before serving by steaming in the simmering basket or Varoma above 400 g water 20 min/Varoma/speed 1.
