Black and White Cookies

Ingredients

  • ¼ organic lemon, thinly peeled (peel only, no pith)
  • 3 ½ oz sugar
  • 3 ½ oz unsalted butter, soft, cut into pieces
  • 2 oz homemade vanilla sugar (see Tip)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 oz whole milk
  • 7 oz pastry flour
  • 2 oz corn starch
  • 2 tsp baking powder

Icing

  • 14 oz confectioners sugar
  • 2 oz water
  • 1 oz freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • ½ tsp natural vanilla extract
  • 1 oz dutch-processed cocoa powder
Nutrition
per 1 piece

Preparation

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350°F. Line two baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. Place thinly peeled lemon skin and sugar into mixing bowl and zest 30 sec/speed 9.
  3. Add butter and vanilla sugar and mix 1 min/speed 3.
  4. Add eggs, milk, pastry flour, corn starch and baking powder and mix 20 sec/speed 4.
  5. Form dough with aid of a tablespoon, using 2 heaping tbsps per cookie and shape six small balls for each baking sheet. Place 3 in. apart onto prepared baking sheets to allow for cookies to spread during baking.
  6. Bake one baking sheet after another for 12-15 minutes (350°F) or until golden. Allow to completely cool before icing. Clean mixing bowl.

Icing

  1. Place confectioners sugar, water, lemon juice and vanilla extract into mixing bowl and mix 15 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix a further 5 sec/speed 4. Transfer half the white icing into a bowl with a lid and set aside (approx. 7 oz).
  2. Add cocoa into mixing bowl with remaining icing and mix 15 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and add extra water 1 tsp at a time if too thick (should be the consistency of honey). Mix a further 5 sec/speed 4. Place chocolate icing into a bowl. Using an icing spatula cover the flat side of half a cookie with the chocolate icing then use the white icing to cover the other half of the cookie. Allow to dry on a cooling rack. Serve or store in an airtight container.

Useful Items

parchment paper, bowl with lid, bowl, icing spatula, cooling rack, baking sheets, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • These cookies are very delicate, handle with care when icing.
    - Please refer to the Basic Cookbook for the Homemade Vanilla Sugar recipe.
  • Decorate Black and White Cookies with chocolate icing only, white icing only, or both as shown in photo.
