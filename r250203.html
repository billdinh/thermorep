Smoked trout pasta with lemon and capers

Ingredients

  • 1200 g water
  • 120 g olive oil
  • 350 g spaghetti
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1 - 2 fresh chillies, to taste, and deseeded, if preferred
  • 2 pinches salt, to taste, plus extra for seasoning
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper, to taste, plus extra for seasoning
  • 4 pieces lemon peel (5 x 2 cm each), no white pith
  • 150 g hot smoked trout, flaked into pieces (see Tip)
  • 40 - 60 g lemon juice, to taste
  • 3 tbsp pickled capers, drained
  • 3 - 4 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only, roughly chopped

Preparation

  1. Place water and 20 g of the oil into mixing bowl and heat 8 min/100°C/speed 1.
  2. Add spaghetti and cook 7-10 min/100°C//speed , until al dente, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  3. Drain pasta using simmering basket, then transfer into a thermal serving bowl and cover to keep warm. Rinse and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Place garlic, chilli, salt, pepper and lemon peel into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Repeat chopping, if necessary, to a fine consistency.
  5. Add remaining 100 g oil and sauté 3 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  6. Add trout, lemon juice and capers and cook 1 min//speed . Transfer mixture into bowl with pasta. Add parsley and gently stir to combine with aid of spatula. Adjust seasoning to taste before serving.

Useful Items

thermal serving bowl or other large bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • You can replace the trout with hot smoked salmon, if desired. You can find hot smoked fish fillets in either the seafood section or the refrigerated food section of most major supermarkets.
