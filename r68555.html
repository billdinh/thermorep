Red Thai chicken curry

Ingredients

  • 100 g red Thai curry paste
  • 400 g canned coconut milk
  • 220 g canned bamboo shoots, cut into slices
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • 3 fresh Kaffir lime leaves
  • 600 g skinless chicken thigh fillets, cut into slices (1 x 6 cm)
  • 30 g palm sugar
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 100 g frozen green peas
  • 100 - 200 g broccoli, cut into florets
  • 20 fresh Thai basil leaves, adjust to taste
  • fresh coriander, leaves only, for garnishing
  • fresh red chilli, cut into thin slices, for garnishing

Preparation

  1. Place red Thai curry paste and 60 g of the coconut milk into mixing bowl and cook 2 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  2. Add remaining coconut milk, bamboo shoots, cornflour and Kaffir lime leaves and cook 6 min/100°C//speed .
  3. Add chicken thigh and cook 8-10 min/100°C//speed .
  4. Add palm sugar or light brown sugar, fish sauce, frozen green peas and broccoli and cook 2 min/Varoma//speed .
  5. Add Thai basil leaves and cook 1 min/Varoma//speed . Carefully remove Kaffir lime leaves from mixing bowl and discard.
  6. Garnish to taste with fresh coriander and thinly sliced red chilli. Serve hot.
