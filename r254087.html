Pumpkin Spice Cake with Maple Buttercream

Ingredients

Cake

  • 12 oz unsalted butter, room temperature, in pieces (½ in.), plus extra to grease
  • 6 oz sugar
  • 6 oz brown sugar
  • 2 oz whole milk
  • 4 large eggs
  • 15 oz all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp homemade pumpkin spice mix
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • &frac18; tsp salt

Buttercream Frosting

  • 4 oz maple sugar
  • 10 oz confectioners sugar
  • 12 oz unsalted butter, softened and in pieces
  • 1 tsp maple syrup
  • 12 oz homemade pumpkin butter

Preparation

Cake

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease two round cake pans (9 in.), line bottoms with parchment and set aside.
  2. Place butter, sugar, brown sugar, milk and eggs into mixing bowl and mix 30 sec/speed 4.
  3. Add flour, pumpkin spice mix, baking soda, baking powder and salt and with aid of spatula mix 30 sec/speed 4.
  4. Divide batter between prepared pans and bake for 30 minutes (350°F). Clean mixing bowl. Let cakes cool in pans for 10 minutes before inverting onto wire racks to cool completely. Meanwhile make Buttercream Frosting.

Buttercream Frosting

  1. Place maple sugar into mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 10.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Add confectioners sugar, butter, and maple syrup and whip 1 min/speed 3. Remove butterfly whisk.
  3. Evenly cut cakes in half horizontally. Place one sliced layer on a serving plate. Spread a third of the pumpkin butter evenly over the top. Repeat with remaining layers and remaining pumpkin butter, placing the final cake layer bottom side up. With an icing spatula spread top and sides of cake with Buttercream Frosting.
  4. Cut into slices and serve.

Useful Items

round cake pans (9 in.), parchment paper, icing spatula, wire racks, serving plate, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Make sure butter is softened to room temperature not melted when making Buttercream Frosting.
  • Cakes and frosting can be made a day ahead and stored in the refrigerator covered with cling wrap. The next day brush sides with maple syrup before icing with Buttercream Frosting.
