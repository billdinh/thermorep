Multigrain Banana Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 10 g unsalted butter, soft
  • 240 g semi-skimmed milk
  • 60 g fat-free plain yoghurt
  • 10 g groundnut oil, plus extra for frying
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 banana, very ripe, cut in pieces
  • 1 Tbsp honey
  • 60 g plain flour
  • 60 g wholemeal flour
  • 25 g rolled oats
  • 3 Tbsp wheat bran
  • 2 Tbsp flaked almonds
  • 1 ½ tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
425 kJ / 101 kcal
Protein
3.6 g
Carbohydrates
13 g
Fat
3.5 g

Preparation

  1. Place butter, milk, yoghurt, oil, egg, banana and honey in mixing bowl then mix 15 sec/speed 3.
  2. Add plain flour, wholemeal flour, oats, bran, almonds, bicarbonate of soda and salt then mix 10 sec/speed 3. Set aside to rest in mixing bowl for 15 minutes.
  3. Heat oil in a large non-stick frying pan over a medium heat. Pour small ladlefuls of batter into pan and fry for 1-2 minutes or until bubbles form on top. Turn over and cook for a further 1 minute, then remove and keep warm. Repeat with remaining batter.
  4. Serve pancakes warm with maple syrup or yoghurt and fresh fruit.

Useful Items

large non-stick frying pan, small ladle, fish slice, hob

Hints & Tricks

  • Lazy Sunday mornings are the perfect time to enjoy a big stack of homemade pancakes. With slow-releasing oats and nutrient-rich almonds, this healthier version includes bananas for natural sweetness. For a special treat, serve with lashings of maple syrup!
