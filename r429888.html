Sweet Potato Strudel with Spinach and Pine Nuts

Ingredients

Pastry

  • 370 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 40 g sunflower oil
  • 120 g water, lukewarm
  • 10 g vinegar
  • 1 medium egg
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt

Filling and Assembly

  • 500 g water
  • 2 ½ tsp fine sea salt, plus extra to taste
  • 600 g sweet potatoes, cut in pieces (2-3 cm)
  • 500 g fresh spinach leaves
  • 50 g pine nuts
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper, plus extra to taste
  • ½ tsp ground cumin
  • ½ tsp garam masala
  • 100 g quark (curd cheese)
  • 60 g soya milk
  • 15 g olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 2 tsp black cumin seeds

Preparation

Pastry

  1. Place flour, oil, water, vinegar, egg and salt in mixing bowl then knead 2 min/. Tip out, wrap in cling film and place in fridge for approx. 30 minutes. Meanwhile, make filling.

Filling and Assembly

  1. Place water and ½ tsp salt in mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket and weigh in sweet potatoes. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh in 250 g spinach. Cover Varoma then steam 10 min/Varoma/speed 1. Meanwhile, toast pine nuts in a dry frying pan until golden brown.
  2. Add remaining 250 g spinach to Varoma dish, cover Varoma and steam 10 min/Varoma/speed 1. Meanwhile, preheat oven to 180°C and grease a springform tin (Ø 26 cm).
  3. Set Varoma aside. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and discard steaming water.
  4. Place cooked sweet potatoes, 1½ tsp salt, pepper, cumin and garam masala in mixing bowl then purée 15 sec/speed 7.
  5. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in quark, soya milk and oil. Remove bowl, add ½ tsp salt and mix.
  6. On a well-floured surface, roll out chilled dough to a very thin, long rectangle (approx. 20 cm x 90 cm). Spread half the quark mixture the whole length of the rectangle in the middle then add sweet potato purée, steamed spinach and half the toasted pine nuts. Season with salt and pepper, to taste. Roll long edges over filling and squeeze ends together.
  7. Gently shape log into a spiral and place in prepared tin. Top strudel with remaining quark mixture, sprinkle with black cumin seeds and remaining toasted pine nuts then bake for 45-50 minutes (180°C) until golden. Serve sweet potato strudel with vegan gravy (see tip).

Useful Items

frying pan, rolling pin, springform cake tin (Ø 26 cm), cling film, oven, hob, refrigerator
