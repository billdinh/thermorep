Meatballs with Tomato Sauce

Ingredients

Meatballs

  • 40 g bread, fresh or stale, cut in pieces
  • 80 g milk
  • 6 - 12 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 1 garlic clove (optional)
  • 400 g beef mince, broken in pieces
  • 2 medium eggs
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • oil, for greasing

Tomato Sauce

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 50 - 80 g onions, quartered
  • 20 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 600 g tinned chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper

Preparation

Meatballs

  1. Place a small bowl on mixing bowl lid then weigh in bread and milk. Set bowl aside and leave to soak.
  2. Place parsley and garlic (if using) in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add beef mince, eggs, salt, and pepper. Squeeze milk out of bread, add to mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl.
  4. Grease Varoma dish and tray. With oiled hands, shape small meatballs (Ø 4 cm) and place in Varoma dish and tray. Set aside.

Tomato Sauce

  1. Place garlic, onions and oil in mixing bowl, chop 5 sec/speed 5, then sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  2. Add chopped tomatoes, salt and pepper. Place covered Varoma into position and cook 20 min/Varoma/speed 1. Check meatballs are thoroughly cooked then serve hot, with sauce.
