Meatballs in Spanish sauce

Ingredients

Meatballs

  • 200 g carrots, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 - 2 sprigs parsley, fresh (only the leaves)
  • 50 g breadcrumbs
  • 2 eggs
  • 50 g milk
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 pinch nutmeg, ground
  • 1 pinch cinnamon, ground
  • 1 pinch black pepper, ground
  • 500 g mixed minced meat, (250 g of beef and 250 g of pork)
  • 50 g pine nuts (optional)
  • 20 g olive oil (to grease the tray)
  • flour (for batter)

Spanish sauce

  • 250 g onion, cut in quarters
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 50 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbsp tomato purée (optional)
  • 100 g white wine
  • 300 g water
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 pinch nutmeg, ground
  • 1 pinch pepper, ground
  • 1 tbsp beef extract
  • 1 pinch cloves, ground
  • 1 tbsp parsley, fresh, chopped

Preparation

Meatballs

  1. Pre-heat oven to 180°C.
  2. Place carrots, garlic, parsley and breadcrumbs into mixing bowl and blend 10 sec/speed 7.
  3. Add eggs, milk, salt, nutmeg, cinnamon, pepper and mix 20 sec/speed 3.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Add minced meat and mix 10 sec/speed 2. Transfer to a bowl and add pine nuts. Finish by mixing with spatula.
  5. Grease a baking tray. Put some olive oil on your hands, roll the meatballs into shape, dip them in the flour and place them on the baking tray.
  6. Bake 10 minutes (180ºC) on one side and 5 minutes on the other. Remove from oven and place the meatballs into a casserole dish.

Spanish sauce

  1. Place onion, garlic and olive oil into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Sauté 7 min/120°C/speed 1.
  2. Add tomato purée and sauté 2 min/120°C/speed 2.
  3. Add white wine and sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  4. Add water, salt, nutmeg, pepper, beef extract, clove and steam 10 min/Varoma/speed 1. Place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid to help evaporation and avoid spattering.
  5. Pour over the meatballs and bake 10 minutes (180°C). Sprinkle with parsley and serve immediately.

Useful Items

baking tray, casserole dish, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • If you prefer the sauce thinner, blend 20 sec/speed 5 at the end of step 8.
  • Serve with diced fried potatoes.
