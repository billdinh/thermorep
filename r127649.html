Chocolate Affogato

Ingredients

  • 20 g dark chocolate, 70% cocoa
  • 600 g whole milk
  • 50 g cocoa powder
  • 30 g caster sugar
  • 40 g brandy
  • 4 scoops vanilla ice cream (approx. 200 g)
Nutrition
per 1 glass
Calories
1392 kJ / 333 kcal
Protein
10 g
Carbohydrates
30 g
Fat
16 g

Preparation

  1. Place dark chocolate in mixing bowl, grind 10 sec/speed 10 and transfer to a plate. Lightly moisten the rims of 4 serving glasses with water and dip it in the grated dark chocolate.
  2. Place whole milk, cocoa powder and caster sugar in mixing bowl, blend 15 sec/speed 4, then heat 5 min/70°C/speed 2.
  3. Add brandy and mix 5 sec/speed 3. Transfer to garnished glasses, place one scoop of vanilla ice cream in each glass and finish with the remaining grated dark chocolate. Serve immediately.
