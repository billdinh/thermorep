Spiced Pears with Sangria

Ingredients

  • 6 pears, peeled
  • 50 oz red wine, e.g. merlot or burgundy (2 bottles)
  • 1 stick cinnamon, halved
  • 6 green cardamom pods
  • 2 star anise
  • 6 whole cloves
  • 6 black peppercorns
  • 2 oranges, thin peel only, no pith
  • 2 in. fresh ginger, in 6 pieces
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • ½ whole nutmeg, grated
  • 2 apples, diced
  • 1 nectarine, diced
  • 1 orange, diced
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 4 oz ice cubes
  • 16 oz sparkling water
Nutrition
per 6 portions
Calories
3443 kJ / 823 kcal

4 g

Preparation

  1. Place pears into Varoma dish.
  2. Place wine, cinnamon, cardamon, star anise, cloves, peppercorns, orange peel, fresh ginger, ground ginger and nutmeg into mixing bowl, set Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 50 min/Varoma/speed 2.5. Remove pears and set aside.
  3. With a fine mesh sieve, strain spiced wine mixture over a large pitcher and discard solids. Return 6 oz of the strained wine to mixing bowl.
  4. Add apples, nectarine and orange to remaining wine in pitcher. Refrigerate until ready to use.
  5. Add sugar into mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and without Varoma lid cook 15 min/212°F/speed 2.
  6. Pour cooked syrup over reserved pears.
  7. Just before serving, add sparkling water to Sangria in pitcher and stir gently. Divide ice between glasses. Pour Sangria over ice and serve with Spiced Pears.

Useful Items

fine mesh sieve, vegetable peeler, large pitcher, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Slice bottoms of pears so they stand flat for serving.
