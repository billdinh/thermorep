Wholefood Pancakes with Vanilla and Cinnamon Poached Apples

Ingredients

Vanilla and Cinnamon Poached Apples

  • 3 cinnamon sticks
  • 2 vanilla pods, split lengthways, seeds scraped (reserve pods and seeds)
  • 250 g coconut sugar
  • 400 g apples, green, peeled, cored and cut into eighths
  • 1000 g water

Wholefood Pancakes

  • 100 g brown rice
  • 100 g buckwheat grains
  • 2 bananas, peeled and chopped (approx. 190 g)
  • 1 large egg
  • 300 g milk of choice (e.g. cow's milk, rice milk)
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • butter, for frying

Preparation

Vanilla and Cinnamon Poached Apples

  1. Place all poached apple ingredients in mixing bowl and cook 10 min/100°C//speed .
  2. Mix contents of mixing bowl well with spatula, then cook for a further 10 min/100°C//speed . Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Wholefood Pancakes

  1. Place rice and buckwheat in mixing bowl then grind 1 min/speed 9.
  2. Add bananas and egg then mix 14 sec/speed 4.
  3. Add milk and baking powder then mix 30 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then mix again 6 sec/speed 6. Transfer batter into a bowl and place in refrigerator for 30 minutes.
  4. Remove pancake batter from refrigerator and mix with spatula. Place a medium-sized non-stick frying pan over medium-high heat. Place a little butter or oil into hot pan and pour in a small ladle of pancake mixture. Cook for 3 minutes, or until bubbles form on top of pancake then flip and cook for a further 3 minutes, or until browned. Set pancake aside and cover to keep warm.
  5. Repeat with remaining batter to make approx. 16 pancakes, mixing the batter occasionally with spatula between batches to keep batter smooth.
  6. Remove vanilla bean pods and cinnamon sticks from reserved poached apples. Serve pancakes hot topped with poached apples and syrup.

Useful Items

bowl, soft spatula, medium-sized non-stick frying pan, small ladle, refrigerator, hob

Hints & Tricks

  • For a decadent dessert, serve pancakes with blueberries and fresh cream or raspberries and vanilla bean ice cream.
  • For a lactose-free version, ensure you use grapeseed oil rather than butter for frying, and a dairy-free milk, such as nut or coconut milk.
