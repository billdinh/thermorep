Miso dressing

Ingredients

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 3 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 80 g rice wine vinegar
  • 70 g water
  • 50 g olive oil
  • 10 g sesame oil
  • 110 g shiro (white) miso paste
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
156 kJ / 37 kcal
Protein
0.7 g
Carbohydrates
1.1 g
Fat
3.3 g

Preparation

  1. Place garlic and ginger into mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add all remaining ingredients and chop 15 sec/speed 5. Transfer into a sealable container and place into refrigerator until ready to serve.

Useful Items

sealable container, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • We used this dressing in our Miso coleslaw (please refer to the recipe on this recipe chip), however you can serve it with any green salad, warm or cold noodles or drizzled over steamed vegetables or fish.
  • You can store this dressing in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.
