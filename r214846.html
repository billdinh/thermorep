Waffles

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs, separated
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 85 g unsalted butter, diced
  • 285 g whole milk
  • 225 g plain flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Preparation

  1. Preheat waffle maker.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk, place egg whites and sugar in mixing bowl then whip 2 min/speed 4 until stiff peaks form. Remove butterfly whisk, transfer whipped egg whites to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place butter in mixing bowl and melt 2 min/70Â°C/speed 1.
  4. Add milk, flour, baking powder, salt, vanilla and egg yolks then mix 10 sec/speed 5.
  5. Fold whipped egg whites into batter with spatula.
  6. Cook waffles according to waffle maker instructions and serve hot with your choice of toppings.

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with warm maple syrup, fresh berries, powdered sugar or a scoop of your favourite ice cream.
  • Once cooled, waffles can be frozen and quickly reheated in a toaster.
  • When whipping egg whites, make sure your mixing bowl and blade are clean and free of any fat, otherwise any fat residue will prevent the whites from whipping properly.
  • Add citrus zest or chocolate chips to your batter before folding in whipped egg whites.
