Ben's Chocolate Cookies

Ingredients

  • 220 g dark chocolate, small pieces or callets
  • 30 g salted butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 130 g granulated sugar
  • ½ tsp real vanilla extract
  • 35 g plain flour
  • ¼ tsp baking powder
  • sea salt flakes, for sprinkling (optional)

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C and line 2 baking trays with baking paper.
  2. Place chocolate and butter in mixing bowl then heat 3 min/50°C/speed . Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and heat again 2 min/50°C/speed . Transfer warm chocolate butter to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Insert butterfly whisk. Place eggs, sugar and vanilla then whisk 3 min/speed 4 until light and fluffy.
  4. Mix 1 min 30 sec/speed 2 while very slowly pouring the melted chocolate butter through the hole in the TM lid onto the egg mixture.
  5. Add flour and baking powder then mix 10 sec/speed 2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix again 10 sec/speed 2. Remove butterfly whisk.
  6. Spoon 12 portions out onto prepared baking trays, leaving plenty of space between each scoop. The mixture will be quite loose at this stage. Sprinkle with a few sea salt flakes if desired. Bake 10-12 minutes (180°C) then allow to cool completely on the tray before serving.

Useful Items

2 baking trays and baking paper, 2 baking trays, baking paper, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • This recipe is from Benjamin Chocolatier (Ben Axford), who has used Thermomix for many years both as a chef and as a chocolatier.
  • After mixing in the flour and baking powder in Step 4, remove butterfly whisk, add dried cherries, pecans, chocolate chips or a mixture (75 g total weight), then stir 4 sec//speed 2 to mix them in before shaping and baking.
