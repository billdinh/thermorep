Chicken Pizzaiola

Ingredients

  • 80 g Edam cheese, cut in pieces
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 150 g onions, quartered
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 400 g tinned chopped tomatoes
  • 1 vegetable stock cube (for 0.5 l)
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 4 chicken breasts, skinless
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • ½ tsp ground pepper
  • 1 ½ tsp dried oregano

Preparation

  1. Place cheese in mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place oil, onions and garlic in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Sauté 5 min/120°C/speed 1.
  3. Add tomatoes, stock cube and sugar. Place Varoma into position and place chicken breasts in Varoma dish and tray. Season with salt and pepper, cover and steam 20-25 min/Varoma/speed 1 until chicken is cooked through. Near to the end of this time,  preheat oven to 200°C.
  4. Remove chicken and place in an ovenproof dish. Pour tomato sauce over chicken and sprinkle with reserved grated cheese and oregano. Bake for 10-15 minutes (200°C) until cheese is golden. Serve hot.

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with a side salad or steamed vegetables.
