Savoury Flapjacks

Ingredients

  • 200 g Parmesan cheese, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 25 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 160 g onions, quartered
  • 200 g carrots, cut in pieces (3 cm)
  • 200 g courgettes, cut in pieces (3 cm)
  • 200 g rolled oats
  • 2 medium eggs
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
636 kJ / 152 kcal
Protein
8 g
Carbohydrates
8 g
Fat
8 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C and line a square cake tin (20 cm) with baking paper.
  2. Place Parmesan and parsley in mixing bowl then grate 10 sec/speed 10.
  3. Add onions, carrots and courgettes then chop 10 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add oats and eggs then mix 15 sec/speed 4. Transfer to prepared tin and bake for 30-40 minutes (190°C) until golden brown.
  5. Remove from oven and cut into pieces, allowing to cool in tin for 10 minutes before transferring to a wire rack.
  6. Serve warm or at room temperature.

Useful Items

baking paper, wire rack, square baking tin (20 cm), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • A savoury version of the well-known syrupy favourite, these flapjacks are a great way of getting children to eat more vegetables. Tasty cheese is combined with fresh herbs and vegetables to make an economical snack, or serve with a side salad for lunch.
