Hazelnut Meringue with Banana and Toffee Filling

Ingredients

Hazelnut Meringue

  • sunflower oil, for greasing
  • 250 g caster sugar
  • 150 g hazelnuts, with skin
  • 5 egg whites
  • 1 tbsp white wine vinegar

Filling

  • 75 g toffees (e.g. Werthers)
  • 1 banana (approx. 120 g)
  • 100 g cream, min. 30% fat, chilled

Topping

  • 100 g dark chocolate (50% cocoa), cut in small pieces
  • 80 g cream, min. 30% fat
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract

Cake Assembly

  • 50 g dark chocolate, one piece
  • 35 g hazelnuts, blanched, roasted

Preparation

Hazelnut Meringue

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C. Line the bases of 2 springform tins (Ø 20 cm) with baking paper and grease with sunflower oil.
  2. Place sugar in mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place hazelnuts in mixing bowl and grind 15 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Clean mixing bowl thoroughly.
  4. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg whites in mixing bowl and whisk without measuring cup 3 min/speed 3.5.
  5. Then whisk again without measuring cup 4 min/speed 3, add vinegar, reserved grated sugar and ground nuts slowly, a tablespoon at a time, through mixing bowl lid. Remove butterly whisk.
  6. Divide meringue between the prepared tins (approx. 240 g per tin, see tip) and bake for 35-45 minutes (190°C) until dry. Turn off the oven and allow to cool in the oven (approx. 2 hours). Meanwhile clean and dry mixing bowl, then prepare filling.

Filling

  1. Place toffees in mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 10.
  2. Add banana and mix 5 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Insert butterfly whisk. Place cream in mixing bowl and whip without measuring cup speed 3, without setting a time, until stiff, watching carefully to avoid over-whipping. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl using spatula.
  4. Add reserved grated toffees and mix 30 sec/speed 1. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.

Topping

  1. Place dark chocolate in mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl using spatula.
  2. Add cream and vanilla extract then melt 3 min/75°C/speed 1.

Cake Assembly

  1. Place one layer of reserved hazelnut meringue on a serving plate and spoon over the reserved toffee-cream filling. Put the second layer on top. Spoon over the reserved chocolate-cream mixture. Carefully draw a vegetable peeler or a sharp knife across the broad surface of the piece of dark chocolate. Decorate with chocolate curls and roasted hazelnuts. Serve cut in slices.

Useful Items

springform tins (Ø 20 cm), baking paper, vegetable peeler, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • You can use the built-in Thermomix scales to measure out the 240 g cake batter for each cake tin. Turn scales on, then lift mixing bowl out and pour some of the cake batter out. When you return the mixing bowl to the base, it will show a minus figure indicating the weight removed (- 240 g).
