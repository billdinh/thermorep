Cheesecake (No-Bake)

Ingredients

Cheesecake

  • 7 oz plain cookies, (see Tip)
  • 3 oz unsalted butter, softened
  • 9 oz water
  • 3 oz ready-made lemon gelatin powder, sweetened, (see Tip)
  • 14 - 16 oz cream cheese
  • 7 oz heavy whipping cream
  • 1 oz baker's sugar, to taste

Jelly Topping

  • 7 oz jelly (e.g. strawberry, raspberry, plum)
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 - 2 tsp unflavored gelatin powder, unsweetened (see Tip)
  • 2 oz water

Preparation

Cheesecake

  1. Place cookies into mixing bowl and crush 5 sec/speed 10 or until crushed.
  2. Add butter and mix 5 sec/speed 5.
  3. Spread crumb mixture into base of a nonstick springform pan (9½ - 10 in.). Press down firmly to make a compact layer using a spoon or fingertips. Place springform pan in the freezer. Clean mixing bowl.
  4. Place water into mixing bowl and heat 5 min/212°F/speed 1.
  5. Add lemon gelatin, cream cheese, whipping cream and sugar and mix 20 sec/speed 5.
  6. Remove springform pan from the freezer and transfer contents of mixing bowl into springform pan. Chill in refrigerator until filling is set (approx. 4 hr.). Clean mixing bowl.

Jelly Topping

  1. Place jelly, lemon juice, gelatin powder and water into mixing bowl and heat 3 min/175°F/speed 2.
  2. Allow mixture to cool for 10 minutes then pour over the set cheesecake. Again chill cheesecake in refrigerator, for at least one hour. Serve cold.

Useful Items

nonstick springform pan (9½ - 10 in.), freezer, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Ready-made gelatin dessert powder contains gelatin powder, sugar and flavorings. To replace 3 oz lemon-flavored dessert powder, use 0.5 oz unflavored gelatin powder, 3 oz sugar and 1 grated lemon zest.
  • To replace gelatin powder (not jello dessert powder) with gelatin sheets, use same weight of gelatin and soak sheets in cold water for five minutes. Drain, squeeze out water, discard soaking water and add gelatin to mixture.
  • Vanilla wafers, sugar cookies or graham crackers can be used for cheesecake base.
