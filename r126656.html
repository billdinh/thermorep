Mediterranean pearl barley risotto

Ingredients

  • 70 g shallots (2-3 shallots)
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 250 g pearl barley, medium, washed and drained
  • 100 g dry white wine, vegan
  • 750 g water, hot
  • 1 tbsp vegetable stock paste, homemade, vegan (see tip)
  • ½ tsp fine salt
  • 2 pinches ground white pepper
  • ¾ tsp dried thyme (optional)
  • 40 g pine nuts
  • 400 g courgette, sliced (5 mm)
  • 50 g sun-dried tomatoes in oil, cut into strips (5 mm)
  • 60 g almond butter, white

Preparation

  1. Place shallots in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add olive oil and sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  3. Add pearl barley and cook 3 min/100°C//speed 1 without measuring cup.
  4. Add wine and deglaze 1 min/100°C//speed  without measuring cup.
  5. Add water, stock cube, salt, pepper and thyme, position simmering basket on mixing bowl lid instead of measuring cup and cook 20 min/100°C//speed . Meanwhile, roast pine nuts in a pan without fat and set aside.
  6. Add courgette slices to mixing bowl, use spatula to carefully stir in and continue to cook 10 min/100°C//speed .
  7. Allow risotto to rest in mixing bowl for 3–5 minutes. Add dried tomatoes, roasted pine nuts and almond butter, use spatula to carefully stir in and serve immediately.
