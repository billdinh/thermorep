Easy Satay Chicken

Ingredients

Satay Sauce

  • ½ tsp fennel seeds
  • ½ tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander seeds
  • 1 tbsp ground turmeric
  • 3 in. ginger, fresh root, peeled (approx. 1 oz)
  • 5 garlic cloves
  • 1 - 2 fresh chilies, seeds removed if preferred, to taste (e.g. serrano)
  • 2 tbsp Tamari sauce
  • 1 tbsp tamarind pulp
  • 2 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp dark brown sugar
  • 13 - 14 oz coconut cream
  • 2 oz peanut butter

Chicken, Rice and Vegetables

  • 14 oz jasmine rice
  • 35 oz water
  • 24 oz chicken thighs, skinless, boneless, cut into pieces (1¼ in.-1½ in.)
  • 3 ½ oz carrots, cut into thin strips
  • 3 ½ oz broccolini, cut into thirds
  • 3 ½ oz cauliflower, cut into small florets (1¼ in.)
  • 2 oz red bell pepper, cut into thin strips
  • 2 oz yellow onion, cut into thin wedges
  • 3 oz sugar snap peas, trimmed and cut into halves

Preparation

Satay Sauce

  1. Place all spices into mixing bowl and grind 30 sec/speed 10.
  2. Add ginger, garlic and chili and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and chop a further 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add all remaining Satay Sauce ingredients and cook 3 min/195°F/speed 1, and blend 30 sec/speed 10. Transfer sauce into a large bowl and set aside to keep warm.

Chicken, Rice and Vegetables

  1. Insert simmering basket and weigh in rice. Remove simmering basket with spatula and rinse rice under cold running water until water runs clear.
  2. Place water into mixing bowl, and re-insert simmering basket with rice. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh in chicken. Secure Varoma lid and cook 12 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  3. Stir chicken in Varoma dish to ensure even cooking. Insert Varoma tray, and weigh in carrot, broccolini, cauliflower, pepper and onion. Secure Varoma lid and cook again 5-8 min/Varoma/speed 2, or until rice is cooked. Set Varoma aside. Remove simmering basket with spatula, and transfer rice into another large bowl to keep warm. Discard water from mixing bowl.
  4. Place sugar snap peas and reserved Satay Sauce into mixing bowl, add steamed chicken and steamed vegetables from Varoma into mixing bowl and cook 10 min/212°F//speed . Serve Easy Satay Chicken with rice.

Hints & Tricks

  • Try making your own peanut butter to use in this recipe. Please refer to The Basic Cookbook for the Peanut Butter recipe.
  • To save time buy pre-cut vegetables at the store.
  • Serrano chili can be replaced with Thai red chili when available.
