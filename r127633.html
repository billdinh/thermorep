Pork tenderloin parcels

Ingredients

  • 2 - 4 garlic cloves, to taste
  • 40 g olive oil
  • 150 g leeks, cut into slices
  • 150 g carrots, cut into slices
  • 1 pinch fine salt, plus extra to taste
  • 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper, plus extra to taste
  • 100 g white wine
  • 1 tbsp ground paprika
  • 1600 g pork tenderloins (400 g each)
  • 1000 g water
Nutrition
per 8 portions

5857 kJ / 1400 kcal

Preparation

  1. Place garlic and olive oil into mixing bowl and chop 8 sec/speed 5.
  2. Add leek, carrot, salt and pepper and sauté 5 min/120°C//speed .
  3. Add wine and paprika and cook 2 min/120°C//speed .
  4. Place tenderloins on 4 separate squares of baking paper (30 cm x 30 cm) and distribute sautéd vegetables evenly over each fillet. Fold baking paper to close packets and place parcels seam side up, 2 in Varoma dish and 2 on Varoma tray, tucking the folded ends of paper underneath the parcels. Leave some holes of the Varoma dish and tray unobstructed for steam to circulate.
  5. Place water into mixing bowl, place Varoma into position and steam 25 min/Varoma/speed 2. Carefully open one parcel and check if tenderloin is cooked and prolong cooking time if necessary. Remove Varoma and allow to cool slightly. Serve tenderloins sliced and drizzled with some of the cooking liquid from the parcels.

Hints & Tricks

  • Accompany the tenderloins with white rice or mashed potatoes.
