Cornbread

Ingredients

  • 3 oz unsalted butter
  • 12 ½ oz buttermilk
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 oz honey
  • 6 oz cornmeal, finely ground
  • 4 ½ oz all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tbsp oil
Nutrition
per 1 slice
Calories
750 kJ / 179 kcal
Protein
4 g
Carbohydrates
21 g
Fat
8 g

Preparation

  1. Place a cast iron skillet (9 in.) in the oven and preheat oven to 425°F.
  2. Place butter into mixing bowl and melt 2 min/175°F/speed 1.
  3. Add buttermilk, egg, honey, cornmeal, flour, salt, baking powder and baking soda and mix 15 sec/speed 4.
  4. Carefully place 2 tbsp oil into hot cast iron skillet and return skillet to oven for 2 minutes to heat the oil. Transfer batter from mixing bowl to hot skillet, return to oven and reduce temperature to 400°F. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until lightly browned and a wooden skewer inserted in the center comes out clean. Allow to cool slightly before slicing and serving warm.

Useful Items

wooden skewer, cast iron skillet (9 in.)

Hints & Tricks

  • To replace buttermilk: use 12.5 oz milk and 1 tsp vinegar, 8 oz buttermilk and 4 oz milk, or 4 oz yogurt and 8 oz milk.
  • For sweet fluffy cornbread: increase butter to 3.5 oz, replace honey with 2.5 oz-3.5 oz sugar, reduce cornmeal to 5 oz, increase flour to 5.5 oz and increase baking powder to 4 tsp.
  • For cheddar and jalapeños cornbread: add 3.5 oz grated cheddar and 2 chopped jalapeños before baking.
  • If you don’t have a cast iron skillet, use an 8 inch square pan or a 9 inch round pan, placing pan in oven only a few minutes before baking.
  • Serve day-old cornbread sliced and toasted for breakfast, spread with butter and honey or maple syrup.
