Mouhallabieh (milk pudding)

Ingredients

  • ¼ tsp mastic gum (see Tips)
  • 60 g white sugar
  • 800 g full cream milk
  • 90 g cornflour
  • 20 g rose water
  • 20 g orange blossom water
  • 50 g shelled unsalted pistachio nuts
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
730 kJ / 173.8 kcal
Protein
4.6 g
Carbohydrates
24.4 g
Fat
6.6 g

Preparation

  1. Place mastic gum and sugar in mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 10.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Add milk and cornflour and cook 8-10 min/90°C/speed 3, until thick.
  3. Add rose water and orange blossom water and mix 5 sec/speed 3. Remove butterfly whisk
  4. Divide mixture between 8 dariole moulds (120 ml) and allow to cool (approx. 10 minutes). Place into refrigerator to set for a minimum of 4 hours or overnight. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  5. Place pistachios into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 6.
  6. To serve, unmould puddings onto serving plates and sprinkle with chopped pistachios.

Useful Items

serving plates, dariole moulds (120 ml), refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Originating in Greece, mastic is an edible plant resin that can be used as a stabiliser. You can find mastic in specialty food stores, health food stores and online.
