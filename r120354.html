Ravioli with zucchini sauce

Ingredients

  • 30 g Parmesan cheese, cut into pieces (approx. 3 cm)
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 50 g olive oil
  • 300 g zucchini, cut into pieces (2 cm)
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • water, for boiling pasta
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 5 anchovy fillets in oil, drained
  • 2 sprigs fresh basil, leaves only
  • 100 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 500 g fresh ravioli pasta (see Tips)

Preparation

  1. Place Parmesan cheese into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  2. Place garlic into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add oil, zucchini and pepper and cook 10 min/Varoma/speed 1. Meanwhile, place a large saucepan with water and salt over high heat and bring to the boil.
  4. Add anchovies, basil and cream to mixing bowl and blend 1 min/speed 5- 10, increasing speed gradually from speed 5 to speed 10.
  5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and cook 2 min/100°C/speed 2. Transfer into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and cover to keep warm.
  6. Once water is boiling, place ravioli into saucepan and cook for 5 minutes or until al dente. Drain ravioli, then divide between serving bowls. Drizzle with zucchini sauce and sprinkle with reserved Parmesan before serving.

Useful Items

bowl, thermal serving bowl or other large bowl, large saucepan, sieve, serving bowls, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • To make your own ravioli, please refer to The Basic Cookbook or Everyday Cookbook for pasta dough recipe.
  • You can use this sauce to flavor any variety of pasta.
