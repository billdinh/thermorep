Orange, Carrot and Lemon Vitamin Juice

Ingredients

  • 2 oranges, unwaxed, washed
  • 1 lemon, unwaxed, washed
  • 50 - 70 g ice cubes
  • 30 g caster sugar, or to taste (optional)
  • 50 - 60 g carrots, cut in pieces
  • 500 - 700 g water
Nutrition
per 1 glasses
Calories
227 kJ / 54 kcal
Protein
0.5 g
Carbohydrates
12.9 g
Fat
0.1 g

Preparation

  1. Remove thin peel from oranges and lemon, set aside, then remove white pith and discard. Quarter the flesh of the fruit, remove pips, then set aside.
  2. Place ice cubes and sugar (if using) in mixing bowl then mix 6 sec/speed 7.
  3. Add orange flesh, lemon flesh, all or half of the zest (depending on your taste) and carrots then blend 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add water up to the 1 litre mark and mix 2 sec/speed 6. Strain through a fine sieve or nut milk bag and serve immediately for maximum nutrients.

Useful Items

zester or potato peeler, fine sieve or nut milk bag

Hints & Tricks

  • Add more water for a thinner consistency.
  • Carrot is a natural preservative that keeps the juice in the fridge for 24 hours without deterioration.
  • Use the discarded fruit pulp to make:

      Citrus marmalade: add 200 g sugar and 15 g butter to the pulp, then cook 10-15 min/100°C/speed 4.

     Citrus Pie: make a shortcrust pastry. Place fruit pulp in the base of an oven-proof dish and lay pastry over the top then bake.

     Cake: add 2 eggs, 1 tsp baking powder, 300 g flour, 20 g vanilla sugar and 100 g sugar then blend 30 sec/speed 6. Pour into a cake tin and bake at 180°C. 

     Sorbet: add 300 g ice cubes, 1 egg white and 100 g sugar then mix 2 min/speed 5.
