Poppy seed and apple cake

Ingredients

Cake

  • 1 lemon, zest only, no white pith
  • 1 orange, zest only, no white pith
  • 100 g walnuts
  • 250 g poppy seeds
  • 300 - 350 g Golden Delicious apples, peeled and cored (approx. 3 apples)
  • 250 g unsalted butter, softened and cut into pieces
  • 6 eggs
  • 250 g caster sugar
  • 1 tsp gluten free baking powder (see Tips)
  • 60 g gluten free cornflour
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract

Chocolate ganache

  • 100 g gluten free dark chocolate, broken into pieces
  • 80 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • slivered almonds, for decorating

Preparation

Cake

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Line a square cake tin (20 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place lemon and orange peel into mixing bowl and grate 15 sec/speed 8.
  3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Add walnuts and chop 2 sec/speed 6. Transfer into a large bowl and set aside.
  4. Place 125 g of the poppy seeds  into mixing bowl and mill 2 min/speed 8. Transfer into bowl with walnuts.
  5. Place remaining poppy seeds into mixing bowl and mill 2 min/speed 8. Transfer into bowl with walnuts and milled poppy seeds.
  6. Place apples into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 8. Transfer into bowl with poppy seed mixture and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  7. Place butter into mixing bowl and melt 2 min/80°C/speed 4, or until melted. Transfer into a separate bowl and set aside.
  8. Insert butterfly whisk. Place eggs and sugar into mixing bowl and mix 4 min/speed 4.  Add reserved melted butter and mix 20 sec/speed 3. Remove butterfly whisk.
  9. Add baking powder, cornflour, vanilla and reserved zests, walnut, poppy seed and apple mixture and mix 10 sec/speed 4, until well combined.
  10. Transfer into prepared tin and bake for 35-40 minutes (180°C) until firm, or a wooden skewer inserted into the centre of the cake comes out clean. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  11. Allow cake to cool in tin for 5 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack to cool completely (approx. 1 hour).

Chocolate ganache

  1. Place chocolate into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add cream and melt 2-3 min/50°C/speed 3. Set aside to cool slightly (approx. 5 minutes).
  3. Transfer cake onto a serving plate. Spread with ganache and sprinkle with slivered almonds before serving.
