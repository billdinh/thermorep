Bread and tomato soup, Rolled spinach omelette and Steamed ratatouille vegetables

Ingredients

  • 60 g medium-hard cheese (e.g. Gruyère, Emmental, Cheddar), cut in pieces
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 50 g extra virgin olive oil, plus extra for serving
  • 50 g stale bread roll, cut in pieces (1-2 cm),
  • 200 g frozen chopped spinach, defrosted
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tsp salt, plus extra to taste
  • 50 g onions, cut in halves
  • 50 g carrots, cut in pieces
  • 700 g ripe tomatoes, cut in pieces,
  • 6 - 8 fresh basil leaves
  • 500 g water
  • 1 heaped tsp vegetable stock paste, homemade
  • 400 g mixed vegetables (e.g. peppers, courgettes, aubergines), cut in pieces (2-3 cm)

Preparation

  1. Place cheese in mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place garlic cloves and 10 g extra virgin olive oil in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5, then sauté 2 min/120°C/speed 1.
  3. Add bread and sauté without measuring cup 5 min/120°C//speed 1. Meanwhile, line Varoma tray with baking paper with enough to go 2 cm up the sides. Transfer sautéed garlic bread to Varoma dish and set aside.
  4. Place spinach, 10 g extra virgin olive oil, eggs and ½ tsp salt in mixing bowl and chop 30 sec/speed 5.5. Transfer spinach-egg mixture to lined Varoma tray, spread to the borders, sprinkle with reserved grated cheese and insert Varoma tray in Varoma dish.
  5. Place onions, carrots, 30 g extra virgin olive oil, tomatoes, 1-2 basil leaves, water, vegetable stock paste and ½ tsp salt in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  6. Insert simmering basket and add mixed vegetables to it. Set Varoma in position and cook 30 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove Varoma. With aid of baking paper lining, roll up the spinach omelette, keeping it wrapped in the baking paper. Meanwhile remove simmering basket using spatula, transfer cooked vegetables to a shallow serving dish, season with salt to taste and stir. Unwrap the spinach omelette and cut in slices (approx. 2 cm). Transfer slices to serving dish with vegetables and keep warm. Transfer tomato soup to a serving bowl, add reserved steamed garlic bread to the soup, drizzle with some extra virgin olive oil and garnish with roughly torn basil leaves. Serve the soup hot as a starter and the rolled omelette warm as a main dish, with the ratatouille vegetables on the side.

Hints & Tricks

  • 'Pappa al pomodoro', or bread and tomato soup, is classic comfort food in many Italian homes. While the soup is bubbling away, steam the next soothing course: a spinach and cheese rolled omelette presented in beautiful green spirals, served with hearty Mediterranean vegetables on the side.
  • For a smooth soup, after removing Varoma and simmering basket in step 6, blend 1 min/speed 5-10, increasing speed gradually. Add the steamed garlic bread and serve as described in step 6.
  • Replace spinach in the rolled omelette with vegetables of your choice (e.g. cabbage, courgette).
