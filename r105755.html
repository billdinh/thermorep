Rote Grutze (red fruit pudding)

Ingredients

  • 250 g white wine (see Tips)
  • 10 g rum (optional)
  • 25 g cornflour
  • 50 g white sugar (see Tips)
  • 600 g fresh mixed berries
Nutrition

Preparation

  1. Place wine, rum (optional), cornflour, sugar and 300 g of the mixed berries into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 9, then cook 9 min/100°C/speed 3.
  2. Add remaining 300 g mixed berries and mix 15 sec//speed 2.
  3. Divide pudding between 6 serving glasses (approx. 125 ml each) and place into refrigerator for a minimum 2 hours or until ready to serve.

Useful Items

6 serving bowls or glasses, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • If using whole strawberries, cut into halves before adding to mixing bowl.
  • Frozen mixed berries can be used instead of fresh, but they will need to be partially defrosted, and whole berries cut into halves.
  • Adjust sugar to taste, depending on the type of berries you use.
  • Serve with vanilla custard or ice cream.
  • You can replace the wine with grape, apple or cherry juice, simply adjust the sugar to taste.
