Sweetcorn and potato chowder

Ingredients

  • 150 g brown onion, cut into quarters
  • 100 g carrots, cut into pieces (2-3 cm)
  • 1 yellow capsicum (approx. 150 g), deseeded and cut into pieces (2-3 cm - see Tips)
  • 400 g potatoes, peeled and cut into pieces (2-3 cm)
  • 20 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 - 2 tsp caraway seeds
  • 1 tsp fennel seeds
  • 400 g filtered water
  • 200 g plant-based milk of choice
  • 1 tbsp stock paste (see Tips)
  • 300 g frozen corn kernels
  • 400 g canned butter beans, rinsed and drained (approx. 240 g after draining)
  • salt, to taste
  • ground black pepper, to taste
  • fresh coriander leaves, for garnishing

Preparation

  1. Place onion, carrot, capsicum and 200 g of the potatoes into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7.
  2. Add oil, caraway seeds and fennel seeds and sauté 4 min/120°C/speed 2.
  3. Add water, milk, stock paste and remaining 200 g potatoes, then cook 10 min/100°C//speed 1.
  4. Add corn and cook 10 min/95°C//speed 1. Transfer three-quarters of the soup into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and cover to keep warm.
  5. Blend remaining soup in mixing bowl 1 min/speed 4-9, increasing speed gradually from speed 4 to speed 9.
  6. Add butter beans, salt and pepper and cook 2 min/90°C//speed 1. Pour into thermal serving bowl with chunky soup and stir to combine. Garnish with coriander leaves to serve.

Useful Items

thermal serving bowl or other large bowl, serving bowls

Hints & Tricks

  • Please refer to the Mushroom stock paste recipe on this recipe chip or to The Basic Cookbook for the Vegetable stock paste recipe.
  • Using a red or green capsicum will alter the colour of your chowder.
