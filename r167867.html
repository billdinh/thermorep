Caponata

Ingredients

  • 32 oz water
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 17 oz eggplant, cut into ½ in. pieces (approx. 1 lg. eggplant)
  • 10 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves and tender stems
  • 12 fresh basil leaves, plus extra to garnish
  • 7 oz onion, halved (approx. 1 med. onion)
  • 3 oz celery, cut into 1 in. pieces (approx. 3 stalks)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 oz olive oil
  • 1 can diced tomatoes (14.5 oz), drained
  • 1 oz red wine vinegar
  • 10 oz red bell pepper, seeds removed and cut into ½ in. pieces (approx. 1 pepper)
  • 2 oz pitted Kalamata olives
  • 1 oz capers, rinsed and drained
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 oz toasted pine nuts, to garnish
Nutrition
per 1 portion

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in water and add salt. Weigh in eggplant and set aside to soak for 20 minutes. Rinse and pat dry.
  2. Place parsley and basil into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place onion, celery, garlic and olive oil into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and without measuring cup sauté 5 min/250°F//speed .
  4. Add tomatoes and red wine vinegar, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 5 min/250°F//speed .
  5. Add reserved eggplant, red pepper, reserved chopped parsley and basil, olives, capers, salt and pepper and placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid cook 10 min/250°F//speed . Garnish with pine nuts and basil. Serve hot, cold or at room temperature.
