Crab Stuffed Lobster Tails

Ingredients

  • 2 fresh lobster tails (approx. 6-8 oz ea.)
  • ½ oz bread crumbs
  • 1 lemon, thin peel only, no pith, plus 2 tsp juice
  • 5 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only, plus extra to garnish
  • ¼ tsp salt (optional)
  • ¼ tsp celery seeds
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 oz mayonnaise
  • &frac18; tsp paprika
  • &frac18; tsp ground black pepper
  • 6 oz jumbo lump crab meat, canned, drained
  • 3 - 4 oz unsalted butter, melted, divided
  • 1 lemon, in wedges

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line a baking sheet with foil and set aside.
  2. Lay the lobster tails flat on a work surface and with kitchen shears cut down the back shell, stopping just before the fanned tail. Pull out lobster meat, all in one piece and set meat and shells aside.
  3. Place bread crumbs, lemon peel, parsley, salt and celery seed into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and chop a further 3 sec/speed 8.
  4. Add egg, mayonnaise, 2 tsp lemon juice, paprika, and pepper and mix 10 sec/speed 3.
  5. Add crab meat and mix 10 sec//speed 1. Spoon mixture into reserved lobster shells, top with reserved lobster tail meat and place on prepared baking sheet. Brush tops with 1 oz melted butter.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes (350°F) until cooked through. Garnish with parsley and serve with lemon wedges and 2-3 oz melted butter.

Useful Items

pastry brush, baking sheet, aluminum foil, oven
