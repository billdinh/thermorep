Tarte au Citron Reconstructed

Ingredients

Base

  • 50 g Speculoos biscuits
  • 10 g butter

Lemon Curd

  • ½ lemon, peeled, quartered and deseeded
  • 35 g butter
  • 65 g caster sugar
  • 1 medium egg
  • 1 egg yolk, from medium egg

Swiss Meringue

  • 60 g caster sugar
  • 2 egg whites, from medium eggs
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
Nutrition
per 1 portion

2682 kJ / 641 kcal

Preparation

Base

  1. Place biscuits and butter in mixing bowl then crush 15 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.

Lemon Curd

  1. Place lemon in mixing bowl then chop 15 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add butter, sugar, eggs and egg yolk then heat 8 min/90°C/speed 2. Mix 30 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside to cool. Meanwhile, thoroughly clean and dry mixing bowl.

Swiss Meringue

  1. Insert butterfly whisk. Place sugar, egg whites and lemon juice in mixing bowl then whisk 4 min/37°C/speed 1 -4, increasing speed gradually during first minute. Remove butterfly whisk.
  2. Divide base mixture between 2 serving glasses. Add a layer of cooled lemon curd and finish with a layer of meringue. Place in fridge to chill for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Before serving, lightly brown meringue using a blowtorch. Serve chilled.

Useful Items

2 glasses, kitchen blowtorch, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Vary the flavour by substituting lemon for orange.
