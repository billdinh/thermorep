Carrot and Pineapple Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 50 g carrots, cut in pieces
  • 400 g fresh pineapple, cut in pieces
  • 80 g bananas, cut in pieces
  • 1 Tbsp fresh coriander, leaves only
  • 2 sprigs fresh mint, leaves only
  • 300 g water
Nutrition
per 100 g
Calories
109 kJ / 26 kcal
Protein
0 g
Carbohydrates
6 g
Fat
0 g

Preparation

  1. Place carrots in mixing bowl and grate 4 sec/speed 7.
  2. Add pineapple, bananas, coriander, mint and water then blend 1 min/speed 9. Serve.
