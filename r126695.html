Crostini with mushrooms and fontina

Ingredients

  • 15 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 1 onion, cut into halves
  • 30 g butter
  • 10 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 400 g fresh button mushrooms, cut into slices
  • 10 g red wine
  • 1 tsp fine salt
  • ½ tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 250 g Fontina cheese, in slices
  • 20 slices bread (eg. ciabatta, baguette)

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Line baking tray with baking paper.
  2. Place parsley in mixing bowl, chop 2 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place onion in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add butter and oil and sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  5. Add mushrooms, red wine, salt and pepper and sauté 5 min/100°C//speed 1.
  6. Add chopped parsley and stir 5 sec//speed 1.
  7. Top bread slices with cheese, arrange crostini on baking tray and allow to melt 10–15 minutes (180°C).
  8. Top crostini with mushrooms, season with a little black pepper as desired and serve immediately.

Useful Items

baking tray, baking paper, bowl, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Top bread slices with cheese first before preparing the mushrooms.
  • Try using ½ tsp herbes de Provence instead of parsley.
  • The Italian fontina cheese can be replaced with gruyère.
