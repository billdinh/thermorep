Spiced coriander prawns

Ingredients

  • 350 g large raw prawn cutlets
  • 20 g nigella seeds
  • 1 - 2 fresh green chillies, trimmed and cut into halves
  • 30 sprigs fresh coriander (approx. 60 g), leaves, stalks and roots, cut into thirds (see Tips)
  • ¼ tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 tsp garlic paste (see Tips)
  • 1 tsp ginger paste (see Tips)
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 20 g lemon juice
  • 10 g olive oil, plus extra for frying
  • 1 lemon, to serve

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh prawns into it. Set bowl aside.
  2. Place nigella seeds, chillies, coriander, turmeric, garlic paste, ginger paste and salt into mixing bowl and mill 3 sec/speed 9.
  3. Add lemon juice and oil and mix 10 sec/speed 8 or until a paste forms.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then mix 5 sec/speed 8. Transfer paste into bowl with prawns, stir to coat, then set aside to marinate for 20 minutes.
  5. Place a large frying pan over medium heat and add oil. Place prawns with paste into pan and fry for 1-2 minutes each side, until just cooked. Transfer onto a serving dish and serve immediately with lemon.

Useful Items

bowl, serving bowl, large frying pan, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • Clean and scrape down the roots of your coriander with a spoon to remove the tougher, more fibrous bits on the outside of the roots, before adding to the mixing bowl.
  • To make your own ginger and garlic pastes, please refer to the recipes on this recipe chip.
  • Try serving these prawns with Spiced tomato chutney. To make your own chutney, please refer to the recipe on this recipe chip.
