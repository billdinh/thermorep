Asian nachos

Ingredients

Wonton nachos

  • 125 g wonton wrappers, cut on the diagonal to form triangles
  • olive oil spray, for coating

Pork mince

  • 100 g mozzarella cheese, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 100 g cheddar cheese, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 20 g peanut oil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 3 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 1 fresh long red chilli, trimmed and deseeded, if preferred
  • 500 g lean pork mince
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 20 g soy sauce
  • 40 g hoisin sauce
  • 20 g oyster sauce

Asian salad

  • ¼ red cabbage, finely shredded (approx. 200 g)
  • 2 spring onions/shallots, trimmed and cut into thin slices
  • 4 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only
  • 1 avocado, flesh only, cut into bite-size pieces
  • ½ lime, juice only
  • 1 tbsp rice wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • sea salt, to taste
  • ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1 - 2 fresh jalapeño chillies, trimmed to taste, to serve (see Tip)

Preparation

Wonton nachos

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C. Line 2 baking trays (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Spray wonton wrappers on both sides with oil and place in a single layer onto prepared baking trays. Bake for 2-3 minutes (220°C), until lightly golden. Remove trays from oven, turn over wontons and bake for a further 1-2 minutes (220°C) or until lightly golden. Remove from oven and set aside to cool.

Pork mince

  1. Place cheeses into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  2. Place oil, garlic, ginger and chilli into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7.
  3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and cook 2 min/120°C/speed 1.
  4. Add pork and cook 3 min/120°C//speed 1, without measuring cup. Scrape base and blades of mixing bowl with spatula to break up any lumps.
  5. Add sugar, soy sauce, hoisin sauce and oyster sauce and cook 3 min/120°C//speed 1, without measuring cup.
  6. Preheat oven grill. Transfer pork mixture into a baking dish and top with reserved cheeses. Place dish under preheated oven grill for 2-3 minutes or until cheese melts and is golden brown.

Asian salad

  1. Place cabbage, spring onions, coriander, avocado, lime juice, vinegar, sesame oil, salt and pepper into a bowl and toss gently to combine.
  2. Serve mince with Asian salad, jalapeños and baked wontons.

Useful Items

bowl, baking trays (30 x 40 cm), baking paper, baking dish, oven grill, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • You can replace the fresh jalapeño with pickled jalapeño, if desired.
  • To mince your own meat, please refer to the guide in the front of The Basic Cookbook. Please note that it may increase the total time of the recipe.
