Artisan Plant Pizza

Ingredients

Base

  • 50 g spelt flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 50 g buckwheat flour
  • ½ tsp dried instant yeast
  • 2 pinches fine sea salt
  • 4 - 5 Tbsp water
  • 2 tsp olive oil

Crumb

  • 50 g cashew nuts
  • ¾ tsp fine sea salt
  • ¼ tsp garlic granules
  • ½ tsp dried mixed herbs

Topping

  • 3 Tbsp tomato purée
  • vegetables, as desired, sliced thinly for topping

Preparation

Base

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Place spelt flour, buckwheat flour, yeast and salt in mixing bowl then mix 5 sec/speed 4.
  2. Add water and oil then mix 10 sec/speed 4. Knead 3 min/. Meanwhile, dust a baking tray with flour. Tip out dough onto a floured surface and roll into a circle (Ø 20 cm). Transfer to prepared baking tray then set aside. Wash and dry mixing bowl then prepare crumb and toppings.

Crumb

  1. Place cashews, salt, garlic granules and mixed herbs in mixing bowl then grind 40 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.

Topping

  1. Top pizza base with tomato purée and vegetables of choice (see tip). Top with reserved crumb, to taste. Bake for 10-15 minutes (180°C) until golden.
  2. Serve in slices.

Useful Items

rolling pin, baking tray, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Homemade flours are easy to make in the Thermomix®. In this recipe, you can grind spelt and buckwheat grains to create the flours for the pizza base yourself. Also try grinding nuts and pulses for a range of other uses.
  • For the topping use vegetables of choice. Thinly sliced peppers, courgettes or mushrooms all work well.
  • Any leftover crumb can be used to top lasagnes and savoury bakes as desired.
