Courgette and Pine Nut Cake

Ingredients

  • 150 g butter, diced, plus extra for greasing
  • 1 lemon, thin peelings of skin and 40 g juice
  • 170 g caster sugar
  • 300 g courgettes, cut in pieces (4 cm)
  • 3 large eggs
  • 220 g plain flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 220 g sultanas
  • 70 g pine nuts

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Grease sides of a deep spring form tin (Ø 28 cm) with butter and line base with baking paper.
  2. Place lemon peelings and 20 g sugar in mixing bowl then grate 10 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and grate again 10 sec/speed 10.
  3. Add courgettes and chop 3 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place butter and remaining 150 g sugar in mixing bowl then cream 20 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then cream again 20 sec/speed 4.
  5. Add eggs and mix 20 sec/speed 4 until incorporated (the mixture will look curdled).
  6. Add flour and baking powder then mix 20 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  7. Add reserved courgette-lemon peel mixture, sultanas, 50 g pine nuts and lemon juice then stir in with spatula.
  8. Insert spatula through hole in mixing bowl lid and stir while mixing 15 sec//speed 3. Transfer to prepared tin, spread evenly and scatter remaining 20 g pine nuts over the top. Bake for 40 minutes (180°C) until golden and a skewer inserted in centre comes out clean. Leave to cool completely in tin before removing to serve.

Useful Items

baking paper, spring form tin (Ø 28 cm), skewer, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Gluten-free plain flour and gluten-free baking powder can also be used, if desired.
  • The courgettes for this recipe need to be sweet so this is best made in the early summer when courgettes are at their smallest. Otherwise choose small baby courgettes.
