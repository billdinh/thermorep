Pumpkin and golden syrup pudding

Ingredients

  • 200 g unsalted butter, cut into pieces, plus extra for greasing
  • 120 g golden syrup, plus extra 2 tbsp for coating
  • 300 g pumpkin, peeled and cut into pieces (3-4 cm)
  • 3 eggs
  • 240 g self-raising flour
  • 1 tsp mixed spice
  • ½ tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 1000 g water
  • 60 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • thickened cream or ice cream, to serve (optional)

Preparation

  1. Grease a deep-sided pudding basin (approx. 19 cm, ensure basin fits comfortably into Varoma dish - see Tip). Place 2 tablespoons of the golden syrup into bottom of basin and spread to coat base. Set basin aside.
  2. Place pumpkin into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 9.
  3. Add 180 g of the butter and 60 g of the golden syrup and heat 3 min/100°C/speed 1.
  4. Beat 30 sec/speed 3, adding 1 egg at a time through hole in mixing bowl lid.
  5. Add flour, mixed spice and bicarbonate of soda and combine 30 sec/speed 4. Transfer into prepared pudding basin, cover top of basin with a piece of baking paper, then cover with a piece of aluminium foil. Fold paper and foil down over edges of basin to create a lid. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  6. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position and lay 2 wooden skewers (18-19 cm in length) across base of Varoma dish and carefully place basin on top. Secure Varoma lid and steam 50 min/Varoma/speed 1 or until a wooden skewer inserted into the centre of the pudding comes out clean. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  7. Place cream, remaining 60 g golden syrup and remaining 20 g butter into mixing bowl and heat 4 min/80°C/speed 1. Carefully turn out pudding onto a serving plate and drizzle with sauce. Serve with thickened cream or ice cream (optional).

Useful Items

baking paper, aluminium foil, wooden skewers, pudding basin, serving plate

Hints & Tricks

  • Ensure your pudding basin fits comfortably into Varoma dish; the Varoma lid must be able to close securely.
