Barbecue sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 brown onion (approx. 120 g), cut into halves
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 250 g tomato sauce (gluten free) (see Tips)
  • 50 g apple cider vinegar
  • 50 g honey
  • 100 g brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce (gluten free) (see Tips)
  • 60 g water
  • ½ tsp paprika

Preparation

  1. Place onion and garlic into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7.
  2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Add all remaining ingredients and mix 5 sec/speed 2, then cook 20 min/100°C/speed 2, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  3. Insert measuring cup. Blend 30 sec/speed 1 -8, increasing speed gradually from speed 1 to speed 8.
  4. Transfer into a sealable jar or container. Allow to cool, then place into refrigerator until ready to use.

Useful Items

sealable storage jar or container, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Be sure to check the label of your tomato sauce to ensure it's gluten free. To make your own tomato sauce, please refer to the recipe in The Basic Cookbook or Everyday Cookbook.
  • To make your own gluten free worcestershire sauce, please refer to the recipe on this recipe chip.
  • You can store this barbecue sauce in the refrigerator for up to 1 week.
