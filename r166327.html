Layered patties and vegetables with bell pepper sauce

Ingredients

  • 20 - 30 g fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 150 g spring onions, cut into pieces
  • 900 g lamb shoulder meat, minced
  • 1 tsp salt, plus extra to taste
  • 1 tsp ground white pepper, plus extra to taste
  • 400 - 500 g onions, 300 g cut in halves
  • 150 g red peppers, cut into pieces
  • 20 g olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 400 g canned whole tomatoes
  • 70 g tomato purée (concentrated)
  • 200 g water
  • 400 g potatoes, sliced (0.5 cm)
  • 350 g fresh tomatoes, sliced (0.5 cm)
  • 200 - 250 g green peppers, cut into 8 rings

Preparation

  1. Place parsley and spring onions into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add lamb mince, ½ tsp salt, ½ tsp ground white pepper and mix 30 sec/speed 3. Transfer into a bowl and refrigerate for 20 minutes.
  3. Place 300 g onions cut in halves, red bell peppers and olive oil into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5, then remove measuring cup and sauté 5 min/120°C/speed . Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add canned tomatoes, tomato purée, water, ½ tsp salt, ½ tsp pepper and mix 5 sec/speed 3.
  5. Lightly grease Varoma dish and tray. Slice remaining 100-200 g onions, crosswise to obtain 8 slices. Distribute 5 onion slices in Varoma dish and 3 in Varoma tray. Shape mince into 8 patties.| Place patties on top of onions and cover each patty with a potato slice, a tomato slice and a green pepper ring. Any leftover potato slices can be distributed between the patties. Season with salt and pepper, set Varoma into position and cook 50 min/Varoma/speed 1. Serve layered patties and vegetables with pepper sauce.

Hints & Tricks

  • For an even more appealing presentation, transfer steamed patties into an ovenproof dish, pour some sauce on top and brown for 5 minutes under the grill.
  • To rewarm or gratin: transfer layered patties and vegetables into an oven dish, pour some sauce on top and place under the preheated oven grill for 5 minutes or until grilled.
  • Extra sauce could be used for further uses as e.g. to dress rice or pasta.
