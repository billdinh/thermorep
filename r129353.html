Chicken and bacon casserole

Ingredients

Chicken

  • 150 g white bread, slightly stale
  • 15 g parsley
  • 150 g cheese, medium-hard (e.g. Edam), cut into pieces
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 800 g chicken breast fillet, cut into pieces (3-4 cm)
  • 1 tsp fine salt
  • 20 g honey
  • 20 g mustard

Sauce and assembly

  • 1000 g milk
  • 100 g plain flour
  • 40 g butter
  • 1 lemon, juice only
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp fine salt
  • 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp mustard
  • 200 g bacon slices, cut into pieces

Preparation

Chicken

  1. Place bread and parsley in mixing bowl and grate 15 sec/speed 9. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place cheese in mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place olive oil and garlic in mixing bowl, chop 2 sec/speed 8 and sauté 5 min/120°C/speed 1.
  4. Add chicken, salt, honey and mustard and cook 10 min/120°C//speed .
  5. Then shred 4 sec//speed 4 (see tip). Transfer to a bowl and set aside.

Sauce and assembly

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Place milk, plain flour, butter, lemon juice, paprika, salt, pepper and mustard in mixing bowl and cook 9 min/90°C/speed 4.
  3. Arrange chicken on bottom of a casserole dish (approx. 30 cm x 20 cm x 5 cm), cover with bacon, cheese and finish with sauce. Cover with breadcrumbs and bake in oven at 180°C for about 20 minutes. Serve hot.

Useful Items

rectangular baking dish (30 cm x 20 cm x 5 cm), bowl, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve chicken and bacon casserole with rice.
  • For a chunkier texture, reduce chopping time in step 5 or do not chop at all.
