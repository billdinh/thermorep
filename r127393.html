Sunrise Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 350 g ripe mangoes, cut in pieces, frozen in a single layer (see tip)
  • 200 g fresh pineapple, cut in pieces
  • 4 passion fruits
  • 2 bananas (approx. 200 g), cut in pieces
  • 200 g strawberries, frozen
  • 80 g lemon juice, freshly squeezed
Nutrition
per 1 glass
Calories
473 kJ / 113 kcal
Protein
2 g
Carbohydrates
21 g
Fat
1 g

Preparation

  1. Place mangoes, pineapple and passion fruits in mixing bowl then blend 1 min 30 sec/speed 10. Transfer mango-pineapple mixture to 7 glasses and place in the freezer.
  2. Place bananas, strawberries and lemon juice in mixing bowl then blend 1 min 30 sec/speed 10. Pour banana-strawberry mixture on top of mango-pineapple mixture and serve immediately. 

Useful Items

freezer

Hints & Tricks

  • Freeze mango in individual pieces or small clusters. Cut mango into pieces, spread out on a tray in a single layer, freeze, then use frozen mango in recipe or transfer to a freezer bag for later use.
  • Replace the bananas with mango sorbet for a more tropical, refreshing flavour.
