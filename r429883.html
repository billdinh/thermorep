Pear and Goat's Cheese Parcels

Ingredients

  • 1 pear (approx. 260 g), firm, cored and quartered, sliced ​​(5 mm)
  • 125 g soft goat's cheese, sliced (1 cm)
  • 3 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 sprig fresh rosemary, small, leaves only
  • 40 g walnut halves
  • 30 g honey
  • 30 g olive oil
  • fine sea salt, to taste
  • ground black pepper, to taste
  • 500 g water
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1054 kJ / 252 kcal
Protein
5 g
Carbohydrates
16 g
Fat
18 g

Preparation

  1. Cut 4 squares of baking paper (approx. 30 cm).
  2. Divide pear and goat's cheese slices between prepared sheets of baking paper, layering alternately in the centre of each, then sprinkle with lemon juice.
  3. Place rosemary and walnuts in mixing bowl then chop 2 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Remove 2 tsp to a small bowl and set aside.
  4. Add honey and oil then mix 5 sec/speed 3. Distribute evenly over pear and goat's cheese, then sprinkle with salt and pepper, to taste. Tie up parcels with kitchen string. Place 2 parcels in Varoma dish and 2 on Varoma tray. Insert Varoma tray into Varoma dish.
  5. Place water in mixing bowl then place covered Varoma into position and steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 1. Transfer parcels to serving plates and open. Sprinkle with reserved walnut mixture and serve warm.

Useful Items

baking paper, kitchen string

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve on its own as a starter or with a green salad.
