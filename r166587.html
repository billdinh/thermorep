Vegetable Shumai

Ingredients

  • 4 oz fresh mixed mushrooms, e.g. cremini, portabello, shitake, oyster
  • 2 oz shallots
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • ½ oz fresh ginger, peeled
  • 1 tsp toasted sesame oil
  • 4 oz carrots, cut into pieces (approx. 2 carrots)
  • 4 oz snow peas, trimmed
  • 2 oz celery, cut into pieces (approx. 2 stalks)
  • 15 sprigs cilantro, leaves and tender stems
  • 1 tsp mirin
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 egg white
  • 40 oz water
  • non-stick cooking spray
  • 20 - 25 dumpling wrappers, e.g. shumai wrappers
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
259 kJ / 62 kcal
Protein
2 g

Preparation

  1. Place mushrooms, shallots, garlic, ginger and sesame oil into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and sauté 5 min/250°F/speed 1.
  2. Add carrots, snow peas, celery, cilantro, mirin and soy sauce and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and chop a further 5 sec/speed 5.
  3. Add egg white and mix 5 sec/speed 8. Transfer vegetable mixture into a bowl and set aside.
  4. Spray Varoma tray with cooking spray and set aside. Place water into mixing bowl and heat 10 min/Varoma/speed 1. Meanwhile assemble shumai.
  5. Place 1 heaping tbsp of vegetable mixture into center of each shumai wrapper, pinch edges as you gather the sides of the wrapper around vegetable filling, leaving the top uncovered. Tap shumai on your work surface to lightly flatten the bottom, allowing it to stand upright.
  6. Transfer shumai into prepared Varoma tray. Place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 8-10 min/Varoma/speed 1 until wrappers are translucent and cooked through. Serve immediately with soy sauce.
