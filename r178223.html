Zoodles with broccoli pesto

Ingredients

Broccoli pesto

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 50 g broccoli, broken into florets
  • 20 g blanched almonds
  • 30 g Parmesan cheese, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 80 g grapeseed oil
  • 1 Â½ tsp apple cider vinegar
  • 2 - 3 sprigs fresh basil, leaves only

Zoodles

  • 500 g water
  • 1 - 2 zucchinis (approx. 200 g), spiralised (see Tips)
  • 1 - 2 carrots (approx. 200 g), spiralised (see Tips)

Preparation

Broccoli pesto

  1. Place all pesto ingredients into mixing bowl and blend 10 sec/speed 7 or until mixture forms a paste. Transfer into a large bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Zoodles

  1. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish into position and place zucchini and carrot into it. Secure Varoma lid and steam 8-10 min/Varoma/speed 1 or until just tender.
  2. Set Varoma aside for 1-2 minutes and allow to drain, leaving Varoma lid on to keep warm. Transfer zoodles into bowl with pesto and toss gently to combine before serving.

Useful Items

large bowl, spiralizer

Hints & Tricks

  • Use a spiraliser to create zoodles or peel vegetables into ribbons and slice ribbons into long lengths.
