Vanilla Sugar

Ingredients

  • 1 vanilla bean pod, cut into pieces
  • 7 oz sugar

Preparation

  1. Place vanilla pod pieces and sugar into a jar. Close jar and shake to mix vanilla pieces with sugar. Allow to dry for 2-3 days.
  2. Place jar contents into mixing bowl and grind 20 sec/speed 10. Use vanilla sugar as needed or transfer back into jar and store in a dry place.

Useful Items

jar with twist-off lid

Hints & Tricks

  • 1 tsp homemade vanilla sugar is roughly equivalent to 3 tsp (0.5 oz) store-bought vanilla sugar.
  • Use vanilla pods left over from other recipes (rinse and dry pods after first use). This vanilla sugar will not be as strong and aromatic.
  • Quick version: without first drying for 2-3 days, place vanilla pod pieces with sugar into mixing bowl and grind 1 min/speed 10. This vanilla sugar will be flavorful, but may have a tendency to clump.
