Nectarine and Cherry Panzanella Salad

Ingredients

Panzanella

  • 12 oz bread, cubed (1 in.)
  • 1 oz extra virgin olive oil
  • ½ tsp salt

Dressing

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 2 ½ oz extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 ½ oz white vinegar
  • 1 pinch sugar
  • 1 heaping tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper

Salad

  • 8 oz fresh cherries, pitted
  • ½ package arugula (approx. 8 oz)
  • 1 nectarine, hard, cut into pieces (½ in.)
  • ½ Lebanese cucumber, cut into pieces (½ in.)

Preparation

Panzanella

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line a baking sheet with parchment and set aside.
  2. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in bread cubes and oil, sprinkle with salt and toss to coat. Transfer to prepared baking sheet and bake for 15 minutes (350°F) or until golden brown. Set aside to cool.

Dressing

  1. Place garlic, oil, vinegar, sugar, mustard, salt and pepper into mixing bowl and blend 15 sec/speed 8. Set mixing bowl aside.
  2. Place reserved bread cubes into a large serving bowl and toss with reserved dressing.

Salad

  1. Add cherries, arugula, nectarines and cucumbers to serving bowl and toss to coat. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

parchment paper, large serving bowl, baking sheet, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Use apples instead of nectarines and baby spinach instead of arugula.
