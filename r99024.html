Pâtè with Riesling

Ingredients

  • 400 g water
  • 1 tsp sea salt, plus an extra 3/4 tsp for soaking
  • 250 g duck livers
  • ½ dried bay leaf
  • 6 whole black peppercorns
  • 150 g Riesling
  • 180 g salted butter, softened, cut into pieces
  • 1 tbsp port
  • &frac18; tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • bread, toasted, to serve

Preparation

  1. Place a large bowl onto mixing bowl lid, then weigh water, ¾ tsp salt and livers into it. Set bowl aside and allow livers to soak for 30 minutes. Drain livers and discard salted water.
  2. Place bay leaf, peppercorns, Riesling and drained livers into mixing bowl and cook 10-12 min/80°C/speed 1, or until livers are cooked. Transfer livers into simmering basket and set aside to drain. Discard cooking liquid.
  3. Place cooked livers, butter, port or cognac, 1 tsp salt and pepper into mixing bowl and mix 20-30 sec/speed 8, or until a mousse consistency is achieved.
  4. Transfer into a terrine or ramekin (350 ml capacity) and refrigerate for a minimum 2 hours. Serve with toasted bread.

Useful Items
