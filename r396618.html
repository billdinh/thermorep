Vegetarian quiche

Ingredients

Pastry

  • 45 g rolled oats
  • 60 g hazelnuts
  • 80 g sunflower seeds
  • 115 g plain flour
  • 100 g butter, chilled and cut into pieces, plus extra for greasing
  • 1 egg
  • ½ - 1 tsp cold water

Filling

  • 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed
  • boiling water
  • 60 g vintage cheddar cheese, cut into pieces
  • 1 leek, trimmed and cut into thin slices
  • 20 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 40 g fresh baby spinach leaves
  • salt, to season
  • ground black pepper, to season
  • 4 eggs
  • 60 g milk of choice
  • 60 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 3 raw baby beetroots (orange or red), trimmed and cut into thin slices

Preparation

Pastry

  1. Place oats, hazelnuts and sunflower seeds into mixing bowl and mill 5 sec/speed 7, until finely chopped.
  2. Add flour and butter and mix 5 sec/speed 5, to a fine breadcrumb mixture.
  3. Add egg and water and mix 5 sec/speed 5, until mixture just comes together, then knead 1 min/. Transfer onto a silicone bread mat and shape into a round disc, then wrap in plastic wrap and place into refrigerator for 15-20 minutes. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Preheat oven to 160°C. Grease a loose-based round fluted tart tin (23 x 3.5 cm).
  5. On a silicone bread mat or piece of baking paper, roll out pastry into a circle large enough to cover base and sides of prepared tin (approx. 27 cm). Transfer pastry into prepared tin and press into base and sides. Trim edges of pastry. Place into refrigerator for 10-15 minutes.
  6. Remove pastry case from refrigerator. Line with baking paper, then fill with pie weights, rice or dried beans. Bake for 15 minutes (160°C), then remove from oven and remove baking paper and pie weights. Bake for a further 10-15 minutes or until lightly golden. Set aside to cool slightly.

Filling

  1. Place asparagus into a bowl and carefully cover with boiling water. Blanch for 1-2 minutes, then remove from bowl and refresh under cold running water to stop the cooking process. Set aside to drain.
  2. Place cheese into mixing bowl and grate 3 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Rinse mixing bowl.
  3. Place leek and oil into mixing bowl and cook 4 min/100°C/speed 1.
  4. Add spinach and cook 30 sec/100°C//speed 1. Scatter leek mixture over reserved pastry base and sprinkle with half of the reserved grated cheese. Arrange reserved asparagus over cheese and season with salt and pepper. Do not clean mixing bowl.
  5. Place eggs, milk and cream into mixing bowl and whisk 5 sec/speed 4. Add cream mixture to pastry case, scatter with remaining cheese and decorate with beetroot slices. Bake for 35-45 minutes (160°C) or until golden and set. Leave to cool slightly in tin (approx. 10 minutes) before serving.

Useful Items

loose-based round fluted tart tin (23 cm), silicone bread mat, baking paper, rolling pin, pie weights, rice or dried beans, bowl, plastic wrap, oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Baby's serve: wait until your baby is confidently managing finger foods (which may not happen until 8-9 months or older) before offering them some of this quiche, cut into appropriate-sized pieces.
  • Toddler's serve: serve as is, cut up as needed.
