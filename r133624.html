Frozen Raspberry Ripple Meringue

Ingredients

  • 500 g fresh raspberries
  • 40 g icing sugar
  • 40 g Cointreau
  • 500 g double cream
  • 90 g meringue nests, roughly broken in pieces, plus extra for garnish
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1590 kJ / 380 kcal

2.5 g

Preparation

  1. Line a savarin mould (Ø 20 cm) with 2 layers of cling film, overlapping in the middle, then set aside.
  2. Place 250 g raspberries, icing sugar and Cointreau in mixing bowl then blend 10 sec/speed 8. Pour through a fine sieve to remove seeds, collecting purée in a bowl below. Set aside. Clean mixing bowl.
  3. Insert butterfly whisk. Place cream in mixing bowl then whisk without setting a time/speed 3.5 until soft peaks form - watching carefully to avoid over-whipping. Transfer to a large bowl.
  4. Place bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in meringue. Remove bowl from lid then gently fold meringue into cream with a metal spoon.
  5. Return bowl with cream to mixing bowl lid and weigh in 120 g reserved raspberry purée. Remove bowl from lid then gently fold purée into cream, creating a marbled effect. Spoon into prepared tin, wrap in cling film then freeze until solid (approx. 4 hours). Cover bowl containing remaining raspberry purée and set aside in fridge.
  6. When ready to serve, remove mould from freezer and tip out onto a serving plate, then remove cling film. Arrange remaining 250 g raspberries in middle, scatter with extra crumbled merginue shells, drizzle with reserved raspberry purée and serve.

Useful Items

ring mould (20 cm) (e.g. savarin or bundt tin), cling film, fine sieve or nut milk bag, large bowl, serving plate, refrigerator, freezer
