Seared Scallops with Sweet Potato Purée

Ingredients

  • 3 oz leeks, white part only, cut into pieces
  • ¾ tsp salt, divided
  • 6 oz sweet potatoes, peeled, cut into pieces (approx. 1 sm.)
  • 8 oz water
  • 1 tsp vegetable stock paste
  • ½ vanilla bean, seeds scraped out (optional)
  • 1 tsp apple cider vinegar
  • ½ tsp ground ginger
  • 1 pinch ground white pepper, to taste
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 6 jumbo sea scallops, cleaned, patted dry with paper towels
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 3 oz whole milk
  • baby arugula, to garnish
  • 8 blackberries, to garnish

Preparation

  1. Place leeks and ¼ tsp salt into mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 5.
  2. Add sweet potatoes and chop a further 4 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add water, stock paste, vanilla bean seeds and vanilla bean pod, cider vinegar, ginger and white pepper and cook 15 min/212°F/speed 1. Meanwhile 3-5 minutes before purée is finished cooking, begin heating a skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Add oil to skillet, season scallops with ½ tsp salt and pepper and cook until browned (approx. 2 min. per side). Transfer to a plate and keep warm.
  5. Add milk to mixing bowl and purée 1 min/speed 5-10 increasing speed gradually. Adjust seasoning to taste.
  6. To serve, divide sweet potato purée between two plates and top with seared scallops. Garnish with arugula and blackberries and serve warm.
