Pulled pork with apple and fennel salad

Ingredients

Pulled pork

  • 140 g red onion, cut into halves
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 1 - 2 tbsp taco seasoning (see Tips)
  • 20 g rice malt syrup
  • 50 g apple cider vinegar
  • 40 g Worcestershire sauce
  • 20 g soy sauce
  • 400 g canned chopped tomatoes
  • 50 g tomato paste
  • 900 g pork loin, cut into cubes (3 cm)
  • 1 ½ tbsp cornflour

Salad and assembly

  • 1 red apple, cut into thin slices (see Tips)
  • 1 fennel bulb, cut into thin slices (see Tips)
  • 60 g rocket
  • bread, of choice (see Tips)
  • 1 avocado, flesh only, cut into thin slices
  • sour cream, to serve
  • fresh coriander leaves, to serve
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges (optional)

Preparation

Pulled pork

  1. Place onion, oil and taco seasoning into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 4.
  2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then cook 5 min/100°C/speed 1, without measuring cup.
  3. Add syrup, vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, soy sauce, tomatoes and tomato paste and cook 4 min/100°C/speed 1. Transfer into a large bowl to cool (approx. 30 minutes). Place large bowl with marinade onto mixing bowl lid and weigh pork into it. Stir well to combine. Cover with plastic wrap and place into refrigerator for a minimum of 1 hour or overnight to marinate.
  4. Place pork and marinade into mixing bowl and cook 20-25 min/90°C//speed 1, until pork is tender. Using simmering basket, carefully strain liquid into a jug.
  5. Place strained pork back into mixing bowl and shred 5 sec//speed 5. Transfer into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and cover to keep warm.
  6. Place reserved liquid and cornflour into mixing bowl and cook 5 min/100°C/speed 2 or until thickened. Transfer half of the sauce into bowl with reserved pork and stir to coat. Pour remaining sauce into a bowl and set aside.

Salad and assembly

  1. Place apple, fennel and rocket into a bowl and gently toss to combine.
  2. Top bread with shredded pork, apple and fennel salad and sliced avocado. Garnish with sour cream and coriander leaves, then serve with reserved sauce and lime wedges (optional).

Useful Items

large bowl, plastic wrap, jug, thermal serving bowl or other large bowl, bowl, salad bowl, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Try making your own Taco seasoning and Flat bread (please refer to the recipes on this recipe chip).
  • You can use any kind of bread (such as bread rolls or pita bread) in this recipe.
  • Use a mandolin to easily cut your apple and fennel into thin slices.
