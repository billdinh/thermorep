Cheesecake (Baked)

Ingredients

Base

  • 5 oz unsalted butter, cut into pieces, plus extra to grease
  • 9 oz plain cookies, (see Tip)
  • 1 oz brown sugar,

Filling

  • 24 oz cream cheese, room temperature
  • 5 oz heavy whipping cream,
  • 8 oz baker's sugar
  • 1 ½ tsp homemade vanilla sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 egg yolk, from large egg

Preparation

Base

  1. Grease the bottom and sides of a springform pan (8 in.) generously with butter.
  2. Place butter into mixing bowl and melt 2 min/212°F/speed 1.
  3. Add cookies and sugar and crush 10 sec/speed 7.
  4. Spread crumb mixture into base of prepared springform pan. Press down firmly to make a compact layer using a spoon or fingertips. Refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  5. Preheat oven to 425°F and clean mixing bowl.

Filling

  1. Place cream cheese, whipping cream, sugar and vanilla sugar into mixing bowl and mix 20 sec/speed 4.
  2. Add eggs and egg yolk and mix 20 sec/speed 4. Pour cheesecake filling over crumb base.
  3. Bake on a baking sheet on the lowest shelf of oven for 10 minutes (425°F). Reduce temperature to 300°F and bake for 45-50 minutes or until mixture is almost set. Allow to cool for one hour in turned-off oven with door ajar. Allow to cool to room temperature, then cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours before slicing and serving.

Useful Items

springform pan (8 in.), baking sheet, refrigerator, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Cooling the cake slowly in the oven helps prevent surface cracks. If a crack does form, you can disguise it with one of the following: a dusting of confectioners sugar, whipped cream, caramel sauce, chocolate sauce or fruit coulis.
  • Vanilla wafers, sugar cookies or graham crackers can be used for cheesecake base.
  • Lime or Lemon Cheesecake: when adding cream cheese, add grated zest and juice of 2 limes or small lemons. Garnish cake with lime or lemon zest or wedges.
  • Raspberry Swirl Cheesecake: after pouring cheesecake filling over crumb base clean the mixing bowl. Place 5.5 oz raspberries into mixing bowl and blend 30 sec/speed 10. Pass purée through a fine sieve into a bowl. Stir 2 tbsp sugar into purée and drop purée onto unbaked cheesecake with a teaspoon. Swirl sauce into filling with a wooden skewer or a toothpick. Bake as directed by the recipe.
