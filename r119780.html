Celebration saffron and sage bread with ginger butter

Ingredients

Saffron and sage bread

  • 200 g mixed dried fruit (e.g. apricots, cranberries, sultanas)
  • 100 g nuts (e.g. walnuts, pecans, pistachios)
  • 150 g water
  • 1 pinch saffron
  • 1 dried bay leaf
  • 4 allspice berries
  • 500 g baker's flour
  • 10 g dried instant yeast
  • 50 g honey
  • 3 eggs
  • 40 g unsalted butter, softened
  • 150 g full cream milk
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 15 fresh sage leaves, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon

Ginger butter

  • 120 g glacé ginger
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • 150 g unsalted butter, cut into cubes (2 cm)

Preparation

Saffron and sage bread

  1. Place dried fruit into mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 6. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place nuts into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 5. Transfer into bowl with dried fruit and set aside.
  3. Place water, saffron, bay leaf and allspice berries into mixing bowl and cook 6 min/Varoma/speed 1. Transfer into a small bowl and allow to cool to room temperature. Strain, using a fine-mesh sieve, into a jug then discard solids and set infused water aside. Rinse and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Place flour and yeast into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 4.
  5. Add honey, 2 of the eggs, butter, milk and salt and knead 2 min/, slowly adding infused water through hole in mixing bowl lid until combined.
  6. Add sage, cinnamon and reserved dried fruits and nuts and knead 1 min/. Transfer onto a silicone bread mat or lightly floured work surface and shape into a ball. Wrap dough in bread mat or place into an oiled bowl and cover with plastic wrap. Leave to prove until doubled in size (approx. 2 hours). Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  7. Preheat oven to 170°C. Line a large baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  8. Transfer dough onto a lightly floured work surface and divide into 12 equal portions (see Tips). Form into small balls and arrange on prepared baking tray in a Christmas tree shape. Whisk remaining egg in a small bowl and brush each ball with egg. Bake for 30-35 minutes (170°C), rotating tray if necessary after 20 minutes to ensure even baking, until golden brown and sounds hollow when tapped.

Ginger butter

  1. Place glacé ginger into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add ground ginger and butter and blend 10 sec/speed 4. Transfer into a sealable container and set aside in refrigerator until ready to serve.
  3. Serve bread with butter.

Useful Items

bowl, fine mesh sieve, jug, small bowl , plastic wrap, silicone bread mat, baking tray (30 x 40 cm), baking paper, whisk, pastry brush, sealable container, oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • You can replace allspice berries with whole cloves in this recipe.
  • Instead of forming into balls, dough can be plaited and arranged in a wreath shape.
