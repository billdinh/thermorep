Mini chive pikelets with mushrooms

Ingredients

Chive pikelets

  • 5 fresh chives, cut into quarters
  • 20 g butter, plus extra for frying
  • 140 g self-raising flour
  • 1 pinch sea salt
  • ¼ tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 180 g sour cream
  • 125 g full cream milk
  • 1 egg

Mushrooms

  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary (approx. 10 cm long), leaves only
  • 4 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves only
  • 2 spring onions/shallots (approx. 30 g), trimmed and cut into quarters
  • 8 slices prosciutto (approx. 160 g), rind removed and cut into halves
  • 40 g olive oil
  • 360 g button mushrooms, brushed clean and cut into slices
  • 2 tsp tamari sauce (optional)
  • 2 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper, to taste
  • fresh chives, finely chopped, for garnishing

Preparation

Chive pikelets

  1. Place chives into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula again.
  2. Add butter and melt 2 min/50°C/speed 1.
  3. Add all remaining pikelet ingredients and mix 10 sec/speed 5, or until smooth and combined.
  4. Place a medium-sized non-stick frying pan with a little butter over a medium-high heat. Place 2 teapsoons of batter into frying pan and fry until bubbles form on surface of pikelet. Flip pikelet, then contiue to fry for 1 minute. Set pikelet aside to cool. Repeat with remaining batter, frying in batches to make approx. 40 mini pikelets. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Mushrooms

  1. Place rosemary, thyme, spring onions/shallots and prosciutto or bacon into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula again.
  2. Add all remaining ingredients, except chives or micro herbs, and sauté 4 min/Varoma//speed , without measuring cup. Transfer into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and cover to keep warm.
  3. Garnish each pikelet with 1 tsp of the mushrooms, topped with finely chopped chives or micro herbs. Serve warm.

Useful Items

turner / fish slice, medium-sized non-stick frying pan, thermal serving bowl or other large bowl, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • For a slightly more decadent canapé, top mushrooms on each pikelet with 1 teaspoon mascarpone cheese just before serving.
