Pineapple parcels

Ingredients

  • ¼ - ½ vanilla pod, cut in half lengthwise
  • 50 g brown sugar
  • 1 lime, preferably organic, zest only
  • 1 pineapple
  • 30 - 40 g rum (optional)
  • 400 g water
  • 4 fresh mint leaves, finely chopped
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
739 kJ / 177 kcal
Protein
1 g
Carbohydrates
35 g
Fat
0 g

Preparation

  1. Place vanilla pod, brown sugar and lime zest in mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. With a sharp knife, peel pineapple, cut vertically in 4-6 wedges (depending on size of pineapple) and remove core. Cut each wedge in 3-5 mm slices and place on a sheet of baking paper or heat-resistant cling film, keeping the slices aligned to preserve the shape of the pineapple wedge. Drizzle pineapple with rum, sprinkle with reserved sugar mixture and close the parcels. Arrange parcels in Varoma dish and tray and allow to marinate for 20-30 minutes.
  3. Place water in mixing bowl, set Varoma in position and steam 15 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove Varoma. To serve, carefully open the parcels, arrange pineapple wedges on dessert plates, sprinkle with chopped mint leaves and serve warm or cold.

Useful Items

baking paper or heat-resistant cling film

Hints & Tricks

  • For 4 portions, the pineapple should weigh 650-800 g peeled (approx. 1500-1700 g unpeeled).
  • Serve warm with a scoop of lime sorbet or an ice cream of your choice (e.g. vanilla, coconut, rum and raisin).
  • After peeling the pineapple you can remove any remaining 'eyes' with a melon baller or the tip of a vegetable peeler.
  • This simple preparation method enhances the natural flavour of a fresh pineapple and makes a lovely light dessert that can be served hot or cold. Though most tasters will not have the patience to wait for it to cool!
  • If served to children, omit rum.
  • Replace lime zest with orange or lemon zest, and brown sugar with coconut sugar.
