Chicken and Farro Harvest Salad

Ingredients

  • 7 oz farro
  • 64 oz water, divided
  • 5 oz extra virgin olive oil
  • 6 oz almond butter
  • 2 oz maple syrup
  • 2 oz whole grain mustard
  • 2 oz apple cider vinegar
  • ½ tsp salt, divided
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper, divided
  • 3 sprigs fresh rosemary, leaves only
  • 24 oz chicken breast fillets, boneless, skinless
  • 6 oz Brussels sprouts, trimmed, halved
  • 12 oz acorn squash, seeded, peeled, diced (½ in.)
  • 9 oz red beets, peeled, sliced (¼ in.)
  • 1 bunch beet greens, sliced (½ in.)

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in farro and 32 oz water. Set aside and let soak 1 hour. Meanwhile make dressing and marinate chicken.
  2. Place a pitcher onto mixing bowl lid, weigh in oil and set aside.
  3. Place almond butter, maple syrup, mustard, vinegar, ¼ tsp salt, ⅛ tsp pepper and rosemary into mixing bowl and mix 1 min 30 sec/speed 5, while slowly pouring reserved oil onto mixing bowl lid, letting it drizzle around measuring cup in a thin stream onto rotating blades to create an emulsion. Return dressing to pitcher and set aside.
  4. Place chicken and 4 tbsp Almond Maple Dressing into a large zip lock bag and refrigerate until needed. Clean mixing bowl.
  5. Place Brussels sprouts into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a serving bowl and set aside.
  6. Drain farro. Place drained farro and 32 oz fresh water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position and place marinated chicken into Varoma bowl, leaving some holes unobstructed for steam to circulate.
  7. Insert Varoma tray and weigh squash onto one side and beets onto the other side. Season with ¼ tsp salt and ⅛ tsp pepper, secure Varoma lid and steam 15 min/Varoma//speed 2. Remove Varoma tray with beets and squash, secure Varoma lid and steam a further 15 min/Varoma//speed 2, until chicken is cooked through. Remove Varoma and set aside.
  8. Drain farro and transfer to bowl with chopped Brussels sprouts.
  9. Slice cooked chicken in ½ inch strips. Add chicken, reserved dressing and beet greens to bowl with farro and toss to coat. Top with steamed vegetables and serve immediately.

Useful Items

large zip lock freezer bag, pitcher, serving bowl, refrigerator
