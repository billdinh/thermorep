Bloody Maria

Ingredients

Pickled vegetables

  • 100 g mixed vegetables, cut into thin slices (i.e. carrots, radishes, red onion, red capsicum, celery heart, celery leaves)
  • 3 tbsp raw sugar
  • 1 tbsp fine salt
  • 250 g white wine vinegar
  • 1 dried bay leaf
  • 6 coriander seeds

Prosciutto batons

  • 8 slices prosciutto

Cocktail mix

  • 2 fresh jalapeño chillies, stalks removed and deseeded if preferred
  • 1 tsp flaked salt
  • 750 g tomato juice
  • 80 g lime juice (approx. 4 limes)
  • 250 g mezcal
  • 10 ice cubes
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika

Assembly

  • 1 tbsp fine salt
  • ½ - 1 tsp chilli powder
  • 2 limes, cut into quarters
  • ice cubes, to serve

Preparation

Pickled vegetables

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh mixed vegetables into it. Stir to combine and set aside.
  2. Place sugar, salt, vinegar, bay leaf and coriander seeds into mixing bowl and cook 8 min/Varoma/speed 2. Pour pickling liquid into bowl over mixed vegetables. Allow to cool, then cover with plastic wrap and place into refrigerator for a minimum of 2 hours to pickle. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Strain pickled vegetables through simmering basket over a jug, reserving pickling liquid. Transfer vegetables into a serving bowl and set aside until ready to serve.

Prosciutto batons

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C. Line a baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper, then line a plate with paper towel and set aside.
  2. Roll each of the 8 slices of prosciutto lengthways over skewers to form long, thin tubes. Place skewers onto prepared baking tray and bake for 6 minutes (200°C). Transfer onto prepared plate to cool, then remove skewers from prosciutto (it will become crispy as it cools).

Cocktail mix

  1. Place a heavy-based chargrill pan over high heat. Add jalapeños and chargrill, turning, until charred on all sides.
  2. Place salt and chargrilled jalapeños into mixing bowl and blend 3 sec/speed 8.
  3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and blend 3 sec/speed 9.
  4. Add tomato juice, lime juice, mezcal, ice, smoked paprika and 1 tablespoon of the reserved pickling liquid, then blend 45 sec/speed 9.

Assembly

  1. Place salt and chilli powder onto a plate and stir to combine. Lightly rub 1 lime quarter around rim of each serving glass, then dip rims into chilli salt.
  2. Place ice into each glass and top with Bloody Maria. Garnish with prosciutto batons and serve immediately with pickled vegetables and limes.

Useful Items

bowl, plastic wrap, serving bowl, jug, baking tray (30 x 40 cm), baking paper, paper towel, 1 plate, wooden skewers, chargrill pan , 8 serving glasses, refrigerator, oven, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • Use leftover pickled vegetables in tacos and burritos.
  • You can replace jalapeños with long green chillies and white wine vinegar with rice vinegar.
  • Virgin bloody Maria: Replace the mezcal with soda water for a non-alcoholic cocktail.
