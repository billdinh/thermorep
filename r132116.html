Chicken in yoghurt

Ingredients

  • 20 g coconut oil
  • 10 fresh curry leaves
  • 150 g brown onion, cut into quarters
  • 2 fresh long green chillies, trimmed and cut into halves
  • ½ bunch fresh coriander, leaves, stalks and roots (approx. 10 g), cut into thirds (see Tips)
  • 1000 g boneless skinless chicken thighs, cut into cubes (2-3 cm)
  • 20 g ginger paste (see Tips)
  • 10 g garlic paste (see Tips)
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tbsp garam masala (see Tips)
  • ½ tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 60 g tomato paste
  • 350 g Greek-style natural yoghurt

Preparation

  1. Place oil into mixing bowl and heat 1 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  2. Add curry leaves, onion, chillies and fresh coriander and chop 3 sec/speed 5.
  3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and cook 5 min/100°C/speed 1.
  4. Add chicken, ginger paste, garlic paste, cumin, ground coriander, garam masala, turmeric and salt and cook 3 min/100°C//speed 1.
  5. Add tomato paste and yoghurt and cook 12-15 min/100°C//speed  or until chicken is cooked through. Transfer onto a serving dish. Serve with your favourite side.

Hints & Tricks

  • Clean and scrape down the roots of your coriander with a spoon to remove the tougher, more fibrous bits on the outside of the roots before adding to the mixing bowl.
  • To make you own ginger paste, garlic paste and garam masala, please refer to the recipes on this recipe chip.
  • You can serve this with rice, bread, chutney or your favourite side. We served ours with Parathas. Please refer to the recipe on this recipe chip.
