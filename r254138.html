Pumpkin Pie

Ingredients

Crust

  • 4 oz unsalted butter, cut into pieces
  • 8 oz pumpkin seeds
  • 3 oz graham crackers
  • 1 oz brown sugar

Filling

  • 3 ½ oz brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 - 3 tsp homemade pumpkin spice mix
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 15 oz canned pumpkin purée
  • 6 oz heavy whipping cream
  • 4 oz whole milk
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 egg yolk, from large egg

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.

Crust

  1. Place butter into mixing bowl and melt 2 min/212°F/speed 1.
  2. Add pumpkin seeds, graham crackers and brown sugar and mix 10 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a pie pan (approx. 10 in.). Evenly press into the bottom and up the sides of pan.

Filling

  1. Place brown sugar, flour, pumpkin spice and salt into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add pumpkin purée, cream, milk, eggs and egg yolk and mix 20 sec/speed 4. Transfer to pie crust.
  3. Bake 50 minutes (350°F) until pie is set 2 inches in from edge, but still slightly wobbly in center. Turn off oven, leave door ajar and let cool 20 minutes. Chill completely in refrigerator (approx. 4 hr.) before cutting into 12 slices and serve.

Useful Items

pie pan (approx. 10 in.), refrigerator, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with whipped cream or ice cream
  • Filling can be refrigerated 30 minutes or up to 24 hours to intensify flavors.
  • Garnish top of pie with pumpkin seeds.
