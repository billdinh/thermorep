Teochew rice porridge with sweet potato

Ingredients

  • 180 g long grain white rice
  • 1200 g water
  • 300 g sweet potato, peeled and cut into cubes (3 cm)
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
594.3 kJ / 141.5 kcal
Protein
2.8 g
Carbohydrates
30.8 g
Fat
0.2 g

Preparation

  1. Place rice, water and sweet potato into mixing bowl and cook 20 min/100°C//speed , placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid. Divide between 6 bowls to serve.

Useful Items

serving bowls

Hints & Tricks

  • This simple rice porridge dish is often served with various small side dishes or as part of banquet meals. It's often considered a comfort food in Singapore.
