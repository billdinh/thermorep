Pork tenderloin with red wine sauce and vegetables

Ingredients

  • 30 g eschalots
  • 150 g red wine
  • 300 g carrots, cut into sticks (1 cm x 7 cm)
  • 1 pork tenderloin (500 g, approx. 6 cm)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 130 g water
  • ½ tsp Meat stock paste (refer to the recipe in The Basic Cookbook)
  • 8 juniper berries (optional)
  • 25 g cherry jam
  • 20 g unsalted butter
  • 1 tsp cornflour

Preparation

  1. Place eschalots and red wine into mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish into position and add carrots to it. Season pork tenderloin with salt and pepper. Place pork tenderloin into Varoma dish with carrots, secure Varoma lid and cook 10 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove Varoma.
  2. Add 100 g of the water, stock paste and juniper berries into mixing bowl. Place Varoma back into position, secure Varoma lid and cook 10-15 min/Varoma/speed 1, depending on desired cooking point of meat (see Tip). Set covered Varoma aside on a plate to keep meat warm. Strain steaming liquid through simmering basket into a bowl.
  3. Place strained steaming liquid, cherry jam and butter into mixing bowl. Place a small bowl onto mixing bowl lid, weigh into bowl remaining 30 g water, add cornflour to it and stir together. Add cornflour-water mixture into mixing bowl and cook 8 min/100°C/speed 1.
  4. Blend sauce 20 sec/speed 7. Cut pork fillet into slices (2 cm) and arrange with carrots in a serving dish. Transfer sauce to a small bowl and serve hot with pork and carrots.

Useful Items

bowl, small bowl, serving dish

Hints & Tricks

  • In step 2, the target cooking point of the pork tenderloin after 10 minutes is pink in the centre and tender. For well-done meat, prolong cooking time by 5 minutes.
