Rice Salad with Eggs and Tuna

Ingredients

  • 1200 g water
  • 1 tsp salt, plus extra to taste
  • 250 g long grain rice (12-16 minutes cooking time)
  • 2 medium eggs
  • 120 g carrots, cut in small cubes
  • 280 g courgettes, cut in small cubes
  • 100 g frozen green peas
  • 200 g tinned tuna in oil (weight includes oil), drained and flaked
  • 100 g olives, pitted, whole or chopped (black or green)
  • 15 - 20 g pickled gherkins, sliced
  • 1 sprig fresh mint, leaves only
  • 1 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 - 2 tsp vinegar (optional)
  • 7 cherry tomatoes, halved or quartered

Preparation

  1. Place water and salt in mixing bowl, insert simmering basket and weigh in rice. Stir rice with spatula to wet all grains.
  2. Place Varoma dish into position and add eggs to it. Insert Varoma tray and add carrots then arrange courgettes over the top. Finish with peas, then cover Varoma with Varoma lid and steam 21 min/Varoma/speed 2. Set Varoma aside. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and cool rice under cold running water. Place simmering basket on a plate to drain then set aside in fridge until required.
  3. Place Varoma back into position and steam again 5 min/Varoma/speed 1. Set Varoma aside and allow eggs and vegetables to cool.
  4. Transfer rice to a large serving bowl, add reserved cooked vegetables, tuna, olives, gherkins and mint. Add oil and vinegar (if using), mix to combine then adjust seasoning, to taste. Peel boiled eggs then slice and use to garnish salad. Add tomatoes then serve at room temperature or chilled.

Useful Items

large serving bowl, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • A colourful and nutritious all-in-one meal to feed the whole family, rice is cooked in the simmering basket while eggs and vegetables are steamed in the Varoma above. Adapt the recipe to your tastes by using a different variety of rice, or try adding sweetcorn or mozzarella cheese before serving.
  • For other types of rice, adjust the cooking time in step 2 according to the time indicated on the packet.
  • For crunchier vegetables, omit additional cooking in step 3.
  • Substitute mint with parsley.
  • Add other ingredients to taste (e.g. sliced frankfurter sausages, sweetcorn, mozzarella cubes).
