Stuffed zucchini

Ingredients

  • 150 g Emmental cheese, cut into pieces (3 cm) (see Tips)
  • 3 zucchini (approx. 20 cm in length), cut into halves lengthways
  • 1 tbsp olive oil, plus extra for drizzling
  • ¼ tsp salt, plus extra to season
  • 1 - 2 pinches ground black pepper, to season
  • 500 g water
  • 1 brown onion (approx. 120 g), cut into halves
  • 100 g rindless bacon rashers, cut into pieces (approx. 1 cm)
  • 80 g cream cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 pinch grated nutmeg

Preparation

  1. Place cheese into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  2. Using a spoon, carefully remove seeds and soft flesh from zucchini, leaving a 5 mm edge all the way around. Transfer flesh into a bowl. Place zucchini shells into Varoma dish, drizzle with oil and season with salt and pepper. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 12 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove Varoma and set aside. Discard water.
  3. Place salt, 2 teaspoons of the olive oil and reserved zucchini flesh into mixing bowl and cook 3 min/100°C/speed 1 , without measuring cup. Place a sieve over a bowl, transfer zucchini mixture into it and set aside to drain. Discard any strained liquid.
  4. Without cleaning mixing bowl, place onion and remaining oil into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5.
  5. Add bacon and cook 8 min/100°C/speed 1, without measuring cup. Allow mixture to cool in mixing bowl for 10 minutes.
  6. Preheat oven to 200°C. Set aside a ceramic or glass baking dish (23 x 30 cm).
  7. Add cream cheese, egg, nutmeg, reserved zucchini flesh and 50 g of the reserved grated cheese to mixing bowl and combine 10 sec//speed 2. Place reserved zucchini skins into baking dish and fill with cream cheese mixture. Sprinkle with remaining grated cheese and bake for 20 minutes (200°C) or until golden brown. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

bowl, sieve, baking dish (30 x 23 x 5 cm), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Emmental cheese can be replaced with Swiss cheese.
