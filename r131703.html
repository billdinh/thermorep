Chocolate Quinoa Porridge

Ingredients

  • 180 g quinoa, rinsed
  • 380 g almond milk, plus extra for serving, if desired
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
  • 30 g dark chocolate, small pieces or callets
  • 1 Tbsp cocoa powder
  • 2 bananas, sliced, for serving
  • 100 g fresh raspberries, for serving
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
773 kJ / 185 kcal
Protein
5.1 g
Carbohydrates
26.7 g
Fat
6.4 g

Preparation

  1. Place quinoa, almond milk and salt in mixing bowl. Place simmering basket instead of measuring cup on mixing bowl lid then cook 15 min/95°C//speed 1.
  2. Add chocolate and cocoa then stir in with spatula. With measuring cup in place, cook 2 min/95°C//speed .
  3. Transfer to serving bowls and serve hot garnished with sliced bananas and raspberries, with extra milk if desired.
