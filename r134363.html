Lupini Cheese

Ingredients

  • 250 g lupini beans, cooked
  • 30 g olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 20 g lemon juice, freshly squeezed
  • 20 g dried instant yeast
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • 360 g water
  • 15 g agar-agar
  • 30 g cornflour

Preparation

  1. Place lupini beans, olive oil, lemon juice, yeast, salt, garlic powder and nutmeg in mixing bowl then mash 30 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then mash again 15 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place water, agar-agar and cornflour in mixing bowl then cook 6 min/100Â°C/speed 4. Meanwhile, line a small bowl with cling film then grease lightly.
  3. Add reserved lupini mixture and mix 30 sec/speed 4. Transfer to prepared bowl and allow to cool. Cover then refrigerate for approx. 2 hours or until set. Unmould just before serving.

Useful Items

small bowl, cling film, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Lupini beans are yellow in colour and a popular ingredient in Mediterranean countries as well as in Latin America. Fava beans are a good substitute if lupini beans are not available.
  • Use apple cider vinegar or rice vinegar instead of lemon juice.
  • Season with onion powder, paprika or herbs.
