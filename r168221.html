Stuffed pancakes (katayef with kashta cream)

Ingredients

Kashta cream

  • 1000 g milk
  • 150 g white bread, crust removed, cut into pieces
  • 100 g cornflour (starch)
  • 1 tbsp blossom water
  • 2 tbsp rose water

Pancakes

  • 600 g water
  • 15 g dried yeast
  • 3 tsp sugar
  • 330 g flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3 tsp baking soda
  • 3 tsp lemon juice, freshly squeezed
  • 10 g sunflower oil

Syrup

  • 300 g sugar
  • 100 g water
  • 1 tsp lemon juice, freshly squeezed
  • 2 tsp orange blossom water

Assembly

  • 110 g unsalted pistachio nuts, chopped

Preparation

Kashta cream

  1. Place milk into mixing bowl and heat 5 min/60°C/speed 1.
  2. Add bread and cornflour and cook 4 min/90°C/speed 2.
  3. Add blossom water and rose water and mix 1 min/90°C/speed 2.5. Transfer into a shallow bowl, cover with cling film and set aside to cool for 40 minutes. Refrigerate for 1 hour. Meanwhile, prepare and cook pancakes.

Pancakes

  1. Place water, dried yeast and sugar into mixing bowl and mix 2 min/37°C/speed 2.5.
  2. Add flour and salt and mix 20 sec/speed 6. Transfer into a bowl, cover with cling film and allow to rise for 1 hour.
  3. Just before frying, add baking soda and lemon juice to the bowl with the pancake batter and mix with spatula. To cook pancakes heat 1 tsp sunflower oil in a non-stick frying pan over medium heat. For each pancake (Ø 7-8 cm), using a small ladle or a half full Arabic coffee/espresso cup, pour batter into pan and cook until bubbles start bursting on pancake surface. Do not turn pancake over. Remove from stove when pores form on the surface of each pancake. Place the pancakes on baking paper and set aside to cool. Repeat with remaining batter.

Syrup

  1. Place sugar, water, lemon juice and blossom water into mixing bowl and heat 10 min/115°C/speed 2. Empty into a heat-resistant bowl and set aside to cool down.

Assembly

  1. Fill each pancake with 1 full tsp of kashta cream. Pinch two edges together in one side leaving other half side open like a horn. Sprinkle or cover cream on open side with chopped pistachios. Serve stuffed pancakes with syrup.

Useful Items

shallow bowl or shallow dish, cling film, small ladle, small cups, heat-resistant bowl, non-stick frying pan, refrigerator, stove
