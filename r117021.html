Seafood Salad with Pearl Barley

Ingredients

  • 15 g fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 5 leaves fresh basil
  • 30 g extra virgin olive oil, plus 2 Tbsp for dressing
  • 700 g water
  • 1 ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • 200 g pearl barley (pre-cooked for 12 mins)
  • 800 g raw mixed seafood
  • 100 g cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 100 g lamb's lettuce or other letttuce
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper

Preparation

  1. Place parsley in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place garlic, basil and olive oil in mixing bowl then heat 3 min/120°C/speed 1. Transfer dressing to a large salad bowl.
  3. Without washing mixing bowl, weigh in water and add salt. Insert simmering basket and weigh in cooked barley.
  4. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh in mixed seafood. Cover Varoma then steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove Varoma and set aside to cool. Meanwhile, remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and drain barley. Add cooked seafood and cooked barley to salad bowl.
  5. Place salad bowl on mixing bowl lid. Weigh in tomatoes and lettuce. Remove bowl from lid. Add reserved chopped parsley, oil, salt and pepper. Mix well then leave to cool in fridge for 1 hour. Remove a short time before serving at room temperature.

Useful Items

salad bowl, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • The salad also works well served with lemon juice as a dressing.
