Tomato Pie

Ingredients

Pastry

  • 150 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 80 g unsalted butter, cold, diced (1 cm)
  • 50 g water, ice cold
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt

Filling

  • 4 tomatoes, medium, cut in approx. 24 slices.
  • fine sea salt, for sprinkling
  • 375 g mozzarella cheese, cut in pieces
  • 140 g Spanish onions, quartered
  • 3 slices bacon, cut in pieces (1 cm)
  • ground black pepper, to taste
  • garlic powder, to taste
  • 2 Tbsp fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 230 g mayonnaise (not low fat - see tip)
  • 5 g fresh parsley leaves, chopped
  • ½ tsp Tabasco® sauce

Preparation

Pastry

  1. Place flour, butter, water and salt in mixing bowl then mix 15 sec/speed 4. Tip out and shape into a disc. Wrap in cling film and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  2. On a lightly-floured surface, roll out chilled pastry then use to line a loose-based fluted tart tin (Ø 24 cm), pressing in well. Trim edges with a knife then prick base with a fork. Cover with cling film and chill in fridge for 15 minutes. Meanwhile, preheat oven to 180°C.
  3. Line chilled pastry base with baking paper and fill with baking beans then blind bake for 15 minutes (180°C) until golden. Remove baking paper and baking beans then bake for a further 5 minutes (180°C). Set aside to cool slightly. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl and make filling.

Filling

  1. Place Varoma dish on a plate then insert Varoma tray. Lay tomato slices on Varoma tray and sprinkle with salt. Cover with paper towels then set aside for 30 minutes. Meanwhile, continue with recipe.
  2. Place mozzarella in mixing bowl and shred 7 sec//speed 5. Set scales to zero and remove 125 g to a bowl (scales will show -125 g). Transfer remainder to a separate bowl and set both aside.
  3. Place onions in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add bacon and sauté 8 min/110°C/speed 0.5. Meanwhile, preheat oven to 180°C..
  5. Sprinkle half reserved smaller bowl of shredded mozzarella over cooled pie base. Cover with half the tomato slices. Season to taste with pepper and garlic powder. Add half the basil then half the cooked bacon mixture. Repeat layers, starting with remaining mozzarella from small bowl.
  6. Place mayonnaise, parsley, Tabasco® and remaining shredded mozzarella in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 3. Spread evenly over pie. Bake for 30-40 minutes (180°C) until bubbling and light brown on top.
  7. Leave pie in tin to cool to room temperature before removing and slicing with a serrated knife.

Useful Items

baking paper, baking beans, rolling pin, cling film, paper towels, serrated knife, loose-based fluted tart tin (Ø 24 cm), oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Tomato pie uses simple fresh ingredients - juicy tomatoes and fresh herbs, layered in a flaky pie crust, with sweet onion and cheese. Top with a traditional mayonnaise and cheese mixture, and you've got a classic Southern tomato pie.
  • It is important not to use low fat mayonnaise in this recipe - the result may not be as desired.
  • Tomato pie can be served as a main dish with a garden salad, as a side on a veggie plate, or along with any main dish protein such as pork, beef, chicken or seafood.
