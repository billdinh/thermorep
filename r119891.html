Roasted carrot dip

Ingredients

  • 400 g carrots, cut into pieces (2 cm)
  • 30 g honey
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 1 orange, zested and juiced
  • 1 fennel bulb (approx. 130 g), fronds trimmed, cut into quarters
  • ½ tsp caraway seeds
  • ¼ tsp grated nutmeg
  • 2 tsp sweet paprika
  • 1 - 1 ½ tsp salt flakes, to taste
  • 60 g tahini
  • 80 g Greek yoghurt
  • 100 g water

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Line a baking tray (20 x 30 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh into it carrots, honey, oil, orange zest and juice, fennel, caraway seeds, nutmeg, paprika and salt then toss to combine. Transfer mixture onto prepared baking tray. Cover tray with aluminium foil and roast for 40 minutes (180°C), or until carrots are soft. Set vegetable mixture aside to cool for 10 minutes.
  3. Place vegetable mixture, tahini, yoghurt and water into mixing bowl and mix 20 sec/speed 6.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix 20 sec/speed 9, or until desired consistency is achieved.
  5. Transfer into a serving bowl and serve immediately or transfer into a sealable container and place into refrigerator until ready to serve.

Useful Items

baking tray (20 x 30 cm), baking paper, bowl, aluminium foil, serving bowl, sealable container, oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • You can replace the carrots with sweet potato.
  • The variety of honey you use will have a significant impact on flavour, so adjust honey to taste.
  • Serve dip with crackers or crudités, such as fresh carrot sticks.
  • This carrot dip can also be served as an accompaniment to barbecued meats.
