Strawberry BBQ Meatballs

Ingredients

Meatballs

  • 2 oz Parmesan cheese, cut into pieces
  • 15 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only, plus extra to garnish
  • 10 mint leaves
  • 4 oz yellow onions, cut into pieces
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 24 oz ground beef, broken into pieces
  • 1 oz panko
  • 1 large egg
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper

Strawberry BBQ Sauce

  • 3 oz yellow onions, cut into pieces
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 8 oz strawberries, hulled, halved
  • 4 oz ketchup
  • 2 oz water
  • 1 oz brown sugar
  • 1 tsp mustard powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 2 tsp vinegar
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce, to taste (optional)
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • &frac18; tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 chipotle chili, in adobo sauce, canned

Preparation

Meatballs

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line a baking sheet with parchment and set aside.
  2. Place Parmesan cheese, parsley and mint into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add onion and garlic and mix 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add ground beef, panko, egg, salt and pepper and mix 10 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix a further 5 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl.
  5. Shape 35 meatballs (approx. 1 oz ea.) and place 1 inch apart on prepared baking sheet. Bake for 25 minutes (350°F). Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl and make BBQ sauce.

Strawberry BBQ Sauce

  1. Place onion and garlic into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add strawberries, ketchup, water, brown sugar, mustard, paprika, vinegar, Worcestershire, salt and pepper and mix 3 sec/speed 5. Place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 15 min/212°F/speed 1.
  3. Add chipotle chili, insert measuring cup and blend 15 sec/speed 4-7, increasing speed gradually.
  4. Transfer meatballs to a serving bowl and cover in Strawberry BBQ Sauce. Garnish with chopped parsley and serve hot.

Useful Items

baking sheet, parchment paper, serving bowl, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • For a main dish, serve hot over steamed rice.
