Chocolate chip cookies

Ingredients

  • 140 g dark chocolate, cut into pieces (1-2 cm)
  • 130 g butter, cut into pieces
  • 100 g caster sugar
  • 100 g light brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tsp homemade vanilla sugar
  • 180 g plain flour
  • ½ tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 pinch salt
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
446 kJ / 107 kcal
Protein
1 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Line two baking trays with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place dark chocolate into mixing bowl and grate 3 sec/speed 6. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place butter, sugar, light brown sugar, egg and vanilla sugar or vanilla extract into mixing bowl and mix 2 min/speed 3.
  4. Add flour, bicarb soda, baking powder and salt and mix 40 sec/speed 4 .
  5. Add reserved grated chocolate and stir 30 sec/speed 2. Form dough with aid of 2 teaspoons into approx. 30 walnut-size balls and place them 5 cm apart onto prepared baking trays. Cookies will flatten as they bake. Bake one baking tray after another for 10-12 minutes (180°C) until cookies are golden brown. Allow to cool on a wire rack before storing in an airtight container or serving.

Useful Items

2 baking trays, baking paper, airtight container, wire rack, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Leftover cookie dough can be rolled into sausage shapes, wrapped in plastic wrap and refrigerated or stored in the freezer for later use.
  • In step 5, add approx. 30 g chopped nuts or raisins in addition to chocolate.
