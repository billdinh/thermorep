Baked Macaroni Cheese with Chorizo and Peppers

Ingredients

  • 225 g macaroni pasta
  • 1 slice white bread, quartered (approx. 25 g)
  • 250 g Cheddar cheese, cut in pieces
  • 30 g unsalted butter, diced
  • 40 g plain flour
  • 340 g semi-skimmed milk
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 100 g chorizo, diced
  • 10 g fresh basil leaves, roughly chopped
  • 70 g preserved red peppers, thinly sliced

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 170°C.
  2. Cook macaroni on hob according to packet instructions, drain then set aside. Meanwhile, continue with recipe.
  3. Place bread in mixing bowl and grate Turbo/1 sec/2-3 times until breadcrumbs. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place Cheddar in mixing bowl and grate 3 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  5. Place butter and flour in mixing bowl then chop 10 sec/speed 6.
  6. Add milk and heat 10 min/90°C/speed 2.
  7. Add reserved grated cheese, salt and pepper then stir 3 min/90°C/speed 3.
  8. Add chorizo, basil and peppers then heat 10 sec//speed 2.
  9. Transfer cooked macaroni to a baking dish, pour over sauce then stir to coat pasta. Cover with reserved breadcrumbs then bake for 15-20 minutes (170°C) until golden.
  10. Allow to rest for 5 minutes then serve hot.

Useful Items

saucepan, colander, baking dish, oven, hob

Hints & Tricks

  • Our bodies are unable to store a lot of carbohydrates, which are used for quick energy, so making sure you have a carbohydrate-rich meal the day before a run will help your muscles. Macaroni cheese is a great, easy option as the sauce can be made ahead and frozen. Defrost in the morning then reheat in the Thermomix®, add to freshly cooked pasta and bake for a delicious comforting meal.
