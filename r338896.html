Veggie Rolls

Ingredients

  • 70 g onions, halved
  • 2 Tbsp mixed fresh herbs (e.g. parsley, dill, thyme)
  • 100 g halved walnuts
  • 3 medium eggs
  • 150 g feta cheese, cut in pieces
  • 1 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 45 g breadcrumbs
  • 95 g oat flakes
  • 500 g puff pastry, ready-rolled (approx. 1.5 packets)
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • sesame seeds, for sprinkling
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
740 kJ / 177 kcal
Protein
5 g

12 g

12 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C and line a baking tray with baking paper.
  2. Place onions, walnuts and herbs in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add 3 eggs, soy sauce, feta, breadcrumbs and oat flakes then mix 20 sec/speed 5.
  4. Lay out puff pastry and cut into 9 rectangles (12 cm x 10 cm) . On the longer side of each rectangle, place a strip of filling (approx. 2 cm) wide, leaving a 1 cm border. Brush the free half of each rectangle with beaten egg. Roll up from the long side, starting with filling. Halve the rolls and place seam-side down on prepared baking tray. Brush tops of rolls with remaining beaten egg, slash three times and sprinkle with sesame seeds. Bake for 20 minutes (200°C) or until golden.
  5. Serve warm or at room temperature.

Useful Items

baking tray and baking paper, pastry brush, baking tray, oven
