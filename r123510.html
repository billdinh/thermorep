Tuna Nicoise with Raspberry Vinaigrette and Honey

Ingredients

Salad

  • 250 g water
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 6 medium eggs
  • 250 g fresh green beans, trimmed
  • 250 g courgettes
  • 250 g cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 200 g iceberg lettuce, cut in pieces
  • 100 g rocket leaves
  • 10 g fresh basil, leaves only
  • 300 g tinned tuna in oil, drained
  • 100 g green olives, pitted

Vinaigrette

  • 100 g olive oil
  • 50 g balsamic vinegar glaze, raspberry
  • 30 g honey
  • 1 lemon, thin peelings of skin and 30 g juice
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper

Preparation

Salad

  1. Place water and ½_tsp salt in mixing bowl then insert simmering basket with eggs inside. Place Varoma dish into position then weigh in green beans and season with remaining ½ tsp salt. Cover then steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 2. Meanwhile, cut courgettes into thin strips using a spiralizer (see tip) then transfer to a serving bowl.
  2. Remove Varoma then rinse green beans under cold running water and set aside. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula then cool eggs under cold running water. Peel cooled eggs and cut in half then set aside.
  3. Place serving bowl on mixing bowl lid. Add cherry tomatoes, lettuce, rocket leaves, basil, tuna, olives, reserved eggs and reserved cooled green beans to serving bowl. Remove serving bowl and prepare vinaigrette.

Vinaigrette

  1. Place all ingredients in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 10. Pour onto salad then chill in fridge for at least 30 minutes before serving.

Useful Items

serving bowl, spiralizer or julienne peeler , refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • The ingredients can be doubled to serve 12 as part of a buffet.
  • If you don't have a spiralizer, use a julienne peeler or peel courgette into ribbons with a standard peeler and slice thinly lengthways.
