Parmesan and Rosemary Buns

Ingredients

  • 50 g Parmesan cheese, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 1 sprig fresh rosemary, leaves only
  • 200 g water
  • 60 g milk
  • 1 Tbsp sugar
  • 1 ½ tsp dried instant yeast
  • 500 g strong white bread flour
  • 80 g butter, soft
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil, plus extra for greasing

Preparation

  1. Place Parmesan and rosemary in mixing bowl then grate 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place water, milk, sugar and yeast in mixing bowl then warm 3 min/37°C/speed 2.
  3. Add flour, butter, 40 g reserved Parmesan-rosemary mixture, egg and salt then knead 5 min/. Meanwhile, line 2 baking trays with baking paper and grease a large bowl.
  4. Transfer dough to prepared large bowl, cover with cling film and leave to rise in a warm place for 30 minutes.
  5. Divide dough into 8 balls, place on prepared baking trays and flatten slightly. Cover again and leave in a warm place for further 30 minutes. Towards end of resting time, preheat oven to 200°C.
  6. Brush bread rolls with 1 Tbsp olive oil and sprinkle with remaining 10 g reserved Parmesan-rosemary mixture. Bake buns for 15-20 minutes (200°C) until golden brown. Transfer to a wire rack and allow to cool before serving with your burger of choice.
