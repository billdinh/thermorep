Shepherd's pie

Ingredients

  • butter, for greasing
  • 1 carrot (approx. 170 g), cut into pieces
  • 80 g frozen green peas
  • 1 brown onion (approx. 180 g), cut into quarters
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 230 g button mushrooms, cut into quarters
  • 400 g lamb mince
  • 50 g stout beer
  • 1 tbsp meat stock paste
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tbsp cornflour
  • 1 tbsp water
  • 600 g mashed potato

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Grease a round deep-sided baking dish (22 cm) and set aside.
  2. Place carrot into mixing bowl and chop 3-5 sec/speed 5. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh green peas into it. Transfer chopped carrot into the bowl with peas and set aside.
  3. Place brown onion into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add olive oil and sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  5. Add button mushrooms and sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  6. Add lamb mince and cook 5 min/100°C//speed 1.
  7. Add stout beer, meat stock paste and tomato paste and cook 5 min/100°C//speed 1.
  8. In a small bowl combine the cornflour and water, then add to mixing bowl. Add reserved carrot and peas and combine 10 sec/speed 1.
  9. Pour into prepared baking dish and top with mashed potato. Bake for 25 - 30 minutes (180°C), or until potato is lightly browned.
  10. Allow to rest for 5 minutes prior to serving.

Useful Items

round deep-sided baking dish (22 cm), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • See Meat stock paste recipe to make it fresh in your Thermomix. Vegetable stock paste can also be used.
  • Make this recipe with beef instead of lamb for a cottage pie.
  • Mince your own meat in the Thermomix to use in this recipe.
