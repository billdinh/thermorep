Jerk Chicken with Sweet Potato Mash and Vegetables

Ingredients

Jerk Chicken

  • 4 chicken breasts, skinless (approx. 140 g each)
  • 10 g fresh root ginger, peeled, cut in round slices (2 mm)
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 - 2 scotch bonnet chilli, halved, deseeded, to taste
  • 1 lime, thin peelings of skin and 20 g juice
  • 1 orange, thin peelings of skin and 20 g juice
  • 2 tsp soft dark brown sugar
  • 2 tsp black peppercorns
  • 1 tsp whole allspice berries
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • 8 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves only
  • 5 g fresh coriander, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 4 spring onions, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 20 g cider vinegar
  • 20 g olive oil

Mash and Vegetables

  • 700 g water
  • 600 g sweet potatoes, peeled, diced (3 cm)
  • 400 g fine green beans, trimmed
  • 400 g frozen peas, defrosted
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 20 g fresh root ginger, peeled, cut in round slices (2 mm)
  • 10 g olive oil
  • 8 - 10 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves only, to taste
  • 1 ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper

Preparation

Jerk Chicken

  1. Place a large bowl on mixing bowl lid, weigh in chicken then set aside.
  2. Place ginger, garlic, chilli, lime peel, orange peel, sugar, peppercorns, allspice, salt, cinnamon and nutmeg in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add thyme, coriander and spring onions then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then chop again 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add orange juice, lime juice, vinegar and oil then mix 5 sec/speed 4. Pour over reserved chicken and stir to coat. Cover with cling film and place in fridge for at least 2 hours or overnight. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl.
  5. Towards the end of marinating time, preheat oven to 180°C and line a roasting tin with foil.
  6. Transfer marinated chicken breasts and marinade to prepared tin and bake for 25-30 minutes (180°C) until cooked thoroughly. Meanwhile, make mash.

Mash and Vegetables

  1. Place water and sweet potatoes in mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh in green beans, making sure that some holes remain uncovered for steam to circulate. Cover Varoma then cook 10 min/Varoma//speed 1.
  2. Remove Varoma lid, insert Varoma tray and weigh peas onto it. Cover Varoma then cook 5 min/Varoma//speed 1. Set covered Varoma aside. Drain sweet potatoes and set aside. Rinse and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Place garlic, ginger and oil in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  4. Add reserved cooked sweet potatoes, thyme, salt and pepper then mash 10 sec/speed 5. Serve jerk chicken hot with peas, beans and sweet potato mash.

Useful Items

cling film, large bowl, roasting tin, aluminium foil, refrigerator, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Sweet potatoes have a lower GI than baking potatoes. What's more, a balance of protein, carbohydrates and vegetables brings the GI of a meal as a whole down. The portion of mash here is smaller than usual, but there are plenty of green vegetables to have alongside the chicken.
