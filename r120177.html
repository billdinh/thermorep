Mediterranean carrot fritters

Ingredients

Red onion and yoghurt dip

  • ½ red onion, cut into quarters
  • 6 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only
  • 150 g natural yoghurt

Carrot fritters

  • 3 slices wholegrain bread, torn into small pieces
  • 6 sprigs fresh mint, leaves only
  • 6 sprigs fresh dill, leaves only, plus extra for serving
  • 500 g water
  • 500 g carrots, cut into pieces (4 cm)
  • 6 dried apricots
  • ½ tsp salt, to taste
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper, to taste
  • 4 spring onions/shallots, cut into thin slices
  • 1 tbsp sultanas
  • 3 tbsp pine nuts
  • 1 tsp dried chilli flakes
  • 1 egg
  • grapeseed oil, for frying

Preparation

Red onion and yoghurt dip

  1. Place onion and coriander into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7.
  2. Add yoghurt and mix 5 sec/speed 3. Transfer into a serving bowl and cover with plastic wrap. Place into refrigerator until ready to serve. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Carrot fritters

  1. Place bread, mint and dill into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a small bowl and set aside.
  2. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh 250 g of the carrots into it. Secure Varoma lid and steam 12 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove Varoma and empty mixing bowl.
  3. Place apricots, salt, pepper, steamed carrots and remaining 250 g carrots into mixing bowl and mix 3 sec/speed 7.
  4. Add spring onions/shallots, sultanas, pine nuts, chilli flakes, egg and reserved breadcrumb mixture and mix 20 sec/speed 3, or until a soft, slightly damp mixture forms.
  5. Using hands, roll 1 tablespoon of mixture into a small rissole shape and place onto a plate. Continue with remaining mixture (makes approx. 25 patties). Place into refrigerator for 30 minutes.
  6. Place a large frying pan over medium-high heat and add just enough oil to coat base of pan. Working in batches, place patties into pan and cook for approx. 4-5 minutes each side, or until golden brown and cooked through.
  7. Place fritters onto a serving platter with red onion and yoghurt dip. Sprinkle dip with extra dill leaves before serving.

Useful Items

serving bowl, plastic wrap, small bowl , large frying pan, serving platter, refrigerator, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • You can make these fritters in advance - they are great for packed lunches, picnics and parties.
