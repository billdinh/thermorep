Four Cheese Pizza

Ingredients

Dough

  • 50 g water
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 tsp dried instant yeast
  • oil, for greasing
  • 180 g buttermilk
  • 400 g strong white bread flour, plus extra for flouring
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt

Topping

  • 50 g Parmesan cheese, cut in pieces (3 cm)
  • 50 g Fontina cheese, cut in pieces (3 cm)
  • 80 g mozzarella, drained, cut in pieces
  • 80 g gorgonzola, cut in pieces (5 mm)
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper

Preparation

Dough

  1. Place water, sugar and yeast in mixing bowl then warm 2 min/37°C/speed 2. Meanwhile, grease a large bowl with oil.
  2. Add buttermilk, flour and salt then knead 3 min/. Transfer dough to prepared bowl, cover with cling film then set aside to rise in a warm place until doubled in size (approx. 1-2 hours). Clean mixing bowl and prepare topping.

Topping

  1. Place Parmesan and Fontina cheese in mixing bowl then grate 8 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place mozzarella in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer to bowl with other cheeses then set aside.
  3. Towards end of rising time, preheat oven to 220°C and line a large baking tray with baking paper. Tip dough out onto a floured work surface and roll out to size of baking tray (approx. 1 cm deep). Transfer pizza base to prepared baking tray.
  4. Scatter reserved chopped cheeses and gorgonzola over dough then sprinkle with pepper. Bake for 15-20 minutes (220°C) until bubbling and golden. Serve pizza hot, cut in slices.

Useful Items

large bowl, cling film, rolling pin, large baking tray, baking paper, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • To make a closed cheese pizza, divide dough in half and roll out 2 thin rectangles (one slightly larger than then the other). Place largest piece in baking tray, spread cheese over then cover with second piece of dough. Cook as directed in recipe.     
  • Use cheeses of your choice as alternatives to the ones listed in the recipe.
  • Try adding herbs (e.g. thyme or marjoram).
  • This is a traditional Italian four cheese pizza, made without a tomato sauce.
