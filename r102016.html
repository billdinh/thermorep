Chocolate Pancakes

Ingredients

Batter

  • 110 g cornflour
  • 50 g caster sugar
  • 20 g cocoa powder
  • 2 large eggs
  • 250 g milk

Chocolate Sauce

  • 70 g milk
  • 100 g dark chocolate, (at least 70%) small pieces or callets
  • 10 - 20 g butter, for frying

Preparation

Batter

  1. Place cornflour, sugar and cocoa in bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add eggs and milk then mix 30 sec/speed 3. Scrape down sides and base of mixing bowl with spatula then mix again 30 sec/speed 3. Transfer batter to a bowl and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.

Chocolate Sauce

  1. Place milk and chocolate in mixing bowl then melt 3 min/60Â°C/speed 0.5.
  2. Heat a knob of butter in a large pancake or frying pan over a medium heat. Pour a small ladle of batter into the centre then spread out thinly. Cook for 1-2 minutes on each side then transfer to a plate. Repeat, adding more butter when necessary until all batter is used. Serve pancakes warm with chocolate sauce.
