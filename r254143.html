Chocolate Peanut Butter Pie

Ingredients

Chocolate Cookie Pie Crust

  • 4 oz unsalted butter, cut into pieces
  • 12 oz chocolate sandwich cookies with creamy centers

Filling

  • 9 oz heavy whipping cream
  • 8 oz cream cheese, room temperature, cut into pieces
  • 12 oz creamy peanut butter
  • 3 oz confectioners sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Topping

  • 6 oz heavy whipping cream
  • 6 oz bittersweet chocolate, broken into pieces
  • 2 oz creamy peanut butter
  • peanuts, to garnish

Preparation

Chocolate Cookie Pie Crust

  1. Place butter into mixing bowl and melt 2 min/212째F/speed 1.
  2. Add cookies and crush 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and crush a further 3 sec/speed 7.
  3. Transfer crumb mixture into a tart pan and press into bottom and up the sides to form a crust. Chill 20 minutes in refrigerator while preheating oven to 350째F. Line pie crust with parchment and baking weights and bake 10 minutes (350째F). Remove parchment and baking weights, transfer to a wire rack and allow to cool. Clean mixing bowl.

Filling

  1. Insert butterfly whisk. Place whipping cream into mixing bowl and without setting a time whip speed 3, until stiff peaks form, watching carefully to avoid overwhipping. Remove butterfly whisk. Transfer whipped cream into a large bowl and set aside.
  2. Place cream cheese, peanut butter, confectioners sugar and vanilla into mixing bowl and mix 5 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix a further 5 sec/speed 4. Transfer to bowl with reserved whipped cream and gently fold in with spatula.
  3. Transfer peanut butter-cream mixture into cooled pie crust and refrigerate until completely set (approx. 2-4 hr.).

Topping

  1. Place whipping cream into mixing bowl and heat 3 min/195째F/speed 3.
  2. Add chocolate and peanut butter and mix 30 sec/speed 3. Refrigerate 20 minutes. Spread topping over chilled pie with spatula and refrigerate until set (approx. 1 hr.). Garnish with peanuts and serve immediately.
