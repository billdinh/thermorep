Cappuccino Cake

Ingredients

Coffee Cakes

  • 220 g unsalted butter, soft, plus extra for greasing
  • 220 g brown sugar
  • 4 medium eggs
  • 50 g strong brewed coffee, cold
  • 40 g liquid caramel
  • 220 g self-raising flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Cappuccino Buttercream Filling

  • 300 g caster sugar
  • 150 g unsalted butter, soft
  • 20 g milk
  • 95 g strong brewed coffee, cold
  • 1 Tbsp cocoa powder, for dusting

Preparation

Coffee Cakes

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Line bases of 3 sandwich cake tins (Ø 18 cm) with baking paper, then grease with butter.
  2. Place butter and brown sugar in mixing bowl then cream 3 min/speed 4.
  3. Insert butterfly whisk. Add eggs and whisk 4 min/speed 4.
  4. Around butterfly whisk, add coffee, liquid caramel, flour and baking powder then mix 5 sec/speed 3.5.
  5. Remove butterfly whisk and carefully finish stirring by hand with spatula, if necessary.
  6. Divide cake batter between prepared tins (approx. 320 g per tin, see tip). Place cake tins in oven, two on the top rack and one on the middle rack and bake for 18-20 minutes (180°C) until a skewer inserted in centre comes out clean. About halfway through baking, rotate the cake from the middle rack to the top rack and move one from the top to the middle. Leave cakes to cool for 5 minutes before removing from tins. Leave to cool completely (approx. 50 minutes) on a wire rack before filling (see tip). Meanwhile, clean and dry mixing bowl.

Cappuccino Buttercream Filling

  1. Place caster sugar in mixing bowl and grind 20 sec/speed 10.
  2. Add butter, milk and 35 g coffee then beat 5 min/speed 4.
  3. Place one coffee cake on a serving plate, brush with 20 g coffee and spread with ⅓ reserved cappuccino buttercream, place second cake on top, brush with 20 g coffee and spread with ⅓ reserved cappuccino buttercream. Place last cake on top, brush with remaining 20 g coffee and spread with remaining cappuccino buttercream. Dust with cocoa powder before serving.

Useful Items

sandwich cake tins (Ø 18 cm), baking paper, wire rack, pastry brush, skewer, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • This cake is lovely served with iced coffee. Make a rich iced coffee, finished with some liquid caramel, and some cream.
  • You can use the built-in Thermomix scales to measure out the exact quantity of cake batter for each cake tin. At the end of the recipe, turn scales on, lift out mixing bowl and pour some cake batter into the prepared tin. When you return the mixing bowl to the base, it will show a minus figure indicating the weight removed (- 320 g).
  • To make the cake using one tin, choose a deep cake tin (Ø 18 cm), and pour in all the batter. Bake for 35-40 minutes in a preheated oven (180°C), and leave to cool for 10 minutes before removing from cake tin. Cool completely on a wire rack, and simply slice in three horizontally before filling.
  • The coffee cake can be made in advance: keep in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 3 days, or keep for up to 1 month in the freezer.
