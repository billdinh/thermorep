Curry laksa

Ingredients

  • 150 g dried rice noodles
  • 3 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 eschalot
  • 1 - 2 fresh chillies, trimmed and cut into halves (see Tips)
  • 4 sprigs fresh coriander, roots and stalks (see Tips)
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 jar laksa paste (approx. 220 g - see Tips)
  • 2 tsp Chicken stock paste (see Tips)
  • 400 g water
  • 4 whole eggs
  • 6 stalks baby bok choy, leaves and stalks separated and cut into pieces (4-5 cm)
  • 200 - 250 g firm white fish fillets, cut into pieces (4 cm)
  • 400 g coconut milk
  • 2 fresh Kaffir lime leaves
  • 2 skinless chicken thigh fillets, cut into pieces (4 cm)
  • boiling water
  • 2 - 4 tsp fish sauce, plus extra to taste
  • 2 - 4 tsp lime juice, plus extra to taste
  • 200 - 250 g medium raw prawns, peeled and deveined
  • 2 spring onions/shallots, cut into thin slices for garnishing
  • 2 - 4 tbsp fried shallots, for garnishing
  • 100 g bean sprouts, for garnishing
  • fresh coriander leaves, for garnishing
  • 1 lime, cut into quarters, to serve

Preparation

  1. Place a large bowl onto mixing bowl lid, weigh noodles into it and set aside.
  2. Place ginger, garlic, eschalot, chillies and coriander root and stalks into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add oil and laksa paste and cook 3 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  4. Add stock paste and water. Insert simmering basket, place whole eggs into it and cook 4 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  5. Place Varoma into position and place bok choy into it. Insert Varoma tray, weigh fish onto it, secure Varoma lid and steam 10 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove Varoma. Transfer fish and bok choy into a thermal serving bowl or another large bowl and cover to keep warm. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and place eggs into a bowl of cold water to cease cooking. Once cool enough to handle, peel eggs and cut into halves lengthways.
  6. Add coconut milk, Kaffir lime leaves and chicken to mixing bowl and cook 10 min/90°C//speed 1. Meanwhile, cover reserved noodles with boiling water and set aside to cook as per packet instructions. Drain and transfer noodles into thermal serving bowl or other large bowl with fish and bok choy and cover to keep warm.
  7. Add fish sauce, lime juice and prawns to mixing bowl and cook 3-5 min/90°C//speed 1, until all ingredients are cooked.
  8. Divide noodles between 4 bowls. Using a slotted spoon divide prawns, chicken, fish and bok choy between bowls. Adjust seasoning of soup by adding extra lime juice and fish sauce to taste, then top up each bowl with laksa. Garnish with spring onions/shallots, fried shallots, bean sprouts, coriander leaves, lime quarters and reserved peeled eggs to serve.

Useful Items

large bowl, thermal serving bowl or other large bowl, bowl, fine mesh sieve, serving bowls, slotted spoon

Hints & Tricks

  • Please refer to laksa paste label instructions and either add more or less laksa paste based on recommended quantities on the label (we used 1 jar (220 g) to approx. 800 g liquid).
  • Depending on the brand and type of laksa paste used, adjust the amount of chillies used to suit your taste.
  • Please refer to The Basic Cookbook or Everyday Cookbook for the Chicken stock paste recipe.
  • Clean and scrape down the roots of your coriander with a spoon to remove the tougher, more fibrous bits on the outside of the roots, before adding it to the mixing bowl.
