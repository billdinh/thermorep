Miso salmon broth

Ingredients

  • 100 g dried instant noodles (see Tip)
  • boiling water, for soaking
  • 50 g shiro (white) miso paste, plus extra 2 teaspoons
  • 50 g sake
  • 50 g mirin
  • 40 g brown sugar
  • 20 g peanut oil
  • 1 garlic clove, cut into thin slices
  • 4 boneless skinless salmon fillets (approx. 800 g total)
  • 800 g hot water
  • 100 g fresh shiitake mushrooms, cut into thin slices (5 mm)
  • 20 g soy sauce
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 150 g bok choy, trimmed and cut into pieces (4 cm) with stalks separated
  • 2 spring onions/shallots, trimmed and thinly sliced, for garnishing
  • black sesame seeds, to serve (optional)

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh noodles into it. Cover with boiling water and set aside.
  2. Place 50 g of the miso paste, sake, mirin and brown sugar into mixing bowl and cook 4 min/120°C/speed 2. Transfer glaze into a ceramic dish and set aside to cool. Do not clean mixing bowl.
  3. Preheat oven to 200°C. Line a baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with aluminium foil and set aside.
  4. Place oil and garlic into mixing bowl and cook 1 min/100°C/speed 1. Meanwhile, place salmon into reserved glaze and turn to coat.
  5. Drain noodles using simmering basket. Add drained noodles, hot water, mushrooms, soy sauce, salt, pepper and remaining 2 teaspoons miso paste to mixing bowl and cook 3 min/100°C//speed 1. Meanwhile, transfer salmon onto prepared baking tray and bake for 7 minutes (200°C). Set remaining glaze aside.
  6. Add bok choy to mixing bowl and cook 4 min/100°C//speed 1.
  7. Remove salmon from oven. Turn oven setting to grill and carefully move oven rack to upper part of oven. Brush salmon with remaining reserved glaze and place under pre-heated grill for 2-3 minutes, until surface blackens and salmon is cooked to your liking. Flake salmon into bite-size pieces.
  8. Divide soup between serving bowls and top with salmon. Garnish with spring onions/shallots and sprinkle with black sesame seeds (optional) to serve.

Useful Items

bowl, ceramic dish, baking tray (30 x 40 cm), aluminium foil, serving bowls, pastry brush, oven, oven grill

Hints & Tricks

  • We used a dried sweet potato noodle for this recipe.
