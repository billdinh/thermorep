Turkey rolls with gorgonzola

Ingredients

  • 10 turkey escalopes
  • 1 shallot
  • 40 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 200 g fresh baby spinach, washed and dried
  • 250 g Gorgonzola or other type of blue cheese (e.g. Roquefort, Stilton), cut into pieces
  • 700 g water
  • 150 g milk
  • 20 g plain flour
  • 1 teaspoon fine salt
  • 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper

Preparation

  1. Place turkey escalopes between two sheets of heat-resistant cling film or baking paper and flatten with a meat mallet to a height of ½ cm.
  2. Place shallot into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add olive oil and sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  4. Add spinach and cook 6 min/100°C/speed 1. Strain spinach well through simmering basket.
  5. Remove cling film or baking paper from turkey escalopes and top with strained spinach. Distribute 100 g Gorgonzola evenly on escalopes. Use heat-resistant cling film or baking paper to roll escalopes up tightly and close rolls to form parcels.
  6. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position, arrange turkey rolls into Varoma dish and Varoma tray and steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 1. Set Varoma aside and empty mixing bowl.
  7. Place remaining Gorgonzola, milk, flour, salt and pepper into mixing bowl and cook 10 min/90°C/speed 4.
  8. Remove cling film or baking paper and arrange rolls on individual plates, drizzle with Gorgonzola sauce and serve immediately.

Useful Items

meat mallet or rolling pin, heat-resistant cling film or baking paper
