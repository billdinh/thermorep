Seedy Almond Low Carb Bread

Ingredients

  • non-stick cooking spray
  • 7 oz almonds
  • 3 ½ oz quick cooking oats, gluten free
  • 1 oz flaxseeds
  • 2 oz sesame seeds
  • 2 oz pumpkin seeds
  • 6 oz Greek yogurt
  • 1 oz extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 oz honey
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • milk, to brush
  • mixed seeds, e.g. flax, sesame and pumpkin seeds
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1038 kJ / 248 kcal
Protein
9 g
Carbohydrates
16 g
Fat
18 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven 350°F. Grease a loaf pan (8 in. x 4 in.) with nonstick cooking spray and set aside.
  2. Place almonds and oats into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 10.
  3. Add flaxseeds, sesame seeds, pumpkin seeds, yogurt, olive oil, honey, eggs, baking powder, baking soda and salt and mix 20 sec/speed 4. Pour batter into prepared loaf pan, brush top with milk and sprinkle with seed mix. Bake for 50 minutes (350°F). Allow to cool in pan 10 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool. Slice and serve.

Useful Items

loaf pan (8 in. x 4 in.), wire rack, oven
