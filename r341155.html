Fruit and yoghurt pizza

Ingredients

  • 20 g raw peanuts
  • 1 tbsp coconut oil
  • 50 g rolled oats
  • 10 g coconut flakes
  • 100 g mixed dried fruit
  • 20 g pecans
  • 1 pinch salt
  • Â½ tsp natural vanilla extract
  • 3 - 4 tbsp coconut yoghurt (see Tip)
  • seasonal fruit of choice, to decorate

Preparation

  1. Place peanuts, coconut oil and 20 g of the oats into mixing bowl and mill 30 sec/speed 8.
  2. Add coconut flakes, dried fruit, pecans, salt, vanilla and remaining 30 g oats and pulse Turbo/1 sec/1-2 times, until a rough dough forms. Transfer mixture onto a plate and shape into a disc (20 cm, approx. 5 mm thickness). Cover with plastic wrap and place into refrigerator until firm (approx. 30 minutes).
  3. Spread coconut yoghurt over chilled pizza base. Top with seasonal fruit just before serving.

Useful Items

plate, plastic wrap, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks
