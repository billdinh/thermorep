Passionfruit and macadamia truffles

Ingredients

  • 150 g macadamia nuts
  • 230 g dark chocolate (70% cocoa), broken into pieces
  • 4 passionfruits, pulp strained (approx. 60 g after seeds discarded - see Tips)
  • 100 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 30 g unsalted butter
  • ½ tsp vanilla bean paste
  • 300 g white chocolate, broken into pieces
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
645.6 kJ / 153.7 kcal

1.9 g

Preparation

  1. Place macadamias into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place dark chocolate into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 10.
  3. Add passionfruit, cream, butter and vanilla and cook 3 min/60°C/speed 2. Transfer into a bowl and set aside to cool (approx. 30 minutes), then cover with plastic wrap and place ganache into freezer for 2 hours or until firm. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Roll 1 teaspoon of the passionfruit ganache into a ball, then roll in reserved chopped macadamias (makes approx. 30 truffles). Transfer onto a plate and place into freezer to firm (approx. 30-60 minutes).
  5. Place white chocolate into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 8, then heat 3 min/80°C/speed 1 or until melted. Transfer into a shallow bowl. Remove truffles from freezer. Dip into melted chocolate to coat completely, then place back onto plate and return to refrigerator to set. Remove truffles from refrigerator just before serving.

Useful Items

bowl, shallow bowl, plastic wrap, plate, freezer, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • You can use tinned passionfruit pulp if fresh ones are not in season. We recommend straining passionfruit to remove seeds.
  • You can store truffles in sealed container in the refrigerator for up to 1 week.
