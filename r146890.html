Broccoli And Blue Cheese Soup

Ingredients

Macadamia and Parmesan Biscuits

  • 70 g Parmesan cheese, cut in pieces (3 cm)
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary, leaves only
  • 70 g macadamia nuts, roasted, salted
  • 50 g butter, softened
  • 100 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • &frac18; tsp ground black pepper, to taste

Broccoli and Blue Cheese Soup

  • 50 g cheddar cheese, cut in pieces (3 cm)
  • 250 g broccoli, cut in florets
  • 300 g water
  • 1 Tbsp vegetable stock paste (see tips)
  • 300 g whole milk
  • 1 Tbsp plain flour
  • 50 - 60 g blue cheese, crumbled
  • 1 - 2 pinches ground black pepper, to serve

Preparation

Macadamia and Parmesan Biscuits

  1. Preheat oven to 180째C. Line a baking tray with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place Parmesan and rosemary in mixing bowl then grate 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place macadamias in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 6.
  4. Add butter, flour, pepper and reserved Parmesan mixture then blend 7 sec/speed 7.
  5. Transfer dough onto a floured work surface and knead lightly.
  6. Roll out dough and cut into 20 rounds using a cookie cutter.
  7. Place rounds onto prepared baking tray and bake for 8 minutes (180째C), or until biscuits are lightly golden brown. Set aside to cool. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Broccoli and Blue Cheese Soup

  1. Place cheddar in mixing bowl and grate 3 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  2. Place broccoli in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add water and stock paste then cook 12 min/90째C/speed 1.
  4. Add milk and flour then blend 15 sec/speed 6.
  5. Add blue cheese and reserved grated cheddar cheese then cook 3 min 30 sec/90째C/speed 1. Blend 10 sec/speed 4, increasing speed gradually to speed 8.
  6. Season with pepper and serve with macadamia and Parmesan biscuits.
