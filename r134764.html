Decadent Chocolate and Coconut Cups

Ingredients

Chocolate Cups

  • 150 g dark chocolate (70% cocoa solids), in small pieces or callets

Chocolate Mousse

  • 180 g dark chocolate (70% cocoa solids), in small pieces or callets
  • 20 g unsalted butter
  • 3 egg yolks, from medium eggs
  • 3 egg whites, from medium eggs
  • 200 g whipping cream

Coconut Filling

  • 130 g flaked coconut
  • 170 g condensed milk
  • 1 mandarin, finely grated zest only, plus extra for serving

Preparation

Chocolate Cups

  1. Place 10 silicone cupcake moulds on a large baking tray.
  2. Place chocolate in mixing bowl and melt 5 min/50°C/speed 1.
  3. Using a pastry brush, paint silicone moulds with melted chocolate ensuring bottom of each case is slightly thicker than the sides. Place baking tray with moulds in the fridge until ready to use.

Chocolate Mousse

  1. Place chocolate and butter in mixing bowl then melt 5 min/50°C/speed 1.
  2. Add egg yolks and mix 10 sec/speed 2. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Thoroughly clean and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg whites in mixing bowl and, without measuring cup, whip 4 min/37°C/speed 3.5. Transfer to a large bowl and set aside.
  4. Without cleaning mixing bowl and with butterfly whisk still in place, place cream in mixing bowl and, without setting a time, whip until firm speed 3.5, watching carefully to avoid over-whipping.
  5. Add reserved chocolate mixture and mix 3 sec/speed 3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then mix again 3 sec/speed 3.
  6. Take a large spoonful of the chocolate mixture and add to reserved egg whites, then fold through to loosen mixture. Gently fold in remaining chocolate mixture until just combined. Fill each chocolate cup almost to the top with mousse then return to fridge to chill for 15 minutes. Remove butterfly whisk. Clean mixing bowl.

Coconut Filling

  1. Place coconut, condensed milk and mandarin zest in mixing bowl then mix 7 sec/speed 3. Spread 1 Tbsp coconut mixture on top of chocolate mousse. Carefully remove silicone moulds and place cups in fridge to set for a further 10-15 minutes.
  2. Garnish with mandarin zest before serving.

Useful Items

pastry brush, large bowl, silicone cupcake moulds, baking tray, refrigerator
