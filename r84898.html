Stuffed Tomatoes with Rice and Cashew & Basil Sauce

Ingredients

Stuffed Tomatoes and Rice

  • 4 beef tomatoes (approx. 1000 g)
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 50 g carrots, cut in pieces
  • 50 g red peppers, cut in pieces
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 150 g beef mince
  • 1 ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 tsp sweet paprika
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • ½ tsp dried oregano
  • ½ tsp dried thyme
  • 20 g breadcrumbs
  • 125 g mozzarella, cut in slices (5 mm)
  • 1 vegetable stock cube (for 0.5 l)
  • 160 g rice
  • 1200 g water

Cashew and Basil Sauce

  • 100 g cashew nuts
  • 20 g fresh basil
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 2 pinches pepper
  • ½ tsp sugar
  • 10 g lemon juice

Preparation

Stuffed Tomatoes and Rice

  1. Remove tops of tomatoes and scoop out flesh, setting tops, hollow tomatoes and flesh aside.
  2. Place onion, garlic, carrots and red pepper in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add olive oil and sauté 4 min/Varoma//speed 1.
  4. Add mince, 1 tsp salt, paprika, pepper, oregano and thyme. Place simmering basket instead of measuring cup on mixing bowl lid then cook 5 min/Varoma//speed 1.
  5. Add breadcrumbs and mix 5 sec//speed 3.
  6. Stuff reserved tomatoes with prepared filling, and top each with two slices of mozzarella. Cover with reserved tops of tomatoes then place in Varoma dish.
  7. Place ½ tsp salt and stock cube in mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket, weigh rice in, then pour water over rice. Place covered Varoma into position and steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 1. Set Varoma and simmering basket aside and keep rice and tomatoes warm. Empty mixing bowl, reserving steaming liquid.

Cashew and Basil Sauce

  1. Place cashew nuts, 350 g tomato flesh and 150 g steaming liquid in mixing bowl then cook 4 min/100°C/speed 1.
  2. Add basil, lemon juice,  salt, ground pepper and sugar then blend 45 sec/speed 4 - 9, increasing speed gradually. Serve stuffed tomatoes and rice with sauce poured over.
