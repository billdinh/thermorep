Decadent Dark Chocolate Brownies

Ingredients

  • 230 g unsalted butter, cut into pieces, plus extra for greasing
  • 440 g Rapadura sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 70 g cocoa powder, plus extra for dusting
  • 100 g plain flour
  • 200 g dark chocolate (55% cocoa solids), broken into pieces
  • 1 pinch sea salt
  • 4 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 tbsp natural vanilla extract

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C. Grease and line a square cake tin (20 cm) and set aside.
  2. Place sugar in mixing bowl and mill 3-4 sec/speed 9.
  3. Add baking powder, cocoa and flour then mix 5 sec/speed 10. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place chocolate in mixing bowl and grate 2 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Chocolate should be roughly chopped into pieces (approx. 1 cm). If necessary, grate for a further 1-2 sec/speed 5. Transfer into a separate bowl and set aside.
  5. Place salt and 230 g butter in mixing bowl then melt 2-3 min/60°C/speed 2 , or until butter has melted. Allow mixture to cool to 37°C in mixing bowl.
  6. Add reserved cocoa mixture and eggs then mix 30 sec/speed 3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Mixture should be well combined. If necessary, mix for a further 5 sec/speed 3.
  7. Add grated chocolate and vanilla extract and mix 10-20 sec//speed 3 with aid of spatula.
  8. Pour mixture into prepared tin and use spatula to evenly spread batter, then bake for 65 minutes (160°C) or until a wooden skewer inserted into centre of the brownie comes out clean.
  9. Allow to cool completely in tin (approx. 30 minutes). Dust with cocoa powder before slicing and serving.

Useful Items

baking paper, square cake tin (20 cm), wooden skewer, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • To check that mixture has reached 37°C in step 5, remove mixing bowl from Thermomix base and set aside to cool. When you return the mixing bowl to Thermomix base the lights will indicate the current temperature of the mixture in the mixing bowl.
  • This recipe freezes well.
