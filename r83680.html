Sausage Meatballs with Butternut Squash & Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 15 g fresh parsley leaves
  • 500 g vegetable stock
  • 1 tsp oil
  • 550 g potatoes, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 600 g butternut squash, cut in cubes (1 cm)
  • 1 ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 3 ½ tsp sweet paprika
  • 500 g sausagemeat
  • 100 g cream
  • 20 g cornflour (starch)
  • 2 pinches ground nutmeg

Preparation

  1. Place parsley in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place stock and oil in mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket and weigh in potatoes.
  3. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh in butternut squash, season with ¼ tsp salt and ¼ tsp pepper, cover Varoma and steam 17 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  4. Meanwhile on a plate mix paprika, ¼ tsp pepper and 1 tsp salt. Divide sausagemeat into 28 walnut-sized balls (Ø 3 cm), roll in prepared mix of spices, then place on Varoma tray. Insert Varoma tray, place Varoma into position and steam 12 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  5. Set Varoma and simmering basket aside and keep warm. Empty mixing bowl, reserving steaming liquid.
  6. Place 400 g steaming liquid (top up with water if necessary), cream, cornflour, nutmeg and prepared parsley in mixing bowl then mix 5 sec/speed 5. Cook 4 min/100°C/speed 2. Serve hot sauce over potatoes, butternut squash and meatballs.
