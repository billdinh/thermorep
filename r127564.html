Oriental beef stew with pearl couscous

Ingredients

  • 4 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only
  • 4 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 150 g onion (1 onion), cut into halves
  • 40 g olive oil
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • 2 pinches saffron powder
  • ¼ tsp smoked paprika powder
  • 1 - 2 pinches ground nutmeg
  • 800 g beef topside steak, trimmed and cut into cubes (3 cm)
  • 1 - 2 tbsp vegetable stock paste, homemade, to taste
  • 2 tsp plain flour
  • ½ tsp fine salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper, freshly ground
  • 300 g pearl couscous
  • 900 g water
  • 180 g carrots, cut into pieces (1-2 cm)
  • 220 g turnip, cut into pieces (1-2 cm)
  • 250 g courgette, cut into pieces (1-2 cm)
  • 100 g raisins
  • 240 g canned chickpeas, rinsed and drained (weight after draining)

Preparation

  1. Place coriander and parsley into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place garlic, onion and 20 g of the oil into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then sauté 1 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Add cinnamon, ground ginger, saffron, paprika and nutmeg and sauté 1 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  4. Add beef, stock paste, flour, salt and pepper and cook 5 min/100°C//speed .
  5. Place a large bowl onto mixing bowl lid, then weigh couscous and remaining olive oil into it. Using your hands, rub couscous to remove the starch. Place large bowl back onto mixing bowl lid, then weigh 500 g of the water into it. Set couscous aside to soak.
  6. Add carrots, turnip, zucchini, reserved coriander and parsley and remaining 400 g of the water and cook 15 min/Varoma//speed .
  7. Drain couscous through Varoma dish. Place Varoma dish with couscous into position, then weigh raisins into it. Add chickpeas, then close Varoma lid and steam 5 min/Varoma//speed .
  8. Stir couscous in Varoma dish with spatula to ensure even cooking, then secure Varoma lid and steam for a futher 5 min/Varoma//speed .
  9. Serve beef stew warm with couscous on the side.
