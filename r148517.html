Tuna poke bowl

Ingredients

Ponzu

  • 60 g lemon juice
  • 80 g soy sauce (see Tips)
  • 30 g rice wine vinegar
  • 2 tsp sesame oil

Poke bowl

  • 200 - 300 g brown rice
  • 1000 g water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 30 g rice wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 350 - 450 g sashimi grade tuna, cut into cubes (1.5 cm)
  • 2 tsp sesame seeds
  • 2 spring onions/shallots, trimmed and cut into thin slices
  • 1 avocado, flesh only, cut into pieces (1 cm)
  • ½ cucumber, cut into cubes (1 cm)
  • ½ nori sheet, cut into strips
  • Japanese pickled ginger, to serve (optional)

Preparation

Ponzu

  1. Place a ceramic or glass bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh lemon juice, soy sauce, rice wine vinegar and sesame oil into it. Stir to combine and set aside.

Poke bowl

  1. Insert simmering basket into the mixing bowl and weigh rice into it. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and rinse rice until water runs clear.
  2. Place water and salt into mixing bowl. Re-insert simmering basket with rice and cook 40-45 min/100°C/speed 4, until rice is cooked. Transfer rice into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place bowl with rice onto mixing bowl lid and weigh rice wine vinegar and sesame oil into it, then stir to combine and set aside.
  4. Place a separate bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh tuna, sesame seeds, spring onions/shallots and 100 g of the reserved Ponzu into it. Gently stir to combine and set aside to marinate for a maximum of 1-2 minutes (the marinade will continue to cook the fish the longer it sits).
  5. Divide rice, marinated tuna, avocado and cucumber between serving bowls. Sprinkle with nori and pickled ginger (optional) and serve immediately, with extra ponzu on the side.

Useful Items

ceramic or glass bowl, bowl, serving bowls

Hints & Tricks

  • To make this recipe gluten free, be sure to use gluten free soy sauce or tamari.
  • Add your favourite toppings such as wasabi, shredded carrot or shredded daikon to your bowl.
  • Vegetarian poke bowl: Replace tuna with 350-450 g firm tofu. Marinate tofu in 100 g ponzu with sesame seeds and spring onions/shallots for 30 minutes. Add to poke bowl with avocado and cucumber.
