Beef and potato massaman curry

Ingredients

  • 200 g frozen green beans, cut into bite-size pieces (see Tips)
  • 400 g canned chickpeas, rinsed and drained (approx. 250 g after draining)
  • 40 g peanut oil
  • 1 brown onion, cut into halves
  • 200 g Massaman curry paste
  • 700 g gravy beef, cut into cubes (4 cm)
  • 270 g coconut cream
  • 4 cardamom pods, bruised
  • 400 g potatoes, cut into cubes (4-5 cm)
  • 70 g roasted unsalted peanuts
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce

Preparation

  1. Place a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh beans and chickpeas into it, then set aside.
  2. Place oil and onion into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add curry paste and cook 3 min/100°C/speed 1.
  4. Add beef and cook 10 min/100°C//speed 1.
  5. Stir mixture with aid of spatula. Add coconut cream and cardamom, then cook 40 min/90°C//speed 1, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  6. Add potatoes into mixing bowl, stirring through with aid of spatula, and cook 40-50 min/90°C//speed 1 or until potatoes and beef and cooked to your liking.
  7. Add peanuts and fish sauce and cook 2-3 min/90°C//speed 1. Transfer curry into bowl with reserved beans and chickpeas, stir to combine and stand for 5-10 minutes or until vegetables are warmed through, then serve.

Useful Items

thermal serving bowl or other large bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • The frozen green beans will thaw while the beef and potatoes are cooking. The residual heat from the curry will heat them through in the last step before serving. If necessary, allow to stand for a few more minutes before serving.
