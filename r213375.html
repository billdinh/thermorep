Lemon Caper Potato Salad

Ingredients

  • 8 oz bacon, cut into ½ in. pieces
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • ½ oz Dijon mustard
  • 1 oz extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 oz freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 oz white vinegar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground white pepper
  • 25 oz water
  • 60 oz Yukon gold potatoes, peeled and cut into ½ in. pieces
  • 4 large eggs
  • 6 oz celery, cut into ¼ in. pieces
  • 5 oz pickles, cut into ¼ in. pieces
  • 3 oz green onions, cut into ¼ in. pieces (approx. 4 onions)
  • 2 tbsp capers
  • 2 oz mayonnaise

Preparation

  1. Place bacon into mixing bowl, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 20 min/250°F/speed . Transfer bacon into a large bowl lined with paper towels. Allow to drain before removing paper towels and set aside.
  2. Place garlic, mustard, olive oil, lemon juice, vinegar, salt and white pepper into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 8. Transfer to bowl with reserved bacon, combine with spatula and set aside.
  3. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position, weigh in potatoes and eggs, secure Varoma lid and steam 27 min/Varoma/speed 3. Remove eggs from Varoma, immediately transfer to simmering basket and run under cold water to stop cooking process.
  4. Peel eggs, transfer to a bowl and set aside. Stir potatoes with spatula to ensure even cooking, secure Varoma lid and steam a further 10-15 min/Varoma/speed 3, or just until potatoes are cooked through.
  5. Immediately transfer potatoes into bowl with bacon mixture, toss to coat potatoes and allow to cool (approx. 30 min.).
  6. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in celery, pickles, green onions, capers and mayonnaise. Transfer to bowl with potatoes and bacon mixture and combine well with spatula.
  7. Slice eggs and arrange on top of potato salad. Serve chilled or at room temperature.

Useful Items

large bowl, paper towels

Hints & Tricks

  • Add 1 oz more mayonnaise for a really creamy salad.
  • Cooling hot potatoes slightly before mixing with mayonnaise will prevent breaking the emulsion.
