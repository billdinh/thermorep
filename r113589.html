Pumpkin, Feta and Caramelised Onion Tart

Ingredients

  • 60 g butter, melted, plus extra butter for greasing
  • 8 sheets filo pastry
  • 170 g red onions, quartered
  • 10 g olive oil
  • 10 g balsamic vinegar
  • 10 g brown sugar
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 3 pinches fine sea salt
  • 550 g pumpkin flesh, diced (1 cm)
  • 15 g honey
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper, or to taste
  • 200 g fresh baby spinach
  • 40 g whipping cream
  • 2 large eggs
  • 15 cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 200 g feta cheese, diced (1 cm)

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C. Line a baking tray with baking paper and set aside. Grease a baking dish or cake tin (approx. 24 cm).
  2. Layer pastry sheets on top of one another in prepared baking dish, spreading 50 g melted butter between sheets with a pastry brush - allowing pastry to overhang dish to form a rim.
  3. Place red onions, olive oil, vinegar, sugar, garlic and 2 pinches salt in mixing bowl then chop 6 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then cook 9 min/Varoma/speed 1. Transfer to a bowl and set aside to cool.
  4. Place pumpkin, honey, remaining 1 pinch salt and pepper in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec//speed 2. Transfer to prepared baking tray and bake for 20 minutes (200°C).
  5. Arrange reserved red onion mixture onto pastry base.
  6. Place baby spinach, cream and eggs in mixing bowl then mix 12 sec/speed 3. Pour onto red onion then top with baked pumpkin.
  7. Sprinkle tart with cherry tomatoes and feta, then brush edges of pie with remaining 10 g melted butter. Brown in oven for 10 minutes (200°C) then lower oven temperature to 180°C and cook for a further 15 minutes (180°C). Serve hot or cold.

Useful Items

square tart tin (24 cm), baking tray and paper, pastry brush, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve accompanied by a green salad.
