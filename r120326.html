Vitamin C powerhouse

Ingredients

  • 1 pear (approx. 180 g), cored and cut into pieces (3-4 cm)
  • 1 apple (approx. 150 g), cored and cut into pieces (3-4 cm)
  • 60 g fresh baby spinach leaves
  • 150 g chilled water
  • 300 g freshly squeezed orange juice (chilled)
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
317 kJ / 75 kcal
Protein
1.4 g
Carbohydrates
15.06 g
Fat
0.16 g

Preparation

  1. Place all ingredients into mixing bowl and blend 2 min/speed 10. Transfer into a jug and place into refrigerator to chill for 20 minutes (optional).
  2. Divide between 4 glasses and serve.

Useful Items

jug, 4 glasses, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • For an extra flavour and nutrition boost, add a 1 cm piece fresh grated ginger.

  • You can store this juice overnight in the refrigerator.
  • The apple, pear and orange contribute Vitamin C and fibre, while the spinach provides iron. Consuming iron and vitamin C together helps increase iron absorption.
