Mini Oregano Focaccias

Ingredients

  • 270 g water
  • 1 ½ tsp dried instant yeast
  • 500 g plain flour
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt, plus extra to taste
  • 8 pinches dried oregano
  • 8 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil, for drizzling, plus extra for greasing

per 1 piece

Preparation

  1. Place water and yeast in mixing bowl then warm 3 min/37°C/speed 2. Meanwhile, grease a large bowl.
  2. Add flour and 1 tsp salt then knead 3 min/. Transfer to prepared bowl, cover with cling film and leave to rest until doubled in size (approx. 2 hours). Meanwhile, line 2 baking trays with baking paper.
  3. Divide rested dough into 8 equal-sized balls, shape into discs (approx. Ø 12 cm) then arrange on prepared trays. Cover with cling film and leave to prove until doubled in size (approx. 1 hour). Towards the end of this time, preheat oven to 200°C.
  4. Using oiled fingertips, make indentations all over risen dough discs. Sprinkle with salt, to taste, and oregano then drizzle each with 1 Tbsp oil. Bake for 15-20 minutes (200°C) until lightly golden. Serve hot or warm.

Useful Items

cling film, baking trays, baking paper, large bowl, oven
