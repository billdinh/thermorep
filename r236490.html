Lentil bolognese

Ingredients

  • 1 brown onion, cut into quarters
  • 1 celery stalk, cut into pieces
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 3 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 1 tbsp dried thyme
  • 3 tsp dried oregano
  • 2 pinches dried chilli flakes, to taste (optional)
  • 40 g olive oil
  • 500 g cherry tomatoes, cut into halves
  • 400 g canned tomatoes
  • 400 g canned lentils, rinsed and drained (approx. 250 g after draining)
  • 190 g sun-dried tomato pesto
  • ½ tsp salt, to taste

Preparation

  1. Place onion, celery, garlic, parsley, thyme, oregano, chilli flakes (optional) and oil into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7.
  2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then sauté 3 min/100°C/speed 1.
  3. Add cherry tomatoes, canned tomatoes, lentils, pesto and salt and cook 17 min/100°C/speed 1. Transfer into a thermal serving bowl or warm ceramic dish and cover to keep warm. Serve as desired (see Tips).

Useful Items

thermal serving bowl or warm ceramic dish

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve Bolognese over your favourite pasta or just with crusty bread.
  • Please refer to The Basic Cookbook or the Everyday Cookbook for the Boiled pasta recipe.
  • For more depth of flavour, make this sauce the day before serving. Simply store in the refrigerator until ready to serve.
