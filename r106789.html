Banoffee arctic roll

Ingredients

Banana ice cream

  • 5 ripe bananas (approx. 500 g), cut into slices
  • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste
  • 45 g pouring (whipping) cream

Caramel sauce

  • 80 g unsalted butter, cut into pieces
  • 110 g double cream
  • 40 g dark brown sugar
  • 40 g caster sugar
  • 40 g pure maple syrup
  • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt

Vanilla sponge

  • butter, for greasing
  • 125 g caster sugar, plus extra for sprinkling
  • 5 eggs, room temperature
  • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste
  • 125 g self-raising flour
  • icing sugar, for dusting

Preparation

Banana ice cream

  1. Line a baking tray with baking paper. Place banana slices onto tray in a single layer and place into freezer overnight.
  2. Place frozen banana, vanilla and cream into mixing bowl and blend 30 sec/speed 1 -10, increasing speed gradually from speed 1 to speed 10.
  3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Blend 30 sec/speed 6, or until thick and creamy.
  4. Transfer banana mixture onto a sheet of baking paper. Using the baking paper to help, roll the mixture tightly together into a cylinder approx. 30 cm in length. Twist ends of baking paper (into a Christmas cracker shape) and place into freezer for 2 hours. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Caramel sauce

  1. Place all caramel sauce ingredients into mixing bowl and cook 10 min/Varoma/speed 2, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  2. Transfer sauce into a sealable container and allow to cool to room temperature (approx. 30 minutes), then place into freezer to set (approx. 40 minutes). Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Vanilla sponge

  1. Preheat oven to 210°C. Grease and line a baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place sugar in mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 9.
  3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Insert butterfly whisk. Add eggs and vanilla and whisk 15 min/speed 4, or until mixture is light and frothy.
  4. Add flour and combine 50 sec//speed 1. Remove butterfly whisk.
  5. Pour mixture onto prepared baking tray. Gently shake tray to ensure mixture spreads evenly across tray (do not spread using a spatula as this may release air in batter). Bake for 10-12 minutes (210°C), or until sponge is golden and starts to shrink away from sides of baking tray.
  6. Carefully flip warm sponge onto a work suface, keeping baking paper attached. Roll long edge of sponge towards you while sponge is still warm so it will hold its shape. Set aside to cool (approx. 5-10 minutes).
  7. Place a sheet of baking paper on the work surface and sprinkle with extra sugar. Place rolled sponge onto sugar and unroll. Peel off rolled baking paper. Trim edges of sponge.
  8. Spread reserved caramel sauce over sponge. Working quickly, unwrap banana ice cream and place onto caramel sauce. Wrap sponge around ice cream. Trim ends, if required.
  9. Use baking paper to tightly wrap artic roll, then wrap in aluminium foil. Place into freezer for a minimum of 30 minutes. Dust with icing sugar before serving.

Useful Items

baking tray (30 x 40 cm), baking tray (20 x 30 cm), baking paper, sealable storage jar or container, aluminium foil, freezer, oven
