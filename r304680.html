Walnut and Blueberry Bran Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 100 g plain flour
  • 90 g oat bran
  • 50 g caster sugar
  • 1 Tbsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 360 g whole milk
  • 90 g rolled oats
  • 20 g runny honey
  • 2 large eggs
  • 120 g fresh blueberries
  • 60 g walnut halves, roughly chopped
  • unsalted butter, for frying
  • maple syrup, for serving

Preparation

  1. Place flour, oat bran, sugar, baking powder and salt in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 3.
  2. Add milk, oats, honey and eggs then mix 20 sec/speed 3.
  3. Add blueberries and walnuts then mix well with spatula. Leave to rest for 30 minutes (see tip).
  4. After this time, heat butter in a frying pan over a medium heat then place ladles of batter in pan to create thick pancakes. Cook on each side until bubbles appear on surface then transfer to a plate. Repeat with remaining batter, adding more butter as necessary.
  5. Serve warm, drizzled with maple syrup.

Useful Items

frying pan, small ladle, hob

Hints & Tricks

  • These fuel-laden pancakes are a good breakfast option. Rich in carbohydrates they will set you up well for a late morning run. They would also make a great recovery snack after exercise.
  • 10 pancakes, 2 per person or 3 if hungry!
  • The pancakes can be made immediately but leaving the mixture to rest makes the batter thicker and the pancakes easier to cook.
