Steamed ginger pudding

Ingredients

  • 80 g vegan butter, for greasing (see Tips)
  • 2 pieces stem ginger (approx. 20 g)
  • 150 g pitted dates
  • 2600 g filtered water
  • 80 g coconut sugar
  • 150 g wholemeal plain flour
  • 2 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 2 tsp ground ginger
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • 180 g plant-based milk of choice
  • 3 tsp apple cider vinegar
  • cream or custard, to serve

Preparation

  1. Grease a pudding basin (1L) with butter, then place into Varoma dish and set aside.
  2. Place stem ginger and dates into mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 7.
  3. Add 100 g of the water into mixing bowl and cook 2 min/90°C/speed 2. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Do not clean mixing bowl.
  4. Insert butterfly whisk. Place butter and sugar into mixing bowl and mix 2 min/speed 4. Remove butterfly whisk.
  5. Add flour, bicarbonate of soda, ground ginger, cinnamon and milk and mix 5 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  6. Add vinegar and reserved date mixture and stir to combine. Transfer into pudding basin and cover with basin lid or place a plate over basin (to prevent steam from getting into basin), ensuring Varoma lid closes securely. Clean mixing bowl.
  7. Place 1000 g of the water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma with pudding basin into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 60 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  8. Remove Varoma and add remaining 1500 g water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma back into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 70 min/Varoma/speed 2. Allow pudding to rest in basin for 40 minutes, then turn out onto a plate. Serve warm with pouring cream or custard (see Tips).

Useful Items

bowl, pudding basin (1L), plate

Hints & Tricks

  • To make your own vegan butter, pouring cream and custard, refer to the recipes on this recipe chip.
  • You can find stem ginger at specialty food stores and online.
