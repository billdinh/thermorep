Macadamia Halibut with Mango Sauce

Ingredients

  • 5 oz macadamia nuts, chopped and toasted
  • 1 oz shredded coconut, toasted

Mango Sauce

  • 20 oz ripe mango flesh (approx. 3 mangos), in pieces (2 in.)
  • 1 jalapeño pepper, de-stemmed (halved, lengthwise)
  • 2 in. fresh ginger, in pieces (¼ in.)
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 stalk fresh lemongrass, in pieces (2 in.)
  • 8 oz water
  • ¼ tsp salt, to taste
  • ground black pepper, to taste

Halibut

  • 32 oz halibut fillets
  • salt, to taste
  • ground black pepper, to taste
  • freshly squeezed lime juice, to taste
  • fresh cilantro, chopped, to garnish

Preparation

  1. Place a small bowl onto mixing bowl lid. Weigh in macadamia nuts and coconut. Mix well with spatula to combine and set aside.

Mango Sauce

  1. Place mango into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add jalapeño, ginger, honey, lemongrass, water, salt and pepper.

Halibut

  1. Set Varoma dish into position and weigh in fish, sprinkle with salt, pepper and lime juice. Evenly distribute seasoned fish between Varoma dish and tray, secure Varoma lid and and steam 20-25 min/Varoma/speed 2 until fish is cooked through. Remove Varoma and set aside, keep halibut warm.
  2. Strain sauce into a bowl with a fine mesh sieve and discard solids. Brush top of steamed halibut lightly with strained sauce and sprinkle with reserved macadamia-coconut mixture.
  3. Ladle a generous amount of Mango Sauce onto each plate, place a piece of fish on the sauce. Garnish with chopped cilantro and serve.

Useful Items

fine mesh sieve, basting brush, small bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • Steaming time will depend on thickness of fish fillets.
