Watermelon canapés with whipped feta and walnuts

Ingredients

Dressing

  • 1 sprig fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 1 sprig fresh mint, leaves only
  • 20 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 pinch sea salt, to taste
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper, to taste

Whipped feta

  • 150 g Danish feta
  • 60 g cream cheese, softened
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 pinches sea salt
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper

Watermelon and walnuts

  • 400 g watermelon
  • 40 g halved walnuts, toasted and broken into halves, for garnishing

Preparation

Dressing

  1. Place parsley and mint into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add all remaining dressing ingredients and blend 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a serving jug and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Whipped feta

  1. Insert butterfly whisk. Place feta and cream cheese into mixing bowl and whip 20 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then mix 30 sec/speed 3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula again.
  2. Add all remaining feta ingredients and combine 20 sec/speed 3. Transfer whipped feta into a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Watermelon and walnuts

  1. Cut watermelon into slices (1 cm thick), then using a cookie cutter (4-5 cm), cut out watermelon rounds. Place watermelon rounds onto a serving platter.
  2. Top each watermelon round with 1 teaspoon of the whipped feta, then garnish with walnut pieces and a drizzle of the reserved dressing. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

serving jug, serving platter, cookie cutter (4-5 cm)
