Venezuelan Shredded Chicken with Arepas

Ingredients

Arepas Dough

  • 280 g precooked maize flour (e.g. Harina Pan)
  • 410 g water
  • 1 ½ - 2 tsp fine sea salt, to taste

Venezuelan Shredded Chicken and Arepas

  • 5 sprigs fresh coriander, plus extra leaves for garnish
  • 40 g onion
  • 1 red pepper (approx. 150 g), cut in pieces
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 2 tsp pickled capers
  • 1 plum tomato, cut in pieces
  • 1 ½ - 2 tsp fine sea salt, to taste
  • ½ - 1 tsp ground black pepper, to taste
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 560 g chicken breast fillets, skinless, cut in bite-size pieces
  • 80 g sherry
  • olive oil, for frying

Preparation

Arepas Dough

  1. Place maize flour, water and salt in mixing bowl then mix 20 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.

Venezuelan Shredded Chicken and Arepas

  1. Place coriander in mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place onion, red pepper, garlic cloves, capers, tomato, salt, black pepper and olive oil in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 5. Sauté 4 min/120°C/speed 1.
  3. Add chicken, sherry and reserved chopped coriander then cook 18 min/100°C//speed 1. Meanwhile, with wet hands divide arepas dough into 8 equal pieces and form into ball shapes. Squeeze each ball between wet hands and shape into the traditional round shape (Ø 10 cm, 1 cm thick). Fill a deep frying pan with 0.5 cm olive oil, over medium heat and fry each flatbread approx. 3 minutes on each side. Drain on paper towels.
  4. Shred chicken 5 sec//speed 4. Serve immediately in fried arepas, garnished with coriander.

Useful Items
