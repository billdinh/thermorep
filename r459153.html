Cauliflower Steaks

Ingredients

  • 1 head cauliflower, large (approx. 36 oz), leaves removed
  • 1 tbsp sweet smoked paprika
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp dried ground cumin
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 3 oz extra virgin olive oil

Sauce

  • 20 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 lemon, thin peel only, no pith, plus 1 oz juice
  • 2 oz extra virgin olive oil
  • ¾ tsp salt, to taste

Cauliflower Purée

  • 2 oz tahini
  • 1 oz extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 lemons, 1 juiced and 1 sliced to garnish
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 2 oz water, plus extra if needed
  • ½ tsp salt, to taste

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F and line a baking sheet with parchment.
  2. Slice cauliflower into four 1 inch steaks, slicing straight through the stem to hold the cauliflower together.
  3. In a small bowl mix paprika, turmeric, garlic powder, cumin powder, onion powder and curry powder.
  4. Brush both sides of cauliflower steaks with olive oil and place onto prepared baking sheet. Sprinkle both sides with paprika mixture and add any leftover cauliflower bits or pieces onto the parchment. Bake for 15 minutes (350°F). Increase oven temperature to 400°F. Turn steaks over and bake 20 minutes (400°F) or until steaks are tender. While steaks are baking, make the sauce.

Sauce

  1. Place parsley, garlic and lemon peel into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Add olive oil, 1 oz lemon juice and salt into mixing bowl and blend 15 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. When cauliflower is done baking, set steaks aside and reserve any loose pieces of cauliflower.

Cauliflower Purée

  1. Place 8 oz reserved baked cauliflower pieces, tahini, olive oil, juice of 1 lemon, smoked paprika, water and salt into mixing bowl and purée 10 sec/speed 4-7, increasing speed gradually, adding water if needed. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and purée 10 sec/speed 7 until smooth, adding extra water if necessary.
  2. Place purée onto a plate, top with cauliflower steaks and drizzle with sauce. Garnish with lemon slices and serve.

Useful Items

baking sheet, parchment paper, pastry brush, oven
