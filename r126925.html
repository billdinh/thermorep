Tabouleh with quinoa and tuna

Ingredients

  • 500 g water
  • ½ tsp fine salt, plus 1 pinch extra to taste
  • 75 g quinoa grains
  • ½ lemon, preferably organic (grated zest and juice only)
  • ½ garlic clove (optional)
  • 20 g spring onions, cut into pieces (optional)
  • 3 - 4 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves washed and dried
  • 2 - 3 sprigs fresh mint, leaves washed and dried
  • 30 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 100 g tomatoes, cut into pieces (2-3 cm)
  • 1 - 2 pinches chili powder, to taste
  • 100 g cucumber, cut into pieces
  • 100 g canned tuna, in brine or water, drained well

Preparation

  1. Place water and ½ teaspoon salt into mixing bowl and bring to the boil: 8 min/100°C/speed 1.
  2. Add quinoa and cook 12 min/100°C//speed . Strain quinoa using simmering basket and transfer to a large bowl. Dry mixing bowl.
  3. Combine the grated zest and juice of the lemon with the quinoa and mix.
  4. If used, place garlic clove, spring onions, coriander and mint into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Chop again 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  6. Add olive oil, 1 pinch of salt to taste, tomatoes and chilli and mix 10 sec/speed 4.
  7. Add cucumber, tuna and chop 5 sec/speed 4. Transfer mixture into bowl with quinoa and mix with aid of spatula.
  8. Serve tabouleh warm or cold.
