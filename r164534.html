Spooky Spaghetti and Meatballs

Ingredients

Meatballs

  • olive oil, to grease
  • 35 oz water
  • 3 oz red quinoa
  • 2 oz yellow onion, cut into pieces
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 oz mixed fresh herbs (e.g. parsley, thyme, oregano)
  • 1 tsp salt, to taste
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • ½ tsp dried red chili flakes (optional)
  • ¼ tsp fennel seeds
  • 20 oz dark turkey meat, boneless and skinless, cut into pieces
  • 1 large egg
  • 25 - 35 black olive slices

Tomato Sauce

  • 4 oz yellow onion, cut into pieces
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 oz olive oil
  • 28 oz canned crushed tomatoes
  • ½ tsp salt, to taste
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper

Spooky Spaghetti

  • 40 oz water
  • 1 ½ tsp salt
  • 12 oz spaghetti

Preparation

Meatballs

  1. Lightly grease Varoma dish and Varoma tray and set aside.
  2. Place water into mixing bowl, insert simmering basket, weigh in quinoa and rinse 10 sec/speed 5. Stir through with spatula and cook 20 min/Varoma/speed 2. Transfer to a bowl and allow to cool slightly. Clean mixing bowl.
  3. Place onions, garlic, herbs, salt, pepper, chili flakes and fennel seeds into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Add turkey and chop Turbo/2 sec/4-5 times until turkey is ground up. Add egg and reserved cooked quinoa and mix 10 sec/speed 5.
  4. Shape mixture into balls (approx. 1 oz ea.) and place in prepared Varoma dish and tray. Gently press 1 slice black olive on top of each ball to form spooky "eyeballs" and set Varoma aside.

Tomato Sauce

  1. Place onion, garlic and olive oil into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and sauté 3 min/250°F/speed 1. Add tomatoes, salt and pepper, place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and cook 15-18 min/Varoma/speed 1, until turkey meatballs are done. Transfer sauce and eyeballs to a serving platter and keep warm.

Spooky Spaghetti

  1. Place water and salt into mixing bowl and bring to a boil 10 min/212°F/speed 1. Weigh in dried pasta through hole in mixing bowl lid and without measuring cup cook for time indicated on package/212°F//speed 1 or until al dente. Drain pasta through Varoma dish. Divide pasta, tomato sauce and eyeballs between 6-8 bowls and serve immediately.

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with grated parmesan cheese or additional black olives.
  • To save time, cook pasta on your stovetop while the sauce and meatballs are cooking.
  • Using dark turkey meat will ensure that the meatballs remain juicy.
  • Keep a bowl of water nearby to moisten hands and prevent sticking while making meatballs.
  • Replace regular spaghetti with squid ink spaghetti. Squid ink pasta is available year round at most specialty Italian markets or can be ordered online.
