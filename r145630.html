Cauliflower and Black Bean Balls

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp chia seeds
  • 500 g water, plus 6 Tbsp water
  • 400 g cauliflower, in pieces (3-4 cm)
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • ¼ tsp ground pepper
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 2 Tbsp red curry paste (30 g)
  • 95 g tomatoes, quartered
  • 60 g onions, quartered
  • 10 g mixed fresh herbs, leaves only, to taste (e.g parsley, coriander)
  • 240 g black beans, tinned, drained (1 x 400 g tin)
  • 100 g polenta, for coating
  • 4 Tbsp sunflower oil, for frying

Preparation

  1. Place chia seeds in mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and mix with 6 Tbsp water then set aside.
  2. Place water in mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh in cauliflower then steam 10 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Remove Varoma dish and discard cooking water.
  4. Place cooked cauliflower, reserved chia mixture, salt, pepper, garlic, curry paste, tomatoes, onions, herbs and black beans in mixing bowl. Blend 20 sec/speed 6 while stirring with spatula through hole in mixing bowl lid.
  5. Place a shallow bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in polenta then set aside. Roll 1½ Tbsp of mixture into 30 balls then roll in polenta to coat. Transfer to a baking tray and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  6. Towards the end of chilling time, heat 2 Tbsp sunflower oil in a frying pan over a medium heat. Fry balls in batches for 5 minutes until golden brown, turning occasionally, adding up to 2 Tbsp extra oil as necessary. Serve warm or cold (see tip).

Useful Items

shallow bowl or dish, baking tray, frying pan, refrigerator, hob

Hints & Tricks

  • These bean balls are so versatile; serve with salad and your favourite dressing, with a ragu sauce with rice or eat them as a snack on the go!
