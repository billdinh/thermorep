Multi-seed Cocoa bars

Ingredients

  • 150 g mixed seeds (e.g. sesame, pumpkin, sunflower)
  • 125 g pecan nuts
  • 2 Tbsp chia seeds
  • 2 Tbsp golden linseeds
  • 75 g coconut oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 30 g cocoa powder
  • 370 g dates, pitted, halved
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 50 - 100 g water, to loosen mixture (optional)
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
903 kJ / 216 kcal

3.8 g

15.3 g

Preparation

  1. Lightly grease a brownie tin (approx. 32 cm x 18 cm) then line with cling film.
  2. Place mixed seeds, pecan nuts, chia seeds and linseeds in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a large bowl and set aside.
  3. Place coconut oil, cocoa, dates and vanilla extract in mixing bowl then blend 20 sec/speed 3. Add 50-100 g water if too thick (mixture has clumped together in one lump) and mix again 20 sec/speed 3. Tip into large bowl with seeds and mix well with spatula. Transfer to prepared tin and press down firmly with the back of a spoon. Cover and place in fridge for 30 minutes.
  4. Slice into bars and wrap in greaseproof paper or foil. Serve or store in fridge for up to 5 days.

Useful Items

brownie tin (32 cm x 18 cm), cling film, greaseproof paper, aluminium foil, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Make these into energy balls by rolling 1tsp of the finished mixture into balls. Allow to set for 5 minutes in the fridge.
