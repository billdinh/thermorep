Lemon ice cream torte

Ingredients

Lemon ice cream

  • 3 egg yolks
  • 150 g white sugar
  • 280 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 220 g full cream milk
  • 100 g lemon juice (approx. 4-5 lemons)

Nut praline

  • 60 g white sugar
  • 2 tbsp shelled unsalted pistachios
  • 1 sprig fresh thyme, leaves only
  • 125 g fresh raspberries, for decorating

Preparation

Lemon ice cream

  1. Place all ice cream ingredients into mixing bowl and cook 7 min/80°C/speed 4. Transfer into a freezable bowl or container and place into freezer for 8 hours or overnight.
  2. Line a round cake tin (18 cm) with plastic wrap and set aside (see Tips).
  3. Cut ice cream into pieces (4 x 4 cm). Place ice cream pieces into mixing bowl and crush 1 min/speed 6, with aid of spatula, or until smooth.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and blend 20 sec/speed 4, or until smooth. Transfer into prepared cake tin and return to freezer until frozen (approx. 8 hours or overnight). Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Nut praline

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C. Line a baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh sugar into it. Sprinkle sugar evenly over prepared baking tray and bake for 6-8 minutes (220°C), or until amber in colour. Remove from oven and immediately sprinkle with pistachios and thyme. Set aside to cool completely (approx. 10 minutes).
  3. Break praline into shards. Place half of the praline into mixing bowl and mill 5 sec/speed 7.
  4. Place milled praline onto a serving plate and unmould ice cream torte on top of it. Decorate with remaining praline shards and raspberries. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

freezable bowl or container, round cake tin (18 cm), plastic wrap, baking tray (30 x 40 cm), baking paper, bowl, serving plate, freezer, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • You can also use a silicone cake tin (omit lining with plastic wrap) to make unmoulding easier.
