Walnut Bread

Ingredients

  • 150 g strong white bread flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 150 g strong wholemeal bread flour
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 ½ tsp dried instant yeast
  • 15 g honey (optional)
  • 20 g walnut oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 200 g water
  • 120 g walnut halves

Preparation

  1. Grease and line a loaf tin (900 g/2 lb) with baking paper.
  2. Place white bread flour, wholemeal bread flour, salt, yeast, honey (if using), walnut oil and water in mixing bowl. Mix 20 sec/speed 4 then knead 4 min/.
  3. Add walnuts and knead 2 min/.
  4. Tip out onto a floured surface and bring together into a ball. Transfer dough to prepared tin and cover with oiled cling film then leave to rest until doubled in size (approx. 2 hours).
  5. Towards the end of this time, preheat oven to 200°C.
  6. Dust risen loaf with flour then bake 30-40 minutes (200°C) until loaf sounds hollow when tapped underneath. Transfer to a wire rack and leave to cool completely before serving in slices.

Useful Items

loaf tin (900 g/2 lb), baking paper, cling film, wire rack, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • The Thermomix® kneading function makes light work of making bread. This dark walnut loaf is so easy to make and goes really well with cheese. For a grain-free alternative, try the Seed and Nut Bread.
  • Bread sticks: In Step 1, line or grease a baking tray. In Step 4, shape dough into breadsticks instead of placing in loaf tin, and allow to rise for 1 hour. Reduce baking time to 12-18 minutes.
  • Bread rolls: In Step 1, line or grease a baking tray. In Step 4, shape dough into rolls instead of placing in loaf tin and allow to rise for 1 hour. Reduce baking time to 12-18 minutes.
