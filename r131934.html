Almond Crusted Salmon with Asparagus Tagliatelle

Ingredients

Almond Crusted Salmon

  • 40 g olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 4 fresh salmon fillets, skinless (approx. 150 g each)
  • 30 g lemon juice, freshly squeezed
  • 20 g Dijon mustard
  • 15 g honey
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 20 g blanched almonds
  • 50 g bread, cut in pieces
  • 5 g fresh parsley

Lemon Tagliatelle with Asparagus

  • 1200 g water
  • 1 heaped tsp vegetable stock paste, homemade
  • 300 g pasta, fresh, tagliatelle
  • 250 g asparagus, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • ½ lemon, preferably organic, grated zest and juice
  • fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Preparation

Almond Crusted Salmon

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C. Grease a casserole dish.
  2. Arrange salmon fillets in the prepared casserole dish and set aside.
  3. Place lemon juice, olive oil, Dijon mustard, honey, garlic cloves and salt in mixing bowl then chop 10 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and brush the salmon fillets with mustard-lemon mixture, then set aside.
  4. Without cleaning mixing bowl, place blanched almonds, bread and parsley in mixing bowl then grate 10 sec/speed 10. Cover marinated salmon fillets with almond breadcrumbs and bake for 15-18 minutes (200°C). Meanwhile clean mixing bowl and continue with recipe.

Lemon Tagliatelle with Asparagus

  1. Place water and vegetable stock paste in mixing bowl then bring to the boil 10 min/100°C/speed 1.
  2. Add tagliatelle and green asparagus then cook without measuring cup for time indicated on packet/100°C//speed 1, or until al dente. Drain cooked pasta and asparagus through Varoma dish, transfer to a bowl and mix with olive oil, lemon juice and grated lemon zest. Serve hot with almond-bread crusted salmon fillets, garnished with chopped parsley.

Useful Items

casserole dish, pastry brush, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • If asparagus is not available, use green peas instead, or a mixture of asparagus and green peas also works well.
  • Dried pasta is easy to cook in the Thermomix. To use dried tagliatelle in this recipe: place dried tagliatelle in mixing bowl, place Varoma dish with asparagus in and cook for: time indicated on packet/Varoma//speed 1.
  • Almonds are a tasty addition to this breadcrumb crust. This crust can also be used for other fish fillets, such as plaice, or any white fish.
