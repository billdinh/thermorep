Bircher Muesli

Ingredients

  • 200 g rolled oats
  • 300 g apple juice
  • 1 pinch ground cinnamon
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • 40 g whole mixed nuts (e.g almonds, hazelnuts, macadamias)
  • 40 g desiccated coconut
  • 40 g dried fruit (e.g figs, cranberries, dates)
  • 1 Granny Smith apple, unpeeled, cored, quartered (approx. 95 g)
  • 50 g Greek yoghurt, for serving
  • mixed fresh fruit (e.g. pomegranate seeds, strawberries, blueberries), for serving

Preparation

  1. Place oats, apple juice, cinnamon and nutmeg in a large bowl then cover with cling film and leave to soak overnight in fridge.
  2. The next day, place nuts, desiccated coconut and dried fruit in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 5.5. Add to bowl with muesli mixture.
  3. Place apple in mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 6. Add to bowl with muesli mixture and gently stir together with spatula. Serve with a spoonful of Greek yoghurt per portion and mixed fresh fruit.

Useful Items

cling film, large bowl, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • This continental recipe soaks oats overnight, making them easier to digest. Combined with raw fruit and topped with yoghurt, it makes a great alternative to processed, shop-bought cereals.
