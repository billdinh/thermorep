Garibaldi Biscuits

Ingredients

  • 110 g self-raising flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 25 g butter, soft
  • 25 g golden caster sugar
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
  • 30 g milk
  • 60 g currants
  • 1 egg white
  • 1 Tbsp icing sugar, for sprinkling
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
239 kJ / 57 kcal
Protein
0.9 g
Carbohydrates
10.1 g
Fat
1.4 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C and line a baking tray with baking paper.
  2. Place flour, butter, sugar and salt in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 6.
  3. Add milk and mix 15 sec/speed 4. Tip out and bring together into a ball. Roll sticky dough out between two sheets of cling film into a rectangle, as thin as possible (approx. 20 cm x 30 cm x 3 mm).
  4. Sprinkle currants over half the surface then fold the other half on top and roll out to a rectangle 20 cm x 30 cm. Trim edges to neaten then transfer to prepared baking tray. Score dough into 16 fingers (approx. 12 cm x 3.5 cm) leaving them attached.
  5. Brush biscuits with egg white then sprinkle with icing sugar. Bake for 12-15 minutes (180°C). Leave for 2 minutes on tray before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely. Serve or store in an airtight container for up to 5 days.

Useful Items

baking tray and paper, cling film, rolling pin, wire rack, pastry brush, airtight container, oven
