Jelly Earthworms in Chocolate Soil

Ingredients

Chocolate Cake

  • 50 g unsalted butter, diced, plus extra for greasing
  • 60 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 50 g sugar
  • 10 g cocoa powder
  • 20 g milk
  • 1 medium egg
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt

Chocolate Mousse and Assembly

  • 100 g dark chocolate, small pieces or callets
  • 120 g drinking chocolate, powdered (e.g. Nesquik®)
  • 500 g cream alternative, 19% fat (e.g. Elmlea), for whipping
  • 120 - 150 g jelly sweets (e.g. worms, spiders)

Preparation

Chocolate Cake

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Grease a loaf tin (approx. 10 cm x 20 cm) and dust with flour.
  2. Place butter in mixing bowl and cream 10 sec/speed 5.
  3. Add flour, sugar, cocoa powder, milk, egg, baking powder and salt then mix 10 sec/speed 5. Finish stirring by hand with spatula if necessary then transfer to prepared tin and level mixture with the back of a spoon.
  4. Bake for 15 minutes (180°C) or until a skewer inserted in centre comes out clean. Leave in tin to cool for 15 minutes then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl.

Chocolate Mousse and Assembly

  1. Once cake is cool, place chocolate in mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add drinking chocolate and cream alternative then whip 20 sec/speed 6. Divide mousse between 8 serving glasses and crumble over cooled chocolate cake to look like soil. Arrange jelly worms on crumbled cake and serve immediately or chill until needed.
