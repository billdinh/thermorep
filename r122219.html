Carrot, Apple and Ginger Soup

Ingredients

  • 180 g dessert apples, peeled, cored, quartered
  • 850 g water
  • 15 g fresh root ginger, peeled, cut in round slices (2 mm)
  • 110 g onions, quartered
  • 55 g butter, diced
  • 150 g celery stalks, cut in pieces (approx. 2 stalks)
  • 400 g carrots, cut in pieces (approx. 4 carrots)
  • 1 vegetable stock cube (for 0.5 l)
  • 400 g tinned chick peas, rinsed, drained (1 x 400 g tin)
  • ½ - 1 tsp fine sea salt, to taste
  • ¼ tsp freshly ground black pepper, or to taste

Preparation

  1. Place apples and 100 g water in mixing bowl then chop 4 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then cook 7 min/100°C/speed 2. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place ginger in mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add onions, butter, celery, carrots and stock cube then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then cook 6 min/100°C/speed 1.
  4. Add remaining 750 g water and cook 10 min/100°C/speed 1.5.
  5. Add chickpeas, reserved apple purée, salt and pepper then cook again 3 min 30 sec/100°C/speed 1.5. Blend 1 min/speed 9 then serve immediately.
