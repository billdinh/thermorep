Lemon and coconut blondies

Ingredients

  • 250 g unsalted butter, cut into pieces, plus extra for greasing
  • 300 g white sugar
  • 1 lemon, zest only, no white pith
  • 4 eggs
  • 80 g shredded coconut, plus extra for sprinkling
  • 230 g plain flour
  • 40 g vegetable oil

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C. Grease and line a square tin (20 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place sugar and lemon zest into mixing bowl and mill 5 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place butter into mixing bowl and melt 3 min/100°C/speed 3.
  4. Add reserved sugar mixture and mix 1 min/speed 3, slowly adding eggs 1 at a time through hole in mixing bowl lid, until combined. Mixture should be thick and glossy.
  5. Add all remaining ingredients and mix 30 sec/speed 5 or until combined. Transfer into prepared tin and sprinkle with extra shredded coconut. Bake for 25 minutes (160°C) or until a wooden skewer inserted into the centre of the slice comes out clean. Allow to cool completely in tin, then cut into 16 squares to serve.

Useful Items

bowl, baking paper, square cake tin (20 cm), wooden skewer, oven
