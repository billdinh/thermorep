Lamb and quinoa stew

Ingredients

  • 20 g dried porcini mushrooms (see Tips)
  • 350 g water
  • 150 g brown onion, cut into quarters
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 celery stalk, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 80 g tomato paste
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 4 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves only
  • 1 tbsp Chicken stock paste (see Tips)
  • ¼ tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 dried bay leaf
  • 200 g carrots, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 500 - 600 g lamb, cut into cubes (2-3 cm)
  • 100 g quinoa
  • salt, to taste
  • ground black pepper, to taste
  • 200 g fresh button mushrooms, cut into quarters
  • 170 g zucchini, cut into pieces (approx. 2 cm)
  • 90 g frozen corn kernels

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh dried mushrooms and water into it. Set aside to soak.
  2. Place onion, garlic, celery and oil into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5, then sauté 3 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Add tomato paste, cumin, thyme, stock paste, cinnamon, bay leaf, carrots, lamb and reserved soaked mushrooms with soaking water and cook 20 min/100°C//speed 1 .
  4. Add quinoa, salt and pepper and cook 10 min/90°C//speed 1.
  5. Add fresh mushrooms and cook 10 min/90°C//speed 1.
  6. Add zucchini and corn and cook 5-10 min/90°C//speed 1 or until meat and vegetables are tender. Divide between bowls to serve.

Useful Items

bowl, serving bowls

Hints & Tricks

  • If you can't find dried porcini mushrooms (or they don't fit into your budget), you can replace them with dried shiitake mushrooms, which are available at most major supermarkets.
  • Please refer to The Basic Cookbook or the Everyday Cookbook for the Chicken stock paste recipe.
  • You can use any seasonal vegetables of choice in this recipe, however cooking time will need to be adjusted accordingly.
