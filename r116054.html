Strawberry and Pear Terrine

Ingredients

  • 75 g water
  • 75 - 80 g lemon juice (approx. 2 lemons)
  • 1000 g pears, peeled, cut in pieces (3 cm)
  • 400 g fresh strawberries, sliced (5 mm)
  • 13 gelatine leaves, soaked in cold water for 5 minutes
  • 10 g agave syrup
Nutrition
per 1 slice
Calories
282 kJ / 67 kcal
Protein
2.8 g
Carbohydrates
13.7 g
Fat
0.1 g

Preparation

  1. Place water and lemon juice in mixing bowl then heat 3 min/100°C/speed 3. Line a large loaf tin (900 g/2 lb) with cling film.
  2. Add pears and cook 10 min/80°C/speed 2. Meanwhile, in cake tin, layer 200 g sliced strawberries.
  3. Blend pears 10 sec/speed 10.
  4. Add squeezed out gelatine and agave syrup then stir 10 sec/speed 5.
  5. Pour half of pear mixture into loaf tin and leave to cool (approx. 1-2 hours), then place in freezer, until just set (approx. 25 minutes). Layer remaining 200 g strawberries on top of pear jelly and pour remaining pear mixture on top. Place in fridge to set overnight.
  6. With aid of cling film, remove terrine from tin and transfer to serving plate. Serve chilled, cut in slices.

Useful Items

900 g loaf tin (2 lb), cling film, serving plate, freezer, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Create individual desserts in ramekins or pudding basins.
