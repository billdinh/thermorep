Blueberry Bakewell Traybake

Ingredients

Base

  • 175 g plain flour
  • 35 g semolina
  • 20 g icing sugar
  • 150 g unsalted butter, diced
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt

Blueberry Compôte

  • 150 g blueberries, fresh
  • 25 g caster sugar

Filling

  • 150 g blanched almonds
  • 150 g caster sugar
  • 120 g unsalted butter, soft
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 ½ tsp natural almond extract
  • 2 Tbsp flaked almonds

Preparation

Base

  1. Line base and sides of a brownie tin (32 cm x 18 cm) with baking paper.
  2. Place flour, semolina, sugar, butter and salt in mixing bowl then mix 25 sec/speed 4. Tip out and press into the base of prepared tin then refrigerate for 20 minutes. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl and make compôte.

Blueberry Compôte

  1. Place blueberries and sugar in mixing bowl then heat 10 min/98°C/speed 1. Transfer to a bowl and set aside to cool. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl and continue with recipe.
  2. Preheat oven to 180°C. Remove tin from fridge and prick pastry base with a fork. Line with baking paper and fill with baking beans then blind bake for 12 minutes (180°C). Remove baking beans and paper then bake for a further 5 minutes (180°C). Meanwhile, make filling.

Filling

  1. Place almonds in mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add sugar, butter, eggs and almond extract then cream 30 sec/speed 3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then cream again 10 sec/speed 3.
  3. Spread cooled blueberry compôte over pastry base. Spoon filling mixture over the top and smooth the surface. Scatter with flaked almonds then bake for 20-25 minutes (180°C) until cooked and golden.
  4. Leave to cool in tin before removing. Cut into 12 pieces to serve.

Useful Items

brownie tin (32 cm x 18 cm), baking paper, baking beans, refrigerator, oven

Hints & Tricks

A baking book wouldn’t be complete without a Bakewell tart, so we’ve teamed it with blueberries here and made it into a traybake.
