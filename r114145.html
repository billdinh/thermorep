Scones

Ingredients

  • 80 g blanched almonds
  • 140 g rice flour (see Tips)
  • 80 g potato starch
  • 40 g arrowroot flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 2 tsp gluten free baking powder (see Tips)
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 80 g unsalted butter, cut into pieces, plus extra to serve
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 100 g full cream milk, plus extra for glazing
  • jam, to serve
  • double thick cream, to serve

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C. Line a baking tray (20 x 30 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place almonds into mixing bowl and mill 15 sec/speed 9.
  3. Add rice flour, potato starch, arrowroot flour, baking powder and salt and mix 5 sec/speed 4.
  4. Add butter and mix 5 sec/speed 4.
  5. Add egg and milk and mix 10 sec/speed 4, or until just incorporated.
  6. Transfer dough onto a silicone bread mat or work surface dusted with arrowroot flour and press out to approx. 2 cm thickness. Dip a scone cutter (6 cm) into arrowroot flour and cut out scones (approx. 8 in total). Repeat rolling and cutting scones until all dough has been used. Brush with milk and bake for 10-12 minutes (220°C), or until golden.
  7. Serve warm with butter, jam and cream.

Useful Items

baking tray (20 x 30 cm), baking paper, rolling pin, pastry brush, silicone bread mat, round cookie cutter (6 cm), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • To make your own rice flour, place 250 g rice into mixing bowl and mill 2 min/speed 10.
  • To make your own Gluten free baking powder, please refer to the recipe on this recipe chip.
