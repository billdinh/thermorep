Baked Parmesan Fish with Sweet Potato Mash

Ingredients

  • 45 g Parmesan cheese, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 80 g sliced white bread, torn in pieces
  • ½ tsp paprika
  • 90 g milk
  • 1 tsp cornflour
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt, or to taste
  • 45 g unsalted butter, diced, plus extra for greasing
  • 6 fish fillets, thick (e.g. cod, haddock, approx. 160 g each)
  • 700 g water
  • 750 g sweet potatoes, peeled, diced (2-3 cm)
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper, or to taste

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C and grease a large baking dish.
  2. Place Parmesan in mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 10.
  3. Add bread and paprika then grate 3 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a wide, shallow dish.
  4. Place 80 g milk, cornflour and ½ tsp salt in mixing bowl then mix 30 sec/speed 3. Transfer to another wide, shallow dish.
  5. Place 25 g butter in mixing bowl and melt 3 min/95°C/speed .
  6. Dip fish fillets in milk mixture then in Parmesan breadcrumbs. Arrange in prepared dish. Drizzle over melted butter then bake for 25-30 minutes (190°C) until cooked (see tip). Meanwhile, make mash.
  7. Place water and sweet potatoes in mixing bowl then cook 12-15 min/100°C//speed  until tender. Drain sweet potatoes in Varoma dish then return to mixing bowl.
  8. Add 20 g butter, 10 g milk, ½ tsp salt and pepper then blend 10 sec/speed 6.
  9. Serve sweet potato mash with baked fish.

Useful Items

large baking dish, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • A take on classic fish and chips. In this healthier alternative, delicate fish fillets are lightly coated in Parmesan breadcrumbs then baked until crisp and golden, while a creamy sweet potato mash is made in the Thermomix®. Serve with green peas or a side salad.
  • Baking time will depend on the thickness of the fish fillets. Adjust time accordingly until fish is cooked.
