Coppa del nonno

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 100 g caster sugar
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract
  • 500 g pouring (whipping) cream, chilled (see Tips)

Preparation

  1. Place egg, sugar and vanilla into mixing bowl and mix 20 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Add cream and beat 30-2 min/speed 4, watching carefully through hole in mixing bowl lid to avoid over-whipping, until a smooth, silky consistency is achieved. Remove butterfly whisk.
  3. Serve immediately or transfer into a freezable storage container and refrigerate or freeze until ready to use.

Useful Items

freezable storage container, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • The beating time in step 2 will depend on the freshness of your cream. The fresher the cream, the longer the beating time required. Check your cream at regular intervals through the hole in mixing bowl lid while beating to avoid over-whipping your cream.
  • Coppa del nonno can be used as a topping or filling for cakes; or as ice cream when frozen.
  • Coffee cream: replace vanilla extract with 5 g granulated coffee and proceed as per recipe.
  • Chocolate cream: replace vanilla extract with 20 g good quality cocoa and proceed as per recipe.
  • Frangelico® cream: add 1 tbsp Frangelico® with the cream in step 2 and proceed as per recipe.
