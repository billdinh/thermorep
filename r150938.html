Pistachio Cheesecakes with Fresh Mango

Ingredients

  • 40 g pistachio nuts, unsalted, shelled
  • butter, for greasing
  • 8 fresh mint leaves
  • 1 lime, zest only
  • 2 Tbsp cornflour
  • 100 g raw cane sugar
  • 250 g cream cheese
  • 250 g ricotta cheese
  • 4 eggs
  • 500 g water
  • fresh mint, plus extra to garnish
  • 1 ripe mango, diced
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1906 kJ / 455 kcal
Protein
15 g
Carbohydrates

Preparation

  1. Place pistachio nuts in mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 7. Generously grease 6 ramekins (Ø 8 cm, height 4 cm, see tip) with butter, add chopped pistachio nuts and rotate ramekins to coat bottoms and sides. Tip out any excess nuts from ramekins and set aside to garnish.
  2. Place mint, lime zest, cornflour and sugar in mixing bowl and grind 15 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add cream cheese, ricotta and eggs and blend 10 sec/speed 4.5. Transfer to prepared ramekins, filling up to 1 cm from the top. Cover each ramekin with heat-resistant cling film. Arrange 4 ramekins in Varoma dish and 2 on Varoma tray. Clean mixing bowl.
  4. Place water in mixing bowl and set Varoma in position. Steam 25 min/Varoma/speed 1. Set Varoma aside uncovered and allow ramekins to cool for 30 minutes. Transfer cheesecakes to fridge and chill for at least one hour. Once chilled, remove cling film and carefully unmould cheesecakes onto dessert plates. Serve cheesecakes garnished with diced mango, mint leaves and reserved chopped pistachio nuts.

Useful Items

heat-resistant cling film, ramekins (Ø 8 cm, height 4 cm), refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Pistachios and mangoes give a fresh, exotic flavour to this dessert and steaming provides the dreamy texture. An added benefit is that you can make them up to two days in advance and have them ready in the fridge as an easy and elegant dessert.
  • Instead of diced mango, serve cheesecakes with mango coulis: place 250 g fresh mango, cut in pieces, 75 g sugar and 15 g lime (or lemon) juice in mixing bowl and blend 30 sec/speed 5-10, increasing speed gradually. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Cook 5 min/90°C/speed 2. Allow sauce to cool before drizzling 1-2 Tbsp over or around each cheesecake.
    - Garnish with seasonal fruit of choice if mangoes are not in season. Frozen mango can be used as well; defrost before dicing.
  • These cheesecakes can be steamed in other small dishes or containers, such as dariole moulds, espresso cups, glasses, verrines or silicone muffin moulds, provided they are heatproof. Steaming time may need increasing by 5-10 minutes if the containers are larger than those recommended in the recipe. If using ramekins or moulds no taller than 4 cm, place them in both the Varoma dish and tray as described in the recipe. If using taller moulds, place in Varoma dish only, and if they don’t all fit, steam them in two batches, adding water as needed to mixing bowl.
    - Make cheesecakes ahead of time and keep refrigerated for 1-2 days.
