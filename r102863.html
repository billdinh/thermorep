Chinese Noodles with Vegetables

Ingredients

  • 40 g carrot, cut in pieces
  • 20 g dried shiitake mushrooms, rehydrated in hot water
  • 20 g dried mushrooms, (e.g. wood ear mushrooms), rehydrated in hot water
  • 40 g king oyster mushroom, fresh
  • 40 g fresh enoki mushrooms
  • 90 g tomatoes, quartered
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 10 g soy sauce
  • 1000 g water
  • 2 vegetable stock cubes (each for 0.5 l)
  • 200 g wheat noodles, thick
  • 100 g firm tofu, coarsely chopped
  • 50 g mangetouts
  • 30 g celery stalk, cut in pieces (2-3 cm)
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt, or to taste
  • ¼ tsp ground pepper, or to taste
  • 1 Tbsp vinegar

Preparation

  1. Place carrots, drained shiitake mushrooms, drained wood ear mushrooms, oyster mushrooms, enoki mushrooms and tomatoes in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add olive oil and soy sauce then, without measuring cup, sauté 2 min/Varoma//speed 1.
  3. Add water and stock cubes then simmer 15 min/Varoma//speed .
  4. Add noodles and cook 3 min/Varoma//speed .
  5. Add tofu, mangetout, celery, salt, pepper and vinegar then mix well with spatula. Cook 2 min/Varoma/speed . Transfer to bowls and serve.

Hints & Tricks

  • Vary mushrooms according to what is available to you.
  • For a different texture, fry the tofu and serve on top of the noodles - for a different flavour use marinated tofu.
