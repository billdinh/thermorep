Pulled pork with steamed buns

Ingredients

Steamed buns

  • oil, for greasing
  • 300 g water
  • 3 tsp dried instant yeast
  • 500 g plain flour
  • 2 tsp fine salt

Pulled pork

  • 1000 - 1200 g pork shoulder, boneless
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 120 g red onion (approx.1 onion), cut into halves
  • 110 g carrot (approx. 1 carrot), cut into quarters
  • 80 g celery stalks (approx. 2 stalks), cut into quarters
  • 80 g fennel bulb (approx. 1/2 bulb), trimmed and thinly sliced
  • 2 tart apples (e.g. Granny Smith), core removed and cut into eighths
  • 40 g olive oil
  • 2 tsp ground smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp mustard
  • ½ tsp dried chilli flakes
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 80 g vinegar
  • 50 g molasses
  • 1 tbsp chicken stock paste, homemade
  • 1 tbsp tomato purée
  • 1 - 2 tbsp milk, for brushing
  • salad leaves to taste, to serve

Preparation

Steamed buns

  1. Grease a large bowl, Varoma dish and Varoma tray with oil and set aside. Place water and yeast into mixing bowl and warm 3 min/37°C/speed 1.
  2. Add flour and salt and knead 2 min 30 sec/. Transfer dough into large oiled bowl, cover with plastic wrap and leave to prove in a warm place until doubled in size (approx. 1 hour).
  3. Transfer proved dough onto lightly floured work surface. Divide dough into 12 equal-sized portions, roll into balls and place into greased Varoma dish and onto Varoma tray. Set Varoma aside. Rinse and dry mixing bowl.

Pulled pork

  1. Cut the shoulder meat into cubes (6-7 cm), then transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Line a baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  3. Place garlic and red onion into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7.
  4. Add carrot and celery and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add fennel, apple and 40 g olive oil sauté 10 min/Varoma//speed 1.
  6. Add reserved pork shoulder meat and sauté 2 min/Varoma//speed 1.
  7. Add paprika, mustard, chilli flakes, brown sugar, vinegar, molasses, stock paste and tomato purée, then place Varoma with buns into position and steam 30 min/Varoma//speed 1.
  8. Set Varoma with buns aside, then place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and continue to cook 40 min/80°C//speed . Meanwhile, preheat oven to 200°C. Transfer steamed buns onto prepared baking tray (30 x 40 cm), then brush with milk and bake for 5-10 minutes (200°C) or until golden brown. Transfer buns onto a wire rack and set aside.
  9. Remove simmering basket, insert measuring cup and shred 3 sec//speed 5. Transfer pork into a large bowl and cover to keep warm.
  10. Fill buns with shredded pork and serve warm with salad leaves on the side.

Useful Items

large bowl, pastry brush, wire rack, baking tray (30 cm x 40 cm), baking paper, cling film

Hints & Tricks

  • Substitute the salad greens for Coleslaw in your pulled pork buns.
  • Serve leftover pulled pork over rice, with corn tortillas or in sandwiches the next day.
  • Cooked pulled pork can easily be frozen and reheated later.
