Zucchini Bread

Ingredients

  • 3 oz unsalted butter, room temperature, plus extra to grease
  • 16 oz zucchini cut into rounds (approx. ½ in.)
  • 4 oz sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 oz plain yogurt
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • 7 ½ oz all-purpose flour
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • &frac18; tsp ground nutmeg
  • 3 oz nuts (optional)

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease a loaf pan (approx. 9 in. x 5 in.) and line with parchment paper, set aside.
  2. Place zucchini into mixing bowl and grate 3-5 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a cheesecloth (approx. 4-6 layers) and squeeze out as much liquid as possible. Discard liquid and set aside zucchini.
  3. Place sugar and eggs into mixing bowl and mix 20 sec/speed 6.
  4. Add yogurt, butter and vanilla and mix 10 sec/speed 6.
  5. Add flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon and nutmeg and mix 20 sec/speed 5.
  6. Add nuts or raisins (if using) and reserved zucchini and mix 5-8 sec/speed 3.5.
  7. Transfer batter to prepared pan and bake 50-60 minutes (350°F) or until an inserted toothpick comes out clean. Allow to rest 10 minutes before transferring to a cooling rack. Allow to cool completely and slice to serve.

Useful Items

parchment paper, cooling rack, toothpick or skewer, loaf pan (approx. 9 in. x 5 in.), cheesecloth, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Walnuts and raisins are common in zucchini bread, but try pine nuts, Brazil nuts, currants and even some freshly chopped herbs like rosemary and lemon thyme.
  • If you want to glaze your zucchini loaf, wait until it is completely cooled.
