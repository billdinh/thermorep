Gaji Namul (Steamed Aubergine Side Dish)

Ingredients

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 20 g dark soy sauce
  • 15 g sesame oil
  • 10 g toasted sesame seeds
  • ½ tsp caster sugar
  • ½ tsp Korean chilli flakes (gochugaru) (see tip)
  • 1000 g water
  • 2 aubergines (approx. 700 g), cut in wedges (2 cm x 15 cm) (see tip)
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 2 spring onions, thinly sliced (5 mm)
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
393 kJ / 95 kcal
Protein
2 g
Carbohydrates
6 g
Fat
6 g

Preparation

  1. Place garlic in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add soy sauce, oil, sesame seeds, sugar and chilli flakes then mix 5 sec//speed 3. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place water in mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish into position, weigh in aubergines then distribute between Varoma dish and Varoma tray. Season with salt and pepper, insert Varoma tray, cover Varoma then steam 18-20 min/Varoma/speed 2 until soft and cooked.
  4. Transfer aubergine to a serving dish, pour over reserved sauce and garnish with spring onions. Serve warm.
