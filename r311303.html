Oaty Ginger Crunch

Ingredients

Oat Base

  • 200 g unsalted butter, diced
  • 80 g golden syrup
  • 260 g plain flour
  • 260 g light brown sugar
  • 120 g rolled oats
  • 2 tsp ground ginger
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt

Chocolate Drizzle

  • 60 g white chocolate, small pieces or callets

Preparation

Oat Base

  1. Preheat oven to 170°C and line a square baking tin (20 cm) with baking paper.
  2. Place butter and syrup in mixing bowl then melt 2 min 30 sec/80°C/speed 2.
  3. Add flour, sugar, oats, ginger, baking powder and salt then mix 15 sec//speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then mix again 15 sec//speed 4. Transfer to prepared tin and flatten to form an even layer. Bake for 20-25 minutes (170°C) until golden brown. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl.
  4. Remove base from oven and leave to cool in tin then prepare chocolate drizzle.

Chocolate Drizzle

  1. Place chocolate in mixing bowl and grate 8 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then melt 2 min/40°C/speed 2.
  2. Drizzle over cooled oat base then cut into squares before completely set. Serve or store in an airtight container until needed.

Useful Items

square baking tin (20 cm), baking paper, airtight container, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • A twist on a traditional, sticky flapjack, these oaty squares make a perfect accompaniment to an afternoon cup of tea. We’ve drizzled over melted white chocolate for an extra finishing touch but it’s just as yummy without!
  • Adjust the quantity of ginger according to taste.
