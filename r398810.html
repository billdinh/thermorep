Black Bean Soup with Parmesan Crisps

Ingredients

  • 4 oz Parmesan cheese
  • 9 oz yellow onions, quartered
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 2 oz extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 - 3 pinches dried chili flakes
  • 1 tsp sweet smoked paprika
  • 29 oz canned diced tomatoes
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp salt, to taste
  • 30 oz canned black beans, drained
  • 14 oz vegetable stock
  • 2 oz orange juice

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Place a silicone mat on a baking sheet and set aside. With a julienne peeler, grate Parmesan. Create a 7 inch circle of prepared cheese directly on the silicone mat and bake for 12 minutes (400°F) or until golden and crisp. Set cheese crisp aside to cool.
  2. Place onions, garlic, olive oil and chili flakes into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and cook 15 min/212°F/speed 1.
  3. Add paprika, tomatoes, oregano and salt and cook 15 min/212°F/speed 1.
  4. Add black beans, vegetable stock and orange juice and simmer 10 min/212°F//speed 1. Stir through with spatula to combine.
  5. Break cooled Parmesan crisp into six pieces. Pour Black Bean Soup into individual bowls, garnish with cheese crisps and serve.

Useful Items

julienne peeler, silicone mat, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • To replace canned black beans with dried: place 10 oz rinsed black beans and 30 oz water into mixing bowl and cook 60 min/212°F/speed 1. Drain before using in recipe.
