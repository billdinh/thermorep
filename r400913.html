Devilled Eggs

Ingredients

  • 400 g water
  • 6 large eggs
  • 30 g mayonnaise
  • 1 tsp white vinegar
  • 1 tsp American mustard
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper, or to taste
  • smoked paprika, for garnishing
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
249 kJ / 60 kcal
Protein
4 g
Carbohydrates
1 g
Fat
4 g

Preparation

  1. Place water in mixing bowl then insert simmering basket. Place eggs in simmering basket and cook 14 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  2. Immediately remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and place simmering basket with eggs under cold running water to stop them cooking. Crack egg shells under cold running water. Peel eggs carefully then gently pat dry with paper towels. Slice eggs in half lengthways then gently remove cooked egg yolks and set aside. Place egg whites on a serving platter. Clean mixing bowl.
  3. Place reserved egg yolks, mayonnaise, white vinegar, mustard, salt and pepper in mixing bowl then mix 20 sec/speed 3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then mix again 10 sec/speed 3.
  4. Evenly distribute heaped teaspoons of yolk mixture into cooked egg whites. Sprinkle with paprika and serve immediately or place in fridge until needed.

Useful Items

paper towels, serving platter, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Substitute yellow mustard with mustard of choice (e.g. Dijon mustard).
  • Vinegar can be substituted with lemon juice.
  • For more attractive presentation, spoon egg yolk mixture into a piping bag and pipe filling into the cavity of the egg white halves.
