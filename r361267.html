Huli Huli Chicken Skewers

Ingredients

  • water, to soak skewers
  • 4 green onions, trimmed, cut in pieces (1 in.)
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 oz fresh ginger root, peeled
  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • 10 oz pineapple juice
  • 4 oz dark brown sugar
  • 4 oz chicken broth, reduced sodium
  • 3 ½ oz dry sherry
  • 2 oz ketchup
  • 2 oz soy sauce, reduced sodium
  • 1 oz Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tsp all-purpose flour
  • 3 red bell peppers, stemmed, seeded, cubed (1 in.)
  • 42 oz boneless, skinless chicken breast, cubed (1 in.)
  • 3 red onions, cubed (1 in.)
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
582 kJ / 139 kcal
Protein
15 g

Preparation

  1. Place skewers into a shallow roasting pan and cover with water. Set aside to soak.
  2. Place green onions, garlic and ginger into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add sesame oil and sauté 3 min/250°F/speed 1.
  4. Add pineapple juice, brown sugar, chicken broth, sherry, ketchup, soy and Worcestershire, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 25 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  5. Add flour (avoiding blades), place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 3 min/Varoma/speed 3. Evenly divide Huli Huli Sauce into two separate bowls and set aside.
  6. Preheat grill or oven to 375°F. Meanwhile thread peppers, chicken and onion onto soaked skewers.
  7. Grill chicken skewers and use half the reserved sauce to baste or bake for 25-30 minutes (375°F) until cooked through, basting every 10 minutes. Serve Chicken Skewers hot with remaining reserved Huli Huli Sauce.

Useful Items

roasting pan, wooden skewers, basting brush, grill or oven
