Garlic Shrimp

Ingredients

  • 32 oz shrimp, raw, peeled and deveined (thawed if frozen)
  • ½ - 1 oz garlic cloves (approx. 5-8 cloves)
  • 2 oz shallots, cut into pieces
  • ½ oz parsley, leaves only
  • 8 oz unsalted butter
  • 2 oz seafood stock
  • 2 oz lemon juice
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper

Preparation

  1. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh in shrimp, secure Varoma lid and set aside.
  2. Place garlic, shallots and parsley into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add butter, seafood stock, lemon juice, salt and pepper. Place Varoma into position and steam 10 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove lid and stir seafood with spatula to ensure even cooking. Secure Varoma lid and steam a further 5-7 min/Varoma/speed 1 until shrimp is cooked through. Remove Varoma and set aside.
  4. Insert measuring cup and mix 5 sec/speed 3. Transfer garlic sauce and steamed shrimp to serving dish and serve immediately.

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with steamed rice, linguine or simply with buttered crusty bread.
