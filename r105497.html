Oatmeal Raisin Cookies

Ingredients

  • 3 ½ oz unsalted butter, cut into pieces
  • 3 oz sugar
  • 4 oz brown sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tsp homemade vanilla sugar (see Tip)
  • 5 oz all-purpose flour
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 6 ½ oz old fashioned rolled oats
  • 5 oz raisins
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
349 kJ / 106 kcal
Protein
2 g
Carbohydrates
18 g
Fat
3 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line two baking sheets with parchment paper and set aside.
  2. Place butter, sugar, brown sugar, egg and vanilla sugar (see Tip) into mixing bowl and mix 2 min/speed 3.
  3. Add flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt, oats, raisins and knead 15 sec/.
  4. Form dough with aid of a tablespoon, using 1-2 heaping tbsps per cookie, depending on desired size. Roll into balls and place 2 inches apart onto prepared baking sheets. Bake one sheet after another for 10-12 minutes each (350°F) until cookies are golden but still moist beneath cracks. Allow to rest on baking sheets for 5 minutes before transferring to a cooling rack to cool completely. Once cool, serve or store in an airtight container.

Useful Items

cooling rack, parchment paper, airtight container, baking sheets, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • If living at high altitude let cookies cool for 10 minutes in an open oven before removing to the cooling rack. This helps avoid shrinkage.
  • Make your Vanilla Sugar before you start the recipe following the Vanilla Sugar recipe in The Basic Cookbook.
  • Stir in chocolate chips (approx. 3 oz) while adding oats and raisins.
