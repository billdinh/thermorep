Pear and Gorgonzola

Ingredients

  • 60 g onions, quartered
  • 30 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 350 g risotto rice
  • 50 g dry white wine
  • 800 g vegetable stock, boiling
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt or to taste
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper or to taste
  • 200 g gorgonzola, cut in small pieces
  • 200 g pears, peeled and cut in pieces (1 cm)
  • 30 g butter
Nutrition

Calories
1182 kJ / 283 kcal

7.7 g

19.36 g

Preparation

  1. Place onions and oil in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then sauté 3 min/100°C/speed 1.
  2. Add rice and sauté, without measuring cup, 3 min/100°C//speed 1.
  3. Add wine and sauté, without measuring cup, 1 min/100°C//speed 1.
  4. Add stock, salt and pepper then cook, with measuring cup on, 11 min/100°C//speed 1.
  5. Add gorgonzola and pears then cook 2 min/100°C//speed 1.
  6. Add butter then transfer to a bowl and mix gently before serving immediately.
