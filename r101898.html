Veganella - Vegan Mozzarella

Ingredients

  • 100 g raw cashew nuts, unsalted
  • 2 level Tbsp psyllium husks
  • 400 g water, cold, plus extra for soaking
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 2 Tbsp lemon juice (25 g)
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
688 kJ / 164 kcal
Protein
6 g
Carbohydrates
6 g
Fat
12 g

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in cashew nuts. Cover with cold water and leave to soak for 2 hours. Meanwhile,place psyllium husks in mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 10. Tip out into a bowl then place bowl on mixing bowl lid. Weigh in 400 g water and set aside to soak until a jelly-like consistency (approx. 2 hours).
  2. After 2 hours, drain soaked nuts through simmering basket. Place nuts in mixing bowl then add soaked psyllium husk mixture, salt and lemon juice. Blend 1 min/speed 10. Transfer mixture to 2 small bowls (approx. Ø 12 cm) or 4 teacups, smooth with the back of a wet spoon and leave to set in fridge for at least 4 hours.
  3. To serve, ease cheese out of mould then cut into slices - works well served seasoned with herbs, chilli flakes, basil pesto or balsamic vinegar (or see tip).

Useful Items

2 small bowls (approx. Ø 12 cm) or 4 teacups, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • If using powdered psyllium husks, omit grinding in step 2.
  • You can serve the Veganella as a caprese salad (with tomato slices, balsamic vinegar and basil pesto).
  • The Veganella is also suitable for melting (e.g. on pizzas or in casseroles), but should only be added for a few minutes towards the end of baking time.
