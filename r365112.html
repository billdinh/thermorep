Vegetable and Hazelnut Cheese Muffins

Ingredients

  • 60 g medium-hard cheese (e.g. Cheddar, Gruyère), cut in pieces (1.5 cm)
  • 100 g carrots, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 70 g hazelnuts, with skin
  • 100 g sunflower oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 180 g milk
  • 2 medium eggs
  • 250 g plain flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • ½ tsp ground cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 250 g frozen green peas, defrosted

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C and grease or line with paper cases a 12 hole muffin tin.
  2. Place cheese in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8.
  3. Add carrots and hazelnuts then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add oil, milk and eggs then mix 8 sec/speed 3.
  5. Add flour, baking powder, salt and cayenne pepper (if using) then mix 10 sec/speed 5.
  6. Add peas and, with aid of spatula, mix 10 sec//speed 3. Transfer to prepared tray, filling holes or cases to ¾ full then bake for 20-25 minutes (180°C) until cooked and golden.
  7. Serve warm or at room temperature.

Useful Items

paper muffin cases, 12 hole muffin tin, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Vegetable cheese muffins with pancetta: Reduce salt to 1 tsp in step 5, then add 100 g pancetta or ham pieces with peas in step 6 and mix 10 seconds longer.
