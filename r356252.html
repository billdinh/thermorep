California Stuffed Artichokes

Ingredients

  • 2 globe artichokes
  • 1 lemon, halved, peeled (reserve thin peel only, no pith)
  • 35 oz water, plus extra to cover artichokes
  • 3 oz Parmesan cheese, cubed
  • ¼ tsp salt, to taste
  • 1 oz raw cashews, unsalted
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 oz parsley, leaves and tender stems
  • 1 oz cilantro, leaves and tender stems
  • 5 oz olive oil
  • 4 oz red onions, in pieces
  • 4 oz fresh sweet corn kernels
  • 2 oz sun-dried tomatoes, in oil, julienned, patted dry
  • 5 oz quinoa
  • 2 tbsp bread crumbs

Preparation

  1. Prepare artichokes (see Tip). Rub cut artichokes with lemon. Transfer prepared artichokes and lemon halves to a large bowl, cover with water and set aside.
  2. Place Parmesan cheese, reserved lemon peel and salt into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 10.
  3. Add cashews, garlic, parsley, cilantro and olive oil and chop 20 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place red onions into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add corn and sun-dried tomatoes and cook 5 min/250°F//speed 0.5. Transfer to bowl with cheese mixture and toss to combine.
  6. Place 35 oz water into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket, weigh in quinoa and rinse 10 sec/speed 5. Place Varoma dish into position, place in prepared artichokes cut side down, secure Varoma lid and steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 2. Carefully remove Varoma. Remove simmering basket with quinoa and set aside. Place Varoma with artichokes back into position and steam a further 10 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  7. Add cooked quinoa to bowl with cheese-vegetable mixture and toss to combine. Carefully spoon mixture into center of artichokes, then pull back leaves and stuff filling between petals. Place into baking dish (2 qt.), top with bread crumbs and place under broiler until browned (approx. 3-5 min.) Serve hot.

Useful Items

large bowl, baking dish (2 qt.), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • To prepare artichokes for steaming: pull off outermost leaves/petals until you reach the lighter yellow leaves (approx. 2-3 layers). Cut the tip off each leaf/petal to remove the thorny part with kitchen shears. Cut off top third of the artichoke. Cut off stem right at the base of the artichoke. Use a teaspoon to remove the fibrous, feathery "choke" buried in the center and discard. Now the artichokes are ready for steaming.
  • To make ahead: prepare and stuff artichokes, cover with foil and refrigerate overnight. To heat, top with bread crumbs and bake for 20-25 minutes (375°F) until heated through.
