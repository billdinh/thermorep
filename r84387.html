Marinaded Tofu with a Tomato and Aubergine Sauce

Ingredients

Tofu

  • 680 g firm tofu, cut in cubes (3 cm)
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 lemon, grated zest and juice
  • 1 bunch fresh parsley, (5-10 g)
  • 10 g olive oil
  • 10 g balsamic vinegar
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper

Tomato and Aubergine Sauce

  • 40 g olive oil
  • 600 g aubergine, cut in pieces (2-3 cm)
  • 600 g fresh ripe tomatoes, quartered
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt, or to taste
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper, or to taste

To serve

  • 1 - 2 Tbsp olive oil, for frying
  • 4 spring onions, finely sliced

Preparation

Tofu

  1. Place a shallow bowl on mixing bowl lid. Weigh tofu into shallow bowl lid then set aside.
  2. Place garlic, lemon zest and juice, parsley, oil, balsamic vinegar, salt and pepper in mixing bowl then mix 3 sec/speed 8. Transfer to bowl with tofu and mix gently until tofu is coated, then cover and leave to marinate while making the sauce.

Tomato and Aubergine Sauce

  1. Place oil, aubergines and tomatoes in mixing bowl then cook 30 min/110°C/speed 1 replacing measuring cup with simmering basket on mixing bowl lid to allow sauce to reduce.
  2. Add salt and pepper and stir with spatula, then transfer to serving bowl and keep warm.

To serve

  1. Heat oil in a non-stick frying pan and fry marinaded tofu for 5-6 minutes until slightly crispy. Arrange over tomato and aubergine sauce in serving dish, scatter with chopped spring onions and serve immediately.

Useful Items

shallow bowl or dish, serving bowl, frying pan, hob

Hints & Tricks

  • Delicious served with steamed greens or spinach.
