Pork Chops with Orange

Ingredients

  • 1 orange, peeled and pith removed, in 8 segments (approx. 100 g), plus extra segments for garnish
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 Tbsp cider vinegar
  • 1 Tbsp wholegrain mustard
  • 3 Tbsp runny honey
  • 1 tsp olive oil, plus extra for cooking
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt, or to taste
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper, or to taste
  • 4 pork chops (approx. 150 g each)
  • ½ tsp dark brown muscovado sugar
  • 2 Tbsp water

Preparation

  1. Place orange segments and garlic in mixing bowl then chop Turbo/1 sec/1-2 times. Scrape down sides and lid of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add vinegar, mustard, honey, oil, salt and pepper in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 4.
  3. Place pork chops in a large dish and pour marinade over. Cover then set aside in fridge for at least 2 hours.
  4. Towards the end of marinating time, heat oil in a griddle pan over a medium heat. Cook pork (reserving marinade) for 12-15 minutes, turning once until cooked as desired with distinctive griddle lines on meat. Meanwhile, place reserved marinade in mixing bowl.
  5. Add sugar and water then, without measuring cup, reduce 5-7 min/100°C/speed 1 until sauce has thickened to desired consistency.
  6. Serve garnished with orange segments and a choice of salad and/or potatoes.
