Potato cakes

Ingredients

  • 1000 g potatoes, cut in pieces
  • 150 g onions, cut in halves
  • 1 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 20 g cornflour
  • 20 g oat flakes
  • 1 egg
  • frying oil
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1818 kJ / 434 kcal

8 g

Preparation

  1. Place potatoes, onions, lemon juice, salt, black pepper, cornflour, oat flakes and egg in mixing bowl and chop 12 sec/speed 5 with aid of spatula.
  2. Pour frying oil into a hot non-stick frying pan, covering the base with a thin layer, and heat. Place 2 heaped Tbsp of mixture per potato cake into the pan and using a spoon, flatten each heap to form a pancake shape (Ø 10 cm). Fry potato cakes on both sides until golden brown and crisp. Drain on paper towels. Serve hot.

Useful Items

non-stick frying pan, frying pan, paper towels, turner / fish slice, hob

Hints & Tricks

  • A traditional German dish, served with sweet or savoury side dishes depending on the region.
  • As the batter can be prepared in seconds, it is easy to repeat this recipe quickly to increase portions for a party.
  • Serve with apple sauce or for a savoury alternative, with sour cream, smoked salmon or bacon.
  • Use fresh potatoes. If you clean the potatoes well, there is no need to peel them.
  • Depending on the kind of potatoes used, and how long the batter is left to rest before frying, you may need to add more cornflour or oat flakes, or to remove excess moisture from the batter with a spoon.
  • Vegetable potato cakes: replace ⅓ of the potatoes with vegetables (e.g. carrots, kohlrabi, cauliflower, courgettes etc.). Double the amount of starch and oat flakes. Season with ground mixed pepper and serve with a sour cream herb dip.
