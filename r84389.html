Polenta with Ratatouille

Ingredients

  • 50 g Parmesan cheese, cut in pieces

Polenta

  • 180 g polenta
  • 20 g butter, plus extra for greasing
  • 700 g water
  • 1 cube vegetable stock cube (for 0.5 l)

Ratatouille

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 100 g onions, quartered
  • 25 g olive oil
  • 200 g peppers yellow or orange, cut in strips (5 mm)
  • 200 g courgettes, sliced (3 mm)
  • 250 g aubergines, cubed (1.5 cm)
  • 300 g tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 pinch sugar
  • 1 Tbsp herbes de Provence, dried
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 250 g cream cheese

Preparation

  1. Grease a baking dish (approx. 30 cm x 20 cm x 5 cm).
  2. Place Parmesan in mixing bowl and grate 15 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.

Polenta

  1. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid. Weigh in polenta and set aside.
  2. Place water and stock cube in mixing bowl then heat 8 min/100°C/speed 1.
  3. Add butter, set on speed 3 without measuring cup and slowly add polenta through hole in mixing bowl lid. Then cook 10 min/80°C/speed 3.5. Transfer polenta to prepared baking dish and even the top with a wet spatula. Clean mixing bowl.

Ratatouille

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C.
  2. Place garlic and onions in mixing bowl then chop 4 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add oil and sauté 3 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  4. Add peppers, courgettes, aubergines, tomatoes, sugar, herbs, salt and pepper then cook 15 min/90°C//speed .
  5. Spread vegetables on top of polenta, then scatter with cream cheese and grated Parmesan. Bake 20 minutes (200°C) and serve hot.

Useful Items

bowl, rectangular baking dish (approx. 30 cm x 20 cm x 5 cm), gratin dish (30 x 25 x 6 cm), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Use other herbs to flavour the polenta e.g. oregano or majoram.
  • Try using different cheeses; Mozzarella or ricotta work well instead of cream cheese.
