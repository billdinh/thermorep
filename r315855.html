Vegan mozzarella

Ingredients

Soaking

  • 120 g raw cashews (see Tips)
  • ½ tsp salt
  • filtered water, for soaking
  • 3 tsp lecithin granules (see Tips)
  • 2 tbsp boiling water

Cheese

  • 100 g coconut oil
  • 35 g tapioca starch
  • 2 tsp agar agar (see Tips)
  • 25 g sauerkraut
  • 1 tbsp nutritional yeast (see Tips)
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice

Preparation

Soaking

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh cashews into it. Add salt, cover with water and set aside to soak for 3-6 hours.
  2. Using simmering basket, drain and rinse cashews.
  3. Place lecithin granules and boiling water into a small bowl and set aside for 30 minutes.

Cheese

  1. Place coconut oil into mixing bowl and heat 2 min/60°C/speed 2. Transfer into a jug and set aside.
  2. Place drained cashews and soaked lecithin granules into mixing bowl and blend 1 min/speed 8.
  3. Add tapioca starch, agar agar, sauerkraut, nutritional yeast and lemon juice and cook 4 min/100°C/speed 4.
  4. Insert butterfly whisk. Emulsify 5 min/speed 4, very slowly pouring reserved coconut oil onto mixing bowl lid, letting it trickle into mixing bowl. Remove butterfly whisk. Transfer into a sterilised sealable container and place into refrigerator until ready to use.

Useful Items

bowl, sealable container, jug, small bowl, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • If you don't have time to soak/activate your cashews, soak nuts in boiling water for 30 minutes, then drain and use as directed.
  • Lecithin granules have a subtle, nutty flavour and are versatile enough to add a creamy texture (when soaked) or a crunchy texture (when sprinkled) to dishes. They may also lower bad cholesterol levels, improve heart health and improve digestion. You can find them at some major supermarkets, chemists and health food stores.
  • We prefer to use refined coconut oil as it has a milder flavour. However, both refined and unrefined coconut oil can be used in this recipe.
  • With a cheesy, umami flavour, nutritional yeast contains all nine essential amino acids, is a great source of B vitamins and is often fortified with vitamin B12 (important for those following a vegan diet). You can find nutritional yeast at health food stores or online.
  • Agar agar yields the best results when combined with hot liquids, then transferred into the refrigerator to set. You can find agar agar powder at health food stores, some Asian food stores and online.
  • To make your own sauerkraut, refer to the recipe on Cookidoo® (www.cookidoo.com.au).
  • You can store this cheese in the refrigerator for up to 1 week.
