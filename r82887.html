Brown Bread Ice Cream with Raspberries

Ingredients

  • 660 g whole milk
  • 220 g double cream
  • 175 g sugar
  • 4 egg yolks, from large eggs
  • 75 g wholemeal bread, crusts removed
  • 125 g raspberries, fresh or frozen, for serving
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1415 kJ / 338 kcal
Protein
6.3 g
Carbohydrates
32.1 g

20.5 g

Preparation

  1. Place milk, cream, 50 g sugar and egg yolks in mixing bowl then cook 8 min/90°C/speed 2. Transfer to a bowl and set aside to cool to room temperature. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl and prepare caramel breadcrumbs.
  2. Preheat grill to 220°C and line a baking tray with baking paper.
  3. Place bread and remaining 125 g sugar in mixing bowl then grate 10 sec/speed 7. Spread thinly on prepared baking tray then place under grill and allow to brown (approx. 10 minutes) stirring every 2 minutes. Remove and allow to cool slightly.
  4. Place caramel breadcrumbs in mixing bowl and crush 5 sec/speed 5. Stir caramel breadcrumbs into custard mix, then pour mixture, up to 4 cm deep, into shallow freezer-proof containers. Cover then, once cool, place in freezer until solid (approx. 10 hours).
  5. Before serving, remove ice cream from freezer and cut into 3 cm chunks. Place half the ice cream pieces in mixing bowl and churn 20 sec/speed 6. Transfer ice cream to a bowl.
  6. Place remaining ice cream pieces in mixing bowl and churn 20 sec/speed 6 then add to ice cream bowl. Serve immediately with raspberries.

Useful Items

baking tray and paper, freezer-proof containers, long sharp knife, grill, freezer

Hints & Tricks

  • For a firmer consistency, place the churned ice cream in the freezer for 15 minutes before serving.
  • This is one that has to be made to truly appreciate the beautifully creamy ice cream with a nutty, sugary flavour. With golden caramel flecks running through it and served with fresh raspberries, this is a really delicious dessert option.
