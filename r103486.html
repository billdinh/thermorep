Paprika chicken with sour cream sauce

Ingredients

  • 6 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 1 brown onion (approx. 150 g), cut into halves
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 3 tsp sweet paprika
  • 40 g tomato paste
  • 100 g white wine
  • 50 g water
  • 2 tsp Chicken stock paste (see Tips)
  • ½ tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 100 g sour cream, plus extra for serving
  • 1000 g boneless skinless chicken thighs, trimmed (optional) and cut into halves

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Place parsley into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place onion and garlic into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add oil, paprika and tomato paste and sauté 3 min/Varoma//speed 2.
  4. Add wine and cook 2 min/Varoma//speed 2, without measuring cup.
  5. Add water, stock paste, pepper and sour cream and cook 2 min/100°C/speed 2.
  6. Place chicken pieces into a casserole dish (approx. 23 cm) and pour sauce over chicken. Bake, uncovered, for 1 hour (180°C). Garnish with reserved parsley and serve with extra sour cream.

Useful Items

bowl, round casserole dish (23 cm), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with mashed potatoes or rice and steamed vegetables.
  • Please refer to The Basic Cookbook or Everyday Cookbook for the Chicken stock paste recipe.
