Harissa sauce

Ingredients

  • 25 g caraway seeds
  • 30 g cumin seeds
  • 250 g fresh red chillies, cut into halves lengthwise, seeds removed
  • ½ tsp salt, adjust to taste
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 70 g tomato purée (concentrated)
  • 20 g red wine vinegar
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika powder
  • 80 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper, adjust to taste

Preparation

  1. Place caraway and cumin seeds into mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 10 and then dry-roast 3 min/120°C/speed 2.5, without measuring cup.
  2. Add chillies and chop 7 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add salt, garlic cloves, tomato puree, vinegar, smoked paprika, extra virgin olive oil, black pepper and mix 20 sec/speed 6.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix 10 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a jar with twist off lid, cover with a layer of olive oil, store in refrigerator and use as needed.
