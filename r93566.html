Seville Orange Curd

Ingredients

  • 180 g granulated sugar
  • 1 orange, thin peelings of skin only
  • 3 large eggs
  • 125 g unsalted butter, diced
  • 100 g orange juice (from approx. 3-4 oranges)

Preparation

  1. Place sugar and orange peel in mixing bowl then grind 10 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add eggs, butter and orange juice then heat 5-7 min/80°C/speed 4 until thickened. Whilst still warm, transfer to glass jars with screw-top lids (see tip), leaving approx. 5 mm. Make sure that the rims of the jars are clean and close immediately.
  3. Leave to cool completely then store in fridge and use within 1 month.

Useful Items

glass jars with screw-top lids, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Delicious as a spread on toast or bread; as a filling in a sponge cake or works well as a filling in a pastry case.
  • To prepare jars, place 500 g water in mixing bowl. Place jars and lids upside down in the centre of the Varoma and steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 1. Leave to drain - do not dry or touch the inside surfaces. Alternatively, thoroughly wash jar and lid in hot, soapy water. Rinse by pouring over boiling water. Prepare for filling, if needed, by placing on a clean tea towel in a roasting tin and heat in the oven at 100˚C until required.
