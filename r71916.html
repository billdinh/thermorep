Custard

Ingredients

  • 40 g raw sugar
  • 1 tbsp cornflour
  • 1 egg
  • 200 g full cream milk
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract (optional)
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
852 kJ / 203 kcal
Protein
7 g
Carbohydrates
31 g
Fat
6 g

Preparation

  1. Place sugar and cornflour into mixing bowl and mill 5 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add egg, milk and vanilla extract (optional) and cook 6 min/90°C/speed 4. Serve immediately.
