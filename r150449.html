Pumpkin, kale and tempeh breakfast bowl

Ingredients

  • 200 g pumpkin, cut into pieces (approx. 1½ cm)
  • 60 g kale, ribs removed and leaves cut into strips (approx. 1 cm)
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 slice bacon (approx. 100 g), cut into pieces (optional)
  • 90 g brown onion
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 3 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 400 g water
  • 4 whole eggs
  • olive oil spray, for frying
  • 300 g tempeh, cut into thin slices (approx. 5 mm)
  • 1 avocado, flesh only, cut into thin slices
  • 2 tbsp dukkah, to serve (see Tip)
  • 80 g sauerkraut, to serve (see Tip)

Preparation

  1. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh pumpkin into it. Insert Varoma tray and weigh kale into it, then set aside.
  2. Place oil and bacon (optional) into mixing bowl and cook 2 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  3. Add onion, garlic, ginger, salt and pepper and chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and sauté 2 min/Varoma/speed 1. Pour onion mixture over kale and stir with aid of spatula. Do not clean mixing bowl.
  5. Place water into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket and place eggs into it. Place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and cook 14 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  6. Meanwhile, place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and spray lightly with oil. Fry tempeh for 1-2 minutes each side, until golden brown and slightly crispy. Transfer onto a plate.
  7. Remove Varoma and set aside. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and carefully set eggs aside in a bowl of cold water to cease cooking (approx. 5 minutes). Once cool enough to handle, peel eggs and cut into halves lengthways.
  8. Divide pumpkin, kale, eggs and tempeh between 4 serving bowls. Top each bowl with avocado, dukkah and sauerkraut to serve.

Useful Items

non-stick frying pan, plate, bowl, serving bowls, stove top
