Summer Berry Pudding

Ingredients

Berry Pudding

  • 1 orange, thin peel only, no pith, plus juice (3 oz)
  • 2 packets/envelopes unflavored gelatin powder, unsweetened (0.25 oz ea.)
  • 8 oz sugar,
  • 1 vanilla bean, in pieces
  • 15 oz fresh strawberries, hulled
  • 10 oz fresh blueberries
  • 4 oz water
  • 3 oz orange liqueur
  • &frac18; tsp salt
  • 12 oz fresh blackberries
  • 10 oz fresh raspberries,
  • 8 oz peaches, diced (½ in.)
  • 2 loaves brioche bread (approx. 16 oz ea.), sliced and crusts removed

Whipped Cream

  • 8 1 - 2 oz heavy whipping cream
  • 1 - 2 tbsp confectioners sugar,
  • strawberries, sliced, to garnish

Preparation

Berry Pudding

  1. Place a small bowl onto mixing bowl lid, weigh in 3 oz orange juice and sprinkle with gelatin. Stir to combine and set aside.
  2. Place sugar, orange peel and vanilla bean into mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add strawberries, blueberries, water, orange liqueur and salt and cook 10 min/212°F//speed 0.5.
  4. Add reserved orange juice/gelatin mixture, blackberries, raspberries and peaches, stir through with spatula and cook 3 min/212°F//speed 0.5. Strain berry mixture over a bowl through a fine mesh sieve.
  5. Line a large bowl (3½ qt) with cling wrap, leaving enough hanging over sides to cover top. Dip one side of bread slices into strained berry juices. Line the bottom and sides of the bowl with the dipped side facing out and slightly overlapping (cut bread to fit as needed).
  6. Spoon remaining berry mixture over bread slices. Cover the top with a layer of brioche and gently press down. Tightly wrap cling wrap over the entire pudding and gently press down. Place a small plate, one which will fit inside the bowl rim, on top of the bread and weigh down with a few canned goods. Place in the refrigerator and let set overnight.
  7. To unmold: unwrap top of the pudding, place a serving plate upside down on top of the bowl and flip the pudding over. Remove bowl, using cling wrap to help keep the pudding on the plate.

Whipped Cream

  1. Insert butterfly whisk. Place whipping cream and sugar into mixing bowl and without setting a time whisk speed 3 until just holding its shape. Remove butterfly whisk.
  2. Slice pudding into wedges, garnish with whipped cream and strawberries and serve.

Useful Items

bowl (3½ qt), serving plate, small bowl, fine mesh sieve, cling wrap, refrigerator
