Homemade Ricotta

Ingredients

  • 1500 g whole milk
  • 30 g white vinegar
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1066 kJ / 253 kcal
Protein
12.8 g
Carbohydrates
23.3 g
Fat
12.8 g

Preparation

  1. Place milk in mixing bowl and heat 10 min/80°C/speed 2.
  2. Add vinegar and heat 1 min/90°C/speed 1. Leave to sit in mixing bowl for 1 minute. The milk will separate into curds and whey.
  3. Set simmering basket over a large jug or bowl and line with muslin or a nut milk bag. Carefully pour ricotta mixture into lined simmering basket.
  4. Leave to drain for a minimum 10 minutes or up to 1 hour, ensuring simmering basket is not submerged in liquid and ricotta is able to drain freely.
  5. Once drained, transfer ricotta into a sealable container, then place in refrigerator until ready to use.

Useful Items

muslin or nut milk bag, large jug, airtight container, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • For a softer ricotta, reduce draining time. For firmer ricotta, increase draining time.
  • Spread ricotta on sourdough toast and top with fruit spread or avocado and sliced tomato.
  • You can use ricotta in cakes and sweet or savoury tarts.
