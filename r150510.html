Rice crisp bars

Ingredients

  • 20 g puffed brown rice
  • 30 g flaked almonds
  • 30 g shelled unsalted pistachio nuts
  • 1 tsp white chia seeds
  • 20 g honey
  • 70 g nut paste (see Tips)
  • 100 g dark chocolate melts
  • 1 tsp coconut oil
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
796 kJ / 189 kcal
Protein
3.8 g
Carbohydrates
14.4 g
Fat
130.1 g

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh puffed rice, almonds, pistachios and chia seeds into it. Set bowl aside.
  2. Place honey, nut paste, chocolate and oil into mixing bowl and melt 4 min/90°C/speed 1 or until melted.
  3. Add reserved nut mixture and combine 30 sec//speed 1. Working quickly, divide mixture evenly between an 8-hole silicone bar mould or into a small loaf tin (18 x 7 x 6 cm) lined with baking paper. Press mixture down firmly into moulds or loaf tin. Place into refrigerator for approx. 1 hour to set. Remove from moulds or tin and cut into pieces (if desired) to serve.

Useful Items

bowl, loaf tin (18 x 7 x 6 cm), baking paper, silicone bar mould, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • You can use any type of nut paste or spread. To make your own nut paste, please refer to the Mixed seed and nut spread recipe on this recipe chip.
  • We recommend using chocolate melts (instead of chocolate bars) in this recipe, as they melt and combine more smoothly.
  • Rice crisp bars can be stored in a sealable container in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.
