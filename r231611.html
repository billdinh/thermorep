Texas White Bean Salad

Ingredients

Cumin Lime Dressing

  • 2 oz olive oil
  • 2 oz Parmesan cheese, cut into pieces
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp onion powder
  • 1 oz apple cider vinegar
  • 2 oz lime juice
  • ½ oz red bell pepper

White Bean Salad

  • 2 cans white beans (approx. 15 oz ea.), rinsed and drained
  • 1 can sweet corn kernels (approx. 14.5 oz), drained
  • 5 oz red onions, quartered
  • 5 oz red bell peppers, cut into pieces (1 in.)
  • 1 oz cilantro, leaves and tender stems
  • 2 - 3 jalapeño chilies, de-seeded and de-stemmed, to taste

Preparation

Cumin Lime Dressing

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid, weigh in olive oil and set aside.
  2. Place Parmesan, pepper, basil, oregano, cumin, sugar, salt and onion powder into mixing bowl and grind 15 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add vinegar, lime juice and bell pepper and blend 1 min/speed 9 while very slowly pouring reserved oil onto mixing bowl lid, letting it drizzle around measuring cup in a thin stream onto rotating blades to create an emulsion. Transfer dressing to a bowl and set aside.

White Bean Salad

  1. Transfer beans and corn into bowl with the dressing.
  2. Place onions, bell peppers, cilantro and jalapeño into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Transfer to bowl with beans. Combine with spatula. Serve immediately or refrigerate and serve chilled.

Hints & Tricks

  • To serve White Bean Salad as a dip, serve with tortilla chips.
