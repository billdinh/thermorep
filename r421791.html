Pumpkin Pie Bread

Ingredients

  • 4 oz cream cheese, softened, cubed
  • 2 oz sugar
  • 6 oz all-purpose flour, divided
  • 4 large eggs, divided
  • 12 oz leftover pumpkin pie, in pieces
  • 2 oz dark brown sugar
  • 1 tsp homemade pumpkin spice mix
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ¼ tsp salt
Nutrition
per 1 slice
Calories
1184 kJ / 283 kcal
Protein
5 g
Carbohydrates
49 g

10 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease and line bottom of a loaf pan (9 in. x 5 in.) with parchment and set aside.
  2. Place cream cheese, sugar, 1.5 oz flour and 1 egg into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix a further 10 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place pumpkin pie, 4.5 oz flour, dark brown sugar, 3 eggs, pumpkin spice mix, baking powder, baking soda and salt into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix a further 10 sec/speed 4.
  4. Pour half the pumpkin batter into prepared pan and spread out evenly. Spoon reserved cream cheese mixture over pumpkin layer and smooth evenly with spatula. Top with remaining pumpkin batter. Bake for 50-60 minutes (350°F), until a toothpick inserted in center of the bread comes out clean. Let cool slightly before serving.

Useful Items

parchment paper, loaf pan (9 in. x 5 in.), toothpick, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with butter, jam or pumpkin butter.
  • Recipe for homemade Pumpkin Spice Mix can be found in the Pumpkin and Spice collection.
