Russian beetroot salad

Ingredients

  • 150 g pickled cucumber, cut into pieces (3-4 cm)
  • 150 g carrots, cut into pieces (3-4 cm)
  • 350 g raw beetroot, cut into pieces (3-4 cm)
  • 500 g water
  • 250 g potatoes, cut into small pieces (approx. 1 cm)
  • 100 g frozen green peas
  • 100 g sauerkraut (optional)
  • 50 g spring onions/shallots, cut into thin slices
  • 2 - 3 sprigs fresh dill, plus extra to garnish
  • 60 g oil
  • 20 g white wine vinegar (optional)
  • 1 tsp salt

Preparation

  1. Place cucumber into mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 4. Transfer into a large salad bowl and set aside.
  2. Place carrots into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 4.5.| Transfer to Varoma tray and set aside.
  3. Place beetroot into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 4.5. Transfer into simmering basket.
  4. Place water into mixing bowl and insert simmering basket. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh potatoes and frozen peas into it. Insert Varoma tray with carrots and secure Varoma lid, then steam 27 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  5. Remove Varoma and set Varoma dish and tray aside separately to cool (approx. 20 min). Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and set aside on a deep plate to drain and cool (see tip). Meanwhile, discard cooking water, place salad bowl with cucumber onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in sauerkraut and spring onions/shallots into it.
  6. Add cooled vegetables from Varoma and simmering basket to salad bowl and set aside.
  7. Place dill, oil, vinegar and salt into mixing bowl and mix 30 sec/speed 6. Transfer into salad bowl, stir to coat all vegetables with dressing, adjust seasoning to taste and serve garnished with extra chopped fresh dill.

Useful Items

large salad bowl, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • For a creamy pink version of the salad, instead of making the vinaigrette in step 7, dress the cooled vegetables with sour cream or mayonnaise (quantity to taste), stir gently to combine, season to taste and garnish with chopped dill.
  • In step 5, if you are in a hurry to cool the vegetables, run cold water over each vegetable separately while they are still in the Varoma dish, Varoma tray and simmering basket.
    - For a traditional Russian flavour, omit the vinegar.
