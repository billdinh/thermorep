Mexican Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 6 poblano chilies
  • 14 oz mozzarella cheese, cut into pieces, divided
  • 21 oz Roma tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 2 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 3 ½ oz water
  • 36 oz boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into pieces (1 in.)
  • 3 ½ oz white onions, sliced
  • 7 ½ oz canned sweet corn kernels, drained
  • vegetable oil, to fry, as needed
  • 18 corn tortillas
  • 5 oz sour cream, divided

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Heat a frying pan over medium-high heat and lay chilies on their sides in pan. Char chilies, turning with tongs, until skins are blackened (approx. 3-8 min.). Wrap chilies in a paper towel, place inside a plastic bag until cool enough to handle.
  3. Place 7 oz mozzarella cheese into mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 8. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Place remaining 7 oz mozzarella cheese into mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 8. Transfer into bowl with reserved grated cheese.
  4. Place tomatoes, garlic, salt, pepper and water into mixing bowl and blend 15 sec/speed 10. Add chicken and cook 18 min/212°F//speed 1. Meanwhile, prepare chilies. Wearing rubber gloves when handling chilies, peel chilies, cut off tops and discard seeds and ribs. Cut into strips and reserve.
  5. Shred chicken 5 sec//speed 4. Add sliced onions, corn and reserved chilies and cook 5 min/212°F//speed 1. Set aside and keep warm.
  6. Heat a deep frying pan with oil over medium-high heat. Fry tortillas until slightly soft and crispy (approx. 20-40 sec. per side). Fold a paper towel in half, place the hot tortilla on one side and fold the other side over. Place the next tortilla on top, add a new paper towel, and continue the process with remaining tortillas.
  7. Lay six tortillas in a baking pan (approx. 16 in. x 14 in.) and add half the chicken mixture. Lay six tortillas over the filling, spread half the sour cream and sprinkle half the reserved grated cheese and cover with remaining chicken mixture. Place remaining tortillas over chicken and spread with remaining sour cream and reserved grated cheese.
  8. Bake for 30-40 minutes (400°F) or until golden brown. Serve hot.

Useful Items

tongs, paper towel, rubber gloves, deep frying pan, baking pan (approx. 16 in. x 14 in.), frying pan, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve Mexican Lasagna with additional sour cream if desired.
