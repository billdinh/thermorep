Healthy banana bread

Ingredients

  • 225 g buckwheat flour (see Tips)
  • 150 g coconut sugar
  • 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 60 g rolled oats
  • 20 g desiccated coconut
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds, plus extra for decorating
  • 4 ripe bananas (3 of the bananas cut into quarters)
  • 1 egg
  • 140 g natural yoghurt
  • 45 g grapeseed oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste
  • 1 tbsp pure maple syrup

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C. Line a loaf tin (25 x 10 x 11 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place buckwheat flour, coconut sugar, bicarbonate of soda, baking powder, cinnamon and salt into mixing bowl and mix 5 sec/speed 4.
  3. Add oats, coconut and chia seeds and mix 5 sec//speed 4.
  4. Add 3 of the bananas (cut into quarters), egg, yoghurt, oil and vanilla and mix 15 sec//speed 4.
  5. Pour mixture into prepared loaf tin. Slice remaining banana lengthways into 4 thin slices, trim to fit width of tin and place on top of banana mixture. Sprinkle with extra chia seeds and drizzle with syrup. Bake for 60 minutes (160°C), or until a wooden skewer inserted in the centre of the loaf comes out clean. Serve warm. 

Useful Items

loaf tin (25 x 10 x 11 cm), baking paper, wooden skewer, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • To make your own buckwheat flour, place buckwheat grains into mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 10.
  • You can toast this bread the next day, with the spread of your choice, for a delicious breakfast option.
