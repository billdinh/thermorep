Grilled scallops with chilli jam

Ingredients

Chilli jam

  • 30 g eschalots
  • 100 g fresh long red chillies (see Tips)
  • 20 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 50 g white sugar
  • 50 g white vinegar
  • 1 ½ tbsp lime juice
  • 20 g palm sugar, broken into small pieces (see Tips)

Scallops

  • 20 scallops
  • extra virgin olive oil, for brushing
  • salt, to season
  • ground black pepper, to season
  • lime juice, to serve
  • fresh coriander leaves, for garnishing

Preparation

Chilli jam

  1. Place eschalots and chillies into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add oil and sauté 5 min/120°C/speed 2, without measuring cup.
  3. Add white sugar, vinegar and lime juice and cook 15 min/100°C/speed 2.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and cook 2 min/120°C/speed 2, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  5. Add palm sugar and mix 20 sec/speed 3 or until sugar has completely dissolved. Transfer into a sterilised sealable jar and set aside to cool completely, then place into refrigerator until ready to use (see Tips).

Scallops

  1. Brush scallops with oil, then season with salt and pepper. Place a frying pan over medium-high heat and cook scallops for 1-2 minutes on each side or until cooked to your preference. Remove from heat and squeeze over lime juice.
  2. Transfer scallops onto a serving platter and top with a small amount of reserved Chilli jam. Garnish with coriander leaves to serve.

Useful Items

sterilised sealable jar, frying pan, pastry brush, serving platter, refrigerator, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • You can replace the red chillies with green chillies in this recipe, if desired.
  • You can replace the palm sugar with brown sugar in this recipe, if desired.
  • Chilli jam can be stored in a sealable jar for up to 2 weeks in the refrigerator.
