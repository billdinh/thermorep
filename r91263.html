Chicken Garam Masala

Ingredients

Garam Masala Spice Mix

  • 2 tsp black peppercorns
  • 2 tbsp coriander seeds
  • 1 tbsp cumin seeds
  • ½ tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1 tsp whole cloves
  • 1 tbsp ground cardamom
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 dried bay leaf

Chicken Garam Masala

  • 1 can crushed tomatoes (approx. 15 oz)
  • 3 oz sour cream,
  • 2 oz unsalted butter
  • 1 tsp sugar (optional)
  • ¾ tsp salt, divided
  • 24 oz boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cubed (1½ in.)

Preparation

Garam Masala Spice Mix

  1. Place peppercorns, coriander seeds, cumin seeds, nutmeg, cloves, cardamom, cinnamon and bay leaf into mixing bowl and grind 40 sec/speed 10. Transfer into an airtight jar and set aside.

Chicken Garam Masala

  1. Place tomatoes, sour cream, butter and sugar into mixing bowl. Place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 12 min/212F/speed 2.
  2. Add ½ tsp salt and 2 tbsp reserved Garam Masala Spice Mix. Place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 3 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Add chicken and ¼ tsp salt, insert measuring cup and cook 6 min/212°F//speed . Adjust seasoning to taste and serve hot.

Useful Items

airtight jar

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with basmati or plain rice and sprinkle with parsley.
  • Serve with plain yogurt to tone down the spiciness.
  • Serve with steamed cauliflower, carrots and green beans.
  • Reserve the rest of the Garam Masala Spice Mix for another curry recipe.
  • For a faster version replace the Garam Masala Spice Mix with a store-bought version.
