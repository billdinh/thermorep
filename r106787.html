Gin and tonic sorbet with cucumber

Ingredients

Sorbet

  • 100 g sugar
  • 200 g tonic water
  • 200 g tonic water, frozen into ice cubes
  • 325 g cucumber, peeled and cut into pieces (2 cm)
  • 25 g lemon juice
  • 70 g gin

Lemon and mint tuile

  • 50 g blanched almonds
  • 30 g unsalted butter
  • 80 g caster sugar
  • 50 g plain flour
  • 2 egg whites
  • 1 lemon, zest only, no white pith
  • 1 sprig fresh mint, leaves only
  • 1 pinch salt

Preparation

Sorbet

  1. Place sugar and tonic water into mixing bowl and cook 5 min/100°C/speed 3. Transfer into a jug and set aside to cool (approx. 30 minutes).
  2. Place frozen tonic water, cucumber, lemon juice, gin and reserved cooled syrup into mixing bowl and mix 3 min/speed 4. Pour mixture, up to 4 cm high, into shallow freezable containers and cover with plastic wrap. Place into freezer until set (approx. 8 hours). Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Cut frozen gin mixture into pieces (approx. 4 cm). Place frozen gin pieces into mixing bowl and crush 30 sec/speed 9, then mix 30 sec/speed 4 with aid of spatula. Transfer into a freezable container and place into freezer for a minimum of 3 hours, or until set. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Lemon and mint tuile

  1. Preheat oven to 170°C. Line 2 baking trays (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place almonds into mixing bowl and chop 6 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place butter into mixing bowl and melt 2 min/70°C/speed 2.
  4. Add all remaining ingredients, including reserved almonds, and mix 10 sec/speed 4.
  5. Divide mixture between prepared trays. Using spatula or the back of a metal spoon, spread out into a thin layer (approx. 1 mm). Bake for 10 minutes (170°C). Carefully cut or break into shards, then return tuiles to oven and bake for a further 1-2 minutes (170°), or until golden brown. Set aside to cool completely.
  6. Serve sorbet with tuiles.

Useful Items

jug, shallow freezable containers (e.g. plastic, silicone), plastic wrap, freezable containers (e.g. plastic, silicone), baking paper, 2 baking trays (30 x 40 cm), bowl, freezer, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • For a refreshing slushie, place sorbet into glasses after mixing in step 3 and serve with a straw or long-handled spoon.
  • We recommend using good quality gin.
