Shortbread

Ingredients

  • 100 g unsalted butter, softened
  • 30 g pure icing sugar
  • 30 g caster sugar
  • 150 g gluten free flour mix (see Tips)
  • 20 g gluten free cornflour
  • 1 tsp gluten free baking powder (see Tips)
  • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 150°C.
  2. Place butter into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Insert butterfly whisk. Add sugars and mix 4 sec/speed 4, or until light and fluffy. Remove butterfly whisk. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add flours, baking powder and vanilla and mix 10 sec/speed 4, then knead 40 sec/. Transfer mixture into a fluted, loose bottomed tart tin (20 cm) and press down evenly. Smooth surface with the back of a spoon. Place into refrigerator for 10 minutes to chill.
  5. Remove from refrigerator and bake for 30-35 minutes (150°C), or until lightly golden and firm to the touch. Remove from oven and score into 12 wedges. Set aside for approx. 15 minutes. While still warm, cut into wedges. Once completely cooled, transfer into a sealable container until ready to serve (see Tips).

Useful Items

fluted loose bottom tart tin (20 cm), sealable container, oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Please refer to the Gluten free flour mix recipe on this recipe chip.
  • To make your own Gluten free baking powder, please refer to the recipe on this recipe chip.
  • Store shortbread in a sealable container for up to 1 week.
  • Shortbread can be kept whole and given as a gift.
