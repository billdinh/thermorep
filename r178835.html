Marble Cake

Ingredients

  • 300 g unsalted butter, cut in pieces, plus extra for greasing
  • 370 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 280 g sugar
  • 70 g milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla sugar, homemade
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt,
  • 1 Tbsp baking powder (15 g)
  • 5 medium eggs
  • 30 g cocoa powder
Nutrition
per 1 slice
Calories
1381 kJ / 330 kcal
Protein
5 g
Carbohydrates
36 g
Fat
18 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Grease and flour a bundt cake tin (Ø 22-24 cm).
  2. Place butter in mixing bowl and mix 40 sec/speed 5.
  3. Add flour, 250 g sugar, 40 g milk, vanilla sugar, salt, eggs and baking powder then, with aid of spatula, mix 40 sec/speed 5. Transfer half of cake mixture to prepared tin, leaving remainder in mixing bowl.
  4. Add cocoa powder, remaining 30 g sugar and remaining 30 g milk to mixing bowl then mix 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then mix again 5 sec/speed 5.
  5. Layer cocoa cake mixture in bundt cake tin. To create a marble pattern, swirl through both layers with a fork. Bake for 50-60 minutes (180°C) until a skewer inserted in centre comes out clean. Leave cake to cool in tin for approx. 15 minutes before transferring to a serving plate and leaving to cool completely. Cut in slices and serve.

Useful Items

bundt cake tin (Ø 22-24 cm), skewer, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • If butter is very soft, omit step 2 and add butter with other ingredients in step 3.
  • Dust with icing sugar or cover with a chocolate glaze to serve.
  • This and many other classic cakes can be found on Cookidoo®, all effortlessly mixed in your Thermomix®.
  • Pound cake: Omit cocoa marbling. Simply transfer all the cake mixture from step 3 into prepared bundt cake tin and bake for 50-60 min (180°C) as described in step 5.
  • Flavour cocoa mixture with ½ tsp rum essence, or with 20 g rum. If using rum, reduce milk to 10 g in step 4.
