Spiced cauliflower pilau

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 cardamom pods (green)
  • 1 tsp fennel seeds
  • 2 whole cloves
  • 1 fresh bay leaf
  • ¼ tsp ground turmeric
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp garlic paste (see Tips)
  • 1 tsp ginger paste (see Tips)
  • 1 tsp Vegetable stock paste (see Tips)
  • 600 g water
  • 700 g cauliflower, cut into florets (3 cm)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • fresh coriander, leaves only, for garnishing
Nutrition
per 1 portions
Calories
241 kJ / 57 kcal
Protein
4 g
Carbohydrates
5.7 g
Fat
1.5 g

Preparation

  1. Place cumin seeds, cardamom pods, fennel seeds, cloves and bay leaf into mixing bowl and cook 3 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  2. Add turmeric, cinnamon, garlic paste and ginger paste and mill 1 min/speed 9.
  3. Add stock paste and 100 g of the water and heat 1 min/90°C/speed 1.
  4. Add half of the cauliflower and chop 5 sec//speed 5. Transfer into Varoma dish and set aside.
  5. Place remaining cauliflower into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec//speed 5. Transfer into Varoma dish with cauliflower.
  6. Place salt and remaining 500 g water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and cook 5 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  7. Stir cauliflower using spatula, then secure Varoma lid and cook 5 min/Varoma/speed 1. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve with your favourite curry.
