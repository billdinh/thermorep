Pork Tenderloin with Whiskey Honey Sauce

Ingredients

  • 20 - 24 oz pork tenderloin, halved to fit Varoma
  • ½ tsp salt, divided
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper, divided
  • 7 oz fennel bulb, halved, cored, sliced (¼ in.)
  • 5 oz red onions, halved, sliced (¼ in.)
  • 1 Pink Lady apple, halved, cored, sliced (½ in.)
  • 5 oz water
  • 4 oz unsalted butter, cut into pieces
  • 2 oz honey
  • 2 oz Irish whiskey
  • 1 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • ½ tsp beef stock paste
  • 4 oz dandelion leaves

Preparation

  1. Place Varoma dish into position, weigh in pork tenderloin, season with ¼ tsp salt and ⅛ tsp pepper. Insert Varoma tray and weigh in fennel, onions and apple and set Varoma aside.
  2. Place water, butter, honey, whiskey, flour, mustard, ¼ tsp salt, stock paste and ⅛ tsp pepper into mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish without Varoma tray into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 12 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  3. Carefully open Varoma lid, insert Varoma tray with vegetables and apple, secure Varoma lid and steam a further 13 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  4. Arrange dandelion leaves on a serving platter. Slice pork into medallions (approx. ½ in.). Transfer pork medallions and fennel mixture on top of greens and drizzle sauce over pork. Serve immediately.

Hints & Tricks

  • For firmer vegetables and apples, insert Varoma tray during the last 8-10 minutes of steaming.
  • The USDA Guidelines and National Pork Board recommend cooking pork chops, roasts and tenderloin to an internal temperature between 145°F (medium rare) and 160°F (medium), followed by a 3 minute rest.
