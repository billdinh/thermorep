Sweet cheese pastry (knafeh)

Ingredients

Syrup

  • 300 g sugar
  • 100 g water
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp blossom water

Pastry

  • 200 g butter, plus extra for greasing
  • 500 g white bread, crust removed and cut into pieces

Kashta cream

  • 1000 g milk
  • 150 g white bread, crust removed and cut into pieces
  • 100 g cornflour (starch)
  • 1 tbsp blossom water
  • 2 tbsp rose water

Cheese filling

  • 250 g akkawi cheese, desalted (see tip) and cut into cubes
  • 250 g mozzarella, cut into cubes

Preparation

Syrup

  1. Place sugar, water, lemon juice and blossom water into mixing bowl and heat 10 min/115°C/speed 2. Transfer into a heat-resistant bowl and set aside to cool down.

Pastry

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C. Grease a cake tin (Ø 32 cm), cover the base with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place bread into mixing bowl and grind 30 sec/speed 9 with aid of spatula.
  3. Add softened butter and mix 10 sec/speed 6 with aid of spatula.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and knead 1 min/. Transfer into prepared tin, spread evenly and press down well with a flat plate or a slightly smaller tin. Set tin with pastry aside.

Kashta cream

  1. Place milk into mixing bowl and heat 5 min/60°C/speed 1.
  2. Add bread and cornflour and heat 4 min/90°C/speed 2.
  3. Add blossom water and rose water and mix 1 min/90°C/speed 2.5. Transfer onto pastry layer in baking tin and set aside.

Cheese filling

  1. Place Akkawi cheese and mozzarella into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 6. Sprinkle evenly on top of kashta cream layer and bake for 35 minutes (200°C) or until edges are golden brown. When still hot, reverse onto a large serving plate and drizzle with reserved syrup. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

baking paper, plate, serving plate, heat-resistant bowl, round cake tin (Ø 32 cm), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • To desalt Akkawi cheese, soak it for 12 hours or overnight in abundant water, changing the water every half hour in the first hours. Drain an pat dry before use.
