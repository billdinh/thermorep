Warm beetroot dish

Ingredients

  • 750 g water
  • 500 g raw beetroot, peeled and cut into quarters
  • 15 g salted butter
  • 10 g lemon juice
  • 1 tsp caster sugar
  • ¼ tsp sea salt
  • 50 g sour cream
  • 1 tsp plain flour
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
568 kJ / 135 kcal
Protein
2.5 g
Carbohydrates
11.6 g
Fat
8 g

Preparation

  1. Place water into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket, then weigh beetroot into it. Cook 35-40 min/Varoma/speed 1, until beetroot is tender.
  2. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and set beetroot aside to drain. Empty mixing bowl.
  3. Place cooked beetroot into mixing bowl and chop 8 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add butter, lemon juice, sugar and salt and cook 2 min/100°C//speed 2.
  5. Place a ceramic or glass bowl onto mixing bowl lid, then weigh sour cream into it. Add plain flour to sour cream and combine.
  6. Add sour cream-flour mixture to mixing bowl and cook 3 min/100°C//speed 2. Serve warm as a side dish.

Useful Items

ceramic or glass bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve as an accompaniment to beef, pork or chicken alongside mashed potatoes.
