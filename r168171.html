Spiced lamb flatbread

Ingredients

Flatbread

  • 20 g olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 150 g water
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 tsp dried yeast
  • 300 g bread flour, plus extra for dusting
  • ½ tsp salt

Meat topping

  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 250 g onions, cut into quarters
  • 500 g ripe tomatoes, cut into quarters
  • 1 tsp salt, plus extra for sprinkling
  • 5 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 5 sprigs fresh mint, leaves only
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • ½ tsp ground cayenne pepper, adjust to taste
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp ground paprika
  • 1 tsp ground allspice
  • ½ tsp dried chilli flakes, adjust to taste
  • 2 tbsp pomegranate molasses
  • 500 g lamb mince

Assembly and baking

  • 30 g pine nuts
  • 2 lemons, cut into quarters

Preparation

Flatbread

  1. Place olive oil, water, sugar and yeast into mixing bowl and mix 2 min/37°C/speed 3.
  2. Add flour, salt and knead 3 min/. Transfer dough into a bowl, cover with cling film and allow to rise in a warm place until doubled in size (approx. 1 h). Meanwhile prepare topping.

Meat topping

  1. Place garlic, onions and tomatoes into mixing bowl and chop 30 sec/speed 5. Transfer mixture into simmering basket, sprinkle with salt and allow to stand for 5 minutes, then squeeze out as much liquid as possible.
  2. Place parsley and mint into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add reserved strained tomato mixture, cumin, cayenne pepper, cinnamon, paprika, all-spice, chilli flakes, pomegranate molasses and lamb mince and knead 3 min/. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.

Assembly and baking

  1. Grease a baking tray or baking stone and place it in oven. Preheat oven to 250ºC.
  2. Turn reserved dough out onto a floured surface. Using floured hands, quickly knead and divide dough into 6 equal pieces. With a rolling pin roll each ball into a thin flat circle (approx. Ø 18-20 cm) and place on the preheated baking tray.
  3. Divide reserved meat topping into 6 equal portions and gently spread evenly and thinly across each piece of dough. Sprinkle each flatbread with a pinch of salt and pine nuts. Bake for 10-15 minutes (250°C) or until the edges of the flatbreads are golden and crispy. Repeat with a second baking tray if necessary.
  4. Squeeze a little lemon juice over each flatbread and serve with lemon quarters.

Useful Items

cling film, baking tray or baking stone, rolling pin, baking tray, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Accompany with salad and a savoury yogurt drink, garnished with fresh chopped mint and parsley leaves.
