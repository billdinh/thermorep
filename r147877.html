Chewy Mint Choc Protein Bites

Ingredients

  • 75 g almond milk, or other non-dairy milk
  • 125 g hazelnut cocoa spread
  • 100 g agave syrup
  • 135 g rolled oats
  • 25 g oat bran
  • 50 g puffed rice
  • 25 g cocoa powder, unsweetened
  • 20 g protein powder
  • 8 - 10 drops peppermint extract, to taste
  • 90 g dark chocolate chips

Preparation

  1. Line a square baking tin (approx. 20 cm) with greaseproof paper.
  2. Place a jug on mixing bowl lid then weigh in 75 g almond milk. Remove jug and set aside.
  3. Place nut spread and syrup in mixing bowl then melt 4 min/100°C/speed 2.
  4. Add rolled oats, oat bran, puffed rice, cocoa powder, protein powder and peppermint extract then stir 20 sec//speed 3. Remove bowl and leave to cool for 10 minutes.
  5. Place mixing bowl back into position then add chocolate chips and stir 15 sec//speed 2.
  6. Without measuring cup, mix without time//speed 2, while adding reserved almond milk slowly through hole in mixing bowl lid until mixture loosens (all 75 g may not be needed). Transfer to prepared tin and press down with the back of a spoon. Cover and place in fridge for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  7. Once chilled, remove from tin and cut into 12 bars. Wrap in greaseproof paper and serve or keep refrigerated for up to 5 days.

Useful Items

square baking tin (20 cm), greaseproof paper, jug, cling film, long sharp knife, refrigerator
