Strawberry Jam

Ingredients

  • 500 g strawberries, halved or quartered as needed (see tip)
  • 20 g lemon juice
  • 375 g sugar
Nutrition
per 1 Total recipe
Calories
7141 kJ / 1704 kcal

4 g
Carbohydrates
402 g
Fat
2 g

Preparation

  1. Place strawberries, lemon juice and sugar in mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish without Varoma lid in place of measuring cup on mixing bowl lid and cook 35-40 min/Varoma/speed 1 until desired consistency is reached (see tips).
  2. Test setting point of jam (see tip) then, while jam is still warm, pour into warm glass jars with screw-top lids (see tip), leaving approx. 5 mm. Make sure that the rims of the jars are clean and close immediately then turn jars upside down for 10 minutes before turning right side up and leaving to cool. Serve or store in fridge for up to 10 days.

Useful Items

glass jars with screw-top lids, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • With temperature-controlled cooking, making jam is easy with Thermomix®. A great way to use up an abundance of fruit when it is in season, refer to Cookidoo® for a range of preserve recipes.
  • Only use ripe fruit in this recipe.
  • Adjust cooking time according to preferred consistency - longer cooking time results in thicker jam.
  • Depending on the fruit, the jam may bubble up into the Varoma. If this happens, reduce temperature to 100°C or even to 90°C and extend cooking time as needed.
  • Setting point test: place a saucer in the freezer for 5-10 minutes). Place |1 tsp jam on the chilled saucer. When jam on saucer is cool, push it with your finger. If the jam wrinkles, it is ready. If not, boil jam for a further 2 minutes then test again.
  • To prepare jars, place 500 g water in mixing bowl. Place jars and lids upside down in the centre of the Varoma and steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 1. Leave to drain - do not dry or touch the inside surfaces. Alternatively, thoroughly wash jars and lids in hot, soapy water. Rinse by pouring over boiling water. Prepare for filling, if needed, by placing on a clean tea towel in a roasting tin and heat in the oven at 100˚C until required.
  • Raspberry jam: substitute strawberries with 500 g raspberries. Reduce cooking time to 8-10 minutes according to preferred consistency.
  • Mixed berries jam: use a total of 250 g strawberries and 250 g raspberries. Reduce cooking time to 25-30 minutes.
