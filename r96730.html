Baked pâté

Ingredients

  • 1000 g water
  • 4 allspice berries
  • 2 dried bay leaves
  • 200 g gravy beef, cut into cubes (3-4 cm)
  • 200 g skinless chicken thighs, cut into cubes (3-4 cm)
  • 400 g pork belly, rind removed and cut into cubes (3-4 cm)
  • 200 g mixed roots and vegetables (e.g. carrots, parsnip, brown onion), cut into pieces (3-4 cm)
  • 10 g dried porcini mushrooms, cut into slices
  • 200 g chicken livers
  • 1 bread roll (approx.60 g)
  • 2 ½ tsp sea salt
  • ¼ tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp dried marjoram
  • ¼ tsp ground ginger
  • 3 eggs
  • 60 g butter, cut into thin slices, plus 1-2 tsp extra for greasing
  • 50 g breadcrumbs, for coating

Preparation

  1. Place water, allspice and bay leaves into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket, then weigh beef, chicken and pork belly into it.
  2. Place Varoma dish into position, then weigh mixed roots and vegetables into it. Add porcini mushrooms to Varoma dish, then secure Varoma lid and steam 50 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Carefully remove Varoma lid, then weigh chicken livers into Varoma dish. Toss contents of Varoma dish with spatula to ensure even cooking, then re-secure Varoma lid and steam 10 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  4. Remove Varoma, then transfer chicken livers and vegetables into a large bowl. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula, transfer contents into large bowl with chicken livers and vegetables, then set bowl aside.
  5. Strain hot stock in mixing bowl through simmering basket into a large jug. Place a ceramic or glass bowl onto mixing bowl lid and place bread roll into it. Weigh 150 g of the hot liquid stock into ceramic or glass bowl, then leave roll to soak for 8-10 seconds. Turn bread roll over using a pair of tongs, then set bowl aside.
  6. Preheat oven to 180°C. Without cleaning mixing bowl, place half of the reserved meat and vegetable mixture into mixing bowl and chop 30 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a separate bowl and set aside.
  7. Place remaining meat and vegetable mixture into mixing bowl and chop 30 sec/speed 5.
  8. Add salt, nutmeg, pepper, marjoram, ground ginger, eggs, soaked bread roll with soaking liquid and reserved minced meat and vegetable mixture and mix 30 sec/speed 5, with aid of spatula.
  9. Grease a loaf tin (25 x 9 x 11 cm) with the extra 1-2 tsp butter, then coat with breadcrumbs. Transfer pâté mixture into loaf tin and spread down surface evenly using the back of a wet spoon.
  10. Top pâté with remaining 60 g butter and bake for 35 minutes (180°C).
  11. Remove from oven and leave pâté at room temperature to cool completely. Once cooled, serve immediately or place into refrigerator until ready to serve.

Useful Items

large bowl, ceramic or glass bowl, tongs, large jug, loaf tin (25 x 9 x 11 cm), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Transfer leftover liquid stock into hot-rinsed storage jar or container to reserve for later use. This stock will make a great base for soups and stews.
  • It is best to use fattier cuts of meat (gravy beef, pork neck and pork belly) to ensure the pâté is smooth and creamy.
  • This pâté is great served alongside, pickles, chutneys, mustard and slices of thick crusty bread.
