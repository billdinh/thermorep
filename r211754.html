Pork Buns (Nikuman)

Ingredients

Dough

  • 180 g water
  • 60 g milk
  • 20 g oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 1 Tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp dried instant yeast
  • 360 g plain flour

Filling and Assembly

  • 2 fresh shiitake mushrooms
  • 2 spring onions, cut in pieces
  • 80 g white cabbage leaf, cut in pieces
  • 20 g fresh root ginger, cut in round slices (2 mm)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 140 g pork mince
  • 40 g Japanese soy sauce
  • ½ tsp sugar
  • 15 g oyster sauce
  • 10 g sesame oil
  • 400 g water

Preparation

Dough

  1. Place water, milk, oil, sugar and yeast in mixing bowl then heat 3 min/37°C/speed 1. Meanwhile, grease a large bowl and spatula.
  2. Add flour and knead 3 min/. Transfer dough with oiled spatula to prepared bowl, cover with cling film and set aside to rest in a warm place for 1 hour or until doubled in size. Clean mixing bowl.

Filling and Assembly

  1. Place mushrooms, spring onions, cabbage, ginger and garlic in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  2. Add pork, soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil and sugar then mix 30 sec/speed 5.
  3. Divide dough into 8 portions. Shape each piece into a ball then press out with fingers into a round, approx. Ø 15 cm. Place ⅛ of filling in centre of each round, and close up by pulling sides into the top. Repeat with remaining dough and filling to make 8 pork buns. Divide filled buns between Varoma dish and tray.
  4. Place water in mixing bowl and place Varoma into position and steam 25 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove Varoma and allow to cool for 10 minutes then carefully remove Varoma lid. Serve hot.
