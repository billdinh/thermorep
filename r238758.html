Kreem-B-Tween

Ingredients

Vanilla Ice Cream

  • 500 g whole milk
  • 500 g double cream
  • 1 vanilla pod, sliced lengthways
  • 6 egg yolks, from medium eggs
  • 150 g sugar

Wafers

  • 100 g salted butter, diced
  • 100 g icing sugar
  • 100 g plain flour
  • 100 g egg whites, from medium eggs

Strawberry Sauce

  • 400 g fresh strawberries, hulled
  • 40 g sugar

Assembly

  • 500 g fresh strawberries, sliced for serving

Preparation

Vanilla Ice Cream

  1. Place milk, cream and vanilla pod in mixing bowl then cook 3 min/60°C/speed . Allow to stand for 5 minutes to infuse then remove vanilla pod.
  2. Add egg yolks and sugar then cook 8 min/80°C/speed 4. Transfer mix to a bowl over ice until completely cool, then freeze for 4 hours.
  3. Once frozen, place in fridge for 10 minutes then cut into cubes. Place half the mixture in mixing bowl and, with aid of spatula, crush 5 sec/speed 8. Transfer mixture into a lined baking tin (2 cm deep).
  4. Place remaining frozen mixture in mixing bowl and, with aid of spatula, crush 5 sec/speed 8. Transfer to tin containing ice cream, smooth out surface with spatula, cover tightly with cling film and freeze overnight.

Wafers

  1. Place butter in mixing bowl and melt 3 min/50°C/speed .
  2. Add sugar, flour and egg whites then mix 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and place in fridge for 1 hour. Clean mixing bowl.
  3. Preheat oven to 170°C and line a baking tray with baking paper.
  4. Take a piece of thin cardboard and, using a ruler, mark out a rectangle 10 cm x 5 cm. Cut out the shape using scissors, keeping both the cut-out section and the rectangle.
  5. Place the cut-out section on the paper-lined baking tray and use it as a stencil. Spread mix over it to about 2-3 mm thick. Carefully lift off the stencil to leave the portion of wafer mix on tray in shape of the rectangle. Repeat process until the tray is full.
  6. Bake for 5-8 minutes (170°C) until golden brown. Carefully lift off wafers and place on wire rack, keeping them flat. Repeat until there are 20 wafers.

Strawberry Sauce

  1. Place strawberries and sugar in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then cook 4 min/100°C/speed .
  2. Blend 20 sec/speed 8 then pour sauce through a fine sieve into a bowl.

Assembly

  1. Mark rectangles on the ice cream using the reserved cardboard rectangle as a template. Lift ice cream out of the tray, using the baking paper to assist, then, with a warmed knife, slice ice cream into rectangles slightly smaller than the marks. Return ice cream rectangles to freezer on tray for 15 minutes.
  2. Select a clean tray that will fit in the freezer. Place one wafer down, top it with a piece of ice cream then place another wafer on top. Return briefly to freezer while preparing serving plates.
  3. Place a tablespoon of strawberry sauce in the centre of each plate. Dust Kreem-B-Tweens with icing sugar, then place one on each plate. Serve with sliced strawberries.

Useful Items

baking tin, baking paper, baking tray and baking paper, thin cardboard, ruler, fine sieve, baking tray, cling film, wire rack, oven, freezer, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Each component can be made in advance and then assembled 15 minutes before serving.
  • When lining the baking tin, run the baking paper under water and squeeze it out then carefully line the tin.
  • The strawberry sauce freezes well and can be served over any ice cream.
