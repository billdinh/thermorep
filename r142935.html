Chicken pad thai

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp peanut oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 200 g flat rice stick noodles
  • boiling water, for soaking
  • 150 g fresh bean shoots
  • 40 g roasted salted peanuts
  • 60 g spring onion/shallot, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 80 g carrot, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 50 g palm sugar
  • 1 ½ tbsp fish sauce
  • 110 g soy sauce
  • 1 ½ tbsp tamarind paste
  • 1 ½ tbsp lime juice
  • ½ tsp chilli powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 500 g water
  • 400 g chicken breasts, cut into strips (1 cm)
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 200 g firm tofu, cut into cubes (1-2 cm)
  • dried chilli flakes, for garnishing
  • 2 lemons, cut into wedges, to serve

Preparation

  1. Line Varoma tray with a piece of wet, well-wrung baking paper, making sure paper goes all the way to the edges to prevent egg from spilling out of the Varoma tray. Lightly grease baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh rice noodles into it. Cover with boiling water and set aside to soak for 10 minutes. Using simmering basket, drain noodles and place back into thermal serving bowl or other large bowl. Place thermal serving bowl or other large bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh bean shoots into it, then cover to keep warm.
  3. Place peanuts into mixing bowl and chop 8 sec/speed 4. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place peanut oil, spring onion/shallot, carrots and garlic into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5.
  5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and cook 3 min/100°C/speed 1. Transfer half of the mixture into Varoma dish. Retain other half in the mixing bowl.
  6. Add palm sugar, fish sauce, soy sauce, tamarind paste, lime juice and chilli powder to mixing bowl and heat 5 min/100°C/speed 1. Transfer sauce into bowl with noodles and stir using spatula to coat. Cover noodles to keep warm and set aside.
  7. Place eggs into mixing bowl and beat 3 sec/speed 5. Transfer into a jug and set aside.
  8. Place water into mixing bowl and place Varoma dish into position. Weigh chicken into Varoma dish, sprinkle with salt and pepper, then stir gently with aid of spatula to combine with garlic mixture. Insert lined Varoma tray and pour reserved egg evenly onto tray. Secure Varoma lid and steam 6 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  9. Stir chicken with aid of spatula. Add tofu to Varoma dish with chicken, re-insert Varoma tray, secure Varoma lid and steam 5-7 min/Varoma/speed 1 or until chicken and egg are cooked through.
  10. Remove Varoma tray with omelette. Slice omelette into thin strips. Transfer noodles, omelette, chicken and tofu into a large serving bowl and stir to combine. Garnish with dried chilli flakes and reserved chopped peanuts, then serve with lemon wedges.
