Chicken and Rice Patties

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Monterey Jack cheese, cut into pieces
  • 2 oz carrots, cut into pieces
  • 2 oz green onions, cut into pieces
  • 4 ½ oz long-grain white rice
  • 30 oz water
  • 12 oz chicken thighs, boneless and skinless, cut into strips (½ in.)
  • ¼ tsp salt, to taste
  • &frac18; tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 oz panko bread crumbs
  • 2 large eggs, whisked
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
490 kJ / 117 kcal
Protein
9 g
Carbohydrates
11 g
Fat
4 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Line a baking sheet with parchment and set aside.
  2. Place cheese into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a large bowl and set aside.
  3. Place carrots and green onions into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer to bowl with reserved cheese and set aside.
  4. Insert simmering basket and weigh in rice and water.
  5. Place Varoma into position. Weigh in chicken, season with salt and pepper, secure Varoma lid and steam 20 min/212°F/speed 4.
  6. Remove Varoma. Transfer cooked rice to bowl with carrots and cheese and set aside. Discard water. Place a small bowl onto mixing bowl lid, weigh in bread crumbs and set aside.
  7. Place steamed chicken into mixing bowl and shred 5 sec//speed 4. Transfer to bowl with cooked rice and with spatula mix in whisked eggs and reserved bread crumbs to combine.
  8. Shape into patties and transfer to prepared baking sheet. Bake for 15 minutes (400°F), turn patties over and bake a further 10 minutes until golden brown. Allow to cool on a wire rack before serving or packing for lunch.

Useful Items

large bowl, baking sheet and parchment paper, wire rack, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • For Chicken and Rice Muffins: spoon mixture (approx. 2.5 oz ea.) into a prepared muffin pan, press down with a spoon to compact and bake for 20-25 minutes (400°F) until tops turn golden brown. Let cool in pan for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack.
  • For pan-fried Chicken and Rice Patties: lightly fry in batches in a non-stick skillet over medium heat with 1-2 tbsp cooking oil.
