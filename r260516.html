Wholegrain mustard

Ingredients

  • 15 g black peppercorns
  • 125 g yellow mustard seeds
  • 180 g white vinegar
  • 120 g white wine
  • 200 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp white sugar
  • Â½ tsp ground turmeric
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
274.8 kJ / 65.4 kcal
Protein
1 g
Carbohydrates
1 g
Fat
6.2 g

Preparation

  1. Place all ingredients into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 4. Transfer into a sterilized glass jar and place in pantry for 5 days. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  2. Place mustard into mixing bowl and mix 30 sec/speed 8. Transfer into a sterilized jar and place into refrigerator for at least 1 week. Serve at room temperature.

Useful Items

sterilised sealable jars, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • You can store this mustard in the refrigerator for up to 8 weeks.
