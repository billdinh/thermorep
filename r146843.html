Pad Thai Noodles with Prawns

Ingredients

  • 200 g dried rice noodles, flat, medium
  • water, boiling, for soaking
  • 80 g roasted peanuts, unsalted,
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 fresh red chilli, deseeded if desired
  • 6 sprigs fresh coriander, roots and stems, reserving leaves for garnishing
  • 20 g olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 1 ½ Tbsp fish sauce
  • 40 g lime juice (approx. 2-3 limes)
  • 1 Tbsp tamarind paste
  • 2 Tbsp soy sauce
  • ¼ tsp shrimp paste
  • 3 tsp palm sugar (approx. 1 cm cube), grated
  • 2 medium eggs
  • 500 g water
  • 350 g raw prawns, peeled
  • 70 g fresh bean sprouts, for garnishing
  • 10 fresh chives, finely chopped, for garnishing
  • 3 Tbsp crispy, fried shallots, for garnishing
  • 1 lime, cut in wedges, for serving

Preparation

  1. Line both Varoma dish and tray with pieces of wet, well-wrung baking paper. Lightly grease baking paper on Varoma tray and set aside.
  2. Soak rice noodles in boiling water as per packet instructions. Drain noodles and place in a large serving bowl then cover to keep warm.
  3. Place peanuts in mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Rinse and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Place garlic, chilli and coriander roots and stems in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add oil and cook 1 min/100°C/speed 1. Transfer half of herb mixture to Varoma dish and set aside.
  6. Add fish sauce, lime juice, tamarind paste, soy sauce, shrimp paste and palm sugar then mix 10 sec/speed 5. Transfer sauce to bowl with noodles and stir to coat noodles. Cover noodles again and set aside to keep warm.
  7. Without cleaning mixing bowl, place eggs in mixing bowl and beat 3 sec/speed 5. Transfer to lined Varoma tray, and spread evenly to create a thin layer. Carefully set Varoma tray aside.
  8. Place water in mixing bowl, place Varoma dish with herb mixture into position then weigh in prawns. Stir gently with spatula to combine.
  9. Carefully insert Varoma tray, then cover Varoma and steam 6 min/Varoma/speed 1. Check prawns and transfer any cooked prawns to bowl with noodles.
  10. Cover Varoma then steam again 5-7 min/Varoma/speed 1 until prawns are fully cooked and eggs are cooked as desired.
  11. Remove Varoma tray. Slice omelette into thin strips then add strips and prawns to bowl with noodles. Stir to combine.
  12. Top Pad Thai with bean sprouts, chives, reserved coriander leaves, shallots and reserved chopped peanuts. Serve immediately with lime wedges on the side.

Useful Items

baking paper, large serving bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • Thermomix® is great for Asian food – whether steaming prawns and an omelette as part of this Pad Thai or cooking rice for Coconut Rice with Asian-inspired Pork and Eggs, the results are always fantastic and flavourful.
  • Shrimp paste, also known as belachan, can be found in some supermarkets or Asian stores.
  • Vegetarian option: substitute prawns with 350 g mushrooms, sliced in step 8 and remove shrimp paste and fish sauce from recipe. Proceed as per recipe.
