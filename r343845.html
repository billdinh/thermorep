Mushroom and Spinach Strata

Ingredients

  • 12 oz dried bread cubes
  • ½ oz extra virgin olive oil, plus extra to grease
  • 8 oz Gruyère cheese, cubed (1 in.), divided
  • 3 oz Parmesan cheese, cubed (1 in.)
  • 4 oz red onions, cut into pieces
  • ½ tsp dried parsley
  • ½ tsp dried thyme
  • 16 oz cremini mushrooms, sliced (¼ in.)
  • 4 oz baby spinach
  • 2 oz sun-dried tomatoes, in oil, lightly drained, sliced
  • 20 oz whole milk
  • 10 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper

Preparation

  1. Lightly grease an oval baking dish (2 qt.) and set aside. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid, weigh in bread cubes and set aside.
  2. Place 7 oz Gruyère cheese into mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a separate bowl and set aside.
  3. Place Parmesan cheese and remaining 1 oz Gruyère into mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 8. Transfer to bowl with grated Gruyère, stir to combine and set aside.
  4. Place onions, olive oil, parsley and thyme into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Add mushrooms and sauté 8 min/250°F//speed 1.
  5. Add spinach and sun-dried tomatoes and sauté 2 min/250°F//speed 1. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  6. Place milk, eggs, mustard, salt and pepper into mixing bowl and mix 15 sec/speed 4.
  7. Layer ⅓ of the bread cubes in prepared baking dish. Top with ½ the mushroom mixture and ½ the cheese mixture. Repeat layers and end with ⅓ layer of bread cubes.
  8. Evenly pour egg mixture over bread cubes in baking dish. Press down mixture with spatula. Cover with cling wrap and refrigerate 1 hour or overnight.
  9. Preheat oven to 350°F. Bake uncovered for 40-45 minutes (350°F) until golden brown. Let stand 10 minutes before serving.

Useful Items

cling wrap, oval baking dish (2.5 qt.), refrigerator, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • The ingredients in this dish are layered to create strata-like layers. However, the bread, mushroom and cheese can all be tossed together before adding the egg mixture.
