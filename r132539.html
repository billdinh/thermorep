Spiced Orange Roast Chicken and Basmati Rice with Raisins

Ingredients

Spiced Orange Roast Chicken

  • 6 - 8 chicken thighs, skin on
  • 6 garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp coriander seeds
  • ½ tbsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tbsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 30 g orange juice, freshly squeezed
  • 1 orange, preferably organic, cut in slices (0.5 cm)
  • 70 g green olives, pitted

Basmati Rice with Raisins

  • 1 tbsp flaked almonds
  • 100 g onions, cut in pieces
  • 150 g carrots, cut in pieces
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 1000 g water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 300 g basmati rice
  • 50 g raisins

Preparation

Spiced Orange Roast Chicken

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C. Line a baking tray with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place chicken thighs in a casserole dish and set aside.
  3. Place garlic cloves, coriander seeds, cumin seeds, oregano, salt and orange juice in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl using spatula.
  4. Chop again 5 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl, rub the chicken thighs with orange-garlic mixture, cover with orange slices and green olives and bake for 40-45 minutes (200°C). Meanwhile prepare the basmati rice with raisins.

Basmati Rice with Raisins

  1. Place almonds on prepared baking tray and bake for 5 minutes (200°C) or until golden.
  2. Place onions, carrots and olive oil in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Sauté 5 min/120°C/speed 1. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place water and salt in mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket, add basmati rice and raisins to it and then add reserved sautéed onions and carrots. Mix using spatula and cook 15-17 min/100°C/speed 4. Remove simmering basket with use of spatula, then transfer rice to a serving dish and garnish with reserved toasted almonds. Serve hot with baked chicken thighs.
