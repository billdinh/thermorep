Cauliflower and broccoli gratin

Ingredients

Breadcrumbs

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 2 slices bread, torn in pieces (approx. 100 g)
  • 10 g fresh parsley, (approx. 1 small handful)
  • 50 g Parmesan cheese, cut in pieces (3 cm)

Béchamel Sauce

  • 40 g butter, cut in pieces
  • 40 g plain flour
  • ¼ - ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • ½ tsp ground nutmeg
  • 500 g milk

Vegetables

  • 500 g water, for steaming
  • 150 - 300 g cauliflower florets, cut in pieces (2-3 cm)
  • 150 - 300 g broccoli florets, cut in pieces (2-3 cm)

Preparation

Breadcrumbs

  1. Place garlic, bread, parsley and Parmesan in mixing bowl and Turbo/2 sec/3-5 times. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.

Béchamel Sauce

  1. Place butter, flour, salt and nutmeg in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  2. Add milk then cook 7 min/90°C/speed 3. Transfer to a baking dish and set aside.

Vegetables

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C. Meanwhile, place water in mixing bowl then set Varoma dish in position. Weigh cauliflower into Varoma dish and broccoli onto Varoma tray, then cover and steam 14 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  2. Transfer steamed vegetables to baking dish containing sauce and stir to coat vegetables. Sprinkle reserved breadcrumb mix on top and bake for 10-15 minutes (220°C) until sauce is bubbling and topping is golden. Serve hot.

Hints & Tricks

  • The recipe serves 6 as a side dish when made with 300 g vegetables. To serve 4-6 as a main course, make it with 600 g vegetables.
  • To make smaller or larger quantities of sauce, decrease or increase the proportions of butter, flour, milk from 20/20/200 g to 150/150/1500 g, adjusting the cooking time as needed.
  • For a thicker sauce, increase flour to 50 g.
  • To make Cheese sauce, add 80 g diced Cheddar cheese (1 cm dice) after cooking in step 3, then cook again 1 min/90°C/speed 4.
  • For a dairy free bechamel sauce, use olive oil instead of butter and use a non-dairy milk, e.g. almond milk, rice milk, oat milk (all easily made in your Thermomix®). Avoid soya milk as it curdles easily in this sauce.
  • For dairy free crumbs, omit Parmesan, and to add more flavour, add a pinch of nutritional yeast flakes.
  • For gluten free crumbs, use gluten free bread.
  • For a gluten free sauce, replace the 40 g plain flour with 30 g gluten-free flour, e.g. lentil flour, chickpea flour, rice flour (all easily ground in your Thermomix®) or cornflour and cook 2 minutes longer in step 3.
