Mozzarella and Tomato Pancake Stack

Ingredients

Pancakes

  • 40 g Parmesan cheese, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 125 g plain flour
  • 300 g whole milk
  • 2 medium eggs
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt

Tomato Sauce

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 80 g onions, quartered
  • 60 g celery stalks, cut in pieces
  • 15 g olive oil
  • 800 g tinned chopped tomatoes
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tsp pomegranate molasses
  • 1 tsp tomato purée
  • 1 Tbsp unsalted butter
  • fine sea salt, to taste
  • ground black pepper, to taste

Béchamel

  • 35 g unsalted butter, diced, plus extra for greasing
  • 35 g plain flour
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • fine sea salt, to taste
  • ground black pepper, to taste
  • 425 g whole milk

Assembly

  • 240 g mozzarella cheese, drained, diced (1 cm)
  • 1 bunch fresh basil, leaves only, chopped

Preparation

Pancakes

  1. Place Parmesan in mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place flour, milk, eggs and salt in mixing bowl then whisk 15 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a jug and set aside to rest for 30 minutes. Meanwhile, rinse mixing bowl and make tomato sauce.

Tomato Sauce

  1. Place garlic, onions, celery and oil in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then sauté 5 min/100°C/speed 0.5.
  2. Add tomatoes, bay leaf, molasses and tomato purée then, with simmering basket in place of measuring cup, simmer 30 min/98°C/speed 0.5. Meanwhile, once batter has rested, heat 1 Tbsp butter in a large frying pan then add a small ladle of batter. Fry until golden and bubbling then flip over. Transfer to a plate and repeat with remaining batter (see tip).
  3. Insert measuring cup and blend tomato sauce briefly 3 sec/speed 5. Season to taste then transfer to a bowl. Clean mixing bowl.

Béchamel

  1. Place butter, flour, nutmeg, salt and pepper in mixing bowl then mix 5 sec/speed 5.
  2. Add milk and cook 7 min/90°C/speed 3.
  3. Add reserved tomato sauce and stir 10 sec/speed 3.

Assembly

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C and grease a gratin dish.
  2. Place a pancake in base of prepared dish. Cover with a thin layer of tomato béchamel. Sprinkle with some mozzarella, Parmesan and basil then top with another pancake. Repeat with remaining fillings until all pancakes have been used, finishing with a top layer of béchamel, Parmesan and basil (no mozzarella). Cover dish with foil, then place on a baking tray to catch any drips and bake for 20 minutes (180°C). Remove foil and bake for a further 10 minutes (180°C) or until bubbling.
  3. Remove from oven and leave to stand for 5 minutes. Slice into wedges and serve with a green salad.

Useful Items

large frying pan, greaseproof paper, gratin dish, aluminium foil, baking tray, jug, ladle, fish slice, hob, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Place greaseproof paper between pancakes in step 4 to prevent them from sticking together.
  • Like savoury pancakes? Like lasagne? Why not combine the two? This irresistible layered pancake stack has the classic flavours of tomato, basil and mozzarella layered between delicate savoury pancakes. Cut in wedges like a pizza and serve alongside steamed vegetables or a fresh green salad for an economical and fun meal.
