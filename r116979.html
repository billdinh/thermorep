Paleo Lasagne

Ingredients

Meat Filling

  • 180 g onions, quartered
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 400 g minced meat (e.g. beef, turkey or pork), broken in pieces
  • 250 g passata
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
  • 2 tsp dried oregano
  • 10 pitted black olives, quartered lengthways

'Cheese' Sauce

  • 100 g ghee
  • 400 g coconut milk, (carton not tin)
  • 4 Tbsp arrowroot
  • 4 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 tsp garlic granules
  • 2 large eggs

Topping

  • 300 - 400 g sweet potato, peeled (approx. 1 medium potato)

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C.

Meat Filling

  1. Place onions and garlic in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add oil and sauté 3 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Add minced meat, passata, salt, oregano and black olives then cook 15 min/Varoma/speed . Transfer to  a bowl and set aside.

'Cheese' Sauce

  1. Place ghee in mixing bowl and melt 2 min/100°C/speed 1.
  2. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in coconut milk then remove from lid.
  3. Add to bowl with coconut milk, arrowroot, lemon juice, salt, garlic granules and eggs then combine with a whisk until no lumps are visible.
  4. Add coconut and arrowroot mixture, stir 1 min/speed 1 then mix 10 sec/speed 4.

Topping

  1. Thinly slice sweet potato into rounds (approx. 1 mm).
  2. Layer a casserole dish (approx. 24 cm x 17 cm) with one layer of sweet potato, one layer of ‘cheese’ sauce then meat filling. Alternate until all filling is used up finishing with sweet potato and then ‘cheese’ sauce on top (about two meat and three sauce and sweet potato layers) - the cheese sauce will be runny at this stage but will thicken as the lasagne cooks.
  3. Bake for 30-40 minutes (190°C) until sweet potato is cooked through. Serve with a mixed salad or roasted vegetables.

Useful Items

wire whisk, long sharp knife, casserole dish, oven
