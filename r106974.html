Chocolate Fudge Cake

Ingredients

Cake

  • 7 oz unsalted butter, in pieces, plus extra to grease
  • 7 oz dark chocolate, minimum 63% cocoa, chips or pieces
  • 3 ½ oz water
  • 9 oz all-purpose flour
  • 1 oz cocoa powder (approx. 4 tbsp)
  • 1 heaping tbsp baking powder
  • 6 ½ oz light brown sugar
  • 6 ½ oz sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 3 oz crème fraîche,

Icing

  • 7 oz dark chocolate, minimum 60% cocoa, chips or pieces
  • 7 oz unsalted butter, in pieces, softened
  • 7 oz confectioners sugar

Preparation

Cake

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F. Grease and line two round cake pans (approx. 8 in.) with parchment and set aside.
  2. Place chocolate into mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add butter and water and melt 2 min/175°F/speed 1.5.
  4. Add flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, brown sugar, sugar, eggs and crème fraîche and mix 20 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix again 10 sec/speed 5.
  5. Divide cake batter between prepared cake pans and bake for 35-40 minutes (325°F) until a skewer inserted in center comes out clean. Clean mixing bowl.
  6. Remove pans from oven and leave to cool for 10 minutes before removing cake from pans and transferring to a wire rack to finish cooling. Meanwhile make icing.

Icing

  1. Place chocolate into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 10 or until chocolate is finely chopped. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl and melt 4 min/120°F/speed 2.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk, add butter and confectioners sugar and mix 30 sec/speed 3.5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix again 20 sec/speed 3.5.
  3. Stack the two cakes together with icing and cover sides and top with remaining icing. Decorate as desired before serving.

Useful Items

parchment paper, toothpick or skewer, round cake pans (approx. 8 in.), wire rack, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • A rich but light cake that is a guaranteed crowd pleaser. It is perfect as a birthday or celebration cake with its thick icing and delicious flavor.
  • Before stacking cakes together trim edges so they are even before icing.
