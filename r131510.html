Salmorejo

Ingredients

  • 150 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 4 eggs, hard-boiled (to garnish)
  • 100 g Serrano ham, diced (to garnish)
  • 1 - 2 garlic cloves
  • 1000 g tomatoes, ripe
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 150 g breadcrumbs, white (from the day before)
  • 30 g vinegar

Preparation

  1. Place a jug onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in the olive oil. Set aside.
  2. Place hard-boiled eggs into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place ham into mixing bowl and chop Turbo/0.5 sec/4-6 times. The number of times will depend on the hardness of the ham. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place garlic, tomatoes and salt into mixing bowl and chop 30 sec/speed 5.
  5. Add breadcrumbs and vinegar. Mix 30 sec/speed 5 and then blend 2 min/speed 10.
  6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Mix 1 min/speed 5 and, while the blades are moving, pour the olive oil little by little onto the mixing bowl lid until incorporated. Transfer the soup to a serving bowl and refrigerate.
  7. Serve very cold and garnish with the Serrano ham and chopped hard-boiled eggs.
