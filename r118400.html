Herb-salted chicken and chickpea casserole

Ingredients

Crumb

  • 40 g manchego cheese, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 80 g day old sourdough bread
  • 60 g hazelnuts, without skin, roasted

Herb salt

  • 10 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves only
  • 3 sprigs fresh oregano, leaves only
  • 3 tsp flaked salt
  • 1 garlic clove

Casserole

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 30 g olive oil, plus 1 tbsp extra for frying
  • 120 g bacon, cut into pieces (1 cm)
  • 2 dried bay leaves
  • 200 g red capsicum, deseeded and cut into strips
  • 300 g water
  • ½ tsp Chicken stock paste (see Tips)
  • 20 g sherry vinegar
  • 6 boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • 400 g canned chickpeas (approx. 250 g after draining)
  • 150 g fresh green beans, trimmed

Preparation

Crumb

  1. Place cheese into mixing bowl and grate 2 sec/speed 8. Transfer into a small bowl and set aside.
  2. Place bread and hazelnuts into mixing bowl and chop 6 sec/speed 8. Transfer into a separate bowl, thoroughly scraping sides of mixing bowl with spatula and set aside. Do not clean mixing bowl.

Herb salt

  1. Place all herb salt ingredients into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a small bowl, thoroughly scraping sides of mixing bowl with spatula and set aside. Do not clean mixing bowl.

Casserole

  1. Place garlic into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add oil, bacon, bay leaves and capsicum and sauté 8 min/Varoma//speed . Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add water, stock paste and sherry vinegar and cook 2 min/Varoma//speed 1.
  4. Season both sides of chicken thighs with reserved herb salt. Place chicken into Varoma dish and tray, ensuring steam can circulate freely. Place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 15 min/Varoma//speed 1. Set Varoma aside.
  5. Add chickpeas and green beans into mixing bowl, place Varoma back into position, secure Varoma lid and cook 5 min/Varoma//speed 2. Check to see if your chicken is cooked through by piercing thickest part of the thigh with a skewer (juices will run clear when cooked). If needed, cook for a further 3 min/Varoma//speed 2 . Set Varoma aside.
  6. Add 60 g of reserved crumb mixture and cook 2 min/100°C//speed 1.
  7. Place a frying pan over medium-high heat and add extra 1 tablespoon oil. When hot, add remaining reserved crumb mixture and cook until golden and crunchy. Cool for 1 minute, then add reserved cheese and stir through.
  8. Divide chicken and casserole between serving plates and sprinkle with crumb. Serve with extra crumb on the side.

Useful Items

small bowl , bowl, metal skewers, frying pan (19 cm), serving plates, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • Please refer to The Basic Cookbook or Everyday Cookbook for the Chicken stock paste recipe.
  • Manchego is a hard sheep's milk cheese; replace with pecorino if desired.
