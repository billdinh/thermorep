Date and Bran Muffins

Ingredients

  • 180 g spelt flour
  • 110 g caster sugar
  • 100 g oat bran
  • 120 g pitted dates
  • 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 125 g vegetable oil
  • 375 g buttermilk
  • 1 large egg
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
1341 kJ / 320 kcal
Protein
6.9 g
Carbohydrates

Preparation

  1. Place flour, sugar, oat bran, dates, bicarbonate of soda and cinnamon in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 6.
  2. Add oil, buttermilk and egg then mix 15 sec//speed 3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then mix again 15 sec//speed 3. If necessary, finish mixing by hand with spatula. Transfer to a jug, cover and refrigerate overnight.
  3. The next day, preheat oven to 170°C and line a 12 hole muffin tin with 10 paper muffin cases.
  4. Divide rested mixture evenly between muffin cases then bake for 25-30 minutes (170°C) until cooked through (see tip) and golden brown.
  5. Serve warm or at room temperature or store in an airtight container until needed.

Useful Items

cling film, jug, 12 hole muffin tin, muffin paper cases, airtight container, refrigerator, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • To test muffins are cooked, lightly press a finger on top of the muffin. If it bounces back up, it is cooked, if not return to the oven for an additional 2 minutes and test again.
  • These muffins can be made and baked on the same day but a better result will be achieved if the mixture is refrigerated overnight and the muffins baked the following day.

These muffins work well as a breakfast on the go. Bran is an excellent source of dietary fibre, and the inclusion of dates means there is less refined sugar than you might usually start your day with.
