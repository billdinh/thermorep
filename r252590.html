Sausage Mummies

Ingredients

  • 510 g water
  • 48 pork sausages, raw
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • 2 tsp milk
  • 510 g puff pastry
  • mustard, to garnish
  • ketchup or dipping sauce, to serve
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
406 kJ / 97 kcal
Protein
2 g
Carbohydrates
5 g
Fat
8 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C. Line 2 baking trays with baking paper.
  2. Place water in mixing bowl and place Varoma dish into position. Place sausages in Varoma then steam 10 min/Varoma/speed 3.
  3. Meanwhile, in a small bowl, whisk together egg and milk, roll out puff pastry (approx. 0.5 cm thick) then lightly brush pastry with beaten egg mixture. Cut pastry into 0.5 cm strips.
  4. Remove Varoma and allow sausages to cool. Dry sausages with kitchen towel and wrap 1 pastry strip (approx. 15 cm long) around each sausage, slightly overlapping pastry to resemble bandages, leaving an open space for the eyes. Place on prepared baking trays and continue with remaining mummies.
  5. Bake for 15-20 minutes (200°C) or until pastry is golden brown and puffed. Allow to cool slightly before garnishing with mustard to create eyes (see tip). Serve warm with ketchup or your favourite dipping sauce.

Useful Items

baking paper, rolling pin, pastry brush, sharp knife, small bowl, large baking trays, kitchen towel, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Make your own puff pastry ahead of time using the recipe in the Basic Cookbook. Roll out pastry to a 0.5 cm thick square and continue with the steps in this recipe.
  • Use the end of a chopstick to help apply eyes to the Sausage Mummies.
  • If preferred, use other small sausages e.g. chipolata.
