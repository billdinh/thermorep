Tzatziki

Ingredients

  • 350 g cucumbers, peeled, deseeded, cut in pieces
  • 1 tsp salt, plus extra to taste
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 5 sprigs fresh dill
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 350 g Greek yoghurt, 10% fat
  • 15 g lemon juice (optional)
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper, plus extra to taste
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
676 kJ / 161 kcal

Preparation

  1. Place cucumber and salt in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Transfer cucumber to simmering basket and drain for approx. 10 minutes. Using a large spoon or your hands, squeeze as much water out of chopped cucumber as possible. Set aside.
  2. Place garlic cloves, dill and olive oil in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5.
  3. Add reserved chopped cucumbers, Greek yoghurt, lemon juice and black pepper and mix 20 sec//speed 2.5. Adjust seasoning, transfer tzatziki to a bowl and refrigerate before serving.

Useful Items

refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve tzatziki with pitta bread, crusty bread, grilled meat or baked potatoes.
  • The cucumbers may be left unpeeled.
  • Replace Greek yoghurt with a combination of 200 g plain yoghurt (3.5% fat) and 200 g curd cheese (20% fat).
