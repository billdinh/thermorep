Popcorn and Buckwheat Bread

Ingredients

  • 30 g olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 170 g popping corn
  • 65 g buckwheat grains, raw
  • 80 g quinoa
  • 40 g chia seeds
  • 1 tsp dried instant yeast
  • 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 25 g honey
  • 250 g water, lukewarm
Nutrition
per 1 slice
Calories
573 kJ / 137 kcal
Protein
3.8 g
Carbohydrates
19.4 g
Fat
4.9 g

Preparation

  1. Place popping corn, buckwheat grains, quinoa and chia seeds in mixing bowl then grind 1 min/speed 10.
  2. Add yeast, bicarbonate of soda, salt, olive oil, honey and water then knead 3 min/. Meanwhile grease a 450 g loaf tin with oil. Transfer dough to greased loaf tin and spread out evenly. Cover loaf tin with cling film and leave to rest for 1 hour.
  3. Preheat oven to 160°C. Bake for 45-55 minutes (160°C) or until a rich brown colour. Allow to cool completely on a wire rack before slicing.

Useful Items

cling film, 450 g loaf tin (1 lb), cooling rack, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Buckwheat flour can be used instead of buckwheat grains.
