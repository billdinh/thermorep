Brussels sprouts and buckwheat winter salad

Ingredients

  • 60 g buckwheat
  • 600 g water
  • ½ tsp salt, plus 1-2 pinches
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 250 g bacon, cut into pieces (2 cm)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 80 g white wine vinegar
  • 2 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 ½ tbsp pure maple syrup
  • 1 - 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 500 g Brussels sprouts, trimmed and outer leaves removed
  • 1 tbsp pine nuts
  • 25 g Parmesan cheese, shaved

Preparation

  1. Insert simmering basket, then weigh buckwheat into it. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and rinse buckwheat until water runs clear. Set aside to drain.
  2. Place water and salt into mixing bowl and heat 2 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  3. Re-Insert simmering basket with buckwheat and cook 9 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  4. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and transfer buckwheat into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and cover to keep warm. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  5. Place garlic into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  6. Add bacon and chop 5 sec/speed 4.
  7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and cook 3 min/Varoma//speed 1. Place a large sieve or colander over a bowl and transfer bacon into sieve to strain liquid. Retain bacon pieces.
  8. Place strained liquid back into mixing bowl.
  9. Place a frying pan over medium heat. Place oil and bacon pieces into frying pan and cook for approx. 5 minutes, or until brown, turning regularly to prevent burning. Transfer bacon into thermal serving bowl or other large bowl with buckwheat and set aside.
  10. Add vinegar, mustard, syrup, salt and pepper into a mixing bowl and mix 5 sec/speed 5.
  11. Add Brussels sprouts and chop 5 sec/speed 5, then cook 4 min/100°C//speed 1. Transfer into bowl with buckwheat and bacon and toss to combine. Scatter over pine nuts and shaved Parmesan just before serving.

Useful Items

thermal serving bowl or other large bowl, large bowl, sieve, frying pan, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • You can serve this salad warm or at room temperature.
