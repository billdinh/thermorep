Salmon Fillets with Orange Glaze, Buckwheat Risotto and Steamed Asparagus

Ingredients

  • ½ oz orange peel, thin skin only, no pith
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 3 ½ oz orange juice, freshly squeezed (approx. 2 medium oranges)
  • 1 oz honey
  • ¼ tsp wasabi paste, to taste (optional)
  • 4 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only (approx. 35 leaves)
  • 2 sprigs fresh dill, leaves only (approx. 28 clumps of fine leaves)
  • 4 sprigs cilantro, leaves only (approx. 42 leaves )
  • 4 fresh salmon fillets, skinless (approx. 16-24 oz total)
  • 1 - 2 pinches salt, plus extra to taste
  • 1 - 2 pinches ground black pepper, plus extra to taste
  • 2 leeks, white part only, cut into quarters (approx. 5.5 oz total)
  • 2 oz extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 oz salted butter
  • 10 ½ oz buckwheat groats
  • 3 ½ oz dry white wine
  • 26 ½ oz water
  • 2 tbsp vegetable stock paste (see Tip)
  • 1 - 2 oz lemon juice, freshly squeezed (approx. 2 small lemons)
  • 14 - 15 oz asparagus, trimmed and cut into halves (approx. 1 bunch)
  • 3 ½ oz cashews, roasted, unsalted (optional)
  • 1 oz unsalted pumpkin seeds
  • 2 oz goat cheese, crumbled

Preparation

  1. Place orange peel and sugar into mixing bowl and zest 30 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add orange juice, honey and wasabi paste into mixing bowl and without measuring cup cook 7-8 min/Varoma/speed 1, until a thick glaze consistency is achieved. Transfer orange glaze to a bowl and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.
  3. Place parsley, dill and cilantro into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place salmon into Varoma dish and tray, season with salt and pepper and set aside.
  5. Place leek, oil and butter into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 6.5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and sauté 3 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  6. Add buckwheat, wine, water and stock paste. Place Varoma with salmon into position. Brush salmon fillets with half the reserved orange glaze, secure Varoma lid and steam 15 min/Varoma//speed 1.5.
  7. Check to see if salmon fillets are cooked to your liking. If salmon is cooked, transfer to a large bowl and cover to keep warm. If salmon is not cooked, leave fillets in Varoma and set Varoma aside.
  8. Add lemon juice and reserved chopped herbs into mixing bowl and season to taste with a little extra salt and pepper if necessary.
  9. Place Varoma back into position, place asparagus into the Varoma dish and onto the Varoma tray arranging around the salmon (if your salmon requires longer cooking). Secure Varoma lid and steam 3-5 min/Varoma//speed 1.5, or until salmon and asparagus are cooked to your liking. Set Varoma aside.
  10. Add cashews and pumpkin seeds or sunflower seeds to mixing bowl and combine 1 min//speed 2.
  11. Place buckwheat and steamed asparagus onto a plate and top with salmon fillets. Brush salmon fillets with remaining orange glaze, garnish risotto with crumbled goat cheese and serve immediately.

Useful Items

pastry brush, large bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • Will serve 4-6 portions, depending on size of fish fillets, appetite levels and number of diners.
  • Please refer to The Basic Cookbook for the Vegetable Stock Paste recipe.
  • Ask attendant at the seafood counter to remove skin and bones from salmon to save time.
