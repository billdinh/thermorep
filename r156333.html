Pork tenderloin with sauerkraut and potato purée

Ingredients

  • 100 g brown onions, cut into halves
  • 100 g bacon cubes
  • 500 - 600 g pork tenderloins
  • 2 tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground pepper
  • 1 - 1 ½ tsp ground sweet paprika
  • 6 - 8 slices lean bacon, smoked (60-80 g)
  • 500 g preserved sauerkraut
  • 1 dried bay leaf (optional)
  • 3 juniper berries (optional)
  • 500 g water
  • 750 g potatoes, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 3 tbsp oil, for frying
  • 250 g onions, cut into halves
  • 140 g full cream milk
  • 40 g unsalted butter
  • 2 - 3 pinches ground nutmeg

Preparation

  1. Place 100 g of the onions into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add bacon cubes and sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1. Meanwhile, season pork tenderloins with ½ tsp of the salt, pepper and sweet paprika, wrap it in bacon slices and set aside.
  3. Add sauerkraut, bay leaf(optional) and juniper berries (optional) into mixing bowl and cook 3 min/120°C/speed 1. Drain contents of mixing bowl through Varoma dish over a bowl. Set Varoma dish aside and reserve the collected cooking liquid.
  4. Place water and 1¼ tsp of the salt into mixing bowl, insert simmering basket and weigh potatoes into it. Season potatoes by sprinkling with reserved cooking liquid. Place Varoma dish containing sauerkraut into position, place wrapped tenderloins onto sauerkraut, secure Varoma lid and steam 35 min/Varoma/speed 1. In the last 10 minutes of cooking time, place a frying pan over high heat and add oil. Slice remaining 250 g onions and fry them until golden brown. Remove Varoma and remove simmering basket with aid of spatula, set both aside and discard contents in mixing bowl.
  5. Place milk, butter, remaining ¼ tsp salt, nutmeg and cooked potatoes into mixing bowl and purée with aid of spatula 25 sec/speed 3.5. Slice the tenderloins and arrange onto a dish with sauerkraut and potato purée. Top the meat and purée with fried onions and serve hot.

Useful Items

frying pan, serving dish, bowl, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • Replace the pork tenderloin with smoked pork loin, thick slices of bacon (1.5 cm) or smoked pork sausages.
    - For a quicker and lighter recipe, omit fried onions and bacon slices.
