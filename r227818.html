Shrimp Scampi

Ingredients

  • 3 garlic cloves
  • ½ oz parsley, leaves and tender stems
  • 2 tsp salt, divided
  • 7 oz unsalted butter, in pieces
  • 40 oz water
  • 24 oz jumbo shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 8 oz angel hair pasta
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
2611 kJ / 624 kcal
Protein
30 g
Carbohydrates
29 g
Fat
44 g

Preparation

  1. Place garlic, parsley and ½ tsp salt into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of bowl with spatula.
  2. Add butter and cook 2 min/212°F/speed 1. Transfer to a bowl and keep warm.
  3. Place water and 1½ tsp salt into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position, weigh in shrimp, secure Varoma lid and steam 15 min/Varoma/speed 1. Carefully remove Varoma lid, stir shrimp with spatula, secure Varoma lid and steam a further 2-5 min/Varoma/speed 1, until shrimp are cooked through. Remove Varoma, transfer shrimp into bowl with reserved butter, combine with spatula and set aside.
  4. Bring water back to a boil 3-5 min/212°F/speed 1. Add pasta through hole in mixing bowl lid and without measuring cup cook for time indicated on package/212°F/speed 1.
  5. Drain pasta through Varoma, toss with reserved shrimp and serve hot.
