Chocolate halva cake

Ingredients

Cake

  • 180 g unsalted butter, softened, plus extra for greasing
  • 30 g white sesame seeds
  • 100 g blanched almonds
  • 150 g caster sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract
  • 180 g black tahini (see Tips)
  • 30 g gluten free flour mix (see Tips)
  • 1 tsp gluten free baking powder (see Tips)

Ganache

  • 200 g dark chocolate (gluten free), broken into pieces
  • 80 g pouring (whipping) cream

Preparation

Cake

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C. Grease and line a round cake tin (20 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place sesame seeds and almonds into mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a small bowl and set aside.
  3. Place butter and sugar into mixing bowl and mix 20 sec/speed 4.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix for a further 20 sec/speed 4.
  5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix for a further 20 sec/speed 4, or until combined.
  6. Mix 20 sec/speed 4, slowly adding eggs one at a time through hole in mixing bowl lid.
  7. Add vanilla and black tahini and mix 10 sec/speed 4.
  8. Add flour mix, baking powder and reserved milled sesame seed mixture and mix 5 sec/speed 5.
  9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix 5 sec/speed 4.
  10. Transfer into prepared tin and bake for 30-35 minutes (160°C) until firm to the touch and a wooden skewer inserted into the centre of the cake comes out clean. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  11. Allow cake to cool in tin for 5 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack to cool completely.

Ganache

  1. Place chocolate into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add cream and heat 3 min/50°C/speed 3, or until smooth.
  3. Allow to cool to room temperature in mixing bowl (approx. 40 minutes) to thicken and cool (see Tips).
  4. Place cooled cake onto a serving plate. Spread ganache over cake and serve (see Tips).

Useful Items

round cake tin (20 cm), baking paper, small bowl , wooden skewer, wire rack, serving plate, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • You can buy black tahini (made from black sesame seeds) at delicatessens and health food stores.
  • Please refer to the Gluten free flour mix recipe on this recipe chip.
  • To make your own Gluten free baking powder, please refer to the recipe on this recipe chip.
  • A square cake tin (20 cm) can be used for this recipe.
  • Different brands and varying quality levels of chocolate set at different times, so cooling and setting time for ganache is a guideline only (Step 14).
  • Cake is best eaten on the day of baking.
