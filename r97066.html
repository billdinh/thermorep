Chicken Chia Burgers

Ingredients

  • 40 g quinoa
  • 220 g water
  • 100 g carrots, cut in pieces
  • 100 g courgettes, cut in pieces
  • 500 g chicken, boneless thigh or breast, diced (3 cm)
  • 2 - 3 tsp chia seeds, to taste
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • ¼ - ½ tsp ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1 - 2 Tbsp olive oil, for brushing
  • 8 burger buns
  • 8 cos lettuce leaves
  • 2 tomatoes, sliced

Preparation

  1. Place quinoa and water in mixing bowl then cook 17 min/100°C//speed 1. Transfer to a bowl and set aside to cool.
  2. Place carrots and courgettes in mixing bowl then chop 10 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add chicken and mince 15 sec/speed 5.
  4. Add chia seeds, salt, pepper and reserved cooked quinoa then mix 15 sec//speed 5 until well combined. Tip out and shape into 8 equal-sized patties. Place on a plate, cover with cling film and place in fridge for 15 minutes to firm up. Meanwhile, light barbecue or preheat grill to 220°C.
  5. Brush patties with oil and cook for 20-25 minutes on a medium barbecue or under a hot grill (220°C), turning halfway through, until golden brown on each side and chicken is thoroughly cooked. Serve with burger buns, lettuce leaves and tomatoes.

Useful Items

cling film, pastry brush, refrigerator, grill

Hints & Tricks

  • These burgers are a great make-ahead option for a delicious midweek meal, or for adding to a weekend barbecue when extra guests arrive! For other great burger recipes refer to Cookidoo®.
  • If mixture is wet and difficult to handle, use floured hands to shape the patties.
