Chicken noodle soup

Ingredients

  • 100 g onions, quartered
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 100 g carrots, sliced (1 cm)
  • 100 g celery stalks, sliced (1 cm)
  • 1100 g water
  • 1 - 2 chicken stock cubes (for 0.5 l each), to taste
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper, or to taste
  • 2 dried bay leaves
  • 100 g dried Chinese egg noodles (medium)
  • 300 g cooked chicken meat, diced (1-2 cm)
Nutrition

Preparation

  1. Place onions and oil in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add carrots and celery then sauté 5 min/120°C//speed 0.5.
  3. Add water, chicken stock cube, basil, oregano, pepper and bay leaves then simmer 8 min/98°C//speed 0.5.
  4. Add noodles and chicken then cook 10 min/98°C//speed 0.5, while stirring with spatula through hole in mixing bowl lid, until noodles are tender. Adjust seasoning if necessary and discard bay leaves then ladle into warm bowls and serve.
