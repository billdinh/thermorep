Pecan Pie Tarts

Ingredients

Pastry

  • 2 ½ oz unsalted butter, chilled, cut into pieces, plus extra to grease
  • 5 oz pastry flour
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 oz confectioners sugar
  • 2 egg yolks, from large eggs
  • 1 tbsp whole milk, chilled

Filling

  • 7 oz pecan halves
  • 2 oz soft dark brown sugar
  • 2 oz unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 4 oz maple syrup
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 - 3 tbsp apple jelly (optional)

Preparation

Pastry

  1. Grease 20 tart pans and set aside.
  2. Place flour, baking powder, salt, butter, sugar, egg yolks and milk into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 6. Wrap dough in cling wrap and refrigerate for 20 minutes.
  3. Roll dough out between 2 pieces of cling wrap to ⅛-¼ inch thick. Cut 20 circles using a round cookie cutter (2½ in.). Place dough into tart pans and press into shape. Prick dough with a fork and place in freezer while preparing the filling.

Filling

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in pecans. Reserve 20 pecan halves. Place remaining pecans into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 5.
  2. Add sugar, butter, maple syrup and eggs and mix 15 sec/speed 3. Fill chilled tarts with filling, garnish with reserved nuts and bake for 20-25 minutes (350°F). Allow to cool 5 minutes and while still hot brush tops with jelly. Cool completely before removing from tart pans. Serve immediately or store in an airtight container.

Useful Items

cling wrap, round cookie cutter (2½ in.), rolling pin, small tart pans (approx. 1½ in. base), refrigerator, freezer, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Dough should not be overworked. If dough becomes soft, place it in the refrigerator (approx. 15 min.) until it can be handled easily again.
  • Replace tart pans with a 12-hole cupcake pan. Grease generously with butter. Divide dough and roll out 12 circles (3¼ in.). Place pastry into each cupcake hole and press into shape. After tarts cool, run a small knife around edges of tarts to loosen for easy removal.
  • Place tarts back in the oven for 5 minutes to caramelize and set the jelly.
  • Replace pecan halves with pecan pieces.
  • Orange zest can be added to filling to intensify the flavor.
