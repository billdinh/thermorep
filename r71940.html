Prawn stir-fry

Ingredients

Five spice powder

  • 2 tsp szechuan peppercorns
  • 3 star anise
  • 2 tsp fennel seeds
  • 2 cinnamon quills
  • 6 whole cloves

Stir-fry

  • 1 cm cube fresh ginger, peeled
  • ½ - 1 long red chilli, deseeded and cut into halves, to taste
  • ½ lime, zest and juice only, no white pith
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 - 2 tsp palm sugar, grated (or 1 - 2 cm cube), to taste
  • 4 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 300 g raw prawns, peeled and cleaned
  • 120 g basmati rice
  • 800 g water
  • 50 g fresh green beans, trimmed and cut into thirds
  • ¼ red capsicum, cut into strips (1 cm) then strips cut into halves
  • 3 broccolini, cut into thirds
  • 4 snow peas, cut into thirds
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp rice wine vinegar
  • 1 spring onion/shallot, green part only, finely sliced, to serve
  • fresh coriander, leaves only, to serve

Preparation

Five spice powder

  1. Place all five spice ingredients into mixing bowl and dry roast 5 min/120°C/speed 1. Allow to cool then mill 30 sec/speed 10. Transfer into a sealable jar or container to store until ready to use.

Stir-fry

  1. Place ginger, chilli, lime zest, garlic and palm sugar into mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add 1 teaspoon sesame oil, lime juice, soy sauce, reserved five spice powder and prawns and combine 5 sec//speed 1. Transfer into a ceramic or glass bowl and place into refrigerator to marinate. Rinse and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Insert simmering basket and weigh rice into it. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and rinse rice until water runs clear. Place water into mixing bowl then insert simmering basket and cook 8 min/100°C/speed 4.
  4. Place Varoma dish into position. Place green beans, capsicum, broccolini and snow peas into Varoma dish and steam 4 min/Varoma/speed 1. Check rice is cooked. Transfer rice and vegetables into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and cover to keep warm. Empty mixing bowl.
  5. Place remaining 3 teaspoons sesame oil into mixing bowl and heat 1 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  6. Add reserved marinated prawns, honey and rice wine vinegar and cook 2 min 30 sec/Varoma//speed , or until prawns are cooked through.
  7. Strain cooking liquid through simmering basket into a bowl and set aside. Place cooked prawns into mixing bowl with reserved rice and vegetables and combine 3 sec//speed 2.
  8. Serve hot with finely sliced spring onion/shallot, coriander and a drizzle of the reserved cooking liquid.

Useful Items

sealable storage jar or container, ceramic or glass bowl, thermal serving bowl or other large bowl, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Recipe for one: reduce prawns to 150 g and rice to 60 g.  In step 6 only use 2 tsp sesame oil and proceed as per recipe.
  • Prawns are high in protein, omega 3 fatty acids and selenium whilst being low in saturated fats and calories.
  • Freeze any leftover prawns in a plastic freezer bag to make the Prawn and saffron risotto another day.
  • You can substitute soy sauce with tamari to make this recipe gluten free.
  • Store leftover Five spice powder in a cool, dark place.
