Linguini with Bacon and Prawns

Ingredients

  • 1200 g water
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 250 g fresh linguini pasta
  • 50 g shallots, halved
  • 50 g onions, quartered
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 100 g bacon cubes
  • 150 g raw prawns, medium size, peeled
  • 150 g double cream
  • 2 egg yolks, from medium eggs
  • fresh lemon thyme, leaves only, for garnish
Nutrition
per 1 portion

1192 kcal

45 g

Preparation

  1. Place water and salt in mixing bowl then heat 10 min/100°C/speed 1.
  2. Add linguini through hole in mixing bowl lid, then without measuring cup, cook select time indicated on packet/100°C//speed 1 until al dente. Drain pasta using simmering basket then transfer to a serving bowl, cover and set aside.
  3. Place shallots, onion, oil and garlic in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Heat 5 min/120°C/speed 1.
  4. Add bacon and prawns then sauté 3 min/120°C/speed .
  5. Add cream and egg yolks then cook 2 min/90°C/speed . Mix with cooked pasta, then sprinkle with lemon thyme before serving.

Useful Items

serving bowl, cling film

Hints & Tricks

  • If the water foams during cooking, add 1 tsp of oil or butter to reduce foam.
