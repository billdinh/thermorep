Satay Turkey Skewers

Ingredients

  • 300 g unsalted peanuts
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • Â½ tsp dried chilli flakes
  • 1 tsp brown sugar
  • 1 lime, freshly squeezed juice only
  • 1 tsp fish sauce
  • 5 g fresh coriander leaves
  • 50 g light soy sauce
  • 150 g coconut milk
  • 300 g turkey breasts, skinless, cut in strips (1.5 cm wide)
  • 300 g water

Preparation

  1. Place 50 g peanuts in mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place remaining 250 g peanuts in mixing bowl and grind 5 sec/speed 9 until fine, then blend 20 sec/speed 8 until a paste.
  3. Add garlic, chilli flakes, sugar, lime juice, fish sauce, coriander, soy sauce and coconut milk then blend 5 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides and base of mixing bowl with spatula then blend again 5 sec/speed 8. Transfer half the sauce to bowl with chopped peanuts, mix together then cover with cling film and place in fridge. Transfer remaining sauce to a large sealable food bag.
  4. Add turkey to food bag with sauce, seal then mix together to coat all the meat. Set aside in fridge to marinate for up to 3 hours or continue with recipe (see tip).
  5. When ready, remove marinated turkey from bag and ask an adult to thread turkey strips onto small skewers. Divide skewers between Varoma dish and Varoma tray then insert Varoma tray.
  6. Place water in mixing bowl. Place covered Varoma into position and steam 15 min/Varoma/speed 2. Carefully remove Varoma then serve turkey skewers with reserved sauce.

Useful Items

skewers, cling film, sealable food bag, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Thanks to the authentic but mild flavours in the satay sauce, this recipe is a great way to introduce kids to Asian food. Get them to help make the sauce, then older children can help thread the marinated meat onto the skewers. Serve warm with a salad for a light weeknight dinner or cold as part of a finger buffet.
  • For a nut-free version, substitute peanuts with sesame seeds. Toast for a few minutes in a frying pan on hob before using to coat the turkey.
  • Substitute turkey with chicken.
  • You can also cut the meat into cubes (approx. 2 cm) then assemble the kebabs, adjusting cooking time accordingly.
  • To make a complete meal, steam 40 g rice per person in the simmering basket with 1000 g water for 22 min/Varoma/speed 3, adding Varoma with turkey skewers for final 10 minutes.
  • The longer you can leave the turkey skewers to marinate, the better the finished result, but they are still delicious when cooked immediately!
  • The turkey skewers can also be grilled. Preheat a grill or BBQ to high then grill for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until cooked.
