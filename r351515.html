Thai Prawn Patties

Ingredients

Plum Sauce

  • 100 g dried candied plums, pitted
  • 5 garlic cloves
  • 2 tsp soy sauce
  • 2 Tbsp sugar
  • 1 piece tangerine peel (1 cm x 4 cm), soaked until soft then chopped
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • 10 g fresh root ginger, peeled, cut in round slices (2 mm)
  • 300 g water
  • 1 tsp cornflour, mixed with 1 Tbsp water

Prawn Patties

  • 600 g raw prawns, peeled, deveined
  • 70 g pork mince, broken in pieces
  • 1 medium egg
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 120 g strong white bread flour, plus extra for dusting
  • vegetable oil, for frying

Preparation

Plum Sauce

  1. Place plums in mixing bowl and chop 15 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add garlic, soy sauce, sugar, tangerine peel, salt, ginger and water then chop 10 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then cook 20 min/100°C/speed 1.
  3. Add cornflour mixture and mix 30 sec/speed 3. Transfer to an airtight container and store in fridge until cooled. Clean mixing bowl.

Prawn Patties

  1. Place prawns, pork, egg, sugar, sesame oil and pepper in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then chop again 5 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl.
  2. Place a plate on mixing bowl lid, weigh on flour then remove plate. With wet hands, take 2 Tbsp prawn paste and shape into a ball. Coat with flour and flatten lightly into a patty. Place on a plate and dust with extra flour. Repeat until all mixture has been used (approx. 20 patties). Place in fridge for approx. 30 minutes.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a frying pan over a medium heat, shallow fry patties for 5 minutes, turning halfway through, until golden brown. Transfer to paper towels to drain excess oil then serve warm with plum sauce.

Useful Items

airtight container, frying pan, paper towels, refrigerator, hob

Hints & Tricks

  • These prawn patties are a perfect snack served with a cold drink such as lychee juice.
  • The plum sauce may be prepared in advance. It can be stored in the fridge for approx. 7-10 days in an airtight container. This sauce goes well with seafood or white meat.
