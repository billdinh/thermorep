Chai tea

Ingredients

  • ¼ tsp cardamom seeds
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 500 g water
  • 300 g whole milk
  • 3 cloves
  • 5 tsp black tea leaves
  • 50 - 70 g caster sugar, adjust to taste
Nutrition
per 1 cup
Calories
489 kJ / 116 kcal
Protein
2 g
Carbohydrates
26 g
Fat
2 g

Preparation

  1. Place cardamom seeds and cinnamon stick into mixing bowl and coarsely grind 30 sec/speed 9.
  2. Add water, milk, whole cloves, black tea leaves and sugar in mixing bowl and heat 9 min/80°C/speed 3.
  3. Strain tea through fine-meshed sieve into tea cups or heat-proof glasses. Discard contents of sieve. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

fine-mesh sieve, tea cups

Hints & Tricks

  • For a stronger tea, cook for a further 1 min/100°C/speed 3.
