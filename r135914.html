Baked Doughnuts

Ingredients

Doughnut Batter

  • 35 g olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 180 g plain flour
  • 100 g granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 170 g plain yoghurt
  • 80 g milk

Topping

  • 50 g caster sugar
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 40 g unsalted butter, melted

Preparation

Doughnut Batter

  1. Preheat oven to 170°C and grease 2 x 6-hole doughnut tins.
  2. Place flour, sugar, baking powder, bicarbonate of soda and salt in mixing bowl then mix 5 sec/speed 3. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place egg, vanilla extract, yoghurt, milk and oil in mixing bowl then mix 5 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add reserved flour mixture and mix 7 sec/speed 4. Finish mixing by hand with spatula if necessary. Divide between prepared doughnut holes up to ⅔ full. Bake for 10-12 minutes (170°C) until cooked and golden.
  5. Leave to cool in tins for a few minutes, then loosen with a knife and carefully remove to a wire rack. Meanwhile, make topping.

Topping

  1. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid then weigh in sugar and add cinnamon. Remove from lid and stir until combined. Brush melted butter onto curved side of doughnuts then dip in cinnamon sugar mixture.
  2. Serve warm or at room temperature.

Useful Items

2 x 6 hole doughnut tins (Ø 8 cm), wire rack, pastry brush, oven

Hints & Tricks

We couldn’t give you a traditional doughnut recipe in a baking book, so we’ve adapted this one to remove the frying element. And you’d never know – the texture and flavour are just as you’d expect!
