Wholewheat Spaghetti with Avocado Sauce and Vegan 'Parmesan'

Ingredients

Vegan 'Parmesan'

  • 100 g blanched almonds
  • 100 g cashew nuts
  • 1 Tbsp savoury yeast flakes (vegetarian)
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 tsp garlic powder

Spaghetti

  • 1200 g water
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 350 g dried wholewheat spaghetti
  • 20 g fresh basil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 320 g ripe avocados, cut in pieces
  • 2 limes, juice only (30 g)
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 150 g yellow cherry tomatoes, quartered
  • 100 g tinned sweetcorn, drained

Preparation

Vegan 'Parmesan'

  1. Place all 'Parmesan' ingredients in mixing bowl and grind 15 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.

Spaghetti

  1. Place water and ½ tsp salt in mixing bowl then heat 10 min/100°C/speed 1.
  2. Add spaghetti through hole in mixing bowl lid and cook time indicated on packet/100°C//speed 1. Drain spaghetti through Varoma dish, place in a serving bowl, cover to keep warm then set aside.
  3. Place basil and garlic in mixing bowl then chop 2 sec/speed 7.
  4. Add avocado, lime juice, oil, and remaining ½ tsp salt then mince 10 sec/speed 5. Transfer to bowl with spaghetti.
  5. Place bowl with spaghetti on mixing bowl lid and weigh in tomatoes and sweetcorn. Remove and mix together. Serve sprinkled with reserved vegan Parmesan.
