Polenta Mandarin and Almond Cake

Ingredients

  • 250 g butter, soft, plus extra for greasing
  • 320 g mandarins (approx. 4), unpeeled
  • 1000 g water
  • 60 g whole raw macadamia nuts
  • 220 g blanched almonds
  • 220 g caster sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 170 g polenta
  • 1 tsp gluten free baking powder
  • icing sugar, for dusting

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 170°C. Grease and line a spring form cake tin (Ø 22 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Insert simmering basket and weigh mandarins into it. Add water to mixing bowl and cook 30 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and set mandarins aside to cool. Empty mixing bowl.
  3. Place cooled whole mandarins in mixing bowl and blend 10 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then repeat blending 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Place macadamia nuts and almonds in mixing bowl then mill 30 sec/speed 9. Transfer to bowl with mandarin purée and set aside.
  5. Insert butterfly whisk. Place caster sugar and 250 g butter in mixing bowl then whip 30 sec/speed 4.
  6. Add eggs and mix 10 sec/speed 3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Remove butterfly whisk.
  7. Add reserved mandarin purée, reserved crushed nuts, polenta and baking powder then mix 10 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then mix again 10 sec/speed 5 if necessary.
  8. Transfer cake batter to prepared tin, then bake for 60-70 minutes (170°C) or until a wooden skewer inserted in the centre of the cake comes out clean. Allow to cool completely (approx. 30 minutes), then dust with icing sugar before serving.

Useful Items

spring form tin (Ø 22 cm), baking paper, baking paper, wooden skewer, oven
