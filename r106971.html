Chocolate Hazelnut Torte with Fresh Berries

Ingredients

  • 6 ½ oz unsalted butter, cut into pieces, plus extra to grease
  • 3 oz blanched hazelnuts (see Tip)
  • 6 ½ oz dark chocolate (approx. 56% cocoa), chips or pieces
  • 4 large eggs
  • 5 ½ oz sugar
  • 9 oz whipped cream, to serve
  • 10 ½ oz fresh mixed red berries, to serve

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F. Grease and line base of a round cake pan with parchment paper and set aside.
  2. Place hazelnuts and chocolate into mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 9.
  3. Add butter and chop 10 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Insert butterfly whisk. Add eggs and sugar and whisk 5 min/speed 3. Transfer mixture to prepared pan and bake for 25-30 minutes (325°F) until outside is firm and middle is just set (there should be slight movement in the middle). Allow to cool completely in pan before transferring to a serving plate. Serve cold with whipped cream and fresh berries.

Useful Items

round cake pan (8 in.), parchment paper, serving plate, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • This Chocolate Hazelnut Torte makes a great dessert when you need to serve a gluten-free option.
  • English double cream (48% milk fat) can also be used to replace whipped cream if available.
  • To blanch hazelnuts: place hazelnuts in a saucepan of boiling water and 1 tbsp baking soda. Boil for 5 minutes or until water turns dark and skins are easily removed.
  • To toast hazelnuts: place blanched hazelnuts into mixing bowl and cook 8 min/212°F/. Allow to cool before use.
  • Replace plain blanched hazelnuts with blanched toasted hazelnuts.
