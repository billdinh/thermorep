Paneer tikka

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp Greek-style natural yoghurt
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp Tandoori paste (see Tips)
  • 250 - 300 g paneer, cut into pieces (3 cm) (see Tips)
  • ½ red capsicum, cut into pieces (2 cm)
  • cold water, for soaking
  • olive oil, for brushing
  • 1 tsp garam masala (see Tips), for sprinkling

per 1 portion

Preparation

  1. Place yoghurt, salt and Tandoori paste into mixing bowl and mix 20 sec/speed 3.
  2. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh paneer and capsicum into bowl. Transfer yoghurt mixture into bowl with paneer and carefully coat, taking care not to break up the paneer. Cover with plastic wrap and transfer into refrigerator to marinate for a minimum of 30 minutes or overnight. Place 6 wooden skewers into a bowl of cold water and soak for 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat grill to medium and line a baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with aluminium foil. Lightly brush foil with oil. Thread paneer and capsicum onto skewers, alternating ingredients and leaving space between each one. Brush paneer with oil and place onto prepared tray.
  4. Grill for 1-2 minutes, carefully turning once. Remove from heat and sprinkle garam masala over paneer. Allow to cool slightly before removing from tray. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

plastic wrap, 6 wooden skewers, bowl, baking tray (30 x 40 cm), aluminium foil, pastry brush, refrigerator, oven grill

Hints & Tricks

  • To make your own Tandoori paste, paneer and garam masala, please refer to the recipes on this recipe chip.
  • A firmer paneer is better for this recipe.
