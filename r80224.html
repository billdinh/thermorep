Brussels sprouts with lime crumb

Ingredients

  • 2 pieces lime zest, no white pith (approx.1 x 3 cm strips)
  • 30 - 40 g bread (approx.1 slice), frozen and cut into quarters
  • 1 tbsp flaked almonds
  • 50 g pancetta, cut into pieces (1-2 cm)
  • 2 tsp avocado oil, plus extra for drizzling
  • 1 garlic clove
  • ½ tsp balsamic glaze, to taste
  • 1 tbsp water
  • 200 g Brussels sprouts, trimmed and cut into halves
  • 1 pinch salt, to taste
  • 2 - 3 pinches ground black pepper, to taste

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C. Line a baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place lime zest into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 8.
  3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and repeat grating 10 sec/speed 8 until a fine consistency is achieved.
  4. Add bread and chop Turbo/0.5 sec/1 time.
  5. Add almonds and pancetta and mix 5 sec//speed .
  6. Spread mixture onto prepared baking tray and drizzle with a little avocado oil. Toast in the oven for 5-10 minutes (200°C) or until bread mixture is golden brown and pancetta is crispy. Stir bread mixture 1-2 times while toasting to ensure it has evenly browned. Set aside to cool.
  7. Place garlic into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  8. Add oil, balsamic glaze and water and cook 1 min/100°C/speed 1.
  9. Add Brussels sprouts, salt and pepper and cook 4-6 min/100°C//speed  or until cooked to your preference.
  10. To serve, place Brussels sprouts and lime crumb together in a serving bowl and toss gently to combine.

Useful Items

baking tray (30 x 40 cm), baking paper, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • This is a great side dish to serve with roasted potatoes and your choice of meat or fish.
  • To enjoy this dish as leftovers the next day, leave the lime crumb and Brussels sprouts separate. Store the Brussels sprouts in the refrigerator and the lime crumb at room temperature. Reheat Brussels sprouts the following day and combine with lime crumb just before serving.
  • To make this dish gluten free, use gluten free bread. Always read the labels of your products to ensure no wheat or gluten has been used as part of the production process.
  • Recipe for one: Reduce all ingredients, except water, by half. Leave the water at 1 tbsp in step 6 and proceed as per recipe.
