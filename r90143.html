Brinjal (eggplant pickle)

Ingredients

  • 30 g fresh ginger, peeled and cut into slices (2 mm)
  • 1 long green chilli, cut into halves
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 tsp ground turmeric
  • 2 tsp ground ginger
  • ½ tsp chilli powder
  • 150 g malt vinegar
  • 110 g brown sugar
  • 1 ½ tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 ½ tsp fenugreek seeds
  • 130 g vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp sea salt
  • 600 g eggplant, cut into pieces (1 cm)

Preparation

  1. Place ginger, green chilli and garlic into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add turmeric, ground ginger, chilli powder, 75 g of the malt vinegar and brown sugar and mix 20 sec/speed 4. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Place cumin seeds, fenugreek seeds and oil into mixing bowl and sauté 3 min/Varoma/speed 1    .
  4. Add reserved spice mixture and cook 5 min/100°C/speed 1.
  5. Add salt, eggplant and remaining 75 g malt vinegar and cook 10-15 min/100°C//speed 1, or until eggplant is tender. Transfer into a hot-rinsed storage jar with a sealable lid and allow to cool to room temperature (approx. 1 hour).
  6. Store in a cool, dark place for 2-4 weeks to allow flavours to infuse before using.
