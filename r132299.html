Overnight Oats with Peach Compote

Ingredients

Compote

  • 300 g peaches, peeled, cut in 8 segments
  • 15 g coconut sugar
  • 1 Tbsp water
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 4 allspice berries

Oats

  • 150 g rolled oats
  • 400 g almond milk
  • 3 Tbsp chia seeds
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1023 kJ / 245 kcal

7.7 g

7.9 g

Preparation

Compote

  1. Place peaches, sugar, water, lemon juice and allspice berries in mixing bowl then cook 10 min/95°C//speed . Transfer to a bowl and set aside until cool, then refrigerate. Meanwhile, prepare oats.

Oats

  1. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in oats, milk and chia seeds. Remove bowl from lid and mix together well then cover with cling film and set aside in fridge overnight.

Assembly

  1. To assemble, divide half of oat mixture between 4 jars. Top with half of cooled peach compote then add remaining oats, finishing with a layer of compote. Best served chilled.

Useful Items

cling film, 4 jars with screw-top lids, refrigerator
