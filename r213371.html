Shredded Jerk Chicken

Ingredients

  • 32 oz boneless, skinless chicken thighs, quartered
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 tsp black peppercorns
  • 1 - 1 ½ tsp salt, to taste
  • 1 tsp allspice berries
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • 1 habanero chili, stem and seeds removed
  • 1 lime, peel only, no pith
  • 1 orange, peel only, no pith
  • ½ oz ginger, peeled
  • 1 oz fresh cilantro, leaves only
  • 4 green onions, trimmed, cut into pieces (1 in.)
  • 3 oz freshly squeezed orange juice
  • 2 oz apple cider vinegar
  • 1 oz freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 1 oz extra virgin olive oil

Preparation

  1. Place chicken into a large bowl and set aside. Place garlic, sugar, peppercorns, salt, allspice, cinnamon, nutmeg, habanero chili, lime and orange peels and ginger into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add cilantro and green onions and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and chop a further 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add orange juice, vinegar, lime juice and olive oil and mix 5 sec/speed 4.
  4. Pour jerk marinade into large bowl with reserved chicken and combine with spatula. Cover and refrigerate 1 hour, up to overnight.
  5. Place marinated chicken into mixing bowl, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 15 min/212°F//speed 1, then shred 5 sec//speed 4.
  6. Remove lid, insert simmering basket and strain juices. Transfer chicken to a bowl and serve immediately or cover and refrigerate until ready to serve.

Useful Items

large bowl, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve as a sandwich between potato buns with sliced red onion.
  • Habanero chilies are hot; use caution when handling.
  • Marinate whole chicken thighs overnight and grill instead of cooking and shredding in Thermomix®.
  • Jerk Chicken Mayonnaise: after straining, reserve cooking liquid, transfer chicken to a separate bowl and set aside. Return cooking liquid to mixing bowl, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 40 min/212°F/speed 3. Transfer reduced juices (approx. 2.5 oz) to a bowl and combine with 1 tbsp mayonnaise.
