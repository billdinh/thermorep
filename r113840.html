Almond Biscotti

Ingredients

  • 9 oz all-purpose flour
  • 7 oz sugar
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • ½ oz anise-flavored liqueur
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 egg yolk, from large egg
  • 5 ½ oz almonds, with skin
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
236 kJ / 56 kcal
Protein
1 g
Carbohydrates
8 g
Fat
2 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and set aside.
  2. Place flour, sugar, salt, baking powder, liqueur, eggs and egg yolk into mixing bowl and knead 1 min/.
  3. Add almonds and mix 40 sec/. Transfer to prepared baking sheet; dough should be thick and sticky at this point—do not add more flour! Shape dough into 3 logs (12 in. x 1¼ in.). Place 2 inches apart on prepared baking sheet. Bake 30 minutes (350°F) or until golden brown. The baked dough will still be soft, allow to cool 10 minutes. Reduce oven temperature to 300°F.
  4. Place baked logs on a cutting board and diagonally cut each into 12 slices using a sharp knife. Place slices, cut side down, on baking sheet and bake a further 15 minutes (300°F). Allow to cool completely before storing in an airtight container.

Useful Items

airtight container, baking sheet, parchment paper, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Ideal to serve with coffee or tea.
  • In Tuscan tradition, Biscotti are served with the sweet dessert wine "Vin Santo".
