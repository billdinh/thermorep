Steamed Hazelnut Cheesecakes with Orange

Ingredients

  • 40 g blanched hazelnuts
  • butter, for greasing
  • 3 fresh basil leaves, plus extra for garnish
  • ½ orange, thin peelings of skin, plus segments of flesh
  • 2 tsp cornflour
  • 20 g caster sugar
  • 100 g cream cheese
  • 80 g ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 500 g water

Preparation

  1. Place 20 g hazelnuts in mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 7. Liberally grease 2 ramekins (Ø 8 cm) or dariole moulds with butter then add chopped nuts and rotate ramekin or mould to coat bottom and sides. Tip out excess nuts and set aside.
  2. Place remaining 20 g hazelnuts in mixing bowl and chop 1 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place basil, orange peel, cornflour and sugar in mixing bowl then grind 15 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then grind again 15 sec/speed 9.
  4. Add cream cheese, ricotta and egg then blend 10 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then heat 3 min/70°C/speed 3.
  5. Blend again 10 sec/speed 4, then pour into prepared ramekins or moulds, stopping 1 cm from the top. Place ramekins or moulds into Varoma dish and cover with a double layer of paper towel. Rinse mixing bowl.
  6. Place water in mixing bowl then place covered Varoma into position. Steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 1. Once cooked, allow cheesecakes to sit in ramekins for 30 minutes then gently remove and arrange on serving plates. Place in fridge to chill. Once chilled, serve garnished with orange slices, extra basil leaves and a sprinkle of reserved chopped hazelnuts.

Useful Items

2 ramekins (8 cm) or dariole moulds, paper towel, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • These desserts can be made ahead of time and left in the fridge to chill overnight. Use a seasonal fruit of choice to garnish.
  • To ease cheesecakes out of moulds, run the tip of a knife around the edge to loosen, then hold upside-down over a plate and shake gently.
