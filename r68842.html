Tomato ketchup

Ingredients

  • 1000 g fresh tomatoes, cut into quarters
  • 180 g red capsicum, deseeded, cut into pieces
  • 130 g red onion, cut into halves
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 long red chilli, deseeded, cut into pieces
  • 100 g white wine vinegar
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • ½ tsp mustard seeds
  • 10 black peppercorns
  • 1 dried bay leaf
  • 1 tsp hot paprika
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 100 g dark brown sugar
Nutrition
per 50 g
Calories
225 kJ / 53 kcal
Protein
1 g
Carbohydrates
11 g

0.2 g

Preparation

  1. Place tomatoes, capsicum, onion, garlic, chilli and vinegar into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7 then cook 40 min/120°C/speed 2, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  2. Add nutmeg, mustard seeds, peppercorns, bay leaf, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt and brown sugar and cook 15-20 min/120°C/speed 2, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid. Mixture should thicken during cooking time.
  3. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula, insert measuring cup then blend 1 min/speed 5 -10, increase speed gradually. Transfer into a warmed large storage jar or bottle and tightly close lid then turn jar or bottle immediately upside down for 10 minutes and allow to cool before refrigerating. Use tomato ketchup as needed once cooled.

Useful Items

large glass storage jar or bottle, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve tomato ketchup at your next barbecue or with Aussie meat pies (see recipe).
  • Tomato ketchup will keep for up to 3 months in the refrigerator.
