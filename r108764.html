Mocha ice cream cake with Champagne snaps

Ingredients

Coffee almond base

  • 80 g raw sugar
  • 400 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract
  • 1 egg white
  • 1 tbsp instant granulated coffee
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 60 g slivered almonds, lightly toasted

Coffee chocolate top

  • 100 g raw sugar
  • 300 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 200 g dark chocolate (70% cocoa), broken into pieces
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract
  • 2 tbsp espresso coffee, brewed

Champagne snaps

  • butter, for greasing
  • 40 g flaked almonds
  • 130 g raw sugar
  • 100 g Champagne (see Tips)

Preparation

Coffee almond base

  1. Line a baking tray with baking paper, then place the ring only of a 20 cm springform cake tin (base removed) on top of the baking paper. Set aside.
  2. Place raw sugar into mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a bowl, then place 40 g of the milled sugar into mixing bowl.
  3. Insert butterfly whisk. Add cream and vanilla and whip until soft peaks form/speed 4, watching cream carefully to avoid overwhipping. Remove butterfly whisk. Transfer into a separate bowl and set aside. Thoroughly clean and dry mixing bowl and butterfly whisk.
  4. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg white, granulated coffee and salt into mixing bowl and whip 2 min 30 sec/37°C/speed 4 or until stiff peaks form. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Mix 30 sec/speed 3, slowly adding remaining milled sugar, 1 spoonful at a time through hole in mixing bowl lid.
  6. Add reserved whipped cream and mix 5 sec/speed 2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  7. Add almonds and mix 5 sec/speed 2. Spread mixture evenly inside springform ring onto baking paper. Place into freezer for a minimum of 2 hours to set. Clean and dry mixing bowl and butterfly whisk.

Coffee chocolate top

  1. Place sugar into mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Place cream into mixing bowl and whip until soft peaks form/speed 4, watching cream carefully to avoid overwhipping. Transfer cream into a separate bowl and set aside. Remove butterfly whisk. Thoroughly clean and dry mixing bowl and butterfly whisk.
  3. Place chocolate into mixing bowl and melt 3 min/50°C/speed 1. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and melt for a further 1-2 min/50°C/speed 1 or until just melted.
  4. Insert butterfly whisk. Add egg yolk, eggs and reserved milled sugar and mix 4 min/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add vanilla and brewed espresso and mix 15 sec/speed 3. Add reserved whipped cream and combine 10 sec//speed 2. Pour chocolate mixture over frozen base and spread over evenly using spatula. Cover with plastic wrap and place into freezer for a minimum of 6 hours or overnight. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Champagne snaps

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C. Grease a baking tray (30 x 40 cm). Line with a large piece of baking paper to completely cover base and sides of tray (to ensure all toffee stays on the baking paper).
  2. Spread almonds onto baking tray and bake for 3 minutes (220°C) or until lightly golden. Transfer almonds into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place sugar into mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 9.
  4. Add Champagne and cook 13 min/Varoma/speed 2, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid. Carefully pour mixture onto lined baking tray. Bake for 4 minutes (220°C), then remove from oven and carefully tilt tray from side-to-side to evenly distribute mixture around the tray. Place back into oven and bake for 1 minute (220°C). Remove from oven and carefully tilt tray from side-to-side to evenly distribute mixture. Place back into oven and bake for a further 1 minute (220°C). Remove from oven and carefully tilt tray from side-to-side to evenly distribute mixture. Toffee should now be a dark amber colour. Sprinkle toffee with reserved almonds and allow to cool completely (approx. 30 minutes).
  5. Remove cake from tin and transfer onto a serving plate. Break Champagne toffee into pieces and decorate cake just before serving.

Useful Items

baking paper, springform cake tin (20 cm), bowl, plastic wrap, serving plate, baking tray (30 x 40 cm), freezer, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • You can replace the Champagne with sparkling wine.
  • To save time, make the ice cream cake in advance and store in the freezer.
