Vegetable-potato-meat purée

Ingredients

  • 40 - 60 g potatoes, cut in pieces
  • 90 - 100 g vegetables, cut in pieces
  • 20 - 30 g meat, sinew free, cut in pieces
  • 400 g water
  • 30 - 45 g orange juice
  • 2 - 2 ½ tsp rapeseed oil (approx. 10 g)
Nutrition
per 1 Total recipe
Calories
831 kJ / 199 kcal
Protein
8 g
Carbohydrates
14 g
Fat
12 g

Preparation

  1. Place potatoes, vegetables and meat in mixing bowl, chop 3 sec/speed 5 and transfer to simmering basket.
  2. Place water in mixing bowl, insert simmering basket and cook 15 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and transfer cooking liquid to a bowl.
  4. Transfer contents of simmering basket to mixing bowl and add orange juice, rapeseed oil and approx. 60 g reserved cooking liquid. Purée 30 sec/speed 10.
  5. To cool purée, with simmering basket in place pf measuring cup, mix 8 min/speed 3. Transfer to a bowl, check temperature is suitable for your baby and serve.
