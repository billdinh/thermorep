Double Peanut Cookies

Ingredients

  • unsalted butter, to grease
  • 5 oz unsalted dry-roasted peanuts, divided, plus extra for decoration
  • 4 oz unsalted butter, cold, cut into pieces, plus extra for greasing
  • 3 ½ oz sugar
  • 3 oz light brown sugar
  • 9 oz all-purpose flour
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
  • unsalted dry-roasted peanuts, for decoration

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F and grease two baking sheets and set aside.
  2. Place 3 oz peanuts into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and blend 10 sec/speed 6.
  3. Add butter, sugar, light brown sugar, flour, egg, vanilla extract, baking soda and salt and mix 25 sec/speed 5.
  4. Add 2 oz peanuts and mix 15 sec//speed 3. Scrape down mixing bowl with spatula and mix a further 15 sec//speed 3. Dough will still look crumbly but will come together while shaping into balls. Roll cookies using 1 heaping tbsp of dough each (approx. 1 ½ in. wide) and place on greased baking sheets 2 in. apart. Place 1 peanut on top of each round, pressing slightly into dough.
  5. Bake for 15-20 minutes (325°F) until golden and still slightly soft. Allow to rest on baking sheets for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely. Once cool, store in an airtight container until ready to serve.

Useful Items

airtight container, wire rack, baking sheets, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • These cookies are called Double Peanut Cookies because you make peanut butter first then add whole peanuts for decoration.
  • For 16 cookies shape dough into rounds (approx. 1.6 oz ea).
