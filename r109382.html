Shrimp & Crab Linguine

Ingredients

  • 1 organic lemon, (approx. 5 oz) thin peel only (no pith), plus juice (approx. 2 oz)
  • 1 ¼ tsp salt, divided
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 35 ½ oz water
  • 2 oz olive oil, divided
  • 9 oz pasta, linguine
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 6 ½ oz peeled raw shrimp
  • 3 oz crème fraîche
  • 6 oz jumbo lump crab meat, white, brown or mixture
  • 2 ½ oz arugula, plus extra to garnish
  • 1 oz pine nuts, toasted
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1958 kJ / 468 kcal
Protein
24 g
Carbohydrates
52 g

Preparation

  1. Place lemon peel, ¼ tsp salt and pepper into mixing bowl and zest 30 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place water, 1 tsp salt and 1 tsp oil into mixing bowl and bring to a boil 9 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  3. Add pasta and cook for time indicated on package/212°F//speed 1. Rinse pasta under cold running water, drain well using simmering basket and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.
  4. Place garlic into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add shrimp and oil and sauté 2 min/212°F//speed 0.5.
  6. Add crème fraîche, crabmeat, arugula and reserved lemon zest and heat 3 min/195°F//speed 0.5.
  7. Add drained linguine and lemon juice and stir 2 min/175°F//speed . To serve, sprinkle with toasted pine nuts and garnish with arugula.
