Raw chocolate snaps

Ingredients

Chocolate

  • 200 g raw cacao butter, cut into pieces (1-2 cm), plus extra for greasing
  • 50 - 60 g raw cacao powder
  • 100 g honey
  • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste
  • 1 pinch sea salt

Hazelnut and orange topping

  • 40 g hazelnuts, toasted and roughly chopped
  • ½ orange, zest only, no white pith
  • &frac18; tsp sea salt flakes

Macadamia and coconut topping

  • 40 g macadamia nuts, toasted and roughly chopped
  • 20 g shredded coconut, toasted

Preparation

Chocolate

  1. Grease and line 2 baking trays with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Thoroughly clean and dry mixing bowl. Place cacao butter into mixing bowl and chop 20 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Melt 10-14 min/37°C/speed 1, or until completely melted. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add all remaining ingredients and warm 10 min/37°C/speed 1.
  5. Blend chocolate 10 sec/speed 5, then pour half of the chocolate mixture onto each of the prepared baking trays.

Hazelnut and orange topping

  1. Scatter hazelnuts, orange zest and salt over chocolate on 1 of the baking trays. Set aside in the freezer to set (approx. 30 minutes).

Macadamia and coconut topping

  1. Scatter macadamias and coconut over chocolate on remaining baking tray. Place into the freezer to set (approx. 30 minutes).
  2. When chocolate has set, break into pieces and serve.

Useful Items

baking trays, baking paper, freezer

Hints & Tricks

  • Raw chocolate snaps need to be stored in a sealable container in the refrigerator or in a freezable container in the freezer.
