Bourbon-Glazed Chicken Wings

Ingredients

  • 40 oz chicken wings
  • 5 oz bourbon whiskey
  • 3 oz dark brown sugar
  • 3 oz ketchup
  • 1 oz unsalted butter, cut into pieces
  • 2 - 3 tbsp hot sauce (e.g., Frank's Hot Pepper Sauce®)
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
Nutrition

2025 kJ / 484 kcal

Preparation

  1. Place a large bowl onto mixing bowl lid, weigh in chicken wings and set aside.
  2. Place bourbon, sugar, ketchup, butter, hot sauce, maple syrup, and mustard into mixing bowl, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 5 min/212°F/speed 2.
  3. Transfer half the bourbon sauce into a bowl and set aside. Pour remaining bourbon sauce over reserved chicken wings and toss to coat.
  4. Place coated chicken wings into mixing bowl, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 15 min/Varoma//speed . Meanwhile, preheat oven to 450°F and line a baking sheet with foil.
  5. Transfer chicken wings to prepared baking sheet and arrange in a single layer. Discard sauce in mixing bowl. Brush chicken wings with half the reserved sauce and bake for 10 minutes (450°F).
  6. Brush remaining sauce onto chicken wings and bake a further 10 minutes (450°F). Serve hot.

Useful Items

baking sheet, aluminum foil, basting brush, large bowl, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with ranch or blue cheese dipping sauce.
