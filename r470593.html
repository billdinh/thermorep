Vegan Spaghetti and 'Meatballs'

Ingredients

Vegan Parmesan

  • 4 oz raw cashews
  • 2 tsp nutritional yeast
  • ½ tsp salt

"Meatballs"

  • 13 oz water, divided
  • 3 ½ oz dried green lentils
  • 1 tbsp ground flaxseed
  • 4 oz yellow onion, in pieces
  • 4 oz cremini mushrooms
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • ½ oz extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 oz panko bread crumbs
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper

Marinara

  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 oz extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbsp coconut sugar
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 28 oz canned diced tomatoes
  • 12 oz dried spaghetti pasta
  • fresh basil leaves, to garnish

Preparation

Vegan Parmesan

  1. Place cashews, nutritional yeast and salt into mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.

"Meatballs"

  1. Place 12 oz water and lentils into mixing bowl and cook 20 min/212°F//speed 1. Drain lentils, discard water and transfer cooked lentils to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place a small bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in 1 oz water. Remove bowl from lid, add flaxseed and whisk together. Set aside to thicken. Meanwhile, line a baking sheet with parchment.
  3. Place onions, mushrooms, garlic and oil into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and without measuring cup cook 8 min/250°F//speed .
  4. Add reserved lentils, reserved flaxseed mixture, panko, dried basil, dried oregano, salt and pepper and mix 10 sec//speed 3. Divide mixture into 1 oz pieces and roll into balls. Place on prepared baking sheet and refrigerate 30 minutes. Meanwhile preheat oven to 350°F, rinse mixing bowl and make sauce.

Marinara

  1. Place garlic, olive oil, coconut sugar, dried basil, salt and pepper into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add tomatoes and cook 45 min/212°F/speed 1. Meanwhile bake "meatballs" for 30 minutes (350°F), until browned.
  3. Cook spaghetti on stove top according to package instructions and keep warm.
  4. Transfer spaghetti to a large serving plate and toss with Marinara and "Meatballs". Garnish with Vegan Parmesan and fresh basil and serve.

Useful Items

small bowl, baking sheet, large serving plate, parchment paper, oven, refrigerator, stove top
