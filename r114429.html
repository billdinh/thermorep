Smoked salmon muffins with steamed eggs and herbed relish

Ingredients

Herbed relish

  • 50 g cornichons
  • 1 tbsp capers, rinsed
  • 2 tbsp fresh chives, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 spring onion/shallot, white and light green part only, cut into pieces
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 80 g soft feta cheese (i.e. Danish feta)
  • 100 g crème fraîche
  • ½ lemon, juice only
  • 40 g extra virgin olive oil

Smoked salmon muffins

  • 80 g unsalted butter, plus extra for greasing
  • 250 g Gruyère cheese, cut into pieces (2 cm)
  • 50 g Pecorino cheese, cut into pieces (2 cm)
  • 60 g spring onions/shallots, white and light green parts only, cut into pieces
  • 4 sprigs fresh dill, leaves only
  • 2 eggs
  • 220 g full cream milk
  • 50 g crème fraîche
  • 1 tsp sea salt flakes
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 250 g plain flour
  • 200 g smoked salmon, cut into short, thin slices
  • 6 stalks asparagus, cut into thin slices

Steamed eggs

  • 700 g water
  • 12 eggs, chilled
  • 100 g rocket leaves (optional)
  • 200 g cherry tomatoes (optional)

Preparation

Herbed relish

  1. Place cornichons, capers, chives, parsley, salt and spring onion/shallot into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add remaining relish ingredients and blend 10 sec/speed 3, or until combined.
  3. Transfer into a sealable container and place into refrigerator until ready to serve. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Smoked salmon muffins

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Grease a 12-hole muffin tray with butter and set aside.
  2. Place Gruyère and Pecorino into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place spring onions/shallots and dill into mixing bowl and chop Turbo/1 sec/1 time. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add butter and sauté 3 min/90°C/speed 2.
  5. Add eggs, milk, crème fraîche, salt, baking powder, flour and reserved cheese mixture and mix 10 sec/speed 4.
  6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix for a further 5 sec/speed 4.
  7. Add salmon and asparagus. Combine gently with spatula, then combine 20 sec//speed 4.
  8. Transfer batter into prepared muffin tray, filling muffin holes to the top. Bake for 20 minutes (180°C), or until puffed and golden. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Steamed eggs

  1. Place water into mixing bowl and heat 7 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  2. Place up to 6 eggs into Varoma dish, place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and cook 8 min/Varoma/speed 1 (see Tips).
  3. Carefully remove eggs and set aside. Place up to another 6 eggs into Varoma dish, secure Varoma lid and cook 8 min/Varoma/speed 1. Repeat cooking with any remaining eggs as needed, topping up water as needed.
  4. Rinse cooked eggs under water until they are cool enough to touch, then peel eggs and cut into halves.
  5. Serve warm muffins with eggs, relish, rocket (optional) and cherry tomatoes (optional).

Useful Items

sealable container, 12-hole muffin tray, bowl, refrigerator, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Eggs are cooked until just set. For soft-boiled eggs cook 7 min/Varoma/speed 1. For hard-boiled eggs, cook 9 min/Varoma/speed 1. If using room temperature eggs, reduce your cooking time. For a guide on steaming eggs please refer to The Basic Cookbook or Everyday Cookbook.
  • This is a great dish for using what you have available in your fridge. Gruyère can be replaced with another hard cheese (e.g. Comté); dill can be replaced with tarragon or mint; crème fraîche can be replaced with Greek yoghurt or sour cream and soft feta can be replaced with goat's curd.
  • For a vegetarian option, replace smoked salmon with roasted zucchini or red capsicum.
