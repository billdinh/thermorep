Beetroot soup with herb dumplings

Ingredients

Herb dumplings

  • 10 - 15 g fresh herbs (e.g. basil, parsley, wild garlic, chervil, mixed soft-leaf herbs), leaves only
  • 200 g milk
  • 60 g butter, cut in pieces, plus extra for greasing
  • ¾ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground nutmeg (optional)
  • 150 g wheat semolina
  • 1 egg

Beetroot soup

  • 500 g raw beetroot, peeled, cut in pieces,
  • 700 g water
  • 150 g white wine
  • 2 heaped tsp vegetable stock paste, homemade
  • 25 g flour
  • ½ - 1 tsp salt
  • 2 pinches ground nutmeg
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 100 g cream
  • 1 tbsp white wine vinegar (optional)

Preparation

Herb dumplings

  1. Place herbs in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place milk, butter, salt and nutmeg in mixing bowl and heat 3 min/80°C/speed 1.
  3. Add wheat semolina and cook 2 min 30 sec/100°C/speed 3. Remove mixing bowl and allow semolina mixture to cool for approx. 10 minutes. Meanwhile, grease Varoma dish and tray.
  4. Set mixing bowl back in position, add egg and reserved chopped herbs and stir 15 sec/speed 3. Transfer to a plate and using wet hands or two teaspoons, form 30 dumplings (Ø approx. 2.5 cm). Arrange dumplings in Varoma dish and on Varoma tray and set aside.

Beetroot soup

  1. Place beetroot in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 4.5.
  2. Add water, white wine, vegetable stock paste, flour, salt, nutmeg and black pepper and mix 5 sec/speed 3.
  3. Set Varoma dish and tray in position and cook 25 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  4. Set Varoma aside, add cream and white wine vinegar to mixing bowl, insert measuring cup and blend 1 min 30 sec/speed 5-10, increasing speed gradually. Season to taste. Transfer soup to bowls, add dumplings and serve hot.
