Mocha Cake

Ingredients

Coffee and Chocolate Sponge

  • 230 g unsalted butter, soft, plus extra for greasing
  • 100 g brewed espresso coffee, milky (see tip)
  • 130 g caster sugar
  • 130 g light brown sugar
  • 240 g spelt flour
  • 1 ½ tsp baking powder
  • 4 large eggs
  • 30 g cocoa powder

Coffee and Chocolate Icing

  • 280 g unsalted butter, soft
  • 500 g icing sugar
  • 3 tsp instant coffee, dissolved in 1 Tbsp boiling water then set aside to cool
  • 150 g dark chocolate, small pieces or callets
  • 70 g coffee beans, chocolate-coated, for garnish

Preparation

Coffee and Chocolate Sponge

  1. Preheat oven to 170°C. Grease sides and line bases of 2 cake tins (Ø 20 cm) with baking paper.
  2. Place coffee, caster sugar, light brown sugar and butter in mixing bowl then mix 30 sec/speed 4.
  3. Add flour, baking powder and eggs then mix 20 sec/speed 5. Set scales to zero then transfer half the mixture to a prepared cake tin (scales should read approx. -510 g).
  4. Add cocoa to remaining mixture in mixing bowl then mix 15 sec/speed 3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then mix again 15 sec/speed 3. Transfer to second prepared cake tin. Bake sponges for 25-30 minutes (170°C) until a skewer inserted in centre comes out clean. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl.
  5. Leave sponges to cool in tins for 5 minutes before removing to a wire rack to cool completely. Meanwhile, make icing.

Coffee and Chocolate Icing

  1. Place 250 g butter, icing sugar and dissolved coffee in mixing bowl then mix 30 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then mix again 30 sec/speed 5.
  2. Once sponges are cool, use a bread knife to slice each in half horizontally to create 4 even layers. Spread a layer of icing over a pale sponge, then place a chocolate sponge on top.
  3. Repeat with remaining sponges, spreading icing between each one until all four sponge layers are used. Ice top and sides of cake then transfer to a cake stand. Clean mixing bowl.
  4. Place chocolate in mixing bowl and grate 8 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add 30 g butter and melt 5 min/45°C/speed 1. Pour onto centre of cake, letting it drizzle down sides.
  6. Decorate with chocolate-coated coffee beans and serve.

Useful Items

2 round cake tins (Ø 20 cm), baking paper, skewer, wire rack, bread knife, serving plate or cake stand, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • To make milky espresso coffee, dissolve 1 Tbsp ground espresso in 70 g water and 30 g milk or use 70 g espresso coffee from a coffee machine and top up with 30 g milk.

No baking book would be complete without a coffee cake, and we’ve added some chocolate to this one to make it extra decadent.
