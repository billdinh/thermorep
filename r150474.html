Sweet potato, parsnip and broccoli frittata

Ingredients

  • olive oil, for greasing
  • 100 g Parmesan cheese, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 200 g sweet potato, peeled and cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 250 g broccoli, cut into florets
  • 1 parsnip (approx. 130 g), peeled and cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 8 eggs
  • 60 g wholegrain mustard
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • 60 g milk
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
770 kJ / 183 kcal
Protein
14.7 g
Carbohydrates
6.5 g

10.4 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C. Grease a deep-sided baking dish (18 x 28 cm) and set aside.
  2. Place Parmesan cheese into mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 10. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place sweet potato, broccoli and parsnip into mixing bowl and grate 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add eggs, mustard, salt, pepper, milk and reserved grated Parmesan and combine 30 sec//speed 3. Transfer into prepared baking dish and bake for 35-45 minutes (190°C) or until a wooden skewer inserted into the centre of the frittata comes out clean. Cool slightly, then serve.

Useful Items

deep-sided baking dish, bowl, wooden skewer, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • For a complete meal, serve with a green salad.
