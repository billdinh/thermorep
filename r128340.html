Cheesy veggie oat bars

Ingredients

  • 200 g Parmesan cheese, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 25 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 160 g onions, cut into quarters
  • 200 g carrots, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 200 g courgettes, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 200 g oat flakes
  • 2 eggs
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
636 kJ / 152 kcal
Protein
8 g
Carbohydrates
8 g
Fat
8 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C and line a square cake pan (20 cm) with baking paper.
  2. Place cheese and parsley into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 10.
  3. Add onions, carrots and courgettes and chop 10 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add oats and eggs and mix 15 sec/speed 4. Transfer to prepared cake pan and bake 30-40 minutes (190°C) until golden brown.
  5. Remove from oven and cut into pieces, allowing to cool in pan for 10 minutes before transferring to a cooling rack. Serve warm or at room temperature.

Useful Items

baking paper, cooling rack, square baking tin (20 cm x 20 cm), oven
