Greek lamb rolls with yoghurt dressing

Ingredients

Yoghurt dressing

  • 200 g Greek-style natural yoghurt
  • ¼ tsp sumac
  • ½ tsp dried mint

Flatbreads

  • 170 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 100 g hot water

Greek lamb rolls

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 2 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 500 g lamb mince
  • ¼ tsp dried chilli flakes
  • ½ tsp ground cumin
  • ½ tsp ground coriander
  • ½ tsp paprika
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp dried mint
  • water, for brushing
  • olive oil, for brushing
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges, to serve

Preparation

Yoghurt dressing

  1. Place yoghurt, sumac and dried mint into mixing bowl and mix 5 sec/speed 4. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.

Flatbreads

  1. Place flour, salt and hot water into mixing bowl and mix 15 sec/speed 4. Transfer onto a floured silicone bread mat or work surface and shape into a ball. Cover with a damp kitchen towel and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Greek lamb rolls

  1. Place garlic and parsley into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add mince, chilli flakes, cumin, coriander, paprika, salt, pepper and dried mint and mix 10 sec//speed 3, until well combined. Divide into 4 portions (approx. 130 g each), then roll into four sausages (approx. 12 cm in length). Set aside.
  3. On a silicone bread mat or clean work surface, roll reserved dough into a large rectangle (30 x 34 cm) and cut into 4 rectangles. Place 1 reserved sausage onto each rectangle horizontally along long edge. Brush all edges with water and roll up lightly to enclose sausages. Press sides of the pastry together to form a sealed roll. Brush rolls with oil.
  4. Place a large frying pan over medium-high heat and fry rolls, turning regularly, until browned on all sides (approx. 5 minutes). Turn heat down to low and cook, turning frequently, until cooked through (approx. for a further 10 minutes - see Tips).
  5. Serve lamb rolls with reserved Yoghurt dressing and lemon wedges (see Tips).

Useful Items

silicone bread mat, bowl, pastry brush, kitchen towel, rolling pin, large frying pan, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • You can shallow fry Greek rolls in a frying pan over medium heat for a crispy, brown finish, if desired. Cooking times may vary.
  • We served our lamb rolls with a Greek salad on the side.
  • To mince your own meat, please refer to the guide in the front of The Basic Cookbook. Please note that it may increase the total time of the recipe.
