Halloumi Salad

Ingredients

  • 15 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 20 g red wine vinegar
  • 10 g lemon juice
  • ½ Tbsp honey
  • ¼ tsp garlic powder
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • &frac18; tsp ground black pepper
  • 400 g lettuce (e.g. romaine), quartered
  • 80 g red onions, quartered
  • 60 g pitted black olives, halved
  • 8 cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 250 g halloumi cheese, grilled, sliced (1 cm)
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1257 kJ / 302 kcal
Protein
16.8 g
Carbohydrates
7.8 g
Fat
22 g

Preparation

  1. Place oil, vinegar, lemon juice, honey, garlic powder, salt and pepper in mixing bowl then mix 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a small jug and set aside.
  2. Place 200 g lettuce in mixing bowl then chop 2 sec/speed 4. Transfer to serving bowl. Place remaining 200 g lettuce in mixing bowl then chop 2 sec/speed 4. Transfer to serving bowl.
  3. Place red onions in mixing bowl then chop 4 sec/speed 5. Transfer to serving bowl. Add olives and tomatoes to serving bowl.
  4. Add halloumi then toss salad with reserved dressing or serve on the side.

Useful Items
