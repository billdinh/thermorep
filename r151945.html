Sangria

Ingredients

  • 250 g apples, unpeeled, cores removed, cut in pieces
  • 1 orange
  • 1 lemon
  • 150 g sugar
  • 600 g red wine
  • 30 - 50 g vermouth
  • 30 - 50 g brandy
  • 30 - 50 g gin
  • Â¼ tsp ground cinnamon (optional)
  • 10 ice cubes
  • 330 g lemon soda

Preparation

  1. Place apples in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a 2 litre jug and set aside.
  2. With a vegetable peeler, thinly peel off the zest of one orange and one lemon in long strands and set aside. Remove white pith and pips from both citrus fruits. Place peeled orange, peeled lemon and sugar in mixing bowl and blend 1 min/speed 10.
  3. Add red wine, vermouth, brandy, gin, cinnamon (if using), ice cubes and lemon soda and mix 30 sec/speed 2. Transfer to jug containing chopped apples and serve garnished with orange and lemon zest.

Useful Items

litre jug, vegetable peeler

Hints & Tricks

  • In summer, replace apples with firm ripe peaches, peeled, cut into pieces and omit chopping.
  • If possible use very cold ingredients. If ingredients aren't cold, more ice cubes can be added to serving jug.
  • You can prepare the recipe ahead of time, adding the ice cubes and soda just before serving.
