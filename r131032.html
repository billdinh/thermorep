Mussel fritters with chilli mayonnaise

Ingredients

Chilli mayonnaise

  • 1 fresh chilli, trimmed and deseeded
  • 80 g mayonnaise (see Tips)
  • 2 tsp lime juice

Mussel fritters

  • 500 g water
  • 1000 g mussels, New Zealand green lipped, cleaned and debearded
  • 2 spring onions/shallots, cut into pieces (approx. 3 cm)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only, plus 24 leaves for garnishig
  • 100 g full cream milk
  • 50 g plain flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 egg
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 pinches freshly ground black pepper
  • 5 - 6 tbsp vegetable oil, plus extra for frying
  • 30 g sweet potato, unpeeled and julienned (approx 5 cm long)
  • 20 g fresh ginger (5 cm piece), julienned
  • 1 fresh red chilli, trimmed, deseeded and julienned

Preparation

Chilli mayonnaise

  1. Place chilli into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add mayonnaise and lime juice and mix 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer into a bowl and place into refrigerator until ready to serve. Rinse mixing bowl.

Mussel fritters

  1. Place water into mixing bowl and boil 5 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  2. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh mussels into it. Secure Varoma lid and steam 4 min/Varoma/speed 1. Stir with spatula to ensure even cooking. Secure Varoma lid and steam for a further 4 min/Varoma/speed 1 or until mussels are opened and/or just cooked. Set Varoma aside to cool (approx. 20 minutes). Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Remove cooled mussel meat from shells and roughly chop. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place spring onions/shallots, garlic and 2 sprigs coriander leaves into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add milk, flour, baking powder, egg, salt and pepper and mix 5 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  6. Add mussel meat and mix 4 sec//speed 3, or until combined.
  7. Place frying pan over medium heat and drizzle with oil. Working in batches, place heaped tablespoons of batter into pan and cook, turning once, until golden brown and crisp (approx. 2 minutes each side). Transfer onto a plate lined with paper towel (makes approx. 12 fritters in total).
  8. Keep frying pan over medium heat and place 5-6 tablespoons oil into pan. Add sweet potato, ginger and chilli and fry until crispy (approx. 2 minutes). Transfer onto a plate lined with paper towel. Add coriander leaves to pan and fry for a few seconds until crisp, then transfer onto plate with sweet potato.
  9. Top each fritter with a teaspoon of Chilli mayonnaise and fried sweet potato mixture before serving.

Useful Items

bowl, non-stick frying pan, plate, paper towel, refrigerator, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • The New Zealand green lipped mussel differs from other mussel species in that it has dark brown/green shells with green lips around the edges, and only has one adductor muscle. It is also one of the largest mussel species.
  • Please refer to The Basic Cookbook or Everyday Cookbook for Mayonnaise recipe.
