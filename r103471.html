Meatball subs

Ingredients

  • 60 g day-old bread (approx. 2 slices), torn into pieces
  • 90 g cheddar cheese, cut into cubes (2-3 cm)
  • ½ tsp dried chilli flakes
  • 1 egg
  • 170 g water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 500 g beef mince
  • 1 onion (approx. 150 g), cut into eighths, layers separated
  • 50 g butter
  • 200 g tomato sauce
  • 40 g white vinegar
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 tsp English mustard
  • 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 4 bread rolls (e.g Vienna, baguette, approx. 15 -18 cm long)
  • 8 - 10 Swiss cheese slices
  • 40 g rocket leaves, to serve
  • 2 pickled gherkins, cut into thin slices, to serve

Preparation

  1. Place day-old bread, cheese and chilli flakes into mixing bowl and mill 5 sec/speed 8.
  2. Add egg, 120 g of the water and salt into mixing bowl and blend 5 sec/speed 5.
  3. Add beef mince and combine 10 sec//speed 4.
  4. Roll mince mixture into 20-25 balls (approx. 40 g each). Place meatballs into Varoma dish and tray and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  5. Place onion and butter into mixing bowl and cook 5 min/100°C//speed 1.
  6. Add tomato sauce, vinegar, sugar, 50 g of the water, mustard and Worcestershire sauce. Place Varoma into position and cook 10 min/Varoma//speed 2.
  7. Remove Varoma lid and turn meatballs, then secure Varoma lid and cook 5 min/Varoma//speed 2 or until meatballs are cooked through.
  8. Remove Varoma and transfer cooked meatballs into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and cover to keep warm. Secure mixing bowl lid and continue cooking sauce 8 min/Varoma//speed 2, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid. Transfer sauce into thermal serving bowl with meatballs and gently stir to combine, then cover to keep warm and set aside.
  9. Preheat grill to medium-high heat. Line a baking tray (30 cm x 40 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  10. Cut bread rolls in half lengthways (do not cut all the way through) and place onto prepared baking tray cut-side facing up. Grill for 1-2 minutes, or until golden brown. Remove from grill and place 2 cheese slices onto each bun, then place under grill for approx. 1 minute, or until cheese has melted.
  11. Remove bread rolls from grill and fill with 4-5 meatballs, and spoon over sauce and onions. Garnish with rocket and sliced gherkins and serve with remaining sauce on the side.

Useful Items

thermal serving bowl or other large bowl, baking tray (30 x 40 cm), baking paper, oven grill
