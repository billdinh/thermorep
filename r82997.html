Prawn and Chorizo Paella

Ingredients

  • 15 g fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 120 g onions, quartered
  • 300 g red peppers, seeds and stalk removed, cut in pieces
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 200 g chorizo, peeled, cut in pieces (0.5 cm)
  • 300 g paella rice
  • 800 g chicken stock
  • 1 pinch saffron
  • 250 g peeled, raw prawns
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 25 g lemon juice
  • 1 lemon, cut into slices for garnish

Preparation

  1. Place parsley in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a small bowl and set aside.
  2. Place onions, peppers and oil in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  3. Add chorizo and cook again 5 min/100°C//speed 1.
  4. Add rice, stock and saffron then cook 20 min/100°C//speed  until rice is tender and stock absorbed.
  5. Add prawns, salt and pepper stir in with spatula then cook 4 min/100°C//speed .
  6. Add lemon juice and reserved parsley, stir through with spatula then serve with lemon slices.
