Steamed cabbage and pork dumplings

Ingredients

Dumpling wrappers

  • 1 tsp vegetable oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 150 g water
  • 300 g plain flour
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • water, plus extra as needed

Filling and dumplings

  • 400 g white cabbage leaves, cut into pieces
  • 1 ½ tsp salt
  • 350 g pork mince
  • 530 g water
  • 20 g soy sauce
  • 10 g rice wine
  • ½ tsp sugar
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil (15 g)

Preparation

Dumpling wrappers

  1. Oil Varoma dish, Varoma tray and a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place water, flour, oil and salt into mixing bowl and knead 2 min/. If dough seems dry, add 10 g water (2 teaspoons) and knead for a further 1 min/. Transfer dough into prepared bowl and form into a ball. Cover bowl and allow to rest for 15 minutes. Meanwhile, prepare the filling.

Filling and dumplings

  1. Place cabbage leaves and 1 teaspoon of the salt into mixing bowl and chop with aid of spatula 20 sec/speed 4. Transfer into simmering basket and set aside to drain.
  2. Place mince, 30 g of the water, soy sauce, rice wine, sugar, remaining ½ teaspoon salt, pepper and sesame oil into mixing bowl and mix 2 min/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Squeeze excess moisture out of cabbage by pressing it against sides of simmering basket, then add cabbage to mixing bowl and mix 6 sec//speed 6. Transfer filling onto a large plate and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Turn rested dough out onto a floured surface. Divide dough into 4 equal pieces and shape each piece into a rope (2 cm diameter). Use spatula to cut each rope into 8-10 small pieces and shape them into balls.
  5. With a rolling pin, roll each ball of dough into a thin round shape (9 cm diameter). Place 1 tablespoon of the filling into the centre of each and fold the wrapper in half over the filling to make a semi-circle shape. Pinch the seam in the centre and hold it with one hand while using the other hand to pleat the top edge of the wrapper from the middle outwards on both sides. Arrange dumplings in prepared Varoma dish and tray.
  6. Place remaining 500 g water into mixing bowl, place Varoma into position and steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove Varoma and serve dumplings immediately.

Useful Items

rolling pin, bowl, large plate

Hints & Tricks

  • These dumplings are usually served with a black vinegar dipping sauce or soy sauce, or a mixture of both (e.g. 3 tablespoons soy sauce + 1 tablespoons black vinegar). Chopped garlic and chilli can also be added.
    - If you're in a hurry, use ready-made wonton wrappers available from Asian grocers and some supermarkets.
    - You can prepare dumplings ahead of time and freeze them. Steam as described in recipe without thawing, adding 5 minutes to steaming time.
