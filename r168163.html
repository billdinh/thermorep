Okra and meat stew (bamia)

Ingredients

  • 1000 g fresh okra, stem ends removed
  • 3 tbsp white vinegar
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 200 g onions, cut into quarters
  • 10 sprigs fresh coriander, roughly chopped (optional)
  • 60 g olive oil
  • 300 g beef, cut into cubes (3 cm)
  • 1 tsp salt, adjust to taste
  • 1 pinch freshly ground pepper
  • 500 g canned chopped tomatoes
  • 70 g tomato purée (concentrated)

Preparation

  1. Place Varoma dish into position, weigh in okra, drizzle with vinegar and set aside.
  2. Place garlic, onions, coriander and olive oil into mixing bowl, chop 3 sec/speed 5, then sauté 6 min/120°C/speed 1.
  3. Add beef, salt, pepper and sauté 10 min/120°C/speed .
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and tomato puree. Place Varoma into position and steam 30 min/Varoma//speed . Remove Varoma.
  5. Add steamed okra into mixing bowl, gently mix with spatula and cook 2 min/Varoma/speed . Transfer into a deep serving dish and serve hot.

Hints & Tricks

  • If frozen okra is used, steaming time in step 4 may be reduced.
  • Vegetarian okra stew: avoid meat and skip step 3, adding salt and pepper at step 4.
  • Traditionally okra without meat is served cold while okra with meat is served hot.
  • Serve with rice or pilaf.
