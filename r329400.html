Oat milk

Ingredients

  • 125 g rolled oats
  • 700 g filtered water, plus extra for rinsing and soaking
  • 2 pitted dates (optional)

Preparation

  1. Place simmering basket onto mixing bowl lid and weigh oats into it. Rinse well with water and transfer into a bowl. Cover with water and set aside to soak (approx. 20-60 minutes - see Tips).
  2. Using simmering basket, drain and rinse soaked oats well to remove any starchy residue.
  3. Place water, drained oats and dates (optional) into mixing bowl and blend 1 min/speed 4-9, increasing speed gradually from speed 4 to speed 9. Using a nut milk bag, strain mixture into a large jug (squeezing to extract all liquid). Cover and place into refrigerator until ready to use (see Tips).

Useful Items

nut milk bag, bowl, large jug, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • You can soak your oats for several hours (even overnight), if desired. Not only does soaking help soften the oats, but it also makes them easier to digest.
  • For a thicker milk, reduce water to 450 g.
  • You can store this milk in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.
