Courgettes with Corn

Ingredients

  • 15 g Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 175 g sweet corn, tinned (drained)
  • 600 g courgettes, diced (1-2 cm) (approx. 3 courgettes)
  • ¼ tsp dried basil
  • ¼ tsp dried oregano
  • 40 g lime juice
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt, or to taste
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper, or to taste
  • 2 Tbsp fresh coriander, leaves only, roughly chopped
Nutrition
per 4 portions
Calories
583 kJ / 139 kcal
Protein
4.4 g
Carbohydrates
10.1 g
Fat
9 g

Preparation

  1. Place Parmesan (if using) in mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 9. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place oil and garlic in mixing bowl then chop 2 sec/speed 7. Sauté 3 min/100°C/speed .
  3. Add sweet corn and mash 5 sec/speed 5.
  4. Add diced courgettes, basil and oregano then cook 10 min/100°C//speed  until courgettes are tender.
  5. Add lime juice, salt, pepper and fresh coriander then stir with spatula. Serve, sprinkled with reserved grated Parmesan (if using).
