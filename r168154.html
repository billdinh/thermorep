Yoghurt soup with meat dumplings (Shish barak)

Ingredients

Dumplings

  • 300 g flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 150 g milk
  • 50 g oil
  • 2 pinch salt
  • 3 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 50 g onion, cut into quarters
  • 100 g beef, finely minced
  • 1 pinch Arabian spice mix
  • 1 pinch ground cinnamon
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • 1 pinch ground white pepper
  • 20 g pine nuts (optional)

Soup

  • 20 g olive oil
  • 10 sprigs fresh coriander, roughly cut
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1500 g yoghurt
  • 2 tbsp cornflour (starch) (35 g)
  • 1 pinch salt, adjust to taste

Preparation

Dumplings

  1. Place flour, milk, oil and 1 pinch salt into mixing bowl and knead 2 min 30 sec/. Transfer dough into a bowl, cover with cling film and set aside for 30 minutes.
  2. Place parsley and onion into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 8.
  3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, add minced meat, Arabic spice mix, cinnamon, nutmeg, pepper, 1 pinch salt, pine nuts and mix 10 sec/speed 2. Transfer filling into a bowl and refrigerate. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Preheat oven to 180°C. Line a baking tray with baking paper and set aside.
  5. Divide reserved dough into 4 equal pieces. On a lightly floured surface, with aid of a rolling pin, rollout each piece of dough (1 mm thick).
  6. Cut dough with a round cookie cutter (Ø 5-6 cm) and place ¼ tsp filling in the centre of each circle. Fold one end over filling to shape crescents, wrap the ends of the crescents around your finger and join into tortellini shaped dumplings. Transfer onto prepared baking tray.
  7. Bake in preheated oven (180°C) for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown and crispy. Transfer into a plate and set aside.

Soup

  1. Place oil, coriander and garlic into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 8, then sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  2. Place yoghurt, corn flour and salt into mixing bowl and mix 15 sec/speed 5.
  3. Insert butterfly whisk and heat 25 min/80°C/speed 1. Remove butterfly whisk.
  4. Transfer soup into a tureen, add dumplings and garnish with sautéed coriander and garlic. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

cling film, baking tray and paper, rolling pin, plate, soup tureen, cookie cutter, refrigerator, oven
