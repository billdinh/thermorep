Cabbage rolls

Ingredients

Cabbage rolls

  • 10 large savoy cabbage leaves
  • 200 g long grain rice
  • 1700 g water
  • 120 g brown onion, cut into halves
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 100 g carrot, cut into pieces (1-2 cm)
  • 6 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 30 g butter
  • 250 g chicken breast fillet, cut into pieces (3-4 cm)
  • 200 g pork mince
  • 2 tsp sea salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper

Tomato sauce

  • 100 g brown onion, cut into halves
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 30 g butter
  • 800 g canned tomatoes
  • 1 - 2 tbsp Vegetable stock paste (see Tips)
  • ½ tsp sea salt, to taste
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper, to taste
  • 2 tsp cornflour
  • ½ tsp raw sugar

Preparation

Cabbage rolls

  1. Remove the hard core (approx. 5-6 cm) from the bottom of each cabbage leaf. Lay 5 of the leaves into Varoma dish, push down and secure Varoma lid into position.
  2. Insert simmering basket, then weigh rice into it. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and rinse rice until water runs clear.
  3. Place 1000 g of the water into mixing bowl, then re-insert simmering basket with rice. Place Varoma into position and cook 11 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  4. Remove Varoma and carefully remove cabbage leaves. Rinse leaves briefly under cold water, then set aside. Place remaining cabbage leaves into Varoma dish, push down and secure Varoma lid. Place Varoma back into position above rice and cook 5 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  5. Remove Varoma and carefully remove cabbage leaves. Rinse leaves briefly under cold water, then set aside. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and set rice aside to drain. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  6. Place onion, garlic, carrot and parsley into mixing bowl and chop 6 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  7. Add butter and sauté 3 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  8. Add chicken and mince 12 sec/speed 6.
  9. Add pork mince, salt, pepper and reserved drained rice and combine 15 sec//speed 4, with aid of spatula. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then repeat combining 15 sec//speed 4, with aid of spatula. Transfer mixture into a bowl and set aside.
  10. Place 1 cooked cabbage leaf onto a work surface, then place approx. 2-3 tablespoons (approx. 140 g) of the mince mixture down the centre of the leaf. Fold the left and right sides of leaf over filling, then gently roll up bottom flap to create a sausage shape. Repeat with remaining mince mixture and cabbage leaves to make a total of 10 cabbage rolls.
  11. Place 3-4 cabbage rolls into Varoma dish, then place remaining 6-7 cabbage rolls onto Varoma tray. Place remaining 700 g water into mixing bowl, place Varoma into position and steam 30 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  12. Transfer cooked cabbage rolls into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and cover to keep warm. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Tomato sauce

  1. Place onion and garlic into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add butter and sauté 3 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Add tomatoes and blend 30 sec/speed 9.
  4. Add stock paste, salt, pepper, cornflour and sugar, then cook 14 min/Varoma/speed 2, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  5. Remove simmering basket, insert measuring cup and blend 15 sec/speed 6. Serve cabbage rolls immediately, topped with tomato sauce.
