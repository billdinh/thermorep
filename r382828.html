Coriander turmeric chicken bites

Ingredients

  • 100 g sesame seeds
  • 2 cm piece fresh turmeric, peeled
  • 2 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 4 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only
  • 1 fresh long red chilli, trimmed and deseeded (optional)
  • 600 g skinless, boneless chicken thighs, cut into pieces (2-3 cm)
  • 1 tbsp tamari
  • olive oil, for shallow frying
  • mixed baby salad leaves, to garnish (optional)
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges, to serve

Preparation

  1. Place a plate onto mixing bowl lid and weigh sesame seeds onto it. Set aside.
  2. Place turmeric, ginger, coriander and chilli (optional) into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add chicken and tamari and chop 20 sec/speed 5.
  4. Shape tablespoonfuls of chicken mixture into patties (makes approx. 18 patties), then coat in reserved sesame seeds.
  5. Place a non-stick frying pan over low-medium heat and add oil. Working in batches, fry patties for approx. 8 minutes on each side, until golden and cooked through. Transfer onto a plate lined with paper towel to drain. Garnish with salad greens and serve with lemon wedges.

Useful Items

plate, non-stick frying pan, paper towel, stove top
