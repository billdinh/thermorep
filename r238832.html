Egg Salad Sandwiches

Ingredients

  • 14 oz water
  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 oz green onions, cut into ½ in. pieces (approx. 2 onions)
  • 2 oz mayonnaise, to taste
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 3 pinches salt
  • 3 pinches ground pepper
  • 1 pinch paprika
  • 1 tbsp fresh chives, chopped
  • 10 slices white bread
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
653 kJ / 156 kcal
Protein
6 g
Carbohydrates
13 g
Fat
9 g

Preparation

  1. Place water into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket, place in eggs and cook 14 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove simmering basket with spatula and place eggs under cold running water to stop cooking. Cool eggs to room temperature, peel and set aside. Empty mixing bowl.
  2. Place green onions into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Add mayonnaise, mustard, salt, pepper, paprika and chives and chop 10 sec/speed 4.
  3. Cut eggs into quarters, add into mixing bowl and mix 1 sec//speed 4. Divide mixture onto 5 bread slices, top with remaining 5 slices, cut into halves and serve.
