Spiced oranges and dates with yoghurt cream

Ingredients

  • 50 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 80 g Greek-style natural yoghurt
  • 60 g cream cheese
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • ½ vanilla bean, split into halves lengthways, seeds scraped
  • 30 g shelled unsalted pistachio nuts (see Tip)
  • 4 pitted fresh (Medjool) dates
  • 5 Navel oranges, 1 juiced and 4 segmented
  • 1 sprig fresh mint, leaves only, cut into pieces
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon (optional)

Preparation

  1. Insert butterfly whisk. Place cream, yoghurt, cream cheese, honey and vanilla seeds into mixing bowl and mix 20 sec/speed 4, until well combined. Remove butterfly whisk. Transfer into a bowl, cover with plastic wrap and place into refrigerator for 10 minutes. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  2. Place pistachios into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7, until roughly chopped. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place dates into mixing bowl and chop 6 sec/speed 8, until roughly chopped.
  4. Add orange juice and combine 10 sec//speed 2. Divide date mixture between serving bowls. Divide orange segments between serving bowls, then top with yoghurt cream and garnish with chopped pistachios, mint leaves and cinnamon (optional).

Useful Items

bowl, plastic wrap, serving bowls, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • You can use soaked, then dehydrated nuts in this recipe.
