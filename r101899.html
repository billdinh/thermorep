Herb Cashew Spread

Ingredients

  • 200 g raw cashew nuts, unsalted
  • 15 g mixed fresh herbs (e.g. parsley, basil, coriander)
  • 2 heaped Tbsp brewer's yeast
  • 110 g water, cold, plus extra for soaking
  • 15 g lemon juice
  • 40 g neutral oil (e.g. sunflower oil, ground nut oil)
  • 1 garlic clove
  • ½ - ¾ tsp fine sea salt, to taste
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 1 - 2 Tbsp fresh chives, snipped (2 mm), to taste

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in cashew nuts. Cover with cold water and leave to soak overnight (or for at least 8 hours).
  2. Drain soaked cashews through simmering basket then run under cold running water. Set aside to drain.
  3. Place herbs in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8.
  4. Add drained cashew nuts, beer yeast flakes, 110 g water, lemon juice, oil, garlic, salt and pepper then blend 1 min/speed 7. Scrape down sides and lid of mixing bowl with spatula then blend again 30 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides and lid of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add fresh chives and mix 8 sec//speed 2. Season to taste, then transfer to a small serving bowl. Serve or cover and store in fridge until needed.

Hints & Tricks

  • For an alternative flavour, substitute the fresh herbs with fresh peppers, dried tomatoes, chilli or paprika.
  • The spread will keep covered in the fridge for 3-5 days.
  • Enjoy the spread as is, or with boiled potatoes or roasted vegetables.
