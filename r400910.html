Beef Stroganoff

Ingredients

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 100 g onions, quartered
  • 30 g unsalted butter, diced
  • 2 Tbsp sweet paprika
  • 700 - 900 g beef fillet steak, cut in strips (approx. 1 cm x 5 cm)
  • 225 g fresh button mushrooms, quartered
  • 225 g water
  • 2 Tbsp tomato purée
  • 1 beef stock cube (for 0.5 l), crumbled
  • ¼ - 1 tsp ground cayenne pepper, to taste (optional)
  • 2 Tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 Tbsp cornflour
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 100 g sour cream
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1783 kJ / 447 kcal

Preparation

  1. Place garlic and onions in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add butter and 1 Tbsp paprika then, with simmering basket in place of measuring cup, sauté 5 min/100°C/speed 1.
  3. Add beef and, with simmering basket in place of measuring cup, sauté 4 min/120°C//speed 1.
  4. Add mushrooms, water, tomato purée, beef stock, remaining 1 Tbsp paprika, cayenne pepper (if using), Dijon mustard, cornflour, salt and pepper then cook 10 min/100°C//speed 1.
  5. Add sour cream and mix 1 min//speed 1. Serve hot.
