Chicken Tacos

Ingredients

Chicken

  • 28 oz chicken breast fillets, boneless, skinless, cut in halves
  • 1 oz olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp ground paprika
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 2 tsp dried thyme
  • 2 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper, ground
  • 1 tsp chipotle chili powder
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper

Coleslaw

  • 5 oz red cabbage, cut into pieces
  • 1 Granny Smith apple, cored and quartered
  • 1 tbsp freshly squeezed lime juice
  • ¼ tsp ground cumin
  • ¾ tsp salt
  • 1 oz olive oil
  • 14 oz water
  • 12 corn tortillas
  • cilantro leaves, to garnish
  • ¼ - ½ red onion, sliced, to garnish
  • 1 avocado, sliced

Preparation

Chicken

  1. Place a large bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in chicken, drizzle with olive oil, season with salt and set aside.
  2. Place paprika, oregano, thyme, onion powder, garlic powder, cayenne, chipotle powder and black pepper into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 10. Spread the rub over chicken and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

Coleslaw

  1. Place cabbage, apple, lime juice, cumin, salt and olive oil into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Line the bottom of Varoma dish with foil. Prick foil with a fork to allow steam to circulate, place marinated chicken breast into Varoma and set aside.
  3. Place water into mixing bowl, place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 25 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  4. Cut chicken in diagonal slices and transfer onto a serving plate.
  5. Place a frying pan over medium heat and heat tortillas. Divide tortillas between 6 plates. Top tortillas with coleslaw, chicken, cilantro leaves, red onion and avocado. Serve warm.
