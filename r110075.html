Chocolate Marquise

Ingredients

  • 9 oz dark chocolate (50% cacao), in pieces
  • 7 oz unsalted butter, room temperature, in pieces
  • 5 oz sugar
  • 6 large egg yolks,
  • 2 oz whisky
  • 6 egg whites
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder, for dusting

Preparation

  1. Line a round cake pan (approx. 10 in.-11 in.) with cling wrap, smooth out wrinkles and set aside.
  2. Place chocolate into mixing bowl and chop 20 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add butter, sugar, egg yolks and whisky and cook 4 min/120°F/speed 4. Transfer chocolate mixture to a large bowl, set aside and allow to cool to room temperature (approx. 10-15 min.). Meanwhile, clean and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg whites and salt into mixing bowl and whip 4 min/speed 4. Remove butterfly whisk transfer whipped egg whites to a bowl and set aside.
  5. Remove ¼ of the whipped egg whites and fold into cooled chocolate mixture until incorporated. Add remaining whipped egg whites and gently fold into chocolate mixture with spatula. Pour into prepared pan.
  6. Smooth surface and wrap with cling wrap. Place in freezer for at least 4 hours. Remove from freezer 15 minutes before serving. Remove the plastic wrap and turn Chocolate Marquise onto a chilled serving plate. Sprinkle with unsweetened cocoa powder, slice and serve immediately.

Useful Items

round cake pan (approx. 10 in.-11 in.), cling wrap, large bowl, freezer

Hints & Tricks

  • This recipe contains under cooked eggs.
