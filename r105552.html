Avgolemono (chicken soup with egg and lemon)

Ingredients

  • 1200 g water
  • 500 g chicken wings, skin on
  • 1 carrot, cut into pieces
  • 1 potato (approx. 100 g), cut into pieces
  • 1 celery stalk, cut into pieces
  • 1 brown onion (approx. 150 g), cut into halves
  • 2 tsp Vegetable stock paste (see Tips)
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • salt, to taste
  • ground black pepper, to taste
  • 100 g white rice (medium-grain)
  • 1 egg
  • 40 g lemon juice (approx. 1 lemon)
  • 1 sprig fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only, for garnishing

Preparation

  1. Place water into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket. Add chicken, carrot, potato, celery, onion, stock paste, oil, salt and pepper into simmering basket and cook 40 min/100°C/speed 2, or until chicken is cooked through.
  2. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula. Transfer chicken into a bowl and set aside to cool slightly. Discard vegetables.
  3. Remove chicken meat from bones, discarding skin and bones. Transfer shredded chicken meat into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and cover to keep warm.
  4. Add rice to stock in mixing bowl and cook 15 min/100°C//speed . Using simmering basket, strain stock into a jug. Transfer rice into bowl with chicken.
  5. Place a jug onto mixing bowl lid and weigh 250 g of the stock into it. Add remaining stock to chicken and rice and cover to keep warm. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  6. Place egg into mixing bowl and mix 30 sec/speed 8.
  7. Add lemon juice and mix 30 sec/speed 5.
  8. Further mix 20 sec/speed 2, slowly adding reserved stock through hole in mixing bowl lid until combined and frothy.
  9. Transfer lemon mixture into bowl with chicken, rice and stock and gently combine. Transfer into serving bowls, garnish with parsley and serve immediately.

Useful Items

bowl, thermal serving bowl or other large bowl, jug
