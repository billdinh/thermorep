Candy Corn Pudding

Ingredients

  • 170 g caster sugar
  • 6 egg yolks, from large eggs
  • 3 Tbsp cornflour
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • 850 g whole milk
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 120 g unsalted butter, diced
  • 5 - 8 drops orange food colouring
  • 340 g whipping cream
  • 2 tsp icing sugar, to taste (optional)
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1941 kJ / 464 kcal
Protein
7 g

32 g

35 g

Preparation

  1. Insert butterfly whisk. Place sugar, egg yolks, cornflour, salt, milk and vanilla extract in mixing bowl, mix 20 sec/speed 4 then cook 25 min/90°C/speed 4.
  2. Add butter and mix 20 sec/speed 4. Divide half the pudding between 4-6 dessert bowls. Cover with cling film directly touching the surface of pudding and set aside.
  3. Transfer remaining pudding to a bowl, add orange food colouring and stir with a spoon until fully mixed. Cover bowl with cling film directly touching the surface of pudding. Refrigerate all puddings until set (approx. 1 hour). Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl.
  4. Spoon orange pudding over plain pudding in dessert bowls, creating two distinct layers. Set aside.
  5. Insert butterfly whisk. Place whipping cream and icing sugar in mixing bowl and, without setting a time, whip at speed 3, until stiff peaks form, watching carefully to avoid over-whipping (see tip).
  6. Top each pudding with whipped cream and serve immediately.

Useful Items

cling film, large bowl, dessert bowls, fridge

Hints & Tricks

  • Orange food coloring can be made by mixing 1 part red food colouring to 2 parts yellow food colouring. Adjust intensity of colour as desired.
  • The required time to whip cream depends on the temperature, fat content and quality of the cream. Take care not to overwhip or the cream will turn into butter. Watch carefully through the hole in the mixing bowl lid and listen for change in the mixing sound. Approximate whipping time is 1-2 minutes.
  • If preferred, cling film could be replaced with lightly oiled baking paper.
  • Garnish Candy Corn Pudding with Halloween sprinkles, candy corn or candy pumpkins.
