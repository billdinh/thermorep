Salad with Bacon, Watermelon and Yoghurt Dressing

Ingredients

  • 30 g olive oil, plus extra for frying
  • 15 slices streaky bacon, cured and smoked (approx. 180 g)
  • 150 g red onions, quartered
  • 150 g fresh baby spinach
  • 150 g tinned chickpeas, drained, rinsed
  • 240 g plain yoghurt
  • 50 g peanuts, roasted, unsalted
  • 1 lemon, grated zest from half and approx. 25 g juice
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 200 g feta cheese, diced (1 cm)
  • 500 g watermelon, diced (1 cm), seeds removed

Preparation

  1. Heat oil in a frying pan over a medium-high heat and fry bacon slices until crisp then drain on paper towels.
  2. Place red onions and oil in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Sauté 5 min/120°C/speed 1. Meanwhile, place spinach and chickpeas in a serving bowl.
  3. Transfer sautéed onions with oil to serving bowl.
  4. Place cooked bacon in mixing bowl and grind 5 sec/speed 9. Transfer to serving bowl.
  5. Place yoghurt, peanuts, lemon juice, grated lemon zest and salt in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 5. Pour over salad, add cheese and watermelon then toss. Refrigerate until needed - serve chilled.

Useful Items

frying pan, paper towel, serving bowl, hob, refrigerator
