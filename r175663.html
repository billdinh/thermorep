Pistachio and Almond Gelatos

Ingredients

  • 200 g pistachio nuts, unsalted, shelled
  • 360 g sugar
  • 200 g almonds
  • 1 ½ tsp almond extract
  • 1000 g whole milk
  • 50 g cornflour
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
4929 kJ / 1177 kcal
Protein
30 g
Carbohydrates
121 g
Fat
61 g

Preparation

  1. Place pistachios and 180 g sugar in mixing bowl then chop 15 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place almonds, 180 g sugar and almond extract in mixing bowl then chop 15 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place milk and cornflour in mixing bowl then cook 10 min/90°C/speed 3. Transfer half to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Add reserved chopped pistachios to mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 5. Pour gelato mixture into shallow freezer-proof containers, up to 4 cm deep, and cover with cling film. Leave to cool completely before freezing for at least 8 hours or until solid. Meanwhile, continue with recipe.
  5. Place reserved milk mixture and reserved chopped almonds in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 5. Pour gelato mixture into another shallow freezer-proof container, up to 4 cm deep, and cover with cling film. Leave to cool completely before freezing for at least 8 hours or until solid.
  6. To serve, place containers with frozen gelato in fridge for 10 minutes, then cut pistachio gelato into pieces (4 cm) with a sharp knife. Place pieces in mixing bowl and crush 20 sec/speed 6, then mix 10 sec/speed 4. Transfer gelato to a serving bowl and return to freezer. Meanwhile, continue with recipe.
  7. Cut almond gelato into pieces (4 cm) with a sharp knife. Place pieces in mixing bowl and crush 20 sec/speed 6, then mix 10 sec/speed 4. Transfer gelato to a serving bowl, and serve both immediately.

Useful Items

cling film, shallow freezer-proof containers (e.g. aluminium, silicone), long sharp knife, freezer, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Two deliciously nutty flavours in one recipe! Bring Italy to your kitchen with this easy recipe for classic gelatos.
