Umali

Ingredients

  • 2 sheets puff pastry
  • 70 g pine nuts
  • 70 g raisins
  • 100 g blanched almonds
  • 50 g pistachios, shelled and unsalted
  • 1000 g milk
  • 100 g cream
  • 80 g white sugar
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tbsp natural vanilla extract
  • 20 g unsalted butter, cut into pieces, plus extra for greasing

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 200˚C. Line 2 baking trays (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Cut puff pastry sheets into strips (1½ cm wide), then lay out onto prepared trays and bake for 9-11 minutes (200˚C) or until golden and crispy. Set aside to cool while proceeding with recipe. Reduce oven temperature to 180°C.
  3. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh pine nuts and raisins into it, then set aside.
  4. Place almonds into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 7. Transfer into bowl with raisin mixture and set aside.
  5. Place pistachios into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a separate bowl and set aside.
  6. Place milk, cream, sugar, cinnamon and vanilla into mixing bowl and cook 7 min/90°C/speed 3.
  7. Lightly grease a casserole dish (approx. 28 cm) with butter and set aside.
  8. Cover base of casserole dish with half of the baked puff pastry strips. Sprinkle with half of the reserved almond mixture. Cover with remaining puff pastry strips and remaining almond mixture. Slowly pour milk mixture over almonds, allowing pastry to stand for 15 minutes to absorb liquid.
  9. Sprinkle with reserved pistachios and butter, then bake for 20-25 minutes (180°C) or until golden brown. Serve warm.

Useful Items

bowl, casserole dish (28 cm), baking trays (30 x 40 cm), baking paper, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Substitute chopped pistachios with 30 g desiccated coconut for a different flavour.
