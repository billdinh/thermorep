Chicken à la king

Ingredients

  • 2 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 1000 g water
  • 150 g brown onion, cut into quarters
  • 2 - 3 tsp Chicken stock paste, to taste (see Tips)
  • 500 - 600 g chicken tenderloins, cut into halves
  • 100 g salted butter, cut into pieces
  • 200 g button mushrooms, cut into quarters
  • ½ red capsicum, cut into thin slices (5 mm)
  • ½ green capsicum, cut into thin slices (5 mm)
  • 2 tbsp gluten free flour (see Tips)
  • ½ tsp ground paprika
  • 50 g sherry
  • 200 g thickened cream
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper

Preparation

  1. Place parsley into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place water, onion and stock paste. Insert simmering basket and weigh chicken into it, then cook 8 min/95°C//speed 4.
  3. Stir chicken with aid of spatula to ensure even cooking and cook for a further 7 min/95°C//speed 4.
  4. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula. Transfer chicken into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and cover to keep warm. Strain cooking liquid through a fine-mesh sieve into a jug and set aside. Discard onion.
  5. Place butter into mixing bowl and melt 1 min/100°C/speed 1.
  6. Add mushrooms and both capsicums and cook 2 min/100°C//speed 1.
  7. Add flour and paprika and cook 1 min/90°C//speed 1. Scrape down sides and base of mixing bowl with spatula.
  8. Add sherry, cream, pepper and 250 g of the reserved cooking liquid and cook 5 min/90°C//speed 1.
  9. Add reserved chicken and reserved parsley and cook 1 min/90°C//speed 1. Serve warm with sides of choice (see Tips).

Useful Items

bowl, thermal serving bowl or other large bowl, jug, fine-mesh sieve

Hints & Tricks

  • Please refer to the The Basic Cookbook for the Chicken stock paste recipe.
  • You can replace gluten free flour with plain flour, if desired.
  • Serve with a side of rice, steamed potatoes or noodles. We served our dish with mashed potatoes (please refer to the The Basic Cookbook for the recipe).
