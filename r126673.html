Cocoa mocha almonds

Ingredients

  • 1 egg white
  • 1 pinch fine salt
  • 100 g sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla sugar, homemade
  • ½ tsp ground cardamom
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1 tbsp instant coffee
  • 400 g blanched almonds

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 150°C. Line baking tray with baking paper.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg white and salt in mixing bowl and beat until stiff 2 min/speed 3.5.
  3. Add sugar, vanilla sugar, cardamom, cinnamon, cocoa and espresso and beat again 20 sec/speed 3.5 weiterschlagen. Remove butterfly whisk.
  4. Add almonds and stir in 20 sec//speed 1. Stir again with aid of spatula, then spread almonds on baking tray. Bake for 15 minutes (150°C)
  5. Allow almonds to cool for 20 minutes. Break into bite-sized pieces and store in jars or little bags
