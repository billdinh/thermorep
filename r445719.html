Creamy Buffalo Chicken Pasta

Ingredients

  • 10 oz hot sauce, e.g. Frank's Red Hot Original
  • 6 oz unsalted butter, in pieces
  • ½ oz Worcestershire sauce
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • 20 oz chicken tenders
  • 4 oz yellow onions, in pieces
  • 1 garlic clove
  • ½ oz extra virgin olive oil
  • 55 oz water
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 12 oz penne pasta
  • 4 oz cream cheese, in pieces
  • 6 oz blue cheese crumbled
  • fresh parsley leaves, to garnish

Preparation

  1. Place hot sauce, butter, Worcestershire sauce and garlic powder into mixing bowl and cook 5 min/160°F/speed 1. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Cut parchment twice as large as Varoma. Lay chicken onto parchment and pour half the buffalo sauce over chicken. Fold edges of parchment and wrap chicken to create a parcel, tucking the folded ends of parchment underneath the parcel, ensuring the packet is closed. Place parcel in Varoma dish, secure Varoma lid and set aside.
  3. Place onions, garlic and oil into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and without measuring cup sauté 5 min/250°F/speed 1.
  4. Add water and salt, set Varoma with chicken into position and cook 33-35 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  5. Remove Varoma and set aside. Drain and discard liquid from parchment package. Set chicken aside and keep warm.
  6. Add pasta into mixing bowl and without measuring cup cook for time indicated on pasta package/212°F/speed 1 or until pasta is al dente. Drain cooked pasta through Varoma dish.
  7. Place drained pasta, cooked chicken, cream cheese and remaining buffalo sauce, to taste, into mixing bowl and mix 5 min//speed  with aid of spatula. Divide into bowls, top with blue cheese and fresh parsley and serve.
