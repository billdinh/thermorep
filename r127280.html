Aubergine mille-feuille with yoghurt and mustard sauce

Ingredients

  • 1750 g water
  • 360 g plum tomatoes (6 tomatoes)
  • 500 g aubergines (1-2 pieces)
  • 250 g mozzarella (2 balls)
  • 6 slices smoked salmon (120-130 g)
  • 1 plain yoghurt
  • 1 tsp mustard
  • 20 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 6 fresh basil leaves, for decoration
  • 30 g slivered almonds, toasted, for decoration

Preparation

  1. Place water into mixing bowl and heat 13 min/100°C/speed . Meanwhile, make a cross cut at the base of tomatoes and place 3 tomatoes in simmering basket.
  2. Insert simmering basket and blanch tomatoes 1 min/100°C/speed 1. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and set tomatoes aside.
  3. Insert simmering basket with the other 3 tomatoes and blanch 1 min/100°C/speed 1. Peel all blanched tomatoes, cut into 4 pieces and remove seeds. Place on Varoma tray and set aside.
  4. Wash aubergines and cut into slices of approx. 1 cm thick (allow 2 slices per person). Put into Varoma dish, place Varoma tray with tomatoes, cover and place Varoma into position. Steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 1. Set Varoma aside and let it cool. Drain and rinse mixing bowl.
  5. Cut mozzarella into 12 slices of approx. 5 mm thick. On a plate, make layers starting with one slice of aubergine, two of tomato and one of mozzarella. Continue with another slice of aubergine, two of tomato and one of mozzarella. Finish by placing a slice of smoked salmon on top. Repeat for the remaining servings.
  6. Prepare the sauce by placing natural yoghurt, mustard and olive oil into mixing bowl. Mix 10 sec/speed 5. Pour some of the sauce over each mille-feuille and the rest into a sauce boat. Garnish with a fresh basil leaf and a few slices of toasted almonds. Sprinkle each mille-feuille with a little olive oil and serve.
