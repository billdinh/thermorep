Turkey burgers

Ingredients

  • 500 g turkey meat, boneless, skinless
  • 1 bread roll, stale and cut into quarters
  • 1 onion, cut into halves
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp paprika, sweet
  • 1 tsp mustard, medium hot
  • 1 tsp fine salt
  • Â½ tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp vegetable broth powder
  • 100 g water
  • oil for frying

Preparation

  1. Dice turkey (approx. 2 cm), remove sinews and allow to freeze for approx. 1 hour.
  2. Place pieces of bread roll in mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 8.
  3. Add onion and chop 3 sec/speed 8.
  4. Add frozen turkey, egg, paprika, mustard, salt, pepper, vegetable broth powder and water, and chop 30 sec/speed 7 with aid of spatula.
  5. Shape 10 burgers from mixture (or 20 mini meatballs) and fry in hot oil until golden on both sides. Serve hot or cold.

Useful Items

frying pan, freezer, stove

Hints & Tricks

  • If desired, also chop fresh herbs such as parsley or basil with onion.
  • Serve burgers with ketchup.
