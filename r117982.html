Warm Caesar salad with polenta croutons

Ingredients

Polenta croutons

  • 30 g Parmesan cheese, crust removed and cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 2 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 300 g water
  • 1 tsp Vegetable stock paste (see Tips)
  • 80 g fine polenta
  • 20 g salted butter

Caeser salad dressing

  • 150 g grapeseed oil
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 anchovy fillet, in oil (optional)
  • 100 g mayonnaise (see Tips)
  • 20 g lemon juice (approx. ½ lemon)
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce, (gluten free) (see Tips)
  • 2 tsp Dijon mustard, (gluten free)

Caesar salad

  • 900 g water
  • 500 g chicken tenderloins
  • salt, to season
  • ground black pepper, to season
  • olive oil, for drizzling, plus extra for brushing
  • 4 eggs, chilled
  • 40 g pancetta (approx. 8 slices)
  • 1 baby cos lettuce (approx. 185 g), trimmed and cut into quarters lengthways
  • Parmesan cheese, shaved, to serve

Preparation

Polenta croutons

  1. Line a baking dish (10 x 20 cm) with plastic wrap and set aside.
  2. Place Parmesan into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a small bowl and set aside.
  3. Place parsley into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a separate small bowl and set aside.
  4. Place water and stock paste into mixing bowl and heat 3 min/100°C/speed 1.
  5. Add polenta and cook 10 min/100°C/speed 2, or until polenta coats the back of a spoon in a thick layer.
  6. Add butter, reserved parsley and half of the reserved Parmesan and mix 10 sec/speed 4.
  7. Cool slightly (approx. 10 minutes), then transfer mixture into prepared baking dish, smoothing with aid of spatula to create an even surface then cover with plastic wrap. Allow to cool completely, then place into refrigerator for a minimum of 30 minutes. Clean and dry mixing bowl. Meanwhile, continue with salad dressing.

Caeser salad dressing

  1. Place a jug onto mixing bowl lid and weigh oil into it. Set jug aside.
  2. Place garlic into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7.
  3. Insert butterfly whisk. Add anchovy, mayonnaise, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce and mustard and whisk 30 sec/speed 4.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then mix speed 3.5, gradually pouring oil onto mixing bowl lid, letting it trickle into mixing bowl to emulsify dressing. Remove butterfly whisk.
  5. Transfer dressing into a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Caesar salad

  1. Place 500 g of the water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish into position and insert Varoma tray, then weigh chicken onto it. Season with salt and pepper and drizzle with oil. Secure Varoma lid and cook 13-15 min/Varoma/speed 2 until chicken is cooked to your liking. Remove Varoma and transfer chicken into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and cover to keep warm. Set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  2. Place remaining 400 g water into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket and place eggs into it. Cook 9-10 min/Varoma/speed 1, or until eggs are cooked to your liking (see Tips). Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and place it, with eggs, under cold running water to cease cooking.
  3. Preheat oven grill. Line a baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with aluminium foil and grease with oil. Set aside.
  4. Remove cooled polenta from refrigerator and turn out onto a chopping board. Remove plastic wrap and cut into cubes (2 x 2 cm).
  5. Place polenta cubes onto half of the prepared baking tray and sprinkle with remaining reserved Parmesan. Place pancetta onto remaining half of baking tray. Grill polenta and pancetta for 4-6 minutes, until cheese has melted and cubes begin to turn golden brown and pancetta is crisp. Set aside.
  6. Divide lettuce between serving plates. Slice reserved chicken and place on top of lettuce. Peel eggs and cut into halves and add to salad. Top salad with polenta croutons and pancetta. Drizzle with reserved dressing and garnish with shaved Parmesan before serving.
