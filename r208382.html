Bolivian Rice Pilaf

Ingredients

  • 250 g carrots, cut in sticks (5 cm x 0.5 cm)
  • 3 Tbsp coconut oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1 ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 2 Tbsp dried sage
  • 2 Tbsp fresh oregano
  • 3 Tbsp mint leaves
  • 1000 g water
  • 100 g quinoa grains
  • 250 g wild rice mix
  • 6 spring onions, cut in pieces
  • 120 g red peppers, diced (1 cm)
  • 2 - 3 limes, zest and juice, to taste

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C and line a baking tray with baking paper.
  2. Place carrots, 2 Tbsp coconut oil and garlic cloves on prepared baking tray, season with ½ tsp salt and black pepper then roast for 20 minutes (200°C) or until cooked. Meanwhile, continue with recipe.
  3. Place sage, oregano and mint leaves in mixing bowl then chop 2 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place water and 1 tsp salt in mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket and add quinoa and wild rice mixture to it then cook 20-22 min/100°C/speed 4. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and transfer quinoa and wild rice mixture to a serving dish then set aside. Clean mixing bowl.
  5. Place 1 Tbsp coconut oil and spring onions in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  6. Sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  7. Add red peppers and sauté 2 min/120°C//speed 1. Transfer to serving dish with quinoa and wild rice mixture. Add roasted carrots, reserved chopped herbs, zest and juice of limes and mix with spatula. Serve warm or cold.

Useful Items

baking tray and paper, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve this rice with meat or poultry.
