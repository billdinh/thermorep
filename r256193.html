Salmon niçoise salad

Ingredients

  • 500 g water, plus extra cold water for soaking
  • 1 tsp salt, plus extra to season
  • 400 g new baby potatoes, unpeeled and cut into quarters
  • 4 whole eggs, washed (see Tips)
  • 4 skinless salmon fillets (approx. 800 g)
  • olive oil, for drizzling
  • ground black pepper, to season
  • 200 g fresh green beans, trimmed and cut into halves (approx. 5 cm)
  • 1 eschalot
  • 4 anchovy fillets
  • ½ tsp caster sugar
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 40 g lemon juice (approx. 1 lemon)
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 100 g cherry tomatoes, ripe, cut into halves
  • 40 g pitted black olives,
  • 2 spring onions/shallots, trimmed and cut into thin slices

Preparation

  1. Place water and salt into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket into mixing bowl and weigh potatoes into it. Place whole eggs on top of potatoes.
  2. Place Varoma dish into position and place salmon fillets into it, then drizzle with oil and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Insert Varoma tray and weigh beans onto it. Secure Varoma lid and cook 15 min/Varoma/speed 4. Remove Varoma and set aside. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and place eggs into a bowl of cold water to cool. Check to see if potatoes, eggs, beans and fish are cooked to your liking.
  4. If further cooking is required, re-insert simmering basket with potatoes. Place Varoma back into position, secure Varoma lid and cook for a further 2-4 min/Varoma/speed 4 or until potatoes, salmon and beans are cooked to your liking (see Tips). Transfer into a large bowl to cool. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  5. Place eschalot and anchovy fillets into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  6. Add sugar, mustard, lemon juice and oil and mix 5 sec/speed 6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. When eggs are cool enough to handle, peel and cut into halves. Flake reserved salmon and add to large bowl with potatoes and beans. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh tomatoes and olives into it, transfer into large bowl. Add spring onions/shallots and eggs. Add Anchovy dressing to bowl, toss gently to combine, then serve.

Hints & Tricks

  • Using cold eggs from the refrigerator will increase cooking time. We used X-Large eggs (59 g) in this recipe; using smaller eggs will reduce the cooking time. Please refer to The Basic Cookbook for timings for both soft and hard boiled eggs. Take care when peeling soft boiled eggs.
  • Cooking times may vary according to the thickness of the salmon fillets.
