Savoiardi with mango and passionfruit

Ingredients

Savoiardi biscuit

  • 6 eggs, separated
  • 2 pinches sea salt
  • 180 g caster sugar
  • 150 g plain flour
  • 60 g cornflour

Mango and passionfruit

  • 250 g water
  • 800 g fresh mango (approx. 3-4 mangos), flesh only, cut into cubes (1-2 cm)
  • 130 g caster sugar
  • 2 - 3 tbsp fresh passionfruit pulp
  • 2 eggs
  • 500 g mascarpone cheese, room temperature

Preparation

Savoiardi biscuit

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Line a baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg whites and salt into mixing bowl and whip 3-5 min/37°C/speed 3, without measuring cup. Remove butterfly whisk, then transfer whipped egg whites into a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry butterfly whisk.
  3. Re-insert butterfly whisk. Place egg yolks and sugar into mixing bowl and beat 4 min/speed 3.
  4. Add flour, cornflour and reserved whipped egg whites and mix 20 sec/speed 1. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then repeat mixing 20 sec/speed 1. Remove butterfly whisk.
  5. Spread mixture onto prepared baking tray, then bake in the oven for 12 minutes (180°C). Allow to cool slightly (approx. 5-10 minutes), then use a cookie cutter (6 cm) to cut out biscuits (approx. 24). Set biscuits aside.

Mango and passionfruit

  1. Place water, 200 g of the mango and 30 g of the sugar into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 5, then cook 12 min/100°C/speed 1.
  2. Add passionfruit pulp and mix 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer into a bowl and set aside to cool. Clean and dry mixing bowl and butterfly whisk.
  3. Dice remaining 600 g of the mango flesh into small cubes (1-2 cm) and set aside.
  4. Re-insert butterfly whisk. Place eggs and remaining 100 g of the sugar into mixing bowl and mix 6 min/speed 4. Remove butterfly whisk.
  5. Add mascarpone and mix 30-40 sec/speed 3, or until mascarpone is combined.
  6. To assemble, dip reserved savoiardi biscuits into reserved cooled mango and passionfruit mixture to coat. Place 1 soaked biscuit into the bottom of 8 serving glasses, then cover each biscuit with 2 tablespoons of the mascarpone mixture. Top mascarpone mixture with fresh mango pieces. Repeat layering process until all ingredients have been used.
  7. Place glasses into refrigerator and leave to chill for at least 1 hour before serving.

Useful Items

baking tray (30 x 40 cm), baking paper, round cookie cutter (6 cm), 8 serving glasses, oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • All of the elements of this recipe can be made the day before however, it is best to assemble the dessert within 1-2 hours of serving to prevent the biscuits from becoming too soft.
