Peanut caramel slice

Ingredients

  • 260 g roasted peanuts (see Tips)
  • water, for soaking

Base

  • 60 g extra virgin coconut oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 150 g rolled oats
  • 50 g pecans (see Tips)
  • 100 g pitted fresh (Medjool) dates
  • 20 g desiccated coconut
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 2 tsp pure maple syrup

Filling

  • 100 g pitted fresh (Medjool) dates
  • 60 g coconut cream
  • 30 g extra virgin coconut oil
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract
  • 100 g banana

Topping

  • 150 g dark chocolate, broken into pieces
  • 3 tsp grapeseed oil

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh peanuts into it. Cover with water (approx. 1500 g) and set aside to soak for 2-3 hours, or until softened.

Base

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C. Line a large baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper and set aside. Grease and line a slice tin (16 x 26 cm) with baking paper, allowing baking paper to hang over the sides, and set aside.
  2. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh oats and pecans into it. Scatter oats and pecans onto prepared baking tray and bake for 15 minutes (160°C), until lightly toasted. Set aside to cool.
  3. Place cooled oats and pecans into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 6.
  4. Add dates, coconut, cinnamon, oil and syrup and mix 10 sec/speed 6. Transfer mixture into prepared tin and press evenly and firmly into base, smoothing with the back of a spoon. Place into refrigerator. Do not clean mixing bowl.

Filling

  1. Drain peanuts well. Place peanuts into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 6.
  2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and chop 10 sec/speed 7.
  3. Add all remaining filling ingredients and mix 10 sec/speed 6.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix 15-20 sec/speed 8, until almost smooth.
  5. Transfer mixture onto base and spread evenly with aid of spatula. Place into refrigerator while preparing topping. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Topping

  1. Place chocolate into mixing bowl and grate 15 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add grapeseed oil and heat 3 min/60°C/speed 1 or until smooth.
  3. Remove slice from fridge and smooth top evenly with the back of a spoon (if needed). Spread melted chocolate over slice and smooth with aid of spatula. Place into refrigerator until firm (approx. 2 hours) or overnight. Cut into slices to serve.

Useful Items

bowl, baking tray (30 x 40 cm), slice tin (16 x 26 cm), baking paper, colander , oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • You can use activated or roasted peanuts in this recipe. You can also use raw peanuts, however, they will provide a less "caramel" flavour.
  • You can use raw or activated pecans in this recipe (instead of roasted). If using activated, there is no need to bake the pecans with the oats.
  • If you prefer, you can use a square cake tin (21 cm).
