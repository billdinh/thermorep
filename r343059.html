Iranian Barbari

Ingredients

  • 225 g strong white bread flour, plus extra for sprinkling and dusting
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 140 g water, lukewarm
  • 2 tsp dried instant yeast
  • olive oil, for greasing and brushing

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in flour and salt. Remove bowl and stir to combine then make a well in the centre.
  2. Place water and yeast in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 5. Pour yeast-water into well in flour. Sprinkle a little more flour over the centre then leave in a warm place for 15 minutes.
  3. Transfer flour mixture to mixing bowl then knead 10 min/. Meanwhile, grease a large bowl.
  4. Transfer dough to prepared bowl, cover with oiled cling film and leave to rise until doubled in size (45-60 minutes). Meanwhile, line a baking tray with baking paper.
  5. Knock back dough and divide into 6 pieces. On a lightly floured surface, roll each one out to 10 cm x 5 cm, 1 cm thick. Place on prepared baking tray and slash the tops 4 times diagonally.
  6. Cover with oiled cling film and leave to rise again for 20 minutes. Meanwhile, preheat oven to 200°C.
  7. Brush lightly with oil and bake for 12-15 minutes (200°C) until lightly golden.
  8. Serve warm.

Useful Items

cling film, rolling pin, baking tray, baking paper, pastry brush, large bowl, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • A type of Iranian thick flatbread that are traditionally made much larger. These individual breads are easier to make and bake in your oven at home. Best served fresh.
