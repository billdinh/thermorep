Steamed orange puddings with almond milk ice cream

Ingredients

Almond milk ice cream

  • 280 g almond milk (see Tips)
  • 220 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste
  • 20 g honey
  • 140 g raw sugar

Orange puddings

  • 70 g unsalted butter, cut into pieces (2 cm), plus extra for greasing
  • 140 g caster sugar
  • 120 g day old bread
  • 200 g almond milk (see Tips)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 orange, juice and finely grated zest, no white pith
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • 120 g wholemeal flour
  • 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 800 g water

Preparation

Almond milk ice cream

  1. Place all almond milk ice cream ingredients into mixing bowl and cook 5 min/80°C/speed 4.
  2. Transfer into a shallow container (1L capacity) and place into freezer for 4 hours or until firm. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Cut ice cream into pieces, place into mixing bowl and blend 20 sec/speed 9.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix 20 sec/speed 4.
  5. Transfer into a freezable container and place into freezer overnight or until ready to serve. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Orange puddings

  1. Grease 8 x 125 ml ramekins (see Tips) with butter then sprinkle ½ teaspoon caster sugar into each ramekin to coat with sugar.
  2. Tear bread into pieces, place into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 6.
  3. Add almond milk, eggs, orange juice, three-quarters of the zest, cinnamon, butter and remaining 120 g sugar and mix 20 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add flour and bicarbonate of soda and mix 10 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and divide mixture between prepared ramekins.
  5. Place water into mixing bowl. Place ramekins into Varoma dish, place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 25 min/Varoma/speed 2, or until firm and springy (depending on size of ramekins, you may need to steam puddings in batches). If steaming in batches, check water level during cooking process and top up if necessary.
  6. Remove puddings and leave to rest for 5 minutes then turn out onto serving plates. Remove ice cream from freezer and stand for 5 minutes to soften. Sprinkle remaining orange zest over puddings and serve with Almond milk ice cream. 

Useful Items

shallow freezeable container, freezable bowl or container, 8 ramekins (125 ml), serving plates, freezer

Hints & Tricks

  • To make your own almond milk, place 100 g raw almonds and 500 g water into mixing bowl and blend 1 min/speed 10. Strain through a nut-milk bag.
  • Ensure ramekins fit comfortably into Varoma dish so that the lid closes securely and steam can circulate freely.
  • Single (large) orange pudding: To make 1 large pudding, grease and coat a pudding basin (20 cm diameter). Ensure it fits into Varoma dish and lid can close securely. Proceed as per recipe, increasing steaming to 40 min/Varoma/speed 2.
