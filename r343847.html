Baked Salmon Cakes with Corn Salsa

Ingredients

Salmon Cakes

  • 3 green onions, trimmed, cut into pieces
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp whole black peppercorns
  • 11 oz fresh salmon, skinless, boneless, cubed (1 in.)
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 oz panko
  • 1 tbsp capers, drained but not rinsed
  • 1 tsp lemon juice

Corn Salsa

  • 4 oz red onions, cut into pieces
  • 3 oz red bell peppers, de-seeded, de-stemmed, cut into pieces
  • 1 fresh jalapeño chili, de-seeded, de-stemmed
  • 8 oz sweet corn kernels
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • cilantro, to garnish

Preparation

Salmon Cakes

  1. Place green onions, salt and pepper into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8.
  2. Add salmon, egg, panko, capers and lemon juice and mix 5 sec//speed 5. Transfer to a bowl, cover with cling wrap and refrigerate 20 minutes. Meanwhile clean mixing bowl.
  3. Preheat oven to 375°F. Line a baking sheet with parchment and set aside.
  4. Shape reserved salmon mixture into eight small patties (approx. 2 oz ea.). Place onto prepared baking sheet and bake for 7-9 minutes, until cooked through to your liking. Meanwhile make the corn salsa.

Corn Salsa

  1. Place onions, bell peppers and jalapeño into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place corn and salt into mixing bowl and cook 4 min/250°F//speed 0.5.
  3. Add reserved onion mixture and cook 2 min/250°F//speed 0.5.
  4. Divide Corn Salsa between four bowls and garnish with cilantro. Top with 2 salmon cakes and serve immediately.

Useful Items

cling wrap, baking sheet, parchment paper, refrigerator, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with lemon wedges.
