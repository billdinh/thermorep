Rice pudding

Ingredients

  • 200 g Arborio rice, rinsed, drained
  • 780 - 800 g full cream milk
  • 60 g caster sugar
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg (optional)
  • 1 pinch ground cinnamon (optional)
  • 1 banana, cut into slices, to serve (optional)
  • light brown sugar, to serve
  • maple syrup, to serve

Preparation

  1. Place Arborio rice or short grain rice and 750 g of the full cream milk into mixing bowl and cook 30 min/90°C//speed 1.5. Rice should be tender after cooking.
  2. Add caster sugar, natural vanilla extract, eggs, ground nutmeg (optional), ground cinnamon (optional) and remaining 30 - 50 g of the full cream milk and mix 1 min//speed 3.
  3. Pour into a ThermoServer or large bowl and allow to rest for at least 10 minutes prior to serving.
  4. Serve with sliced banana (optional), a dusting of brown sugar and some maple syrup to taste.

Useful Items

ThermoServer or large bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • Chocolate rice pudding: add 1-2 tbsp cocoa powder, to taste in step 2 and proceed as per recipe.
  • Serve with other fruits of choice (e.g. strawberries, mangos).
  • Arborio is the preferred variety of rice for this recipe.
