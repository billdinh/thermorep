Buckwheat mushroom tartlets

Ingredients

Pastry

  • 200 g buckwheat
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 100 g salted butter, cut into pieces
  • 50 g water
  • 1 egg yolk
  • gluten free flour mix, for dusting (see Tips)

Filling

  • 40 g Parmesan cheese, crust removed and cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 300 g Swiss brown mushrooms
  • 1 brown onion, cut into quarters (approx. 130 g)
  • 1 sprig fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 20 g unsalted butter
  • 20 g vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp gluten free flour mix
  • 100 g full cream milk
  • 1 egg
  • 50 g thickened cream
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 pinch grated nutmeg
  • 10 sprigs fresh chives, finely sliced, for garnishing

Preparation

Pastry

  1. Place buckwheat into mixing bowl and mill 1 min/speed 9.
  2. Add salt and butter and mix 5 sec/speed 5.
  3. Add water and egg yolk and knead 30 sec/. Transfer dough onto a floured silicone bread mat or work surface and shape into a ball. Form pastry into a flat disc and wrap in plastic wrap, then place into refrigerator to chill for 30 minutes. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Filling

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C. Set aside 2 x 12-hole muffin trays.
  2. Place Parmesan cheese into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place mushrooms into mixing bowl and chop 1 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a separate bowl and set aside.
  4. Place onion and parsley into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 6.  Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add butter and oil and cook 5 min/100°C/speed 1.
  6. Add reserved mushrooms and cook 5 min/Varoma//speed 1.
  7. Add flour and cook 1 min/100°C//speed 1.
  8. Add milk and cook 3 min/100°C//speed 1. Set aside in mixing bowl to cool for 5 minutes.
  9. Add egg, cream, salt, pepper and nutmeg and combine 5 sec//speed 2. Set aside in mixing bowl.
  10. Remove pastry from refrigerator and roll out thinly between 2 sheets of baking paper (3 mm thickness). Using a round cookie cutter (7 cm), cut out pastry rounds and use rounds to line muffin trays. Re-roll remaining pastry (3 mm thickness) and cut out pastry rounds (7 cm) to line remaining muffin trays (24 pastry rounds in total).
  11. Fill pastry cases with mushroom mixture (approx. 1 tablespoon), then sprinkle with reserved grated Parmesan cheese.
  12. Bake for 15 minutes (200°C), or until puffed and set. Allow to cool in tray before removing (approx. 15 minutes), then garnish with chives and serve warm.

Useful Items

silicone bread mat, plastic wrap, 2 x 12-hole muffin trays, bowl, baking paper, rolling pin, round cookie cutter (7 cm), refrigerator, oven
