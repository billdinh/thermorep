Gluten-Free Date, Apple and Walnut Tea Bread

Ingredients

  • 60 g olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 240 g brown rice
  • 80 g pitted dates
  • 140 g apples, quartered
  • 1 ½ tsp gluten free baking powder
  • 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • ½ tsp xantham gum
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 60 g sugar
  • 80 g walnut pieces
  • 3 large eggs
  • 100 g water

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C and grease a large loaf tin (900 g) with oil then set aside.
  2. Place rice in mixing bowl and grind 2 min/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place dates and apples in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  4. Add oil, reserved ground rice, baking powder, bicarbonate of soda, xantham gum, salt, sugar, walnuts, eggs and water then mix 20 sec/speed 4.
  5. Transfer to prepared tin and bake for approx. 50 minutes (180°C) or until a skewer inserted in the centre comes out clean. Remove loaf from tin and allow to cool on a wire rack.  Slice before serving.

Useful Items

900 g loaf tin (2 lb), cooling rack, skewer, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • The walnuts can be substituted for almonds; chop with dates and apple in step 3.
