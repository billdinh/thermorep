Shepherd's Pie

Ingredients

Ground Beef

  • 1 oz unsalted butter, plus extra to grease
  • 3 ½ oz yellow onions, halved
  • ½ oz fresh parsley,
  • 16 oz ground beef, broken into pieces
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1 tbsp Madeira wine (optional)

Mashed Potatoes

  • 32 oz russet potatoes, sliced (¼ in.)
  • 13 oz whole milk
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper, to taste
  • 3 oz unsalted butter, cut into pieces, divided
  • 2 tbsp bread crumbs (optional)

Preparation

Ground Beef

  1. Grease a baking dish (approx. 9 in. x 9 in. x 4 in.) and set aside.
  2. Place onions and parsley into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add butter and without measuring cup sauté 5 min/250°F/speed 1.
  4. Add ground beef, salt, pepper and Madeira wine, stir with spatula to break up if necessary and cook 3 min/250°F/speed 1. Transfer into prepared baking dish and set aside.

Mashed Potatoes

  1. Insert butterfly whisk. Place potatoes, milk, salt and black pepper into mixing bowl and without measuring cup cook 25-30 min/195°F/speed 1 (until potatoes are soft enough to mash). Meanwhile, preheat oven to 425°F.
  2. Add 2 oz butter, insert measuring cup and mash 30 sec/speed 3. Remove butterfly whisk.
  3. Transfer mashed potatoes to baking dish, spread over cooked ground beef and smooth the surface with a fork. Sprinkle with bread crumbs and dot with 1 oz butter. Bake for 15 min (425°F) or until golden brown. Serve hot.

Useful Items

baking dish (approx. 9 in. x 9 in. x 4 in.), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Check that potatoes are very tender before mashing. If they still seem firm, cook for the additional 5 minutes.
  • Serve with a green salad, steamed green peas or steamed carrots.
  • Add 7 oz carrots (cut in pieces) before chopping with onions and parsley.
  • Instead of raw ground meat, use leftover cooked beef, chopped for a few seconds on speed 5.
