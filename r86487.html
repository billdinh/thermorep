Meatloaf

Ingredients

Meatloaf

  • 20 g olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 120 g onions
  • 70 g bread, stale, soaked and squeezed
  • 800 g mixed minced meat
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp paprika

Sauce

  • water, up to 250 g mark
  • 100 g double cream
  • 2 tsp cornflour
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • ½ tsp paprika

Preparation

Meatloaf

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C and grease a large loaf tin (900 g).
  2. Place onions and oil in mixing bowl then chop 4 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 2.
  3. Add soaked and squeezed out bread roll, minced meat, eggs, salt, pepper and paprika then mix 30 sec//speed 4 while stirring with spatula through hole in mixing bowl lid. Finish mixing with spatula if necessary.
  4. Place mixture in loaf tin and press down with spatula. Bake for 1 hour 10 minutes (180°C). Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl.

Sauce

  1. Once meatloaf is cooked, weigh meat juice from loaf tin into mixing bowl, topping up with water to reach 250 g. Cover meat and set aside.
  2. Add cream, cornflour, salt, pepper and paprika to mixing bowl avoiding the blade, then cook 5 min/90°C/speed 3.
  3. Remove meatloaf from tin and cut in slices. Serve hot with the sauce.

Useful Items

900 g loaf tin (2 lb), aluminium foil, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Mushrooms can be added to the sauce.
  • This recipe can easily be halved.
  • Place 1-2 rosemary sprigs on the meat loaf before baking.
  • Serve with mashed potatoes.
