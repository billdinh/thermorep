Apple and Pear Detox Juice

Ingredients

  • 4 oz ripe pear, cut into pieces (approx. 1 pear)
  • 3 ½ oz apple, cut into pieces (approx. 1 apple)
  • 2 oz fresh spinach leaves
  • 5 ½ oz water, chilled
  • 10 ½ oz freshly squeezed orange juice
  • 3 ice cubes
Nutrition
per 1 glass
Calories
293 kJ / 70 kcal
Protein
1 g
Carbohydrates
14 g
Fat
0 g

Preparation

  1. Place pear, apple, spinach leaves, water, orange juice and ice cubes into mixing bowl and blend 1 min 30 sec/speed 10. Serve immediately.
