Caramelised Onion and Goat's Cheese Tartlets

Ingredients

Caramelised Onions

  • 45 g unsalted butter, diced
  • 300 g red onions, thinly sliced (5 mm)
  • 30 g light brown sugar
  • 20 g balsamic vinegar

Assembly

  • 3 sheets filo pastry
  • 60 g soft goat's cheese, cut in small pieces (1 cm)
  • 2 - 3 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves only

Preparation

Caramelised Onions

  1. Place 20 g butter in mixing bowl and melt 1 min 30 sec/50°C/speed . Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place onions, remaining 25 g butter, sugar and balsamic vinegar in mixing bowl then, with simmering basket in place of measuring cup, cook 20-30 min/120°C//speed  until onions are soft.

Assembly

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Place 1 sheet of filo pastry on a chopping board, keeping the rest covered with a damp tea towel. Brush lightly with reserved melted butter then cut into squares (approx. 6 cm). Press 3 squares of pastry into each hole of a 24 hole mini muffin tin, alternating the angle of each layer. Repeat with remaining pastry. Bake for 3-5 minutes (180°C) until edges are lightly golden.
  2. Add 1-2 tsp caramelised onions to each filo case then top with a piece of goat's cheese and a few thyme leaves. Bake for a further 2-3 minutes (180°C) until goat's cheese is slightly melted. Serve warm or cold.

Useful Items

24 hole mini muffin tin, pastry brush, tea towel, oven
