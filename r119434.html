Mini caramelly cupcakes with coconut cream cheese topping

Ingredients

Coconut cream cheese topping

  • 500 g thick coconut cream (min. 65% coconut cream) (see Tips)
  • 80 g pure maple syrup
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste
  • 1 tbsp agar agar powder (see Tips)

Cupcakes

  • coconut oil spray, for greasing
  • 250 g coconut sugar
  • 120 g light olive oil
  • 400 g gluten free self-raising flour
  • 210 g water
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp gluten free baking powder
  • 1 tsp apple cider vinegar

Preparation

Coconut cream cheese topping

  1. Place a small bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh 40 g of the coconut cream into it. Set aside.
  2. Place all remaining ingredients into mixing bowl and cook 15 min/100°C/speed 4.
  3. Transfer into a separate bowl and place into refrigerator for a minimum of 1 hour or until set. Clean and dry mixing bowl. Meanwhile, prepare cupcakes.

Cupcakes

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C. Line 2 x 24-hole mini muffin trays with mini cupcake liners and spray liberally with coconut oil. Set trays aside.
  2. Place sugar into mixing bowl and mill 1 min/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl and lid with spatula.
  3. Add remaining cupcake ingredients (in the order listed) and mix 20 sec/speed 4 .
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix 20 sec//speed 4. Transfer mixture into prepared cupcake liners to fill them ¾ full. Using damp fingers, smooth top of cupcakes. Bake for 15-18 minutes (160°C), or until they are puffed and golden and a skewer inserted into the centre of the cupcakes comes out clean. Transfer onto a wire rack to cool completely. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Assembly

  1. Cut coconut topping into cubes. Place cubes into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 5.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk . Add reserved 40 g coconut cream and whip 30 sec/speed 4 .
  3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl and whip for a further 30 sec/speed 4 .
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl and whip for a further 30 sec/speed 4 .
  5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl and whip for a further 30 sec/speed 4, or until light and fluffy (see Tips).
  6. Transfer into a piping bag with a decorative nozzle and decorate cupcakes as desired. Serve immediately or transfer into a sealable container and store at room temperature until ready to serve (see Tips).

Useful Items

Hints & Tricks

  • To ensure your coconut cream contains at least 65% coconut cream, check the label.
  • Some coconut products are manufactured with equipment used with dairy products. If dairy allergies are a concern for you, be sure to check the label of your coconut cream.
  • You can buy agar agar powder at health food stores or online.
  • These cupcakes are best on the day they are made; they will keep for up to 24 hours but may dry out a little. If preparing in advance, store in a freezable storage container in the freezer (for up to 1 month). Simply defrost before serving.
  • After step 12, if icing looks like it has split, scrape down the sides of mixing bowl with spatula and heat 2 min/37°C/speed 2, or until icing comes together and is smooth enough to pipe.
