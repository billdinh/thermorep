White chocolate and raspberry self-saucing pudding

Ingredients

Pudding

  • 60 g unsalted butter, plus extra for greasing
  • 80 g white chocolate, broken into pieces
  • 160 g plain flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 80 g caster sugar
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract
  • 120 g full cream milk
  • 1 egg
  • 160 g frozen raspberries

Sauce

  • 80 g white chocolate, broken into pieces
  • 300 g full cream milk
  • 50 g caster sugar
  • 3 tsp cornflour
  • 1100 g water
  • 20 g flaked almonds, toasted, for decorating
  • icing sugar, for decorating
  • 200 g cream, pouring (whipping), for serving (optional)

Preparation

Pudding

  1. Grease a round ceramic dish (18 x 6 cm) and set aside.
  2. Place chocolate into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a small bowl and set aside.
  3. Place butter into mixing bowl and melt 2 min/100°C/speed 2.
  4. Add flour, baking powder, sugar, vanilla, milk, egg and reserved grated chocolate and mix 5 sec/speed 4.
  5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix 3 sec/speed 4.
  6. Add raspberries and gently combine with aid of spatula. Transfer into prepared dish and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Sauce

  1. Place chocolate and milk into mixing bowl and cook 2 min/100°C/speed 2, until mixture is smooth.
  2. Add sugar and cornflour and mix 5 sec/speed 4.
  3. Pour sauce over the back of a spoon onto reserved pudding. Cover pudding with a piece of wet, well-wrung baking paper. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position and place pudding into it. Secure Varoma lid and steam 45-50 min/Varoma/speed 1, or until a wooden skewer inserted into the centre of the cake comes out clean.
  5. Sprinkle with almonds and dust with icing sugar, then serve warm with cream (optional).
