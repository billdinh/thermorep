Strawberry Yoghurt with Chocolate Chips

Ingredients

  • 100 g dark chocolate, small pieces or callets
  • 300 g fresh strawberries, halved
  • 200 g sugar
  • 1000 g whole milk
  • 30 g milk powder
  • 120 g plain yoghurt (full fat with live cultures)

Preparation

  1. Place chocolate in mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 9. Transfer to a small bowl and set aside.
  2. Place strawberries and sugar in mixing bowl then, with simmering basket in place of measuring cup on mixing bowl lid, cook 15 min/120°C/speed 1. Divide between 10 sterilised jars then allow to cool completely (approx. 30 minutes). Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl.
  3. Once strawberry mixture has cooled, place milk, milk powder and yoghurt in mixing bowl then heat 4 min/50°C/speed 3. Divide between jars, seal then wrap jars in tea towels and set aside for 12 hours. Store in fridge until ready to serve.
  4. Serve with reserved grated chocolate scattered over the top.

Useful Items

sterilised jam jars, tea towels, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • The ideal temperature to ferment yoghurt is about 43°C. If you have an airing cupboard, this is the perfect place to leave the yoghurt to set.
