Pistachio Almond Twists

Ingredients

  • 490 g quick puff pastry, homemade (1 batch)
  • 250 g marzipan, homemade
  • 30 g pistachio nuts, unsalted
  • 30 g blanched almonds
  • 30 g light brown sugar
  • plain flour, for dusting
  • 1 medium egg, beaten
  • 1 Tbsp icing sugar, for dusting

Preparation

  1. Make 1 batch quick puff pastry (see page XX) and marzipan (see page XX).
  2. Preheat oven to 180°C and line 2 large baking trays with baking paper.
  3. Place pistachios, almonds and sugar in mixing bowl then chop 3-4 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a small bowl and set aside.
  4. Cut chilled pastry in half then, on a lightly floured surface, roll each piece into a square (approx. 30 cm x 30 cm).
  5. Roll out marzipan to same size as one piece of pastry.
  6. Brush one square of pastry with beaten egg then sprinkle over  ⅓ reserved nut mixture. Lay rolled out marzipan over nuts then brush with reserved beaten egg. Sprinkle with ⅓  reserved nut mixture.
  7. Lay second square of pastry on top of marzipan layer, brush with reserved beaten egg, then trim edges to form a neat square (28 cm x 28 cm).
  8. Cut square into 8 strips, 3.5 cm wide. Twist each strip 3 to 4 times, then transfer to prepared baking trays. Brush for a final time with reserved beaten egg then scatter over remaining nut mixture.
  9. Bake for 15-18 minutes (180°C) until puffed and golden. Leave to cool for 5 minutes then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
  10. Serve dusted with icing sugar.

Useful Items

2 large baking trays, baking paper, rolling pin, long sharp knife, pastry brush, wire rack, oven

Hints & Tricks

This sweet, nutty pastry is lovely served with a hot drink. And there’s something extra satisfying about making your own pastry and marzipan.
