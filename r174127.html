White Bread Rolls

Ingredients

  • 2 oz unsalted butter, chilled, plus extra to grease
  • 23 oz bread flour, plus extra to dust
  • 1 - 2 tsp salt, to taste
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 1 ½ tsp rapid rise yeast
  • 14 oz whole milk
  • 1 large egg

Preparation

  1. Line a baking sheet with parchment and grease a large bowl with butter and set aside.
  2. Place flour, salt, sugar and yeast into mixing bowl and mix 5 sec/speed 6.
  3. Add butter and chop Turbo/1 sec/3 times.
  4. Add milk and egg and mix 15 sec/speed 4 then knead 4 min/. Transfer dough into prepared bowl, cover with cling wrap and let rise in a warm place until doubled in size (approx. 2 hr.).
  5. Transfer dough to a lightly floured surface and roll out to a thickness of ¾ inch, with a rolling pin. Divide dough into twelve equal pieces and form each into a long roll (approx. 6 in.).
  6. Place rolls onto prepared baking sheet, leaving ¼ inch space between them. Cover with greased cling wrap or damp kitchen towel and let rise again in a warm place for 1 hour or until doubled in size. Towards the end of resting time, preheat oven to 425ºF.
  7. Dust rolls with flour and bake for 5 minutes (425ºF). Lower oven temperature to 400ºF and bake a further 5-10 minutes (400ºF) until golden brown. Cool on a wire rack before serving.

Useful Items

parchment paper, large bowl, rolling pin, cling wrap, wire rack, baking sheet (approx. 11 in. x 17 in.), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • For a soft crust, cover rolls with a kitchen towel as soon as you remove them from the oven.
  • This simple white bread recipe can be used for any occasion.
  • This dough can also be shaped into 12 round rolls, perfect for school lunches, picnics and barbecues.
