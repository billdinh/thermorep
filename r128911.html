Leek quiche

Ingredients

Pastry

  • 300 g plain flour
  • ½ tsp fine salt
  • 150 g butter, cold, diced, plus extra for greasing
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 50 g water, ice cold

Filling

  • 200 g Gouda cheese or any other medium-hard cheese, cut into pieces
  • 150 g cooked ham, cut into pieces
  • 200 g double cream
  • 4 eggs
  • ¼ tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 ½ tsp fine salt
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • 400 g leeks, sliced in rounds (5 mm)

Preparation

Pastry

  1. Place flour, salt and butter in mixing bowl then chop 10 sec/speed 6.
  2. Add egg yolk and water then pulse Turbo/1 sec/2 times until just pulling together; if the dough is too dry add 1 tsp more water at a time with a Turbo pulse after each addition. Tip out onto a large piece of cling film and form into a flat disc about 1 cm thick.
  3. Wrap in cling film and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Preheat oven to 180°C and grease a fluted tart tin (Ø 28 cm).
  5. Roll out pastry and press into tart tin. Prick pastry base with a fork, line with baking paper and baking weights (e. g. dried beans). Bake blind for 15 minutes (180°C) or until golden. Remove baking weights and bake for a further 5 minutes (180°C). Meanwhile prepare filling.

Filling

  1. Place gouda cheese in mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 5.
  2. Add ham, double cream, eggs, pepper, salt and nutmeg in mixing bowl then mix 15 sec//speed 4.
  3. Spread leeks evenly over base of pastry case. Pour ham and cheese mixture over leeks in tart tin and bake 35 minutes (180°C). Serve hot, cut in slices.

Useful Items

cling film, fluted tart tin (Ø 28 cm), rolling pin, baking paper, baking beans, refrigerator, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • As a starter, the quiche serves 8.
    To serve 8 for a main course, the recipe can be doubled. Use a deeper tart tin and precook the leeks by steaming in the Varoma dish before arranging in the tart tin.
  • The ham can be substituted with bacon cubes.
