Panna Cotta with Whiskey Cream

Ingredients

  • 11 ½ oz organic heavy whipping cream, divided
  • 2 oz sugar, divided
  • 3 oz dark chocolate (50% cocoa)
  • 1 tsp unflavored gelatin powder
  • 4 oz cream cheese, divided
  • 4 ½ oz quark cheese, divided (see Tip)
  • 1 tsp unflavored gelatin powder (sprinkle avoiding the blade top)
  • 3 oz whiskey cream liqueur
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1952 kJ / 467 kcal
Protein
4 g
Carbohydrates
30 g

36 g

Preparation

  1. Place 4.5 oz cream, 1 oz sugar and chocolate into mixing bowl and heat 2 min 30 sec/140°F/speed 2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add 1 tsp gelatin (sprinkle avoiding the blade top) and mix 30 sec/140°F/speed 3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, including blades.
  3. Add 2 oz cream cheese and 2 oz quark cheese and mix 30 sec/speed 3. Divide the mixture into 6 dessert glasses to make a layer of chocolate panna cotta. Chill in refrigerator until set (approx. 30 minutes). Clean mixing bowl.
  4. Place 7 oz heavy whipping cream and 1 oz sugar into mixing bowl and whip 2 min 30 sec/140°F/speed 2.
  5. Add remaining 1 tsp gelatin (sprinkle avoiding the blade top) and mix 30 sec/140°F/speed 2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, including blades.
  6. Add 2 oz cream cheese, 2.5 oz quark cheese and whiskey cream liqueur and mix 30 sec/speed 3.5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix a further 15 sec/speed 4. Divide the mixture into the 6 dessert glasses to make second layer of panna cotta cream and chill in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours. Serve each glass decorated to your liking.

Useful Items

dessert glasses (6 oz each), refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • You can decorate with chocolate macarons, sprinkled with grated chocolate or toasted and puffed rice.
  • Quark cheese can be replaced with Cloumage Cheese®, Fromage Frais, mascarpone, sour cream or crème fraiche.
