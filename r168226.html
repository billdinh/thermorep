Semolina cake (namoura)

Ingredients

Semolina cake

  • 50 g butter, cut in pieces, plus extra for greasing
  • 200 g sugar
  • 400 g yoghurt
  • 450 g medium wheat semolina
  • 150 g fine semolina
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Syrup

  • 100 g water
  • 300 g sugar
  • 1 tsp blossom water
  • 1 tsp rose water (optional)
  • 1 tsp lemon juice, freshly squeezed

Assembly and baking

  • 12 blanched almonds

Preparation

Semolina cake

  1. Grease a baking dish (32 cm x 22 cm x 4-6 cm) and set aside.
  2. Place sugar into mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 9.
  3. Add yoghurt and mix 20 sec/speed 4.
  4. Add medium semolina and fine semolina and mix 10 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add butter and baking powder and mix 1 min/speed 3. Transfer into prepared baking dish, spread evenly, cover with a tea towel and set aside for 1 hour. Meanwhile prepare syrup.

Syrup

  1. Place water, sugar, blossom water, rose water (if using) and lemon juice into mixing bowl and cook 10 min/115°C/speed 2. Transfer into a heat-resistant bowl and set aside to cool.

Assembly and baking

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Score cake into 12 pieces (squares or diamonds) and place a blanched almond in the centre of each piece. Bake for 25 minutes (180°C) or until golden brown. Pour cooled syrup onto hot cake, place back into oven and bake another 2 minutes. Set aside to cool before serving.

Useful Items

tea towel, heat-resistant bowl, rectangular baking dish (32 cm x 22 cm x 4-6 cm), oven
