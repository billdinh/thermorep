Stuffed jacket potatoes (two ways)

Ingredients

Jacket potatoes

  • 6 potatoes or sweet potatoes (medium-large)

Chipotle mince filling

  • 120 g tasty cheese, cut into pieces (3-4 cm)
  • 500 g water
  • 500 g beef mince
  • 1 brown onion, cut into halves
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 carrot, cut into pieces
  • 3 tbsp chipotle chillies in adobo sauce (including 3 chillies - see Tips)
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 130 g barbecue sauce
  • 40 g extra virgin olive oil
  • ½ red capsicum, cut into pieces (1 cm)

Broccoli and cheese filling (vegetarian)

  • 120 g tasty cheese, cut into pieces (3-4 cm)
  • 500 g water
  • 200 g broccoli, cut into florets
  • 1 brown onion, cut into halves
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • ½ red capsicum, cut into halves
  • 40 g oil
  • 250 g cream cheese, cut into pieces
  • 3 tsp wholegrain mustard
  • 1 - 2 pinches salt
  • 1 - 2 pinches ground black pepper

Preparation

Jacket potatoes

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Prick potatoes all over with a fork and wrap individually in aluminium foil. Place onto a baking tray and bake for 45-60 minutes (180°C) or until cooked (see Tips). While potatoes are baking, continue with recipe to make filling of choice (Chipotle mince or Broccoli and cheese).

Chipotle mince filling

  1. Place cheese into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place water into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket, weigh mince into it and cook 10 min/100°C/speed 1.
  3. Stir mince with aid of spatula and cook for a further 10 min/100°C/speed 1.
  4. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula, transfer cooked mince into a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  5. Place onion, garlic, carrot, chipotle chillies, salt, barbecue sauce and oil into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7.
  6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and cook 3 min/100°C/speed 2.
  7. Add capsicum and reserved mince, then combine 5 sec//speed 2. Transfer into a bowl and set aside until needed.

Broccoli and cheese filling (vegetarian)

  1. Place cheese into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place water into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket, weigh broccoli into it and cook 10 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula, transfer broccoli into a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Place onion, garlic, capsicum and oil into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7.
  5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and cook 3 min/100°C/speed 1.
  6. Add reserved broccoli and chop 2 sec//speed 4.
  7. Add cream cheese, mustard, salt and pepper and combine 5 sec//speed 2. Transfer into a separate bowl and set aside until needed.

Assembly

  1. Using tongs, carefully unwrap cooked potatoes. Line baking tray with foil and place potatoes back onto prepared tray. Cut potatoes in half lengthways (do not cut all the way through) or scoop out a hole in the top. Fill with prepared filling of choice, then top with reserved cheese and bake for 10 minutes (180°C) or until cheese melts. Serve while warm.

Useful Items

bowl, baking tray, aluminium foil, tongs, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • You can find chipotle chillies in adobo sauce at specialty food stores and at some major supermarkets in the international food aisle.
  • Baking time for potatoes may vary, depending on their size.
  • To feed a large crowd, bake up to 12 potatoes and make a batch of both the Chipotle mince filling and the Broccoli and cheese filling.
