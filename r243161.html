Tamales Oaxaqueños

Ingredients

  • 184 oz water, divided
  • 15 - 20 banana leaves, trimmed (6 in. x 10 in.)
  • 5 oz carrot, cut into pieces (1 in.)
  • 3 oz celery, cut into pieces (1 in.)
  • 4 ½ oz onion, cut into pieces (1 in.)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 17 oz whole chicken breasts, boneless, skinless, cut into pieces (approx. 1 in.)
  • 8 &frac15; oz authentic Mexican mole sauce, jarred
  • 4 ½ oz vegetable shortening
  • 17 ½ oz masa harina
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 3 tsp salt, to taste

Preparation

  1. Place 53 oz water into mixing bowl and boil 10 min/speed 2. Meanwhile trim banana leaves (6 in. x 10 in.) and place banana leaves in a casserole dish and pour boiled water over to soak.
  2. Place 53 oz water, carrots, celery, onions and garlic into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position and weigh in chicken, secure Varoma lid and cook 22 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove Varoma and place onto inverted Varoma lid, strain broth with simmering basket into a bowl and discard vegetables.
  3. Place steamed chicken into mixing bowl and shred 4 sec//speed 4.
  4. Add 10.5 oz reserved chicken broth and mole sauce and cook 15 min/175°F//speed 2. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  5. Insert butterfly whisk. Place shortening into mixing bowl and whip 3 min/speed 3.
  6. Remove butterfly whisk. Add masa harina, 25 oz water, baking powder and salt and mix 2 min/speed 3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix a further 2 min/speed 3. Transfer to a bowl, cover and set aside.
  7. Drain leaves, discard water and dry leaves with paper towels. Place 3 oz prepared dough in the center of each banana leaf and spread outward and evenly into a rectangle (approx. 8 in. x 4 in.). Place 2 oz reserved chicken mixture in the center of each. Using the bottom half of the banana leaf fold the dough in half upward and away from you. Fold both sides towards the center while pressing the filling into the center. Place tamales into Varoma open side up and seam side down and set aside.
  8. Place 53 oz water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 75 min/Varoma/speed 1. Let rest in the Varoma for 20 minutes. Serve hot.

Useful Items

casserole dish, paper towels

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with sour cream and queso.
  • Banana leaves may be replaced with corn husks. To prepare corn husks: boil a large pot of water, add corn husks and turn off heat. Let soak for 1 hour. Pat dry with paper towels before using.
