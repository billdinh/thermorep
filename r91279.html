Cheese Crackers

Ingredients

  • 7 oz Edam cheese, cut into pieces
  • 7 oz all-purpose flour
  • 4 oz unsalted butter, chilled, cut into pieces
  • 1 large egg
  • ½ - 1 tsp salt, to taste
  • cumin seeds, for sprinkling (optional)
  • sesame seeds, for sprinkling (optional)
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
164 kJ / 39 kcal
Protein
1 g
Carbohydrates
2 g
Fat
3 g

Preparation

  1. Place cheese into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 7.
  2. Add flour, butter, egg and salt and mix 30 sec/speed 4.
  3. Remove dough from mixing bowl, place on a long sheet of cling wrap and shape a thin log (approx. 16 in. x 1½ in.). Wrap log in cling wrap and refrigerate until firm enough to cut into slices (approx. 2 hr.).
  4. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line a baking sheet (approx. 16 in. x 14 in.) with parchment and set aside.
  5. Cut dough log into thin slices (⅛ in.), place slices onto prepared baking sheet, sprinkle with cumin and/or sesame seeds and bake for 10-15 minutes (350°F) until golden brown. Allow to cool on a wire rack before serving or storing in an airtight container.

Useful Items

cling wrap, parchment paper, baking sheet (approx. 16 in. x 14 in.), wire rack, refrigerator, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve cheese crackers with beer or wine.
  • The dough can be prepared a day or two in advance, or frozen for several weeks, sliced and baked shortly before serving.
  • Replace Edam with any strong-flavored hard or semi-hard cheese (e.g. cheddar).
  • Omit salt in dough and sprinkle with coarse salt before baking.
  • Grate an additional 2 oz cheese, separately, and reserve for sprinkling on crackers before baking.
  • For Cheese Straws: instead of shaping dough into a log for slicing, form into a flattened ball and wrap in cling wrap. Refrigerate for 1 hour, then roll out dough (⅛ in. thick) between 2 sheets of parchment paper and cut strips (approx. 4 in. x ½ in.) with a fluted pastry wheel. Bake as directed by the recipe.
  • You can make these crackers gluten-free by substituting 7 oz flour with 7 oz of gluten-free flour.
