Seriously Seedy Crackers

Ingredients

  • 9 oz bread flour, plus extra to dust
  • 1 oz sunflower seeds
  • 1 oz pumpkin seeds
  • 1 oz brown flaxseeds
  • ½ oz sesame seeds
  • ½ oz poppy seeds
  • 2 oz olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3 ½ oz water
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
222 kJ / 53 kcal
Protein
2 g
Carbohydrates
6 g
Fat
3 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F. Line 2 baking sheets with parchment paper and set aside.
  2. Place all ingredients into mixing bowl and mix 15 sec/speed 3, then knead 1 min 30 sec/.
  3. Transfer dough onto a lightly floured work surface and bring together. Halve the dough and roll out each half very thinly, (approx. 1/16 in.). Cut into squares with a knife or into circles using a pastry cutter (2 in.) and place on baking sheets.
  4. Bake for 20-25 minutes (325°F) until lightly golden. Allow to cool on baking sheets, and serve or store in an airtight container.

Useful Items

parchment paper, airtight container, rolling pin, baking sheets (approx. 16 in. x 14 in.), pastry cutter, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Make up your own blend of seeds or add some grated Parmesan cheese for added flavor.
