Warm Caramel Spice Martini

Ingredients

Cinnamon Spice Mix

  • 1 pinch ground cardamom
  • ¾ tsp ground nutmeg
  • ¾ tsp ground cloves
  • 1 tbsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 ½ tsp ground ginger

Spiced Caramel Syrup

  • 7 oz raw sugar
  • 2 pinches baking soda
  • 2 pinches salt
  • 8 ½ oz water

Martini

  • 3 oz water
  • 1 ½ oz rum
  • 2 tsp ouzo
  • 1 ½ tsp lemon juice (approx. ½ lemon)
  • cocktail cherries, to garnish
  • orange zest, to garnish (optional)

Preparation

Cinnamon Spice Mix

  1. Place all spice mix ingredients into a sealable jar or container, seal, shake gently to combine and set aside.

Spiced Caramel Syrup

  1. Place 2 tsp cinnamon spice mix and all remaining syrup ingredients into mixing bowl and without measuring cup heat 5 min/250°F/speed 1. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and without measuring cup, cook a further 25 min/212°F/speed 1. Transfer syrup into a sealable storage jar and set aside to cool (approx. 30 minutes).

Martini

  1. Place 3 oz of spiced caramel syrup into mixing bowl and heat 1 min/140°F/speed 1.
  2. Add water, rum, ouzo and lemon juice, and mix 10 sec/speed 3.
  3. Divide mixture between 2 martini glasses and garnish with 1-2 cherries and a twist of orange zest before serving.

Useful Items

martini glasses, sealable storage jar

Hints & Tricks

  • Use leftover Cinnamon Spice Mix in pancake or waffle batters, or to flavor baked pastries or mashed sweet potatoes.
  • The Spiced Caramel Syrup (19.5 oz) can be prepared ahead of time and kept in a sealable storage jar or container in a dark place for 1-2 weeks. Use leftover syrup on ice cream or in baking.
