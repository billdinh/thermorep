Fresh coconut chutney

Ingredients

  • 115 g fresh coconut flesh (see Tips)
  • 50 g chickpea (besan) flour
  • 50 g raw unsalted peanuts
  • 3 fresh long green chillies, trimmed and cut into halves
  • ½ tsp garlic paste (see Tips)
  • 5 sprigs fresh coriander, stalks and leaves
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp ghee (see Tips)
  • ½ tsp black mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 15 fresh curry leaves

Preparation

  1. Place coconut, chickpea (besan) flour, peanuts, chillies, garlic paste, coriander and salt into mixing bowl and blend 1 min/speed 8 or to a paste. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Do not clean mixing bowl.
  2. Place ghee into mixing bowl and heat 1 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Add mustard and cumin seeds and heat 30 sec/Varoma/speed 1.
  4. Add curry leaves and cook 30 sec/Varoma/speed 1. Transfer into bowl with coconut paste, stir to combine, then serve.

Hints & Tricks

  • You can use fresh or frozen coconut flesh in this recipe. Fresh coconut flesh freezes well and can also be purchased pre-frozen in most Asian stores.
  • To make your own garlic paste and ghee, please refer to the recipes on this recipe chip.
  • Try serving this chutney with Rava Idli or Vada, please refer to the recipes on this recipe chip.
