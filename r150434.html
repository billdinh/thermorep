Poached chicken with liquid stock

Ingredients

  • 180 g brown onions
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1500 g water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 2 - 3 sprigs mixed fresh herbs, leaves and stems (i.e. rosemary, oregano, thyme, mint, basil, sage)
  • 500 g skinless chicken thigh fillets, cut into pieces (6-8 cm)

Preparation

  1. Place onion and garlic into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 3.
  2. Add water, salt, pepper and herbs to mixing bowl and cook 8 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Insert simmering basket, weigh chicken into it and cook 8-10 min/90°C/speed 2. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula, carefully transfer chicken into a bowl and set aside to cool. Place simmering basket over a jug and strain liquid stock into it. Set aside to cool. Serve chicken as desired (see Tips) and reserve stock to use in future recipes.

Useful Items

bowl, jug

Hints & Tricks

  • Shredded chicken: Place cooled, drained chicken into mixing bowl and shred 4 sec/ /speed 4. Serve as desired.
  • You can reduce this recipe by using only 200 g chicken and 1200 g water. All remaining ingredient amounts remain the same. Proceed as per recipe, poaching chicken 8 min/100°C/speed 2.
  • Try seasoning your chicken with the Peri peri spice mix or the Taco seasoning before placing into simmering basket (please refer to seasoning recipes on this recipe chip).
  • Liquid chicken stock is a great base for soups, stews, even bread (simply replace the water with liquid stock). You can store liquid stock in the refrigerator for up to to 2 days or in the freezer for up to 1 month.
  • Serve poached chicken with your favourite steamed vegetables and mash or shred the chicken and add to salads and wraps (see the variation)
  • Add a variety of ingredients (such as chilli or ginger) to your poaching liquid to give it an extra flavour boost.
