Buckwheat Crêpes

Ingredients

Crêpes

  • 550 g almond milk
  • 200 g Gruyère cheese, cut in pieces (2 cm) (optional)
  • 170 g buckwheat flour
  • 10 g chia seeds
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • vegetable oil, for frying

Assembly

  • 16 slices cooked ham
  • 8 medium eggs
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1000 kJ / 239 kcal
Protein
18 g
Carbohydrates
16 g

11 g

Preparation

Crêpes

  1. Place a jug on mixing bowl lid, weigh in 350 g almond milk then set aside.
  2. Place Gruyère (if using) in mixing bowl and grate 8 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place buckwheat flour and chia seeds in mixing bowl then grind 30 sec/speed 10.
  4. Add salt and 200 g almond milk then mix 1 min/speed 3.
  5. Insert butterfly whisk. Without measuring cup, mix 1 min/speed 3 while slowly adding reserved almond milk through hole in mixing bowl lid. Transfer to a jug.
  6. Heat oil in a non-stick frying pan over a medium heat. Pour in a ladle of batter then tilt pan to completely cover the base. Cook for 2-3 minutes or until edges are lightly browned, then turn over and cook for a further 1 minute. Transfer crêpe to a large plate and repeat with remaining batter to make 8 crêpes.

Assembly

  1. Heat oil in 2 non-stick frying pans. Place 1 crêpe in a frying pan over a low heat, place 2 slices ham on top then sprinkle with a little grated Gruyère (if using). Meanwhile, crack an egg into the other pan and fry gently until cooked as desired. Place fried egg on top of the crêpe and fold in on all 4 sides to form a square, then transfer to a serving plate. Repeat with remaining crêpes, ham, eggs and Gruyère (if using).
  2. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

non-stick frying pans, jug, ladle, hob
