Three Course Meal for 2 - Vegetable Soup, Chicken Pie with Rice & Steamed Apple

Ingredients

Flavored Bread Crumbs

  • 2 oz bread roll
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 5 sprigs fresh cilantro

Three Courses

  • 9 oz carrots, cut into pieces (1 in.), divided
  • 9 oz zucchini, cut into pieces (1 in.), divided
  • 3 ½ oz leek, white part only, sliced, divided
  • 18 16 - 24 oz boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into strips (4 in. x ¾ in.)
  • 2 tsp salt, divided
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 2 sprigs fresh thyme (optional)
  • 1 apple, cut into eighths
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 lemon, grated zest only
  • 5 oz yellow onions, halved
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 oz olive oil
  • 1 dried bay leaf
  • 23 oz water
  • 4 oz long-grain rice

Béchamel Sauce

  • 9 oz milk
  • 1 oz all-purpose flour
  • ½ oz unsalted butter
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg

Preparation

Flavored Bread Crumbs

  1. Place bread roll, garlic and fresh cilantro into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Rinse mixing bowl.

Three Courses

  1. Place 4 oz carrots, 4 oz zucchini and 1.5 oz leek into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 4. Transfer into Varoma dish.
  2. Place chicken into Varoma dish on top of vegetables and season with 1 tsp salt, pepper and thyme. Set aside.
  3. Place apple, sugar, cinnamon and lemon zest on a sheet of parchment, fold parchment to close package tightly, place onto Varoma tray and set aside.
  4. Place onions, garlic and oil into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  5. Add bay leaf and sauté 5 min/250°F//speed .
  6. Remove bay leaf, add 5 oz carrots, 5 oz zucchini, 2 oz leek, water and 1 tsp salt. Insert simmering basket, weigh rice into it and mix well with spatula to wet all rice grains. Insert Varoma tray into Varoma dish, place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and cook 20 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove Varoma and remove simmering basket with spatula and set aside. Keep rice warm until ready to serve.
  7. Insert measuring cup and blend 1 min/speed 5-9, increasing speed gradually. Transfer vegetable soup into a bowl and keep warm.

Béchamel Sauce

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Place milk, flour, butter, salt, pepper and nutmeg into mixing bowl and cook 5 min/195°F/speed 4. Place reserved steamed chicken and vegetables into a casserole dish, cover with béchamel sauce and sprinkle with reserved flavored bread crumbs. Bake for approximately 20 minutes (350°F) or until brown. Serve vegetable soup as a starter, followed by baked chicken and vegetable casserole with rice and finish with steamed apple for dessert.

Useful Items

parchment paper, casserole dish, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Steamed cinnamon apple can be served with vanilla or any other flavor of ice cream.
  • Zucchini, carrots and leek can be replaced with the same weight of other vegetables, such as broccoli.
  • Replace chicken breasts with turkey breasts or white fish fillets.
