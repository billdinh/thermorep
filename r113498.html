Chicken Burger with Yoghurt Sauce

Ingredients

Burger

  • 30 g bread, torn in pieces
  • 1 - 2 sprigs fresh rosemary, leaves only, to taste
  • 1 - 2 garlic cloves, to taste
  • 500 g chicken breasts, skinless, cut in pieces (2-3 cm)
  • 1 large egg
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 - 2 pinches ground black pepper, to taste

Yoghurt Sauce

  • 125 g plain yoghurt
  • 5 fresh chives, finely snipped (2 mm)
  • 1 Tbsp lemon juice
  • ½ tsp grated lemon zest (optional)

Preparation

Burger

  1. Place bread in mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place rosemary and garlic in mixing bowl then chop 10 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add chicken and mince 10 sec/speed 7.
  4. Add egg, reserved breadcrumbs, salt and pepper then mix 20 sec/speed 4.
  5. Line a plate with baking paper. Divide mixture into 4 pieces then, using wet hands, form burgers (Ø 10-12 cm) and place on lined plate. Cover with cling film and refrigerate for 30 minutes. Clean mixing bowl and prepare yoghurt sauce.

Yoghurt Sauce

  1. Place yoghurt, chives, lemon juice and lemon zest (if using) in mixing bowl then mix 20 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a small bowl, cover with cling film and refrigerate until needed.

To Serve

  1. Towards end of chilling time, preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Transfer burgers to a baking tray and bake for 15-20 minutes (180°C) until cooked through. Serve topped with yoghurt sauce in a bun of your choice.

Useful Items

large plate, cling film, baking paper, baking tray, refrigerator, oven
