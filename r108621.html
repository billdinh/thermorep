Gluten free carrot and almond cake

Ingredients

Almond and carrot cake

  • 100 g grapeseed oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 220 g blanched almonds
  • 300 g carrot, cut into pieces
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract
  • 3 eggs
  • 150 g light brown sugar
  • 2 tsp gluten free baking powder
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 2 tsp ground ginger
  • 1 pinch salt

Frosting

  • 120 g raw sugar
  • ½ orange, zest only, no white pith
  • 90 g unsalted butter
  • 125 g cream cheese

Decoration

  • 1 tbsp walnuts
  • 1 tbsp shelled unsalted pistachio nuts
  • almonds, for decorating
  • hazelnuts, without skin, for decorating
  • pecans, for decorating

Preparation

Almond and carrot cake

  1. Preheat oven to 170°C. Grease and line a cake tin (20 cm) and set aside.
  2. Place almonds into mixing bowl and mill 5-10 sec/speed 7 or to a fine powder consistency. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place carrot into mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 6.
  4. Add all remaining ingredients, including reserved almond meal, and mix 10 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Pour into prepared tin and bake for 45-60 minutes (170°C) or until a wooden skewer inserted into the centre of the cake comes out clean. Allow cake to cool for 30 minutes in tin, then transfer onto a wire rack to cool completely. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Frosting

  1. Place sugar and orange zest into mixing bowl and mill 50 sec/speed 10.
  2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mill 50 sec/speed 10.
  3. Add butter and chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  4. Add cream cheese and mix 25 sec/speed 5. Spread frosting onto completely cooled cake, then place into refrigerator to set for 30 minutes. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Decoration

  1. Place walnuts and pistachios into mixing bowl and chop 8 sec/speed 4 . Remove cake from refrigerator and decorate with chopped nuts and whole almonds, hazelnuts and pecans before serving.

Useful Items

round cake tin (20 cm), baking paper, bowl, wooden skewer, wire rack, oven, refrigerator
