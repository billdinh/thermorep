Chocolate Orange Mousse

Ingredients

  • 7 oz dark chocolate, minimum 60% cocoa, chips or pieces
  • 7 oz organic heavy whipping cream
  • 1 tbsp orange liqueur (e.g. Cointreau® or Grand Marnier®)
  • 4 large eggs, separated
  • 2 oz sugar
  • 1 orange peel, cut into thin peelings, to serve

Preparation

  1. Place chocolate into mixing bowl and grind 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add 2 oz whipping cream and mix 10 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and melt 4 min/120°F/speed 2.
  3. Add remaining 5 oz whipping cream, liqueur and egg yolks and mix 15 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Thoroughly clean and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Place sugar into mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 10.
  5. Insert butterfly whisk. Add egg whites and whisk 3 min 30 sec/speed 4 until stiff peaks form. Add ⅓ whisked egg whites to chocolate mixture and fold in with spatula. Add remaining whisked egg whites and fold in with spatula.
  6. Pour into 6 serving glasses and chill in the refrigerator until set (approx. 2 hours). Serve garnished with thin pieces of orange peel.

Useful Items

serving glasses or bowls, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • An easy, luxurious chocolate mousse recipe with a hint of orange flavor.
  • This mousse is so rich, you could make 12 small servings instead of 6 large servings.
  • Omit the liqueur for a non-alcoholic version.
  • If English double cream (48% milk fat) is available you can use it to replace organic heavy whipping cream. This cream has more fat for a thicker and richer mousse.
