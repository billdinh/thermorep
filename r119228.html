Bliss ball rocket skewers

Ingredients

  • 100 g shredded coconut
  • 70 g sunflower seeds
  • 200 g dried apricots
  • 1 tbsp water
  • 20 - 30 g dark coconut nectar syrup (see Tips)
  • 1 tsp coconut oil
  • ½ fresh pineapple
  • ½ watermelon
  • ½ rockmelon
  • 16 strawberries, cut into rounds (5 mm)
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
622 kJ / 148 kcal
Protein
2.9 g
Carbohydrates

Preparation

  1. Place coconut into mixing bowl and mill 7 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place sunflower seeds, apricots, water, coconut nectar, coconut oil and 50 g of the reserved milled coconut into mixing bowl and mix 20 sec/speed 6.
  3. Roll 2 teaspoons of mixture into a ball, then coat in reserved milled coconut and place into a sealable container. Repeat with remaining mixture (approx. 32 balls). Transfer into refrigerator until ready to use.
  4. Place pineapple face down and cut into slices (1.5 cm). Remove skin and discard. Cut approx. 4-5 triangles from half of the slices. Cut remaining slices into approx. 3-4 rectangles. Make a small hole in the centre of the rectangle, then make 2 diagonal cuts to the edges of rectangle (to cut out a triangle shape). Set aside small triangles to use in a future recipe.
  5. Place watermelon face down and cut into slices (1.5 cm). Cut approx. 4-5 triangles from half of the slices. Cut remaining slices into approx. 3-4 rectangles. Make a small hole in the centre of the rectangle, then make 2 diagonal cuts to the edges of rectangle (to cut out a triangle shape). Set aside small triangles to use in a future recipe.
  6. Place rockmelon face down and cut into slices (1.5 cm). Cut approx. 4-5 triangles from half of the slices. Cut remaining slices into approx. 3-4 rectangles. Make a small hole in the centre of the rectangle, then make 2 diagonal cuts to the edges of rectangle (to cut out a triangle shape). Set rectangle shape aside. Set aside small triangles to use in a future recipe.
  7. Using a wooden skewer, thread 1 rectangle shape (watermelon, pineapple or rockmelon) onto base of skewer, top with 1 strawberry slice, 1 bliss ball, another strawberry slice, another bliss ball and 1 triangle (watermelon, pineapple or rockmelon). Transfer onto a serving platter (see Tips). Repeat using remaining ingredients (makes 16 rockets total). Transfer into refrigerator until ready to serve.

Useful Items

bowl, sealable storage container, wooden skewers, serving platter, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Adjust dark coconut nectar syrup to taste.
  • Try sticking the "rockets" into half of a styrofoam ball (or half of a rockmelon or watermelon) to serve.
  • Serve extra fruit in salads or smoothies. If you have extra bliss balls, use then as "moon rock" decorations.
