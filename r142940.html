Thai-style grilled pork skewers

Ingredients

  • ¼ tsp coriander seeds
  • ¼ tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled and cut into thin, round slices (2 mm)
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • ½ stalk fresh lemongrass, tough outer layer removed, cut into pieces (2 cm)
  • ½ tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp white sugar
  • 20 g fish sauce
  • 100 g coconut milk
  • 680 g pork fillet, cut into large pieces (3-4 cm) (see Tips)
  • warm water, for soaking

Preparation

  1. Place coriander seeds and cumin seeds into mixing bowl and toast 10 min/Varoma/speed 2,  without measuring cup.
  2. Add ginger, oil, garlic and lemongrass and chop 10 sec/speed 10.
  3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and chop 10 sec/speed 10.
  4. Add ground turmeric, salt, sugar, fish sauce and coconut milk and mix 5 sec/speed 3. Transfer marinade into a bowl.
  5. Place bowl with marinade onto mixing bowl lid and weigh pork into it. Stir well to coat, then cover with plastic wrap and place into refrigerator for a minimum of 6 hours or overnight.
  6. Soak 12 wooden skewers in a bowl of warm water for 30 minutes. Preheat grill and line a large baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with aluminium foil and set aside.
  7. Thread marinated pork onto skewers (approx. 3 pieces per skewer) and place onto prepared tray. Grill for 18-25 minutes, turning occasionally, or until pork is cooked through, then serve.

Useful Items

bowl, plastic wrap, large bowl, wooden skewers, baking tray (30 x 40 cm), aluminium foil, refrigerator, oven grill

Hints & Tricks

  • You can use either pork fillet or diced pork shoulder in this recipe.
