Pineapple and lemon thyme purée

Ingredients

  • 350 g fresh pineapple flesh, cut into pieces
  • 90 g banana, cut into pieces
  • 1 - 2 sprigs fresh lemon thyme, leaves only
  • natural yoghurt, to serve (optional - see Tips)
Nutrition

Preparation

  1. Place pineapple, banana and lemon thyme into mixing bowl and blend 15 sec/speed 8 or until smooth.
  2. Set aside 1 serving, then freeze the remainder in individual portions for future use. Serve with natural yoghurt (optional).

Useful Items

ice cube trays, freezer

Hints & Tricks

  • You can replace the natural yoghurt with kefir milk in this recipe.
  • Fruit purées like this one also make a lovely addition to your baby's porridge or rice cereal if you're serving one. Just be sure to give your baby plenty of iron-rich, savoury purées too. Fruit is nutritious and it's important that your baby develops a liking for various fruit flavours but offering less sweet options is crucial too.
