Crab Cakes

Ingredients

  • 5 ½ oz bread, in pieces (¾ in.)
  • 1 oz shallots, peeled and halved
  • 5 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only (approx. 0.2 oz)
  • 1 oz celery stalk, sliced (¼ in.)
  • 2 oz red bell pepper, in pieces (¼ in.)
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 2 jumbo eggs
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp Old Bay Seasoning®
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 1 pinch freshly ground pepper, to taste
  • 16 - 18 oz jumbo lump crab meat
  • oil, for frying

Preparation

  1. Line a baking sheet (approx. 16 in. x 14 in.) with parchment or foil and set aside.
  2. Place bread into mixing bowl and grind 30 sec/speed 9. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place shallots, parsley and celery into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 6.
  4. Add red pepper and chop 6 sec/speed 4.
  5. Add mayonnaise, eggs, Dijon mustard, Worcestershire sauce, Old Bay Seasoning®, salt and pepper and mix 8 sec/speed 3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  6. Add crab meat and 4 tbsp of reserved bread crumbs and mix 5 sec//speed .
  7. Shape into 6 crab cakes (approx. ½ cup ea.). Place remaining bread crumbs on a plate, lightly coat crab cakes with bread crumbs and transfer to prepared baking sheet. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
  8. Heat a large frying pan to medium high heat and coat with oil. When oil is hot, place crab cakes in pan and fry until golden brown (approx. 3-5 min. per side). Be careful as oil may splatter. Drain on paper towels and serve immediately.

Useful Items

baking sheet (approx. 16 in. x 14 in.), parchment paper, aluminum foil, frying pan, plate, paper towels, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Crab cakes can be broiled instead of fried. Broil on high 3-4 minutes each side or until golden brown
  • Serve crab cakes with sweet chili dipping sauce, lemon wedges, or tartar sauce.
  • Sprinkle crab cakes with fresh cilantro leaves, chopped chives or the green part of scallions, sliced thinly.
  • If using store-bought bread crumbs or Panko, simply add with the shallots and omit grinding of bread.
  • Add 1-2 tsp fresh cilantro or dill leaves (5-6 sprigs, leaves only)
