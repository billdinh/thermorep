Soy goulash with dumplings

Ingredients

Dumplings

  • 300 g bread rolls, stale, vegan, cut in half and then into slices (1 cm) (see tip)
  • 10 g fresh parsley leaves
  • 70 g onions, cut into halves
  • 180 g soy milk, unsweetened
  • 30 g soy flour, full fat, as egg replacement
  • 60 g water
  • 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
  • 1 tsp fine salt
  • 2 pinches freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 pinches ground nutmeg

Soy goulash

  • 250 g onions, cut into quarters
  • 1 - 2 garlic cloves
  • 30 g rapeseed oil
  • 150 g soy chunks, dried, not soaked
  • 500 g canned crushed tomatoes
  • 550 g water
  • 100 g dry red wine, vegan
  • 1 tbsp vegetable stock paste, homemade, vegan (see tip)
  • 55 g soy sauce
  • 50 g tomato purée
  • 1 tbsp sweet paprika powder
  • ½ tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp fresh marjoram
  • 1 tsp cocoa powder, unsweetened
  • 200 g mixed peppers (e.g. yellow and green), cut into strips (5 mm x 4 cm)
  • 150 g button mushrooms, sliced (5 mm) or small ones cut in half
  • 10 g fresh parsley leaves, chopped, for garnishing

Preparation

Dumplings

  1. Place 150 g bread roll in mixing bowl, chop 8 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place remaining 150 g bread roll in mixing bowl, chop 8 sec/speed 6 and add to chopped bread.
  3. Place parsley and onion in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add oil and sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  5. Add soy milk and heat 2 min/60°C/speed 1.
  6. Place soy flour in a bowl and mix to a smooth paste with water.
  7. Add soy flour mixture, yeast flakes, salt, pepper, nutmeg and chopped bread roll to mixing bowl and mix 20 sec//speed 3.5 with aid of spatula.
  8. Grease 2 pieces foil (approx. 30 x 30 cm) with oil. Remove dumpling mixture from mixing bowl, knead by hand, divide into 2 halves and roll (length max. 17 cm, Ø approx. 5 cm). Wrap tightly in foil, close at either end and place in Varoma, ensuring enough steam slits remain open.
  9. Rinse mixing bowl.

Soy goulash

  1. Place onion and garlic in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add oil and sauté 3 min 30 sec/120°C/speed 1.
  3. Add soy chunks, crushed tomatoes, water, red wine, stock cube, soy sauce, tomato puree, paprika powder, pepper, marjoram and cocoa powder and use spatula to stir. Place Varoma containing dumplings into position and cook 23 min/Varoma//speed 1. Set Varoma aside.
  4. Add mixed peppers and mushrooms to mixing bowl and use spatula to carefully stir in. Reposition Varoma and cook 7 min/Varoma//speed 1. Set Varoma aside.
  5. Season goulash to taste, carefully unwrap dumplings and cut each roll into 8 slices (approx. 2 cm thick). Arrange dumpling slices on 4 plates with goulash, garnish with chopped parsley and serve hot.

Useful Items

Hints & Tricks

  • Nowadays, many bakeries sell vegan bread rolls. A split wheat or plain roll is most suitable. Cut the roll in half and slice (1 cm).
  • If the dumpling dough is too dry in step 7, add 20 g soy drink to mixing bowl and mix 5 sec/ /speed 3.5 with aid of spatula.
  • Soy chunks and soy flour (as egg replacements) are available in health food shops.
  • Prepare the Vegetable stock paste featured in The Basic Cook Book without parmesan and with vegan wine for a vegan variation of this paste.
