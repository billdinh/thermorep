Chicken with couscous and saffron

Ingredients

  • 250 g couscous
  • 1 piece fresh ginger (2 cm)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1500 g chicken pieces (thighs, drumsticks and breast cut into quarters), skinless and boneless
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 100 g raisins
  • 1 ½ tsp ground cumin
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • ½ tsp ground turmeric (optional)
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 2 pinches freshly ground black pepper
  • 700 g water
  • 1 - 2 pinches saffron
  • 600 g red onions, cut into slices (5 mm)
  • 240 g dried chickpeas, soaked in 800 g water the day before, rinsed and drained

Preparation

  1. Place a large bowl onto mixing bowl lid, weigh in couscous and set aside.
  2. Place ginger and garlic into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 9. Transfer to a large bowl.
  3. Place large bowl with ginger-garlic mixture onto mixing bowl lid and add chicken pieces, olive oil, and raisins into it and season with 1 tsp cumin, cinnamon, turmeric, raisins,1 tsp salt and 1 pinch pepper. Mix well with spatula to coat all chicken pieces and set aside.
  4. Place water, saffron, ½ tsp cumin, 1 tsp salt, 1 pinch pepper, 200 g sliced onions and drained and rinsed chickpeas into mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish into position, distribute 200 g sliced onions in Varoma dish, place marinated chicken pieces on top and cover with 200 g sliced onions, steam 20 min/Varoma//speed 1.
  5. Carefully open Varoma and stir chicken to ensure even cooking, close Varoma again and steam a further 20 min/Varoma//speed 1.
  6. Remove Varoma and set aside. Insert simmering basket and strain some of the liquid from mixing bowl over the reserved couscous (enough to cover the couscous plus 1½ cm on top). Mix gently with a fork and allow to rest for 15 minutes.
  7. Remove simmering basket, set Varoma back in position, stir chicken again, cover Varoma and steam a further 15 min/Varoma//speed 1. Empty couscous onto a wide, deep platter, make a hole in the middle and fill with chicken. Pour over the whole content of mixing bowl (chickpeas and stock). Serve hot.

Useful Items

large bowl, serving platter

Hints & Tricks

  • For a more intense flavour, marinate the chicken prepared in step 2 overnight in the refrigerator. Continue the next day with the preparation from step 3.
  • When using canned chickpeas, rinse and drain them well and add them to the mixing bowl at the end of step 5.
