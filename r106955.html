Mushroom Stroganoff

Ingredients

  • ½ oz fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 8 oz yellow onions, quartered (approx. 1 lg. onion)
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 4 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves only
  • 1 oz unsalted butter
  • 2 tbsp corn starch
  • 22 oz fresh mushrooms, sliced (¼ in.)
  • 1 oz tomato paste
  • 2 oz white wine
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • ½ tsp salt, to taste
  • 2 oz sour cream
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
720 kJ / 172 kcal
Protein
6 g
Carbohydrates
17 g
Fat
9 g

Preparation

  1. Place parsley into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place onions, garlic and thyme into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add butter and corn starch, avoiding the blade, and cook 2 min/250°F/speed 2.
  4. Add mushrooms and sauté 5 min/250°F//speed . Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add tomato paste, white wine, Dijon mustard and salt and cook 5 min/250°F//speed 0.5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  6. Add sour cream and mix 30 sec//speed 1. Combine with spatula as needed. Garnish with reserved chopped parsley and serve hot.
