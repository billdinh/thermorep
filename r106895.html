Lychee Sorbet with Vodka

Ingredients

  • 20 oz canned lychees
  • 1 oz sugar
  • 2 fresh mint leaves
  • 4 tbsp vodka
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
552 kJ / 131 kcal
Protein
1.6 g
Carbohydrates
31 g
Fat
0.2 g

Preparation

  1. Drain lychee syrup through simmering basket into a pitcher and transfer syrup into ice cube trays (approx. 10 oz liquid). Place in freezer for 4 hours or overnight, until frozen. Place drained lychees in refrigerator until ready to use.
  2. Place sugar and mint into mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 9.
  3. Add lychee fruit and frozen lychee ice cubes and crush 5-10 sec/speed 8, or until smooth. Transfer into a freezable storage container and place in freezer for 4 hours or until frozen.
  4. Divide sorbet between 4 dessert glasses and add 1 tbsp vodka to each. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

small pitcher, dessert glasses, ice cube trays, freezable storage container, refrigerator, freezer

Hints & Tricks

  • Any fruit sorbet would work well in this cocktail.
  • For a non-alcoholic drink, place a scoop of lychee sorbet into a tall glass and fill with any lemon-lime or club soda.
