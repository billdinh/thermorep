Chicken broth with bread dumplings

Ingredients

  • 40 g Parmesan cheese, crust removed, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 250 g day old bread rolls, cut into pieces (2-3 cm),
  • 80 g brown onion, cut into halves
  • 6 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves and stalks
  • 25 g unsalted butter
  • 200 g full cream milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 2 pinches ground nutmeg
  • 1000 g water
  • 4 chicken wings (approx. 600 g)
  • 200 g mixed vegetables (e.g. carrots, onions, celery), cut into pieces (2-3 cm)

Preparation

  1. Place Parmesan cheese into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place bread into mixing bowl and chop 6 sec/speed 5, with aid of spatula, into large breadcrumbs. Transfer into another bowl and set aside.
  3. Place onions and parsley leaves into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5, with aid of spatula. Reserve parsley stalks. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl using spatula.
  4. Add butter and sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  5. Add milk and warm 2 min/60°C/speed 1.
  6. Add reserved breadcrumbs, eggs, 1 tsp of the salt, pepper and nutmeg and mix 15 sec//speed 3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl using spatula and mix a further 15 sec//speed 3. Transfer mixture into a bowl. Using damp hands, form 30 balls (4 cm) and arrange them into Varoma dish and onto tray. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  7. Place water, chicken wings, mixed vegetables, parsley stalks and remaining 1 tsp salt into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and cook 30 min/Varoma//speed . Remove Varoma and divide dumplings between 4 bowls. Strain broth through a fine mesh sieve over dumplings. Serve immediately, garnished with reserved grated Parmesan.

Useful Items

fine mesh sieve, bowls

Hints & Tricks

  • To make your own bread, refer to the recipes in The Basic Cookbook.
    - After straining the broth in, allow chicken wings to cool for a few minutes, then remove meat from the bones with the aid of a fork, discarding the skin, and add chicken meat to soup bowls or use in another recipe.
  • The broth has a delicate chicken flavour. For a stronger broth, add extra vegetables or meat.
    - For an even easier version, replace chicken wings with 2 heaped teaspoons Chicken stock paste (refer to the recipe in The Basic Cookbook).
    - Add 120 g bacon or ham, cut into cubes, to dumplings.
