Strawberry Caipiroska

Ingredients

  • 5 ½ oz fresh strawberries, hulled
  • 1 - 2 tbsp simple syrup (see Tips)
  • 1 oz lime flesh (approx. 1 lime), peeled, no white pith and seeds removed
  • 2 oz vodka
  • 7 oz ice cubes
Nutrition
per 1 glass
Calories
612 kJ / 146 kcal
Protein
1.4 g
Carbohydrates
19 g
Fat
0.2 g

Preparation

  1. Place all ingredients into mixing bowl and blend 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer into glasses and serve.

Useful Items

serving glasses

Hints & Tricks

  • The Strawberry Caipiroska cocktail is a sweet variation on Caipirinha that, instead of Cachaça, has Vodka and an addition of strawberry pulp.
  • To make your own simple syrup place 9 oz water and 8 oz caster sugar into mixing bowl and coo 5 min/212°F/speed 1. Transfer into a jar and place in refrigerator to cool completely (approx. 15 minutes). Any leftover syrup can be stored in a sealed jar in the refrigerator and used in other cocktails.
  • If using frozen strawberries you will need to add 3.5 oz of water and change the quantities to 7 oz frozen strawberries and 3.5 oz ice. Proceed as per recipe.
