Squid and Shrimp Risotto

Ingredients

  • 15 large shrimp
  • 3 garlic cloves, divided
  • 3 oz extra virgin olive oil, divided
  • 28 oz water
  • 6 oz red onion, quartered
  • 10 ½ oz squid, sliced into rings (½ in.)
  • 2 oz white wine
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp dried parsley
  • 1 pinch saffron threads
  • 9 oz Arborio rice
  • 1 tsp salt
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
558 kJ / 133 kcal

6.4 g

Preparation

  1. Peel and devein shrimp, reserve shrimp shells and refrigerate shrimp until needed. Place shrimp shells, 1 garlic clove and 1 oz olive oil into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 5, then sauté 10 min/250°F/speed .
  2. Add water and cook 10 min/212°F/speed 1. Strain through a fine sieve, reserve liquid and discard solids. Rinse mixing bowl and mixing bowl lid.
  3. Place onion, 2 garlic cloves and 2 oz olive oil into mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 4. Sauté 8 min/250°F/speed .
  4. Add squid, wine, paprika and parsley, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup on top of mixing bowl lid and cook 13 min/Varoma//speed .
  5. Add saffron and reserved shrimp broth, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup on top of mixing bowl lid and cook 3 min/212°F//speed .
  6. Add rice and salt, place Varoma into position, add reserved shrimp, secure Varoma lid and cook 13 min/212°F//speed . Remove Varoma and set aside. Transfer risotto to a bowl and let sit 5 minutes. Divide between 15 small bowls and top each with one cooked shrimp. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

fine sieve, small bowls, refrigerator
