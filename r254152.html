Country Peach and Custard Pie

Ingredients

Pie Crust

  • 1 homemade pie crust, chilled (9 in.)

Filling

  • 15 oz peaches, sliced (½ in.), (approx. 4 peaches)
  • decorative sugar, to sprinkle
  • 10 oz heavy whipping cream
  • 5 oz sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 oz corn starch (approx. 4 tbsp)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 oz unsalted butter, cut into pieces
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground cinnamon

Preparation

Pie Crust

  1. Adjust oven rack to lower middle position. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. Line chilled pie crust with parchment and baking weights and blind-bake for 15 minutes (375°F). Remove parchment and weights and bake a further 10 minutes (375°F) until lightly golden. Transfer to a wire rack and allow to cool completely.

Filling

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in peaches. Transfer peaches into pre-baked pie crust, arranging in a circle. Sprinkle with decorative sugar.
  2. Place whipping cream, 5 oz sugar, eggs, corn starch, vanilla, butter and salt into mixing bowl and cook 7 min/195°F/speed 3.5.
  3. Spoon filling into pie crust with peaches. Sprinkle with cinnamon and decorative sugar and bake for 40 minutes (375°F), until filling is firm but jiggles slightly in the center. Allow to cool, serve warm or chilled.

Useful Items

parchment paper, baking weights, wire rack, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Baking weights can be replaced with 16 oz dry beans.
  • Filling can be added directly to an uncooked pie crust and baked for 50-60 minutes (350°F).
  • Replace fresh peaches with frozen peaches, thawed or use sliced canned peaches, drained.
