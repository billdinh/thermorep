Conchiglie pasta with fresh tomato sauce and goat's cheese

Ingredients

  • 80 g goat's cheese, medium hard, cut into pieces (2-3 cm)
  • 20 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 300 g ripe tomatoes, seeds removed and cut into cubes (0.5-1 cm)
  • 1 ½ tsp fine salt
  • 1 sprig fresh basil, leaves washed and dried
  • 650 g water
  • 350 g pasta, dried, type conchiglie or other short type dried pasta

Preparation

  1. Place goat's cheese into mixing bowl and roughly grate: 10 sec/speed 6. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry the mixing bowl.
  2. Place olive oil into mixing bowl. Add garlic clove and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Sauté 3 min/100°C/speed 1.
  4. Add tomatoes, ½ tsp salt and basil, cook 5 min/100°C//speed 1.
  5. Add water, 1 tsp salt and bring to the boil 7 min/100°C/speed 1.
  6. Add pasta through the hole in the mixing bowl lid and cook without the measuring jug for time indicated on packet/100°C//speed  or until al dente.
  7. Transfer pasta into a large bowl and dress with the reserved goat's cheese.
  8. Leave to rest for 1 minute and serve.

Useful Items

Hints & Tricks

  • While cooking the pasta, check the pasta through the hole in the mixing bowl lid. Depending on the type and quality of the pasta, it may be necessary to add 20-30 g of hot water during the cooking time to achieve the creamy result typical of this type of pasta.
  • If you are using canned cherry tomatoes, strain them first using the simmering basket.
