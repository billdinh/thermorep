Sweet spiced nuts

Ingredients

  • 1 egg white
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • ½ tsp ground allspice
  • ½ tsp ground smoked paprika
  • 3 tsp brown sugar
  • ¾ tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp flaked salt
  • 150 g raw almonds
  • 150 g raw cashews
  • olive oil spray

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 140°C. Line a large baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg white into mixing bowl and whip 1 min/speed 4.
  3. Add cinnamon, allspice, paprika, sugar, cumin and salt and mix 4 sec/speed 2.
  4. Add almonds and cashews and mix 10 sec//speed 1. Remove butterfly whisk.
  5. Transfer nuts onto prepared baking tray and spread into an even layer. Lightly spray with olive oil. Bake for 35-40 minutes (140°C), or until golden and crisp. Allow nuts to cool on tray. Break into pieces and serve.

Useful Items

baking tray (30 x 40 cm), baking paper, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • You can store nuts in a sealable container in the pantry for up to 1 week.
