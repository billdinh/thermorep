Soft cinnamon doughnuts

Ingredients

Cinnamon sugar

  • 50 g raw sugar
  • ¼ tsp ground cinnamon

Doughnuts

  • oil, for greasing
  • 70 g raw sugar
  • 60 g grapeseed oil
  • 120 g plain flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 2 pinches ground nutmeg
  • 120 g full cream milk
  • 1 egg

Preparation

Cinnamon sugar

  1. Place sugar into mixing bowl and mill 4 sec/speed 9 .
  2. Add cinnamon and mix 3 sec/speed 2. Transfer into a bowl or large snaplock bag and set aside.

Doughnuts

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Spray a 12-hole doughnut tin well with oil and set aside.
  2. Place sugar into mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 9 . Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add oil, flour, baking powder, salt, nutmeg, milk and egg and mix 20 sec/speed 4 .
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix 5 sec/speed 4. Set aside in mixing bowl for 10 minutes.
  5. Fill prepared mould halfway with mixture, then bake for 10 minutes (180°C). Allow to cool in tin for 5 minutes. While still warm, place doughnuts into bowl or bag with cinnamon sugar and toss to coat completely. Serve warm.

Useful Items

bowl, snaplock bag, 12-hole doughnut tray, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Doughnuts are best served on the day they are made, however can be stored in a sealable container for up to 1 day.
