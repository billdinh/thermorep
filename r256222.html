Linguine with lemon and prosciutto

Ingredients

  • 60 g fresh baby spinach leaves
  • 1200 g boiling water
  • 300 g linguine pasta
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 100 g unsalted butter, cut into pieces
  • 2 tbsp pickled capers
  • 1 lemon, zested and juiced
  • 200 g fresh mozzarella, torn into pieces (see Tips)
  • 100 g prosciutto, torn into bite-size pieces
  • ground black pepper, to season

Preparation

  1. Place a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh spinach into it. Set aside.
  2. Place boiling water, pasta and salt into mixing bowl and cook 10-12 min/95°C//speed  (see Tips), until pasta is cooked to your liking. Drain pasta using simmering basket. Transfer into thermal serving bowl with spinach and toss to combine. Cover to keep warm. Rinse mixing bowl with cold water and dry.
  3. Place garlic into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add butter and cook 2 min/90°C/speed 2.
  5. Add capers, lemon zest and juice and heat 1 min/90°C/speed 2. Transfer into thermal serving bowl with pasta and spinach and toss to coat.
  6. Divide pasta between serving bowls and top with mozzarella and prosciutto. Season with pepper before serving immediately.

Useful Items

thermal serving bowl or other large bowl, serving bowls

Hints & Tricks

  • If water bubbles up into the mixing bowl lid while pasta is cooking, add 1 tsp of oil to the cooking water to reduce frothing.
  • For the best results, use a good-quality mozzarella (we used buffalo mozzarella).
