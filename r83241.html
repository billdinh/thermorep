Pork cannelloni with vegetable sauce

Ingredients

Cannelloni

  • 80 g Parmesan cheese, crust removed and cut into cubes (3 cm)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 brown onion (approx.150 g), cut into halves
  • 3 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 100 g prosciutto, cut into pieces
  • 20 g extra virgin olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 500 g pork mince
  • 1 tsp sweet paprika
  • 50 g breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp sea salt, to taste
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper, to taste
  • 18 - 22 cannelloni shells

Vegetable sauce

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 brown onion (approx. 150 g), cut into halves
  • 2 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 2 sprigs fresh basil, leaves only
  • 2 sprigs fresh oregano, leaves only
  • 20 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 celery stalk, cut into quarters
  • 100 g sweet potato, peeled and cut into pieces (4-5 cm)
  • 1 carrot, cut into quarters
  • 100 g zucchini, cut into quarters
  • 100 g red capsicum, deseeded and cut into quarters
  • 80 g white wine
  • ½ tsp dried fennel seeds
  • 1 tsp Vegetable stock paste (see Tips)
  • 400 g canned whole peeled tomatoes

Preparation

Cannelloni

  1. Place Parmesan cheese into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place garlic, onion, parsley and prosciutto or chorizo into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add oil and sauté 3 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  4. Add all remaining cannelloni ingredients, except cannelloni shells, and combine 30 sec//speed 4.
  5. Lightly oil the Varoma dish and tray. Stuff cannelloni shells with pork mixture and place them into greased Varoma dish and onto greased Varoma tray. Set Varoma aside. Rinse and dry mixing bowl.

Vegetable sauce

  1. Place garlic, onion and herbs into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7.
  2. Add oil and sauté 3 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Add celery, sweet potato, carrot, zucchini and red capsicum and chop 5 sec/speed 7
  4. Add all remaining ingredients, place Varoma into position and cook 18 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  5. Carefully rotate the cannelloni between the Varoma dish and tray to ensure even cooking, then secure Varoma lid and cook for a further 6-9 min/Varoma/speed 2 or until cannelloni are cooked. Set Varoma aside.
  6. Insert measuring cup into mixing bowl lid and blend 1 min/speed 6. Serve cannelloni immediately with vegetable sauce on top, sprinkled with reserved grated Parmesan cheese.
