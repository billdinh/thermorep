Rhubarb, Orange and Almond Cake

Ingredients

  • 400 g rhubarb, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 200 g golden caster sugar
  • 150 g unsalted butter, diced, plus extra for greasing
  • 120 g almonds
  • 1 orange, thin peelings of skin and 50 g juice
  • 2 large eggs
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • 100 g self-raising flour
  • 25 g flaked almonds

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in rhubarb and 50 g sugar. Remove, mix together then set aside.
  2. Preheat oven to 180°C. Grease sides and line base of a spring form tin (Ø 20 cm) with baking paper.
  3. Place almonds and orange peel in mixing bowl then grind 10 sec/speed 10.
  4. Add eggs, baking powder, remaining 150 g sugar, butter, flour and orange juice then mix 20 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix again 10 sec/speed 5.
  5. Add reserved rhubarb and sugar then fold in gently with spatula. Transfer to prepared tin and sprinkle with flaked almonds. Bake for 50-60 minutes (180°C) until golden brown and cooked through. Cool in tin for 20 minutes before removing to a wire rack.
  6. Serve warm or cold, cut in slices, with cream.

Useful Items

spring form tin (Ø 20 cm), baking paper, wire rack, oven

Hints & Tricks

This soft-textured cake is best enjoyed with a fork and pouring cream. The tartness of the rhubarb is offset by the sweet almond sponge, and the orange adds a great citrus flavour.
