Turkey vegetable purée

Ingredients

  • 250 g sweet potato, peeled and cut into pieces
  • 100 g carrot, peeled and cut into pieces
  • 240 g filtered water, plus extra if needed
  • 1 pear, peeled, cored and cut into pieces (see Tips)
  • 200 g skinless turkey thigh, cut into cubes (2-3 cm - see Tips)
  • 20 g butter (see Tips)

Preparation

  1. Place sweet potato and carrot into mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 6.
  2. Add water and cook 8 min/100°C/speed 2.
  3. Add pear into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket, weigh turkey into it, then cook 9 min/100°C/speed 2.
  4. Stir turkey with aid of spatula to ensure even cooking, then cook 2-3 min/100°C/speed 2, until turkey is cooked through.
  5. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula. Add turkey and butter into mixing bowl and blend 1 min/speed 4-9, increasing speed gradually from speed 4 to speed 9, to desired consistency.
  6. Set aside 1 serving, then freeze the remainder in individual portions for future use. If needed, stir through a little more water or your baby's milk to get the consistency just right before serving.

Useful Items

ice cube trays, freezer

Hints & Tricks

  • Turkey thigh has three times the iron of turkey breast, making it a better cut for babies, who need plenty of iron-rich foods once they start solids.
  • You can replace the pear with apple in this recipe.
  • You can replace the turkey with skinless chicken thigh in this recipe.
  • You can replace the butter with extra virgin olive oil, however, many babies seem to prefer the taste of butter. But occasionally using olive oil - or a little of each - will help them to accept its more peppery flavour.
