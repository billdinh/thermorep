Prawn and Sweet Potato Fritters

Ingredients

  • 300 g sweet potatoes, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 30 g fresh coriander, stalks only, reserving leaves
  • 3 spring onions, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 300 g prawns, raw, peeled, deveined
  • 100 g plain flour
  • 150 g water
  • 1 pinch baking powder
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt, plus extra for sprinkling
  • 1 pinch ground white pepper
  • 1000 g vegetable oil
  • 2 limes, halved
  • 200 g sweet chilli dipping sauce

Preparation

  1. Place sweet potatoes, coriander stalks and spring onions in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  2. Add prawns and chop 5 sec/speed 5.5, until chopped but with clearly visible pieces of prawn throughout the mixture.
  3. Preheat oven to 50°C.
  4. Add flour, water, baking powder, salt and pepper then stir 20 sec/speed 1. Leave mixture to stand for 10 minutes before cooking.
  5. Heat vegetable oil to a depth of 2 cm in a wide, shallow pan suitable for frying. When oil is hot, carefully drop dessertspoonfuls of prawn mixture into the hot oil, leaving plenty of space between each one.
  6. Fry fritters for 2 minutes on both sides, until crisp and browned. Remove with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towel. Fry in batches, keeping cooked fritters warm in oven (50°C).
  7. Garnish fritters with coriander leaves, season with salt and serve warm with lime halves and sweet chilli sauce.

Useful Items

frying pan, slotted spoon, paper towel, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • The quantity of oil needed for frying will vary according to the size of your frying pan. It should be approx. 2 cm deep in the pan.
  • To test the temperature of the oil, and the seasoning of the prawn mixture, carefully drop one teaspoon of mixture in the pan, it should fry quickly. Remove with a slotted spoon, cool slightly then taste, adjusting seasoning of fritters if desired.
