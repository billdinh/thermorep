Prawn balls with pickled vegetables

Ingredients

Sweet soy sauce

  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 4 eschalots (approx. 70 g), cut into halves
  • 1 tsp Chicken stock paste (see Tips)
  • 130 g water
  • 1 tsp cornflour
  • 40 g soy sauce
  • 40 g brown sugar
  • ¼ - ½ tsp dried chilli flakes, to taste

Pickled vegetables

  • 400 g water
  • 200 g malt vinegar
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 140 g carrot, cut into matchsticks
  • 140 g fresh Lebanese cucumber, peeled, deseeded and cut into matchsticks
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 4 cm cube fresh ginger, peeled
  • 2 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 tbsp peanut oil
  • 140 g red onion, cut into thin strips
  • 4 sprigs fresh coriander, stalks and leaves cut into thin slices (see Tips)
  • 1 tbsp black sesame seeds

Prawn balls

  • 3 cm cube fresh ginger, peeled
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 4 sprigs fresh coriander, roots, stalks and leaves
  • 2 spring onions/shallots, trimmed and cut into halves
  • 400 g prawn flesh
  • 3 tbsp cornflour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp rice wine vinegar
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • &frac18; tsp ground white pepper
  • ½ tsp lime juice
  • 500 g water

Preparation

Sweet soy sauce

  1. Place oil, garlic and eschalots into mixing bowl and cook 5 min/Varoma/speed 1, without measuring cup.
  2. Add stock paste and water and cook 5 min/Varoma/speed 1, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  3. Add cornflour, soy sauce, sugar and chilli flakes and cook 5 min/Varoma/speed 1, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  4. Strain sauce through simmering basket into a serving jug, discard contents in simmering basket and set sauce aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl and simmering basket.

Pickled Vegetables

  1. Place water, vinegar and salt into mixing bowl and bring to the boil 4 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  2. Add carrot and cucumber and cook 30 sec/Varoma//speed . Strain vegetables through simmering basket and set aside to cool. Discard liquid.
  3. Place garlic and ginger into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add oils and onion and cook 5 min/Varoma//speed , without measuring cup. Drain oil from onion and set onion aside to cool for 5 minutes.
  5. Place cooked onion, reserved vegetables, coriander and sesame seeds into a large bowl and stir to combine. Set aside.

Prawn balls

  1. Without cleaning the mixing bowl, place ginger, garlic, coriander and spring onions/shallots into mixing bowl and chop 6 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add all remaining ingredients, except water, and combine 5 sec/speed 5.
  3. Line Varoma dish and tray with wet, well-wrung baking paper. Using damp hands, roll prawn mixture into 20 balls (approx. 1 tablespoon each), and place balls onto Varoma dish and tray. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish and tray into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 15-20 min/Varoma/speed 1, or until balls are cooked through.
  5. Divide reserved pickled vegetables between 6 serving bowls, top with 3-4 balls and drizzle with reserved sauce to serve.

Useful Items

serving jug, large bowl, baking paper, 6 serving bowls

Hints & Tricks

  • Clean the roots of your coriander before adding to the mixing bowl. Scrape down the root to remove the tougher, more fibrous bits on the outside.
  • Please refer to The Basic Cookbook or Everyday Cookbook for the Chicken stock paste recipe.
  • Black sesame seeds can be found at speciality food stores, however you can also use white sesame seeds in this recipe.
  • Serve any remaining Sweet soy sauce on the side as a dipping sauce.
