Spiced pumpkin mini loaves with cream cheese frosting

Ingredients

Spiced pumpkin loaves

  • 500 g water
  • 80 g pumpkin, peeled and cut into cubes (2-3 cm)
  • 2 pieces orange zest, no white pith (1 x 10 cm strips)
  • 120 g butter, softened
  • 80 g soft dark brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 40 g sultanas
  • 80 g currants
  • 20 g desiccated coconut
  • 200 g self-raising flour
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • ¼ tsp ground nutmeg
  • ¼ tsp ground cardamom
  • ¼ tsp ground cloves
  • ¼ tsp ground ginger
  • 40 g full cream milk
  • 2 tbsp orange juice

Cream cheese frosting

  • 60 g cream cheese, room temperature
  • 20 g unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 80 g icing sugar
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp full cream milk
  • 1 pinch ground cinnamon, for sprinkling

Preparation

Spiced pumpkin loaves

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Grease 4 holes of an 8-hole mini loaf tray or 4 individual mini loaf tins and set aside.
  2. Place water into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket and weigh pumpkin into it. Add orange zest and cook 13 min/Varoma/speed 2, or until pumpkin is soft. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and set pumpkin and zest aside to cool for 5 minutes. Discard water in mixing bowl.
  3. Place steamed pumpkin and orange zest, butter and brown sugar into mixing bowl and mix 15 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add all remaining pumpkin loaf ingredients and mix 5 sec//speed 4.
  5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and repeat mixing 5 sec//speed 4.
  6. Transfer into prepared tray or tins and bake for 20-25 minutes (180°C) or until a wooden skewer inserted into the centre of each loaf comes out clean. Cool loaves in tray for 15 minutes, then remove and transfer onto a wire rack to cool completely (approx. 30 minutes).

Cream cheese frosting

  1. Place all cream cheese frosting ingredients, except cinnamon, into mixing bowl and and repeat mixing 10 sec/speed 5.
  2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix 10 sec/speed 5.
  3. To serve, decorate each loaf with cream cheese frosting, sprinkle with cinnamon and cut each loaf in half.

Useful Items

8-hole mini loaf tray or 4 individual mini loaf tins, wire rack, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Wrap portions in plastic wrap and store in the freezer to enjoy another day.
