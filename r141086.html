Gnocchi with Sausages and Steamed Spinach, Apricots with Honey and Walnuts

Ingredients

  • 220 g apricots, fresh, halved (approx. 4)
  • 20 g honey
  • 20 g walnut halves, coarsely chopped
  • 1 tsp fresh lemon thyme, leaves only
  • 40 g Parmesan cheese, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 100 g onions, quartered
  • 220 g raw sausages, skinned (approx. 4)
  • 100 g fresh mushrooms, quartered
  • 100 g fresh spinach leaves
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1000 g water
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 400 g gnocchi

Preparation

  1. Distribute apricots, honey, walnuts and lemon thyme between 2 squares of baking paper (approx. 30 cm). Wrap to form parcels then place in Varoma dish.
  2. Place Parmesan in mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 9. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.
  3. Place oil and onions in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  4. Add sausage meat and heat 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  5. Add mushrooms, spinach and soy sauce then cook 7 min/120°C/speed . Transfer to a serving bowl and keep warm.
  6. Place water and salt in mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket and weigh in gnocchi. Place covered Varoma into position and cook 12 min/Varoma/speed 2. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula then combine gnocchi with reserved sausagemeat mixture.
  7. Sprinkle with reserved grated Parmesan then serve as a main course with apricot en papillotes as dessert.

Hints & Tricks

  • This quick recipe produces a tasty main and a healthy dessert for two. Delicate apricots are steamed over the gnocchi which are served with a tasty sausage sauce – two courses that are ready in 35 minutes.
