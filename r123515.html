Prawn and Cucumber Salad

Ingredients

Salad

  • 30 g olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 Tbsp brown mustard seeds
  • 30 g water
  • 270 g mangetout
  • 300 g prawns, raw, large, peeled

Dressing

  • 80 g watercress
  • 150 g cucumber, thinly sliced (2 mm)
  • 10 g honey
  • 15 g lemon juice
  • 100 g olive oil
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
Nutrition

Preparation

Salad

  1. Place oil, garlic and mustard seeds in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 8. Sauté 5 min/120°C/speed 1.
  2. Add water and mangetout then cook 4 min/120°C//speed . Transfer to a serving bowl using a slotted spoon.
  3. Add prawns and sauté 4 min/100°C//speed . Transfer to serving bowl using slotted spoon, then discard cooking liquid and clean mixing bowl.

Dressing

  1. Once prawns and mangetout are cool, place serving bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in watercress and cucumber. Set serving bowl aside.
  2. Place honey, lemon juice, oil and salt in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 5. Pour over salad then place in fridge to chill for at least 30 minutes. Serve cold.

Useful Items

serving bowl, slotted spoon, refrigerator
