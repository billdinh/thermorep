Eggnog Snickerdoodles

Ingredients

Cookie Dough

  • 6 oz unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 5 oz brown sugar
  • 3 oz sugar
  • 2 oz eggnog
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • 12 oz all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp cream of tartar
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • ¼ tsp ground nutmeg
  • ¼ tsp salt

Sugar Coating

  • 1 oz sugar
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • &frac18; tsp ground nutmeg

Preparation

Cookie Dough

  1. Insert butterfly whisk. Place butter, brown sugar and sugar into mixing bowl and whip 30 sec/speed 3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and whip a further 30 sec/speed 3.
  2. Add eggnog and vanilla and mix 10 sec/speed 3. Remove butterfly whisk.
  3. Add flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, cinnamon, nutmeg and salt and mix 20 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix a further 20 sec/speed 4.
  4. Scoop out dough (approx. 2 tbsp ea.) and roll into balls. Transfer dough balls to a plate and refrigerate 30 minutes. Preheat oven to 375°F.

Sugar Coating

  1. Place a small bowl on top of mixing bowl lid and weigh in sugar, cinnamon and nutmeg. Stir to combine and set aside.
  2. Line baking sheets with parchment. Roll each ball in sugar mixture and place onto prepared baking sheets. Bake for 10-12 minutes (375°F) until lightly golden. Let cool completely before packaging or serving.

Useful Items

baking sheets, parchment paper, refrigerator, oven
