Mushroom bolognaise

Ingredients

  • 20 g dried porcini mushrooms
  • 40 g Parmesan cheese, cut into pieces
  • 200 g mixed vegetables (e.g celery, carrot and onion), cut into pieces
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 40 g olive oil
  • 400 g fresh mushrooms of choice, cut into quarters
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 50 g dry white wine
  • 400 g tomatoes, cut into quarters
  • 1 - 1 ½ tsp salt, to taste
  • ½ - 1 tsp ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1 tsp white sugar
  • ½ tsp Vegetable stock paste (see Tips)
  • 100 g water
  • 1 dried bay leaf
  • 1 sprig fresh rosemary, leaves only
  • 1 - 1 ½ tsp dried oregano

Preparation

  1. Place dried mushrooms into mixing bowl and mill 50 sec/speed 10. Transfer into a small bowl and set aside.
  2. Place Parmesan into mixing bowl and grate 15 sec/speed 10. Transfer into a separate bowl and set aside. Rinse mixing bowl.
  3. Place mixed vegetables, garlic and 20 g of the oil into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then cook 6 min/100°C/speed 1, without measuring cup.
  5. Add fresh mushrooms and remaining 20 g oil and chop 4 sec/speed 4.
  6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and cook 4 min/Varoma/speed 1, without measuring cup.
  7. Add tomato paste and cook 2 min/100°C/speed 1.
  8. Add wine and cook 2 min/100°C/speed 1.
  9. Add tomatoes, salt, pepper, sugar, stock paste, water and reserved porcini powder and cook 15 min/100°C//speed 1, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  10. Add bay leaf, rosemary and oregano into mixng bowl and cook 5 min/100°C//speed 2.
  11. Serve bolognaise with cooked pasta of choice and sprinkle with reserved Parmesan cheese before serving.

Hints & Tricks

  • You can make extra dried porcini powder, then store it in a sealable storage container in the pantry for up to 3 months.
  • Please refer to The Basic Cookbook or Everyday Cookbook for the Vegetable stock paste recipe.
  • To cook pasta in your Thermomix, please refer to the recipe in The Basic Cookbook or Everyday Cookbook.
