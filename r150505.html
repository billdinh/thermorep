Hommus dip

Ingredients

  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 2 tbsp olive oil, plus extra to serve
  • 800 g canned chickpeas, rinsed and drained (approx. 500 g after draining)
  • 60 g lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp tahini
  • sea salt, to taste
  • zaatar, for sprinkling (optional - see Tips)
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
299 kJ / 71 kcal

Preparation

  1. Place garlic into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add oil and cook 1 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Add chickpeas and lemon juice and mix 30 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add tahini and salt and blend 10 sec/speed 5, until smooth. Transfer into a serving bowl and sprinkle with za'atar (optional) and extra oil (optional) before serving.

Useful Items

serving bowl, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Roasted onion hommus: Replace 400 g chickpeas with 250 g sautéed or roasted onion. Proceed as per recipe. Reserve 2 tablespoons roasted onion for garnishing, if desired.
  • Spinach and lemon hommus: Replace 400 g chickpeas with 300 g chopped fresh baby spinach leaves and an extra 20 g lemon juice. Proceed as per recipe.
  • Butternut pumpkin hommus: Replace 400 g chickpeas with 350 g roasted butternut pumpkin, ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon and ¼ teaspoon ground cumin. Proceed as per recipe.
  • For an extra flavour boost, try sprinkling hommus with some Za'atar seasoning (please refer to the recipe on this recipe chip).
  • You can store this dip in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.
