Sourdough soup (White borscht)

Ingredients

Sourdough starter

  • 150 g wholemeal flour
  • 1000 g warm water
  • 45 g rye bread (approx. 2-3 slices), cut into quarters
  • 1 garlic clove, cut into slices

Soup

  • 8 slices dried porcini mushrooms, cut into thirds
  • 1100 g water
  • 4 eggs
  • 70 g brown onion, cut into halves
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 150 g bacon, cut into cubes (1 cm)
  • 100 g chorizo sausage, cut into slices (0.5 cm)
  • 200 g potatoes, peeled and cut into cubes (1 cm)
  • 2 tbsp Vegetable stock paste (see Tips)
  • 2 dried bay leaves
  • 3 allspice berries
  • ¼ tsp sea salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 2 tsp dried marjoram
  • 1 tsp fresh horseradish, grated (optional)
  • 50 g sour cream

Preparation

Sourdough starter

  1. Place a large ceramic or glass bowl onto mixing bowl lid, then weigh wholemeal flour, warm water and rye bread into it. Add garlic, then combine well with a wooden spoon. Cover with a kitchen towel or muslin cloth and set aside to ferment at room temperature for 3 days, keeping bowl away from direct sunlight. Stir starter well using a wooden spoon twice a day.
  2. On day 4, strain starter mixture through a fine mesh sieve. Discard sieve contents and reserve the liquid starter. This is a sour tasting starter that forms the base of the soup.

Soup

  1. Place a small bowl onto mixing bowl lid, then weigh 100 g of the water into it. Add porcini or shiitake mushrooms, then set aside to soak for 20 minutes.
  2. Place 400 g of the water into mixing bowl, insert simmering basket and place eggs into it. Cook 14 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and run eggs under cold water to cease cooking. Set boiled eggs aside to cool completely. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Place onion and garlic into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5.
  4. Add oil and sauté 3 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  5. Add bacon or speck and chorizo or Polish sausage and sauté 3 min/Varoma//speed 1.
  6. Add remaing 600 g of water, potatoes, stock paste, bay leaves and allspice, then cook 10 min/100°C//speed 1.
  7. Add salt, pepper, 300 g of the reserved sourdough starter and soaked dried mushrooms with soaking water and cook 5 min/100°C//speed 1.
  8. Add marjoram, horseradish (optional) and sour cream and cook 3 min/100°C//speed 1. Peel hardboiled eggs and cut into quarters. Serve soup immediately, garnished with hardboiled egg.

Useful Items

small bowl , fine mesh sieve, kitchen towel, ceramic or glass bowl, muslin cloth, large ceramic or glass bowl, wooden spoon

Hints & Tricks

  • Leftover starter will last in the refrigerator in a sealed jar for 2-3 days and can be used to make a 2nd batch of soup. 
  • Serve soup with crusty bread on the side.
