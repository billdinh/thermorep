Quick massaman beef curry

Ingredients

  • 800 g water
  • 300 g small potatoes, cut into pieces (2 cm)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp peanut oil
  • 50 g roasted unsalted peanuts
  • 1 brown onion (approx. 180 g), cut into quarters
  • 100 g Thai massaman curry paste
  • 800 g beef rump steak, cut into strips (1 x 2.5 cm)
  • 250 g coconut cream
  • 20 g palm sugar
  • 30 g fish sauce
  • 25 g tamarind paste
  • 1 tsp Meat stock paste (see Tips)
  • 1 cinnamon quill
  • 2 fresh Kaffir lime leaves
  • 2 tbsp fresh coriander leaves, torn into pieces

Preparation

  1. Place water into mixing bowl, insert simmering basket and weigh potatoes into it. Sprinkle potatoes with salt, then steam 12 min/Varoma/speed 2. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and set aside to drain. Rinse and dry mixing bowll.
  2. Place oil, peanuts, onion and curry paste into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7, then cook 3 min/100°C/speed 1. Scrape down lid and sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add beef and cook 3 min/100°C//speed 1.
  4. Add coconut cream, sugar, fish sauce, tamarind paste, stock paste, cinnamon, Kaffir lime leaves and steamed potatoes and cook 10-12 min/90°C//speed 1 ,  without measuring cup or until meat is cooked to your liking. Divide between serving bowls and garnish with coriander before serving.
