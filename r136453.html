Bread Stuffed with Eggs and Green Beans

Ingredients

Filling

  • 5 g fresh basil, leaves only
  • 120 g pitted black olives
  • 500 g water
  • 2 tsp fine sea salt
  • 6 medium eggs
  • 200 g fresh green beans, cut in pieces

Assembly

  • 200 g olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 medium egg
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 1 sandwich loaf (approx. 500 g)

Preparation

Filling

  1. Place basil and olives in mixing bowl then chop 2 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place water and 1 tsp salt in mixing bowl and insert simmering basket. Place eggs in simmering basket and place Varoma dish into position, then weigh in green beans. Season with remaining 1 tsp salt and cook 20 min/Varoma/speed 2. Set Varoma aside and remove simmering basket with aid of spatula. Discard cooking water.
  3. Run eggs under cold water, then peel and cut into small pieces.
  4. Mix eggs and green beans with basil and olive mixture.

Assembly

  1. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in oil then set aside.
  2. Place garlic in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8.
  3. Add egg, salt and pepper then mix 1 min 30 sec/speed 4 while very slowly pouring reserved oil onto mixing bowl lid, letting it drizzle around measuring cup in a thin stream onto rotating blades to create an emulsion. Pour into bowl with reserved filling and stir.
  4. Make a horizontal cut through the length of the bread, taking care not to cut all the way through. Remove the soft bread from the inside, tear into small pieces and mix in with reserved filling. Fill bread with prepared mixture, close tightly, wrap in foil and refrigerate for approx. 5 hours. Serve in slices.
