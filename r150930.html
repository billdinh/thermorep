Steamed Bread Rolls

Ingredients

  • 950 g water
  • 15 g fresh yeast, crumbled
  • vegetable oil, for greasing
  • 250 g bread flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3 tsp sesame seeds, white or black, plus extra for sprinkling

Preparation

  1. Place 150 g water and yeast in mixing bowl and warm 2 min/37°C/speed 1. Meanwhile, grease Varoma dish and tray and set aside.
  2. Add bread flour, salt and sesame seeds and knead 2 min 30 sec/. Transfer dough to a floured surface.
  3. Form dough into 10-12 balls (Ø 3 cm), place in prepared Varoma dish and tray, cover with Varoma lid and let dough rise until doubled in size (approx. 1 hour). Sprinkle bread rolls with sesame seeds.
  4. Place 800 g water in mixing bowl, set Varoma in position and steam 40 min/Varoma/speed 1. Allow to cool completely on a wire rack before serving fresh or toasted.

wire rack

Hints & Tricks

  • Give these soft steamed rolls a try – you’ll be surprised by how tender and fluffy they are. Play with the colourful variations, invent your own, and don’t hesitate to toast the sliced rolls if you prefer the crunch of oven-baked bread!
  • Cooling bread rolls completely before serving ensures a tender outside and fluffy texture inside.
  • Green Steamed Bread Rolls: before step 2, add 50 g spinach leaves and an additional 50 g flour and mix 10 sec/speed 9. Continue with the recipe in step 2, omitting sesame seeds.
    - Red Steamed Bread Rolls: before step 2, add 30 g tomato purée and an additional 50 g flour and mix 10 sec/speed 5. Continue with the recipe in step 2, omitting sesame seeds.
