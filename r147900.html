Puy Lentil and Sausage Casserole

Ingredients

  • oil, for frying
  • 6 raw sausages (approx. 600 g)
  • 80 g onions, quartered
  • 120 g carrots, cut in pieces
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 250 g dried Puy lentils
  • 650 g water
  • 1 clove
  • 3 - 4 sprigs fresh thyme, to taste
  • 2 - 3 sprigs fresh rosemary, to taste
  • 1 dried bay leaf
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper, or to taste
  • mustard, for serving

Preparation

  1. Heat oil in a frying pan then brown sausages over a high heat. Transfer to Varoma dish and set aside.
  2. Place onions, carrots and garlic in mixing bowl then chop 6 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add lentils, water, clove, thyme, rosemary, bay leaf, salt and pepper.
  4. Place covered Varoma dish into position then steam 40 min/100°C//speed 1. Remove bay leaf then serve sausages with lentils, accompanied by mustard.

Useful Items

Hints & Tricks

  • Lentils are a great addition to any dish, providing plenty of protein and fibre. This recipe uses Puy lentils which are known for their nutty and slightly peppery flavour. For other lentil recipes try Lentil Moussaka, Steamed Pumpkin with Curried Lentils or Quick Lamb Dhansak.
