Chocolate ice cream

Ingredients

  • 450 g milk (see Tips)
  • 150 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 120 g pure maple syrup
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 30 g raw cacao powder (see Tips)
  • waffle cones, to serve (optional)
Nutrition
per 1 portions
Calories
1058 kJ / 252 kcal
Protein
6 g
Carbohydrates
21.8 g

15.9 g

Preparation

  1. Place milk, cream, syrup and egg yolks into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 3, then cook 8 min/80°C/speed 4.
  2. Add cacao and mix 30 sec/speed 4. Immediately transfer into ice cube trays and place into freezer overnight or until frozen.
  3. Place frozen cubes into mixing bowl and blend 1 min/speed 1 -6, increasing speed gradually from speed 1 to speed 6.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and blend 1 min/speed 1 -6,  increasing speed gradually from speed 1 to speed 6 or until smooth and creamy. Serve immediately in waffle cones (optional) or bowls (see Tips).

Useful Items

2-3 ice cube trays, serving bowls, freezeable containers, freezer

Hints & Tricks

Chocolate raspberry ice cream: Once ice cream is smooth and creamy, add 100 g frozen raspberries and mix 5 sec/speed 4.

  • For a lighter ice cream, use 600 g milk and leave out the cream.
  • If you don’t have any raw cacao on hand you can use regular cocoa powder instead.
  • If ice cream is too soft, place into a freezable container and place into freezer until desired consistency is achieved.
  • If the ice cream becomes too firm to scoop, place it into the refrigerator or on the kitchen bench until thawed to your desired softness before serving. You may wish to store all frozen desserts in individual portions, as refreezing thawed leftovers can be a safety hazard.
