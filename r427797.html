Honeycomb

Ingredients

Preparation

  1. To guarantee the success of recipes using Sugar Stage modes, the ingredients used must match exactly the quantity and description given. The recipe should also be cooked immediately after placing the sugar with the other ingredients in the mixing bowl.
  2. Line a baking tin with baking paper then set aside on a heat-resistant surface.
  3. Measure out 1 Tbsp bicarbonate of soda so ready when cooking time ends. Place sugar, honey and 80 g water in mixing bowl then, without measuring cup, start Sugar Stages .
  4. Avoiding blades, immediately add reserved bicarbonate of soda and quickly stir with spatula until mixture foams up. Transfer immediately to prepared tin and, using spatula, immediately scrape off any mixture left on rim of mixing bowl so mixing bowl lid closes securely for Pre-clean mode.
  5. Place remaining 1000 g water and start Clean .
  6. Leave to cool completely to room temperature then break into pieces. Store in an airtight container at room temperature (see tip) and use as needed.

Useful Items

Hints & Tricks

  • Honeycomb is a delectable treat and great for decorating or adding to desserts. Serve sprinkled over ice cream or a layered chocolate cake.
  • Honeycomb cannot be left out in the open for an extended length of time, as it will draw moisture from the air and become sticky. Although best eaten soon after it is made, it can be stored in a bag, or wrapped in cling film and stored in airtight containers, for up to 1 week. Dipping honeycomb in chocolate will help extend the shelf-life by a few days.
