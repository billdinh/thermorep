Double Peanut Cookies

Ingredients

  • 120 g unsalted butter, diced, plus extra for greasing
  • 140 g peanuts, roasted, unsalted, plus extra for decoration
  • 100 g granulated sugar
  • 80 g light brown sugar
  • 250 g plain flour
  • 1 medium egg
  • 1 tsp real vanilla extract
  • ½ tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
Nutrition
per 1 piece

894 kJ / 214 kcal

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 170°C and grease two baking trays then set aside.
  2. Place 80 g peanuts in mixing bowl and chop 20 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then blend 10 sec/speed 6.
  3. Add butter, granulated sugar, light brown sugar, flour, egg, vanilla extract, bicarbonate of soda and salt then mix 25 sec/speed 5.
  4. Add 60 g peanuts and mix 15 sec//speed 3. Shape into rounds (approx. 4 cm) and place on greased baking trays 5 cm apart to allow room for them to spread. Place 1 peanut on top of each round, pressing into dough slightly.
  5. Bake for 12-14 minutes (170°C) until golden and still slightly soft. Cool for 5 minutes on trays before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely. Once cool, store in an airtight container until ready to serve.

Useful Items

2 baking trays, wire rack, airtight container, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • These biscuits are called double peanut cookies as you make peanut butter first then add whole peanuts for decoration.
