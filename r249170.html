Beef and mushroom hot pot

Ingredients

  • 700 - 800 g stewing beef (gravy beef or chuck steak), cut into cubes (4-5 cm)
  • 40 g plain flour
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 celery stalks, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 1 tbsp oil, plus extra for brushing
  • 1 brown onion, cut into eighths
  • 100 g bacon, cut into pieces (2 cm)
  • 2 carrots (approx. 250 g), cut into rounds (1 cm)
  • 100 g red wine
  • 1 tbsp Meat stock paste (see Tips)
  • 2 dried bay leaves
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp ground white pepper
  • 4 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves only
  • 200 g fresh button mushrooms, cut into halves
  • 350 g potatoes, peeled and cut into thin slices (5 mm)

Preparation

  1. Place bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh beef and flour into it. Stir to combine and set aside.
  2. Place garlic and celery into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add oil, onion and bacon and cook 5 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  4. Add carrots, wine, stock paste, bay leaves, Worcestershire sauce, salt, pepper, thyme and reserved beef, then cook 30 min/90°C//speed 1. Meanwhile, preheat oven to 180°C and set aside a casserole dish (approx. 22 cm).
  5. Add 100 g of the mushrooms into mixing bowl, stir with aid of spatula and cook 4 min/100°C//speed 1.
  6. Add remaining mushrooms and cook 4 min/100°C//speed 1.
  7. Transfer beef mixture into casserole dish. Layer potato slices over mixture, overlapping slices. Brush with oil and bake for 45-60 minutes (180°C) or until beef is tender and potatoes are golden. Allow to cool for 10-20 minutes before serving.

Useful Items

bowl, pastry brush, casserole dish (22 cm), oven
