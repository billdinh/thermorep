Easy satay chicken

Ingredients

Satay sauce

  • ½ tsp dried fennel seeds
  • ½ tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander seeds
  • 1 tbsp ground turmeric
  • 2 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 5 garlic cloves
  • 1 - 2 fresh red chillies, deseeded if preferred, to taste
  • 2 tbsp tamari sauce
  • 1 tbsp tamarind paste
  • 2 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp dark brown sugar
  • 270 g coconut cream
  • 60 g peanut butter (see Tips)

Chicken, rice and vegetables

  • 400 g jasmine rice
  • 1200 g water
  • 600 g skinless chicken thigh fillets, cut into pieces (3-4 cm)
  • 100 g carrots, cut into thin strips
  • 100 g broccolini, cut into thirds
  • 100 g cauliflower, cut into small florets (3 cm)
  • 50 g red capsicum, cut into thin strips
  • 50 g brown onion, cut into thin wedges
  • 80 g sugar snap peas, trimmed and cut into halves

Preparation

Satay sauce

  1. Place all spices into mixing bowl and mill 30 sec/speed 10.
  2. Add ginger, garlic and chilli and chop 30 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add all remaining satay sauce ingredients and cook 3 min/90°C/speed 1, then blend 30 sec/speed 10. Transfer sauce into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and set aside to keep warm.

Chicken, rice and vegetables

  1. Insert simmering basket, then weigh rice into it.  Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and rinse rice until water runs clear.
  2. Place water into mixing bowl, then re-insert simmering basket with rice. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh chicken into it. Secure Varoma lid and cook 10 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  3. Stir chicken in Varoma dish to ensure even cooking. Insert Varoma tray, then weigh carrot, broccolini, cauliflower, capsicum and brown onion onto it. Secure Varoma lid and cook a further 8-10 min/Varoma/speed 2 , or until rice is cooked.  Set Varoma aside. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula, then transfer rice into a seperate thermal serving bowl or other large bowl to keep warm. Empty mixing bowl.
  4. Place sugar snap peas and reserved satay sauce into mixing bowl, then add steamed chicken and steamed vegetables from Varoma to mixing bowl and cook 5 min/90°C//speed . Serve satay chicken and vegetables with rice.
