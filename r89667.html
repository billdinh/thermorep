Beetroot, pear and blue cheese salad

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 50 g pecan nuts
  • ½ orange, zest only, no white pith (optional)
  • 2 tbsp macadamia oil
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 800 g water
  • 800 g raw beetroot, peeled and cut into cubes (2 cm)
  • 200 g fresh green beans, trimmed and cut into halves
  • 120 g rocket leaves
  • 100 - 200 g blue cheese, thinly sliced
  • 1 pear, thinly sliced

Preparation

  1. Place butter and pecans into mixing bowl and sauté 8 min/Varoma//speed . Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  2. Place orange zest (optional) into mixing bowl and grate 30 sec/speed 8, or until a fine consistency is achieved. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add macadamia oil, honey and vinegar and warm 1 min/37°C/speed 3. Transfer dressing into a serving jug and set aside.
  4. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position, insert Varoma tray and weigh beetroot onto it. Secure Varoma lid and steam 28 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  5. Remove Varoma tray with beetroot. Weigh green beans into Varoma dish, then re-insert Varoma tray with beetroot, secure Varoma lid and steam for a further 5 min/Varoma/speed 1. Set Varoma aside and allow beetroot and beans to cool (approx. 20 min).
  6. Place a serving platter onto mixing bowl lid, then weigh rocket leaves onto it.
  7. Add cooled beetroot and beans to serving platter. Drizzle over reserved dressing, then top with reserved butter pecans, blue cheese and pear. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

serving jug, serving platter

Hints & Tricks

 

  • The steaming of the beetroot can be easily done well in advance. Transfer beetroot into a sealable storage container and place in refrigerator to store. Dress and assemble salad, as per recipe, just before serving.
