Chicken and pear pie

Ingredients

Gluten free pastry

  • butter, for greasing
  • 140 g rice flour
  • 70 g arrowroot flour
  • 90 g cornflour, plus extra for dusting
  • ¼ tsp sea salt
  • 2 tsp xanthan gum
  • 160 g butter, chilled
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp apple cider vinegar
  • 40 g water, chilled

Chicken and pear filling

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 leek, white part only, cut into pieces
  • 50 g butter
  • 600 g skinless chicken thigh fillets, cut into cubes (3 cm)
  • 3 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves only
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 50 g water
  • 2 - 3 tbsp cornflour
  • 1 tbsp Vegetable stock paste (see Tips)
  • 50 g white wine
  • 150 g pear (approx. 1 pear), peeled, core removed and cut into cubes (2 cm)
  • 50 g pouring (whipping) cream

Preparation

Gluten free pastry

  1. Grease a high-sided pie dish (23 cm) and set aside.
  2. Place rice flour, arrowroot flour, cornflour, sea salt and xanthan gum into mixing bowl and combine 5 sec/speed 4.
  3. Add butter and combine 10 sec/speed 6.
  4. Add egg, vinegar and water and mix 10 sec/speed 6, then knead 30 sec/.
  5. Transfer dough onto a silicone bread mat or floured work surface and work into a tight ball. Wrap in plastic wrap and place into refrigerator to chill for a minimum 30 minutes.
  6. Preheat oven to 180°C. Roll out ⅔ of the pastry between a silicone bread mat and a sheet of baking paper or between two sheets of baking paper into a circle (25 cm).
  7. Line pie dish with rolled out pastry, then place into refrigerator to chill for 15 minutes. Wrap remaining pastry in plastic wrap and place into refrigerator until ready to use.
  8. Blind bake pastry case for 20 minutes (180°C) or until lightly golden. Set pastry case aside to cool while you prepare the filling.

Chicken and pear filling

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C.
  2. Place garlic and leek into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add butter and sauté 3 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  4. Add chicken, thyme and pepper and cook 5 min/90°C//speed 1. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Place a ceramic or glass bowl onto mixing bowl lid, then weigh water into it. Add cornflour to ceramic or glass bowl and stir until cornflour has dissolved.
  6. Add cornflour and water mixture, stock paste and wine and cook 5 min/90°C//speed 1.
  7. Add pear and cook 8 min/90°C//speed 1.
  8. Add cream and combine 20 sec//speed 1.
  9. Transfer chicken and pear filling into pastry case. Remove remaining pastry from refrigerator, then roll out on a silicone bread mat or floured work surface into a circle (23 cm).
  10. Place pastry circle over pie and seal the edges by pressing down with your fingertips or a fork. Make two slits in the center of the pastry to allow steam to escape, then bake for 40-50 minutes (200°C) or until pastry is golden brown. Serve hot.

Useful Items

high-sided pie dish (23 cm), plastic wrap, silicone bread mat, rolling pin, baking paper, ceramic or glass bowl, refrigerator, oven

Hints & Tricks
