Couscous with apricots, hazelnuts and feta, mixed vegetables

Ingredients

  • 150 g hazelnuts
  • 150 g dried apricots
  • 300 g onions, cut in quarters
  • 20 g garlic cloves
  • 60 g olive oil, plus extra for drizzling
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 200 g couscous
  • 1 heaped tsp vegetable stock paste, homemade
  • 600 g water
  • 200 g carrots, cut in slices (5 mm)
  • 200 g courgettes, cut in slices (7-10 mm)
  • 150 g red peppers, cut in bite-size pieces
  • 100 g frozen green peas
  • 200 g feta cheese, cut in small cubes (5 mm)
  • 2 tsp fresh parsley, chopped, to garnish (optional)

Preparation

  1. Place hazelnuts and dried apricots in mixing bowl and chop 5-10 sec/speed 5 or until roughly chopped (see tip). Transfer to a large bowl and set aside.
  2. Place onions, garlic cloves, olive oil and salt in mixing bowl and chop 6 sec/speed 4.5, then sauté 13 min/120°C/speed .
  3. Add couscous and vegetable stock paste and stir 10 sec//speed 2.
  4. Add 200 g water, stir using spatula, cover with mixing bowl lid and allow couscous to absorb liquid for 5 minutes. Meanwhile, place simmering basket on mixing bowl lid and add carrots, courgettes, red peppers then green peas and set aside.
  5. Transfer soaked couscous to large bowl containing chopped nuts and apricots. Add 150 g feta cubes and stir gently to mix well. Spread a quarter of the couscous mixture loosely on Varoma dish, leaving some holes unobstructed so steam can circulate, and spread remaining couscous on Varoma tray. Place 400 g water in mixing bowl and insert simmering basket with vegetables. Set Varoma in position and steam 17 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  6. Transfer couscous to a large serving dish and gently fluff the couscous using a fork. Drizzle steamed vegetables with 1 tbsp olive oil and stir gently to coat. Remove simmering basket using spatula and arrange vegetables around the couscous. Serve hot, garnished with chopped parsley and reserved feta cheese.
