Cauliflower Cheese Bake

Ingredients

  • 3 garlic cloves, divided
  • 3 sprigs parsley
  • 3 oz bread
  • ½ tsp salt, divided, to taste
  • ½ tsp freshly ground black pepper, divided, to taste
  • 2 tsp olive oil, divided
  • 3 oz Gruyère cheese, aged, cut into pieces
  • 2 oz Parmesan cheese
  • 26 oz water, divided
  • 35 oz cauliflower, cut into florets (approx. 1½ lg. cauliflower)
  • 1 tsp vegetable stock paste
  • 10 oz low fat milk
  • ½ oz corn starch
  • 2 tsp Dijon mustard
  • ½ tsp dried thyme
  • 2 oz light cream cheese, e.g. Neufchâtel

Preparation

  1. Place 1 garlic clove, parsley, bread, ¼ tsp salt and ¼ tsp pepper into mixing bowl and grate 15 sec/speed 8. Add 1 tsp oil and mix 5 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a baking sheet and toast under broiler until golden brown, about 1½ minutes. Stir through for even cooking and toast a further 1½ minutes. Set aside to cool for 10 minutes.
  2. Place Gruyère and Parmesan cheeses into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Remove 2.5 oz of cheese and add to cooled bread crumbs, stir through and set aside. Set aside remaining cheese for sauce in a separate bowl.
  3. Place 18 oz water into mixing bowl, place Varoma dish into position and weigh in cauliflower, secure Varoma lid and steam 25 min/Varoma/speed 2. Set Varoma aside and discard water.
  4. Place 2 garlic cloves and 1 tsp oil into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7, then sauté 2 min/212°F/speed 2.
  5. Add stock paste, water, milk, corn starch, mustard and thyme, ¼ tsp salt and ¼ tsp pepper and cook 7 min/195°F/speed 3. Meanwhile adjust oven rack to highest position and preheat broiler to high setting.
  6. Add cream cheese and reserved grated cheese and mix 5 sec/speed 5. Adjust seasoning to taste.
  7. Arrange cauliflower in a casserole dish, cover with sauce and sprinkle with reserved bread crumb mixture. Place under the broiler for approximately 10 minutes or until golden brown. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

casserole dish (9 in. x 13 in.), baking sheet, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Oven can be used instead of broiler. Set oven to 400°F and bake until golden.
