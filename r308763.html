Vegan Mushroom and Spinach Lasagne

Ingredients

Pesto

  • 110 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 40 g nutritional yeast flakes
  • 30 g flaked almonds
  • 20 g fresh basil leaves
  • ½ lemon, freshly squeezed juice only
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt, plus extra to taste
  • ground black pepper, to taste

Tofu Ricotta

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 395 g firm tofu, drained, broken in pieces
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 20 g water
  • ½ lemon, freshly squeezed juice only
  • 2 Tbsp nutritional yeast flakes
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt

Mushroom and Spinach Filling

  • 10 g olive oil
  • 450 g chestnut mushrooms, thinly sliced
  • 250 g fresh baby spinach
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt

Marinara Sauce

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 100 g onions, quartered
  • 100 g carrots, cut in pieces
  • 10 g olive oil
  • 1 ½ tsp dried thyme
  • 1 pinch chilli flakes (optional)
  • 1 pinch caster sugar
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 800 g tinned chopped tomatoes

Assembly

  • 12 dried lasagne pasta sheets (see tip)

Preparation

Pesto

  1. Place a jug on mixing bowl lid and weigh in 100 g oil. Set jug aside.
  2. Place nutritional yeast, almonds, basil, 10 g oil, lemon juice, garlic and salt in mixing bowl then blend 10 sec/speed 7.
  3. Without measuring cup, set speed 3 and drizzle in reserved oil through hole in mixing bowl lid until a loose pesto forms (you may not need the full amount). Season to taste then transfer to a bowl and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.

Tofu Ricotta

  1. Place garlic in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add tofu, oil, water, lemon juice, nutritional yeast and salt then mix 10 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Rinse mixing bowl.

Mushroom and Spinach Filling

  1. Place oil in mixing bowl and heat 3 min/100°C/speed 0.5.
  2. Add mushrooms and sauté 3 min/100°C//speed 0.5.
  3. Add spinach and salt then, with aid of spatula, sauté 3 min/98°C//speed  until spinach is wilted.
  4. Add 2 Tbsp reserved pesto then stir 10 sec//speed 2. Drain in simmering basket then set aside. Clean mixing bowl.

Marinara Sauce

  1. Place garlic, onions, carrots and oil in mixing bowl then chop 4 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then sauté 5 min/100°C/speed .
  2. Add thyme, chilli flakes (if using), sugar, salt and pepper then mix 10 sec/speed 3.
  3. Add tomatoes and, with simmering basket in place of measuring cup, simmer 20 min/98°C/speed .

Assembly

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C.
  2. Pour enough marinara sauce into a baking dish to cover the base. Cover with a layer of 3 lasagne sheets. Spread with ⅓ tofu ricotta followed by ⅓ mushroom and spinach filling. Pour over ¼ marinara sauce then dot with 3 tsp pesto.
  3. Repeat layers twice more, finishing with 3 lasagne sheets and remaining marinara sauce. Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes (190°C). Remove foil and bake for a further 20-30 minutes (190°C) until edges are golden.
  4. Remove from oven and leave to stand for 10 minutes before serving, dotted with spoonfuls of remaining pesto.

Useful Items

aluminium foil, baking dish, jug, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Creamy white sauce made from tofu? Zingy, vibrant pesto made without Parmesan? Thanks to the immensely flavourful ingredients and some clever substitutions, you won’t miss the meat or dairy in this hearty vegan lasagne.
  • Most dried pasta should be egg free and therefore suitable for a vegan diet. For a non-vegan diet, dried lasagne sheets can be substituted with fresh lasagne sheets if preferred.
