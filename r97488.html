Savoury herb baked ricotta

Ingredients

Ricotta

  • 1800 g full cream milk
  • 80 g white vinegar

Grain free crackers

  • 30 g chia seeds
  • 120 g water
  • 280 g raw almonds
  • 60 g sunflower seeds
  • 30 g linseeds
  • 1 tsp sea salt flakes
  • 1 tsp granulated garlic
  • ½ - 1 tsp dried chilli flakes
  • sea salt flakes, for sprinkling
  • 30 g sesame seeds

Baked ricotta

  • oil, for greasing
  • 120 g Parmesan cheese, crust removed and cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 1 egg
  • 2 - 3 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves only
  • ½ tsp dried chilli flakes
  • 1 - 2 pinches salt
  • 1 - 2 pinches ground black pepper

Preparation

Ricotta

  1. Place milk into mixing bowl and heat 15 min/80°C/speed 2.
  2. Add vinegar and heat 7 min/90°C/speed 1. Allow to sit in mixing bowl for 2 minutes. Milk will separate into curds and whey.
  3. Line simmering basket with 2 layers of muslin cloth or a nut milk bag and set over a large bowl. Pour mixture into simmering basket and allow to drain for 30 minutes. Do not squeeze liquid from ricotta. Set drained ricotta (approx. 300-340 g) aside in a bowl. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Grain free crackers

  1. Place a small bowl onto mixing bowl lid, then weigh chia seeds and water into it. Set bowl aside for 1 hour.
  2. Preheat oven to 170°C. Line 2 baking trays with baking paper and set aside.
  3. Place almonds, sunflower seeds, linseeds, sea salt flakes, granulated garlic and chilli flakes into mixing bowl and mix 20 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add reserved soaked chia seed mixture and mix 20 sec/speed 4.
  5. Place 1 large piece of baking paper onto work surface and pour mixture onto baking paper. Top with another large sheet of baking paper and roll out mixture to approx. 2-3 mm thickness. Remove top sheet of baking paper and sprinkle with sesame seeds and additional sea salt flakes, and press down gently.
  6. Using a knife or pizza cutter, cut dough into triangles, transfer onto prepared baking trays and bake for 15-20 minutes (170°C), or until golden brown. Leave to cool on baking trays for 5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
  7. Increase oven temperature to 180°C. Clean and dry mixing bowl..

Baked ricotta

  1. Grease a ramekin (10-15 cm) or other small baking dish and set aside.
  2. Place Parmesan cheese into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 9.
  3. Add all remaining ingredients, including reserved ricotta, and mix 10 sec//speed 3. Transfer into prepared ramekin or baking dish and bake for 25-30 minutes (180°C), or until set and golden brown. Serve baked ricotta hot with grain free crackers.
