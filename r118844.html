Pear mulled wine

Ingredients

  • 750 g red wine (i.e. Cabernet Merlot or other medium-bodied wine)
  • 1 orange, zested and juiced
  • 4 pears, peeled, cored and cut into quarters
  • 150 g sugar
  • 200 g water
  • 1 pimento (see Tips)
  • 1 cardamom pod, crushed
  • 1 cinnamon quill
  • 4 black peppercorns
Nutrition
per 1 portions
Calories
574 kJ / 136 kcal

Preparation

  1. Place all ingredients into mixing bowl and cook 18 min/90°C//speed 1.
  2. Strain liquid through a fine-mesh sieve into serving glasses (see Tips). Serve warm.

Useful Items

8 serving glasses, fine mesh sieve

Hints & Tricks

  • Pimento is also known as allspice.
  • Reserve pears to serve with cream or ice cream. They also make a lovely topping for rice pudding.
  • Mulled wine will keep for up to 3 days in the refrigerator. It can be served both cold or warm.
