Mini Laxpuddings

Ingredients

  • 350 g waxy Charlotte potatoes, peeled, sliced (0.5 cm)
  • 1250 g water
  • butter, for greasing
  • 10 g fresh dill
  • 150 g whole milk
  • 50 g double cream
  • 2 medium eggs
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 130 g smoked salmon, cut in pieces

Preparation

  1. Insert simmering basket and weigh in potato slices. Pour over 500 g water then steam 13 min/Varoma/speed 1. Meanwhile, grease 4 individual ramekins (Ø 8 cm x 5 cm deep).
  2. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and set aside. Drain cooking water and allow mixing bowl to cool.
  3. Place dill in cooled mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a small bowl and set aside.
  4. Place milk, cream, eggs, salt and pepper in mixing bowl then mix 15 sec/speed 3.
  5. Divide a third of reserved cooked potatoes between ramekins, forming a layer. Form another layer with half of smoked salmon and half of reserved chopped dill. Top with another third of potatoes, followed by remaining salmon and chopped dill. Finish with remaining potatoes. Pour over custard and grind pepper on top. Place ramekins in Varoma dish and cover the surface of each one with a piece of baking paper.
  6. Place remaining 750 g water in mixing bowl, place covered Varoma into position then steam 40 min/Varoma/speed 1 or until custard is set (a knife inserted should come out clean). Set covered Varoma aside for puddings to rest for 10 minutes. Serve hot.

Useful Items

4 individual ramekins (Ø 8 cm), baking paper

Hints & Tricks

  • It is traditional to brush the tops with melted butter before serving.
  • These small pots are deceptively filling. Makes a nice lunch option for when you want to impress - serve with a rocket salad.
  • A Swedish classic that is traditionally baked in one large pot. Here we've converted it to mini versions and steamed them in the Varoma!
