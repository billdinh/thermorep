Fresh fruit punch

Ingredients

  • 200 g strawberries, hulled
  • 100 g water
  • 2 oranges, flesh only, cut into quarters
  • 600 g fresh pineapple pieces (3 cm)
  • 200 g ice cubes
  • 750 g lemonade (2 x 375 g cans)
Nutrition
per 1 portions
Calories
346 kJ / 82 kcal
Protein
0.4 g
Carbohydrates
19.1 g
Fat
0.2 g

Preparation

  1. Place strawberries and water into mixing bowl and purée 10 sec/speed 6, until smooth. Transfer into 2 ice cube trays. Place into freezer to set overnight.
  2. Place oranges, pineapple and ice cubes into mixing bowl and blend 2 min/speed 8. Transfer into a large serving bowl. Cover with plastic wrap and place into refrigerator until ready to serve.
  3. Just before serving, add lemonade and strawberry ice cubes, then stir to combine. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

2 ice cube trays, plastic wrap, large serving bowl, freezer, refrigerator
