Gorgonzola cheesecakes with pear jam

Ingredients

  • 60 g unsalted butter, plus extra for greasing
  • 10 g blanched almonds
  • 20 g buckwheat groats
  • 1 sprig fresh sage, leaves only
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 70 g walnuts
  • 80 g dried pear
  • 100 g cream cheese
  • 120 g Gorgonzola cheese (dolce or soft)
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 45 g balsamic vinegar
  • 40 g water
  • 25 g brown sugar
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • fresh thyme leaves, for garnishing

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C. Grease 12 mini loose-base tartlet tins (3.5 cm base) or 24 mini silicone mini muffin cases with butter.
  2. Place almonds, buckwheat groats and sage into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 8.
  3. Add butter, salt, 10 g of the walnuts and 10 g of the dried pear and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Press 1 teaspoon of the mixture into the base of each tartlet tin or silicone mini muffin case. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Place cream cheese, Gorgonzola, egg, lemon juice and remaining 60 g walnuts into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 4. Spoon 1½ teaspoons of the mixture into each mould over base mixture and bake for 15 minutes (160°C) or until filling is set. Transfer onto a wire rack to cool before removing from tins or moulds. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  5. Place remaining 70 g dried pears into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  6. Add vinegar, water, sugar and pepper and cook 5-8 min/100°C//speed 1, until thick and sticky. Top each cheesecake with ½ teaspoon of the pear jam and garnish with thyme leaves to serve.

Useful Items

mini loose-bottom tartlet tins (3.5 cm), wire rack, silicone mini muffin cases, oven
