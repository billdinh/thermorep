Clams with Garlic Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1000 g venus clams
  • 2 tsp fine sea salt
  • 70 g olive oil
  • 6 garlic cloves, large
  • 15 g mustard
  • 150 g water, plus extra for soaking
  • 15 g white wine
  • fresh coriander leaves, roughly chopped, for garnishing
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1272 kJ / 304 kcal
Protein
24 g
Carbohydrates
7 g
Fat
19 g

Preparation

  1. Place a large bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in clams. Remove bowl, cover clams with water then add salt. Leave to soak for approx. 2 hours to release the sand. After this time, drain clams in Varoma dish and set aside.
  2. Place oil in mixing bowl and heat 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  3. Add garlic, chop 5 sec/speed 5 then sauté 5 min/120°C/speed 2.
  4. Add mustard, water and wine. Place covered Varoma with clams into position and steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  5. Transfer clams to a serving dish, drizzle with sauce and garnish with coriander before serving hot.
