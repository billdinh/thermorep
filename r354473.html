Chicken, Basil and Roasted Red Pepper Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 80 g Parmesan cheese, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 - 2 fresh red chillies, halved, deseeded, to taste
  • 100 g onions, quartered
  • 40 g olive oil
  • 700 g roasted red peppers, preserved, drained, 350 g cut in pieces, 350 g halved then thinly sliced (1 cm)
  • 150 g water
  • 10 g fresh basil leaves, plus 10 g roughly chopped
  • 500 g chicken breast fillets, skinless, diced (2 cm)
  • 200 g double cream
  • 1 chicken stock cube (for 0.5 l), crumbled
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt, plus extra to taste
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper, plus extra to taste
  • 250 g dried spaghetti
  • 60 g toasted pine nuts

Preparation

  1. Place Parmesan in mixing bowl and grate 8 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place garlic, chillies, onions and oil in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then sauté 3 min/100°C/speed 1.
  3. Add 350 g peppers cut in pieces , 100 g water and 10 g basil then blend 1 min/speed 8.
  4. Add chicken, 50 g water, cream, chicken stock, salt and pepper then cook 10 min/100°C//speed .
  5. Add spaghetti through hole in mixing bowl lid and cook 13-14 min/100°C//speed 1 until pasta is al dente, inserting measuring cup once pasta disappears into bowl.
  6. Transfer to a large serving bowl and place on mixing bowl lid. Weigh into bowl remaining 350 g sliced peppers, 10 g roughly chopped basil and 40 g reserved grated Parmesan. Remove bowl from lid and stir gently to combine.
  7. Season to taste, garnish with pine nuts and sprinkle over remaining grated Parmesan. Serve immediately.
