Lime meringue tartlets

Ingredients

Pastry

  • 1 egg
  • 50 g caster sugar
  • 120 g unsalted butter, cut into pieces
  • 1 lime, zest only (no white pith)
  • 220 g plain flour

Lime curd

  • 120 g caster sugar
  • 3 fresh limes, juice only (approx. 60 g)
  • 1 tsp cornflour
  • 60 g unsalted butter, cut into pieces
  • 3 egg yolks

Meringue

  • 2 egg whites, room temperature
  • ¼ tsp cream of tartar
  • ½ tsp vanilla bean paste
  • 100 g icing sugar (see Tips)

Preparation

Pastry

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C and set aside 12 mini loose-bottom tartlet tins (3.5 cm base).
  2. Place egg and sugar into mixing bowl and mix 20 sec/speed 5.
  3. Add butter, lime zest and flour and mix 20 sec/speed 4. Transfer pastry onto a silicone bread mat or lightly floured work surface and shape into a ball. Wrap in plastic wrap and place into refrigerator to chill (approx. 30 minutes).
  4. Roll out pastry to approx. 5 mm thick. Using a round cookie cutter (10 cm), cut out 12 pastry rounds and press into mini tartlet tins. Dock each pastry case with a fork, line with baking paper and fill with pie weights, rice or dried beans. Bake for 12 minutes (180°C) or until dry to the touch. Set aside to cool completely, then remove from tins. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Lime curd

  1. Insert butterfly whisk. Place all Lime curd ingredients into mixing bowl and cook 20 min/80°C/speed 3. Remove butterfly whisk. Spoon 2 teaspoons of curd into base of each pastry case, then place onto a baking tray (30 x 40 cm). Thoroughly clean and dry mixing bowl and butterfly whisk.

Meringue

  1. Increase oven to 200°C. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg whites and cream of tartar into mixing bowl and whip 2 min/70°C/speed 3, without measuring cup, until stiff peaks form.
  2. Add vanilla paste and cook 2 min/60°C/speed 3, adding icing sugar 1 tablespoon at a time through hole in mixing bowl lid. Cook for a further 5 min/60°C/speed 3. Remove butterfly whisk.
  3. Transfer meringue into a piping bag fitted with a plain nozzle (1 cm) and decorate the top of each tart as desired. Bake for 10 minutes (200°C) or until meringue is lightly browned. Remove from oven and transfer onto a wire rack to cool completely (approx. 1 hour) before serving.

Useful Items

silicone bread mat, rolling pin, pie weights, rice or dried beans, round cookie cutter (10 cm), piping bag with a plain nozzle (1 cm), mini loose-bottom tartlet tins (3.5 cm), plastic wrap, baking paper, baking tray (30 x 40 cm), wire rack, fork, oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • To make your own icing sugar, please refer to the recipe in The Basic Cookbook or Everyday Cookbook.
  • To save time, make the lime curd while pastry cases are baking and cooling.
