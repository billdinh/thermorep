Minted Lamb Casserole with Steamed Vegetables

Ingredients

  • 5 sprigs fresh mint, leaves only
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 80 g red onions, quartered
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 400 g lamb meat for stew, boneless, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • ½ - 1 tsp fine sea salt, to taste
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 200 g new potatoes, cut in pieces (1 cm)
  • 160 g fresh green peas
  • 400 g tinned chopped tomatoes
  • 100 g water
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 300 g cauliflower florets (2-3 cm)
  • 200 g carrots, sliced (5 mm)

Preparation

  1. Place mint in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place garlic, onions and oil in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then sauté 4 min/120°C/speed .
  3. Add lamb, half the reserved chopped mint, salt and pepper then cook 10 min/120°C//speed .
  4. Add potatoes, peas, tomatoes, water and bay leaf then, with simmering basket in place of measuring cup, cook 10 min/100°C//speed .
  5. Place Varoma dish into position then weigh in cauliflower and carrots. Cover Varoma and steam 20-25 min/100°C//speed  until vegetables are cooked. Set covered Varoma aside.
  6. Add remaining reserved chopped mint and stir in by hand with spatula. Serve casserole hot with steamed vegetables.
