Purple Cauliflower Soup

Ingredients

  • 2 oz purple onion, cut into pieces
  • 1 garlic clove
  • ½ oz olive oil
  • 18 - 19 oz purple cauliflower, broken into florets, outer leaves to garnish
  • 29 oz unsweetened almond milk, plain
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • ½ tsp salt, to taste
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper, to taste
  • plain yogurt, to serve
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
494 kJ / 118 kcal
Protein
5 g
Carbohydrates
8 g
Fat
9 g

Preparation

  1. Place onion, garlic and oil into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5, then cook 5 min/250°F/speed 1.
  2. Add cauliflower, almond milk, lemon juice, salt and pepper and chop 8 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and cook 14 min/195°F/speed 1. Blend 1 min/speed 3-10, increasing speed gradually.
  3. Adjust seasoning, garnish with a swirl of yogurt and serve hot.
