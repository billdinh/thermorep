Steamed Beef with Ground Rice

Ingredients

Beef

  • 150 g rice
  • 2 Tbsp chilli sauce
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • ½ tsp Sichuan pepper
  • 4 spring onions, cut in lengths (3 cm) (approx. 45 g)
  • 15 g fresh root ginger, peeled, cut in round slices (2 mm)
  • 40 g tamari
  • 10 g vegetable oil
  • 600 g beef, topside, sliced (1 cm)

To Cook

  • 800 g water
  • 240 g rice
  • 1 iceberg lettuce (approx. 400 g)
  • sesame oil, for serving
  • 2 spring onions, finely chopped, for garnish

Preparation

Beef

  1. Place 150 g rice in mixing bowl and grind 40 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl then dry roast 4 min/Varoma/speed 3. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place chilli sauce, salt, sugar, Sichuan pepper, spring onions, ginger, tamari and vegetable oil in mixing bowl then blend 15 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides and lid of mixing bowl then blend again 15 sec/speed 5.
  3. Using a rolling pin, flatten beef between pieces of baking paper until ½ cm thick. Cut into pieces no larger than 2 cm x 2 cm. Place in a large bowl, add marinade and stir to coat. Set aside to marinate for 20 minutes.

To Cook

  1. Place 800 g water in mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket and weigh in 240 g rice. Rinse 5 sec/speed 5 then steam 15 min/Varoma/speed 2.5. Meanwhile, line Varoma dish and tray with lettuce leaves, leaving some holes visible so steam can circulate. Coat beef pieces with reserved ground rice and arrange on lettuce leaves.
  2. Place covered Varoma into position and steam rice and beef for a further 6 min/Varoma/speed 2.5, prolong time if necessary to cook beef to taste. Serve with a drizzle of sesame oil and a scattering of finely chopped spring onions.

Useful Items

large bowl, long sharp knife, baking paper, rolling pin

Hints & Tricks

  • This Sichuan beef dish is quite quick and perfect for a simple supper, complete with rice.
