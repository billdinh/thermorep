Butternut Squash and Chestnut Rolls

Ingredients

Roasted Butternut Squash

  • 800 g butternut squash, peeled, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • extra virgin olive oil, for drizzling
  • 1 pinch dried chilli flakes
  • fine sea salt, to taste
  • ground black pepper, to taste

Puff Pastry

  • 200 g unsalted butter, diced (1-2 cm) then frozen for 30 minutes
  • 200 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 90 g water, cold
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt

Filling

  • 50 g Parmesan cheese, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 5 - 6 fresh sage leaves, to taste
  • 200 g cooked, peeled chestnuts
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 100 g onions, quartered
  • 30 g extra virgin olive oil
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt

Assembly

  • 1 medium egg, beaten

Preparation

Roasted Butternut Squash

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C. Place butternut squash pieces in a roasting tin and drizzle with oil. Season with chilli flakes, salt and pepper to taste, then roast for 35-40 minutes (200°C) until soft and browned around the edges. Meanwhile, make pastry.

Puff Pastry

  1. Place butter, flour, water and salt in mixing bowl then mix 20 sec/speed 6. Tip dough out, shape into a ball and flatten to a rough square. Wrap in cling film and place in fridge for 20 minutes.
  2. On a lightly-floured surface, roll out chilled dough to a long rectangle 3 times as long as it is wide (approx. 60 cm x 20 cm). Fold bottom third up to cover middle third and top third down over this. Seal edges then give dough a quarter turn so that the fold is on the left. Repeat rolling and folding process twice more.
  3. Wrap in cling film and place in freezer for 5 minutes. Meanwhile, preheat oven to 200°C and line 2 baking trays with baking paper.

Filling

  1. Place Parmesan in mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place sage and chestnuts in mixing bowl then chop 2 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place garlic, onions, oil and salt in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then sauté 2 min/120°C/speed 1.
  4. Add reserved chopped sage and chestnuts then sauté 2 min/120°C/speed 1.
  5. Add reserved roasted butternut squash and reserved grated Parmesan then mix 10 sec/speed 3.

Assembly

  1. Roll pastry out on a well-floured surface into a rectangle (40 cm x 50 cm x 5 mm). Cut widthways into 3 equal-sized pieces then brush down one long side of each piece of pastry with beaten egg.
  2. Place filling down the centre of each piece of pastry then fold over to enclose, using a fork to seal securely. Cut each log into 8 equal-sized pieces, brush surfaces with beaten egg then slash the top of each piece twice diagonally with a knife. Transfer to prepared trays.
  3. Bake for 20-25 minutes (200°C) until browned. Leave to cool on trays for 10 minutes before transferring to a wire rack. Serve warm or cold.

Useful Items

cling film, rolling pin, pastry brush, wire rack, baking trays, baking paper, roasting tin, refrigerator, freezer, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Flaky pastry wrapped around a delicious filling - there’s a reason sausage rolls are so popular! This meat-free version combines colourful and sweet butternut squash with earthy sage and chestnuts for a savoury snack that’s bursting with flavour.
  • A great make-ahead recipe, unbaked rolls can be frozen in an airtight container, then defrosted fully and baked when needed.
