Green fig and lime marmalade

Ingredients

  • 1000 g boiling water
  • 2 limes, cut into very thin slices (2-3 mm), then cut into halves and deseeded
  • 50 g lime juice (approx. 2-3 limes)
  • 1000 g green figs, cut into halves and stems removed
  • 500 g raw sugar

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and carefully weigh 500 g of the boiling water into it. Add sliced limes and soak for 5 minutes. Using simmering basket, strain limes and place back into bowl on mixing bowl lid. Carefully weigh remaining 500g boiling water into bowl with limes and soak for a further 5 minutes. Strain and set aside.
  2. Place lime juice, figs and sugar into mixing bowl and cook 30 min/100°C/speed 2.
  3. Add soaked limes and cook 25 min/Varoma/speed 1, or until set (see Tips), placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  4. Allow to cool, then serve or transfer into 4 sterilised jam jars and seal (see Tips).

Useful Items

bowl, 4 jam jars with twist-off lids

Hints & Tricks

  • When in season, use ripe black figs and reduce sugar to 350 g.
  • Setting point test. Place saucer into freezer (5-10 minutes). Place 1 tsp jam onto chilled saucer. When jam is cool, push it with your finger. If jam wrinkles, it is ready. If not, cook for a further 2-3 minutes, then test again.
  • You can store jarred jam in the refrigerator for 3-4 weeks.
