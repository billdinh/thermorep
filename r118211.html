Pasta

Ingredients

  • 150 g lupin flour
  • 70 g gluten free cornflour, plus extra for dusting
  • 1 ½ tsp xanthan gum
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 60 g water, plus extra for boiling

Preparation

  1. Place lupin, cornflour, xanthan gum, nutmeg and 1 teaspoon of the salt into mixing bowl and mix 5 sec/speed 4.
  2. Add eggs and water and knead 2 min/. Transfer dough onto a silicone bread mat or lightly floured work surface and shape into a ball. Wrap dough in silicone bread mat or plastic wrap and set aside for a minimum of 10 minutes.
  3. Divide dough into 4 even pieces and use a pasta machine to roll out (see Tips). Cut into required shapes and lengths. Use cornflour to dust dough as necessary if it becomes sticky.
  4. Place a large saucepan over high heat. Place water (approx. 3L) and remaining 1 teaspoon salt into saucepan and bring to the boil. Working in batches, add pasta into saucepan and cook for 4-5 minutes, or until al dente. Use simmering basket to drain pasta. Serve immediately with your favourite sauce. 

Useful Items

silicone bread mat, plastic wrap, pasta machine, large saucepan, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • You can buy lupin flour at specialty food stores, health food stores or online.
  • If you do not have a pasta machine, pasta can be rolled out thinly and cut by hand.
  • If using a pasta machine, gluten free pasta should not be rolled any thinner than setting NO. 4 on your pasta machine.
