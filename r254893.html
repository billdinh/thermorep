Morello Cherry Brownies

Ingredients

  • 120 g white chocolate, in small pieces or callets
  • 180 g dark chocolate, in small pieces or callets
  • 200 g unsalted butter, diced, plus extra for greasing
  • 120 g granulated sugar
  • 80 g caster sugar
  • 70 g black treacle
  • 4 medium eggs
  • 200 g plain flour
  • 30 g cocoa powder
  • 150 g morello cherries, tinned, in syrup, drained

Preparation

  1. Place white chocolate in mixing bowl and grate 4 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a large bowl and set aside.
  2. Place dark chocolate in mixing bowl and grate 8 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add butter, granulated sugar, caster sugar and black treacle then melt 6 min/50째C/speed 2. Allow temperature to cool to 37째C (approx. 10 minutes). Meanwhile, preheat oven to 180째C then grease and line base of a square cake tin (23 cm) with baking paper.
  4. Add eggs, plain flour and cocoa powder then mix 15 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Mix again 10 sec/speed 4. Transfer mixture and morello cherries to large bowl with white chocolate and gently mix with spatula. Pour into prepared tin then bake for 25 minutes (180째C).
  6. Leave to cool in tin before removing. Cut into 20 pieces and serve.

Useful Items

baking paper, square cake tin (23 cm), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Brownies will keep for 3 days wrapped in foil.
  • If you do not have a square cake tin, brownies can be baked in a baking tray.
  • A skewer test after baking is not necessary as the cake should be moist.
