Pearl Barley Risotto with Mushrooms

Ingredients

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 50 g olive oil
  • 400 g mixed fresh mushrooms (brown and white), sliced
  • 10 g soy sauce
  • 3 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves only, plus extra for garnish
  • 1 dried bay leaf
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 100 g onions, quartered
  • 40 g white wine
  • 250 g pearl barley
  • 800 g water
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1498 kJ / 358 kcal
Protein
7 g
Carbohydrates
49 g
Fat
14 g

Preparation

  1. Place garlic and 30 g olive oil in mixing bowl, chop 3 sec/speed 8 then sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  2. Add mushrooms, soy sauce, thyme, bay leaf and ½ tsp salt then cook 7 min/120°C//speed . Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place onions and remaining 20 g olive oil in mixing bowl, chop 5 sec/speed 5 then sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  4. Add wine and cook 2 min/120°C/speed 1.
  5. Add pearl barley, water and remaining ½ tsp salt then cook 30 min/100°C//speed .
  6. Drain liquid from reserved mushrooms then stir into pearl barley with spatula. Serve sprinkled with thyme leaves.
