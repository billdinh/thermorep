Spicy Hot Chocolate with Almond Milk

Ingredients

  • 8 oz almonds, skin on
  • 3 ½ oz Mexican chocolate, cut into 1 in. pieces
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • ½ tsp vanilla sugar
  • ¼ tsp ground nutmeg
  • &frac18; tsp ground cayenne pepper, to taste
  • 24 oz water
Nutrition
per 1 portions
Calories
1937 kJ / 463 kcal
Protein
14 g
Carbohydrates
28 g
Fat
36 g

Preparation

  1. Place almonds, chocolate, cinnamon stick, vanilla sugar, nutmeg and cayenne into mixing bowl and chop 20 sec/speed 9.
  2. Add water and mix 2 min/speed 9.
  3. Transfer into a nut milk bag and squeeze by hand into a pitcher or large bowl. Discard solids.
  4. Place strained almond milk into mixing bowl and heat 6 min/195°F/speed 2. Froth 20 sec/speed 4. Serve hot.

Useful Items

nut milk bag, pitcher

Hints & Tricks

  • To make your own vanilla sugar: place 1 vanilla bean pod, cut into pieces and 7 oz. sugar into a jar with a twist-off lid. Close jar and shake to mix vanilla pieces with sugar. Allow to dry for 2-3 days. Place jar contents into mixing bowl and grind 20 sec/speed 10.
