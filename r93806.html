Citrus salt

Ingredients

  • 10 g lemon zest (approx.1 lemon), no white pith
  • 10 g lime zest (approx. 2 limes), no white pith
  • 250 g sea salt
Nutrition
per 10 g
Calories
1 kJ / 0 kcal
Protein
0 g
Carbohydrates
0 g
Fat
0 g

Preparation

  1. Place lemon and lime zest onto a baking tray (20 x 30 cm) lined with baking paper, then dehydrate in the oven for 20 minutes at 120°C, or until dry but not discoloured. Set zest aside to cool (approx. 10 minutes).
  2. Place cooled lemon and lime zest into mixing bowl and mill 20 sec/speed 10, until a fine consistency is achieved.
  3. Add ½ of the sea salt and chop 6 sec/speed 7.
  4. Add remaining sea salt and chop 2 sec/speed 7. Transfer citrus salt into a sealable container and store in a cool, dark place until ready to use.

Useful Items

baking tray (20 x 30 cm), baking paper, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Use citrus salt to season chicken, seafood or any dish you want to add a tangy citrus flavour to.
  • Citrus salt makes a lovely table gift at Christmas time. Transfer into small glass jars and decorate with festive ribbon or gift tags to give to friends and family.
