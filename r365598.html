Breakfast Eggs with Bacon and Roasted Peppers

Ingredients

  • 12 slices bacon
  • 120 g roasted red peppers, preserved, drained, sliced (5 mm)
  • 6 pitted black olives, halved (optional)
  • 6 medium eggs
  • fine sea salt, to taste
  • ground black pepper, to taste
  • 500 g water
Nutrition

Preparation

  1. Arrange 2 slices bacon in the base of 6 ThermomixÂ® ramekins size 1 in a cross. Top with red peppers and olives (if using). Break an egg into each ramekin. Season well with salt and pepper then cross overhanging bacon slices across the top of each egg. Cover each ramekin with heat-resistant cling film and place in Varoma dish.
  2. Place water in mixing bowl, place covered Varoma into position and steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 1 (see tip). Remove Varoma and carefully remove cling film. To serve, tip out breakfast eggs onto serving plates or toasted bread.

Hints & Tricks

  • Cooking time for the eggs is based on refrigerated, medium eggs cooked waxy. Adjust cooking time to cook eggs according to personal preference.
