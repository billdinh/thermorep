Eggplant and tomato breakfast hash with crispy tempeh

Ingredients

  • 2 sprigs fresh mint, leaves only
  • 1 sprig fresh basil, leaves only
  • 1 eggplant (approx. 400 g), cut into pieces (approx. 2 cm)
  • ¼ tsp salt, plus extra for sprinkling
  • 25 g extra virgin olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 2 tsp tomato paste
  • 1 - 2 dashes Tabasco® sauce
  • 1 - 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 250 g cherry tomatoes
  • 1 pinch sugar
  • 300 g tempeh (marinated or plain), cut into thin slices (approx. 5 mm)
  • 4 slices toast

Preparation

  1. Place mint and basil into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Line a baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with 2 layers of paper towel. Place eggplant onto paper towel, sprinkle generously with salt, then set aside for approx. 20 minutes to extract bitter juices. Rinse and pat dry on both sides with paper towel (see Tips) and set aside.
  3. Place oil and garlic into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and cook 2 min/120°C/speed 2.
  5. Add reserved eggplant and cook 3 min/120°C//speed 1.
  6. Add tomato paste and Tabasco® sauce and cook 3 min/120°C//speed 1.
  7. Add pepper, tomatoes, sugar, ¼ teaspoon of the salt and reserved herbs, then mix 2 min/120°C//speed 1.
  8. Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and lightly grease with oil. Fry tempeh for 1-2 minutes each side, until golden brown and slightly crispy.
  9. Divide hash and tempeh between serving plates and serve on toast.

Useful Items

bowl, baking tray (30 x 40 cm), paper towel, non-stick frying pan, serving plates, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • When drying eggplant, press down to extract as much liquid as possible.
  • To make this recipe gluten free, serve hash with gluten free toast.
