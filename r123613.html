Moroccan Quorn with Bulgar Wheat

Ingredients

Spiced Quorn

  • 70 g onions, quartered
  • 25 g olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 10 g fresh root ginger, peeled, cut in round slices (2 mm)
  • 350 g Quorn mince
  • 1 Tbsp ground cumin
  • 1 Tbsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 2 tsp ground turmeric
  • 550 g water
  • 250 g tinned chopped tomatoes
  • 1 vegetable stock cube (for 0.5 l)
  • 10 dried apricots, pitted, halved

Bulgar Wheat

  • 200 g bulgar wheat
  • 5 sprigs fresh mint, leaves only, chopped
  • 1 dash olive oil
  • 2 pinches fine sea salt, or to taste
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper, or to taste
  • 30 g lemon juice

Preparation

Spiced Quorn

  1. Place onions and oil in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Sauté 5 min/100°C/speed .
  2. Add garlic and ginger then chop 3 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add Quorn, cumin, coriander, cinnamon and turmeric then fry 3 min/100°C//speed . Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add water, tomatoes, stock cube and apricots. Place simmering basket instead of measuring cup on mixing bowl lid and cook 20 min/98°C//speed . Meanwhile, prepare bulgar wheat.

Bulgar Wheat

  1. Cook bulgar wheat on hob according to packet instructions.
  2. Fluff up bulgar wheat with a fork. Add mint, olive oil, salt, pepper and lemon juice to bulgar wheat then mix using spatula. Serve spiced Quorn on top of some bulgar wheat, with a little yoghurt or harissa on the side.
