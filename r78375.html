Beetroot gazpacho with horseradish créme

Ingredients

Beetroot gazpacho

  • 300 g raw beetroot, peeled and cut into pieces
  • 100 g potatoes, peeled and cut into pieces
  • 1 tbsp Vegetable stock paste (see Tips)
  • 500 g water
  • 1 Lebanese cucumber, cut into pieces
  • 1 - 2 spring onions/shallots, trimmed and cut into pieces
  • 250 g natural yoghurt
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice, plus extra to taste
  • 100 g water, chilled
  • 1 pinch sea salt, to taste
  • 1 pinch ground white pepper, to taste

Horseradish créme

  • 100 g natural yoghurt
  • 1 - 2 tbsp jarred pure horseradish, grated
  • 1 - 1 ½ tbsp fresh chives, finely chopped, to serve

Preparation

Beetroot gazpacho

  1. Place beetroot and potato into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add stock paste and water and cook 15 min/100°C/speed 2. Allow to cool completely in mixing bowl before proceeding (approx. 1 hour).
  3. Add cucumber, spring onions/shallots, yoghurt, lemon juice and chilled water and blend 1 min - 1 min 30 sec/speed 7, or until smooth. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Season to taste with salt and pepper and check soup consistency. If you would prefer a thinner soup, add an additional 50 g chilled water, then mix 5 sec/speed 5.
  5. Transfer gazpacho into a bowl and place in refrigerator for 15 minutes to chill. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Horseradish créme

  1. Place yoghurt and horseradish into mixing bowl and combine 10 sec/speed 3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then mix 5 sec/speed 3.
  2. Serve gazpacho chilled, topped with horseradish créme and a sprinkle of chives.
