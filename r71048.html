Mango cheesecake

Ingredients

Almond and macadamia base

  • 40 g butter, plus extra for greasing
  • 120 g blanched almonds
  • 120 g macadamia nuts
  • 1 pinch sea salt
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup
  • 40 g desiccated coconut

Cheesecake

  • 5 titanium strength gelatine leaves
  • 120 g coconut sugar
  • 1 mango, flesh only (approx. 260 g)
  • 500 g cream cheese, softened and cut into pieces
  • 300 g pouring (whipping) cream

Mango coulis

  • 20 g coconut sugar
  • 200 g mango, flesh only, plus extra finely sliced for garnishing
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Preparation

Almond and macadamia base

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.  Grease and line a springform cake tin (20 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place almonds and macadamias into mixing bowl and chop 4-6 sec/speed 6, or until evenly chopped.
  3. Add salt, maple syrup, coconut and 40 g butter and mix 20 sec/speed 4, or until combined.
  4. Transfer mixture into prepared cake tin, then using the back of a spoon, press down firmly to form a base.
  5. Bake for 12-16 minutes (180°C) or until lightly brown.  Set base aside in tin and allow to cool. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Cheesecake

  1. Place gelatine leaves into a small bowl of cold water and set aside to soak for 5 minutes. Squeeze excess water from gelatine leaves and set aside. Discard soak water.
  2. Place coconut sugar into mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 9.
  3. Add mango and cream cheese and mix 30 sec/speed 5, with aid of spatula.
  4. Add cream and squeezed gelatine leaves and combine 40 sec/speed 5 or until gelatine is dissolved.
  5. Pour mixture evenly over base in tin and set aside in refrigerator to set (approx. 5 hours).

Mango coulis

  1. Place coconut sugar into mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 9.
  2. Add mango and lemon juice and blend 15 sec/speed 7.
  3. Spread mango coulis over cheesecake and place into refrigerator to set for 30 minutes.
  4. Garnish with extra finely sliced mango and serve chilled.

Useful Items

baking paper, springform cake tin (20 cm), oven, refrigerator
