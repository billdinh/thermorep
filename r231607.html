Chicken Fajita Soup

Ingredients

  • 4 corn tortillas, cut into strips (¼ in. x 1 in.)
  • 10 sprigs cilantro, leaves and tender stems only
  • 8 oz white onions, halved
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp fajita seasoning, homemade or packaged
  • 1 oz extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 - 2 jalapeño peppers, cut into rings
  • 32 oz water
  • 4 heaping tsp chicken stock paste
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 8 oz canned diced tomatoes
  • 5 oz canned hominy
  • 12 oz chicken breast fillets, boneless, skinless, cut into pieces (1 in.)
  • 2 tbsp freshly squeezed lime juice, to taste

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Place tortilla strips on a baking sheet and bake for 10-15 minutes (350°F) or until golden brown. Set aside and allow to cool.
  2. Place cilantro into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place onions, garlic, fajita seasoning and olive oil into mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 5. Add jalapeño rings and sauté 8 min/212°F/speed 1.
  4. Add water, chicken stock paste, salt, tomatoes and hominy. Place Varoma into position, weigh in chicken, secure Varoma lid and steam 25 min/Varoma/speed 2. Remove Varoma dish and place onto inverted Varoma lid. Transfer soup to a serving bowl.
  5. Place chicken into mixing bowl and shred 4 sec//speed 4. Transfer into soup and combine with spatula.
  6. Garnish with reserved chopped cilantro, lime juice and reserved baked tortilla strips. Serve hot.

Useful Items

baking sheet, serving bowl, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Use the fajita seasoning recipe from the Tex-Mex Cookbook or use a store-bought version.
