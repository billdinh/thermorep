Spiced apple liqueur

Ingredients

  • 2 Granny Smith apples, peeled and cored
  • 1 cinnamon quill
  • 1 whole clove
  • 1 star anise
  • 250 g honey, plus extra to taste
  • 700 g vodka
  • ice cubes, to serve

per 1 portion

3935 kJ / 937 kcal

Preparation

  1. Place apples and cinnamon into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 9.
  2. Add clove, star anise, honey and vodka and mix 30 sec/speed 4.
  3. Transfer into a clean, sealable jar and store in a cool, dark place for a minimum of 1 week. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Line simmering basket with a nut milk bag or muslin cloth and place over a jug. Strain liquid and discard solids. Add more honey, if desired. Serve immediately, over ice or mixed with soda, or transfer into a sterilised jar or bottle to further mature for 1 week (see Tips).

Useful Items

sealable jar or bottle, nut milk bag or muslin cloth, jug

Hints & Tricks

  • Further maturation will result in a mellower drink.
  • The type of honey used will have a significant effect on the final flavour; use a floral honey for an aromatic result.
  • Liqueur will keep indefinitely in a cool, dark place.
