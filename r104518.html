Panna Cotta with Peaches and Thyme Syrup

Ingredients

Panna Cotta

  • 250 g whipping cream
  • 250 g plain yoghurt
  • 60 g sugar
  • 4 gelatine leaves
  • 30 g almond liqueur (e.g. Amaretto)

Peach Topping

  • 15 - 20 g lemon juice, to taste
  • 30 g soft brown sugar
  • 2 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves only
  • 1 peach, peeled, stoned, sliced

Preparation

Panna Cotta

  1. Place cream, yoghurt and sugar in mixing bowl then heat 6 min/90°C/speed 2. Meanwhile, place gelatine leaves in a bowl of cold water and set aside to soak for 5 minutes, then squeeze out to remove excess water.
  2. Add almond liqueur and softened gelatine then mix 15 sec/speed 6. Pour into 6 individual moulds then, once cool, cover and place in fridge until set (approx. 6 hours). Meanwhile, make topping.

Peach Topping

  1. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid then weigh in lemon juice, brown sugar, thyme and peach slices. Remove bowl then stir together with spatula, making sure all fruit is coated, then cover and set aside to marinate while panna cottas set.

To Serve

  1. Once panna cottas are set, dip moulds in warm water (5-10 seconds) to loosen, then invert onto plates. Top with peach slices and syrup then serve.

Useful Items

6 individual moulds, cling film, refrigerator
