Mushroom Risotto

Ingredients

  • 40 g Parmesan cheese, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 20 g fresh parsley, cut in pieces
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 60 g onions, quartered
  • 300 g fresh mushrooms
  • 200 g dry white wine
  • 250 g risotto rice (e.g. arborio)
  • 450 g boiling water
  • 1 vegetable stock cube (for 0.5 l)
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 60 g water, hot
  • 20 g unsalted butter

Preparation

  1. Place Parmesan and parsley in mixing bowl then grate 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place oil, onions and 150 g mushrooms in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then cook 3 min/100°C/speed 1.
  3. Cut remaining 150 g mushrooms into slices (5 mm). Add wine, sliced mushrooms and rice then, without measuring cup, cook 2 min/100°C//speed .
  4. Add boiling water, vegetable stock, salt and pepper then cook 13 min/100°C//speed . Check the texture - the rice should be al dente (with a bite); if not yet cooked enough, cook again 1-2 min/100°C//speed  until al dente.
  5. Add hot water, butter and reserved Parmesan mixture. Stir in gently with spatula - it should be quite soupy at this stage as the mixture will absorb more liquid as it settles. Leave to rest for 2 minutes. Serve immediately.
