Pasta Salad with Salami

Ingredients

  • 8 - 10 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 100 g red peppers, cut in pieces
  • 100 g green peppers, cut in pieces
  • 200 g red onions, (100 g quartered, 100 g thinly sliced)
  • 100 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 tsp brown sugar
  • 2 tsp fine sea salt
  • 3 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 Tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1000 g water
  • 300 g dry pasta, coloured
  • 1 fennel bulb, thinly sliced (180 g)
  • 4 celery stalk, thinly sliced (150 g)
  • 200 g salami, cut in thin strips

Preparation

  1. Place parsley in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place red peppers, green peppers, 100 g quartered red onions, olive oil, garlic, brown sugar, 1 tsp salt, lemon juice and mustard in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 5. Transfer dressing to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place water in mixing bowl and heat 10 min/100°C/speed 1.
  4. Add pasta and 1 tsp salt then cook select time indicated on packet/100°C//speed . Drain pasta, rinse briefly with cold water to stop the cooking, then transfer to a serving bowl.
  5. Place serving bowl on mixing bowl lid. Weigh in remaining 100 g sliced red onions, fennel, celery and salami. Remove serving bowl from lid. Just before serving, toss salad with reserved dressing and sprinkle with reserved chopped parsley.
