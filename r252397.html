Prawn and noodle stir-fry

Ingredients

Noodles and sauce

  • 250 g dried egg noodles
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 3 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 1 - 2 fresh chillies, to taste and deseeded, if preferred
  • 4 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves, for garnishing, and stalks
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • ½ tsp ground turmeric (see Tips)
  • ½ tsp ground cumin
  • 3 tsp fish sauce
  • 2 tsp raw sugar
  • 2 tsp lime juice
  • 165 g coconut milk

Prawn stir-fry

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 brown onion, cut into wedges and layers separated
  • ½ red capsicum, deseeded and cut into thin slices
  • 1 carrot, cut into matchsticks
  • 150 g zucchini, cut into batons (5 x 1 cm)
  • 350 g medium raw prawns, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 - 2 pinches ground white pepper, to taste

Preparation

Noodles and sauce

  1. Cook noodles as per packet instruction. Drain and transfer into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and set aside. Meanwhile, continue with recipe.
  2. Place garlic, ginger, chilli and coriander stalks into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down side mixing bowl with aid of spatula.
  3. Add oil, turmeric, cumin, fish sauce, sugar, lime juice and coconut milk and heat 6 min/90°C/speed 1. Transfer into bowl with noodles and stir to combine. Cover to keep warm. Do not clean mixing bowl.

Prawn stir-fry

  1. Place garlic, ginger and oil into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7.
  2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with aid of spatula and cook 1 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Add onion, capsicum, carrot and zucchini and cook 2 min 30 sec/Varoma//speed 1.
  4. Add prawns, soy sauce and pepper and cook 3-4 min/Varoma//speed 1 or until prawns and vegetables are cooked to your liking. Serve over reserved noodles and garnish with coriander leaves.

Useful Items

thermal serving bowl or other large bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • For the best result, prepare all ingredients before starting recipe.
  • You can replace the ground turmeric with a 1-2 cm piece of peeled fresh turmeric.
