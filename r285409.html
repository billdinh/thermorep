Chicken Enchiladas

Ingredients

  • 7 oz panela cheese, cut into pieces
  • 23 oz green tomatillos, halved
  • 2 oz white onion
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 oz canned chipotle chilies, in adobo sauce
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 16 oz chicken breast fillets, boneless, skinless, cut lengthwise
  • salt, to taste
  • ground black pepper, to taste
  • oil, to fry
  • 12 corn tortillas
  • sour cream, to garnish

Preparation

  1. Place cheese into mixing bowl and grate 3 sec/speed 4. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place tomatillos, onion, garlic, chipotle and salt into mixing bowl and blend 20 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh in chicken. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Secure Varoma lid and cook 20 min/Varoma/speed 1. Set Varoma aside. Pour sauce into a bowl and set aside.
  4. Without washing the mixing bowl, place cooked chicken into it and shred 5 sec//speed 4.
  5. Heat a frying pan over high heat and add oil. Fry tortillas until warm (approx. 2-3 sec.). Drain on paper towels.
  6. Fill warm tortillas with shredded chicken and fold seam side down. Pour reserved sauce on top and finish with reserved grated cheese and sour cream. Serve warm.

Useful Items

frying pan, paper towels

Hints & Tricks

  • If sauce is too tart add ¼ tsp baking soda or a pinch of sugar and mix 3 sec/speed 4.
