Hash Browns

Ingredients

  • 1000 g floury potatoes, peeled, cut in pieces (approx. 5 cm)
  • 170 g onions, quartered
  • 170 g green apples (e.g. Granny Smiths), peeled, cored, cut in pieces
  • 2 heaped Tbsp cornflour
  • 1 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • ¼ tsp ground cinnamon (optional)
  • frying oil

Preparation

  1. Place potatoes in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a large bowl and set aside.
  2. Place apples and onions in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer to large bowl with reserved potatoes and mix with spatula. Transfer chopped mixture to a clean kitchen towel and squeeze out as much liquid as possible. Discard liquid.
  3. Place potato mixture, cornflour, lemon juice, salt, pepper and cinnamon in mixing bowl then, with aid of spatula, mix 10 sec//speed 4.
  4. Add frying oil to a large non-stick pan over a medium heat. Place evenly spaced ladles of potato mixture on preheated frying pan and press down with the back of a spatula. Fry until golden brown and crispy on the outside (approx. 6-7 minutes each side). Drain on paper towel. Repeat until all remaining potato mixture is used, then serve hot.

Useful Items

tea towel, non-stick frying pan, ladle, paper towel, fish slice, hob

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with fried or poached eggs, or with a dollop of sour cream and freshly cut chives.
