Cauliflower buffalo bites

Ingredients

  • 40 g brown rice
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 - 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 1 cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 90 g raw cashews
  • 70 g Sriracha® sauce
  • 60 g coconut oil
  • 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce (vegan and gluten free - see Tips)
  • 240 g filtered water

Preparation

  1. Place rice, onion powder, garlic powder, salt and pepper into mixing bowl and mill 30 sec/speed 10. Transfer into a large bowl and set aside.
  2. Preheat oven to 230°C. Line a baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  3. Dip each cauliflower floret into reserved rice flour mixture and coat evenly. Shake off excess and place onto prepared baking tray. Bake for 5 minutes (230°C).
  4. Place cashews, Sriracha®, oil, Worcestershire sauce and water into mixing bowl and blend 1 min/speed 10. Transfer into a large bowl.
  5. Remove cauliflower from oven and carefully dip each piece into buffalo sauce to coat. Place back onto baking tray and bake for 10 minutes (230°C). Remove from oven, turn cauliflower over and spoon 2 teaspoons of the buffalo sauce over each piece, then bake for further 5 minutes (230°C). Serve with Blue cheese tofu dressing (see Tips).

Useful Items

baking tray (30 x 40 cm), baking paper, large bowl, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • You can find vegan Worcestershire sauce at some health food stores or online. To make your own, refer to the Worcestershire sauce recipe on Cookidoo® (www.cookidoo.com.au).
