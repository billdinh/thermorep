Warm berry cobbler

Ingredients

  • 500 g frozen mixed berries (see Tips)
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 140 g raw sugar, plus extra for sprinkling
  • 1 pear, peeled, cored and cut into thin slices (see Tips)
  • 200 g fresh mixed berries (see Tips)
  • 80 g white rice flour (see Tips)
  • 60 g potato starch
  • 20 g arrowroot flour
  • 1 tsp gluten free baking powder (see Tips)
  • &frac18; tsp salt
  • 40 g unsalted butter, softened and cut into pieces
  • 60 g full cream milk
  • 1 egg
  • mascarpone cheese, to serve (optional)

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Place frozen berries, lemon juice and 100 g of the sugar into mixing bowl and cook 10 min/90°C//speed 1, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid. Transfer into a baking dish (24 cm) and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Arrange pear and fresh berries evenly on top of berry coulis. Set aside.
  4. Place rice flour, potato starch, arrowroot, baking powder, salt, butter, milk, egg and remaining 40 g sugar into mixing bowl and mix 20 sec/speed 5. Place spoonfuls of mixture (approx. 12) evenly on top of berry mixture.
  5. Sprinkle sugar over dumplings. Bake for 20-25 minutes (180°C). Serve warm with mascarpone cheese (optional).

Useful Items

ovenproof baking dish (24 cm), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • We used a mixture of blueberries, strawberries, blackberries and raspberries for this recipe.
  • You can substitute the pear with apple in this recipe.
  • To make your own rice flour, place 200 g rice into mixing bowl and mill 2 min/speed 10. Use as needed.
  • To make your own Gluten free baking powder, please refer to the recipe on this recipe chip.
  • Cobbler can be served with cream, ice cream or custard if not using mascarpone cheese.
