Cottage Pie

Ingredients

Minced Meat

  • 100 g onions, quartered
  • 20 g fresh parsley
  • 30 g butter, plus extra for greasing
  • 450 g beef mince, broken in pieces
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper, adjust to taste
  • 1 Tbsp Madeira wine (optional)

Mashed Potatoes

  • 900 g floury potatoes, sliced (5 mm)
  • 360 g milk
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper, or to taste
  • 50 g butter, plus extra for topping
  • 2 Tbsp breadcrumbs (optional)

Preparation

Minced Meat

  1. Place onions and parsley in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add butter and, without measuring cup, sauté 5 min/120°C/speed 1.
  3. Add beef mince, salt, pepper and wine (if using), stir meat with spatula to break up mince if necessary then cook 3 min/120°C//speed 1. Transfer to a greased baking dish and set aside.

Mashed Potatoes

  1. Insert butterfly whisk. Place potatoes, milk, salt and black pepper in mixing bowl then, without measuring cup 30-35 min/95°C//speed 1 until tender (see tip). Meanwhile, preheat oven to 220°C.
  2. Add butter to mixing bowl, insert measuring cup and mash 30 sec/speed 3.
  3. Spread mashed potatoes over mince in baking dish. Smooth surface with a fork. Sprinkle with breadcrumbs (if using) and dot with butter. Bake for 15 minutes (220°C) or until golden brown and meat is cooked. Serve hot.

Hints & Tricks

  • Cook off the mince, whip up the mashed potato, then layer them in a dish and bake for a much loved recipe. This is a great one to make in advance and freeze for a convenient family meal for 4 people. Defrost in the fridge before reheating.
  • Serve with steamed green peas or carrots.
  • After step 2, add 200 g carrots (cut in pieces) to mixing bowl, chop 10 sec/speed 4, then sauté 5 min/Varoma/speed 1. Proceed with recipe in step 3.
  • Instead of raw minced meat, use leftover cooked beef, minced for a few seconds on speed 5.
