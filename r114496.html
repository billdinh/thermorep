Maple, Pecan and Banana Bread

Ingredients

Sponge

  • 100 g butter, soft
  • 230 g plain flour
  • 80 g light brown sugar
  • 50 g maple syrup
  • 300 g bananas, in pieces (approx. 3 bananas )
  • 3 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50 g pecan nuts

Pecan Topping

  • 60 g light brown sugar
  • 30 g plain flour
  • 30 g butter, soft
  • ½ tsp ground nutmeg
  • 60 g pecan nuts

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C and line a large loaf tin (900 g/2 lb) with baking paper.
  2. Place butter, plain flour, sugar, maple syrup, bananas, baking powder, cinnamon, salt and eggs in mixing bowl then mix 30 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add pecan nuts and mix 1 min/speed 2. Transfer to prepared tin and level top with spatula. Set aside while preparing topping.

Pecan Topping

  1. Place sugar, flour, butter and nutmeg in mixing bowl then mix 8 sec/speed 6.
  2. Add pecans and mix 2 sec/speed 4. Arrange clumps of topping over batter in tin then bake for 50-60 minutes (180°C) until a skewer inserted in centre comes out clean, covering with foil halfway through to prevent top from browning too much. Allow to cool in tin for 10 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
  3. Serve in slices, keeping any leftovers in an airtight container.

Useful Items

900 g loaf tin (2 lb), baking paper, skewer, aluminium foil, wire rack, oven
