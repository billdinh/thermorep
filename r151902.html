Beef goulash with bread dumplings

Ingredients

Bread dumplings

  • 300 g bread rolls, slightly stale, cut in slices (1 cm)
  • 5 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 80 g onions, cut in halves
  • 25 g butter
  • 220 g milk
  • 3 eggs
  • ½ tsp salt, adjust to taste
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 2 pinches ground nutmeg

Beef goulash and steamed dumplings

  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 450 g onions, cut in quarters
  • 30 g oil
  • 750 g beef (for stewing), cut in pieces (2-3 cm), patted dry with paper towels
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 50 g tomato purée
  • 350 g tinned chopped tomatoes or fresh
  • 200 g red wine
  • 2 Tbsp sweet paprika
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • ½ tsp ground caraway
  • 1 tsp grated lemon zest
  • 1 Tbsp fresh marjoram
  • 3 dried bay leaves
  • 5 juniper berries
  • 1 pinch ground cloves
  • 300 g water
  • 30 g plain flour
  • 200 g peppers, red and green, cut in thin strips

Preparation

Bread dumplings

  1. Place bread rolls in mixing bowl and chop 12 sec/speed 6 with aid of spatula. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place parsley and onions in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add butter and sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  4. Add milk and warm 1 min 30 sec/60°C/speed 1.
  5. Add reserved breadcrumbs, eggs, salt, black pepper and nutmeg and mix 20 sec//speed 3 with aid of spatula. Transfer to a bowl and combine with spatula. Clean mixing bowl.

Beef goulash and steamed dumplings

  1. Place garlic cloves, onions and oil in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  2. Add beef and sauté 5 min/120°C//speed .
  3. Add sugar, tomato purée, chopped tomatoes, red wine, sweet paprika, salt, black pepper, caraway, lemon zest, marjoram, bay leaves, juniper berries and cloves and cook 40 min/100°C//speed 1. Meanwhile, lightly grease Varoma dish and tray. With wet hands, form 12 dumplings from the reserved dumpling mixture and place them in greased Varoma dish and Varoma tray.
  4. Add 100 g water, place Varoma with dumplings into position and steam 20 min/Varoma//speed 1. Set Varoma aside.
  5. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh flour and 50 g water into it. Mix flour and water.
  6. Add flour-water mixture, 150 g water and peppers to mixing bowl, place Varoma back into position and cook 8 min/90°C//speed 1. Season goulash to taste and serve hot with dumplings.
