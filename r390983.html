Rice Crispy Treat Pizza

Ingredients

  • 2 oz crispy rice cereal
  • 3 oz bananas, in pieces
  • 2 oz strawberries, halved, hulled, plus extra, to garnish (3 sliced)
  • 5 oz cream cheese, room temperature
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 4 oz mini marshmallows
  • 2 oz coconut oil
  • 1 tsp cocoa powder
  • 1 tbsp unsweetened shredded coconut
Nutrition
per 1 slice
Calories
887 kJ / 212 kcal
Protein
2 g
Carbohydrates
22 g
Fat
14 g

Preparation

  1. Place a large bowl onto mixing bowl lid, weigh in rice cereal and set aside.
  2. Place bananas, halved strawberries, cream cheese, vanilla and salt into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 3.5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix a further 10 sec/speed 3.5. Transfer fruit mixture to a bowl and refrigerate.
  3. Place marshmallows, coconut oil and cocoa powder into mixing bowl and cook 5 min/212°F/speed 1. Meanwhile, line a pie pan (9 in.) with cling wrap.
  4. Blend 10 sec/speed 4. Transfer marshmallow mixture to bowl with cereal and stir through with spatula until fully combined. Press mixture into prepared pie pan and refrigerate until set (approx. 15 min.).
  5. Remove cling wrap and transfer rice cereal crust onto a serving platter. Evenly spread reserved fruit mixture over the cereal crust, leaving a ½ inch border. Arrange sliced strawberries over fruit cream, sprinkle with shredded coconut and refrigerate until ready to slice and serve.

Useful Items

pie pan (9 in.), serving platter, large bowl, cling wrap, refrigerator
