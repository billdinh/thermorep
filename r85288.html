Crab & Prawn Linguine

Ingredients

  • 1 lemon, thin peelings of skin and juice
  • 1000 g water
  • 1 ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • 20 g olive oil, plus 1 tsp
  • 250 g pasta, linguine
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 150 g peeled, raw prawns
  • 80 g crème fraîche
  • 200 g crabmeat, white, brown or mixture
  • 70 g rocket, plus extra for serving
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 30 g pine nuts, toasted

Preparation

  1. Place lemon peel in mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place water, 1 tsp salt and 1 tsp oil in mixing bowl then bring to the boil 9 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  3. Add pasta and cook select time according to packet instructions/100°C//speed 1. Using simmering basket, drain pasta well under cold running water then set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Place garlic in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add prawns and oil then sauté 2 min/100°C//speed 0.5.
  6. Add crème fraîche, crabmeat, rocket, salt, pepper and reserved chopped lemon peel then heat 3 min/90°C//speed 0.5.
  7. Add drained linguine and lemon juice then stir through 2 min/80°C//speed . Serve scattered with toasted pine nuts and garnished with rocket.

frying pan, hob

Hints & Tricks

  • Toast your own pine nuts in a frying pan over a high heat, stirring constantly. Once golden brown, transfer to a bowl and set aside.
