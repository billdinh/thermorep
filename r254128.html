Shrimp Pasta Salad with Cilantro Pesto

Ingredients

Cilantro Pesto

  • 2 oz Parmesan cheese, cut into pieces
  • 2 oz pine nuts
  • 2 oz cilantro, leaves and tender stems (approx. 2-3 bunches)
  • 2 oz red onion, cut into pieces
  • 3 garlic cloves, to taste
  • 1 ¼ tsp salt
  • 5 oz olive oil
  • 2 oz lemon juice, (approx. 2 lemons)

Pasta Salad

  • 40 oz water
  • 32 oz large shrimp, peeled, deveined
  • 9 oz asparagus, cut into pieces ( 1 in.)
  • 16 oz gemelli pasta, dry
  • 1 can sweet corn kernels, drained (approx. 15 oz)
  • 1 jar roasted red peppers, drained, cut into strips, to taste

Preparation

Cilantro Pesto

  1. Place Parmesan and pine nuts into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Add cilantro, onion, garlic, salt, oil and lemon juice and mix 20 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.

Pasta Salad

  1. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position, weigh in shrimp, add 4 tbsp Cilantro Pesto and mix through with spatula. Insert Varoma tray, weigh in asparagus, secure Varoma lid and steam 15 min/Varoma/speed 1. Carefully remove Varoma lid, stir shrimp with spatula, secure Varoma lid and steam a further 2-5 min/Varoma/speed 1, until shrimp are cooked through. Remove Varoma, transfer shrimp and asparagus to serving bowl.
  2. Add pasta through hole in mixing bowl lid and cook for time indicated on package/Varoma//speed 1.
  3. Drain cooked pasta and add to serving dish.
  4. Add corn, peppers and remaining Cilantro Pesto to pasta in serving bowl. Stir through with spatula to combine. Serve hot.
