Couscous with lamb and vegetables

Ingredients

  • 4 sprigs fresh coriander, roughly chopped
  • 4 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 120 g onions, cut into quarters
  • 500 g lamb shoulder meat, cut into pieces (4 cm)
  • 3 tsp salt,
  • ½ tsp freshly ground black pepper, adjust to taste
  • 60 g olive oil
  • 150 g carrots
  • 80 g turnips
  • 200 g courgettes
  • 300 g couscous, medium
  • 850 g water
  • 1 ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • 100 g raisins
  • 260 g canned chickpeas, drained
  • 1 pinch saffron powder

Preparation

  1. Place coriander and parsley into mixing bowl and chop 7 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place onions into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  3. Add pieces of lamb meat, 2 tsp salt, pepper and 40 g olive oil and cook 10 min/120°C//speed . Meanwhile, cut carrots, turnips and courgettes into chunks (approx. 4 cm) and set aside. Place couscous, 1 tsp salt and 20 g olive oil in a bowl. Rub couscous in a circular motion between your hands to moisten grains evenly and remove any lumps that may form, cover with 350 g water and stir using spatula to moisten couscous evenly. Cover bowl and set aside.
  4. Add reserved vegetables, chopped coriander and parsley, cinnamon, ginger and 500 g water to mixing bowl. Gently fluff the couscous with a fork to separate grains and transfer to Varoma tray. Place Varoma dish and tray into position and cook 15 min/Varoma//speed . Remove Varoma and set aside.
  5. Add raisins, chick peas and saffron, cook again 15 min/100°C//speed . Transfer lamb and vegetables to deep plates. Cover with sauce and serve with couscous.
