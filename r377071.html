Green curry paste

Ingredients

  • 6 fresh long green chillies, trimmed
  • 4 spring onions/shallots, trimmed and cut into pieces
  • 3 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only
  • 2 stalks fresh lemongrass, white part only
  • 3 fresh Kaffir lime leaves
  • Â½ - 1 tsp shrimp paste, to taste (see Tips)
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp ground coriander
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 - 4 tsp fish sauce, to taste
  • 20 g peanut oil, plus extra if needed

Preparation

  1. Place all ingredients into mixing bowl and blend 30 sec/speed 9.
  2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and blend for a further 30 sec/speed 9. If paste is too dry, add a little extra oil and stir to combine. Transfer into a sealable container and place into refrigerator until ready to use (see Tips). Add 2-3 tablespoons of the paste to your favourite curry.

Useful Items

sealable container, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • You can find shrimp paste at Asian food stores and at some major supermarkets in the international food aisle.
  • You can store curry paste in the refrigerator for up to 2 weeks, or freeze in ice cube trays and store in the freezer for up to 3 months.
