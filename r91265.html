Pork Tenderloin with Mustard Sauce

Ingredients

  • 16 oz pork tenderloin, sliced (½ in.)
  • 3 ½ oz button mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 ½ oz dry vermouth
  • 3 ½ oz whole milk
  • 2 oz yellow onions, halved
  • 1 garlic clove (optional)
  • 2 tsp whole grain mustard
  • 1 tsp hot mustard
  • 1 chicken stock cube
  • 1 tsp salt,
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper, to taste
  • 2 tbsp crème fraîche
  • 1 2 - 3 heaping tbsp corn starch,
  • 2 - 3 sprigs fresh parsley, to garnish

Preparation

  1. Place pork tenderloin and mushrooms into simmering basket, alternating layers of meat and layers of mushrooms and set aside.
  2. Place vermouth, milk, onions, garlic, whole grain mustard, hot mustard, chicken stock cube, salt and black pepper into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket with pork and mushrooms and cook 10 min/212°F/speed 1.
  3. Stir contents of simmering basket with spatula to ensure even cooking. Cook a further 5 min/212°F/speed 1. Meat should be cooked evenly without being dry. If necessary stir again with spatula and cook a further 5 min/212°F/speed 1. Remove simmering basket with spatula, transfer pork and mushrooms to a serving dish, set aside and keep warm.
  4. Add crème fraîche and corn starch into mixing bowl and cook 3 min/175°F/speed 4. Coat meat and mushrooms with sauce, sprinkle with parsley and serve immediately.

Hints & Tricks

  • Cooking time depends on thickness of meat slices.
