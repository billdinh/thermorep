Green goodness soup

Ingredients

  • ½ orange, zest and juice
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 fresh bird's eye chilli
  • 1 spring onion/shallot, trimmed and cut into pieces
  • 350 g water
  • 70 g kale, cut into pieces
  • ½ Lebanese cucumber
  • 4 sprigs fresh basil, leaves only
  • 40 g lemon juice (approx. 1 lemon)
  • 50 g sunflower seed kernels, soaked for 1 hour, then drained
  • 1 avocado, flesh only
  • 1 - 1 ½ tbsp miso paste, to taste
Nutrition

Preparation

  1. Place orange zest, garlic, chilli and spring onion/shallot into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Repeat chopping 10 sec/speed 9, or until a fine consistency is achieved. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add all remaining ingredients, including orange juice, and blend 1 min/speed 7, then heat 2 min/50°C/speed 1.
  4. Insert measuring cup and blend 20 sec/speed 9. Serve soup warm or chilled.
