Stuffed chicken with herbed mushroom rice

Ingredients

Herb butter

  • 1 lemon, zest and juice only (no white pith)
  • ½ bunch fresh coriander, leaves only
  • 10 g fresh chives, cut into thirds
  • ½ bunch fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 100 g unsalted butter
  • 1800 - 2500 g whole chicken

Stuffed chicken and rice

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 80 g eschalots, cut into quarters
  • 100 g bacon, cut into pieces (approx. 1 cm)
  • 150 g fresh brown mushrooms, cut into quarters
  • 150 g fresh portobello mushrooms, cut into quarters
  • 150 g frozen spinach
  • 300 g basmati rice
  • 800 g water
  • 1 tsp Chicken stock paste (see Tips)
  • 20 g soy sauce
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 50 g dry Marsala wine

Preparation

Herb butter

  1. Line a roasting tray with aluminium foil and set aside.
  2. Place lemon zest, coriander, chives, parsley, nutmeg, salt and pepper into mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add lemon juice and butter and mix 2 min/speed 3. Carefully spread one-third of the herb butter under the skin of the chicken, pulling skin away from the flesh gently but firmly. Leave remaining butter in mixing bowl. Transfer chicken onto prepared roasting tray and set aside.

Stuffed chicken and rice

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Add garlic, eschalots and bacon into mixing bowl and sauté 8 min/100°C/speed 1.
  3. Add all mushrooms and cook 5 min/Varoma/speed .
  4. Add spinach, rice, 750 g of the water, stock paste, soy sauce, thyme, salt and pepper and cook 10 min/100°C//speed .
  5. Stuff chicken with mushroom rice mixture. Place a small bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh wine and remaining 50 g water into it. Drizzle wine mixture over chicken. Roast chicken for 75 minutes (180°C - see Tips), basting every 20 minutes during cooking.
  6. Transfer any remaining rice into an ovenproof baking dish, cover with aluminium foil and place into oven with chicken and cook for a further 20 minutes or until chicken is cooked (180°C). Serve chicken with rice.

Useful Items

roasting tray, aluminium foil, ovenproof baking dish, small bowl, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Please refer to The Basic Cookbook or Everyday Cookbook for the Chicken stock paste recipe.
  • Depending on the size/weight of your chicken, cooking times may vary. We suggest roasting your chicken for 20 minutes, then adding an additional 20 minutes per 500 g.
