Chocolate Orange Steamed Pudding

Ingredients

Pudding

  • 100 g dark chocolate, small pieces or callets
  • 1 orange, thin peelings of skin only
  • 80 g unsalted butter, diced, plus extra for greasing
  • 180 g caster sugar
  • 80 g milk
  • 10 g cocoa powder
  • 1 orange, thinly sliced (3 mm)
  • 150 g self-raising flour
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
  • 2 medium eggs
  • 2300 g water, boiling, for steaming

Chocolate Sauce

  • 120 g dark chocolate, small pieces or callets
  • 120 g double cream
  • 40 g water

Preparation

Pudding

  1. Generously grease an 850 ml/1.5 pint pudding basin with butter then set aside.
  2. Place chocolate and orange peelings in mixing bowl then grate 10 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides and base of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add butter, sugar, milk and cocoa powder then heat 2 min/80째C/speed 1. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then heat again 2-3 min/80째C/speed 1. Meanwhile, arrange 5-6 orange slices in base and around edges of buttered pudding basin.
  4. Add flour, salt and eggs then mix 20 sec/speed 4. Finish mixing with spatula then transfer to prepared pudding basin. Cover with a buttered circle of greaseproof paper, with a pleat in the middle to allow for pudding to expand. Cover with lid or secure with a circle of foil, again with a pleat in the middle. Secure with string then place in Varoma dish.
  5. Place 1400 g boiling water in mixing bowl. Place covered Varoma into position and steam 90 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  6. Add remaining 900 g boiling water before steaming again 60-70 min/Varoma/speed 2. Set covered Varoma aside for 10 minutes while making sauce.

Chocolate Sauce

  1. Place chocolate, cream and water in mixing bowl then melt 3 min/60째C/speed 2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and melt again 2 min/60째C/speed 2.
  2. Remove lid or foil and greaseproof paper from pudding basin then turn out onto a serving plate. Serve warm with chocolate sauce poured over.

Useful Items

aluminium foil, kitchen string, serving plate, greaseproof paper, pudding basin with lid (850 ml/1.5 pt.)

Hints & Tricks

  • Instead of the more traditional fruit laden Christmas pudding, this steamed chocolate dessert provides an alternative ending to a Christmas Day meal. Bursting with the flavours of zesty orange and rich chocolate, this pudding can be left to steam gently in the Varoma while the turkey is roasting in the oven.
