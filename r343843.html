Blueberry Coffee Cake

Ingredients

Streusel

  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter, plus extra to grease
  • 2 ½ oz sugar
  • 2 oz brown sugar
  • 1 oz all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 1 pinch ground ginger
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • 1 oz chopped pecans

Cake

  • 6 oz plain yogurt, unsweetened
  • 2 oz vegetable oil
  • 6 oz sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 8 oz all-purpose flour, divided
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • 10 oz fresh blueberries, plus extra to garnish

Preparation

Streusel

  1. Preheat oven 350°F. Grease a round cake pan (8 in.) and set aside.
  2. Place sugar, brown sugar, butter, flour, cinnamon, salt, ginger and nutmeg into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix a further 5 sec/speed 4.
  3. Add pecans and mix 2 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.

Cake

  1. Place yogurt, oil, sugar, vanilla, eggs, 7.5 oz flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt and nutmeg into mixing bowl and mix 15 sec/speed 4. Transfer half the batter into prepared cake pan, sprinkle with half the streusel, add remaining batter and top with remaining streusel.
  2. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid, weigh in blueberries and 0.5 oz flour and toss together to coat. Fold into batter with spatula.
  3. Bake for 50-55 minutes (350°F) or until toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Cool completely before slicing. Garnish with fresh blueberries and serve.
