Blueberry Scones with Honey and Walnut Butter

Ingredients

Blueberry Scones

  • 350 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 1 Tbsp baking powder
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
  • 125 g unsalted butter, diced
  • 80 g caster sugar
  • 180 g buttermilk
  • 200 g fresh blueberries
  • 2 Tbsp milk
  • 2 Tbsp Demerara sugar

Honey and Walnut Butter

  • 60 g walnut halves
  • 180 g unsalted butter, soft
  • 120 g runny honey
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt

Preparation

Blueberry Scones

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C and line a baking tray with baking paper.
  2. Place flour, baking powder and salt in mixing bowl then mix 4 sec/speed 5.
  3. Add butter and caster sugar then mix 4 sec/speed 6.
  4. Add buttermilk and mix 20 sec/.
  5. Add blueberries then stir gently with spatula until partly mixed in (mixture will be clumpy).
  6. Tip out onto a lightly-floured surface, bring dough together into a ball (some blueberries will burst) then flatten to a disc approx. 2.5 cm thick. Use a pastry cutter (Ø 6 cm) to cut out scones and place on prepared tray. Re-roll any trimmings and cut out more scones until all dough is used (approx. 12-14 scones).
  7. Brush tops of scones with milk, sprinkle with Demerara sugar then bake for 12-14 minutes (200°C) until risen and golden. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl and make butter.

Honey and Walnut Butter

  1. Place walnuts in mixing bowl and chop 1 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place butter, honey and salt in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add reserved walnuts then mix 10 sec//speed 3. Transfer to a small serving bowl.
  4. Serve scones warm with honey and walnut butter.

Useful Items

baking tray and baking paper, round pastry cutter (Ø 6 cm), pastry brush, oven
