Chocolate Fondue

Ingredients

  • ½ vanilla bean
  • 4 oz dark chocolate chips
  • 3 oz semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • &frac18; tsp salt
  • 3 oz heavy whipping cream
  • 3 oz raspberry liqueur
  • 1 oz unsalted butter, in pieces
  • mixed fresh fruit, e.g. strawberries, raspberries, banana, pineapple, to serve
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
2021 kJ / 483 kcal
Protein
3 g
Carbohydrates
42 g
Fat
30 g

Preparation

  1. Split vanilla bean lengthwise, scrape out seeds and discard pod. Place vanilla seeds, dark chocolate, semi-sweet chocolate and salt into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add heavy cream, raspberry liqueur and butter and without measuring cup heat 5 min/195°F/speed 1.5. Transfer to a small serving bowl for dipping. Serve with mixed fresh fruit.

Useful Items

small serving bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • Assorted dipper suggestions: marshmallows, pretzels, shortbread Madeleines or baked goods (pound cake or angel food cake, cubed).
  • Replace raspberry liqueur with orange liqueur.
