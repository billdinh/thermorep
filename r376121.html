Cajun Shrimp Steamer with Garlic Butter

Ingredients

Garlic Butter

  • 2 oz yellow onions, in pieces
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp dried parsley
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 12 oz unsalted butter, cubed
  • 1 oz lemon juice
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper

Cajun Shrimp Steamer

  • 4 oz yellow onions, in pieces
  • 6 garlic cloves
  • 16 oz water
  • 8 oz dry white wine
  • 3 dried bay leaves
  • 16 oz baby red potatoes, rinsed
  • 14 oz andouille sausage, pre-cooked, sliced (1 in.)
  • 2 ears of corn, cut into rounds (1 in.)
  • 40 oz large raw shrimp, easy peel
  • 2 tbsp Cajun spice mix
  • chopped parsley, to garnish
  • 2 lemons, sliced into wedges

Preparation

Garlic Butter

  1. Place onion, garlic, parsley and salt into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add butter, lemon juice and pepper and melt 5 min/195°F/speed 1. Transfer to a serving bowl and set aside.

Cajun Shrimp Steamer

  1. Place onions, garlic, water, wine and bay leaves into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket into mixing bowl and weigh in potatoes.
  2. Set Varoma dish into position and weigh in sausage and corn. Add shrimp, sprinkle with Cajun spice, secure Varoma lid and steam 10 min/Varoma/speed 2. Carefully remove Varoma lid and stir contents with spatula to ensure even cooking, secure Varoma lid and steam a further 5-10 min/Varoma/speed 2, until shrimp are cooked through. Remove Varoma and simmering basket. Slice potatoes in half and transfer to a large serving platter along with the shrimp, corn and sausage.
  3. Garnish with parsley and serve with reserved melted garlic butter and lemon wedges.

Useful Items

serving bowl, serving platter

Hints & Tricks

  • Easy peel shrimp are already split and deveined.
