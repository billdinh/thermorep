Steamed fish fillets in a creamy leek sauce

Ingredients

  • 500 g hot water
  • 4 potatoes (approx. 500 g), peeled and cut into cubes (1 cm)
  • olive oil, for greasing
  • 4 firm white fish fillets, cut into pieces (6 cm - see Tips)
  • ½ tsp salt, plus extra to season
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper, plus extra to season
  • 2 eschalots
  • 50 g unsalted butter
  • 300 g leeks, white part only, cut into thin slices (5 mm)
  • 50 g white wine
  • 1 tsp gluten free cornflour (see Tips)
  • 200 g thickened cream
  • 2 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only, chopped for garnishing

Preparation

  1. Place hot water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish into position and place potatoes into it. Lightly oil Varoma tray and place fish onto it, then season with salt and pepper. Secure Varoma lid and steam 15-18 min/Varoma/speed 4, until fish is cooked to your liking. Transfer potatoes and fish into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and cover to keep warm. Discard water.
  2. Place eschalots into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add butter, leeks, wine, salt and pepper and sauté 3 min/120°C//speed 2.
  4. Add cornflour and cook 1 min/120°C//speed 2. Scrape down sides and base of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add cream and cook 5 min/120°C//speed 2, without measuring cup, until sauce thickens.
  6. Divide steamed fish and potatoes between serving plates. Pour over leek sauce and garnish with parsley to serve.

Useful Items

serving plates, thermal serving bowl or other large bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • For best results, use fresh fish fillets for this recipe. Cooking times may vary depending on type and thickness of fillets used.
  • You can replace the gluten free cornflour with plain flour, if desired.
