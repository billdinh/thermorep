Chicken Stock Paste

Ingredients

  • 300 g chicken meat, boneless and skinless, sinew free (from approx. 500 g chicken legs), cut in pieces
  • 200 g mixed vegetables, white (e.g. celeriac, onions, garlic, leek), cut in pieces as needed
  • 4 sprigs fresh mixed herbs (e.g. rosemary, thyme, parsley), leaves only
  • 150 g coarse sea salt (see tip)
  • 100 g white wine
  • 1 dried bay leaf
  • 1 clove
  • 5 coriander seeds

Preparation

  1. Place chicken in mixing bowl and mince 5 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place mixed white vegetables and herbs in mixing bowl then chop 10 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add salt, wine, bay leaf, clove, coriander seeds and reserved minced chicken then, with simmering basket in place of measuring cup, cook 25 min/Varoma/speed 2. Mixture will thicken and become fairly dry.
  4. Insert measuring cup and blend 1 min/speed 4-7, increasing speed gradually. Transfer to a glass jar with screw-top lid (see tip), leaving approx. 5 mm. Make sure that the rim of the jar is clean and close immediately then leave to cool before using or storing in fridge (see tip).

Useful Items

glass jar with screw-top lid, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • The salt in this recipe is used as the preserving agent. When mixed with water, the stock does not taste too salty. If you wish to decrease the salt in the recipe, you must store the stock paste in the freezer.
  • To prepare jar, place 500 g water in mixing bowl. Place jar and lid upside down in the centre of the Varoma and steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 1. Leave to drain - do not dry or touch the inside surfaces. Alternatively, thoroughly wash jar and lid in hot, soapy water. Rinse by pouring over boiling water. Prepare for filling, if needed, by placing on a clean tea towel in a roasting tin and heat in the oven at 100ËšC until required.
  • Store in the fridge for up to 3 months, or freeze in cubes for up to 6 months.
  • 1 heaped tsp stock paste is roughly equivalent to 1 shop-bought chicken stock cube (for 0.5 l).
  • To make chicken stock, use 1 heaped tsp per 500 g water.
  • Adapt the stock paste recipe using the natural ingredients of your choice.
