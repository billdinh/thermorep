Chawan mushi (Japanese savoury egg custard)

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs
  • 800 g water
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp dashi powder (see Variation)
  • 2 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 100 g pork mince (optional)
  • 30 g fresh shiitake mushrooms, sliced, or any small mushrooms, sliced
  • 6 medium raw prawns
  • 30 frozen green peas (approx. 25 g)
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
576 kJ / 138 kcal

Preparation

  1. Place eggs, 300 g of the water, salt, dashi powder, soy sauce, ground pepper and pork mince (optional) into mixing bowl and mix 20 sec/speed 2.5. Transfer to 6 ceramic ramekins (approx. 8 cm diameter, height 4.5 cm), filling to 1 cm from the top of each. Rinse mixing bowl.
  2. Place remaining 500 g water into mixing bowl. Place 3 ramekins into Varoma dish and cover loosely with a sheet of baking paper. Insert Varoma tray, place remaining ramekins onto it and cover loosley with a sheet of baking paper. Place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 12 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Carefully remove Varoma lid and tray, lift sheets of baking paper and carefully arrange mushrooms, 1 prawn and 5 green peas on top of each steamed egg dish. Re-insert Varoma tray, replace baking paper, secure Varoma lid and steam for a further 10 min/Varoma/speed 1. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

ramekin (8cm), baking paper

Hints & Tricks

  • Steam in any heat-resistant cup or small bowl of similar size. If your dishes are a different size or they are not ceramic, you may have to adjust the cooking time. Once you know the perfect cooking time for your own dishes, make a note of it for next time.
  • Dashi, or Japanese cooking stock, adds flavour to the chawan mushi. You can replace the instant dashi powder with ½ tsp homemade chicken or vegetable stock paste: first dilute the stock paste in some of the water before adding it in step 1, and reduce salt to ¼ tsp. Or omit dashi powder and use 300 g stock instead of water in step 1. Finally you can omit the dashi powder altogether; the dish will have a more subtle egg flavour.
