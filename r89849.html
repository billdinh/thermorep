Ingredients

Prune and orange stuffing

  • 1 garlic clove
  • ½ brown onion (approx. 70 g)
  • 20 g butter
  • 80 g pitted prunes
  • 2 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 1 sprig fresh rosemary (approx. 10 cm), leaves only
  • ½ - 1 orange, zest only, no white pith, finely grated
  • 2 tsp orange juice
  • 30 g sunflower seeds
  • 100 g day old bread (approx. 2-3 slices), torn into pieces (3 cm)
  • 1 pinch sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper

Spicy Cajun rub

  • 160 g sea salt
  • 1 tbsp chilli powder
  • 1 tbsp ground smoked paprika
  • 1 tbsp dried onion flakes
  • 1 tbsp granulated garlic
  • ½ tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp ground cayenne pepper
  • 1 tbsp dried thyme
  • 1 tbsp dried basil
  • 1 tbsp dried oregano
  • 1 tbsp coriander seeds
  • ¼ tsp ground white pepper
  • 2 tsp ground black pepper

Whole chicken

  • 1000 g water
  • 1 whole uncooked chicken (1.2-1.6 kg)

Preparation

Prune and orange stuffing

  1. Place garlic, onion and butter into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then sauté 3 min/Varoma/speed 1, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  2. Add prunes, parsley, rosemary, orange zest and orange juice and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then cook 2 min/100°C/speed 1.
  3. Add all remaining stuffing ingredients and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then repeat chopping 5 sec/speed 7. Transfer stuffing into a bowl and set aside to cool completely before use. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Spicy Cajun rub

  1. Place all spice rub ingredients into mixing bowl and combine 5 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a sealable storage jar and set aside until use.

Whole chicken

  1. Place water into mixing bowl and place Varoma dish into position.
  2. Place whole chicken into Varoma dish and fill chicken cavity with reserved cooled stuffing. Rub 1-2 tablespoons of the reserved Spicy Cajun rub into chicken skin.
  3. Secure Varoma lid and steam 60 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  4. Check to see if your chicken is cooked through. If chicken is not cooked, secure Varoma lid and steam for a further 10 min/Varoma/speed 1, or until chicken is cooked. Transfer chicken onto a serving platter, then carve and serve immediately.

Useful Items

sealable storage jar, serving platter

Hints & Tricks

  • Use leftover Spicy Cajun rub on steamed or roasted poultry (e.g. chicken, duck, turkey). You can also transfer it into individual jars and gift to friends and family.
  • This is a great interchangeable recipe. Try using any of the stuffing and spice rubs to create different flavour combinations. Rub the British spice rub or the Brown sugar spice rub into your chicken and stuff with Apricot stuffing, Cranberry and bacon stuffing or the Apple and sage stuffing.
  • Ensure your stuffing is completely cool before using.
  • If you don't want to use a stuffing in your chicken or are strapped for time, fill the chicken cavity with 2 garlic cloves, cut into slices; 1 whole lemon and 2 sprigs fresh rosemary in step 6 and proceed as per recipe.
