Goat's cheese feta roulade

Ingredients

Roulade

  • 60 g salted butter
  • 50 g Gruyère cheese
  • 3 eggs, separated
  • 120 g water
  • 40 g cornflour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 30 g tomato paste
  • 1 tsp sweet paprika
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper

Cheese filling

  • 5 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 5 sprigs fresh mint, leaves only
  • 5 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only
  • 150 g goat's cheese feta, cut into pieces
  • 100 g cream cheese
  • 10 sprigs fresh chives

Preparation

Roulade

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C. Line a baking tray (25 x 35 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place butter into mixing bowl and melt 2 min/50°C/speed 2. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Place cheese into mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 4. Transfer into a separate bowl and set aside. Thoroughly clean and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg whites into mixing bowl and whip 3 min/speed 4, without measuring cup, until stiff peaks form. Remove butterfly whisk. Transfer egg whites into a separate bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  5. Place egg yolks and water into mixing bowl and cook 5 min/70°C/speed 4, or until doubled in volume.
  6. Add cornflour, baking powder, tomato paste, paprika, salt, pepper, reserved butter and reserved cheese and mix 15 sec/speed 3.
  7. Add reserved egg whites and mix 5 sec/speed 3. Spread mixture evenly across prepared baking tray and bake for 8 minutes (200°C).  Turn out roulade onto a piece of plastic wrap and roll up from the narrow edge (using plastic wrap to assist), then tightly seal and set aside to cool for 10 minutes while preparing cheese filling. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Cheese filling

  1. Place parsley, mint and coriander into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add feta and cream cheese and mix 20 sec/speed 3. Unroll roulade and spread cheese filling evenly across roulade to the edges. Roll up tightly (using plastic wrap to assist) and seal tightly in plastic wrap. Place into refrigerator for a minimum 2 hours. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Place chives into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Garnish roulade with chives before serving.

Useful Items

baking tray (25 x 35 cm), baking paper, bowl, plastic wrap, oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Roulade can be prepared the day before and stored in the refrigerator until ready to serve.
