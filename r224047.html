Pizza Pinwheels

Ingredients

Dough

  • 120 g water
  • 1 tsp dried instant yeast
  • 15 g olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 220 g strong white bread flour, plus extra for dusting
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt

Filling and Assembly

  • 80 g red onions, quartered
  • 2 - 3 sprigs fresh parsley leaves
  • 150 g Cheddar cheese, cut in pieces
  • 80 g barbecue sauce
  • 180 g chicken breasts, cooked, sliced (1 cm)

Preparation

Dough

  1. Place all dough ingredients in mixing bowl and knead 3 min/. Meanwhile, grease a large bowl with oil. Remove dough, shape into a ball and transfer to prepared bowl. Cover with cling film and set aside to rise in a warm place until doubled in size (approx. 1 hour).

Filling and Assembly

  1. After this time, preheat oven to 220°C and line a baking tray with baking paper.
  2. Place onions and parsley in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place Cheddar in mixing bowl and grate 6 sec/speed 7. Transfer to another bowl and set aside.
  4. On a floured surface, roll dough out to a rectangle (approx. 40 cm x 30 cm). Spread barbecue sauce over surface of dough, then add cooked chicken, reserved onion and parsley mixture, and reserved grated Cheddar. Tightly roll dough on long side into a roll, cut in 2-3 cm wide slices and place on prepared baking tray.
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes (220°C) until cheese is bubbling. Serve warm or allow to cool before packing in picnic boxes.

Useful Items

large bowl, cling film, baking tray and baking paper, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • For an alternative filling: Spread 80 g cream cheese on dough, sprinkle with 150 g grated mozzarella or Emmental cheese, then add 150 g bacon in strips. Roll up, slice and bake.
  • Children can assist with this recipe, weighing ingredients in and setting the controls. Never leave them unattended with Thermomix®, while it is running or between steps.
  • Children can also help with rolling and topping the pastry.
