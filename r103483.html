Eggplant rotolo

Ingredients

  • 50 g Parmesan cheese, crust removed and cut into pieces (2 cm)
  • 3 pieces lemon zest (approx. 1 x 8 cm strips), no white pith
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 carrot, cut into quarters
  • 1 brown onion (approx. 150 g), cut into halves
  • 1 celery stalk, trimmed and cut into halves
  • 20 g olive oil, plus extra for drizzling
  • 750 g pork mince
  • 150 g red wine
  • 80 g tomato paste
  • 400 g canned chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp dried Italian herbs
  • 1 ½ tsp salt, plus extra for seasoning
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper, plus extra for seasoning
  • 2 eggplants, cut into thin slices lengthways (15-18 slices, approx. 5 mm thick)
  • 250 g smooth ricotta cheese
  • 3 sprigs fresh basil, leaves only
  • 30 g pine nuts, toasted (optional)

Preparation

  1. Place Parmesan and lemon zest into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  2. Place garlic, carrot, onion, celery and oil into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Sauté 5 min/Varoma//speed 2.
  3. Add mince and red wine, mix well using spatula and cook 5 min/Varoma//speed 2, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  4. Add tomato paste, chopped tomatoes, dried herbs, salt and pepper into mixing bowl and cook 10 min/Varoma/speed 1, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  5. Preheat oven grill. Line a baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  6. Drizzle eggplant slices with extra oil, and season with salt and pepper. Place half of the eggplant slices onto prepared baking tray and grill on one side only for 8-10 minutes or until slightly golden. Remove and set aside. Repeat grilling with remaining eggplant slices.
  7. Preheat oven to 200°C. Place two-thirds of the mince mixture into base of a baking dish (30 x 21 cm). Loosely roll up each eggplant slice, grilled side facing outwards, and place standing up on top of mince mixture. Spoon remaining mince mixture into each eggplant roll, and fill any gaps between eggplant rolls with mince mixture. Place spoonfuls of ricotta on top of each eggplant roll. Sprinkle with reserved Parmesan mixture, then bake for 30 minutes (200°C). Scatter with basil leaves and toasted pine nuts (optional) just before serving.

Useful Items

bowl, baking tray (30 x 40 cm), baking paper, ovenproof dish (30 x 21 cm), oven grill, oven
