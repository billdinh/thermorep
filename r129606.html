Steamed orange puddings

Ingredients

Batter

  • 150 g unsalted butter, cut into pieces and softened, plus extra for greasing
  • 1 orange, zest only, no white pith
  • 150 g raw sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 200 g self-raising flour
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 150 g buttermilk
  • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste
  • 3 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves only (optional)
  • 1000 g water

Orange sauce

  • 250 g orange juice
  • 80 g orange marmalade
  • 20 g caster sugar
  • 2 oranges, peeled and segmented
  • 40 g unsalted butter
  • 20 g orange liqueur (optional)

Preparation

Batter

  1. Grease 8 dariole moulds or ramekins (6 cm) and line base and sides with baking paper. Ensure all moulds fit comfortably into Varoma dish.
  2. Place orange zest and sugar into mixing bowl and mill 15 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add butter and mix 20 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides and bottom of mixing bowl with spatula and mix 20 sec/speed 4.
  4. Scrape down sides and bottom of mixing bowl with spatula. Add eggs and mix 10 sec/speed 3. Scrape down sides and bottom of mixing bowl.
  5. Add flour, salt, buttermilk and vanilla and mix 10 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides and bottom of mixing bowl.
  6. Add thyme leaves (optional) and mix 5 sec/speed 4. Divide batter evenly between each mould, filling three-quarters full. Cover each pudding with a disc of baking paper (touching pudding) and place into Varoma dish.
  7. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  8. Carefully rotate puddings, secure Varoma lid and steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove Varoma and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Orange sauce

  1. Place orange juice, marmalade and sugar into mixing bowl and cook 15 min/100°C/speed 2, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  2. Place orange segments into simmering basket. Insert simmering basket and cook 5 min/100°C/speed 2. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and transfer oranges into a bowl.
  3. Add butter and orange liqueur (optional) to mixing bowl and cook 1 min/100°C/speed 2, without measuring cup. Transfer into bowl with orange segments and cover to keep warm.
  4. Unmould puddings onto serving plates. Serve with orange segments, reserved sauce and whipped cream.

Useful Items

baking paper, dariole moulds or ramekins (6 cm), bowl, serving plates

Hints & Tricks

  • The classic steamed British sponge pudding, steamed over water to create a delicate pudding.
