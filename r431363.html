Cranberry Pear Cider

Ingredients

  • 1 orange, thin peel only, no pith, plus 3 oz juice, plus orange wedges to serve
  • 1 lemon, thin peel only, no pith, plus 2 oz juice
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 10 whole cloves
  • 5 whole allspice berries
  • 12 oz pears, cored, quartered
  • 8 oz apples, cored, quartered (e.g. Honeycrisp)
  • 6 oz cranberries, plus extra, sugared to garnish (see Tip)
  • 6 oz brown sugar
  • ½ oz ginger, cut into pieces
  • 38 oz water
  • 8 sprigs fresh rosemary, to garnish (see Tip)
  • ice cubes, to serve (optional)

Preparation

  1. Place orange peel, lemon peel, cinnamon stick, cloves and allspice into mixing bowl and grind 5 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add pears, apples, cranberries, brown sugar and ginger and chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  3. Add water, 3 oz orange juice and 2 oz lemon juice and without measuring cup cook 18 min/212°F/speed 2. Strain through a fine mesh sieve and divide into glasses.
  4. Thread prepared sugared cranberries (see Tip) onto rosemary sprigs, place onto glasses with a wedge of orange and serve warm or over ice.

Hints & Tricks

  • To sugar cranberries: rinse and drain approximately 24 cranberries. Toss cranberries with ¼-⅔ cup sugar in a small bowl to coat. Spread in a single layer on a baking sheet and allow to dry (approx. 1 hr.).
  • Add one jigger (1½ oz) bourbon, whiskey or brandy.
