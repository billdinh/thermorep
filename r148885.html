Goats Cheese Eggs with Watercress and Beetroot

Ingredients

  • 80 g cooked beetroot, diced (5 mm)
  • 2 pinches fine sea salt
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 1 Tbsp balsamic vinegar

Goat's Cheese Eggs

  • 40 g flaked almonds
  • 270 g goat's cheese log
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil

Watercress Salad Nest

  • 50 g fresh basil leaves
  • 40 g almonds, smoked
  • 50 g Parmesan cheese, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 50 g olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 60 g watercress

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in beetroot. Remove bowl and add salt, pepper and balsamic vinegar then stir to coat.

Goat's Cheese Eggs

  1. Place almonds in mixing bowl then chop 2 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Cut cheese into 8 equal pieces and roll into egg-shaped balls. Roll balls in reserved chopped almonds to coat completely.
  3. Heat 2 Tbsp oil in a large frying pan over a medium heat then fry for approx. 1 minute until golden-brown all over. Carefully remove and drain on paper towel.

Watercress Salad Nest

  1. Place basil, smoked almonds, Parmesan, oil and garlic in mixing bowl then blend 20 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then blend again 10 sec/speed 10.
  2. To serve, divide watercress between 4 plates to create nests then place 2 Tbsp pesto from mixing bowl in the centre of each plate. Rest goat's cheese eggs on pesto then spoon reserved beetroot around. Serve as an Easter starter.
