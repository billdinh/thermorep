Blackened Fish Burger

Ingredients

Sriracha Mayonnaise

  • 4 oz mayonnaise
  • 1 oz sriracha, to taste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano

Fish Patties

  • 4 oz shallots, in pieces
  • 1 oz panko
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • ½ tsp ground coriander
  • ½ tsp ground cayenne pepper
  • ¼ tsp ground cumin
  • 24 oz fresh cod fillets, cubed
  • oil, to fry
  • 6 ciabatta rolls, toasted
  • roasted red peppers, sliced, to serve
  • lettuce, to serve
  • potato chips, to serve

Preparation

Sriracha Mayonnaise

  1. Place mayonnaise, sriracha and oregano into mixing bowl and mix 30 sec/speed 3. Transfer to a bowl and refrigerate.

Fish Patties

  1. Place shallots and panko into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add paprika, salt, garlic powder, pepper, coriander, cayenne and cumin and mix 5 sec/speed 4.
  3. Add fish and mix 10 sec//speed 4. Transfer to a bowl.
  4. Divide and shape fish mixture into six equal sized patties (approx. 5 oz ea. and 3½ in. x ½ in.) directly onto parchment. Cover with cling wrap and refrigerate 20 minutes.
  5. Place a frying pan over medium-high heat and add oil. Fry patties until blackened slightly and cooked through (approx. 3 min. per side).
  6. Spread rolls with reserved mayonnaise, roasted red peppers and lettuce. Top with cooked fish patties and serve with potato chips.

Useful Items

cling wrap, frying pan, parchment paper, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • The mayonnaise and fish patties can be made ahead. Patties can be made up to a day in advance.
  • To freeze: form patties, place on a baking sheet and freeze in a single layer. Then place in a freezer-safe food storage bag, with a square of parchment paper between patties. Cook from frozen or thawed.
  • While most fillets will have the larger bones removed to be considered "boneless," it doesn't hurt to check for smaller pins, or bones, before dicing.
