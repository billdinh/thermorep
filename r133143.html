Blueberry and Raspberry Plaited Loaf

Ingredients

Dough

  • 100 g milk
  • 50 g water
  • 30 g unsalted butter, plus extra for greasing
  • 2 tsp dried instant yeast
  • 500 g strong white bread flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 30 g sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt

Filling

  • 30 g granulated sugar
  • 1 lemon, thin peelings of skin and 20 g juice
  • 50 g fresh blueberries
  • 70 g fresh raspberries
  • 100 g cream cheese
  • 50 g icing sugar

Glaze

  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp water
  • 2 Tbsp pearl sugar

Preparation

Dough

  1. Place milk, water, butter and yeast in mixing bowl then warm 2 min/37°C/speed 1.
  2. Add flour, sugar, egg, and salt then mix 30 sec/speed 4. Knead 4 min/. Meanwhile, grease a large bowl and large baking tray.
  3. Transfer dough to prepared bowl, cover with cling film and leave to rise in a warm place until doubled in size (approx. 1 hour). Meanwhile, make filling.

Filling

  1. Place sugar and lemon peel in mixing bowl then grind 10 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add blueberries, raspberries and 20 g lemon juice then cook 4 min/100°C/speed 0.5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside to cool.
  3. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in cream cheese and icing sugar. Remove from lid, mix together well then set aside.

Assembly

  1. Once dough has doubled in size, tip out onto a lightly floured surface and roll into a rectangle (40 cm x 30 cm). Transfer to prepared baking tray.
  2. Spread reserved cream cheese filling mixture down middle of longest length, leaving a 10 cm border at the sides and a 3 cm border at the ends. Spread cooled fruit compôte over cream cheese layer.
  3. On a diagonal, cut 2 cm wide strips into border of dough on each side. Alternating from side to side, fold strips one at a time over filling, overlapping each, removing corners for a neater finish at ends. Cover loosely with greased cling film and leave to rise in a warm place for 1 hour.
  4. Towards the end of proving time, preheat oven to 190°C.

Glaze

  1. Place egg and water in a small bowl then beat to combine. Brush risen loaf with egg wash then sprinkle with pearl sugar.
  2. Bake for 20-25 minutes (190°C) until golden brown, covering with foil halfway through if browning too much. Transfer to a wire rack and allow to cool.
  3. Serve at room temperature, cut in slices.

Useful Items

cling film, large bowl, baking tray, rolling pin, soft spatula, pastry brush, wire rack, aluminium foil, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • To save time, prepare the dough and filling the evening before and set aside in the fridge overnight. Continue with the assembly in the morning.
  • Use all blueberries or all raspberries instead of a mixture, if preferred. Or use a fruit jam instead.

This impressive-looking bread makes a lovely morning treat. It can be prepared in advance too, making sure you’re ready for any friends popping in for coffee.
