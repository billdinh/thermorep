Crêpes

Ingredients

  • 50 g butter, plus extra for frying
  • 500 g milk
  • 200 g water
  • 250 g plain flour
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 pinch salt
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
872 kJ / 209 kcal
Protein
7 g
Carbohydrates
21 g
Fat
11 g

Preparation

  1. Place butter into mixing bowl and melt 2 min/70°C/speed 2.
  2. Add milk, water, plain flour, eggs and salt and mix 10 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then mix a further 10 sec/speed 4. The batter should have the consistency of liquid cream. If it seems too thick, add a little milk. Allow batter to rest in refrigerator for at least 30 minutes before using.
  3. Lightly grease a non-stick frying pan with butter and heat on medium-high. Pour in a small ladle of batter, twirling the pan as you pour to cover the base of the pan completely. Cook for 2 - 3 minutes or until edges of crêpe are light golden. Turn crêpe over and cook for a further 1 minute. Transfer to a ThermoServer or a plate and cover with a kitchen towel to keep warm. Repeat with remaining batter. Serve warm.

Useful Items

turner / fish slice, ThermoServer, non-stick frying pan (22 cm), kitchen towel, ladle, refrigerator, stove

Hints & Tricks

  • To substitute butter with oil, omit step 1 and add 50 g oil along with all other ingredients in step 2. Use oil to grease the frying pan in step 3 and proceed as per recipe.
  • Crêpes can be served with sweet or savoury fillings.
  • Crêpes can be frozen for later use. Place a piece of baking paper or plastic wrap between each crêpe before freezing so they can be easily defrosted. After defrosting, wrap crêpes in aluminium foil and warm in a preheated oven (150ºC) for 5-10 minutes.
  • Vanilla crêpes: add 40 g vanilla sugar in step 2 and proceed as per recipe.
  • Orange crêpes: add 1 tsp grated orange zest in step 2 and proceed as per recipe.
  • Rum-flavoured crêpes: add 80 g sugar and 2 tbsp rum in step 2 and proceed as per recipe.
  • Gluten free crêpes: substitute flour with buckwheat flour and proceed as per recipe.
  • Dairy free crêpes: substitute butter with 40 g oil and milk with soy milk. Place all ingredients into mixing bowl and combine 20 sec/speed 4, then proceed as per recipe.
