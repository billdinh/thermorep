Witches' Finger Cookies

Ingredients

Almond Cookies

  • 4 ½ oz sugar
  • 8 oz unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp almond extract (optional)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 14 oz all-purpose flour

Decoration

  • 7 oz grape jelly
  • 46 sliced almonds

Preparation

Almond Cookies

  1. Place sugar into mixing bowl and grind 15 sec/speed 9.
  2. Add butter, egg, almond extract, vanilla, salt and flour. With aid of spatula through hole in mixing bowl lid mix 15 sec/speed 6. Divide dough in half and shape into disks. Tightly wrap dough in cling wrap and refrigerate 1 hour. Clean mixing bowl.
  3. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line a baking sheet with parchment and set aside.
  4. Working with half the dough at a time, roll into ¾ inch ropes. Cut into finger-like pieces (approx. 46 total). Use a toothpick to mark 3 lines for the "knuckles". At one end of each piece, apply pressure to form the nail bed cavity. Place on prepared baking sheet (approx. 1 in. apart) and bake for 12-18 minutes (350°F), or until lightly browned and cooked through. Allow to cool 5 minutes before placing on cooling rack to cool completely.

Decoration

  1. Place grape jelly into mixing bowl and heat 3 min/195°F/speed 1. Dip almond slices into jelly and place on fingernail cavity. Let set for 2 hours before serving. Store in an airtight container.

Useful Items

cling wrap, parchment paper, toothpick, cooling rack, airtight container, baking sheet, refrigerator, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • For a gluten-free version: replace all-purpose flour with gluten-free flour.
  • Coconut flakes can be used to replace almonds for fingernails. Break or cut large coconut flakes into nail shapes.
  • For a truly ghoulish cookie: lightly dip the end opposite the 'fingernail' into the hot jelly and place back onto baking sheet. Drizzle remaining jelly over fingers to look like blood splatter. Let set for 2 hours before serving.
  • Use 2 tsp of vanilla extract if desired, when not using almond extract.
  • Replace grape jelly with strawberry, mixed berry or another similarly colored jelly.
  • If jelly sets before you have time to complete decorating, just reheat remaining jelly in mixing bowl 2 min/195°F/speed 1.
  • Use a spoon to help drizzle jelly over almond fingers.
