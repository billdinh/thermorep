Sweet and Spicy Chicken Wings

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp salt, divided
  • 1 ½ tsp fresh ground black pepper
  • 64 oz chicken wings
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 2 jalapeño chilies, to taste
  • 8 oz yellow onion, quartered
  • ½ oz unsalted butter
  • 8 oz grape jelly
  • 4 oz bourbon
  • ½ oz Worcestershire sauce
  • ¼ tsp cayenne pepper optional
  • 28 oz water

Preparation

  1. Line a large baking pan (approx. 18 in. x 12 in. x 2 in.) with parchment and set aside. Place 1½ tsp salt and pepper into a small bowl and mix together. Place a large bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in chicken wings, sprinkle with salt and pepper mixture and toss through until well coated. Add olive oil and toss through again until well coated, cover with cling wrap and refrigerate (see Tip).
  2. Place garlic and jalapeño into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add onion and chop 3 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add butter, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 10 min/250°F/speed .
  5. Add grape jelly, bourbon, Worcestershire sauce, cayenne and ½ tsp salt, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 10 min/250°F/speed . Transfer to prepared baking pan and set aside.
  6. Place water into mixing bowl. Transfer the reserved wings into Varoma dish, arrange wings to ensure some holes remain unobstructed to allow for even distribution of steam, place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 15 min/Varoma/speed 3. Preheat oven to 450°F.
  7. Remove Varoma lid, turn over chicken wings, secure Varoma lid and steam a further 15 min/Varoma/speed 3.
  8. Transfer cooked wings to reserved baking pan and toss through glaze to coat.
  9. Arrange wings in a single layer and bake for 12-15 minutes (450°F), turn wings over and bake a further 12-15 minutes until caramelized and sticky. Serve warm or cold

Useful Items

large bowl, cling wrap, large baking pan (approx. 18 in. x 12 in. x 2 in.), parchment paper, refrigerator, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Omit pre-cooking wings in Varoma and bake in the oven or on the grill. Ensure chicken wings are fully cooked inside before serving if using this method. Recommended minimum internal temperature is 165°F.
  • Halve the glaze recipe if only cooking 32 oz. of chicken wings.
  • Chicken wings can be placed into a zip lock bag with salt and pepper and oil and left overnight in the refrigerator to marinate longer.
  • This recipe is based on small wings. For large wings or whole pieces break up into smaller pieces and steam 15 minutes then turn over and steam a further 15 minutes before adding glaze and baking.
  • Grape jelly can have different amounts and types of sugar depending on the brand. The sugar aids in the caramelization process, make sure glaze is the consistency of honey before adding it to the wings. Extend cooking of glaze by five minute intervals until desired consistency is achieved.
