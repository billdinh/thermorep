Cheesy Crust Pizza

Ingredients

  • 460 g mozzarella, drained, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 230 g water
  • 2 tsp dried instant yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 400 g strong white bread flour
  • 2 tsp fine sea salt, or to taste
  • 35 g olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 200 g onions, quartered
  • 400 g tinned chopped tomatoes

Preparation

  1. Place 230 g mozzarella in mixing bowl and grate 15 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place remaining 230 g mozzarella in mixing bowl and grate 15 sec/speed 6. Transfer to bowl with mozzarella and set aside.
  3. Place 30 g oil, water, yeast and sugar in mixing bowl then mix 20 sec/speed 2.
  4. Add flour and 1 tsp salt then knead 2 min/. Meanwhile, lightly grease a large bowl. Transfer to prepared bowl, cover with cling film and leave in a warm place until doubled in size (approx. 1 hour).
  5. Place onions and 5 g oil in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  6. Add tomatoes and 1 tsp salt then, with simmering basket in place of measuring cup, cook 10 min/120°C/speed 1.
  7. Preheat oven to 220°C.
  8. Place dough in centre of a pizza tray. Flatten into a disc and stretch dough evenly to edge of tray. Cover with greased cling film and leave to rest for 10 minutes. Stretch dough to hang over edge of tray (approx. 3 cm).
  9. Place half the reserved grated mozzarella in a ring around inside edge of pizza dough. Fold overhanging edge of dough over cheese and gently pinch all along the edge to firmly seal. Prick pizza base every 2.5 cm with a fork, leaving the cheesy crust edges intact. Bake for 10 minutes (220°C).
  10. Evenly spread sauce over pizza base and top with remaining grated mozzarella. Bake for a further 25 minutes (220°C) or until crust has set. Slice and serve immediately.

Useful Items

large bowl, pizza tray, cling film, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Top pizza with pepperoni or cooked, crumbled sausage.
  • Add more flavour to the sauce or the cheesy crust by adding minced garlic or fresh/dried basil or oregano.
