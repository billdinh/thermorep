XO sauce

Ingredients

  • 40 g dried scallops (see Tips)
  • 150 g boiling water
  • 200 g peanut oil (see Tips)
  • 6 fresh long red chillies, trimmed and deseeded
  • 60 g dried shrimp (see Tips)
  • 80 g eschalots
  • 2 - 3 garlic cloves
  • 40 g fresh ginger, peeled and cut into slices (1 cm)
  • 100 g smoked bacon, cut into pieces (1 cm)
  • 1 fresh bird's eye chilli, deseeded if preferred
  • 40 g light soy sauce
  • 40 g Chinese cooking wine
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh dried scallops and boiling water into it. Set aside to soak for 30 minutes.
  2. Place oil and long red chillies into mixing bowl and cook 8 min/90째C/speed 1. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place dried shrimp and scallops, with soaking liquid, into mixing bowl and blend 10 sec/speed 8. Transfer into a separate bowl and set aside.
  4. Place eschalots, garlic, ginger, bacon, bird's eye chilli and cooked chillies, reserving chilli oil, into mixing bowl and blend 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add reserved chilli oil and heat 10 min/120째C/speed 1. Scrape base of mixing bowl with spatula to loosen mixture.
  6. Add reserved scallop paste and cook 30 min/100째C/speed 1.5. Scrape base of mixing bowl with spatula to loosen mixture.
  7. Add soy sauce, wine and sugar, then cook 20 min/70째C/speed 1.5, without measuring cup. Transfer into a sealable container and allow to cool completely before serving.

Useful Items

bowl, sealable container

Hints & Tricks

  • Dried scallops and dried shrimp can be purchased at Asian food stores. If desired, scallops can be omitted and dried shrimp quantity increased to 100 g.
  • You can replace the peanut oil with vegetable oil in this recipe.
  • You can store sauce in a sealable container in the refrigerator for up to 1 month.
