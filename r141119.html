Dirt puddings

Ingredients

  • 130 - 150 g plain chocolate biscuits
  • 20 g cocoa powder
  • 60 g white sugar
  • 30 g cornflour
  • 2 eggs
  • 500 g full cream milk
  • 200 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 200 g vanilla yoghurt
  • 40 g caster sugar, to taste
  • lollies of choice or other edible decorations (see Tip)

Preparation

  1. Place biscuits into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 6, until they resemble breadcrumbs. Transfer into a sealable container and set aside until ready to use.
  2. Place cocoa powder, white sugar and cornflour into mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add eggs and milk and cook 7 min/90°C/speed 4. Divide evenly between 12 mini terracotta flower pots and place into refrigerator until completely cool (approx. 20 minutes). Do not clean mixing bowl.
  4. Insert butterfly whisk. Place cream, yoghurt and caster sugar into mixing bowl and whip 20-40 sec/speed 3 or until just thick.  Remove butterfly whisk.
  5. Remove chocolate custard cups from refrigerator and top evenly with yoghurt mixture. Cover with plastic wrap and place into refrigerator for 1 hour or overnight to cool and set. Top with reserved biscuit crumbs and decorate with lollies or other edible decorations just before serving.

Useful Items

sealable container, 12 mini terracotta flower pots, plastic wrap, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Use any kind of edible decorations to finish off your dirt puddings to suit the theme of your party (e.g. flowers, ladybirds, bugs and boulders).
