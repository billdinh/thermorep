Star anise and pistachio kulfi

Ingredients

  • 20 g unsalted shelled pistachios
  • 80 - 100 g raw sugar
  • 1 star anise
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • ½ tsp cardamom seeds (see Tips)
  • 750 g evaporated milk
  • 2 tbsp cornflour (see Tips)
  • 120 g pouring (whipping) cream

Preparation

  1. Place pistachios into mixing bowl and chop3 sec/speed 6. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place sugar, star anise, cinnamon and cardamom seeds into mixing bowl and mill 1 min/speed 10.
  3. Add evaporated milk and cornflour and cook 8-10 min/90°C/speed 4 or until mixture thickens.
  4. Add cream and reserved chopped pistachios and mix 15 sec/speed 3. Allow to cool (approx. 30 minutes) before dividing mixture between 4 kulfi moulds, dariole moulds or other small freezable containers and set aside to cool completely. Cover with plastic wrap and place into freezer for 2 hours or until just starting to set. Insert a popsicle stick into each kulfi (optional) and place back into freezer for a minimum of 5 hours or overnight. Remove from freezer and set aside for 10-15 minutes to soften slightly before serving.

Useful Items

bowl, popsicle sticks , plastic wrap, 4 kulfi or dariole moulds, freezer

Hints & Tricks

  • If you are unable to find cardamom seeds (the seed already removed from the cardamom pod), place pods into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec//speed 5. Remove pods and reserve seeds.
  • To ensure your recipe is gluten free, always check the labels of your products to make sure they don't contain traces of wheat or gluten.

Chai masala kulfi: Omit pistachios. Replace star anise with ¼ teaspoon fennel seeds, 3 whole cloves and ¾ teaspoon ground ginger. Proceed as per recipe, milling for an additional 10 sec/speed 6. When adding evaporated milk and cornflour, include 1 tablespoon black tea leaves and proceed as per recipe.
