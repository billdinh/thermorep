Quinoa Salmon Patties

Ingredients

  • 60 g quinoa, red, rinsed
  • 300 g water
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 lemon, thin peelings of skin, plus wedges for garnish
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 20 g spring onions, cut in pieces (1 cm)
  • 6 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only
  • 6 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 80 g bread, torn in pieces
  • 1 medium egg
  • 240 g fresh salmon fillets, skinless, cut in chunks (2 cm)
  • 1 Tbsp pickled capers, drained
  • olive oil, for frying

Preparation

  1. Place quinoa and water in mixing bowl then cook 15 min/100°C//speed 1. Strain through a fine sieve, discarding liquid then set aside in a bowl to cool.
  2. Place garlic, lemon peel, salt, pepper, spring onions, coriander and parsley in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then chop again 3 sec/speed 8.
  3. Add bread and chop 5 sec/speed 8.
  4. Add cooled quinoa, egg, salmon and capers then mix 5 sec/speed 4. Using wet hands, shape into 8 patties then place on a large plate. Cover and place in fridge to firm up for at least 30 minutes.
  5. Heat oil in a non-stick frying pan over a medium heat. Fry patties for 3-5 minutes on each side until cooked and golden.
  6. Serve 2 patties per portion, warm, with lemon slices to squeeze over at the table.
