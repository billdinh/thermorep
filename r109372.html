Strawberry Mousse Waffle Cones

Ingredients

  • 10 ½ oz dark chocolate, to line ice cream cones
  • 10 waffle cones (see Tip)
  • 10 ½ oz fresh strawberries, hulled and cut in half
  • 7 oz sugar
  • 2 tsp unflavored gelatin powder
  • 6 ½ oz cream cheese
  • 9 oz heavy whipping cream
  • 10 marshmallows
  • fresh mint leaves, to garnish
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
690 kJ / 165 kcal
Protein
3 g

Preparation

  1. Place chocolate into mixing bowl and chop 8 sec/speed 7. Melt chocolate 4 min/120°F/speed 3.5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and melt 1 min/120°F/speed 3.5. Transfer to a bowl and use melted chocolate to line inside of ice cream cones. Once the internal cone is covered with a thin layer of chocolate, turn upside down on a cooling rack and allow to drain. Refrigerate to set chocolate. Clean mixing bowl.
  2. Place strawberries and sugar into mixing bowl and mix 20 sec/speed 8.
  3. Add gelatin and heat 5 min/140°F/speed 2. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place cream cheese and whipping cream into mixing bowl and mix 45 sec/speed 10.
  5. Add reserved strawberry mixture and mix 20 sec/speed 3. Transfer to a bowl and refrigerate 45-60 minutes.
  6. Transfer strawberry mousse into an icing bag with a star tip. Place 1 marshmallow into the base of each cone, pipe chilled strawberry mousse into cones and garnish with fresh mint leaves. Refrigerate until ready to serve.

Useful Items

cooling rack, icing bag and (star) piping tip, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Garnish the strawberry mousse with candy sprinkles or melted chocolate.
  • Use an ice cream cone stand or place each cone in a tall glass for support while filling and as a place to hold until serving.
  • If using a flat based cone, you will need 10 extra marshmallows to fill the space in the base of the cone.
  • Store bought chocolate-lined waffle cones will save time.
  • Try other fresh fruits like blackberries, raspberries or mangos.
  • For a firmer, richer mousse, replace organic heavy whipping cream with double cream.
