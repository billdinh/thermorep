Chestnut and Fennel Soup - Zuppa di castagne e finocchio

Ingredients

  • 120 g onions, quartered
  • 100 g leeks, white part only, cut in pieces
  • 120 g fennel, cut in pieces
  • 2 - 3 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves only, to taste
  • 50 g salted butter, diced
  • 100 g dry white wine
  • 350 g cooked, peeled chestnuts
  • 1 chicken stock cube (for 0.5 l)
  • 800 g water
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt, or to taste
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper, or to taste

Preparation

  1. Place onions, leeks, fennel, thyme and butter in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then cook 10 min/98°C/speed .
  2. Add wine and, without measuring cup, simmer 3 min/100°C/speed 0.5.
  3. Add chestnuts, stock cube, water, salt and pepper then cook 20 min/98°C/speed 0.5. Blend 1 min/speed 5-7, increasing speed gradually to speed 7.
  4. Adjust seasoning to taste, then serve warm.
