Raw Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 10 ½ oz pecans
  • 5 ½ oz almonds
  • 2 oz raw cacao butter
  • 6 ½ oz raw cacao powder, divided
  • 3 oz raw honey, divided
  • 5 ½ oz pitted Medjool dates
  • 5 ½ oz dried figs
  • 1 ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • 7 oz coconut oil
  • 3 oz dark agave syrup
  • fennel pollen, to garnish

Preparation

  1. Line a springform pan with parchment paper and set aside.
  2. Place pecans into mixing bowl and chop 6 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place almonds into mixing bowl and chop 6 sec/speed 10. Transfer to another bowl and set aside.
  4. Place cacao butter into mixing bowl and melt 4 min/120°F/speed 1. Add reserved almonds, 1.5 oz cacao powder and 1.5 oz honey into mixing bowl and mix 6 sec/speed 4. Evenly press mixture onto bottom of prepared pan. Place in freezer for 30 minutes until firm.
  5. Place dates and figs into mixing bowl and chop Turbo/2 sec/3 times. Add reserved pecans, 1.5 oz cacao powder, 1.5 oz honey and vanilla and mix 10 sec/speed 8. Evenly spread filling mixture on top of chilled base. Place in freezer for 1 hour until firm.
  6. Place coconut oil, 3.5 oz cacao powder and agave into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 5. Evenly spread frosting over chilled cake and sprinkle with pollen. Place in freezer for 30 minutes, until firm.
  7. Run a sharp knife around the edge of the springform pan and remove cake. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

parchment paper, springform pan (8 in.), sharp knife, freezer

Hints & Tricks

  • Tightly wrap cake in plastic wrap and keep chilled until ready to serve.
  • Fennel pollen is an incredibly powerful spice, with notes of licorice, citrus, and handmade marshmallows. You can purchase fennel pollen at farmer's markets, specialty spice shops or online.
