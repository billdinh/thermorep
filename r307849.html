BBQ Pulled Jackfruit with Coleslaw

Ingredients

Coleslaw

  • 3 oz radishes, stems removed, in pieces (½ in.)
  • 3 oz carrots, in pieces (½ in.)
  • 3 oz lacinato kale, ribs removed, in pieces (½ in.)
  • 1 oz red onion
  • 1 oz olive oil
  • 1 oz unsweetened almond milk, plain
  • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tsp honey
  • ¼ tsp Dijon mustard
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper

BBQ Pulled Jackfruit

  • 3 oz red onion, in pieces
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • ½ oz olive oil
  • 3 oz bbq sauce
  • 1 oz water
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • ½ tsp chili powder, to taste
  • ½ tsp salt
  • &frac18; tsp ground black pepper
  • 14 oz jackfruit, canned in brine, rinsed, drained
  • 6 hamburger buns
  • 3 oz red onions, thinly sliced, to serve

Preparation

Coleslaw

  1. Place radishes, carrots, kale and onion into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl.
  2. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in olive oil and set aside.
  3. Place almond milk, vinegar, honey, mustard, salt and pepper into mixing bowl and blend 1 min 30 sec/speed 4 while pouring reserved olive oil onto mixing bowl lid, letting it drizzle around measuring cup in a thin stream onto rotating blades to create an emulsion. Toss dressing with reserved vegetables and set aside.

BBQ Pulled Jackfruit

  1. Place 3 oz onion, garlic and oil into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and cook 3 min/250°F/speed 1.
  2. Add bbq sauce, water, brown sugar, paprika, chili powder, salt and pepper and cook 2 min/212°F/speed .
  3. Add jackfruit and cook 2 min/212°F//speed 2. Serve hot on hamburger buns, topped with Coleslaw and thinly sliced red onions.
