Coriander pesto

Ingredients

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 45 g fresh coriander, stalks and leaves only, cut into pieces (approx. 1½ bunches)
  • 50 g raw unsalted cashews
  • 40 g sunflower seeds
  • 10 g pepitas
  • 40 g lemon juice (approx. 1 lemon)
  • 100 g avocado oil
  • ¼ tsp salt, to taste
  • 1 tsp dulse flakes (optional - see Tips)
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
635 kJ / 151 kcal
Protein
2.5 g
Carbohydrates
1.7 g
Fat
15 g

Preparation

  1. Place garlic, coriander, cashews, sunflower seeds and pepitas into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7.
  2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and blend 8 sec/speed 8.
  3. Add lemon juice, oil, salt and dulse flakes (optional) and mix 10 sec/speed 4. Transfer into a sealable container and place into refrigerator until ready to serve.

Useful Items

sealable container, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Dulse is a type of sea vegetable (red algae) that is high in vitamins and minerals, including iodine, magnesium and zinc, and it adds an umami flavour boost. You can find it in most health food stores.
  • We used this pesto in our Coriander pesto pizza (please refer to the recipe on this recipe chip).
  • You can store this pesto in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.
