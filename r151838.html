Chicken with creamy vegetable sauce

Ingredients

  • 400 g water
  • 130 g carrots, cut in pieces
  • 60 g celeriac, cut in pieces
  • 25 g fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 1 chicken stock cube (for 0.5 l)
  • 600 g chicken breasts, cut in strips (5 x 2 cm)
  • ½ tsp salt, plus extra to taste
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper, plus extra to taste
  • 150 g button mushrooms, cut in slices
  • 30 g plain flour
  • 100 g cream
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce (optional)
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 30 g butter, cut in pieces

Preparation

  1. Place water, carrots, celeriac, parsley and chicken stock cube in mixing bowl. Season chicken fillet strips with salt and pepper and place into Varoma dish. Place Varoma into position and cook 15 min/Varoma/speed 1. Meanwhile, place sliced button mushrooms on Varoma tray and set aside.
  2. Carefully open Varoma lid away from you and flip chicken strips with spatula to ensure chicken is thoroughly cooked. Insert Varoma tray with button mushrooms, close Varoma lid and cook for a further 10 min/Varoma/speed 1. Set Varoma aside.
  3. Add flour, cream, Worcestershire sauce (if using) and lemon juice to mixing bowl and cook 1 min/100°C/speed 6.
  4. Add cooked button mushroom slices and butter and cook 1 min/80°C//speed 1. Adjust seasoning to taste and serve chicken and sauce separately or cover chicken with sauce before serving.
