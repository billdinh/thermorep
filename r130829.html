Pulled Pork Rolls

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp dried chilli flakes
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp mustard powder
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 450 g pork fillet, cut in pieces (5 cm)
  • 700 g water, plus 1 Tbsp water
  • 70 g barbecue sauce
  • 2 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp cider vinegar
  • 4 brioche burger buns

Preparation

  1. In a bowl, place chilli flakes, paprika, mustard powder, salt and pepper then mix together well. Place bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in pork pieces. Remove bowl, rub spice mix over pork pieces then place pork in Varoma dish and set aside.
  2. Place water in mixing bowl. Place covered Varoma into position then steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 2 until pork is cooked through. Discard contents of mixing bowl.
  3. Place cooked pork in mixing bowl then shred 10 sec//speed 4.
  4. Add barbecue sauce, Dijon mustard, cider vinegar and remaining 1 Tbsp water then mix 5 sec//speed 1. Stir once more with spatula by hand. Serve hot, in a brioche burger bun, with salad or coleslaw.
