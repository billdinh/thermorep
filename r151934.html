Floating Islands

Ingredients

Vanilla Sauce (Crème Anglaise)

  • 450 g milk
  • 6 egg yolks, from medium eggs
  • 70 g sugar
  • 15 g vanilla sugar, homemade

Meringue Islands

  • 30 g sugar
  • 6 egg whites, from medium eggs
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
  • 30 g liquid caramel (see tip) (optional)
  • 25 g pistachio nuts, unsalted, shelled, chopped (optional)

Preparation

Vanilla Sauce (Crème Anglaise)

  1. Place milk, egg yolks, sugar and vanilla sugar in mixing bowl, heat 7 min/80°C/speed 4 then mix 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a serving bowl or individual dessert bowls and leave to cool. Clean and dry mixing bowl thoroughly.

Meringue Islands

  1. Place sugar in mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 10.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Add egg whites and salt then whisk 6 min/70°C/speed 3.5 or until stiff. Remove butterfly whisk.
  3. Divide egg whites into 6 portions on Varoma tray, leaving any liquid to drain for a few minutes. Transfer egg whites onto cool or lukewarm vanilla sauce. Just before serving, drizzle with liquid caramel and sprinkle with chopped pistachios. Serve immediately.
