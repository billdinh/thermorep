Chocolate Mousse

Ingredients

  • 50 g sugar
  • 200 g dark chocolate (50% cocoa solids), small pieces or callets
  • 100 g cream, min. 30 % fat (whipping or double)
  • 4 medium eggs, separated (see tip)
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt

Preparation

  1. Place sugar in mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add chocolate and cream then melt 4 min/50Â°C/speed 2.
  3. Add egg yolks (see tip) and mix 15 sec/speed 4. Transfer mixture to a medium-sized bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl thoroughly.
  4. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg whites and salt in mixing bowl then whisk 3 min/speed 3.5 or until stiff. Add whisked egg whites to reserved chocolate mixture and carefully fold in with spatula. Divide between 6 individual dessert bowls and refrigerate for at least 3 hours before serving.

Hints & Tricks

  • Constant stirring while heating results in a reliable molten chocolate sauce, which is then folded into perfectly whipped cream to create a decadent dessert.
  • Take care, this recipe contains raw eggs.
  • For a darker mousse, use chocolate with 70% cocoa.
  • Add 2-3 tsp orange-flavoured liqueur or rum in step 3.
  • Fold in finely grated orange zest or chocolate chips in step 4, after folding in the egg whites.
