Peach Juice

Ingredients

  • 240 g water
  • 120 g sugar
  • 6 sprigs fresh mint, leaves only, plus extra for garnish
  • 300 g peaches, peeled, stone removed
  • 150 g ice cubes
  • 1 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 400 - 500 g sparkling mineral water, to taste
Nutrition
per 1 glasses
Calories
642 kJ / 154 kcal
Protein
0.3 g
Carbohydrates
25.2 g
Fat
0.7 g

Preparation

  1. Place water, sugar and mint in mixing bowl then heat 5 min/120°C/speed 1. Strain through a fine sieve or nut milk bag collecting liquid below, discarding mint.
  2. Place peaches in mixing bowl and chop 30 sec/speed 5 -10, increasing speed gradually. Scrape down sides and lid of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add reserved syrup and mix 10 sec/speed 3. Pour into a bowl and allow to cool before placing in fridge until chilled (approx. 2 hours).
  4. Place ice in mixing bowl and crush 10 sec/speed 5. Divide evenly between 6 glasses.
  5. Place chilled peach mixture and lemon juice in mixing bowl then add sparkling water. Mix 10 sec/speed 3. Pour over crushed ice in glasses then garnish with mint leaves and serve immediately.

Useful Items

large bowl, fine sieve or nut milk bag, 6 glasses, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • For a sweeter version, lemonade can be used instead of sparkling mineral water.
