Tex-mex burger

Ingredients

Burger buns

  • 50 g water
  • 10 g fresh yeast
  • 50 g milk
  • 15 g caster sugar
  • 250 g plain flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 40 g butter
  • 1 pinch fine salt
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds

Barbecue sauce

  • 30 g shallots
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 15 g olive oil
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper
  • 50 g brown sugar
  • 125 g tomato passata
  • 2 tbsp mustard
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice (optional)
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp cornflour (starch)

Tex-mex burgers

  • 100 g onions, cut into halves
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 25 g olive oil
  • 2 tsp Mexican spice mix or any other spice mix of choice
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper
  • 150 g tomatoes, diced
  • 50 g canned sweetcorn kernels, drained
  • 600 g beef mince, formed into 5 burger patties
  • 6 slices Emmental cheese or any other medium-hard cheese (e.g. cheddar, gouda)
  • 30 g salad leaves, for garnishing

Preparation

Burger buns

  1. Place water, yeast, milk, sugar, flour and 1 egg into mixing bowl and knead 5 min/.
  2. Add butter and salt and knead 2 min/. Transfer dough onto a previously floured work surface. Pull edges of dough towards centre 3-4 times. Roll into a ball, place in a large bowl, cover with a kitchen towel and allow to rise for 1-2 hours in a warm place until doubled in size. Clean mixing bowl.
  3. At the end of first proving, transfer dough to floured work surface and knock it back firmly with your hands to let the gas escape. Shape into a log, then divide into 5 balls. Transfer buns to a baking tray lined with baking paper, making sure there is 5 cm between them. Cover with a kitchen towel and allow to rise again for 1 hour.
  4. Preheat oven to 210°C.
  5. At the end of the second proving, break remaining egg into a bowl and beat it with a fork. Brush buns with egg using a pastry brush. Sprinkle buns with sesame seeds and bake for 15-20 minutes (210°C). Take buns out of oven and allow to cool.

Barbecue sauce

  1. Place shallots and garlic into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add olive oil and Cayenne pepper and sauté 2 min/120°C/speed  without measuring cup.
  3. Add brown sugar and sauté 2 min/100°C/speed  without measuring cup.
  4. Add tomato passata, mustard, lemon juice and Worcester sauce and cook 5 min/100°C/speed .
  5. Add cornstarch and cook 2 min/100°C/speed 1,then blend 30 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.

Tex-mex burgers

  1. Place onions and garlic in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add 10 g olive oil, spice mix and cayenne pepper and sauté 4 min/120°C/speed  without measuring cup.
  3. Add tomatoes and corn and cook 1 min/100°C//speed . Heat remaining olive oil in a non-stick frying pan and cook burgers for 2 minutes on each side. Halve buns, spread barbecue sauce on both sides, place onion mixture, then a burger and a slice of Emmental on base. Place top of bun on burgers, place in oven and cook for 5 minutes (200°C). Remove from oven, add some salad leaves to garnish and serve immediately.
