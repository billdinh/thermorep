Butter Chicken

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tbsp cumin seeds
  • 2 cinnamon sticks, broken into pieces
  • 14 cardamom pods
  • 1 tsp black peppercorns
  • 2 heaping tsp sweet smoked paprika
  • 4 tsp coriander seeds
  • ½ tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1 tbsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 8 oz plain yogurt
  • 1 heaping tsp tomato paste
  • 6 chicken thighs, air-chilled, boneless, skinless (approx. 30 oz)
  • 4 oz yellow onions, quartered
  • 1 piece ginger (2 in.)
  • 4 oz unsalted butter
  • 8 oz tomatoes (fresh, halved or canned)
  • 1 heaping tsp salt
  • 20 curry leaves, (optional)
  • 8 oz heavy whipping cream

Steamed Rice

  • 8 oz basmati rice
  • 35 oz water
  • 10 sprigs cilantro, chopped, to garnish

Preparation

  1. Place chili powder, cumin, cinnamon, cardamom, peppercorns, paprika, coriander, nutmeg, and turmeric into mixing bowl and without measuring cup dry roast 4 min/212°F/speed 1, then insert measuring cup and grind 1 min/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Remove half the mixture, transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Add lemon juice, yogurt and tomato paste and mix 20 sec/speed 3. Transfer yogurt mixture to a large bowl, add chicken and mix well with spatula to coat. Cover with cling wrap and place in the refrigerator to marinate for 3-24 hours.
  3. Place onions, ginger, butter and reserved spice mixture into mixing bowl and chop 15 sec/speed 7, then cook 10 min/212°F/speed 1.
  4. Add tomatoes and salt and blend 10 sec/speed 9.
  5. Add chicken with marinade, curry leaves and whipping cream and cook 18 min/212°F//speed 1 or until chicken is cooked through. Set aside and keep warm. Do not wash mixing bowl.

Steamed Rice

  1. Place simmering basket onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in rice. Remove simmering basket with rice and rinse rice under cold running water until water runs clear (approx. 30 sec.).
  2. Place water into mixing bowl, insert simmering basket with rice and mix 5 sec/speed 5, then steam 18 min/Varoma/speed 2. Transfer to a bowl and keep warm.
  3. Transfer chicken to a serving dish and sprinkle with chopped cilantro. Serve immediately with rice.

Useful Items

cling wrap, large glass bowl, serving dish, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Replace boneless, skinless chicken thighs with 6 bone-in chicken thighs, air-chilled (approx. 30 oz). Follow recipe and cook 32-35 min/212°F//speed 1 or until chicken is cooked through.
