Hash browns

Ingredients

  • 36 oz russet potatoes, peeled, cut into pieces (approx. 2 in.)
  • 6 oz green apples, e.g. Granny Smith, peeled, cored, cut into pieces (approx. 2 in.)
  • 6 oz yellow onions, cut into pieces (approx. 2 in.)
  • 2 heaping tbsp corn starch
  • ½ oz lemon juice
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • ¼ tsp ground cinnamon (optional)
  • frying oil
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1310 kJ / 313 kcal

6 g

7 g

Preparation

  1. Place potatoes into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a large bowl and set aside. Place apples and onions into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer to large bowl with reserved potatoes and mix through with spatula.
  2. Transfer chopped mixture to a clean kitchen towel and squeeze out as much liquid as possible. Discard liquid.
  3. Place potato mixture, corn starch, lemon juice, salt, pepper and cinnamon into mixing bowl and with aid of spatula mix 10 sec//speed 4.
  4. Pour frying oil into large non-stick pan over medium heat. Place evenly spaced ladles of potato mixture on preheated frying pan and press down with the back of a spatula. Fry until golden brown and crispy on the outside (approx. 6-7 min. each side). Drain on paper towels. Repeat with remaining potato mixture. Serve hot.

Useful Items

kitchen towel, non-stick frying pan, ladle, paper towels, plastic or silicone spatula

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with fried or poached eggs or with a dollop of sour cream and freshly cut chives.
