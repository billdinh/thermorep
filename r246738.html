Pear and peach crumble

Ingredients

Crumble

  • 70 g wholemeal flour
  • 120 g plain flour
  • 100 g light brown sugar
  • 100 g unsalted butter, cold and cut into pieces, plus extra for greasing
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 50 g rolled oats
  • 50 g flaked almonds

Peach and pear filling

  • 1 orange, zest only, no white pith
  • 30 g light brown sugar
  • 600 g pears, peeled, cored and cut into quarters
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 410 g canned peaches, drained (approx. 245 g after draining)

Preparation

Crumble

  1. Place flours, sugar, butter and baking powder into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 4.
  2. Add oats and almonds and mix 5 sec/speed 4. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.

Peach and pear filling

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Grease a pie dish (23 cm) and set aside.
  2. Place orange zest and sugar into mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add pears and cinnamon and chop 3 sec/speed 3.5, then cook 6 min/100°C//speed .
  4. Add drained peaches and cook 1 min/100°C//speed . Transfer into prepared pie dish (23 cm) and top with reserved crumble. Place onto a baking tray and bake for 20-25 minutes (180°C) or until golden. Allow to cool slightly, then serve warm.

Useful Items

bowl, baking tray, pie dish (23 cm), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • When in season, use fresh peaches. You will need to blanch fresh peaches in boiling water to soften them and easily remove the skins. Proceed as per recipe.
  • You can serve this with cream, ice cream or yoghurt.
