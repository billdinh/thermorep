Chicken with red peppers

Ingredients

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 40 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 350 g red peppers, cut into pieces (2-3 cm)
  • 1 sprig fresh basil
  • 2 teaspoon fine salt
  • 1 sprig fresh thyme
  • 1 sprig fresh rosemary
  • 1 pinch red chilli (optional)
  • 1 dried bay leaf
  • 2 - 3 anchovy fillets, in oil or brine, well drained
  • 700 g chicken legs, skinless, bone in (thighs and drumsticks separated)
  • 100 g lager beer
  • 50 g vegetable broth (optional)
  • 1 pinch ground white pepper

Preparation

  1. Place 1 garlic clove and 20 g olive oil into mixing bowl and sauté 2 min/120°C/speed 1.
  2. Add peppers, basil and 1 teaspoon of salt, cook 15 min/100°C//speed 1. Transfer to a bowl, discard the garlic and set aside.
  3. Place remaining olive oil, remaining clove of garlic, thyme, rosemary, chilli, bay into mixing bowl and sauté 2 min/120°C/speed 1.
  4. Add the anchovies, chicken pieces and beer and cook 25 min/120°C//speed 1. If necessary, add the broth and extend the cooking time by several minutes.
  5. Add the cooked peppers, season, continue to cook 10 min/100°C//speed 1. Transfer the chicken and peppers to a serving plate, let it rest for a few minutes and serve.

Useful Items

bowl, serving plate

Hints & Tricks

  • Depending on the size of the chicken thighs, it may be necessary to extend the cooking time in step 5 by a few minutes.
