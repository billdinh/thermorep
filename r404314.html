Apple Tarte Tatin with Crème Anglaise

Ingredients

Crème Anglaise

  • ½ vanilla bean
  • 4 oz sugar
  • 16 oz whipping cream
  • 5 oz whole milk
  • 6 egg yolks, from large eggs

Apple Tarte Tatin

  • 4 oz dark brown sugar
  • 2 oz unsalted butter, in pieces
  • ¼ tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 pinch ground cloves
  • ¼ tsp ground nutmeg
  • ¼ tsp ground ginger
  • 1 oz calvados
  • 4 Fuji apples, peeled, cored and quartered
  • 1 sheet puff pastry, ready-made, thawed per pkg. instructions

Preparation

Crème Anglaise

  1. Place vanilla bean and sugar into mixing bowl and grind 20 sec/speed 10.
  2. Add whipping cream, milk and egg yolks and cook 10 min/185°F/speed 3. Transfer to a bowl and refrigerate until chilled. Clean mixing bowl.

Apple Tarte Tatin

  1. Place brown sugar, butter, cinnamon, cloves, nutmeg and ginger into mixing bowl and cook 10 min/212°F/speed 1.
  2. Add calvados and blend 5 sec/speed 4. Pour caramel mixture into bottom of a cast iron skillet or oven-proof frying pan (9 in.).
  3. Arrange apples peeled side up over caramel mixture.
  4. Place pan on stove top over medium-high heat for 4-5 minutes. Allow to cool (approx. 20 min.). Preheat oven to 425°F.
  5. Lay thawed puff pastry over apples, press down slightly and trim edges if necessary. With a sharp knife, cut a small hole in center of pastry, to vent.
  6. Bake for 20 minutes (425°F). Reduce heat to 350°F and bake a further 20-25 minutes (350°F). Remove from oven and allow to cool 5 minutes. Invert tart onto a large serving plate and allow to cool slightly before serving warm with Crème Anglaise.

Useful Items

large serving plate, cast iron skillet (9 in.) or oven-proof frying pan (9 in.), stove top, oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • For an ice cream treat: freeze Crème Anglaise in ice cube trays overnight. To serve, place frozen cubes into mixing bowl and blend 40 sec/speed 10 with aid of spatula.
  • To grind your own spices: place 2 sticks cinnamon, halved, 4 whole cloves, 1 whole nutmeg and 1 tsp ground ginger into mixing bowl and grind 1 min/speed 10. Use 1 tsp of mixture for Apple Tarte Tatin and transfer remainder to an airtight container and save for another use.
