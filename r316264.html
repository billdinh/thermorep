Carrot Cake Energy Balls

Ingredients

  • 100 - 120 g carrots, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 3 medjool dates, pitted, halved
  • 4 dried apricots, pitted
  • 2 Tbsp apple sauce (unsweetened)
  • 2 Tbsp maple syrup
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 80 g rolled oats
  • 30 g coconut flour
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • ¼ tsp ground nutmeg
  • ¼ tsp ground ginger
  • 10 - 20 g desiccated coconut, for rolling
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
305 kJ / 72 kcal
Protein
1.5 g
Carbohydrates
11.7 g

1.5 g

Preparation

  1. Place carrots in mixing bowl then grate 3 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place dates, apricots, apple sauce, maple syrup and vanilla extract in mixing bowl then chop 6 sec/speed 7.
  3. Add oats, coconut flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger and reserved grated carrot then mix 10 sec/speed 3. Scrape down sides and lid of mixing bowl with spatula then mix again 10 sec/speed 3.
  4. Place desiccated coconut in a bowl. Take heaped tablespoons of mixture, form into 12 balls then roll in coconut until covered.
  5. Serve or store in fridge in an airtight container for up to 5 days.

Useful Items

airtight container, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Light and fluffy, these energy-laden balls have all the elements of a carrot cake wrapped up in a tasty ball! For additional protein, a scoop of protein powder could be added instead of the coconut flour. A delicious pre or post run snack, which non-runners would enjoy as well!
