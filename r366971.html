Mongolian Beef with Rice and Steamed Orange Cake

Ingredients

Steamed Orange Cake

  • 4 oz unsalted butter, cubed, plus extra to grease
  • 6 tbsp orange marmalade, divided
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 orange, preferably organic, thinly sliced in half-moons
  • 2 ½ oz raw almonds
  • 4 oz sugar
  • 4 oz all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • ¼ tsp baking soda
  • &frac18; tsp salt

Rice

  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 6 green onions, cut into pieces
  • 5 garlic cloves
  • 1 oz ginger, peeled, cut into pieces
  • 30 oz water
  • 3 ½ oz hoisin sauce
  • 2 ½ oz soy sauce, light
  • 2 oz brown sugar
  • 2 oz Chinese cooking wine
  • 6 oz jasmine rice

Mongolian Beef

  • 12 oz broccolini, trimmed, halved lengthwise
  • 28 oz prime beef sirloin, sliced (2 in. x ¼ in.)
  • 1 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 3 green onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds, for garnish (optional)

Preparation

Steamed Orange Cake

  1. Lightly grease a round cake pan (approx. 8 in.).
  2. Combine 4 tbsp marmalade and honey in a small bowl. Evenly spread mixture into bottom of prepared pan, top with orange slices and set aside.
  3. Place almonds and sugar into mixing bowl and grind 8 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add butter and heat 4 min/170°F/speed 1, then mix 3 sec/speed 4.
  5. Add flour, 2 tbsp marmalade, eggs, baking powder, vanilla, baking soda and salt and mix 15 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix a further 15 sec/speed 4. Pour batter into prepared cake pan, place pan into Varoma dish (see Tip) and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.

Rice

  1. Place sesame oil, green onions, garlic and ginger into mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and sauté 3 min/250°F/speed 1.
  2. Add water, hoisin, soy, brown sugar and cooking wine.
  3. Place simmering basket onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in rice. Remove simmering basket and rinse rice under cold running water until water runs clear. Insert simmering basket with rinsed rice into mixing bowl, set Varoma with cake into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 30 min/Varoma/speed 2. Remove Varoma and set cake aside to cool for 10 minutes before inverting onto a serving platter. Transfer steamed rice to a bowl and keep warm.

Mongolian Beef

  1. Set Varoma dish instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and without Varoma lid steam 12 min/Varoma/speed 2 to reduce sauce.
  2. Weigh broccolini into Varoma dish, secure Varoma lid and steam 8 min/Varoma/speed 2. Remove Varoma and keep broccolini warm.
  3. Add beef and flour into mixing bowl, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 8 min/195°F//speed 1. Transfer beef over reserved rice and pour the sauce over beef, to taste. Arrange broccolini onto serving dish and garnish with green onions and sesame seeds. Serve Mongolian Beef with Rice hot and finish with slightly warm or cooled Steamed Orange Cake for dessert.

Hints & Tricks

  • Place a canning lid ring on the bottom of the Varoma dish and set cake pan on top. This method will allow steam to circulate and ensure an evenly steamed cake.
