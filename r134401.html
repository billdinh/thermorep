Stuffed Seitan Roll with Champagne Rice

Ingredients

Seitan Roll

  • 100 g onions, quartered
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 30 g soy sauce
  • 100 g tinned kidney beans, drained (drained weight)
  • 30 g fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 1100 g water
  • 200 g vital wheat gluten
  • 1 courgette, thickly sliced lengthways (see tip)
  • 1 carrot, thickly sliced lengthways (see tip)
  • 1 celery stalk
  • 10 g fresh basil, leaves only
  • 60 g hard cheese, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 40 g bread, torn in pieces
  • 1 medium egg

Champagne Rice

  • 30 g olive oil
  • 30 g shallots
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 500 g water
  • 500 g Champagne
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • ½ tsp ground turmeric
  • 350 g long grain rice
  • 50 g pecan nuts
  • 50 g banana, sliced
  • 1 Tbsp fresh chives, chopped

Preparation

Seitan Roll

  1. Place onions, garlic, pepper, soy sauce, beans and 10 g parsley in mixing bowl then chop 10 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add 100 g water and vital wheat gluten then mix 10 sec/speed 4. Tip out onto a large piece of cling film and flatten into a rectangle. Arrange courgette, carrot and celery in centre then roll up using cling film, enclosing vegetables in centre. Discard cling film and place roll in Varoma dish.
  3. Place remaining 1000 g water in mixing bowl, place covered Varoma into position, then steam 40 min/Varoma/speed 2. Towards end of steaming time, preheat oven to 200°C. Remove Varoma and place seitan roll in an oven-proof dish. Clean mixing bowl.
  4. Place remaining 20 g parsley, basil, cheese and bread in mixing bowl then grate 7 sec/speed 7.
  5. Add egg and mix 10 sec/speed 3. Cover seitan roll with mixture then bake for 20 minutes (200°C) or until golden brown. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl and make rice.

Champagne Rice

  1. Place oil, shallots and garlic in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Sauté 5 min/120°C/speed 1. Transfer to simmering basket.
  2. Place water, Champagne, salt and turmeric in mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket and weigh in rice. Stir well with spatula then cook 20 min/100°C/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and garnish with pecans, sliced banana and chives. Slice seitan roll and serve with the rice.

Useful Items

cling film, oven-proof dish, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Cut the courgette and carrot so that they are a similar size to the stick of celery.
