Chicken and Tomato Sincronizadas

Ingredients

Guacamole

  • 5 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only
  • 100 g onions, quartered
  • 2 fresh jalapeño chillies, deseeded
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 tomato, quartered
  • 2 avocados, stoned, cut in pieces (approx. 450 g)
  • 1 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt

Chicken and Tomato Quesadillas

  • 150 g onions, quartered
  • 10 g fresh coriander, leaves only
  • 40 g oil
  • 1 fresh jalapeño chilli, deseeded
  • 400 g tinned chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 230 g chicken breasts, cut in pieces (3 cm)
  • 12 wheat flour tortillas
  • 100 g Cheddar cheese, grated

Preparation

Guacamole

  1. Place coriander in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5.
  2. Add onions, jalapeno chillies, garlic and tomato then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place avocados, lemon juice and salt in mixing bowl then mix 5 sec/speed 4.
  4. Add reserved coriander and onion-chilli mix, then mix 10 sec/speed 3. Transfer to a serving bowl.

Chicken and Tomato Quesadillas

  1. Place onions, coriander, oil and jalapeno chillis in mixing bowl then chop 2 sec/speed 5. Sauté 5 min/120°C/speed .
  2. Add chopped tomatoes and salt then cook 10 min/120°C/speed .
  3. Add chicken and cook 10 min/120°C/speed 1. Holding measuring cup in place, shred 4 sec//speed 4.
  4. Place 6 tortillas on work surface and distribute chicken filling between them, spreading out thinly towards edges. Sprinkle each with grated cheese, then cover with the remaining 6 tortillas.
  5. Heat a non-stick frying pan then cook each sincronizada, one at a a time, for 2-3 minutes on each side, until heated through and cheese has melted. Cut each sincronizada in quarters and serve with the guacamole.

Useful Items

serving bowl, large non-stick frying pan, hob

Hints & Tricks

  • Although similar to quesadillas, these sincronizadas use flour tortillas, whereas traditional quesadillas are made with corn tortillas.
  • Placing the avocado stone in the centre of the guacamole helps to prevent it from discolouring.
