Braised Chicken with Chestnuts

Ingredients

  • 10 g fresh root ginger, peeled, cut in round slices (2 mm)
  • 10 g vegetable oil
  • 2 star anise
  • 1 tsp ground Sichuan pepper (see tip) (optional)
  • 500 g chicken thighs, boneless and skinless, cut in pieces (5 cm)
  • 300 g cooked chestnuts, peeled
  • 10 g dark soy sauce
  • 30 g light soy sauce
  • 10 g granulated sugar
  • 200 g water
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 2 spring onions, cut in pieces (4 cm), for garnishing
Nutrition
per 1 portion

Preparation

  1. Place ginger, oil, star anise and Sichuan pepper in mixing bowl then sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  2. Add chicken and cook 5 min/120°C//speed 1. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add chestnuts, dark soy sauce, light soy sauce, sugar, water and salt then, without measuring cup, reduce 20 min/Varoma//speed . Garnish with spring onions and serve hot with steamed rice.
