Risotto with Parmesan Cheese

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ oz Parmesan cheese, cubed (1 in.)
  • 1 shallot (approx. 1 oz)
  • 1 ½ oz unsalted butter, divided
  • ½ oz extra virgin olive oil
  • 10 oz risotto rice (e.g. Carnaroli, Arborio), 14-15 minutes cooking time (see Tip)
  • 2 oz dry white wine
  • 25 oz water
  • 1 stock cube, beef or vegetable
  • ½ - 1 tsp salt, to taste

Preparation

  1. Place Parmesan cheese into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 10. Scrape cheese out of mixing bowl with spatula, transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place shallot into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add 1 oz butter and oil and sauté 3 min/250°F/speed 1.
  4. Add rice and without measuring cup sauté 3 min/250°F//speed 1.
  5. Add wine and without measuring cup cook 1 min/212°F//speed 1.
  6. Add water, stock cube and salt and scrape bottom of mixing bowl thoroughly with spatula to loosen rice. Place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 12-13 min/212°F//speed 1. Allow risotto to rest in mixing bowl for 1 minute, then transfer into a serving bowl. Using spatula, combine risotto with 0.5 oz butter and reserved Parmesan cheese and serve immediately.

Hints & Tricks

  • Risotto is best served immediately.
  • Adjust cooking time according to time indicated on rice package.
  • Liquid absorption can vary according to type of rice. If risotto seems dry before it is cooked through, add more liquid.
  • Rule of thumb for creating your own risotto recipe: for every 3.5 oz risotto rice you will need 10 oz liquids (water and wine) combined with stock paste. The maximum quantity of rice that can be cooked at any one time for this basic recipe is 17.5 oz. Note that adding other ingredients (e.g. mushrooms or sausage) will increase overall volume of the dish, so adapt rice quantity accordingly.
  • Saffron risotto: add 2 pinches saffron.
  • Mushroom risotto: while adding butter and olive oil add 9 oz fresh mushrooms (cut into pieces if too large) or 2 oz dried mushrooms (soaked in water for 30 minutes, water squeezed out). Proceed as directed by recipe. Saffron can also be added in this variation.
  • Sausage and apple risotto: while adding butter and olive oil, add 5.5 oz sausage meat (cut open a fresh sausage and crumble in the ground meat). While adding risotto rice, add 3.5 oz green apple.
