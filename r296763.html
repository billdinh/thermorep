Prawn Saganaki

Ingredients

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 - 2 fresh red chillies, halved, deseeded if desired, to taste
  • 2 - 3 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only, to taste, plus extra for garnishing
  • 150 g onions, quartered
  • 40 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 240 g tinned cherry tomatoes, drained (1 x 400 g tin)
  • 100 g passata
  • 50 g white wine
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 400 g large raw prawns, peeled, deveined
  • 150 g feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1 lemon, cut in wedges
  • crusty bread, for serving

Preparation

  1. Place garlic, chillies, parsley, onions and oil in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then sauté 5 min/100°C/speed 1.
  2. Add tomatoes, passata, wine, salt and pepper then, with simmering basket in place of measuring cup, cook 8 min/100°C//speed 1. Meanwhile, preheat grill to 200°C.
  3. Add prawns and mix by hand with spatula. Transfer to a baking dish (approx. 30 cm x 20 cm) and scatter over feta. Grill for 3-5 minutes (200°C) until feta is slightly browned and prawns are cooked.
  4. Garnish with extra parsley and lemon wedges, then serve immediately with crusty bread.

Useful Items

baking dish (approx. 30 cm x 20 cm), grill

Hints & Tricks

  • Transport yourself to a beautiful, sun-drenched Greek island with this prawn dish that’s bursting with flavour. Plump, juicy prawns are grilled in a rich tomato sauce and topped with crumbled feta, fresh parsley and plenty of freshly-squeezed lemon juice. The only thing needed is some crusty bread to soak it all up.
