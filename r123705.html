Buttermilk balsamic sorbet

Ingredients

  • 600 g buttermilk
  • 1 ½ tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp white balsamic vinegar
  • 4 - 8 strawberries, hulled and cut into dice (5 mm), for garnishing
Nutrition
per 1 portions
Calories
532 kJ / 126 kcal
Protein
6.5 g
Carbohydrates
17.9 g
Fat
3 g

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh buttermilk into it. Transfer into ice cube trays and place into freezer overnight or until frozen.
  2. Place frozen buttermilk cubes into mixing bowl and crush 30 sec/speed 1 -9, gradually increasing speed from speed 1 to speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add honey and vinegar and blend 1 min/speed 5, with aid of spatula.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then blend 30 sec/speed 5 or until well combined and smooth.
  5. Garnish with strawberries and serve immediately (see Tips).

Useful Items

2-3 ice cube trays, bowl, freezable storage container, freezer

Hints & Tricks

  • If ice cream is too soft for your liking, transfer into a freezable container and place into freezer until firm or until ready to serve.
  • If ice cream becomes too firm to scoop, place it into the refrigerator or on the kitchen bench until thawed to your desired softness before serving. You may wish to store all frozen desserts in individual portions, as refreezing thawed leftovers can be a safety hazard.
