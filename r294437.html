Apple and Peanut Cereal Bars

Ingredients

  • 100 g apples, tart (e.g. Granny Smith), cored, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 30 g water
  • 150 g raw unsalted peanuts
  • 80 g olive oil
  • 100 g honey
  • 10 g chia seeds
  • 150 g oat flakes
  • 30 g chocolate puffed rice
  • 15 g desiccated coconut
  • 50 g pumpkin seeds
  • 30 g linseeds
  • 20 g sesame seeds
  • 100 g dried cranberries
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
740 kJ / 177 kcal
Protein
5 g
Carbohydrates
16 g
Fat
11 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C and line a baking tin (20 cm x 30 cm x 5 cm) with baking paper.
  2. Place apples and water in mixing bowl then cook 5 min/100°C/speed 1.
  3. Add peanuts, oil, honey and chia seeds then mix 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add oat flakes, puffed rice and coconut then mix 2 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add pumpkin seeds, linseeds, sesame seeds and cranberries then, with aid of spatula, mix 10 sec/speed 2. Transfer to prepared baking tin then press down and level with the back of a spoon.
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes (180°C) until golden then remove and leave to cool in tin. Once cool, cut into bars (approx. 3 cm x 10 cm).
  7. Serve or wrap in baking paper and store in an airtight container until needed.

Useful Items

baking paper, airtight container, long sharp knife, rectangular baking tin (approx. 30 cm x 20 cm x 5 cm), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • These cereal bars will keep for up to 1 week in an airtight container.
