Chocolate zucchini mini loaves

Ingredients

  • 200 g grapeseed oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 180 g raw sugar
  • 300 g plain flour
  • 50 g cocoa powder
  • 2 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp sea salt
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 250 g zucchini, cut into pieces (3-4 cm)
  • 70 g walnuts (optional)
  • 4 eggs

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Grease an 8-hole mini loaf tray and set aside.
  2. Place sugar into mixing bowl and mill 5 sec/speed 9.
  3. Add flour, cocoa, bicarbonate of soda, baking powder, salt and cinnamon and mix 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place zucchini and walnuts (optional) into mixing bowl and chop 2-3 sec/speed 5 or until evenly chopped. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add oil and eggs and combine 3 sec//speed 2.
  6. Add reserved flour and cocoa mixture and combine 5 sec//speed 4.
  7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and repeat combining 5 sec//speed 4.
  8. Transfer into prepared mini loaf tray and bake for 25-30 minutes (180°C) or until a wooden skewer inserted into the centre of each loaf comes out clean. Cool loaves in tray for 15 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely (approx. 30 minutes). Cut each loaf in half before serving.

Useful Items

8-hole mini muffin loaf tray (8 x 4.5 x 4 cm), wooden skewer, wire rack, bowl, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Chocolate zucchini cake: transfer batter into a greased springform cake tin (24 cm) in step 7 and bake for 45-50 minutes (180°C) or until a wooden skewer inserted into the centre of the cake comes out clean.
  • Chocolate zucchini muffins: transfer batter into 2 greased 12-hole muffin trays in step 7 and bake for 15-20 minutes (180°C) or until a wooden skewer inserted into the centre of a muffin comes out clean.
  • For an extra treat, frost your mini-loaves with Chocolate ganache or Buttercream icing (please refer to The Basic Cookbook or Everyday Cookbook for these recipes).
  • Wrap portions in plastic wrap and store in the freezer to enjoy another day.
