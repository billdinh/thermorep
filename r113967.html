Walnut and Chocolate Crunch Cookies

Ingredients

  • 7 oz unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 ½ oz brown sugar
  • 3 ½ oz sugar
  • 14 oz all-purpose flour
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 3 ½ oz walnut pieces
  • 3 ½ oz chocolate chips
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
522 kJ / 125 kcal

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F and line two baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. Place butter, eggs, brown sugar and sugar into mixing bowl and mix 15 sec/speed 4.
  3. Add flour, salt, baking soda, walnuts and chocolate chips and mix with spatula through opening in lid stirring while kneading 30 sec/.
  4. Using a tablespoon, form dough into walnut size balls (each cookie will be approx. 1 oz to get 40 cookies) and place 2 inches apart on prepared baking sheets. Gently flatten cookies.
  5. Bake one baking sheet after another for 15 minutes or until cookies are golden.
  6. Allow to cool then serve or store in an airtight container.

Useful Items

parchment paper, airtight container, baking sheet, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Paired with a cold glass of milk this makes the perfect snack!
