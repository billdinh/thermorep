Black & white chocolate tartufo

Ingredients

  • 120 g white chocolate, broken into pieces
  • 1 tsp vanilla paste
  • 300 g full cream milk
  • 300 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 40 g sugar
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 80 g dried apricots, cut into small pieces (5 mm)
  • 80 g glacé cherries, cut into pieces
  • 60 g shelled and unsalted pistachios, cut into pieces
  • 600 g dark chocolate, broken into pieces
  • 1 tsp chilli powder (optional)

Preparation

  1. Place white chocolate into mixing bowl and grate 4 sec/speed 8.
  2. Add vanilla, milk, cream, sugar and egg yolks and cook 9 min/80°C/speed 2, then mix 10 sec/speed 3 -8 , increasing speed gradually from speed 3 to speed 8.
  3. Transfer mixture into a shallow freezable container. Place into freezer for a minimum of 8 hours. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Remove ice cream from freezer and cut into pieces (4 cm). Place half of the pieces into mixing bowl and mix 20 sec/speed 6.
  5. Add 40 g of the apricots, 40 g of the cherries and 30 g of the pistachios and mix 10 sec/speed 3. Transfer back into shallow freezable container and set aside.
  6. Place remaining ice cream pieces into mixing bowl and mix 20 sec/speed 6.
  7. Add remaining 40 g apricots, 40 g cherries and 30 g pistachios and mix 10 sec/speed 3. Transfer mixture into container with ice cream and place into freezer until mixture is firm (approx. 3 hours). Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  8. Line a baking tray (20 x 30 cm) with baking paper (ensure tray will fit into freezer). Scoop out ice cream with a spoon and using your hands, shape into approx. 32 bite-sized balls (approx. 25 g). Place onto tray and return to freezer until hard (2-3 hours).
  9. Place dark chocolate into mixing bowl and grate 8 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  10. Add chilli (optional) and heat 4 min/50°C/speed 2, until chocolate melts.
  11. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Mix until temperature reaches 37°C/speed 2 , without measuring cup. Transfer into a bowl.
  12. Working in batches, remove ice cream balls from freezer. Using two spoons, dip each ball into chocolate, coating them all over, and place back into freezer to set for a minimum of 20 minutes, or until ready to serve (see Tips).

Useful Items

shallow freezable container, baking tray (20 x 30 cm), baking paper, bowl, 2 spoons, freezer

Hints & Tricks

  • It is necessary to work quickly to coat ice cream balls in dark chocolate before they melt. Have a couple of trays on rotation in the freezer.
