Deconstructed Lemon Tarts

Ingredients

Base

  • 80 g cinnamon biscuits (e.g. Speculoos), halved
  • 15 g unsalted butter, diced

Lemon Curd

  • 1 lemon, peeled, white pith and pips removed, quartered
  • 70 g unsalted butter, diced
  • 125 g caster sugar
  • 2 medium eggs
  • 1 egg yolk, from medium egg

Meringue

  • 90 g caster sugar
  • 3 egg whites, from medium eggs
  • 30 g freshly squeezed lemon juice

Preparation

Base

  1. Place biscuits and butter in mixing bowl then chop 15 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.

Lemon Curd

  1. Place lemon in mixing bowl and chop 15 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add butter, sugar, eggs and egg yolk, cook 8 min/90°C/speed 2, then blend 30 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Thoroughly clean and dry mixing bowl.

Meringue

  1. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid, weigh in sugar and set aside.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg whites and lemon juice in mixing bowl then, without measuring cup, whisk 4 min/37°C/speed 4, slowly adding reserved sugar through hole in mixing bowl lid. Remove butterfly whisk.
  3. Divide base between serving glasses. Add a generous layer of lemon curd then top with a thinner layer of meringue. Use a blowtorch to lightly brown tops of meringues (see tip). Place in fridge until needed and serve chilled.

Useful Items

serving glasses, cook's blowtorch, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • If a blowtorch is not available, meringues can be browned under the grill. To do this, serve in ceramic dishes, and grill under a medium heat for 30-60 seconds. Glasses may crack under high heat of a grill.
  • Substitute lemons with oranges.
  • Substitute cinnamon biscuits with butter biscuits or gluten-free biscuits.
  • Substitute lemon with juice of 2 lemons and skip step 2.
