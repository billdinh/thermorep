Bamia (okra and meat stew)

Ingredients

  • 800 g okra, trimmed
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 200 g brown onion, cut into halves
  • 10 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only
  • 60 g olive oil
  • 500 g chuck beef, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 400 g canned chopped tomatoes
  • 70 g tomato paste

Preparation

  1. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh okra into it. Set Varoma aside.
  2. Place garlic, onion, coriander and oil into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5, then cook 5 min/100°C/speed 1.
  3. Add beef, salt and pepper and cook 10 min/100°C//speed , without measuring cup. Scrape down base and sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add tomatoes and tomato paste, place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and cook 30 min/Varoma//speed .
  5. Transfer okra into a thermal serving bowl or other large serving bowl. Add meat stew and gently stir with aid of spatula to combine. Serve with sides of choice (see Tips).

Useful Items

Hints & Tricks

  • This dish is often served with rice.
