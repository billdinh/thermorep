Steamed chicken roll and vegetables with vegetable sauce

Ingredients

  • 100 g unsalted pistachio nuts, shelled
  • 1000 g water
  • 400 g carrots, cut into pieces (4-5 cm)
  • 1000 g chicken breast fillets, cut into cubes (3 cm)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 2 ½ tsp salt
  • 2 ½ tsp ground white pepper
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • 450 g mixed vegetables (e.g. carrot, courgette, potato etc.), cut into slices
  • 100 g onions, cut into quarters
  • 100 g mixed vegetables (e.g. carrot, courgette, potato), cut into slices
  • 2 dried bay leaves
  • 50 g olive oil
  • 1 pinch pepper
  • 50 g butter, cut in pieces

Preparation

  1. Place pistachio nuts into mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 8. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place 500 g water into mixing bowl, set Varoma dish into position, weigh in carrots, cover Varoma and steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove Varoma and set aside, discard water from mixing bowl.
  3. Place chicken cubes into mixing bowl and chop 15 sec/speed 5.
  4. Add 1 garlic clove, vinegar, 1 tsp salt, pepper, cinnamon and mix 1 min/speed 8. Transfer mince mixture into a deep bowl and set aside.
  5. Place a sheet of baking paper on a work surface. Spread ¼ reserved ground pistachio nuts on baking paper forming a square (approx.18 cm x 18 cm). Take ¼ reserved mince mixture and press it between your palms to form a thin layer. Distribute meat onto ground pistachio layer to cover it completely. Place steamed carrots along one edge and roll with aid of baking paper forming a roll. Remove baking paper. Repeat to form 4 rolls and arrange them on Varoma tray.
  6. Arrange 450 g mixed vegetables in Varoma dish, insert Varoma tray with chicken rolls and set aside.
  7. Place 500 g water, 1 garlic clove, onions, 100 g mixed vegetables, bay leaves, 1 tsp salt and olive oil into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position and steam 30 min/Varoma/speed 1.5.
  8. Remove Varoma and set aside. Strain content of mixing bowl through a sieve and reserve cooking liquid (broth). Remove bay leaves, place back the strained vegetables into mixing bowl, add 150 g of the cooking liquid, ½ tsp salt and 1 pinch pepper and butter, blend 10 sec/speed 5. Serve chicken rolls with vegetables and pour some sauce on top. Serve remaining sauce on the side.

Useful Items

baking paper, deep bowl, sieve

Hints & Tricks

  • For a thicker sauce, dissolve 20 g corn flour in 50 g water, add it into mixing bowl in step 8 and mix 10 sec/speed 5.
  • Reserve the extra cooking liquid for other preparations, e.g. soups or risottos.
