Sticky Jamaican Ginger Cake

Ingredients

  • 225 g self-raising flour
  • 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp mixed spice
  • 2 Tbsp ground ginger
  • 110 g butter, soft
  • 40 g fresh root ginger, peeled, cut in round slices (2 mm)
  • 20 g crystallised ginger
  • 110 g dark brown muscovado sugar
  • 280 g evaporated milk
  • 110 g golden syrup
  • 110 g treacle
  • 1 medium egg

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C then line a large loaf tin (900 g/2 lb) with baking paper.
  2. Place flour, bicarbonate of soda, cinnamon, mixed spice and ground ginger in mixing bowl then mix 5 sec/speed 4.
  3. Add butter and chop Turbo/2 sec/2 times then transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place root ginger, crystalised ginger and sugar in mixing bowl then grind 4 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add evaporated milk, golden syrup and treacle then heat 2 min/50°C/speed 2.
  6. Add reserved spiced flour mixture then mix 10 sec/speed 3.5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then mix again 5 sec/speed 3.5.
  7. Add egg then mix 10 sec/speed 4.5. Transfer mixture to prepared tin then bake for 35-45 minutes (180°C) until a skewer inserted in centre comes out clean, covering with foil halfway through to prevent top from browning too much. Leave in tin to cool completely before removing.
  8. Wrap cooled loaf in foil and leave for at least a day before serving. The cake will get stickier and more moist the longer it is kept. Slice or store in an airtight container until needed.

Useful Items

900 g loaf tin (2 lb), baking paper, aluminium foil, skewer, oven
