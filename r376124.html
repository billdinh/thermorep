Chicken with Mashed Potatoes and Gravy

Ingredients

Chicken and Mashed Potatoes

  • 30 - 35 oz chicken breast fillets, boneless, skinless (5-6 oz ea.)
  • 6 oz plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tbsp ground smoked paprika, hot
  • 2 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp salt, to taste
  • 6 oz yellow onion, chopped
  • 32 oz chicken stock
  • 30 oz Yukon gold potatoes, cubed (2 in.)
  • 6 oz unsalted butter, in pieces
  • 2 oz whipping cream

Gravy

  • 2 oz unsalted butter, in pieces
  • 2 oz all-purpose flour
  • salt, to taste
  • ground black pepper, to taste
  • fresh parsley leaves, to garnish

Preparation

Chicken and Mashed Potatoes

  1. Place a large bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in chicken, yogurt, paprika, turmeric, chili, salt and chopped onion. Mix well to coat and set aside.
  2. Place stock into mixing bowl, set Varoma into position and place coated chicken into Varoma dish and tray, secure Varoma lid and steam 10 min/Varoma/speed 2.5.
  3. Remove Varoma and with a slotted spoon, slowly add potatoes into mixing bowl, return Varoma with chicken back into position, secure Varoma lid and cook 18 min/Varoma//speed 1.5.
  4. Remove chicken, set aside and keep warm. Strain potatoes through simmering basket and reserve 16 oz cooking liquid and set aside.
  5. Place strained potatoes, butter and whipping cream into mixing bowl and mash 7 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl, set aside and keep warm.

Gravy

  1. Place butter and flour into mixing bowl and cook 3 min/212°F/speed 1. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add reserved cooking liquid and cook 12 min/212°F/speed 2. Adjust seasoning, adding more salt and pepper, to taste. Garnish chicken with parsley and serve with mashed potatoes and gravy.

Useful Items

large bowl, slotted spoon

Hints & Tricks

  • Marinate chicken for up to 2 hours in the refrigerator, if desired.
