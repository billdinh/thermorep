Stir-Fried Tofu in Hot Sauce

Ingredients

  • 100 g pork mince, broken in pieces
  • 1 Tbsp light soy sauce
  • 1 tsp ground white pepper
  • 1 tsp Shaoxing rice wine
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 5 g vegetable oil
  • 600 g silken tofu, diced (2-3 cm)
  • 5 g sesame oil
  • 2 Tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 - 2 Tbsp chilli broad bean paste, to taste
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • 2 spring onions, sliced (3 mm), for garnishing
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
612 kJ / 146 kcal
Protein
13 g
Carbohydrates
7 g
Fat
8 g

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in mince, soy sauce, pepper and rice wine. Remove bowl, mix and set aside to marinate for 15 minutes.
  2. Place garlic in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add vegetable oil and reserved marinated mince then sauté 5 min/120°C/speed 2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Insert butterfly whisk. Add tofu, sesame oil, oyster sauce, chilli broad bean paste and salt then cook 5 min/100°C/speed . Transfer to serving dish, sprinkle sliced spring onions on top and serve immediately with steamed rice.
