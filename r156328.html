Chicken and capsicum souvlaki with rice

Ingredients

Marinade

  • 20 g fresh ginger, peeled and cut into thin slices
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 100 g soy sauce
  • 50 g honey
  • 1 - 2 tsp toasted sesame oil to taste
  • 25 g barbecue sauce
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper

Chicken and rice

  • 800 g skinless chicken breast fillets, cut into cubes (2-3 cm)
  • 400 g mixed capsicums (eg red, yellow, green) deseeded and cut into pieces (2-3cm)
  • 1 tsp Vegetable stock paste (refer to the recipe in The Basic Cookbook)
  • Â½ tsp salt
  • 300 g long grain white rice
  • 1000 g water
  • 2 tbsp cornflour (starch)

Preparation

Marinade

  1. Place ginger, garlic, soy sauce, honey, sesame oil, barbecue sauce and pepper into mixing bowl and mix 5 sec/speed 6. Transfer marinade into a bowl.

Chicken and rice

  1. Add chicken to marinade, stir to coat and place into refrigerator to marinate for at least 15 minutes (this time can be used for preparing the capsicums).
  2. Thread the chicken cubes, alternating with capsicum, onto skewers (approx. 18 cm long) and place skewers onto Varoma dish and tray. Place remaining marinade, stock paste and salt into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket, weigh long-grain rice into it, then add water to mixing bowl over rice. Stir using spatula to ensure all rice grains are wet. Place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 25 min/Varoma/speed 1. Set Varoma aside. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula, transfer rice into a serving bowl and cover to keep warm. Transfer cooking liquid to a bowl.
  3. Place 400 g of the reserved cooking liquid and cornflour into mixing bowl, place Varoma back into position, secure Varoma lid and cook 5 min/Varoma/speed 2. Set Varoma aside and adjust seasoning to taste. Serve hot, serving rice with chicken skewers with sauce on the side, or drizzle some sauce over the skewers and rice before serving.

Useful Items

large bowl, wooden skewers, serving bowl, bowl, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • For a stronger flavour, marinate the chicken overnight in the refrigerator in step 2.
