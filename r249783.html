Mussels in turmeric and ginger broth

Ingredients

  • 3 spring onions/shallots, cut into halves
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 fresh Kaffir lime leaves
  • 1 stalk lemongrass, cut into thirds
  • 20 g sesame oil
  • 5 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 1 cube palm sugar (1-2 cm)
  • 10 sprigs fresh coriander, roots and stalks for broth, leaves reserved for garnishing
  • 1 fresh long red chilli, deseeded if preferred, plus extra, thinly sliced for garnishing
  • ¾ tsp ground turmeric
  • 400 g coconut milk
  • 500 g water
  • 1 - 2 tsp Chicken stock paste, to taste (see Tips)
  • 1000 g fresh mussels, scrubbed and debearded
  • 1 tsp fish sauce
  • 20 g lime juice (approx. 1 lime)

Preparation

  1. Place spring onions/shallots, garlic, Kaffir lime leaves, lemongrass, oil, ginger, palm sugar, coriander roots and stalks, chilli and turmeric into mixing bowl and chop 6 sec/speed 8.
  2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and sauté 3 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Add coconut milk, water and stock paste. Place Varoma into position and weigh mussels into it. Secure Varoma lid and steam 7 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  4. Carefully stir mussels to ensure even cooking, secure Varoma lid and steam for a further 7 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  5. Divide mussels between serving bowls and discard any that have not opened during cooking.
  6. Add fish sauce and lime juice to mixing bowl and mix 6 sec//speed 2. Pour broth over mussels and garnish with reserved fresh coriander leaves and sliced chillies to serve.

Hints & Tricks

  • You can replace the ground turmeric with a 1-2 cm piece fresh, peeled turmeric, if desired.
  • Please refer to The Basic Cookbook or the Everyday Cookbook for the Chicken stock paste recipe.
