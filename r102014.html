Ham, Mushroom and Cheese Crêpes

Ingredients

Batter

  • 300 g milk
  • 200 g plain flour
  • 20 g unsalted butter, diced
  • 3 medium eggs
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 100 g water

Filling

  • 60 g unsalted butter, diced, plus extra for frying
  • 50 g plain flour
  • 500 g milk
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt, or to taste
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper, or to taste
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg (optional)
  • 100 g Gruyère cheese, cut in pieces
  • 50 g shallots, halved
  • 300 g button mushrooms, sliced
  • 100 g ham, sliced, roughly torn in pieces

Preparation

Batter

  1. Place 200 g milk, flour, butter, eggs and salt in mixing bowl then mix 30 sec/speed 3.5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add water and remaining 100 g milk then mix 30 sec/speed 3.5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside to rest for at least 30 minutes. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl and make filling.

Filling

  1. Place 40 g butter, flour, milk, salt, pepper and nutmeg (if using) in mixing bowl then cook 7 min/90°C/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl, cover with cling film and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.
  2. Place Gruyère cheese in mixing bowl and grate 8 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place shallots in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add mushrooms and remaining 20 g butter then, without measuring cup, sauté 2 min/120°C/speed 2.
  5. Add reserved sauce and 50 g reserved grated cheese then mix 10 sec//speed 3.5.
  6. Preheat oven to 210°C.
  7. Once batter has rested, heat a knob of butter in a large pancake or frying pan over a medium heat. Pour a small ladle of batter into the centre then spread out thinly. Cook for 1-2 minutes on each side then transfer to a plate. Repeat, adding more butter as necessary until all batter is used.
  8. Divide ham evenly between crêpes then add approx. 3 Tbsp mushroom filling to each. Roll crêpes up then place in a baking dish (approx. 30 cm x 22 cm). Sprinkle with remaining grated cheese then bake for 10 minutes (210°C). Serve hot.

Useful Items

cling film, pancake pan or frying pan, small ladle, baking dish (approx. 30 cm x 22 cm), oven, hob
