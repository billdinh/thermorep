Gingerbread Marshmallows

Ingredients

Corn Starch Mixture

  • 2 oz corn starch
  • 2 oz confectioners sugar

Gingerbread Marshmallows

  • non-stick spray oil
  • 2 ½ tbsp unflavored gelatin powder
  • 4 tsp ground cinnamon, divided
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • ½ tsp ground nutmeg
  • ¼ tsp ground cloves
  • 8 oz water, divided
  • 6 ½ oz sugar
  • 6 ½ oz dark brown sugar
  • 4 oz corn syrup
  • 1 oz molasses
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 5 egg whites
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
423 kJ / 101 kcal

1 g

Preparation

Corn Starch Mixture

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in corn starch and confectioners sugar. Stir to combine and set aside.

Gingerbread Marshmallows

  1. Wrap a square baking pan (9 in. x 9 in.) with foil and lightly grease with non-stick spray. Lightly dust prepared pan with some reserved corn starch mixture and set remaining mixture aside.
  2. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid, add gelatin, 2 tsp cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg and cloves and stir to combine. Weigh in 4 oz water, stir to combine and set aside for gelatin to bloom.
  3. Place sugar, brown sugar, 4 oz water, corn syrup, molasses and salt into mixing bowl, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 15 min/250°F/speed 1.
  4. Add reserved gelatin mixture, insert measuring cup and cook 2 min/212°F/speed 1.
  5. Insert butterfly whisk. Place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and whisk 10 min/speed 4. Remove butterfly whisk. Transfer sugar mixture to a large bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl and butterfly whisk (see Tip).
  6. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg whites into mixing bowl and without measuring cup whisk 2 min/speed 3.5.
  7. Mix 4 min/speed 4 while slowly pouring reserved warm sugar mixture through hole in mixing bowl lid. Transfer to prepared pan and set aside to set (approx. 4-6 hrs.).
  8. Remove marshmallow mixture from foil and dust well with reserved corn starch mixture. Use a mini gingerbread man cookie cutter to cut into 24 pieces and fully coat each marshmallow in corn starch mixture. Store in an airtight container, layered between sheets of parchment heavily dusted in corn starch mixture. Dust with 2 tsp cinnamon and package for gift giving.

Useful Items

aluminum foil, airtight container, large bowl, square baking pan (9 in. x 9 in.), mini gingerbread man cookie cutter

Hints & Tricks

  • This recipe works great with a second Thermomix® mixing bowl and butterfly whisk.
  • Package individually in cellophane baggies with some corn starch "snow" mixture in the bag.
