S'mores Pie

Ingredients

Graham Cracker Pie Crust

  • 5 oz unsalted butter, cut into pieces
  • 10 oz graham crackers, broken into pieces
  • 1 oz sugar

Filling

  • 10 oz semi-sweet chocolate, broken into pieces
  • 12 oz heavy whipping cream
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 egg yolk, from large egg
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • &frac18; tsp salt

Topping

  • 9 oz sugar
  • 6 egg whites, from large eggs
  • ¼ tsp cream of tartar
  • &frac18; tsp salt

Preparation

Graham Cracker Pie Crust

  1. Place butter into mixing bowl and melt 2 min/212°F/speed 1.
  2. Add graham crackers and sugar and crush 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and crush a further 5 sec/speed 7.
  3. Transfer crumb mixture into a pie pan and press into bottom and up the sides to form a crust. Chill 15 minutes in refrigerator. Meanwhile clean mixing bowl and make filling.

Filling

  1. Place a large bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in chocolate. Set aside.
  2. Place whipping cream, eggs, egg yolk, vanilla and salt into mixing bowl and cook 6 min/195°F/speed 3.
  3. Place a sieve above bowl with reserved chocolate and strain cream mixture into bowl. Stir through with spatula until chocolate is melted. Pour chocolate mixture into pie crust and refrigerate until fully set (approx. 4 hr.). Clean mixing bowl.

Topping

  1. Place sugar, egg whites, cream of tartar and salt into mixing bowl and cook 4 min 30 sec/175°F/speed 4.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk and blend 2 min 30 sec/speed 4 until egg whites are smooth, thick and glossy. Remove butterfly whisk. Spread topping over chilled pie with spatula creating decorative swirls and peaks. Using a culinary torch, evenly toast the topping to your liking. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

pie pan (approx. 9 in.), fine-mesh sieve, culinary torch, large bowl, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • To brown topping in the oven: position the oven shelf to one rung below the top level. Preheat the broiler. Broil until tips are browned, 30-60 seconds.
  • Replace whipped topping with marshmallows.
