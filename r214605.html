Japanese Mayonnaise (Kewpie Style)

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp cider vinegar
  • 2 Tbsp dashi stock
  • 120 g grapeseed oil
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • ½ tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 egg yolk, very fresh, from large egg
Nutrition
per 250 g
Calories
4917 kJ / 1195 kcal

Preparation

  1. Place vinegar and dashi in mixing bowl then, without measuring cup, simmer 4 min/98°C/speed .
  2. Place a jug on mixing bowl lid and weigh in oil. Set jug aside.
  3. Add mustard, sugar, salt and egg yolk then whisk 10 sec/speed 4. Whisk again 4 min/37°C/speed 4 then, after 1 minute slowly pour reserved oil onto mixing bowl lid, letting it drizzle around measuring cup in a thin stream onto rotating blades. Transfer to a squeezy bottle.
  4. Refrigerate and use within 1 week.

Useful Items

squeezy bottle, jug, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Arguably one of Japan's most famous condiments, kewpie style mayonnaise is more silky and creamy than its British counterpart and a chef favourite around the world.
