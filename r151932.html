Vanilla Custard

Ingredients

  • ½ - 1 vanilla pod, to taste
  • 500 g milk
  • 2 egg yolks, from medium eggs
  • 50 g sugar
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
  • 20 - 30 g cornflour (see tip)
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
805 kJ / 192 kcal
Protein
6 g
Carbohydrates
25 g
Fat
8 g

Preparation

  1. Split vanilla pod lengthways and scrape out seeds. Place seeds and vanilla pod in mixing bowl.
  2. Add milk, egg yolks, sugar, salt and cornflour then cook 7 min/90°C//speed 3.
  3. Transfer to a serving bowl or individual dessert bowls and remove vanilla pod. Serve lukewarm or cover with cling film in direct contact with the custard and store in fridge until ready to serve.

Useful Items

cling film, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • For a thick, creamy vanilla custard, this reliable all-in-one recipe produces perfect results every time. For a thinner, pouring custard use the Crème Anglaise recipe on Cookidoo®.
  • This is a thick custard. For a pouring custard, reduce cornflour to 10 g or use the Crème Anglaise (vanilla sauce) recipe.
  • Vanilla custard with whisked egg whites: when the custard is ready, transfer to a bowl, clean and dry mixing bowl thoroughly and insert butterfly whisk. Place 2 egg whites in mixing bowl and whisk 2 min/speed 3.5. Fold whisked egg whites carefully using a spoon into the lukewarm custard. Cover and refrigerate until ready to serve.
  • Pastry cream (crème pâtissière): proceed as directed in the recipe, using 1 vanilla pod, 500 g whole milk, 2 eggs, 2 egg yolks, 80 g sugar and 50 g flour. For a richer flavour and smoother texture, after cooking in step 2 add 50 g cold, diced unsalted butter and mix 10 sec/speed 4.
  • Substitute vanilla pod with 10 g homemade vanilla sugar.
