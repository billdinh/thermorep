Open fish burgers

Ingredients

Fish burgers

  • 2 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 6 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves and stalks
  • 500 g firm white fish fillets, cut into pieces (2-3 cm)
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp kecap manis
  • ¼ tsp salt, plus extra to season
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper, plus extra to season
  • 100 g panko breadcrumbs
  • 2 tbsp plain flour
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp milk

Red cabbage salad

  • 50 - 80 g red cabbage, finely shredded
  • 6 - 8 snow peas, cut into thin slices
  • ¼ cucumber, deseeded and cut into thin slices
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 2 tsp fish sauce
  • 2 tsp brown sugar

Chilli mayo

  • 50 g mayonnaise (see Tips)
  • 1 - 2 tbsp hot chilli sauce, to taste

Assembly

  • 1 - 2 tbsp oil, for frying
  • 3 bread rolls, cut into halves horizontally (see Tips)

Preparation

Fish burgers

  1. Place ginger, garlic and coriander into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add fish, fish sauce, kecap manis, salt, pepper and 20 g of the panko breadcrumbs and combine 10-15 sec//speed 4. Divide mixture into 6 portions and shape into patties. Transfer patties onto a plate, cover and place into refrigerator for 20 minutes. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Place flour onto a plate and season with extra salt and pepper. Place egg and milk into a bowl and whisk to combine. Place a separate bowl onto mixing bowl lid, weigh remaining 80 g panko breadcrumbs into it and set aside.

Red cabbage salad

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh cabbage into it. Add snow peas, cucumber, lime juice, fish sauce and sugar, then stir to combine. Cover and place into refrigerator until ready to serve (see Tips).

Chilli mayo

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh mayonnaise into it. Add hot chilli sauce and stir to combine. Cover and place into refrigerator until ready to serve (see Tips).

Assembly

  1. Line a plate with paper towel and set aside. Place a large frying pan over medium heat and add oil. Working in batches, coat burger patties in flour, then dip into egg wash and then coat with panko breadcrumbs. Place into frying pan and cook for 4-5 minutes on each side until golden and cooked through. Transfer burgers onto lined plate and cover to keep warm.
  2. If desired, preheat the grill and toast bread rolls. To serve, spread rolls with reserved Chilli mayo, then top with a fish burger and Red cabbage salad.

Useful Items

bowl, plate, frying pan (20 cm), paper towel, plastic wrap, whisk, refrigerator, oven grill, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • To make your own mayonnaise, please refer to the recipe in The Basic Cookbook or the Everyday Cookbook.
  • We served our fish burgers atop Mick's ciabatta rolls (please refer to the recipe on this recipe chip), but you can use any kind of bread rolls or burger buns.
  • The Red cabbage salad can be stored for up to 3 hours before serving, and the Chilli mayo can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.
