Pepper, Onion and Sausage Galettes

Ingredients

Pastry

  • 440 g plain flour, plus extra for rolling
  • ¾ tsp fine sea salt
  • 2 tsp fresh thyme leaves
  • 220 g butter, diced, plus extra for greasing
  • 70 - 100 g water

Filling

  • 400 - 500 g red onions, quartered
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 350 - 400 g red peppers, cut in strips (5 mm)
  • 350 - 400 g courgettes, cut in strips (5 mm)
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 400 g sausages, raw
  • 1 Tbsp milk

Preparation

Pastry

  1. Place flour, salt and thyme leaves  in mixing bowl then chop 10 sec/speed 8.
  2. Add butter and water then mix Turbo/2 sec/3-4 times until just clinging together. Tip out and form into a ball. Divide into two equal pieces and wrap each in cling film and place in fridge to rest for 30-45 minutes. Meanwhile, make filling.

Filling

  1. Grease two large baking trays then set aside.
  2. Place onions and oil in mixing bowl then chop 4 sec/speed 4.
  3. Add red peppers, courgettes, salt and pepper then cook 5 min/120°C//speed . Drain through a sieve to remove juices then set aside.
  4. Preheat oven to 200°C. Fry sausages in a frying pan over a high heat, turning until browned on all sides. Remove and cut into bite-sized pieces then set aside.
  5. On a lightly floured surface, roll each piece of pastry out into a circle (Ø 30 cm). Transfer each to a baking tray. Arrange half the vegetables over the top of each pastry disc, leaving a 5 cm border around the edge then arrange sausage pieces over vegetable layer.
  6. Fold edges of pastry up around filling then brush crust with milk. Bake for 30-40 minutes (200°C) until pastry is cooked. Serve warm, cut in slices.

Useful Items

cling film, 2 baking trays, fine sieve, frying pan, rolling pin, pastry brush, refrigerator, hob, oven
