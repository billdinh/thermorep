Chicken and Apple Breakfast Sausage and Egg Sandwiches

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp dried sage
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 ½ tsp salt, divided
  • ½ tsp fennel seeds, to taste
  • ¼ tsp ground cayenne pepper (optional)
  • &frac18; tsp ground black pepper
  • 4 oz yellow onions, in pieces
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 16 oz ground chicken, broken into pieces
  • 4 oz Pink Lady apples, diced (¼ in.)
  • 2 ½ oz bread crumbs
  • ½ oz maple syrup
  • 6 eggs
  • 25 oz water
  • 8 mini croissants, halved
  • 8 slices cheddar cheese

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line a baking sheet with foil and set aside.
  2. Place sage, thyme, 1 tsp salt, fennel seeds, cayenne and black pepper into mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 10.
  3. Add onion and garlic and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add chicken, apples, bread crumbs and maple syrup and mix 10 sec//speed 3.5. Divide chicken mixture into 8 equal sized portions (approx. 3 oz ea.) and shape into patties (approx. 3 in.). Place on prepared baking sheet and bake for 15-18 minutes (350°F), until cooked through. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl and prepare eggs.
  5. Line Varoma tray with a piece of wet, well-wrung parchment, ensuring paper goes all the way to the edges to prevent egg from spilling and set aside. Place eggs and ½ tsp salt into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 4. Carefully pour egg mixture into prepared Varoma tray.
  6. Place water into mixing bowl. Set Varoma into position, carefully insert Varoma tray with eggs, secure Varoma lid and steam 15 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  7. Top the bottom half of each croissant with a cooked sausage patty. Add steamed eggs and cheese, sandwich with croissant tops and serve.

Useful Items

baking sheet, aluminum foil, parchment paper, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • To cut parchment to fit Varoma: tear off a piece of parchment paper wide enough to cover Varoma tray. Place Varoma tray on top of parchment and cut around the tray to form an oval shape. Ensure parchment does not cover side vents to allow circulation of steam.
  • Toast croissants before assembly. Place croissants on a baking sheet cut side down and bake 3-4 minutes (350°F), until warm and slightly toasted.
