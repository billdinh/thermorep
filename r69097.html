Shortbread

Ingredients

  • 100 g butter, cut into pieces, plus extra for greasing
  • 100 g plain flour
  • 50 g rice flour
  • 50 g caster sugar, plus extra for dusting

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C. Grease a round cake tin (20 cm) and set aside.
  2. Place plain flour, rice flour, caster sugar and butter into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 6, then knead 20 sec/. Mixture will be a breadcrumb consistency.
  3. Transfer mix into prepared cake tin then lightly press down to form a smooth surface. Dust with additional caster sugar and bake for 30-40 minutes (160°C) until lightly golden and firm to the touch.
  4. Allow to cool slightly then score using a sharp knife into equal portions. Transfer to a wire rack and allow to cool completely.
  5. If desired, dust again with additional caster sugar and serve.

Useful Items

round cake tin (20 cm), wire rack, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • This recipe can be doubled. Increase cooking time to approx. 50 minutes or until shortbread is lightly golden and firm to the touch.
  • If shortbread isn't served immediately, transfer into an airtight container to store until use.
