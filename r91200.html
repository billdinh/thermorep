Halibut and Potatoes with Tomato Sauce

Ingredients

  • 28 oz potatoes, cut into pieces (1¼ in.-1½ in.)
  • 1 oz olive oil, plus extra to grease
  • 6 halibut fillets (4-7 oz each)
  • 1 tsp salt, divided, plus extra to season, to taste
  • 5 oz onions, cut into halves
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 3 ½ oz carrots, cut into pieces
  • 2 oz white wine (optional)
  • 15 oz tomatoes, cut into pieces
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 3 ½ oz water
  • salt, to season, to taste
Nutrition
per 1 portion

Preparation

  1. Place Varoma dish into position, weigh in potatoes. Lightly grease Varoma tray and insert into Varoma dish, weigh in halibut, season with salt and set Varoma with potatoes and halibut aside.
  2. Place onions, garlic, carrots, oil, wine, tomatoes, pepper, 1 tsp salt and water into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  3. Place Varoma without Varoma tray into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 15 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  4. Insert Varoma tray into Varoma dish, secure Varoma lid and steam a further 15 min/Varoma/speed 1. Season potatoes with salt to taste. Serve halibut and potatoes with the tomato sauce.
