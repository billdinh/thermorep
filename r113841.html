Nutty Twists

Ingredients

  • 2 packages puff pastry, (10 oz each), 2 sheets joined together into rectangle (10 in. x 19 in.), refrigerated
  • 7 oz whole hazelnuts, with skin
  • 2 oz sugar
  • 1 tbsp homemade vanilla sugar
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 3 ½ oz light whipping cream
  • ½ oz confectioners sugar
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
582 kJ / 139 kcal
Protein
2 g
Carbohydrates

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Line 2 baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. Prepare or roll out both packages of puff pastry. Prepare or roll out puff pastry as described in Tip.
  3. Place hazelnuts, sugar, vanilla sugar and cinnamon into mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add cream and mix 15 sec/speed 4. Spread nut mixture evenly over one of the prepared, chilled and joined sheets of pastry with a spoon.
  5. Place the second joined sheet of pastry directly on top of the one with the nut mixture, align the edges and press down gently.
  6. Using a pastry wheel or a sharp knife, cut narrow strips about 1 inch wide, beginning from one short side of the rectangle to the other and then cut the strips in half to make 36 strips (approx. 1 in. x 5 in.).
  7. Twist each strip twice. Place them on prepared baking sheets and bake one tray after another for 10-15 minutes (425°F) or until golden.
  8. Allow twists to cool before dusting with confectioners sugar. Serve or store in an airtight container.

Useful Items

parchment paper, pastry wheel, baking sheets, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • To prepare puff pastry: roll out both sheets of pastry from one package and place them side by side on a large sheet of cling wrap or baking parchment. Join pastry sheets together by overlapping with ¼ in. seam at the middle. Gently press seam together to form one large sheet of pastry (approx. 10 in. x 17 in.). Place another sheet of cling wrap or parchment paper on top of the joined pastry rectangle. Repeat process with the second package. Gently roll up and refrigerate until required.
  • Make confectioners sugar before starting the recipe by following instructions in The Basic Cookbook.
  • Nutty Twists are best eaten fresh.
  • For almond twists: replace hazelnuts with almonds.
  • For poppy seed twists: replace nuts with poppy seeds, ground together with the sugar and vanilla sugar (omit the cinnamon) for 30 sec/speed 9.
  • For peanut twists: replace hazelnuts with roasted, salted peanuts, reducing the sugar to 1 oz, the cream to 2 oz and omitting the cinnamon.
