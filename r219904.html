Orange Balsamic Beet and Quinoa Salad

Ingredients

Orange Balsamic Vinaigrette

  • 3 ½ oz extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 oz shallots, quartered
  • ½ oz orange peel, thin peel only, no pith
  • 1 garlic clove
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 2 oz freshly squeezed orange juice
  • ½ oz balsamic vinegar
  • ½ oz honey

Quinoa Salad

  • 35 oz water
  • 7 oz quinoa
  • 16 oz baby beets, small red and yellow, peeled, cut into wedges (½ in.)
  • 6 oz feta cheese, crumbled
  • 4 oz chopped pistachios, unsalted
  • orange segments, to garnish
  • cilantro leaves, to garnish

Preparation

Orange Balsamic Vinaigrette

  1. Place a pitcher on top of mixing bowl lid, weigh in olive oil and set aside.
  2. Place shallots, orange peel, garlic, salt and pepper into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and chop a further 3 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add orange juice, balsamic vinegar and honey and mix 3 min/speed 4 while slowly pouring reserved olive oil onto mixing bowl lid, letting it drizzle around measuring cup in a thin stream onto rotating blades to create an emulsion. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.

Quinoa Salad

  1. Place water into mixing bowl and insert simmering basket. Weigh quinoa into simmering basket and rinse 10 sec/speed 5.
  2. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh in beets, secure Varoma lid and steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 2 until fork tender. Remove Varoma, place Varoma lid under Varoma dish to catch drips, transfer beets to a bowl and set aside to cool. Remove simmering basket with spatula, transfer quinoa to a large bowl and allow to cool.
  3. Add beets and reserved vinaigrette to bowl with quinoa, and toss with spatula to combine. Sprinkle with crumbled feta and chopped pistachios. Garnish with orange segments and cilantro leaves. Serve cold or at room temperature.
