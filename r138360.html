Farmhouse Fruit Cake

Ingredients

  • 75 g unsalted butter, diced, plus extra for greasing
  • 250 g self-raising flour
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
  • ¾ tsp ground mixed spice
  • 120 g light brown sugar
  • 150 g milk
  • 50 g brandy
  • 1 large egg
  • 100 g raisins
  • 100 g sultanas
  • 50 g mixed peel
  • 75 g glacé cherries, halved

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Grease and line a cake tin (Ø 16 cm) with baking paper.
  2. Place flour, salt and mixed spice in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 4.
  3. Add butter and sugar then mix 15 sec/speed 3.
  4. Add milk, brandy and egg then mix 20 sec/speed 3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add raisins, sultanas, mixed peel and cherries then mix 15 sec//speed 2. Finish mixing by hand with spatula then transfer to prepared tin. Bake for 50-60 minutes (180°C) until a skewer inserted in centre comes out clean. Leave to cool in tin for 10 minutes before removing to a wire rack to cool completely.
  6. Serve in slices.

Useful Items

round cake tin (Ø 16 cm), baking paper, wire rack, skewer, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • If baking this cake around Christmas time, make it more festive - swap the sugar to dark brown sugar, increase mixed spice to 1 tsp, add ¼ tsp ground nutmeg and increase brandy proportion (keeping the same total amount of liquid).

Fruit cakes are rarely considered quick to prepare, which will make this classic a firm favourite in your baking repertoire. Packed with dried fruit and spices, it pairs beautifully with a cup of tea.
