Broccoli almond soup

Ingredients

  • 1 brown onion, cut into quarters
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil (see Tips)
  • 340 g broccoli, broken into florets
  • 1 sweet corn cob (approx. 170 g), kernels removed
  • 700 g liquid vegetable stock (see Tips)
  • 60 g fresh baby spinach leaves
  • 40 g almond butter (see Tips)
  • sea salt, to taste
  • ground black pepper, to taste
  • micro herbs, to serve (optional)

Preparation

  1. Place onion, garlic and oil into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7.
  2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then sauté 5 min/120°C/speed 1, until softened.
  3. Add broccoli, corn kernels and chop 5 sec/speed 7.
  4. Add liquid stock, then cook 10 min/100°C/speed 1.
  5. Add spinach and almond butter, then blend 1 min/speed 4-8, increasing speed gradually from speed 4 to speed 8, until smooth and creamy.
  6. Set aside baby's serve (see Tips).
  7. Season adult serves with salt and pepper, to taste. Garnish with micro herbs to serve (optional).
