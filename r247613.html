Baked Spinach and Cheese Strata

Ingredients

  • 180 g Gruyère cheese, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 60 g Parmesan cheese, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 680 g milk
  • 9 large eggs
  • 2 Tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 2 pinch ground black pepper
  • 100 g onions, quartered
  • 50 g unsalted butter, diced, plus extra for greasing
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 280 g frozen spinach, defrosted and water squeezed out (drained weight)
  • ¼ tsp ground nutmeg
  • 250 g bread, cut in cubes (3 cm)

Preparation

  1. Place Gruyère and Parmesan in mixing bowl then grate 12 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place milk, eggs, mustard, ½ tsp salt and 1 pinch pepper in mixing bowl then mix 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place onions, butter and garlic in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then sauté 2 min/100°C/speed 1. Add spinach, nutmeg, remaining ½ tsp salt and 1 pinch pepper then sauté 2 min/100°C/speed 1.
  4. Grease a baking dish (30 cm x 20 cm). Place ⅓ bread cubes in baking dish and top with ⅓ reserved spinach mixture then ⅓ reserved grated cheese. Repeat layering twice, ending with a final layer of grated cheese.
  5. Pour reserved egg mixture evenly over baking dish, then cover with cling film and place in fridge for 8 hours or overnight.
  6. The next day, preheat oven to 180°C. Remove strata from fridge and leave to stand at room temperature for 30 minutes.
  7. Remove cling film and bake for 45-55 minutes (180°C) until puffed, golden brown, and cooked through. Leave to stand for 5 minutes before serving.

Useful Items

cling film, baking dish (approx. 30 cm x 20 cm), refrigerator, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • This baked strata is the perfect make-ahead brunch dish, as it can be prepared the night before and baked in the morning when needed.
  • While any bread can be used in this dish, slightly stale brioche or sourdough works particularly well.
