Za'atar flat bread (mankouche)

Ingredients

Dough

  • 270 g water
  • 2 tsp dried yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 50 g olive oil
  • 500 g bread flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 1 tsp salt

Topping and baking

  • 50 - 80 g za'atar or za'atar mix substitute
  • 130 g olive oil

Preparation

Dough

  1. Place water, dried yeast, sugar and olive oil into mixing bowl and mix 1 min 30 sec/37°C/speed 3.
  2. Add bread flour, salt and mix 10 sec/speed 4 and then knead 2 min/. Transfer to a bowl and cover with cling film. Set aside to rise for 1 hour or until it doubles in size.
  3. Preheat oven to 200°C. Line a baking tray with baking paper and set aside.
  4. Divide reserved dough into 8 equal pieces. Using a rolling pin roll-out each piece on a floured surface into a circle (Ø 18 cm), pinch the edges between your fingers creating a ridged pattern all around and place on prepared baking tray.

Topping and baking

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in dried za’atar and olive oil, mix well with a spoon. Divide mixture evenly and spread it on the flat breads. Bake for 7-8 minutes (200°C) or until lightly golden. Serve warm.

Useful Items

cling film, rolling pin, baking tray and paper, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Top mankouche additionally with black olives, cherry tomatoes or mint leaves.
  • Cheese Mankouche: substitute topping with cheese topping. In step 4 place into mixing bowl 200 g Akkawi cheese and 200 g Gruyère and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Roll-out the flat breads, spread 50 g chopped cheese on each and spread evenly. Proceed as described in the recipe.
