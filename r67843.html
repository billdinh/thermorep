Butter biscuits

Ingredients

  • 250 g plain flour
  • 100 g caster sugar, plus extra for coating
  • 100 g butter, cut into pieces
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp baking powder
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
434 kJ / 103 kcal
Protein
1.7 g
Carbohydrates
14 g
Fat
4.4 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Line a baking tray with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place plain flour, caster sugar, butter, egg and baking powder into mixing bowl and mix 20 sec/speed 6.
  3. Place some sugar onto a plate. Pinch together 1 tbsp dough and roll into a small ball (approx. 3 cm). Dip the top in sugar, place onto prepared baking tray and flatten with a fork into a round biscuit shape. Repeat with remaining dough. Bake 20 minutes (180°C) or until light golden. Allow to cool on a wire rack before serving or storing in an airtight container.

Useful Items

baking tray and paper, wire rack, airtight container, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • In step 2, add natural flavours such as vanilla sugar, lemon zest, orange zest, ground cinnamon, or chopped candied ginger.
