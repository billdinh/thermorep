Hazelnut Meringues with Rhubarb & Cream Filling

Ingredients

Hazelnut Meringues

  • 1 oz hazelnuts, plus extra to garnish, to taste (approx. 0.5 oz)
  • 7 oz sugar
  • 8 egg whites, from large eggs

Rhubarb Compote and Whipped Cream Filling

  • 6 oz sugar
  • 1 orange, thin peel only, no pith
  • 1 stick cinnamon, broken into pieces
  • 1 star anise
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • 12 oz rhubarb, in pieces (½ in.), divided
  • 6 oz heavy whipping cream
  • confectioners sugar, to dust

Preparation

Hazelnut Meringues

  1. Preheat oven to 300°F. Place a small bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in hazelnuts. Transfer hazelnuts onto a baking sheet and bake for 10 minutes (300°F). Remove from oven, set roasted nuts aside and allow to cool completely.
  2. Line two baking sheets with parchment. Draw six 3½ inch circles on each piece of parchment and set aside.
  3. Place sugar into mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Once cooled, place reserved roasted hazelnuts into mixing bowl and chop Turbo/2 sec/1-2 times. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Thoroughly clean and dry mixing bowl.
  5. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg whites into mixing bowl and whip 5 min/100°F/speed 3.5. Without measuring cup, whip a further 5 min/100°F/speed 3.5 while adding reserved ground sugar 1 tbsp at a time through hole in mixing bowl lid.
  6. Remove butterfly whisk. Transfer whipped egg whites to a large bowl and carefully and slowly fold in 1 oz reserved ground hazelnuts, until just incorporated. Reserve remaining hazelnuts to garnish. Carefully spoon whipped egg mixture inside circles drawn on the parchment. Spread meringue to edges forming 12 meringue disks.
  7. Bake meringues for 10 minutes (300°F), reduce heat to 250°F and bake 60 minutes (250°F). Turn off oven and without opening oven door allow meringues to cool (approx. 2 hrs.). Meanwhile clean mixing bowl and make filling.

Rhubarb Compote and Whipped Cream Filling

  1. Place sugar and orange peels into mixing bowl, insert measuring cup and grate 3 sec/speed 8.
  2. Add cinnamon, star anise, nutmeg and 6 oz rhubarb and cook 5 min/212°F//speed 1. Add remaining 6 oz rhubarb and cook 4 min/175°F//speed 1. Refrigerate filling until cold, remove and discard cinnamon and star anise. Clean mixing bowl.
  3. Insert butterfly whisk. Place whipping cream into mixing bowl and without measuring cup whip speed 3.5 until cream is barely holding its shape (approx. 25 sec.). Remove butterfly whisk.
  4. Sandwich two Hazelnut Meringues with Whipped Cream and Rhubarb Compote. Repeat with remaining meringues. Garnish with chopped hazelnuts, dust with confectioners sugar and serve immediately.

Useful Items
