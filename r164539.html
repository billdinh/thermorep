Candy Corn Pudding

Ingredients

  • 6 oz sugar
  • 6 egg yolks, from large eggs
  • 3 tbsp corn starch
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 30 oz whole milk
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 4 oz unsalted butter
  • 5 - 8 drops orange food coloring
  • 12 oz heavy whipping cream
  • 2 tsp confectioners sugar, to taste (optional)
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1941 kJ / 464 kcal
Protein
7 g
Carbohydrates
32 g
Fat
35 g

Preparation

  1. Insert butterfly whisk. Place sugar, egg yolks, corn starch, salt, milk and vanilla into mixing bowl and mix 20 sec/speed 4, then cook 25 min/195°F/speed 4.
  2. Add butter and mix 20 sec/speed 4. Remove butterfly whisk. Divide half the pudding between 4-6 parfait cups. Cover parfait cups with cling wrap directly touching the surface of pudding and set aside.
  3. Transfer remaining pudding into a bowl, add orange food coloring and stir with a spoon until fully mixed. Cover bowl with cling wrap directly touching the surface of pudding. Refrigerate all puddings until set (approx. 1 hr.). Clean and dry mixing bowl and butterfly whisk.
  4. Spoon orange pudding over plain pudding in parfait cups, creating two distinct layers and set aside.
  5. Insert butterfly whisk. Place whipping cream and confectioners sugar into mixing bowl and without setting a time, whip at speed 3, until desired stiffness is achieved, watching carefully to avoid overwhipping (see Tip). Remove butterfly whisk.
  6. Top each pudding cup with whipped cream and serve immediately.

Useful Items

parfait cups, cling wrap, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Orange food coloring can be made by mixing 1 part red food coloring to 2 parts yellow food coloring. Adjust intensity of color to taste.
  • Time required to whip cream depends on temperature, fat content and quality of the cream. Take care not to overwhip or the cream will turn into butter. Watch periodically though hole in mixing bowl lid and listen for change in the mixing sound. Approximate whipping time is 1-2 minutes.
  • If preferred, cling wrap could be replaced with lightly oiled parchment.
  • Garnish Candy Corn Pudding with Halloween sprinkles, candy corn or candy pumpkins.
