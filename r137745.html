Linguine with salmon and herbs

Ingredients

  • 1200 g water
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 320 g dried linguine
  • 60 g extra virgin olive oil, plus 1 tablespoon extra
  • 2 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 4 fresh chives, cut into halves
  • 1 eschalot (approx. 40 g)
  • 20 - 40 g lemon juice, to taste
  • 50 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 200 g smoked salmon, cut into pieces
  • 1 tbsp pickled capers, drained (optional)
  • ground black pepper, to taste

Preparation

  1. Place water into mixing bowl and bring to the boil 10 min/100°C/speed 1.
  2. Add salt and pasta through hole in mixing bowl lid and cook 8-12 min/100°C//speed 1 or until pasta is al dente. Drain pasta and transfer into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl. Stir through 1 tablespoon of oil to loosen pasta. Cover to keep warm and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Place parsley and chives into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  4. Without cleaning mixing bowl, place eschalot and oil into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7.
  5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and cook 2 min/100°C/speed 1.
  6. Add lemon juice, cream, salmon, capers (optional), pepper and reserved herbs and heat 1 min/90°C//speed . Transfer into thermal serving bowl with pasta and stir to combine. Divide between serving bowls and serve warm.
