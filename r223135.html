Seafood and corn chowder

Ingredients

Gremolata

  • 1 lemon, zest only, no white pith
  • 50 g Parmesan cheese, cut into pieces
  • 5 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 2 - 3 tbsp pine nuts

Chowder

  • 2 - 3 leeks, white and light green parts only (200 g), cut into pieces
  • 200 g fennel, trimmed and cut into pieces
  • 40 g salted butter
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • 2 tbsp dry sherry (see Tips) (optional)
  • 650 g water
  • 1 tbsp Chicken stock paste (see Tips)
  • 40 g lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 200 g potatoes, peeled and cut into cubes (1 cm)
  • 1 sprig fresh thyme, leaves only
  • 1 dried bay leaf
  • 100 g bacon, cut into pieces (1-2 cm) (optional)
  • 500 g fresh seafood marinara mix
  • 400 g canned sweet corn kernels, drained (approx. 300 g after draining)
  • ground black pepper, to season

Preparation

Gremolata

  1. Place lemon and Parmesan into mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add parsley and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Transfer mixture into a bowl. Add pine nuts to bowl, stir to combine and set aside until ready to use. Do not clean mixing bowl.

Chowder

  1. Place leek and fennel into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add butter and cornflour and cook 3 min/100°C/speed 2.
  3. Add sherry (optional) and cook 2 min/100°C/speed 2.
  4. Add water, stock paste, lemon juice, fish sauce, potatoes, thyme, bay leaf and bacon (optional) and cook 10 min/100°C//speed 1.
  5. Add seafood mix, corn and pepper and cook 7-10 min/100°C//speed 1 or until potatoes are soft and seafood is cooked. Serve soup sprinkled with Gremolata.

Hints & Tricks

  • Fish chowder: Replace the seafood marinara mix with 500 g firm white fish, salmon or haddock, cut into pieces (4-5 cm).
  • You can replace the dry sherry with Pernod.
  • For more texture (and protein), add hard-boiled eggs, cut into quarters, to soup just before serving.
  • Please refer to The Basic Cookbook or the Everyday Cookbook for the Chicken stock paste recipe.
