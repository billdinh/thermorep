Mixed fruit compote with goat's cheese and granola on toast

Ingredients

Mixed fruit compote

  • ½ orange, zest only, no white pith
  • 1 pear (approx. 150 g), peeled and cut into pieces (3-4 cm)
  • 1 peach (approx. 140 g), peeled and cut into pieces (3-4 cm)
  • 30 g dried apricots, cut into halves
  • 2 tbsp orange juice
  • ½ tsp vanilla bean extract
  • 1 tbsp pure maple syrup
  • 8 - 10 strawberries, hulled and cut into halves

Pistachio and oat granola

  • 40 g light brown sugar
  • 40 g pure maple syrup
  • 1 ½ tbsp olive oil
  • ¼ tsp sea salt
  • 130 g shelled unsalted pistachios
  • 100 g rolled oats
  • 20 g flaked almonds
  • 4 slices fruit bread, thickly sliced
  • 100 - 150 g goat's cheese
  • 1 - 2 tbsp honey, for garnishing

Preparation

Mixed fruit compote

  1. Place orange zest into mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then repeat milling 10 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add pear, peach, apricot, orange juice and vanilla extract and cook 8 min/90°C//speed .
  3. Add maple syrup and strawberries and cook for a further 2 min/90°C//speed , or until fruit is tender.
  4. Transfer into a sealable container and allow to cool to room temperature before placing in the refrigerator until required.

Pistachio and oat granola

  1. Preheat oven to 150°C. Line a deep-sided roasting tray (32 x 23 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place sugar, maple syrup, olive oil and salt into mixing bowl and heat 3 min/80°C/speed 1.
  3. Add pistachios, oats and almonds and mix 6 sec/speed 4, or until mixture is combined.
  4. Spread mixture onto prepared roasting tray and bake for 8 minutes (150°C). Remove tray from oven, stir mixture and bake for a further 6-8 minutes (150°C), or until granola is dry and lightly golden. Set aside to cool (approx. 15 minutes).
  5. Once cooled, transfer granola into a sealable container to store until ready to use.
  6. Lightly toast the fruit bread, then spread each slice with goat's cheese. Top with cooled fruit compote, then sprinkle with a little granola and a drizzle of honey, just before serving.

Useful Items

sealable container, baking paper, deep-sided roasting tray (32 x 23 cm), toaster, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve leftover pistachio and oat granola on top of natural yoghurt and fresh fruit or as a base for homemade granola bars.
  • Try using a variety of seasonal fruits in the compote.
  • Rhubarb topping: Place 500g water into mixing bowl. Line Varoma dish with a piece of wet and well-wrung baking paper. Place Varoma into position and weigh 350 g rhubarb, cut into lengths (5 cm) on top of the baking paper. Drizzle with 20 g honey; ½ orange, zest only, no white pith; 2-3 cm piece ginger, peeled and grated; and 1-2 pinches cinnamon. Secure Varoma lid and steam 13-15 min/Varoma/speed 1, or until rhubarb is tender. Set rhubarb aside in a bowl, drizzle with an additional 20 g honey and allow to cool. Use the rhubarb topping in place of the mixed fruit compote.
