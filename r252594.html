Butter Beer

Ingredients

  • 60 g unsalted butter, diced
  • 60 g whipping cream
  • 30 g dark brown sugar
  • 15 g maple syrup
  • ½ tsp vanilla bean paste
  • 30 g sweetened condensed milk
  • 185 g water
  • 15 g gelatine powder
  • 240 g sugar
  • 100 g egg whites
  • 20 tin US "cream soda" cans, chilled (approx. 330 ml)

Preparation

  1. Place 30 g butter, whipping cream, brown sugar, maple syrup and vanilla bean paste in mixing bowl then cook 9 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  2. Add remaining 30 g butter and sweetened condensed milk then mix 15 sec/speed 2. Transfer to a heatproof bowl and set caramel aside to cool (approx. 30 minutes).
  3. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in 140 g water. Sprinkle gelatine powder over and set bowl aside.
  4. Place sugar and remaining 45 g water in mixing bowl then heat 12 min/Varoma/speed 3.
  5. Add reserved gelatined water and reserved caramel then mix 10 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  6. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg whites in mixing bowl and whip 4 min/50°C/speed 4 or until egg whites form stiff peaks. Mix 1 min/speed 3 while slowly adding reserved caramel sauce through hole in mixing bowl lid onto rotating blades.
  7. Pour 1 can of cream soda into a tall glass or stein (see tip) and spoon fluffy caramel sauce on top of cream soda. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

small bowl, tall glasses, heatproof glass dish

Hints & Tricks

  • Make your own vanilla bean paste: place 6-10 whole vanilla bean pods in mixing bowl and grind 20 sec/speed 10 or until pulverised. Add 3 Tbsp pure vanilla extract and 2 tsp xanthan gum (optional) then stir 3 sec/speed 3. Transfer to a glass jar and use as required.
  • Vanilla bean paste can be replaced with vanilla extract.
  • Make sure mixing bowl, lid and butterfly whisk are thoroughly clean and dry. Any residual grease or moisture will prevent eggs from properly aerating.
