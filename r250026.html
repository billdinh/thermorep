Lamb stew with couscous

Ingredients

Moroccan spice mix

  • 3 tsp ground ginger
  • 3 tsp ground coriander
  • 3 tsp ground cumin
  • 3 tsp paprika
  • 1 ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 ½ tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 ½ tsp ground white pepper
  • ½ tsp chilli powder
  • ½ tsp cayenne pepper
  • ½ tsp ground nutmeg

Lamb stew

  • 750 g boneless lamb shoulder, cut into pieces (2.5 cm)
  • ½ red onion, cut into quarters
  • 1 stalk fresh coriander, leaves only, plus extra for garnishing
  • 1 orange, segemented and segments cut into halves
  • 2 brown onions, cut into quarters
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 800 g canned crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp Meat stock paste (see Tips)
  • 100 g water
  • 1 stalk fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 230 g couscous
  • 150 g dried apricots, cut into pieces (1 cm)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 2 tbsp flaked almonds
  • Greek-style natural yoghurt, to serve

Preparation

Moroccan spice mix

  1. Place all spice mix ingredients into mixing bowl and dry roast 8 min/Varoma/speed 1. Transfer into a large bowl and set aside.

Lamb stew

  1. Place bowl with Moroccan spice mix onto mixing bowl lid and weigh lamb into it. Stir to evenly coat lamb pieces in spice mix. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and place into refrigerator for a minimum of 20 minutes.
  2. Place red onion and coriander into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a large bowl. Add orange segments to bowl with onion mixture and set aside.
  3. Place brown onion, garlic and oil into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and sautè 2 min/Varoma//speed 2.
  5. Add marinated lamb and cook 5 min/Varoma//speed 1.
  6. Add tomatoes, stock paste, water and parsley and cook 55 min/90°C//speed , placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  7. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh couscous into it. Transfer couscous into a sieve and rinse under cold running water. Line Varoma tray with a piece of wet, well-wrung baking paper, then spread couscous evenly over tray using spatula.
  8. Add apricots, salt and pepper into mixing bowl, then place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and cook 20 min/Varoma//speed 1. Carefully transfer couscous into bowl with orange mixture and stir to combine.
  9. Divide couscous mixture and stew between serving bowls, garnish with flaked almonds and extra coriander leaves, then serve with yoghurt.

Useful Items

large bowl, plastic wrap, serving bowls, sieve, bowl, baking paper, refrigerator
