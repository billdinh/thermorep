French Onion Soup

Ingredients

  • 3 oz Gruyère cheese, cut into pieces
  • 18 oz yellow onions, halved
  • 1 oz olive oil
  • 1 oz unsalted butter
  • ½ tsp salt, plus extra to taste
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper, plus extra to taste
  • 2 tsp all-purpose flour
  • 32 oz water
  • 1 stock cube, chicken or beef
  • 12 slices baguette, slightly stale, or other crusty bread

Preparation

  1. Place cheese into mixing bowl and grate 6 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place onions, olive oil, butter, salt and pepper into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 4, then sauté 10-15 min/250°F//speed 1. Scrape bottom of mixing bowl with spatula to loosen caramelized onions.
  3. Sauté a further 10-15 min/250°F//speed . Scrape bottom of mixing bowl with spatula to loosen caramelized onions.
  4. Add flour, water and stock cube and mix 10 sec//speed 3, then cook 20 min/212°F//speed 1. Adjust seasoning to taste.
  5. Preheat oven grill or broiler to 400°F.
  6. Pour soup into ovenproof soup bowls, place 2 baguette slices on top of each bowl and sprinkle with reserved grated Gruyère cheese. Place bowls under the broiler until cheese is melted and slightly brown. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

ovenproof soup bowls, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Replace 7 oz of water with 7 oz of white wine.
  • Sauté the onions up to 15 minutes to increase caramelization although some may stick to the bottom of the mixing bowl.
