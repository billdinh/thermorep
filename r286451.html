Tortilla Soup

Ingredients

  • 6 Roma tomatoes, halved
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 ½ oz white onion, halved
  • 8 ½ oz chicken stock
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 ½ tsp ancho chili powder
  • ½ oz vegetable oil
  • 5 oz panela cheese (Mexican fresh cheese), cubed (⅓ in.)
  • 1 avocado, peeled and cubed (⅓ in.)
  • fresh cilantro leaves, to garnish
  • sour cream, to serve
  • 1 lime, cut into 6 wedges, to serve
  • tortilla strips, as needed, to serve

Preparation

  1. Heat a large cast iron skillet over high heat until smoking. Add tomatoes, garlic and onion to the dry pan and cook, turning a few times, until charred on all sides but still holding their shapes (approx. 3-4 min.).
  2. Place charred tomatoes, garlic and onion, chicken stock, salt, chili powder and vegetable oil into mixing bowl and blend 30 sec/speed 5-8 increasing speed gradually, then cook 10 min/250°F/speed 1.
  3. Adjust seasoning to taste, garnish with cheese, avocado, cilantro, sour cream and lime. Serve hot with tortilla strips.
