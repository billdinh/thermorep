Coconut rice pudding with fresh mango

Ingredients

  • 200 g Arborio rice
  • 950 g water
  • 750 g coconut milk
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract
  • ¼ tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 100 g honey
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 mango, flesh only, cut into thin slices
  • shredded coconut, toasted, for decorating
  • fresh mint leaves, for decorating
  • edible flowers, for decorating (optional)

Preparation

  1. Insert simmering basket into mixing bowl and weigh rice into it. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and rinse rice until water runs clear.
  2. Place water into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket with rice and cook 10 min/100°C/speed 4, until rice is almost cooked. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and set aside to drain. Rinse and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Place coconut milk and cooked rice into mixing bowl and cook 5 min/90°C//speed 2, without measuring cup.
  4. Add vanilla, cinnamon, salt and honey and cook 5 min/90°C//speed 2, until thick and creamy.
  5. Add eggs and combine 10 sec//speed 2. Divide between serving bowls, then top with mango, toasted coconut, mint leaves and edible flowers (optional) before serving.
