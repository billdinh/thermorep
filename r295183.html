Sunday Morning Breakfast Bake

Ingredients

  • 20 - 40 g unsalted butter, to taste, plus extra for greasing
  • 420 g ciabatta bread, sliced (1.5 cm)
  • 100 g Parmesan cheese, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 100 g Cheddar cheese, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • ½ long red chilli, halved, deseeded if preferred (optional)
  • 3 spring onions, finely chopped
  • 20 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 100 g red peppers, deseeded, quartered
  • 120 g Parma ham, cut in strips (1-2 cm)
  • 2 tomatoes, deseeded, cut in slices (0.5-1 cm)
  • 2 pinches ground cayenne pepper
  • ½ tsp dried oregano
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt, or to taste
  • 6 medium eggs
  • 70 g milk

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C and grease a baking dish (32 cm x 20 cm x 5 cm).
  2. Spread butter over bread slices and arrange in prepared baking dish. Overlap slices slightly to ensure the dish is filled and the base is covered.
  3. Place Parmesan and Cheddar in mixing bowl then grate 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.
  4. Place garlic, chilli (if using) and spring onions in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add oil and sauté 2 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  6. Add peppers and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  7. Add Parma ham, tomatoes, cayenne pepper, oregano and salt then mix 10 sec//speed 2. Spread mixture evenly over and around bread slices and set aside.
  8. Without cleaning mixing bowl, place eggs and milk in mixing bowl then mix 5 sec/speed 5. Pour egg mixture over covered bread and sprinkle with reserved grated cheese.
  9. Bake for 45-50 minutes (160°C) until golden brown and just firm to the touch. Leave to rest for 5 minutes before serving.

Useful Items

baking dish (32 cm x 20 cm x 5 cm), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Substitute Parma ham with any kind of ham.
