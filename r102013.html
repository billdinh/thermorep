Pineapple and Rum Pancakes

Ingredients

Pancake Batter

  • 150 g plain flour
  • 80 g caster sugar
  • 40 g sunflower oil, plus extra for frying
  • 300 g coconut milk
  • 3 large eggs
  • 100 g water

Filling

  • 100 g fresh pineapple, cut in pieces
  • 70 g brown sugar
  • 20 g white rum (optional)
  • 20 g desiccated coconut

Preparation

Pancake Batter

  1. Place flour and sugar in mixing bowl then blend 10 sec/speed 7.
  2. Add sunflower oil, coconut milk and eggs then mix 30 sec/speed 3.5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add water and mix 30 sec/speed 3. Transfer to a bowl and let stand for at least 30 minutes. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl.
  4. Heat a little oil in a large pancake or frying pan over a medium heat. Pour a small ladle of batter into the centre then spread out thinly. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side then transfer to a plate and keep warm. Repeat, adding more oil when necessary until all batter is used.

Filling

  1. Place pineapple and brown sugar in mixing bowl then cook 5 min/90Â°C/speed 1 then blend 10 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add rum (if using) and stir 20 sec/speed 3. Serve pancakes hot or cold topped with sauce and sprinkled with grated coconut.

Useful Items

pancake pan or frying pan, small ladle, hob

Hints & Tricks

  • Omit the rum for a child friendly dessert.
  • Replace pineapple with other fruit such as banana.
