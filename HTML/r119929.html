Steamed Cod with Vizcaina Sauce

Ingredients

  • 50 g roasted red peppers, preserved
  • ½ tsp smoked paprika
  • 250 g onions, quartered
  • 200 g red onions, quartered
  • 100 g olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 100 g Iberian ham, sliced, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 100 g water
  • 900 g cod fillets, (loins) skinless, boneless
  • 1 small handful fresh parsley, leaves only, for garnish

Preparation

  1. Place roasted red reppers and smoked paprika in mixing bowl then purée 30 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl then set aside.
  2. Place onions, red onions, oil, garlic and salt in mixing bowl then chop 4 sec/speed 4. Cook 10 min/100°C/speed 1.
  3. Add ham and cook 10 min/100°C/speed 1.
  4. Add reserved pepper purée and cayenne pepper then cook 5 min/100°C/speed 1.
  5. Add water and heat 5 min/100°C/speed 2. Meanwhile, grease Varoma dish and tray lightly with oil.
  6. Place Varoma dish into position then weigh in half the cod. Insert Varoma Tray then weigh remaining cod onto it. Cover Varoma then steam 10 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove Varoma, pour sauce into a serving dish and place steamed cod on top. Serve immediately garnished with fresh parsley.

Hints & Tricks

  • This recipe originates in Vizcaya, the Biscay region of the Basque Country. Vizcaina sauce’s distinctive flavour and warm orange-red colour comes from smoky dried Spanish peppers, substituted here with roasted peppers and paprika.
