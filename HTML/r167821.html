Lentil Mushroom Stroganoff

Ingredients

  • 3 ½ oz yellow onion, cut into pieces (approx. 1 sm. onion)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 5 oz dried brown lentils
  • 30 oz water, divided
  • 10 oz cremini mushrooms, sliced (approx. ¼ in.)
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 tbsp corn starch
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tsp dried thyme
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 3 ½ oz low fat sour cream
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
657 kJ / 157 kcal
Protein
8 g
Carbohydrates
21 g
Fat
5 g

Preparation

  1. Place onion, garlic, olive oil and paprika into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Insert simmering basket, weigh in lentils and rinse under cold water. Remove lentils from simmering basket and add to mixing bowl. Add 20 oz water and cook 20 min/212°F/speed 1.5.
  3. Add mushrooms, 10 oz water, tomato paste, Dijon, corn starch, Worcestershire sauce, thyme, salt and pepper and with simmering basket instead of measuring cup on top of mixing bowl lid, cook 20 min/212°F//speed 2.
  4. Add sour cream and stir 1 min/speed 1.5. Serve immediately.
