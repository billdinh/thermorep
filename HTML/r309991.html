Mocha Mousse (No Added Sugar)

Ingredients

  • 125 g coffee, brewed, hot (see tip)
  • 2 Â½ tsp gelatine powder
  • 240 g ricotta cheese
  • 1 tsp cocoa powder, unsweetened
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 Tbsp agave syrup
  • 230 g double cream
  • cocoa powder, unsweetened, for sprinkling (optional)
  • 120 g fresh raspberries, for serving

Preparation

  1. Place a jug on mixing bowl lid and weigh in hot coffee. Remove from mixing bowl lid, add gelatine and stir until dissolved. Set aside to cool slightly.
  2. Place ricotta, cocoa powder, vanilla extract and agave syrup in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl then mix again 5 sec/speed 3.
  3. Add cooled coffee mixture and mix 10 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Insert butterfly whisk. Place cream in mixing bowl and, without measuring cup, whip without setting a time/speed 3 until soft peaks form, watching carefully to avoid over-whipping.
  5. Add ricotta coffee mixture and mix 10 sec/speed 3. Remove butterfly whisk.
  6. Transfer mixture to ramekins, cover with cling film and chill in fridge for at least 1 hour. Sprinkle with cocoa powder (if using) and top each with 4-5 raspberries before serving.

Useful Items

jug, cling film, ramekins, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Brew approx. 7-10 g ground coffee in 200 g water in a French press for 2-3 minutes, to taste.
