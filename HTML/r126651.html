Spinach and tomato quiche

Ingredients

Pastry

  • 250 g wheat flour
  • 120 g vegan margarine, cold, cut into pieces, plus extra for greasing
  • 60 g water, cold
  • ½ tsp fine salt

Topping and preparation

  • 1 - 2 garlic cloves
  • 30 g soy flour, full fat, as egg replacement
  • 60 g water
  • 400 g silken tofu, drained
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
  • 1 tbsp starch
  • 1 tsp fine salt
  • ¼ tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • ¼ tsp ground nutmeg
  • 200 g spinach leaves, frozen, defrosted, well drained, roughly chopped (see tip)
  • 2 tbsp breadcrumbs, vegan
  • 150 g cherry tomatoes, cut in half
  • 30 g flaked almonds

Preparation

Pastry

  1. Place wheat flour, margarine, water and salt in mixing bowl and knead 15 sec/speed 5 kneten. Remove pastry from mixing bowl, shape into a ball, wrap in cling film and chill for approx. 30 minutes. Meanwhile, rinse mixing bowl.

Topping and preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C. Grease a quiche dish or loose-bottomed baking tin (Ø 26 cm).
  2. Place garlic in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Place soy flour in a bowl and mix to a smooth paste with water.
  4. Add soy flour mixture, silken tofu, olive oil, yeast flakes, starch, salt, pepper and nutmeg to mixing bowl and stir 20 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Pull drained spinach apart a little, add to mixing bowl and mix 10 sec//speed 3.
  6. Press pastry into dish or tin and create a border of approx. 3 cm in height. Sprinkle pastry with breadcrumbs, spread out spinach filling, press cherry tomatoes face up into filling and garnish with flaked almonds.
  7. Bake quiche for 45–50 minutes (190°C), remove from oven and allow to cool in dish/tin for approx. 10 minutes. Cut quiche into 8 slices and serve warm.

Useful Items

cling film, quiche tin (Ø 26 cm), bowl, refrigerator, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Approx. 500 g frozen spinach leaves will be needed for 200 g defrosted and drained spinach.
  • Soy flour is available in health food shops. I it is used as a vegan egg replacement.
