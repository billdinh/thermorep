Lamb meatballs in spicy tomato sauce (lamb kofta)

Ingredients

Lamb meatballs

  • oil for greasing
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 200 g onions, cut into quarters
  • 10 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 10 sprigs fresh mint, leaves only
  • 10 sprigs fresh coriander, roughly cut
  • 1 tsp Arabian spice mix
  • 2 tsp ground sumac
  • 1 tsp salt, adjust to taste
  • 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper, adjust to taste
  • 500 g lamb mince

Steamed meatballs, spicy tomato rice and sauce

  • 250 g basmati rice
  • 2 tsp coriander seeds
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 200 g onions, cut into quarters
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 2 fresh green chillies, cut into halves and deseeded
  • 900 g ripe tomatoes, cut into quarters
  • 1 heaped tsp vegetable stock paste, homemade
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp salt, adjust to taste
  • 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper, adjust to taste
  • 300 g water
  • 1 heaped tbsp cornflour (starch)
  • 5 sprigs fresh coriander, finely choppped

Preparation

Lamb meatballs

  1. Grease Varoma dish and tray.
  2. Place garlic, onions, parsley, mint and coriander into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add Arabian spice mix, sumac, salt, pepper and lamb mince and mix 30 sec//speed 3. Transfer to a bowl, with oiled hands, shape small meatballs (Ø 3 cm) and place them into prepared Varoma dish and tray. Set aside.

Steamed meatballs, spicy tomato rice and sauce

  1. Place simmering basket onto mixing bowl lid, weigh in rice, rinse below running water. Place simmering basket in a bowl with enough water to cover the rice and set aside to soak. Meanwhile go on with the recipe.
  2. Place coriander seeds into clean and dry mixing bowl and dry roast 3 min/120°C/speed 1, then grind 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place garlic, onions and olive oil into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and then sauté 5 min/120°C/speed 1.
  5. Add reserved ground coriander seeds and cook 1 min/100°C/speed 2.
  6. Add chillies, tomatoes, vegetable stock paste, oregano, salt and pepper and chop 30 sec/speed 6.
  7. Insert simmering basket with rice, add water and mix with aid of spatula. Place Varoma into position and steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 4. Set Varoma aside, remove simmering basket with aid of spatula, transfer rice into a bowl and moisture the rice by adding 200 g of cooking liquid from the mixing bowl. Mix gently with a fork and set aside.
  8. Place closed Varoma back into position and steam 7 min/Varoma/speed 2. Set Varoma aside.
  9. Add cornflour into mixing bowl and reduce sauce 3 min/Varoma/speed 2, without measuring cup. Transfer meatballs to a serving dish, pour remaining sauce on top and garnish with finely chopped fresh coriander. Serve warm with reserved rice.
