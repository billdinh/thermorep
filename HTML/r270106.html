Tomato paste

Ingredients

  • 1500 g Roma tomatoes, very ripe and cut into quarters
  • 1 dried bay leaf
  • 1 tbsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp white sugar
  • olive oil, to cover
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
83.3 kJ / 19.8 kcal
Protein
0.7 g
Carbohydrates
1.8 g
Fat
0.9 g

Preparation

  1. Place 750 g of the tomatoes into mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 5.
  2. Add remaining 750 g of tomatoes and chop 4 sec/speed 5.
  3. Add bay leaf, oregano, salt and sugar and cook 60 min/90°C/speed 2, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid. Cook for a further 30 min/100°C/speed 2, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and cook 30-45 min/Varoma/speed 2, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid, to desired consistency.
  5. Insert measuring cup. Blend 30 sec/speed 4-9, increasing speed gradually from speed 4 to speed 9.
  6. Allow mixture to cool slightly, then transfer to a sterilised sealable jar. Top with a thin layer of oil and cool to room temperature. Store in refrigerator until ready to use (see Tips).

Useful Items

sterilised sealable jar, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Tomato paste can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 1 month or freeze in ice cube trays to store for up to 6 months.
  • Covering the paste with a layer of oil helps to prevent bacteria and mould from forming.
