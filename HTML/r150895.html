Steamed Aubergine and Ricotta Lasagne

Ingredients

  • 50 g Parmesan cheese, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 5 - 10 g fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 1 - 2 pinches ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1100 g water
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 160 g dried lasagne pasta sheets
  • 300 g aubergines, diced (approx. 1 cm)
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 50 g onions, cut in pieces
  • 50 g carrots, cut in pieces
  • 30 g celery stalk, cut in pieces
  • 30 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 500 g tinned chopped tomatoes
  • 250 g ricotta cheese, drained

Preparation

  1. Choose a rectangular dish (approx. 23 cm x 15 cm x 7 cm) that fits in Varoma dish (see tip) and set aside. Place Parmesan cheese, parsley and pepper in mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer 2 tsp to a small bowl and the remainder to another small bowl, and set both aside.
  2. Place 500 g water and 1 tsp salt in mixing bowl and bring to a boil 5 min/100°C/speed 1. Pour boiling water into rectangular dish and add one pasta sheet at a time to soak. Stir gently to keep pasta sheets separate in the first few minutes of soaking.
  3. Place garlic, onions, carrots, celery and extra virgin olive oil in mixing bowl, chop 5 sec/speed 6, then, without measuring cup, sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  4. Add aubergines, 1 tsp salt and tomatoes and cook 15 min/Varoma//speed 1.
  5. Drain pasta sheets by pouring onto Varoma tray. Cover base of the rectangular dish with a thin layer of aubergine ragout. Place a layer of pasta sheets over the sauce, cover with another thin layer of ragout, sprinkle with one third of the larger amount of reserved Parmesan-parsley mixture and distribute 4-5 heaped tsp ricotta on top without spreading. Repeat to make 4 pasta layers, finishing with a layer of ragout. Cover lasagne dish with damp baking paper and place in Varoma dish.
  6. Place 600 g water in mixing bowl, set Varoma in position and steam 30 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove Varoma, remove baking paper, sprinkle lasagne with remaining Parmesan-parsley mixture, cut in squares and serve hot.

Useful Items

rectangular dish (approx. 23 cm x 15 cm x 7 cm), baking paper

Hints & Tricks

  • Steamed lasagne? Really? Really! Our testers were amazed at the delicate texture of the pasta, combined with the rich aubergine sauce and fresh ricotta. The Parmesan topping, green from being grated with fragrant parsley, is an eye-catching alternative to the usual grilled cheese topping.
  • Mix 1 tsp grated lemon zest into ricotta before using it in step 5.
    - Artichoke Lasagne: replace aubergines with sliced artichoke hearts (fresh or preserved).
    - Courgette Lasagne: replace aubergines with sliced courgettes.
    - If you cannot find a suitable container, the lasagne can be steamed in the Varoma tray: line the Varoma tray with a large sheet of baking paper (approx. 40 cm x 35 cm), with the excess hanging over the edges. Assemble the lasagne as directed by the recipe, then fold the overhanging baking paper over the lasagne. Insert Varoma tray in Varoma dish and proceed as directed by the recipe in step 6. To serve, cut lasagne and plate each portion individually.
  • Use any kind of rectangular or oval container (ceramic, heat-resistant plastic etc.) that fits in the Varoma dish while leaving some holes unobstructed for steam to circulate. You can also fold a disposable aluminimum tin if it is too large. Or use the Varoma tray (see variations).
