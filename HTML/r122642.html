Strawberry and Nectarine Polenta and Almond Cake

Ingredients

  • 125 g butter, diced, plus extra for greasing
  • 250 g light brown sugar
  • 60 g Greek yoghurt
  • 1 lemon, zest and 30 g juice
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 200 g ground almonds
  • 200 g polenta
  • 3 medium eggs
  • 100 g fresh strawberries, hulled, 50 g diced (0.5 cm), 50 g finely sliced
  • 100 g nectarines, stoned, 50 g diced (0.5 cm), 50 g finely sliced
  • 150 g crème fraîche, to serve

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 170°C. Grease and line base and sides of a spring form cake tin (20 cm) with baking paper.
  2. Place butter, sugar and yoghurt in mixing bowl then cream 30 sec/speed 4.
  3. Add lemon zest, salt, baking powder, ground almonds, polenta and lemon juice then mix 30 sec/speed 4.
  4. Add eggs and mix 20 sec/speed 5.
  5. Add diced strawberries and nectarines then mix 10 sec//speed 3.5. Transfer batter to prepared tin and smooth surface. Arrange fruit slices on top then bake for 1 hour 15 minutes (170°C) or until a skewer inserted in centre comes out clean, covering with foil if top of cake becomes too brown. Leave to cool for 10 minutes then remove from tin and allow to cool on a wire rack.
  6. Serve slightly warm with crème fraîche.

Useful Items

spring form tin (Ø 20 cm), baking paper, wire rack, aluminium foil, oven
