Creamed rice pudding with apple and pear compote

Ingredients

  • 80 g Arborio rice
  • 480 g full cream milk
  • 1 apple, peeled, core removed and diced
  • 1 pear, peeled, core removed and diced
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • 1 pinch ground cinnamon
  • 1 pinch ground cardamom
  • 1 tsp light brown sugar
  • 30 g caster sugar
  • 1 tsp pure maple syrup
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
Nutrition
per 1 portion

2104 kJ / 501 kcal

Preparation

  1. Place Arborio rice and full cream milk into mixing bowl.
  2. Insert simmering basket. Line simmering basket with a piece of wet, well-wrung baking paper. Place all compote ingredients into lined simmering basket and cook 25-30 min/90°C//speed 1.5, or until fruit is soft and rice is tender. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula. Transfer compote into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and cover to keep warm.
  3. Add caster sugar, maple syrup and egg to rice in mixing bowl and combine 1 min//speed 3.
  4. Serve rice pudding immediately with reserved compote.

Useful Items

thermal serving bowl or other large bowl, baking paper

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve creamed rice pudding with other fruits of choice (e.g. strawberries, mangos).
  • Arborio is the preferred variety of rice for this recipe, but you can use sushi rice leftover from the Miso chicken and rice if you prefer.
  • Recipe for one: reduce all ingredients by half and cook for 20 minutes in step 2.
