Lentil moussaka

Ingredients

  • 40 g butter, plus extra for greasing
  • 200 g dried red lentils
  • water, for soaking
  • 120 g Parmesan cheese, cut into cubes (3 cm)
  • 50 g olive oil
  • 1 brown onion (approx. 150 g), cut into quarters
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 red capsicum, deseeded and cut into pieces
  • 400 g canned whole tomatoes (including liquid)
  • 120 g red wine
  • 1 sprig fresh oregano, leaves only
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • 2 tsp sea salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 eggplant (approx. 550 g), cut into slices (1 cm)
  • 500 g full cream milk
  • 40 g spelt flour

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C. Grease a casserole dish (30 x 20 cm) and set aside.
  2. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid, then weigh red lentils into it. Add enough water to cover lentils and set aside to soak for 2 hours.
  3. Place Parmesan into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place olive oil, onion, garlic and capsicum into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  5. Add tomatoes (including liquid) and mix 2 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  6. Add red wine, oregano, ginger, salt and pepper and cook 10 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  7. Place Varoma into position, then arrange eggplant slices into it, ensuring steam can circulate freely. Secure Varoma lid and steam 10 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove Varoma and set aside.
  8. Drain lentils through simmering basket. Add drained lentils to mixing bowl and cook 7 min/100°C//speed 2.
  9. Transfer steamed eggplant slices into the base of prepared casserole dish, then top with lentil mixture. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  10. Place butter, milk and flour into mixing bowl and cook 8 min/90°C/speed 4.
  11. Add 60 g of the reserved Parmesan and mix 5 sec/speed 4.
  12. Transfer cheese sauce into casserole dish and sprinkle with remaining reserved Parmesan. Bake for 30 minutes (200°C) or until top is lightly golden brown. Cut moussaka into squares and serve hot.
