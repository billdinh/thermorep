TM5 TM6

Steamed Chinese greens

Ingredients

  • 1 spring onion/shallot, trimmed and cut into thirds
  • 1 fresh long red chilli, trimmed and cut into halves, deseeded if preferred
  • 1 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 20 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp tamari, gluten free (see Tip)
  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • 2 tsp pure maple syrup
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 1000 g boiling water
  • 250 g broccoli, broken into florets
  • 150 g pak choy, cut into quarters lengthways
  • 150 g sugar snap peas, cut into halves
  • fried shallots, for garnishing
  • fresh coriander leaves

Preparation

  1. Place spring onion/shallot, chilli, ginger and garlic into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add olive oil and sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 2.
  3. Add tamari, sesame oil, syrup and pepper and heat 5 min/100°C/speed 2 or until reduced slightly. Transfer into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and set aside.
  4. Carefully place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh broccoli, pak choy and sugar snap peas into Varoma dish and onto tray, secure Varoma lid and steam 5 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  5. Remove Varoma lid and stir vegetables with aid of spatula. Secure Varoma lid and cook for a further 4-5 min/Varoma/speed 2 or until just tender. Transfer into thermal serving bowl with sauce and gently stir to combine. Garnish with fried shallots and fresh coriander before serving.

Useful Items

thermal serving bowl or other large bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • Be sure to check the label of your tamari, as not all brands are gluten free.
