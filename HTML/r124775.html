Mini Apple Loaf Cakes

Ingredients

  • 200 g butter, soft, plus extra for greasing
  • 300 g eating apples, peeled, cored, quartered (approx. 3)
  • 1 lemon, thin peelings of skin and 30 g juice
  • 100 g light brown sugar, plus 1 Tbsp for sprinkling
  • 1 ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • 250 g plain flour
  • 40 g vanilla sugar
  • 3 medium eggs
  • 1 ½ tsp baking powder

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 170°C and grease 8 individual mini loaf tins.
  2. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in 150 g apple quarters. Remove apples and cut into thin slices (2-3 mm) then return to bowl.
  3. Add to bowl on mixing bowl lid lemon juice, light brown sugar and ½ tsp cinnamon then remove bowl and mix together gently with spatula. Set bowl aside.
  4. Place lemon peel and 30 g flour in mixing bowl then grate 30 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add butter, light brown sugar, vanilla sugar and eggs then mix 15 sec/speed 4.
  6. Add remaining 150 g apple quarters then chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  7. Add remaining 220 g flour, baking powder and 1 tsp cinnamon then mix 15 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then mix again 15 sec/speed 4.
  8. Divide mixture between prepared loaf tins, levelling the tops.
  9. Arrange reserved apple slices over the top, overlapping them slightly then sprinkle 1 Tbsp light brown sugar over apples.
  10. Bake for 30-35 minutes (170°C) until golden brown and a skewer inserted in centre comes out clean. Allow to cool in tins for 5 minutes before removing to a wire rack to cool completely. Serve once cool.
