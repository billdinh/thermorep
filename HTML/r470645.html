Kung Pao Chicken

Ingredients

Glaze

  • 4 green onions, cut into pieces
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 10 - 15 dried red chilies, stems removed
  • 1 tbsp corn starch
  • 1 tbsp dark brown sugar
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • ¼ tsp dried chili flakes, to taste
  • 3 oz fermented black bean paste
  • 2 oz chicken stock
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp black vinegar
  • 1 tbsp Chinese cooking wine
  • 2 tsp peanut oil

Sauté

  • 20 oz water
  • 24 oz chicken breast, skinless, boneless, cubed (1 in.)
  • 1 oz peanut oil
  • 6 oz bell peppers, diced (½ in.)
  • 6 oz zucchini, sliced (¼ in.)
  • 3 oz carrots, sliced (¼ in.)
  • 2 oz raw unsalted peanuts, plus extra to garnish
  • green onions, sliced on the bias, to garnish
  • cooked rice, to serve (see Tip)

Preparation

Glaze

  1. Place green onions and garlic into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add whole dried red chilies, corn starch, sugar, ground ginger, dried chili flakes, black bean paste, chicken stock, soy sauce, black vinegar, cooking wine and peanut oil and without measuring cup cook 7 min/Varoma/speed 1. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.

Sauté

  1. Place water into mixing bowl. Set Varoma dish into position, weigh in chicken, secure Varoma lid and steam 8 min/Varoma//speed 2. Carefully remove Varoma lid, stir chicken with spatula, secure Varoma lid and steam a further 7-10 min/Varoma//speed 2, until chicken is cooked through. Remove Varoma and set chicken aside to keep warm. Discard steaming liquid and clean mixing bowl.
  2. Place peanut oil into mixing bowl and without measuring cup, heat 3 min/250°F/speed .
  3. Add bell peppers, zucchini, carrots and half the reserved glaze and without measuring cup, cook 4 min/250°F//speed .
  4. Add reserved steamed chicken, remaining glaze and peanuts, insert measuring cup and cook 2 min/250°F//speed . Garnish with peanuts and green onions and serve with cooked rice.
