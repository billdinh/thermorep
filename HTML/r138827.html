Ice cream cone cupcakes

Ingredients

Cupcakes

  • 24 - 28 mini ice cream cones (see Tips)
  • 250 g white sugar
  • 250 g unsalted butter, cut into pieces and softened
  • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste
  • 200 g buttermilk
  • 4 eggs
  • 400 g self-raising flour
  • 1 pinch salt
  • natural food colouring of choice (approx. 3 colours)

Buttercream icing

  • 500 g icing sugar (see Tips)
  • 240 g unsalted butter, cut into pieces and softened
  • 2 tsp boiling water
  • ½ tsp natural vanilla extract
  • 100s and 1000s sprinkles (or sprinkles of choice)

Preparation

Cupcakes

  1. Place 24-30 ice cream cones into mini muffin trays and set aside (see Tips). Set aside 3 bowls.
  2. Place sugar into mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 10.
  3. Add butter and mix 10 sec/speed 4.
  4. Add vanilla, buttermilk, eggs, flour and salt and blend 10 sec/speed 5.
  5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and blend 10 sec/speed 5, until smooth.
  6. Divide batter evenly between the reserved bowls (approx. 400 g in each bowl). Add a different natural food colour to each bowl and stir well to combine, until you reach desired colour.
  7. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  8. Layer heaped teaspoons of each of the coloured batters into cones, filling cones 2 cm from the top. Bake for 18-22 minutes (180°C) or until a wooden skewer inserted into the centre of each cupcake comes out clean. Set aside and allow to cool in tray. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Buttercream icing

  1. Place sugar and butter into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Add boiling water and vanilla and mix 2 min/speed 3.  Remove butterfly whisk. Transfer into a piping bag with a nozzle (see Tips). Pipe icing on top of cupcakes and decorate with sprinkles to serve.

Useful Items

24-hole mini muffin tray, 3 bowls, wooden skewer, piping bag with star nozzle, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Flat bottom ice-cream cones usually come in packets of 24 and in various sizes. This recipe fills approx. 30 cones, depending on the size of the cone. If you choose to make only 24 cones, use remaining batter to make standard cupcakes.
  • We recommend using a soft, sifted icing sugar for this recipe.
  • If you are not using icing immediately, place into refrigerator until needed. Before using, bring back to room temperature or stir until soft enough to spread or pipe.
  • Gluten free ice cream cone cupcakes: Replace 400 g of the self-raising flour with 400 g gluten free self-raising flour.
