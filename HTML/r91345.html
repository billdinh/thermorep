Ranch Dressing

Ingredients

  • 1 garlic clove
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 2 heaping tbsp parsley, leaves only
  • 2 tbsp fresh dill
  • 1 tbsp fresh chives
  • 9 oz mayonnaise
  • 4 oz sour cream
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • ½ tsp white vinegar
  • ¼ tsp paprika
  • ¼ tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 dash Tabasco
  • 2 - 4 oz buttermilk, to taste

Preparation

  1. Place garlic and salt into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add parsley, dill and chives and chop 20 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add mayonnaise, sour cream, Worcestershire sauce, black pepper, vinegar, paprika, cayenne pepper and Tabasco and mix 40 sec/speed 3.
  4. Add buttermilk to achieve desired texture and mix 20 sec/speed 3. Transfer Ranch Dressing to a bottle or airtight storage container and refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving.

Useful Items

bottle or airtight storage container

Hints & Tricks

  • For tartar sauce: add an additional garlic clove, replace the dill with pickles and continue with the recipe.
  • Before using the Ranch Dressing, thin it with more buttermilk to achieve desired consistency.
