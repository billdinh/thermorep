Chicken and Quinoa Salad

Ingredients

Marinade

  • 1 orange, thin peel only, no pith
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black peppercorns
  • 1 tsp dried red chili flakes
  • 4 oz orange juice
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 24 oz chicken breasts, air-chilled, boneless and skinless (approx. 3-4 breasts)

Dressing

  • 6 oz extra virgin olive oil
  • 6 sprigs fresh oregano, leaves only
  • 1 garlic clove
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 oz orange juice
  • 1 oz apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 1 tsp stone ground mustard

Steaming

  • 7 oz quinoa
  • 35 oz water
  • 16 oz red beets, peeled, sliced into half-moons (¼ in.)
  • 6 oz fresh green beans, trimmed and halved
  • 6 oz beet greens, cut into thin ribbons
  • 6 oz blackberries
  • 5 oz goat cheese, crumbled or cut into pieces

Preparation

Marinade

  1. Place orange peel, garlic, oregano, salt, peppercorns and chili flakes into mixing bowl and grind 7 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add orange juice and honey and mix 5 sec/speed 8. Transfer marinade into a large zip lock bag and add chicken. Refrigerate 30 minutes. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl and make dressing.

Dressing

  1. Place a pitcher onto mixing bowl lid, weigh in oil and set aside. Place oregano, garlic and salt into mixing bowl and chop 7 sec/speed 9.
  2. Add orange juice, vinegar, honey and mustard and blend 1 min 30 sec/speed 10 while very slowly pouring reserved oil onto mixing bowl lid, letting it drizzle around measuring cup in a thin stream onto rotating blades to create an emulsion. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.

Steaming

  1. Insert simmering basket into mixing bowl and weigh in quinoa and water and rinse 10 sec/speed 5.
  2. Place Varoma into position and add marinated chicken. Insert Varoma tray, weigh in beets and beans, secure Varoma lid and steam 20-23 min/Varoma/speed 2 until chicken is cooked through.
  3. Slice chicken into ¼ inch pieces. Transfer chicken, beets, beans and quinoa into a bowl and toss with beet greens and blackberries. Garnish with goat cheese and serve warm.

Useful Items

large zip lock freezer bag, pitcher, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • To easily slice goat cheese: place in freezer until firm enough to slice, grate or crumble.
