Pork with Black Bean Sauce

Ingredients

Marinade

  • 3 oz Stone's Green Ginger Wine
  • 24 oz pork tenderloin (approx. 2-3 fillets), trimmed and cut into strips (½ in. x 4 in.)
  • 2 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 1 tsp olive oil

Black Bean Sauce

  • ½ - 1 fresh chili, seeds removed if preferred (e.g. serrano) (optional)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 can low sodium black beans (approx. 15 oz)
  • 2 oz soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 3 oz water
  • 1 tsp brown sugar

Rice and Vegetables

  • 10 ½ oz long-grain white rice
  • 32 oz water
  • 1 bunch broccolini (approx. 6.5 oz), trimmed and cut into thirds
  • 1 can bamboo shoots (approx. 8 oz), rinsed and drained
  • 1 green onion, trimmed and thinly sliced, to garnish
  • 1 - 2 sprigs fresh cilantro, leaves only, to garnish

Preparation

Marinade

  1. Place a ceramic or glass bowl onto mixing bowl lid, weigh in ginger wine and pork. Add hoisin sauce and oil, stir to combine and set aside.

Black Bean Sauce

  1. Place chili and garlic into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Drain black beans through simmering basket, rinse well and allow to drain. Add 5.5 oz of drained black beans into mixing bowl and set remaining beans aside.
  3. Add all remaining sauce ingredients and cook 3 min/212°F/speed 5. Transfer into a large bowl to keep warm. Rinse mixing bowl.

Rice and Vegetables

  1. Insert simmering basket into mixing bowl, weigh in rice, remove simmering basket and rinse rice under cold running water until water runs clear.
  2. Place water into mixing bowl, insert simmering basket with rice and cook 5 min/Varoma/speed 2. Place Varoma into position and place reserved pork and marinade into Varoma dish. Secure Varoma lid and cook 8 min/Varoma/speed 2. Set Varoma aside. Stir rice in simmering basket with spatula, and stir pork in Varoma dish to ensure even cooking. Place Varoma dish back into position.
  3. Insert Varoma tray and place broccolini, bamboo shoots and remaining drained black beans onto it. Secure Varoma lid and cook 6-8 min/Varoma/speed 2, or until all pork is cooked.
  4. Combine pork with black bean sauce, vegetables and black beans. Serve over rice and garnish with green onions and cilantro leaves.

Useful Items

ceramic or glass bowl, large bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • For a sweeter variation replace Chinese cooking wine with Mirin. This can be found in specialty grocery stores and Asian supermarkets.
  • Stone's Green Ginger Wine can be found in specialty liquor stores and can be replaced with Madeira wine and ½ tsp of ground ginger.
