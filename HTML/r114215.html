Bacon and cinnamon scrolls with salted maple glaze

Ingredients

Filling

  • 8 bacon rashers, streaky (approx. 240 g)
  • 50 g unsalted butter, chilled and cut into cubes (2 cm)
  • 1 tbsp ground cinnamon
  • 50 g raw sugar

Dough

  • 150 g full cream milk
  • 90 g unsalted butter, chilled and cut into cubes (2 cm), plus extra for greasing
  • 300 g plain flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 50 g raw sugar
  • 1 pinch salt

Salted maple glaze

  • 30 g unsalted butter
  • 50 g pure maple syrup
  • 60 g icing sugar
  • 1 tbsp water
  • ¼ tsp sea salt flakes, plus extra for garnishing

Preparation

Filling

  1. Position oven rack to top shelf in oven. Preheat oven to 190°C (grill setting) and line a baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper. Line a plate with paper towel and set aside.
  2. Place bacon onto prepared baking tray and grill for 5 minutes. Transfer onto lined plate to absorb excess oil. Heat oven to 190°C (oven setting).
  3. Place butter, cinnamon and sugar into mixing bowl and mix 15 sec/speed 4. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Do not clean mixing bowl.

Dough

  1. Place a small jug or bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh milk into it, then set aside.
  2. Grease a round springform cake tin (22 cm) and set aside.
  3. Place flour, baking powder and butter into mixing bowl and mix 5 sec/speed 5, until mixture resembles breadcrumbs.
  4. Add sugar and salt and knead 1 min/, gradually pouring milk through hole in mixing bowl lid until a soft dough forms.
  5. Transfer dough onto a lightly floured silicone bread mat or work surface and roll out into a rectangle (approx. 20 x 35 cm). Sprinkle reserved butter mixture evenly over dough.
  6. With the long edge of the pastry closest to you, place bacon pieces vertically over filling, leaving a finger width gap between each piece, allowing bacon to slightly overhang the top edge (so you can see where the pieces are as you roll the scroll).
  7. Rolling up from the long edge, roll dough into a long sausage shape. Cut dough into 8 scrolls, cutting in the gaps between the bacon.
  8. Place scrolls into prepared cake tin and bake for 25-30 minutes (190°C), or until golden brown. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Salted maple glaze

  1. Place all glaze ingredients into mixing bowl and cook 2 min/60°C/speed 3, then blend 10 sec/speed 6.
  2. Remove scrolls from oven and immediately pour three-quarters of the glaze over scrolls and sprinkle with extra salt flakes. Serve warm with extra glaze.

Useful Items

1 plate, baking paper, baking tray (30 x 40 cm), paper towel, bowl, small jug, springform cake tin (22 cm), silicone bread mat, rolling pin, oven, oven grill

Hints & Tricks

  • Cut dough into smaller pieces to make smaller, bite-sized scrolls.

I'm always looking for new ways with bacon! The idea for this dish came out of an American-style breakfast of cinnamon waffles with bacon, and my love of using baked treats to make a special occasion out of breakfast. You wouldn't eat these indulgent scrolls every day, but they are a showstopper for a celebratory brunch.
