Pumpkin Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

  • vegetable oil, to grease

Dough

  • 4 oz canned pumpkin purée
  • 4 oz sour cream
  • 2 ½ oz unsalted butter, room temperature, diced (½ in.)
  • 2 ½ oz sugar
  • 2 oz whole milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 19 oz bread flour, plus extra to dust
  • 1 tbsp dried instant yeast
  • 1 tsp homemade pumpkin spice mix
  • 1 tsp salt
  • vegetable oil, to grease
  • 12 oz homemade pumpkin butter
  • 3 ½ oz chopped pecans

Glaze

  • 5 oz brown sugar
  • 4 oz cream cheese, room temperature
  • ½ tsp homemade pumpkin spice mix
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 oz whole milk

Preparation

  1. Lightly oil a baking pan (approx. 9 in. x 13 in. x 2 in.) and set aside.

Dough

  1. Place pumpkin purée, sour cream, butter, sugar, milk and eggs into mixing bowl and heat 4 min/110°F/speed 1.
  2. Add flour, yeast, pumpkin spice mix and salt and mix 10 sec/speed 5 then knead 3 min/. Transfer dough to a lightly floured surface, dust with flour and form into a ball.
  3. Lightly grease a large bowl with oil and transfer dough into prepared bowl, turning dough to coat all sides. Cover with cling wrap, let rise in a warm place for 1½-2 hours or until dough has doubled in size.
  4. Punch down risen dough and turn out onto a lightly floured work surface. Roll the dough with a rolling pin into a rectangle (approx. 24 in. x 12 in.). Spread pumpkin butter evenly over dough leaving a ½ inch border and evenly sprinkle with chopped pecans. Lightly press pecans into dough.
  5. Roll up dough into a long log shape, pinching at edges to seal. Slice log into 12 equal pieces (approx. 1½ in.) and place on prepared pan leaving a ½ inch space between each. Cover with cling wrap and set in a warm place to rise for 1½ hours or until rolls have doubled in size. Meanwhile, preheat oven to 375°F.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes (375°F) until golden brown. Set aside and allow to cool slightly.

Glaze

  1. Place sugar into mixing bowl grind 20 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add cream cheese, pumpkin spice mix, vanilla and milk and mix 30 sec/speed 4. Drizzle glaze over rolls. Transfer to a serving dish and serve warm.

Useful Items

cling wrap, baking pan (approx. 9 in. x 13 in. x 2 in.), rolling pin, serving dish, large bowl, oven
