Aubergine Bhurta

Ingredients

  • 500 g water
  • 500 g aubergines, cut in pieces (3 cm)
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 250 g onions, quartered
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
319 kJ / 76 kcal
Protein
1.4 g
Carbohydrates
5.4 g
Fat
5.5 g

Preparation

  1. Place water in mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position and weigh aubergines into Varoma dish. Steam 15 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  2. Set Varoma aside and discard steaming water.
  3. Place garlic in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8.
  4. Add onions and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add oil and cumin then, without measuring cup, sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  6. Add reserved steamed aubergine, salt and pepper then mix 6 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Cook 2 min/100°C/speed 3. Serve warm.
