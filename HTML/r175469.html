Garlic Pull-Apart Knots

Ingredients

  • 1 oz olive oil, plus extra to grease
  • 15 oz bread flour
  • 2 ½ oz whole wheat flour
  • 9 oz warm water (100°F-110°F)
  • ½ oz sugar
  • 2 ¼ tsp rapid rise yeast
  • 3 tsp salt, divided
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • ½ oz fresh flat leaf parsley, leaves only (optional)
  • 2 ½ oz unsalted butter

Preparation

  1. Lightly grease a large bowl and a springform pan and set aside.
  2. Place bread flour, whole wheat flour, water, sugar, yeast, 1 oz oil and 2 tsp salt into mixing bowl and knead 2 min/.
  3. Form dough into a ball and transfer into prepared bowl. Cover with cling wrap and let dough rise until doubled in size (approx. 1 hr.).
  4. Place garlic and parsley into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down side of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add butter and 1 tsp salt and mix 30 sec/speed 4.
  6. Preheat oven to 350°F. Divide dough in half. Place each half onto a lightly floured surface. Evenly stretch each dough into a rectangle (approx. 9 in. x 12 in.), with lightly floured fingertips. Spread each with half the reserved garlic butter. Cut dough into 6 pieces (3 in. x 6 in.) and roll the dough so the garlic butter is wrapped in the center. Gently stretch the dough just enough to allow you to easily tie into a knot shape. Place into the springform pan. Repeat with remaining dough.
  7. Cover with a damp kitchen towel and set pan in a warm place until doubled in size (approx. 20 min.).
  8. Bake for 35 minutes (350°F) or until golden. Serve warm.

Useful Items

large bowl, cling wrap, springform pan (10 in.), kitchen towel, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Make in advance method: dough can be made in advance and stored in a refrigerator for up to 2 days. Remove dough from refrigerator and allow it to rise (approx. 1 hr.) before proceeding with the filling.
  • For a glazed or shiny look to your garlic knots, brush with olive oil or melted butter immediately before and after baking.
