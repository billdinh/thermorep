Double Pea and Chorizo Soup

Ingredients

  • 120 g dried split peas
  • 5 g fresh mint
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 40 g spring onions, cut in pieces (2-3 cm)
  • 100 g onions, quartered
  • 15 g olive oil
  • 1 tsp paprika, plus extra for garnish
  • ½ tsp ground ginger
  • 1250 g water
  • 2 tsp fine sea salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 200 g frozen green peas
  • 175 g chorizo, cut in pieces (0.5 cm)

Preparation

  1. Place split peas in mixing bowl and grind 1 min/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place mint in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place garlic, spring onions, onions, oil, paprika and ginger in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then sauté 4 min/100°C/speed 1.
  4. Add water, reserved ground split peas, salt and pepper. Mix 10 sec/speed 4 then cook 30 min/100°C/speed 2.
  5. Add frozen peas and chorizo and cook 5 min/100°C/speed 2.
  6. Add reserved mint and mix in with spatula before serving hot with a sprinkling of paprika.
