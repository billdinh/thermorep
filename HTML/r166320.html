Chicken with bulgur and vegetables

Ingredients

  • 800 g chicken breast fillets, cut into cubes (3 cm)
  • 50 g olive oil
  • 50 g lemon juice, freshly squeezed
  • 1 tbsp ground coriander
  • 1 pinch ground paprika
  • 2 pinches ground cumin
  • 300 g bulgur, coarse
  • 100 g onions, cut into quarters
  • 100 g carrots, cut into pieces
  • 100 g courgettes, cut into pieces
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 pinch pepper
  • 1000 g water
  • 300 g tomato passata
  • 1 tbsp pine nuts, to garnish
  • 50 g fresh tomatoes, deseeded and diced, to garnish
  • 1 sprig fresh parsley, leaves only, chopped, to garnish

Preparation

  1. Place a deep bowl onto mixing bowl lid, weigh in chicken, 20 g olive oil, lemon juice, ground coriander, paprika, 1 pinch cumin. Mix with aid of spatula and set aside for at least 2 hours to marinate.
  2. Place simmering basket onto mixing bowl lid, weigh in bulgur, rinse under running water, place on a plate and set aside to strain.
  3. Place onions, carrots, courgettes, remaining 30 g olive oil, salt, pepper and 1 pinch cumin into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5, then remove measuring cup and sauté 8 min/120°C/speed 1. Transfer into simmering basket with bulgur and mix with aid of spatula.
  4. Place water into mixing bowl, insert simmering basket with bulgur and vegetables and add tomato passata on top. Place Varoma dish and tray into position, distribute marinated chicken in Varoma tray and steam 40 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  5. Remove Varoma and set aside. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and set aside. Transfer bulgur and chicken into a deep serving bowl, pour remaining steaming liquid on top and let absorb. Garnish with pine nuts, diced tomato and fresh chopped parsley before serving.
