Greek Cheese Filo Pie (Tiropita)

Ingredients

  • 600 g feta cheese, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 180 g unsalted butter, diced
  • 100 g fine semolina
  • 700 g milk
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 6 medium eggs
  • 1 - 2 pinches ground black pepper, to taste
  • 16 sheets filo pastry
  • water, cold, for sprinkling
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds, for garnishing (optional)

Preparation

  1. Place feta in mixing bowl and grate 8 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.
  2. Place 130 g butter in mixing bowl and melt 2 min/100°C/speed 2. Transfer to another bowl, cover and set aside.
  3. Place remaining 50 g butter, semolina, milk and salt in mixing bowl then cook 7 min/100°C/speed 3.
  4. Without measuring cup, mix 1 min 30 sec/100°C/speed 3, adding eggs, one at a time, through hole in mixing bowl lid.
  5. Add pepper and reserved grated feta then mix 10 sec/speed 6.
  6. Preheat oven to 200°C and grease a baking dish (approx. 30 cm x 20 cm) with reserved melted butter.
  7. Brush 8 sheets filo pastry with reserved melted butter and arrange over base of prepared baking dish, one after another, allowing filo to extend over edges of baking dish.
  8. Transfer cheese mixture to baking dish, in an even layer, then fold over overhanging pieces of pastry. Brush remaining 8 sheets filo pastry with remaining melted butter and layer over cheese filling. Tuck edges of filo down around sides of dish, then score top layer of pie with a sharp knife into 8 equal-sized portions, sprinkle with water and garnish with sesame seeds (if using).
  9. Bake for 25-30 minutes (200°C) until golden brown. Leave to cool for 5 minutes before serving warm or at room temperature.

Useful Items

baking dish (approx. 30 cm x 20 cm), pastry brush, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Add 2 tsp dried mint in step 4 for a different flavour.
  • Cover filo pastry sheets with a damp tea towel to prevent them drying out as you work.
  • Serve tiropita with a green salad.
