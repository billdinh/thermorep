Wholemeal spelt pastry

Ingredients

  • 260 g wholemeal spelt flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 100 g olive oil
  • 90 g chilled water
  • 1 egg
  • 1 ½ tsp apple cider vinegar
  • 70 g sesame seeds
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1227 kJ / 292 kcal
Protein
9.2 g
Carbohydrates
20.5 g
Fat
18.8 g

Preparation

  1. Place all pastry ingredients into mixing bowl and mix 8 sec/speed 4 or until combined. Transfer pastry onto a silicone bread mat or a lightly floured work surface and knead slightly to form a smooth dough. Shape into a ball, wrap in plastic wrap and place into refrigerator for a minimum of 30 minutes or until ready to use (see Tips).

Useful Items

silicone bread mat, plastic wrap, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • We used this pastry in the Spinach and feta galette (please refer to the recipe on this recipe chip), but it can be used for any type of sweet or savoury pastry dish.
  • Pastry can be divided into portions, wrapped in plastic wrap and stored in the freezer for up to 2 weeks.
  • To blind bake pastry: Preheat oven to 200°C. Roll out pastry to line the base and sides of a greased tart tin and prick the base with a fork or pastry docker to prevent air bubbles and rising. Line pastry base with baking paper and fill with rice, dried beans or pie weights (this will stop the pastry base from rising during baking). Place tart tin onto a baking tray and bake for 15 minutes (200°C). Remove from oven, then remove baking paper with rice, dried beans or pie weights. Return pastry case to oven and bake for a further 5-10 minutes (200°C) or until light golden.
