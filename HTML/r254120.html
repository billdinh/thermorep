Thai Peanut Crunch Salad

Ingredients

  • 5 oz peanuts, roasted and salted
  • 2 oz fish sauce
  • 3 oz soy sauce
  • 2 ½ oz rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 in. fresh ginger
  • 2 oz freshly squeezed orange juice
  • 2 oz freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 43 oz water
  • 8 oz dried rice noodles, Thai
  • ½ Lebanese cucumber, diced (½ in.)
  • ½ red bell pepper, diced (½ in.)
  • ½ iceberg lettuce head, shredded
  • 2 mangoes, diced (½ in.)
  • 4 - 6 scallions, thinly sliced
  • 1 ½ - 2 bunches cilantro, chopped, to taste
  • 15 - 20 fresh mint leaves, chopped

Preparation

  1. Place peanuts into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place fish sauce, soy sauce, vinegar, oil, sugar, ginger, orange and lime juices into mixing bowl and blend 15 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place water into mixing bowl and bring to a boil 10 min/212°F/speed 1. Add rice noodles through hole in mixing bowl lid and without measuring cup cook for time indicated on package/212°F//speed 1 or until al dente (see Tip).
  4. Drain noodles and rinse with cold water, keeping the noodles moving in the cold water until noodles are cool. Drain again and place into a large serving bowl.
  5. Add cucumber, bell pepper, lettuce, mango, scallions, cilantro, mint, and half the peanuts and dressing to reserved noodles and toss to combine. Garnish with remaining peanuts and serve immediately.

Hints & Tricks

  • Wash vegetables, fruit and herbs before use.
  • Prepare vegetables and herbs while noodles are cooking.
