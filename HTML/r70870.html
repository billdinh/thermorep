Thai beef salad

Ingredients

Beef

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 4 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp rice wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 400 g beef fillet, cut into thin strips (6 x 1 cm)
  • 500 g water

Dressing

  • 2 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 20 g avocado oil
  • ½ - 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp rice wine vinegar
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce or tamari
  • 40 g lime juice (approx. 2 limes)
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar

Salad

  • ¼ cabbage (approx. 330 g), cut into pieces
  • ¼ red onion, to taste
  • 90 g slivered almonds, raw or toasted
  • 100 g tatsoi or fresh baby spinach leaves
  • long red chilli, finely sliced, for garnishing (optional)
  • fresh lime cheeks, to serve

Preparation

Beef

  1. Place garlic and ginger into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add fish sauce, rice wine vinegar and sesame oil and combine 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer marinade into a ceramic or glass bowl. Place bowl with marinated onto mixing bowl lid and weigh beef into it. Combine well, then cover with plastic wrap and set aside in the refrigerator to marinate for 1 hour.
  3. Insert Varoma tray and line with a piece of wet and well-wrung baking paper. Place marinated beef onto baking paper and set Varoma aside.
  4. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and cook 6 min/Varoma/speed 1. Stir beef with spatula to ensure even cooking, then secure Varoma lid and cook for a further 4 min/Varoma/speed 1. Stir beef again with spatula, secure Varoma lid and cook for a further 1-2 min/Varoma/speed 1, or until beef is cooked. Transfer beef into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and cover to keep warm. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Dressing

  1. Place ginger into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add all remaining dressing ingredients and mix 6 sec/speed 5. Transfer dressing into a serving jug and set aside until ready to use.

Salad

  1. Place cabbage and onion into mixing bowl and chop 4-5 sec/speed 4, or until evenly chopped. Transfer into a large serving bowl and set aside.
  2. Place serving bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh almonds and tatsoi or baby spinach into it. Toss through reserved dressing. Top salad with beef fillet, garnish with finely sliced red chilli (optional) and lime cheeks, then serve immediately.

Useful Items

ceramic or glass bowl, plastic wrap, baking paper, serving jug, large serving bowl, thermal serving bowl or other large bowl, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Using tamari instead of soy sauce will make this a gluten free recipe.
