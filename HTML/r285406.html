Gluten-Free Apple Fritters with Caramel Sauce

Ingredients

Caramel Sauce

  • 3 ½ oz sugar
  • 1 pinch baking soda
  • 3 ½ oz heavy whipping cream

Chocolate Sauce

  • 3 ½ oz semi-sweet chocolate, cut into pieces
  • 4 ½ oz heavy whipping cream

Fritters

  • 4 oz sushi rice
  • 1 oz masa harina
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 4 oz beer
  • frying oil
  • 9 oz Granny Smith apples, cored and sliced (1 in. thick)
  • 1 pinch salt (optional)
  • 1 pinch ground cinnamon

Preparation

Caramel Sauce

  1. Place sugar, baking soda and whipping cream into mixing bowl and cook 65 min/195°F/speed 2. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.

Chocolate Sauce

  1. Place chocolate and whipping cream into mixing bowl and warm 5 min/195°F/speed 1. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.

Fritters

  1. Place sushi rice and masa harina into mixing bowl and grind 1 min/speed 10.
  2. Add eggs, baking powder and beer and mix 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix a further 5 sec/speed 5.
  3. Place enough oil (approx. 2 in.) in a cooking pot and place on the stove on high heat.
  4. Dip the apple slices in fritter mixture to coat. Fry in hot oil until lightly brown. Transfer apple fritters into a dish lined with paper towel to remove any excess oil. Sprinkle with salt and cinnamon. Serve immediately with caramel and chocolate sauces.
