Beetroot Gravadlax

Ingredients

Salmon Gravadlax (prepare 48 hours in advance)

  • 1 orange, thin peelings of skin
  • 2 juniper berries
  • 150 g cooked beetroots, peeled
  • 200 g coarse sea salt
  • 120 g Demerara sugar
  • 30 g vodka
  • 1 fresh salmon fillet, boned, skin on (approx. 600 g)

Lemon Dill Cream

  • 100 g double cream
  • 20 g lemon juice
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt, to taste
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper, to taste
  • 5 g fresh dill, approx. 1 small handful, leaves only

Preparation

Salmon Gravadlax (prepare 48 hours in advance)

  1. Place orange zest and juniper berries in mixing bowl then grind 5 sec/speed 9.
  2. Add beetroot and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add salt, sugar and vodka then mix 20 sec/speed 2. Overlap two large pieces of cling film on a dinner plate. Place salmon skin-side down on plate and cover completely with beetroot mixture. Wrap tightly in cling film then leave to marinate in fridge for 48 hours. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl.

Lemon Dill Cream

  1. After marinating time, insert butterfly whisk. Place cream, lemon juice, salt and pepper in mixing bowl then whisk 1 min/speed 3. Remove butterfly whisk. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add dill and mix 3 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a small bowl and refrigerate until ready to serve.
  3. Rinse beetroot cure off salmon completely then peel off skin, using a sharp knife if necessary. Slice thinly and serve with lemon dill cream on the side.

Useful Items

large plate, cling film, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • For a more complete dish, serve with raw vegetables or fruits (tomatoes, grapes, apples), or a potato salad.
  • Placing a heavy weight on the fish during marinating helps moisture to be squeezed out and marinade to penetrate into the fish. In this case, turn the fish over several times during marinating.
  • Gravadlax can be kept in the fridge for up to 5 days.
  • Gravadlax can be wrapped in cling film (without marinade) and frozen for up to 2 months. Part-thaw for about 1 hour before slicing.
  • Fry the slices of salmon lightly to add a crunchy side.
