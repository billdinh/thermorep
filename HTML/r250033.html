Pecan pie cheesecake cups

Ingredients

  • 100 g pecans
  • 70 g unsalted butter, softened
  • 120 g digestive biscuits, broken into pieces
  • 40 g bourbon (optional - see Tips)
  • 80 g brown sugar
  • 60 g pure maple syrup
  • 1 ½ tsp vanilla bean paste
  • 250 g cream cheese
  • 150 g pouring (whipping) cream

Preparation

  1. Place pecans into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 5. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place 40 g of the butter into mixing bowl and melt 1 min/100°C/speed 2.
  3. Add biscuits, 20 g of the bourbon (optional) and 20 g of the reserved pecans and mix 3 sec/speed 6. Place 1 tablespoon of the crumb mixture into base of 8 small serving glasses (approx. 100 ml) and place into refrigerator until ready to use.
  4. Place sugar, remaining 20 g bourbon, 20 g of the syrup, ½ tsp of the vanilla, remaining 30 g butter and remaining 80 g pecans into mixing bowl and heat 4 min/Varoma//speed 2, without measuring cup. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  5. Place cream cheese, cream, remaining 40 g syrup and remaining 1 tsp vanilla into mixing bowl and mix 20 sec/speed 4.
  6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Insert butterfly whisk. Whip 15 sec/speed 3, until light and smooth. Remove butterfly whisk.
  7. Remove glasses from refrigerator and divide cheesecake mixture evenly between glasses. Spoon over reserved pecan mixture. Place into refrigerator for a minimum of 10 minutes or until ready to serve (see Tips).

Useful Items

bowl, small serving glasses, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • You can omit the bourbon for a non-alcoholic version.
  • You can make this recipe up to 2 days in advance, but store the base and cheesecake mixture separately until ready to assemble.
