Fajitas

Ingredients

  • 1 oz fajita seasoning, homemade or packaged
  • 1 oz olive oil
  • 2 oz lime juice
  • 24 oz chicken breast fillet, boneless, skinless, cut into strips (approx. ½ in. x 3 in.)
  • 9 oz red onions, halved and sliced (approx. ⅛ in.), divided
  • 30 oz water
  • 7 oz green bell peppers, halved and sliced (approx. ⅛ in.)
  • 7 oz red bell peppers, halved and sliced (approx. ⅛ in.)
  • ¼ tsp salt, to taste
  • ¼ tsp freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 8 flour tortillas (approx. 8 in.)

Preparation

  1. Place fajita seasoning, olive oil and lime juice into mixing bowl and mix 15 sec/speed 3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add chicken and mix 15 sec//speed 1.5.
  3. Place a large piece of parchment (approx. 25 in.) into Varoma dish and transfer chicken and sauce to parchment. Place Varoma into position and weigh in 2 oz onions on top of chicken. Fold paper loosely into a parcel and lay seam side down into Varoma dish ensuring some holes remain unobstructed to allow even distribution of steam. Set Varoma with chicken aside.
  4. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 15 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  5. Carefully open Varoma lid and parcel. Add green peppers, red peppers and remaining 7 oz onions over chicken. Season with salt and pepper, fold parchment to close parcel, secure Varoma lid and steam a further 10 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  6. Transfer cooked vegetables and chicken into a serving bowl, stir through with spatula to combine and serve with flour tortillas.

Useful Items

parchment paper, serving bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • Use the fajita seasoning recipe from the Tex-Mex Cookbook or use a store-bought version.
  • Serve with rice, beans, wedges of lime and chopped cilantro.
  • For more traditional Fajitas: place batches of steamed chicken and vegetables into a cast iron frying pan and fry on high heat for about 1 minute each side and serve immediately.
  • Chicken strips can be replaced with beef strips.
