Easter Carrot Cheese Dip

Ingredients

  • 200 g Red Leicester cheese, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 1 carrot, approx. 100 g, cut in pieces (3 cm)
  • 2 spring onions, cut in pieces (3 cm)
  • 500 g cream cheese
  • 2 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 ½ tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp paprika
  • 1 - 1 ½ tsp fine sea salt, to taste
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 1 bunch fresh parsley
  • crackers, for serving

Preparation

  1. Place Red Leicester in mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place carrot and spring onions in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then chop again 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add 75 g reserved grated cheese, cream cheese, mustard, garlic powder, paprika, salt and pepper then mix 15 sec/speed 3.
  4. Taking spoonfuls of the mixture at a time, transfer to a large plate or serving platter and form into a carrot, using a spoon to smooth the sides. Cover 'carrot' with remaining reserved grated cheese, patting down to adhere to surface.
  5. Arrange parsley at one end to look like the leaves of a carrot then serve dip immediately with crackers, or cover with cling film and place in fridge until needed.

Useful Items

cling film, large serving plate, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Children can assist with this recipe, weighing ingredients in and setting the controls. Never leave them unattended with Thermomix®, while it is running or between steps.
  • Children can also help with the shaping of the 'carrot'.
  • Make several smaller 'carrots' if preferred.
