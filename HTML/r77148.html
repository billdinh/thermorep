Quinoa ANZAC biscuits

Ingredients

  • 120 g butter
  • 40 g golden syrup
  • 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 30 g grapeseed oil
  • ½ tsp white vinegar
  • 150 g quinoa flakes
  • 100 g water
  • 120 g brown sugar
  • 170 g gluten free plain flour (see Tips)
  • 30 g desiccated coconut
Nutrition

612 kJ / 145 kcal

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C. Line a baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place butter and golden syrup into mixing bowl and heat 2 min/60°C/speed 1, or until butter has melted.
  3. Add bicarbonate of soda and mix 5 sec/speed 3.
  4. Add all remaining ingredients and knead 1 min/.
  5. Spoon tablespoonfuls of mixture onto prepared baking tray, allowing room for biscuits to spread during baking.
  6. Bake for 12-15 minutes (160°C) or until golden brown.
  7. Cool on baking tray for 5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely (approx. 30 minutes) before serving. 

Useful Items

baking tray (30 x 40 cm), baking paper, wire rack, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Make your own Gluten free plain flour using the recipe in this collection.
  • To ensure gluten free recipes are gluten free, always read the labels of your products to ensure no traces of wheat or gluten have been used as part of the production process.
  • Store biscuits in a sealable container for up to 1 week.
