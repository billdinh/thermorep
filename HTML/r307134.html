Pork Tenderloin with Cherry Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 oz shallots, in pieces
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 8 oz water
  • 2 oz cherry jam
  • 1 tsp balsamic vinegar
  • ½ oz unsalted butter
  • ½ tsp salt, divided
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper, divided
  • 16 oz pork tenderloin
  • 12 oz baby rainbow carrots, peeled, halved lengthwise
  • 1 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • fresh cherries, pitted, halved, to garnish (optional)
  • chopped parsley, to garnish

Preparation

  1. Place shallots and garlic into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add water, cherry jam, balsamic vinegar, butter, ¼ tsp salt and ⅛ tsp pepper into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position, weigh in pork tenderloin and carrots. Arrange carrots around pork, season with ¼ tsp salt and ⅛ tsp pepper, secure Varoma lid and steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Remove Varoma, transfer pork and carrots to a serving platter and keep warm.
  4. Add flour into mixing bowl, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 6 min/Varoma/speed 3. Insert measuring cup and purée 30 sec/speed 8. Slice tenderloin into medallions and drizzle with cherry sauce. Garnish with cherries and chopped parsley and serve hot.

Hints & Tricks

  • The USDA Guidelines and National Pork Board recommend cooking pork chops, roasts and tenderloin to an internal temperature between 145° F (medium rare) and 160° F (medium), followed by a 3 minute rest.
