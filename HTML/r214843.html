Chicago-Style Deep Dish Pizza

Ingredients

  • olive oil, for greasing
  • 315 g water, at room temperature
  • 3 tsp dried instant yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 570 g plain flour
  • 30 g cornmeal, medium
  • 60 g unsalted butter, melted
  • 45 g olive oil
  • 3 tsp fine sea salt
  • 370 g mozzarella, cut in pieces (approx. 2 cm)
  • 230 g Parmesan cheese, cut in pieces (approx. 1 cm)
  • 270 g sausage, Italian, (e.g luganega), cut in pieces (approx. 2 cm)
  • 200 g onions, quartered (approx. 1 large)
  • 15 g garlic cloves
  • 800 g tinned chopped tomatoes
  • ½ tsp dried basil
  • ½ tsp dried oregano
  • ½ tsp fresh rosemary
  • ¼ tsp dried chilli flakes, to taste
  • 115 g pepperoni sausage, to taste
  • 115 g salami, to taste
  • 170 g back bacon, sliced, diced (approx. 1 cm)

Preparation

  1. Lightly grease a large glass bowl and set aside. Place water, yeast and sugar in mixing bowl then mix 20 sec/speed 2.
  2. Add flour, cornmeal, butter, olive oil and salt then knead 4 min/. Transfer dough to prepared bowl and shape into a ball. Cover with cling film and allow to rise in a warm place until doubled in size (approx. 1 hour). Meanwhile, prepare your toppings and sauce.
  3. Place 200 g mozzarella in mixing bowl and grate 15 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl. Place remaining 170 g mozzarella in mixing bowl and grate 15 sec/speed 6, then add to bowl with grated mozzarella then set aside.
  4. Place 115 g Parmesan in mixing bowl and grate 15-20 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a separate bowl and set aside. Place remaining 115 g Parmesan in mixing bowl and grate 15-20 sec/speed 10, add to bowl with previously grated Parmesan, and refrigerate cheeses until needed.
  5. Place sausage in mixing bowl and cook 15 min/100°C//speed 0.5. Transfer sausage to a bowl lined with paper towels and set aside.
  6. Place onions, garlic and olive oil in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Sauté 5 min/120°C/speed 1.
  7. Add tomatoes, salt, basil, oregano, rosemary and chilli flakes. With simmering basket in place of measuring cup on mixing bowl lid, cook 5 min/100°C/speed 1. Set sauce aside until ready to use.
  8. Preheat oven to 220°C. Place risen pizza dough in the centre of a deep dish pizza pan. Flatten dough into a circle and begin evenly pressing the dough outwards to the edge and up sides of the pan. Cover with cling film and allow to rest for 10 minutes.
  9. Once dough has rested, stretch dough one last time out to edge of pan and up sides. Prick pizza base (not edges) every 2 cm with a fork. Parbake crust for 10 minutes (220°C).
  10. Sprinkle reserved mozzarella evenly over pizza. Next, layer over pepperoni, salami, bacon and reserved Italian sausage. Pour reserved tomato sauce evenly over the top. Bake for 25-30 minutes (220°C) until crust is golden brown and sauce is bubbling.
  11. Sprinkle reserved grated Parmesan cheese over top of pizza. Allow to rest for at least 5 minutes before slicing.

Useful Items

large glass bowl, paper towel, pizza tray, cling film, refrigerator, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • For a vegetarian pizza, replace meats with equal amounts of sautéed mushrooms, peppers, red onions, pitted olives, shallots, garlic and courgette or squash.
