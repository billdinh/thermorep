Tuna Tartare

Ingredients

Sushi Rice

  • 14 oz sushi rice
  • 32 oz water, plus extra, cold, to rinse
  • 4 oz rice wine vinegar
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 oz sugar, to taste
  • 1 pinch salt (optional)

Tuna Tartare

  • 10 oz sushi grade tuna, cubed (1 in.)
  • 1 tsp mirin
  • 2 tsp ponzu sauce
  • 1 ½ tsp soy sauce, plus extra to serve
  • ½ tsp toasted sesame oil
  • 1 tsp wasabi, plus extra to serve
  • 1 tsp fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground white pepper
  • 1 lime, zested, plus 1 tsp juice
  • 1 ripe avocado, thinly sliced
  • black sesame seeds, to garnish

Preparation

Sushi Rice

  1. Line a baking sheet with parchment and set aside.
  2. Place simmering basket onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in rice. Remove simmering basket with rice and rinse under cold running water until water runs clear.
  3. Place water, rice wine vinegar, vegetable oil, sugar and salt into mixing bowl, insert simmering basket with rinsed rice and rinse a further 5 sec/speed 4 and steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 4. Remove simmering basket with spatula and set aside to drain. Discard steaming water. Transfer drained rice to prepared sheet pan and let cool. Clean mixing bowl.

Tuna Tartare

  1. Place tuna, mirin, ponzu, 1½ tsp soy, sesame oil, 1 tsp wasabi, ginger, salt, pepper, lime zest and 1 tsp lime juice into mixing bowl and mix Turbo/2 sec/1 time. Transfer to a bowl and refrigerate until ready to use (use within one hour of refrigeration).
  2. Lightly oil a round cookie cutter (3 in.), place the cutter onto the middle of a plate, spoon 2 heaping tbsp of steamed rice into cutter and gently press down to compact, then spoon 2 heaping tbsp of Tuna Tartare over rice layer. Gently remove cookie cutter and repeat with remaining ingredients.
  3. Garnish with a slice of avocado and black sesame seeds. Serve with wasabi and soy sauce.

Useful Items

cookie cutter (3 in.), baking sheet, parchment paper, refrigerator
