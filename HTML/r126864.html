Chicken pastilla

Ingredients

  • 200 g blanched almonds
  • 40 g sugar
  • 30 g parsley, leaves only
  • 500 g onions, cut into quarters
  • 50 g butter
  • ½ tsp salt, adjust to taste
  • 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper, adjust to taste
  • 1 pinch ground saffron
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 6 eggs
  • 700 g chicken breast fillet, cut into pieces (2 cm)
  • ½ tsp fine salt, adjust to taste
  • 1 tsp ground allspice
  • 60 g olive oil
  • 8 sheets brick pastry

Preparation

  1. Place almonds and sugar into mixing bowl and chop 8 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place parsley into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place onions into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add butter, ½ tsp salt, ½ tsp pepper, saffron, 1 tsp ground cinnamon and sauté 8 min/Varoma/speed  without measuring cup.
  5. Add half of chopped parsley and mix 10 sec//speed 3. Transfer ¼ of onion-spice mixture to a bowl and set aside.
  6. Add eggs and mix 30 sec/speed 4. Transfer onion-egg filling to a bowl and set aside.
  7. Prepare chicken filling: place reserved onion-spice mixture, remaining chopped parsley, chicken, ½ tsp salt, ½ tsp pepper, 1 tsp cinnamon and allspice into mixing bowl and sauté 4 min/Varoma//speed  without measuring cup.
  8. Preheat the oven to 160°C. Brush a sheet of baking paper with olive oil using a pastry brush and use it to line a round springform tin (Ø 22 cm). Line the prepared tin with 3 sheets of brick pastry, overlapping them and allowing them to overhang over the sides of the tin, then distribute reserved onion-egg filling. Cover with 2 sheets of brick pastry and distribute chicken filling on top. Cover with 2 more sheets of brick pastry and spread with almonds and sugar. Bring overhanging edges of pastry sheets to the middle. Cover with last sheet of brickpastry, tucking it into sides of pastilla.
  9. Brush top generously with oil and bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes (160°C). Turn pastilla out onto a shallow baking dish, remove baking paper and brush other side generously with oil and return to oven for 20 minutes. Serve hot, with almond layer facing up.

Useful Items

baking paper, pastry brush, bowl, springform tin (Ø 22 cm), baking dish, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • In Morocco, pastilla is served dusted with icing sugar.
  • Pigeon pastilla is the most traditional version of this meat pie. Substitute the chicken in this recipe with pigeon meat, skin and bones removed.
