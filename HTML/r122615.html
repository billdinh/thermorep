Hot turmeric apple cider

Ingredients

  • 1000 g green apples, cored and cut into quarters
  • 1 orange, juice only
  • 1 cinnamon quill, broken into pieces
  • 10 g fresh turmeric, peeled and cut into slices
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
619 kJ / 147 kcal
Protein
1 g
Carbohydrates
32.2 g
Fat
0.3 g

Preparation

  1. Place apples and orange juice into mixing bowl and chop 40 sec/speed 9 or until pulped.
  2. Place a sieve over a glass bowl or large jug and line with a nut milk bag or muslin cloth. Pour mixture into lined sieve, squeezing to extract as much juice as possible. Discard pulp. Rinse mixing bowl.
  3. Place strained juice, cinnamon quill and turmeric into mixing bowl and heat 10 min/70°C//speed 1, until spices have infused and juice has warmed through. Using a fine mesh sieve, strain mixture into glasses to serve.

Useful Items

sieve, glass bowl or large jug, nut milk bag or muslin cloth, serving jug or punch bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • You can use green or red apples for this cider, or a mix of both. Red apples typically produce a sweeter result.
  • If you’re making this cider for a special occasion, you might like to add 1-2 tbsp ginger wine, brandy or spiced rum after heating. Warm for a further 2 min/70°C//speed 1 after adding alcohol.
