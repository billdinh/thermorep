Date and Orange Jam

Ingredients

  • 500 g oranges, peeled and quartered
  • 1 lemon, peeled and quartered
  • 500 g pitted dates
  • 200 g raw cane granulated sugar
  • 100 g water

Preparation

  1. Place oranges, lemon and dates in mixing bowl then chop 10 sec/speed 7.
  2. Add sugar and water. Place simmering bakset instead of measuring cup on mixing bowl lid (allowing the jam to reduce slightly while cooking) then cook 25 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Test for setting point (see Tip) then pour into sterilized jars. Turn jars upside down for 10 minutes before turning right side up and allowing to cool. Serve or store in a cool, dry place.

Useful Items

sterilized jam jars

Hints & Tricks

  • Cooking time will vary according to juiciness of the oranges.
  • Setting point test: place a saucer in the freezer (5-10 minutes). Place 1 tsp jam onto the saucer. When cool, push jam with your finger. If it wrinkles, it is ready. If not, boil jam for a further 2 min/110°C/speed 3, then test again.
