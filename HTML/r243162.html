Gorditas de Chicharrón

Ingredients

Salsa

  • 12 oz green tomatillos, halved
  • 4 oz white onions, cut into pieces
  • 1 garlic clove
  • ½ oz dried 'chile de árbol' chilies, stems removed
  • 4 sprigs cilantro
  • ½ tsp salt, to taste

Dough

  • 24 ½ oz water
  • 14 oz masa harina
  • 1 tsp salt

Filling

  • ½ oz dried guajillo chilies, stems and seeds removed
  • 1 whole clove
  • 1 allspice berry
  • 12 oz green tomatillos, halved
  • 2 ½ oz white onions, cut into pieces
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • ½ oz vegetable oil
  • 1 sprig fresh thyme
  • 1 sprigs fresh marjoram
  • 1 sprig fresh oregano
  • 7 oz water
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 8 oz chicharrones (pork cracklins), crushed or cut into small pieces
  • vegetable oil, to grease

Preparation

Salsa

  1. Place tomatillos, onions, garlic, 'chile de árbol', cilantro and salt into mixing bowl and chop 30 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and refrigerate until needed. Clean mixing bowl.

Dough

  1. Place water into mixing bowl and heat 2 min/120°F/speed 2.
  2. Add masa harina and salt and knead 3 min/. Transfer to a bowl, cover with cling wrap and refrigerate until needed. Clean mixing bowl.

Filling

  1. Place guajillo, clove, and allspice into mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 9.
  2. Add tomatillos, onions, garlic, oil, thyme, marjoram and oregano and chop 5 sec/speed 5, then cook 15 min/250°F/speed .
  3. Add water and salt and mix 1 min/speed 5-10 increasing speed gradually. Add chicharrónes, stir through with spatula to coat and cook 24 min/212°F/speed 1.
  4. Divide reserved dough into 12 equal pieces (approx. 3 oz ea.). Working in batches, roll dough into balls and press into a circle (approx. 5 in.) with a tortilla press or between two pieces of cling wrap. Place 1 heaping tbsp (approx. 2 oz) reserved chicharrónes filling in the center and gather the edges towards the top to create a parcel. Pinch and seal the edges, while at the same time applying gentle and even downward pressure, returning it to a smaller disk shape (approx. 3½ in.). Repeat with remaining ingredients.
  5. Heat 1-2 tsp oil in a large cast iron pan or heat a comal over medium heat and cook gorditas in batches until dough is cooked through, golden and crispy (approx. 5 min. per side). Drain on paper towels. Serve hot with reserved Salsa.

Useful Items

tortilla press or rolling pin, comal or shallow cast iron pan (12 in.), cling wrap, paper towels, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Garnish Gorditas de Chicharróne with crumbled cotija cheese, Mexican crema, shredded lettuce and/or chopped cilantro.
  • Chicharrónes can be ground in the mixing bowl before starting recipe: place 8 oz chicharrónes into mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  • Pan fry gorditas in hot oil (360°F-370°F) until cooked through and browned (approx. 3-4 min. per side). Drain on paper towels and serve hot.
