Freekeh pilaf with broad beans

Ingredients

  • 5 sprigs fresh coriander, roughly chopped
  • 5 sprigs fresh dill, leaves only
  • 1200 g water
  • 200 g fresh broad beans
  • 300 g freekeh (see tip)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 100 g onions, cut into quarters
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 2 tsp vegetable stock paste, homemade
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper
  • 50 g lemon juice, freshly squeezed
  • 20 g unsalted pistachio nuts, roasted, finely chopped for garnish

Preparation

  1. Place coriander and dill into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place 500 g water into mixing bowl. Set simmering basket on mixing bowl lid, weigh broad beans in it and rinse under cold running water. Insert simmering basket and steam 10 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove basket with the aid of spatula, transfer beans into a bowl and allow them to cool. Empty mixing bowl.
  3. Set simmering basket on mixing bowl lid, weigh freekeh in it, rinse under cold running water and set aside.
  4. Place garlic, onions and olive oil into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5, scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Cook 3 min/120°C/speed 2. Meanwhile remove skin from beans and set aside.
  6. Add freekeh into mixing bowl and cook 3 min/100°C/speed 2.
  7. Add remaining 700 g water, vegetable stock paste, salt and pepper and cook 20 min/100°C//speed 1 or until al dente. Transfer to a deep serving dish, add lemon juice, reserved herbs and peeled broad beans, mix gently and adjust seasoning if needed. Allow to rest for a few minutes to absorb the liquid and serve warm sprinkled with chopped pistachio nuts.

Hints & Tricks

  • The beans come out of their skin easily if you break the skin and press them gently between forefinger and thumb.
  • For a complete meal accompany this dish with meat or fish.
  • Freekeh (also called farik) is wheat harvested while young and green and undergoes a roasting process. It can be replaced with other whole grains like quinoa, brown rice, spelt or bulgur, in this case adjust cooking time accordingly.
