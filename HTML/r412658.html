Pull-Apart Dinner Rolls

Ingredients

  • 2 oz unsalted butter, plus extra to grease
  • 17 oz all-purpose flour, plus extra to dust
  • 2 ¼ tsp dried instant yeast (1 pkg)
  • 4 oz water, plus 1 tsp for egg wash
  • ¾ tsp salt
  • 1 oz sugar
  • 4 oz plain Greek yogurt
  • 3 large eggs, divided

Preparation

  1. Line a baking dish (9 in. x 13 in.) with parchment and grease a large bowl and set aside.
  2. Place flour, yeast, 4 oz water, salt, sugar, yogurt, 2 eggs, and butter into mixing bowl and mix 5 sec/speed 5. Knead 3 min/.
  3. Place dough into prepared bowl, cover with cling wrap and let rise until doubled in size (approx. 1 hr.). Punch down dough and reshape into a ball.
  4. Transfer dough ball to a lightly floured work surface and cut into 12 equal pieces. Roll into balls and place in prepared baking dish. Cover with cling wrap and allow to rise 40 minutes. Meanwhile, combine 1 egg and 1 tsp water in a small bowl, whisk to combine and set aside. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  5. Brush tops of rolls with egg wash and bake for 20 minutes (375°F) until golden brown. Allow to cool 5 minutes before serving.

Useful Items

parchment paper, large bowl, cling wrap, baking dish (9 in. x 13 in.), pastry brush, oven
