Aubergine Parmigiana - Parmigiana di melanzane

Ingredients

  • 150 g Parmesan cheese, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 60 g bread, torn in pieces
  • 2 - 3 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves only
  • 200 g mozzarella, cut in pieces
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 150 g onions, quartered
  • 15 g olive oil
  • 1000 g aubergines, sliced lengthways (1 cm) (approx. 3 aubergines)
  • 800 g tinned chopped tomatoes (2 x 400 g tins)
  • 10 fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt

Preparation

  1. Place Parmesan in mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place bread and thyme in mixing bowl then grate 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place mozzarella in mixing bowl and grate Turbo/1 sec/1 time. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place garlic, onions and oil in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then sauté 5 min/120°C/speed 1. Meanwhile, arrange aubergine slices in overlapping layers in Varoma dish and tray, leaving some holes uncovered for steam to circulate, then set aside.
  5. Add tomatoes, basil and salt to mixing bowl. Place covered Varoma into position then cook 20 min/Varoma/speed 1. Towards the end of this time, preheat oven to 200°C.
  6. Cover the base of a baking dish (30 cm x 24 cm) with ⅓ tomato sauce, sprinkle over ½ grated Parmesan and then add a layer of ½ the steamed aubergine slices. Scatter over reserved grated mozzarella, then a layer of ⅓ tomato sauce. Lay remaining aubergine slices on top, then a layer of remaining tomato sauce. Scatter remaining Parmesan and reserved breadcrumbs over the top.
  7. Bake for 15-18 minutes (200°C) until topping is golden and sauce is bubbling. Serve hot.

Useful Items

baking dish (30 cm x 24 cm), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • To make this recipe suitable for vegetarians, substitute the Parmesan cheese for a vegetarian hard cheese.
  • A classic dish of southern Italy, while this way of preparing aubergine is associated largely with Naples, it is also popular across Calabria and Sicily. Our version of this delicious dish features aubergines that are sliced and then steamed in the Varoma. Once cooked, the aubergines are layered with a flavourful tomato sauce and plenty of mozzarella cheese and grated Parmesan, before being baked until hot and bubbling. Serve accompanied with a green salad.
