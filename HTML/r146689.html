Whole Orange Cake with Spiced Syrup

Ingredients

Whole Orange Cake

  • 200 g unsalted butter, diced, plus extra for greasing
  • 1 orange, washed, unpeeled and quartered
  • 3 medium eggs
  • 200 g Rapadura sugar
  • 300 g self-raising flour
  • 20 g orange juice

Spiced Syrup

  • 160 g water
  • 180 g Rapadura sugar
  • 2 tsp honey
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • ½ tsp ground cardamom
  • ½ tsp fennel seeds
  • 1 star anise
  • 1 orange, zest only, no white pith

Preparation

Whole Orange Cake

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Grease and line a spring form tin (20 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place butter and orange in mixing bowl then blend 15 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then blend again 5 sec/speed 8.
  3. Add all remaining cake ingredients and mix 10 sec/speed 5. Mix contents of mixing bowl well with spatula, then mix again 5 sec/speed 5.
  4. Pour mixture into prepared tin, then bake for 50-60 minutes (180°C), or until a skewer inserted in centre comes out clean.
  5. Allow cake to cool (approx. 45 minutes), then remove from tin and set aside.

Spiced Syrup

  1. Place all syrup ingredients in mixing bowl and cook 9 min/100°C/speed 2. Allow to cool, then strain through a fine sieve and set aside until required.
  2. Using a skewer, poke holes in the top of the cake and pour ⅓ of the spiced syrup over the top. Allow cake to absorb syrup for a few minutes, then slice and serve.

Useful Items

spring form tin (Ø 20 cm), baking paper, skewer, fine sieve, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve extra syrup in a jug or poured over pancakes, vanilla bean ice cream or mascarpone cheese another day.
  • As the whole orange is used in this recipe, look for unwaxed, organic oranges. If you cannot get these, ensure you scrub the outside of your oranges well to remove as much residue as possible from the zest.
