Chocolate Crunch Bars

Ingredients

Crust

  • 4 oz graham crackers, broken into pieces
  • 3 oz unsalted butter, cubed
  • 2 oz semi-sweet chocolate chips

Topping

  • 3 oz graham crackers, broken into pieces
  • 4 oz unsalted butter, cubed
  • 8 oz brown sugar
  • 4 oz whole milk
  • 4 oz hazelnut and chocolate spread (Nutella®)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 oz unsweetened coconut flakes
  • 8 oz quick cooking oats
  • 4 oz sliced almonds

Chocolate Layer

  • 10 oz semi-sweet chocolate chips

Preparation

Crust

  1. Line a square baking pan (8 in. x 8 in.) with parchment, letting edges hang over sides of pan.
  2. Place graham crackers, butter and chocolate chips into mixing bowl and grind 5 sec/speed 8. Press into the bottom of prepared pan and refrigerate.

Topping

  1. Place graham crackers into mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place butter, brown sugar and milk into mixing bowl and cook 5 min/195°F/speed 1.
  3. Add reserved ground graham crackers, hazelnut spread, vanilla extract, coconut, oats and almonds and mix 10 sec//speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.

Chocolate Layer

  1. Place chocolate chips into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and melt 6 min/110°F/speed 1. Spread melted chocolate evenly over crust and refrigerate until set.
  2. Spread reserved topping over chocolate layer and refrigerate until set. Remove from pan using the parchment to lift out the bars. Cut Chocolate Crunch Bars into 36 squares and serve.

Useful Items

square baking pan (8 in. x 8 in.), parchment paper, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Make your own Nutella® following the recipe for Chocolate Hazelnut Spread in The Basic Cookbook.
