Iraqi cheese pies (Cheese chureck)

Ingredients

Dough

  • 50 g water
  • 170 g salted butter, cut into pieces, plus extra for greasing
  • 180 g milk
  • 2 tsp white sugar
  • 3 tsp dried instant yeast
  • 2 eggs
  • 650 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 1 tsp salt

Cheese filling

  • 200 g feta cheese, cut into cubes (2 cm)
  • 180 g haloumi, cut into cubes (2 cm)
  • 250 g mozzarella, cut into cubes (2 cm)

Assembly

  • 1 tbsp water, plus extra for sealing
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds

Preparation

Dough

  1. Place water, butter, milk, sugar and yeast into mixing bowl and mix 2 min/37°C/speed 3.
  2. Mix 30 sec/speed 3, adding 2 eggs, one at a time, through hole in mixing bowl lid.
  3. Add flour and salt and knead 3 min/. Lightly grease a large bowl with butter and set aside. Place dough into prepared bowl and shape into a ball. Cover bowl with plastic wrap or a damp kitchen towel and set aside to prove in a warm place until doubled in size (approx. 1 hour).
  4. Preheat oven to 180°C. Line 2 baking trays (approx. 40 x 35 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  5. Transfer reserved dough onto a lightly floured work surface and knead briefly with your hands until combined. Divide into 3 equal portions (approx. 360 g each). Divide each portion into 4 equal portions (approx. 90 g each), then set aside, keeping them covered with a damp kitchen towel while making filling. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Cheese filling

  1. Place feta, haloumi and mozzarella into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 5, stirring with aid of spatula. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.

Assembly

  1. Using a rolling pin, roll out each piece of dough into a rectangle (approx. 30 x 10 cm). Place approx. 3 tablespoons of the cheese filling along the bottom of the long side of the dough. Brush opposite side of the rectangle with a little water and roll up dough into a log, enclosing filling. Press lightly at the edges to seal. Pull edges of dough log around, to create a ring shape, then transfer onto prepared baking trays, seam side down.
  2. Crack egg into a small bowl, add 1 tablespoon water and beat lightly with a fork. Brush tops of cheese pies with egg mixture, sprinkle with sesame seeds and bake for 20 minutes (180°C) or until golden. Transfer to a wire rack to cool slightly before serving.

Useful Items

large bowl, plastic wrap or kitchen towel, baking paper, wire rack, baking trays (40 x 35 cm), rolling pin, pastry brush, kitchen towel, bowl, small bowl, fork, oven
