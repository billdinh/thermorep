Lamb’s lettuce cream soup, Broccoli with olive sauce

Ingredients

Black olive sauce

  • 200 g black olives, pitted
  • 40 g fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 40 g capers, pickled, drained
  • 10 g lemon juice
  • 70 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 pinches salt

Lettuce cream soup and steamed broccoli

  • 80 g onions, cut in halves,
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 20 g butter
  • 600 g broccoli, cut in florets (5 cm)
  • 2 pinches salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 150 g potatoes, cut in pieces (3 cm)
  • 1 tsp vegetable stock paste, homemade
  • 600 g water
  • 200 g lamb's lettuce
  • 80 g cream
  • 1 pinch sugar
  • ½ tsp lemon juice
  • ¼ tsp Worcestershire sauce

Preparation

Black olive sauce

  1. Place black olives, parsley, garlic cloves, capers, lemon juice, extra virgin olive oil and salt in mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.

Lettuce cream soup and steamed broccoli

  1. Place onions and garlic clove in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl using spatula.
  2. Add butter and sauté 5 min/120°C/speed 1. Meanwhile, place broccoli in Varoma dish, season with salt and ¼ tsp black pepper and set aside.
  3. Add potatoes to mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5.
  4. Add ¼ tsp black pepper, vegetable stock paste and water, set Varoma in position and cook 20 min/Varoma/speed 1. Set Varoma aside.
  5. Add lamb’s lettuce, cream, sugar, lemon juice and Worcestershire sauce to mixing bowl and blend 2 min/speed 5-10, increasing speed gradually. Adjust seasoning to taste. Serve soup as a starter and broccoli with olive sauce as a side dish, along with a main dish of your choice.
