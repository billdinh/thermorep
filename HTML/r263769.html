Fruit and Sago Pudding

Ingredients

  • 1500 g water
  • 100 g sago pearls
  • 300 g coconut cream
  • 400 g milk
  • 40 g watermelon flesh, diced (1 cm)
  • 40 g dragon fruit, peeled, diced (1 cm)
  • 40 g mango, ripe, peeled, diced (1 cm)
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
844.6 kJ / 201.9 kcal
Protein
4.9 g
Carbohydrates
16.3 g
Fat
13.2 g

Preparation

  1. Place water in mixing bowl and bring to the boil 15 min/100°C/speed 1.
  2. Cook 25 min/100°C/speed 1 while slowly pouring sago pearls through hole in mixing bowl lid, then place measuring cup into position.
  3. Drain sago pearls using Varoma dish and rinse with cold water to wash away the stickiness. The sago pearls should now be transparent pearls.
  4. Transfer to serving bowls and stir in coconut cream and milk. Serve with watermelon, dragon fruit and mango on top.
