Tomato Risotto with Lemon Ricotta Stuffed Chicken Breast

Ingredients

Ricotta Stuffed Chicken

  • 40 g Parmesan cheese, cut in pieces
  • 15 g fresh flat-leaf parsley
  • 15 g fresh mint
  • 1 tbsp lemon zest
  • 200 g ricotta cheese, chilled
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 6 chicken breasts, skinless

Tomato Risotto

  • 1 shallot
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 15 g fresh flat-leaf parsley
  • 40 g olive oil
  • 300 g risotto rice
  • 200 g ripe tomatoes, quartered
  • 60 g dry white wine
  • 500 g water
  • 1 heaped tbsp vegetable stock paste, homemade
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 20 g tomato purée (concentrated)
  • 30 g butter

Preparation

Ricotta Stuffed Chicken

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Line a baking tray with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place Parmesan cheese in mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place parsley, mint and lemon zest in mixing bowl then chop 10 sec/speed 7. Transfer half of the parsley-mint mixture to a bowl for garnish.
  4. Add ricotta, salt and black pepper to remaining mixture in mixing bowl then mix 20 sec/speed 2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Mix again 10 sec/speed 2.
  6. Halve chicken breasts horizontally. Place one half of each of the chicken breasts in the prepared baking tray. Place approx. 1 tbsp ricotta mixture onto each, dividing equally between chicken breasts. Top each with the other half of the chicken breast and secure with toothpicks. Roast for 20 minutes (180°C). Meanwhile clean and dry mixing bowl, then continue with recipe.

Tomato Risotto

  1. Place shallot, garlic clove, parsley and olive oil in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  3. Add risotto rice and tomatoes then sauté without measuring cup 3 min/120°C//speed 1. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add white wine and cook without measuring cup 1 min/100°C//speed 1.
  5. Add water, vegetable stock paste, salt, black pepper and tomato purée. Scrape bottom of bowl with spatula to loosen rice and cook 12-13 min/100°C//speed 1.
  6. Add butter and reserved grated Parmesan cheese then stir using spatula. Allow to rest in mixing bowl for 2 minutes.
  7. Garnish the stuffed chicken with reserved chopped parsley-mint mixture and serve with risotto immediately.
