Molten Caramel Centre Muffins

Ingredients

  • 55 g butter, soft, plus extra for greasing
  • 20 g plain flour, plus extra for flouring
  • 150 g dulce de leche
  • 1 egg yolk, from medium egg
  • 1 medium egg
  • 10 g caster sugar
  • ¼ tsp ground cinnamon
  • ½ tsp natural vanilla extract
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
2425 kJ / 580 kcal

11.3 g

32.2 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C. Grease and flour 2 soufflé moulds or ramekins then place on a baking tray.
  2. Place butter and dulce de leche in mixing bowl then heat 3 min/60°C/speed 1.
  3. Insert butterfly whisk. Add egg yolk, egg and sugar then whisk 2 min/speed 4.
  4. Add cinnamon, vanilla extract and flour then mix 1 min/speed 4. Remove butterfly whisk. Pour into prepared soufflé moulds then bake for 10 minutes (220°C). Serve immediately in dishes or turned out onto plates.

Useful Items

2 soufflé moulds or ramekins, baking tray, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Any type of caramel sauce works well, such as salted caramel sauce.
  • Do not be tempted to bake for longer than 10 minutes otherwise the centres will not be molten.
