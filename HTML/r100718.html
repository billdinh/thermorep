Ham and Onion Bread

Ingredients

  • 100 g Emmental, cut in pieces
  • 250 g curd cheese (quark)
  • 120 g milk
  • 100 g olive oil
  • 15 g sugar
  • 300 g strong white bread flour
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 100 g dried onions
  • 100 g bacon cubes
  • 2 Tbsp plain yoghurt
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
891 kJ / 213 kcal
Protein
8 g
Carbohydrates
18 g
Fat
12 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C and line a large baking tray with baking paper.
  2. Place Emmental in mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 6.
  3. Add curd cheese, milk, oil, sugar, flour, salt and baking powder then mix 30 sec/.
  4. Add dried onions and bacon then mix 20 sec//speed 2 while stirring with spatula through hole in mixing bowl lid.
  5. Divide dough into 16 equal-sized pieces, shape into rolls and place on prepared baking tray. Divide plain yoghurt evenly over the tops of the rolls then bake for 22 minutes (200°C). Serve warm or cold.

Useful Items

baking tray and paper, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with a salad for a tasty lunch.
