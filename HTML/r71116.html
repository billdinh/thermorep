Wholefood pancakes with vanilla and cinnamon poached apples

Ingredients

Vanilla and cinnamon poached apples

  • 1000 g water
  • 3 cinnamon quills
  • 2 vanilla bean pods, split lengthways, (seeds and pods)
  • 250 g coconut sugar
  • 400 g green apples, peeled, cores removed and cut into eighths

Wholefood pancakes

  • 100 g brown rice
  • 100 g buckwheat groats
  • 2 bananas, peeled and chopped (approx. 190 g)
  • 1 egg
  • 300 g milk of choice (e.g. cow's milk, rice milk)
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • butter or oil, for frying

Preparation

Vanilla and cinnamon poached apples

  1. Place all poached apple ingredients, including vanilla pods and seeds, into mixing bowl and cook 10 min/100°C//speed .
  2. Mix contents of mixing bowl well with spatula, then cook for a further 10 min/100°C//speed . Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Wholefood pancakes

  1. Place rice and buckwheat into mixing bowl and mill 1 min/speed 9.
  2. Add bananas and egg and mix 14 sec/speed 4.
  3. Add milk and baking powder and mix 30 sec/speed 4.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then mix for a further 6 sec/speed 6. Transfer batter into a bowl and place in refrigerator for 30 minutes.
  5. Remove pancake batter from refrigerator and mix with spatula. Place a medium-sized non-stick frying pan over medium-high heat and add a little butter or oil. Pour in a ladle of pancake mixture (approx. ¼ cup). Cook for 3 minutes, or until bubbles form on top of pancake then turn and cook for a further 3 minutes or until browned. Set pancake aside and cover to keep warm.
  6. Repeat with remaining batter to make approx. 16 pancakes, mixing the batter occasionally with spatula to keep batter smooth.
  7. Remove vanilla bean pods and cinnamon quills from reserved poached apples. Serve pancakes hot topped with poached apples.

Useful Items

medium-sized non-stick frying pan, ladle, bowl, refrigerator, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • For a decadent dessert, serve pancakes with blueberries and fresh cream or raspberries and vanilla bean ice cream.
  • For a lactose free version, ensure you use grapeseed oil rather than butter for frying and a dairy free milk, such as nut or coconut milk.
