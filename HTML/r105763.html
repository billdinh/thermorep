Vegetable chilli

Ingredients

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 120 g onion, cut into halves
  • 1 long red chilli, cut into halves and deseeded if preferred
  • 20 g vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp ground coriander
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 400 g canned tomatoes
  • 200 g water
  • 1 tsp white sugar
  • 350 g pumpkin, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 200 g cauliflower florets
  • 1 red capsicum, deseeded and cut into thin slices
  • ½ green capsicum, deseeded and cut into thin slices
  • 250 g zucchini, cut into slices (1 cm)
  • 400 g canned black beans
  • 100 g sour cream
  • 1 avocado, cut into pieces (1 cm)
  • 3 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only

Preparation

  1. Place garlic, onion and chilli into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add oil, cumin, coriander, cinnamon, salt and pepper and cook 3 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  3. Add tomatoes, water and sugar. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh pumpkin and cauliflower into it. Add capsicum on top. Insert Varoma tray and weigh zucchini slices onto it. Secure Varoma lid and steam 15-18 min/Varoma/speed 1, until vegetables are just tender. Remove Varoma and set aside.
  4. Add beans and cooked vegetables to mixing bowl and cook 5 min/Varoma//speed 2, or until cooked to your liking. Divide amongst serving bowls and top with sour cream, avocado and coriander. Serve immediately.
