Chard in Parmesan Broth with Tortellini

Ingredients

  • 4 oz yellow onion, in pieces (1 in.)
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil, divided
  • 5 sprigs thyme, leaves only
  • 5 sprigs parsley, leaves only
  • 1 dried bay leaf
  • 1 tsp black peppercorns
  • 24 oz vegetable stock
  • 8 oz dry white wine
  • 6 oz Parmesan cheese rinds, in pieces (2 in.)
  • 20 oz tortellini, store bought, any type
  • 3 oz rainbow chard, stems removed and chopped, greens in pieces (1 in.)
  • Parmesan cheese, shaved, to garnish

Preparation

  1. Line simmering basket with parchment and set aside.
  2. Place onion, garlic and 1 tbsp olive oil into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add thyme, parsley, bay leaf and peppercorns and without measuring cup sauté 5 min/250°F//speed .
  4. Add vegetable stock and white wine and without measuring cup cook 8 min/212°F//speed 1.
  5. Insert prepared simmering basket and weigh in Parmesan cheese. Set Varoma dish into position, weigh in tortellini and 1 tbsp olive oil and toss to coat, secure Varoma lid and steam for time indicated on pasta package/Varoma//speed , or until al dente. Meanwhile add chard greens to Varoma in last 2 minutes of steaming.
  6. Remove Varoma and set aside. Remove simmering basket and discard cheese rinds. Divide tortellini and chard greens between bowls, pour in broth and discard bay leaf. Garnish with chopped chard stems and shaved Parmesan and serve immediately.

Hints & Tricks

  • Replace Parmesan rinds with 2 inch chunks of Parmesan.
