Cereal Bread

Ingredients

  • 13 ½ oz water, plus extra to fill pan
  • 2 ¼ tsp active dry yeast
  • 17 ½ oz bread flour
  • 2 oz mixed seeds, e.g, sunflower, flax or sesame seeds
  • ½ oz poppy seeds
  • 2 heaping tsp salt
  • whole milk, to brush

Preparation

  1. Place water and yeast into mixing bowl and warm 2 min/100°F/speed 1.
  2. Add flour, mixed seeds, poppy seeds and salt and knead 5 min/.
  3. Transfer to a lightly floured surface, shape into a smooth ball and place into a large bowl. Cover with cling wrap and let rise in a warm place until doubled in size (approx. 1-2 hrs.).
  4. Punch down dough and transfer to a lightly floured surface, shape into a smooth ball and transfer to parchment. Cover with a kitchen towel and let rise in a warm place until doubled in size (approx. 1-2 hrs.).
  5. At the end of the second rise, place a baking sheet into the oven and place a small pan of water on the bottom of the oven. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  6. Score the loaf with a sharp knife, brush with milk and carefully slide the loaf with the parchment onto the hot baking sheet and bake for 40 minutes (400°F.). Allow to cool before slicing.

Useful Items

cling wrap, kitchen towel, parchment paper, baking sheet, sharp knife, pastry brush, large bowl, pan, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • After brushing loaf with milk, sprinkle on additional seeds and nuts.
