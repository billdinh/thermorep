Berry halva semifreddo

Ingredients

  • 400 g coconut cream (see tip)
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds
  • 70 g unsalted pistachio nuts
  • 150 g tahini paste
  • 120 g honey, adjust to taste
  • 2 tsp natural vanilla extract
  • 150 g Greek yoghurt, strained
  • 180 g mixed frozen red berries (e.g. raspberries, strawberries, blueberries)
  • 65 g pomegranate seeds, to garnish

Preparation

  1. Place coconut cream in the freezer for one hour to chill (see tip).
  2. Place sesame seeds into mixing bowl and dry roast 5 min/120°C/speed 2. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place pistachio nuts into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7, transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place tahini paste, chilled coconut cream, 70 g honey and vanilla into mixing bowl and mix 2 min/speed 4.
  5. Insert butterfly whisk. Add 35 g chopped pistachio nuts and yoghurt and mix 15 sec/speed 3. Remove butterfly whisk. Transfer the mixture into a round silicone mould of 1 lt capacity.
  6. Place frozen berries and remaining 50 g honey into mixing bowl, mix 30 sec/speed 6, with aid of spatula.
  7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix again 30 sec/speed 6, with aid of spatula.
  8. Add the berry mixture to silicone mould with halva mixture and swirl with a fork to create a marble effect. Cover and freeze for at least 3 hours or overnight.
  9. Remove semifreddo from the freezer, reverse into a serving plate and garnish with reserved chopped pistachio nuts, roasted sesame seeds and pomegranate seeds. Refrigerate until soft enough to slice and serve.

Useful Items

serving plate, half-sphere silicone mould (Ø 20 cm, 1 l), freezer, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Use 100 % natural coconut cream; coconut cream that has emulsifying agents added may cause the mixture to separate into creamy and watery layers.
  • Do not prolong the freezing time or the coconut cream will become difficult to handle.
