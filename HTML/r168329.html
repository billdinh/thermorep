Banana Liqueur Pudding

Ingredients

  • 3 oz boiling water
  • 1 tbsp gelatin powder, unflavored
  • 1 vanilla bean
  • 2 oz sugar
  • 1 oz cornstarch
  • 20 oz whole milk
  • 3 egg yolks, from large eggs
  • 1 oz unsalted butter, cubed (1 in.)
  • 1 oz banana liqueur
  • 6 oz heavy whipping cream
  • 4 bananas, sliced, to serve

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl on top of mixing bowl lid and weigh in boiling water and add gelatin powder, stir thoroughly until dissolved and set aside.
  2. Place vanilla bean, sugar and cornstarch into mixing bowl and grind 40 sec/speed 10.
  3. Add milk and egg yolks and cook 15 min/195Â°F/speed 2.
  4. Add reserved gelatin, butter and liqueur and mix 20 sec/speed 6.
  5. Transfer to a bowl and refrigerate until set (approx. 4-8 hr.).
  6. Insert butterfly whisk. Place whipping cream into mixing bowl and without setting a time whip speed 3.5 until desired stiffness is achieved, watching carefully to avoid over-whipping. Divide chilled pudding into individual dessert bowls, alternating with layers of whipped cream and sliced bananas. Serve cold.

Hints & Tricks

  • Replace gelatin powder with 3 gelatin sheets (silver) and 5 oz of cold water. Soak for 5 minutes. Squeeze out as much water as possible and add squeezed gelatin with the butter and liqueur.
  • Personalize by substituting fruit and liqueur options to taste.
