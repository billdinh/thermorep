Layered lamb curry

Ingredients

Sri Lankan curry powder

  • 25 g coriander seeds
  • 10 g cumin seeds
  • ¾ tsp fenugreek seeds
  • 1 tsp fennel seeds
  • ½ cinnamon quill
  • ¼ tsp whole cloves
  • ¼ tsp cardamom seeds (see Tips)
  • ¼ tsp black mustard seeds
  • ½ tsp black peppercorns
  • 2 sprigs fresh curry leaves

Lamb curry

  • 350 - 400 g lamb backstrap
  • 200 g tri-colour quinoa
  • 400 g water, plus extra for soaking
  • 360 g brown onions, cut into quarters
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 20 g coconut oil
  • 1 - 2 dried red chillies, trimmed and cut into halves
  • 4 cardamom pods, crushed
  • 1 sprig fresh curry leaves, leaves only
  • ½ tsp paprika
  • ¼ - 1 tsp chilli powder (optional)
  • ¼ tsp ground turmeric
  • 2 stalks fresh lemongrass, bruised and cut into halves
  • salt, to taste
  • 350 g pumpkin, peeled and cut into pieces (2 cm)
  • 200 g cherry tomatoes, cut into halves
  • 100 g frozen baby peas
  • 270 g coconut milk

Preparation

Sri Lankan curry powder

  1. Place coriander seeds, cumin seeds, fenugreek seeds and fennel seeds into mixing bowl and dry roast 3 min/Varoma/speed 2. Allow to cool for 5 minutes.
  2. Add remaining curry powder ingredients and mill 1 min/speed 10 or until a fine powder. Transfer into a sealable container until ready to use.

Lamb curry

  1. Place Varoma into position, insert Varoma tray and weigh lamb onto it. Rub 1-2 tablespoons of the reserved curry powder over it. Set aside.
  2. Insert simmering basket and weigh quinoa into it. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and rest in a large bowl. Cover with water and allow to soak for 5 minutes. Rinse under running water until water runs clear.
  3. Place onions, garlic and ginger into mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add oil and heat 5 min/100°C/speed 1.
  5. Add chillies, cardamom pods, curry leaves, paprika, chilli powder, turmeric and 3-4 teaspoons of the reserved curry powder and cook 1 min 30 sec/100°C/speed 1.
  6. Add water, lemongrass and salt. Insert simmering basket with quinoa into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position, weigh pumpkin into it, secure Varoma lid and cook 5 min/Varoma//speed 1.
  7. Insert Varoma tray with lamb, secure Varoma lid and cook 15-17 min/Varoma//speed 1 or until lamb is cooked to your liking. Remove Varoma and set aside. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and set quinoa aside.
  8. Add cherry tomatoes, peas and coconut milk to mixing bowl and cook 3 min/90°C//speed 2. Remove and discard lemongrass. Cut lamb into thick slices. Serve quinoa, pumpkin and lamb with curry sauce.

Useful Items

sealable container, large bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • You can buy cardamom seeds from Indian food stores or you can bruise cardamom pods and remove the seeds.
  • You can store the Sri Lankan curry powder in a sealable container in the pantry for up to 3 months.
