Meatballs with Romesco Sauce

Ingredients

Romesco Sauce

  • 6 ½ oz red bell pepper, deseeded and quartered (approx. 1 lg. pepper)
  • 4 ½ oz fresh tomato, halved (approx. 1 lg. tomato)
  • 4 oz red onion, unpeeled (½ lg. onion)
  • 2 garlic cloves, unpeeled
  • 2 oz blanched almonds
  • ½ oz sherry (optional)
  • ¼ tsp smoked paprika
  • 2 oz olive oil
  • ½ oz red wine vinegar
  • ¼ tsp sea salt, to taste
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper, to taste
  • 2 tsp dried chili flakes

Meatballs

  • 8 oz Italian sausage, casing removed, cut into pieces
  • 4 oz red onion (approx. ½ lg. onion)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 3 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 1 oz stale bread, torn into pieces (approx. 1 slice)
  • ¼ tsp ground nutmeg
  • ¼ tsp ground fennel
  • ¼ tsp smoked paprika
  • ¼ tsp sea salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 large egg
  • 16 oz ground beef
  • 1 oz olive oil, to grease

Preparation

Romesco Sauce

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Line a baking pan with parchment. Place bell pepper, tomato, red onion and garlic onto prepared pan and roast for 20-25 minutes (400°F) or until vegetables are softened and lightly blackened.
  2. Transfer roasted vegetables immediately into a ceramic or glass bowl and cover tightly with cling wrap. Leave to stand for 10 minutes. The steam created in the bowl will loosen vegetable skins making them easier to peel.
  3. Remove roasted vegetables from bowl and transfer onto paper towels to drain. Gently peel vegetables and discard skins. Transfer peeled vegetables back into bowl and set aside.
  4. Place blanched almonds into mixing bowl and without measuring cup roast 8 min/Varoma/speed 1. Set aside in mixing bowl to cool (approx. 5 min.).
  5. Add all remaining Romesco Sauce ingredients, including reserved peeled vegetables, and blend 30 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then blend a further 30 sec/speed 5, or until smooth. Transfer sauce into a large bowl until ready to use.

Meatballs

  1. Place sausage, red onion, garlic, parsley and bread into mixing bowl and chop Turbo/1 sec/3-4 times until desired consistency is achieved. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add nutmeg, fennel, paprika, salt, pepper, egg and ground beef and blend 10 sec/speed 7. Roll mixture into walnut sized balls (approx. 30 meatballs) and set aside.
  3. Place a frying pan over medium-high heat and add olive oil. Cook meatballs in batches for 10-12 minutes, or until golden and cooked through, turning occasionally.
  4. Transfer meatballs onto a plate lined with paper towels to drain excess oil. Transfer to a serving platter and serve with reserved Romesco Sauce.

Useful Items

parchment paper, ceramic or glass bowl, cling wrap, paper towels, large bowl, frying pan, baking pan (approx. 18 in. x 12 in. x 2 in.), serving platter, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • If ground fennel is unavailable: place nutmeg, fennel seeds, paprika, salt and pepper and grind 1 min/ speed 10 before starting meatball recipe and omit adding these ingredients when asked in recipe.
  • To bake meatballs instead of frying: preheat oven to 400°F. Line a baking pan (18 in. x 12 in. x 2 in.) with parchment. Place meatballs ½ inch apart in pan. Bake uncovered for 10-20 minutes (400°F) or until cooked through.
