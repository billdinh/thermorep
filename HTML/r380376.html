Strawberry pear purée

Ingredients

  • 500 g filtered water, plus extra if needed
  • 500 g filtered water
  • 2 pears (approx. 200 g), peeled, cored and cut into pieces (3-4 cm)
  • 250 g strawberries, hulled (see Tips)
  • 30 g butter

Preparation

  1. Place water into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket, place pears into it and steam 10 min/100°C/speed 2 or until tender (see Tips). Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and discard water.
  2. Place strawberries, butter and cooked pear into mixing bowl and blend 15 sec/speed 8.
  3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then blend 10 sec/speed 7 or until smooth.
  4. Set aside 1 serving, then freeze the remainder in individual portions for future use. If needed, stir through a little more water or your baby's milk to get the consistency just right before serving.

Useful Items

ice cube trays, freezer

Hints & Tricks

  • Where possible, try to source organic strawberries. Unfortunately, strawberries have been found to contain higher amounts of pesticide residues than many other fruits, so they're one fruit I always try to buy organically grown.
  • The cooking time in Step 1 will vary depending on the ripeness of the pears.
  • Fruit purées like this one also make a lovely addition to your baby's porridge or rice cereal if you're serving one. Just be sure to give your baby plenty of savoury purées too. Fruit is nutritious and it's important that your baby develops a liking for various fruit flavours but offering less sweet options is crucial too.
