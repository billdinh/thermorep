Chocolate Chilli Ice Cream

Ingredients

  • 150 g dark chocolate (50% cocoa solids), small pieces or callets
  • 1 - 2 fresh red chillies, deseeded, to taste
  • 500 g milk
  • 10 g cocoa powder, unsweetened
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 egg yolk, from large egg
  • 50 g caster sugar
  • 500 g double cream
  • 150 g chocolate chips

Preparation

  1. Place chocolate and chillies in mixing bowl then chop 10 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add milk and cocoa then cook 8 min/90Â°C/speed 2. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place whole egg, egg yolk and caster sugar in mixing bowl then mix 1 min/speed 4.
  4. Add reserved chocolate milk mixture and mix 10 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and set aside to cool (30-40 mins). Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl.
  5. Insert butterfly whisk. Place cream in mixing bowl and whip speed 3, without setting a time until thickened but still creamy. Remove butterfly whisk. Transfer to a separate bowl then add chocolate mixture a little at a time until fully combined.
  6. Add chocolate chips to bowl and stir in with spatula. Transfer mixture to a freezer-proof container and freeze for 12 hours.
  7. To serve, remove ice cream from freezer and place in fridge for 15 minutes. Turn mixture out, then cut in pieces (3 cm).
  8. Place half the frozen pieces in mixing bowl and churn 1 min/speed 7 while stirring with spatula through hole in mixing bowl lid. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, churn again 1 min/speed 7 then transfer to a bowl.
  9. Place remaining frozen pieces in mixing bowl then churn 1 min/speed 7 while stirring with spatula through hole in mixing bowl lid. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and churn again 1 min/speed 7. Transfer to bowl with ice cream then serve immediately.
