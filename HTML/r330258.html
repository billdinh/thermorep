Garlic and White Bean Stew

Ingredients

  • 80 g kale, thick stems removed, cut in pieces
  • 100 g onions, quartered
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 20 garlic cloves (see tip)
  • 200 g dry white wine
  • 2 dried bay leaves
  • 2 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves only
  • 1 pinch dried chilli flakes (optional)
  • 1 vegetable stock cube (for 0.5 l), crumbled
  • 240 g tinned cannellini beans, rinsed, drained (1 x 400 g tin)
  • 240 g tinned butter beans, rinsed, drained (1 x 400 g tin)
  • 500 g water
  • ½ lemon, finely grated zest and juice
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt, or to taste
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper, or to taste
  • crusty bread, for serving

Preparation

  1. Place kale in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place onions and oil in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then sauté 5 min/120°C/speed 1.
  3. Add garlic and cook 3 min/100°C//speed .
  4. Add wine and, with simmering basket in place of measuring cup, cook 10 min/100°C//speed .
  5. Add bay leaves, thyme, chilli flakes (if using), vegetable stock, cannellini beans, butter beans and water then, with simmering basket in place of measuring cup, cook 20 min/100°C//speed .
  6. Add reserved chopped kale, stir by hand with spatula then, with simmering basket in place of measuring cup, cook 2-3 min/100°C//speed  until kale is cooked as desired.
  7. Add lemon zest and lemon juice then stir with spatula. Season with salt and pepper then divide between serving bowls. Serve hot with crusty bread.
