Panforte

Ingredients

  • butter, for greasing
  • 40 g cocoa powder (70% cocoa), plus extra 2 tbsp for dusting
  • 100 g dark chocolate (70% cocoa), broken into pieces
  • 160 g dried figs
  • 150 g hazelnuts, roasted and skins removed
  • 120 g blanched almonds
  • 110 g plain flour
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 2 tsp ground ginger
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper (optional)
  • 2 pinches ground nutmeg
  • ½ tsp chilli powder (optional)
  • 200 g light brown sugar
  • 210 g honey

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C. Grease and line a springform cake tin (25 cm). Grease the baking paper lining the tin and dust with 1 tablespoon of the extra cocoa powder, ensuring the bottom and sides of tin are covered. Set baking tin aside.
  2. Place dark chocolate into mixing bowl and melt 3 min/50°C/speed 1. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then melt for a further 2-3 min/50°C/speed 1, or until chocolate has fully melted. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place figs into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 7.
  4. Add hazelnuts and almonds and chop 3 sec/speed 5, or until a roughly chopped consistency is achieved.
  5. Add flour, cinnamon, ginger, pepper (optional), nutmeg, chilli powder (optional) and 40 g cocoa powder and combine 10 sec//speed 3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  6. Add reserved melted chocolate and combine 5 sec//speed 2. Transfer mixture into a large bowl and set aside.
  7. Place sugar and honey into mixing bowl and cook 5 min/Varoma/speed 1. Transfer into the large bowl and combine well with spatula.
  8. Transfer mixture into prepared cake tin and smooth over the top. Bake for 30-35 minutes (160°C). The centre will feel soft but will firm as the panforte cools.
  9. Allow to cool for 15 minutes, then run a knife around the edge between baking paper and tin. Remove baking paper from panforte and transfer onto a wire rack to cool completely.
  10. Once cooled, dust panforte with 1 tablespoon extra cocoa powder, then serve immediately or transfer into a sealable storage container to store until use.

Useful Items

large bowl, baking paper, springform cake tin (25 cm), wire rack, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Wrap your panforte in greaseproof paper and place into a gift box to give as a lovely, traditional festive gift for family and friends.
