Gluten-Free Crepes

Ingredients

  • 6 oz gluten-free flour, with xanthan gum
  • 10 oz almond milk
  • 8 oz water
  • 1 oz coconut oil, plus extra to fry
  • 2 oz maple syrup (optional)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract (optional)
  • ½ tsp salt
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
1013 kJ / 242 kcal
Protein
2 g
Carbohydrates
24 g
Fat
16 g

Preparation

  1. Place flour, milk, water, oil, maple syrup, vanilla and salt into mixing bowl and mix 30 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Heat a frying pan to medium heat and before cooking each crepe, place ½ tsp coconut oil into frying pan and spread evenly over surface. Ladle a scoop of batter onto hot frying pan. Tilt the pan with a circular motion so batter coats the surface evenly. Cook until bubbles form all over top then flip to the other side (approx. 3-4 min. each side).
  3. Place crepes on a plate, cover with foil and keep warm while cooking remaining batter. Served warm or cold.

Useful Items

ladle, aluminum foil, frying pan

Hints & Tricks

  • This mix can be made a day in advance. Transfer batter to a bowl, cover and store in the refrigerator until ready to use. If batter is too thick, add almond milk until it becomes the consistency of thick cream.
  • Replace flour with grain of choice. Place 6 oz grains into mixing bowl and grind 1 min/speed 10 before adding remaining ingredients. Some grains may require more water than others. Texture of batter should be consistency of thick cream.
  • Gluten-free flour can be replaced with all-purpose flour, coconut flour, almond flour or ground buckwheat.
  • Replace almond milk with soy milk, coconut milk or rice milk.
  • Replace coconut oil with avocado oil or olive oil.
