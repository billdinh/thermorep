Quinoa Broth

Ingredients

  • 140 g quinoa, rinsed and well drained
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 sprig fresh rosemary, leaves only
  • 25 g extra virgin olive oil, plus extra for garnish
  • 100 g leeks, cut in rounds (1 cm)
  • 100 g carrots, peeled, diced (1 cm)
  • 100 g white cabbage, cut in thin strips (1 cm)
  • 6 cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 1 ½ vegetable stock cubes (for 0.5 l each)
  • 750 g water, boiling
  • 150 g tinned kidney beans, rinsed

Preparation

  1. Place quinoa in mixing bowl and toast 4 min/120°C/speed 1. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place garlic and rosemary in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add oil, leeks, carrots, cabbage, tomatoes, salt and pepper then cook 2 min/100°C/speed 1.
  4. Add reserved quinoa, stock cubes and water then cook 15 min/100°C/speed 1.
  5. Add beans and stir well with spatula.
  6. Transfer to serving bowls, drizzle with a little olive oil and serve hot.

Hints & Tricks

  • As this soup cools it thickens as the quinoa absorbs more liquid. Any leftovers can be made into a whole new meal of delicious croquettes. Once cooled and thickened, add grated Parmesan and chopped basil to taste. Mix 10 sec/speed 5. Form the mixture into balls, then flatten (approx. 4 cm) and roll in breadcrumbs. Cook for 10-15 minutes (180°C), turning during cooking, until golden brown. Two meals in one!
  • The consistency of the soup can be altered according to your preference. If you want a more soupy soup, add 50-100 g more water in step 4.
