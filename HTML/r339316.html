Special Un-Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 4 medium eggs
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 3 - 4 garlic cloves, to taste
  • 4 spring onions, cut in pieces (3 cm)
  • 30 g sesame oil
  • 1 red pepper (approx. 100 g), quartered
  • 2 carrots (approx. 100 g), sliced (1 cm)
  • 1000 g water
  • 1 chicken stock cube (for 0.5 l), crumbled
  • 300 g basmati rice, rinsed
  • 100 g cooked ham, cubed (1 cm)
  • 150 g cooked, peeled prawns
  • 100 g cooked chicken meat, cubed (1 cm)
  • 100 g frozen peas, cooked
  • 100 g tinned sweetcorn, drained
  • 2 Tbsp oyster sauce
  • 2 - 3 Tbsp dark soy sauce, to taste
  • 1 Tbsp sweet chilli sauce (optional)

Preparation

  1. Place eggs, salt and pepper in mixing bowl then mix 5 sec/speed 3. Line Varoma tray with a large piece of wet baking paper with enough to go 3 cm up the sides and carefully pour egg mixture onto prepared Varoma tray. Spread egg out evenly to edges of Varoma tray then set aside. Clean mixing bowl.
  2. Place garlic, spring onions and oil in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add red pepper and carrots then chop 3 sec/speed 5. Transfer to Varoma dish, distributing evenly, leaving some holes uncovered for steam to circulate.
  4. Place water and chicken stock in mixing bowl then insert simmering basket and weigh in rice. Place covered Varoma into position and steam 10 min/Varoma/speed 4.
  5. Carefully remove Varoma lid, insert Varoma tray, cover Varoma then steam 5 min/Varoma/speed 4.
  6. Carefully remove Varoma lid and tray then weigh ham, prawns and chicken into Varoma dish. Insert Varoma tray, cover Varoma then steam 3-5 min/Varoma/speed 4 until rice and eggs are cooked (see tip).
  7. Remove Varoma, remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and transfer rice to a large bowl.
  8. Remove steamed eggs and cut into small pieces. Add eggs and contents of Varoma dish to bowl with rice.
  9. Place bowl with rice on mixing bowl lid and weigh in peas and sweetcorn. Remove bowl from lid then add oyster sauce, soy sauce and chilli sauce (if using). Mix well with spatula and serve immediately.

Useful Items

baking paper, large bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • Be careful to not overcook eggs as they will become rubbery in texture. Eggs will continue cooking when stirred into rice so, for best results, they should be just cooked in step 6.
