Frangipane cake

Ingredients

Sweet shortcrust pastry

  • 50 g raw sugar
  • 100 g butter, chilled, cut into cubes
  • 230 g plain flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 pinch sea salt
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract

Frangipane cake

  • 150 g butter, cut into cubes, plus extra for greasing
  • 50 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 150 g roasted unsalted almonds
  • 150 g caster sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp dried instant yeast
  • 40 g blanched almonds
  • 250 g smooth apricot jam
  • icing sugar, to dust

Preparation

Sweet shortcrust pastry

  1. Place sugar into mixing bowl and mill 5 sec/speed 10.
  2. Add all remaining pastry ingredients and mix 20-25 sec/speed 5. Transfer pastry onto a silicone bread mat or work surface, then work into a flattened ball. Wrap in plastic wrap, then transfer into refrigerator to rest for 30 minutes. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Frangipane cake

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Grease a springform cake tin (26 cm) with butter, then dust lightly with plain flour.
  2. Once pastry has rested, roll out between 2 sheets of baking paper to 5 mm thickness, then transfer into prepared cake tin. Prick the bottom of the pastry with a fork to prevent air bubbles and rising. Cut around the edge of the pastry leaving a 1.5 cm border of pastry around the bottom of the tin. Place cake tin with pastry into freezer for another 15 minutes.
  3. Remove from freezer and line pastry with a piece of baking paper, fill pastry with pie weights (e.g. rice or beans). Bake for 10-13 minutes (180°C) or until slightly golden. Remove baking paper and weights and bake for a further 3-5 minutes or until golden brown. Remove from oven and set aside to cool, reduce oven temperature to 160°C.
  4. Place roasted almonds and 50 g of the sugar into mixing bowl and mill 8 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  5. Without rinsing mixing bowl, place butter into mixing bowl and warm 1 min/37°C/speed 1.
  6. Add eggs, vanilla, yeast, blanched almonds, plain flour, remaining 100 g of the sugar and the reserved almond mixture and mix 10 sec/speed 6.
  7. Place springform tin with pastry onto mixing bowl lid, then weigh apricot jam into it. Spread jam evenly over pastry base in tin, then pour over cake batter in mixing bowl.
  8. Bake for 30 minutes (160°C), or until a wooden skewer inserted into the centre of the cake layer comes out clean. Remove cake from oven and leave to cool in springform cake tin.
  9. Once cooled, remove from cake tin and dust with icing sugar and serve.

Useful Items

silicone bread mat, plastic wrap, springform cake tin (26 cm), rolling pin, baking paper, pie weights, rice or dried beans, wooden skewer, refrigerator, oven, freezer

Hints & Tricks

  • Decorate the top of your cake with fresh seasonal fruits before dusting with the icing sugar and serving.
