Chicago-Style Deep Dish Pizza

Ingredients

  • 11 oz water, room temperature
  • 3 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 20 oz all-purpose flour
  • 1 oz yellow cornmeal, medium grind
  • 2 oz unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 ½ oz olive oil, divided, plus extra to grease
  • 3 tsp fine sea salt, divided
  • 13 oz mozzarella cheese, diced (approx. 1 in.), divided
  • 8 oz Parmesan cheese, diced (approx. ½ in.), divided
  • 9 ½ oz Italian sausage, broken into small pieces (approx. 1 in.)
  • 7 oz yellow onions, quartered (approx. 1 large)
  • ½ oz garlic cloves
  • 28 oz canned crushed tomatoes
  • ½ tsp dried basil
  • ½ tsp dried oregano
  • ½ tsp rosemary
  • ¼ tsp dried red chili flakes, to taste
  • 4 oz sliced pepperoni, to taste
  • 4 oz sliced salami, to taste
  • 6 oz Canadian bacon, diced (approx. ¼ in.)

Preparation

  1. Lightly grease a large glass bowl and set aside. Place water, yeast and sugar into mixing bowl and mix 20 sec/speed 2. Add flour, cornmeal, butter, 1 oz olive oil and 2 tsp salt into mixing bowl and knead 4 min/. Transfer dough into greased bowl and shape into a ball. Cover with cling wrap and allow to rise in a warm spot until doubled in size, about 1 hour. Meanwhile, prepare your toppings and sauce.
  2. Place 7 oz mozzarella into mixing bowl and grate 15 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Place remaining 6 oz mozzarella into mixing bowl and grate 15 sec/speed 6, transfer to bowl with grated mozzarella. Place 4 oz Parmesan into mixing bowl and grate 15-20 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a separate bowl and set aside. Place remaining 4 oz Parmesan into mixing bowl and grate 15-20 sec/speed 10, add to bowl with previously grated Parmesan and refrigerate cheeses until needed.
  3. Place Italian sausage into mixing bowl and cook 15 min/212°F//speed 0.5. Transfer sausage to a bowl lined with paper towels and set aside.
  4. Place onions, garlic and 0.5 oz olive oil into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Sauté 5 min/250°F/speed 1.
  5. Add tomatoes, 1 tsp salt, basil, oregano, rosemary and chili flakes. With simmering basket instead of measuring cup on top of mixing bowl lid cook 5 min/212°F/speed 1. Set sauce aside until ready to use.
  6. Preheat oven to 425°F. Place pizza dough (once it has doubled in size) in the center of a deep dish pizza pan (14 in.). Flatten dough into a disk shape and begin evenly pressing the dough outwards to the edge and up the sides of the pan. Cover with cling wrap and allow to rest 10 minutes.
  7. Once the dough has rested, stretch dough one last time out to the edge of the pan and up the sides. Prick the pizza base (not the edges) every inch or so with a fork.
  8. Parbake crust for 10 minutes (425°F).
  9. Sprinkle reserved mozzarella evenly over the pizza. Next, layer the pepperoni, salami, Canadian bacon and reserved Italian sausage. Pour reserved tomato sauce evenly over the top.
  10. Bake for 25-30 minutes (425°F) until crust is golden brown and sauce is bubbling.
  11. Sprinkle reserved grated Parmesan cheese over the top of the pizza. Allow to rest at least 5 minutes before slicing.

Useful Items

large glass bowl, bowl, paper towels, deep dish pizza pan (14 in.), cling wrap, refrigerator, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • For a vegetarian pizza, replace meats with equal amounts of sautéed mushrooms, bell peppers, red onions, pitted olives, shallots, garlic and zucchini or squash.
