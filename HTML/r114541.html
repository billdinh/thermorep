Breakfast cereal clusters

Ingredients

  • 200 g rice malt syrup
  • 100 g coconut sugar
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 400 g apple sauce (see Tips)
  • 60 g coconut oil
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp teff (see Tips)
  • 2 tsp natural vanilla extract
  • 2 tbsp chia seeds
  • 100 g quinoa flakes
  • 60 g puffed buckwheat (see Tips)
  • 20 g millet puffs (see Tips)
  • 40 g amaranth grain, popped (see Tips)
  • 60 g sesame seeds
  • 250 g raw almonds
  • 100 g shredded coconut
  • 150 g dried cranberries
  • 80 g dried blueberries
  • 50 g dried apples, cut into pieces
  • milk of choice, to serve (optional)
  • natural yoghurt, to serve (optional)

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C. Line 2 baking trays (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place syrup, sugar, honey, apple sauce, oil, cinnamon and salt into mixing bowl and heat 2 min/100°C/speed 3. Transfer into a large bowl.
  3. Add teff, vanilla and chia seeds into large bowl with syrup mixture and set aside.
  4. Place a separate bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh into it quinoa flakes, buckwheat, millet, amaranth, sesame seeds, almonds and coconut. Transfer into large bowl with mixture and stir well to combine.
  5. Spread mixture evenly across prepared baking trays and bake for 40 minutes (160°C) until crisp and golden brown, stirring mixture and rotating trays once or twice during baking to ensure even colour.
  6. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh dried fruit into it. Remove trays from oven, transfer dried fruit onto tray and stir to combine.
  7. Allow to cool completely before breaking into bite-size clusters. Transfer into a sealable container. Serve with milk (optional) or yoghurt (optional).

Useful Items

baking tray (30 x 40 cm), baking paper, large bowl, bowl, 2 baking trays, sealable container, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • To make your own apple sauce, refer to The Basic Cookbook or Everyday Cookbook.
  • Teff, millet, amaranth and puffed buckwheat can be purchased at most supermarkets and at health food stores.
