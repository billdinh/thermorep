Thai Green Curry Paste

Ingredients

  • 2 oz fresh green chilies, e.g. small Thai, stems removed
  • 2 ½ oz fresh green chilies, e.g. long green Thai, stems removed
  • ½ oz garlic cloves (approx. 6 cloves)
  • ½ oz fresh lemongrass
  • 2 oz shallots
  • ½ oz fresh galangal (Thai ginger)
  • ½ oz fresh turmeric
  • 1 oz shrimp paste
  • 2 - 4 fresh kaffir lime leaves
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 3 oz water

Preparation

  1. Place all ingredients into mixing bowl and grind 30 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix 30 sec/speed 10.
  2. Transfer into sterile glass jars and store in the refrigerator until ready to use.

Useful Items

sterilized jars with twist off lids

Hints & Tricks

  • Will keep up to two weeks in the refrigerator.
  • Kaffir lime leaves are also known as lime leaves or makrut lime leaves. Kaffir lime stem usually holds 2 leaves per stem.
  • Replace ground coriander and cumin with coriander and cumin seeds and grind 1 min/speed 10 before starting recipe. Add remaining ingredients.
  • If using Thai chilies or bird's eye chilies, reduce the number of chilies used as these are much spicier. It's easier to begin with less, and add more flavor as you adjust to taste.
  • Grinding hot chilies can create powerful vapors. Remove mixing bowl lid with awareness to avoid inhaling sharp fumes.
  • For a hotter green chili paste replace 1 oz of serrano with 1 oz bird's eye red chilies (stems removed).
