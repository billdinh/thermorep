Apple cinnamon muffins

Ingredients

  • 60 g walnuts
  • 60 g almonds
  • 150 g apple (unpeeled), cored and cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 2 eggs
  • 60 g milk (see Tips)
  • 90 g brown sugar (see Tips)
  • 120 g macadamia oil
  • 150 g wholemeal self-raising flour
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C. Line a 12-hole muffin tray with paper cases and set aside.
  2. Place walnuts into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 5, until coarsely chopped. If preparing muffins for young children, chop for a further 1-2 sec/speed 5, until finely chopped. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place almonds into mixing bowl and mill 15 sec/speed 9 or until finely ground.
  4. Add apple and grate 3 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add eggs, milk, sugar and oil and combine 10 sec//speed 3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  6. Add flour, cinnamon and 40 g of the reserved walnuts and combine 15 sec//speed 3.
  7. Divide mixture between paper cases and sprinkle with remaining reserved chopped walnuts. Bake for 20-25 minutes (160°C), or until a skewer inserted into the centre of the muffins comes out clean. Allow to cool in tin for 5 minutes, then transfer onto a wire rack to cool further before serving.

Useful Items

wooden skewer, bowl, 12-hole muffin tray, muffin wrap papers, wire rack, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • You can use your preferred brown sugar in this recipe – either regular brown sugar or a less refined option such as Rapadura sugar (Panela) or coconut sugar. You can also use honey if you prefer.
  • Dairy-free variation: Replace the cow's milk with almond milk.
  • Nut-free variation: You can replace the almonds with a seed mix (such as a mix of sunflower seeds, pepitas and linseeds, milled until finely ground) and leave out the walnuts. Replace macadamia oil with a light olive oil.
