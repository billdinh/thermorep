Seriously Lemony Slice

Ingredients

Base

  • 180 g plain flour
  • 125 g caster sugar
  • 125 g unsalted butter, cold, diced

Lemon Layer

  • 300 g caster sugar
  • 4 lemons, thin peelings of skin from 2, juice from 4
  • 4 large eggs
  • 40 g plain flour

Preparation

Base

  1. Preheat oven to 180째C and line a brownie tin (32 cm x 18 cm) with baking paper.
  2. Place flour and sugar in mixing bowl then mix 5 sec/speed 5.
  3. Add butter and mix 15-20 sec/speed 8 until just clinging together. Tip out into prepared tin then use fingertips to flatten and form an even layer. Bake for 15 minutes (180째C).
  4. Remove from oven then reduce oven temperature to 140째C.

Lemon Layer

  1. Place 30 g sugar and lemon peel in mixing bowl then grind 10 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then grind again 20 sec/speed 10.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Add 270 g sugar and eggs then mix 3 min/speed 4.
  3. Add flour and lemon juice then mix 10 sec/speed 3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then mix again 10 sec/speed 3. Remove butterfly whisk, pour into tin over base then bake for 40 minutes (140째C) or until just set.
  4. Leave to cool in tin completely. Cut into bars and serve or store in an airtight container until needed.
