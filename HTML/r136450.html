Salmon with Broccoli, Couscous and Honey Mustard Sauce

Ingredients

  • 20 g oil
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 tsp sweet paprika
  • 1 ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 600 g water
  • 2 fresh salmon fillets, skinless (approx. 250 g)
  • 200 g broccoli florets
  • 80 g couscous
  • 1 shallot, halved
  • 1 ½ tsp honey
  • 1 ½ tsp mustard
  • 1 tsp cornflour
Nutrition

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid. Weigh in 10 g oil and add ½ tsp turmeric, ½ tsp paprika, ½ tsp salt and ¼ tsp pepper then remove and mix together.
  2. Place 500 g water in mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh in salmon fillets. Spread reserved seasoned oil over fillets and steam 12 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Insert Varoma tray and weigh in broccoli then steam again 8 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  4. Set Varoma aside and empty mixing bowl, reserving cooking liquid.
  5. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in couscous. Add 160 g reserved cooking liquid and ½ tsp salt, stir together then set aside for 5 minutes. Meanwhile, continue with recipe.
  6. Place shallot in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  7. Add remaining 10 g oil and sauté 2 min/120°C/speed 1.
  8. Add 100 g reserved cooking liquid, 100 g water, honey, mustard, ½ tsp turmeric, ½ tsp sweet paprika, ½ tsp salt, ¼ tsp pepper and cornflour then mix 3 sec/speed 5. Cook 2 min/100°C/speed 1.
  9. Blend 30 sec/speed 7-9, increasing speed gradually. Arrange salmon, broccoli and couscous on two plates then serve with honey mustard sauce.
