Buckwheat, Almond and Seed Bread

Ingredients

  • 100 g buckwheat grains, raw
  • 120 g blanched almonds
  • 180 g pumpkin seeds
  • 60 g sunflower seeds
  • 60 g linseeds
  • 30 g chia seeds
  • 30 g psyllium husks
  • 1 heaped tsp fresh thyme leaves
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 300 g water
  • olive oil, for greasing
Nutrition
per 1 slice
Calories
814 kJ / 195 kcal

7.6 g

Preparation

  1. Place buckwheat grains in mixing bowl and grind 1 min/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then grind again 30 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add almonds and 120 g pumpkin seeds then grind 40 sec/speed 10.
  3. Add sunflower seeds, linseeds, chia seeds, psyllium husks, fresh thyme leaves, salt, water and remaining 60 g pumpkin seeds then mix 15 sec//speed 3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Mix again 15 sec//speed 3. Grease a 450 g loaf tin with olive oil and transfer mixture to tin. Even out top of dough with back of oiled spatula then cover and leave to rest for 1 hour.
  5. Preheat oven to 180°C. Bake loaf 50 minutes (180°C) or until top is brown and skewer inserted in centre comes out clean. Cool for 10 minutes in tin before transferring to a wire rack. Cool completely before cutting in slices.

Useful Items

450 g loaf tin (1 lb), skewer, cooling rack, oven
