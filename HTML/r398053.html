Vegetable Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 4 oz zucchini
  • 4 oz carrots
  • 2 ½ oz red pepper

Cilantro Aioli

  • 9 oz vegetable oil
  • 1 oz ginger, peeled, in pieces
  • 1 oz cilantro leaves, plus extra to garnish
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tbsp freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper

Vegetable Pancakes

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 3 oz green onions, sliced (2 in.)
  • 3 oz red onions, quartered
  • 2 oz leeks, cleaned and sliced (2 in.)
  • 4 oz all-purpose flour
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 4 oz mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 - 4 tbsp oil, as needed
  • fresh cilantro leaves, to garnish

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in zucchini, carrots and red pepper. With a julienne peeler, prepare weighed vegetables, return to bowl and set aside

Cilantro Aioli

  1. Place a pitcher onto mixing bowl lid, weigh in vegetable oil and set aside.
  2. Place ginger and cilantro into mixing bowl and chop 7 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add egg, lemon juice, salt, and pepper mix 4 min/160°F/speed 4, while very slowly pouring reserved vegetable oil onto mixing bowl lid, letting it drizzle around measuring cup in a thin stream onto rotating blades to create an emulsion. Transfer to a bowl and refrigerate. Clean mixing bowl.

Vegetable Pancakes

  1. Place garlic, green onions, red onions, and leeks into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add flour, garlic powder, baking powder, salt, eggs, soy sauce, sesame oil and mix 5 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add reserved julienned zucchini, carrot and red pepper. Add sliced mushrooms and mix 20 sec//speed 4.
  4. Heat a large frying pan with oil over medium heat. Place ⅓ cup of batter onto pan and press batter down with back of spatula to flatten slightly. Cook for 2-3 minutes per side, adding additional oil as needed. Garnish with cilantro and serve warm with Cilantro Aioli.

Useful Items

large frying pan, pitcher, julienne peeler
