Rye Flatbreads

Ingredients

  • 70 g oat flakes
  • 500 g water
  • 20 g fresh yeast, crumbled
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 100 g sunflower oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 200 g rye flour
  • 380 g bread flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 1 ½ tsp salt
  • ½ - 1 tsp fennel seeds, dried, to taste (optional)

Preparation

  1. Place oat flakes in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 9. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place water and yeast in mixing bowl then warm 3 min/37°C/speed 1.
  3. Add honey and sunflower oil then mix 45 sec/37°C/speed 3.
  4. Add rye flour, 130 g bread flour, salt and fennel seeds then mix 10 sec/speed 6. Allow dough to rise in mixing bowl for 40 minutes. Push down risen dough with spatula.
  5. Add remaining 250 g bread flour and reserved chopped oat flakes to mixing bowl then knead 3 min/. Transfer sticky dough to a very well floured surface. With floured hands, form dough into a ball, then cover and allow to rise for 15 minutes. Meanwhile preheat oven to 250°C and line 2 baking trays with baking paper.
  6. Divide dough into 8 equal pieces and with oiled hands shape each piece into a flatbread (approx. Ø 16-18 cm). Place 2 on each tray and bake in batches for 8-10 minutes (250°C) or until dark brown. Remove baked flatbreads from oven and transfer to a cooling rack. Place prepared baking tray with unbaked flatbread in oven and bake as before. Repeat until all flatbreads are baked. Allow to cool slightly before serving.

Useful Items

2 baking trays, baking paper, cooling rack, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve this delicious rye flatbread as a side dish or as a snack, or for breakfast with butter or jam.
