Chocolate Coco Bars

Ingredients

Coconut Filling

  • 7 oz shredded coconut, unsweetened
  • 4 oz sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 vanilla bean, seeds only

Dark Chocolate Coating

  • 8 ½ oz dark chocolate (70%), broken into pieces
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
1288 kJ / 311 kcal
Protein
4 g
Carbohydrates
21 g
Fat
22 g

Preparation

Coconut Filling

  1. Place coconut, condensed milk and vanilla seeds into mixing bowl and mix 30 sec/speed 4. Transfer to cling wrap and form into a rectangle (approx. 6 in. x 5 in.). Wrap tightly and refrigerate 15 minutes. Clean mixing bowl.

Dark Chocolate Coating

  1. Place chocolate into mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 10. Transfer half the grated chocolate into a bowl and set aside.
  2. With remaining grated chocolate in mixing bowl melt 5 min/120°F/speed 1. Meanwhile, remove reserved coconut from refrigerator and cut into rectangles (approx. 3 in. x 1 in.) and set aside. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and melt chocolate a further 2 min/120°F/speed 1.
  3. Add reserved chocolate and without measuring cup mix 5 min/speed 2 to temper the chocolate. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and without measuring cup mix a further 2 min/speed 2.

Assembly

  1. Transfer melted chocolate into a bowl and working with one coconut piece at a time, dip in melted chocolate, gently shake off excess chocolate and place on parchment. Refrigerate 20 minutes for coating to set and serve.

Useful Items

cling wrap, parchment paper, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • After dipping coco bars into chocolate, sprinkle with unsweetened shredded coconut or chopped nuts.
