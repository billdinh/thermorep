Tagliatelle with Porcini Mushrooms

Ingredients

  • 20 g fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 50 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 100 g fresh porcini mushrooms, sliced
  • 100 g fresh mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 ¼ tsp fine sea salt,
  • 1200 g water
  • 300 g dried tagliatelle
  • 1 - 2 pinches ground black pepper, to taste

Preparation

  1. Place parsley in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Remove 1 Tbsp chopped parsley to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Add garlic and 30 g oil then sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1. Remove garlic if desired.
  3. Add porcini mushrooms, fresh mushrooms and ¼ tsp salt then cook 12 min/100°C//speed . Transfer to a serving bowl and keep warm.
  4. Place water in mixing bowl and bring to the boil 10 min/100°C/speed 1.
  5. Add tagliatelle and remaining 1 tsp salt then, without measuring cup, cook time indicated on packet/100°C//speed 1 or until al dente. Drain pasta through Varoma dish, then transfer to bowl with mushrooms. Add pepper, remaining 20 g oil and reserved chopped parsley then combine with spatula. Serve hot.

Hints & Tricks

  • This recipe uses two types of mushrooms for greater depth of flavour. Dried pasta cooks beautifully in the mixing bowl, however, if you prefer, you can make your own Pasta Dough from scratch.
  • The quantity of salt can be increased to taste depending on the quality of the mushrooms.
  • Serve with grated Parmesan cheese.
  • Porcini mushrooms are also known as ceps.
  • Porcini mushrooms provide a unique flavour. Use only porcini mushrooms or replace them entirely with your favourite variety, for a different and equally delicious result.
  • You can also make this dish with homemade pasta. Cook pasta in a saucepan while sautéing porcini mushrooms in the Thermomix®.
