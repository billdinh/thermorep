Chocolate chip muffins

Ingredients

  • 150 g butter, softened, cut into pieces, plus extra for greasing
  • 100 g dark chocolate, cut into pieces
  • 2 eggs
  • 200 g caster sugar
  • 300 g plain flour
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 300 g full cream milk
  • 2 tsp baking powder
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
1356 kJ / 324 kcal
Protein
5 g
Carbohydrates

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Grease a 12-hole muffin tray and set aside.
  2. Place dark chocolate into mixing bowl and grate 6-10 sec/speed 5. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place butter, eggs, caster sugar, plain flour, salt, milk and baking powder into mixing bowl and mix 30 sec/speed 4.
  4. Add reserved grated chocolate and mix 15 sec//speed 4. Transfer batter into prepared muffin tray. Bake muffins for 30 minutes (180°C) or until golden brown. Allow muffins to cool for 10 minutes before removing from muffin tray. Serve warm or cold.

Useful Items

12-hole muffin tray, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • If you don't have a muffin tray, stack groups of 3 muffin liners together on a baking tray to serve as muffin moulds.
  • Double chocolate muffins: insert a piece of chocolate into the centre of each muffin before baking.
  • Blueberry muffins: skip step 2. Instead of chocolate, in step 4 add blueberries (approx. 150 g), either fresh or frozen (unthawed), and stir 10 sec//speed 1. You can also fold the blueberries in with the spatula.
