Coconut Shake

Ingredients

  • 15 g dates, pitted
  • 90 g bananas, sliced
  • ½ vanilla pod, seeds only
  • 300 g tinned coconut milk, shaken
  • 100 g coconut milk drink (in carton)
  • 150 g ice cubes (approx. 12 ice cubes), plus extra for serving
  • desiccated coconut, for garnish
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
688 kJ / 165 kcal
Protein
1.2 g
Carbohydrates
9.9 g
Fat
13.4 g

Preparation

  1. Place dates, banana and vanilla seeds in mixing bowl then blend 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add tinned coconut milk, cartoned coconut milk drink and ice then blend 15 sec/speed 9. Dip rims of 4 glasses in the shake then dip in desiccated coconut, before filling with ice cubes. Pour shake over ice and serve immediately.
