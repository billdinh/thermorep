Carrot Hummus

Ingredients

  • 1 can garbanzo beans (approx.16 oz), drained
  • 3 ice cubes
  • 6 oz carrots, in pieces (½ in.)
  • 3 oz apple, honey crisp (approx. ½ apple), peeled and cut into pieces
  • 3 tbsp orange juice
  • 1 oz olive oil
  • 3 tbsp tahini
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder, to taste
  • ¼ tsp ground ginger
  • ¼ tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp salt, to taste
  • sliced raw vegetables, to serve
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
356 kJ / 85 kcal
Protein
4 g
Carbohydrates
9 g
Fat
5 g

Preparation

  1. Place garbanzo beans, ice, carrots, apple, orange juice, olive oil, tahini, turmeric, ginger, garlic and salt into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 4-7 increasing speed gradually. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix 1 min/speed 8.
  2. Serve with sliced vegetables.
