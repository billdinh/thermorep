Crab Tortelloni, Red Mullet

Ingredients

Pasta

  • 75 g water
  • 1 pinch saffron
  • 250 g pasta flour, type 00
  • 6 egg yolks, from medium eggs

Filling

  • 50 g double cream
  • 100 g breadcrumbs, white
  • 300 g fresh crab meat (50 g brown, 250 g white)
  • 1 lemon, juice only, to taste
  • egg wash, for sealing
  • pasta flour, for dusting

Dressing

  • 2 limes, finely grated zest of one, juice of both
  • 30 g palm sugar
  • 20 g fish sauce
  • 30 g water
  • 10 g sesame oil
  • 40 g rapeseed oil
  • 1 stick fresh lemon grass, white part only, finely sliced
  • 2 shallots, finely chopped
  • ½ bunch fresh coriander
  • 10 fresh mint leaves
  • 1 Tbsp sesame seeds, toasted
  • fine sea salt, to taste
  • ground black pepper, to taste

Fish

  • 1000 g water
  • 1 stick fresh lemon grass, white part only, finely sliced
  • 5 g fresh root ginger, cut in round slices (2 mm)
  • 6 fresh kaffir lime leaves, shredded
  • 1 lemon, zest only
  • 6 red mullet fillets, small, pin-boned and scaled
  • 30 g olive oil, for brushing
  • fine sea salt, to taste
  • ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1 mooli, medium, cut in long julienne strips
  • fresh coriander shoots small, for garnish

Preparation

Pasta

  1. Place water and saffron in a small saucepan over a high heat and reduce to 40 g.
  2. Place flour, egg yolks and saffron water in mixing bowl then knead 3 min/. Tip out and form into a ball. Wrap in cling film and rest in fridge for 4 hours.

Filling

  1. Place cream in mixing bowl and bring to a boil 3 min/95°C/speed 2.
  2. Add breadcrumbs and stir 10 sec/speed 2 then leave to cool.
  3. Add brown crabmeat and mix 20 sec/speed 3.
  4. Add white crabmeat and stir 5 sec/speed .
  5. Add lemon juice to taste. Form mixture into 18 small balls and place on a tray in the fridge to set their shape. Clean mixing bowl.
  6. Once pasta has chilled, roll out as thin as possible using a pasta machine. Cut 18 discs using a 7 cm cutter. Place a ball of chilled crab mixture in middle of each disc. Brush egg wash around the edge then fold top half over and seal edges, ensuring no air is trapped inside. Curl the semi circle around your finger and push corners to gether to form a belly button shape. Repeat to form all the tortelloni. Place on a lightly floured tray, cover with cling film and chill until needed.

Dressing

  1. Place all ingredients in mixing bowl and emulsify 1 min/speed 3. Pulse Turbo/1 sec/2 times. Season to taste then transfer to a jug or squeezy bottle and set aside.

Fish

  1. Place water, lemon grass, ginger, lime leaves and lemon zest in mixing bowl. Bring to a boil 10 min/Varoma/speed 1. Meanwhile, brush Varoma dish and tray with oil. Cut each fillet into 3 pieces. Brush with oil and season. Place tortelloni in Varoma dish, and fish on Varoma tray.
  2. Place Varoma into position then steam 4-5 min/Varoma/speed 1. Meanwhile, place mooli in a bowl and cover with a little dressing, turning it to coat.
  3. Remove Varoma and keep covered. Warm remaining dressing in a small saucepan on the hob.
  4. To serve, place 3 pieces of mullet in a deep plate, with 3 tortelloni. Place the mooli 'spaghetti' in the centre of each plate. Garnish with coriander shoots and drizzle the warm dressing around.

Useful Items

saucepan, pastry brush, pasta machine, round pastry cutter (Ø 7 cm), tray, cling film, squeezy bottle, hob, fridge
