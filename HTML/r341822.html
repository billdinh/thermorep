Maca chocolate bark

Ingredients

  • 50 g raw cacao butter
  • 40 g pure maple syrup
  • 10 g maca powder
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract
  • 40 g raw cacao powder
  • 30 g raisins
  • 1 pinch salt
  • nuts, seeds and dried fruit of choice (see Tips)

Preparation

  1. Line a baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place cacao butter into mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 7, then melt 10 min/37°C/speed 1 or until melted.
  3. Add syrup, maca powder and vanilla and mix 1 min/37°C/speed 2.
  4. Add cacao powder, raisins and salt and mix 5 sec/speed 2. Spoon mixture onto prepared tray and spread out thinly (3-5 mm) using a palette knife. Decorate with nuts, seeds and/or dried fruit of choice, then place into refrigerator to set (approx. 30 minutes). Break into pieces to serve.

Useful Items

baking tray (30 x 40 cm), baking paper, palette knife, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • We decorated our bark with pepitas, goji berries and dried cranberries.
  • You can store leftover bark in a sealable container in the refrigerator for up to 1 week.
  • Individual maca chocolates: Spoon maca chocolate mixture into individual silicone chocolate moulds and place into refrigerator until set (approx. 30 minutes). Once set, remove from moulds and serve.
