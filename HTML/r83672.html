Chicken with Carrot & Red Pepper Rice and Creamy Mustard Sauce

Ingredients

  • 500 g chicken breast fillets, skinless, cut in strips (1 cm)
  • ½ lemon, thin peel
  • 35 g olive oil
  • ¾ tsp fine sea salt
  • ½ tsp ground pepper
  • 1 tsp sweet paprika
  • 300 g carrots, cut in pieces
  • 150 g red pepper, cut in pieces
  • 1 tsp vegetable stock paste
  • 200 g wild rice
  • 1000 g water
  • 100 g cream
  • 2 tsp wholegrain mustard
  • 20 g cornflour
  • 15 g fresh chives, snipped

Preparation

  1. Place a large bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in chicken breast pieces, then set aside.
  2. Place lemon peel in mixing bowl and chop Turbo/2 sec/4 times, scraping down with spatula in between as needed.
  3. Add 30 g oil, ½ tsp salt, ¼ tsp pepper, ½ tsp paprika then mix 10 sec/speed 5. Pour over chicken in bowl, cover with cling film and place in fridge to marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Place carrots and red pepper in mixing bowl then chop 4 sec/speed 5. Transfer chopped vegetables to Varoma dish.
  5. Place stock paste and 5 g oil in mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket and weigh rice into it, then weigh in water over the rice.
  6. Place Varoma into postion, insert Varoma tray and place marinaded chicken fillets on it. Cover Varoma and steam 25 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  7. Set Varoma and simmering basket aside. Transfer rice, carrots and red pepper to a bowl and mix well, then cover with cling film to keep warm. Transfer chicken fillets to a separate bowl and cover with cling film. Empty mixing bowl, reserving steaming liquid.
  8. Place 400 g reserved steaming liquid (topping up with more water if needed), cream, mustard, ¼ tsp salt, ¼ tsp pepper, ½ tsp paprika and cornflour in mixing bowl then mix 5 sec/speed 5.
  9. Add chives and cook 4 min 30 sec/100°C/speed 2. Adjust seasoning to taste, then transfer sauce to a serving jug. Serve vegetables and rice with chicken fillets and sauce.

Useful Items

large bowl, cling film, refrigerator
