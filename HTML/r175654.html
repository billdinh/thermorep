Kingclip with Chakalaka Vegetables and Dill Butter

Ingredients

Dill Butter

  • 25 g fresh dill
  • ¼ - ¾ tsp fine sea salt, to taste
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 150 g unsalted butter, soft, diced

Klingclip with Chakalaka Vegetables

  • 300 g onions, halved
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 20 g olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 1 - 2 tsp dried chilli flakes, to taste
  • 1 heaped Tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp sweet paprika
  • 1 ½ tsp smoked paprika
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt, plus extra to taste
  • 300 g carrots, cut in sticks (1 cm x 8 cm)
  • 30 g sugar beet molasses
  • 1 tsp mustard
  • 1 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 8 white fish fillets, skinless, approx. 100 g each (e.g. kingclip, coalfish, cape hake)
  • ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1 Tbsp orange zest, grated
  • 600 g tinned chopped tomatoes
  • 50 g water
  • 120 g red peppers, cut in bite-sized pieces
  • 500 g large white beans, tinned (e.g. borlotti or white haricots blanc), drained

Preparation

Dill Butter

  1. Place dill, salt and pepper in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add butter and mix 8 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.

Klingclip with Chakalaka Vegetables

  1. Place onions and garlic in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add olive oil, chilli flakes, curry powder, sweet and smoked paprika powder and salt then sauté 8 min/120°C/speed 1.
  3. Add carrots, sugar beet molasses, mustard and Worcestershire sauce then sauté 4 min/120°C//speed 1. Meanwhile, lightly grease Varoma dish and tray. Season both sides of white fish fillets with salt and black pepper to taste, arrange on Varoma dish and tray then sprinkle each fillet with ¼ Tbsp orange zest. Insert Varoma tray in Varoma dish.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and water to mixing bowl, set Varoma in position then steam 17-20 min/Varoma//speed 1. Set Varoma aside.
  5. Add red peppers and drained beans to mixing bowl then cook 4 min/120°C//speed . Season to taste. Serve fish with chakalaka and dill butter.
