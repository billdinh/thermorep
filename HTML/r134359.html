Spelt and Buckwheat Bread

Ingredients

  • butter, for greasing
  • oat flakes, for sprinkling
  • 350 g spelt grains
  • 150 g buckwheat grains
  • 350 g water
  • 2 tsp dried instant yeast
  • 1 tsp runny honey
  • 2 tsp fine sea salt
  • 40 g white wine vinegar
  • 90 g sunflower seeds
  • 90 g linseeds
  • 50 g sesame seeds

Preparation

  1. Grease 2 loaf tins (2 x 450 g/1 lb) and sprinkle with oats.
  2. Place 250 g spelt grains in mixing bowl and grind 1 min/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl.
  3. Place remaining 100 g spelt grains and buckwheat grains in mixing bowl then grind 1 min/speed 10. Add to bowl with ground spelt and set aside.
  4. Place water, yeast and honey in mixing bowl then heat 1 min 30 sec/37°C/speed 1.
  5. Add reserved spelt and buckwheat flour, salt, vinegar, sunflower seeds, linseeds and sesame seeds then knead 4 min/. Transfer dough to prepared tins, sprinkle with oats then cover and leave to rise in a warm place until doubled in size (approx. 40 minutes). Towards the end of rising time, preheat oven to 200°C.
  6. Bake for 40 minutes (200°C). Remove loaves from tins and allow to cool on a wire rack before slicing to serve.

Useful Items

cling film, wire rack, 2 x loaf tins (450 g/1 lb), oven
