Steamed Poussins and Fruity Quinoa

Ingredients

  • 2 poussins

Marinade

  • 30 g olive oil
  • 20 g lemon juice
  • 2 tsp cumin seeds
  • ½ tsp sweet paprika
  • 2 tsp harissa paste
  • 1 Tbsp fresh flat-leaf parsley, chopped, plus extra for garnish
  • 2 poussins (approx. 400 g each)

Quinoa

  • 20 g fresh flat-leaf parsley, plus extra for garnish
  • 40 g dried apricots
  • 40 g dried prunes, stones removed
  • 25 g pine nuts, lightly toasted in a dry pan
  • 50 g raisins
  • 1000 g water
  • 120 g quinoa grains

Preparation

Marinade

  1. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in oil and lemon juice. Remove bowl from mixing bowl lid, add cumin seeds, paprika, harissa paste and parsley then stir in. Add poussins and coat with sauce. Transfer to refrigerator and leave to marinate for at least 2 hours, turning occasionally in the marinade.

Quinoa

  1. Place parsley in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8. Add apricots and prunes then chop 10 sec/speed 5. Add pine nuts and raisins, stir in with spatula then transfer mixture to a large serving dish and set aside.
  2. Place water in mixing bowl, insert simmering basket and weigh in quinoa. Remove simmering basket and set aside.
  3. Place marinated poussins in Varoma dish, cover and place Varoma into position then steam 50 min/Varoma/speed 2.5.
  4. Remove Varoma and set on a plate while you insert simmering basket with quinoa. Place Varoma back into position and steam again 20 min/Varoma/speed 2.5.
  5. Remove Varoma and set aside. Transfer quinoa to dish with chopped fruit, parsley and nuts and stir in. Place the poussins on top and serve garnished with chopped parsley.

Useful Items

bowl, large serving plate, refrigerator
