Salsa Verde

Ingredients

  • 100 - 150 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 15 g fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 10 g fresh coriander, leaves only
  • 6 anchovy fillets, tinned, drained
  • 1 Tbsp pickled capers, drained, rinsed
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 Tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 Tbsp red wine vinegar
  • fine sea salt, to taste
  • ground black pepper, to taste

Preparation

  1. Place a jug on mixing bowl lid and weigh in oil. Set jug aside.
  2. Place parsley, coriander, anchovies, capers and garlic in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then chop again 2 sec/speed 6.
  3. Add mustard and vinegar then mix 3 sec/speed 1. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Mix at speed 3 while very slowly pouring oil onto mixing bowl lid, letting it drizzle around measuring cup in a thin stream onto rotating blades until desired consistency is achieved. Season to taste then pour over vegetables or fish in a serving dish or transfer to a jug to serve.

Hints & Tricks

  • Great served with fish or lamb, or poured over steamed green beans.
  • Use leftover herb stalks to flavour stocks or soups.
