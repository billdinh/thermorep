Quick porridge

Ingredients

  • 60 g rolled oats
  • 1 pinch ground cinnamon (see Tips)
  • 1 tbsp dried goji berries
  • 1 tsp shredded coconut
  • 1 tsp pepitas
  • 1 tsp white chia seeds
  • 220 g milk (see Tips)
  • 150 g hot water

Preparation

  1. Place oats into mixing bowl and mill 3 sec/speed 10.
  2. Add all remaining ingredients and cook 4 min/100Â°C/speed 2. Divide between serving bowls and serve with toppings of choice, as desired (see Tips).

Hints & Tricks

  • You can make this porridge using a mix of dried fruits, nuts, spices and seeds of choice.
  • You can replace the cow's milk with any milk of choice. In step 2, before adding milk you can place dry ingredients into a snap lock bag and store for up to 1 month.
  • To save time in the morning, measure spices, fruit and seeds into a snaplock bag and store in the pantry.
  • To add a little variety, top your porridge with fresh fruit, nut butters and/or maple syrup, if desired.
