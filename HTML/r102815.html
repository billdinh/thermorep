Leftover Turkey and Veg Bake

Ingredients

  • 500 - 600 g mixed vegetables, cooked leftovers, cut in pieces
  • 500 - 600 g turkey meat, boneless and skinless, cooked leftovers, cut in pieces
  • 150 g Lancashire cheese, or other creamy hard cheese, cut in pieces
  • 100 g onions, quartered
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 4 fresh sage leaves
  • 25 g butter
  • 15 g plain flour
  • 350 g milk
  • fine sea salt, to taste
  • 150 g breadcrumbs
  • 1 tsp fresh thyme, leaves only

Preparation

  1. Fill a 6 cm deep baking dish (approx. 22 cm x 30 cm) with cooked leftover vegetables and turkey.
  2. Place cheese in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place onions in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 8. Spread over vegetables and turkey then season with pepper. Rinse and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Place sage leaves in mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 10. Transfer to bowl with cheese and set aside.
  5. Place butter, flour and milk (see tip) in mixing bowl then cook 6 min/90°C/speed 4. Meanwhile, preheat oven to 180°C.
  6. Add reserved chopped sage and cheese then mix 2 min/speed 2. Season to taste then pour over turkey and vegetables.
  7. Sprinkle breadcrumbs and thyme leaves evenly over the top. Bake for 30 minutes (180°C) to gently brown the top then serve hot.

Useful Items

baking dish (approx. 22 cm x 30 cm), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Increase the amount of sauce by adding up to 400 g milk, especially if using more than 1 kg meat and vegetables (combined).
  • Make your own breadcrumbs by putting bread pieces in mixing bowl then grind 10 sec/speed 10.
  • Works just as well with leftover roast chicken.
