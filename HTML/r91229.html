Chocolate Syrup

Ingredients

  • 9 oz sugar
  • ½ vanilla bean pod, cut into pieces
  • 9 oz water
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 3 ½ oz cocoa powder, (approx. 8 heaping tbsp)
Nutrition
per 1 Total recipe
Calories
5883 kJ / 1406 kcal
Protein
22 g
Carbohydrates
272 g

20 g

Preparation

  1. Place sugar and vanilla pod into mixing bowl and grind 25 sec/speed 10.
  2. Add water and salt, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and reduce 9 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  3. Add cocoa powder, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and reduce 4 min/Varoma/speed 4. Transfer chocolate syrup into a bottle or small carafe and serve hot or cold.

Useful Items

bottles or airtight storage container

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with desserts such as ice cream, semolina pudding, vanilla pudding, fresh fruit, pancakes, etc.
  • This chocolate syrup is well tolerated by many allergy sufferers, as it is dairy-free and nut-free.
  • This chocolate syrup will keep for weeks in the refrigerator.
  • Add 7 oz milk to any syrup remaining in the mixing bowl and heat 3 min/175°F/speed 2 to enjoy a delicious hot chocolate drink.
  • To make a thicker chocolate spread for bread: after final reduction place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and reduce chocolate syrup a further 2-3 min/Varoma/speed 2.
