Pumpkin Muesli Bars

Ingredients

  • 15 oz muesli
  • 4 oz honey
  • 4 oz canned pumpkin purée
  • 2 oz coconut oil
  • 2 oz desiccated coconut flakes, divided
  • 2 oz raw pumpkin seeds, divided
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 - 2 tbsp homemade pumpkin spice mix
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1577 kJ / 377 kcal
Protein
7 g
Carbohydrates
57 g
Fat
15 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven 350°F. Line a square baking pan (8 in. x 8 in.) with parchment.
  2. Place muesli, honey, pumpkin purée, oil, 1 oz coconut flakes, 1 oz pumpkin seeds, vanilla, salt and pumpkin spice mix into mixing bowl and mix 20 sec/speed 3.
  3. Transfer mixture to prepared baking pan. Sprinkle 1 oz coconut flakes and 1 oz pumpkin seeds over the mixture, gently press down and bake 18 minutes (350°F).
  4. Let cool completely in baking pan. Cut into 8 bars and serve.

Useful Items

parchment paper, square baking pan (8 in. x 8 in.), oven
