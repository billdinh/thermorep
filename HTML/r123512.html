Pasta and Chickpea Salad

Ingredients

Salad

  • 1200 g water
  • 10 g olive oil, plus 1 Tbsp
  • 2 tsp fine sea salt
  • 250 g dry pasta (e.g. penne)
  • 250 g cooked chickpeas
  • 200 g cherry tomatoes, quartered
  • 100 g watercress
  • 40 g mozzarella, diced (1 cm)

Vinaigrette

  • ½ fresh red chilli, deseeded if desired
  • 50 g olive oil
  • 20 g lemon juice
  • 15 g fresh parsley, leaves only
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper

Preparation

Salad

  1. Place water, oil and salt in mixing bowl then heat 10 min/120°C/speed 1.
  2. Add pasta and cook select time according to packet instructions/100°C/speed . Drain pasta using Varoma dish and stir through 1 Tbsp oil then leave to cool. Place chickpeas, cherry tomatoes, watercress and mozzarella in a serving bowl and toss to combine.

Vinaigrette

  1. Place chilli in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 10.
  2. Add oil, lemon juice, parsley, salt and pepper then mix 5 sec/speed 7. Pour onto salad and serve.
