Pecan Pie Cheesecake Bars

Ingredients

Biscuit Base

  • 75 g unsalted butter, diced
  • 160 g digestive biscuits, halved

Pecan Pie Layer

  • 110 g pecan nuts
  • 75 g light brown sugar
  • 100 g golden syrup
  • 80 g double cream
  • 50 g unsalted butter, diced
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt

Cheesecake Layer

  • 450 g cream cheese
  • 130 g caster sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Preparation

Biscuit Base

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Line base and sides of a brownie tin (32 cm x 18 cm) with baking paper.
  2. Place butter in mixing bowl and melt 2 min 30 sec/60°C/speed 2.
  3. Add biscuits and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Transfer to prepared tin and press down with the back of a spoon to form an even layer. Set aside in fridge while continuing with recipe.

Pecan Pie Layer

  1. Place nuts in mixing bowl and roughly chop 2 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place brown sugar, golden syrup, cream, butter and salt in mixing bowl then heat 6 min/100°C/speed 0.5.
  3. Add reserved chopped nuts and mix 10 sec/speed 2. Remove tin from fridge and pour nuts over biscuit layer, spreading out to edges, then set aside. Clean mixing bowl and make cheesecake topping.

Cheesecake Layer

  1. Place cream cheese, sugar, eggs and vanilla extract in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 3.5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then mix again 10 sec/speed 3.5. Gently pour cheesecake mixture over nut layer.
  2. Bake for 40 minutes (180°C) until golden. Leave to cool in tin then place in fridge to set completely (minimum 3 hours).
  3. Remove from tin, cut lengthways down middle then slice into 3 cm-wide bars and serve.

Useful Items

brownie tin (32 cm x 18 cm), baking paper, oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

Two classic desserts in one amazing traybake! The smoothness of the cheesecake is nicely complemented by the nuttiness of the pecans. This would be perfect served at a party.
