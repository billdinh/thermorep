Sun-dried tomato tartare

Ingredients

  • 2 - 3 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 4 - 5 sprigs fresh chives
  • 20 g baby pickled capers, rinsed
  • 1 dill pickled cucumber (approx. 40 g)
  • ¼ tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • salt, to taste
  • 125 g sun-dried tomatoes, drained
  • caper berries, for garnishing (optional)
  • bread or crackers, to serve (optional)

Preparation

  1. Place all ingredients, except sun-dried tomatoes, into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 6.
  2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and chop 3 sec/speed 6.
  3. Add sun-dried tomatoes and chop 3 sec/speed 6. Divide into 4 equal portions. Place a small round cookie cutter (4 cm) onto a serving plate. Press 1 portion into cookie cutter, then gently remove cutter. Repeat with remaining mixture. Garnish with a caper berry (optional) and serve with fresh bread or crackers (optional).
