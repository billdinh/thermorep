Dinner for 2 - creamy vegetable soup, steamed chicken with vegetable tagliatelle and mustard sauce

Ingredients

Soup, steamed chicken and vegetable tagliatelle

  • 3 - 3 ½ oz onions, cut into halves
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 oz olive oil
  • 1 - 2 carrots, medium
  • 1 zucchini, large
  • 14 oz water, plus extra for soup
  • 2 level tsp salt
  • 16 oz mixed vegetables (e.g. potato, zucchini, carrots, leek), cut into pieces
  • 16 oz chicken breast fillets, cut into strips (2 in. x ¾ in.)
  • ½ oz unsalted butter (optional)

Sauce

  • 2 oz crème fraîche
  • 1 tbsp mustard
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 3 - 4 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 1 egg yolk (optional)

Preparation

Soup, steamed chicken and vegetable tagliatelle

  1. Place onions, garlic clove and olive oil into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Use a vegetable peeler to peel carrots and zucchini into long thin strips similar to tagliatelle pasta, stop before reaching the core of the carrots or the seeds in the zucchini. Place carrot and zucchini tagliatelle loosely into Varoma dish and set aside. Cut remaining carrot and zucchini cores cut in pieces, add into mixing bowl and heat 1 min 30 sec/175°F/speed 1, without measuring cup.
  3. Add water and salt, insert simmering basket and weigh mixed vegetables into it. Place chicken on Varoma tray, insert into Varoma dish and place Varoma into position. Steam 20 min/Varoma//speed .
  4. Remove Varoma and keep warm. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and transfer contents into mixing bowl. Add butter and blend 1 min/speed 5-10, increasing speed gradually.
  5. Add water to the middle mark on mixing bowl (depending on type of vegetables and desired soup consistency) and mix 10 sec/speed 5.

Sauce

  1. To make the sauce, transfer almost all the soup into a serving bowl, keeping enough soup in the mixing bowl to cover the lowest blade of the mixing knife. Add crème fraîche, mustard, salt, pepper, parsley and egg yolk and mix 20 sec/speed 7, then heat 2 min/140°F/speed 2.

Serving

  1. Serve the creamy vegetable soup hot or allow to cool, cover and refrigerate for later use. Transfer chicken to a serving dish, arrange vegetable tagliatelle around the chicken and serve hot with sauce .

Hints & Tricks

  • In step 1, replace olive oil with 1 oz butter.
    - Replace chicken fillets with fish fillets (1 in. thick). For the sauce, replace mustard with freshly squeezed juice from half a lemon and add a pinch of cumin (optional).
    - Vary or add vegetables according to the season and your preferences. For instance, add broccoli florets or turnips, cut into pieces, to the Varoma and steam together with the vegetable tagliatelle.
    - Can cook up to 24 oz of chicken.
