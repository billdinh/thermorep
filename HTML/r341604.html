Asparagus with Romesco Sauce

Ingredients

  • 30 g blanched almonds
  • 25 g blanched hazelnuts
  • 1 garlic clove, unpeeled
  • 500 g water
  • 350 - 400 g green asparagus, woody ends removed
  • 20 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 ½ tsp sweet paprika
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 100 g roasted red peppers, preserved, drained, reserving 20 g oil, cut in pieces
  • 80 g tomatoes, quartered
  • ½ fresh red chilli, halved, deseeded (optional)
  • 1 Tbsp red wine vinegar

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C.
  2. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in almonds and hazelnuts. Transfer to a baking tray, add garlic and set aside.
  3. Place water in mixing bowl then place Varoma dish into position. Weigh in asparagus and drizzle with 20 g olive oil. Sprinkle ½ tsp paprika, ¼ tsp salt and 1 pinch pepper over asparagus then mix well to coat. Cover Varoma and steam 15-20 min/Varoma/speed 1 until asparagus is cooked. Meanwhile, roast nuts and garlic for 10 minutes (160°C). Leave to cool then peel garlic. Once asparagus is cooked, set covered Varoma aside to keep warm. Discard cooking water.
  4. Place reserved roasted nuts in mixing bowl then grind 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  5. Place reserved peeled garlic, preserved peppers, tomatoes and 20 g reserved oil from peppers in mixing bowl then blend 10 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides and lid of mixing bowl with spatula then heat 3 min/100°C/speed 1.
  6. Add reserved ground nuts, chilli (if using), vinegar, 1 tsp paprika, ½ tsp salt and 1 pinch pepper then blend 20 sec/speed 6-9, increasing speed gradually.
  7. Transfer asparagus to a serving dish and serve warm with romesco sauce.
