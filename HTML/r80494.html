Mediterranean seafood rice with chorizo

Ingredients

  • 400 g fresh mussels, shells scrubbed and de-bearded
  • 160 g onions, cut in halves
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 40 g olive oil
  • 200 g raw squid rings
  • 200 g mixed peppers (e.g. red, green), cut in pieces (3 cm)
  • 50 g spicy chorizo, cut in thin slices
  • 400 g canned whole tomatoes, including juice, roughly chopped
  • 1 ½ tsp salt
  • 2 pinches saffron threads (0.1 g)
  • 200 g water
  • ½ tsp chicken stock paste, homemade
  • 350 g white short-grain rice, for soup, 13 min cooking time (see tip)
  • 50 g raw prawns, peeled
  • 1 lemon, cut in wedges, for serving

Preparation

  1. Scrub and de-beard mussels if necessary, place in Varoma dish and set aside.
  2. Place onions, garlic cloves and olive oil in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 4, then sauté 4 min/120°C/speed .
  3. Add squid rings, mixed peppers and chorizo to mixing bowl. Set Varoma in position and cook 10 min/Varoma//speed . Set covered Varoma aside on a plate to keep mussels warm.
  4. Add tomatoes, salt, saffron, water and chicken stock paste to mixing bowl and cook 10 min/100°C//speed .
  5. Add short-grain rice and prawns to mixing bowl and cook 13 min/100°C//speed . Meanwhile remove mussels from their shells, reserving a few in their half shell to garnish, and set aside.
  6. Transfer rice to a serving plate, top with mussels and squeeze some lemon juice over the rice and mussels. Serve hot, garnished with lemon wedges and a few mussels on their half shells.
