Chocolate crackle surprise

Ingredients

  • 250 g Copha®, cut into 4 pieces (see Tips)
  • 50 g desiccated coconut
  • 50 g icing sugar
  • 30 g cocoa powder
  • 130 g crispy rice cereal (e.g. Rice Bubbles®)
  • 100 g milk chocolate chips
  • 100 g lollies of choice (see Tips)

Preparation

  1. Set aside 6-hole silicone sphere moulds (6.5 cm).
  2. Place Copha® into mixing bowl and heat 8-10 min/100°C/speed 1, until completely melted. Allow to cool slightly in mixing bowl (approx. 5 minutes).
  3. Add coconut, icing sugar and cocoa powder and mix 10 sec/speed 3.
  4. Add rice cereal and combine 10 sec//speed 3. Transfer mixture into a large bowl.
  5. Place 2 tablespoons of the mixture into each mould. Working quickly, use your finger tips or the back of a spoon to press mixture around all sides of the mould, right to the edge of the mould. Transfer moulds into refrigerator to set (approx. 10-15 minutes). Carefully unmould crackles and transfer onto a large plate, then place into refrigerator until needed.
  6. Using spatula, stir chocolate mixture to ensure Copha® is evenly distributed. Place 2 tablespoons of the mixture into each mould. Working quickly, use your finger tips or the back of a spoon to press mixture around all sides of the mould, right to the edge of the mould. Transfer moulds into refrigerator to set (approx. 10-15 minutes). Carefully unmould crackles and transfer onto a large plate, then place into refrigerator until needed.
  7. Using spatula, stir chocolate mixture to ensure Copha® is evenly distributed. Place 2 tablespoons of the mixture into each mould. Working quickly, use your finger tips or the back of a spoon to press mixture around all sides of the mould, right to the edge of the mould. Transfer moulds into refrigerator to set (approx. 10-15 minutes). Carefully unmould crackles and transfer onto a large plate, then place into refrigerator until needed. Recipe makes a total of 18 semi-circles. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  8. Place chocolate chips into mixing bowl and heat 2 min/50°C/speed 1.
  9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and heat 1 min/50°C/speed 1 or until melted. Transfer into a shallow bowl.
  10. Fill half of the crackle semi-circles with lollies of choice. Carefully brush edges of the remaining 9 semi-circles with the melted chocolate, then join the lolly-filled semi-circles and press together gently to seal and form a ball. Transfer into refrigerator to set for 1 hour (see Tips).
  11. Serve crackle surprises as party favours.

Useful Items

6 circle silicone sphere mould , large bowl, large plate, bowl, pastry brush, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Copha® is a solid vegetable shortening, and can be found at most major supermarkets in the refrigerated section.
  • Crackle surprises can be stored in the refrigerator in an airtight container for 1 week.
  • You can fill with small chocolates, jelly beans, or even a small toy.
  • Chocolate crackles: Place 1-2 tablespoons of crackle mixture into base of cupcake liners. Transfer into a sealable container and store in the refrigerator until ready to serve.
