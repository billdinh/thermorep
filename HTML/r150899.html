Honey Lime Chicken with Mango Chutney

Ingredients

Marinated Chicken

  • 30 g honey
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • ½ - 1 tsp sweet paprika, to taste
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 lime, zest (cut in 1 cm pieces) and juice
  • 4 chicken supremes, skinless (approx. 800 g) (see tip)

Mango Chutney and Steamed Chicken

  • 10 - 15 g fresh coriander
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 100 g onions, quartered
  • 75 g red peppers, cut in pieces
  • 1 - 2 fresh jalapeño chillies, deseeded, to taste
  • 500 g ripe mangoes, cut in small pieces (1-2 cm)
  • 2 tsp fine sea salt
  • 100 g sugar
  • 250 g water

Preparation

Marinated Chicken

  1. Place honey, pepper, salt, sweet paprika, garlic, lime zest and 20 g lime juice in mixing bowl then blend 30 sec/speed 8.
  2. Place chicken in a bowl and drizzle marinade over it, stir to coat then cover and allow to marinate for 30 minutes in fridge. Meanwhile, line Varoma tray with baking paper with enough to go 2 cm up the sides. Insert Varoma tray in Varoma dish. Clean and dry mixing bowl and continue with recipe.

Mango Chutney and Steamed Chicken

  1. Place coriander and approx. 50 g lime zest in mixing bowl then chop 10 sec/speed 8. Reserve 1-2 tsp coriander-lime zest mixture in a small bowl.
  2. Add garlic, onions, red peppers and jalapeño chillies then chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  3. Add mangoes, salt, sugar, 70 g lime juice and water. Arrange marinated chicken on lined Varoma tray, set covered Varoma into position and cook 28 min/Varoma//speed 1, until chicken is thoroughly cooked. Set covered Varoma aside on a plate to keep warm. Transfer 450 g mango chutney to a glass jar with screw-top lid (see tip), leaving approx. 5 mm. Make sure that the rim of the jar is clean and close immediately. Leave to cool completely and store in fridge or freezer (see tip). Transfer remaining mango chutney to a wide serving dish and allow to cool for a few minutes until lukewarm.
  4. Slice chicken diagonally and arrange over mango chutney in serving dish, then sprinkle with reserved chopped coriander-lime zest mixture. Serve warm, or refrigerate and serve chilled.

Useful Items

baking paper, cling film, glass jar with screw-top lid, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Steaming this zesty chicken keeps the meat beautifully moist. Any leftover mango chutney can be stored in the fridge and served with your favourite Thermomix® curry.
  • A chicken supreme is a breast of chicken with the wing bone still attached.
  • To prepare jar, place 500 g water in mixing bowl. Place jar and lid upside down in the centre of the Varoma and steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 1. Leave to drain - do not dry or touch the inside surfaces. Alternatively, thoroughly wash jar and lid in hot, soapy water. Rinse by pouring over boiling water. Prepare for filling, if needed, by placing on a clean tea towel in a roasting tin and heat in the oven at 100˚C until required.
  • Once cooled completely, mango chutney can be stored in the fridge for up to 2 weeks or in freezer-proof containers in the freezer for up to 6 months. Serve with steamed meat or fish, your favourite cheese, or a curry.
  • For a stronger flavour, marinate chicken in step 2 for a few hours or overnight.
  • For a complete meal, in step 5 steam in Varoma dish 400-600 g vegetables (e.g. carrots, potatoes, green beans, courgettes, broccoli), cut in bite-size pieces, until cooked as desired.
