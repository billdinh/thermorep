Bread and Tomato Soup, Rolled Spinach Omelette and Steamed Ratatouille Vegetables

Ingredients

  • 50 g extra virgin olive oil, plus extra for serving
  • 2 6 - 8 fresh basil leaves, to taste, 5-6 roughly torn
  • 60 g medium-hard cheese, (e.g. Gruyère, Emmental, Cheddar), cut in pieces
  • 2 garlic cloves,
  • 50 g extra virgin olive oil,
  • 50 g stale white bread, cut in pieces (1-2 cm)
  • 200 g frozen chopped spinach, defrosted
  • 4 medium eggs,
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt,
  • 50 g onions, quartered
  • 50 g carrots, cut in pieces
  • 700 6 - 8 g ripe tomatoes, cut in pieces
  • 1 1 - 2 fresh basil leaves, to taste
  • 500 g water
  • 1 heaped tsp vegetable stock paste, homemade
  • 400 g mixed vegetables (e.g. peppers, courgettes, aubergines), cut in pieces (2-3 cm)

Preparation

  1. Place cheese in mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place garlic and 10 g oil in mixing bowl, chop 5 sec/speed 5 then sauté 2 min/120°C/speed 1.
  3. Add bread and, without measuring cup, sauté 5 min/120°C//speed 1. Meanwhile, line Varoma tray with baking paper with enough to go 2 cm up the sides. Transfer sautéed garlic bread to Varoma dish and set aside.
  4. Place spinach, 10 g oil, eggs and ½ tsp salt in mixing bowl then chop 30 sec/speed 5.5. Transfer to lined Varoma tray, spread to the edges, sprinkle with reserved grated cheese and insert Varoma tray in Varoma dish.
  5. Place onions, carrots, 30 g oil, tomatoes, 1-2 basil leaves, water, vegetable stock and ½ tsp salt in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  6. Insert simmering basket and weigh in mixed vegetables. Place covered Varoma into position and cook 30 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove Varoma. With aid of baking paper lining, roll up spinach omelette, keeping it wrapped in baking paper. Meanwhile, remove simmering basket with aid of spatula, transfer cooked vegetables to a shallow serving dish, season with salt to taste and stir. Unwrap spinach omelette and cut in slices (approx. 2 cm). Transfer slices to serving dish with vegetables and keep warm. Transfer tomato soup to serving bowls, add reserved steamed garlic bread to soup, drizzle with oil and garnish with 5-6 roughly torn basil leaves. Serve soup hot as a starter and warm rolled omelette as a main dish with ratatouille vegetables on the side.

Hints & Tricks

  • A two course meal for four, all cooked at the same time in your Thermomix® – a delicious soup, followed by a beautifully steamed omelette with vegetables. Making two courses simultaneously couldn’t be easier!
  • Replace spinach in the rolled omelette with vegetables of your choice (e.g. cabbage, courgette).
  • For a smooth soup, after removing Varoma and simmering basket in step 6, blend 1 min/speed 5-10, increasing speed gradually. Add the steamed garlic bread and serve as described in step 6.
