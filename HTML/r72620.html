Damper with golden syrup butter

Ingredients

Damper

  • 450 g self-raising flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 1 pinch sea salt
  • 80 g unsalted butter, cut into pieces (2 cm)
  • 20 g golden syrup
  • 90 g water
  • 100 g full cream milk

Golden syrup butter

  • 70 g golden syrup
  • 200 g unsalted butter, softened

Preparation

Damper

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C. Line a baking tray with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place flour, salt, butter and golden syrup into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 6.
  3. Add water and milk and knead 20-25 sec/.
  4. Transfer dough onto a lightly floured silicone bread mat or work surface and shape into a round loaf (approx. 18 cm), then place on prepared baking tray.
  5. Dust with a little extra flour, then bake for 25-30 minutes (200°C), or until damper is cooked through and sounds hollow when tapped on the underside. Transfer onto a wire rack to cool. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Golden syrup butter

  1. Place golden syrup into mixing bowl and warm 30 sec/37°C/speed 1.
  2. Add butter and mix 30 sec/speed 3. Transfer into a sealable container and place into refrigerator until ready to use.
  3. Serve damper warm with golden syrup butter.

Useful Items

baking tray (20 x 30 cm), baking paper, silicone bread mat, wire rack, sealable storage container, oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Damper is traditionally wrapped in aluminium foil and cooked on the coals of an open fire. An outdoor pizza oven can also be used to bake damper.
  • To make your own butter to use in this recipe please refer to the recipe in The Basic Cookbook or the Everyday Cookbook.
