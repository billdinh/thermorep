Crème Fraîche Apple Pie

Ingredients

Pie Crust

  • 2 homemade pie crusts, chilled (9 in.)

Filling

  • 6 Pink Lady apples (approx. 44 oz)
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 6 oz crème fraîche
  • 2 large eggs, divided
  • 2 oz sugar
  • 1 oz brown sugar
  • 1 oz all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • ½ tsp ground ginger
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 1 ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp turbinado sugar (optional)

Preparation

Pie Crust

  1. Line a pie pan with one pie crust dough. Roll out the second pie dough to ¼ inch thick. Cut rolled dough into strips (½ in.-1 in.). Refrigerate pie crust and dough strips for 15 minutes. Meanwhile prepare apples.

Filling

  1. Core, peel and slice apples (¼ in.). Place a large bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in 32 oz apple slices. Add lemon juice, toss to coat and set aside.
  2. Place crème fraîche, 1 egg, sugar, brown sugar, flour, cinnamon, ginger, salt and vanilla into mixing bowl and mix 5 sec/speed 3.
  3. Place reserved sliced apples into chilled pie dough, piled high in the middle. Pour crème fraîche mixture evenly over apples. Create a lattice crust on top of apples with prepared dough strips and chill in freezer for 15 minutes.
  4. Preheat oven to 425°F. Place 1 egg into a bowl and lightly whisk with a fork.
  5. Brush top of pie with whisked egg, sprinkle with turbinado sugar and bake for 25 minutes (425°F). Reduce oven temperature to 375°F and bake 30-40 minutes (375°F), until golden brown. Let cool completely before serving.

Useful Items

pie pan (approx. 9 in.), pastry brush, large bowl, refrigerator, oven, freezer
