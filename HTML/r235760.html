Thai Garlic Pork with Rice and Vegetables

Ingredients

  • 80 - 100 g long grain rice, as desired
  • 80 g mangetout, trimmed
  • 1000 g water
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 10 g groundnut oil
  • 3 - 4 garlic cloves, to taste
  • 120 - 150 g pork fillet, sliced thinly (1 cm)
  • 1 Tbsp oyster sauce
  • 2 tsp light soy sauce
  • 1 - 2 tsp palm sugar, to taste
  • 50 - 100 g water, depending on desired sauce consistency

Preparation

  1. Insert simmering basket. Weigh rice into simmering basket, add mangetout on top of rice. Pour over water then sprinkle with salt. Cook 20 min/Varoma/speed 4. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and keep contents warm. Discard cooking water.
  2. Place oil and garlic in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, chop again 3 sec/speed 7 then fry 4 min/100°C/speed .
  3. Add pork and fry 4 min/100°C//speed 0.5.
  4. Add oyster sauce, soy sauce and sugar then cook 3 min/100°C//speed 0.5.
  5. Add water and cook 5 min/100°C//speed 0.5.
  6. Serve garlic pork with steamed rice and mangetout.
