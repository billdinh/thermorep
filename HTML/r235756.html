Sweet Potato Curry

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp oil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 shallots, halved
  • ½ - 1 red chilli, to taste, deseeded if desired
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 2 Tbsp tomato purée
  • 200 g water
  • ½ vegetable stock cube (for 0.5 l)
  • 250 g sweet potatoes, peeled, diced (2-3 cm)
  • 100 g broccoli, cut in florets (3-4 cm)
  • fine sea salt, to taste
  • ground black pepper, to taste
  • fresh coriander leaves, roughly chopped, to garnish
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
2428 kJ / 581 kcal

12 g

Preparation

  1. Place oil, garlic and shallots in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then sauté 5 min/100°C/speed .
  2. Add chilli, cumin seeds, curry powder and turmeric then fry 2 min/100°C/speed .
  3. Add water, vegetable stock and tomato purée then heat 5 min/98°C/speed .
  4. Add sweet potatoes and broccoli then, with simmering basket in place of measuring cup, simmer 10 min/98°C//speed 0.5 until sweet potato is tender.
  5. Season to taste, sprinkle with coriander and serve with rice or Indian bread.
