Banana Nut Crunch

Ingredients

  • 100 g blanched almonds
  • 100 g raw cashew nuts
  • 100 g walnut halves
  • 100 g blanched hazelnuts
  • 30 g freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 20 g honey
  • 1 banana, very ripe, cut in pieces
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
  • 30 g desiccated coconut
  • 20 g sesame seeds
  • 50 g banana chips

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 150°C and line a baking tray with baking paper.
  2. Place almonds in mixing bowl and grind 8 sec/speed 7.
  3. Add cashew nuts, walnuts and hazelnuts then chop 5 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place lemon juice, honey, banana and salt in mixing bowl then mix 5 sec/speed 8.
  5. Add reserved nut mixture, coconut and sesame seeds then mix 20 sec//speed 3. Spread on prepared baking tray and bake for 20 minutes (150°C) with a wooden cooking spoon handle in the oven door, so that it is slightly ajar (see tip). After 20 minutes, break up mixture with a spoon and mix to achieve an even colour. Bake for another 10 minutes (150°C), turn the mixture again then bake for a final 10 minutes (150°C) watching carefully, to prevent browning too much (total baking time approx. 40 minutes).
  6. Allow banana nut crunch to cool, then break into pieces. Add banana chips then transfer to an airtight container. Store in a cool, dry place and remove in portions.

Useful Items

baking tray and paper, wooden spoon, airtight container, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Swap sesame seeds for chia seeds.

  • Keeping the oven door slightly ajar helps to release any moisture and make the mixture as crunchy as possible.
  • The mix may still be soft after baking, but becomes firm after cooling.
  • This banana nut crunch is an energetic and gluten-free treat.
  • Serve with milk or yogurt and fresh fruit or just nibble on its own.
