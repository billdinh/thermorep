Ham and zucchini quiche

Ingredients

Pastry

  • 60 g cornflour
  • 150 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 150 g unsalted butter, chilled and cut into pieces
  • 30 g cold water

Filling

  • 50 g Parmesan cheese, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 1 sprig fresh dill, leaves only
  • 500 g water
  • 800 g zucchini, deseeded and flesh cut into pieces (3-4 cm)
  • 50 g olive oil
  • 1 brown onion (approx. 120 g), cut into quarters
  • 100 g ham, cut into pieces (approx. 1 cm)
  • 4 eggs
  • 50 g pouring (whipping) cream (optional)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper

Preparation

Pastry

  1. Place all pastry ingredients into mixing bowl and mix 20 sec/speed 6. Transfer pastry onto a silicone bread mat or lightly floured work surface and shape into a disc. Wrap pastry in plastic wrap and place into refrigerator for 45 minutes. Meanwhile, continue with filling. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Filling

  1. Place Parmesan cheese and dill into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  2. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh 600 g of the zucchini into it. Place Varoma tray into position and weigh remaining 200 g zucchini into it, then secure Varoma lid and steam 15 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove Varoma and set aside to drain. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Preheat oven to 180°C. Place a baking tray (30 x 40 cm) onto oven shelf.
  4. Place oil and onion into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5, then cook 5 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  5. Add reserved zucchini and mix 3 sec/speed 4. Set aside in mixing bowl for 10 minutes to cool.
  6. Add ham, eggs, cream (optional), salt, pepper and reserved cheese mixture and mix 4 sec//speed 3.
  7. Place pastry between 2 sheets of floured baking paper and roll out thinly (approx. 5 mm thickness). Line base and sides of a tart tin (24-26 cm) with pastry and trim edges. Carefully pour filling into tin and place onto heated baking tray. Bake for 40-45 minutes (180°C) or until firm and golden. Allow to cool slightly before serving.

Useful Items

silicone bread mat, plastic wrap, bowl, baking tray (30 x 40 cm), baking paper, rolling pin, tart tin (24-26 cm), refrigerator, oven
