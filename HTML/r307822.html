Citrus Salad with Beet Vinaigrette

Ingredients

Beet Vinaigrette

  • 3 oz olive oil
  • 3 oz red beets, peeled, cut into pieces
  • 2 oz water
  • 2 oz balsamic vinegar
  • 2 oz shallots, cut into pieces
  • 1 garlic clove
  • ½ tsp salt, to taste
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 3 oz orange juice
  • 1 oz champagne vinegar

Citrus Salad

  • 1 oz baby arugula
  • 1 Cara cara orange, peel and pith removed, sliced in rounds (¼ in.)
  • 1 red grapefruit, peel and pith removed, sliced in rounds (¼ in.)
  • 2 tangerines, peel and pith removed, sliced in rounds (¼ in.)
  • 2 oz fennel bulb, thinly sliced (approx. 1 sm. bulb)
  • 1 oz edible flowers
  • 1 oz pistachios, chopped (approx. ¼ cup) (optional)
  • 2 tbsp pomegranate arils/seeds (optional)

Preparation

Beet Vinaigrette

  1. Place a pitcher onto mixing bowl lid, weigh in olive oil and set aside.
  2. Place beets, water and balsamic vinegar into mixing bowl, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 7 min/Varoma/speed 2. Strain over a bowl through a fine sieve, reserve beet juice and discard solids.
  3. Place shallots, garlic, salt and pepper into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add orange juice, champagne vinegar and 2 oz reserved beet juice and mix 2 min/speed 4 while slowly pouring reserved olive oil onto mixing bowl lid, letting it drizzle around measuring cup in a thin stream onto rotating blades to create an emulsion. Return beet vinaigrette to pitcher and set aside.

Citrus Salad

  1. Place arugula on a serving platter and top with oranges, grapefruits, tangerines and fennel, and garnish with edible flowers, pistachios and pomegranate arils. Drizzle with reserved beet vinaigrette and serve.

Useful Items

pitcher, fine sieve, serving platter

Hints & Tricks

  • Not all flowers are edible, some are poisonous. Flowers should be purchased from a reliable source.
  • Those that suffer from pollen allergies, should not consume edible flowers.
