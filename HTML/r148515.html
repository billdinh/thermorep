Coconut and beetroot balls

Ingredients

  • 210 g raw beetroots, peeled and cut into pieces (approx. 2 cm)
  • 12 pitted fresh (Medjool) dates
  • 130 g desiccated coconut, plus extra for coating
  • 80 g raw cashews (see Tips)
  • 2 tbsp cacao powder
  • Â½ tsp vanilla bean paste
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
333 kJ / 79 kcal
Protein
1.4 g
Carbohydrates
4 g
Fat
6.1 g

Preparation

  1. Place all ingredients into mixing bowl and blend 15 sec/speed 7, until well combined and sticking together.
  2. Place extra desiccated coconut onto a plate and set aside.
  3. Using wet hands, roll tablespoons of mixture into balls (makes approx. 22 in total). Roll balls in desiccated coconut and place into refrigerator for a minimum of 30 minutes to set before serving (see Tips).

Useful Items

large plate, sealable container, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • You can use soaked, then dehydrated nuts in this recipe.
  • Store leftover balls in a sealable container in the freezer for up to 1 month.
