Cheat's cauliflower fried rice

Ingredients

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 450 g cauliflower, broken into florets
  • 500 g water, plus 1 tablespoon extra
  • 150 g frozen peas
  • 1 carrot, cut into matchsticks
  • 300 - 400 g raw prawns, peeled and deveined
  • 3 spring onions/shallots, cut into slices
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp gluten free soy sauce, plus extra to taste
  • black sesame seeds, to serve (optional)
  • crispy fried onions, to serve (optional)
  • fresh coriander, leaves only, to serve (optional)

Preparation

  1. Place garlic, ginger and oils into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7.
  2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then cook 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  3. Add cauliflower and chop 5 sec//speed 5. Transfer into Varoma dish and set aside. Rinse mixing bowl.
  4. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position and weigh peas into it. Add carrots and stir to combine. Insert Varoma tray and weigh prawns onto it, then add spring onions/shallots. Secure Varoma lid and cook 15 min/Varoma/speed 2 (see Tips).
  5. Crack eggs into a jug, add soy sauce and the remaining 1 tablespoon water and beat lightly with a fork. Place a large non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add a little oil. Add egg mixture and swirl around the base of pan, then cook for 5 minutes or until cooked to your liking. Remove cooked egg onto a clean plate or work surface, roll up into a log and cut into pieces.
  6. Transfer cauliflower, prawns and vegetables into a large bowl, season with extra soy sauce and stir to combine. Divide between serving bowls, then top with egg rolls, sesame seeds (optional), crispy fried onions (optional) and coriander (optional) before serving.

Useful Items

jug, non-stick frying pan, plate, large bowl, serving bowls, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • Baby's serve: wait until your baby is confidently managing finger foods (which may not be until 8-9 months or older) before offering them some of this Cauliflower cheat's fried rice, cut up into appropriately-sized pieces.
  • Toddler's serve: serve as is, cut up as needed.
  • To save time, cook the omelette while the vegetables and prawns are steaming.
