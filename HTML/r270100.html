Diane sauce

Ingredients

  • 6 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 100 g unsalted butter
  • 100 g brandy
  • 2 tsp tomato paste
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 200 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • ¾ tsp Chicken stock paste (see Tips)
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
882.7 kJ / 210.2 kcal
Protein
1 g
Carbohydrates
2 g
Fat
22.4 g

Preparation

  1. Place parsley into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place garlic into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add butter and sauté 4 min/Varoma/speed 2, without measuring cup.
  4. Add brandy and cook 5 min/Varoma/speed 2, without measuring cup.
  5. Add tomato paste, mustard, Worcestershire sauce, cream and stock paste and cook 2 min/90°C/speed 2, without measuring cup. Add reserved parsley and stir through with aid of spatula. Transfer into a jug to serve.

Useful Items

bowl, jug

Hints & Tricks

  • Please refer to The Basic Cookbook or Everyday Cookbook for the Chicken stock paste recipe. Alternatively, you can use any type of stock paste you have on hand.
  • Diane sauce is traditionally served with grilled steak, but it's also lovely with pork chops or a nut roast (vegetarian meat loaf).
