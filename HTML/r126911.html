Cabbage soup

Ingredients

  • 2 garlic cloves, core removed
  • 80 g onions, cut into halves
  • 1 tsp dried fennel seeds
  • 40 g olive oil, plus extra for serving
  • 600 g water
  • 1 tsp fine salt
  • 400 g white cabbage, cut into strips
  • 200 g potato, cut into pieces (1-2 cm)
  • 200 g canned butter beans
  • 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper

Preparation

  1. Place garlic, onion and fennel seeds into mixing bowl, chop 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add the olive oil and sauté 5 min/120°C/speed 1.
  3. Add water and salt, place Varoma dish into position and arrange the cabbage inside, insert tray and place the potatoes on top, close with the lid and steam 15 min/Varoma/speed 1. Carefully remove Varoma lid, turn upside-down and place Varoma dish on top.
  4. Place steamed cabbage and potatoes into mixing bowl, cook 5 min/100°C//speed 1.
  5. Add beans and cook 5 min/100°C//speed 1. Transfer into a soup tureen or large serving bowl.
  6. Serve hot sprinkled with black pepper and a dash of olive oil.

Useful Items
