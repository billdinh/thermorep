Focaccia with Rosemary and Honey

Ingredients

Dough

  • 13 ½ oz water
  • 22 oz bread flour
  • 5 tsp dried instant yeast
  • 1 oz extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 pinch sugar
  • 1 tsp salt

Topping

  • 3 oz extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 tbsp water
  • 3 oz honey, plus extra to drizzle
  • 1 ½ tsp salt
  • 1 tsp dried chili flakes (optional)
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary, leaves only, to sprinkle

Preparation

Dough

  1. Line a baking pan (approx. 14 in. x 16 in. x 2 in.) with parchment and set aside.
  2. Place water, flour, yeast, olive oil, sugar and salt into mixing bowl and knead 3 min/. With aid of oiled spatula, transfer dough onto prepared baking pan. With wet hands, flatten and spread dough to edges of baking pan.

Topping

  1. Place olive oil, water and honey into mixing bowl and mix 30 sec/speed 6. Brush mixture over top of dough with a pastry brush and sprinkle with salt, chili and rosemary.
  2. Dimple top of dough with bottom of spatula or fingertips and place in a cold oven. Set temperature to 350°F and bake for 50-55 minutes (350°F) or until crisp and golden brown. Serve warm with a drizzle of honey.

Useful Items

baking pan (approx. 14 in. x 16 in. x 2 in.), pastry brush, parchment paper, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Focaccia can be served with meals or as a sandwich bread.
  • Baking this recipe in a cold oven to start saves rising time. As the oven heats the dough rises in the oven.
  • The dough is quite wet but keeping its surface well oiled prevents it from sticking to fingers.
  • Use scissors to cut focaccia into squares.
