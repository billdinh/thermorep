Versatile Chunky Soup

Ingredients

  • 20 g olive oil
  • 100 - 150 g mixed vegetables, for sautéing (e.g. onions, shallots, leeks), cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 350 - 400 g mixed vegetables (e.g. courgettes, carrots, pumpkin, sweet potatoes, celeriac, parsnips), cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 50 g frozen mixed vegetables (optional)
  • 700 g water (see tip)
  • 1 vegetable stock cube (for 0.5 l), crumbled
  • ½ - 1 tsp fine sea salt, to taste
  • 60 - 80 g dried pasta, for soup (e.g. orzo), to taste
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
670 kJ / 160 kcal
Protein
5 g
Carbohydrates
20 g
Fat
5 g

Preparation

  1. Place oil, mixed vegetables for sautéing and mixed vegetables for soup in mixing bowl then sauté High Temp. .
  2. Add frozen mixed vegetables (if using), water, vegetable stock and salt then cook 15 min/100°C/speed 2.
  3. Add pasta and cook time indicated on packet/100°C//speed 1. Serve, garnished as desired.
