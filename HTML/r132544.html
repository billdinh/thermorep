Chicken Balls in Tomato Sauce with Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 400 g chicken breast fillets, skinless

Tomato Sauce

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 80 g onions, halved
  • 3 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 3 tsp mixed herbes de Provence
  • ¾ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 ½ tsp vegetable stock paste, homemade
  • 600 g tinned chopped tomatoes
  • 150 g water

Chicken Balls

  • 110 g Parmesan cheese, cut in pieces
  • 50 g bread, stale, cut in pieces
  • 60 g milk
  • ¾ tsp dried oregano
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 1 egg

Spaghetti

  • 1 - 2 garlic cloves, to taste
  • 15 g olive oil
  • 1200 g water
  • 1 ½ tsp salt
  • 270 g spaghetti

Preparation

  1. Cut chicken breast fillets in pieces (3 cm), freeze in a single layer for 30 minutes. Meanwhile continue with recipe.

Tomato Sauce

  1. Place garlic clove, onions, parsley and olive oil in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 8, then sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  2. Add mixed herbs de Provence, salt, black pepper, vegetable stock paste, chopped tomatoes and water then mix 7 sec//speed 4. Transfer to a casserole dish and set aside.
  3. Preheat oven to 200°C.

Chicken Balls

  1. Place Parmesan cheese and bread in mixing bowl then grate 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Remove semi-frozen chicken meat from freezer, place in mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 6.
  3. Add milk, grated Parmesan-bread mixture, oregano, salt, black pepper and egg then mix 10 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl, shape with oiled hands 16 meatballs (approx. Ø 4 cm) and arrange them in the casserole dish with tomato sauce. Bake for 20-30 minutes (200°C). Meanwhile clean mixing bowl and prepare the spaghetti.

Spaghetti

  1. Place garlic cloves and olive oil in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8, then sauté 2 min/120°C/speed 1.
  2. Add water and bring to the boil 10 min/100°C/speed 1.
  3. Add salt and pasta through hole in mixing bowl lid then cook without measuring cup for time indicated on packet/100°C//speed 1, or until al dente. Drain pasta through Varoma dish and serve hot with the meatballs in tomato sauce.
