Honey Sriracha Turkey Sliders

Ingredients

  • 6 oz honey
  • 3 oz sriracha sauce
  • 4 garlic cloves, divided
  • 3 oz yellow onions, cut into pieces
  • ½ oz parsley leaves
  • 10 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves only
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp red pepper flakes
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 20 oz ground turkey, broken in pieces
  • 2 oz panko
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 oz Monterey Jack cheese, cubed (½ in.)
  • 40 oz water
  • 12 slider buns

Preparation

  1. Place honey, sriracha and 2 garlic cloves into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place onion, 2 garlic cloves, parsley, thyme, salt, red pepper and black pepper into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  3. Add turkey, panko and egg and mix 10 sec/speed 4.
  4. Shape into 12 equal balls (approx. 2 oz ea.).
  5. Press 1 cube of cheese into each meatball and reshape into patties. Distribute patties onto Varoma tray and Varoma dish. Lightly brush patties with half the reserved honey sriracha sauce and set Varoma aside.
  6. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  7. Serve hot on slider buns with remaining honey sriracha sauce.

Hints & Tricks

  • Bake slider patties for 20 minutes (350°F) or until cooked through.
  • Serve with shaved red onion and cornichons or baby gherkins.
