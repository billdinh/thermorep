Chewy Pistachio Biscuits

Ingredients

  • 80 g granulated sugar
  • 90 g pistachio nuts
  • 30 g rice flour
  • 2 egg whites
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract
  • 2 tbsp icing sugar, for dusting
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
222 kJ / 52 kcal
Protein
1.4 g
Carbohydrates
6.8 g
Fat
2.3 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Line 2 baking trays with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place sugar in mixing bowl then mill 4 sec/speed 9. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place 30 g pistachio nuts in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place remaining 60 g pistachio nuts and 30 g reserved ground sugar in mixing bowl and mill 15 sec/speed 8.
  5. Add rice flour and mix 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Throughly clean and dry mixing bowl.
  6. Insert butterfly whisk . Place egg whites in mixing bowl and whisk 2 min/37°C/speed 3.5.
  7. With butterfly whisk still in place, whip 3 min/37°C/speed 3, slowly add remaining reserved ground sugar through hole in mixing bowl lid, 1 tablespoon at a time, until soft peaks form and meringue is glossy. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  8. Add vanilla and reserved pistachio and sugar mixture then combine 4 sec//speed 2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then repeat 4 sec//speed 2.
  9. Dot 10 teaspoons of biscuit mixture on 1 prepared baking tray leaving approx. 6 cm space between each, then top with a sprinkle of reserved crushed pistachios. Repeat with remaining biscuit mixture and second prepared baking tray then garnish with remaining crushed pistachio nuts.
  10. Bake for 9 minutes (180°C) or until browned around the edges. Dust with icing sugar while still warm, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely (approx. 20 minutes) before serving.

Useful Items

fine sieve, 2 baking trays, baking paper, wire rack, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • To ensure gluten free recipes are gluten free, always read the labels of your products to ensure no traces of wheat or gluten have been used as part of the production process.
  • Chewy almond biscuits: omit step 3. Substitute pistachios with 60 g blanched almonds in step 4 and garnish with 30 g flaked or slivered almonds instead of 30 g crushed pistachios before baking.
