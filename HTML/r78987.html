Lamb Moussaka

Ingredients

Eggplant and mince layers

  • 900 g water
  • 750 g eggplant (approx. 1.5), cut into round slices (0.5 cm)
  • 2 ½ tsp sea salt
  • 30 g olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 1 brown onion (approx. 150 g), cut into halves
  • 30 g red wine
  • 400 g canned chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • ¼ tsp ground cloves
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1000 g lamb mince, broken into pieces

Béchamel topping

  • 170 g Parmesan cheese, crust removed and cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 40 g unsalted butter
  • 40 g plain flour
  • 500 g full cream milk
  • ½ tsp sea salt
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper

Preparation

Eggplant and mince layers

  1. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position and weigh eggplant into Varoma dish and onto Varoma tray, ensuring steam will be able to circulate freely. Sprinkle eggplant with ½ tsp of the salt, then secure Varoma lid and steam 10 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  2. Carefully flip eggplant slices in Varoma dish and tray with tongs, then secure Varoma lid and steam for a further 6 min/Varoma/speed 1, until eggplant slices are cooked but still retaining their shape. Transfer eggplant slices onto paper towel to absorb most of the liquid and set aside. Rinse and dry mixing bowl, Varoma dish and Varoma tray.
  3. Preheat oven to 180°C. Grease a deep-sided baking dish (32 x 25 x 4.5 cm) with oil and set aside.
  4. Place onion into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add olive oil and sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  6. Add wine, chopped tomatoes, tomato paste, remaining salt, pepper, ground cloves and ground cinnamon to mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position and grease dish and tray with olive oil. Weigh lamb mince into Varoma dish and onto Varoma tray, then secure Varoma lid and cook 10 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  7. Stir contents of Varoma with spatula, then secure Varoma lid and cook for a further 5 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  8. Stir contents of Varoma with spatula again, secure Varoma lid and cook for a further 2 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  9. Transfer mince into mixing bowl and combine 5 sec//speed 4.
  10. Arrange half of the eggplant slices over base of prepared baking dish. Spread half of the mince mixture over eggplant, then cover with remaining eggplant slices. Top eggplant with remaining mince mixture and set baking dish aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Béchamel topping

  1. Place Parmesan cheese into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Rinse and dry mixing bowl.
  2. Place butter into mixing bowl and melt 3 min/100°C/speed 1.
  3. Add flour and cook 3 min/100°C/speed 1.
  4. Add milk, salt and pepper and cook 6 min/90°C/speed 4.
  5. Add 50 g of the reserved Parmesan cheese and combine using the spatula. Pour béchamel over the mince mixture in baking dish and sprinkle with remaining Parmesan. Bake for 35-45 minutes (180°C) or until golden brown. Serve hot.

Useful Items

tongs, paper towel, deep-sided baking dish (32 x 25 x 4.5 cm), bowl, oven
