Steak Stir Fry Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ oz extra virgin olive oil, divided
  • 3 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
  • 2 in. ginger, peeled, thinly sliced
  • 4 oz fresh shiitake mushrooms, thinly sliced
  • 5 oz carrots, spiralized
  • 5 oz zucchini, spiralized
  • 4 green onions, trimmmed, cut on bias
  • 8 oz filet mignon, thinly sliced (¼ in.)
  • 2 oz hoisin sauce
  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • 5 oz fresh baby spinach
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
2473 kJ / 591 kcal
Protein
41 g
Carbohydrates
36 g
Fat
34 g

Preparation

  1. Place 0.5 oz olive oil into mixing bowl and heat 5 min/250°F/speed 1. Add garlic, ginger and mushrooms and without measuring cup cook 6 min/250°F//speed 1. Meanwhile with a spiralizer, prepare carrots and zucchini.
  2. Add prepared carrots and zucchini, insert measuring cup and cook 2 min/250°F//speed 1. Transfer to a large bowl and set aside.
  3. Place 1 oz olive oil into mixing bowl and without measuring cup, heat 4 min/250°F/speed 1.
  4. Add green onions and beef and without measuring cup, cook 3-4 min/250°F//speed 1, until beef is cooked to your liking. Insert simmering basket, hold it in place with spatula to drain and discard cooking liquid.
  5. Remove simmering basket. Add hoisin sauce and sesame oil to beef mixture, stir through with spatula to coat. Toss vegetable mixture with spinach and top with beef and onion mixture. Serve warm.

Useful Items

large bowl, vegetable spiralizer

Hints & Tricks

  • Cooking time for beef will depend on how thinly it's sliced.
