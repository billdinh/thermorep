Crumpets

Ingredients

  • 8 oz whole milk
  • 9 oz bread flour
  • 1 tbsp active dry yeast
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 8 oz water
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • vegetable oil, to grease and fry
  • unsalted butter, to serve
  • jam, to serve

Preparation

  1. Place milk into mixing bowl and heat 2 min/105°F/speed 1.
  2. Add flour, yeast, salt, and sugar and mix 20 sec/speed 3. Let rest in mixing bowl 10 minutes. Meanwhile, grease a large bowl and set aside.
  3. Add water and baking soda and knead 2 min/. Transfer to prepared bowl and let rest 30 minutes.
  4. Lightly grease a frying pan and set over a low to medium heat. Grease the inside of egg rings and arrange in frying pan. Once rings are hot, spoon ⅓ cup batter into each ring. Cook 4-5 minutes until bubbles pop and batter is mostly cooked. Lift off the rings, turn crumpets over and cook a further 1 minute. Re-grease and reheat the rings and frying pan before cooking the next batch of crumpets. Serve Crumpets while still warm, spread generously with butter and jam (see Tip).

Useful Items

frying pan, egg rings, large bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • If making in advance, re-heat crumpets by toasting lightly on both sides before serving.
