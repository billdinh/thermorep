Chicken and Cheddar Meatballs

Ingredients

  • 4 oz cheddar cheese, in pieces
  • 4 oz yellow onion, in pieces
  • 2 oz carrots, in pieces
  • 2 oz celery, in pieces
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 16 oz ground chicken, broken into pieces
  • 2 oz bread crumbs
  • 1 large egg
  • ½ tsp salt, to taste
  • ketchup, to serve
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
247 kJ / 59 kcal
Protein
5 g
Carbohydrates
2 g
Fat
4 g

Preparation

  1. Place cheddar cheese into mixing bowl and grate 3 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place onion, carrots, celery, garlic and oil into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and sauté 3 min/250°F/speed 1.
  3. Add chicken, reserved grated cheddar, bread crumbs, egg and salt and mix 5 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix a further 5 sec/speed 4.
  4. Preheat oven to 400°F. Line a baking sheet with foil.
  5. Roll mixture into balls (approx. 1 oz) and place on prepared baking sheet. Refrigerate 20 minutes.
  6. Bake for 17-20 minutes (400°F), until cooked through. Serve with ketchup.

Useful Items

baking sheet, aluminum foil, oven, refrigerator
