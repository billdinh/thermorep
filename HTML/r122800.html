Spiced almond eggnog

Ingredients

  • 2 egg yolks
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 400 g unsweetened almond milk
  • 400 g milk
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp cornflour
  • ¼ tsp ground nutmeg, plus extra to serve
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon, plus extra to serve
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
850 kJ / 202 kcal
Protein
9.1 g
Carbohydrates
17.1 g
Fat
11.2 g

Preparation

  1. Place egg yolks, eggs and honey into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 5.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Add almond milk, milk and vanilla and cook 10 min/70°C/speed 2.
  3. Add cornflour. Using a fine-mesh sieve, sift nutmeg and cinnamon into mixing bowl and cook 14 min/70°C/speed 2, until slightly thickened. Blend 20 sec/speed 4 or until frothy. Remove butterfly whisk.
  4. Strain eggnog through fine-mesh sieve into serving glasses. Dust with extra nutmeg and cinnamon before serving.

Useful Items

fine mesh sieve, 4 small serving glasses

Hints & Tricks

  • If you’re making this eggnog for a special occasion, you can add 1-2 tbsp brandy or dark rum when adding spices.
  • Dairy-free eggnog: Replace the cow’s milk and use 800 g almond milk.
