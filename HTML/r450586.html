Cheesy Triangle Scones

Ingredients

  • 450 g self-raising flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 120 g unsalted butter, cold, diced
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
  • 120 g Cheddar cheese, mature, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 250 g milk

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C and line a large baking tray with baking paper or a silicone mat.
  2. Place flour, butter, baking powder and salt in mixing bowl then grate Turbo/1 sec/5-6 times until rough breadcrumbs.
  3. Add Cheddar and chop Turbo/1 sec/0 times.
  4. Add milk, mix 5 sec/speed 4 then knead 30 sec/. Tip out onto a floured surface and bring together into a ball. Roll out into a round (2.5 cm thick) then use spatula to cut into 8 wedges. Arrange on prepared tray and bake for 15-18 minutes (220°C) until cooked and golden. Best served fresh on day they are made.

Useful Items

large baking tray, baking paper, rolling pin, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • For a gluten-free version, use a gluten-free, self-raising flour and gluten-free baking powder.
  • For a dairy-free version, use a dairy-free spread and dairy-free milk (almond or oat milk work well). Substitute Cheddar with 25 g nutritional yeast flakes.
  • Add 1-2 Tbsp chopped parsley or chives if desired.
