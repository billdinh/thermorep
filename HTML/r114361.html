Spiced carrot porridge

Ingredients

Topping

  • 100 g crème fraîche
  • 200 g Greek yoghurt
  • ½ lemon, juice and finely grated zest, no white pith

Porridge

  • 60 g walnuts, plus extra for garnishing
  • 280 g carrots, cut into pieces (4 cm)
  • 160 g rolled oats
  • 950 g full cream milk
  • 40 g pure maple syrup, plus extra to serve
  • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste
  • 30 g raisins
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • ¼ tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1 pinch sea salt

Preparation

Topping

  1. Insert butterfly whisk. Place crème fraîche into mixing bowl and beat 1 min/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add yoghurt, lemon juice and zest and blend 40 sec/speed 2. Remove butterfly whisk. Transfer into a sealable storage container, then place into refrigerator until ready to use. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Porridge

  1. Place walnuts into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 4. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place carrots into mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and repeat chopping 4 sec/speed 5 if necessary to create small diced pieces.
  3. Add all remaining ingredients, including reserved chopped walnuts, and cook 15 min/90°C//speed 2.
  4. Serve porridge immediately with reserved topping, walnuts and a drizzle of maple syrup.

Useful Items

sealable storage container, bowl, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • You can substitute milk with any nut milk of choice.
  • You can replace the raisins with dried apricots or dates, and replace walnuts with pecans or pistachios.
