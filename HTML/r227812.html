Corn and Bacon Chowder

Ingredients

  • 8 oz bacon, cut into pieces (½ in.)
  • 6 oz yellow onion, quartered
  • 5 oz red bell pepper, cut into pieces (1 in.)
  • 32 oz frozen sweet corn, defrosted, divided
  • 15 oz reduced fat milk (2%)
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1146 kJ / 274 kcal
Protein
9 g
Carbohydrates
32 g
Fat
14 g

Preparation

  1. Place bacon into mixing bowl, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 20 min/Varoma//speed 0.5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, place simmering basket onto mixing bowl lid and cook a further 5 min/Varoma//speed 0.5. Transfer bacon into simmering basket positioned over a bowl to collect bacon drippings. Transfer bacon to a bowl, set aside 1 heaping tbsp of bacon for garnish and reserve bacon drippings.
  2. Place onion and red pepper into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5.
  3. Add 16 oz corn and 1 tbsp bacon drippings and without measuring cup sauté 10 min/Varoma//speed 1. Transfer to bowl with cooked bacon and set aside.
  4. Place remaining 16 oz corn and milk into mixing bowl and cook 15 min/195°F/speed 1 then increasing speed gradually blend 1 min/speed 9.
  5. Add reserved cooked bacon mixture and stir through with spatula. Garnish with reserved bacon and serve immediately.
