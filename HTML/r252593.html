Beef Stew with Ghoulish Mashed Potato Ghosts

Ingredients

Mashed Potatoes

  • 900 g floury potatoes, peeled, cubed (approx. 1.5 cm)
  • 340 g milk
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 30 g salted butter

Beef Stew

  • 850 g stewing steak, sinew free, cubed (approx. 2.5 cm)
  • 30 g Worcestershire sauce
  • ¼ tsp ground cloves (optional)
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • 110 g onions, quartered
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 80 g carrots, cut into 1 cm rounds (approx. 2 large carrots)
  • 80 g celery, cut into 1 cm rounds (approx. 2 stalks)
  • 80 g parsnips, cut into 1 cm rounds (approx. 2 small parsnips)
  • 2 Tbsp plain flour
  • 3 Tbsp tomato purée
  • 1 beef stock cube (for 0.5 l)
  • 350 g water
  • 110 g frozen peas, plus 12 for ghost eyes
  • 110 g sweetcorn kernels, frozen
  • 110 g white button mushrooms, fresh, sliced or diced

Preparation

Mashed Potatoes

  1. Insert butterfly whisk. Place potatoes, milk and 1 tsp salt in mixing bowl then cook 30 min/90°C/speed 1.
  2. Add butter and mash 30 sec/speed 3. Transfer to a bowl, set aside and keep warm. Clean mixing bowl.

Beef Stew

  1. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in meat. Add Worcestershire sauce, cloves, 1 tsp salt and pepper. Stir together and set aside.
  2. Place onions, garlic and olive oil in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and sauté 2 min/120°C/speed 1.
  3. Add reserved meat, carrots, celery and parsnips then cook 3 min/100°C/speed 1.
  4. Add flour, tomato purée, stock cube and water then cook 30 min/100°C//speed .
  5. Add peas, sweetcorn and mushrooms then cook 20 min/100°C//speed  until beef is tender.

Assembly

  1. Transfer stew into 6 serving bowls. Place mashed potatoes in an icing bag with a 5 cm cut base (without nozzle). Pipe ghost shapes into stew and garnish each ghost with 2 reserved peas for eyes. Serve immediately.
