Classic Egg and Cress Sandwiches

Ingredients

  • 400 g water
  • 4 medium eggs
  • 2 spring onions (approx. 25 g), cut in pieces (3 cm)
  • 120 g mayonnaise
  • 1 - 1 Â½ tsp Dijon mustard, to taste
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 2 pinches fine sea salt
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 8 slices wholemeal bread
  • 20 g cress, for garnishing

Preparation

  1. Place water in mixing bowl, insert simmering basket and place eggs into it. Steam 14 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  2. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula, run eggs under cold water for 2-3 minutes then peel. Discard cooking water.
  3. Place spring onions in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7.
  4. Add reserved boiled eggs, mayonnaise, mustard, cayenne pepper (if using), salt and pepper then mix 2 sec/speed 4.
  5. Divide egg mayonnaise mixture between 4 slices bread, top with cress then sandwich together with remaining 4 slices bread. Cut off crusts with a serrated knife then cut each sandwich into 3 fingers. Serve immediately.
