Curry powder

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp whole black peppercorns
  • 2 ½ tbsp coriander seeds
  • 1 tbsp cumin seeds
  • 2 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • 5 dried chillies
  • 1 tbsp rock salt
  • 2 tsp granulated garlic
  • 4 cardamom pods (see Tips)
Nutrition
per 1 total recipe
Calories
724 kJ / 172 kcal
Protein
7.5 g
Carbohydrates
17 g
Fat
6 g

Preparation

  1. Place all ingredients into mixing bowl and dry roast 8 min/Varoma/speed 2. Allow to cool, then mill 30 sec/speed 10. Transfer into a sealable jar or container to store until ready to use. 

Useful Items

sealable storage jar or container

Hints & Tricks

  • This fragrant blend is similar to a traditional Madras curry powder, perfect to enhance flavours in curries or when you are looking to add warmth to a meal.
  • You can substitute 4 cardamom pods with 1 tsp ground cardamom
