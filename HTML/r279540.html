Beetroot and Herring Open Sandwich (Smørrebrød)

Ingredients

  • 700 g water
  • 400 g raw beetroots, peeled, cut in pieces (1-2 cm)
  • 4 medium eggs
  • 1 tsp fresh dill, leaves only, plus extra for garnish
  • 140 g sour cream
  • 1 tsp wholegrain mustard
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt, or to taste
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper, or to taste
  • 8 slices coarse rye bread
  • 280 g marinated herring fillets (e.g. marinated in dill), drained, cut in pieces (drained weight)
  • 120 g cucumber, thinly sliced then halved
  • 25 g red onion, thinly sliced
  • 60 g pickled capers, drained
Nutrition
per 1 slice

1398 kJ / 333 kcal

Preparation

  1. Place water in mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish into position then weigh in beetroot. Cover then steam 25 min/Varoma/speed 2. Meanwhile, place eggs in simmering basket and set aside.
  2. Remove Varoma then, with aid of spatula, insert simmering basket with eggs. Place covered Varoma back in position then cook 8 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Set Varoma aside and leave beetroot to cool. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and leave eggs to cool then peel and thinly slice. Discard steaming water and clean mixing bowl.
  4. Once beetroot is cool, place 1 tsp dill leaves in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8.
  5. Add sour cream, cooled beetroot, mustard, salt and pepper then blend 2 min/speed 8 or until smooth, scraping down sides of mixing bowl with spatula when necessary.
  6. Spread beetroot purée over one side of rye bread slices. Arrange herring on top followed by slices of cucumber, red onion and reserved egg slices. Top with capers and garnish with extra dill then serve immediately.
