Tortilla with Spanish Salsa

Ingredients

Potato Tortilla

  • 200 g onions, quartered
  • 200 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 400 - 600 g potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 1 ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • 6 large eggs

Salsa

  • 200 - 250 g onions, quartered
  • 50 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 Tbsp tomato purée
  • 100 g white wine
  • 300 g water
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • 1 pinch ground cloves
  • 1 Tbsp fresh parsley, leaves only, roughly chopped

Preparation

Potato Tortilla

  1. Place onions and oil in mixing bowl then chop 4 sec/speed 4. Sauté 7 min/120°C/speed 4.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Add potatoes and ½ tsp salt then cook 13 min/120°C//speed 1. Remove butterfly whisk. Pour into simmering basket placed over a bowl to drain oil. Transfer drained potatoes to a large bowl.
  3. Place eggs and ¾ tsp salt in mixing bowl then beat 10 sec/speed 3. Pour over cooked potatoes then mix.
  4. Heat a non-stick frying pan over a high heat then pour in mixture and cook until golden on both sides (approx. 5-7 minutes). Set aside to cool slightly. Clean mixing bowl.

Salsa

  1. Place onions, oil and garlic in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 5. Sauté 7 min/120°C/speed 1.
  2. Add tomato purée and heat 2 min/120°C/speed 2.
  3. Add wine and reduce 3 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  4. Add water, salt, pepper, ground nutmeg and ground cloves. Replace measuring cup with simmering basket and cook 10 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  5. Add parsley and stir in with spatula. Cut tortilla into pieces in frying pan and pour over salsa. Stir well and heat for 5-10 minutes. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

large bowl, non-stick frying pan, frying pan, hob

Hints & Tricks

  • For a thinner sauce, crush 30 sec/speed 5 at the end of step 6.
  • You may find it easier to halve or quarter the tortilla in the pan before turning it over to cook the other side.
