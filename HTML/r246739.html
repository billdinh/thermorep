Apple shortcake

Ingredients

  • 500 g water
  • 500 g Granny Smith apples (approx. 4), peeled, cored and cut into eighths
  • 125 g unsalted butter, plus extra for greasing
  • 120 g raw sugar
  • 120 g self-raising flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 120 g plain flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 passionfruit, pulp only (optional)
  • 1 tsp lemon zest
  • caster sugar, for sprinkling
  • ground cinnamon, for sprinkling

Preparation

  1. Place water into mixing bowl, place Varoma dish into position and weigh apples into it. Secure Varoma lid and cook 10-15 min/Varoma/speed 1 or until just tender. Set Varoma aside and allow apples to cool completely (approx. 30 minutes). Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  2. Grease a springform cake tin (23 cm) and set aside.
  3. Place sugar into mixing bowl and mill 5 sec/speed 10.
  4. Add butter and chop 3 sec/speed 6.
  5. Add flours and egg and knead 1 min 30 sec/. Transfer onto a silicone bread mat or a lightly floured work surface and divide into 2 equal portions. Press 1 portion of dough into base of prepared cake tin. Place remaining dough and pastry case into refrigerator to chill (approx. 20 minutes).
  6. Preheat oven to 180°C. Remove pastry case from refrigerator and place reserved apples, passionfruit pulp (optional) and lemon zest into pastry case. Roll out remaining dough portion on a silicone bread mat or a lightly floured work surface. Cut into strips (1-2 cm). Arrange pastry strips over filling as desired (see Tips). Sprinkle with caster sugar and ground cinnamon and bake for 30-35 minutes (180°C) or until golden. Serve hot or set aside to cool completely before serving.

Useful Items

silicone bread mat, rolling pin, springform cake tin (23 cm), refrigerator, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with ice cream, whipped cream or vanilla custard.
  • To save time, you can replace the apples with 400 g tinned apples (pie fruit apples). Omit cooking the apples and then proceed with recipe.
  • You can plait or layer the strips of pastry over filling. Alternatively, use an egg flip to arrange pastry strips over filling in a rustic design.
