Chicken Korma

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp coriander seeds
  • 1 Tbsp cumin seeds
  • ½ tsp cardamom seeds
  • 250 g onions, quartered
  • 100 g ripe tomatoes, peeled, quartered
  • 30 g fresh root ginger, peeled, cut in round slices (2 mm)
  • 10 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 - 2 jalapeño chillies, fresh, halved, deseeded, to taste
  • 1 ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • ¼ tsp ground cloves
  • 80 g plain yoghurt
  • 100 g coconut milk, light
  • 850 g chicken breasts, cut in pieces (3 cm)
  • 180 g long grain rice

Preparation

  1. Place coriander, cumin and cardamom seeds in mixing bowl then toast 2 min/Varoma/speed 0.5. Grind 1 min/speed 9. Scrape down sides and lid of mixing bowl with spatula and grind again 1 min/speed 9. Scrape down sides and lid of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add onions, tomatoes, ginger, oil, garlic, chillies, salt, turmeric, cinnamon and cloves then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then cook 10 min/120°C/speed 1 .
  3. Add yoghurt and coconut milk then mix 30 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides and lid of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add chicken and stir in well with spatula. Cook 15 min/100°C//speed  . Meanwhile, cook rice on hob according to packet instructions.
  5. Serve chicken korma with the rice.

Useful Items

saucepan, hob

Hints & Tricks

  • To remove tomato skins easily, use a knife to make an 'X' in the skin at the base of the tomato then place tomatoes in boiling water for 1 minute. Remove tomato with a slotted spoon and allow to cool enough to handle, then peel off skin.

  • The curry can also be made using skinless and boneless chicken thighs although the calorie content will then be higher. Cut thighs in 3 cm pieces and cook for 30 minutes in step 4.
