Vegetable Patties

Ingredients

Cucumber Yogurt Sauce

  • 4 oz cucumber, in pieces
  • 6 oz plain yogurt
  • 2 tsp freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • &frac18; tsp salt

Vegetable Patties

  • 8 oz dried garbanzo beans
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 20 fresh cilantro leaves
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 3 oz onion, in pieces
  • 5 oz carrots, in pieces (1 in.)
  • 3 ½ oz celery, in pieces (1 in.)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 oz water
  • 3 oz frozen peas
  • 2 oz fresh sweet corn kernels
  • vegetable oil, to fry
  • mixed salad greens, to serve
  • ground paprika, to serve

Preparation

Cucumber Yogurt Sauce

  1. Place cucumber, yogurt, lemon juice and salt into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and refrigerate until ready to serve. Clean mixing bowl.

Vegetable Patties

  1. Place garbanzo beans, curry powder, turmeric, cilantro, salt and pepper into mixing bowl and grind 20 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place onion, carrots and celery into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  3. Add reserved garbanzo flour, eggs, water, peas and corn into mixing bowl and mix 5 sec//speed 5.
  4. Place a large frying pan over med-high heat. Add ¼ inch of oil into bottom of pan. Divide batter into 6 portions and press into a patties (see Tip). Once oil is hot, add patties being careful not to overcrowd the pan. Cook for 3-5 minutes per side, until golden brown. Repeat with remaining patties.
  5. Serve with mixed greens, Cucumber Yogurt Sauce and garnish with paprika.

Useful Items

large frying pan, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Press patties between two small plates or use a hamburger press.
  • For a spicier version, add 1 tsp chili flakes while grinding garbanzo beans.
