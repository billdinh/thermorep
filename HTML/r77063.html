Pea and Mint Risotto

Ingredients

Pea and Mint Purée

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 50 g shallots, halved
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 250 g frozen green peas
  • 100 g water
  • 1 bunch fresh mint leaves (approx. 10-15 g)

Risotto

  • 30 g Parmesan cheese, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 60 g shallots, halved
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 200 g dry white wine
  • 250 g risotto rice
  • 450 g salt-free vegetable stock, hot
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt or more to taste
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper or more to taste
  • 100 g frozen green peas
  • pea shoots, for serving

Preparation

Pea and Mint Purée

  1. Place garlic, shallots and oil in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and sauté 3 min/100°C/speed 1.
  2. Add 250 g frozen peas and water then cook 7 min/100°C/speed 1.
  3. Add mint then blend 2 min/speed 10. Transfer purée to a bowl and set aside, then rinse and dry mixing bowl. Rinse and dry mixing bowl.

Risotto

  1. Place Parmesan in dry mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer to bowl with pea purée and set aside.
  2. Place shallots and oil in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and sauté 3 min/100°C/speed 1.
  3. Add wine followed by rice and cook 2 min/100°C//speed .
  4. Add hot stock, salt and pepper and cook 10 min/100°C//speed .
  5. Add 100 g frozen peas and stir in with spatula. Cook 4 min/100°C//speed 
  6. Add reserved pea purée and Parmesan and stir in with spatula. Taste risotto and adjust seasoning if desired, then leave to rest for 2 minutes with lid on. Serve immediately in shallow bowls with a garnish of pea shoots.
