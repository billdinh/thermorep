Chicken and Cauliflower Curry

Ingredients

  • ½ tsp chilli powder
  • 3 tsp cumin seeds
  • 3 tsp coriander seeds
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • ½ tsp ground turmeric
  • 3 cardamom pods
  • ½ cinnamon stick
  • 6 black peppercorns
  • 1 Tbsp plain flour
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 400 g tinned chopped tomatoes
  • 50 g pitted dates
  • 400 g cauliflower florets
  • 1 Tbsp vegetable stock paste
  • 3 pinches fine sea salt
  • 500 g chicken thighs, skinless, boneless, cubed (3-4 cm)
  • 3 Tbsp fresh coriander, chopped, for garnish
  • 2 Tbsp fresh mint, chopped, for garnish
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1279 kJ / 304 kcal

16.6 g

Preparation

  1. Place chilli powder, cumin seeds, coriander seeds, ginger, turmeric, cardamom, cinnamon, peppercorns and flour in mixing bowl then toast 10 min/120°C/speed 1. Leave to cool for 10 minutes then grind 1 min/speed 9.
  2. Add garlic, onion and oil then chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  3. Add tomatoes, dates, cauliflower, stock paste and salt then cook 3 min/100°C//speed .
  4. Add chicken and cook 15-17 min/100°C//speed  until chicken is cooked through.
  5. Leave to cool slightly then serve garnished with coriander and mint.
