Crab Tart

Ingredients

Pastry

  • 1 tsp black peppercorns
  • 50 g Parmesan cheese, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 220 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 170 g butter, cold, diced (2 cm)
  • 1 egg yolk, from large egg
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt

Filling

  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 egg yolks, from large eggs
  • 280 g double cream
  • 2 tsp lemon juice
  • ½ tsp tabasco sauce
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 450 g crabmeat (half brown, half white)

Preparation

Pastry

  1. Place peppercorns in mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 10.
  2. Add Parmesan and grate 15 sec/speed 8.
  3. Add flour, butter, egg yolk and salt then pulse Turbo/2 sec/5-8 times until clinging together. Tip out and form a disc about 3 cm thick, wrap in cling film then chill for 30 minutes.
  4. Roll chilled pastry out on a lightly floured surface into a circle approx. Ø 30 cm. Line a fluted, loose-bottomed tart tin (Ø 28 cm) with pastry, pressing in well then trim edges with a knife. Prick base with a fork then place in fridge to rest for 30 minutes. Meanwhile, preheat oven to 180°C.
  5. Line pastry with greaseproof paper and fill with baking beans then bake blind for 20 minutes (180°C). Remove beans and paper then bake for a further 10 minutes (180°C). Leave in tin to cool on a wire rack.

Filling

  1. Place eggs and egg yolks in mixing bowl then beat 5 sec/speed 8.
  2. Add cream, lemon juice, Tabasco, salt and pepper then mix 5 sec/speed 5.
  3. Add crabmeat and stir in with spatula then transfer mixture to cooked pastry case. Bake for 30-40 minutes (180°C) until just firm to touch (centre should still have a slight wobble).
  4. Serve warm - best accompanied by mixed dressed salad leaves.

Useful Items

cling film, rolling pin, fluted tart tin (Ø 28 cm), wire rack, greaseproof paper, baking beans, refrigerator, oven
