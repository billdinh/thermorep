Shredded chicken and sweet potato salad with cranberry dressing

Ingredients

Chicken salad

  • 500 g water
  • 160 g sweet potato, peeled and cut into pieces (2-3 cm)
  • 30 g leek, white part only, cut into thin slices (2 cm)
  • 2 pinches dried oregano, to season
  • 2 pinches ground cayenne pepper, to season
  • 2 pinches ground coriander, to season
  • 300 g skinless chicken breast fillets, cut into thirds
  • extra virgin olive oil, to drizzle
  • 70 g mixed baby salad leaves (e.g. watercress, spinach, silverbeet)
  • 3 radishes, cut into thin slices (optional)
  • 50 g feta cheese, crumbled, for garnishing

Cranberry dressing

  • 50 g dried cranberries
  • 1 - 2 tbsp red wine vinegar, to taste
  • 70 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • ½ garlic clove
  • 2 - 3 tbsp water
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • sea salt, to taste
  • ground black pepper, to taste

Preparation

Chicken salad

  1. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh sweet potato and leek into it. Season sweet potato and leek with 1 pinch oregano, 1 pinch cayenne pepper and 1 pinch ground coriander, then drizzle with olive oil.
  2. Insert Varoma tray and weigh chicken breast onto it. Season chicken with remaining oregano, cayenne pepper and coriander, then drizzle with more olive oil. Steam 15-17 min/Varoma/speed 1. Check to ensure chicken and sweet potato are cooked. Once cooked, transfer chicken, sweet potato and leek onto a plate and place into refrigerator to cool (approx. 30 minutes). Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Place cooled chicken into mixing bowl and shred 3 sec//speed 5, or until desired texture is achieved.
  4. Arrange mixed baby salad leaves onto a serving platter (thinly slice larger greens, if required). Add sliced radish (optional) and reserved leek, sweet potato and shredded chicken. Garnish salad with feta cheese then place into refrigerator while you prepare the dressing. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Cranberry dressing

  1. Place cranberries into mixing bowl and chop 8 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add all remaining dressing ingredients and blend 20 sec/speed 8. Transfer dressing into a serving jug.
  3. Remove salad from refrigerator then, drizzle with cranberry dressing and serve immediately.

Useful Items

serving platter, serving jug

Hints & Tricks

  • Use leftover turkey breast from the Turkey and lemon aioli wraps or the Turkey breast roulade with apricot and hazelnut stuffing instead of chicken.
  • Recipe for one: reduce the salad ingredients by half and proceed as per recipe. Dressing quantities need to remain the same for accurate mixing. Leftover dressing can be stored in the refrigerator in an airtight container for 1-2 weeks.
  • Cherry tomato dressing: Place 150 g cherry tomatoes, ½ garlic clove, 30 g olive oil, 2 tbsp red wine vinegar, 20 g fresh basil leaves, 1 pinch dried chilli and salt and pepper to taste into mixing bowl and blend 10 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and repeat blending 10 sec/speed 6. Use dressing immediately or store in refrigerator until ready to serve.
