Asian Chopped Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 15 - 20 g fresh root ginger, peeled, cut in round slices (2 mm), to taste
  • 60 g honey
  • 60 g brown rice vinegar
  • 50 g sesame oil
  • 20 g vegetable oil
  • 20 g soy sauce
  • 1 pinch dried chilli flakes (optional)
  • 250 g red cabbage, cut in pieces
  • 120 g kale, tough stems removed
  • 1 carrot (approx. 120 g), cut in pieces (3 cm)
  • 1 red pepper (approx. 130 g), quartered
  • 1 yellow pepper (approx. 130 g), quartered
  • 3 spring onions (approx. 30 g), cut in pieces (3 cm)
  • 20 g fresh coriander leaves, plus extra for garnishing
  • 150 g edamame beans
  • 100 g roasted peanuts, unsalted
  • 30 g sesame seeds

Preparation

  1. Place garlic and ginger in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then chop again 2 sec/speed 7.
  2. Add honey, vinegar, sesame oil, vegetable oil, soy sauce and chilli flakes (if using) then mix 20 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place cabbage in mixing bowl and chop 1 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a large salad bowl and set aside.
  4. Place 60 g kale in mixing bowl, chop 2 sec/speed 6 then transfer to bowl with cabbage.
  5. Place remaining 60 g kale in mixing bowl, chop 2 sec/speed 6 then transfer to bowl with cabbage.
  6. Place carrot, red pepper, yellow pepper, spring onions and coriander in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 5.
  7. Add edamame beans, peanuts and sesame seeds then transfer to bowl with cabbage and kale. Add reserved dressing to bowl with chopped vegetables then stir with spatula to combine.
  8. Garnish with extra coriander then serve immediately.
