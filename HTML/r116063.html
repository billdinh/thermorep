Poached salmon and beetroot blinis

Ingredients

Beetroot and salmon topping

  • 1000 g water
  • 100 g raw beetroot, peeled and cut into small cubes (5 mm)
  • 350 g fresh boneless, skinless salmon fillets
  • 1 lemon, juice and zest only, no white pith
  • 100 g watercress
  • 1 tbsp Greek yoghurt
  • 1 tbsp cream cheese
  • 3 sprigs fresh dill, leaves only, plus extra for garnishing
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp freshly ground black pepper

Blinis

  • 2 tsp unsalted butter
  • 150 g buckwheat flour
  • 50 g wholemeal self-raising flour
  • 1 level tsp baking powder
  • 225 g full cream milk
  • 1 egg
  • ½ tsp sea salt, plus extra to season
  • olive oil spray, for frying
  • freshly ground black pepper, to season

Preparation

Beetroot and salmon topping

  1. Place water into mixing bowl, place Varoma dish into position and weigh beetroot into it. Insert Varoma tray and weigh salmon onto it. Secure Varoma lid and steam 15 min/Varoma/speed 2, or until the fish is just cooked through. Remove Varoma lid and place upside down, placing Varoma dish and tray on top. Set aside to cool. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  2. Place lemon zest into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 9.
  3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and repeat grating 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  4. Insert butterfly whisk. Place watercress, yoghurt, cream cheese and half of the lemon juice into mixing bowl and mix 3 sec/speed 4. Remove butterfly whisk. Transfer mixture into a separate bowl and set aside. Do not clean mixing bowl.
  5. Place dill into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  6. Add cooked salmon, reserved lemon zest, salt, pepper and remaining lemon juice and combine 3 sec//speed 3, or until salmon starts to break up.
  7. Add reserved yoghurt mixture and combine 10 sec//speed . Transfer into a bowl and place into refrigerator. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Blinis

  1. Place butter into mixing bowl and melt 40 sec/50°C/speed 4.
  2. Add buckwheat flour, self-raising flour, baking powder, milk, egg and salt and mix 30 sec/speed 5, or until smooth.
  3. Place a large frying pan over medium heat and lightly spray with oil. Place tablespoons of batter into frying pan and cook until bubbles appear and burst on the surface (1-2 minutes). Flip blinis and cook for a few seconds, until lightly golden. Transfer onto a plate and set aside to cool. Repeat with remaining batter, spraying pan with oil as needed (makes approx. 25 blinis).
  4. Place 1 heaped teaspoon of salmon mixture onto each blini. Top each blini with ½ teaspoon reserved beetroot and extra dill. Season to taste with salt and pepper before serving.
