Christmas Baked Cod (Portugal)

Ingredients

Breadcrumbs

  • 100 g cornbread, cut in pieces
  • 15 g olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 Tbsp fresh coriander, leaves only

Olive Topping

  • 100 g pitted black olives
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp grated Parmesan cheese
  • ½ lemon, juice only
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt

Cod

  • 800 g water
  • 500 g floury potatoes, peeled, cut in pieces (4 cm)
  • 2 pinches fine sea salt
  • 100 g onions, quartered
  • 65 g olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 800 g cod fillets, skinless, cut in pieces (4 cm)
  • 2 medium eggs, separated
  • 70 g double cream

Preparation

Breadcrumbs

  1. Place all breadcrumb ingredients in mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.

Olive Topping

  1. Place all topping ingredients in mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.

Cod

  1. Place water, potatoes and 1 pinch salt in mixing bowl then cook 20 min/100°C//speed . Drain potatoes using simmering basket and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.
  2. Place onions, 50 g olive oil and garlic in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Sauté 5 min/120°C/speed 1.
  3. Add cod and cook 7 min/100°C/speed 1. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Preheat oven to 200°C and grease a large baking dish (30 cm x 20 cm).
  5. Place reserved cooked potatoes, egg yolks, 15 g olive oil and cream in mixing bowl then mix 30 sec/speed 3. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Thoroughly clean and dry mixing bowl.
  6. Insert butterfly whisk. Place reserved egg whites and 1 pinch salt in mixing bowl then whisk 2 min/speed 3.5.  Remove butterfly whisk and gently fold whites into reserved purée.
  7. In prepared baking dish layer cod, mashed potatoes, breadcrumbs and olive topping. Bake for 25-30 minutes (200°C) until golden brown. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

rectangular baking dish (approx. 30 cm x 20 cm x 5 cm), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • In Portugal this is traditionally made with salt cod which has been soaked and then shredded before using.
