Dessert pizzas (Jo Whitton)

Ingredients

Bases

  • 80 g coconut flour
  • 300 g raw almonds
  • ½ tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 8 eggs
  • 130 g macadamia oil
  • 220 g coconut milk
  • 1 tbsp pure maple syrup

Chocolate hazelnut spread

  • 60 g hazelnuts, roasted and skins removed
  • 50 g dairy free dark chocolate, broken into pieces (approx. 2 cm)
  • 15 g raw cacao powder
  • 100 g coconut milk
  • 70 g coconut oil
  • 70 g pure maple syrup

Zabliagone

  • 3 egg yolks
  • 2 tsp pure maple syrup
  • ½ tsp natural vanilla extract
  • 500 g frozen mixed berries (see Tips)
  • 80 g flaked almonds

Preparation

Bases

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Line two large round pizza trays (30 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place coconut flour, almonds, bicarbonate of soda and salt into mixing bowl and mill 15 sec/speed 8.
  3. Add eggs, oil, coconut milk and syrup and blend 30 sec/speed 6.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and blend 10 sec/speed 6.
  5. Divide batter between prepared pizza trays, spreading batter with spatula to edges of each tray.
  6. Bake for 15 minutes (180°C) or until just beginning to brown. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Chocolate hazelnut spread

  1. Place hazelnuts into mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 8.
  2. Add chocolate and mill 8 sec/speed 8.
  3. Add remaining spread ingredients and cook 6 min/60°C/speed 3.
  4. Divide chocolate spread between cooked pizza bases, using spatula to spread to edges. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Zabliagone

  1. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg yolks,  syrup and vanilla  into mixing bowl and cook 10 min/50°C/speed 4.
  2. Divide mixed berries evenly between pizza bases. Drizzle zabliagone over berries and sprinkle with flaked almonds.
  3. Bake for 15 minutes (180°C), or until zabliagone is lightly browned at edges. Allow to cool for 5 minutes before slicing and serving.

Useful Items

2 pizza trays (30 cm), baking paper, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • You can use fresh or frozen berries in this recipe.
  • Pizza bases can be pre-made and parbaked through step 5. Allow bases to cool, then store in a sealed container in the refrigerator for 1 day, or in the freezer for up to 2 weeks. If frozen, thaw bases before proceeding as per recipe.
  • Recipe can be halved to make 1 pizza.
