Fatayer with cheese and mint

Ingredients

Dough

  • 250 g full cream milk
  • 80 g olive oil
  • 1 ½ tsp dried instant yeast
  • 350 g baker's flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt

Filling

  • 6 - 8 sprigs fresh mint, leaves only
  • 100 g Parmesan cheese, crust removed and cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 160 g cream cheese
  • 3 tsp nigella seeds
  • plain flour, for dusting

Preparation

Dough

  1. Place milk, oil and yeast into mixing bowl and warm 2 min/37°C/speed 1.
  2. Add flour, sugar and salt and knead 2 min 30 sec/.
  3. Transfer dough onto a silicone bread mat or lightly dusted work surface. Form dough into a ball and transfer into a large bowl. Cover with plastic wrap and leave to prove at room temperature (approx. 1 hour 30 minutes) until doubled in size. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Filling

  1. Place mint and Parmesan cheese into mixing bowl and chop 8 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add cream cheese and nigella seeds and mix 10 sec/speed 4. Transfer filling into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Preheat oven to 200°C. Line 2 large baking trays (40 x 35 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  4. Transfer dough onto silicone bread mat or a lightly floured work surface. Divide dough into 12 equal portions (approx. 50 g each). Roll out or press each portion of the dough into a square (approx. 15 cm). Roll up all sides to make a smaller square (approx. 8 cm). Place 1 tablespoon of the filling into the centre of each square and press out evenly to cover centre of dough. Twist opposite corners once to form a neat boat shape. Transfer onto prepared baking trays and bake for 15-17 minutes (200°C) or until golden. Serve warm or at room temperature.

Useful Items

large bowl, plastic wrap, bowl, baking paper, rolling pin, silicone bread mat, baking trays (40 x 35 cm), oven
