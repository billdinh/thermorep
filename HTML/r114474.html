Pumpkin and Spinach Pie

Ingredients

Filling

  • 200 g onions, quartered
  • 50 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 400 g pumpkin flesh, diced (3 cm)
  • 400 g fresh spinach leaves
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • 3 large eggs, beaten

Pastry

  • 400 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 70 g sunflower oil
  • 150 g water
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 egg, beaten, for brushing

Preparation

Filling

  1. Place onions and oil in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and sauté 5 min/120°C/speed 1.
  2. Add pumpkin and cook 10 min/120°C/speed 1.
  3. Add 200 g spinach and cook 2 min/120°C/speed 1. Drain mixture using simmering basket then return pumpkin mixture to mixing bowl.
  4. Add remaining 200 g spinach, salt, pepper and nutmeg then sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  5. Add beaten eggs and mix 10 sec//speed 3. Transfer to a bowl and set aside to cool. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl and make pastry.

Pastry

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Place flour, oil, water and salt in mixing bowl then knead 30 sec/. Tip dough out onto work surface, form into a ball and divide into 2 equal pieces.
  3. On a lightly floured work surface, roll out 1 piece of pastry (approx. 3 mm thick) and transfer to a tart tin (Ø 25 cm), allowing it to overhang sides of tin. Using fingers press pastry into sides of tin. Line pastry with baking paper and fill with baking beans. Bake blind for 5 minutes (180°C), remove baking beans and paper then bake for a further 3 minutes (180°C) until lightly golden. Meanwhile, roll out remaining pastry in a circle for a pie lid.
  4. Spread pumpkin filling over pastry, trim overhanging edges of pastry case then cover with reserved pastry lid. Press lid and bottom edges together with fingers to seal pie. Brush surface of lid with beaten egg, then make a small hole in middle of pie to allow steam to escape.
  5. Bake for 45-50 minutes (180°C) until golden brown and serve immediately.

Useful Items

quiche or tart tin (Ø 25 cm), baking paper, baking beans, rolling pin, long sharp knife, pastry brush, oven
