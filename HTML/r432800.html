Creamy Pea Soup with Serrano Chips

Ingredients

  • 80 g shallots, halved
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 3 sprigs fresh mint, leaves only
  • 300 g frozen green peas, defrosted
  • 500 g water
  • 100 g dry white wine
  • 200 g milk
  • 30 g plain flour
  • 2 vegetable stock cubes (for 0.5 l each), crumbled
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • oil, for frying
  • 100 g Serrano ham, sliced
  • 100 g cream (see tip)
  • 6 tsp crème fraîche

Preparation

  1. Place shallots, garlic and mint in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add peas, water, wine, milk, flour, vegetable stock, salt and pepper then cook 10 min/98°C/speed 2. Meanwhile, heat a little oil in a non-stick frying pan over a medium heat and fry ham until crispy then transfer to paper towels and set aside to cool.
  3. Add cream, heat 2 min/98°C/speed 2 then blend 20 sec/speed 10. Divide between serving bowls and top each with 1 tsp crème fraîche. Garnish with reserved Serrano ham and serve immediately.

Useful Items

non-stick frying pan, paper towels, hob

Hints & Tricks

  • Add single cream for a slightly lighter soup or double cream for a richer soup.
