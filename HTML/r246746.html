Tom kha gai

Ingredients

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 1 tsp oil
  • 1 - 2 fresh red chillies, deseeded and cut into thin slices
  • 2 tsp Chicken stock paste (see Tips)
  • 600 g water
  • 400 g coconut milk
  • 450 g boneless skinless chicken thighs, cut into strips (1 x 6 cm)
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 stalk lemongrass, cut into halves
  • 1 tsp raw sugar
  • 4 fresh Kaffir lime leaves
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 3 - 4 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only

Preparation

  1. Place garlic and ginger into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7.
  2. Add oil and chilli and sauté 1 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Add stock paste, water, coconut milk, chicken, fish sauce, lemongrass, sugar and Kaffir lime leaves and cook 20 min/90°C//speed .
  4. Add lime juice and coriander and stir with spatula to combine, then serve.

Hints & Tricks

  • Please refer to The Basic Cookbook or the Everyday Cookbook for the Chicken stock paste recipe.
  • Tom kha gai is a spicy and sour soup made with coconut milk, popular in Thai and Lao cuisines.
