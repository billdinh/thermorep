Kingfish pie

Ingredients

Dough

  • 150 g water
  • 350 g flour
  • 1 egg
  • 25 g milk powder
  • 30 g vegetable oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 1 tsp salt

King fish filling

  • 600 g white fish fillet, skinless, boneless (e.g. kingfish), cut into cubes
  • 50 g lemon juice, freshly squeezed
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 50 g tamarind paste
  • 50 g hot water
  • 50 g fresh coriander, roughly chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 200 g red onions, cut into quarters
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 300 g tomatoes, cut into quarters
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 tsp vegetable stock paste, homemade
  • 1 tbsp dried chilli flakes, adjust to taste

Assembly and baking

  • vegetable oil, for greasing
  • flour, for dusting
  • 50 g toasted pine nuts
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten with 1 tbsp water

Preparation

Dough

  1. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid, weigh water in it and set aside.
  2. Place flour, egg, milk powder, oil and salt into mixing bowl and mix 20 sec/speed 5.
  3. Mix speed 3 while slowly pouring reserved water onto mixing bowl lid, letting it drizzle around measuring cup in a stream onto rotating blades to create a soft dough. Transfer dough onto a work surface, divide dough into 2 equal pieces. Transfer into a lightly oiled bowl, cover with a towel and set aside for 1 hour. Meanwhile clean and dry mixing bowl.

King fish filling

  1. Place kingfish cubes in a bowl, add lemon juice and salt, refrigerate to marinate. Meanwhile, go on with the recipe
  2. In a small bowl mix tamarind in hot water and pass mixture through a fine sieve into a bowl to remove seeds and skins. Set aside.
  3. Place coriander into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 8. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place garlic, onions, ground cumin and oil into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  6. Add tomatoes, sugar, vegetable stock, reserved tamarind mixture and chilli flakes and chop 10 sec/speed 6.
  7. Place simmering basket instead of measuring cup on mixing bowl lid and cook 15 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  8. Add chopped coriander leaves into mixing bowl and mix with spatula. Transfer to a bowl and allow to cool.

Assembly and baking

  1. Preheat oven to 170ºC. Grease a round baking dish (approx. Ø 30 cm), dust with flour and set aside.
  2. Transfer pieces of dough onto a lightly floured surface and knead them briefly. Using a rolling pin, roll out one piece of dough into a circle slightly larger than the prepared baking dish and use it to cover base and sides of baking dish. Spread half of the tamarind-tomato sauce into dough-lined baking dish, place pieces of marinated fish over the sauce, spoon over the remaining tomato sauce and sprinkle with pine nuts. Roll out the second piece of dough and cover pie. Seal well around the edges, brush pie surface with an egg beaten with 1 tbsp water and bake for 30-40 minutes (170°C), or until golden brown. Serve warm.

Useful Items

tea towel, rolling pin, fine-mesh sieve, round baking dish (Ø 30 cm), pastry brush, bowl, refrigerator, oven
