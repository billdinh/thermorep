Tropical Cashew Rice

Ingredients

  • 9 oz jasmine rice
  • water, to submerge rice
  • 1 can coconut milk (13.5 oz)
  • 1 can coconut cream (13.5 oz)
  • 6 oz water
  • ½ tsp salt, to taste
  • &frac18; tsp ground black pepper
  • 12 oz Spam®, sliced (½ in.)
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 in. fresh ginger root, peeled
  • 8 oz yellow onions, halved, sliced in half moons (¼ in.)
  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 4 oz frozen peas
  • 4 oz cashews, roasted, unsalted, chopped
  • 4 green onions, thinly sliced rounds
  • 1 pineapple

Preparation

  1. Place simmering basket onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in rice. Remove simmering basket with rice and submerge in a bowl of water. Allow rice to soak for one hour.
  2. Rinse soaked rice under cold running water. Insert simmering basket with rinsed rice into mixing bowl. Add coconut milk, coconut cream, water, salt and pepper. Place Varoma dish without lid instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and steam 25 min/Varoma/speed 2. Meanwhile, heat a large skillet over medium heat. Pan fry Spam® slices (approx. 3 min. per side) until browned. Transfer to paper towels and set aside to drain. Dice seared Spam® (approx. ¼ in.) and set aside.
  3. Carefully remove simmering basket with spatula. Transfer steamed rice to a large bowl, set aside and keep warm. Empty and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Place garlic and ginger into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add onions, sesame oil, and soy sauce and sauté 5 min/250°F//speed 0.5.
  6. Add peas and reserved diced Spam® and cook 3 min/250°F//speed . Transfer to bowl with reserved rice. Add cashews and green onions and stir through with spatula. Slice unpeeled pineapple lengthwise, cutting through leaves and base. Score pineapple flesh and scoop to remove flesh. Fill pineapple halves with Tropical Cashew Rice and serve.

Useful Items

skillet or frying pan, paper towels, large bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • Use excess fresh pineapple in smoothies, salads, salsas and more.
