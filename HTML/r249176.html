Chocolate cream pie

Ingredients

Choc biscuit base

  • 100 g unsalted butter, cut into pieces, plus extra for greasing
  • 1 tbsp raw sugar
  • 250 g Chocolate Ripple biscuits™

Chocolate custard filling

  • 150 g dark chocolate chips
  • 70 g raw sugar
  • 60 g cornflour
  • 600 g milk
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract

Whipped cream

  • 250 g cream
  • 1 tbsp icing sugar
  • dark chocolate, shaved for garnishing

Preparation

Choc biscuit base

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Grease a round pie dish (approx. 24 cm).
  2. Place butter into mixing bowl and melt 3 min/100°C/speed 1.
  3. Add sugar and biscuits and crush 5 sec/speed 7. Transfer into prepared pie dish and evenly press into base and sides. Bake for 8 minutes (180°C). Set aside to cool (approx. 10 minutes). Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Chocolate custard filling

  1. Place chocolate, sugar and cornflour into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add milk, salt, egg yolks and vanilla and cook 7-10 min/90°C/speed 3 or until custard is thick. Transfer into pie dish over cooled base and set aside for a minimum of 30 minutes to cool to room temperature. Cover with plastic wrap and place into refrigerator for a minimum of 3 hours or overnight, until set.

Whipped cream

  1. Insert butterfly whisk. Place cream and sugar into mixing bowl and whip without setting time/speed 3, until soft peaks form, watching carefully to avoid overwhipping. Remove butterfly whisk. Spread whipped cream over pie and garnish with shaved chocolate before serving.

Useful Items

pie dish (24 cm), plastic wrap, oven, refrigerator
