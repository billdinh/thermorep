Pear Crumble with Coconut and Hazelnuts

Ingredients

Filling

  • 700 g ripe pears (e.g. Conference) peeled, cored, cut in pieces (2-3 cm) (approx. 8 pears)
  • 100 g water
  • 230 g sugar

Topping

  • 75 g hazelnuts
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 200 g plain flour
  • 150 g butter, cold, diced
  • 50 g desiccated coconut

Preparation

Filling

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Place pears in a baking dish (approx. 22 cm x 30 cm) and pour over water and 80 g sugar.

Topping

  1. Place hazelnuts in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place baking powder, flour, remaining 150 g sugar, butter and desiccated coconut in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 5.
  3. Add reserved chopped nuts and mix with spatula. Scatter crumble topping over pears in baking dish then bake for 40 minutes (180°C) until bubbling and golden. Serve warm - works well accompanied by pouring cream or ice cream.

Useful Items

baking dish (approx. 22 cm x 30 cm), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Vary the nuts according to taste. Almonds, cashews, walnuts or madacamia nuts also work well.
