Vegetarian Sausage Rolls

Ingredients

  • plain flour, for dusting
  • 640 g all butter puff pastry, frozen (2 ready rolled sheets)
  • 180 g onions, quartered
  • 1 Tbsp dried oregano
  • 100 g shelled walnuts
  • 90 g rolled oats
  • 150 g feta cheese, cut in pieces (3 cm)
  • 50 g breadcrumbs
  • 3 medium eggs
  • 1 Tbsp tamari
  • milk, for brushing
  • sesame seeds, for sprinkling (optional)
Nutrition

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C and line a baking tray with baking paper. On a floured work surface, halve puff pastry sheets.
  2. Place onions in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add oregano, walnuts and oats then chop 3 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add feta, breadcrumbs, eggs and tamari then mix 20 sec/speed 5.
  5. Place ¼ of filling on long edge of each pastry sheet half and roll. Cut each roll into 6 equal portions and arrange on prepared baking tray. Brush pastry with milk and sprinkle with sesame seeds (if using).
  6. Bake for 20 minutes (200°C) until crisp and golden. Leave to cool slightly before serving warm.

Useful Items

baking tray and baking paper, pastry brush, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Sprinkle 1 tsp cayenne pepper on pastry in step 1 for extra flavour.
  • Serve with tomato ketchup. These are also ideal for lunch boxes or snacks.
  • If you are not serving the sausage rolls immediately, leave to cool slightly before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely. Store in an airtight container for up to 3 days.
  • Freeze uncooked sausage rolls in an airtight container, separated by baking paper. Allow to thaw thoroughly before baking for 20 minutes at 200°C.
