Asian Vinaigrette

Ingredients

  • 5 g fresh coriander leaves
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 10 g fresh root ginger, peeled, cut in round slices (2 mm)
  • 50 g rice vinegar
  • 1 Tbsp honey
  • 2 Tbsp light soy sauce
  • 2 Tbsp sesame oil
  • 50 g groundnut oil
  • 15 g spring onions, cut in pieces (5 mm)

Preparation

  1. Place coriander in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place garlic and ginger in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 8.
  3. Add rice vinegar, honey, soy sauce and sesame oil then mix 10 sec/speed 6.
  4. Place a jug on mixing bowl lid and weigh in oil. With measuring cup in place, mix 1 min/speed 3 while slowly pouring reserved oil onto mixing bowl lid, letting it drizzle around measuring cup in a thin stream onto rotating blades to create an emulsion.
  5. Add spring onions and reserved chopped coriander, then mix using spatula. Serve immediately or store in a container in the fridge for up to one week.

Useful Items

jug, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve over a green salad topped with grilled chicken or tofu. This dressing also makes a flavourful dipping sauce for spring rolls or steamed buns.
