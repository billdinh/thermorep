Almond Cheese

Ingredients

  • 150 g blanched almonds
  • 160 g water, plus extra for soaking
  • 20 g lemon juice
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
Nutrition

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in almonds. Remove bowl and cover almonds with water then set aside to soak for approx. 6 hours.
  2. Place water, lemon juice, garlic, salt and drained almonds in mixing bowl then grind 15 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides and lid of mixing bowl with spatula then grind again 15 sec/speed 7. Strain mixture through a muslin cloth over a bowl, squeezing to ensure all liquid is removed. Discard liquid. Shape almond pulp into a ball, cover then set aside in fridge until ready to serve.

Useful Items

muslin or nut milk bag, refrigerator
