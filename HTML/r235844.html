Baked Thai Fishcakes with Chilli Lime Dipping Sauce

Ingredients

Chilli Lime Dipping Sauce

  • 3 - 4 fresh red chillies, halved and deseeded if desired, to taste
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 200 g water
  • 150 g caster sugar
  • 40 g white wine vinegar
  • 2 limes, zest of 2, juice of 1
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt

Fishcakes

  • 20 g fresh coriander, leaves only
  • 1 fresh red chilli, halved and deseeded
  • 20 g fresh root ginger, peeled, cut in round slices (2 mm)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 3 spring onions, cut in pieces (4 cm) (approx. 50 g)
  • 1 lime, finely grated zest only
  • 1 tsp light soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp cornflour
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp palm sugar
  • 350 g white fish fillets, skinless (e.g. cod, plaice), cut in pieces
  • 350 g large raw prawns, peeled
  • 1000 g water
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 250 g brown short grain rice, rinsed

Preparation

Chilli Lime Dipping Sauce

  1. Place chillies and garlic in mixing bowl then grate 3 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then grate again 3 sec/speed 8.
  2. Add water, sugar, vinegar, lime zest, lime juice and salt the, with simmering basket in place of measuring cup on mixing bowl lid, heat 20 min/120°C/speed 1. Transfer to a bowl and set aside to cool. Clean mixing bowl.

Fishcakes

  1. Place coriander in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8. Add chilli, ginger and garlic then chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add spring onions, lime zest, soy sauce, sesame oil, cornflour, cumin and palm sugar then mix 5 sec/speed 5.
  3. Add fish and prawns then chop 3 sec/speed 5.
  4. Shape mixture into 12 balls then flatten into fish cakes. Transfer to a plate, cover with cling film, and place in fridge to chill for 30 minutes.
  5. Preheat oven to 200°C and line a baking tray with baking paper.
  6. Place water and salt in mixing bowl then insert simmering basket. Weigh rice into simmering basket and cook 35 min/Varoma/speed 3.5. Meanwhile, continue with recipe.
  7. Place fish cakes on prepared baking tray and bake 25-30 minutes (200°C) until golden brown.
  8. To serve, divide rice between 4 plates, place 3 fish cakes on each, and serve with dipping sauce.

Useful Items

cling film, baking tray and baking paper, refrigerator, oven
