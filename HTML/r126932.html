Chicken parcels with vegetable stuffing and potatoes

Ingredients

Chicken parcels

  • 200 g courgettes
  • 300 g red peppers
  • 200 g mozzarella, diced
  • 1 pinch fine salt, plus extra to taste
  • 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper, plus extra to taste
  • 1 pinch dried basil
  • 800 g chicken breast fillets (approx. 4-6 fillets)
  • 1 tsp vegetable salt
  • 150 g yellow pepper
  • 600 g water
  • 400 g potatoes, sliced
  • 1 chicken stock cube (for 0.5 l)
  • 30 g plain flour
  • 30 g cream, 30-40% fat

Preparation

Chicken parcels

  1. Place 100 g courgettes and 100 g red pepper, cut into pieces, into the mixing bowl and chop coarsely 6 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add mozzarella, salt, pepper and basil and mix 5 sec/speed 3.Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Butterfly each chicken breast, open it and beat with a meat tenderiser. Stuff each escalope with some of the vegetable and mozzarella mixture and close the parcel with a toothpick. Season with vegetable salt and extra salt and pepper to taste. Arrange the parcels in the Varoma dish. Insert the Varoma tray. Cut the remaining peppers and courgettes into slices and arrange in the Varoma tray.
  4. Place water into mixing bowl, place the simmering basket in position and place the potatoes inside. Insert the Varoma and cook 30 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove Varoma and remove simmering basket with spatula and set aside. Keep potatoes warm until ready to serve. Retain the cooking liquid in the mixing bowl.
  5. Place stock paste, flour and cream into the mixing bowl and cook 3 min/100°C/speed 3. Then mix 15 sec/speed 5-10, increasing speed gradually.
  6. Remove the toothpicks from the chicken parcels and arrange the chicken parcels on a serving plate.
  7. Serve hot with the potatoes, vegetables and sauce.

Useful Items

meat mallet or rolling pin

Hints & Tricks

  • Variation for sauce: You can also serve the chicken parcels with a cold tomato sauce. At the end of step 4, empty the mixing bowl. Place 100 g onion and 1 clove of garlic into mixing bowl, chop: 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Add 400 g tomatoes (quartered), one tablespoon olive oil, ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper, ½ teaspoon herb salt or any type of flavoured salt, ½ teaspoon dried basil, ½ teaspoon pepper and 1 pinch sugar, mix: 12 sec/speed 5.
  • You can enjoy this dish with any type of vegetable. You can also serve it cold, cut into slices and used as a garnish for fresh salads.
