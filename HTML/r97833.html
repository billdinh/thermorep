Blueberry Vanilla Choc Brownies

Ingredients

  • 200 g blanched almonds
  • 120 g raw cacao nibs
  • 50 g raisins
  • 50 g cacao powder
  • 2 Tbsp blueberry powder
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract or powder
  • 70 g agave syrup
  • 40 g olive oil
  • 55 g water
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
191 kJ / 46 kcal
Protein
1.1 g
Carbohydrates
2.3 g
Fat
3.6 g

Preparation

  1. Line a cake tin (approx. 20 cm square) with greaseproof paper and set aside.
  2. Place almonds and cacao nibs in mixing bowl then grind 20 sec/speed 10.
  3. Add raisins and mix 7 sec/speed 10.
  4. Add cacao powder, blueberry powder and vanilla then mix 5 sec/speed 7.
  5. Add agave syrup, olive oil and water then mix 10 sec/speed 7. Press mixture into prepared tin. Chill in fridge for at least 2 hours. Cut into squares (approx. 2 cm x 2 cm) to serve.

Useful Items

square cake tin (20 cm), greaseproof paper, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • To remove any mixture from the blades, spin 5 sec/speed 10.
  • The brownies will keep in an airtight container in the fridge for up to two weeks.
  • Blueberry powder can be omitted (if so, then use less water) but is very easy to make in the Thermomix: dry fresh blueberries in a dehydrator then grind 1 min/speed 10.
