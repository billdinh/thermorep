Beef Stew with Ghoulish Mashed Potato Ghosts

Ingredients

  • 30 oz stew meat, beef, cubed (approx. 1 in.)
  • 1 oz Worcestershire sauce
  • ¼ tsp ground cloves (optional)
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • 32 oz russet potatoes, peeled, cubed (approx. ½ in.)
  • 12 oz whole milk
  • 1 oz salted butter
  • 5 oz yellow onion, cut into pieces (approx. 1 md. onion)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 oz olive oil
  • 3 oz carrots, cut into ¼ in. rounds (approx. 2 lg. carrots)
  • 3 oz celery, cut into ½ in. pieces (approx. 2 stalks)
  • 3 oz parsnips, cut into ¼ in. rounds (approx. 2 sm. parsnips)
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 3 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 heaping tsp beef stock paste
  • 12 oz water
  • 4 oz green peas (fresh or frozen), plus 12 extra peas to make ghost eyes
  • 4 oz yellow corn (fresh or frozen)
  • 4 oz fresh white button mushrooms, sliced or diced

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl on top of mixing bowl lid and weigh in stew meat. Add Worcestershire sauce, ground cloves, 1 tsp salt and pepper. Stir to coat beef and set aside.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Place potatoes, milk and 1 tsp salt into mixing bowl and cook 30 min/195°F/speed 1. Add butter and mash 30 sec/speed 3. Remove butterfly whisk. Transfer mashed potatoes to a bowl, set aside and keep warm. Clean mixing bowl.
  3. Place onion, garlic and olive oil into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and sauté 2 min/250°F/speed 1.
  4. Add reserved meat, carrots, celery and parsnips and cook 3 min/212°F/speed 1.
  5. Add flour, tomato paste, stock paste and water and cook 30 min/212°F//speed .
  6. Add peas, corn and mushrooms and cook 20 min/212°F//speed  until beef is tender.
  7. Transfer into 6 bowls. Place mashed potatoes into an icing bag with a 2 inch cut base (without nozzle). Pipe ghost shapes onto stew, garnish each ghost with 2 peas for the eyes and serve immediately.
