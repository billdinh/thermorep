Sweet Potato and Spinach Curry with Cauliflower Rice

Ingredients

  • 450 g cauliflower, cut in florets (3 cm), stalks removed
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 120 - 150 g onions, quartered (1 onion)
  • 2 - 3 garlic cloves, to taste
  • 1 red chilli, or to taste, halved, deseeded if desired (optional)
  • 2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 3 tsp curry powder
  • 600 g sweet potatoes, peeled, diced (3 cm)
  • 60 g tomato purée
  • 500 g water
  • 1 vegetable stock cube (for 0.5 l), crumbled
  • 200 g fresh spinach leaves
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt, or to taste
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper, or to taste

Preparation

  1. Place cauliflower in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then chop again 2 sec//speed 5. Transfer to Varoma tray, leaving some holes unobstructed for steam to circulate, then set aside.
  2. Place oil, onions, garlic and chilli (if using) in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then sauté 4 min/100°C/speed .
  3. Add cumin seeds and curry powder then sauté 2 min/100°C/speed .
  4. Add sweet potatoes, tomato purée, water and vegetable stock then cook 8 min/100°C//speed .
  5. Place covered Varoma into position and cook 8 min/Varoma//speed 0.5 until sweet potato is tender. Set covered Varoma aside.
  6. Add spinach, salt and pepper then, place covered Varoma back into position and cook 2 min/Varoma//speed 0.5. Serve curry hot with the cauliflower rice.
