Bangers and mixed mash with caramelised onions

Ingredients

Bangers and mixed mash

  • 400 g potatoes, peeled and cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 100 - 150 g cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 100 g water
  • 1 tsp salt, to taste
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper, to taste
  • 12 - 15 sausages of choice (2-3 per person)
  • 300 g frozen peas
  • 20 - 40 g salted butter

Caramelised onions

  • 2 red onions (approx. 300 g), cut into thin slices
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 60 g unsalted butter

Preparation

Bangers and mixed mash

  1. Place potato, cauliflower, water, salt and pepper into mixing bowl and cook 15 min/100°C/speed 1. Meanwhile, cook sausages as desired. Set sausages aside and keep warm.
  2. Add peas and butter to mixing bowl and cook 5 min/100°C/speed 1, then blend 10-15 sec/speed 7 or to desired consistency. Transfer mash into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and cover to keep warm. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Caramelised onions

  1. Place onion, sugar, vinegar and butter into mixing bowl and cook 10 min/Varoma//speed 1. Cook for a further 5 min/100°C//speed 1, without measuring cup.
  2. Divide mixed mash between serving plates and top with sausages and caramelised onions.
