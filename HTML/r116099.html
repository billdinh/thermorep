Mushroom Parmentier

Ingredients

Gratin

  • 30 g Parmesan cheese, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 30 g ground almonds
  • 30 g breadcrumbs

Mushrooms

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 3 - 4 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 50 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 500 g fresh wild mushrooms, sliced
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper

Potato Purée

  • 600 g floury potatoes, peeled, cut in small pieces (2 cm)
  • 300 g whole milk
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 30 g butter

Preparation

Gratin

  1. Place Parmesan in mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 9.
  2. Add ground almonds and breadcrumbs then mix 5 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.

Mushrooms

  1. Place garlic, parsley and oil in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 7. Sauté 5 min/120°C/speed 1.
  2. Add mushrooms, salt and pepper then sauté 10 min/120°C//speed 1. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.

Potato Purée

  1. Insert butterfly whisk. Place potatoes, milk, salt, nutmeg and pepper in mixing bowl then cook 30 min/90°C/speed 1. Towards end of cooking time, preheat oven to 200°C.
  2. Add butter and mix 40 sec/speed 3. Remove butterfly whisk.
  3. Spread out half the potato purée in a baking dish (approx . 22 cm x 30 cm) forming a layer. Pour over reserved mushrooms then cover with remaining potato purée. Sprinkle with reserved gratin topping. Bake for 15-20 minutes (200°C) until golden. Serve hot.
