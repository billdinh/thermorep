Leek and smoked salmon dip

Ingredients

  • 5 sprigs fresh chives, plus extra chopped for garnishing
  • 1 leek (approx. 180 g), trimmed and white part cut into pieces
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 30 g salted butter
  • 20 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 lemon, zested (no white pith) and juiced
  • 30 g dry white wine
  • 250 g cream cheese
  • 150 - 185 g hot smoked salmon, flaked
  • salt, to taste
  • ground black pepper, to taste

Preparation

  1. Place chives into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place leek and garlic into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add butter, oil and lemon zest and cook 10 min/100°C/speed 1.
  4. Add wine and lemon juice and cook 4 min/120°C/speed 1. Set aside in mixing bowl to cool (approx. 30 minutes).
  5. Add cream cheese and mix 10-20 sec/speed 4, until smooth. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  6. Add salmon, salt, pepper and reserved chives and mix 10 sec//speed 3 or until combined. Transfer into a serving bowl and garnish with extra chopped chives. Store in refrigerator until ready to serve (see Tips).

Useful Items

bowl, serving bowl, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • You can replace the chives with dill in this recipe, if desired.
  • You can store dip in a sealable container in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.
  • Use chilled dip to fill gougères, serve on biscuits or as part of a cheese board.
