Watermelon Canapés with Whipped Feta and Walnuts

Ingredients

Dressing

  • 1 sprig fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 1 sprig fresh mint, leaves only
  • 20 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt, or to taste
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper, or to taste

Whipped Feta

  • 150 g feta cheese, in pieces
  • 60 g cream cheese
  • 20 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 pinches fine sea salt
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper

Watermelon and Walnuts

  • 400 g watermelon (approx. half a large watermelon)
  • 40 g halved walnuts, toasted, for garnishing

Preparation

Dressing

  1. Place parsley and mint in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add oil, lemon juice, salt and pepper then blend 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer to a serving jug and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Whipped Feta

  1. Insert butterfly whisk. Place feta and cream cheese in mixing bowl then whip 20 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then mix 30 sec/speed 3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl again.
  2. Add all remaining feta ingredients and combine 20 sec/speed 3. Transfer whipped feta to a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Watermelon and Walnuts

  1. Cut watermelon into slices (1 cm thick), then using a cookie cutter (Ø 4-5 cm), cut out 30 watermelon rounds. Place watermelon rounds onto a serving plate.
  2. Top each watermelon round with 1 teaspoon whipped feta, then garnish with walnut pieces and a drizzle of reserved dressing. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

serving jug, serving plate, cookie cutter
