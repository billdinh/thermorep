Gingerbread Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

Sponge

  • 3 medium eggs
  • 25 g milk
  • 120 g sugar
  • 50 g plain flour
  • 50 g cornflour
  • 20 g cocoa powder, plus extra for dusting
  • 1 ½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 ½ tsp gingerbread spices (see tip)

Filling and Assembly

  • 6 gelatine leaves
  • 600 g whipping cream
  • 40 g vanilla sugar, homemade
  • 200 g white chocolate, in small pieces or callets
  • 30 g orange juice
  • 150 g blackcurrant jam

Preparation

Sponge

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C and line a springform cake tin (Ø 26 cm) with baking paper (do not grease rim).
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Place eggs, milk and sugar in mixing bowl then whisk 5 min/37°C/speed 4.
  3. Avoiding whisk, add flour, cornflour, cocoa powder, baking powder and gingerbread spices then mix 5 sec/speed 3. Remove butterfly whisk. If necessary, finish mixing by hand with spatula.
  4. Transfer to prepared tin and bake for 20 minutes (180°C). Leave to cool in tin completely before removing. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl and butterfly whisk.

Filling and Assembly

  1. Soak gelatine leaves in a bowl of cold water.
  2. Meanwhile, insert butterfly whisk then place 400 g whipping cream and vanilla sugar in mixing bowl. Whip speed 3 until stiff peaks form, watching carefully to avoid over-whipping. Remove butterfly whisk then transfer to a bowl and refrigerate.
  3. Place chocolate in mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add 200 g whipping cream and heat 2 min/60°C/speed 2.
  5. Add squeezed-out gelatine and mix 30 sec/speed 3. Transfer to a large bowl and refrigerate for 5-10 minutes.
  6. Add reserved whipped vanilla cream to chocolate mixture in large bowl and fold in with spatula.
  7. Slice cooled sponge horizontally. Place top half on a plate, cut-side up, and enclose it with a baking ring or springform tin. Cut bottom sponge half into 12 slices.
  8. Sprinkle orange juice over cake base in tin then spread jam over. Spread white chocolate cream over jam. Place the 12 pieces of cake carefully on top with the underside facing up, and refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
  9. Remove cake from tin, dust with cocoa powder then cut into 12 slices to serve.

Useful Items

springform cake tin (Ø 26 cm), baking paper, baking circle, oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • To make your own gingerbread spice mix, combine 2 Tbsp ground ginger, 2 Tbsp ground cinnamon, 1 Tbsp ground all spice, 1 Tbsp ground nutmeg and 1 Tbsp ground cloves. Store in an airtight container and use as required.
