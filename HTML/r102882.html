Oriental Pork Meatballs with Noodles

Ingredients

Meatballs

  • 350 g pork tenderloin, cut in 3 cm slices then frozen for 30 minutes
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 3 sprigs fresh coriander
  • 2 spring onions, cut in lengths (2 cm) (approx. 30 g)
  • 1 carrot, cut in pieces (approx. 100 g)
  • 10 g toasted sesame oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 1 large egg
  • ½ Tbsp Chinese five spice powder
  • 1 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 - 2 pinches dried chilli flakes (optional)
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt, or to taste
  • 3 pinches ground pepper, or to taste
  • water, for brushing

Vegetables and Noodles

  • 2 vegetable stock cubes (each for 0.5 l)
  • 1000 g water
  • 1 carrot (approx. 100 g)
  • 1 courgette (approx. 100 g)
  • 1 pepper (approx. 120 g)
  • 50 g tinned bamboo shoots, drained
  • 250 g egg noodles, dried, fine
  • 4 sprigs coriander leaves, as garnish

Preparation

Meatballs

  1. Place 150 g part-frozen pork tenderloin in mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place remaining 200 g part-frozen pork tenderloin in mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 6 . Add to bowl and set aside.
  3. Place garlic, coriander, spring onions and carrot in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides and lid of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add sesame oil, reserved minced pork, egg, Chinese five spice, soy sauce, chilli (if using), salt and pepper then mix 30 sec//speed 4 while stirring with spatula through hole in mixing bowl lid. Grease Varoma dish with oil.
  5. Using wet hands, form 1 heaped tablespoon of mixture into a golf ball sized ball and place in prepared Varoma dish. Repeat with remaining mixture to form 18 balls. Brush balls with water then set dish aside.

Vegetables and Noodles

  1. Place stock cubes and water in mixing bowl then bring to the boil 8 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  2. Place covered Varoma into position and steam 15 min/Varoma/speed 2. Meanwhile, prepare carrot and courgette in ribbons, slice pepper and drain bamboo shoots. Mix together then transfer to Varoma tray.
  3. Insert Varoma tray then cover Varoma and steam 10 min/Varoma/speed 2. Set Varoma aside.
  4. Place a large bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in noodles. Remove bowl then pour cooking water onto noodles and allow to stand until soft (approx. 3 minutes). Using tongs, transfer noodles to a serving dish and toss with steamed vegetables. Arrange meatballs on top and sprinkle with coriander. Serve warm with sauce of choice, such as soy sauce (see tip).

Useful Items

tongs, bowl, pastry brush, vegetable peeler, serving bowl, large bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • To give finished meatballs a crispy coating, finish off by frying in an oiled pan until lightly browned on all sides.
  • Drizzle dish with some of the fragrant cooking water instead of a sauce, if desired.
