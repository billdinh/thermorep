Chocolate Coffee Puddle Pudding

Ingredients

Pudding

  • 150 g butter, diced, plus extra for greasing
  • 300 g milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 300 g self-raising flour
  • 50 g cocoa powder
  • 250 g light muscovado sugar

Sauce

  • 2 Tbsp instant coffee granules
  • 500 g water, boiling
  • 150 g light muscovado sugar
  • 20 g cocoa powder

  • vanilla ice cream or cream, for serving

Preparation

Pudding

  1. Preheat oven to 170°C and grease a large ovenproof dish.
  2. Place butter in mixing bowl and melt 2 min/80°C/speed 1.5.
  3. Add milk and eggs then mix 10 sec/speed 4.
  4. Add flour, cocoa powder and sugar then mix 20 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then mix again 20 sec/speed 5. Pour into prepared baking dish, place on a lipped baking tray and set aside.

Sauce

  1. Dissolve coffee in 20 g boiling water.
  2. Place sugar, cocoa powder, remaining 480 g water and dissolved coffee mixture in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 5. Heat 3 min/100°C/speed 1. Pour gently over sponge mixture.
  3. Bake for 30-35 minutes (170°C) until risen and feels just firm to the touch. The sauce wil sink into the pudding and be runny underneath. Allow to sit for 10 minutes before serving hot with vanilla ice cream or cream.

Useful Items

ovenproof dish for serving, large lipped baking tray, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Can also be made in pudding basins for individual servings. Reduce cooking time to 15-25 minutes.
  • There is no need to wash the mixing bowl between making the pudding and sauce.
  • The coffee can be omitted for a pure chocolate pudding.
