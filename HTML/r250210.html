Meatballs in red pepper sauce

Ingredients

Meatballs

  • 150 g brown onion, cut into quarters
  • 2 rindless bacon rashers (approx. 100 g), cut into pieces (1 cm)
  • 40 g day old bread, crusts removed
  • 2 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 30 g Parmesan cheese, cut into pieces (3 cm) (optional)
  • 500 g beef mince
  • 1 tsp dried Italian herbs
  • 1 tsp ground sweet paprika
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper

Red pepper sauce

  • 20 g olive oil
  • 150 g brown onion, cut into quarters
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 20 g sultanas (see Tips)
  • 100 g ripe tomatoes, cut into quarters
  • 2 - 4 tsp Chicken stock paste, to taste (see Tips)
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 250 g roasted whole red pepper strips
  • 100 g water
  • 4 pita breads, warmed, to serve (see Tips)
  • crispy fried onions, to serve (optional)

Preparation

Meatballs

  1. Place onion, bacon, bread, parsley and Parmesan (optional) into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add mince, dried Italian herbs, paprika, salt and pepper and combine 15 sec//speed 7. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Red pepper sauce

  1. Place oil, onion, garlic, sultanas and tomatoes into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 8.
  2. Add stock paste, Worcestershire sauce, roasted red peppers and water and cook 10 min/120°C//speed 2. Meanwhile, roll mince mixture into balls (approx. 30 g each, makes approx. 20 meatballs) and divide between Varoma dish and tray.
  3. Place Varoma dish into position and insert Varoma tray. Secure Varoma lid and steam 12 min/Varoma/speed 4 or until meatballs are cooked. Remove Varoma and transfer meatballs and sauce into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl. Serve with pita bread and crispy onions, if desired (see Variation).

Hints & Tricks

  • You can replace sultanas with 1 tsp sugar, if desired.
  • Please refer to The Basic Cookbook for the Chicken stock paste recipe.
  • We served this dish with pita bread, but you can serve with any kind of side (such as pasta, zoodles or potatoes).
  • To mince your own meat, please refer to the guide in the front of The Basic Cookbook. Please note that it may increase the total time of the recipe.
  • To make crispy onions (to top meatballs): Line a plate with paper towel and set aside. Thinly slice 1 brown onion and separate into rings. Place 1 tablespoon plain flour into a bowl and toss onion rings to coat. Place a frying pan over medium-high heat, add 4 tablespoons oil and fry onions, stirring frequently, until crisp. Transfer onto prepared plate to drain, then serve with meatballs and sauce.
