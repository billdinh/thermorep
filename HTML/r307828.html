Shrimp and Crab Stack

Ingredients

  • 4 jumbo shrimp, unpeeled
  • 1 tsp salt, to taste, divided
  • 30 oz water
  • 2 ½ oz jasmine rice
  • 6 oz jumbo lump crab meat, drained, (3.5 oz when drained)
  • 1 avocado, small (approx. 3 oz flesh)
  • 2 tsp lemon juice
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • micro herbs, to garnish
  • 2 tbsp ponzu sauce

Preparation

  1. Place shrimp into Varoma, season with ½ tsp salt and set aside.
  2. Insert simmering basket into mixing bowl. place into simmering basket water, rice and ½ tsp salt and rinse 30 sec/speed 5, then cook 10 min/Varoma/speed 4. Place Varoma into position over rice, secure Varoma lid and steam 5 min/Varoma/speed 4. Remove Varoma and let shrimp cool.
  3. Remove simmering basket and set rice aside to cool. Rinse and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Place crab, avocado, lemon juice and pepper into mixing bowl and mix 5 sec//speed 3. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  5. To assemble the stack use a ring mold to layer in half the reserved rice and press down firmly. Add half the crab mixture and press down.
  6. With kitchen shears, cut along the outer curve of each shrimp shell, cutting through the shell and about halfway into the shrimp itself, exposing any vein. Rinse shrimp under cold water to remove the vein, then peel the shrimp, leaving tail intact. Place two shrimp on top of the crab mixture. Carefully remove ring mold and garnish with half the micro herbs. Repeat with remaining ingredients. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
  7. Drizzle with ponzu sauce just before serving.

Useful Items

ring mold (approx. 3 in. x 2 in.), kitchen scissors, refrigerator
