Chocolate Chestnut Cheesecake

Ingredients

Pastry Base

  • 50 g caster sugar
  • 100 g unsalted butter, diced
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
  • 2 egg yolks, from medium eggs
  • 70 g plain flour

Filling

  • 100 g dark chocolate, small pieces or callets
  • 100 g caster sugar
  • 220 g double cream
  • 250 g chestnut purée
  • 250 g cream cheese
  • 3 medium eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1200 g water

Chocolate Ganache Topping

  • 100 g dark chocolate, small pieces or callets
  • 80 g double cream

Preparation

Pastry Base

  1. Line base of a springform tin (Ø 20 cm, see tip) with baking paper.
  2. Place sugar in mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 10.
  3. Add butter and vanilla extract then mix 20 sec/speed 4.
  4. Add baking powder, salt, egg yolks and flour then mix 10 sec/speed 3. Transfer to prepared tin. With wet hands, press dough down evenly into tin then place in freezer for 20 minutes (or in fridge for 2 hours).
  5. Towards the end of this time, preheat oven to 150°C. Bake pastry base for 30-35 minutes (150°C) until lightly golden. Leave in tin to cool completely before continuing with recipe.

Filling

  1. Place chocolate and 50 g sugar in mixing bowl then grind 8 sec/speed 9.
  2. Add 110 g cream and melt 4 min/50°C/speed 1.
  3. Add 120 g chestnut purée and mix 10 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then mix again 10 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place remaining 110 g cream, 50 g sugar, 130 g chestnut purée, cream cheese, eggs and vanilla extract in mixing bowl then mix 20 sec/speed 8.
  5. Add reserved chocolate mixture and mix 5 sec/speed 3. Transfer to tin on top of cooled pastry case.
  6. Place water in mixing bowl and place cheesecake in Varoma dish. Place covered Varoma into position and steam 50 min/Varoma/speed 3. If any steaming water has collected on top of cheesecake, dab with a paper towel. Leave in tin to cool, in fridge, for several hours or overnight. Clean mixing bowl.

Chocolate Ganache Topping

  1. Place chocolate in mixing bowl and grate 10 sec//speed 9.
  2. Add cream and heat 3 min/50°C/speed 3. Pour ganache over chilled cheesecake and serve in slices.

Useful Items

springform tin (Ø 20 cm), baking paper, paper towel, freezer, oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • It is important to use a 20 cm tin which sits just proud of the rim of the base of the Varoma dish. This will allow the steam to circulate properly as it shouldn't block any of the holes.
  • Once the ganache is in place, you can add more decorations such as nuts, chocolate truffles or fresh fruit.
