Paneer makhani

Ingredients

  • 30 g raw cashews
  • hot water, for soaking
  • 400 g fresh tomatoes, cut into halves and deseeded
  • 1 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 2 cardamom pods (green)
  • 1 cinnamon quill
  • 2 whole cloves
  • 1 dried bay leaf
  • ½ tsp fenugreek seeds
  • 1 fresh long green chilli, trimmed, cut into halves and deseeded
  • 1 tsp garlic paste (see Tips)
  • ½ tsp ginger paste (see Tips)
  • ¼ - ½ tsp Kashmiri chilli powder
  • ½ tsp ground cumin
  • 1 pinch ground turmeric
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 - 1 ¼ tsp garam masala (see Tips)
  • 180 g water
  • ½ tsp sugar
  • 100 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 250 - 300 g paneer, cut into batons (1 x 6 cm) (see Tips)
  • canola oil, for frying

Preparation

  1. Place a small bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh cashews into it. Cover with hot water and set aside to soak for 5 minutes. Drain cashews and discard soaking liquid.
  2. Place tomatoes and drained cashews into mixing bowl and blend 20 sec/speed 8 or until smooth. Transfer into a jug and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Place butter into mixing bowl and melt 1 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  4. Add cardamom pods, cinnamon, whole cloves, bay leaf, fenugreek seeds and green chilli and sauté 2 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  5. Add garlic paste and ginger paste and sauté 1 min/100°C/speed 1.
  6. Add reserved tomato purée and cook 5 min/Varoma/speed 1, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid, until thickened.
  7. Add chilli powder, cumin, turmeric and salt and sauté 4 min/100°C/speed 1, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  8. Add garam masala, water and sugar and cook 5 min/100°C/speed 1, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid, or until sauce thickens.
  9. Add cream and mix 40 sec/80°C/speed 2. Transfer into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl. Carefully remove whole cloves and bay leaf and discard. Cover to keep warm.
  10. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh paneer into it. Set aside.
  11. Place a large frying pan over medium heat and add oil. Place paneer into pan and cook 4-5 minutes, turning once, until lightly browned. Transfer curry into serving bowl and top with paneer. Serve warm.

Hints & Tricks

  • To make your own garlic paste, ginger paste, garam masala and paneer, please refer to the recipes on this recipe chip.
  • You can serve this with rice, bread, chutney or your favourite side. We served ours with Naan, please refer to the recipe on this recipe chip. Garnish with coriander, if desired.
