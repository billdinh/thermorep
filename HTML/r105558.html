Taramasalata

Ingredients

  • 150 g day-old white bread, crust removed and bread cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 200 g water
  • 20 g brown onion (optional)
  • 50 g salmon roe
  • 20 g lemon juice, to taste
  • 30 g olive oil

Preparation

  1. Place a ceramic or glass bowl onto mixing bowl lid, then weigh bread and water into it. Set aside for 5 minutes to allow bread to absorb water.
  2. Using simmering basket, drain bread, squeezing out any excess liquid with aid of spatula. Set bread aside.
  3. Place onion (optional) into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add roe and lemon juice and mix 1 min/speed 7.
  5. Add oil and reserved bread and mix 20 sec/speed 6, with aid of spatula, until combined.
  6. Serve immediately or place into refrigerator until ready to use.

Useful Items

ceramic or glass bowl, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with crusty bread.
  • For a milder taste, omit onion in step 3.
