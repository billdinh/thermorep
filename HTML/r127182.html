Beetroot hummus

Ingredients

Hummus

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 200 g canned chickpeas, drained
  • 300 g beetroot, cooked
  • 30 g lemon juice, freshly squeezed
  • 50 g tahini (see tip)
  • 1 tsp fine salt
  • 50 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 pinch curry powder
  • 1 pinch ground cumin

Presentation

  • 24 Belgian endive leaves (approx. 2-3 heads)
  • 200 g beetroot, cooked and cut into very thin slices
  • 1 dash extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp toasted sesame seeds

Preparation

Hummus

  1. Place garlic, chickpeas, beetroot, lemon juice, tahini (or sesame seeds), salt, olive oil, curry and cumin into mixing bowl and blend 1 min/speed 5-10, increasing speed gradually.

Presentation

  1. Place 1 slice of beetroot on each endive leaf, top with 1 tbsp hummus and another slice of beetroot coiled like a cone. Sprinkle with olive oil, pepper and toasted sesame seeds. Serve immediately.
