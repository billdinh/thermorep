Cheesy Corn Dogs

Ingredients

  • 5 oz yellow cornmeal, medium grind
  • 3 ½ oz cheddar cheese, diced (½ in.)
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 8 oz all-purpose flour
  • 2 ½ oz unsalted butter, cubed (½ in.)
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp ground mustard
  • 4 oz whole milk
  • 3 oz honey
  • 8 all beef hot dogs
  • 4 oz cheddar cheese, sliced (approx. 1 in. x ¼ in.)
  • ketchup, to serve
  • yellow mustard, to serve

Preparation

  1. Place cornmeal, diced cheddar cheese and sugar into mixing bowl and grind 5 sec/speed 8.
  2. Add flour, butter, baking powder, salt and ground mustard and mix 5 sec/speed 8.
  3. Add milk and honey and mix 5 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and knead 20 sec/. Roll out dough between 2 sheets of parchment into a square (approx. 12 in. x 12 in.). Trim as needed. Refrigerate 20 minutes.
  4. Cut hot dogs in half, creating 16 mini hot dogs. Make a pocket in each mini dog by cutting a shallow slit lengthwise, ensuring both ends of hot dog remain intact and stuff with a slice of cheese (approx. 1 in. x ½ in.). Insert a lollipop stick (see Tip) into each stuffed mini hot dog and set aside.
  5. Preheat oven to 375°F. Line a baking sheet with foil and set aside.
  6. Cut the rolled out dough into squares (approx. 3 in. x 3 in.). Wrap a piece of dough around each stuffed hot dog and press to form a corn dog shape. Pinch seams and roll gently to shape. Place on prepared baking sheet, seam-side down and freeze for 30 minutes. Bake for 20-25 minutes (375°F) until golden brown. Let cool slightly (approx. 5 min.) and serve with ketchup and mustard.

Useful Items

parchment paper, aluminum foil, baking sheet, lollipop sticks, rolling pin, refrigerator, oven, freezer

Hints & Tricks

  • If lollipop sticks are unavailable replace with short skewers.
  • Chilled dough cuts more precisely, clings less and retains its shape better during baking. If dough becomes too soft, refrigerate until it becomes cold enough to hold its shape.
