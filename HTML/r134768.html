Sri Lankan Red Beef Curry

Ingredients

Curry Powder

  • 30 g coriander seeds
  • 30 g cumin seeds
  • 40 g long grain rice
  • 2 Tbsp dried fennel seeds
  • 10 cardamom pods
  • 5 cloves
  • 10 dried curry leaves
  • 1 cinnamon stick

Beef Curry

  • 6 cardamom pods
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 tsp ground fenugreek
  • 12 cloves
  • 1 tsp black peppercorns
  • 20 g fresh root ginger, peeled, cut in round slices (2 mm)
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1 stalk fresh lemon grass, white part only
  • 2 fresh red chillies, deseeded
  • 50 g olive oil
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 12 dried curry leaves
  • 400 g tinned chopped tomatoes
  • 1000 g braising steak, cubed (3 cm)
  • 400 g coconut milk
  • 2 tsp fine sea salt
  • 2 Tbsp vinegar
  • rice, steamed, for serving

Preparation

Curry Powder

  1. Place all curry powder ingredients in mixing bowl and toast 15 min/Varoma/speed 2. Allow to cool slightly, then grind 30 sec/speed 10. Transfer to an airtight jar until required.

Beef Curry

  1. Place cardamom, cinnamon, fenugreek, cloves and peppercorns in mixing bowl then toast 2 min/100°C//speed 1. Allow to cool slightly, then grind 30 sec/speed 10. Repeat if necessary until fine.
  2. Add ginger, onion, garlic, lemon grass and chillies then chop 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add oil, turmeric, curry leaves and 2 tsp reserved curry powder then sauté 3 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  4. Add tomatoes, steak, coconut milk, salt and vinegar then, with simmering basket in place of measuring cup on mixing bowl lid, cook 45 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  5. With simmering basket still in place, cook a further 15 min/100°C//speed 1. Serve immediately with steamed rice.
