Sweet and sour meatballs with vegetables

Ingredients

  • olive oil, for greasing
  • 500 g pork fillet, cut into cubes (3 cm), slightly frozen
  • ¼ tsp ground ginger
  • ¼ tsp ground coriander
  • ½ tsp sea salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 3 tbsp cornflour
  • 4 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 brown onion, cut into pieces (4 cm)
  • 1 green capsicum, deseeded and cut into pieces (4 cm)
  • 1 red capsicum, deseeded and cut into pieces (4 cm)
  • 2 carrots, cut into matchsticks
  • 220 g canned bamboo shoots, drained (optional)
  • 820 g canned pineapple pieces in natural juice, drained, juice reserved
  • 2 tbsp white wine vinegar
  • 50 g tomato passata (purée)
  • 100 g sherry
  • 40 g soft brown sugar

Preparation

  1. Lightly grease Varoma tray and set aside.
  2. Place 250 g of the pork into mixing bowl and mince Turbo/1 sec/4-6 times. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place remaining pork into mixing bowl and mince Turbo/1 sec/4-6 times. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add reserved pork mince, ground ginger, ground coriander, salt, pepper, sesame oil, 2 tbsp of the cornflour, 1 tbsp of the soy sauce and egg yolk and combine Turbo/1 sec/2 times. Form mixture into balls (approx. 3 cm in diameter) and place onto greased Varoma tray.
  5. Place Varoma dish into position and place onion into base of Varoma dish, then add green capsicum, red capsicum and carrot. Weigh bamboo shoots (optional) and drained pineapple pieces on top. Place any vegetables that don't fit in the Varoma dish with the meatballs onto the Varoma tray. Set Varoma side.
  6. Place reserved pineapple juice, vinegar, tomato passata (purée) and remaining 3 tbsp soy sauce into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and cook 30 min/Varoma/speed 2. Set Varoma aside.
  7. Add sherry, brown sugar and remaining 1 tbsp cornflour into mixing bowl and cook 2 min/100°C/speed 4.
  8. Place meatballs and vegetables onto serving plates. Pour over sauce and serve immediately.

Useful Items

bowl, serving plates

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve sweet and sour meatballs with a side of cooked rice.
