Chinese-style drinking porridge

Ingredients

  • 20 g brown rice (see Tips)
  • 20 g millet
  • 30 g rolled oats
  • 20 g raw almonds
  • 20 g raw cashews
  • 20 g pepitas
  • 1000 g filtered water (see Tips)
  • salt, to taste (optional)
  • sweetener of choice, to taste (optional - see Tips)

Preparation

  1. Place brown rice, millet, rolled oats, almonds, cashews and pepitas into mixing bowl and mill 30 sec/speed 9.
  2. Add water and cook 15-23 min/100Â°C/speed 4, to desired consistency.
  3. Add salt or sweetener to taste (see Tips) and mix 20 sec/speed 5. Divide between 4 cups or bowls and serve immediately.

Hints & Tricks

  • If possible, we recommend using organic brown rice in this recipe.
  • For a gluten free version, you can replace the oats with quinoa flakes.
  • This is a thin drinking porridge; for a thicker porridge, reduce water to 800 g and proceed as per recipe.
  • To sweeten this porridge, we suggest using pure maple syrup, coconut sugar, palm sugar, banana or a nut butter. You could also add a variety of spices such as cinnamon, turmeric, mixed spice and ground ginger for more flavour.
