Chicken casserole with saffron and orange

Ingredients

  • 1 orange, preferably organic, zest and juice only
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 pinch ground ginger
  • 650 g chicken (1 whole chicken, see tip)
  • 200 g courgettes, cut into slices
  • 1 tsp fine salt
  • 150 g onions, cut into pieces
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 30 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 100 g dry white wine
  • 1 sachet saffron powder
  • 200 g chicken stock

Preparation

  1. Place orange zest, coriander, pepper and ginger into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add orange juice and emulsify 5 sec/speed 6.
  3. Place the chicken in a container and baste with the prepared marinade. Leave in the refrigerator for 30 minutes. Turn once.
  4. Arrange the courgettes in the Varoma tray and season with salt. Set Varoma aside.
  5. Place onion and garlic into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  6. Add the olive oil and sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  7. Tie chicken tightly with kitchen string. Place tied chicken above the mixing knife, add any remaining marinade, white wine, saffron dissolved in a little stock, salt and chicken stock. Cook 20 min/Varoma/speed .
  8. Turn the chicken. Place the Varoma into position with the courgettes and continue to cook 15 min/Varoma/speed .
  9. Set Varoma aside and keep warm. Remove the meat from the mixing bowl and keep warm (see tip). Reduce the sauce 10 min/Varoma/speed 1 without measuring cup.
  10. Cut the meat into portions and serve with steamed courgettes and the sauce.

Hints & Tricks

  • The recipe is suitable for a chicken or cockerel of 650-800 g. It is also possible to cook chicken pieces in the mixing bowl instead of a whole chicken.
  • If preferred, cook other vegetables in the Varoma dish and tray while the chicken is cooking (potatoes, pumpkin, broccoli etc.). To add a Middle-Eastern touch, garnish with dried fruit (pine nuts, pistachios and almonds). Serve with basmati or pilaf rice.
  • For a crispy version of the chicken, broil in a hot oven under the grill while you finish cooking the sauce.
