Cardamom And Lime Syrup Cake

Ingredients

Cake

  • 200 g unsalted butter, softened, plus extra for greasing
  • 1 Tbsp cardamom pods (see tips)
  • 240 g sugar
  • 200 g cashew nuts, roasted, unsalted
  • 170 g plain yoghurt
  • 4 medium eggs
  • 240 g self-raising flour

Syrup

  • 120 g sugar
  • 1 lime, finely grated zest only
  • 1 Tbsp lime juice
  • 90 g water

Preparation

Cake

  1. Preheat oven to 170°C. Grease and line a round cake tin (Ø 20 cm) with baking paper then set aside.
  2. Place cardamom pods in mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 6 to separate seeds. Remove and discard pods, leaving seeds in mixing bowl.
  3. Add sugar and grind 10 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add cashews and grind 3 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add butter, yoghurt and eggs then mix 15 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  6. Add flour and mix with aid of spatula 10 sec/speed 4, or until combined. Transfer to prepared cake tin and bake for 55 minutes (170°C), or until a skewer inserted in centre comes out clean. Clean mixing bowl.

Syrup

  1. Place sugar and lime zest in mixing bowl then grind 20 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add lime juice and water then cook 5 min/Varoma/speed 1. Pierce cake all over using wooden skewer and pour sauce over warm cake while still in tin. Allow to cool in tin (approx. 20 minutes) before serving.

Useful Items

round cake tin (Ø 20 cm), baking paper, wooden skewer, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • 2 tsp ground cardamom can be used instead of cardamom pods. Omit step 2 then add ground cardamom with sugar in step 3.
  • This cake has a strong cardamom flavour. If preferred, reduce cardamom pods to 2 tsp and proceed as per recipe.
