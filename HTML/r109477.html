Spring frittata

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 500 g water
  • 150 g zucchini (approx. 2), peeled into ribbons (see Tips)
  • 120 g fresh baby spinach leaves
  • 180 g raw prawns (approx. 12), shelled and deveined, leaving tail intact
  • 3 spring onions/shallots, trimmed and cut into pieces
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 6 eggs
  • 120 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 2 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 2 sprigs fresh dill, leaves only
  • watercress, for garnishing
  • frisée, or lettuce of choice, for garnishing
  • snow pea sprouts, for garnishing
  • 1 tbsp caperberries, rinsed and drained (optional)
  • 100 g Danish feta, cut into pieces (see Tips)
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges, to serve

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 170°C. Lightly grease a ceramic pie dish (22 cm) and set aside.
  2. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish into position, then weigh zucchini and spinach into it. Place Varoma tray into positionand weigh prawns onto it. Secure Varoma lid and steam 5 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  3. Stir vegetables and prawns to ensure even cooking, then secure Varoma lid and steam 3-5 min/Varoma/speed 2, until spinach is wilted and prawns are cooked to your liking. Remove Varoma and set aside. With aid of spatula, carefully squeeze out any excess moisture from spinach and zucchini. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Place oil, spring onions/shallots and garlic into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7.
  5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and cook 2 min/Varoma//speed .
  6. Add eggs, cream, salt and pepper and mix 5 sec/speed 4. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  7. Place parsley and dill into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7.
  8. Add reserved egg mixture and reserved spinach and zucchini and combine 10 sec//speed .
  9. Pour mixture into prepared dish and bake for 35-40 minutes (170°C), or until centre of frittata is firm.
  10. Garnish frittata with watercress, frisée, snow pea sprouts, caperberries (optional) and reserved prawns. Sprinkle with feta and serve warm with lemon wedges.

Useful Items

ceramic pie dish (22 cm), bowl, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • When peeling zucchini, do not use the seeded core. Reserve core to use to make Vegetable stock paste (see recipe in The Basic Cookbook or Everyday Cookbook).
  • Danish feta has a creamier texture than Greek or Australian feta. You can use ricotta cheese instead of feta in this recipe.
