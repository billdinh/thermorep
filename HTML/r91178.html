Pancakes and Waffles

Ingredients

  • 2 oz unsalted butter, plus extra to fry
  • 11 - 15 oz whole milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 oz sugar
  • 7 oz all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
588 kJ / 140 kcal
Protein
4 g
Carbohydrates
16 g
Fat
6 g

Preparation

  1. Place butter into mixing bowl and melt 2 min/160°F/speed 1.
  2. Add milk, eggs, sugar, flour, baking powder and salt and mix with aid of spatula 10 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of bowl with spatula and mix a further 3 sec/speed 5.
  3. To cook pancakes, heat ½ tsp butter in a frying pan over medium-high heat. For each pancake, pour in a ladle of batter and cook 1-2 minutes or until bubbles start bursting on pancake surface. Turn pancake over and cook a further 1 minute. Place pancake onto a serving plate and cover to keep warm. Repeat with remaining batter. Serve hot.

Useful Items

small ladle, frying pan, stove

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve pancakes with maple syrup, whipped cream, chocolate, caramel sauce or jam.
  • If pancake mix is too thick add more milk to thin to desired texture.
  • To replace butter with oil, omit melting butter for the batter and replace with 2 oz oil when adding other ingredients. Use oil instead of butter for frying.
  • Blueberry or raspberry pancakes: scatter 1 tbsp berries onto each pancake right after pouring batter into pan, before bubbles start to form.
  • Chocolate pancakes: add 2 tsp cocoa powder with all other ingredients.
  • For waffles add an extra 2 oz butter to pancake recipe. Cook in waffle maker following manufacturer's instructions for use.
