Jaffa madeleines

Ingredients

  • 100 g unsalted butter, softened and cut into pieces
  • 60 g caster sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 orange, zest only, no white pith
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 60 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 1 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • icing sugar, for dusting (see Tips)

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 170°C.
  2. Place butter into mixing bowl and melt 2 min/100°C/speed 2. Transfer into a jug and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Insert butterfly whisk. Place sugar, eggs, orange zest and salt into mixing bowl and beat 4 min/speed 4. Meanwhile, use approx. 1 tablespoon of the melted butter and a pastry brush to grease a 12-hole madeleine tray, then dust with flour, shaking out any excess. Set aside.
  4. Remove butterfly whisk. Add flour, cocoa powder, baking powder and remaining melted butter and mix 5 sec/speed 3.
  5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix 3 sec/speed 3 or until just combined. Divide mixture between madeleine moulds and bake for 8-10 minutes (170°C) or until madeleines spring back when lightly pressed in the centre. Remove from oven, cool in the tin for 2 minutes, then transfer onto a wire rack to cool completely. Dust with icing sugar to serve (see Tips).

Useful Items

madeleine tray, wire rack, jug, pastry brush, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • To make your own icing sugar, please refer to the recipe in The Basic Cookbook.
  • Madeleines are best eaten on the day they are made.
