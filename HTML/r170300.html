Moroccan Chicken and Couscous

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ Tbsp garam masala
  • 3 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • ¾ tsp ground black pepper
  • 600 g chicken thighs, boneless and skinless, cut in bite size pieces
  • 250 g couscous
  • 630 g water
  • 30 g sunflower oil
  • 10 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 80 g onions, cut in halves
  • 1 Tbsp sunflower oil
  • 60 g green olives, pitted
  • 1 tsp chicken stock paste
  • 1 lemon, zest and juice

Preparation

  1. Combine garam masala, ground turmeric, salt and black pepper in a small bowl. Spread half on a plate, toss the chicken thighs in the spice mixture until completely coated and arrange them in Varoma dish, make sure that a few holes remain unobstructed to allow steam to circulate. Reserve remaining spice mixture.
  2. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid, weigh couscous, 250 g water and sunflower oil in it, mix together with a fork and set aside.
  3. Place parsley in mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place garlic cloves and onions in mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl using spatula.
  5. Add 1 tbsp sunflower oil, remaining spice mixture and sauté 4 min/120°C/speed 1. Meanwhile, insert Varoma tray in Varoma dish. Fluff couscous with a fork and transfer and distribute it on Varoma tray.
  6. Add 380 g water, green olives, chicken stock paste, lemon zest and juice in mixing bowl, set Varoma in position and steam 20-23 min/Varoma//speed 1. Remove Varoma, add the cooked chicken, half of the steamed couscous and half of the reserved chopped parsley to sauce in mixing bowl, combine using spatula.
  7. Serve chicken and couscous hot in sauce, with the remaining steamed couscous. Garnish with remaining chopped parsley.
