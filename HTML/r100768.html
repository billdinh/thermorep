Sofrito Toasts with Serrano Ham

Ingredients

  • 50 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
  • 70 g onions, quartered
  • 400 g tinned chopped tomatoes
  • 1 baguette, thinly sliced (1 cm)
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 100 g Serrano ham, or other cured ham

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C.
  2. Place oil in mixing bowl and heat 7 min/120°C/speed .
  3. Add garlic and fry 5 min/120°C/speed .
  4. Add onions and chop 5 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add tomatoes and cook 20 min/120°C/speed . Meanwhile, toast baguette slices in oven for 5 minutes (200°C).
  6. Add salt and pepper then blend 1 min/speed 5-10, increasing speed gradually.
  7. Spread a little sofrito on each slice and top with a small piece of ham. Serve immediately or chill until needed, then serve at room temperature.

Hints & Tricks

  • A colourful and tasty canapé, these bites are super easy to put together. Consisting of a rich tomato layer topped with salty ham, these can be made ahead of time then chilled until needed.
  • For a vegetarian version, omit ham and garnish with olives, cheese or cherry tomato halves.
