TM6

Caramel Toffees

Ingredients

  • 315 - 315 g sugar
  • 80 - 80 g unsalted butter, cold, diced (3 cm)
  • 250 - 250 g cream, min. 30% fat (whipping or double cream)
  • 1030 - 1030 g water
  • 1 - 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 - 2 tsp vanilla extract (optional)
  • 1 - 2 tsp sea salt flakes (optional)
  • 50 - 50 g vinegar
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
153 kJ / 37 kcal
Protein
0.2 g
Carbohydrates
4 g
Fat
2 g

Preparation

  1. To guarantee the success of recipes using Sugar Stage modes, the ingredients used must match exactly the quantity and description given. The recipe should also be cooked immediately after placing the sugar with the other ingredients in the mixing bowl.
  2. Line a baking tin with baking paper.
  3. Place sugar, butter, cream, 30 g water, lemon juice and vanilla extract (if using) in mixing bowl then start Sugar Stages . Immediately transfer to prepared tin and, using spatula, immediately scrape off any mixture left on rim of mixing bowl so mixing bowl lid closes securely for Pre-clean mode. Leave caramel to cool for 5 minutes then garnish to taste with salt (if using).
  4. Place remaining 1000 g water and vinegar in mixing bowl then start Clean .
  5. Leave caramel to cool for a further 35 minutes then, while still warm, cut with a sharp knife into rectangles (approx. 1 cm x 3 cm). Leave to cool completely then wrap individually in baking paper squares (5 cm). Store in an airtight container at room temperature until needed (see tip).

Useful Items

sharp knife, baking paper, airtight container, baking tin

Hints & Tricks

  • Homemade toffees are successful every time in Thermomix®. Wrap them up in pretty paper to make great gifts for birthdays, Christmas or for your teacher.
  • Store toffees in a cool place, individually wrapped and in an airtight container to prevent moisture absorption, for 1-2 weeks.
  • Adding lemon juice prevents the sugar from burning or crystallising, without affecting the flavour.
