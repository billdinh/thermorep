Lasagna rolls

Ingredients

  • 8 dried lasagne pasta sheets
  • 25 g Parmesan cheese, cut into pieces
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 30 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 150 g frozen spinach leaves, defrosted and squeezed
  • 1 pinch fine salt
  • 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • 150 g ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 100 g tomato passata
  • 100 g mozzarella, roughly chopped
  • 1 tsp dried oregano

Preparation

  1. Soak the lasagne sheets in tepid water for 20 minutes. Move them now and then to prevent them from sticking together
  2. Meanwhile, place Parmesan cheese in mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.
  3. Place garlic and olive oil in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  4. Add spinach, salt, pepper and nutmeg and cook 4 min/120°C/speed 1.
  5. Combine ricotta, Parmesan cheese and egg, chop 15 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a dish and set aside.
  6. Preheat the oven to 200°C with the grill function. Line a baking tray with baking paper and set aside.
  7. Strain the lasagne sheets well and lay them on a clean cloth. Distribute 2-3 tablespoons of the filling along the long side of the lasagne sheet and roll up. Cut the roll into 3 parts and arrange the smaller rolls on the prepared tray. Top each roll with 1-2 tablespoons of tomato passata and sprinkle with mozzarella and oregano.
  8. Cook in a hot oven for 10 minutes (200°C). Remove from the oven, pierce each roll with a small skewer and serve.

Useful Items

baking tray and paper, tea towel, bowl, wood skewers, oven
