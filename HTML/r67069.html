Minestrone

Ingredients

  • 80 g onions, cut into halves
  • 120 g leek, white part only, cut into pieces
  • 40 g extra virgin olive oil, plus extra to serve
  • 400 g mixed vegetables (e.g. potatoes, carrots, zucchini, celery, tomatoes, silverbeet leaves, pumpkin), cut into pieces (4 cm)
  • 1000 g water
  • 1 tbsp vegetable stock paste
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 120 g ditalini pasta or similar short dried pasta
  • 200 g canned cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
  • fresh basil leaves, torn into pieces, to serve (optional)
  • grated Parmesan cheese, to taste

Preparation

  1. Place onions and leek into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add extra virgin olive oil and sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  3. Add mixed vegetables and chop 6-10 sec/speed 4.
  4. Add water and vegetable stock paste and cook 15 min/100°C//speed 1.
  5. Add salt, ditalini pasta and beans and cook time indicated on pasta package/100°C//speed 1. Serve soup hot, drizzled with extra virgin olive oil and sprinkled with basil leaves (optional) and grated Parmesan cheese.
