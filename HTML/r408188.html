Apple Wild Rice

Ingredients

  • 3 oz yellow onions, in pieces
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • ½ tsp dried sage
  • ½ tsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 6 oz Pink Lady apples, cubed (1 in.)
  • 3 oz wild rice
  • 4 oz long-grain brown rice
  • 32 oz chicken stock
  • 8 oz apple cider
  • 1 tsp salt
  • fresh parsley, to garnish
  • 4 oz pomegranate arils/seeds, to garnish

Preparation

  1. Place onions, garlic, sage and olive oil into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and sauté 5 min/250°F/speed . Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add apples and chop 14 sec/speed 4, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 3 min/250°F//speed . Transfer apple mixture to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Insert simmering basket and weigh in wild and brown rice. Remove simmering basket and rinse rice under cold water until water runs clear. Place simmering basket with rinsed rice into mixing bowl. Add chicken stock, apple cider and salt over rice and mix 5 sec/speed 6. Cook 40-42 min/Varoma/speed 3, until rice is cooked to your liking.
  4. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula, transfer rice to a serving bowl and fold in reserved apple mixture with spatula. Garnish with parsley and pomegranate arils. Serve hot.

Hints & Tricks

  • Wild rice is cooked properly when kernels are tender with a chewy texture and some have burst open to reveal a cream-colored interior.
