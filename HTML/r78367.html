Spanish green lentil and chorizo soup

Ingredients

Soup

  • 1 brown onion, (approx. 150 g), cut into halves
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 red capsicum, deseeded and cut into pieces
  • ½ tsp ground smoked paprika
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 1 carrot, cut into pieces
  • 2 celery stalks, cut into pieces
  • 100 g pumpkin, cut into cubes (2-3 cm)
  • 150 g French green lentils, soaked overnight and drained
  • 1 dried bay leaf
  • 600 g water
  • 2 tbsp Vegetable stock paste (see Tips)
  • 2 chorizo sausages (approx. 250 g), cut into slices (1 cm)

Pancetta croutons

  • 4 slices sourdough bread, each slice cut into 3 strips
  • 12 slices flat pancetta

Preparation

Soup

  1. Place onion, garlic, capsicum and paprika into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add olive oil and sauté 5 min/Varoma/speed 1, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  3. Add carrot and celery and chop 4 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add all remaining soup ingredients and cook 30 min/100°C//speed .

Pancetta croutons

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Line a baking tray with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Wrap 1 pancetta slice around each of the bread slices and place on prepared baking tray. Bake for 5-10 minutes (180°C) or until crispy. Serve soup hot with pancetta croutons.

Useful Items

baking tray (20 x 30 cm), baking paper, oven

Hints & Tricks
