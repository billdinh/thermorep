Steamed Banana Loaf

Ingredients

  • ½ oz coconut oil, to grease
  • 2 oz coconut butter
  • 8 oz coconut sugar
  • 7 oz canned coconut milk (approx. ½ can)
  • 8 - 9 oz bananas, halved (approx. 2-3 bananas)
  • 10 oz whole wheat flour
  • 1 large egg (optional)
  • &frac18; tsp salt
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 oz blueberries
  • 35 oz water

Preparation

  1. Grease and line a loaf pan (approx. 9 in. x 5 in. x 3 in.) with parchment and set aside.
  2. Place coconut butter and coconut sugar into mixing bowl and melt 4 min/160°F/speed 3.
  3. Add coconut milk and bananas and mix 15 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add flour, egg, salt, baking powder, cinnamon and vanilla extract and mix 15 sec/speed 3. Finish mixing through with spatula. Transfer batter into prepared pan and sprinkle with blueberries. Place pan in Varoma dish and secure Varoma lid.
  5. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position and steam 55 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  6. Remove pan from Varoma and allow to cool for 10 minutes before removing cake from pan. Serve warm or cold.

Useful Items

parchment paper, loaf pan (approx. 9 in. x 5 in. x 3 in.)

Hints & Tricks

  • Coconut sugar can be replaced with apple sauce. Coconut butter can be replaced with unsalted butter.
  • Carefully remove Varoma lid inverting quickly to avoid dripping condensation onto your cake.
  • This cake is very moist; remove cake from pan by lifting edges of parchment paper.
