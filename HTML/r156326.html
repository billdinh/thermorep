Honey lime chicken supremes with Mango chutney

Ingredients

Marinated chicken

  • 30 g honey
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ - 1 tsp ground sweet paprika
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 lime, zest cut into pieces, no white pith (1 cm) and juice
  • 4 skinless chicken supremes (approx. 800 g) (see Tips)

Mango chutney and steamed chicken supremes

  • 10 - 15 g fresh coriander leaves or stalks or both
  • 3 - 6 limes, zest cut into pieces, no white pith (1 cm) and juice
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 100 g brown onions, cut into halves
  • 75 g red capsicum, cut into pieces
  • 1 - 2 fresh jalapeño chillies, deseeded if preferred
  • 500 g ripe mango, flesh only, cut into small pieces (1-2 cm)
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 100 g sugar
  • 250 g water

Preparation

Marinated chicken

  1. Place honey, pepper, salt, paprika, garlic clove, lime zest and 20 g lime juice into mixing bowl and blend 30 sec/speed 8.
  2. Place chicken into a bowl and drizzle marinade over it, stir to coat then place into refrigerator to marinate for 30 minutes. Meanwhile line Varoma tray with baking paper, allowing a 2 cm overhang on all sides. Insert Varoma tray into Varoma dish. Clean and dry mixing bowl and continue with recipe.

Mango chutney and steamed chicken supremes

  1. Place coriander and approx. 50 g lime zest into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 8. Transfer 1-2 tsp coriander-lime zest mixture into a small bowl.
  2. Add garlic, onions, capsicum and jalapeño chillies to mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  3. Add mango, salt, sugar, 70 g lime juice and water. Place marinated chicken onto lined Varoma tray, place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and cook 28 min/Varoma//speed 1. Set covered Varoma aside on a plate to keep warm. Transfer mango chutney into a 450 g sterilised sealable jar with lid and set aside for another use (see Tips). Transfer remaining mango chutney into a wide serving dish and allow to cool for a few minutes until room temperature. Slice the chicken supremes diagonally and arrange them over chutney in the serving dish, then sprinkle with reserved coriander-lime zest mixture. Serve warm or allow to cool, then cover, place into refrigerate and serve chilled.

Useful Items

baking paper, large bowl, small bowl, serving dish, sealable storage jar, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • A chicken supreme is a half breast of chicken with the upper part of the wing still attached.
    - For a stronger flavour, marinate the chicken in step 2 for a few hours or overnight.
    - Once it has cooled completely, the mango chutney can be stored in the refrigerator for up to two weeks or stored in freezable containers in the freezer for up to 6 months.
  • For a complete meal, in step 5 weigh 400-600g vegetables (eg carrots, potatoes, green beans, zucchinis,broccoli), cut into bite-size pieces into Varoma dish and steam until tender.
