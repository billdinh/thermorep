S'mores cupcakes

Ingredients

Biscuit base

  • 130 g digestive biscuits
  • 70 g unsalted butter, cut into pieces

Chocolate cupcakes

  • 80 g raw sugar
  • 150 g plain flour
  • 20 g Dutch cocoa powder
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 1 pinch sea salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 80 g buttermilk
  • 50 g vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract
  • 50 g boiling water
  • 120 g dark chocolate chips

Chocolate sauce

  • 80 g dark chocolate chips
  • 50 g full cream milk
  • 10 g unsalted butter
  • ¼ tsp natural vanilla extract

Marshmallow frosting

  • 100 g white sugar
  • ¼ tsp cream of tartar
  • 30 g water
  • 2 egg whites

Preparation

Biscuit base

  1. Preheat oven to 170°C. Line a 12-hole muffin tray with cupcake liners and set aside.
  2. Place biscuits into mixing bowl and crush 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place butter into mixing bowl and melt 2 min/100°C/speed 1.
  4. Add reserved biscuit crumbs and mix 5 sec/speed 5. Place 3 teaspoons of the mixture into each cupcake liner and press down to form a base. Set muffin tray aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Chocolate cupcakes

  1. Place sugar into mixing bowl and mill 3 sec/speed 9.
  2. Add flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, bicarbonate of soda and salt and mix 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place eggs, buttermilk, oil and vanilla into mixing bowl and mix 5 sec/speed 4.
  4. Add boiling water and mix 5 sec/speed 3.
  5. Add chocolate chips and reserved flour mixture, then combine 5 sec//speed 2.
  6. Divide mixture between cupcake liners and pour over biscuit base. Bake for 15-18 minutes (170°C) or until a wooden skewer inserted into the centre of the cupcake comes out clean. Leave to cool in tin for 5 minutes, then transfer onto a wire rack to cool completely.
  7. Place cupcakes into refrigerator for 1 hour to allow biscuit base to set. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Chocolate sauce

  1. Place all sauce ingredients into mixing bowl and cook 2 min/50°C/speed 3. Transfer into a serving jug and set aside (see Tips). Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Marshmallow frosting

  1. Place sugar, cream of tartar and water into mixing bowl and heat 3 min/100°C/speed 1. Transfer sugar syrup into a jug and set aside. Thoroughly clean and dry mixing bowl.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg whites into mixing bowl and whip 3 min/37°C/speed 3, without measuring cup.
  3. With measuring cup removed, mix 5 min/speed 3,slowly pouring reserved sugar syrup onto mixing bowl lid allowing it to trickle into mixing bowl, until frosting is light and fluffy. Scrape any sugar syrup remaining on mixing bowl lid into mixing bowl using spatula. Remove butterfly whisk.
  4. Transfer frosting into a piping bag with a plain round nozzle (1 cm) and decorate the top of each cooled cupcake. Place cupcakes into refrigerator for 30 minutes or until frosting is firm. Once firm, drizzle cooled chocolate sauce over frosting just before serving.

Useful Items

cupcake liners, 12-hole muffin tray, bowl, wooden skewer, wire rack, jug, piping bag with round nozzle (1 cm), oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • If you own a kitchen blow torch, very lightly torch the top of the marshmallow frosting to add a little colour before drizzling with chocolate sauce.
  • Leave chocolate sauce at room temperature while completing the cupcakes -- this will make it easier to drizzle onto the cupcakes. Store any leftover chocolate sauce in a sealable container in the refrigerator.
  • Cupcakes can be made a day ahead, to allow base to set overnight.
