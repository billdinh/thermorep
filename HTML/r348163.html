Curried Prawns with Rice

Ingredients

  • 1200 g water
  • 35 g curry powder
  • 200 g rice
  • 50 g frozen green peas
  • 150 g carrots, peeled, diced (1 cm)
  • 2 medium eggs, lightly beaten
  • 70 g onions, quartered
  • 10 g vegetable oil
  • 100 g raw tiger prawns, peeled, deveined, cut in pieces (2 cm) (see tip)
  • 30 g fresh pineapple, peeled, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • ½ tsp rice wine
  • 1 tsp fish sauce
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt

Preparation

  1. Line Varoma tray with baking paper, going 2 cm up the sides, leaving some holes uncovered for steam to circulate.
  2. Place water and 30 g curry powder in mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket and weigh in rice. Place Varoma dish into position then weigh in peas and carrots. Insert prepared Varoma tray and pour on eggs. Cover Varoma and steam 18 min/Varoma/speed 2. Set covered Varoma aside.
  3. If rice is not cooked, insert measuring cup into mixing bowl lid and cook again 10 min/Varoma/speed 2 or until cooked. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula, transfer rice to a large bowl, cover to keep warm and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.
  4. Place onions and oil in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Insert butterfly whisk then sauté 5 min/120°C/speed .
  5. Add prawns, pineapple, 5 g curry powder, rice wine, fish sauce and salt then, without measuring cup, stir-fry 7 min/Varoma//speed . Remove butterfly whisk. Transfer to a serving dish then add reserved cooked rice, peas, carrots and eggs (see tip). Mix well and serve hot.

Useful Items

baking paper, aluminium foil

Hints & Tricks

  • If desired, frozen peas and carrots can be added to cook in the sauce in step 6 instead of steamed in step 2.
  • Tiger prawns are large prawns with stripey markings. If tiger prawns are not available, substitute with any raw prawns.
  • If there is a black line down the back of the prawn, it means that the vein is full. It’s not harmful to eat, but the prawn looks better without it, and it can be gritty to eat. To devein prawns, lift the vein out using the tip of a small sharp knife or a cocktail stick.
  • For more attractive presentation, in step 6, roll up steamed eggs using baking paper and slice thinly. Arrange seafood and sliced eggs on rice.
