Pollock and Coriander Fishcakes with Savoy Rice

Ingredients

Fish Cakes

  • 15 g fresh coriander, leaves only
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 50 g olive oil, plus extra for frying
  • 500 g pollock fillets, cut in pieces
  • 500 g water
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 400 g breadcrumbs

Savoy Rice

  • 30 g olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 150 g Savoy cabbage, cut in pieces
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 250 g long grain rice
  • 800 g water

Preparation

Fish Cakes

  1. Place coriander in mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add garlic and oil then chop 2 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  3. Add pollock, water and salt then cook 10 min/100°C/speed 1.
  4. Remove lid and leave to cool for 10 minutes then add breadcrumbs and, without measuring cup, mix 10 sec/speed 4, while stirring with spatula through hole in mixing bowl lid. Set aside until mixture is cool enough to handle then form into patties, using 50 g mixture for each fish cake. Heat a little oil in a frying pan over a medium-high heat and fry fish cakes for approx. 3 minutes on each side until golden brown. Set aside and keep warm.

Savoy Rice

  1. Place oil and garlic in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 8. Sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  2. Add cabbage and salt then, without measuring cup, chop 8 sec/speed 5. Sauté 7 min/100°C/speed 1.
  3. Place rice in simmering basket then pour contents of mixing bowl on top. Insert simmering basket, add water then stir with spatula. Cook 30 min/Varoma/speed 4. Serve fishcakes with rice and a sauce of choice.
