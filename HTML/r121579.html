Simple herb gravy

Ingredients

  • 40 g unsalted butter
  • 1 tbsp white rice flour (see Tips)
  • 1 tbsp sorghum flour
  • 500 g water
  • 1 tbsp Chicken stock paste (see Tips)
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce (gluten free) (see Tips)
  • salt, to taste
  • ground black pepper, to taste
  • 2 tsp fresh herbs, finely chopped (see Tips)

Preparation

  1. Place butter into mixing bowl and melt 1 min/100°C/speed 2.
  2. Add rice flour and sorghum and cook 2 min/100°C/speed 2.
  3. Add water, stock paste and Worcestershire sauce and cook 8-10 min/100°C/speed 2, until flours have dissolved and gravy has thickened.
  4. Season to taste. Transfer into a serving jug and top with fresh herbs. Serve gravy warm.

Hints & Tricks

  • To make your own rice flour, place 250 g rice into mixing bowl and mill 2 min/speed 10. Use as needed.
  • Please refer to The Basic Cookbook or Everyday Cookbook for the Chicken stock paste recipe. Alternatively, you can replace the Chicken stock paste with Vegetable or Meat stock paste.
  • To make your own gluten free Worcestershire sauce, please refer to the recipe on this recipe chip.
  • Using fresh herbs, such as parsley and tarragon, adds more flavour to your gravy.
