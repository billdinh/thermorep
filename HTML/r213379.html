Strawberry Shortcakes

Ingredients

Strawberries

  • 16 oz fresh strawberries, hulled and quartered
  • 2 oz sugar
  • ½ oz lemon peel, no pith

Shortcakes

  • 10 ½ oz all-purpose flour
  • 3 ½ oz sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ¼ tsp baking soda
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 2 ½ oz unsalted butter chilled and diced (¼ in.)
  • 4 oz sour cream
  • 1 oz whole milk, plus extra to brush
  • 1 large egg, lightly beaten

Whipped Cream and Assembly

  • 14 oz heavy whipping cream, chilled
  • 1 - 2 tsp confectioners sugar, to taste
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract

Preparation

Strawberries

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Line a baking sheet with parchment and set aside.
  2. Place a large bowl onto mixing bowl lid, weigh in strawberries and set aside.
  3. Place sugar and lemon peel into mixing bowl and grate 15 sec/speed 10. Transfer to large bowl with strawberries, stir with spatula to combine and set aside.

Shortcakes

  1. Place flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt and butter into mixing bowl and blend 5 sec/speed 8.
  2. Add sour cream, 1 oz whole milk and egg and mix 5 sec/speed 5.
  3. Transfer to prepared baking sheet and quickly but gently, shape into a rectangle (approx. 1 in. thick). Cut into 8 equal squares. Brush tops with milk and bake 12-15 minutes (400°F) until tops are lightly golden brown. Cool on a wire rack. Clean mixing bowl.

Whipped Cream and Assembly

  1. Insert butterfly whisk and place whipping cream, sugar and vanilla into mixing bowl and without setting a time whip speed 3 until desired consistency is achieved. Watch carefully to avoid overwhipping. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Split shortcakes, fill with reserved strawberries and whipped cream and serve.

Useful Items

parchment paper, large bowl, pastry brush, baking sheet, wire rack, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Making square shortcakes is a great way to prevent over-handling of dough and helps reduce waste.
