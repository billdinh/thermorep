Courgette and Avocado Beignets with Feta Cream, Coriander Chilli Pesto and Salad

Ingredients

Beignets

  • 80 g unsalted butter, diced
  • 150 g water
  • 2 pinches fine sea salt
  • 120 g plain flour
  • 3 medium eggs
  • 120 g courgettes, cut in pieces
  • ½ ripe avocado, small, diced (5 mm)
  • 1 fresh green chilli, large, thinly sliced
  • 50 g spring onions sliced (1 cm)
  • 2 Tbsp fresh coriander leaves
  • ½ lime, finely grated zest and juice
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper

Coriander Chilli Pesto

  • 25 g fresh coriander, leaves and stalks
  • 20 g Parmesan cheese, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 40 g rapeseed oil
  • 1 Tbsp toasted pine nuts
  • 1 fresh green chilli, large, halved, deseeded
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • ½ lime, freshly squeezed juice only
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt, or to taste
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper, or to taste

Feta Cheese Cream

  • 200 g feta cheese, cut in pieces
  • 100 g plain yoghurt
  • 100 g double cream
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt, or to taste
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper, or to taste

Courgette and Avocado Salad

  • 1 courgette, green, small, cut in thin strips with a vegetable peeler
  • 1 courgette, yellow, small, cut in thin strips with a vegetable peeler
  • 1 ripe avocado, small, diced (1 cm)
  • 1 lime, juice and finely grated zest
  • 1 Tbsp rapeseed oil
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt, or to taste
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper, or to taste

Cooking and Serving

  • sunflower oil, for deep frying
  • 2 Tbsp toasted pine nuts, roughly chopped
  • coriander cress, for garnishing
  • sumac, for sprinkling

Preparation

Beignets

  1. Place butter, water and 1 pinch salt in mixing bowl then heat 3 min 30 sec/100°C/speed 1.
  2. Add flour and blend 1 min/speed 8. Scrape down sides and lid of mixing bowl with spatula then leave to rest for 5 minutes.
  3. Without measuring cup, mix speed 3 while adding eggs, one at a time, through hole in mixing bowl lid. Insert measuring cup then blend 1 min/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl, place cling film directly on surface of choux pastry mixture and place in fridge for at least 30 minutes. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl.
  4. Place courgettes in mixing bowl and coarsely grate Turbo/0.5 sec/0 times. Transfer to bowl with choux pastry mixture and add to bowl avocado, chilli, spring onions, coriander, lime zest, lime juice, 1 pinch salt and pepper then stir well with spatula. Cover with cling film and return to fridge for 20 minutes.

Coriander Chilli Pesto

  1. Place all pesto ingredients in mixing bowl and blend 30 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then blend again 30 sec/speed 10. Adjust seasoning to taste then transfer to a bowl and chill until needed. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl and make feta cheese cream.

Feta Cheese Cream

  1. Place feta, yoghurt, cream, salt and pepper in mixing bowl then blend 20 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a small bowl, adjust seasoning if required and chill until needed.

Courgette and Avocado Salad

  1. Place all salad ingredients in a bowl and mix together then place in fridge until needed.

Cooking and Serving

  1. Heat sunflower oil in a deep fat fryer or deep saucepan to 160°C. Carefully place heaped teaspoonfuls of beignet mixture into hot oil, then fry in batches for 4-5 minutes until golden brown. Remove with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels.
  2. Using a palette knife, spread 1 Tbsp feta cheese cream on each plate. Place 3 beignets on each plate. Divide salad between the plates. Dollop some coriander chilli pesto on the side, and garnish with pine nuts, coriander cress and sumac.

Useful Items

cling film, deep saucepan or deep fat fryer, slotted spoon, paper towels, palette knife, refrigerator
