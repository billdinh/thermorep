Buffalo Burger with Bourbon Glazed Onions

Ingredients

Buffalo Burgers

  • 6 - 8 fresh sage leaves
  • 2 ½ oz shallots
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 tsp mustard powder
  • &frac18; tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 16 oz ground buffalo meat

Bourbon Glazed Onions

  • 18 oz red onions, quartered
  • ½ oz olive oil
  • ½ oz unsalted butter
  • 2 oz dark brown sugar
  • ¼ tsp salt, plus extra to taste
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper, plus extra to taste
  • 2 oz bourbon

Cooking Burgers and Assembly

  • salt, to taste
  • 4 hamburger buns, toasted
  • ketchup, to taste
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese
  • 4 oz Roma tomatoes, sliced
  • 4 - 8 pieces butter lettuce

Preparation

Buffalo Burgers

  1. Place sage, shallots, garlic, mustard powder, and pepper into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7.
  2. Add buffalo meat and mix 7 sec//speed 4. Divide and shape into four equal-sized patties and create a dimple in the center by pressing down with your fingers to prevent them from rising while cooking. Transfer to a plate, cover with cling wrap and place in the refrigerator. Clean mixing bowl.

Bourbon Glazed Onions

  1. Place onions, olive oil, butter, sugar, salt and pepper into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 4. Place simmering basket instead of measuring cup on mixing bowl lid and sauté 30 min/250°F//speed .
  2. Add bourbon, scrape bottom of mixing bowl with spatula and sauté a further 15 min/250°F//speed . Transfer to a bowl and keep warm.

Cooking Burgers and Assembly

  1. Season patties with salt and cook over medium high heat on a grill or stovetop to taste (see Tip).
  2. Spread ketchup on bottom bun, add patty and top with cheese, Bourbon Glazed Onions, tomatoes, butter lettuce and top bun. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

cling wrap, grill, thermometer, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Buffalo meat has very little fat and is best cooked rare. Buffalo rare is approximately 135°F, medium is approximately 140°F, and well done is approximately160°F.
  • Ground buffalo meat can be replaced with buffalo meat. To grind buffalo meat: place a zip lock bag onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in 8 oz buffalo meat that has been cut into1 inch pieces. Repeat this step with another zip lock bag with remaining 8 oz buffalo meat and place both bags in freezer until partially frozen (approx.1 hour). Place one 8 oz bag of frozen buffalo meat into mixing bowl after chopping shallots and grind 5 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Place remaining 8 oz bag of frozen buffalo meat into mixing bowl and grind 5 sec/speed 6. Add reserved ground buffalo and shallots and mix together 5 sec//speed 4. Use spatula to evenly combine before proceeding with recipe.
