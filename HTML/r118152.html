Tiraccino

Ingredients

Mascarpone cream

  • 3 eggs, separated
  • 120 g caster sugar
  • 250 g mascarpone cheese

Chocolate

  • 30 g dark chocolate, broken into pieces

Coffee syrup

  • 150 g strong brewed espresso coffee
  • 70 g dry Marsala wine
  • 40 g raw sugar

Assembly

  • 150 g savoiardi biscuits
  • 200 g full cream milk
  • 1 tbsp Dutch cocoa powder

Preparation

Mascarpone cream

  1. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg yolks and 60 g of the sugar into mixing bowl and blend 2 min/speed 4, until mixture is thick and pale. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add mascarpone cheese and mix 30 sec/speed 2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and ensure mascarpone cream is incorporated. Remove butterfly whisk. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Thoroughly clean and dry mixing bowl and butterfly whisk.
  3. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg whites into mixing bowl and beat 2 min/37°C/speed 3.5,  without measuring cup. Mix for a further 1 min/speed 2.5, slowly adding remaining 60 g sugar through hole in mixing bowl lid. Remove butterfly whisk.
  4. Add egg white mixture to reserved mascarpone mixture and gently fold together with a large metal spoon until mixture is airy but smooth. Cover with plastic wrap and place into refrigerator for 30 minutes. Clean and dry mixing bowl and butterfly whisk.

Chocolate

  1. Place dark chocolate into mixing bowl and grate 6 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a small bowl and set aside.

Coffee syrup

  1. Without cleaning mixing bowl, place coffee, Marsala and sugar into mixing bowl and cook 2 min 30 sec/70°C/speed 3. Set aside in mixing bowl to cool.

Assembly

  1. Break biscuits into pieces to line base of each mug or glass. Divide biscuits between glasses and pour over cooled coffee syrup. Gently press down on biscuits to form a layer. Top with mascarpone cream mixture and sprinkle with grated chocolate. Depending on size of cups, repeat layering. Place into refrigerator until ready to serve. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  2. Place milk into mixing bowl and heat 3 min/70°C/speed 1, then mix 30 sec/speed 8, until frothed. Gently spoon froth (not milk) over tiraccino and dust with cocoa powder before serving.

Useful Items

bowl, plastic wrap, small bowl , 6 glasses or mugs, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Replace Marsala wine with Kahlua, Tia Maria or brandy, or omit liqueur for a non-alcoholic version.
  • All elements, except frothed milk, can be made and assembled up to 1 day in advance and stored in the refrigerator.
