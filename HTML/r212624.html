Eggplant with Feta Cheese and Sundried Tomatoes

Ingredients

  • 1 - 2 eggplants, large, unpeeled (approx. 30 oz total) sliced in rounds (approx. ¼ in.)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 9 oz balsamic vinegar
  • 1 oz brown sugar
  • 3 ½ oz fresh baby spinach
  • 1 oz fresh basil leaves (approx. 30 med. leaves), to taste
  • 15 dried tomatoes in oil, cut into 30 strips
  • 8 oz feta cheese, cut into 30 sticks
  • 30 pinches freshly ground black pepper (approx. 3 tsp)
  • 6 green onions, cut into thin strips, (approx. 5 strips ea.) to bundle (optional)

Preparation

  1. Place 30 eggplant slices onto paper towels and sprinkle each side with salt. Allow to rest for 15 minutes.
  2. Stand eggplant slices vertically, in "Hasselback" formation (see Tip), into Varoma dish and tray while staggering to leave some holes for steam to circulate. Secure Varoma lid and set aside.
  3. Place balsamic vinegar and brown sugar into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position and steam 15 min/Varoma/speed 2. Remove Varoma and allow to cool. Transfer balsamic reduction to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Lay steamed eggplant slices onto a baking sheet lined with parchment. Place onto each eggplant slice: 3-4 spinach leaves, 1 basil leaf, 1 strip dried tomato and 1 stick feta cheese. Sprinkle with pepper and reserved vinegar reduction, to taste. Roll each eggplant tightly and tie with green onion or secure with a toothpick to serve.

Useful Items

paper towels, baking sheet and parchment paper, toothpick

Hints & Tricks

  • If using premade or store-bought balsamic reduction: replace balsamic vinegar and brown sugar with 8 oz of water to steam the eggplant.
  • Trim ends of eggplant discarding pieces too small to roll. Cut eggplant in ¼ inch slices and arrange back into an eggplant shape staggering pieces to allow air to circulate in Varoma.
  • If using toothpicks to secure rolled eggplants: chop green onion and add onto eggplant slices along with spinach, basil, tomato and feta.
  • Replace sundried tomatoes in oil with dried tomatoes that have been added to warm water for 30 minutes.
  • Store remaining balsamic reduction in an airtight container for up to two weeks.
  • Replace feta cheese with marinated tofu.
