Ginger Chicken Meatballs in Scallion Broth

Ingredients

Meatballs

  • 3 scallions, trimmed, cut into pieces (2 in.)
  • 2 oz red bell peppers, cut into pieces
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • ½ oz ginger, peeled, cut into pieces
  • 1 oz panko bread crumbs
  • 1 tsp dried red chili flakes
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 24 oz ground chicken, thigh meat
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce, low sodium

Broth

  • 12 scallions, trimmed, cut into pieces (2 in.)
  • 2 tsp fish sauce
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 50 oz chicken broth, low sodium
  • ½ oz ginger, peeled, thinly sliced
  • 6 oz soba noodles
  • 10 oz broccolini, trimmed, cut into pieces (½ in.)

Preparation

Meatballs

  1. Place scallions, bell peppers, garlic, ginger, panko, chili flakes and egg yolk into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  2. Add chicken, oil and soy sauce and mix 7 sec/speed 3. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Rinse mixing bowl.
  3. Line Varoma dish and Varoma tray with wet parchment paper, leaving some vent holes open. Roll ground chicken mixture into walnut sized meatballs (approx. 35) and place onto prepared Varoma dish and Varoma tray.

Broth

  1. Place scallions, fish sauce, salt and oil into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and sauté 3 min/250°F/speed 1.
  2. Add broth and ginger. Place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 18-20 min/Varoma/speed 2 until meatballs are cooked through. Set meatballs aside and keep warm.
  3. Add soba noodles and broccolini into mixing bowl and with aid of spatula through hole in mixing bowl lid cook 5 min/212°F//speed . Serve hot with reserved meatballs.

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with chili garlic sauce.
  • Check Ginger Chicken Meatballs for doneness after 20 minutes and if necessary, cook a further 5 min/Varoma/speed 2.
