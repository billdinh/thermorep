Spinach, Orange and Carrot Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 orange, zest thinly peeled, pith removed, flesh segmented, deseeded
  • 25 g red split lentils, rinsed
  • 40 g onions, quartered
  • 150 g carrots, cut in pieces
  • 50 g butter
  • 700 g chicken stock
  • 50 g orange juice
  • ¼ tsp ground nutmeg
  • ½ - 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 250 g fresh spinach
  • single cream, for drizzling
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
744 kJ / 178 kcal
Protein
4.5 g
Carbohydrates
13 g
Fat
12 g

Preparation

  1. Place orange zest and lentils in mixing bowl then grind 40 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add onions, carrots and butter then chop 4 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then sauté 3 min/100°C/speed 1.
  3. Add stock, orange juice, nutmeg, salt, pepper and orange segments then cook 10 min/90°C/speed 2.
  4. Add spinach and mix 2 min/speed  whilst stirring with spatula through hole in mixing bowl lid.
  5. Blend 2 min/speed 10 then serve with a drizzle of cream.
