Mimosa float

Ingredients

  • 3 oranges, juice and zest, finely grated
  • 50 g sugar
  • ½ tsp citric acid (see Tips)
  • ½ tsp tartaric acid (see Tips)
  • 1 bottle sparkling wine (750 g)
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
205 kJ / 48 kcal
Protein
0.7 g
Carbohydrates
11 g
Fat
0.1 g

Preparation

  1. Place zest and sugar into mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 10.
  2. Add orange juice, citric acid and tartaric acid and cook 5 min/100°C/speed 1. Using a fine-mesh sieve, strain liquid into a jug. Transfer liquid into ice cube trays or a shallow freezable container and place into freezer for a minimum of 4 hours or overnight to set. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Place frozen orange mixture into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  4. Insert butterfly whisk. Mix 5 sec/speed 3. Remove butterfly whisk. Place mixture into a freezable container and place into freezer for a further 2 hours or until solid.
  5. Place small scoops of sorbet into champagne glasses and top with sparkling wine. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

ice cube tray, fine mesh sieve, jug, freezable storage container, champagne glasses, freezer

Hints & Tricks

  • Citric acid and tartaric acid can be found in most major supermarkets.
  • After freezing mixture, sorbet can be formed into frozen balls (using hands or spoons) and returned to freezer, ready to add to sparkling wine.
