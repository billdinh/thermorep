Spice-rubbed chicken with maple glaze

Ingredients

  • ¼ tsp ground smoked paprika
  • ¼ tsp ground cayenne pepper
  • ¼ tsp dried oregano
  • ¼ tsp garlic flakes
  • 2 pinches salt
  • 2 pinches freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 skinless chicken breast fillets (approx 400-500 g) (see Tips)
  • 100 g water
  • 80 g maple syrup
  • ¼ tsp dried chilli flakes
  • 100 g fresh baby spinach leaves
  • 50 g frozen green peas, defrosted

Preparation

  1. Place paprika, cayenne pepper, oregano, garlic flakes, salt and pepper into a ceramic or glass bowl and combine. Add chicken to bowl and rub spices into the chicken breast. Set aside for 10 minutes.
  2. Place water, maple syrup and chilli flakes into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position and place spice-rubbed chicken into Varoma dish. Steam 12-15 min/Varoma/speed 1, or until chicken is cooked through. Transfer chicken into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and cover to keep warm.
  3. Continue to cook maple syrup for a further 3 min/100°C/speed 2, without measuring cup.
  4. Add spinach and peas and cook 1 min/100°C//speed 1, or until peas are heated through and spinach has wilted.
  5. Place chicken breast on top of spinach and peas. Drizzle chicken with remaining maple glaze and serve immediately.

Useful Items

ceramic or glass bowl, thermal serving bowl or other large bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • Recipe for one: Reduce all ingredients by half except water and maple syrup. Use 100 g water and 80 g maple syrup and proceed as per above.
  • Serve Spice-rubbed chicken with a side of Rosemary and thyme polenta chips.
  • If you are using large chicken breasts, you will need to cut each breast into halves lengthways prior to rubbing with spices. This will ensure it is cooked completely through in the cooking times indicated.
