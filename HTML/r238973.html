Chicken Caesar pasta salad

Ingredients

Caesar dressing

  • 120 g light olive oil
  • 50 g Parmesan cheese, cut into pieces (approx. 3 cm)
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 3 anchovy fillets, drained
  • 50 g mayonnaise (see Tips)
  • 20 g lemon juice
  • 2 tsp Dijon mustard

Chicken pasta salad

  • 1700 g boiling water
  • 300 g dried spiral pasta
  • 6 whole eggs
  • 500 g chicken tenderloins
  • 1 - 2 pinches salt
  • 1 - 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 200 g bacon, cut into pieces (1-2 cm)
  • 1 - 2 spring onions/shallots, cut into pieces
  • 8 - 12 leaves cos lettuce, cut into pieces (3-4 cm)
  • Parmesan cheese, shaved, for garnishing

Preparation

Caesar dressing

  1. Place a jug onto mixing bowl lid and weigh oil into it. Set aside.
  2. Place Parmesan and garlic into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 9.
  3. Insert butterfly whisk. Add anchovies, mayonnaise, lemon juice and mustard and mix 1 min/speed 4.
  4. Mix 1-2 min/speed 4, slowly pouring oil through hole in mixing bowl lid, until combined. Transfer into a bowl, cover with plastic wrap and place into refrigerator until ready to use. Clean and dry mixing bowl and butterfly whisk.

Chicken pasta salad

  1. Place 1200 g of the boiling water into mixing bowl. Add pasta. Place Varoma into position and place eggs into Varoma dish. Insert Varoma tray, weigh chicken onto it and season with salt and pepper. Secure Varoma lid and cook 9-10 min/Varoma/î€ƒ/speed 1 or until pasta is just cooked.
  2. Remove Varoma. Drain pasta and transfer into a large bowl. Place remaining 500 g boiling water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma with chicken and eggs back into position. Secure Varoma lid and cook 7-10 min/Varoma/speed 1 or until chicken and eggs are cooked to your liking. Cut chicken into bite-size pieces and transfer into bowl with pasta and set aside to cool for 10 minutes. Place into refrigerator to cool completely (approx. 30 minutes). Set eggs aside to cool, then peel once cool enough to handle and cut into quarters.
  3. Place a frying pan over medium-high heat. Add bacon and fry for 3-4 minutes, until crispy. Transfer onto a plate lined with paper towel and set aside to cool.
  4. Add spring onions/shallots, lettuce, cooked bacon and reserved Caesar dressing to chicken pasta mixture and toss to combine. Top with quartered eggs and garnish with shaved Parmesan to serve.

Useful Items

jug, bowl, large bowl, plate, paper towel, frying pan, plastic wrap, fine mesh sieve, refrigerator, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • This dish is great for workday lunches and picnics, but we recommend keeping the dressing separate and adding just before serving.
  • To make your own mayonnaise, please refer to the recipe in The Basic Cookbook or the Everyday Cookbook.
