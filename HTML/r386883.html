Chilli con carne

Ingredients

  • 1 brown onion, cut into quarters
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 fresh long red chilli, trimmed and deseeded if preferred (optional)
  • 2 sprigs fresh oregano, leaves only
  • 20 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp sweet paprika
  • 1 red capsicum, deseeded and cut into pieces (1 cm)
  • 1 carrot, peeled and cut into pieces (1 cm)
  • 60 g tomato paste
  • 400 g canned tomatoes (see Tips)
  • 1 tbsp Meat stock paste (see Tips)
  • 500 g beef mince
  • 400 g canned kidney beans, rinsed and drained (approx. 250 g after draining)
  • fresh coriander, leaves only, for garnishing
  • multigrain tortillas, baked and broken into chips, to serve (see Tips)
  • sour cream, to serve (optional)

Preparation

  1. Place onion, garlic, chilli (optional), oregano and oil into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7.
  2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and cook 3 min/100°C/speed 2.
  3. Add cumin, paprika, capsicum, carrot, tomato paste, tomatoes and stock paste. Insert simmering basket, weigh mince into it and cook 12 min/100°C//speed 2, without measuring cup.
  4. Using spatula, gently stir and break up mince in simmering basket, then cook 4 min/100°C//speed 2, without measuring cup. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and add cooked mince into mixing bowl.
  5. Add kidney beans, stir to combine using spatula and cook 2 min/90°C//speed 1. Garnish with coriander and serve with baked tortilla chips and sour cream (optional).
