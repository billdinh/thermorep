Spiced matcha latte

Ingredients

  • 400 g milk (see Tips)
  • 1 - 2 tsp matcha powder, to taste, plus extra for sprinkling
  • 1 tsp honey
  • ¼ tsp ground cinnamon, plus extra to taste
  • &frac18; tsp ground nutmeg, plus extra to taste
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
634 kJ / 151 kcal
Protein
6.9 g
Carbohydrates
15.9 g
Fat
7 g

Preparation

  1. Place milk into mixing bowl and heat 4 min/80°C/speed 2.
  2. Add matcha powder, honey and spices and blend 30 sec/80°C/speed 6 or until combined.
  3. Add additional matcha and spices to taste, if desired, then blend 15 sec/speed 8, until combined and frothy.
  4. Divide between cups or mugs. Using a fine-mesh sieve, sprinkle with extra matcha powder to serve.

Useful Items

2 mugs or a serving jug, fine mesh sieve

Hints & Tricks

  • You can use cow’s milk, almond milk or soy milk to make this latte. My favourite is to use half cow’s milk and half almond milk for a light, fragrant and creamy result.
