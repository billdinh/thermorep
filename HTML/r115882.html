Black bean tomato soup with coriander lime cream

Ingredients

  • 20 g olive oil
  • 1 brown onion (approx. 120 g), cut into halves
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 80 g celery stalks, cut into pieces
  • 2 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only, plus extra for garnishing
  • 50 g roasted red capsicum
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • ½ tsp chilli powder
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 400 g canned black beans, rinsed and drained (approx. 250 g after draining)
  • 400 g canned diced tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp Vegetable stock paste (see Tips)
  • 500 g water
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 1 lime, zest and juice (1 tsp), no white pith, plus extra lime wedges for serving
  • 70 g sour cream

Preparation

  1. Place oil, onion, garlic, celery, 1 sprig of the coriander leaves and roasted capsicum into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5, then cook 5 min/100°C/speed 1.
  2. Add cumin, chilli powder, tomato paste and pepper and cook 1 min/100°C/speed 1.
  3. Add beans, tomatoes, stock paste, water and salt and cook 25 min/100°C//speed 1. Transfer half of the soup into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and cover to keep warm.
  4. Blend remaining soup in mixing bowl 10 sec/speed 5. Transfer into thermal serving bowl or other large bowl with reserved soup and cover to keep warm. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  5. Place remaining 1 sprig coriander leaves and lime zest into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  6. Add lime juice and sour cream and mix 5 sec/speed 5.
  7. Ladle soup into serving bowls and garnish with coriander lime cream and coriander leaves. Serve with lime wedges.

Useful Items

kitchen towel, thermal serving bowl or other large bowl, ladle, serving bowls

Hints & Tricks
