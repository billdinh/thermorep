Blackberry Muffins with Cardamom Crunch Topping

Ingredients

Cardamom Crunch Topping

  • 30 g plain flour
  • 30 g light brown sugar
  • 25 g unsalted butter, cold, diced
  • ½ tsp ground cardamom

Muffins

  • 120 g granulated sugar
  • 1 lemon, thin peelings of skin only
  • 100 g unsalted butter, diced
  • 100 g plain yoghurt
  • 180 g plain flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 250 g blackberries, fresh

Preparation

Cardamom Crunch Topping

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C and line a 12 hole muffin tin with paper muffin cases.
  2. Place flour, sugar, butter and cardamom in mixing bowl then mix Turbo/1 sec/3 times. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.

Muffins

  1. Place 40 g sugar and lemon peel in mixing bowl then grind 10 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add remaining 80 g sugar, butter and yoghurt then mix 10 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add flour, eggs and baking powder then mix 10 sec/speed 3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Fold in blackberries with spatula then divide evenly between prepared muffin cases. Scatter reserved topping over muffins then bake for 15-20 minutes (190°C) until firm and browned. Transfer to a wire rack and allow to cool.
  5. Serve at room temperature or store in an airtight container until needed.

Useful Items

muffin paper cases, 12 hole muffin tin, wire rack, airtight container, oven

Hints & Tricks

These muffins are full of flavour from the addition of juicy berries and the topping provides a nice sweet, crunchy finish.
