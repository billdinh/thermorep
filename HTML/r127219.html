Spicy turkey kebabs

Ingredients

  • 500 g turkey meat, boneless, skinless
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 2 fresh red chillies
  • 30 g roasted salted peanuts
  • 6 black peppercorns
  • 1 tsp yeast extract (e.g. Bovril®, Cenovis®, Marmite®)
  • 4 pieces orange zest, no white part (1,5 x 5 cm)
  • 50 g orange juice, freshly squeezed
  • 20 g dry sherry wine
  • 30 g brown sugar
  • ½ tsp ground paprika
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 50 g soy sauce

Preparation

  1. Immerse wooden skewer sticks in cold water for 30 minutes. Cut turkey in thin strips of 2 x 10 cm and of 5 mm thickness. Skewer each strip of turkey onto skewer stick passing through the meat several times so that it is flat along the stick. Place all skewers in airtight container and set aside.
  2. Place garlic cloves into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7.
  3. Add cayenne peppers, peanuts, peppercorns, yeast extract, orange peel, orange juice, sherry, brown sugar, paprika, vinegar and soy sauce and chop 10 sec/speed 7. Pour mixture over kebabs. Leave to marinate in refrigerator for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Strain kebabs reserving the marinade. Pour the reserved marinade into mixing bowl and place Varoma dish into position. Place skewers inside, some perpendicular to others. Cover Varoma and steam 15 min/Varoma/speed 1. Set Varoma aside and place skewers on a hot dish. Pour sauce over skewers and serve immediately.

Useful Items

air-tight container, wood skewers, refrigerator
