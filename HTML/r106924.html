Cauliflower Rice Pilaf with Yams

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Parmesan cheese, cubed (¾ in.) (optional)
  • 1 - 2 oz pistachio nuts, unsalted, shelled, peeled
  • 1 - 2 oz pine nuts
  • 1 - 2 oz slivered almonds
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • ½ tsp ground cumin
  • ½ tsp ground ginger
  • ½ tsp ground coriander
  • ½ tsp ground turmeric
  • 24 ½ - 26 ½ oz cauliflower, broken into florets (1 in.)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 4 green onions, roots removed and quartered
  • 4 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only (approx. 0.5 oz), divided
  • 4 sprigs cilantro, leaves only (approx. 0.5 oz), divided
  • 7 oz fresh mushrooms, thinly sliced
  • 1 oz white wine
  • 17 ½ oz water
  • 10 ½ oz yams, cubed (approx. ¾ in.)
  • 2 oz currants
  • 3 oz fresh baby spinach, leaves only, lightly chopped
  • 12 soft dried apricots, thinly sliced (approx. 3 oz)
  • ½ tsp salt, to taste
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper, to taste

Preparation

  1. Place Parmesan cheese into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.
  2. Place pistachios, pine nuts, almonds and spices into mixing bowl and dry roast 5 min/Varoma/speed 1. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place cauliflower florets into mixing bowl and chop 2-3 sec//speed 5. Mix cauliflower with spatula and chop again if necessary, 1-2 sec//speed 5 until an even consistency is achieved. Transfer into Varoma dish and set aside.
  4. Place garlic, green onions, half the parsley and half the cilantro into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add mushrooms and wine and mix 10 sec//speed 1. Pour mushroom mixture over cauliflower in Varoma.
  6. Place water into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket and weigh in yams.
  7. Secure Varoma lid, place Varoma into position and steam 15-18 min/Varoma/speed 2, or until yams are cooked. Remove Varoma, remove simmering basket with spatula and set yams aside to cool and drain for 5 minutes.
  8. Place a large serving bowl onto mixing bowl lid, weigh in currants and baby spinach.
  9. Add dried apricots, reserved spice-nut mixture, cooled cauliflower-mushroom mixture and cooled yams to serving bowl. Season to taste, then stir through remaining parsley and cilantro leaves with spatula.
  10. Serve immediately topped with reserved Parmesan cheese or cover and place in refrigerator until ready to use.

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve Cauliflower Rice Pilaf with Yams as a main course, or as a side dish. It makes a great salad to take to your next barbecue or picnic.
  • Cauliflower rice is an easy grain-free alternative to rice when made in the Thermomix®.
