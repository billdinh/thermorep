Rabbit stew

Ingredients

  • 20 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 60 g brown onion
  • 10 sprigs fresh mixed herbs, leaves only (e.g. parsley, thyme, oregano), plus extra for garnishing
  • 400 g canned chopped tomatoes
  • 800 g rabbit meat, cut into pieces (approx. 6 cm)
  • 50 g water
  • 40 g dry white wine
  • 1 tsp Chicken stock paste (see Tips)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper

Preparation

  1. Place oil, garlic, onion and mixed herbs into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5, then cook 3 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  2. Add tomatoes and cook 10 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Add rabbit, water, wine, stock paste, salt and pepper and cook 30-35 min/100°C//speed , until tender and cooked through. Transfer into a serving bowl, garnish with fresh herbs and serve immediately.

Hints & Tricks

  • Please refer to The Basic Cookbook or Everyday Cookbook for the Chicken stock paste recipe.
  • Serve stew with polenta or steamed vegetables such as carrots, broccoli or cauliflower rice.
