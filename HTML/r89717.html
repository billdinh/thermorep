Thai noodle and seafood salad

Ingredients

  • 200 g rice vermicelli noodles
  • 10 dried shiitake mushrooms
  • 60 g peanut oil
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 6 spring onions/shallots, white part only (green fronds reserved)
  • 1 - 2 fresh bird's eye chillies, plus extra for garnishing
  • 12 sprigs fresh coriander, roots and stems (leaves reserved)
  • 200 g medium-sized raw prawns, peeled
  • 200 g calamari hoods (approx. 2-3 hoods), cut into 1.5 x 5 cm strips
  • 230 g canned bamboo shoots, drained
  • 1 carrot, cut into julienne slices
  • 120 g lime juice (approx. 6 limes)
  • 100 g fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp palm sugar (approx. 2 cm cube), grated
  • 60 g sweet chilli sauce
  • 2 tomatoes, deseeded and cut into strips (1 cm) (optional)
  • 1 - 2 pinches sea salt, to taste
  • 1 - 2 pinches ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges, to serve

Preparation

  1. Place a ceramic or glass bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh vermicelli noodles into it. Add enough hot water to cover and leave to soak for 2-3 minutes or until noodles are soft. Drain noodles using simmering basket and set aside.
  2. Place shiitake mushrooms into the ceramic or glass bowl and add enough hot water to cover. Leave mushrooms to soak for 12-13 minutes.
  3. Transfer drained vermicelli noodles into a large serving bowl and rub with 20 g of the peanut oil (approx. 1 tbsp). Drain shiitake mushrooms using simmering basket, then remove stems and discard. Slice mushroom cups, then transfer into a separate bowl to the noodles and set aside.
  4. Place garlic, spring onions/shallots, chillies, coriander roots and stems and remaining 40 g peanut oil into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down side of mixing bowl with spatula, then sauté 2 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  5. Add prawns and sauté 1-2 min/Varoma//speed 1. Using a pair of tongs, carefully remove prawns from mixing bowl and transfer into the large serving bowl with the noodles. Leave remainder of contents in mixing bowl.
  6. Add calamari and sauté 1-2 min/Varoma//speed 1. Using a pair of tongs, remove calamari from mixing bowl and transfer into the large serving bowl with the prawns and noodles. Leave remainder of contents in mixing bowl.
  7. Add reserved shiitake mushroom slices, bamboo shoots, carrot, lime juice, fish sauce and palm sugar and sauté 3 min/Varoma//speed 1. Transfer all mixing bowl contents into the large serving bowl with the noodles, prawns and calamari.
  8. Place serving bowl onto mixing bowl lid, then weigh sweet chilli sauce into it. Add tomatoes, reserved coriander leaves and reserved spring onion/shallot fronds to serving bowl and gently combine with spatula.
  9. Season salad to taste with salt and pepper, then place into refrigerator to chill (approx. 30 minutes). Serve salad chilled, with extra finely sliced bird's eye chilli and lime wedges on the side.

Useful Items

ceramic or glass bowl, large serving bowl, tongs, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Clean the roots of your coriander before adding to the mixing bowl. Scrape down the root to remove the tougher, more fibrous bits on the outside.
