Hazelnut Bavarian

Ingredients

  • 4 gelatine leaves, titanium strength
  • 100 g hazelnuts, toasted
  • 500 g cream, 35-37 % fat, chilled
  • 6 egg yolks, from medium eggs
  • 120 g caster sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste
  • 1 Tbsp water
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1629 kJ / 388 kcal
Protein
6.5 g
Carbohydrates
17.3 g

Preparation

  1. Place gelatine leaves into a ceramic or glass bowl and cover with cold water. Leave to soak until gelatine leaves have softened and swelled.
  2. Place hazelnuts in mixing bowl and chop 8 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Without rinsing mixing bowl, insert butterfly whisk. Place cream in mixing bowl and whip until cream has thickened/speed 3, watching cream carefully through hole in mixing bowl lid to avoid overwhipping (approx. 2-3 minutes). Remove butterfly whisk. Transfer to a separate bowl, cover with cling film and refrigerate.
  4. Without rinsing mixing bowl, re-insert butterfly whisk. Place egg yolks, caster sugar and vanilla bean paste in mixing bowl then cook 9 min/70°C/speed 3.
  5. Squeeze gelatine leaves to remove excess water. Add squeezed gelatine leaves and ¾ of the reserved hazelnuts then mix 30 sec/speed 3. Transfer to a large bowl. Set remaining ¼ of the hazelnuts aside for garnishing.
  6. Fold reserved whipped cream into hazelnut mixture. Lightly brush water or rum into 8 individual ramekins (6-8 cm), then add Bavarian mixture. Cover ramekins with cling film and refrigerate for a at least 6 hours.
  7. Once set, remove from fridge and garnish with remaining hazelnuts before serving.

Useful Items

ceramic or glass bowl, cling film, ramekins, pastry brush, large bowl, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Chocolate Bavarian: substitute the hazelnuts with 100 g chocolate, broken into pieces. Grate chocolate 8 sec/speed 7 in step 1, then proceed as per recipe.
  • Vanilla Bavarian: omit the hazelnuts in step 2. Substitute the vanilla paste with 1 whole vanilla bean pod, seeds scraped and proceed as per recipe.
