Courgette and Carrot Quinoa Salad

Ingredients

  • 250 g quinoa
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 700 g water
  • 1 vegetable stock cube (for 0.5 l)
  • 450 g courgettes, cut in pieces
  • 300 g carrots, cut in pieces

Dressing

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 30 g mixed fresh herbs, leaves only (e.g. basil, parsley)
  • 40 g lemon juice
  • 20 g vinegar (e.g. balsamic, cider)
  • 50 g olive oil
  • 2 tsp fine sea salt
  • ½ - ¾ tsp ground black pepper, to taste
  • 2 pinches sugar
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1233 kJ / 295 kcal
Protein
7 g
Carbohydrates
33 g
Fat
15 g

Preparation

  1. Place quinoa and oil in mixing bowl then fry 3 min/120°C//speed 1.
  2. Add water and stock cube then cook 22 min/90°C//speed . Drain through a fine sieve, discarding cooking water, then place in a serving bowl.
  3. Place courgettes and carrots in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 4 while stirring with spatula through hole in mixing bowl lid. Add to serving bowl with quinoa.

Dressing

  1. Place garlic and herbs in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add lemon juice, vinegar, oil, salt, pepper and sugar then mix 5 sec/speed 4. Pour over quinoa and vegetables and mix together well with spatula.
  3. Allow to chill in fridge for 1 hour before serving.

Useful Items

fine sieve, serving bowl, refrigerator
