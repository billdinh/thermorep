Lemon and Herb Salmon Burgers with Parmesan Courgettes

Ingredients

Parmesan Courgettes

  • 60 g Parmesan cheese, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 60 g white bread, cut in pieces
  • 1 medium egg
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 500 g courgettes, cut in batons (5 cm x 1 cm)

Mayonnaise

  • 150 g sunflower oil
  • 1 medium egg
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • 2 tsp Dijon mustard

Salmon Burgers

  • 60 g shallots, halved
  • 5 g fresh dill leaves
  • 1 Tbsp fresh tarragon, cut in pieces
  • 400 g fresh salmon fillets, skinless, cut in pieces
  • 80 g frozen green peas
  • 1 Tbsp grated lemon zest
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt

Serving

  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 4 burger buns
  • 160 g cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 8 green lettuce leaves, for serving

Preparation

Parmesan Courgettes

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C and line a baking tray with baking paper.
  2. Place Parmesan and bread in mixing bowl then grate 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. In a bowl, lightly whisk egg and salt. Dip courgette batons into whisked egg, then roll in the breadcrumb mixture and place on prepared tray. Bake for 25 minutes (180°C). Meanwhile, continue with recipe.

Mayonnaise

  1. Without cleaning mixing bowl, place a jug on mixing bowl lid, weigh in oil and set aside.
  2. Place egg, pepper, salt, vinegar and mustard in mixing bowl. Mix 1 min 30 sec/speed 4 while very slowly pouring reserved oil onto mixing bowl lid, letting it drizzle around measuring cup in a thin stream onto rotating blades to create an emulsion. Transfer mayonnaise to a serving bowl and set aside.

Salmon Burgers

  1. Place shallots, dill and tarragon in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Chop again 5 sec/speed 5 then scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add salmon, peas, lemon zest and salt then chop 10 sec/speed 8. Transfer mixture to a bowl, divide into 8 and shape into burgers (approx. Ø 5 cm).

Serving

  1. Heat a frying pan with oil over a medium-high heat. Fry burgers for 5-6 minutes on each side or until golden brown on the outside and cooked through. Slice buns in half and lightly toast in hot pan. Place two salmon burgers between 2 bun halves and top with tomatoes, lettuce and reserved mayonnaise. Serve immediately with Parmesan courgettes.

Useful Items

jug, frying pan, baking tray, baking paper, oven, hob

Hints & Tricks

  • This is a great example of all the elements of a meal being made in your Thermomix®: the burgers, mayonnaise and courgette coating – you can even make the burger buns if you have time!
  • Fresh mayonnaise is simple to make in the Thermomix®. Make double the quantity and store in the fridge for up to 3 days.
  • The salmon burgers can be made with any firm-fleshed fish, such as haddock, pollock, trout or tuna.
  • For a more satisfying burger bun, use toasted ciabatta or focaccia bread.
  • Add 5 g extra dill to the mayonnaise to flavour it.
