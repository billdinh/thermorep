Vegan tiramisu

Ingredients

Sponge

  • 100 g almond milk
  • 1 tsp apple cider vinegar
  • 80 g plain flour
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • ¼ tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 90 g dairy free margarine
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 80 g caster sugar
  • ¼ tsp vanilla bean paste

Coffee syrup

  • 200 g brewed espresso coffee
  • 50 g Kahlua®

Vegan mascarpone

  • 300 g raw cashews
  • 70 g pure maple syrup
  • 120 g coconut oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste
  • 200 g almond milk

Assembly

  • 1 - 2 tsp unsweetened cocoa powder, for dusting
  • 30 g dairy free chocolate, shaved (optional)

Preparation

Sponge

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Line a baking tray (25 x 17 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh milk and vinegar into it. Stir to combine and set aside to curdle.
  3. Place a separate bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh flour, baking powder and bicarbonate of soda into it. Stir to combine and set aside.
  4. Place margarine, salt, sugar and vanilla paste into mixing bowl and beat 1 min/speed 3 .
  5. Insert butterfly whisk. Add half of the reserved flour mixture and half of the reserved milk mixture, then mix 30 sec/speed 4 . Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  6. Add remaining flour and milk mixture and mix 30 sec/speed 4.
  7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix 10 sec/speed 3 .  Remove butterfly whisk .
  8. Transfer mixture onto baking tray and spread out. Bake for 18-20 minutes (180°C) or until golden brown. Set aside to cool on baking tray. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Coffee syrup

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh coffee and Kahlua® into it. Set bowl aside.

Vegan mascarpone

  1. Place cashews, syrup, coconut oil, vanilla and milk into mixing bowl and blend 1 min/speed 8 or until smooth. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add 120 g of the reserved coffee syrup and mix and 30 sec/speed 5. Set mascarpone mixture aside in mixing bowl.

Assembly

  1. Using a round cookie cutter (approx. 5 cm depending on glass size), cut out 12 circles from baked sponge. Place 1 sponge into the base of 6 serving glasses (250 ml). Drizzle 1-2 teaspoons of the reserved coffee syrup on top of each sponge. Top with 2 tablespoons of the mascarpone mixture and sprinkle with cocoa powder. Place into freezer for 10 minutes to set.
  2. Repeat layering process of sponge, coffee syrup and mascarpone mixture to create another layer. Cover glasses with plastic wrap and place into refrigerator for a minimum of 2 hours or overnight to set. Just before serving, dust with extra cocoa powder and decorate with chocolate shavings (optional).

Useful Items

baking tray (25 x 17 cm), baking paper, bowl, serving glasses (250 ml), round cookie cutter (5 cm), plastic wrap, oven, freezer, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Dairy free chocolate is often vegan, but always check the label of your products.
  • You can replace almond milk with soy milk, if preferred.
