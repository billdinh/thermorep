Pork and Mushroom Broth

Ingredients

Sauce

  • 5 g sugar
  • 30 g soy sauce
  • 120 g rice vinegar
  • 1 ½ tsp fine sea salt

Marinade

  • 100 g pork meat, lean, sinew free, cut in thin strips
  • 5 g soy sauce
  • 5 g sherry
  • 5 g cornflour
  • 20 g water
  • 5 g sesame oil, plus extra for drizzling
  • 1 fresh red chilli, halved, deseeded
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper, plus extra for sprinkling

Broth

  • 25 g dried shiitake mushrooms
  • 15 g dried wood ear mushrooms (see tip)
  • 15 g dried enokitake mushrooms
  • 1560 g water, plus enough extra, hot, for soaking
  • 30 g cornflour
  • 5 g vegetable oil
  • 2 medium eggs, beaten
  • 1 beef stock cube (for 0.5 l)
  • 250 g tofu, cut in small cubes
  • 20 g fresh coriander leaves

Preparation

Sauce

  1. Place sugar, soy sauce, rice vinegar and salt in mixing bowl then mix 5 sec/speed 3. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.

Marinade

  1. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid, weigh in pork and set aside.
  2. Place soy sauce, sherry, cornflour, water, sesame oil, chilli, salt and pepper in mixing bowl then mix 5 sec/speed 3. Pour over meat, stir well and leave to marinate for up to 1 hour (see tip). Meanwhile, continue with recipe.

Broth

  1. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid then weigh in shiitake mushrooms, wood ear mushrooms and enokitake mushrooms. Remove bowl from lid then cover with hot water. Set aside and leave to soak for approx. 30 minutes. Drain mushrooms in Varoma dish, squeezing well to remove excess water. Cut mushrooms in small pieces and set aside.
  2. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid then weigh in cornflour and 60 g water. Remove bowl from lid, stir well and set aside.
  3. Place another bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in oil. Remove bowl from lid, add eggs and mix well. Set aside.
  4. Place reserved mushroom pieces, beef stock and remaining 1500 g water in mixing bowl then heat 20 min/100°C/speed .
  5. Add reserved meat with marinade and cook 5 min/100°C/speed .
  6. Add tofu and reserved cornflour mixture, stir gently with spatula then cook 5 min/100°C//speed .
  7. Remove measuring cup and cook 1 min/100°C//speed . Meanwhile, add reserved egg mixture through hole in mixing bowl lid.
  8. Add reserved sauce and stir 1 min/100°C//speed . Transfer to soup bowls then drizzle with sesame oil and sprinkle with pepper. Garnish with coriander leaves before serving hot.
