Rough puff pastry

Ingredients

  • 300 g unsalted butter, cut into pieces (2 cm) and frozen
  • 140 g potato starch
  • 140 g gluten free cornflour, plus extra for dusting
  • 50 g brown rice flour (see Tips)
  • 50 g millet flour (see Tips)
  • 2 tsp xanthan gum
  • ¼ tsp guar gum (see Tips)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 180 g chilled water

Preparation

  1. Place all ingredients into mixing bowl and knead 30-40 sec/; the butter should still be visible in small chunks within the pastry dough.
  2. Dust a silicone bread mat or work surface with extra cornflour. Transfer dough onto it and, with floured fingertips, gently form into a ball and then shape into a rectangle.
  3. With the shorter end of the dough closest to you, roll dough into a long rectangle (approx. 60 x 20 cm), dusting pastry and rolling pin with more cornflour as needed, and lifting dough off work surface to ensure dough doesn't stick.
  4. Once rolled, start folding pastry over itself into a total of 6 folds (each fold approx. 10 cm wide).
  5. Wrap pastry in plastic wrap and place into refrigerator to rest for 30 minutes. After shaping pastry as needed (rolling out, lining tart tin, etc.), place into refrigerator to rest for a further 30 minutes before baking. Use as needed.

Useful Items

silicone bread mat, rolling pin, plastic wrap, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • To make your own rice flour, place 250 g rice into mixing bowl and mill 2 min/speed 10. Use as needed.
  • You can buy millet flour and guar gum at health food stores.
