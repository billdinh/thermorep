Pumpkin loaf

Ingredients

  • butter, for greasing, plus extra to serve (optional)
  • 40 g walnuts (optional)
  • 60 g raw almonds
  • 150 g pumpkin, peeled and cut into pieces (3-4 cm)
  • 1 banana, peeled and broken into pieces
  • 2 eggs
  • 60 g milk
  • 100 g brown sugar (see Tips)
  • 120 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 80 g wholemeal self-raising flour
  • 100 g self-raising flour
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 150°C. Grease and line a loaf tin (25 x 10 x 7 cm) with baking paper, extending baking paper over two sides of loaf tin, and set aside.
  2. Place walnuts (optional) into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 5 or until coarsely chopped. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place almonds into mixing bowl and mill 15 sec/speed 9 or until finely ground.
  4. Add pumpkin and banana and blend 6 sec/speed 6 or until pumpkin is grated and banana is mashed. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add eggs, milk, sugar and oil and combine 10 sec//speed 3.
  6. Add flours, cinnamon and reserved walnuts (optional) and combine 15 sec//speed 3. Pour mixture into prepared loaf tin and bake for 60-70 minutes (150°C), or until a skewer inserted into the centre of the loaf comes out clean. Allow loaf to cool in tin for 20 minutes then transfer onto a wire rack to cool completely. Once cooled, cut into slices and serve with butter (optional).

Useful Items

loaf tin (25 x 10 x 7 cm), baking paper, bowl, wire rack, wooden skewer, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • You can use your preferred brown sugar in this recipe – either regular brown sugar or a less refined option such as Rapadura sugar (Panela) or coconut sugar.
  • Nut-free loaf: Leave out the walnuts and replace the almonds with an equal quantity of seed mix (such as a mix of sunflower seeds, pepitas and linseeds, milled until finely ground).
