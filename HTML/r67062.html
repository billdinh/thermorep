Buttercream icing

Ingredients

  • 120 g white sugar
  • 250 g unsalted butter, softened, cut into pieces
  • 60 g milk
  • ½ - 1 tsp natural vanilla extract (optional)
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
225 kJ / 53 kcal
Protein
0.09 g
Carbohydrates
2.9 g
Fat
4.8 g

Preparation

  1. Place white sugar into mixing bowl and mill 20 sec/speed 10.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Add butter, milk and vanilla extract (optional) and mix 3 min/speed 3. Use as needed.

Useful Items

icing spatula, icing bag

Hints & Tricks

  • Fill or frost cakes using a icing spatula, or place icing into a piping bag to pipe decorations onto cakes or pastries.
  • Icing can be kept in an airtight container in the refrigerator for later use. Allow icing to soften at room temperature before using.
  • Coffee buttercream icing: add ½-1 tsp instant coffee in step 2 and proceed as per recipe.
  • Chocolate buttercream icing: add 1-3 tsp cocoa powder in step 2 and proceed as per recipe.
  • Almond praliné buttercream: add 2 tbsp praliné (ground caramelised almonds) in step 2 and proceed as per recipe.
