Paprika chicken with creamy paprika sauce

Ingredients

  • 200 - 300 g basmati rice
  • 1000 g water
  • 200 g fresh green beans, tops and tails removed
  • 150 g pumpkin flesh, cut into thin slices (5 mm)
  • 1 - 2 zucchinis (approx. 200 g), cut into thin slices (5 mm)
  • 1 - 2 carrots (approx. 150 g), cut into thin rounds (5 mm)
  • 600 - 700 g skinless chicken breast fillets, cut into slices (2 cm)
  • 1 - 2 pinches herb salt, to taste
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • 1 ½ - 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tbsp ghee
  • 60 g raw cashews
  • 1 - 2 tsp Chicken stock paste (see Tips)
  • sea salt, for seasoning
  • freshly ground pepper, for seasoning

Preparation

  1. Insert simmering basket into mixing bowl and weigh rice into it. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and rinse rice until water runs clear.
  2. Place water into mixing bowl and insert simmering basket with rice.
  3. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh beans and pumpkin into it. Add zucchini and carrots into the bowl and set aside.
  4. Place Varoma into position and weigh chicken onto tray. Sprinkle with salt, garlic powder and 1 teaspoon of the paprika, then dot with ghee. Secure Varoma lid and mix 5 sec/speed 6 (see Tips), then cook 10 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  5. Set Varoma aside and stir rice using spatula. Place vegetables into Varoma dish, turn chicken over on Varoma tray, secure Varoma lid and cook 12-15 min/Varoma/speed 2 or until chicken, rice and vegetables are cooked to your liking.
  6. Transfer rice, chicken and vegetables into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and cover to keep warm. Pour reserved cooking liquid into a jug and set aside.
  7. Without cleaning mixing bowl, place cashews, stock paste, remaining paprika and 250 g reserved cooking liquid into mixing bowl and blend 1 min/speed 9. Add salt and pepper for seasoning, to taste.
  8. Divide rice between serving plates. Add vegetables and chicken and drizzle with sauce before serving.

Useful Items

bowl, thermal serving bowl or other large bowl, jug, serving plates

Hints & Tricks

  • You can replace the Chicken stock paste with Vegetable stock paste, if preferred. Please refer to The Basic Cookbook or Everyday Cookbook for the stock paste recipes.
  • You can replace the ghee with butter.
  • Washing rice quickly before cooking ensures the rice is wet before cooking, which helps ensure even cooking.
  • You can use any mix of vegetables you like in this recipe, just ensure they are cut into small pieces (larger pieces will take longer to cook, which could affect the texture of your chicken and rice).
  • Nut free sauce: When making the sauce, replace cashews and reserved cooking liquid with 250 g coconut cream and cook 1 min/90°C/speed 4. Season to taste.
