Raspberry Oatmeal Breakfast Cake

Ingredients

  • unsalted butter, for greasing
  • 150 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 50 g wholemeal flour
  • 75 g porridge oats
  • 75 - 125 g caster sugar, to taste (see tip)
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • 170 g skimmed milk
  • 50 g fat-free plain yoghurt
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 Tbsp cornflour
  • 175 g fresh raspberries

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C. Grease and flour a square baking tin (20 cm).
  2. Place plain flour, wholemeal flour, oats, sugar, baking powder and salt in mixing bowl then mix 20 sec/speed 3.
  3. Add milk, yoghurt, egg and cornflour then mix 10 sec/speed 3.
  4. Add raspberries and fold in gently with spatula. Transfer batter to prepared tin then bake for 20-25 minutes (200°C) until a skewer inserted in centre comes out clean.
  5. Leave to cool in tin for at least 10 minutes before removing and slicing into squares. Serve warm or cold.

Useful Items

square baking tin (20 cm), skewer, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Bursting with fresh, juicy raspberries, this breakfast cake is made with wholemeal flour and oats for extra nutrients. It's a sweeter treat that can be enjoyed for breakfast, or as a mid-morning pick-me-up with a cup of tea or coffee.
  • Adjust quantity of sugar depending on ripeness of raspberries and personal taste.
