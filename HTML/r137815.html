Sausage ragu with orecchiette

Ingredients

  • 40 g Parmesan cheese, crust removed and cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 1 tbsp fennel seeds
  • 1 brown onion (approx. 120 g), cut into halves
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 500 g raw sausages (pork), casings removed and meat crumbled
  • 50 g dry white wine
  • 400 g canned chopped tomatoes
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1000 g water
  • 300 g orecchiette pasta

Preparation

  1. Place Parmesan into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place fennel seeds into mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 9.
  3. Add onion, garlic and oil and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add sausage meat and cook 3 min/100°C/speed 1. Stir base of mixing bowl and around blades with aid of spatula to break up meat.
  5. Add wine and cook 3 min/100°C/speed 1, without measuring cup.
  6. Add tomatoes, pepper and 1 teaspoon of the salt and cook 20 min/100°C//speed 1. Transfer into thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and cover to keep warm. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  7. Place water into mixing bowl and heat 8 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  8. Add pasta and remaining 1 teaspoon salt and cook 8-12 min/90°C//speed  or until pasta is cooked to your liking.
  9. Drain pasta using Varoma dish and transfer into thermal serving bowl or other large bowl with meat sauce. Stir until well combined, then serve with reserved Parmesan cheese.
