Salmon Fillets with Buckwheat and Asparagus

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp orange zest
  • 100 g orange juice (approx. 1 orange)
  • 30 g honey
  • ¼ tsp wasabi paste, to taste (optional)
  • 4 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 2 sprigs fresh dill, leaves only
  • 4 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only
  • 4 fresh salmon fillets, skin optional (approx. 500-600 g total)
  • 1 - 2 pinches sea salt, plus extra to taste
  • 1 - 2 pinches ground black pepper, plus extra to taste
  • 1 leek, white part only, quartered
  • 20 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 20 g butter
  • 300 g buckwheat grains
  • 100 g dry white wine
  • 750 g water
  • 2 Tbsp vegetable stock paste (see Tips)
  • 40 g lemon juice (approx.1 lemon)
  • 1 bunch asparagus, woody ends removed and cut in half
  • 100 g cashew nuts, roasted, unsalted (optional)
  • 30 g sunflower seeds
  • 50 g goat's cheese, crumbled

Preparation

  1. Place orange zest, orange juice, honey and wasabi paste (optional) in mixing bowl then cook 6-7 min/Varoma/speed 1, without measuring cup, until a thick glaze consistency is achieved. Transfer orange glaze to a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  2. Place parsley, dill and coriander in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place salmon in Varoma dish and tray, season with salt and pepper and set aside.
  4. Place leek, oil and butter in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then sauté 3 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  5. Add buckwheat, white wine, water and stock paste in mixing bowl. Place Varoma with salmon into position. Brush salmon fillets with half of the reserved orange glaze, then secure Varoma lid and steam 15 min/Varoma//speed 1.5.
  6. Check to see if salmon fillets are cooked to your liking. Transfer to a serving bowl and cover to keep warm. If salmon is not cooked, leave fillets in Varoma and set Varoma aside.
  7. Add lemon juice and reserved chopped herbs to mixing bowl, then season to taste with a little extra salt and pepper if necessary.
  8. Place Varoma back into position, then place asparagus in Varoma dish and tray, arranging around the salmon if applicable. Secure Varoma lid and steam 3-5 min/Varoma//speed 1.5, or until salmon and asparagus are cooked to your liking. Set Varoma aside.
  9. Add cashews (optional) and sunflower seeds to mixing bowl then mix 1 min//speed 2.
  10. Place buckwheat and steamed asparagus on a plate and top with salmon fillets. Brush salmon fillets with remaining orange glaze, then garnish with crumbled goat's cheese and serve immediately.
