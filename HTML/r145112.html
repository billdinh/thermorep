Black forest pudding

Ingredients

  • 60 g unsalted butter, cut into pieces, plus extra for greasing
  • 1300 g water
  • 140 g soft dark brown sugar
  • 60 g cocoa powder
  • 180 g plain flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 130 g full cream milk
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract
  • 40 g dark chocolate melts
  • 400 g canned cherries, drained and juice reserved
  • 2 tsp arrowroot starch
  • 60 g Kirsch (optional)
  • double cream, to serve

Preparation

  1. Grease a dish (18 x 6 cm) and set aside (see Tip).
  2. Place butter into mixing bowl and melt 1 min/100°C/speed 2. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Do not clean mixing bowl.
  3. Place 300 g of the water, 70 g of the sugar and 30 g of the cocoa into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 4, then cook 3 min/100°C/speed 2. Transfer sauce into a jug and set aside. Do not clean mixing bowl.
  4. Place flour, baking powder, salt, eggs, milk, vanilla, remaining 30 g cocoa, remaining 70 g sugar and reserved melted butter into mixing bowl and mix 5 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add chocolate and combine 5 sec//speed 4. Scrape down sides and base of mixing bowl with spatula to ensure mixture is completely combined. Transfer batter into prepared dish. Gently pour reserved sauce over batter. Cover with a piece of baking paper and seal with a piece of aluminium foil. Clean mixing bowl.
  6. Place remaining 1000 g water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish into position, lay 2 wooden skewers (18-19 cm) across base of Varoma dish and carefully place baking dish on top. Secure Varoma lid and steam 45-50 min/Varoma/speed 1 or until centre of pudding is cooked. Remove Varoma and set aside. Discard steaming water.
  7. Place reserved cherry juice, arrowroot and Kirsch (optional) into mixing bowl and mix 5 sec/speed 4, then cook 5 min/100°C/speed 3,  without measuring cup. Place cherries into a bowl and pour sauce over cherries.
  8. Serve pudding warm with cherry sauce and double cream.

Useful Items

round ceramic dish (18 x 6 cm), bowl, jug, baking paper, aluminium foil, wooden skewers

Hints & Tricks

  • As long as your Varoma lid will fit securely in place, you can steam pudding in an oval ceramic dish (19-24 x 7 cm).
