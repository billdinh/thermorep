Chocolate, Rum and Espresso Pudding with Chocolate Caramel Sauce

Ingredients

Puddings

  • 230 g strong brewed coffee
  • 185 g unsalted butter, diced, plus extra for greasing
  • 300 g caster sugar
  • 150 g dark chocolate (70% cocoa solids), small pieces or callets
  • 75 g rum
  • 75 g eggs (approx. 1.5 medium eggs), whisked
  • 185 g plain flour, plus extra for flouring
  • 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt

Chocolate Caramel Sauce

  • 470 g whipping cream
  • 1 ¼ Tbsp liquid glucose
  • 150 g milk chocolate, small pieces or callets
  • 240 g granulated sugar
  • 75 g unsalted butter, cold, diced
  • ice cream, for serving (optional)

Preparation

Puddings

  1. Place coffee, butter and sugar in mixing bowl then boil 3 min/100°C/speed 1.
  2. Add chocolate and emulsify 10 sec/speed 3. Remove lid and leave to cool until temperature reads 40°C. Meanwhile, grease and flour 8 dariole moulds and preheat oven to 150°C.
  3. Add rum and eggs then mix 20 sec/speed 3.
  4. Add flour, bicarbonate of soda and salt then mix 1 min/speed 5. Fill prepared moulds up to three-quarters full and bake for 20-25 minutes (150°C) until risen and set. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl and prepare sauce.

Chocolate Caramel Sauce

  1. Place cream and glucose in mixing bowl then boil 4 min/100°C/speed 1.
  2. Add chocolate and emulsify 10 sec/speed 3.
  3. Place a non-stick frying pan over a high heat. When hot, slowly add sugar and stir with a wooden spoon until a light caramel forms. When sugar has melted fully, stir in butter
  4. Once butter and syrup are combined, carefully pour caramel sauce over chocolate cream in mixing bowl. Mix 1 min/100°C/speed 3.
  5. Demould puddings on warm plates and serve, covered with chocolate caramel sauce, with ice cream if desired.

Useful Items

dariole moulds, non-stick frying pan, wooden spoon, serving plates, oven, hob

Hints & Tricks

  • Use pasteurised whole eggs from cartons, for convenience.
