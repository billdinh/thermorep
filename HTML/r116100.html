Warm Salad with Lentils, Cauliflower and Beans

Ingredients

  • 150 g brown lentils, dried (soaked for 30 minutes, then rinsed)
  • 400 g water
  • 150 g red onions, quartered
  • 1 dried bay leaf
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 200 g fresh green beans, cut in pieces
  • 200 g cauliflower florets, small
  • 10 sprigs fresh parsley
  • 10 g sherry vinegar
  • 40 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp zaatar

Preparation

  1. Place rinsed lentils, water, 50 g red onions, bay leaf, garlic and ½ tsp salt in mixing bowl.
  2. Insert simmering basket and weigh in beans and cauliflower then cook 25 min/100°C//speed . Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and place vegetables in a serving bowl. Drain lentils through simmering basket, remove bay leaf and pour over vegetables. Clean mixing bowl.
  3. Place parsley in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7.
  4. Add remaining 100 g red onions, vinegar, oil, remaining ½ tsp salt and zataar then chop 4 sec/speed 4. Pour into serving bowl, mix well and serve.

Hints & Tricks

  • Zataar is a mixture of spices and seeds very popular in the Middle East. It is available in large supermarkets or can be made at home with sumac, dried oregano, thyme, cumin, ground cumin, ground black pepper, fennel, marjoram, toasted sesame seeds, etc. The ratios of each vary according to the tastes of each region or family.
