Yoghurt Parfait, Quinoa and Apple Crumble

Ingredients

Crumble

  • 50 g hazelnuts
  • 30 g plain flour
  • 80 g oat flakes
  • 50 g dark brown sugar
  • 40 g coconut oil

Quinoa

  • 80 g quinoa
  • 450 g water
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 lemon, thin peelings of skin only
  • 1 tsp dark brown sugar
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt

Compote

  • 400 g apples, peeled, cored, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 50 g sugar
  • 1 pinch ground cinnamon

Assembly

  • 480 g Greek yoghurt

Preparation

Crumble

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C.
  2. Place all crumble ingredients in mixing bowl and grind 4 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a baking tray and bake for 12 minutes (200°C). Set aside to cool.

Quinoa

  1. Place all quinoa ingredients in mixing bowl and cook 15 min/100°C//speed . Strain mixture through simmering basket, then transfer to a bowl and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.

Compote

  1. Place apples, sugar and cinnamon in mixing bowl then cook 7 min/120°C//speed . Transfer to a bowl and allow to cool.

Assembly

  1. Once cool, divide mixture between 4 serving glasses, layering yoghurt first, followed by cooked quinoa, compote then crumble. Set aside in fridge until ready to serve.

Useful Items

baking tray, serving glasses, oven, refrigerator
