Crème Fraîche Ice Cream with Salted Caramel

Ingredients

Candied Almonds

  • 2 egg whites, from large eggs
  • ¼ tsp ground nutmeg
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • 8 oz whole raw almonds, skin on
  • 5 oz sugar

Crème Fraîche Ice Cream

  • 12 oz whole milk
  • 2 oz heavy whipping cream
  • 1 oz nonfat powdered milk
  • 2 oz sugar
  • ½ tsp corn starch
  • 2 oz crème fraîche
  • 1 heaping tsp vanilla bean paste

Salted Caramel

  • 3 oz unsalted butter
  • 5 oz heavy whipping cream
  • 2 oz sugar
  • 4 oz brown sugar
  • ½ tsp salt, or to taste

Preparation

Candied Almonds

  1. Insert butterfly whisk into mixing bowl. Add egg whites and without measuring cup whip 30 sec/speed 4. Remove butterfly whisk.
  2. Add nutmeg, cinnamon, almonds and sugar and without measuring cup cook 10 min/250°F//speed 1. Transfer to a baking sheet lined with parchment, spread out Candied Almonds and allow to set 8 hours minimum.

Crème Fraîche Ice Cream

  1. Place milk and 2 oz whipping cream into mixing bowl and heat 5 min/105°F/speed 1.
  2. Add powdered milk, 2 oz sugar and corn starch and heat 8 min/160°F/speed 3. Add crème fraîche and vanilla bean paste and blend 30 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a shallow freezable container, cover with cling wrap and set aside to cool then freeze for at least 4 hours or overnight until completely frozen. Clean mixing bowl.

Salted Caramel

  1. Place 2 oz candied almonds into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place butter, 5 oz whipping cream, 2 oz sugar, brown sugar and salt into mixing bowl and without measuring cup cook 12 min/Varoma/speed 2. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.

Assembly

  1. Cut ice cream into pieces (1½ in. x 1½ in.) with a sharp knife. Place ice cream pieces into mixing bowl and with aid of spatula blend 30 sec/speed 8. Transfer half of the ice cream mixture into a shallow freezable container. Layer with half the Salted Caramel, top with half the chopped Candied Almonds and a final layer of remaining ice cream, cover with cling wrap and freeze for at least 4 hours or until desired consistency is reached.
  2. Serve in bowls with remaining Salted Caramel and chopped Candied Almonds.

Useful Items

shallow freezable container (e.g. aluminum, silicone), cling wrap, baking sheet and parchment paper, freezer

Hints & Tricks

  • Vanilla bean paste can be replaced with an equal amount of vanilla extract.
