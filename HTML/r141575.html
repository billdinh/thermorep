Cocoa Amaranth Balls

Ingredients

  • 300 g peanuts, dry-roasted, unsalted
  • 1 tsp sunflower oil
  • 20 g cocoa powder
  • 90 g agave syrup
  • 2 pinches fine sea salt
  • 5 Tbsp puffed amaranth
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
248 kJ / 59 kcal
Protein
2 g
Carbohydrates
3 g
Fat
4 g

Preparation

  1. Place 100 g peanuts in mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place remaining 200 g peanuts in mixing bowl and crush 15 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add oil and stir 15 sec/speed 7 until creamy.
  4. Add cocoa powder, agave syrup, salt, amaranth and reserved chopped peanuts then stir for 15 sec//speed 3. Scrape down sides and base of mixing bowl then mix 5 sec//speed 3. Form 26 balls (Ø 3 cm) then place in fridge for at least 1 hour until ready to serve.

Useful Items

refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Store the balls in the fridge for up to 2 weeks.
