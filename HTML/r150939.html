Chocolate-Centred Dumplings with Cherry Sauce

Ingredients

Dumplings

  • 120 g milk
  • 30 g sugar
  • 20 g butter, plus extra for greasing
  • 20 g fresh yeast, crumbled
  • 280 g plain flour
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 1 egg
  • 16 pieces chocolate (approx. 100 g)
  • 500 g water

Cherry Sauce

  • 600 g fresh cherries, pitted, or frozen cherries, defrosted
  • 10 - 15 g cornflour
  • 30 - 40 g sugar, to taste

Preparation

Dumplings

  1. Place milk, sugar, butter and yeast in mixing bowl and warm 3 min/37°C/speed 2.
  2. Add flour, salt and egg and knead 3 min/. Meanwhile, grease Varoma dish and tray with butter.
  3. Divide dough into 16 equal pieces, press each flat and place a chocolate piece in the centre of each. Form dumplings by pinching dough together to close it around chocolate pieces. Place 7 dumplings in prepared Varoma dish and 9 in Varoma tray. Allow to rise in covered Varoma in a warm place for 30 minutes.
  4. Place water in mixing bowl, set Varoma in position and steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 1. Set Varoma aside and keep covered. Empty mixing bowl.

Cherry Sauce

  1. Place cherries, cornflour and sugar in mixing bowl and blend 15 sec/speed 8.
  2. Remove measuring cup, set Varoma in position and cook sauce 8 min/100°C/speed 2. Serve dumplings and cherry sauce immediately.
