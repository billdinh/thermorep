Cream of red pepper and ginger soup

Ingredients

  • 150 g red peppers, cut into pieces (3 x 3 cm)
  • 150 g red onions, cut into quarters
  • 2 garlic cloves (unpeeled)
  • 30 g extra virgin olive oil, plus 1 tbsp extra for roasting
  • 30 g fresh ginger root
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 120 g potatoes, peeled and cut into pieces (2 x 2 cm)
  • 800 g water
  • 1 ½ tbsp vegetable stock paste, homemade
  • 1 tsp fine salt
  • 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 4 tbsp curd cheese (quark)

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C. Line a baking tray with baking paper.
  2. Place pepper, onion and garlic cloves on baking tray and sprinkle with 1 tbsp olive oil. Roast for 30 minutes (200°C).
  3. Place ginger in mixing bowl and blend 2 sec/speed 7.
  4. Scrape down ginger from sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Add olive oil, cumin and coriander and cook 5 min/100°C/speed .
  5. Add potato, water, stock paste, salt and pepper and cook 30 min/100°C/speed .
  6. Peel roasted garlic and add to mixing bowl with roasted pepper and onion. Cook 5 min/100°C/speed . Then blend 40 sec/speed 5-10, increasing speed gradually. Transfer to soup bowls and garnish with 1 tbsp curd cheese or Greek yoghurt. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

baking tray and paper, soup bowls, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Nutritional comments: This is a light, rich in minerals recipe that can be combined with a light main course.
