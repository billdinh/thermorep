Steamed pistachio cheesecakes with mango

Ingredients

  • 80 g unsalted pistachio nuts, shelled
  • butter, for greasing
  • 4 fresh mint leaves, plus extra to garnish
  • 1 lime, zest only, no white pith
  • 1 tbsp cornflour
  • 30 g raw sugar
  • 150 g cream cheese
  • 120 g ricotta cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 500 g water
  • 2 fresh mango cheeks, flesh only, diced

Preparation

  1. Place 40 g of the pistachio nuts into mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 7. Liberally grease 4 ramekins (6 - 8 cm) or dariole moulds with butter then add chopped pistachio nuts and rotate ramekin or mould to coat the bottom and sides. Remove any excess nuts from ramekins or moulds and set aside for garnishing.
  2. Place remaining 40 g pistachio nuts into mixing bowl and chop 1 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place mint, lime zest, cornflour and sugar into mixing bowl and mill 15 sec/speed 9.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Repeat milling 15 sec/speed 9 if necessary until a fine consistency is achieved.
  5. Add cream cheese, ricotta and eggs and blend 10 sec/speed 4, then heat 3 min/70°C/speed 3. Repeat blending 10 sec/speed 4, then pour into prepared ramekins or moulds, stopping 1 cm from the top. Place ramekins or moulds into Varoma dish and cover with a double layer of paper towel. Rinse mixing bowl.
  6. Place water into mixing bowl and place Varoma into position. Steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 1. Once cooked, allow cheesecakes to sit in ramekins for 30 minutes then gently remove cheesecakes from ramekins or moulds and place onto serving plates. Place cheesecakes into refrigerator to chill.
  7. Once chilled, serve cheesecakes garnished with diced mango, extra mint leaves and a sprinkle of reserved pistachio nuts.

Useful Items

4 ramekins (6 - 8 cm) or dariole moulds, paper towel

Hints & Tricks

  • These desserts can be made ahead of time and left in the refrigerator to chill overnight. Use a seasonal fruit of choice to garnish if mangos are not in season. Frozen mango cheeks can be used as well, simply allow to thaw before dicing.
  • If desired, you can line your ramekins or moulds with plastic wrap first before adding the chopped pistachio nuts, for easier removal after steaming. Ensure there is some plastic wrap hanging over the edges of the ramekin to ensure cheesecakes come out cleanly.
  • Serve cheesecakes with cream or yoghurt on the side.
  • Pistachios are botanically related to peaches, nectarines and mangos - making this the perfect dessert to bring these natural partners together.
  • Any leftover pistachio nuts can be sprinkled on top of yoghurt or bircher muesli for breakfast.
