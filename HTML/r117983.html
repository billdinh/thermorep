Lupin with broad bean salad

Ingredients

Salad

  • 1 tsp Vegetable stock paste (see Tips)
  • 150 g lupin flakes (see Tips)
  • 1500 g water
  • 250 g frozen broad beans
  • 1 bunch asparagus, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • water, chilled
  • 4 spring onions/shallots, trimmed and cut into thin slices
  • ½ lemon, zested and juiced
  • 40 g fresh baby spinach leaves
  • 1 avocado flesh only, cut into slices
  • 40 g watercress
  • salt, to season
  • freshly ground black pepper, to season

Dressing

  • 30 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 50 g grapeseed oil
  • 1 tsp caster sugar
  • ¼ tsp mustard powder (gluten free)
  • 50 g white wine vinegar (gluten free)
  • salt, to taste
  • freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 100 g feta cheese, broken into pieces

Preparation

Salad

  1. Place stock paste, lupin flakes and 1000 g of the water into mixing bowl and cook 8 min/100°C//speed 2. Transfer cooked lupin flakes into a nut milk bag or fine mesh sieve, leave to cool for 5 minutes, then squeeze well to drain excess liquid. Set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  2. Place remaining 500 g water into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket and weigh broad beans into it. Place Varoma dish into position and place asparagus into it. Secure Varoma lid and steam 10 min/Varoma/speed 2. Remove Varoma and set aside. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and transfer broad beans into a bowl of cold water for 2 minutes to cease cooking. Place asparagus into a large bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Peel broad beans, discard pods and add to beans in large bowl with asparagus.
  4. Add spring onions/shallots, lemon zest and juice, spinach, avocado and watercress into large bowl with beans and asparagus. Season with salt and pepper and set aside.

Dressing

  1. Place olive oil, grapseed oil, sugar, mustard powder, vinegar, salt and pepper into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 6.
  2. To serve, place drained lupin flakes onto a serving platter. Place reserved vegetables on the side, then sprinkle with feta cheese and drizzle with dressing before serving.

Useful Items

nut milk bag or fine mesh sieve, bowl, large bowl, serving platter

Hints & Tricks

  • Please refer to The Basic Cookbook or Everyday Cookbook for the Vegetable stock paste recipe.
  • You can buy lupin flakes at health food stores or online.
  • You can store dressing in a sealable jar in the refrigerator for up to 2 weeks.
