Chicken and pesto risoni

Ingredients

Pesto

  • 40 g Parmesan cheese, crust removed and cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 1 tbsp pine nuts
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 3 - 4 sprigs fresh basil, leaves only (approx. 20 g)
  • 40 g olive oil

Chicken pasta

  • 150 g carrot, cut into pieces (2 cm)
  • 150 g leek, white and light green parts only, cut into pieces
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 400 g skinless chicken thigh fillets, cut into pieces (approx. 3 cm)
  • 2 tsp Chicken stock paste (see Tips)
  • 500 g water
  • 250 g dried risoni pasta
  • 100 g frozen green peas, thawed

Preparation

Pesto

  1. Place Parmesan into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 9.
  2. Add pine nuts, garlic, basil and oil and chop 10 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.

Chicken pasta

  1. Place carrot, leek and oil into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then cook 5 min/100°C/speed 1.
  3. Add chicken, stock paste and water and cook 10 min/100°C//speed .
  4. Add risoni and cook 4 min/100°C//speed .
  5. Scrape down sides and bottom of mixing bowl with spatula, then cook 4 min/100°C//speed . Scrape down sides and bottom of mixing bowl with spatula.
  6. Add peas and combine 1 min//speed .
  7. Add reserved pesto to mixing bowl and combine gently using spatula. Transfer into serving bowls and serve warm.

Hints & Tricks

  • Please refer to The Basic Cookbook or Everyday Cookbook for the Chicken stock paste recipe.
  • You can replace the chicken thigh fillets with chicken breasts in this recipe.
  • Make a double batch of pesto and serve pasta with extra pesto on the side.
