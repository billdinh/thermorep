Cottage Pie

Ingredients

  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 8 oz yellow onions, cut into pieces
  • 6 oz carrots, peeled, cut into pieces
  • 5 oz celery, cut into pieces
  • 6 oz tomatoes, quartered
  • 2 oz red bell pepper, de-seeded, cut into pieces
  • 2 sprigs fresh oregano, leaves only
  • ½ oz extra virgin olive oil
  • 20 oz ground beef, broken into pieces
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • ½ tsp salt, to taste
  • 35 oz potatoes, peeled, cut into pieces (1 in.)
  • 8 oz whole milk
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 4 oz unsalted butter

Preparation

  1. Place garlic, onion, carrots, celery, tomatoes, red pepper, oregano and olive oil into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5, then cook 30 min/212°F//speed .
  2. Add ground beef, tomato paste and salt, gently stir through with spatula and cook 20 min/212°F//speed 1. Transfer into an oval baking dish (approx. 3 qt.) and set aside to keep warm.
  3. Place potatoes, milk, salt and pepper into mixing bowl and cook 30 min/195°F//speed . Meanwhile preheat oven to 400°F.
  4. Add butter to mixing bowl and blend 15 sec/speed 7.
  5. Spread mashed potatoes over meat mixture, then crosshatch the top with a fork. Bake for 30 minutes (400°F) until browned and bubbling. Allow to rest 10 minutes before serving.

Useful Items
