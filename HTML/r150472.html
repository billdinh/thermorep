Asparagus and broad bean salad

Ingredients

  • 100 g feta cheese, crumbled
  • 60 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbsp white balsamic vinegar
  • 1 - 2 pinches salt
  • 1 - 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 500 g water
  • 300 g frozen broad beans
  • 300 g asparagus, trimmed and cut into pieces (4 cm)
  • cold water, for soaking
  • 1 tbsp fresh basil, leaves only
  • 1 tbsp fresh mint, leaves only
  • 40 g rocket

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh feta into it. Set bowl aside.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Place oil, vinegar, salt and pepper into mixing bowl and mix 20 sec/speed 4. Remove butterfly whisk. Transfer into a small bowl and set aside. Do not clean mixing bowl.
  3. Place water into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket and weigh broad beans into it. Place Varoma into position and weigh asparagus into it. Secure Varoma lid and steam 10-12 min/Varoma/speed 2. Remove Varoma and set aside. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and transfer broad beans into a bowl of cold water for 2 minutes to cease cooking.
  4. Place asparagus into a large bowl. Peel broad beans and add beans to bowl with asparagus (discard pods). Add basil, mint and rocket to bowl and gently toss to combine. Drizzle with dressing and sprinkle with reserved feta before serving.
