Yoghurt Bread

Ingredients

  • 180 g water
  • 10 g sugar
  • 25 g fresh yeast
  • 200 g plain yoghurt
  • 550 g strong white bread flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 1 ½ tsp salt
  • oil, for greasing
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
225 kcal
Protein
5.4 g
Carbohydrates
42.8 g
Fat
2.9 g

Preparation

  1. Place water, sugar and yeast in mixing bowl then heat 1 min 30 sec/37°C/speed 1 .
  2. Add yoghurt, flour and salt then mix 20 sec/speed 4. Knead 3 min/. Meanwhile, grease a large baking tray then set aside.
  3. Tip dough out onto a floured surface and divide into 10 equal-sized pieces. Shape into balls then place on prepared baking tray. Using a sharp knife make slits on top of rolls then cover with greased cling film and leave in a warm place until doubled in size (approx. 1-2 hours). Towards end of rising time, preheat oven to 180°C.
  4. Bake rolls for 22-25 minutes (180°C) until rolls are golden and sound hollow when tapped underneath. Transfer to a wire rack and allow to cool before serving.

Useful Items

cling film, baking tray, wire rack, oven

Hints & Tricks

 

  • Try adding 80 g granola, raisins or seeds of choice before dividing dough and shaping into rolls.
