Plant Candy Bar Bites

Ingredients

Nougat

  • 120 g coconut flour
  • 140 g blanched almonds
  • 140 g runny honey
  • 25 g peanuts, roasted, unsalted, in halves

Date Caramel

  • 175 g dates, pitted
  • water, warm
  • ¼ tsp natural vanilla extract
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 35 g coconut oil

Coating

  • 70 g coconut oil
  • 55 g cacao powder
  • 80 g maple syrup

Preparation

Nougat

  1. Place coconut flour and almonds in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add honey and mix 10 sec/speed 8.
  3. Add peanuts and mix well with spatula. Form mixture into 6 logs (10 cm x Ø 2 cm) then wrap in baking paper and freeze for 30 minutes. Meanwhile, wash and dry mixing bowl then prepare date caramel layer.

Date Caramel

  1. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in dates. Cover dates with warm water and set aside for 30 minutes. Once dates have soaked, drain using a sieve over a jug, reserving soaking water.
  2. Place drained dates, vanilla extract, salt, 100 g soaking water and coconut oil in mixing bowl then blend 10 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then blend again 10 sec/speed 10 until smooth- adding more soaking water if needed.
  3. Unwrap nougat then press down slightly with a palette knife to create a flat top, then spread caramel over top of nougat logs and return to freezer for 45 minutes. Wash and dry mixing bowl.

Coating

  1. Place coconut oil in mixing bowl and melt 2 min/50°C/speed 2.
  2. Add cacao powder and maple syrup then mix 30 sec/speed 2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then mix again 1 min 30 sec/speed 2. Pour over date caramel layer then set aside in fridge to harden. Once coating has set, cut each log into 6 pieces.
  3. Refrigerate until ready to serve.

Useful Items

baking paper, fine sieve, jug, palette knife, soft spatula, long sharp knife, freezer, refrigerator
