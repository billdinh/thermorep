Leek and ham tart

Ingredients

Pastry

  • 100 g unsalted butter, plus extra for greasing
  • 200 g baker's flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 100 g ricotta cheese, well drained (see Tips)
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt

Filling

  • 200 g Gouda, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 350 g leeks, trimmed and cut into rings (approx. 5 mm)
  • 400 g water
  • 150 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 4 eggs
  • 100 g ham, cut into pieces (2 cm), (see Tips)
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg

Preparation

Pastry

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Lightly grease a tart tin (26 cm) and set aside.
  2. Place butter, flour, ricotta cheese, baking powder and salt into mixing bowl and mix 20 sec/speed 4. Transfer pastry onto a silicone bread mat or floured work surface and shape into a ball. Wrap pastry in plastic wrap and place into refrigerator for 30 minutes.
  3. Transfer pastry onto a silicone bread mat or floured work surface and roll out to a 2 mm thickness.
  4. Transfer pastry into prepared tin. Remove any excess pastry hanging over edges and ensure there are no tears or holes in pastry. Prick the pastry base with a fork or pastry docker to prevent air bubbles and rising.
  5. Line pastry base with baking paper and fill with pie weights, rice or dried beans. Blind bake for 15 minutes (180°C), or until pastry is slightly golden. Remove baking paper and pie weights and set pastry aside to cool while preparing the filling.
  6. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Filling

  1. Place gouda into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 5. Transfer cheese into a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  2. Place leeks into Varoma dish. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 12 min/Varoma/speed 2. Set Varoma aside. Discard water.
  3. Place cream, eggs, ham, pepper, salt, nutmeg and reserved cheese into mixing bowl and mix 15 sec//speed 4. Spread the reserved leeks into pre-baked tart case. Carefully pour cheese mixture over leeks and bake for 35 minutes (180°C) or until set and golden brown. Serve warm or cold.

Useful Items

silicone bread mat, rolling pin, tart tin (26 cm), pastry docker, baking paper, pie weights, rice or dried beans, bowl, oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • You can substitute the ham with bacon or speck.
  • You can substitute the ricotta cheese with quark cheese.
  • To make your own ricotta cheese, please refer to the recipe in the "Thermomix Favourites" collection, or visit the Official Thermomix Recipe Community.
  • Serve with a green salad.
