Banana Split Milkshake

Ingredients

  • 130 g sugar
  • 600 g milk
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 50 g cocoa powder
  • 200 g cream, 35-37 % fat, chilled
  • 3 bananas (approx. 300 g), cut in pieces, frozen in a single layer (see tip)
  • 50 - 60 g brown sugar, to taste
  • 150 g ice cubes
  • 4 cocktail cherries

Preparation

  1. Place sugar in mixing bowl and grind 15 sec/speed 10. Remove 1 tbsp icing sugar and set aside in a bowl.
  2. Add 100 g milk, salt and cocoa powder to remaining icing sugar in mixing bowl, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and reduce 4 min/Varoma/speed 2. Transfer chocolate sauce to a bowl and allow to cool for 15 minutes. Clean mixing bowl.
  3. Insert butterfly whisk. Place cream and reserved 1 tbsp icing sugar in mixing bowl then whip without measuring cup speed 3, without setting a time, until desired stiffness is achieved, watching carefully to avoid overwhipping.  Remove butterfly whisk, transfer whipped cream to an icing bag.
  4. Place bananas, brown sugar, 500 g milk and ice cubes in mixing bowl then blend 1 min 30 sec/speed 10. Transfer to 4 glasses and garnish with reserved whipped cream, chocolate sauce and one cocktail cherry on top of each one. Serve immediately.

Hints & Tricks

  • Bananas should be frozen in individual pieces. Cut bananas in pieces, spread out on a tray, freeze, then use frozen fruit in recipe or transfer to a freezer bag for later use.
  • Garnish with toasted hazelnuts, or sprinkles.
  • Replace the chocolate sauce with a caramel sauce: Place 150 g milk caramel sweets in mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 7. Add 120 g cream and a pinch of salt and melt 5 min/90°C/speed 2. Transfer caramel sauce to a bowl and allow to cool for 10 minutes.
