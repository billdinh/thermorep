Quick prawn stir-fry

Ingredients

  • 5 - 6 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 capsicum (red or green), deseeded and cut into julienne slices
  • 400 g large raw prawns, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 - 2 pinches ground black pepper, plus extra for seasoning
  • 120 g fresh baby spinach leaves

Preparation

  1. Place coriander into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and set aside.
  2. Place garlic and 1 tablespoon of the oil into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7.
  3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and heat 2 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  4. Add capsicum and cook 1 min/Varoma//speed 1 or until softened. Transfer into bowl with coriander and cover to keep warm.
  5. Add remaining 1 tablespoon oil and heat 4-5 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  6. Add prawns and cook 2-3 min/Varoma//speed 1, until cooked.
  7. Add soy sauce, pepper and spinach and cook 1-2 min/Varoma//speed 1 or until spinach starts to wilt. Transfer into bowl with capsicum, season with extra pepper and stir to combine before serving.

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with rice or vermicelli noodles.
