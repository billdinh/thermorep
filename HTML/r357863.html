Chicken and feijoa curry

Ingredients

  • 250 g basmati rice
  • 1050 g water, plus extra for soaking
  • 250 g brown onion, cut into halves
  • 3 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 fresh long red chilli, trimmed
  • 2 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only, plus extra for garnishing
  • 50 g ghee
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp garam masala (see Tips)
  • ½ tsp ground turmeric
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 20 g tomato paste
  • 1 tbsp Chicken stock paste (see Tips)
  • 800 - 1000 g boneless skinless chicken thighs, cut into pieces (4 cm)
  • 3 - 4 feijoas (approx. 320 g), washed and cut into wedges (see Tips)
  • 1 tsp salt

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh rice into it. Cover with water and set aside to soak (see Tips).
  2. Place onion, ginger, garlic, chilli and coriander into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add ghee, cumin, ground coriander, garam masala, turmeric and pepper and sauté 2 min/120°C/speed 2.
  4. Add tomato paste, stock paste, chicken and 50 g of the water and cook 15 min/100°C//speed , placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  5. Add feijoas and cook 5 min/100°C//speed , placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid. Transfer into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and cover to keep warm.
  6. Drain rice using simmering basket, then rinse until water runs clear. Place salt and remaining 1000 g water into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket with rice and cook 10 min/100°C/speed 4 or until rice is cooked to your liking.
  7. Divide curry and rice between serving bowls and garnish with extra fresh coriander leaves to serve.

Useful Items

bowl, thermal serving bowl or other large bowl, serving bowls

Hints & Tricks

  • Soaking the rice reduces the amount of time needed to cook it.
  • To make your own Garam masala, please refer to the recipes on Cookidoo® (www.cookidoo.com.au).
  • Please refer to The Basic Cookbook for the Chicken stock paste recipe.
  • The quantity of feijoas required will depend on the size of your fruit.
  • If using frozen feijoas, defrost completely and drain off any excess moisture before using.
