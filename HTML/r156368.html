Steamed chocolate cake with whipped cream

Ingredients

Chocolate cake

  • 4 eggs, separated
  • 100 g sugar
  • 125 g dark chocolate (50% cocoa), cut into pieces (2 cm)
  • 125 g unsalted butter, cut into pieces, plus extra for greasing
  • 100 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 1 tsp baking powder (5 g)
  • 600 g water

Whipped cream and chocolate ganache filling

  • 315 g pouring (whipping) cream, chilled
  • 80 g dark chocolate (50% cocoa), cut into pieces (2 cm)

Preparation

Chocolate cake

  1. Insert butterfly whisk. Place 4 egg whites and 50 g of the sugar into mixing bowl and whisk 3 min/speed 3.5. Remove butterfly whisk, transfer whisked egg whites to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place dark chocolate and butter into mixing bowl and melt 4 min/70°C/speed 2.
  3. Insert butterfly whisk. Add remaining 50 g sugar and 4 egg yolks and whisk 3 min/speed 3.5. Meanwhile, grease a round metal cake tin (20 cm) with butter and dust with flour. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add flour, baking powder and whisked egg whites and stir 35 sec/speed 1. Remove butterfly whisk. Transfer cake batter into prepared cake tin and spread evenly. Clean mixing bowl and butterfly whisk.
  5. Place water into mixing bowl. Place steaming rack or chopsticks into bottom of Varoma dish (see Tips) and position cake tin on top. Place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and and steam 30 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove Varoma, transfer cake tin onto a wire rack and allow to cool for 45 minutes. Meanwhile, rinse mixing bowl under cold water to cool and proceed with recipe.

Whipped cream and chocolate ganache filling

  1. Insert butterfly whisk. Place 250 g of the cream into very cold mixing bowl and whip speed 3, without setting a time, until desired stiffness is achieved, watching carefully to avoid over-whipping. Remove butterfly whisk. Transfer into a bowl and place into refrigerate.
  2. To make chocolate ganache filling, place dark chocolate and remaining 65 g cream into mixing bowl and melt 3 min/50°C/speed 2. Slice cooled cake horizontally and fill with ganache. Spread whipped cream on top of cake and serve.

Useful Items

wire rack, round cake tin (20 cm), steaming rack, large bowl, bowl, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • A cake tin measuring 20 cm should rest on the sides of the Varoma dish, above the steaming holes. To ensure the holes are not blocked, use a steaming rack, 2 small chopsticks or a wooden chopstick broken in two to support the cake tin off the bottom of the Varoma dish.
  • In addition to or instead of ganache filling, add a layer of jam before spreading with whipped cream, then top the cake with seasonal fruit.
    - Dust the top with cocoa or garnish with chocolate shavings.
    - Instead of steaming the cake in a tin, use the Varoma tray. Line with baking paper with enough to go 2 cm rise up the sides, and steam for 30 min/Varoma/speed 1, or until a skewer inserted into the centre of the cake comes out clean. Proceed with cooling instructions.Use 300 g cream instead of 250 g cream, to cover the larger surface of the cake.
