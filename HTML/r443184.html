Strawberry Kiwi Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 10 oz ice cubes
  • 5 oz strawberries, hulled
  • 5 oz kiwis, peeled, halved (approx. 2)
  • 1 tbsp flaxseed, plus extra to garnish
  • 1 tbsp hulled pumpkin seeds, plus extra to garnish
  • Â½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • 5 oz water
  • 1 oz baby spinach
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
418 kJ / 100 kcal

14 g

5 g

Preparation

  1. Place ice, strawberries, kiwi, flaxseed, pumpkin seeds, cinnamon and water into mixing bowl and chop 20 sec/speed 10.
  2. Transfer half the strawberry-kiwi mixture (approx. 10 oz) to a cup and set aside.
  3. Add spinach to mixing bowl and chop 15 sec/speed 10.
  4. Spoon a layer of reserved strawberry-kiwi mixture into two glasses. Gently spoon a layer of reserved spinach mixture over the strawberry-kiwi mixture in each glass, continue adding layers until all smoothie mixture is used. Garnish with flaxseeds and pumpkin seeds. Serve immediately.
