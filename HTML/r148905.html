Kale Pesto Pasta Salad

Ingredients

Kale Pesto

  • 50 g walnut halves
  • 80 g kale, tough stalks removed, chopped
  • water, boiling
  • 30 g Parmesan cheese, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 25 g fresh basil leaves (1 large handful)
  • 80 g olive oil
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper

Pasta Salad

  • 1200 g water
  • 400 g orzo
  • 300 g cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 yellow pepper, sliced (5 mm) (approx. 150 g)
  • 300 g bocconcini, torn in halves
  • 100 - 120 g rocket

Preparation

Kale Pesto

  1. Place walnuts in mixing bowl and toast 8 min/120°C/speed 1. Meanwhile, place kale in a large bowl and pour over boiling water. Allow to wilt for 30 seconds then strain through Varoma dish. Once cool enough to handle, squeeze out excess moisture and set aside.
  2. Add Parmesan to nuts in mixing bowl and grate 7 sec/speed 10.
  3. Add basil, oil, garlic, reserved kale, salt and pepper then mix 7 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then mix again 10 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a glass jar and set aside.

Pasta Salad

  1. Place water in mixing bowl and bring to a boil 8 min/100°C/speed 2.
  2. Add orzo pasta and cook time indicated on packet/100°C//speed . Meanwhile, place tomato halves, yellow pepper slices and mozarella in a large salad bowl.
  3. Drain pasta using simmering basket then add to salad bowl then place it on mixing bowl lid and weigh in 150 g pesto. Remove from lid, stir together well then set aside to cool.
  4. Stir in rocket just before serving as part of your buffet menu.

Useful Items

large bowl, glass jar, large salad bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • Self clean the mixing bowl to remove any pasta stuck to the base.
  • Make the pesto on its own, then serve with pasta and cooked chicken as an alternative main course.
  • Bocconcini are bite-sized balls of mozzarella. If unavailable, use the larger mozzarella balls and cut in pieces.
  • Serves 10-12 as part of a buffet menu.
