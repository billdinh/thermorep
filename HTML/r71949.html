Asparagus risotto

Ingredients

  • 40 g Parmesan cheese, crust removed and cut into cubes (3 cm), plus extra shaved to serve
  • 200 g green asparagus (approx. 1 bunch)
  • 2 tsp vegetable stock paste (see Tips)
  • 440 g water
  • 2 spring onions/shallots, trimmed and cut into pieces
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 180 g Arborio rice
  • 160 g dry white wine
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper, to taste
  • 2 - 3 tsp butter, to serve (optional)

Preparation

  1. Place Parmesan cheese into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 8. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  2. Cut asparagus stalks into pieces and set spears (tops) aside. Place asparagus stalks, vegetable stock paste and water into mixing bowl and cook 10 min/100°C/speed , then blend 30 sec/speed 5 -10, increasing speed gradually from speed 5 to speed 10. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Place spring onion/shallots, garlic and oil into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and cook 1 min/100°C/speed 1.
  5. Add Arborio rice and wine and cook 1 min/100°C//speed 1, without measuring cup.
  6. Add reserved asparagus purée and cook 15 min/100°C//speed 1.
  7. Add reserved asparagus spears and pepper and combine 1 min//speed 1.
  8. Transfer risotto into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and cover to keep warm. Add butter (optional) and half of the reserved Parmesan cheese to thermal serving bowl or other large bowl, then stir to combine with spatula.
  9. Allow risotto to rest for 5 minutes then serve with remaining Parmesan cheese sprinkled over the top.

Hints & Tricks

  • Please refer to The Basic Cookbook or the Everyday Cookbook for the Vegetable stock paste recipe.
  • Asparagus is available from September to March. If you're lucky enough to get hold of green, white and purple asparagus, use all three for extra colour.
  • Use leftover asparagus in Potted scrambled eggs with asparagus. Off-cuts can be saved and used to make Vegetable stock paste.
  • Try adding a squeeze of fresh lemon juice to your asparagus dishes, as the citric tang really complements the earthy tones.
  • Leftover risotto can be used to make Arancini balls. Search "Arancini" on the Recipe Community home page (recipecommunity.com.au).
  • Recipe for one: reduce all ingredients by half and proceed as per recipe.
