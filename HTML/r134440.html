Cheese Biscuits

Ingredients

  • 50 g groundnut oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 400 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 150 g Emmental cheese, cut in pieces
  • 200 g water
  • 2 tsp dried instant yeast
  • 1 pinch caster sugar
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 Tbsp Italian seasoning
  • 4 Tbsp sunflower seeds
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
204 kJ / 49 kcal
Protein
2 g
Carbohydrates
5 g
Fat
2 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C. Grease 2 large baking trays with oil then dust with flour.
  2. Place cheese in mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 10. Transfer ⅓ grated cheese to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place flour, oil, water, yeast, sugar, salt and Italian seasoning in mixing bowl then knead 1 min/.
  4. Roll out dough to approx. 40 cm x 28 cm, cut in half lengthways then cut into strips (1 cm) and place on baking trays. Sprinkle with remaining grated cheese and sunflower seeds then bake for 15 minutes (220°C). Allow to cool on a wire rack then store in an airtight container.
  5. Wrap in cellophane bags or arrange in boxes to present as gifts - perfect served with an aperitif.

Useful Items

2 large baking trays, wire rack, airtight container, rolling pin, oven
