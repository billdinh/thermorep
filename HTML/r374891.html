Hot chocolate on a stick

Ingredients

  • 600 g dark chocolate (70% cocoa), broken into pieces (see Tips)
  • 200 g milk chocolate, broken into pieces
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • ¼ tsp ground nutmeg
  • 30 g cocoa powder
  • mini marshmallows, to taste

Preparation

  1. Place dark chocolate, milk chocolate, cinnamon, nutmeg and cocoa powder into mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 10.
  2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and melt 4 min/60°C/speed 1.
  3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and melt for a further 1 min/60°C/speed 2, until completely melted and smooth. Fill moulds of choice with mixture (see Tips).
  4. Place a paper straw, popsicle stick or small wooden spoon in the centre of each chocolate "lollipop". Top with mini marshmallows, then place into refrigerator until set (approx. 1 hour).
  5. Carefully remove from moulds, then store in a sealable container in a cool, dry place or wrap up to give as a gift (see Tips). To serve, place 1 "lollipop" into a cup of hot milk, stirring until melted and smooth.

Useful Items

straws, sealable container, popsicle sticks, silicone cupcake moulds, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • You can replace dark chocolate (70% cocoa) with milk chocolate for a sweeter result, if desired.
  • Quantity of lollipops made will depend on your chosen moulds. We used silicone cupcake moulds and filled 8 moulds with 100 g chocolate mixture each.
  • Hot chocolate on a stick can be stored in a cool, dry place for up to 2 months.
