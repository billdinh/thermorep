Courgette and Feta Risotto

Ingredients

  • 180 g courgettes, cut in pieces (4 cm)
  • 50 g onions, quartered
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 40 g butter, cut in pieces
  • 40 g olive oil
  • 320 g risotto rice
  • 70 g white wine
  • 1 vegetable stock cube (for 0.5 l)
  • 680 g water
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 75 g feta cheese, diced (1 cm)
  • 6 leaves fresh basil, finely sliced

Preparation

  1. Place courgettes in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place onions and garlic in mixing bowl then chop 4 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add butter and olive oil then cook 3 min/120°C//speed 1.
  4. Insert butterfly whisk. Add rice and cook 1 min/120°C//speed 1.5. Scrape down sides and base of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add wine and reserved chopped courgette then, without measuring cup, cook 2 min/Varoma//speed 1.5. Scrape down sides and base of mixing bowl with spatula.
  6. Add stock and water then cook 16 min/100°C//speed 1.5. Remove butterfly whisk.
  7. Add salt, pepper and feta then stir 30 sec//speed 2. Add basil and stir through with spatula. Serve immediately.
