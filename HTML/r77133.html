Sliders with walnut or sweet potato patties

Ingredients

Eggplant relish

  • 2 eggplants, (approx. 800 g), cut into cubes (2 cm)
  • 2 tbsp sea salt, for sprinkling
  • 2 celery stalks, cut into pieces (4 cm)
  • 3 plum tomatoes, cut into pieces (4 cm)
  • 1 brown onion (approx. 150 g), cut into halves
  • 120 g olive oil
  • 70 g red wine vinegar
  • 2 tbsp raw sugar
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 tbsp raisins
  • 2 tbsp pine nuts
  • 2 tbsp pickled capers
  • 12 pitted green olives, sliced
  • 1 sprig fresh basil, leaves only
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper, to taste

Walnut patties

  • 100 g walnuts
  • 1 brown onion (approx. 150 g), cut into halves
  • 500 g ricotta cheese
  • 100 - 150 g fresh breadcrumbs, plus extra for coating
  • 1 sprig fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 pinches sea salt, to taste
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper
  • olive oil, for frying

Sweet potato patties

  • 1000 g water
  • 600 g sweet potato, peeled and cut into pieces (4 cm)
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 leek, white part only (approx. 100 g), cut into pieces
  • 2 tsp butter, plus extra for frying
  • 2 tsp tamari
  • ¾ tsp sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 1 egg
  • ¼ tsp dried chilli flakes
  • 120 g plain flour
  • fresh breadcrumbs , for coating

Assembly

  • 10 slider buns, to serve
  • rocket leaves, to serve
  • goat's milk feta, to serve

Preparation

Eggplant relish

  1. Place eggplant into a colander, set over a large bowl, and generously salt. Compress eggplant by weighing it down with something heavy (e.g. bag of flour or sugar) and set aside to drain for 1 hour. Rinse eggplant, then pat dry with paper towel. Set eggplant aside.
  2. Place celery and tomato into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place onion into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add olive oil and sauté 3 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  5. Add reserved eggplant and sauté 7 min/Varoma//speed 1.
  6. Add reserved celery-tomato mixture and cook 10 min/Varoma//speed 1.
  7. Add all remaining relish ingredients and cook 15 min/100°C/speed 1. Transfer relish into a sealable storage jar and set aside to cool. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Walnut patties

  1. Place walnuts into mixing bowl and chop Turbo/0.5 sec/4 times. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place onion into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add ricotta, breadcrumbs, parsley, eggs, salt, black pepper, cayenne pepper and reserved walnut meal and mix 10-20 sec/speed 3, or until combined.
  4. Form mixture into 10 round, flat patties (approx. 7 cm in diameter), then coat each patty with extra breadcrumbs. Place patties into refrigerator to cool and firm (approx. 30 minutes).
  5. Place a medium-sized non-stick frying pan over medium-high heat and add a little oil. Fry patties on both sides (approx. 3 minutes) until golden brown then transfer into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and set aside to keep warm. Repeat with remaining patties.

Sweet potato patties

  1. Place water into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket, then weigh sweet potato into it. Cook 20 min/Varoma/speed 1. Transfer into a bowl and set aside to cool. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  2. Place garlic and leek into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add butter and sauté 3 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  4. Add cooled, reserved sweet potato, tamari, salt, pepper, egg, chilli flakes and flour and combine 10-20 sec/speed 4.
  5. Form mixture into 10 round, flat patties (approx. 7 cm in diameter), then coat in breadcrumbs and set aside. Place patties into refrigerator to cool and firm (approx. 30 minutes).
  6. Place a medium-sized non-stick frying pan over medium-high heat, then add a little butter. Fry patties on both sides (approx. 3 minutes) until golden brown.
  7. Transfer patties into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and set aside to keep warm. Repeat with remaining patties.

Assembly

  1. Assemble patties of choice in slider buns. Top with rocket leaves, goat's milk feta and reserved eggplant relish. Serve warm.

Useful Items
