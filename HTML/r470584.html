Vegan 'Crab' Cakes

Ingredients

"Crab" Cakes

  • 1 can chickpeas (15 oz can), liquid reserved
  • 1 nori sheet, crumbled, in pieces
  • 1 tbsp dried parsley
  • 2 tsp dried dill
  • 2 tsp Old Bay Seasoning®
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 tsp vegan mayonnaise
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 3 green onions, cut into pieces
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 can hearts of palm (14 oz), drained
  • 1 ½ oz panko bread crumbs

Sriracha Vegan Mayonnaise

  • 3 oz vegan mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp sriracha
  • vegetable oil, to fry
  • ground paprika, to sprinkle (optional)
  • fresh dill, to garnish
  • 1 lemon, cut in wedges

Preparation

"Crab" Cakes

  1. Line a baking sheet with parchment and set aside.
  2. Drain chickpeas over a bowl, reserve liquid, transfer chickpeas to a separate bowl and set both aside.
  3. Place 3 oz reserved chickpea liquid into mixing bowl and mix 45 sec/speed 6.
  4. Add half the reserved chickpeas, nori, dried parsley, dried dill, Old Bay seasoning, salt, Dijon, vegan mayonnaise, lemon juice, green onions and garlic and mix 20 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add remaining chickpeas, hearts of palm and panko and mix 8 sec/speed 3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix a further 8 sec/speed 3. Divide mixture and shape into 10 patties (approx. 2 oz ea.). Transfer to prepared baking sheet and refrigerate 30 minutes. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl and prepare Sriracha Vegan Mayonnaise.

Sriracha Vegan Mayonnaise

  1. Place a small bowl onto mixing bowl lid, weigh in vegan mayonnaise and sriracha and mix to combine. Cover with cling wrap and refrigerate until needed.
  2. Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat. Working in batches, fry "Crab" Cakes for 3-5 minutes each side. Place on paper towels to drain. Serve with reserved Sriracha Vegan Mayonnaise, sprinkle with paprika and garnish with fresh dill and lemon wedges.

Useful Items

cling wrap, paper towels, large frying pan, baking sheet, parchment paper, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • To bake: preheat oven to 350°F. Place "Crab" Cakes onto a parchment paper lined baking sheet. Spray with cooking spray and bake 20-25 minutes (350°F) flipping patties half way through.
