Beetroot risotto

Ingredients

  • 2 raw beetroots (approx. 300 g total), peeled and cut into quarters
  • 2 sprigs fresh dill, leaves only, plus extra for garnishing (see Tips)
  • 150 g red onion, cut into halves
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 300 g Arborio rice
  • 50 g white wine
  • 20 g balsamic vinegar
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 700 g water
  • 1 ½ tbsp Vegetable stock paste (see Tips)
  • goat's cheese, to serve

Preparation

  1. Place beetroot, dill, onion and garlic into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7.
  2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and chop 2 sec/speed 7.
  3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Add oil and sauté 5 min/120°C/speed 2.
  4. Insert butterfly whisk. Add rice and sauté 1 min/120°C//speed 2, without measuring cup.
  5. Add wine, vinegar and pepper and cook 2 min/Varoma//speed 1.5, without measuring cup. Scrape bottom of mixing bowl well with spatula to loosen rice.
  6. Add water and stock paste and cook 13 min/100°C/speed 0.5, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  7. Remove butterfly whisk. Serve risotto topped with goat's cheese and dill fronds.
