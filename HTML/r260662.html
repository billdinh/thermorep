Mushroom sauce

Ingredients

  • 2 - 3 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 40 g salted butter
  • 3 eschalots, cut into halves
  • 50 g white wine
  • 250 g fresh shiitake mushrooms, cut into slices
  • 150 g fresh portobello mushrooms, cut into slices
  • 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 250 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • salt, to taste
  • ground black pepper, to taste
  • 10 g fresh chives, finely chopped

Preparation

  1. Place parsley into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place garlic, butter and eschalots into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 5, then cook 5 min/Varoma/speed 2, without measuring cup.
  3. Add wine and cook 5 min/Varoma/speed 1, without measuring cup.
  4. Add all mushrooms and cook 5 min/Varoma//speed 1.
  5. Add Worcestershire sauce, cream and reserved parsley and cook 2 min 30 sec/100°C/speed 1.
  6. Add salt, pepper and chives and stir with aid of spatula, then serve while warm.
