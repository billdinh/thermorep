Cyndi's Gluten-Free Bread

Ingredients

  • 220 g rice, (e.g. brown, long grain, white or a mixture)
  • 80 g millet grain
  • 40 g chickpea, dried
  • 20 g raw cane sugar
  • 140 g cornflour
  • 2 tsp xantham gum
  • 1 level tsp dried instant yeast
  • 1 heaped tsp fine sea salt
  • 40 g mixed seeds, (e.g. sesame, sunflower, linseed)
  • 400 g water
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 1 Tbsp cider vinegar
  • 2 large eggs

Preparation

  1. Line a 900 g loaf tin with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place rice in mixing bowl and grind 1 min 30 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place millet, chickpeas and sugar in mixing bowl then grind 1 min 30 sec/speed 10.
  4. Add prepared rice flour, cornflour, xantham gum, yeast, salt, mixed seeds, water, oil, cider vinegar and eggs and mix 15 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Mix again 5 sec/speed 6. Transfer mixture to prepared loaf tin then set aside to rise until doubled in size (about 45 minutes).Towards end of resting time, preheat oven to 180°C.
  6. Bake bread 40-50 minutes (180°C) or until loaf sounds hollow when tapped on the bottom when removed from tin. Allow to cool completely before cutting into slices.

Useful Items

900 g loaf tin (2 lb), baking paper, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Quinoa can be used instead of millet grain.
  • Use either 1 level tsp dried instant yeast or 15 g fresh yeast.
