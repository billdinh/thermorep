Chicken Pesto Tagliatelle

Ingredients

Chicken Marinade

  • 24 oz chicken breast, skinless and boneless, cut into strips (½ in.)
  • 2 oz lemon juice
  • 1 tsp paprika

Pesto

  • 2 ½ oz Parmesan cheese, cut into pieces (½ in.)
  • 2 oz fresh basil, leaves and tender stems
  • 1 oz pine nuts
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 4 oz extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 pinch salt

Tagliatelle and Cooking

  • 42 ½ oz water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 14 oz tagliatelle
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Preparation

Chicken Marinade

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in chicken, lemon juice and paprika. Mix with spatula to combine and set aside to marinate for 30 minutes while making the pesto.

Pesto

  1. Place Parmesan into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 10. Remove 1 tbsp grated Parmesan and set aside.
  2. Add to remaining Parmesan in mixing bowl, basil, pine nuts, garlic, olive oil and salt and chop 20 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside while cooking chicken and pasta.

Tagliatelle and Cooking

  1. Place water and salt into mixing bowl and bring to a boil 10 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  2. Add tagliatelle pasta through hole in mixing bowl lid and cook for time indicated on package/212°F//speed . Meanwhile, heat frying pan on stove over medium-high heat, add oil and fry chicken pieces for 3-4 minutes each side until golden and cooked.
  3. Drain tagliatelle through Varoma dish and transfer to a serving bowl.
  4. Place serving bowl with tagliatelle onto mixing bowl lid and add 4 oz pesto and cooked chicken. Mix together and serve immediately sprinkled with reserved grated Parmesan.

Useful Items

frying pan, serving bowl, bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • Tagliatelle nests can be used. You will need to remove mixing bowl lid and place nests inside rather than adding through hole in lid.
