Cauliflower macaroni cheese

Ingredients

  • 60 g Parmesan cheese, cut into pieces (2-3 cm), plus extra shaved to serve (optional)
  • 60 g vintage cheddar cheese, cut into pieces (2-3 cm)
  • 1000 g water
  • 150 g dried macaroni pasta
  • 240 g cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 1 tsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 30 g butter, cut into pieces
  • 40 g plain flour
  • 250 g milk of choice
  • pinch sea salt, to taste (optional)
  • pinch ground black pepper, to taste (optional)

Preparation

  1. Place cheeses into mixing bowl and grate 6 sec/speed 5. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place water into mixing bowl and heat 8 min/100°C/speed 2.
  3. Add pasta into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket and weigh cauliflower into it, then cook 10 min/100°C//speed 1 or until pasta and cauliflower are tender.
  4. Carefully remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and set aside. Drain pasta, then transfer into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl. Stir oil through pasta, then cover to keep warm. Dry mixing bowl.
  5. Preheat oven to 220°C. Place butter and flour into mixing bowl and cook 5 min/90°C/speed 1.
  6. Add milk and reserved grated cheeses, then cook 7 min/90°C/speed 3.
  7. Add reserved cauliflower and blend 40 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  8. Add reserved pasta and combine 8 sec//speed 3. For older children and adults, add a pinch of sea salt (optional) and ground black pepper (optional).
  9. Divide between 5 x 250 ml capacity ramekins. Place onto a baking tray and bake for 10 minutes (220°C) or until golden on top. Set aside to cool slightly, then garnish with shaved Parmesan (optional) just before serving.

Useful Items

thermal serving bowl or other large bowl, ramekins (8 cm, 250 ml), baking tray, bowl, oven
