Texas Sheet Cake

Ingredients

Cake

  • 6 oz unsalted butter, sliced, plus extra to grease
  • 8 oz whole milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 oz sour cream
  • 1 oz amaretto liqueur
  • 12 oz sugar
  • 9 oz all-purpose flour
  • ¼ tsp baking soda
  • 1 ¼ tsp baking powder
  • ¼ tsp salt

Frosting

  • 3 oz whole pecans
  • 3 oz unsalted butter, sliced
  • 2 oz whole milk
  • 16 oz confectioners sugar
  • 1 oz amaretto liqueur

Preparation

Cake

  1. Preheat oven 350°F and grease a baking pan (approx. 9 in. x 13 in. x 2 in.).
  2. Place butter and milk into mixing bowl and heat 4 min/195°F/speed 2. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Insert butterfly whisk. Place eggs into mixing bowl and whisk 2 min/speed 3.5. Remove butterfly whisk.
  4. Add sour cream, amaretto, sugar, flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt and mix 10 sec/speed 4.
  5. Add reserved milk-butter mixture and mix 20 sec/speed 4.
  6. Transfer batter to prepared baking pan and bake 30-35 minutes (350°F) or until golden brown and center of cake springs back when touched. Cool to room temperature.

Frosting

  1. Place pecans into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place butter and milk into mixing bowl and heat 5 min/212°F/speed 2.
  3. Add confectioners sugar and amaretto and mix 20 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and cool to room temperature.
  4. Frost cake and garnish with reserved chopped pecans. Serve immediately.

Useful Items
