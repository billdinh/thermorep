Apple Cake

Ingredients

  • 4 ½ oz unsalted butter, softened, cut into pieces, plus extra to grease
  • 3 - 4 tart apples (approx. 6 oz each) peeled, quartered, cores and seeds removed
  • 3 large eggs
  • 5 oz baker's sugar
  • 1 oz brandy (optional)
  • 7 oz all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 pinch salt
Nutrition
per 1 slice
Calories
1471 kJ / 351 kcal
Protein
5 g
Carbohydrates
45 g

16 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease a round cake pan (10 in.-11 in.) and set aside.
  2. With a sharp knife, make parallel incisions on the outside of the apples, cutting down but taking care not to cut completely through. Set apple quarters aside.
  3. Place butter, eggs, sugar, brandy, flour, baking powder and salt into mixing bowl and mix 20 sec/speed 6. Pour mixture into cake pan and spread evenly with spatula. Place reserved apple quarters on top, one next to the other, with incisions facing up.
  4. Bake for 30-40 minutes (350°F) or until a skewer inserted into center of cake comes out clean. Allow cake to cool in pan (approx. 10 min.), transfer to a serving plate and serve.

Useful Items

round cake pan (10 in.-11 in.), wooden skewer, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • You can leave the apples unpeeled.
  • Dust top of cake with confectioners sugar before serving.
  • If a smaller pan is used, increase baking time (e.g. 40-45 minutes for a 9 in. pan). Fewer apples will be necessary for topping the smaller surface of the cake.
  • Apples can be replaced with pears (prepared as directed for the apples), plums (halved, stones removed), peaches (halved, stones removed) and prepared as directed for the apples), apricots (halved, stones removed), or any other preferred fruit. The amount of fruit pieces depends on the size of the cake. You need enough to arrange evenly over the surface of the cake.
  • Brandy can be replaced with rum.
