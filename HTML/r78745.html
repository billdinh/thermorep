Hash browns

Ingredients

  • 1000 g potatoes, peeled and cut into halves
  • 1 brown onion, cut into halves (approx.150 g)
  • 1 garlic clove (optional)
  • 60 g plain flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp sea salt, plus extra to taste
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper, plus extra to taste
  • grapeseed oil, for frying and greasing
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
384 kJ / 91 kcal
Protein
3 g

11.5 g

Preparation

  1. Place potatoes into mixing bowl and chop 3-5 sec/speed 5, until an evenly chopped chunky consistency is achieved. Transfer chopped potato into a nut milk bag, muslin cloth or simmering basket and squeeze potato firmly to remove any excess liquid. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place onion and garlic (optional) into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add plain flour, eggs, salt, pepper and reserved chopped potato and combine 20 sec//speed 4.
  4. Heat a little oil in a frying pan over a medium-high heat. Grease 4 egg rings with grapeseed oil and place into frying pan. Pour potato mixture into egg rings, filling each egg ring ¾ of the way.
  5. Fry on both sides until golden brown and cooked through (approx. 3½ minutes on each side). Transfer into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl, lined with paper towel to blot excess oil. Repeat with remaining batter, re-greasing egg rings with oil between each batch. Season to taste with salt and pepper and serve hot.

Useful Items

nut milk bag or muslin cloth, frying pan, egg rings, paper towel, bowl, thermal serving bowl or other large bowl, turner/fish slice, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve your hash browns as part of a 'big breakfast' or with sour cream on the side, garnished with finely sliced spring onion/shallots.
