Bacon and Ranch Chicken Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 oz aged white cheddar cheese, cut into pieces (1 in.)
  • 8 oz bacon, diced (¼ in.)
  • 24 oz chicken breasts, air-chilled, boneless and skinless, cut into pieces (1 in.)
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 garlic clove
  • ½ oz fresh chives, sliced (1 in.), (approx. 1 bunch)
  • 6 sprigs dill, leaves only
  • 4 oz sour cream
  • 4 oz mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp freshly squeezed lemon juice, to taste
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • ½ tsp Dijon mustard, (optional)
  • ¼ tsp salt, to taste
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper, to taste

Preparation

  1. Place cheddar cheese into mixing bowl and grate 3 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.
  2. Place bacon into mixing bowl and without measuring cup cook 15 min/250°F//speed . Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and cook a further 15 min/250°F//speed 
  3. Add chicken, salt and pepper, insert measuring cup and cook 10 min/212°F//speed .
  4. Drain chicken and bacon through simmering basket discarding juice. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  5. Place garlic, chives, and dill into mixing bowl and chop 6 sec/speed 7.
  6. Add sour cream, mayonnaise, lemon juice, onion powder, mustard, salt and pepper and blend 10 sec/speed 4.
  7. Add reserved grated cheese and chicken-bacon mixture and mix 3 sec//speed 4. Serve immediately or refrigerate and serve chilled.
