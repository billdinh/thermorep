Devilled Chicken with Coleslaw

Ingredients

Chicken

  • 1000 g chicken, thighs and drumsticks, skin on
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 100 g tomato ketchup
  • 1 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tsp English mustard powder
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • ¼ tsp ground ginger
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt

Coleslaw

  • 25 g onion
  • 50 g carrots, cut in pieces
  • 300 g cabbage, red or green or a mixture of both, cut in pieces
  • 50 g mayonnaise
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • ¼ - ½ tsp fine sea salt, to taste
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper

Preparation

Chicken

  1. With a sharp knife, make several deep slashes in the chicken pieces, cutting down to the bone. Place in a large plastic food bag.
  2. Place oil, ketchup, Worcestershire sauce, mustard powder, coriander, cumin, ginger and salt in mixing bowl then mix 20 sec/speed 3. Transfer sauce to plastic bag with chicken and toss until well coated. Seal bag and set aside in fridge for at least 1 hour (see tip). Clean mixing bowl.
  3. Once chicken has marinated, preheat oven to 190°C. Arrange chicken pieces in a non-stick roasting tin and pour marinade over. Roast for 35-40 minutes (190°C) until dark brown and cooked. Meanwhile, make coleslaw.

Coleslaw

  1. Place onion, carrots, cabbage, mayonnaise, lemon juice, salt and pepper in mixing bowl then chop 4 sec/speed 4.5, while stirring with spatula through hole in mixing bowl lid. Serve with hot, cooked chicken.

Useful Items

large food bag, long sharp knife, roasting tin, refrigerator, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with a jacket potato or green salad.
  • The longer the chicken is left to marinate, the better the end flavour.
