Salmon kabsa

Ingredients

  • 250 g basmati rice
  • 1000 g water
  • 160 g brown onion, cut into quarters
  • 6 garlic cloves
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 2 tsp harissa (see Tips)
  • ½ tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 150 g tomatoes, cut into quarters
  • 15 sprigs fresh coriander, roughly chopped, 5 sprigs finely chopped for garnishing
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 fresh long red chilli, deseeded and cut into pieces (optional)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 600 g fresh boneless, skinless salmon fillets, cut into bite-size pieces (see Tips)
  • 2 dried limes, pierced with a knife (see Tips)
  • 1 cinnamon quill
  • 4 cardamom pods
  • 4 whole cloves
  • 15 g pine nuts, toasted for garnishing

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh rice and 200 g of the water into it, then set aside to soak.
  2. Place onion, garlic and oil into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and sauté 3 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  4. Add harissa, turmeric, cumin and sauté 1 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  5. Add tomatoes, 10 sprigs of the coriander, tomato paste, chilli (optional), salt and pepper and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh salmon into it. Remove bowl. Place 4 heaped tablespoons of the tomato mixture into bowl, and gently stir to coat. Set aside to marinate while proceeding with recipe.
  6. Cook remaining mixture in the mixing bowl 7 min/100°C/speed 1. Transfer tomato mixture into a bowl. Do not clean mixing bowl.
  7. Drain rice using simmering basket. Insert simmering basket with rice into mixing bowl. Add remaining 800 g water, dried limes, cinnamon quill, cardamom and cloves and reserved tomato mixture to simmering basket (the liquid will drip into mixing bowl). Stir to combine with spatula to make ensure limes and spices are covered by rice.
  8. Divide salmon between Varoma dish and tray. Place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 14-16 min/Varoma/speed 2 or until salmon is cooked to your liking. Remove Varoma and set aside.
  9. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula, transfer cooked rice into a serving dish and remove cinnamon quill. Serve salmon on top of rice, sprinkled with pine nuts and remaining coriander (see Tips).

Useful Items

bowl, serving dish

Hints & Tricks

  • You can find dried limes at specialty food stores, Middle Eastern food stores or online. Alternatively, you can replace the dried limes with 4 pieces of lime zest.
  • You can replace the harissa with baharat spice (available at some major supermarkets or spice stores).
  • You can replace the salmon with white fish of choice.
  • Before serving, you can remove the cardamom pods and whole cloves, if desired. Squeeze the cooked dried limes over the fish for extra flavor.
