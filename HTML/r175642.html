Lentils and Rice with Fried Onions (Mujadarrah)

Ingredients

  • 1400 g water
  • 300 g dried green lentils
  • 2 ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 150 g long grain rice
  • ½ - ¾ tsp ground black pepper, to taste
  • 620 g onions, quartered
  • 20 g unsalted butter
  • 1 Tbsp brown sugar
  • 100 g goat's milk yoghurt
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
2155 kJ / 515 kcal
Protein
23 g
Carbohydrates
72 g
Fat
10 g

Preparation

  1. Place water, green lentils and salt in mixing bowl then cook 20 min/100°C//speed 1.5.
  2. Add rice and cook 15 min/100°C//speed 1.5. Transfer to Varoma dish, rinse with hot water then allow to drain. Season to taste with salt and black pepper then set aside and keep warm. Rinse mixing bowl.
  3. Place onions in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add butter and sugar then sauté 12 min/120°C/speed 1. Transfer sautéed onions to lentil and rice mixture then mix using spatula. Serve immediately garnished with yoghurt.
