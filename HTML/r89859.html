Steamed scallops with cauliflower and truffle purée

Ingredients

Cauliflower truffle purée

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 20 g butter
  • 300 g cauliflower, broken into florets (3 cm)
  • 100 g full cream milk
  • 1 tsp truffle oil
  • ¼ teaspoon sea salt, to taste

Scallops

  • 500 g water
  • 16 scallops in shells
  • fresh chives, finely chopped, for garnishing

Preparation

Cauliflower truffle purée

  1. Place garlic into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add butter and cook 2 min/100°C/speed 1.
  3. Add cauliflower and milk and cook 12 min/90°C/speed 3.
  4. Add truffle oil and salt and blend 30 sec/speed 7, or until a thick, yet smooth consistency is achieved. Season to taste with extra salt if required, then transfer into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and cover to keep warm. Rinse and dry mixing bowl.

Scallops

  1. Place water into mixing bowl and place Varoma into position. Carefully remove scallops from their shells, then place scallop meat onto Varoma tray. Secure Varoma lid and steam 4 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  2. Turn scallops on Varoma tray to ensure even cooking, then secure Varoma lid and steam for a further 2-6 min/Varoma/speed 1, or until scallops are cooked. Steaming time will depend on the size of the scallops purchased.
  3. Clean scallop shells well, then place ½-1 tablespoon of reserved cauliflower truffle purée onto each shell. Top purée with steamed scallops and garnish with finely chopped chives. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

thermal serving bowl or other large bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • You can grill your scallops if your prefer. Remove scallops from shells, then place into a hot medium-sized non-stick frying pan and grill for 1-2 minutes either side. Proceed as per recipe in step 7.
  • This recipe makes 16 scallops, perfect for serving 8 people as an appetiser or 4 people as an entrée.
