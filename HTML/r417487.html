Turkey and Corn Soup

Ingredients

  • 4 oz yellow onion, in pieces
  • 2 oz celery, in pieces
  • 2 oz carrots, in pieces
  • 1 - 2 jalapeño peppers, to taste, de-stemmed and de-seeded (optional)
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • ½ tsp ground cumin
  • ½ tsp ground coriander
  • ¾ tsp salt, to taste
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • ½ oz olive oil
  • 28 oz chicken stock
  • 10 oz mashed potatoes
  • 8 oz gravy
  • 12 oz cooked turkey meat, shredded
  • 1 can cannellini beans (approx. 15 oz), drained
  • 1 can sweet corn kernels (approx. 15 oz), drained
  • fresh parsley leaves, to garnish
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1033 kJ / 247 kcal

Preparation

  1. Place onion, celery, carrots, jalapeños, garlic, cumin, coriander, salt, pepper and oil into mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and sauté 5 min/250°F/speed 1.
  2. Add chicken stock, mashed potatoes and gravy, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 30 min/212°F/speed 2.
  3. Add cooked turkey, cannellini beans and corn, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 5 min/212°F//speed 1.
  4. Divide soup into bowls, garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot.
