Crispy Japanese chicken with pickled cucumbers

Ingredients

Pickled cucumbers

  • 400 g baby Lebanese cucumbers, cut into ribbons
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp caraway seeds
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 3 sprigs fresh dill, leaves only
  • 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tsp honey
  • ½ lemon, juice only (approx. 20 g)

Japanese pepper seasoning

  • 1 tbsp orange peel, dehydrated (see Tips)
  • 1 tbsp black peppercorns
  • 1 tbsp white sesame seeds
  • 1 tbsp black sesame seeds (see Tips)
  • 1 tsp wakame flakes (see Tips)
  • 1 tsp dried chilli flakes
  • 1 tsp sea salt

Crispy chicken

  • 3 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 30 g tamari
  • 1 egg
  • 500 g chicken tenderloins
  • 50 g gluten free cornflour, for coating
  • vegetable oil, for deep frying

Preparation

Pickled cucumbers

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh cucumbers into it. Add salt and toss to combine. Set aside.
  2. Place caraway seeds into mixing bowl and toast 3 min/Varoma/speed 1. Transfer into a separate bowl and set aside.
  3. Place garlic and dill into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add vinegar, honey, lemon juice and reserved caraway seeds and combine 20 sec//speed 2. Transfer into bowl with cucumber. Cover with plastic wrap and place into refrigerator to marinate for 1 hour. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Japanese pepper seasoning

  1. Place dehydrated orange peel and peppercorns into mixing bowl and mill 20 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add sesame seeds, wakame, chilli flakes and salt and mill 20 sec/speed 8. Transfer into a large bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Crispy chicken

  1. Line a plate with paper towel and set aside.
  2. Place ginger and garlic into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add tamari and egg and mix 5 sec/speed 4. Transfer marinade into a large bowl. Add chicken and toss to coat. Set bowl with chicken aside.
  4. Place bowl with reserved Japanese seasoning onto mixing bowl lid and weigh cornflour into it. Stir to combine. Toss chicken pieces in seasoning to coat well. Transfer onto a seperate plate.
  5. Place a large saucepan over medium heat. Add enough oil to cover chicken and bring to 175°C. To check your oil is at 175 °C, drop a small cube of bread into the oil, it should turn golden brown in 15-20 seconds. Keep your oil at this temperature while frying. Working in batches, place chicken pieces into oil and fry for 4-6 minutes, turning, until cooked and outside is crispy. Transfer onto prepared plate.
  6. Serve chicken with pickled cucumbers.

Useful Items

bowl, plastic wrap, large bowl, 1 plate, paper towel, large saucepan, refrigerator, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • To dehydrate your citrus peel, peel zest from the fruit using a vegetable peeler (no white pith). Place zest onto a baking tray and bake for 20 minutes (120°C). The peel should be dried, but not discoloured.
  • You can buy black sesame seeds at specialty food stores, spice stores or online.
  • Wakame flakes (a type of seaweed) are available from health food stores.
  • You can use Japanese pepper seasoning to add flavour to chicken, beef or in any Japanese-style dish.
  • You can make the pickle up to 3 days in advance and store in the refrigerator.
