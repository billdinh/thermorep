Orzo with Salmon, Green Beans and Avocado Sauce

Ingredients

Salad

  • 24 oz salmon fillets, boneless
  • 2 oz lemon juice
  • 1 ½ tsp salt, divided
  • &frac18; tsp ground black pepper
  • 3 sprigs fresh dill, leaves only
  • 3 ½ oz fresh green beans, trimmed, halved
  • 3 oz fennel bulb, trimmed and thinly sliced (approx. 1 sm.)
  • 53 oz water
  • 16 oz dried orzo pasta

Sauce

  • 12 ½ oz avocado (approx. 3 sm.)
  • 4 ½ oz plain yogurt
  • 2 oz lemon juice
  • ½ oz yellow mustard
  • 2 sprigs fresh dill
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 5 oz red onion, halved, thinly sliced
Nutrition
per 1 portion

Preparation

Salad

  1. Place Varoma into position and weigh in salmon, season with lemon juice, ½ tsp salt, pepper and dill. Insert Varoma tray, weigh in green beans and fennel. Remove Varoma and set aside.
  2. Place water and 1 tsp salt into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove Varoma and set aside. Add pasta into mixing bowl and cook for time indicated on package/212°F//speed . Drain pasta and allow to cool.

Sauce

  1. Place avocado, yogurt, lemon juice, mustard, dill and salt into mixing bowl and blend 10 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and blend a further 5 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Mix pasta with reserved green beans and fennel, onions and reserved avocado sauce. Flake steamed salmon with a fork, discard skin and fold into pasta. Serve warm or cold.
