Spotted Dick Pudding

Ingredients

  • 100 g butter, diced, plus extra for greasing
  • 120 g plain flour
  • 100 g sugar
  • 120 g white bread, torn in pieces
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 ½ tsp baking powder
  • 50 g milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 100 g currants
  • 30 g mixed candied fruit in small pieces
  • 1400 g water, for steaming

Preparation

  1. Grease a 1 litre pudding basin then set aside.
  2. Place flour, sugar, bread, salt and baking powder in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 9.
  3. Add butter, milk and eggs then mix 20 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add currants and mixed candied fruit then mix 15 sec//speed 3. Finish mixing with spatula then transfer to prepared pudding basin.
  5. Cover surface with a buttered circle of greaseproof paper, then cover basin with a large circle of greaseproof paper, with a pleat in the middle to allow for pudding to expand. Cover with a circle of aluminium foil, again with a pleat in the middle. Secure both with string then place pudding into Varoma dish. Clean mixing bowl.
  6. Place 1000 g water in mixing bowl and place covered Varoma into position. Steam 90 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  7. Add remaining 400 g water before steaming again 60 min/Varoma/speed 2. Remove foil and greaseproof paper then turn out pudding onto a plate. Serve hot.

Useful Items

1 litre pudding basin, aluminium foil, greaseproof paper, butcher's string, serving plate

Hints & Tricks

  • This classically dome-shaped steamed British pudding, with its fruity sponge and zesty overtones, is comfort food at its best. Steamed puddings, this one with currants and candied fruits, cook beautifully in the Varoma. Serve sliced in wedges with hot custard.
