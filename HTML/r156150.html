Vegetable soup, eggs en cocotte with spinach

Ingredients

  • 400 g carrots, cut into pieces
  • 300 g leeks, cut into pieces
  • 200 g turnips, cut into pieces
  • 100 g potatoes, cut into pieces (2 cm)
  • 800 g water
  • 2 tsp salt, plus extra to taste
  • 2 pinches ground pepper, plus extra to taste
  • 200 g fresh baby spinach
  • 40 g butter, cut into pieces
  • 100 g tomatoes, diced
  • 8 eggs, chilled (see Tips)
  • 400 g white bread, toasted and cut into strips

Preparation

  1. Place carrots, leeks and turnips in mixing bowl and chop 7 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl using spatula.
  2. Add potatoes, water, 1 tsp of the salt and 1 pinch of the pepper. Line Varoma dish with baking paper and set in position. Add spinach to Varoma dish and season with 20 g of the butter and Â½ tsp salt. Secure Varoma lid and steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Remove Varoma, drain cooked spinach and, using a fork, divide among 8 ramekins (approx. 7 cm diameter). Arrange diced tomatoes over the spinach. Crack |1 egg into each ramekin and season to taste with remaining salt and pepper. Arrange 3 ramekins in Varoma dish and 5 on Varoma tray, set Varoma back into position, secure Varoma lid and cook for a further 11 min/Varoma/speed 1 (see Tips). Remove Varoma and set aside.
  4. Add remaining 20 g butter to mixing bowl, insert measuring cup and blend 1 min/speed 5-10, increasing speed gradually from speed 5 to speed 10. Serve soup hot as a starter, followed by the eggs en cocotte with toasted bread fingers for dipping.

Useful Items

baking paper, ramekin (7cm), ramekins (7 cm)

Hints & Tricks

  • To make your own bread, refer to the recipes in The Basic Cookbook.
    - For a different presentation, prepare and serve the eggs in heat-resistant glass jars.
    - The cooking time for the eggs in step 3 can be adjusted according to the size of the eggs, their temperature and your cooking preference. Chilled medium eggs (53-63 g) cooked as directed by the recipe will result in firm egg whites and runny egg yolks. If using room temperature eggs, cook 10 min/Varoma/speed 1. Increase cooking time by 1 min 30 sec if you prefer semi-hard egg yolks.
