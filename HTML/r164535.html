Butter Beer

Ingredients

  • 2 oz unsalted butter, divided
  • 2 oz heavy whipping cream
  • 1 oz dark brown sugar
  • ½ oz maple syrup
  • ½ tsp vanilla bean paste
  • 1 oz sweetened condensed milk
  • 6 ½ oz water, divided
  • ½ oz gelatin powder
  • 8 ½ oz sugar
  • 3 ½ oz egg whites, from large eggs (approx. 3-4 eggs)
  • 20 cans cream soda, chilled

Preparation

  1. Place 1 oz butter, whipping cream, brown sugar, maple syrup and vanilla bean paste into mixing bowl and cook 9 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  2. Add 1 oz butter and condensed milk and mix 15 sec/speed 2. Transfer to a heat proof bowl and set aside to cool (approx. 30 min.).
  3. Place a bowl on top of mixing bowl lid and weigh in 5 oz water, sprinkle gelatin over it and set aside.
  4. Place sugar and 1.5 oz water into mixing bowl and heat 12 min/Varoma/speed 3. Add reserved gelatin water and reserved caramel and mix 10 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  5. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg whites into mixing bowl and whip 4 min/120°F/speed 4 or until egg whites form stiff peaks. Without measuring cup, mix 1 min/speed 3 while slowly adding reserved caramel sauce through hole in mixing bowl lid onto rotating blades. Remove butterfly whisk.
  6. Pour 1 can cream soda into a tall glass or stein and spoon fluffy caramel sauce on top of cream soda. Repeat with remaining glasses and serve immediately.

Useful Items

heat proof bowl, tall glasses

Hints & Tricks

  • To make vanilla bean paste: place 6-10 whole vanilla bean pods into mixing bowl and grind 20 sec/speed 10 or until pulverized. Add 3 tbsp pure vanilla extract and 2 tsp xanthan gum (optional) and stir 3 sec/speed 3. Transfer into a glass jar and use as required.
  • Vanilla bean paste can be replaced with vanilla extract.
  • Ensure mixing bowl, lid and butterfly whisk are thoroughly clean and dry before whipping eggs. Any residual grease or moisture will prevent eggs from properly aerating.
