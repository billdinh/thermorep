Spiced pear jam and chorizo

Ingredients

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 3 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 1 lemon, zest only, no white pith
  • 430 g pears (approx 3-4 pears), cores removed and cut into quarters
  • 1 apple, core removed and cut into quarters
  • 1 pinch saffron threads
  • ½ tsp ground smoked paprika
  • 40 g lemon juice (approx. 1 lemon)
  • 200 g raw sugar
  • 600 g chorizo (approx. 3-4), thinly sliced

Preparation

  1. Place garlic, ginger and lemon zest into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add all remaining ingredeints, except chorizo, and cook 35 min/100°C/speed 1, placing simmering basket instead of  measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Cook for a further 10 min/Varoma/speed 2, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid. Transfer jam into a hot-rinsed storage jar with a sealable lid and set aside to cool slightly.
  4. Place a medium-sized non-stick frying pan over high heat and add chorizo to pan. Fry for 2-3 minutes turning over occasionally until sizzling and crispy on both sides.
  5. Serve chorizo warm with spiced pear jam on the side.

Useful Items

sealable storage jar, turner / fish slice, medium-sized non-stick frying pan
