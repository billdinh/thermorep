Savoury tofu quiche

Ingredients

Pastry

  • 230 g spelt flour
  • 60 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 1 sprig fresh rosemary, leaves only
  • 1 sprig fresh thyme, leaves only
  • 100 g dairy free margarine
  • 100 g almond milk

Filling

  • 100 g cherry tomatoes, cut into halves (optional)
  • 100 g canned baby beetroot, drained and cut into pieces (1 cm - see Tips)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 150 g leek, white part only, cut into pieces
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 350 g firm tofu, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 150 g almond milk
  • 2 tsp savoury yeast flakes
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 3 tsp onion salt
  • 3 tsp dried basil
  • 100 g frozen broad beans, thawed and shelled
  • 1 sprig fresh basil, leaves only, for garnishing

Preparation

Pastry

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Set aside a tart tin (26 cm).
  2. Place flours, salt, rosemary and thyme into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 10 .
  3. Add margarine and milk and mix 20 sec/speed 4. Transfer dough onto a silicone bread mat or floured work surface and shape into a ball. Wrap dough in plastic wrap and place into refrigerator (approx. 30 minutes). Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Place pastry onto a lightly dusted silicone bread mat or work surface and roll out to 5 mm thickness. Line tart tin with pastry, trim off any excess and prick base with a fork.
  5. Place a piece of baking paper into pastry shell and add pie weights, dried beans or rice. Bake for 15 minutes (180°C). Remove from oven, carefully remove baking paper with weights. Return pastry to oven to bake for a further 5-10 minutes (180°C) or until lightly golden. Set pastry aside.

Filling

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh tomatoes into it and set aside. Place another bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh beetroot into it and set aside.
  2. Place garlic, leek and oil into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5 .
  3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and cook 3 min/100°C/speed 1. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place tofu, milk, yeast flakes, pepper, onion salt and dried basil into mixing bowl and blend 1 min 30 sec/speed 6 .
  5. Transfer tofu mixture into bowl with leek mixture. Place bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh broad beans into it. Gently stir to combine. Transfer mixture into baked pastry case, then arrange reserved tomatoes (optional) and reserved beetroot over mixture.
  6. Bake quiche for 10-15 minutes (180°C), until golden and tofu mixture is set. Cool slightly, then garnish with basil leaves just before serving.

Useful Items

tart tin (26 cm), silicone bread mat, plastic wrap, rolling pin, tart tin (26 cm), baking paper, pie weights, rice or dried beans, bowl, oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • You can use wholemeal spelt flour in this recipe.
  • You can used canned beetroot or roast raw beetroot in the oven before starting recipe.
  • You can vary this recipe to use whatever vegetables are in season (e.g. zucchini, carrot, broccoli) - for harder vegetables, we recommend cutting into slightly smaller pieces.
