Baingan ka bharta

Ingredients

  • 2 eggplants (approx. 350 g each)
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 14 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves and stalks, plus extra leaves for garnishing
  • 2 brown onions, cut into halves
  • 1 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 1 fresh long green chilli, trimmed and cut into halves
  • 40 g ghee (see Tips)
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 3 tomatoes, cut into pieces (1 cm)
  • 1 tbsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • ¼ - ½ tsp salt, to taste
Nutrition
per 1 portions
Calories
691 kJ / 164 kcal

5 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven grill. Line a baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with aluminium foil and set aside. Cut a 4 cm vertical slit into each eggplant and place 1 garlic clove into centre of each slit, then place onto prepared baking tray. Grill eggplants, turning carefully using tongs, until completely blackened and soft (approx. 5-8 minutes). Transfer into a bowl and allow to cool (approx. 30 minutes). Cut eggplants into halves and set garlic aside. Scoop eggplant flesh into a separate bowl and set aside. Discard skins.
  2. Place fresh coriander leaves and stalks into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a separate bowl and set aside.
  3. Place onion, ginger, chilli, remaining raw garlic and reserved roasted garlic into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add ghee and cumin seeds and sauté 7 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  5. Add tomatoes, ground coriander, turmeric and salt and cook 3 min/Varoma//speed 1.
  6. Add reserved eggplant flesh and cook 12 min/Varoma//speed 2.
  7. Add reserved chopped coriander and stir through using spatula. Transfer into a serving dish and garnish with extra coriander leaves before serving.

Useful Items

ice cream scoop, bowl, baking tray (30 x 40 cm), aluminium foil, oven grill

Hints & Tricks

  • You can also grill the eggplant on the barbecue.
  • To make your own ghee, please refer to the recipe on this recipe chip.
  • Serve with naan or rice of choice.
