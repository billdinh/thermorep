Chocolate Salami

Ingredients

  • 100 g caster sugar
  • 150 g rich tea biscuits, halved
  • 100 g hazelnuts
  • 50 g pistachio nuts, unsalted, shelled
  • 200 g dark chocolate, pieces or callets
  • 100 g milk chocolate, pieces or callets
  • 150 g coconut oil
  • icing sugar, for rolling

Preparation

  1. Place sugar in mixing bowl and grind to icing sugar 15 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a plate and set aside.
  2. Place biscuits in mixing bowl and chop 6 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place hazelnuts and pistachios in mixing bowl then chop 6 sec/speed 5. Transfer to bowl with biscuits.
  4. Place dark chocolate, milk chocolate and coconut oil in mixing bowl then melt 3 min/50°C/speed 2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl and melt again 2 min/50°C/speed 2.
  5. Add reserved chopped nuts and biscuits then mix 20 sec//speed 2. Transfer to a shallow bowl and chill in fridge for 45 minutes until mixture is firmer.
  6. Halve the chilled mixture and roll each piece on cling film into a sausage shape (25 cm long, Ø 4 cm). Wrap logs in cling film, sealing the ends. Chill in fridge for at least 3 hours until set.
  7. Carefully remove cling film then roll logs in icing sugar until each log looks like a salami. Wrap in cling film or pack as a present, then store in fridge. Remove from fridge 1 hour before serving in slices (1-2 cm).

Useful Items

shallow bowl or dish, cling film, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • An Italian and Portuguese dessert, which resembles a meat salami once finished, is made from dark chocolate, broken biscuits, butter and eggs. We think this recipe provides a fantastic treat to finish off any meal.
  • Try varying the nuts or adding raisins or dried cherries to the mixture.
  • For a boozy version, add 2 Tbsp rum, brandy or amaretto in Step 5.
