Salmon and coleslaw filling for sandwiches

Ingredients

  • 250 g sunflower oil
  • 1 egg
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp mustard
  • 20 g ketchup
  • 1 lemon, juice only
  • ½ tsp chilli powder
  • 10 g fresh coriander
  • 250 g Chinese cabbage, cut into pieces
  • 150 g carrot, cut into pieces
  • 80 g red radishes
  • 300 g smoked salmon, cut into strips
  • 2 baguette loaves
  • hazelnuts, chopped, to sprinkle (optional)

Preparation

  1. Place a jug onto the mixing bowl lid, weigh oil into jug and set aside.
  2. Place egg, salt, pepper, mustard, ketchup, lemon juice and chilli powder into mixing bowl. Mix 1 min 30 sec/speed 4 while very slowly pouring reserved oil onto mixing bowl lid, letting it drizzle around measuring cup in a thin stream onto rotating blades. Transfer mayonaise to a bowl and set aside. Rinse mixing bowl.
  3. Place coriander and cabbage into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a large bowl and set aside.
  4. Place carrots and radish in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer to bowl with cabbage, mix with 180 g mayonnaise and salmon. Fill baguettes with salmon filling and sprinkle with hazelnuts (optional). Cut baguette into pieces and serve fresh.

Useful Items

jug, bowl, large bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • Use left over mayonnaise for salads or dips.
