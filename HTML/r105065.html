Spicy Minced Chicken Salad

Ingredients

  • 20 g Thai sticky rice
  • 400 g chicken breasts, cut in pieces
  • 100 g water
  • 5 g fresh root ginger, cut in round slices (2 mm)
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 80 g shallots, halved
  • 4 spring onions, cut in lengths (2 cm)
  • 4 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only
  • 4 sprig fresh mint, leaves only
  • 1 Tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 Tbsp lime juice
  • ½ tsp chilli powder
  • ½ tsp sugar
  • 2 little gem lettuce, heads, leaves separated
  • 100 g cucumber, sliced (3 mm)
  • 50 g fresh bean sprouts

Preparation

  1. Toast Thai sticky rice in a frying pan for 5-10 minutes until golden brown.
  2. Place toasted rice in mixing bowl and grind 1 min/speed 10. Transfer to a small bowl and set aside.
  3. Place chicken in mixing bowl then mince 2 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a small bowl and set aside.
  4. Place water, ginger, garlic, salt and pepper in mixing bowl then cook 4 min/100°C/speed .
  5. Add reserved minced chicken then cook 4 min/100°C//speed . Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and stir and release any chicken from blade then cook again 4 min/100°C//speed . Drain chicken using simmering basket and discard ginger and garlic.
  6. Place shallots, spring onions, coriander, leaves from 2 mint sprigs, fish sauce, lime juice, chilli powder and sugar in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  7. Add reserved drained chicken and remaining mint leaves then mix 10 sec//speed 2 .
  8. Arrange on little gem lettuce leaves on a platter with sliced cucumber and bean sprouts then sprinkle with reserved ground sticky rice, to taste, for an authentic nutty flavour.

Useful Items

frying pan, serving platter, hob

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with tamarind sauce or other oriental sauce of choice on the side.
