Prawn Gratin with Pink Peppercorns

Ingredients

  • 2 shallots, halved (approx. 30 g)
  • 5 g olive oil plus 1 Tbsp
  • 1 tsp cognac
  • 1 tsp pink peppercorns
  • 300 g sour cream
  • butter, for greasing
  • 400 g peeled, raw prawns
  • 3 pinches fine sea salt, or to taste
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper, or to taste
  • 1 Tbsp fresh chives, chopped
  • 2 egg yolks, from large eggs

Preparation

  1. Place shallots in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add 5 g olive oil and cook 3 min/90°C/speed .
  3. Add cognac, pink peppercorns and sour cream. Without measuring cup, reduce 15 min/90°C/speed . Leave to cool for 15 minutes. Meanwhile, preheat grill to 230°C and butter 6 gratin dishes.
  4. Heat 1 Tbsp oil in a frying pan and fry prawns with 2 pinches of salt and 1 pinch of pepper until prawns are pink all over. Divide prawns between prepared gratin dishes.
  5. Once sauce has cooled for 15 minutes, insert butterfly whisk. Add chives, egg yolks and remaining 1 pinch salt then whisk 15 sec/speed 4.  Remove butterfly whisk then pour sauce over prawns. Grill for 4 minutes (230°C) until lightly browned and bubbling.
  6. Serve hot, with bread and a side salad if desired.

Useful Items

gratin dish (40 x 25 x 6 cm) or 6 small gratin dishes, frying pan, grill, hob
