Mango, Passionfruit and Lime Cheesecake

Ingredients

Base

  • 50 g butter, diced, plus extra for greasing
  • 75 g gingernut biscuits
  • 75 g digestive biscuits

Filling

  • 1 lime, thin peelings of skin only
  • 150 g granulated sugar
  • 140 g sour cream
  • 400 g cream cheese (full fat)
  • 4 medium eggs
  • 1200 g water, for steaming

Mango and Passion Fruit Topping

  • 30 g granulated sugar
  • 2 ripe mango (destoned weight approx. 500 g)
  • 1 passion fruit, pulp and seeds

Preparation

Base

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C. Grease sides and line base of a spring form tin (Ø 20 cm) with baking paper.
  2. Place gingernuts, digestives and butter in mixing bowl then crush 15 sec/speed 10. Transfer to prepared tin and press down with back of a spoon forming a firm biscuit layer. Bake for 10 minutes (160°C) then set aside to cool. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl and prepare filling.

Filling

  1. Place lime peelings and sugar in mixing bowl then grind 10 sec/speed 9.
  2. Add sour cream, cream cheese and eggs then blend 20 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then blend again 10 sec/speed 5. Pour cheesecake mixture onto cooled biscuit base. Cover tin in aluminum foil then place in Varoma dish.
  3. Place water in mixing bowl. Place covered Varoma into position then steam 60 min/Varoma/speed 2. Check to see that cake is set in middle. If not, re-wrap and cook for a further 15-20 min/Varoma/speed 2. Remove cake from Varoma and allow to cool in tin before placing in fridge for a minimum of 4 hours to set. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl.

Mango and Passion Fruit Topping

  1. Once cheesecake is set, place sugar in mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 10.
  2. Cut 1 mango into pieces and add to mixing bowl. Blend 30 sec/speed 5 -10, increasing speed gradually. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then blend again 5 sec/speed 5.
  3. Remove cheesecake from tin then pour mango purée over surface, spreading out evenly, letting it drizzle down sides. Cut remaining mango into long slices and arrange over purée layer. Scatter passion fruit pulp and seeds over top then serve chilled.

Useful Items

spring form tin (Ø 20 cm), baking paper, aluminium foil, oven, refrigerator
