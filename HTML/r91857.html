Palak Paneer

Ingredients

  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 10 g fresh ginger, sliced in rounds (2 mm)
  • 250 g onions, quartered
  • 25 g sunflower oil
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 200 g fresh tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 fresh green chilli, deseeded
  • ½ tsp chilli powder
  • ½ tsp ground nutmeg
  • ½ tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 300 g fresh spinach
  • 100 g coconut milk
  • 20 g fresh coriander
  • 200 - 230 g paneer cheese, diced (2-3 cm)
Nutrition
per 1 portion

1449 kJ / 346 kcal

Preparation

  1. Place garlic, ginger, onions, oil and salt in mixing bowl then chop 6 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then cook 4 min/120°C/speed 2.
  2. Add tomatoes, fresh chilli, chilli powder, nutmeg, cumin and garam masala then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Cook 4 min/100°C/speed 1.
  3. Add spinach and cook 5 min/100°C/speed 2. Chop spinach 6 sec/speed 6 then scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add coconut milk and coriander then blend 6 sec/speed 6.
  5. Add paneer and heat 3 min/90°C//speed . Serve immediately.
