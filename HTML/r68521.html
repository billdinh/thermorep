Aussie meat pies

Ingredients

Shortcrust pastry

  • 200 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 100 g butter, cut into pieces, chilled
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 50 - 60 g water, chilled

Iced puff pastry

  • 90 g ice cubes
  • 180 g unsalted butter, cut into pieces, frozen
  • 200 g plain flour
  • 1 ½ tsp salt

Pie filling

  • butter, for greasing
  • 2 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 brown onion (approx. 180 g), cut into halves
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp cornflour
  • 500 g beef fillet, cut into pieces (2 cm)
  • 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp Vegemite®
  • 100 - 120 g button mushrooms, cut into thin slices
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper, to taste

Preparation

Shortcrust pastry

  1. Place plain flour, butter and salt into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 6, or until mixture resembles breadcrumbs.
  2. Add water and knead 20 sec/.
  3. Transfer dough onto ThermoMat or floured work surface then knead dough into a tight ball. Wrap shortcrust pastry in plastic wrap and place in refrigerator to chill for 30 minutes before using.

Iced puff pastry

  1. Place ice cubes and unsalted butter into mixing bowl and chop 15 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add plain flour and salt and mix 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then knead 2 min/.
  3. Wrap iced puff pastry in plastic wrap then place in refrigerator to chill for 30 minutes before using.

Pie filling

  1. Grease a large (Texas-size) six-hole muffin tray with butter and set aside.
  2. Place fresh flat-leaf parsley into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place garlic cloves and brown onion into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add olive oil and sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  5. Add cornflour, beef fillet, Worcestershire sauce, soy sauce, Vegemite®, button mushrooms, pepper and reserved chopped parsley and cook 10 min/100°C//speed 1. Allow to cool completely before assembling pies.
  6. Preheat oven to 200°C.
  7. Using a rolling pin, roll out shortcrust pastry on a ThermoMat or floured work surface to a 2 mm thickness then line prepared muffin tray with pastry, cutting away any excess.
  8. Reform excess pastry into a ball then roll out again. Cut into 6 discs large enough to double line the base of each pie. Place 1 disc in the base of each pie and press down gently. Place lined tray into the freezer for 15 minutes to chill.
  9. Once chilled, fill each pie shell with cooled beef mixture.
  10. Using a rolling pin, roll out iced puff pastry on a ThermoMat or floured work surface to a 5 mm thickness. Cut into 6 discs, large enough to form the top of each pie. Place 1 disc on top of each pie and seal by pressing around the edges.
  11. Slice across top of each pie to form a slit in the pastry then bake for 30 - 35 minutes (200°C) or until pastry is golden and puffy. Allow to cool slightly before removing from tray. Serve pies warm.

Useful Items

ThermoMat, plastic wrap, Texas-size 6 hole muffin tray, rolling pin, refrigerator, freezer, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve meat pies with tomato ketchup.
  • Roll out, wrap and freeze any leftover pastry to use at a later date.

For a mince meat pie, mince your meat by chopping Turbo/0.5 sec/2-4 times and proceed as per recipe.
