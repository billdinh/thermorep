Texas-style lovely legs

Ingredients

  • 1 brown onion, cut into halves
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 - 2 fresh chillies, trimmed and deseeded if preferred
  • 80 g brown sugar
  • 1 - 1 ½ tbsp smoked paprika
  • ½ tsp cayenne pepper
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 50 g water
  • 60 g tomato sauce
  • 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1500 - 2000 g chicken lovely legs (see Tips)

Preparation

  1. Place onion, garlic, chillies, sugar, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, water, tomato sauce and Worcestershire sauce into mixing bowl and blend 1 min/speed 7. Transfer into a large ceramic or glass bowl.
  2. Using a sharp knife, make 2-3 cuts into both sides of each chicken leg. Place bowl with marinade onto mixing bowl lid and weigh chicken into it. Stir well to coat chicken. Cover with plastic wrap and place into refrigerator for a minimum of 3 hours or overnight.
  3. Preheat oven to 200°C. Line a baking tray with baking paper. Place chicken onto baking tray, reserving any marinade, and bake for 40 minutes (200°C).
  4. Preheat grill. Carefully drain any excess liquid from baking tray. Baste chicken in reserved marinade and grill, turning occasionally (approx. 3-4 minutes each side) until chicken is crispy and golden. Serve with side of choice (see Tips).

Useful Items

large ceramic or glass bowl, baking paper, baking tray, long sharp knife, plastic wrap, oven, oven grill, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Lovely legs are chicken legs with the skin and part of the bone removed. If you can't find lovely legs, you can use drumsticks.
  • Serve with coleslaw, a green salad, steamed vegetables, rice, beans, salsa and/or guacamole.
