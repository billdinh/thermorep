Beef tapenade crostini

Ingredients

Tapenade

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 tbsp pickled capers, rinsed
  • 100 g pitted Kalamata olives
  • 1 green chilli, deseeded and cut into halves (optional)
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 1 - 2 pinches ground black pepper

Beef tapenade

  • 300 - 350 g beef fillet, sinew trimmed
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 garlic clove
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 sprig fresh thyme, leaves only
  • 1 sprig fresh rosemary, leaves only
  • 500 g water

Assembly

  • 1 baguette of choice (approx. 25 cm long), cut into thin slices (5-7 mm)
  • olive oil, for brushing
  • 50 g sour cream
  • 2 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 Roma tomatoes, cut in halves lengthways, then cut into thin slices
  • micro herbs, for garnishing

Preparation

Tapenade

  1. Place all tapenade ingredients into mixing bowl and blend 10 sec/speed 7.
  2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and chop for a further 10 sec/speed 7, to a chunky paste. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.

Beef tapenade

  1. Lightly coat beef in mustard, place into Varoma dish and set aside.
  2. Place garlic, pepper, thyme, rosemary and 2 tablespoons of the reserved tapenade into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Rub over mustard-coated beef and set aside in Varoma for 30 minutes (see Tips).
  3. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish, with beef, into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 20-25 min/Varoma/speed 1, until cooked to your liking (see Tips). Transfer beef onto a chopping board and allow meat to rest for 10 minutes, then cut into thin slices. Continue with recipe while meat is resting.

Assembly

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Line a baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper.
  2. Meanwhile, brush both sides of the baguette slices with oil and place in a single layer onto prepared baking tray. Bake for 6 minutes (180°C) on each side or until golden. Set aside to cool.
  3. Place a small bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh sour cream into it. Add mustard and stir to combine. To serve, spread each crostini with ½ teaspoon of the tapenade, then top with a slice of tomato, a slice of beef, a small dollop of the mustard mixture and garnish with micro herbs before serving.

Useful Items

baking tray (30 x 40 cm), pastry brush, baking paper, bowl, small bowl, chopping board, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • You can marinate the beef in the mustard and tapenade mixture for several hours in the refrigerator. Remove from refrigerator approx. 30 minutes before cooking and proceeding with recipe.
  • Cooking times may vary, depending on the cut and size of your meat. Ensure that the Varoma lid closes securely before cooking meat.
  • To save time, toast and cool your crostini while the beef steams in the Varoma.
