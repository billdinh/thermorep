Blueberry Peach Jam (No Added Sugar)

Ingredients

  • ½ lemon, thin peelings of skin only
  • 70 g grape juice, unsweetened
  • 200 g peaches, very ripe, peeled, stoned, cut in pieces
  • 50 g fresh blueberries
  • 1 ¼ tsp gelatine powder

Preparation

  1. Place lemon peel in mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place grape juice in mixing bowl and heat 5 min/100°C/speed 1. Meanwhile, sterilise jar (see tip).
  3. Add peaches, blueberries and reserved lemon zest then cook 5 min/100°C/speed 1.5. Blend 20 sec/speed 3-5, increasing speed gradually.
  4. Add gelatine and mix 15 sec/speed 3. Transfer to warm, sterilised jar and leave to cool to room temperature. Store in fridge until needed (see tip).

Useful Items

sterilised jam jar, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • To sterilise glass jar, preheat oven to 120°C. Thoroughly wash jar and lid in hot soapy water. Rinse by pouring boiling water over them, then place on a baking tray and heat for approx. 20 minutes (120°C) until required. Always pour hot jam into still warm jars.
  • Red or white grape juice can be used in this recipe.
  • Sugar in jam usually acts as a preservative. The lack of refined sugar in this jam means it will not keep as long as a regular jam. Use refrigerated jam within 4 weeks or divide into smaller jars, freeze and defrost when needed.
