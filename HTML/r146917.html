Asian Chicken Noodle Soup

Ingredients

Chicken

  • 1 uncooked, whole chicken (1.2-1.4 kg)
  • 1 lemon, halved
  • 1 brown onion (approx.150 g), quartered
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 2 cm fresh root ginger, peeled
  • 3 spring onions, trimmed and quartered
  • 3 sprigs fresh coriander, stems and leaves
  • 2 Tbsp miso
  • 2 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 Tbsp mirin

Broth

  • 1 onion halved, (approx. 150 g)
  • 1 - 2 fresh red chillies, deseeded if preferred
  • 3 - 4 dried shiitake mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 Tbsp chicken stock paste (see tips)
  • 1550 g water
  • 4 large eggs, washed
  • 400 g udon noodles, rinsed and separated
  • 120 g carrots (approx. 1-2 carrots), halved lengthways and cut into slices (1 cm)
  • 100 - 150 g tinned baby corn, drained and cut into halves
  • 150 - 200 g tenderstem broccoli, trimmed and cut in pieces (4-5 cm)
  • 1 - 2 tsp fine sea salt, to taste
  • 1 - 2 spring onions, trimmed and thinly sliced
  • 2 - 3 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only
  • 1 Tbsp crispy, fried shallots, for garnishing
  • nori seaweed, cut into thin strips, for garnishing (optional)

Preparation

Chicken

  1. Place chicken in Varoma dish and fill chicken cavity with lemon and onion.
  2. Place garlic, ginger, spring onions and coriander in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add miso paste, soy sauce and mirin then mix 5 sec/speed 4. Rub garlic-ginger marinade all over chicken and set Varoma aside.

Broth

  1. Place onion and chilli in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 5. Transfer to simmering basket and set aside.
  2. Place mushrooms, oil, stock paste and water in mixing bowl then insert simmering basket with onion and chilli. Place Varoma with chicken into position and steam 45 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Add eggs to Varoma dish, arranging them around the chicken, then cover Varoma with Varoma lid and steam 10-15 min/Varoma/speed 1, or until chicken and eggs are cooked. Set Varoma aside and allow chicken and eggs to cool. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and discard contents.
  4. Re-insert simmering basket, then weigh udon noodles into it and cook 1 min 30 sec/100°C/speed 1. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula, drain noodles, then divide between 4 serving bowls.
  5. Re-insert simmering basket, then weigh in carrots, corn and tenderstem broccoli then cook 5-8 min/100°C//speed 1. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula, drain vegetables, then divide between the serving bowls placing over noodles. Season broth to taste with salt, then set aside to keep warm.
  6. Shred reserved cooled chicken meat with 2 forks and discard carcass. Divide shredded chicken evenly between the serving bowls.
  7. Peel reserved cooled eggs, cut each egg in half and add 2 halves to each serving bowl. Pour broth into serving bowls, then garnish with spring onions, coriander leaves, fried shallots and nori strips (if using). Serve immediately.
