Banana oat pikelets

Ingredients

  • 65 g rolled oats
  • 1 banana (approx. 90 g), cut into pieces
  • 4 eggs
  • 160 g milk of choice
  • 40 g butter, softened, plus extra to serve
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 150 g spelt flour (see Tip)
  • 2 tsp baking powder

Preparation

  1. Place oats into mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 8.
  2. Add banana (approx. 90 g), eggs, milk, butter and chia seeds and blend 30 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add flour and baking powder and blend 5 sec/speed 5 or until combined.
  4. Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat. Working in batches, place tablespoonfuls of batter into pan and fry for 2-3 minutes on each side or until golden and cooked through (makes approx. 24 pikelets). Transfer onto a plate and cover with a kitchen towel to keep warm.
  5. Serve pikelets warm with butter.

Useful Items

non-stick frying pan, plate, kitchen towel, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • Spelt flour is a primitive relative of modern wheat and a richer source of protein, fibre, calcium, iron and zinc than plain white flour. You can use wholemeal spelt flour instead of white spelt flour if you prefer. With the added goodness from the oats and chia seeds, these pikelets are nutrient-dense either way.
