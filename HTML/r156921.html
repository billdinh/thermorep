Boston Baked Beans

Ingredients

  • 8 oz red onions, quartered (approx. 1 lg. onion)
  • 8 garlic cloves
  • 3 oz celery, sliced (3 in.), (approx. 1 lg. stalk)
  • 3 oz carrots, sliced (3 in.), (approx. 1 lg. carrot)
  • 2 oz extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbsp sweet smoked paprika
  • 8 oz smoked bacon, sliced (approx. 1 in. x ¼ in.)
  • 5 oz smoked ham, sliced (approx. 1 in. x ¼ in.)
  • 10 oz dried navy beans, soaked overnight and drained
  • 29 oz water, divided
  • 3 oz hot mustard
  • 2 oz ketchup
  • 2 tbsp molasses
  • 2 tbsp maple syrup
  • 2 oz tomato paste
  • 2 dried bay leaves
  • 1 - 2 oz Worcestershire sauce, to taste
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
Nutrition

Preparation

  1. Place onions, garlic, celery and carrots into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add oil and cook 8 min/212°F/speed .
  3. Add paprika, bacon, ham, beans, 19 oz water, mustard, ketchup, molasses, maple syrup, tomato paste, bay leaves, Worcestershire sauce, pepper and salt and cook 90 min/212°F//speed .
  4. Add 10 oz water and cook a further 90 min/212°F//speed  or until beans are cooked to your liking (see Tip). Serve hot.
