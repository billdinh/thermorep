Tandoori chicken

Ingredients

Tandoori chicken

  • 2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 tsp ground coriander
  • 4 cardamom pods, seeds only
  • 1 tsp salt flakes
  • 6 black peppercorns
  • 4 cm piece fresh ginger (approx. 30 g), peeled and cut into slices (1 cm)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 long red chillies, cut into halves and deseeded
  • 1 tsp mustard of choice
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • 50 g tomato paste (reduced salt)
  • 160 g Greek yoghurt
  • 50 g lemon juice
  • 40 g vegetable oil
  • 8 - 12 chicken drumsticks

Mint dressing

  • 200 g Greek yoghurt
  • 60 g fresh mint leaves (1-2 bunches)
  • 50 g brown onion
  • 100 g fresh tomatoes, cut into halves
  • 1 tsp salt flakes

Assembly

  • 60 g slivered almonds
  • 5 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only

Preparation

Tandoori chicken

  1. Place cumin, coriander, cardamom seeds, salt and peppercorns into mixing bowl and toast 5 min/100°C/speed 1 , without measuring cup.
  2. Remove lid and allow to cool for 2 minutes, then mill 30 sec/speed 9.
  3. Add ginger, garlic, chillies, mustard, chilli powder, turmeric and cinnamon and chop 3 sec/speed 5.
  4. Add tomato paste, yoghurt, lemon juice and oil and mix 30 sec/speed 5.
  5. Cut 2 deep slits into each chicken drumsticks and place chicken into a bowl. Pour paste mixture over chicken and stir to coat chicken. Cover with plastic wrap and place into refrigerator for a minimum of 30 minutes (see Tips). Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  6. Preheat oven to 200°C. Line a baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper and place a wire rack over it. Transfer marinated chicken onto wire rack and bake for 20 minutes (200°C), turning over once after 10 minutes, until cooked through.

Mint dressing

  1. Place all dressing ingredients into mixing bowl and mix 5 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and, if a finer texture is desired, mix for a further 2 sec/speed 6.
  2. Transfer into a sealable container and place into refrigerator until ready to serve. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Assembly

  1. Place almonds into mixing bowl and toast 10 min/Varoma/speed 1 ,  without measuring cup.
  2. Scatter toasted almonds and coriander over chicken and serve with mint dressing on the side.

Useful Items

bowl, plastic wrap, baking paper, wire rack, baking tray (30 x 40 cm), sealable container, refrigerator, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Chicken can marinate in the refrigerator for up to 24 hours.
  • You can use chicken wings or marylands in this recipe (adjust cooking time as needed).
  • Tandoori cauliflower: Replace chicken with 1 cauliflower, cut into medium-sized florets. Make paste and marinate as per recipe. Reduce temperature to 160°C and bake for 40 minutes (160°C) until tender. Serve with mint dressing.
