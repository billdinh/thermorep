Flaky filo fingers

Ingredients

  • 180 g unsalted butter, cut into pieces (3 cm), plus extra for greasing
  • 2 - 3 spring onions/shallots, trimmed and cut into quarters
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 500 g frozen chopped spinach, thawed and excess moisture squeezed out (approx. 250 g after moisture removed)
  • 200 g feta cheese, crumbled (see Tips)
  • 250 g ricotta cheese, drained
  • 1 egg
  • ¼ - ½ tsp salt
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 18 - 20 sheets filo pastry (approx. 375 g)

Preparation

  1. Grease 2 baking trays (30 x 40 cm) with butter and set aside.
  2. Place butter into mixing bowl and melt 3 min/100°C/speed 2. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place spring onions/shallots into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Add oil and cook 2 min/100°C/speed 1.
  5. Add spinach and cook 1 min/100°C/speed 1,  without measuring cup. Set aside in mixing bowl to cool for 10 minutes.
  6. Add feta, ricotta, egg, salt and pepper and combine 15-20 sec//speed 3. Set aside in mixing bowl.
  7. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  8. Place 1 sheet of filo pastry onto a clean work surface, keeping remaining pastry covered with a clean, damp kitchen towel. Brush filo sheet with reserved melted butter. Place a second sheet on top, then brush with butter. Place a third sheet on top and brush with butter. Cut pastry into 3 strips (each 14 cm wide), then cut into halves (forming 14 cm x 13.5 cm rectangles).
  9. Place 1 tablespoon of the reserved spinach mixture along bottom edge of each rectangle. Fold in side edges by 1 cm and brush with melted butter. Fold over end of pastry to overlap filling, then roll up, making sure edges are tucked inside and filling is sealed, forming a finger shape. Place seam side down onto prepared baking trays, leaving at least a 3 cm gap between each roll. Repeat with remaining pastry, filling and butter (makes approx. 36 fingers - see Tips).
  10. Brush tops of fingers with melted butter and bake for 18-20 minutes (180°C), until lightly golden and crisp. Serve warm or at room temperature.

Useful Items

2 baking trays (30 x 40 cm), bowl, kitchen towel, pastry brush, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • A firm (Australian) feta works best for this recipe.
  • Unbaked filo fingers can be placed into a freezable storage container and frozen for up to 3 weeks. They can be baked from frozen for 18-20 minutes (180°C), until lightly golden and crisp.

Cevapcici (meat) filling:

  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 brown onion (approx. 100 g), cut into quarters
  • 100 g red capsicum, deseeded and cut into pieces
  • 3 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 250 g each pork mince, beef mince and lamb mince
  • 1 egg white
  • 1 tsp salt, to taste
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper, to taste
  • ½ tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • ½ tsp paprika

Preparation:

1. Preheat oven to 200°C. Place garlic, onion, capsicum and parsley into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.

2. Add minces and egg white and combine 30 sec//speed 3.

3. Add salt, pepper, bicarbonate of soda and paprika and combine 30 sec//speed 3, with aid of spatula.

4. Using damp hands, roll 1 tbsp of the mixture into a sausage shape (approx. 5-7 cm). Proceed as per recipe from preparing filo pastry. Bake fingers for 20-25 minutes (200°C).

Leftover cevapcici mixture can be used to make koftas or in a bolognaise sauce.
