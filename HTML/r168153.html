Vegetable harrira soup

Ingredients

  • 20 g fresh coriander, roughly chopped
  • 20 g fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 2 - 3 garlic cloves
  • 200 g onions, cut into quarters
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • ¼ tsp ground cayenne pepper, adjust to taste
  • 1 ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 ½ tsp ground paprika
  • 1 ¼ tsp ground ginger
  • 200 g carrots, cut into pieces
  • 150 g celery, cut into pieces
  • 400 g canned crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp salt, adjust to taste
  • 100 g dried green lentils, soaked for approx. 15 min, drained
  • 400 g water
  • 150 g canned chickpeas, drained, weight after draining
  • 2 tsp chicken stock paste, homemade
  • 1 lemon, juice (30 g) and zest

Preparation

  1. Place coriander and parsley into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Chop 2 sec/speed 8. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place garlic, onions, olive oil, turmeric, cumin, cayenne pepper, cinnamon, paprika and ginger into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and sauté 5 min/120°C/speed 2.
  5. Add carrots and celery and chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  6. Add canned tomatoes, salt, lentils, water, chickpeas and chicken stock paste and cook 30 min/100°C//speed 1.
  7. Add lemon juice, lemon zest, reserved chopped parsley and coriander, mix 5 sec//speed 3 with aid of spatula, taste and adjust seasoning as needed. Serve warm.
