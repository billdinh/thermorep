Korean barbecue chicken with rice

Ingredients

Chicken rice

  • 100 g red cabbage, cut into pieces (5-6 cm)
  • 1 carrot (approx. 100 g), cut into matchsticks
  • 1000 g water
  • 250 g jasmine rice
  • 1 tsp salt, plus extra to season
  • 500 g chicken tenderloins, cut into strips (approx. 2 cm wide)
  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • freshly ground black pepper, to season

Korean barbecue sauce

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 long red chilli, deseeded if preferred
  • 3 cm cube fresh ginger, peeled
  • 40 g soy sauce
  • 50 g rice wine vinegar
  • 60 g water
  • 60 g soft dark brown sugar
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 pinch salt, to season
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper, to season

Assembly

  • 130 g bean sprouts
  • 50 g fresh baby spinach leaves
  • 4 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only
  • 2 spring onions/shallots, white part only, cut into thin slices for garnishing
  • 1 tsp black sesame seeds, for garnishing

Preparation

Chicken rice

  1. Place cabbage into mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 4. Transfer into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl. Add carrot to thermal serving bowl with cabbage and set aside.
  2. Without cleaning mixing bowl, place water into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket and weigh rice into it. Add salt to simmering basket. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh 250 g of the chicken into it. Drizzle with 1 teaspoon of the sesame oil and season with salt and pepper. Insert Varoma tray, place remaining chicken onto it, then drizzle with remaining 1 teaspoon sesame oil and season with salt and pepper. Secure Varoma lid and cook 12 min/100°C/speed 4.
  3. Remove Varoma and set aside. Remove simmering basket with the aid of spatula and transfer rice into thermal serving bowl with reserved cabbage and carrot and stir to combine. Cover and set aside to keep warm. Place Varoma dish and tray with chicken back into position, secure Varoma lid and cook 5-7 min/Varoma/speed 1, or until chicken is cooked through. Transfer chicken into thermal serving bowl with rice and vegetables. Cover to keep warm and set aside. Discard water, then clean and dry mixing bowl.

Korean barbecue sauce

  1. Place all sauce ingredients, except eggs, salt and pepper, into the mixing bowl and blend 20 sec/speed 8.
  2. Place Varoma dish into position, insert Varoma tray and line with a piece of wet, well-wrung baking paper. Carefully pour beaten eggs onto tray and season with salt and pepper. Secure Varoma lid and steam 8 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  3. Carefully transfer omelette to a clean work surface and cut into thin strips. Cover with kitchen towel to keep warm.

Assembly

  1. Transfer reserved chicken mixture into a large bowl. Add bean sprouts, spinach, coriander, Korean barbecue sauce and omelette strips and gently stir to combine.
  2. Divide between 6 serving bowls and garnish with spring onions and sesame seeds before serving.

Useful Items

thermal serving bowl or other large bowl, baking paper, kitchen towel, large bowl, 6 serving bowls

Hints & Tricks

  • Black sesame seeds can be found at specialty food stores, however you can also use white sesame seeds in this recipe.
