Quinoa and Chia Chips

Ingredients

  • 100 g quinoa
  • 50 g chia seeds
  • 70 g water
  • 10 g sesame seeds
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
62 kJ / 15 kcal
Protein
0.65 g
Carbohydrates
2.09 g
Fat
0.44 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C and line a baking tray with baking paper.
  2. Place quinoa and chia seeds in mixing bowl then grind 30 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides and lid of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add water, sesame seeds and salt then mix 6 sec/speed 6. Finish stirring by hand with spatula.
  4. Form into walnut-sized balls. Lay balls between 2 sheets of cling film then, roll out each to a thickness of 1-2 mm. Use a pastry cutter (Ø 8 cm) to cut out circles. Arrange on prepared baking tray.
  5. Bake for 5 minutes (200°C). Repeat with remaining dough, re-rolling any trimmings. Allow to cool before serving or store in an airtight container until needed.

Useful Items

baking tray and baking paper, airtight container, cling film, rolling pin, round pastry cutter (Ø 8 cm), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • For curved chips, try using a baguette tray instead of a flat baking tray to bake the chips.
  • Serve with dips.
