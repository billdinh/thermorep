Chicken, Aubergine, Spinach and Lentil Curry, Quick Flatbreads, Frozen Fruit Sorbet

Ingredients

Preparation Before Demo

  • 320 - 350 g aubergines, diced (1 cm) (1-2 aubergines)
  • 400 g chicken breasts, halved horizontally then sliced (0.5 cm)
  • 200 g sugar snap peas (optional)

Quick Flatbreads

  • 350 g strong white bread flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 250 g plain yoghurt
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • ¼ tsp granulated sugar

Frozen Fruit Sorbet

  • 50 g sugar, adjust to taste
  • 500 g frozen fruit, (e.g. banana and strawberry mix)
  • 2 tsp lemon juice (optional)
  • 1 egg white, from medium or large egg (optional)

Aubergine, Spinach and Lentil Curry

  • 120 g onions, quartered (1 onion)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 20 g fresh root ginger, peeled, cut in round slices (2 mm)
  • 1 fresh green chilli, halved (with seeds)
  • 15 g olive oil
  • 150 g red split lentils, rinsed
  • 15 g tomato purée
  • 300 g water
  • 3 tsp garam masala
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 vegetable stock cube (for 0.5 l)
  • 350 g cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 400 g coconut milk
  • 150 g fresh spinach leaves

Preparation

Preparation Before Demo

  1. Prepare aubergines and set aside in a bowl. Place chicken in Varoma dish, cover with Varoma lid then set aside. Place sugar snap peas (if using) on Varoma tray then set aside.

Quick Flatbreads

  1. Place flour, yoghurt, baking powder, salt and sugar in mixing bowl then mix 15 sec/speed 6. Knead 1 min/. Meanwhile, lightly flour a bowl. Transfer kneaded dough to prepared bowl and set aside. Meanwhile, self-wash mixing bowl by covering blade with water and a drop of washing up liquid. Set for 1 min/60°C/speed 6. Discard water, rinse and wipe dry then make sorbet.

Frozen Fruit Sorbet

  1. Place sugar in mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 10.
  2. Add frozen fruit, lemon juice (if using) and egg white (if using) then, while stirring with spatula through hole in mixing bowl lid, mix 1 min 30 sec/speed 5.
  3. Insert butterfly whisk then whip 1 min/speed 3. Transfer to serving bowls and enjoy, freezing any leftovers in a freezer-proof container. Rinse mixing bowl, then make curry.

Aubergine, Spinach and Lentil Curry

  1. Place onions, garlic, ginger, chilli and oil in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then sauté 3 min/100°C/speed 1.
  2. Add aubergines, lentils, tomato purée, water, garam masala, salt and stock cube. Place prepared Varoma with chicken into position then cook 8 min/Varoma//speed 1.5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and stir chicken to separate pieces.
  3. Insert Varoma tray with sugar snap peas (if using) then cook 6 min/Varoma//speed 1.5. Meanwhile, divide dough into 8 pieces using spatula then shape each into a ball. Roll each ball out into a circle (approx. Ø 20 cm). Heat a griddle pan over a medium-high heat and cook bread for 1 minute on each side, then set aside to keep warm.
  4. Returning to curry, set Varoma aside then add tomatoes and coconut milk to mixing bowl. Return Varoma to position then cook 5 min/100°C//speed 1.
  5. Remove Varoma and set aside. Add spinach to mixing bowl and stir through with spatula. Serve curry with chicken and sugar snap peas on top, accompanied by the flatbreads.

Useful Items

rolling pin, serving bowls, large bowl, griddle pan, freezer-proof container, hob

Hints & Tricks

  • Fruits should be frozen in individual pieces or small clusters. Cut fruit in pieces, spread out on a tray, freeze, then use frozen fruit in recipe or transfer to a freezer bag for later use.
  • A frying pan can be used if a griddle pan is not available.
  • For a vegetarian version, omit the chicken.
