Spinach and feta galette

Ingredients

Wholemeal spelt pastry

  • 260 g wholemeal spelt flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 100 g olive oil
  • 90 g chilled water
  • 1 egg
  • 1 ½ tsp apple cider vinegar
  • 70 g sesame seeds

Spinach and feta filling

  • 500 g fresh baby spinach leaves
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 180 g brown onion, cut into quarters
  • 5 spring onions/shallots, trimmed and cut into thirds
  • 200 g feta cheese, cut into pieces (approx. 2 cm)
  • 200 g cottage cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 egg whites
  • 1 sprig fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper

Assembly

  • 1 egg white, for glazing
  • 1 tbsp pine nuts, for sprinkling
  • 60 g mixed baby leaf salad

Preparation

Wholemeal spelt pastry

  1. Place all pastry ingredients into mixing bowl and mix 8 sec/speed 4 or until combined. Transfer pastry onto a silicone bread mat or a lightly floured work surface and knead slightly to form a smooth dough. Shape into a ball, wrap in plastic wrap and place into refrigerator for 30 minutes. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Spinach and feta filling

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C. Line a baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place 250 g of the spinach into mixing bowl and chop 6 sec/speed 6, with aid of spatula.
  3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and cook 2 min/90°C/speed 2, until spinach wilts. Transfer spinach into simmering basket to drain. Rinse mixing bowl (this prevents spinach from catching).
  4. Place remaining 250 g spinach into mixing bowl and chop 6 sec/speed 6, with aid of spatula.
  5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl and cook 2 min/90°C/speed 2, until spinach wilts. Transfer into simmering basket and use spatula to squeeze out as much excess water as possible. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  6. Place oil, onion and spring onions/shallots into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5.
  7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and cook 3 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  8. Add feta, cottage cheese, egg, egg whites, parsley, pepper and reserved drained spinach and combine 10 sec//speed 3.

Assembly

  1. Transfer dough onto a silicone bread mat or a lightly floured work surface and roll out dough into a disc (approx. 35 cm). Carefully transfer onto lined baking tray (30 x 40 cm). Place filling into centre of the pastry and spread out (approx. 22 cm). Fold uncovered pastry over edges of filling, allowing pastry to fall naturally. Brush pastry with egg white and sprinkle galette with pine nuts. Bake for 45 minutes (190°C) or until pastry is golden. Allow to cool for a minimum of 10 minutes before cutting into wedges. Serve with a mixed leaf salad.

Useful Items

silicone bread mat, plastic wrap, baking paper, rolling pin, pastry brush, baking tray (30 x 40 cm), refrigerator, oven
