Sticky drumettes

Ingredients

  • 30 g piece fresh ginger, peeled and cut into pieces
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 20 g vegetable oil
  • 80 g light soy sauce
  • 50 g honey
  • 40 g tomato paste
  • 1 tsp mustard
  • 60 g orange juice (approx. 1 orange)
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 900 g chicken drumettes
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds
  • 2 spring onions/shallots, trimmed and cut into thin slices

Preparation

  1. Place ginger and garlic into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7.
  2. Add oil, soy sauce, honey, tomato paste, mustard, orange juice and pepper and blend 20 sec/speed 6.
  3. Place chicken drumettes into a sealable container or snaplock bag and add marinade. Seal container or bag and place into refrigerator for a minimum of 2 hours (up to 24 hours).
  4. Preheat oven to 190°C.
  5. Place marinated drumettes into a roasting tray (20 x 30 cm), reserving marinade. Bake for 15 minutes (190°C). Spoon over reserved marinade, sprinkle with sesame seeds and bake for a further 10-15 minutes (190°C) until sticky and caramelised.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with spring onions/shallots.

Useful Items

snaplock bag, sealable container, large oven-proof dish or roasting tray (approx. 30 cm), refrigerator, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Chicken drumsticks can also be used in this recipe, reduce oven temperature to 180°C and cook for 25 minutes, then glaze and cook for a further 20 minutes.
