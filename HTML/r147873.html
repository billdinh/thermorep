Five Grain Porridge with Cardamon and Cinnamon Drizzle

Ingredients

Cardamom and Cinnamon Drizzle

  • 60 g maple syrup
  • 3 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp ground cardamom

Porridge

  • 1000 g coconut water
  • 100 g amaranth grains
  • 80 g quinoa grains
  • 90 g pinhead oats
  • 80 g millet grains
  • 40 g linseeds
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1211 kJ / 289 kcal

Preparation

Cardamom and Cinnamon Drizzle

  1. Place a small bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in syrup. Remove, add cinnamon and cardamom then mix until combined.

Porridge

  1. Place coconut water and amaranth in mixing bowl then cook 15 min/95°C//speed 3.5.
  2. Add quinoa, oats, millet and linseeds then cook 13 min/95°C//speed 2. Serve immediately with cardamon and cinnamon drizzle.
