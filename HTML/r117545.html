Greek Style Pork Stew

Ingredients

  • 18 oz water
  • 1 ½ tsp salt
  • 9 oz white basmati rice
  • 7 oz onions, quartered
  • 7 oz red bell peppers, quartered, seeds removed
  • 1 oz olive oil
  • 1 can diced tomatoes (approx. 29 oz)
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 24 oz pork tenderloin, cut into strips (½ in. x 1 in.)

Preparation

  1. Place water and ½ tsp salt into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket, weigh in white basmati rice, remove simmering basket and rinse rice under cold running water until water runs clear. Place simmering basket with rice back into mixing bowl and cook 16 min/212°F/speed 4. Transfer rice into a serving bowl and keep warm.
  2. Place onions, red peppers and oil into mixing bowl and chop 8 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and sauté 5 min/212°F/speed 1.
  3. Add tomatoes, paprika, 1 tsp salt and black pepper into mixing bowl and cook 10 min/212°F//speed 1.
  4. Add pork, stir with spatula and cook 4-6 min/212°F//speed 1 until pork is cooked and still tender. Serve rice and pork with vegetables of choice.

Hints & Tricks

  • When short on time cook rice on your stove top while Greek Style Pork Stew is cooking in mixing bowl. Replace first step with: Place a saucepan on mixing bowl lid and weigh in rice, water and ½ tsp salt. Cook on stove top according to packet instructions. Keep warm until serving with Greek Style Pork Stew.
