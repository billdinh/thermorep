Breaded Celeriac with Herby Yoghurt Sauce

Ingredients

Breaded Celeriac

  • 50 g Parmesan cheese, cut in pieces
  • 80 g white bread stale, in pieces
  • 500 g water
  • 800 g celeriac peeled, cut across the width in slices (1 cm)
  • 4 Tbsp plain flour
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 20 g butter, for frying

Herby Yoghurt Sauce

  • 20 g mixed herbs, fresh, e.g. parsley, chives, dill
  • 300 g plain yoghurt
  • 2 pinches fine sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • ¼ tsp curry powder

Preparation

Breaded Celeriac

  1. Place Parmesan in mixing bowl and grate 20 sec/speed 10.
  2. Add bread and grate 5 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place water in mixing bowl. Put Varoma dish into position. Put celeriac slices on Varoma dish and tray, cover then steam 25 min/Varoma/speed 1. Check celeriac is cooked and set aside. Discard steaming water and clean mixing bowl.
  4. Place flour onto a plate then add salt and pepper. Crack eggs into a bowl. Coat celeriac slices in the seasoned flour, then dip in beaten egg, then in Parmesan crumb mix.
  5. Heat butter in a non-stick frying pan on hob over a medium heat. Fry celeriac slices on both sides for 2-3 minutes until golden brown, then transfer to a plate and set aside.

Herby Yoghurt Sauce

  1. Place herbs in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add yoghurt, salt, pepper and curry powder then mix 5 sec/speed 3. Adjust seasoning if needed then serve sauce with the celeriac slices.

Useful Items

serving plate, frying pan, non-stick pan, hob

Hints & Tricks

  • The celeriac can be steamed ahead, then floured, egged and breadcrumbed before frying and serving.
