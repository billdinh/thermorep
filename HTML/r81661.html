TM5 TM6

Layered chicken dinner

Ingredients

Chicken

  • 1 whole chicken (1.2 - 1.4 kg)
  • 1 lemon, flesh only (reserve zest)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary (approx. 10 cm each), leaves only
  • 4 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 1 tsp ground paprika
  • 1 tbsp sea salt, plus extra to taste

Pumpkin soup

  • 120 g leek (approx. 1), trimmed and cut into quarters
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 700 g butternut pumpkin, deseeded, peeled and cut into pieces (5 cm)
  • 950 g water
  • 230 g potatoes (approx. 5), skins on, cut into eighths
  • 170 g carrots (approx. 2), cut into pieces (2-3 cm)

Gravy

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 300 g eschalots (approx. 8), cut into halves
  • 50 g unsalted butter, cut into pieces
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary (approx. 10 cm each), leaves only
  • 120 g red wine
  • 1 - 2 tsp Chicken stock paste (see Tips)
  • 250 g water
  • 1 tsp cornflour
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper, to taste
  • 100 g broccoli, cut into large florets
  • 100 g fresh green beans, trimmed

Preparation

Chicken

  1. Place whole chicken into Varoma dish and fill chicken cavity with whole peeled lemon, garlic and rosemary. Set Varoma with chicken aside.
  2. Place reserved lemon zest and parsley into mixing bowl and chop 20 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add paprika and salt and mill 10 sec/speed 9. Rub mixture over chicken skin and set Varoma aside.

Pumpkin soup

  1. Without rinsing mixing bowl, place leek and garlic into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add oil and sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  3. Add pumpkin and water to mixing bowl. Place Varoma with chicken into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 55 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  4. Weigh potatoes and carrots into Varoma dish, arranging them around the whole chicken. Secure Varoma lid, then steam 15 min/Varoma/speed 1. Check chicken is cooked. If not, prolong cooking time as necessary. You may want to set vegetables aside before proceeding. Once chicken is cooked, remove Varoma and transfer chicken, potatoes and carrots into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and cover to keep warm.
  5. Insert measuring cup. Blend 30 sec/speed 5-9, increasing speed gradually from speed 5 to speed 9. Transfer pumpkin soup into a separate thermal serving bowl or large bowl and cover to keep warm. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Gravy

  1. Place oil, eschalots and butter into mixing bowl and sauté 7 min/120°C/speed 1, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  2. Add rosemary, wine, stock paste and water and cook 8 min/Varoma/speed 1, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  3. Add cornflour and pepper to mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh broccoli and green beans into it. Secure Varoma lid, then steam 8 min/Varoma/speed 1. Set Varoma aside and transfer broccoli and green beans into the thermal serving bowl or large bowl with the chicken and cover to keep warm, then strain gravy through simmering basket into a serving jug. Discard basket contents.
  4. Serve pumpkin soup for entrée, followed by chicken, vegetables and gravy.

Useful Items

serving jug, thermal serving bowl or other large bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • Please refer to The Basic Cookbook for the Chicken stock paste recipe.
  • Depending on the size of the chicken, you may have to adjust cooking times slightly.
  • Change the vegetables to suit your taste preferences and adjust cooking times as required for firmer or softer vegetables.
