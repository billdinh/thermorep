Cod with Citrus Butter

Ingredients

  • 1 orange, thin peel only, no pith, plus juice (approx. 2.5 oz)
  • 1 lemon, thin peel only, no pith, plus juice (approx. 2.5 oz)
  • 1 tsp anise seeds
  • ½ tsp salt, to taste
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper, to taste
  • 25 oz water,
  • 14 oz potatoes, cut into pieces
  • 4 cod fillets, (4-7 oz each, max of 28 oz)
  • 3 oz unsalted butter, plus extra to grease
  • 14 oz zucchini, sliced (¼ in.)
  • 3 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only, chopped
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1716 kJ / 411 kcal

32 g

Preparation

  1. Place orange peel, lemon peel, anise seeds, salt and pepper into mixing bowl and zest 15 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and zest a further 15 sec/speed 9. Transfer to a large bowl and set aside.
  2. Place water into mixing bowl, insert simmering basket and weigh in potatoes.
  3. Lightly grease Varoma tray. Season both sides of cod fillets with ¼ of the reserved chopped zest. Place fish onto Varoma tray, set Varoma dish into position and insert Varoma tray, secure Varoma lid and steam 15 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  4. Remove Varoma tray. Weigh zucchini into Varoma dish. Insert Varoma tray, turn fish over, secure Varoma lid and steam a further 10 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  5. Remove Varoma. Remove simmering basket with spatula. Transfer steamed zucchini, fish and potatoes onto a large dish, set aside and keep warm. Empty mixing bowl.
  6. Place butter and remaining citrus zest into mixing bowl and heat 4 min/140°F/speed 1.
  7. Add orange and lemon juice into mixing bowl and mix 30 sec/speed 3. Place cod, zucchini and potatoes onto plates. Pour warm citrus butter over meal, sprinkle with parsley and serve immediately.
