Roti

Ingredients

  • 500 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 240 g water
  • 60 g sunflower oil
  • ½ tsp sea salt
  • ghee, melted, for brushing (see Tips)
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
568 kJ / 135 kcal

2.8 g

Preparation

  1. Place all ingredients, except ghee, into mixing bowl and knead 1 min/.
  2. Transfer dough onto a silicone bread mat or floured work surface and shape into a ball. Wrap in silicone bread mat or place into a bowl and cover with plastic wrap or kitchen towel. Allow to rest for 10 minutes.
  3. Divide dough into 20 equal balls (approx. 40 g each). On a lightly floured work surface, use a rolling pin to roll each ball of dough into a thin circle (approx. 15 cm).
  4. Place a large non-stick frying pan over medium-high heat. Working 1 at a time, place roti into frying pan and fry for 30 seconds or until black dots appear underneath. Flip over and fry for another 30 seconds or until black dots appear. Flip roti again, allowing it to puff up. Using the back of a spoon, gently press down to press air into the flattened part of the roti. Transfer onto a plate, and brush with a thin layer of melted ghee. Cover with aluminium foil while frying remaining roti. Serve warm.

Useful Items

bowl, silicone bread mat, plastic wrap or kitchen towel, rolling pin, aluminium foil, 1 plate, large non-stick frying pan, pastry brush, stove top
