Cashew Nut Prawn Stir-Fry

Ingredients

  • 200 g raw prawns, peeled, deveined (see tip)
  • ¾ tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 Tbsp rice wine
  • 1 Tbsp cornflour
  • 30 g oil
  • 5 g fresh root ginger, peeled, cut in round slices (2 mm)
  • 2 dried shiitake mushrooms, soaked until soft, cut in pieces (see tip)
  • 50 g fresh beansprouts
  • 40 g red peppers, cut in pieces
  • 40 g yellow peppers, cut in pieces
  • 80 g raw cashew nuts, honey roasted (see tip)
  • 1 pinch ground white pepper
  • fresh coriander, for garnishing

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in prawns. Season with ½ tsp salt, rice wine and cornflour, mix well and leave to marinate. Meanwhile, continue with recipe.
  2. Place oil and ginger in mixing bowl then sauté 5 min/120°C/speed .
  3. Add shiitake mushrooms and beansprouts then stir-fry 3 min/100°C//speed .
  4. Add marinated prawns and stir-fry 5 min/100°C//speed .
  5. Add red peppers, yellow peppers, cashew nuts, ¼ tsp salt and white pepper then cook 3 min/100°C//speed . Transfer to a serving dish. Garnish with coriander and serve immediately.
