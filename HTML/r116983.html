Salmon and Courgette en Croute

Ingredients

  • 40 g leek, sliced (2 cm)
  • 250 g courgettes, cut in match sticks (5 cm x 2 mm)
  • 30 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 pinches fine sea salt
  • 2 pinches ground white pepper
  • 20 g white wine
  • plain flour, for dusting
  • 250 g puff pastry, ready-made
  • 400 g fresh salmon fillets, skinless, cut in pieces (2-3 cm)

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C and line a baking tray with baking paper.
  2. Place leek in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add courgettes and olive oil then cook 4 min/100°C//speed .
  4. Add 1 pinch salt, 1 pinch pepper and wine then, without measuring cup, cook 6 min/Varoma//speed . Drain mixture using a sieve then transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  5. On a floured surface, roll out pastry to a thin rectangle (approx. 25 cm x 15 cm) and place on prepared tray. Over half the pastry, spread half the courgette mixture and place salmon pieces on top, then cover with remaining courgette mixture and season with 1 pinch salt and 1 pinch pepper. Fold over rest of pastry and press edges together, trim any excess and seal well with a fork. Pierce several holes in centre of pastry with a fork and bake for 25-30 minutes (200°C).
  6. Allow to stand for 5 minutes before serving.

Useful Items

baking tray and paper, medium mesh sieve, rolling pin, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • It is recommended that you drain the courgettes after cooking to avoid excess liquid in the pastry.
  • Use any leftover pastry trimmings for decoration.
  • Brush the pastry with milk or egg wash before baking, for a more golden, shiny finish.
  • The Quick Puff Pastry recipe from The Basic Cookbook is ideal for this recipe.
