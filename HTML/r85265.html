Smoked Salmon and Courgette Pasta

Ingredients

  • 1000 g water
  • 1 ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 350 g pasta, eg. Amori or Penne
  • 1 lemon, thin peelings of skin, plus juice from ½ lemon
  • 350 g courgettes, cut in pieces (3 cm)
  • 230 g single cream
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 220 g smoked salmon, diced
  • 20 sprigs fresh chives, snipped (5 mm)

Preparation

  1. Place water, 1 tsp salt and oil in mixing bowl then bring to the boil 9 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  2. Add pasta and cook select time according to packet instructions/100°C//speed . Using simmering basket, drain pasta well under cold running water then set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Place lemon rind in mixing bowl and chop 30 sec/speed 10.
  4. Add courgettes and chop 4 sec/speed 4 then scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add cream, ¼ tsp salt and pepper then heat 4 min/90°C//speed 1.
  6. Add cooked pasta and heat 6 min/98°C//speed 1.
  7. Add salmon, chopped chives and 15 g lemon juice then stir in using spatula. Serve immediately.
