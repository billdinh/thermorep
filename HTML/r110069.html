Chocolate Decadence Muffins

Ingredients

  • 5 ½ oz white chocolate, in pieces or chips
  • 3 ½ oz unsalted butter, room temperature, in pieces
  • 4 oz sugar
  • 1 tbsp natural vanilla extract
  • 7 oz whole milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 9 oz all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 3 oz unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 12 pecans, for garnish
Nutrition
per 1 piece

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease 12 ramekins or place paper muffin liners into a muffin pan and set aside.
  2. Place white chocolate into mixing bowl and chop Turbo/1 sec/1 time. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place butter, sugar, vanilla extract, milk and eggs into mixing bowl and mix 1 min/speed 3.
  4. Add flour, baking powder, cocoa powder and mix 30 sec/speed 5.
  5. Add reserved chopped white chocolate and mix 10 sec//speed 2. Pour batter into greased ramekins or muffin liners (approx. ⅔ full). Place a pecan on each to garnish.
  6. Bake 20-25 minutes (350°F). Allow to cool before removing from muffin pan. Serve warm or cold.

Useful Items

muffin pan, ramekins, cupcake liners, oven
