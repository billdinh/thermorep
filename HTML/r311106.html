Eggplant 'Meatballs' with Romesco Sauce

Ingredients

Eggplant "Meatballs"

  • olive oil, to rub eggplant
  • 1 eggplant (large, approx. 9 oz)
  • 1 oz dried porcini mushrooms
  • 8 oz water
  • 3 oz Parmesan cheese, cut into pieces
  • 4 oz yellow onions, cut into pieces
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 5 sprigs parsley, leaves and tender stems
  • 10 fresh basil leaves
  • 6 oz bread crumbs
  • 1 large egg
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 3 oz pine nuts

Hazelnut Romesco

  • 5 oz hazelnuts
  • ½ oz Parmesan cheese, cut into pieces, plus extra, shaved, to garnish
  • 5 oz roasted red peppers, canned or bottled, drained, cut into pieces
  • 4 ½ oz tomatoes, cut into pieces
  • 2 oz olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 10 sprigs parsley, leaves and tender stems
  • 6 oz water
  • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • ½ tsp ground cayenne pepper, to taste
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • &frac18; tsp ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1 - 2 zucchini (approx. 7 oz)
  • 5 - 6 carrots (approx. 12 oz)

Preparation

Eggplant "Meatballs"

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F. Line two baking sheets with foil. Rub eggplant skin with olive oil, pierce the skin all over with a fork and place on prepared baking sheet. Roast for 25-30 minutes (450°F) until skin has charred and interior is fork tender. Once cooled (approx. 30 min.) peel eggplant and cut into pieces. Discard skin. Reduce oven to 350°F.
  2. Place dried mushrooms and water into mixing bowl and cook 6 min/212°F//speed 2. Transfer to a heatproof bowl, cover with cling wrap and allow to rest 10 minutes. Strain mushrooms and reserve liquid. Dry mixing bowl.
  3. Place Parmesan into mixing bowl and grate 15 sec/speed 10.
  4. Add onion, garlic, parsley and basil and chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  5. Add reserved eggplant, reserved mushrooms, 2 tbsp reserved mushroom liquid, bread crumbs, egg and salt and mix 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  6. Add pine nuts and mix 10 sec/speed 4. Form 30 "meatballs" (approx. 1 oz. ea.) and place on prepared baking sheet. Bake for 30 minutes (350°F) or until golden brown. Keep warm while preparing sauce.

Hazelnut Romesco

  1. Approximately 10 minutes before the "meatballs" are done, place hazelnuts and 0.5 oz Parmesan into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 6.
  2. Add roasted red peppers, tomatoes, oil, garlic, parsley, 3 tbsp reserved mushroom liquid, water, vinegar, paprika, cayenne, salt and pepper and cook 6 min/212°F/speed 1. Meanwhile, using a spiralizer prepare zucchini and carrot "noodles". Place Varoma into position and weigh in prepared carrots, secure Varoma lid and steam 5 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Carefully open Varoma lid away from you and weigh in prepared zucchini, secure Varoma lid and steam 3 min/Varoma/speed 1. Transfer vegetables into bowls.
  4. Insert measuring cup into mixing bowl lid and purée sauce 1 min/speed 3-8 increasing speed gradually.
  5. Gently toss Eggplant "Meatballs" with Hazelnut Romesco and spoon over vegetable "noodles". Garnish with Parmesan and serve.

Useful Items

baking sheets, aluminum foil, cling wrap, heatproof bowl, fine mesh sieve, vegetable spiralizer, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Garnish with hand-torn basil leaves or more pine nuts.
  • Eggplant is done when an inserted fork slides all the way in with no resistance.
