Cava Cocktail Surprise

Ingredients

Cava Jelly Beans

  • 3 gelatine leaves
  • water, cold, for soaking
  • oil, for greasing
  • 70 g fresh strawberries, hulled, halved if large
  • 200 g sparkling wine
  • 100 g sugar

Cava Cocktail

  • 280 g sparkling wine
  • 200 g frozen strawberries, hulled, halved if large
  • 20 g caster sugar
  • 250 g ice cubes

Preparation

Cava Jelly Beans

  1. Place gelatine leaves in a bowl, cover with cold water then leave to soak for 10 minutes. Meanwhile, lightly grease a silicone ice cube tray with oil and continue with recipe.
  2. Place strawberries in mixing bowl and blend 3 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place 80 g sparkling wine and sugar in mixing bowl then heat 2 min/60°C/speed .
  4. Add squeezed-out gelatine leaves then mix 15 sec/speed 2.
  5. Add reserved blended strawberries and remaining 120 g sparkling wine then mix 15 sec/speed 2. Transfer to prepared ice cube tray and place in fridge for at least 4 hours.

Cava Cocktail

  1. Place sparkling wine, strawberries, sugar and ice cubes in mixing bowl then mix 30 sec/speed 10 until smooth. Pour into 6 glasses, decorate with prepared jelly beans and serve.

Useful Items

ice cube tray, silicone ice cube tray, glasses, refrigerator
