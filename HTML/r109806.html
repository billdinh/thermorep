BBQ chicken winglets

Ingredients

  • 1 brown onion (approx. 120 g), cut into halves
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 250 g tomato sauce (gluten free) (see Tips)
  • 50 g apple cider vinegar
  • 50 g honey
  • 100 g brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce (gluten free) (see Tips)
  • 60 g water
  • ½ tsp paprika
  • 1000 g chicken winglets (see Tips)
  • spring onions/shallots, trimmed and cut into thin slices, for garnishing (optional)

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C. Line bottom and sides of a baking tray or casserole dish (30 x 40 cm) with aluminium foil.
  2. Place onion and garlic into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add all remaining ingredients, except chicken winglets and spring onion and mix 5 sec/speed 2 or until well combined.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and cook 20 min/100°C/speed 2, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  5. Insert measuring cup. Blend 30 sec/speed 1 -7, increasing speed gradually from speed 1 to speed 7.
  6. Place chicken winglets onto prepared tray or dish and pour barbecue sauce over chicken. Roast for 45 minutes (190°C), turning winglets every 15 minutes during roasting, until winglets are cooked through. Garnish with spring onion (optional) and serve warm with dipping sauce of choice (see Tips).

Useful Items

baking tray (30 x 40 cm), aluminium foil, casserole dish (30 x 40 cm), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • To make your own tomato sauce, please refer to the recipe in The Basic Cookbook or Everyday Cookbook.
  • To make your own gluten free Worcestershire sauce, please refer to the recipe on this recipe chip.
  • You can use chicken winglets or drumettes in this recipe.
  • Serve with a gluten free Ranch or blue cheese dipping sauce (visit the Official Thermomix Recipe Community (www.recipecommunity.com.au) for recipe suggestions).
