Eggplant and porcini bites with turmeric tahini dressing

Ingredients

Eggplant and porcini bites with roasted broccoli

  • 20 g dried porcini mushrooms
  • 60 g filtered water
  • 1 red onion (approx. 120 g), cut into quarters
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil, plus extra for drizzling
  • 1 eggplant (approx. 480 g), cut into pieces (approx. 2 cm)
  • 100 g breadcrumbs of choice (see Tips)
  • 5 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • ½ tsp ground cayenne pepper
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • ½ tsp ground cumin
  • 2 lemons, 1 zested, no white pith and 1 cut into halves lengthways, then into thin slices
  • ¼ tsp salt, plus extra to taste
  • 1 - 2 pinches ground black pepper, plus extra to taste
  • 250 g broccoli, cut into florets, including approx. 2 cm of stalk

Turmeric tahini dressing

  • 20 g pine nuts
  • 60 g tahini (see Tips)
  • 120 g filtered water
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • ½ tsp ground turmeric

Assembly

  • 60 g rocket
  • ½ red onion, cut into thin slices
  • 1 carrot, cut into julienne
  • 1 Lebanese cucumber, cut into cubes (1-2 cm)
  • 4 pita breads

Preparation

Eggplant and porcini bites with roasted broccoli

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C. Line a baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh porcini mushrooms and water into it, then set aside.
  3. Place onion, garlic and oil into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add eggplant and cook 10 min/120°C/speed 2. Scrape down sides and bottom of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add breadcrumbs, coriander leaves, ground coriander, cayenne, cinnamon, cumin, lemon zest, ¼ tsp of the salt, 1-2 pinches of the pepper and soaked porcini mushrooms with soaking liquid (see Tips), then blend 5 sec/speed 6. Transfer into a bowl and set aside to cool (approx. 30 minutes). Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  6. Roll 1 tablespoon of the eggplant mixture into a ball and place onto prepared baking tray. Repeat with remaining mixture (makes approx. 24 balls in total).
  7. Place baking tray, with eggplant bites, onto mixing bowl lid and weigh broccoli onto it. Add lemon slices onto baking tray. Drizzle with oil, season with salt and pepper and roast for 15-18 minutes (200°C), turning once during cooking.

Turmeric tahini dressing

  1. Place all dressing ingredients into mixing bowl and heat 3 min/80°C/speed 2, then blend 1 min/speed 10. Transfer into a sealable container and set aside until ready to serve (see Tips).

Assembly

  1. Divide rocket, onion, carrot, cucumber, pita breads, broccoli, lemon slices and eggplant bites between 4 serving bowls. Serve with Turmeric tahini dressing.

Useful Items

bowl, baking tray (30 x 40 cm), baking paper, sealable container, serving bowls, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • To make your own breadcrumbs, place 200 g bread into mixing bowl and chop 9-10 sec/speed 4.
  • To make your own tahini, refer to the recipe on Cookidoo® (www.cookidoo.com.au).
  • To make this recipe gluten free, use gluten free breadcrumbs and serve with gluten free pita bread (or flatbread).
  • Make sure you discard the last tablespoon of the porcini mushroom soaking liquid as this may contain any sediment from soaking.
  • You can store leftover Turmeric tahini dressing in a sealable container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.
