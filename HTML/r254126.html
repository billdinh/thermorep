Nicoise Salad

Ingredients

  • 16 oz tuna steaks (1 in. thick)
  • 2 tbsp liquid aminos, e.g. Bragg®
  • 3 oz extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 oz shallots, in pieces
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 oz white wine vinegar
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 - 4 anchovies, preserved in olive oil, to taste
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper, divided
  • 20 oz water
  • 4 large eggs
  • 10 oz fingerling potatoes, sliced in rounds (¼ in.)
  • ½ tsp dried parsley
  • ½ tsp salt, to taste
  • 6 oz French green beans, trimmed
  • romaine lettuce (approx. 9 oz)
  • 15 - 20 cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 jar Nicoise olives, drained, to taste
  • ½ red onion, thinly sliced into half moons

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in tuna. Add liquid aminos, cover and refrigerate 30 minutes. Place a pitcher onto mixing bowl lid, weigh in oil and set aside.
  2. Place shallots, garlic, vinegar, Dijon, anchovies and ⅛ tsp pepper into mixing bowl and mix 1 min/speed 4, while slowly pouring reserved oil onto mixing bowl lid, letting it drizzle around measuring cup in a thin stream onto rotating blades to create an emulsion. Return dressing into pitcher and set aside.
  3. Insert simmering basket and add water and eggs.
  4. Place Varoma into position and weigh in potatoes. Season with parsley, salt and ⅛ tsp pepper. Insert Varoma tray, add green beans and arrange marinated tuna over top. Secure Varoma lid and steam 10-15 min/Varoma/speed 2, until cooked through.
  5. Remove Varoma. Remove simmering basket with spatula and run eggs under cold water to stop the cooking process. Remove Varoma tray and set aside to cool. Place Varoma dish back into position, secure Varoma lid and steam potatoes a further 10-15 min/Varoma/speed 2, until tender. Remove Varoma and let potatoes cool slightly.
  6. Place romaine lettuce into a serving bowl. Peel and slice reserved eggs. Flake or slice tuna. Arrange eggs, tuna, cooled potatoes, green beans, cherry tomatoes, olives and onion on salad greens. Serve with reserved dressing.

Useful Items

serving bowl, pitcher, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve when potatoes, tuna, eggs and green beans are warm or serve chilled.
  • Wash herbs and vegetables before use.
