Hokey pokey ice cream

Ingredients

Ice cream

  • 60 g water
  • 30 g cornflour
  • 400 g milk
  • 340 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 100 g white sugar
  • 1 tbsp vanilla bean paste
  • 2 pinches salt

Honeycomb

  • 150 g white sugar
  • 70 g honey
  • 40 g water
  • 1 tbsp golden syrup
  • 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda

Preparation

Ice cream

  1. Place a small bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh water and cornflour into it. Mix until smooth and set aside.
  2. Place remaining ingredients into mixing bowl and heat 5 min/100°C/speed 2.
  3. Add the reserved cornflour mixture and cook 5 min/100°C/speed 3 or until slightly thickened. Transfer mixture into a shallow freezable container and set aside to cool for 15 minutes, then place into freezer overnight.
  4. Remove ice cream from freezer and set aside for approx. 10 minutes, until soft enough to cut into pieces (4 x 4 cm). Place ice cream into mixing bowl and crush 1 min/speed 7, with aid of spatula, or until smooth.
  5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and blend for a further 40 sec/speed 4 or until smooth and creamy. Transfer into a large freezable container and return to the freezer. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Honeycomb

  1. Line a baking tray (20 x 30 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh sugar, honey, water and syrup into it. Place a heavy-based saucepan over low heat and add sugar mixture. Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring until sugar dissolves. Increase heat to high, bring to the boil and cook, without stirring, for 5-7 minutes, until a rich golden brown colour (watch carefully as mixture burns easily). Remove from the heat and carefully but quickly whisk in bicarbonate of soda until dissolved. Carefully pour mixture evenly onto prepared tray, do not spread. Set aside to cool completely (approx. 30 minutes). Transfer the honeycomb onto a clean work surface and break into bite-sized pieces.
  3. Place reserved ice cream into mixing bowl and mix 5 sec//speed 3.
  4. Add 100 g of the reserved Honeycomb and mix 5 sec//speed 3, until just combined. Transfer into freezable container and return to the freezer until firm enough to serve (approx. 4 hours or overnight).
  5. Serve ice cream sprinkled with remaining Honeycomb (see Tips).

Useful Items

small bowl, baking tray (20 x 30 cm), baking paper, bowl, heavy-based saucepan, whisk, shallow freezeable container (e.g. aluminium, silicone), large freezable container, stove top, freezer

Hints & Tricks

  • This ice cream is best when eaten within 2 days of making.
  • You can store any leftover honeycomb in a sealable container in the pantry for up to 1 week.
