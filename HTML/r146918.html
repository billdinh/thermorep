Jalapeño Chicken Lettuce Wraps

Ingredients

Guacamole

  • 3 sprigs fresh coriander
  • ¼ red onion (approx. 10-15 g)
  • 2 avocados
  • 1 tomato, deseeded and quartered
  • 1 pickled jalapeño chilli, drained (optional)
  • 1 - 2 tsp lime juice (approx. 1 lime), to taste
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt, to taste
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper, to taste

Coriander and Jalapeño Chicken

  • ¼ tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 chilli powder
  • ½ tsp cumin seeds
  • ½ tsp dried oregano
  • ½ tsp sweet paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic, granulated
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • 500 g chicken fillets
  • 5 sprigs fresh coriander
  • 1 pickled jalapeño chilli, drained
  • 40 g lime juice (approx. 2 limes)
  • 50 g honey
  • ¼ tsp onion salt

Warm Freekeh Salad

  • 250 g freekeh
  • 1000 g water
  • 1 Tbsp vegetable stock paste (see tips)
  • 80 - 100 g red peppers, deseeded and cut in pieces
  • 100 g macadamia nuts halved (optional)
  • ½ red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 sweet corn cobs, kernels only
  • 3 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only, roughly chopped
  • 1 pinch onion salt, to taste
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper, to taste
  • pickled jalapeño chillies, drained, sliced to serve
  • 1 cos lettuce, leaves separated, to serve
  • 100 g sour cream, to serve (optional)

Preparation

Guacamole

  1. Place coriander and onion in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add all remaining guacamole ingredients and mix 2 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl, cover and place in fridge until ready to use. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Coriander and Jalapeño Chicken

  1. Place cayenne pepper, dried chilli, cumin seeds, dried oregano, sweet paprika, granulated garlic and ground cinnamon in mixing bowl then grind 30 sec/speed 10.
  2. Place Varoma into position, insert Varoma tray and weigh in chicken. Remove Varoma, then rub spice mixture over the chicken. Set Varoma with chicken aside.
  3. Without cleaning mixing bowl, place coriander and jalapeños in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add lime juice, honey and onion salt then cook 3 min/100°C/speed 3. Transfer jalapeño sauce to a jug and set aside.

Warm Freekeh Salad

  1. Without cleaning mixing bowl, insert simmering basket and weigh in freekeh. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and rinse freekeh until water runs clear.
  2. Place water and stock paste in mixing bowl, then re-insert simmering basket with freekeh.
  3. Place Varoma with chicken into position, then drizzle over half the reserved jalapeño sauce. Secure Varoma lid and steam 15 min/Varoma/speed 2. Remove Varoma and turn chicken pieces over on Varoma tray to ensure even cooking. Stir freekeh in simmering basket with aid of spatula.
  4. Place Varoma dish into position, then weigh in red peppers and macadamias (if using). Add red onion and corn kernels to Varoma dish, then re-insert Varoma tray with chicken, secure Varoma lid and cook 3-5 min/Varoma/speed 2, or until chicken is fully cooked.
  5. Remove Varoma and transfer chicken to a serving platter. Transfer steamed vegetables to a serving bowl, then add coriander leaves and remaining jalapeño sauce.
  6. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and allow freekeh to drain. Transfer drained freekeh in serving bowl with the vegetables and season to taste with onion salt and pepper.
  7. Fill a lettuce leaf with the freekeh salad, top with a spiced chicken, extra jalapeño chillies and a dollop of reserved guacamole and sour cream (optional).
