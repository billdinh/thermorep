Non-dairy Mexican chocolate ice cream

Ingredients

  • 200 g dairy free dark chocolate
  • 500 gram unsweetened coconut milk
  • 150 g sugar
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 1 pinch salt (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • ¼ teaspoon ground chilli
  • 1 tbsp natural vanilla extract
  • 2 fresh red chillies, deseeded if preferred, for garnishing

Preparation

  1. Place chocolate into mixing bowl and grate 8 sec/speed 8.
  2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Add coconut milk, sugar, egg yolks and salt (optional), then cook 8 min/90°C/speed 2 and blend 5 sec/speed 4 -7, increasing speed gradually from speed 4 to speed 7.
  3. Add cinnamon, ground chilli and vanilla. Blend 4 sec/speed 5. Pour mixture into a shallow freezable container and place into freezer (approx. 8 hours) until frozen. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Cut the ice cream into pieces (3-4 cm) and place half of the ice cream pieces into mixing bowl and blend 20 sec/speed 6, then mix 10 sec/speed 4, with aid of spatula. Transfer into a freezable container and set aside.
  5. Place remaining ice cream pieces into mixing bowl and blend 20 sec/speed 6, then mix 10 sec/speed 4 with aid of spatula. Transfer into freezable container with ice cream.
  6. Cover with plastic wrap and return to freezer for 4 hours or until set. Garnish with fresh chilli before serving.

Useful Items

shallow freezeable container, plastic wrap, freezer

Hints & Tricks

  • Adjust the amount of ground chilli used in step 4 to your taste.
  • Use egg whites to make meringues. You can also freeze eggwhites to use in the future.
