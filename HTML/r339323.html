Raw Chocolate, Raspberry and Macadamia Bars

Ingredients

Base

  • 12 medjool dates, pitted, quartered
  • 130 g macadamia nuts
  • 60 g coconut oil
  • 30 g cacao powder

Raspberry Layer

  • 90 g raw cashew nuts
  • 260 g fresh raspberries
  • 130 g raw honey
  • 20 g desiccated coconut, plus extra for sprinkling
  • 20 g coconut oil

Topping

  • 90 g coconut oil
  • 80 g raw honey
  • 60 g cacao powder

Preparation

Base

  1. Line a square baking tin (20 cm) with baking paper.
  2. Place dates, macadamia nuts, coconut oil and cacao powder in mixing bowl then mix 20 sec/speed 7. Transfer to prepared tin, smooth over with spatula then place in freezer for 30 minutes. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl.

Raspberry Layer

  1. After freezing time, place cashews in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add raspberries, honey, desiccated coconut and coconut oil then mix 10 sec/speed 7. Transfer to tin and smooth over base layer with spatula. Place in freezer for 2-4 hours until firm. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl.

Topping

  1. After freezing time, place coconut oil in mixing bowl and melt 2 min/90°C/speed 2.
  2. Add honey and cacao powder then mix 10 sec/90°C/speed 3. Transfer to tin and smooth over with spatula. Place in freezer for 15 minutes then remove and slice into 16 squares.
  3. Store in a freezer-proof container in freezer until needed, sprinkling with extra desiccated coconut before serving.

Useful Items

baking paper, square baking tin (20 cm), freezer-proof container, freezer

Hints & Tricks

  • For a vegan instead of a raw version, substitute raw honey with maple syrup.
