Lemon Raspberry Trifle

Ingredients

Lemon Curd

  • 8 ½ oz sugar
  • 1 lemon, thin peel only, no pith (preferably organic)
  • 4 ½ oz unsalted butter, cubed
  • 3 large eggs
  • 5 ½ oz lemon juice

Raspberry Curd

  • 8 ½ oz sugar
  • 4 ½ oz unsalted butter, cubed
  • 5 large eggs
  • 10 oz frozen raspberries

Whipped Cream and Assembly

  • 16 oz heavy whipping cream
  • 1 package ladyfingers (7 oz)
  • 12 oz fresh raspberries, divided

Preparation

Lemon Curd

  1. Place sugar and lemon peel into mixing bowl and grind 20 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add butter, eggs and lemon juice and without measuring cup cook 20 min/195°F/speed 2. Insert measuring cup and mix 25 sec/speed 6. Transfer Lemon Curd to a bowl and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.

Raspberry Curd

  1. Place sugar into mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 10.
  2. Add butter, eggs and raspberries and without measuring cup cook 20 min/195°F/speed 2. Insert measuring cup and mix 25 sec/speed 6. Transfer Raspberry Curd to a separate bowl and set aside.

Whipped Cream and Assembly

  1. Insert butterfly whisk. Place whipping cream into mixing bowl and without setting a time whip speed 3.5, stopping once cream is just holding its shape to avoid overwhipping. Remove butterfly whisk.
  2. Lay ladyfingers (approx. 2 oz) over the bottom of a trifle bowl. Spread half the Lemon Curd over ladyfingers, top with a quarter of the whipped cream and sprinkle with 2 oz raspberries. Add another layer of ladyfingers, then half the Raspberry Curd. Repeat layers alternating lemon and raspberry curds and finishing with whipped cream. Cut remaining ladyfingers into cubes (approx. 1 in.).
  3. Garnish with remaining raspberries and cubed ladyfingers. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
