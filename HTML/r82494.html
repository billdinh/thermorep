Macadamia and Parmesan biscuits

Ingredients

  • 70 g Parmesan cheese, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary (approx. 10 cm long), leaves only
  • 70 g roasted salted macadamia nuts
  • 50 g butter, softened
  • 100 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • &frac18; tsp ground black pepper, to taste

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Line a baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place Parmesan and rosemary into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place macadamias into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 6.
  4. Add all remaining ingredients, including reserved Parmesan and rosemary mixture, and blend 7 sec/speed 7.
  5. Transfer dough onto a floured silicone bread mat or work surface and lightly knead. Roll out dough to a 5 mm thickness, then cut into 24 rounds using a cookie cutter (5 cm). Place onto prepared baking tray and bake for 8-10 minutes (180°C), or until biscuits are light golden brown. Transfer onto a wire rack and allow to cool completely before serving.

Useful Items

baking paper, rolling pin, cookie cutter, baking tray (30 x 40 cm), bowl, silicone bread mat, wire rack, oven
