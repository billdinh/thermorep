Butternut Squash Coconut Curry

Ingredients

  • 8 oz yellow onion, quartered
  • 1 garlic clove
  • ½ oz oil
  • 2 oz green curry paste
  • &frac18; tsp ground cardamom
  • &frac18; tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 oz fresh lemongrass, in thirds
  • 1 heaping tsp vegetable stock paste
  • 6 oz water
  • 1 can coconut milk (approx. 14 oz)
  • 14 oz butternut squash, peeled and cubed (1 in.)
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 5 oz zucchini, cubed (1 in.)
  • 8 oz cauliflower florets
  • 3 oz red bell pepper, cubed (1 in.)
  • 1 can garbanzo beans (approx. 16 oz), drained
  • mint leaves, sliced, to garnish
  • 1 lime, quartered, to taste
Nutrition
per 1 portion

3515 kJ / 840 kcal

Preparation

  1. Place onion, garlic and oil into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5.
  2. Add curry paste, cardamom and mustard seeds and sauté 3 min/250°F/speed 1.
  3. Add lemongrass, vegetable stock, water, coconut milk, squash and salt and cook 10 min/212°F//speed 1.
  4. Add zucchini, cauliflower, red pepper and beans and cook 5 min/212°F//speed 1.
  5. Remove and discard lemongrass pieces. Garnish with mint, squeeze of lime and serve immediately.
