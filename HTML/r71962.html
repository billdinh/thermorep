Yoghurt and jalapeño dressing

Ingredients

  • 20 g pickled jalapeño chillies, drained
  • 1 spring onion/shallot, trimmed and cut into pieces
  • 10 g lime juice (approx. 1/2 lime)
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 pinches sweet paprika
  • 250 g Greek yoghurt

Preparation

  1. Place jalapeños and spring onion/shallot into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 7.
  2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Repeat chopping if necessary until a finely chopped consistency is achieved.
  3. Add all remaining ingredients and mix 10-15 sec/speed 3, or until well combined. Transfer into a sealable jar or bottle and place into refrigerator until ready to use.

Useful Items

sealable jar or bottle

Hints & Tricks

  • Toss this dressing through your favourite salad, serve with delicious roasted vegetables or enjoy in a pita wrap with tabouli for a quick, natural lunch.
  • Try serving this dressing with Lamb and feta meatballs or with Slow cooked Mexican beef.
  • Use up leftover dressing throughout the week on sandwiches, rolls, salads and wraps.
