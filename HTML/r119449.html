Hearts entwined loaf

Ingredients

  • 60 g unsalted butter, cut into pieces, plus extra for greasing
  • 130 g full cream milk
  • 1 tsp dried instant yeast
  • 20 g sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp salt flakes
  • 220 g baker's flour
  • 20 g walnuts, cut into pieces

Preparation

  1. Grease a large bowl with butter and set aside.
  2. Place milk and butter into mixing bowl and melt 3 min/60°C/speed 1.
  3. Add yeast, sugar and egg and mix 10 sec/speed 3.
  4. Add salt and flour and knead 3 min/. Transfer dough into prepared bowl, cover with a kitchen towel and allow to rise for 1 hour in a warm place.
  5. Preheat oven to 40°C or lowest setting. Line a baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper and set aside. Divide dough into 2 portions and roll into thin logs (approx. 3 cm diameter). Form each log into a heart shape and interlink. Place onto prepared baking tray, sprinkle with walnuts and bake for 20 minutes (40°C).
  6. Increase oven temperature to 190°C and bake for a further 15 minutes (190°C), or until bread is golden brown and sounds hollow when tapped. Serve warm or allow to cool completely before placing into a sealable container until ready to serve. 

Useful Items

large bowl, kitchen towel, baking tray (30 x 40 cm), baking paper, sealable container, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • If preparing ahead of time, you can let the dough rest for 8 hours or overnight in the refrigerator before continuing with baking.
  • The bread will rise in the oven; thinner logs will result in more distinct heart shapes.
