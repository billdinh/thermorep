Nachos with Black Beans and Guacamole

Ingredients

  • 8 oz cheddar cheese, cut into pieces

Guacamole

  • 2 ½ oz white onions
  • 4 oz fresh Roma tomatoes, quartered
  • 8 oz avocado, halved (approx. 1 avocado)
  • 3 oz green tomatillos, quartered
  • 1 oz lime juice
  • ½ tsp salt, to taste

Black Beans

  • 2 cans black beans, undrained (approx. 29 oz)
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • ¼ tsp ground cumin, to taste
  • 1 tsp vegetable shortening
  • 18 oz tortilla chips
  • 4 tbsp sour cream, to taste, to garnish

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
  2. Place cheddar cheese into mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 6. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.

Guacamole

  1. Place onion into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place tomato into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 4.5. Transfer into the bowl with onion and set aside.
  3. Place avocado, tomatillos, lime juice and salt into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 5. Transfer into the bowl with onion and tomato, combine with spatula and set aside.

Black Beans

  1. Place black beans with liquid, minced garlic, cumin and vegetable shortening into mixing bowl and cook 8 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  2. Place tortilla chips in a baking dish and pour the cooked beans on top. Sprinkle with reserved grated cheese and bake 10-15 minutes (425°F) or until cheese is melted and lightly brown. Serve nachos with sour cream and Guacamole.
