Turkey Crumble

Ingredients

Filling

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 200 g onions, quartered
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 120 g leeks, sliced (1 cm)
  • 200 g carrots, sliced (1 cm)
  • 600 g butternut squash, cut in pieces (2-3 cm)
  • 50 g white wine
  • 1 - 1 ½ Tbsp smoked paprika, to taste
  • 300 g turkey mince, broken in pieces
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper

Crumble Topping

  • 40 g Parmesan cheese, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 60 g butter, diced
  • 100 g plain flour
  • 70 g oat flakes

Preparation

Filling

  1. Place garlic, onions and oil in mixing bowl then chop 4 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Sauté 7 min/100°C/speed 1.
  2. Add leeks and carrots then cook 7 min/100°C//speed 1.
  3. Add butternut squash, wine and smoked paprika then cook 10 min/100°C//speed .
  4. Preheat oven to 200°C. Add turkey, salt and pepper then stir in with spatula. Cook 5 min/100°C//speed 1. Drain mixture using simmering basket then transfer turkey mixture to a baking dish and set aside. Clean mixing bowl and prepare crumble topping.

Crumble Topping

  1. Place Parmesan in mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 9.
  2. Add butter and flour then mix 10 sec/speed 4.
  3. Add oats, mix well with spatula then sprinkle on top of turkey. Bake for  30 minutes (200°C) or until golden bown and bubbling. Serve hot with your choice of salad.
