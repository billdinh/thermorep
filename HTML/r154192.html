Cashew panna cotta with raspberry sauce

Ingredients

Cashew panna cotta

  • 100 g raw sugar
  • ½ vanilla bean, cut into pieces (2 cm)
  • 200 g raw cashews
  • 2 ½ tsp agar agar
  • 1 tbsp sunflower oil
  • 500 g water

Raspberry sauce

  • 30 g raw sugar
  • 200 g frozen raspberries, thawed, juice reserved
  • 20 g lemon juice (optional)

Preparation

Cashew panna cotta

  1. Place sugar and vanilla bean into mixing bowl and mill 20 sec/speed 10 .
  2. Add cashews, agar agar, oil and water and blend 1 min 30 sec/speed 10 , then cook 8 min/100°C/speed 3 .
  3. Divide mixture between 6 dariole moulds (120 ml). Set aside to cool for 10 minutes, then cover with plastic wrap and place into refrigerator for a minimum of 3 hours to set. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Raspberry sauce

  1. Place sugar into mixing bowl and mill 8 sec/speed 10 .
  2. Add thawed raspberries with any juices and lemon juice (optional) and mix 15 sec/speed 7. Transfer mixture into a fine-mesh sieve and push sauce through sieve into a jug. Discard any solids.
  3. Using a knife immersed in hot water, unmould panna cottas onto serving plates and drizzle with raspberry sauce to serve.

Useful Items

6 dariole moulds (120 ml), plastic wrap, fine mesh sieve, jug, serving plates, palette knife, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • You can prepare both the panna cotta and the raspberry sauce 1 day in advance and store in the refrigerator. Drizzle with sauce just before serving.
