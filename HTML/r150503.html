Simple steamed salmon

Ingredients

  • 900 - 1000 g fresh boneless, skinless salmon fillet (see Tips)
  • sea salt, to taste
  • ground black pepper, to taste
  • 2 garlic cloves, cut into halves
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary (approx. 8 cm long)
  • 2 sprigs fresh thyme (approx. 8 cm long)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1000 g water
  • lemons, to serve (optional)
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1579 kJ / 376 kcal
Protein
36 g
Carbohydrates
0.5 g
Fat
25.5 g

Preparation

  1. Place salmon onto a piece of wet, well-wrung baking paper and sprinkle with salt and pepper (see Tips). Place garlic, rosemary and thyme onto salmon and drizzle with oil. Tightly wrap salmon in baking paper and place into Varoma dish.
  2. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma with fish into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 20-24 min/Varoma/speed 2 or until salmon is cooked to your liking. Serve with lemon (see Tips).

Useful Items

baking paper

Hints & Tricks

  • Cooking times may vary according to the thickness of your salmon.
  • For more flavour, replace the salt and pepper with the Lemon pepper seasoning (please refer to the recipe on this recipe chip).
  • Salmon pairs beautifully with a variety of flavours. You might like to serve it with our Avocado dressing, Parsnip and bean mash or Asparagus and broad bean salad (please refer to the recipes on this recipe chip).
