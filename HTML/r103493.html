Pork belly with spring onions

Ingredients

  • 50 g soft dark brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1000 g pork rashers, cut into halves
  • 100 g soy sauce
  • 1080 g water
  • 800 g spring onions/shallots (white and light green parts only to total 500 g), cut into pieces (approx. 4 cm); green parts for garnishing
  • 300 g basmati rice
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
2704 kJ / 643 kcal
Protein
63.7 g
Carbohydrates
77.6 g
Fat
7.9 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C.
  2. Place sugar into mixing bowl and mill 15 sec/speed 10.
  3. Add oil and cook 3 min/Varoma/speed .
  4. Add pork, soy sauce and 80 g of the water and cook 5 min/Varoma//speed .
  5. Add 250 g of the spring onions/shallots (white and light green parts only) and braise 10 min/Varoma/speed .
  6. Add remaining 250 g of the spring onions/shallots (white and light green parts only) and braise 10 min/Varoma//speed , placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  7. Transfer pork mixture into a casserole dish (28 x 21 cm) and cover with aluminium foil, then bake for 10 minutes (200°C). Remove foil and bake for a further 15 minutes (200°C). Meanwhile, make rice.
  8. Insert simmering basket, then weigh rice into it. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and rinse rice until water runs clear.
  9. Place remaining 1000 g of the water into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket with rice and cook 14 min/100°C/speed 4. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula. Transfer rice into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and cover to keep warm.
  10. Remove casserole from oven. Preheat oven grill.
  11. Place Varoma dish on top of a glass bowl or large jug and carefully pour pork mixture into Varoma dish, and allow to drain. Transfer drained pork mixture back into casserole dish. Allow drained juices to stand for 5 minutes, or until fat has risen to the surface. Skim fat from juices and return remaining juices back into casserole dish.
  12. Place casserole dish under grill for approx. 5 minutes or until pork has caramelised.
  13. Divide pork belly mixture between 4 serving bowls and garnish with extra spring onions/shallots (green part only) and serve with rice.

Useful Items

casserole dish (28 x 21 cm), aluminium foil, thermal serving bowl or other large bowl, 4 serving bowls, glass bowl or large jug, oven, oven grill

Hints & Tricks

  • Depending on seasonality and the size of your spring onions/shallots, you may need more or less than the 800 g suggested. The total amount of white and light green parts of the spring onions/shallots used should total 500 g.
