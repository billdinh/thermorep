Spanish Tortilla - Tortilla Española

Ingredients

  • 100 g olive oil, plus extra for frying
  • 100 g water
  • 200 g onions, sliced (2 mm)
  • 500 g waxy potatoes, (e.g. Maris Piper), peeled and sliced (5 mm)
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 6 large eggs
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper

per 1 portion

1303 kJ / 311 kcal

Preparation

  1. Place oil and water in mixing bowl then heat 5 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  2. Add onions and cook 5 min/Varoma//speed .
  3. Add potatoes and salt then cook 14 min/100°C//speed . Check potatoes are cooked but not starting to fall apart; if necessary, cook up to a further 3 min/Varoma//speed .
  4. In a large bowl mix together eggs and pepper, stirring well with a whisk. Add potato mixture from mixing bowl to eggs and gently stir together.
  5. Heat a frying pan with oil over a medium heat. Gently pour egg and potato mixture into hot pan and cook for about 10 minutes or until egg is set. Turn tortilla out onto a large plate then slide back into frying pan to cook other side (about 5 minutes). Serve hot, warm or cold.

Useful Items

wire whisk, frying pan, hob

Hints & Tricks

  • Serves 4-6 for lunch or as a main course. 
  • Serves 12 as a tapas.

  • It is important to use waxy potatoes such as Maris Piper or Charlotte potatoes so that the potato slices retain their shape during cooking.
  • If the potatoes do begin to fall apart, don't worry, the tortilla will still taste delicious.

  • Replace onion with leeks, well washed and sliced (2 cm).
  • For a lower GI version, replace potatoes with sweet potatoes and reduce cooking time to 10 minutes in Step 3.
