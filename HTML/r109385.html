Spaghetti with Beef and Olive Meatballs

Ingredients

Meatballs

  • ½ oz fresh parsley, leaves only (approx. 25 sprigs)
  • 2 oz yellow onions
  • 2 oz black olives, pitted
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 24 oz ground beef

Pasta and Sauce

  • 5 ½ oz yellow onions, quartered
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • ½ oz olive oil
  • 35 ½ oz water
  • 2 tsp salt, divided
  • 12 ½ oz spaghetti
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 oz crème fraîche
  • 7 oz cherry tomatoes, halved
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper

Preparation

Meatballs

  1. Place parsley into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8. Remove half the chopped parsley and set aside for later.
  2. Add onions to remaining parsley in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 6.
  3. Add olives and chop 2 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add salt, pepper and beef and mix 10 sec//speed 3.5. Remove meat from mixing bowl, shape into 20 meatballs and set aside.

Pasta and Sauce

  1. Place onions, garlic and oil into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and sauté 3 min/250°F/speed 1.
  2. Add water and 1 tsp salt and bring to a boil 8 min/Varoma//speed 2.
  3. Add pasta through hole in mixing bowl lid and without measuring cup cook for time indicated on package/212°F//speed . Meanwhile, heat a non stick frying pan over medium heat and fry meatballs in 1 tbsp oil, turning until cooked and evenly browned.
  4. When pasta is cooked, drain through simmering basket and discard water. Place cooked and drained pasta, crème fraîche, cherry tomatoes, 1 tsp salt, pepper and reserved parsley into mixing bowl and mix together with spatula. Transfer pasta to bowls, place cooked meatballs on top and serve immediately.

Useful Items

non-stick frying pan

Hints & Tricks

  • Meatballs can be baked in the oven. Place meatballs in a baking pan (13 in. x 9 in.) covered with foil or cooking spray. Bake 18-22 minutes (400ºF) turning half way through cooking, until center of meatball is no longer pink.
