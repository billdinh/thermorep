Tuna Noodle Bake

Ingredients

  • 1 oz olive oil, plus 1 tbsp, divided (plus extra to grease)
  • 43 ½ oz water, divided
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 8 - 9 oz ramen noodles (see Tip)
  • 7 oz aged cheddar cheese (white or yellow), cut into pieces (approx. ¾ in.), divided
  • 2 ½ oz bread, broken into pieces
  • 1 garlic clove
  • ½ oz parsley, leaves only
  • 3 ½ oz yellow onion (approx. ½ large onion), quartered
  • 3 ½ oz red bell pepper, cut into 2 in. slices (approx. ½ large red pepper)
  • 17 oz whole milk
  • 1 oz all-purpose flour
  • 2 ½ oz dry white wine, e.g. chardonnay
  • 1 tbsp chicken stock paste
  • 8 oz button mushrooms, sliced
  • 12 oz canned tuna, drained (approx. 1 x 12 oz can, in water)

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease a casserole dish (8 in. x 11.5 in.) with olive oil and set aside.
  2. Place 40 oz water and ½ tsp salt into mixing bowl and heat 10 min/212°F/speed 1. Add noodles, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook time indicated on package/212°F//speed 1. Drain cooked noodles through simmering basket. Combine noodles and 1 tbsp oil in prepared casserole dish. Stir through to combine, spreading out pasta with spatula. Clean mixing bowl.
  3. Place cheese into mixing bowl and grate 3 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place bread into mixing bowl and chop 15-20 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  5. Place garlic and parsley into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7.
  6. Add onion, bell pepper and 1 oz oil and chop 3 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and sauté 3 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  7. Add milk, flour, wine, stock paste and 3.5 oz water. Cook 8 min/195°F/speed 3.
  8. Add mushrooms, tuna and 2.5 oz reserved cheese and cook 5 min/195°F//speed 2.
  9. Pour sauce over noodles in casserole dish and mix through with spatula.
  10. Combine remaining cheese with reserved bread crumbs and sprinkle over top of tuna bake.
  11. Bake 25 minutes (350°F) until golden. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

casserole dish (8 in. x 11.5 in.), bowl, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Bread crumbs can be replaced with crushed saltines or potato chips.
  • Mushrooms can be replaced with sweet corn and peas.
  • Egg noodles can be replaced with pasta (e.g. spirals or macaroni etc.)
  • Save time by boiling egg noodles according to package directions on the stove while making sauce.
