Chocolate Jaffa Cake (gluten free)

Ingredients

Sponge

  • 100 g olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 45 g gluten free dark chocolate (e.g. Kinnerton), in small pieces
  • 1 orange, thin peelings of skin
  • 180 g pitted dates
  • 2 bananas, ripe (approx. 200 g), cut in pieces
  • 3 large eggs
  • 150 g soya milk
  • 180 g gluten free plain white flour
  • 2 tsp gluten free baking powder
  • 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda

Ganache

  • 200 g gluten free dark chocolate (e.g. Kinnerton), in small pieces
  • 100 g dairy free sunflower margarine
  • 150 g icing sugar
  • 4 Tbsp soya milk

Preparation

Cake

  1. Preheat oven to 170°C. Grease sides and line bases of 2 cake tins (Ø 20 cm) with baking paper.
  2. Place chocolate and orange peel in mixing bowl then grate 10 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place dates in mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 9. Add to reserved chocolate and orange peel then set aside.
  4. Place bananas and eggs in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 5.
  5. Add oil, milk, flour, baking powder, bicarbonate of soda and reserved chocolate mixture then mix 30 sec/speed 5.
  6. Divide evenly between prepared tins. Bake for 25-30 minutes (170°C) or until a skewer inserted in centre comes out clean. Leave to cool in tins before removing. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl.
  7. Once cakes are cool, make ganache.

Ganache

  1. Place chocolate in mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 7.
  2. Add margarine and melt 5 min/50°C/speed 0.5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then melt again 2 min/50°C/speed 0.5.
  3. Add icing sugar and soya milk then mix 10 sec/speed 4.
  4. Place one sponge on a serving plate and spread a thin layer of ganache over then place other sponge on top. Spread remaining ganache over top of cake. Cut in slices to serve.

Useful Items

2 round cake tins (Ø 20 cm), baking paper, skewer, serving plate or cake stand, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Use a brand of chocolate that is gluten, egg, dairy and nut free, such as Kinnerton.
  • For a truly allergy free cake, always check that all your ingredients come from a source free of allergens.

Orange and chocolate is a match made in heaven, so this gluten free cake is definitely not just for those with allergies! The rich ganache gives it a lovely finish.
