Caesar salad dressing

Ingredients

  • 30 - 50 g Parmesan cheese, cut into pieces (3 cm), to taste
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 80 g grapeseed oil
  • 50 g mayonnaise
  • 3 anchovy fillets, cut into pieces
  • 20 g lemon juice (approx. Â½ lemon)
  • 2 tsp Dijon mustard

Preparation

  1. Place Parmesan into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place garlic into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Place a jug onto mixing bowl lid, weigh grapeseed oil into it and set jug aside. Insert butterfly whisk. Add anchovies, mayonnaise, lemon juice, Dijon mustard and reserved grated Parmesan and whisk 1 min/speed 4.
  4. With butterfly whisk still in place, mix speed 4 slowly pouring reserved oil onto mixing bowl lid, letting it drizzle into mixing bowl to emulsify. Take care not to add oil too quickly, as this may cause the dressing to split. Remove butterfly whisk.
  5. Use dressing straight away or transfer into a sealable jar or container and refrigerate until ready to use.

Useful Items

sealable jar or container, small jug, bowl, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Caesar salad dressing can be made one day ahead of time. Bring to room temperature and re-whisk 10 sec/speed 4 prior to using.
