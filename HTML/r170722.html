Chocolate Hazelnut Calzone with Bananas

Ingredients

Dough

  • 1 oz olive oil, plus extra to grease
  • 16 oz bread flour
  • 2 ½ oz whole wheat flour
  • 9 oz warm water
  • ½ oz sugar
  • 2 ¼ tsp rapid rise yeast
  • 2 tsp salt

Calzone

  • 12 tbsp hazelnut and chocolate spread (Nutella®), (approx. 12 oz ), divided
  • 2 bananas, cut into ¼ in. rounds, divided
  • 1 large egg, lightly beaten
  • 1 oz turbinado sugar (optional)

Preparation

Dough

  1. Lightly grease a large bowl and line two baking sheets with parchment paper and set aside.
  2. Place bread flour, whole wheat flour, water, sugar, yeast, 1 oz olive oil and salt into mixing bowl and knead 2 min/.
  3. Form dough into a ball and transfer to prepared bowl. Cover with cling wrap and let dough rise until doubled in size (approx. 1 hr.).

Calzone

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F. Divide dough into four balls and place two pieces of dough onto each prepared baking sheet. Stretch out each dough into a circle (approx. 5.5 in.) using your fingertips. Cover with clean kitchen towels and let rise 5 minutes.
  2. Place 3 tbsp chocolate hazelnut spread (see Tip) onto one half of dough, spreading into a semicircle, leaving a 1 inch border. Spread a quarter of the bananas on top of the hazelnut spread. Fold over dough to make a half-circle and pinch to seal. Cut 2 slits (approx. ½ in.) in top of dough to vent. Repeat with remaining dough. Place calzones on a baking sheet 1 inch apart.
  3. Brush calzones with egg, sprinkle with turbinado sugar and bake for 15 minutes (450°F). Serve warm.

Useful Items

kitchen towels, pastry brush, cling wrap, large bowl, baking sheets, parchment paper, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Add chopped toasted hazelnuts into the calzones before baking for added crunch.
  • Replace prepared chocolate hazelnut spread with homemade from recipe in The Basic Cookbook.
