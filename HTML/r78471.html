Turkey and lemon aioli wraps

Ingredients

Lemon and caper aioli

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 3 pieces lemon zest
  • 12 pickled capers, drained
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 2 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 - 2 tsp lemon juice
  • 250 g grapeseed oil
  • 1 pinch sea salt

Turkey wraps

  • 500 g water
  • 200 g skinless turkey breast, cut into strips (3-4 cm)
  • 1 pinch salt, plus extra to serve
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper, plus extra to serve
  • 50 g Lemon and caper aioli
  • 1 - 2 tsp grated lemon zest, no white pith
  • 2 tortillas
  • 20 g rocket leaves
  • ½ Lebanese cucumber (approx .30 g), peeled into ribbons
  • ½ carrot (approx. 30 g), peeled into ribbons
  • 1 - 2 radishes, cut into thin slices

Preparation

Lemon and caper aioli

  1. Place garlic and lemon zest into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 8.
  2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and repeat chopping 10 sec/speed 8 until a fine consistency is achieved.
  3. Add capers, egg yolks, Dijon mustard and lemon juice and mix 30 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Place a jug onto mixing bowl lid and weigh oil into it. Remove jug.
  5. Mix 2-3 min/speed 4, slowly pouring oil onto mixing bowl lid, letting it trickle into mixing bowl to emulsify.
  6. Season with salt to taste then mix 2 sec/speed 4.
  7. Transfer into a sealable jar or container and place into refrigerator until ready to use.

Turkey wraps

  1. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position, insert Varoma tray and weigh turkey onto Varoma tray. Season turkey with salt and pepper, then steam 10 min/Varoma/speed 1. Stir turkey with spatula to ensure even cooking then secure Varoma lid and steam 2-3 min/Varoma/speed 1, or until turkey is cooked completely.
  2. Transfer turkey onto a plate and place into refrigerator to cool completely (approx. 15-20 minutes). Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Place cooled turkey, lemon caper aioli and grated lemon zest into mixing bowl and shred 2-3 sec//speed 5.
  4. Divide turkey mixture between tortillas, then top with rocket, cucumber, carrot and radish. Season with salt and pepper before rolling up tightly and wrapping in baking paper or plastic wrap to secure. Cut each wrap into halves and serve.

Useful Items

2L jug, sealable storage jar or container, baking paper or plastic wrap, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Select a gluten free tortilla to make this a delicious gluten free lunch.
  • Save any leftover tortillas to use in the Slow-cooked Mexican beef or to enjoy with any leftover Lamb and feta meatballs for lunch the next day.
  • Use any additional turkey breasts to make the Turkey breast roulade with apricot and hazelnut stuffing.
  • Recipe for one: reduce all ingredients by half and continue as above.
