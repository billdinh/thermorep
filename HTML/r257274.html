Chicken Waldorf salad

Ingredients

Chicken

  • 500 g hot water
  • 500 g chicken breasts, cut into slices (1 cm thickness)
  • 1 tsp dried tarragon
  • olive oil, for drizzling
  • salt, to season
  • ground black pepper, to season

Waldorf salad

  • 100 g mayonnaise (see Tips)
  • 20 g lemon juice
  • 60 g sour cream
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 bunch watercress
  • ½ fresh pineapple, peeled, cored, quartered and cut into thin slices (see Tips)
  • 2 celery stalks, cut into thin slices
  • 1 red apple, unpeeled, cored and cut into thin slices
  • 2 spring onions/shallots, trimmed and cut into thin slices
  • 1 orange, peeled and segmented (optional)
  • 50 g walnuts, broken into pieces (optional)

Preparation

Chicken

  1. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position and weigh chicken into it. Sprinkle chicken with tarragon, drizzle with oil and season with salt and pepper, then secure Varoma lid and steam 8-10 min/Varoma/speed 4 or until chicken is cooked. Remove Varoma and set aside to cool. Rinse mixing bowl.

Waldorf salad

  1. Place mayonnaise, lemon juice, sour cream, Dijon mustard, salt and pepper into mixing bowl and mix 5 sec/speed 6. Transfer into a bowl. Add cooled chicken and toss to coat.
  2. Arrange watercress onto a serving platter. Top with pineapple, celery, apple, spring onions/shallots, orange segments (optional) and coated chicken with dressing. Sprinkle with walnuts (optional) to serve.

Useful Items

serving platter, bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • To make your own mayonnaise, please refer to the recipe in The Basic Cookbook.
  • If pineapple isn't available or in season, you can replace it with red or green seedless grapes.
  • To save time, you can prepare your fruit and vegetables while your chicken is steaming.
