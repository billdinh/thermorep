Smoked salmon slice

Ingredients

Rice base

  • 40 g butter, softened, plus extra for greasing
  • 300 g white rice
  • 900 g water
  • 1 egg
  • ¼ tsp sea salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper

Salmon

  • 50 g Parmesan cheese, crust removed and cut into cubes (3 cm)
  • 2 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 40 g butter, softened
  • 40 g plain flour
  • 1 ½ tsp curry powder
  • 320 g full cream milk
  • 50 g mayonnaise (see Tips)
  • 1 egg
  • 40 g lemon juice (approx. 1 lemon)
  • 200 g smoked salmon, cut into pieces (2-3 cm)
  • 1 pinch sea salt, plus extra to taste
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper, plus extra to taste
  • ½ tsp sesame seeds (optional)

Preparation

Rice base

  1. Grease a baking dish (30 x 20 cm) and set aside. Insert simmering basket into mixing bowl and weigh rice into it. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and rinse rice until water runs clear.
  2. Place water into mixing bowl. Re-insert simmering basket with rice, then cook 13-15 min/100°C/speed 4, until rice is cooked. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and set rice aside. Empty water from mixing bowl.
  3. Place egg, salt, pepper, cooked white rice and butter into mixing bowl and combine 10 sec//speed 4. Transfer rice mixture into prepared baking dish, press down firmly to form a base, then set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Salmon

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Place Parmesan into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place parsley into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a separate bowl and set aside.
  4. Place butter, flour, curry powder, milk, mayonnaise and egg into mixing bowl and cook 7 min/90°C/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add lemon juice, salmon, salt, pepper and reserved parsley and cook 1 min/90°C//speed 1.
  6. Adjust seasoning to taste, then transfer salmon mixture into baking dish and spread evenly over rice base.
  7. Sprinkle with reserved Parmesan and sesame seeds (optional), then bake for 30-35 minutes (180°C) until golden.
  8. Allow to cool (approx. 30 minutes), then place into refrigerator to cool completely. Cut into 6 slices and serve cold.

Useful Items

baking dish (30 x 20 cm), bowl, oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Chicken slice: in step 8 reduce cooking time to 4 min/90°C/speed 4. Then, in step 9, substitute the smoked salmon for 300 g skinless chicken thigh fillets, cut into pieces (1 cm) and cook 7 min/90°C//speed 1. Proceed as per recipe.
  • Smoked salmon and brown rice slice: weigh and rinse brown rice as per recipe in step 2. Then, in step 3 increase water to 1000 g, re-insert simmering basket with brown rice and cook 40-45 minutes/100°C/speed 4, until rice is cooked. Proceed as per recipe.
  • To make your own mayonnaise, please refer to The Basic Cookbook or Everyday Cookbook for the recipe.
  • This slice can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 2 days.
