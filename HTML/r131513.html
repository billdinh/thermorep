Olive oil, garlic and almond soup

Ingredients

  • 200 g almonds, raw, skinned
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 100 g breadcrumbs, white (from the day before)
  • 70 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 30 g sherry vinegar
  • 800 - 1000 g water, cold
  • 20 - 30 white grapes, peeled, (to garnish)
  • 30 g almonds, flaked, toasted, (to garnish)
Nutrition

Calories

Preparation

  1. Place almonds, garlic, salt, breadcrumbs, olive oil, vinegar and water into mixing bowl and grind 2 min/speed 5-10 increasing speed gradually. Transfer into a soup tureen and refrigerate until serving.
  2. Serve very cold with peeled white grapes and/or flaked almonds.

Useful Items

refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • If you wish, just before serving, replace 300 g of water with the same weight in ice cubes added at the end and mix at speed 1 until they melt.
