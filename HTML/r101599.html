Chicken and Spinach Lasagne

Ingredients

  • 200 g Cheddar cheese, cut in pieces
  • 80 g olive oil
  • 200 g onions, quartered
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 700 g chicken breasts, skinless, cut in pieces (3 cm)
  • 300 g ripe tomatoes, quartered
  • 100 g white wine
  • 2 cubes chicken stock cubes (for 0.5 l each)
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • ¾ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp dried mixed herbs
  • 12 dry pasta, lasagne sheets
  • 400 g chopped spinach, frozen, thawed, drained, squeezed
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg

Béchamel

  • 600 g milk
  • 10 g butter
  • 30 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 50 g cornflour
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg

Preparation

  1. Place Cheddar in mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place oil, onions and garlic in mixing bowl then chop 2 sec/speed 5. Sauté 7 min/120°C/speed 1 then transfer half to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Add chicken, tomatoes, wine, stock cubes, ½ tsp salt, ½ tsp pepper and mixed herbs then cook 15 min/Varoma//speed . Pour half the chicken filling into an oven-proof dish (approx. 30 cm x 25 cm x 6 cm) and arrange 4 lasagne sheets over the top. Cover with remaining filling then sprinkle with half reserved grated cheese.
  4. Place reserved onions and garlic, spinach, ½ tsp salt, ¼ tsp pepper and nutmeg in mixing bowl then cook 5 min/100°C/speed 1. Meanwhile, place another 4 lasagne sheets over cheese. Spread spinach mixture on top when ready.
  5. Preheat oven to 180°C and clean mixing bowl.

Béchamel

  1. Place all ingredients in mixing bowl then cook 8 min/100°C/speed 4.
  2. Pour a third of the béchamel sauce over spinach mixture, arrange remaining 4 lasagne sheets over the top then pour over remaining sauce. Sprinkle with remaining grated cheese then bake for 30-40 minutes (180°C) until golden. Leave to rest for 5 minutes, then cut into portions and serve.
