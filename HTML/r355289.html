Chocolate Dipped Shortbread Hearts

Ingredients

  • 110 g unsalted butter, diced
  • 100 g caster sugar
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 170 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 200 g dark chocolate, small pieces or callets
  • 1 tsp coconut oil

Preparation

  1. Line 2 baking trays with baking paper.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Place butter, sugar and vanilla in mixing bowl then cream 2 min/speed 3.5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Remove butterfly whisk. Add flour and mix 20 sec/speed 6. Tip out onto a lightly-floured surface, bring together into a ball then wrap in cling film and chill in fridge for 30 minutes.
  4. On a lightly-floured surface, roll out chilled dough to 5 mm thick then cut out hearts using a heart-shaped cookie cutter. Re-roll any trimmings and cut out more hearts until all dough is used.
  5. Place on prepared trays and chill in fridge for 3 hours. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl. Towards the end of chilling time, preheat oven to 180째C.
  6. Bake for 10-15 minutes (180째C) until pale golden. Leave to cool on trays for 5 minutes then carefully transfer to wire racks to cool completely (approx. 30 minutes).
  7. Once shortbread hearts have cooled, place chocolate in mixing bowl and grate 8 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Remove 50 g (scales will show - 50 g), transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  8. Add oil and melt 4 min/50째C/speed 2.
  9. Add reserved grated chocolate, mix 3 min/speed 2 then melt 40 sec/37째C/speed 3. Transfer to a small bowl (see tip).
  10. Dip cooled shortbread hearts in melted chocolate so that half is coated, then place on 2 wire racks to set completely before serving or store in an airtight container (see tip).

Useful Items

baking trays, baking paper, rolling pin, wire racks, cling film, heart-shaped cookie cutter, airtight container, oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • The smaller the bowl, the easier it will be to dip exactly half of the heart into the bowl.
  • The coconut oil makes it easier to work with the melted chocolate. If the chocolate on the biscuits is not set after 1 hour, place biscuits in fridge for 10 minutes to set fully before serving or store in an airtight container.
