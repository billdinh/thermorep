Chick Pea Curry

Ingredients

  • 800 g tinned chick peas, 2 x 400 g tins, drained and rinsed
  • 350 g onions, quartered
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 50 - 60 g fresh ginger, cut in round slices (2 mm)
  • 50 g olive oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • ½ tsp ground chilli powder
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 Tbsp dried green mango powder (amchoor)
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 800 g tinned chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1436 kJ / 343 kcal

12.5 g
Carbohydrates
35.4 g
Fat
16.9 g

Preparation

  1. Prepare chick peas as needed then set aside (see Tip).
  2. Place onions, garlic, ginger and oil in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 7.
  3. Add cumin and sauté 10 min/120°C/speed 2.
  4. Add chilli powder, turmeric, dried green mango powder, garam masala, tomatoes and salt then cook 10 min/100°C/speed 2.
  5. Add chick peas and cook 10 min/100°C//speed 1. Serve garnished with raisins, yoghurt and chopped fresh green chillies as desired.
