Pork Meatballs with Celeriac and Apple

Ingredients

Meatballs

  • 80 g bread, torn in pieces
  • 80 g onions, quartered
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 5 - 8 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves only, to taste
  • 500 g pork mince, broken in pieces
  • ¼ tsp ground nutmeg
  • ¼ tsp ground mace
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper

Celeriac and Apple Purée

  • 750 g celeriac, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 1 Granny Smith apple, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 150 g water
  • 100 g milk
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper

Creamy Cider Sauce

  • 40 g onion, quartered
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 40 g unsalted butter, diced
  • 60 g plain flour
  • 200 g chicken stock
  • 300 g cider
  • 200 g double cream
  • 1 Tbsp Dijon mustard
  • ½ tsp caster sugar
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • vegetable oil, for frying

Preparation

Meatballs

  1. Place bread in mixing bowl and grate 8 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place onions, garlic and thyme in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  3. Add pork, reserved breadcrumbs, nutmeg, mace, salt and pepper then mix 10 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.

Celeriac and Apple Purée

  1. Place celeriac and apple in mixing bowl then chop 4 sec/speed 5.
  2. Add water, milk, salt and pepper then cook 22 min/100°C/speed 3. Meanwhile, shape pork mixture into 20 meatballs then cover and chill in fridge until needed.
  3. Blend cooked celeriac 1 min/speed 4-7, increasing speed gradually. Transfer to a bowl with a lid and set aside to keep warm. Clean mixing bowl.

Creamy Cider Sauce

  1. Place garlic, onion and oil in mixing bowl, chop 5 sec/speed 7 then sauté 2 min/100°C/speed 1.
  2. Add butter and flour then cook 2 min/100°C/speed 2.
  3. Place a jug on mixing bowl lid and weigh in 200 g chicken stock. Remove jug and set 1 min/100°C/speed 2 then slowly add chicken stock through hole in mixing bowl lid.
  4. Add cider, cream, mustard, sugar, salt and pepper then cook 5 min/100°C/speed 4.
  5. Heat oil in a large frying pan over a medium heat. Fry meatballs in batches for 5-7 minutes until browned all over and cooked through.
  6. Serve meatballs immediately with creamy cider sauce and celeriac and apple purée.

Useful Items

large frying pan, jug, hob, refrigerator
