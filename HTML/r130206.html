Bitter Chocolate and Orange Liqueur Tart with Honeycomb Dust

Ingredients

Pastry

  • 90 g caster sugar
  • ¼ orange, thin peelings of skin only
  • 250 g plain flour
  • 175 g unsalted butter, cold, diced
  • 1 egg yolk, from medium egg
  • 2 Tbsp water, chilled
  • 1 medium egg, beaten

Filling

  • 390 g double cream
  • 2 Tbsp liquid glucose
  • 365 g dark chocolate (70% cocoa solids), small pieces or callets
  • 25 g orange liqueur
  • 85 g unsalted butter, diced

Serving

  • 50 g honeycomb
  • crème fraîche, for serving

Preparation

Pastry

  1. Place sugar and orange peel in mixing bowl then grind 15 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add flour and butter then pulse Turbo/1 sec/3-5 times until coarse crumbs.
  3. Add egg yolk and water then pulse Turbo/0.5 sec/1-3 times until just clinging together. Tip out onto a large square of greaseproof paper (30 cm) and flatten to a disc. Cover with another sheet of greaseproof paper (similar size) and place in fridge for 30 minutes. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl and preheat oven to 200°C.
  4. Roll out chilled pastry between greaseproof paper to slightly larger than a quiche or tart tin (Ø 25 cm). Use to line tin, pressing in well. Prick base with a fork then place in fridge for 15 minutes. Line pastry case with baking paper then fill with baking beans and blind bake for 12-15 minutes (200°C). Remove baking beans and paper then bake for a further 5-10 minutes (200°C) until golden. Brush base lightly with beaten egg then set aside on a wire rack to cool completely.

Filling

  1. Place cream and glucose in mixing bowl then simmer 5 min/98°C/speed .
  2. Add chocolate and melt 3 min/40°C/speed 4 until melted.
  3. Add orange liqueur and butter then whisk 3 min/70°C/speed 4. Pour into cooled pastry case, smooth surface with a palette knife then place in fridge for 2 hours or overnight. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl.

Serving

  1. When ready to serve, place honeycomb in mixing bowl and pulse Turbo/0.5 sec/2-5 times until ground to dust. Sprinkle honeycomb dust over tart then serve at room temperature with a little crème fraîche.

Useful Items

greaseproof paper, rolling pin, quiche or tart tin (Ø 25 cm), baking beans, baking paper, pastry brush, wire rack, palette knife, refrigerator, oven
