Tortellini soup

Ingredients

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1500 g water
  • 10 g dried shiitake mushrooms, cut into slices
  • 1 tbsp Chicken stock paste (see Tips)
  • 1 dried bay leaf
  • 4 black peppercorns
  • 1 skinless chicken breast fillet, cut into thin slices (approx. 5 mm)
  • 250 g fresh tortellini
  • 1 carrot, cut into thin slices
  • 2 celery stalks, cut into thin slices
  • 2 - 3 spring onions/shallots, trimmed and finely sliced on the diagonal, for garnishing

Preparation

  1. Place garlic into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7.
  2. Add water, mushrooms and stock paste. Insert simmering basket and place bay leaf, peppercorns and chicken into it. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh tortellini into it. Place Varoma tray into position and place carrot and celery onto it. Secure Varoma lid and cook 17 min/Varoma//speed 1.
  3. Remove Varoma, remove simmering basket with aid of spatula. Discard bay leaf and peppercorns. Transfer chicken and vegetables into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and cover to keep warm.
  4. Place Varoma back into position. Stir tortellini, secure Varoma lid and steam 8-10 min/Varoma/speed 1 or until al dente. Transfer into thermal serving bowl or other large bowl with chicken and vegetables. Pour over broth, cover to keep warm and set aside for 2 minutes.
  5. Divide soup between serving bowls and garnish with spring onions/shallots before serving.
