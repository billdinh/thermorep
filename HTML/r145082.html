Thai green chicken curry

Ingredients

  • 250 g coconut cream
  • 3 tbsp Thai green curry paste
  • 500 g boneless skinless chicken thighs, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 50 g fish sauce
  • 1 tsp palm sugar
  • 300 g fresh mixed mushrooms (i.e. shiitake, button, field), large ones cut into pieces
  • 80 g canned bamboo shoots
  • 7 fresh Kaffir lime leaves
  • fresh sweet Thai basil, for garnishing
  • fresh long green chillies, deseeded and cut into very thin slices, for garnishing (optional)

Preparation

  1. Place coconut cream into mixing bowl and simmer 5 min/90°C/speed 2.
  2. Add curry paste and cook 3 min/90°C/speed 2 or until fragrant.
  3. Add chicken and cook 2 min/90°C//speed .
  4. Add fish sauce and palm sugar and cook 10 min/90°C//speed .
  5. Add mushrooms and bamboo shoots and cook 5 min/90°C//speed 1 or until mushrooms are cooked.
  6. Add Kaffir lime leaves and stir with aid of spatula. Transfer into a large serving bowl and garnish with Thai basil leaves and sliced green chillies (optional). Serve with rice or noodles of choice.
