Orange Sorbet with Sparkling Wine

Ingredients

  • 750 g sparkling wine
  • 600 g orange juice, freshly squeezed (7-9 oranges)
  • 150 g sugar
Nutrition
per 1 glass
Calories
777 kJ / 186 kcal
Protein
1 g
Carbohydrates
30 g
Fat
0 g

Preparation

  1. Place 120 g sparkling wine, 300 g orange juice and sugar in mixing bowl then cook 8 min/100°C/speed 2.
  2. Add remaining 300 g orange juice and mix 3 sec/speed 3. Transfer mixture up to 4 cm deep into freezable containers (e.g. aluminium, silicone) and cover with cling film. Cool mixture completely before freezing for at least 8 hours.
  3. Cut frozen mixture into pieces (approx. 4 cm x 4 cm) with a long sharp knife. Place pieces in mixing bowl and crush with aid of spatula 30 sec/speed 9. Transfer to wine glasses or champagne flutes and pour the remaining 630 g sparkling wine over the sorbet. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

freezable containers (e.g. aluminium, silicone), cling film, freezer

Hints & Tricks

  • For a non-alcoholic version replace the sparkling wine with sparkling water.
  • Replace the orange juice with another citrus juice (e.g. lemon, tangerine, grapefruit).
