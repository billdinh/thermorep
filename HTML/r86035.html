Pissaladière

Ingredients

Dough

  • olive oil, for greasing
  • 80 g water
  • 1 tsp dried instant yeast
  • 200 g strong white bread flour
  • 1 medium egg
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt

Topping

  • 2 tsp mixed fresh herbs, leaves only (e.g. parsley, thyme, rosemary)
  • 80 g olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 500 g onions, quartered
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 60 g tinned anchovies, soaked in a little milk then drained
  • 60 g black olives, pitted

Preparation

Dough

  1. Grease a large bowl with oil then set aside.
  2. Place water in mixing bowl and, avoiding blade, add yeast then heat 2 min/37°C/speed 2.
  3. Add flour, egg and salt then mix 20 sec/speed 7. Knead 2 min/.
  4. Transfer dough to greased bowl, cover with cling film and leave in a warm place until doubled in size (approx. 1 hour). Meanwhile, clean and dry mixing bowl and make topping.

Topping

  1. Place herbs in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7 then scrape down sides of bowl with spatula.
  2. Add oil, onions, salt and ¼ tsp pepper then chop 6 sec/speed 5 until roughly chopped then scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Cook 15 min/100°C/speed . Meanwhile, cut anchovy fillets in half lengthwise. Grease a baking tray (26 cm x 36 cm).
  3. When dough is risen, knead gently then pat or pull into prepared baking tray. Spread onion mixture on top, sprinkle with pepper, then make a lattice pattern with anchovies filling the spaces with olives. Leave to rise for 15 minutes. Meanwhile, preheat oven to 190°C.
  4. Once risen, bake for 20-25 minutes (190°C). Serve hot or at room temperature.

Useful Items

large bowl, cling film, baking tray, oven
