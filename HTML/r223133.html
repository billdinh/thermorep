Gozleme

Ingredients

Dough

  • 200 g milk
  • 1 egg
  • 40 g olive oil
  • 150 g Greek-style natural yoghurt
  • 2 tsp dried instant yeast
  • 600 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp white sugar
  • ½ tsp salt

Filling

  • 150 g tasty cheese, cut into cubes
  • 5 sprigs fresh mint, leaves only
  • 5 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 60 g fresh baby spinach leaves
  • 40 g olive oil
  • 5 garlic cloves
  • 1 brown onion, cut into quarters
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 500 g mince (chicken, beef or lamb)
  • 1 tbsp plain flour
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 30 g lemon juice
  • 250 g feta cheese, crumbled
  • olive oil spray, for frying
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges, to serve

Preparation

Dough

  1. Place milk, egg, oil, yoghurt and yeast into mixing bowl and mix 4 sec/speed 6, then heat 3 min/50°C/speed 2.
  2. Add flour, baking powder, sugar and salt and knead 2 min/. Transfer onto a silicone bread mat or a floured work surface and shape into a ball. Wrap in a silicone bread mat or place into a large glass bowl, cover with plastic wrap and set aside to prove for 30 minutes. Meanwhile, continue with filling.

Filling

  1. Place cheese into mixing bowl and grate 6 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a large bowl and set aside.
  2. Place mint, parsley and spinach into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 6. Transfer into bowl with cheese.
  3. Place oil, garlic, onion, cumin, coriander and paprika into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and cook 3 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  5. Add mince, flour, salt, pepper and lemon juice and cook 4 min/100°C//speed 1.
  6. Scrape down bottom and sides of mixing bowl with spatula and cook 5 min/100°C//speed 1. Transfer mixture into a bowl and set aside to cool.
  7. Place a plate onto mixing bowl lid and weigh feta onto it. Add feta and cooled mince mixture into bowl with cheese and herb mixture and stir to combine.
  8. Divide dough into 16 pieces (approx. 40 g each) and roll out each one into a large circle (15-20 cm). Place approx. 2 tablespoons filling onto one half of the dough, leaving a 1 cm border and fold over dough to enclose filling. Seal edges and flatten gently with the palm of your hand. Repeat with remaining dough and filling.
  9. Place a large frying pan over medium-high heat and lightly spray with oil. Fry gozleme until golden brown (approx. 2-3 minutes each side - see Tips). Serve warm with lemon wedges.

Useful Items

large bowl, bowl, silicone bread mat, large ceramic or glass bowl, plastic wrap, rolling pin, large frying pan, plate, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • Instead of pan frying, you can bake gozleme on a lined baking tray for 10 minutes (200°C). Lightly spray each gozleme with oil before baking.
  • You can adjust the fillings to suit your tastes or replace the meat with steamed potato pieces to make it vegetarian.
  • To add more vegetables to your meal, serve with a salad.
