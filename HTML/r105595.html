Revithia (chickpea soup)

Ingredients

  • 1 brown onion (approx. 150 g), cut into halves
  • 50 g olive oil
  • 250 g dried chickpeas, soaked overnight, rinsed and drained
  • 1350 g water
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 250 g chilled water
  • 30 g plain flour
  • 20 g lemon juice (approx. ½ lemon)
  • 2 sprig fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only

Preparation

  1. Place onion into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add oil and sauté 2 min/100°C/speed 1.
  3. Pureé 10 sec/speed 8 or until onion is smooth. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add chickpeas, water and salt and cook 25 min/Varoma//speed .
  5. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid. Weigh chilled water, flour and lemon juice into it, then stir until smooth.
  6. Add flour mixture to mixing bowl and cook 5 min/100°C//speed . Blend if desired (see Tips).
  7. Garnish with parsley and serve immediately.

Useful Items

bowl, spoon

Hints & Tricks

  • This soup is traditionally served with the chickpeas whole, as it gives the soup texture and depth. However, if you prefer a smoother consistency, blend 1 min/speed 4-9, increasing speed gradually from speed 4 to speed 9.
