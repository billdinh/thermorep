Lamb shanks with risoni and feta

Ingredients

Lamb Shanks

  • 3 pieces lemon zest (approx. 1 x 10 cm strips), no white pith
  • 4 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 2 eschalots (approx. 50 g)
  • 20 g olive oil, plus extra for frying
  • 130 g dry white wine
  • 700 g tomato passata
  • 400 g water
  • 1 tbsp Chicken stock paste (see Tips)
  • 50 g tomato paste
  • 1 tsp salt, plus extra for seasoning
  • 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper, plus extra for seasoning
  • 6 lamb shanks
  • 100 g plain flour
  • 200 g carrot, cut into pieces (1-2 cm)
  • 200 g zucchini, cut into pieces (1-2 cm)
  • 100 g pitted green Sicilian olives
  • 220 g dried risoni pasta

Feta Topping

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 4 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 4 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves only
  • ¼ tsp dried chilli flakes
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 20 g lemon juice
  • 200 g firm feta cheese, cut into cubes (2 cm)

Preparation

Lamb Shanks

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C. Place lemon, parsley, garlic and eschalots into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add oil and wine and cook 3 min/Varoma/speed 1, without measuring cup.
  3. Add passata, water, stock paste, tomato paste, salt and pepper and cook 10 min/100°C/speed 1. Set aside in mixing bowl.
  4. Place shanks and flour into a large snaplock plastic bag and season with salt and pepper. Seal bag and shake to coat, then set aside.
  5. Place a large frying pan over medium-high heat and add oil. Add lamb shanks to pan in batches and sear on each side (approx. 3-4 minutes each side) until browned. Transfer lamb into a casserole dish (approx. 28 cm).
  6. Carefully pour reserved passata mixture over lamb, secure with a tight-fitting lid or aluminium foil and bake for 2½ hours (160°C).
  7. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh carrots, zucchini, olives and risoni pasta into it. Set bowl aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  8. Carefully remove casserole dish from oven, add carrot mixture to casserole dish and gently stir to combine. Cover and bake for a further 40 minutes (160°C) or until lamb is tender. Meanwhile, make feta topping.

Feta Topping

  1. Place garlic, parsley, thyme and chilli flakes into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add oil and lemon juice and sauté 3 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Add feta and combine 3 min//speed 1.
  4. Divide shanks between 6 serving bowls and top with warm feta to serve. 

Useful Items

snaplock bag, large frying pan, casserole dish (28 cm), aluminium foil, bowl, 6 serving bowls, oven, stove top
