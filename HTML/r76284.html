Apple and Blackcurrant Pie

Ingredients

Pastry:

  • 100 g granulated sugar
  • 200 g unsalted butter, chilled, cut into pieces, plus extra for greasing
  • 370 g plain flour
  • 1 medium egg
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt

Filling:

  • 180 g blackcurrant jam
  • 300 g cooking apples, peeled, cored and cut in slices (1/2 cm)

Decoration:

  • 2 tsp icing sugar, for dusting

Preparation

Pastry:

  1. Place sugar in mixing bowl and grind 15 sec/speed 10 then add butter, flour, egg and salt and mix 25 sec/speed 5.
  2. Transfer pastry to a floured work surface, bring together into a flattened ball and wrap in cling film. Chill in refrigerator for 1 hour.
  3. Preheat oven to 180°C and place a baking tray into it. Grease a tart or pie tin (Ø 22 cm) with butter.
  4. Roll out two thirds of the chilled pastry dough between 2 sheets of baking paper to a thickness of 5 mm. Line prepared tin with pastry.

Filling:

  1. Spread jam over pastry base and then top with the sliced apples.
  2. Roll out remaining pastry between 2 sheets of baking paper to a thickness of 5 mm.
  3. Cut pastry with a fluted pastry wheel or knife into 10 strips, each 1 cm wide. Arrange strips on top of pie creating a lattice, woven pattern. Press each strip down firmly to pie edge to seal then trim off any excess pastry.
  4. Carefully place pie on hot baking tray in oven and bake 30 minutes (180°C) or until pastry is golden and filling is bubbling. Dust with icing sugar before serving.

Useful Items

cling film, fluted tart tin (Ø 22 cm), baking tray, rolling pin, baking paper, pastry brush, pastry wheel, refrigerator, oven

Hints & Tricks

Can be served hot or cold with pouring cream.

Try making this pie with blackberry or blueberry jam.
