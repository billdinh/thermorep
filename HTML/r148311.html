Star Pizza

Ingredients

Pizza Dough

  • 310 g water
  • 10 g fresh yeast
  • 500 g strong white bread flour
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
  • olive oil, for greasing

Topping

  • 100 g Parmesan cheese, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 160 g tomato purée
  • 60 g water
  • 90 g chorizo, cut in pieces
  • 1 pinch chilli powder

Assembly and Baking

  • strong white bread flour, for dusting
  • 1 pinch dried oregano

Preparation

Pizza Dough

  1. Place water and yeast in mixing bowl then warm 1 min/37°C/speed 2.
  2. Add flour, oil and salt then knead 3 min/. Meanwhile, grease a large bowl. Transfer kneaded dough to prepared bowl, cover, then set aside in a warm place to rise until doubled in size (approx. 1 hour).

Topping

  1. Place Parmesan in mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place tomato purée, water and chorizo in mixing bowl then chop 12 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add reserved grated Parmesan and chilli powder then mix 7 sec/speed 4.

Assembly and Baking

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C and line a large baking tray with baking paper.
  2. Tip risen dough out on a lightly floured surface and divide into 2 equal-sized pieces. Roll out one piece to a circle (approx. Ø 30 cm) then place on prepared tray. Spread reserved topping over the rolled out dough then sprinkle with oregano.
  3. Roll out second piece of dough to the same size (Ø 30 cm) then lay on top of first piece. Place a bowl or glass (approx. Ø 6 cm) in middle of the dough. Cut pizza into 4, then 8, then 16 equal parts from the bowl to the outside of the round. Gently twist each of the 16 segments 3 times. Remove bowl.
  4. Bake for 25-30 minutes (200°C) until cooked. Serve warm as part of your buffet selection, leaving guests to tear off pieces themselves.
