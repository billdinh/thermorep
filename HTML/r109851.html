Thai Steamed Salmon Parcels

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp groundnut oil
  • 4 spring onions, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 2 fresh red chillies, halved
  • 30 g fresh root ginger, peeled, cut in round slices (2 mm)
  • 2 limes, finely grated zest of both and juice of 1, plus extra for serving
  • 2 Tbsp coconut cream
  • 30 g fresh coriander
  • 1000 g water, plus boiling water for softening lettuce leaves
  • 4 fresh salmon fillets, skin removed (approx. 100 g each)
  • fine sea salt, to taste
  • ground black pepper, to taste
  • 4 leaves round lettuce

Preparation

  1. Place oil, spring onions, garlic, chillies and ginger in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then chop again 3 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl then sauté 5 min/100°C/speed .
  2. Add grated lime zest from 2 limes and juice from 1 lime, coconut cream and coriander then chop 5 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then chop again 3 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a small bowl and set aside to cool.
  3. When stuffing has cooled, place water in mixing bowl and heat 8 min/100°C/speed 1. Meanwhile, season one side of salmon fillets then cut each fillet in half. Spread cooled mixture on four halves then sandwich with remaining halves.
  4. Place lettuce leaves in a bowl and cover with boiling water. Leave for 2 minutes then discard water. Fold a leaf around each salmon sandwich to securely enclose.
  5. Place parcels in Varoma dish, cover and place Varoma into position. Steam 18 min/Varoma/speed 2. Serve hot garnished with lime wedges.
