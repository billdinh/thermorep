Sweet Potato and Spinach Cakes

Ingredients

Dip

  • 250 g Greek yoghurt
  • 20 g lemon juice
  • 10 g olive oil
  • 1 small handful fresh basil
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper

Potato Cakes

  • 100 g chickpeas, dried
  • 400 g sweet potatoes, cut in pieces
  • 400 g tinned cannellini beans, (1 tin, drained weight approx. 250 g)
  • 30 g spring onions, cut in pieces
  • 10 g soy sauce
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 pinch dried chilli flakes
  • 20 g rolled oats
  • 100 g baby spinach leaves
  • 4 - 6 Tbsp coconut oil, for frying

Preparation

Dip

  1. Place Greek yoghurt, lemon juice, olive oil, basil, salt and pepper in mixing bowl then mix 20 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and chill in fridge. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Potato Cakes

  1. Place chickpeas in mixing bowl and grind 1 min 30 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add sweet potatoes, cannellini beans, spring onions, soy sauce, garlic, salt, chilli flakes and oats then mix 20 sec/speed 7.
  3. Add spinach and mix in with spatula.
  4. Shape patties using approx. 2 Tbsp sweet potato mix per patty, flatten and set aside on a board while shaping the rest. Heat a non-stick frying pan over a medium/high heat, and fry patties in coconut oil for 4-5 minutes on each side until well browned. Serve with the chilled yoghurt dipping sauce and salad, if desired.
