Avocado coconut ice cream

Ingredients

  • 400 g coconut milk
  • 60 g raw sugar
  • 1 lime, grated zest, no white pith, and juice (to taste)
  • 1 large avocado, flesh only
Nutrition
per 1 portions
Calories
907 kJ / 215 kcal
Protein
1.7 g
Carbohydrates
11.5 g
Fat
18.3 g

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh coconut milk into it. Transfer into ice cube trays and place into freezer overnight or until frozen.
  2. Add sugar and mill 20 sec/speed 10, or to a fine consistency.
  3. Add frozen coconut cubes, 1-2 teaspoon lime juice (to taste) and half of the grated lime zest and blend 1 min/speed 1 -5, increasing speed gradually from speed 1 to speed 5 or until smooth and creamy. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add avocado and blend 1 min/speed 1 -6 , increasing speed gradually from speed 1 to speed 6.
  5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and blend 1 min/speed 6 or until smooth and creamy.
  6. Sprinkle with remaining lime zest and serve immediately (see Tips).

Useful Items

2-3 ice cube trays, bowl, freezable storage container, freezer

Hints & Tricks

  • If ice cream is too soft for your liking, transfer into a freezable container and place into freezer until desired consistency is achieved.
  • If ice cream becomes too firm to scoop, place it into the refrigerator or on the kitchen bench until thawed to your desired softness before serving. You may wish to store all frozen desserts in individual portions, as refreezing thawed leftovers can be a safety hazard.
