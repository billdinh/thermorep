Critters and crawlers

Ingredients

Cupcakes

  • 300 g white sugar
  • 250 g unsalted butter, cut into pieces and softened
  • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste
  • 250 g buttermilk
  • 4 eggs
  • 430 g self-raising flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 pinch salt

Buttercream icing

  • 500 g icing sugar (see Tips)
  • 240 g unsalted butter, cut into pieces and softened
  • 1 tbsp boiling water
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract
  • natural food colouring of choice (approx. 6 colours)

Assembly

  • lollies of choice, for decorating

Preparation

Cupcakes

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Line 2 x 12-hole muffin trays with cupcake liners and set aside.
  2. Place sugar into mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 10.
  3. Add butter and chop 10 sec/speed 4.
  4. Add all remaining cupcake ingredients and blend 10 sec/speed 5.
  5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and blend 10 sec/speed 5.
  6. Divide mixture evenly between prepared muffin trays and bake for 18-20 minutes (180°C) or until golden and a wooden skewer inserted into the centre of each cupcake comes out clean. Allow to cool in trays for 5 minutes, then transfer onto a wire rack to cool completely. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Buttercream icing

  1. Place sugar and butter into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Add boiling water and vanilla and mix 2 min/speed 3. Remove butterfly whisk. Divide mixture evenly between bowls (approx. 6). Colour each bowl with your choice of natural food colouring to get desired shades, then stir well to combine. Spread icing over cupcakes.

Assembly

  1. To create ladybugs, cut the dome off of each cupcake, then cut a large wedge out of the dome (to create the shell). Spread white icing over level cupcake and spread red icing over shell. Place a large lolly of choice on white icing to make a head (we used a black jube), and 2 small antenna (we used licorice). Top shell with black lollies of choice.
  2. To create dragonflies, spread icing of choice over cupcake. Use a piece of musk stick for the body, cut 2 'slice-shaped' jubes in half lengthways and place next to musk stick to make wings. Place a jube of choice at one end for the head.
  3. To create caterpillars, spread icing of choice over cupcake. Using small coloured chocolates (i.e. Smarties®), insert into icing (so they are standing up), in an 'S' shape. Cut several spearmint leaves into halves lengthways and insert standing up into cupcake.
  4. To create spiders, spread icing of choice over cupcake. Place 2 large lollies of choice into icing to create head and body. Cut licorice laces into 8 pieces to create legs and attach to body.
  5. To create bees, spread icing of choice over cupcakes. Place white, yellow and black candy bullets onto cupcake to form head and body. Cut a licorice strap into small pieces to form 2 wings. Cut 2 short pieces from licorice laces to make antenna.
  6. To create butterflies, spread icing of choice over cupcakes. Place 2 candy bullets lengthways into icing to create a body, then place 2 black candy bullets into one end for the antenna. Insert 2 freckles on either side into icing to create wings. Serve or place into a sealable storage container until ready to serve (see Tips).

Useful Items

12-hole muffin tray, cupcake liners, wooden skewer, wire rack, bowls, sealable storage container, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • We recommend using a soft, sifted icing sugar for this recipe.
  • If you aren't using the Buttercream icing right away, place into the refrigerator until ready to use. To use, bring back to room temperature or stir until softened enough to spread.
  • On hot days, store decorated cupcakes in the refrigerator until ready to serve.
