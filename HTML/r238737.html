Chawanmushi

Ingredients

  • 100 g chicken breast fillets, skinless, sliced (5 mm)
  • 30 g soy sauce
  • 25 g carrots, cut in pieces
  • 2 fresh shiitake mushrooms, sliced
  • 8 cashew nuts
  • 2 sprigs fresh parsley, cut in pieces
  • 250 g dashi stock, cold
  • 15 g mirin
  • 2 medium eggs
  • Â¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • 1000 g water, plus extra hot water for soaking
  • 1 lemon, finely grated zest only

Preparation

  1. Weigh chicken into a small bowl then drizzle with 10 g soy sauce and marinate for 30 minutes.
  2. Cover chicken with water and leave for 5 minutes, then drain, pat dry and set aside.
  3. Place carrots, mushroom, nuts and parsley in mixing bowl then chop 2 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place dashi, remaining 20 g soy sauce, mirin, eggs and salt in mixing bowl then mix 15 sec/speed 4.
  5. Divide mushroom mixture between 4 ramekins, place marinated chicken slices on top then pour over egg mixture, leaving a little space on top.
  6. Cover ramekins with cling film and place in Varoma dish (see tip).
  7. Place water in mixing bowl, place covered Varoma into position and steam 30 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  8. Carefully remove chawanmushi and leave to cool slightly before removing cling film. Sprinkle with lemon zest and garnish with mushroom slices to serve.

Useful Items

ramekins, cling film

Hints & Tricks

  • Subtitute small prawns or various vegetables for the chicken.
  • Use vegetable stock instead of dashi.
  • Dried shiitake mushrooms can be used if soaked in water overnight.
  • If using larger ramekins, the Varoma tray may also need to be used.
