Bulgogi (Pan-fried Marinated Beef)

Ingredients

Marinated Beef

  • 550 - 600 g sirloin steak, thinly sliced (1 cm) against the grain (see tip)
  • 100 g carrots, thinly sliced (5 mm) or julienned
  • 50 g onions, thinly sliced (5 mm)
  • 40 g spring onions, cut in 3 cm pieces then halved lengthways
  • 8 garlic cloves
  • 1 kiwi fruit, peeled, quartered
  • 35 g soft brown sugar
  • 60 g dark soy sauce
  • 30 g sesame oil
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper

Rice and Assembly

  • 1000 g water
  • 1 ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 300 g white long grain rice
  • sesame seeds, for garnishing
  • 2 spring onions, thinly sliced (5 mm), for garnishing
  • kimchi, for serving

Preparation

Marinated Beef

  1. Place a large bowl on mixing bowl lid, weigh in steak, carrots, onions and spring onions then set aside.
  2. Place garlic in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then chop again 5 sec/speed 5.
  3. Add kiwi and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add sugar, soy sauce, oil and pepper then mix 10 sec/speed 3. Transfer to bowl with beef and, using hands, massage marinade into beef. Cover with cling film and place in fridge to marinate for 30 minutes. Meanwhile, cook rice.

Rice and Assembly

  1. Place water and salt in mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket and weigh in rice. Rinse 5 sec/speed 7 then steam 18-20 min/100°C/speed 4 until cooked. Transfer to a bowl and keep warm.
  2. Heat a large frying pan or wok over a medium-high heat. Fry reserved marinated beef and vegetables in batches for 2-3 minutes until cooked. Transfer to a plate and repeat until all beef is cooked.
  3. Garnish beef with sesame seeds and spring onions then serve with reserved steamed rice and kimchi.

Useful Items

large bowl, cling film, large frying pan, hob, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • To slice steak, find the direction of the grain (the lines of muscle fibre) and slice across the grain rather than parallel to it.
