Glazed chicken with hasselback pumpkin

Ingredients

  • 130 g balsamic glaze

Maple butter

  • 50 g pure maple syrup
  • 30 g unsalted butter
  • 1 tsp onion salt
  • 2 tsp cumin seeds

Herb butter

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 4 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 2 tsp onion salt
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 70 g unsalted butter

Chicken and pumpkin

  • 1500 - 1800 g whole chicken
  • 1 lemon, well washed
  • 1000 g water
  • 1 butternut pumpkin, peeled, cut into halves lengthways and deseeded
  • 1 - 2 bunches broccolini, trimmed and cut into halves

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh 100 g of the balsamic glaze into it and set aside.

Maple butter

  1. Place syrup, butter, onion salt and cumin seeds into mixing bowl and melt 3 min/Varoma/speed 1. Transfer into a separate bowl and set aside. Do not clean mixing bowl.

Herb butter

  1. Place garlic, parsley, onion salt and cumin seeds into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add butter and combine 3 sec/speed 5.

Chicken and pumpkin

  1. Place chicken onto a clean work bench and insert whole lemon into cavity. Using your fingers, release skin from flesh around the breasts and fill with reserved Herb butter.
  2. Without cleaning mixing bowl, place water into it. Place chicken into Varoma dish and place Varoma into position. Brush chicken with half of the reserved balsamic glaze. Secure Varoma lid and cook 20 min/Varoma/speed 1. Meanwhile, preheat oven to 180°C. Line a baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper.
  3. Using a sharp knife, cut slits into pumpkin, taking care not to cut all the way through (cuts should be approx. 5 mm apart - see Tips). Place pumpkin onto prepared tray and brush pumpkin with the reserved Maple butter. Bake for 30-40 minutes (180°C) or until pumpkin is cooked and golden (see Tips).
  4. Carefully remove Varoma lid and re-glaze chicken with remaining reserved balsamic glaze. Secure Varoma lid and cook 30-40 min/Varoma/speed 1 or until chicken is just cooked.
  5. Add broccolini to Varoma dish around chicken, secure Varoma lid and steam 3 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  6. Set Varoma aside for chicken to rest. Transfer stock from mixing bowl into a jug and rinse mixing bowl and lid. Strain 200 g of the reserved stock back into mixing bowl. Add remaining 30 g balsamic glaze and cook 10 min/100°C/speed 1, without measuring cup. Serve chicken with pumpkin, broccolini and balsamic sauce.

Useful Items

bowl, baking tray (30 x 40 cm), pastry brush, long sharp knife, jug, baking paper, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • When slicing pumpkin, place the handle of a wooden spoon alongside the pumpkin to act as a guard against cutting all the way through it.
  • While chicken is cooking, prepare pumpkin and place into oven.
