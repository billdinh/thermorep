Tarragon Chicken Quiche

Ingredients

  • 5 oz aged white cheddar cheese, cut into pieces

Pastry

  • 3 oz yellow cornmeal
  • 7 ½ oz all-purpose flour, plus extra to dust
  • 3 ½ oz white wine
  • 2 ½ oz extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 pinch salt

Filling

  • 1 lemon, thin peel only, no pith
  • 1 tsp dried tarragon
  • ½ tsp dried thyme
  • ¾ tsp salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 3 ½ oz shallots, halved
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 12 oz chicken thighs, boneless and skinless, cut into 1 in. pieces
  • 2 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 3 large eggs
  • 8 oz cream

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven 350°F. Place cheese into mixing bowl and grate 7 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.

Pastry

  1. Place cornmeal into mixing bowl and grind 3 sec/speed 7. Add flour, white wine, olive oil and salt and mix 10 sec/speed 4.
  2. Transfer pastry to a lightly floured surface, roll into a circle (approx. ¼ in. thick) with a rolling pin and place into a tart pan. Line pastry with parchment and pie weights and bake for 20 minutes (350°F). Remove the parchment and pie weights and bake for a further 5 minutes. Set aside.

Filling

  1. Place lemon peel, tarragon, thyme, salt and pepper into mixing bowl and grind 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and grind a further 3 sec/speed 7.
  2. Add shallots and garlic and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Add chicken and mustard and cook 6 min/250°F//speed 1, then shred 6 sec//speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place eggs and cream into mixing bowl and mix 30 sec/speed 1.
  4. Sprinkle half the reserved grated cheese over the precooked tart shell. Add chicken mixture, cover with egg mixture and sprinkle remaining cheese on top. Bake for 35-40 minutes (350°F) until filling is set and top is golden brown. Serve warm or cold.

Useful Items

rolling pin, tart pan (approx. 10 in.), parchment paper, pie weights, oven
