Risoni with salmon and spinach

Ingredients

  • 200 g fresh baby spinach leaves
  • 100 g cherry tomatoes, cut into halves (optional)
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 brown onion, cut into halves (approx. 120 g)
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 600 g water
  • 1 tbsp Vegetable stock paste (see Tips)
  • 3 fresh boneless, skinless salmon fillets (approx.150 g each)
  • ¼ tsp salt, plus extra to season
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper, plus extra to season
  • 170 g dried risoni pasta
  • 200 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 2 tsp cornflour
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 sprigs fresh basil, leaves only
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges, for serving (optional)

Preparation

  1. Place a large bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh spinach and cherry tomatoes (optional) into it. Set aside.
  2. Place garlic, onion, paprika and oil into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and cook 3 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  4. Add water and stock paste to mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position, place salmon fillets into Varoma and season with salt and pepper. Secure Varoma lid and steam 8-10 min/Varoma/speed 1 or until fish is cooked to your liking. Remove Varoma and transfer salmon into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and cover to keep warm.
  5. Add risoni to mixing bowl and mix with aid of spatula, then cook 5 min/100°C//speed 1.
  6. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid, weigh cream and cornflour into it and lightly whisk together.
  7. Add cream-cornflour mixture, lemon juice, salt and pepper to mixing bowl and heat 2 min/100°C//speed 1.
  8. Add reserved spinach and tomatoes and basil leaves and stir to combine with aid of spatula. Allow to rest for 2 minutes in mixing bowl. Flake reserved salmon into bite-sized pieces. Transfer risoni onto a serving platter and top with flaked salmon. Serve with lemon wedges (optional).
