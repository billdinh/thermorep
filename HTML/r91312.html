Asian-Style Rice with Eggs and Vegetables

Ingredients

  • 1 oz light sesame oil
  • 3 ½ oz yellow onion, halved
  • 5 ½ oz carrots, cut into large pieces (1¾ in.)
  • 5 ½ oz bacon, cubed, or 5.5 oz Canadian bacon, cubed (see Tip)
  • 1 oz soy sauce, to taste
  • 2 tsp sugar (optional)
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 heaping tsp homemade vegetable stock paste (optional)
  • 12 ½ oz white short-grain rice (18-20 min cooking time)
  • 43 oz water
  • 14 oz mixed vegetables, e.g. broccoli in small florets, frozen peas, cauliflower in small florets, green beans cut into pieces (1 in.), zucchini, cubed (½ in.)

Preparation

  1. Place oil and onions into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5.
  2. Add carrots and chop 2 sec/speed 4.5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add bacon, soy sauce and sugar and without measuring cup sauté 7 min/250°F//speed . Transfer into a large serving bowl, cover and set aside. Meanwhile, line Varoma tray with damp parchment (see Tip) and set aside. Whisk 4 eggs in a small bowl and set aside.
  4. Place stock paste into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket and weigh in rice. Add water over rice into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position, weigh vegetables into Varoma dish. Insert Varoma tray and add reserved eggs. Secure Varoma lid and steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 2 until eggs are cooked to your preference.
  5. Carefully remove Varoma, remove simmering basket with spatula and transfer rice and vegetables into serving bowl with reserved bacon mixture. Use spatula to cut eggs into squares (¾-1¼ in.) in the Varoma tray and transfer into serving bowl. Combine all ingredients in serving bowl with spatula and serve hot.

Useful Items

parchment paper, large serving bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • Use any type of rice, making sure to adapt cooking time according to package instructions. For instance for basmati rice, reduce steaming time as per package instructions.
  • Add chopped herbs, green onions or nuts before serving.
  • Buy Canadian bacon in a whole piece and cut into small cubes for frying, alternatively use ham steaks or cook bacon and drain off excess fat before making recipe.
  • Place vegetables requiring longest cooking time first in the Varoma dish and add the others a few minutes later.
  • Cut parchment to fit Varoma tray. Tear off a piece of parchment wide enough to cover Varoma tray. Place Varoma tray on top of parchment and cut around the tray to form an oval shape. Rinse parchment under running water and squeeze to remove excess moisture before placing into Varoma tray.
  • If one ingredient isn’t fully cooked, transfer the others to the serving bowl and continue to steam remaining ingredient for a few minutes/Varoma/speed 2 before combining with ingredients in serving bowl.
