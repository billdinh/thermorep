Cheese Sticks

Ingredients

  • 200 g Edam cheese, cut in pieces
  • 250 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 200 g butter, soft
  • 1 medium egg
  • coarse salt, as desired, for sprinkling
  • cumin seeds, as desired, for sprinkling
  • sesame seeds, as desired, for sprinkling
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
443 kJ / 106 kcal
Protein
2.8 g
Carbohydrates

6.9 g

Preparation

  1. Place cheese in mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 7.
  2. Add flour, butter and egg then mix 20 sec/speed 5.
  3. Chill dough in fridge for 30 minutes. Meanwhile, preheat oven to 180°C and line a baking tray with baking paper.
  4. Roll out dough on a floured surface to a thickness of 5 mm. Cut strips (approx. 10 cm x 1.5 cm) with a fluted pastry wheel or long sharp knife and place on prepared baking tray.
  5. Sprinkle strips with coarse salt, cumin, sesame seeds, according to taste.
  6. Bake for 15-20 minutes (180°C) until golden. Transfer to a wire rack to cool before serving.

Useful Items

baking tray and paper, long sharp knife, pastry wheel or cutter, rolling pin, wire rack, refrigerator, oven
