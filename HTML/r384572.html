Rice with Duck

Ingredients

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 120 g onions, quartered
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 2 chicken stock cubes (for 0.5 l each)
  • 1 duck, small (up to 1300 g), meat cut in small pieces
  • 100 g chorizo, sliced
  • 100 g bacon, diced
  • 200 g white wine
  • water, up to 1000 g
  • 350 g long grain rice
  • fresh parsley leaves, chopped

Preparation

  1. Place garlic, onions and oil in mixing bowl, chop 5 sec/speed 5 then sauté 5 min/120°C/speed 1.
  2. Add chicken stock, duck, 50 g chorizo, bacon and wine then cook 30 min/100°C//speed . Remove duck meat and set aside, reserving sauce in a jug.
  3. Preheat oven to 200°C.
  4. Weigh reserved sauce into mixing bowl and add water up to 1000 g. Insert simmering basket and weigh in rice. Mix well with spatula and cook 20 min/100°C/speed 4. Transfer rice to a baking dish, mix in reserved duck meat and garnish with remaining 50 g ​​chorizo. Bake for 12-15 minutes (200°C) until browned.
  5. Garnish with parsley before serving hot.

Useful Items

baking dish, jug, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Substitute duck with boneless chicken thighs.
