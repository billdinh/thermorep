Quick Thai chicken curry

Ingredients

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 50 - 100 g onions, to taste
  • ½ - 1 tsp salt, to taste
  • 30 g oil
  • 50 - 100 g Thai red curry paste, to taste
  • 200 g coconut milk
  • 200 g water
  • 400 g waxy potatoes, peeled, cut in bite-size pieces
  • 100 g carrots, cut in bite-size pieces
  • 15 g fish sauce
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 2 - 3 dried kaffir lime leaves
  • 600 g chicken breasts, skinless, cut in cubes (3 cm)
  • 150 g frozen green peas
  • 2 - 3 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only, to garnish
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1914 kJ / 459 kcal
Protein
42 g
Carbohydrates
27 g
Fat
20 g

Preparation

  1. Place garlic clove, onions, salt and oil in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5.
  2. Add Thai curry paste and sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  3. Add coconut milk, water, potatoes, carrots, fish sauce, sugar and kaffir lime leaves and cook 12 min/100°C//speed 1
  4. Add chicken and frozen green peas and cook 8 min/100°C//speed 1. Taste and adjust seasoning then garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.
