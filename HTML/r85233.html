Beetroot Houmous

Ingredients

  • 75 g walnut halves or pieces
  • 20 g sesame seeds
  • 300 g cooked beetroot, peeled, cut in pieces (3 cm)
  • 85 g cream cheese
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 35 g bread, stale, roughly torn
  • &frac18; tsp ground cumin
  • 15 g lemon juice
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt, or to taste
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper, or to taste
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil, for drizzling

Preparation

  1. Place walnuts and sesame seeds in mixing bowl and toast 10 min/120°C/speed 1 then grind 12 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Blend 8 sec/speed 8, scrape down sides of mixing bowl then blend again 8 sec/speed 8.
  3. Add beetroot, cream cheese, garlic , bread, ground cumin, lemon juice, salt and pepper then mix 15 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then mix again 15 sec/speed 8.
  4. Check and adjust seasoning then transfer to a bowl, drizzle with olive oil and serve.
