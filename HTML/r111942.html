White Chocolate Mini Cheesecakes

Ingredients

Cheesecake

  • 150 g digestive biscuits
  • 50 g unsalted butter, soft
  • 3 gelatine leaves
  • 100 g white chocolate, small pieces or callets
  • 200 g double cream
  • 300 g cream cheese
  • 40 g caster sugar
  • 30 g vanilla sugar

Decoration

  • 150 g fresh raspberries
  • icing sugar, to garnish
Nutrition

Preparation

Cheesecake

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C.
  2. Place digestives in mixing bowl and crush 10 sec/speed 7.
  3. Add butter and mix 2 min/50°C/speed 2. Divide biscuit mixture between 12 individual pudding basins (Ø 7 cm) and flatten with back of a spoon, forming a firm layer. Bake for 15 minutes (160°C).
  4. Remove from oven and set aside to cool. Meanwhile, soak gelatine leaves in a bowl of cold water for 5 minutes and prepare cheese layer.
  5. Place white chocolate in mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  6. Add cream and melt 4 min/90°C/speed 2.
  7. Squeeze out excess water from softened gelatine leaves and add to mixing bowl then stir 10 sec/speed 4.
  8. Add cream cheese, sugar and vanilla sugar then blend 15 sec/speed 6. Spoon mixture over reserved biscuit bases then place in fridge to set for at least 4 hours or overnight.

Decoration

  1. To serve, run a knife around edge of cheesecakes to loosen and invert. Garnish with 3 raspberries on top of each cheesecake and sprinkle with icing sugar.

Useful Items

individual pudding basins, oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Make one large cheesecake if preferred. Grease sides and line base of a spring form tin (Ø 22 cm) then cook base for 20 minutes at 160°C. Slice in portions to serve.
