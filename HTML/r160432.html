Marshmallows

Ingredients

Confectioners Sugar and Corn Starch Mixture

  • 3 oz sugar
  • 1 oz corn starch

Marshmallows

  • unsalted butter, to grease
  • 1 oz unflavored gelatin powder (approx. 4 envelopes)
  • 9 oz cold water, divided
  • 11 oz sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste

Preparation

Confectioners Sugar and Corn Starch Mixture

  1. Place sugar and corn starch into mixing bowl and blend 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer to an airtight container and set aside.

Marshmallows

  1. Grease and line a rectangular baking pan (approx. 10 in. x 8 in.) with parchment paper allowing a 1 inch overhang and set aside.
  2. Place a glass bowl onto mixing bowl lid, weigh in gelatin and 5 oz cold water and stir until mixed and set aside.
  3. Place sugar into mixing bowl and grind 5 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add remaining 4 oz water and cook 15 min/212째F/speed 2.
  5. Add reserved gelatin mixture and heat 2 min/212째F/speed 2. Allow mixture to cool in mixing bowl until temperature falls below 115째F (approx. 30 minutes).
  6. Insert butterfly whisk and whip 7 min/speed 4.
  7. Add vanilla and mix 10 sec/speed 3. Remove butterfly whisk.
  8. Transfer mixture into prepared pan, cover with cling wrap and allow to set at room temperature (approx. 1 hour). Once set, unmold marshmallow onto a cutting board. Remove parchment and lightly dust a sharp knife with confectioners sugar mixture. Cut marshmallow into squares and coat all sides of each marshmallow in the confectioners and corn starch mixture. Serve or place marshmallows in an airtight container with remaining confectioners sugar mixture and store in the refrigerator.

Hints & Tricks

  • Use a pizza wheel dusted with the confectioners sugar mixture to cut marshmallows.
  • Using a good quality gelatin such as gelatin sheets or Porcine gelatin will give you lighter marshmallows.
  • Use your favorite freeze dried fruit for flavoring marshmallows. Place your choice of freeze dried fruit into mixing bowl and grind to a powder 15 sec/speed 10. Add (approx. 1 oz) powdered fruit when adding the vanilla. Amount will depend on fruit and flavor you desire.
  • Orange oil and peppermint oil can also be added for flavor.
  • Marshmallows can also be colored with food coloring. Add food coloring until desired color is achieved after inserting butterfly whisk and beat with cooled gelatin mixture.
  • Replace confectioners sugar mixture with toasted coconut to coat marshmallows. Sprinkle coconut on a baking sheet and bake for 10-15 minutes (350째F), stirring coconut every 5 minutes to ensure even baking. Alternatively, toast in a large heavy based frying pan, shaking pan continually so coconut does not burn. Roll marshmallow cubes in slightly cooled toasted coconut.
