Chicken Enchiladas

Ingredients

Chicken

  • 700 g water
  • 800 g chicken breasts, cut in strips (5 cm x 1 cm)
  • fine sea salt, to taste
  • ground black pepper, to taste
  • olive oil, for greasing

Filling

  • 150 g Cheddar cheese, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 100 g mozzarella cheese, drained, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 50 g spring onions, cut in pieces (5 cm)
  • 5 g fresh coriander leaves
  • 100 g red peppers, quartered
  • 50 g tomatoes, quartered
  • 50 g red onions, quartered
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 fresh red chilli, halved, deseeded if desired
  • 250 g sour cream
  • 40 g lime juice
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp chipotle paste
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper

Assembly

  • 8 flour tortillas
  • 400 g tinned chopped tomatoes

Preparation

Chicken

  1. Place water in mixing bowl, place Varoma dish into position and weigh in chicken. Divide chicken between Varoma dish and Varoma tray, leaving some holes unobstructed for steam to circulate, then insert Varoma tray. Cover Varoma and steam 8 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  2. Stir chicken pieces with spatula to ensure even cooking, then cover Varoma and steam again 8 min/Varoma/speed 1. Transfer chicken to a large bowl, season well then set aside. Discard cooking water then rinse and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Preheat oven to 200°C and lightly grease a baking dish (32 cm x 25 cm).

Filling

  1. Place Cheddar and mozzarella in mixing bowl then grate 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place spring onions and coriander in mixing bowl then chop 2 sec/speed 6. Transfer to another bowl and set aside.
  3. Place peppers, tomatoes, onions, garlic and chilli in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add sour cream, 100 g reserved grated cheese, lime juice, reserved cooked chicken, half reserved spring onion mixture, paprika, chipotle paste, salt and pepper then mix 10 sec//speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.

Assembly

  1. Divide filling evenly between tortillas, then roll up and place seam-side down in prepared dish. Top with tinned tomatoes and remaining grated cheese, then sprinkle over remaining spring onion mixture.
  2. Bake for 20-25 minutes (200°C) until bubbling and golden. Serve warm.

Useful Items

baking dish (32 cm x 25 cm), large bowl, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Chicken enchiladas are a Tex-Mex family favourite that can turn any evening into a fiesta. Steam the chicken in the Varoma to make it wonderfully tender, then mix with the rest of the ingredients to create a delicious, easy filling. Serve these moreish enchiladas with sliced avocado and extra sour cream on the side, accompanied by a side salad.
  • Serves 6 adults or 8 children.
