Wholemeal Focaccia

Ingredients

  • 500 g wholemeal bread flour
  • 330 g water
  • 60 g extra virgin olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 2 ½ tsp dried instant yeast
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 2 sprigs rosemary, needles only
  • 1 - 1 ½ tsp coarse salt, to taste

Preparation

  1. Place flour, 310 g water, 40 g oil, yeast, salt and rosemary in mixing bowl then knead 4 min/. Meanwhile, grease a large bowl with oil. Transfer dough to prepared bowl, cover with cling film then set aside to rise in a warm place until doubled in size (approx. 1-2 hours). Meanwhile, line 2 baking trays with baking paper then clean and dry mixing bowl.
  2. Once doubled in size, divide dough in two and place each on a prepared baking tray. Use fingers to spread each piece of dough out to approx. 30 cm x 15 cm (see tip) then cover with cling film and leave to rise in a warm place for 30 minutes. Towards end of rising time, preheat oven to 220°C.
  3. Place remaining 20 g oil and remaining 20 g water in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 4. Divide between rested doughs then spread out and sprinkle with coarse salt. Bake for 15-20 minutes (220°C) until golden brown. Cut into slices and serve warm.

Useful Items

large bowl, cling film, 2 baking trays and baking paper, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • If dough is sticking to fingers whilst spreading it out onto the tray, lightly grease your hands to make it easier.
