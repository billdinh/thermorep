Danish Dream Cake

Ingredients

Sponge

  • 100 g unsalted butter, diced, plus extra for greasing
  • 3 large eggs
  • 250 g caster sugar
  • 100 g milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 250 g plain flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt

Topping

  • 125 g unsalted butter, diced
  • 200 g light brown sugar
  • 50 g milk
  • 100 g desiccated coconut

Preparation

Sponge

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C. Grease and line base of a rectangular baking tin (approx. 30 cm x 20 cm x 5 cm).
  2. Place butter in mixing bowl and melt 2 min/80°C/speed 1. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and, if necessary, melt again 30 sec/80°C/speed 1. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Insert butterfly whisk. Place eggs and sugar in mixing bowl then whisk 5 min/speed 3.
  4. Add milk, reserved melted butter and vanilla extract then mix 4 sec/speed 3.
  5. Add flour, baking powder and salt then mix 20 sec/speed 3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then mix again 10 sec/speed 3. Remove butterfly whisk. Transfer to prepared tin then bake for 22-25 minutes (190°C) until golden. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl and make topping.

Topping

  1. Place butter in mixing bowl and melt 2 min/80°C/speed 1. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and, if necessary, melt again 30 sec/80°C/speed 1.
  2. Add sugar and milk then heat 3 min/100°C/speed 2.
  3. Add coconut then mix 10 sec/speed 3. After sponge has been cooking for 22-25 minutes, pour over topping. Increase oven temperature to 200°C then bake for a further 10 minutes (200°C).
  4. Leave to cool in tin for 5 minutes before removing. Cool completely on a wire rack before serving in slices.

Useful Items

wire rack, rectangular baking tin (approx. 30 cm x 20 cm x 5 cm), baking paper, oven
