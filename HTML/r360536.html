Sweet and Spicy Beef Short Ribs with Polenta

Ingredients

Spicy Rub

  • 2 tbsp brown sugar, packed
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 ½ tsp onion powder
  • ½ tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black peppercorns
  • ½ tsp granulated garlic
  • ½ tsp ground cumin
  • ¼ tsp ground cayenne pepper
  • &frac18; tsp ground cloves
  • 64 oz beef short ribs, bone in

Sweet BBQ Sauce

  • 4 oz red onions, quartered
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 7 oz ketchup
  • 4 oz dark brown sugar
  • 2 oz pineapple juice
  • 2 oz molasses
  • 1 ½ tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 ½ tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • ½ tsp salt, to taste
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • ¼ tsp ground cayenne pepper
  • 40 oz water

Smoked Gouda Polenta

  • 5 oz smoked Gouda cheese, cubed
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 ½ oz unsalted butter, cubed
  • 30 oz water
  • 4 ½ oz polenta
  • 1 tsp salt, to taste
  • freshly ground black pepper, to garnish
  • green onions, julienned, to garnish

Preparation

Spicy Rub

  1. Place brown sugar, chili powder, onion powder, thyme, paprika, salt, peppercorns, garlic, cumin, cayenne and cloves into mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer to a large bowl.
  2. Place bowl with seasoning rub onto mixing bowl lid, weigh in short ribs and firmly rub seasoning into ribs to pack and coat completely. Cover with cling wrap and refrigerate for 1 hour or overnight.

Sweet BBQ Sauce

  1. Place onions and garlic into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add ketchup, brown sugar, pineapple juice, molasses, Worcestershire, Dijon, paprika, salt, pepper and cayenne and cook 10 min/212°F/speed 1, then blend 40 sec/speed 4-8, increasing speed gradually. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place water to mixing bowl. Set Varoma dish into position and line with parchment. Transfer rubbed short ribs onto parchment and fold loosley to form a parcel, secure Varoma lid and steam 75 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  4. Preheat oven to 275°F. Line a baking sheet with foil and set aside.
  5. Transfer steamed short ribs to prepared baking sheet. Brush with a third of the reserved BBQ sauce and bake for 90 minutes (275°F), basting twice more every 30 minutes with remaining BBQ sauce. Meanwhile approximately one hour into baking, make the Polenta.

Smoked Gouda Polenta

  1. Place Gouda cheese into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place garlic and 0.5 oz butter into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and sauté 3 min/250°F/speed .
  3. Add water and boil 10 min/212°F/speed 2. Add polenta and salt and cook 5 min/212°F/speed 2.
  4. Add 2 oz butter and reserved grated Gouda and mix 10 sec/speed 4. Garnish Smoked Gouda Polenta and Sweet and Spicy Beef Short Ribs with black pepper and green onions and serve immediately.

Useful Items

cling wrap, baking sheet, aluminum foil, parchment paper, basting brush, large bowl, refrigerator, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with steamed vegetables.
  • Prepare polenta just before serving as it firms while resting. Firm polenta is great sliced and grilled or lightly pan fried.
  • To curl green onions for garnish: thinly slice onions and place in ice water.
