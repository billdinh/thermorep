Peanut butter parfait, chocolate bark and raspberries

Ingredients

Peanut praline

  • 200 g roasted unsalted peanuts
  • 20 g water
  • 150 g caster sugar
  • 1 tsp salt flakes

Peanut butter parfait

  • 6 egg yolks
  • 120 g full cream milk
  • 400 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 500 g chilled water
  • 100 g ice

Chocolate bark

  • 60 g white chocolate, broken into pieces
  • 110 g dark chocolate, broken into pieces

Raspberry sauce

  • 400 g fresh raspberries
  • 20 g lemon juice
  • 30 g sugar (optional)

Preparation

Peanut praline

  1. Line a baking tray (20 x 30 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place peanuts into mixing bowl and crush 2 sec/speed 6.
  3. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh water and sugar into it. Transfer into a small saucepan and place over high heat. Cook, stirring frequently, for 4-5 minutes or until dark golden in colour. Remove from heat and stir through crushed peanuts and salt. Immediately pour mixture onto prepared tray and set aside to cool.

Peanut butter parfait

  1. Place 250 g of the Peanut praline into mixing bowl and crush 30 sec/speed 7. Place remaining praline into a sealable container and set aside.
  2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix 20 sec/speed 5, or until smooth.
  3. Add egg yolks, milk and cream and cook 9 min/80°C/speed 4.
  4. Place a large bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh chilled water and ice into it. Place an empty bowl into iced water and transfer mixture into empty bowl. Allow to cool, stirring occasionally, ensuring no water spills into mixture. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  5. Transfer parfait mixture into a freezable container and place into freezer for approx. 8 hours or until set, stirring with a fork every few hours.

Chocolate bark

  1. Place white chocolate into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 8, then heat 5 min/50°C/speed 2.
  2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and heat 1 min/50°C/speed 2, until melted. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Without cleaning mixing bowl, place dark chocolate into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 8, then heat 5 min/50°C/speed 2.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and heat 1 min/50°C/speed 2, until melted.
  5. Pour dark chocolate onto a silicone bread mat or acetate sheet and spread out thinly (approx. 2 mm) using spatula. Drizzle white chocolate over dark chocolate and use a toothpick or skewer to swirl through. Set aside or place into refrigerator to set. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Raspberry sauce

  1. Place 200 g of the raspberries, lemon juice and sugar (optional) into mixing bowl and cook 5 min/90°C/speed 2.
  2. Strain mixture through a fine-mesh sieve into a jug, discarding seeds.
  3. Cut Peanut butter parfait into thick slices. Peel chocolate from silicone bread mat, break into shards and serve with raspberry sauce, remaining raspberries and reserved praline.

Useful Items

baking tray (30 x 40 cm), baking paper, saucepan, bowl, sealable container, large bowl, freezable storage container, toothpick or wooden skewer, silicone bread mat, jug, fine mesh sieve, stove top, freezer, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • The parfait, chocolate and sauce can be made ahead of time and the dessert assembled at the last minute.
  • Use 200 g frozen berries to make sauce, if fresh are not available. You can use whatever fruit is in season, such as cherries or strawberries.
