White Christmas

Ingredients

  • butter, for greasing
  • 40 g roasted unsalted macadamia nuts
  • 30 g roasted unsalted pistachio nuts
  • 50 g dried apricots
  • 50 g glacé cherries
  • 500 g white chocolate, broken into pieces
  • 80 g Turkish delight, cut into cubes (1 cm)
  • 50 g marshmallow, cut into cubes (1 cm)
  • 20 g desiccated coconut
  • 80 g Rice Bubbles®

Preparation

  1. Grease a rectangular baking tin (26 x 16 x 3.5 cm). Line the base and sides of tin with baking paper, allowing a 5 cm overhang for easy removal later. Set baking tin aside.
  2. Place macadamias and pistachios into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 5. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place apricots and glacé cherries or dried cranberries into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Transfer into bowl with the nuts and set aside.
  4. Place chocolate into mixing bowl and grate 4 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then melt 3 min/37°C/speed 1. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then melt for a further 4 min/37°C/speed 1. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula again and melt for a further 2-4 min/37°C/speed 1, or until chocolate has fully melted.
  5. Add Turkish delight pieces, marshmallows pieces, desiccated coconut and reserved apricot and nut mixture and combine 15 sec//speed 2, or until combined.
  6. Place a large bowl onto mixing bowl lid, then weigh Rice Bubbles® into it. Transfer white chocolate mixture into large bowl with the Rice Bubbles® and combine until well-coated.
  7. Pour mixture into prepared baking tin and press down firmly to smooth and compact. Place tin into refrigerator to set for 2 hours or until firm. Once set, cut into approx. 20 pieces and serve or transfer into a sealable container and store in refrigerator until ready to use.

Useful Items

baking paper, large bowl, sealable storage container, rectangular baking tin (26 x 16 x 3.5 cm), refrigerator
