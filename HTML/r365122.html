Chickpea Flatbread and Curried Vegetable Dip

Ingredients

Chickpea Flatbread

  • 280 g dried chickpeas
  • 200 g water
  • 50 g olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 Tbsp sesame seeds

Curried Vegetable Dip

  • 30 g olive oil
  • ½ tsp coriander seeds
  • ½ tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 100 g aubergine, diced (1 cm)
  • 100 g courgettes, diced (1 cm)
  • 80 g tinned chopped tomatoes
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 Tbsp fresh coriander leaves, roughly chopped

Preparation

Chickpea Flatbread

  1. Place chickpeas in mixing bowl and grind 40 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then grind again 40 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add water, oil and salt, mix 10 sec/speed 6 then knead 1 min/. Leave to soak for 15 minutes.
  3. Preheat oven to 200°C and line a baking tray with baking paper then grease with oil.
  4. Add baking powder to mixing bowl and knead 1 min/. Transfer to prepared tray and spread with oiled fingertips into a rectangle (28 cm x 22 cm). Sprinkle with sesame seeds and bake for 20 minutes (200°C) or until golden. Leave to cool for 5 minutes then cut in small rectangles. Clean mixing bowl and prepare curried vegetable dip.

Curried Vegetable Dip

  1. Place oil, coriander seeds and cumin seeds in mixing bowl then toast 1 min 30 sec/120°C/speed 1.
  2. Add curry powder, garlic powder and aubergine then sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  3. Add courgettes, tomatoes and salt, cook 5 min/100°C/speed 1 then chop 8 sec/speed 5.
  4. Serve chickpea flatbread with curried vegetable dip, garnished with fresh coriander leaves.

Useful Items

baking tray and baking paper, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Dips and breads are the perfect combination for a quick, healthy lunch, and both are so easy to make with a Thermomix®. Here, dried chickpeas are ground to make flour. Other flours can also be made thanks to the powerful motor and blade. Try with rice, quinoa, spelt or buckwheat.
  • For a creamy spread, replace 40 g tinned chopped tomatoes with 40 g coconut cream.
