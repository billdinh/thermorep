Cookie Dough Ice Cream Sundaes

Ingredients

Ice Cream

  • 600 g whole milk
  • 200 g double cream
  • 180 g caster sugar
  • 6 egg yolks, from medium eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla bean paste
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt (optional)

Cookie Dough

  • 60 g unsalted butter, soft
  • 90 g caster sugar
  • 70 g light brown sugar
  • 130 g plain flour
  • 50 g whole milk
  • ½ tsp vanilla bean paste
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 100 g milk chocolate chips

Assembly

  • 50 g unsalted peanuts
  • 250 g double cream
  • 125 g dark chocolate, small pieces or callets
  • 20 g golden syrup
  • 10 g unsalted butter
  • 8 glacé cherries

Preparation

Ice Cream

  1. Place milk, cream, sugar, egg yolks, vanilla and salt (if using) in mixing bowl then cook 8 min/90°C/speed 2.
  2. Pour mixture, up to 4 cm deep, in a shallow freezer-proof container then cover with a lid or cling film. Leave mixture to cool completely before freezing for at least 10 hours. Meanwhile, rinse mixing bowl and make cookie dough.

Cookie Dough

  1. Place butter, caster sugar and brown sugar in mixing bowl then mix 20 sec/speed 4.
  2. Add flour, milk, vanilla and salt then mix 10 sec/speed 4.
  3. Add chocolate chips and mix 20 sec//speed 2. Tip out and shape dough into a log, wrap in cling film then freeze for at least 1 hour or until needed.
  4. Once ice cream is solid, remove cookie dough from freezer, slice then cut each slice into 1 cm cubes. Set a few pieces of cookie dough aside for decoration.
  5. Place frozen ice cream mixture in fridge for 15 minutes, then cut into cubes (4 cm) with a sharp knife. Place half ice cream cubes in mixing bowl and crush 20 sec/speed 6 then, with aid of spatula, churn 10 sec/speed 4.
  6. Add half the cookie dough cubes and stir with spatula until combined. Return ice cream to freezer-proof container.
  7. Place remaining ice cream cubes in mixing bowl and crush 20 sec/speed 6 then, with aid of spatula, churn 10 sec/speed 4.
  8. Add remaining cookie dough cubes and stir with spatula until combined. Return ice cream to freezer-proof container and freeze until needed. Clean mixing bowl.

Assembly

  1. Place peanuts in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Rinse mixing bowl.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Place 200 g cream in mixing bowl and whip without setting a time/speed 3 until softly whipped, watching carefully to avoid over-whipping. Remove butterfly whisk. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place chocolate in mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add remaining 50 g cream and melt 2 min/50°C/speed 2.
  5. Add golden syrup and butter then mix 30 sec/70°C/speed 3.
  6. Place 2-3 scoops of ice cream in 8 sundae glasses. Top each with 2 Tbsp chocolate sauce and 1-2 Tbsp whipped cream, piped if desired. Sprinkle with reserved chopped nuts then finish with a glacé cherry and reserved cookie dough pieces. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

cling film, long sharp knife, sundae glasses, freezer-proof container, freezer, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • What’s the only thing better than ice cream? Ice cream sundaes! We’ve put a fun twist on the classic by swirling homemade cookie dough into rich vanilla ice cream. Get the kids to help you layer ice cream, warm chocolate sauce and whipped cream into retro sundae glasses, and don’t forget the cherry on top!
