Cracked wheat and tomato kibbeh

Ingredients

Preparation

Kibbeh

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh burghul into it. Cover with hot (not boiling) water and set aside for 30 minutes or until soft. Using a fine-mesh sieve, strain burghul. Place strained burghul into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 6.5.
  2. Add tomato and onion and chop 5 sec/speed 4.
  3. Add remaining kibbeh ingredients and mix 5 sec/speed 6. Transfer into a bowl, cover with plastic wrap and place into refrigerator until ready to use.

Assembly

  1. Half fill a bowl with iced water. Place radish, beetroot, pickled onion and rocket into the bowl and set aside to crisp for 10 minutes. Using the simmering basket, drain vegetables, then transfer onto paper towel to drain further.
  2. Transfer burghul mixture onto a large serving plate and top with yoghurt and sliced vegetables. Drizzle with oil just before serving.

Useful Items

bowl, fine-mesh sieve, paper towel, serving plate, plastic wrap, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • An heirloom (candy stripe) beetroot, or Chioggia beetroot, is a variety that has both pink and white flesh inside. When sliced, you can clearly see the alternating rings of colour. It has a similar earthy aroma, but a sweeter flavour than standard beetroot.
