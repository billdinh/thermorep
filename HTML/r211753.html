Beef Teriyaki with Rice

Ingredients

Marinated Beef

  • 2 tsp honey
  • 70 g teriyaki sauce
  • 20 g sesame oil
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 30 g sake
  • 20 g lime juice
  • 20 g water
  • 1 tsp cornflour
  • 15 g fresh root ginger, peeled, cut in round slices (2 mm)
  • ½ - 1 fresh red chilli, deseeded, to taste
  • 200 g beef, cut in slices (1 mm)

Steamed Rice

  • 1000 g water
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 180 g short grain rice

Beef Teriyaki

  • 10 g sesame oil
  • 250 g sugar snap peas
  • 20 g water
  • 8 spring onions, sliced (3 mm)
  • fine sea salt, to taste
  • 1 tsp toasted sesame seeds, to garnish

Preparation

Marinated Beef

  1. Place ginger, honey, teriyaki sauce, sesame oil, sake, lime juice, water, cornflour, garlic and red chili in mixing bowl then chop 8 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl, add beef and stir to ensure all slices are coated then marinate for 1 hour in fridge. Clean mixing bowl.

Steamed Rice

  1. Place water and salt in mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket, weigh in rice then cook 18 min/100°C/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and cover to keep warm. Empty mixing bowl.

Beef Teriyaki

  1. Place sesame oil and peas in mixing bowl then stir-fry 5 min/120°C//speed 0.5.
  2. Add water and cook 3 min/120°C//speed . Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place marinated beef and marinade in mixing bowl then cook 5 min/120°C//speed 1. Stir beef with spatula to spread evenly in the bottom of mixing bowl.
  4. Add cooked sugar snap peas and spring onions then cook 2 min/100°C//speed 1. Season with salt to taste. Garish with sesame seeds and serve hot with rice.
