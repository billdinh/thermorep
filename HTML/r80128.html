Vanilla and honey toasted muesli

Ingredients

  • 1 cinnamon quill
  • 100 g almonds
  • 80 g pecan nuts or walnuts
  • 60 g dried apricots
  • 250 g rolled oats
  • ½ tsp ground cardamom
  • 70 g sunflower seeds
  • 70 g pepita seeds
  • 20 g sesame seeds
  • 20 g chia seeds
  • 100 g raisins
  • 40 g dried blueberries (optional)
  • 60 g dried cranberries
  • 60 g shredded coconut
  • 1 - 2 tsp natural vanilla extract
  • 130 g honey
  • 40 g grapeseed oil

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 150°C. Line 2 baking trays (40 x 35 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place cinnamon into mixing bowl and mill 1 min/speed 9.
  3. Add almonds and pecans or walnuts and chop Turbo/1 sec/2 times.
  4. Add apricots and chop 3 sec/speed 6.
  5. Add all remaining ingredients and mix 2 min/70°C//speed 2, with aid of spatula. Spread mixture evenly across both prepared baking trays, then toast in the oven for 10 minutes (150°C). Stir mixture on baking trays with spatula to ensure it is evenly toasted, then return to the oven for a further 10-15 minutes (150°C), until golden brown. Allow mix to cool, then transfer into a sealable container to store or serve immediately.

Useful Items

baking trays (40 x 35 cm), baking paper, sealable container, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Apple and cinnamon toasted muesli: omit the vanilla extract and add an additional cinnamon quill in step 2. In step 4, add 90 g pitted dates to the mixing bowl with the apricots and replace the honey with 130 g apple juice in step 5.
  • Maple and orange toasted muesli: add 1 orange, zest only, no white pith in step 4 with the apricots and replace the honey with maple syrup in step 5.
  • Serve toasted muesli with yoghurt, fresh fruit or Stewed rhubarb with apple and strawberries (see The Basic Cookbook).
