Scandinavian Rye Bread

Ingredients

  • 350 g water, plus extra for sprinkling
  • 30 g sugar
  • 2 tsp dried instant yeast
  • 300 g rye flour
  • 100 g plain flour
  • 300 g rye grains, cracked
  • 100 g sunflower seeds
  • 80 g pumpkin seeds
  • 50 g linseeds
  • 220 g buttermilk
  • 180 g dark beer
  • 2 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 Tbsp rolled oats, for sprinkling

Preparation

  1. Place water, sugar and yeast in mixing bowl then warm 2 min/37°C/speed 2. Leave to stand for 20 minutes.
  2. Add rye flour, plain flour, rye grains, sunflower seeds, pumpkin seeds, linseeds, buttermilk, beer and salt then, with aid of spatula, mix 30 sec/speed 4. Knead 5 min/.
  3. Transfer to a large bowl, cover with cling film and set aside at room temperature for 24 hours (see tip).
  4. After this time, preheat oven to 180°C and line a loaf tin (900 g/2 lb) with baking paper. Transfer rested batter to prepared tin. Sprinkle surface with water then scatter with oats. Bake for 1 hour 45 minutes (180°C).
  5. Remove from tin and pull away baking paper, then leave to cool completely on a wire rack before slicing and serving.

Useful Items

large bowl, cling film, loaf tin (900 g/2 lb), baking paper, wire rack, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • The finished loaf will be more sour the longer you leave it in step 4. Can be left for up to 48 hours.
  • Store in the fridge, or freeze until needed.
