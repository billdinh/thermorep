Black Bean Brownies

Ingredients

  • ½ tsp olive oil, plus extra to grease
  • 2 oz semi-sweet chocolate chips, chopped
  • 1 tbsp almond milk, unsweetened
  • 1 can low sodium black beans (approx. 15 oz), rinsed and drained
  • 2 large eggs
  • 5 ½ oz coconut sugar
  • 1 tsp balsamic vinegar
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 5 oz chocolate chips (optional)

per 1 piece

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease a square cake pan (8 in. x 8 in.) and line with parchment, letting edges hang over sides of pan (excess parchment will serve as handles to lift out the brownie).
  2. Place chocolate into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7.
  3. Add olive oil and almond milk and melt 1 min/160°F/speed 2.
  4. Add black beans and purée 20 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add eggs, sugar, balsamic vinegar, baking powder, baking soda, vanilla and salt and mix 30 sec/speed 5. Add chocolate chips and mix 10 sec//speed 4.
  6. Transfer into prepared pan, distributing batter evenly. Bake for 25-28 minutes (350°F) or until toothpick inserted comes out clean. Remove from cake pan using parchment to lift out the brownies. Allow to cool, cut brownies into squares and serve.

Useful Items

parchment paper, toothpick or skewer, square cake pan (8 in. x 8 in.), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • To keep this recipe gluten-free, ensure your baking powder is truly gluten-free.
  • Replace semi-sweet chocolate with 1 oz unsweetened cocoa and omit chopping chocolate. Add cocoa with olive oil and almond milk.
  • Replace almond milk with your favorite milk, e.g. soy, rice, coconut or cow's milk.
