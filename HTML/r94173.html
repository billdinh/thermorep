Best Beer Battered Fish

Ingredients

  • 400 g plain flour
  • 1200 - 1500 g sunflower oil
  • 3 tsp baking powder
  • 550 g ale, (sparkling) or lager, ice cold
  • 800 g cod fillets (4 fillets, each approx. 200 g)
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
2186 kJ / 523 kcal

44.1 g

1.7 g

Preparation

  1. Place a large food bag in mixing bowl and weigh flour into it. Place bag in freezer for 15 minutes.
  2. Heat oil to 180°C in a deep saucepan or deep fat fryer.
  3. Insert butterfly whisk. Place cold flour and baking powder in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 2.
  4. Add ale and whisk 2 min/speed 3 until smooth. Remove butterfly whisk. Batter should be used immediately; dip fish fillets into batter then carefully place in hot oil and deep-fry for 8-10 minutes (180°C) until golden and cooked through. Remove with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels. Serve hot; delicious with mushy peas, chips and lashings of vinegar.

Useful Items

large food bag, temperature probe, deep saucepan or deep fat fryer, slotted spoon, paper towel, freezer

Hints & Tricks

  • Lovely accompanied by minted mushy peas with crème fraîche and tartare sauce.
  • A windy trip to the British seaside…and a nice, hot, paper-wrapped parcel of steaming hot chips, topped with a big fillet of fish in the crispiest batter, drizzled in vinegar. Now you can create your very own version at home!
