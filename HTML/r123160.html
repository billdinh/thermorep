Strawberry chocolate calzones

Ingredients

Hazelnut chocolate spread

  • 150 g raw hazelnuts, skins on
  • 40 g raw cacao powder
  • 100 g pure maple syrup
  • 100 g milk

Calzones

  • 60 g olive oil, plus extra for greasing (see Tips)
  • 200 g lukewarm water
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp dried instant yeast
  • 150 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 150 g wholemeal plain flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 16 - 24 strawberries (approx. 250 g), hulled and cut into slices

Preparation

Hazelnut chocolate spread

  1. Place hazelnuts into mixing bowl and mill 35 sec/speed 9 or until finely ground. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add all remaining hazelnut chocolate spread ingredients and blend 30 sec/speed 4.
  3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and blend 1 min/speed 6 or until smooth. Transfer into a sealable jar or container and place into refrigerator until ready to use (spread will thicken slightly in refrigerator). Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Calzones

  1. Lightly grease a large bowl and set aside.
  2. Place water, sugar and yeast into mixing bowl and mix 20 sec/speed 2.
  3. Add flours, oil and salt and knead 2 min/. Transfer dough onto a lightly floured work surface or silicone bread mat and knead until dough is smooth and no longer sticky. Transfer into prepared bowl, cover with plastic wrap and set aside in a warm place to prove for 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  4. Preheat oven to 200°C. Lightly flour 2 large baking trays (30 x 40 cm) and set aside.
  5. Transfer dough onto a floured work surface or silicone bread mat and divide into 8 equal portions (approx. 60 g each). Roll each portion into an oval shape (approx. 16 x 13 cm). Spread 3 teaspoons of the reserved Hazelnut chocolate spread over half of each oval (to 1 cm from edge). Top with strawberry slices (approx. 2-3 strawberries per calzone). Fold over other half of calzone and press edges together, then twist edges to seal. Carefully transfer calzones onto prepared baking trays. Bake for 15 minutes (200°C), or until golden. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

sealable storage jar or container, large bowl, silicone bread mat, plastic wrap, 2 baking trays (30 x 40 cm), refrigerator, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • You can use homemade Hazelnut chocolate spread (see pg. 184) or store-bought for this recipe. Homemade spread is the much healthier option, as commercial spreads tend to contain mainly sugar and refined vegetable oil.
  • I recommend using a lighter-flavoured extra virgin olive oil as it's much milder.
