Vanilla cashew yoghurt

Ingredients

Soaking

  • 250 g raw cashews (see Tips)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • filtered water, for soaking

Yoghurt

  • 400 g filtered water
  • 1 tbsp vanilla bean paste
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 - 3 tbsp pure maple syrup
  • 2 dairy free probiotic capsules (contents only - see Tips)

Preparation

Soaking

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh cashews into it. Add salt, cover with water and set aside to soak for 3-6 hours.
  2. Using simmering basket, drain and rinse cashews.

Yoghurt

  1. Place water, vanilla, salt and drained cashews into mixing bowl and heat 5 min/100°C/speed 4, then blend 20 sec/speed 4-10, increasing speed gradually from speed 4 to speed 10, until mixture is completely smooth and silky. Allow to cool to 37°C (approx. 90 minutes).
  2. Preheat oven to lowest temperature setting (40°C) or preheat a dehydrator.
  3. Add syrup and the contents of probiotic capsules into mixing bowl and mix 1 min/speed 2. Transfer into warmed thermal serving bowl or other warmed large bowl (see Tips), cover and wrap in a kitchen towel. Place into oven or dehydrator. Turn off heat and leave undisturbed for 12-24 hours. Mixture will be ready when it looks foamy and is coming away from the sides of the bowl. It will also have a strong, but pleasant, tangy smell. Once ready, transfer into refrigerator for a minimum of 4 hours. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Place yoghurt into mixing bowl and blend 20 sec/speed 3. Transfer into a sterilised sealable jar, place into refrigerator and use as required (see Tips).

Useful Items

bowl, sterilised sealable jar, thermal serving bowl or large ceramic bowl, kitchen towel, dehydrator, oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • If you don't have time to soak/activate your cashews, soak nuts in boiling water for 30 minutes, then drain and use as directed.
  • Soaked nuts should be rinsed before use. Store them in a sealable container in the refrigerator and use within 2 days.
  • Probiotics are live micro-organisms that can support digestive health and immune function, including improving resilience to infections and improving digestion of lactose. You can find them in the refrigerated section of most chemists and health food stores. We recommend purchasing chilled probiotics, to ensure the organisms are most effective.
  • To warm a thermal serving bowl or ceramic bowl, fill with hot water, let stand for a few minutes, then discard water and dry bowl.
  • You can store this yoghurt in the refrigerator for up to 1 week.
