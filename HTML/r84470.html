Spinach and Broccoli Terrine

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp olive oil, for greasing
  • 600 g water, for steaming
  • 200 g broccoli florets
  • 400 g fresh baby spinach
  • 80 g double cream
  • 2 Tbsp agar-agar
  • 100 g feta cheese, cubed
  • 100 g soft goat's cheese
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground pepper
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
597 kJ / 143 kcal
Protein
7.3 g
Carbohydrates

11.8 g

Preparation

  1. Oil a large terrine mould then set aside.
  2. Place water in mixing bowl and insert simmering basket. Weigh broccoli into basket then place Varoma dish into position and weigh spinach into Varoma dish. Cover Varoma then steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Once vegetables are cooked, set Varoma and simmering basket aside and discard steaming water.
  4. Place cream and agar-agar in mixing bowl and heat 3 min/100°C/speed 2.
  5. Add feta and goats cheese and mix 50 sec/speed 3.
  6. Add broccoli, drained spinach, salt and pepper and mix 30 sec//speed 3. Transfer to prepared mould and cover with cling film. Allow to cool before placing in fridge to set for at least 4 hours.
  7. Remove from mould before serving cold.

Useful Items

terrine mould, cling film, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with a Thermomix® chopped salad or mixed, dressed salad leaves.
