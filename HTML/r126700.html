Crispy olives

Ingredients

  • 250 g medium-hard cheese, mature, cut into pieces (3 cm) (e.g. Gruyère or Gouda)
  • 150 g plain flour
  • 120 g butter, cold, cut into pieces
  • ½ tsp fine salt
  • 1 pinch hot paprika powder
  • 200 g green stuffed olives, well drained
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
225 kJ / 54 kcal
Protein
1 g
Carbohydrates
2 g
Fat
4 g

Preparation

  1. Place gouda in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 10.
  2. Add flour, butter, salt and paprika powder and knead 1 min 30 sec/. Shape pastry into a roll (Ø 3 cm) and chill for approx. 1 hour. Meanwhile, preheat oven to 220°C and line baking tray.
  3. Cut pastry into slices 1 cm thick. Place an olive on each slice and shape into a ball. Place pastry balls on baking tray, bake for approx. 15 minutes (220°C) and serve hot or cold.

Useful Items

baking tray, baking paper, oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • The cheese is easier to grate if slightly frozen.
  • Use olives stuffed with red pepper, garlic or almonds.
