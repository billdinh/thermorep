Mini Coffee Pecan Layer Cakes

Ingredients

Coffee Pecan Cake

  • 4 Tbsp instant coffee granules
  • 4 Tbsp water, boiling
  • 100 g pecan nuts
  • 250 g unsalted butter, soft
  • 275 g golden caster sugar
  • 300 g self-raising flour
  • 5 medium eggs
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 Tbsp vanilla extract

Assembly

  • 250 g unsalted butter, soft
  • 500 g icing sugar
  • 1 ½ Tbsp milk
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 12 pecan nut halves

Preparation

Coffee Pecan Cake

  1. Preheat oven to 170°C and line a cake tin (approx. 35 cm x 25 cm x 6 cm) with baking paper.
  2. In a small bowl, dissolve coffee in boiling water and set aside to cool slightly.
  3. Place pecans in mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place butter and sugar in mixing bowl then cream 30 sec/speed 4.
  5. Add flour, eggs and baking powder then mix 30 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then mix again 20 sec/speed 4.
  6. Add 2 Tbsp reserved coffee mixture, vanilla and reserved chopped pecans then, with aid of spatula, mix 10-20 sec//speed 4.5, until combined.
  7. Transfer mixture to prepared tin then bake for 15-20 minutes (170°C) until a skewer inserted in the centre comes out clean. Leave sponge in tin to cool for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl and make buttercream.

Assembly

  1. Place butter, sugar, milk, vanilla and remaining 2 Tbsp coffee mixture in mixing bowl then cream 1 min/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then cream again 20 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a large piping bag fitted with a 10 mm star-shaped nozzle.
  2. Using a long, serrated knife, level off the top of the cake to make a flat surface. Carefully slice the cake in half horizontally to make two layers, leaving the top layer in place.
  3. Using a cookie cutter (Ø 5 cm), cut 18 rounds from the double-layered cake to make 36 in total (see tip). Pipe approx. 1 Tbsp icing onto each cake round and layer into 3 to make 12 (3-layered) cakes. Arrange 1 pecan half on top of each cake to finish. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

baking paper, wire rack, cookie cutter (Ø 5 cm), serrated knife, piping bag with star-shaped nozzle, skewer, cake tin (35 cm x 25 cm x 6 cm), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Don't throw away the leftover sponge pieces; they are great for making cake pops.
