Cranberry Macadamia Granola Bar

Ingredients

  • 100 g macadamia nuts, raw, halved
  • 20 g banana chips
  • 50 g brown sugar
  • ½ vanilla pod, seeds only
  • 40 g honey
  • 380 g sweetened condensed milk
  • 200 g rolled oats
  • 80 g desiccated coconut
  • 50 g sesame seeds
  • 75 g dried cranberries
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
883 kJ / 211 kcal
Protein
3.9 g
Carbohydrates
24.5 g
Fat
10.8 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 130°C and line a square cake tin (20 cm) with baking paper.
  2. Place macadamia nuts and banana chips in mixing bowl then grind 4 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place sugar and vanilla seeds in mixing bowl then grind 10 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add honey and condensed milk then mix 10 sec/speed 3.
  5. Add oats, coconut, sesame seeds, cranberries, reserved macadamia-banana mixture and salt then, with aid of spatula, mix 25 sec/speed 3.
  6. Transfer to prepared tin and smooth surface with the back of a spoon. Bake for 30 minutes (130°C) then press down surface with the back of a spoon again and bake for a further 30-35 minutes (130°C) until lightly golden. Turn oven off and leave to stand for 5 minutes.
  7. Cool in tin a little before turning out and cutting into 20 bars. Serve once cooled or store in an airtight container for up to 5 days.

Useful Items

square cake tin (20 cm), baking paper, long sharp knife, airtight container, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Substitute cranberries with other dried fruit such as cherries, raisins or apricots, cut in pieces.
