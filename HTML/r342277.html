Quinoa Salad with Strawberry Vinaigrette

Ingredients

Quinoa

  • 9 oz red quinoa
  • 35 oz water

Vinaigrette

  • 2 oz olive oil
  • 8 oz strawberries, hulled
  • 2 oz lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 2 tbsp honey, to taste
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • ¼ tsp ground ginger
  • ¼ tsp salt

Assembly

  • 4 oz mixed salad greens
  • 8 oz strawberries, hulled, sliced
  • 4 oz red onions, halved, thinly shaved
  • 2 oz pine nuts
  • 6 oz goat cheese, crumbled or sliced in rounds

Preparation

Quinoa

  1. Insert simmering basket into mixing bowl. Weigh in quinoa and water and rinse 10 sec/speed 5, then steam 24 min/Varoma/speed 1. Transfer to a large bowl and refrigerate until cool. Empty mixing bowl.

Vinaigrette

  1. Place a pitcher onto mixing bowl lid, weigh in olive oil and set aside.
  2. Place strawberries, lemon juice, vinegar, honey, mustard, ginger and salt into mixing bowl and mix 2 min/speed 4, meanwhile slowly pouring reserved olive oil onto mixing bowl lid, letting it drizzle around measuring cup in a thin stream onto rotating blades to create an emulsion. Return vinaigrette to pitcher and set aside.

Assembly

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in mixed greens, strawberries, red onions, pine nuts and cooled quinoa, and toss to combine. Add reserved vinaigrette 1-2 tbsp at a time, to taste, and toss to coat. Top with goat cheese and serve with extra dressing on the side.

Useful Items

pitcher, large bowl, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Refrigerate leftover dressing and use within 3 days.
  • Wash vegetables and fruit before using.
