Moroccan Bean Soup

Ingredients

  • 45 g shallots, halved
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 450 g flat green beans (see tip)
  • 200 g courgettes, cut in pieces
  • 200 g tomatoes, cut in pieces
  • 650 g water
  • 5 mint leaves
  • 5 fresh coriander leaves
  • 1 - 2 tsp ground cumin, to taste
  • 1 - 1 ¼ tsp fine sea salt, to taste
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
412 kJ / 98 kcal
Protein
4 g
Carbohydrates
7 g
Fat
6 g

Preparation

  1. Place shallots in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5 . Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add oil and, without measuring cup, sauté 4 min/Varoma/speed .
  3. Add 300 g beans, courgettes, tomatoes and 400 g water then cook 10 min/100°C/speed . Meanwhile, cut remaining 150 g green beans in pieces (approx. 4 cm) and place in Varoma dish.
  4. Place covered Varoma into position then steam 10 min/Varoma/speed . Set Varoma aside.
  5. Add mint, coriander, cumin and salt then blend 1 min/speed 10.
  6. Add remaining 250 g water and stir 10 sec/speed 5 until smooth. Pour soup into bowls and serve with steamed beans as garnish.
