Fruity pear mince tarts

Ingredients

Almond pastry

  • 30 g raw sugar
  • 30 g blanched almonds
  • 210 g plain flour
  • 130 g butter, chilled and cut into pieces
  • ½ tsp mixed spice
  • 1 pinch sea salt
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 3 - 4 tsp water

Marzipan shapes

  • 250 g raw sugar
  • 250 g blanched almonds
  • ¼ tsp natural almond extract
  • 2 tbsp cornflour, plus extra for dusting
  • 1 egg white

Mince filling

  • butter, for greasing
  • 1 orange, zest only, no white pith
  • 750 g pears, peeled, cores removed and cut into quarters
  • 250 g caster sugar
  • 100 g dried cranberries
  • 130 g currants
  • 50 g glacé ginger, cut into pieces
  • 60 g flaked almonds
  • 40 g lemon juice (approx. 1 lemon)
  • 40 g orange juice, (approx. ½ orange)
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp ground cloves
  • 1 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1 tsp ground cardamom
  • 2 tbsp brandy (optional)
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten, for brushing

Brandy cream sauce

  • 70 g raw sugar
  • 200 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 200 g full cream milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 40 g brandy
  • icing sugar, for dusting pies

Preparation

Almond pastry

  1. Place sugar into mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place almonds into mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 8.
  3. Add flour, butter, mixed spice, salt and reserved sugar and mix 10 sec/speed 6, or until mixture resembles breadcrumbs.
  4. Add egg yolk and water or orange juice and knead 20 sec/.
  5. Transfer dough onto a silicone bread mat or floured work surface and work into a ball. Wrap in plastic wrap and place into the refrigerator to rest until ready to use. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Marzipan shapes

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Line a baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place sugar into mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place almonds into mixing bowl and mill 15 sec/speed 7.
  4. Add all remaining marzipan ingredients, including reserved sugar, and mix 40 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Repeat mixing if necessary. If mixture is sticky, add a little more cornflour.
  5. Transfer mixture onto a silicone bread mat or work surface dusted with cornflour. Knead mixture until well combined using the warmth of your hands to help soften and release the oil from the almonds, this will help to bind the dough before rolling. Roll out mixture until 5 mm thick, then using a cookie cutter, cut out marzipan into your festive shapes of choice. Place shapes onto prepared baking tray. Rinse and dry mixing bowl.
  6. Bake for 5 minutes (180°C) or until lightly golden, then set aside to cool (any left over biscuits that have not been used on top of the tarts will need to be cooked for an extra 10 minutes).

Mince filling

  1. Increase oven temperature to 200°C and lightly grease an 18-hole muffin tray and set aside.
  2. Place orange zest into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 7.
  3. Add pears and sugar and chop 3 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add all remaining mince filling ingredients, except brandy and egg, and cook 35 min/Varoma//speed 1. Transfer mince filling into a large bowl. Allow to cool slightly, then stir through brandy (optional) and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  5. Remove almond pastry from refrigerator and roll out between 2 sheets of baking paper until 5 mm thick. Cut pastry to size using a cookie cutter and use to line prepared muffin tray.
  6. Spoon in cooled mince filling and top with marzipan shapes. Brush marzipan shapes with a little beaten egg then bake for 10 minutes (200°C).

Brandy cream sauce

  1. Place sugar into mixing bowl and mill 15 sec/speed 9.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Add cream, milk, eggs and brandy and cook 10 min/80°C/speed 4. Mixture should coat the back of a wooden spoon.
  3. Allow pies to cool slightly, before dusting with icing sugar and serving warm with brandy cream sauce.

Useful Items

plastic wrap, baking tray (30 x 40 cm), baking paper, cookie cutter, rolling pin, 18-hole muffin tray, large bowl, pastry brush, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Fruity pear mince slab: make the Almond pastry and Marzipan shapes as above (steps 1-10). Make Mince filling as above (steps 13-16). Preheat oven to 200°C. Grease and line a 25 x 31 cm slice tin with baking paper. Line tin with almond pastry, then fill pastry with mince filling and bake for 10 minutes (200°C). Top with unbaked marzipan shapes, then bake for a further 10 minutes (200°C). While slab is baking, make Brandy cream sauce as above (steps 18-20).
