Chilli paneer

Ingredients

  • 250 - 300 g paneer, cut into cubes (2 cm) (see Tips)
  • 2 tbsp cornflour (approx. 20 g) (see Tips)
  • 4 tbsp canola oil
  • 1 ½ brown onions (approx. 200 g), cut into halves
  • 2 - 3 fresh green chillies, trimmed and cut into halves (1 cut into thin slices, for garnishing)
  • ½ tsp ground turmeric
  • ½ tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp garam masala (see Tips)
  • 1 tbsp ginger paste (see Tips)
  • 2 tsp garlic paste (see Tips)
  • 1 green capsicum, cut into pieces (2 cm), (approx. 200 g)
  • 1 red capsicum, cut into pieces (2 cm), (approx. 200 g)
  • ¾ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh paneer and cornflour into it. Carefully stir to coat paneer. Set bowl aside.
  2. Place 2 tablespoons of the oil into mixing bowl and heat 2 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Add onions and chillies and chop 3 sec/speed 5, then cook 5 min/100°C/speed 1.
  4. Add turmeric, cumin seeds, garam masala, ginger paste and garlic paste and cook 2 min/100°C/speed 1.
  5. Add capsicums, salt and pepper and cook 5 min/100°C//speed  or until capsicum are tender. Transfer into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and cover to keep warm.
  6. Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add remaining 2 tablespoons oil. When oil is hot, add paneer pieces and fry for 2-3 minutes, turning constantly, until both sides are lightly browned. Transfer paneer into thermal serving bowl with capsicum mixture and gently stir to combine with aid of spatula. Garnish with sliced chilli before serving with your choice of bread (see Tips).

Useful Items

bowl, thermal serving bowl or other large bowl, large non-stick frying pan, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • To make your own paneer, garam masala, ginger paste and garlic paste, please refer to the recipes on this recipe chip.
  • To ensure your recipe is gluten free, always check the labels of your products to make sure they don't contain traces of wheat or gluten.
  • You can serve this with your favourite bread. We served ours with Garlic and coriander naan. Please refer to the recipe on this recipe chip.
