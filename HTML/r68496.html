Pikelets

Ingredients

  • 20 g butter
  • 140 g self-raising flour
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 30 g caster sugar
  • ¼ tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 150 g buttermilk
  • 1 egg
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
667 kJ / 159 kcal
Protein
5.6 g
Carbohydrates
27.4 g
Fat
2.8 g

Preparation

  1. Place butter into mixing bowl and melt 30-40 sec/50°C/speed 4.
  2. Add self-raising flour, salt, caster sugar, bicarb soda, buttermilk and egg and mix 20 sec/speed 5, or until a smooth consistency is achieved.
  3. Place a spoonful of batter into a hot non-stick frying pan then cook until small bubbles appear on surface before flipping to cook other side until lightly golden. Transfer to a ThermoServer or plate and cover with kitchen towel to keep warm. Repeat process with remaining batter. Serve pikelets warm.

Useful Items

kitchen towel, non-stick frying pan (22 cm), ThermoServer, turner / fish slice, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve pikelets with maple syrup, whipped cream or homemade jam.
  • If you don't serve pikelets immediately, allow to cool completely before transferring to an airtight container to store until use.
