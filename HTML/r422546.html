Rice Pudding Ice Cream with Peach Coulis

Ingredients

  • 400 g tinned rice pudding (1 tin)
  • 2 egg whites, from medium eggs (see tip)
  • 2 Tbsp caster sugar, plus 1 tsp optional
  • 240 g tinned peach halves, drained, juice reserved (1 x 400 g tin)
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
726 kJ / 171 kcal
Protein
6 g
Carbohydrates
33 g
Fat
1 g

Preparation

  1. Place tinned rice pudding in a large bowl.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg whites and sugar in mixing bowl then, without measuring cup, whisk 1 min 30 sec/speed 4 or until stiff peaks form. Remove butterfly whisk. Gently fold into rice pudding to avoid knocking too much air out. Transfer to shallow freezer-proof containers, seal, and freeze for 4-5 hours until solid. Clean mixing bowl.
  3. Place peach halves and sugar (if using) in mixing bowl then blend 15 sec/speed 7 until smooth. Scrape down sides and lid of mixing bowl.
  4. Add 40 g reserved peach juice and mix 5 sec/speed 3. Transfer to a serving jug and chill in fridge until required. Clean mixing bowl.
  5. Remove ice cream from freezer 15 minutes before serving.
  6. Turn out ice cream onto a board and cut into squares (3 cm). Place ice cream squares in mixing bowl and churn 30-45 sec/speed 6 until smooth (see tip).
  7. Serve ice cream with peach coulis poured over.

Useful Items

large bowl, serving jug, shallow freezer-proof containers, freezer, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Not a true ice cream but a marvellous, easy and delicious dessert of frozen rice pudding with whipped egg whites.
  • Take care, this recipe contains raw eggs.
  • The ice cream can be returned to the freezer after churning to firm up. This will make it easier to scoop balls of ice cream into serving glasses.
