Cake in a Jar

Ingredients

Chocolate Cake

  • 90 g plain flour
  • 60 g soft brown sugar
  • 15 g cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
  • 50 g milk
  • 50 g water
  • 40 g sunflower oil, plus extra for greasing

Icing, Assembly and Decoration

  • 25 g white chocolate, small pieces or callets
  • 15 g butter
  • 100 g cream cheese
  • 15 g icing sugar
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • 24 tsp Baileys, or other cream liqueur (approx. 80 g)
  • 16 fresh raspberries

Preparation

Chocolate Cake

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C and line a square baking tin (20 cm) with baking paper.
  2. Place flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, bicarbonate of soda and salt in mixing bowl then mix 5 sec/speed 5.
  3. Add milk, water and oil then mix 30 sec/speed 4. Finish mixing by hand with spatula then transfer to prepared baking tin.
  4. Bake for 10 minutes (180°C). Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl. Remove cake from tin and allow to cool on a wire rack. Meanwhile, prepare icing.

Icing, Assembly and Decoration

  1. Place white chocolate in mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 6.
  2. Add butter and melt 2 min/50°C/speed 2.
  3. Add cream cheese, icing sugar and vanilla extract then mix 20 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then mix again 15 sec/speed 4. Transfer icing to a piping bag and set aside.
  4. Use a pastry cutter to cut 24 discs (Ø 4 cm) out of the chocolate cake. Place a disc of sponge cake in the bottom of 8 jars (each jar Ø 5 cm, approx. 80 ml capacity) add 1 tsp Baileys or cream liqueur and top with icing. Repeat process twice more, finishing with a layer of icing. Decorate each jar with 2 raspberries, then close jar lid.
  5. Store in fridge until ready to pack for your picnic.

Useful Items

square baking tin (20 cm), baking paper, wire rack, piping bag, small jars, pastry cutter, oven, refrigerator
