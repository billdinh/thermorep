Thai chilli chicken and basil

Ingredients

  • 20 g fresh Thai basil, leaves only
  • 20 g fresh coriander leaves, plus extra for garnishing
  • 1 brown onion (approx. 180 g), cut into quarters
  • 2 red chillies, deseeded if preferred, plus extra for garnishing
  • 20 g oil
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp sweet chilli sauce
  • 1 tbsp kecap manis (sweet soy sauce)
  • 800 g skinless chicken thigh fillets, cut into slices (6 x 1 cm)
  • 270 g canned coconut milk
  • 30 g lime juice (approx. 3 limes)

Preparation

  1. Place fresh Thai basil leaves and fresh coriander leaves into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place brown onion and red chillies into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7 . Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add oil and sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  4. Add fish sauce, sweet chilli sauce, kecap manis and reserved Thai basil and coriander mixture and cook 2 min/120°C/speed 1.
  5. Add chicken and coconut milk and cook  10-15 min/100°C//speed  or until chicken is cooked through. Transfer into a ThermoServer or large serving bowl and stir through lime juice.
  6. Garnish with extra coriander and sliced chilli to taste before serving.

Useful Items

ThermoServer or large serving bowl

Hints & Tricks

Serve with cooked rice and lime wedges.

For a spicier dish, increase the amount of chilli to taste.
