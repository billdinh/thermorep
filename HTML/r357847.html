Feijoa crumble slice

Ingredients

  • 80 g pecans
  • 200 g unsalted butter, cut into pieces
  • 180 g caster sugar
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract
  • 300 g self-raising flour
  • 40 g lemon juice
  • 70 g desiccated coconut
  • 400 g feijoas (approx. 5 feijoas), peeled and cut into pieces (2 cm - see Tips)
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 170°C. Grease and line a slice tin (18 x 22 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place pecans into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place butter and 150 g of the sugar into mixing bowl and mix 1 min/speed 3.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix for a further 1 min/speed 3.
  5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix for a further 1 min/speed 3.
  6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Add vanilla, flour, lemon juice and 40 g of the coconut and mix 10 sec/speed 7 or until well combined.
  7. Transfer three-quarters of the mixture into base of prepared slice tin and press down firmly and evenly. Bake for 10 minutes (170°C), until just golden brown.
  8. Add remaining 30 g coconut and reserved pecans into mixing bowl with remaining crumble mixture and mix 5 sec//speed 4. Transfer into a bowl and place into refrigerator until ready to use.
  9. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh feijoas and sugar into it. Add cinnamon and stir to combine.
  10. Spread feijoas evenly over base and top with reserved coconut crumble mixture. Bake for 30-35 minutes (170°C), until golden brown. Cut into slices to serve (see Tips).

Useful Items

slice tin (18 x 22 cm), baking paper, bowl, oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Gluten free feijoa crumble slice: Replace self-raising flour with gluten free version, then proceed as per recipe.
  • If using frozen feijoas, defrost completely and drain off any excess moisture before using.
  • Serve with custard, cream or ice cream if desired.
