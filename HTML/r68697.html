Five seed bread

Ingredients

  • 500 g water
  • 2 tsp dried instant yeast
  • butter, for greasing
  • 500 g baker's flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 50 g rolled oats
  • 30 g sesame seeds, plus extra for sprinkling
  • 30 g linseeds, plus extra for sprinkling
  • 30 g sunflower seeds, plus extra for sprinkling
  • 30 g pepita seeds, plus extra for sprinkling
  • 30 g poppy seeds, plus extra for sprinkling
  • 30 g honey
  • 30 g extra virgin olive oil

Preparation

  1. Place water and yeast into mixing bowl and heat 2 min 30 sec/37°C/speed 2. Meanwhile, grease a deep-sided loaf tin and set aside.
  2. Add baker's flour, salt, rolled oats, sesame seeds, linseeds, sunflower seeds, pepitas, poppy seeds, honey, and extra virgin olive oil into mixing bowl and knead  2 min 30 sec/. Dough will have a wet and sticky consistency.
  3. Preheat oven to 200°C. Transfer dough into prepared loaf tin and sprinkle with extra sesame seeds, linseeds, sunflower seeds, pepitas and poppy seeds. Leave to prove in a warm place until doubled in size (approx. 45-60 minutes).
  4. Bake for 40-45 minutes (200°C). Allow to cool in tin 5 minutes. Serve warm or transfer to a wire rack and allow to cool completely before transferring to an airtight container until use.

Useful Items

deep-sided loaf tin, airtight container, wire rack, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with freshly made butter and citrus marmalade for a delicious breakfast, or use to make your favourite sandwiches.
