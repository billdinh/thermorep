Pecan Pie Ice Cream Shake

Ingredients

Bourbon Caramel Sauce

  • 3 oz unsalted butter, in pieces
  • 3 oz dark brown sugar
  • 2 oz bourbon (see Tip)
  • &frac18; tsp salt
  • 2 oz heavy whipping cream

Shake

  • 32 oz vanilla ice cream
  • 6 oz whole milk
  • 6 oz leftover pecan pie, in pieces
  • whipped cream, to serve
  • 4 oz chopped pecans, to garnish

Preparation

Bourbon Caramel Sauce

  1. Place butter, sugar, bourbon and salt into mixing bowl, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 20 min/212°F/speed 2.
  2. Add whipping cream, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 7 min/195°F/speed 3. Transfer to a bowl and allow sauce to cool slightly, then refrigerate for 20 minutes or until needed.

Shake

  1. Place ice cream, milk and pecan pie into mixing bowl, insert measuring cup and mix 1 min/speed 7.
  2. Divide ice cream mixture between glasses and garnish with whipped cream. Drizzle with reserved Bourbon Caramel Sauce, sprinkle with pecans and serve.

Useful Items

tall glasses, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • For a non-alcoholic treat: replace 2 oz bourbon with 1.5 oz water and 2 tsp vanilla extract and continue with recipe.
