Four Cheese Pizza

Ingredients

Pizza Dough

  • olive oil, to grease
  • 15 oz warm water (100°F-110°F)
  • 16 oz bread flour
  • 6 oz semolina
  • 2 ¼ tsp rapid rise yeast
  • 1 tbsp salt

Pizza Sauce

  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1 oz olive oil
  • 12 oz tomato paste
  • 2 tsp dried oregano
  • 2 tsp dried basil
  • ½ tsp dried thyme
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper

For Pizza

  • 6 oz Fontina cheese, cut into pieces
  • 4 oz aged white cheddar cheese, cut into pieces
  • 2 oz pecorino romano, cut into pieces
  • olive oil, to grease
  • bread flour, to dust
  • 8 oz buffalo mozzarella, sliced

Preparation

Pizza Dough

  1. Lightly grease a large bowl and set aside.
  2. Place water, bread flour, semolina, yeast and salt into mixing bowl and knead 2 min/. Form dough into a ball and transfer into prepared bowl. Cover with cling wrap and let dough rise until doubled in size (approx. 1 hr.).

Pizza Sauce

  1. Place garlic and oil into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Sauté 3 min/250°F/speed 1.
  2. Add tomato paste, oregano, basil, thyme, salt and pepper and mix 15 sec/speed 3.5. Transfer to bowl and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.

For Pizza

  1. Place Fontina cheese into mixing bowl and grate 8 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place cheddar and pecorino romano cheeses into mixing bowl and grate 8 sec/speed 6. Transfer to bowl with grated cheese and set aside.
  3. Preheat oven to 500°F. Grease two pizza pans with olive oil (see Tip).
  4. Divide dough in half and place onto prepared pans. Stretch dough out to pan edges, with lightly floured fingers. Cover with clean kitchen towels and let rise 5 minutes.
  5. Spread 6 tbsp reserved pizza sauce evenly over one pizza base and leave other covered. Sprinkle half the grated cheeses over pizza base and top with half the buffalo mozzarella. Bake for 10-12 minutes (500°F). Repeat with remaining dough. Serve warm.

Useful Items

kitchen towels, pizza pans (14 in.), cling wrap, large bowl, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • For a quick meal: freeze baked pizza once cooled and reheat for 7-9 minutes (500°F) before serving.
  • If using a sheet pan divide dough into thirds and make three (12 in.) pizzas. To make four individual (9 in.) pizzas: divide dough into quarters and shape onto a sheet pan lined with parchment and sprinkled with semolina.
  • If using a pizza stone, follow manufacturer's instructions.
