Omelette

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs
  • 1 pinch salt, to taste
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper, to taste
  • 20 - 40 g cheddar cheese, cut into cubes (3 cm)
  • ¼ red onion
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 40 g ham
  • 4 cherry tomatoes, cut into quarters
  • 4 button mushrooms, stalks removed, cut into quarters
  • 10 fresh baby spinach leaves
  • 500 g water
  • ½ avocado, flesh only, cut into thin slices (optional)
  • 1 sprig fresh parsley, to garnish

Preparation

  1. Take a piece of wet and well-wrung baking paper and use to line Varoma tray. Ensure baking paper goes all the way to the edges without blocking the two air vents on the rim. Set Varoma aside.
  2. Place eggs into mixing bowl, season to taste with salt and ground black pepper and blend 6 sec/speed 5. Carefully pour eggs onto baking paper on Varoma tray and set Varoma aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Place cheddar cheese into mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Place red onion into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add oil or butter and sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  6. Add ham and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  7. Add cherry tomatoes and button mushrooms and sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  8. Add baby spinach leaves and cook 1 min/120°C/speed 1. Transfer filling into a ThermoServer or bowl and set aside to keep warm.
  9. Place water into mixing bowl and place Varoma, with eggs, into position. Cook 8-10 min/Varoma/speed 1, or until eggs are just set.
  10. Place reserved filling to cover one half of the omelette. Top with avocado (optional) and reserved cheddar cheese. Fold omelette over then transfer onto a serving plate and gently cut in half. Transfer half onto another serving plate and garnish with parsley to serve.

Useful Items

baking paper, ThermoServer

Hints & Tricks

  • The type of cheddar cheese used will influence the flavour of your omelette. For a fuller flavour use vintage cheddar, or for a more subtle flavour use a mild cheddar.
  • Asparagus, if in season, can be steamed for a few minutes in the Varoma dish while you cook the omelette. Remove asparagus once cooked to preference and proceed as per recipe.
