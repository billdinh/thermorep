Yellow Chicken Adobo

Ingredients

  • 14 oz kabocha squash, peeled, cut into half-moons (½ in. thick)
  • 10 oz cauliflower florets
  • 6 oz yellow onions, cut into pieces
  • 6 garlic cloves
  • 2 tbsp ground turmeric
  • 2 tsp dried red chili flakes
  • 1 ½ tsp salt, to taste
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 27 oz canned coconut milk, divided
  • 2 tbsp tapioca starch
  • 32 oz chicken thighs, air-chilled, boneless, skinless, halved lengthwise
  • 5 oz white vinegar
  • 1 oz ginger, peeled, thinly sliced
  • 1 oz honey
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tsp soy sauce, low sodium
  • 1 fresh jalapeño chili, stems and seeds removed, sliced

Preparation

  1. Place Varoma into position, weigh in squash and cauliflower and set aside.
  2. Place onions, garlic, turmeric, chili flakes, salt and pepper into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and sauté 3 min/250°F/speed 1.
  3. Place a small bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in 1 oz coconut milk and tapioca starch. Whisk until smooth and set aside.
  4. Add chicken, 26 oz coconut milk, vinegar, ginger, honey, fish sauce, soy sauce and reserved coconut milk-starch mixture into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and cook 30 min/Varoma//speed 2.
  5. Ladle chicken adobo mixture into bowls, top with steamed vegetables and garnish with sliced jalapeños. Serve immediately.
