New Orleans Style Cheesecake

Ingredients

Base

  • 6 oz unsalted butter, plus extra to grease
  • 12 oz graham crackers, broken in half
  • 3 oz sugar

Filling

  • 24 oz cream cheese
  • 9 oz sour cream
  • 5 ½ oz sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 egg white

Topping

  • 9 oz sour cream
  • 2 oz sugar

Preparation

Base

  1. Preheat oven to 250°F. Grease a springform pan and line bottom and sides with parchment and set aside.
  2. Place butter into mixing bowl and melt 2 min/212°F/speed 1.
  3. Add graham crackers and sugar and crush 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and crush a further 10 sec/speed 7.
  4. Spread crumb mixture into base and sides of prepared springform pan. Press into a firm compact layer with a spoon or your hands. Place in refrigerator while making the filling. Clean mixing bowl.

Filling

  1. Place cream cheese, sour cream, sugar, egg and egg white into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix a further 10 sec/speed 5.
  2. Evenly pour mixture into prepared crust and bake 10 minutes (250°F). Reduce temperature to 225°F and bake a further 1 hour 50 minutes (225ºF). Clean mixing bowl and proceed with topping.

Topping

  1. Place sour cream and sugar into mixing bowl and mix 8 sec/speed 3.
  2. Evenly spread topping over cheesecake with spatula as soon as it comes out of oven. Allow to cool 15 minutes before placing in refrigerator to chill completely (approx. 4 hr.). Serve chilled.

Useful Items

parchment paper, springform pan (9 in.), oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with mixed berries.
