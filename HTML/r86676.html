Salmon Scotch Eggs with Honey Mustard Dipping Sauce

Ingredients

Honey Mustard Sauce

  • 75 g wholegrain mustard
  • 1 Tbsp white wine vinegar
  • 75 g honey
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper

Eggs

  • 400 g water
  • 10 medium eggs
  • 150 g bread, torn in pieces
  • 400 g smoked salmon, cut in pieces
  • 10 g fresh parsley
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • vegetable oil, for frying (approx. 1.5 litres)

Preparation

Honey Mustard Sauce

  1. Place mustard, vinegar, honey, salt and pepper in mixing bowl then mix 15 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a small serving bowl and set aside.

Eggs

  1. Place water in mixing bowl then insert simmering basket. Place eight eggs in simmering basket and steam 11 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  2. Remove simmering basket immediately with aid of spatula and rinse eggs under cold water to stop cooking process. Discard steaming water then rinse and dry mixing bowl. When cool enough to handle, peel eggs carefully.
  3. Place bread in mixing bowl and grate 15 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place salmon, parsley, remaining two eggs, 100 g prepared breadcrumbs, salt and pepper in mixing bowl then mix 5 sec/speed 8. Tip out contents of mixing bowl onto a chopping board or work surface.
  5. With wet hands, form salmon mixture into a log (16 cm) and cut into eight slices with spatula. Place one slice on your palm, flatten slightly, and cover one cooked egg with it, sealing completely. Keep palms wet to prevent mixture sticking to your hands. Repeat with remaining cooked eggs. Roll coated eggs in the remaining breadcrumbs.
  6. Heat oil in a large saucepan or deep fat fryer to 175°C. Fry eggs in batches for 1-2 minutes turning continuously. Remove from oil and drain on paper towel. Serve with prepared sauce and a salad.

Useful Items

small serving bowl, slotted spoon, paper towel, large saucepan, hob

Hints & Tricks

  • The boiling time for the eggs decreases if eggs are stored at room temperature, are smaller, or you use warm or hot water from the tap.
