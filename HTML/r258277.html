Garlic Chili Sauce

Ingredients

  • 32 oz red jalapeño peppers, stems removed
  • 2 ½ oz shallots, cut into pieces
  • 8 garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 19 oz canned tomato sauce
  • 2 ½ oz rice vinegar
  • 2 oz sugar
  • 1 oz fish sauce
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
17 kJ / 4 kcal
Protein
0 g
Carbohydrates
1 g
Fat
0 g

Preparation

  1. Place red jalapeños, shallots, garlic and oil into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and sauté 15 min/212°F/speed 1.
  2. Add tomato sauce, vinegar, sugar and fish sauce and cook 7 min/212°F/speed 2.
  3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and cook 5 min/195°F/speed 2.
  4. Purée 30 sec/speed 4-6 increasing speed gradually. Strain through a fine-mesh sieve. Pour into bottles and refrigerate.

Useful Items

fine-mesh sieve, glass bottles

Hints & Tricks

  • Adjust seasoning by adding ½ -1 tsp salt or sugar, to taste, just before sauce is puréed.
  • Transfer to decorative jars or containers and label for gift-giving
