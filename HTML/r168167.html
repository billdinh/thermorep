Spinach turnovers (fatayer)

Ingredients

Dough

  • 150 g water
  • 90 g olive oil
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 350 g flour

Filling

  • 400 g fresh spinach leaves, washed and dried
  • 350 g onions, cut into quarters
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sumac
  • 100 g lemon juice, freshly squeezed

Assembly and Baking

  • flour, for dusting

Preparation

Dough

  1. Place water, olive oil, salt and flour into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 4, then knead 1 min/. Transfer into a bowl and cover with cling film. Set aside for at least 30 minutes. Meanwhile prepare the filling.

Filling

  1. Place 200 g spinach leaves into mixing bowl and chop 15 sec/speed 5. Transfer into Varoma dish and set aside.
  2. Place remaining 200 g spinach leaves into mixing bowl and chop 15 sec/speed 5. Transfer into Varoma dish with reserved chopped spinach and strain well by pressing with the back of a spoon.
  3. Place onions, salt, sumac and lemon juice into mixing bowl and chop 20 sec/speed 6. Transfer mixture to Varoma dish with spinach and mix with spatula. Set aside to drain well before assembling.

Assembly and Baking

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Line 2 baking trays with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Divide reserved dough into 4 equal pieces and, on a lightly floured surface, shape each piece into a log. Cut each log into 8 equal pieces. With a rolling pin roll-out each piece of dough into a circle (Ø 10 cm).
  3. Place 1 heaped tsp spinach filling in the centre of each circle. Think of each circle as having 3 sides. Lift two sides and pinch together, then lift the third side and pinch it to the loose edge to form a triangle. Pinch the edges to close the filling in. Transfer to prepared baking trays and bake for 30 minutes (180°C) or until golden brown. Transfer on a cooling rack and serve warm or at room temperature.

Useful Items

cling film, rolling pin, baking trays, baking paper, cooling rack, oven
