Sweet Potato Brownies

Ingredients

Whipped Coconut Cream

  • 400 g coconut milk
  • 10 g agave syrup

Cake

  • 70 g buckwheat grains
  • 150 g blanched almonds
  • 1200 g water
  • 500 g sweet potatoes, cut in pieces
  • 300 g dates
  • 130 g dark chocolate (50% cocoa solids), small pieces or callets
  • 30 g coconut oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 60 g rice milk
  • 2 Tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt

Preparation

Whipped Coconut Cream

  1. Drain coconut milk separating solid cream from milk, collecting both in separate bowls (see tip).
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Place agave syrup and reserved coconut cream in mixing bowl then mix 20 sec/speed 2. Transfer to a bowl then set aside in fridge.

Cake

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C and grease a brownie tin (approx. 32 cm x 18 cm).
  2. Place buckwheat in mixing bowl and grind 20 sec/speed 9. Transfer to a bowl.
  3. Place almonds in mixing bowl and grind 7 sec/speed 7. Transfer to bowl with buckwheat and set aside.
  4. Place water in mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket and weigh in sweet potatoes then steam 20 min/100°C/speed 4. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and transfer cooked potatoes to a bowl. Discard cooking water.
  5. Place dates, chocolate, oil, rice milk and cocoa in mixing bowl then grind 20 sec/speed 7.
  6. Add reserved sweet potatoes and chop 30 sec/speed 9, while stirring with spatula through hole in mixing bowl lid.
  7. Add baking powder, salt, reserved almonds and buckwheat then mix 20 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer to prepared tin and bake for 25 minutes (180°C). Allow to cool in tin before removing and cutting into squares. Serve with reserved whipped coconut cream.

Useful Items

brownie tin (32 cm x 18 cm), refrigerator, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Agave syrup is sweeter than sugar meaning a smaller amount is needed.
  • Place the tin of coconut milk in the fridge for at least 8 hours before beginning the recipe. After this time, drain liquid part and solid part from milk.
  • The reserved coconut milk can be added to smoothies or juices for extra flavour.
