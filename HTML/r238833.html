Curried Chicken, Arugula and Pineapple

Ingredients

  • 14 ½ oz canned diced tomatoes, undrained
  • 1 oz olive oil
  • 1 oz sugar
  • 28 oz chicken breasts, air-chilled, boneless and skinless, cut into strips (½ in.)
  • 3 oz crème fraîche
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • &frac18; tsp ground cayenne pepper
  • 10 whole wheat tortillas
  • 2 oz arugula leaves
  • 10 slices canned pineapple, halved
  • 5 - 6 sprigs cilantro leaves
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
1715 kJ / 410 kcal
Protein
26 g
Carbohydrates
58 g
Fat
11 g

Preparation

  1. Place tomatoes, olive oil and sugar into mixing bowl. Place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 15 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  2. Add chicken, crème fraîche, curry powder, salt and cayenne. Mix well with spatula, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 10 min/212°F//speed 0.5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Distribute over each tortilla some arugula leaves, 1-2 tbsp chicken curry mixture and pineapple slices. Sprinkle with cilantro and serve immediately.
