Spinach and Cheese Soufflés

Ingredients

  • 20 g salted butter, plus extra for greasing
  • 4 egg whites, from large eggs
  • 30 g Parmesan cheese, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 200 g fresh spinach leaves
  • 40 g soft goat's cheese
  • 200 g curd cheese (quark)
  • 15 g cornflour
  • 2 pinches fine sea salt
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 6 fresh basil leaves
  • 4 egg yolks, from large eggs

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C. Grease 6 large ramekins or dariole moulds (Ø 8 cm) for soufflés then set aside.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg whites in mixing bowl and, without measuring cup, whisk until stiff - stop when stiff peaks form/speed 3.5. Remove butterfly whisk. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place Parmesan in mixing bowl and grate 15 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place butter in mixing bowl and melt 1 min 30 sec/90°C/speed .
  5. Add spinach and cook 4 min/100°C/speed 1. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  6. Add goat's cheese, curd cheese, cornflour, salt, pepper, basil and reserved grated Parmesan then blend 30 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  7. Add egg yolks and mix 10 sec/speed 4. Pour onto whisked egg whites and fold in gently. Fill prepared ramekins up to 1 cm from top. Place on a baking tray and bake for 20 minutes (200°C) until risen and browned. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

6 individual ramekins (Ø 8 cm), baking tray, oven
