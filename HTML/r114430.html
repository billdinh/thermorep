Breakfast pizza

Ingredients

Pizza base

  • 100 g brown rice
  • 100 g glutinous rice
  • 50 g potato starch
  • 30 g gluten free cornflour
  • 1 tsp xanthan gum
  • 1 - 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp white vinegar
  • 20 g olive oil, plus extra for drizzling
  • 180 g warm water
  • 20 g sugar
  • 2 tsp dried instant yeast

Sun-dried tomato pesto

  • 250 g sun-dried tomatoes
  • 100 g olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 sprig fresh basil, leaves only
  • 1 tsp dried oregano

Assembly

  • 4 eggs
  • 130 g bocconcini , cut into slices
  • 8 cherry tomatoes, cut into halves
  • salt, to season
  • freshly ground black pepper, to season
  • 40 g fresh baby spinach leaves
  • Worcestershire sauce (gluten free), to serve (optional) (see Tips)

Preparation

Pizza base

  1. Line a pizza tray (30 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place brown rice and glutinous rice into mixing bowl and mill 2 min/speed 8.
  3. Add potato starch, cornflour, xanthum gum, salt and vinegar and mix 5 sec/speed 4.
  4. Add oil, water, sugar and yeast and mix 15 sec/speed 4.
  5. Transfer mixture onto prepared pizza tray. Drizzle dough with extra oil and use finger tips to press dough out evenly to edge of tray. Cover tray with baking paper and leave to prove in a warm place for 30 minutes. Meanwhile, continue with pesto. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Sun-dried tomato pesto

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C.
  2. Place all pesto ingredients into mixing bowl and blend 20 sec/speed 7, or until smooth.
  3. Transfer into a sealable container and place into refrigerator until ready to use (see Tips).

Assembly

  1. Remove baking paper from top of pizza. Bake pizza crust for 5 minutes (220°C). Remove pizza from oven and reduce oven temperature to 200°C. For a crispy crust, carefully remove baking paper from underneath base of pizza. Spread pizza base evenly with half of the tomato pesto. Make a slight indentation in each quarter of the pizza in pesto using the back of a spoon. Crack an egg into each indentation. Arrange bocconcini and tomatoes evenly over pizza, then season with salt and pepper. Return pizza to oven and bake for a further 20-25 minutes (200°C), or until eggs are cooked to your preference. Top with spinach and drizzle with Worcestershire sauce (optional). Slice into 4 portions and serve immediately. 

Useful Items

pizza tray, baking paper, sealable container, oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • To make your own gluten free Worcestershire sauce, please refer to the recipe on this recipe chip.
  • Tomato pesto can be stored in the refrigerator for 1 week.
  • To ensure that your cornflour is gluten free, always check the label to ensure no wheat or gluten has been used as part of the production process.
