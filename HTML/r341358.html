Green goddess pizza

Ingredients

Pizza base

  • 20 g extra virgin olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 120 g filtered water
  • ½ tsp dried instant yeast
  • 200 g wholemeal spelt flour
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 2 tsp nigella seeds

Basil, avocado and hemp pesto

  • 40 g fresh basil, leaves only (approx. 8 large sprigs)
  • 30 g pine nuts
  • 30 g walnuts
  • 30 g hemp seeds (see Tips)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 avocado, flesh only
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 30 g extra virgin olive oil

Assembly

  • 30 g broccoli, broken into small florets
  • 4 - 5 marinated artichoke hearts, cut into halves
  • 10 pitted green olives, cut into halves
  • 1 spring onion/shallot, cut into thin slices
  • 1 tbsp pine nuts
  • 6 slices vegan mozzarella (approx. 40 g - see Tips)
  • salt, to season
  • ground black pepper, to season
  • extra virgin olive oil, for drizzling
  • rocket, for garnishing
  • micro herbs, for garnishing

Preparation

Pizza base

  1. Line a pizza tray (30 cm) with baking paper and set aside. Lightly grease a large bowl with oil and set aside.
  2. Place water and yeast into mixing bowl and heat 2 min/37°C/speed 1.
  3. Add flour, salt, oil and nigella seeds and knead 3 min/. Transfer dough into prepared bowl and cover with a kitchen towel. Set aside to prove until doubled in size (approx. 40-60 minutes). Clean and dry mixing bowl. Meanwhile, proceed with recipe.

Basil, avocado and hemp pesto

  1. Place all pesto ingredients into mixing bowl and chop 20 sec/speed 7.
  2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then blend 5 sec/speed 10 or until smooth. Transfer into a sealable jar or bottle and place into refrigerator until ready to use (see Tips).

Assembly

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C.
  2. Transfer reserved pizza dough onto a silicone bread mat or floured work surface. Roll out into a large circle (26 cm), transfer onto prepared pizza tray and prick dough all over with a fork. Set aside to prove for 15 minutes, then bake for 4 minutes (220°C).
  3. Remove pizza base from oven and spread evenly with 2-3 tablespoons of the reserved pesto, leaving a border (2.5 cm) around the edge. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh broccoli into it. Top pizza with broccoli, artichoke hearts, olives, spring onion/shallot, pine nuts, vegan mozzarella, salt and pepper. Drizzle with oil and bake for a further 8-10 minutes (220°C), until crust is golden. Garnish with rocket and micro herbs to serve.

Useful Items

sealable jar or bottle, baking paper, kitchen towel, silicone bread mat, pizza tray, rolling pin, large bowl, fork, bowl, oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Small and crunchy, hemp seeds are derived from the hemp plant. They contain all the amino acids (proteins) essential for the body and also are high in omega-3 and omega-6. These seeds can be great for reducing joint pain, improving gut health and reducing sugar cravings. You can find them at some major supermarkets, health food stores and online.
  • To make your own vegan mozzarella, refer to the recipe on this recipe chip.
  • You can store pesto in a sealable container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days or in the freezer for up to 3 months.
  • Gluten free green goddess pizza: Replace the wholemeal spelt flour with our Gluten free flour mix (www.cookidoo.com.au) or store-bought gluten free plain flour. Proceed as per recipe.
