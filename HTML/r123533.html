Layered Chicken Dinner

Ingredients

Chicken

  • 1 whole uncooked chicken (1.2 - 1.4 kg)
  • 1 lemon, flesh only (reserve zest)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary (approx. 10 cm each), leaves only
  • 4 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 1 tsp ground paprika
  • 1 tbsp fine sea salt, plus extra to taste

Pumpkin Soup

  • 120 g leeks, trimmed and cut into quarters
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 700 g butternut squash, deseeded, peeled and cut into pieces (5 cm)
  • 950 g water
  • 230 g small potatoes (approx. 5), skins on, cut into eighths
  • 170 g carrots (approx. 2), cut into pieces (2-3 cm)

Gravy

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 300 g eschalots (approx. 8), cut into halves
  • 50 g unsalted butter, cut into pieces
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary (approx. 10 cm each), leaves only
  • 1 - 2 tsp chicken stock paste (see tips)
  • 250 g water
  • 120 g red wine
  • 1 tsp maize flour
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper, to taste

Vegetables

  • 100 g broccoli, cut into large florets
  • 20 fresh green beans, trimmed

Preparation

Chicken

  1. Place whole chicken in Varoma dish and fill chicken cavity with whole peeled lemon, garlic and rosemary. Set Varoma with chicken aside.
  2. Place reserved lemon zest and parsley in mixing bowl then chop 20 sec/speed 9.  Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add paprika and salt then grind 10 sec/speed 9. Rub mixture over chicken skin and set Varoma aside.

Pumpkin Soup

  1. Without rinsing mixing bowl, place leeks and garlic in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add oil and sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  3. Add butternut squash and water. Place Varoma with chicken into position and steam 55 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  4. Weigh potatoes and carrots into Varoma dish, arranging them around the whole chicken. Secure Varoma lid, then steam 15 min/Varoma/speed 1. Check to see if chicken and vegetables are cooked. If not, prolong cooking time as necessary. You may want to set vegetables aside before proceeding (to prevent over cooking). Once chicken is cooked, remove Varoma and transfer chicken, potatoes and carrots into a large bowl and cover to keep warm.
  5. Insert measuring cup then blend 30 sec/speed 5 -9, increasing speed gradually. Transfer pumpkin soup into a separate thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and cover to keep warm. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Gravy

  1. Place oil, eschalots and butter in mixing bowl then sauté 7 min/120°C/speed 1, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  2. Add rosemary, stock paste, water and wine then cook 8 min/Varoma/speed 1, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  3. Add cornflour and pepper. Place Varoma dish into position then weigh broccoli and green beans into it. Secure Varoma lid, then steam 8 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  4. Set Varoma aside and transfer broccoli and green beans into large bowl with chicken and cover to keep warm, then strain gravy through simmering basket into a serving jug. Discard solids.
  5. Serve soup for entrée, followed by chicken, vegetables and gravy.

Useful Items

serving bowl with lid, serving bowl, cling film, serving jug

Hints & Tricks

  • Depending on the size of your chicken, you may have to adjust cooking times slightly.
  • Change the vegetables in this recipe to suit your tastes and adjust cooking times for softer or firmer vegetables depending on what you prefer.
