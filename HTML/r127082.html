Banoffee Pie Milkshake

Ingredients

  • 200 g tinned caramel (dulce de leche)
  • 550 g milk
  • 200 g cream, 35-37 % fat, chilled
  • 3 bananas (approx. 300 g), cut in pieces, frozen in a single layer (see tip)
  • 250 g vanilla ice cream
  • 1 tbsp chocolate shavings
Nutrition
per 1 glass
Calories
1782 kJ / 426 kcal
Protein
8 g
Carbohydrates
47 g
Fat
23 g

Preparation

  1. Place tinned caramel and 30 g milk in mixing bowl then heat 2 min/50°C/speed 4. Transfer caramel sauce to a bowl and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Place cream in mixing bowl and whip without measuring cup speed 3, without setting a time, until desired stiffness is achieved, watching carefully to avoid overwhipping.  Remove butterfly whisk, transfer whipped cream to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place bananas, 520 g milk, vanilla ice cream and 5 tbsp reserved caramel sauce in mixing bowl then blend 30 sec/speed 10. Swirl the remaining caramel sauce around the inside of 6 large glasses. Transfer banana-caramel milkshake to decorated glasses and garnish with reserved whipped cream and chocolate shavings. Serve immediately.
