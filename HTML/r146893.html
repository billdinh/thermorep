Wonton Soup

Ingredients

Wontons

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 2 cm fresh root ginger, peeled, cut in round slices (2 mm)
  • 1 spring onion, white part only
  • ½ - 1 long red chilli, deseeded if preferred (optional)
  • 1 sprig fresh coriander
  • 120 g pork mince, broken in pieces
  • 120 g prawns, raw, peeled, deveined
  • 1 egg yolk, from medium egg
  • 1 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 Tbsp rice wine vinegar
  • peanut oil, for greasing
  • 20 - 30 wonton wrappers, any shape
  • 1 egg white, from medium egg, lightly whisked

Broth

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 cm fresh root ginger, peeled, cut in round slices (2 mm)
  • 2 spring onions, white part only
  • 1 Tbsp peanut oil
  • 1100 g water
  • 1 Tbsp chicken stock paste (see tips)
  • 1 tsp fish sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 140 g pak choi, thinly sliced
  • 20 g dried shiitake mushrooms, soaked in boiling water for 30 minutes, then drained
  • 35 g tinned bamboo shoots, drained

Preparation

Wontons

  1. Place garlic, ginger, spring onion, chilli and coriander in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and repeat chopping 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add pork mince and prawns then chop Turbo/0.5 sec/3-4 times.
  3. Add egg yolk, soy sauce, sesame oil and rice wine vinegar then mix 1 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Grease Varoma dish and tray with peanut oil then set aside.
  5. Place 1 wonton wrapper onto a clean work surface. Place 1 heaped tsp of mince mixture in the middle of wonton wrapper.
  6. Brush all edges of the wrapper with a little egg white, then fold wonton wrapper in half diagonally to enclose filling, forming a triangle shape.
  7. Using your fingertips, seal all edges to eliminate any air pockets. Gently pull 2 remaining corners together (over enclosed filling) and press together to seal.
  8. Repeat with remaining filling and wrappers to make approx. 20 wontons (you may have leftover wrappers). Place all wontons into Varoma dish and onto Varoma tray, then set aside.

Broth

  1. Place garlic, ginger and spring onions in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add oil and sauté 2 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Add water, stock paste, fish sauce and sesame oil then cook 15 min/100°C/speed 1.
  4. Place Varoma with wontons into position and cook 12 min/Varoma/speed 1. Set Varoma aside.
  5. Add pak choi, shiitake mushrooms and bamboo shoots then cook 2 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  6. Divide wontons between serving bowls, then ladle broth over wontons and garnish with extra sliced chilli before serving.
