Quinoa vegetable purée

Ingredients

  • 1260 g water, plus extra if needed
  • 130 g white quinoa
  • 130 g carrot, peeled and cut into pieces
  • 250 g sweet potato, peeled and cut into pieces
  • 50 g butter
  • 200 g zucchini, cut into pieces (2-3 cm)
  • 150 g ripe pear, peeled, cored and cut into pieces
  • ¼ tsp ground cinnamon

Preparation

  1. Place 1200 g of the water into mixing bowl and insert simmering basket. Weigh quinoa into it and cook 20 min/100°C/speed 2. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula. Discard cooking water, pouring water through the simmering basket to catch any quinoa that has fallen through the basket into the mixing bowl. Set quinoa aside to drain. Dry mixing bowl.
  2. Place carrot and sweet potato into mixing bowl and grate 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add butter and remaining 60 g water, then cook 8 min/95°C/speed 2.
  4. Add reserved quinoa, zucchini, pear and cinnamon, then combine 6 sec/speed 6.
  5. Cook 4 min/95°C/speed 3. For older babies who are ready for more texture, serve as is, without further blending.
  6. For younger babies, blend 2 min/speed 1-8, increasing speed gradually from speed 1 to speed 8, until smooth.
  7. Set aside 1 serving, then freeze the remainder in individual portions for future use. If needed, stir through a little more water or your baby's milk to get the consistency just right before serving.

Hints & Tricks

  • Quinoa is gluten free and it's a wonderfully nutritious source of fibre, protein, iron, zinc and calcium. Botanically, it's actually not a grain - it's a seed, from the same family as beetroot and spinach. You might like to add it to your fruit purées too (just cook it first following the same method as this recipe). It tends to thicken purées, so add as much water or your baby's milk as needed to achieve the desired consistency.
