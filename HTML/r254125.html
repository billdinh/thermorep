Curried Cauliflower and Chickpea Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 can chickpeas (approx. 15 oz)
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 3 oz extra virgin olive oil, divided
  • 16 oz cauliflower, broken into md. florets
  • 2 oz tahini, well stirred
  • 1 oz lemon juice
  • 2 oz lime juice
  • 1 tbsp honey, to taste
  • ½ tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced (½ in.), (approx. 4 oz)
  • 4 tbsp raisins
  • 2 tbsp cilantro, chopped
  • ½ package baby spinach, to taste

Preparation

  1. Place chickpeas into simmering basket, rinse with cold running water and allow to drain. Transfer to a serving bowl and set aside.
  2. Place curry powder and 2 oz oil into mixing bowl and without measuring cup cook 10 min/212°F/speed 1.
  3. Add cauliflower, insert measuring cup and cook 10 min/212°F//speed 1.
  4. Add tahini, 1 oz oil, lemon and lime juices, honey, turmeric and salt and mix 1 min//speed 1.
  5. Transfer to bowl with reserved chickpeas. Add bell peppers, raisins, cilantro and spinach. Toss through to combine and serve.
