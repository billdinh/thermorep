Pork belly ramen

Ingredients

  • 40 g dark soy sauce
  • 40 g oyster sauce
  • 20 g fish sauce
  • 270 g pork belly, rind removed and meat cut into pieces (2 cm)
  • 480 g chicken winglets (approx. 4 winglets)
  • 4 sprigs fresh coriander, roots trimmed, stalks and leaves separated
  • 2 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 1 tsp whole black peppercorns
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 100 g fresh shiitake mushrooms, cut into thin slices (3 mm)
  • 1250 g water
  • 3 eggs
  • 150 g fresh ramen noodles
  • 200 g Chinese broccoli (gai lan), trimmed, cut into pieces (2 cm)
  • boiling water, for soaking
  • bean sprouts, to serve
  • 2 spring onions/shallots, trimmed and cut on the diagonal, to serve
  • dried chilli flakes, to serve (optional)

Preparation

  1. Place a large bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh soy sauce into it, then weigh oyster sauce, fish sauce, pork and chicken into it and toss to combine. Cover with plastic wrap and place into refrigerator for a minimum of 3 hours or overnight to marinate.
  2. Place coriander roots and stalks, ginger, peppercorns and garlic into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 7.
  3. Add mushrooms and water. Insert simmering basket and place marinated chicken into it. Place Varoma dish into position and place marinated pork into it. Secure Varoma lid and steam 35 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  4. Add eggs to Varoma dish. Secure Varoma lid and steam 8 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  5. Remove Varoma and transfer pork into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl. Transfer eggs into a separate bowl and set aside to cool. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula, add chicken to bowl with pork and cover to keep warm.
  6. Place a separate thermal serving bowl or other large bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh noodles into it. Set aside.
  7. Place Varoma dish back into position and weigh Chinese broccoli (gai lan) into it. Secure Varoma lid and steam 4-6 min/Varoma//speed 2, until just cooked. Meanwhile, cover noodles with boiling water and set aside for 2 minutes. Drain and return to bowl, cover to keep warm.
  8. Peel cooled eggs and cut into halves.
  9. To serve, divide noodles between 4 serving bowls. Top each bowl with reserved chicken, pork, Chinese broccoli (gai lan) and broth. Garnish with bean sprouts, spring onions/shallots, chilli flakes (optional), peeled eggs and coriander leaves just before serving.

Useful Items

large bowl, plastic wrap, bowl, thermal serving bowl or other large bowl, 4 serving bowls, refrigerator
