Cypriot grain salad

Ingredients

Yoghurt dressing

  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 200 g natural yoghurt

Salad

  • 50 g pumpkin seeds
  • 40 g slivered almonds
  • 45 g sunflower seeds
  • ½ bunch fresh coriander, leaves only
  • ½ bunch fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 2 spring onions/shallots, trimmed and cut into quarters
  • 160 g freekeh
  • 90 g French green lentils
  • 1250 g water
  • 70 g raisins
  • 30 g lemon juice
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 pomegranate, arils removed

Preparation

Yoghurt dressing

  1. Place cumin, honey and yoghurt into mixing bowl and mix 20 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix for a further 20 sec/speed 6. Transfer into a small bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Salad

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Line a baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place simmering basket onto mixing bowl lid and weigh pumpkin seeds, almonds and sunflower seeds into it. Scatter onto baking tray and toast for 5 minutes (180°C) or to your liking.
  3. Place coriander, parsley and spring onions/shallots into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a serving bowl and set aside.
  4. Place simmering basket onto mixing bowl lid and weigh freekah and lentils into it. Rinse under water. Place 1000 g of the water into mixing bowl and insert simmering basket with freekah and lentils then cook 10 min/100°C/speed 3.
  5. Stir lentils with aid of spatula and add remaining 250 g water to cover lentils and freekah. Cook 12 min/80°C/speed 3. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula. Rinse under cold running water, then set aside to drain. Transfer into serving bowl.
  6. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weight raisins and lemon juice into it. Add olive oil, reserved toasted seeds and raisins with lemon juice to serving bowl and toss to combine. Garnish with pomegranate and serve with Yoghurt dressing.

Useful Items

small bowl , baking tray (30 x 40 cm), baking paper, serving bowl, oven
