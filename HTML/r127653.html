Raspberry and Basil Sangria

Ingredients

  • 150 g sugar
  • 250 g water
  • 8 sprigs fresh basil, plus extra to garnish
  • 2 lemons, preferably organic, 1 lemon, halved and 1 lemon, cut in slices
  • 180 g fresh raspberries
  • 500 g sparkling mineral water, chilled
  • 1 bottle white wine (75 cl), chilled

Preparation

  1. Place sugar and water in mixing bowl then cook 8 min/100°C/speed 1.
  2. Add basil and transfer to a bowl. Allow syrup to cool to room temperature for about 20 minutes, then discard the basil sprigs.
  3. Place lemon halves, 80 g raspberries and sparkling water in mixing bowl. Hold measuring cup in place and crush 1 sec/speed 10. Insert simmering basket, hold it in place with spatula and strain lemonade into one large jug.
  4. Add the remaining 100 g raspberries, lemon slices, basil syrup and white wine to the jug. Serve immediately, garnished with basil sprigs.

Useful Items

2 l jug

Hints & Tricks

  • For a non-alcoholic version replace the wine with sparkling water.
  • Replace the raspberries with any kind of berries (e.g. strawberries, blueberries, blackberries).
  • The syrup can be made up to 1 week ahead and stored in the refrigerator.
  • Serve with raspberry basil ice cubes: add a fresh basil leaf and a raspberry or two to each ice cube before freezing.
