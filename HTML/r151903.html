Meat loaf

Ingredients

  • 400 g water
  • 6 eggs
  • 50 g white bread, fresh or stale, cut in pieces
  • 100 g milk
  • 20 g oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 80 g onions, cut in halves
  • 800 g mixed minced meat (e.g. pork and beef), broken up if necessary
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • ½ tsp sweet paprika
Nutrition
per 1 slice
Calories
694 kJ / 166 kcal
Protein
13 g
Carbohydrates
2 g
Fat
12 g

Preparation

  1. Place water in mixing bowl, insert simmering basket and place 4 eggs in it (reserving 2 eggs for later). Steam 14 min/Varoma/speed 1. Immediately remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and run eggs under cold water to stop cooking and set aside.
  2. Place a small bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh white bread and milk into it. Set bowl aside and leave to soak.
  3. Preheat oven to 180°C. Grease a loaf tin (approx. 30 cm x 12 cm x 10 cm) and set aside.
  4. Place oil and onions in mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  6. Squeeze milk out of bread and discard milk. Add bread, mixed minced meat, remaining 2 raw eggs, salt, black pepper and sweet paprika and knead with aid of spatula 1 min/.
  7. Transfer ⅓ of mixture to prepared loaf tin. Peel 4 reserved hard-boiled eggs and place in loaf tin, on top of meat mixture, distributing them along the length of the tin. Cover with remaining meat loaf mixture. Press firmly and smooth the surface, then bake for 40-50 minutes (180°C). Serve warm or cold.

Useful Items

loaf tin (30 cm x 12 cm x 10 cm), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Vary the spices and adjust the amount according to your preference.
  • To use freshly minced meat, freeze pieces (3 cm) of sinew-free meat in a single layer for 30 minutes. Before starting the recipe, place partially-frozen meat in mixing bowl and mince 10-15 sec/speed 6.
