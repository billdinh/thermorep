Carrot, Pumpkin and Chard Pie

Ingredients

Vegetables

  • 25 g vegetarian hard cheese, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 50 g stale white bread, torn in pieces
  • 10 g fresh coriander, leaves only
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 300 g carrots, cut in pieces
  • 5 g fresh root ginger, peeled, cut in round slices (2 mm)
  • 300 g pumpkin flesh, diced (3 cm)
  • 300 g onions, sliced
  • 50 g olive oil
  • 50 g water
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 300 g sweet potatoes, diced (2 cm)
  • 100 g red peppers, cut in pieces (3 cm)
  • 100 g Swiss chard, thick stalks removed, sliced (3 cm)

Béchamel Sauce

  • 500 g whole milk
  • 40 g butter, diced
  • 40 g plain flour
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper

Preparation

Vegetables

  1. Place cheese, bread and coriander in mixing bowl then chop 15 sec/speed 9. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place garlic, carrots and ginger in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place pumpkin, onions, oil, water, reserved carrot mixture and salt in mixing bowl then sauté 5 min/120°C//speed .
  4. Add sweet potatoes and cook 10 min/100°C//speed 0.5.
  5. Add peppers and chard, mix well with spatula then transfer to a deep-sided baking dish (approx. 32 cm x 20 cm x 5 cm). Clean mixing bowl.

Béchamel Sauce

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Place all sauce ingredients in mixing bowl and cook 8 min/90°C/speed 4. Pour over vegetables in baking dish, sprinkle with reserved breadcrumbs then bake for 30 minutes (180°C) until golden brown. Allow to cool for 5 minutes before serving.

Useful Items

baking dish (32 cm x 20 cm x 5 cm), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Sweet potato is rich in vitamin A which is important for eye health.
  • For a dairy-free béchamel sauce use: 700 g soya milk, 30 g groundnut oil, 90 g plain flour, 1 tsp fine sea salt, 1 pinch ground nutmeg, 1 pinch ground black pepper - with the same settings as in step 7.
  • To make the recipe completely dairy-free, omit cheese in step 1.
