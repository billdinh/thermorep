Curried Sausages and Lentils

Ingredients

  • ½ tsp dried fennel seeds
  • ½ tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander seeds
  • 2 tsp ground turmeric
  • 2 cm fresh root ginger, peeled
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1 - 2 fresh red chillies, deseeded
  • 4 sprigs fresh coriander
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 100 g carrots, cut in pieces
  • 2 celery stalks, cut in pieces
  • 100 g red peppers, deseeded and cut in pieces
  • 2 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 Tbsp tamarind paste
  • 1 Tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 Tbsp dark brown sugar
  • 900 g water
  • 300 g French green lentils
  • 500 g uncooked sausages
  • 270 g coconut cream
  • 60 g peanut butter
  • 150 g broccoli florets
  • 150 g cauliflower florets
  • 100 g fresh green beans, trimmed and cut in thirds

Preparation

  1. Place fennel, cumin and coriander seeds in mixing bowl then toast 5 min/Varoma/speed 1. Grind 30 sec/speed 10.
  2. Add ground turmeric, ginger, garlic, chillies and coriander then chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add olive oil and cook 3 min/100°C/speed 1.
  4. Add carrots, celery and red peppers then chop 3 sec/speed 5.
  5. Add soy sauce, tamarind paste, oyster sauce, brown sugar, water and lentils then cook 20 min/Varoma//speed .
  6. Place Varoma into position, insert Varoma tray, then weigh in sausages. Cover Varoma with Varoma lid and cook 15-20 min/Varoma//speed , or until sausages are cooked through. Remove Varoma tray and set sausages aside to cool.
  7. Add coconut cream and peanut butter to mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish into position, then weigh in broccoli, cauliflower and green beans. Cover Varoma with Varoma lid and steam 8-10 min/Varoma//speed , or until vegetables are cooked.
  8. Remove Varoma and set aside. Transfer curried lentils and steamed vegetables to a large bowl and cover to keep warm. Peel casings from reserved cool sausages and cut into slices (1-2 cm). Stir sausage slices through lentil and vegetable mixture then serve.

Hints & Tricks

  • Vegetarian option: omit the sauages and increase vegetables by 500 g. Steam additional vegetables on the Varoma tray in step 7 and adjust cooking time accordingly. You may want to rotate your vegetables in the Varoma dish and tray half-way through cooking time to ensure even cooking.
