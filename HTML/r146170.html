Chicken with sparkling wine cream sauce

Ingredients

  • 600 - 800 g chicken tenderloins
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 20 g lemon juice
  • 4 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves only
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 150 g leeks, white part only, cut into pieces (2 cm)
  • 1 stalk celery, cut into pieces (2 cm)
  • 3 carrots, 1 carrot cut into pieces (2 cm), 2 carrots cut into thin slices (3 mm)
  • 2 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 50 g salted butter
  • 300 g sparkling wine
  • 50 g water
  • 1 tsp Chicken stock paste (see Tip)
  • 2 potatoes, peeled and cut into thin slices (3 mm)
  • 1 zucchini, cut into slices (1 cm)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 100 g double cream

Preparation

  1. Line Varoma dish with baking paper. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh chicken into it, then set aside.
  2. Place 1 of the garlic cloves, Dijon mustard, lemon juice, thyme and oil into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Pour over reserved chicken and stir to coat. Set aside.
  3. Without cleaning mixing bowl, place leek, remaining 2 garlic cloves, celery, 1 carrot (cut into 2 cm pieces) and parsley into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add butter and cook 8 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  5. Add sparkling wine, water and stock paste. Place Varoma dish into position. Place Varoma tray into position and place potatoes, zucchini and remaining 2 carrots onto it. Sprinkle with salt, pepper and oregano and toss to combine. Secure Varoma lid and cook 20 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  6. Stir with spatula to ensure even cooking, then secure Varoma lid and steam 10-15 min/Varoma/speed 2 or until chicken is cooked through and vegetables are cooked to your liking. Remove Varoma. Transfer vegetables into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and cover to keep warm. Transfer chicken into a separate thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and cover to keep warm. Carefully transfer chicken cooking juices in Varoma into a jug.
  7. Add reserved cooking juices (approx. 300 g) into mixing bowl.  Insert measuring cup. Blend 1 min/speed 5 -7, increasing speed gradually from speed 5 to speed 7.
  8. Add cream and cook 5 min/90°C/speed 4.
  9. Divide chicken and vegetables between serving plates and drizzle with sparkling wine cream sauce to serve.

Useful Items

baking paper, thermal serving bowl or other large bowl, jug, 4 serving plates
