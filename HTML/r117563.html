Wild Rice and Tuna Salad

Ingredients

  • 1000 g water
  • 2 tsp fine sea salt
  • 300 g wild rice mixture
  • 100 g frozen green peas
  • 100 g courgettes, cut in pieces (5 mm)
  • 400 g mixed vegetables (e.g. 200 g carrots, 100 g celery, 100 g green beans) cut in pieces
  • 3 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 60 g balsamic vinegar
  • 60 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 200 g tuna, tinned, in brine, drained

Preparation

  1. Place water and 1 tsp salt in mixing bowl, insert simmering basket and weigh in wild rice then cook 20 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  2. Place Varoma dish into position then weigh in peas and courgettes. Cook 10 min/Varoma/speed 1. Transfer cooked peas and courgettes to a large salad bowl. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and add rice to salad bowl. Discard water then clean mixing bowl.
  3. Place mixed vegetables, parsley, remaining 1 tsp salt, pepper, balsamic vinegar and olive oil in mixing bowl then chop 4-5 sec/speed 5.
  4. Add tuna and mix 20 sec/speed 2. Add to salad bowl and stir with spatula. Transfer to a plastic container, allow to cool then seal with lid. Store in fridge until ready to take on your picnic.

Useful Items

salad bowl, plastic container

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve as a main meal or as a side dish, or as part of a buffet or picnic menu.
  • Chopped nuts or grated cheese can be added for additional flavour or texture.
  • Try different vegetables like pumpkin or turnip, cut into pieces.
  • Try whole grains or brown rice in this recipe instead of the wild rice mixture. Soak rice in cold water for at least one hour before starting.
