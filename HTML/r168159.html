Salmon kabsa

Ingredients

  • 250 g basmati rice
  • 1000 g water
  • 160 g onions, cut into quarters
  • 6 garlic cloves
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 2 tsp Arabian spice mix
  • ½ tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 150 g tomatoes, cut into quarters
  • 15 sprigs fresh coriander, 10 sprigs roughly chopped and 5 sprigs finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp tomato purée (concentrated)
  • 1 fresh chilli, deseeded and cut into pieces (optional)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper
  • 600 g fresh salmon fillets, skinless and boneless, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 loomi (dried limes), pierced with a knife
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 4 cardamom pods
  • 4 cloves
  • 15 g toasted pine nuts, to garnish

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh rice and 200 g water into it, set aside to soak.
  2. Place onions, garlic and oil into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  4. Add Arabian spice mix, turmeric, cumin and sauté 1 min/120°C/speed 2.
  5. Add tomatoes, 10 sprigs roughly chopped fresh coriander, tomato purée, chili, salt and pepper and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Distribute salmon in a bowl and add 4 heaped tbsp of the mixture from mixing bowl, mix to coat and set aside to marinate.
  6. Cook remaining mixture in the mixing bowl 7 min/100°C/speed 1.
  7. Drain rice through simmering basket and insert simmering basket into mixing bowl. Add dried limes, cinnamon stick, cardamom and cloves and mix with spatula. Add remaining 800 g water into mixing bowl. Stir contents of simmering basket with spatula again to make sure the limes and spices are covered with the rice.
  8. Distribute salmon pieces into Varoma dish and tray, set Varoma into position and steam 16 min/Varoma/speed 2. Remove Varoma and set aside.
  9. Insert measuring cup and cook rice a further 5 min/Varoma/speed 2. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula, transfer cooked rice to a serving dish and remove cinnamon stick. Place salmon on top of rice. Sprinkle with finely chopped coriander leaves and roasted pine nuts before serving.

Useful Items

bowl, serving dish

Hints & Tricks

  • Squeeze the cooked dried limes over the fish for extra flavor.
  • Serve with Dakkous on the side.
  • When serving, if more moisture is desired you can drizzle salmon and rice with some of the cooking liquid from the mixing bowl.
