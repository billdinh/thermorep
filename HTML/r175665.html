Red Grape and Olive Oil Cake

Ingredients

  • olive oil, for greasing
  • 100 g olive oil
  • 3 large eggs
  • 190 g caster sugar
  • 120 g milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ½ lemon, grated zest only
  • 200 g self-raising flour
  • 90 g maize flour, white
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 280 g red grapes, seedless
  • 2 Tbsp icing sugar, for dusting

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Line base of a springform tin (Ø 24 cm) with baking paper and grease sides with olive oil.
  2. Place a jug on mixing bowl lid, weigh in olive oil then set jug aside.
  3. Insert butterfly whisk. Place eggs and sugar in mixing bowl then mix 3 min/37°C/speed 4.
  4. Mix 1 min/speed 4 while very slowly pouring reserved olive oil onto mixing bowl lid, letting it drizzle around measuring cup in a thin stream onto rotating blades.
  5. Add milk, vanilla extract, grated lemon zest, self-raising flour, maize flour, salt and baking powder then mix 5 sec/speed 3.5.
  6. Remove butterfly whisk and carefully finish stirring with spatula, if necessary. Transfer to prepared tin, cover with 140 g red grapes and bake for 15 minutes (180°C). Scatter over remaining 140 g red grapes and bake for a further 25-30 minutes (180°C), or until golden. Allow to cool in tin for 10 minutes before removing to a serving plate and leaving to cool completely.
  7. Dust with icing sugar before serving.

Useful Items

baking paper, springform tin (Ø 24 cm), jug, serving plate, oven
