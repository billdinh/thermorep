English muffins

Ingredients

  • 15 g millet
  • 120 g plant-based milk of choice
  • 25 g vegan butter (see Tip)
  • 15 g raw sugar
  • 120 g filtered water
  • 1 tsp dried instant yeast
  • extra virgin olive oil, for greasing and frying
  • 190 g wholemeal spelt flour
  • 190 g wholemeal plain flour
  • 1 tsp salt

Preparation

  1. Place millet into mixing bowl and mill 20 sec/speed 10. Transfer onto a plate and set aside.
  2. Place milk, vegan butter and sugar into mixing bowl and heat 2 min 30 sec/90°C/speed 1. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place water and yeast into mixing bowl and heat 1 min/37°C/speed 1. Meanwhile, grease a large bowl and set aside.
  4. Add flours, salt and reserved melted butter mixture and mix 20 sec/speed 4.
  5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then knead 3 min/. Transfer into prepared bowl, cover with plastic wrap and set aside to prove until doubled in size (approx. 2 hours).
  6. Transfer dough onto a silicone bread mat or floured work surface. Roll dough out to approx. 1 cm thickness. Using a pastry cutter or cookie cutter (9 cm), cut out 8 rounds. Dip rounds into reserved millet on each side, then place onto a baking tray. Cover with plastic wrap and set aside to prove for 30 minutes.
  7. Place a large non-stick frying pan over low-medium heat and add a little oil. Working in batches, add muffins to pan and cook, with lid on, for 5 minutes on each side. Remove from heat and transfer onto a wire rack to cool. Cut in halves and toast before serving.

Useful Items

plate, bowl, large bowl, plastic wrap, silicone bread mat, rolling pin, large non-stick frying pan, wire rack, baking tray (30 x 40 cm), cookie cutter (9 cm), stove top
