Blueberry and White Chocolate Traybake

Ingredients

  • 200 g white chocolate, small pieces or callets (see tip)
  • ½ orange, thin peelings of skin only
  • 80 g granulated sugar
  • 220 g unsalted butter, soft, plus extra for greasing
  • 100 g caster sugar
  • 3 medium eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla sugar, homemade
  • 220 g plain flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
  • 125 g fresh blueberries
  • icing sugar, for dusting

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Grease sides and line base of a square cake tin (23 cm) with baking paper.
  2. Place chocolate in mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place orange peel and granulated sugar in mixing bowl then grind 10 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add butter, caster sugar, eggs and vanilla sugar then mix 40 sec/speed 4.
  5. Add flour, baking powder and salt then mix 15 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  6. Add reserved chopped chocolate and mix 20 sec//speed 3 then finish mixing in by hand with spatula. Transfer to prepared tin, then scatter blueberries evenly over the top, pushing some in gently with the back of a spoon. Bake for 40 minutes (180°C), covering with foil after 30 minutes to prevent top browning too much.
  7. Leave in tin to cool then remove. Dust with icing sugar before cutting in squares and serving.

Useful Items

baking paper, aluminium foil, square cake tin (23 cm), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Cookidoo® is full of inspiring cake recipes. Here, all of the preparation is done in your Thermomix®, from chopping the chocolate to making orange-flavoured sugar and mixing the batter together. A great option when feeding a crowd, the pop of colour and flavour from the blueberries is simply delicious.
  • If using chocolate callets, omit step 2 and add to mixture in step 6.
