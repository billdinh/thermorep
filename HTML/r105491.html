Beef Stroganoff

Ingredients

  • 3 ½ oz onions, cut in halves
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 oz unsalted butter
  • 2 tbsp ground sweet paprika
  • 24 - 32 oz filet mignon, cut into strips (approx. ½ in. x 2 in.)
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 8 oz fresh white button mushrooms, quartered
  • 8 oz water
  • 1 heaping tsp beef stock paste
  • ¼ - 1 tsp ground cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 2 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 tbsp corn starch
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 3 ½ oz sour cream
Nutrition
per 1 portion

Preparation

  1. Place onions and garlic into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add butter and 1 tbsp of paprika, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and sauté 5 min/212°F/speed 1.
  3. Add filet mignon, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and sauté 4 min/250°F//speed 1.
  4. Add tomato paste, mushrooms, water, beef stock, 1 tbsp paprika, cayenne pepper, Dijon mustard, corn starch, salt and pepper, insert measuring cup and cook 10 min/212°F//speed 1.
  5. Add sour cream and mix 1 min//speed 1. Serve hot.
