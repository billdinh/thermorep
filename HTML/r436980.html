Vegan Parmesan Crackers

Ingredients

  • 7 oz all-purpose flour, plus extra to dust
  • 2 tbsp nutritional yeast, plus extra to spinkle for topping
  • 1 tsp maple syrup
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 oz extra virgin olive oil, plus extra to brush
  • 4 oz water
  • everything bagel seasoning, to sprinkle for topping
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
67 kJ / 16 kcal
Protein
0 g
Carbohydrates
2 g
Fat
1 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F. Line two baking sheets (approx. 13 in. x 18 in.) with parchment and set aside.
  2. Place flour, nutritional yeast, maple syrup, salt, olive oil and water into mixing bowl and knead 6 sec/. Transfer to a floured surface, knead into a ball and let rest 10 minutes.
  3. Dust work surface with flour and cut dough in half. Roll each piece of dough into a rectangle (12 in. x 18 in.) and using a lightly dusted rolling pin, transfer dough onto prepared baking sheets.
  4. Brush dough with olive oil and cut each rectangle into 40 pieces. Sprinkle dough with nutritional yeast and everything bagel seasoning. Press seasoning into dough and bake for 10-12 minutes (450°F) rotating pans halfway through baking. Serve immediately or store in an airtight container.

Useful Items

parchment paper, rolling pin, pastry brush, baking sheets (approx. 13 in. x 18 in.), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • To make homemade everything bagel seasoning: place 2 tbsp sesame seeds, 2 tbsp poppy seeds, 1 tbsp dried minced onion, 2 tsp dried minced garlic and 1 tsp coarse kosher salt into mixing bowl and mix 5 sec//speed 5. Store in an airtight jar.
