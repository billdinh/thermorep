Asparagus tart with sour cream pastry

Ingredients

Pastry

  • 250 g unsalted butter, cut into cubes, plus extra for greasing
  • 325 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 1 pinch fine salt
  • 160 g sour cream

Filling

  • 500 g water
  • 200 g asparagus, woody ends trimmed
  • cold water, for soaking
  • 100 g cheddar cheese, cut into cubes (2 cm)
  • ½ bunch fresh chives, cut into small pieces (2 mm)
  • 1 lemon, zest only, finely grated, no white pith
  • 100 g ricotta cheese
  • 3 eggs
  • 100 g sour cream
  • ½ tsp salt flakes
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 6 spring onions/shallots, cut into pieces same length as asparagus, then cut into halves

Preparation

Pastry

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C. Grease a rectangular, loose bottom tart tin (30 x 20 cm) and set aside.
  2. Place butter, flour and salt into mixing bowl and mix 8 sec/speed 7.
  3. Add sour cream and mix 10 sec/speed 6, until pastry comes together. Transfer onto a silicone bread mat or lightly floured work surface, carefully form into a ball then wrap in plastic wrap and place into refrigerator for 30 minutes. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Dust a clean work surface with a little flour. Roll out pastry to approx. 5 mm thickness. Transfer into prepared tin with extra pastry overhanging. Place pastry into refrigerator for 10 minutes. Prick pastry base with a fork 12-15 times. Line pastry base with baking paper and fill with rice, dried beans or pie weights and bake for 10 minutes (200°C). Remove baking paper and weights and bake for a further 5 minutes (200°C), until lightly golden and dry. Set aside to cool.

Filling

  1. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh asparagus into it. Secure Varoma lid and steam 8 min/Varoma/speed 1. Transfer asparagus into a bowl with cold water to cease cooking. Once asparagus is cool, drain and pat dry with paper towel. Discard water, then rinse and dry mixing bowl.
  2. Place cheese into mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 6.
  3. Add chives, half of the lemon zest, ricotta, eggs, sour cream, salt and pepper and mix 15 sec/speed 4.
  4. Transfer cheese mixture into tart shell and arrange asparagus and spring onions/shallots on top. Sprinkle with remaining lemon zest and bake for 25-30 minutes (200°C), or until golden and just set. Serve warm or allow to cool to room temperature and place into refrigerator until ready to serve. 

Useful Items

rectangular, loose bottom tart tin (30 x 20 cm), plastic wrap, silicone bread mat, pie weights, rice or dried beans, baking paper, rolling pin, paper towel, bowl, oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • To make individual tarts, bake in mini tart cases. Finely chop steamed asparagus and spring onions/shallots, and bake for 20 minutes (200°C).
  • You can replace asparagus with chopped kale, silverbeet or Swiss chard if preferred.
