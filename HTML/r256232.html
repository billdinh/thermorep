Chilli chicken with corn and avocado cream

Ingredients

Avocado cream

  • 1 - 2 avocados (approx. 250 g), flesh only
  • 100 g sour cream
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper

Chilli chicken

  • 2 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only
  • 50 g salted butter, softened
  • 150 g brown onion, cut into quarters
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 2 - 3 fresh long green chillies, trimmed and cut into halves (to taste)
  • ½ tsp ground cumin
  • 200 g canned chopped tomatoes
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 sweet corn cobs, husks removed and cobs cut into halves (approx. 4 cm)
  • 500 g skinless chicken breast fillets, cut into short strips (1 cm thickness)
  • 4 tortillas, warmed (optional - see Tips)

Preparation

Avocado cream

  1. Place avocado, sour cream, salt and pepper into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 3. Transfer into a bowl and place into refrigerator until ready to serve. Clean and dry mixing bowl (see Tips).

Chilli chicken

  1. Place coriander into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add butter and mix 10 sec/speed 4. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Do not clean mixing bowl.
  3. Place onion, garlic, oil and chillies into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add cumin and sauté 2 min/120°C/speed 2.
  5. Add tomatoes and salt. Place Varoma dish into position and place corn into it. Insert Varoma tray and weigh chicken onto it. Secure Varoma lid and cook 13-15 min/Varoma/speed 2, until chicken and corn are cooked to your liking.
  6. Remove Varoma and set aside. Transfer cooked chicken into chilli tomato sauce and stir with aid of spatula to combine.
  7. Spread corn with reserved coriander butter and serve with chilli chicken, tortillas (optional) and Avocado cream.

Useful Items

bowl, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • To save time, omit the Avocado cream and simply serve with sour cream.
  • You can serve this dish with rice instead of tortillas, if desired, however this may increase the time required for this recipe.
