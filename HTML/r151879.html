Vanilla Ice Cream

Ingredients

  • 2 vanilla pods
  • 600 g whole milk
  • 200 g cream, (whipping or double cream)
  • 180 g sugar
  • 6 egg yolks, from medium eggs
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt (optional)
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1123 kJ / 268 kcal
Protein
6 g
Carbohydrates
27 g
Fat
15 g

Preparation

  1. Split vanilla pods lengthwise and scrape out seeds. Place vanilla seeds and pods in mixing bowl. Add milk, cream, sugar, egg yolks and salt (if using) then cook 8 min/90Â°C/speed 2. Remove vanilla pods.
  2. Pour mixture, up to 4 cm deep, in shallow freezer-proof containers (e.g. aluminium, silicone) and cover with lid or cling film. Leave mixture to cool completely before freezing for at least 10 hours.
  3. Place frozen mixture in fridge for 15 minutes, then cut in pieces (4 cm) with a sharp knife. Place half the pieces in mixing bowl and crush 20 sec/speed 6 then, with aid of spatula, mix 10 sec/speed 4. Transfer ice cream to a bowl.
  4. Place remaining frozen ice cream pieces in mixing bowl and crush 20 sec/speed 6 then, with aid of spatula, mix 10 sec/speed 4. Transfer to bowl with ice cream and serve immediately.

Useful Items

cling film, long sharp knife, shallow freezer-proof containers (e.g. aluminium, silicone), freezer, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • For a firmer consistency, place the ice cream in the freezer for 15 minutes before serving.
  • Garnish with chopped pistachios, coconut flakes or chocolate drops.
  • Top with whipped cream and chocolate or fruit sauce.
