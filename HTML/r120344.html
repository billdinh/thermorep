Quinoa salad with chicken and avocado

Ingredients

  • 4 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 2 - 3 pieces lemon, zest only, no white pith (1 x 4 cm)
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 70 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 400 g skinless chicken breast fillets, cut into cubes (2-3 cm)
  • 2 tsp white wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 1210 g water
  • 100 g quinoa
  • 200 g cherry tomatoes, cut into halves
  • 150 g cucumber, cut into pieces (2-3 cm)
  • 100 g avocado, flesh only, cut into pieces (2-3 cm)

Preparation

  1. Place parsley, garlic, lemon zest and ½ teaspoon of the salt into mixing bowl and chop 7 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add 40 g of the oil and mix 5 sec/speed 4.
  3. Place chicken onto a large piece of baking paper (40 x 35 cm) and drizzle with dressing. Fold ends of paper to create a parcel, secure ends making sure there are no holes. Transfer parcels into Varoma dish and set Varoma aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Place vinegar, mustard, honey, ½ teaspoon of the salt, pepper, 2 teaspoon of the water and remaining 30 g oil into mixing bowl and mix 20 sec/speed 4. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  5. Without cleaning mixing bowl, place remaining 1200 g water and remaining 1 teaspoon salt into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket and weigh quinoa into it. Place Varoma with chicken parcel into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 12 min/Varoma/speed 1. Carefully open parcel and stir chicken with aid of spatula to ensure even cooking. Reseal parcel, secure Varoma lid and steam 12 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove Varoma and transfer chicken into a bowl to cool (approx. 15 minutes).
  6. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula. Tranfer quinoa into a large serving bowl and set aside to cool (approx.15 minutes). Drain excess liquid from chicken using simmering basket, then add chicken to quinoa. Place bowl with chicken mixture onto mixing bowl lid and weigh cherry tomatoes, cucumber and avocado into it, then stir to combine. Serve immediately or place into refrigerator until ready to serve. Drizzle with reserved dressing just before serving.

Useful Items

baking paper, bowl, serving bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • You can replace the chicken with fish in this recipe, but you will need to adjust cooking times accordingly.
