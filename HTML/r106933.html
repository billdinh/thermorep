Artichoke and Butterbean Dip

Ingredients

  • 3 ½ oz artichoke hearts
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 8 oz canned butter beans, drained and rinsed (approx. 1 x 15.5 oz can)
  • ½ tsp ground cumin
  • ½ oz lemon juice
  • 4 oz plain yogurt, unsweetened
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
251 kJ / 60 kcal
Protein
3 g
Carbohydrates
10 g
Fat
1 g

Preparation

  1. Place all ingredients into mixing bowl and increasing speed gradually mix 20 sec/speed 6 Scrape down sides and lid of mixing bowl with spatula and mix a further 10 sec/speed 8.
  2. Transfer to a serving bowl, cover with cling wrap and refrigerate until needed. Serve as a dip or spread.

Useful Items

serving bowl, cling wrap, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with pita and/or assorted sliced or whole raw vegetables.
  • Garnish with black pepper and lemon zest.
  • The term "butter bean" is widely used for a large, flat and yellow/white variety of lima bean.
