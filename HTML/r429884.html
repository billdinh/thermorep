Crispy Aubergine on Creamy Kohlrabi

Ingredients

  • 1 aubergine (approx. 250 g), sliced in rounds (5 mm)
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • ¾ tsp fine sea salt
  • ½ bunch fresh dill, leaves only
  • 100 g tortilla chips, vegan
  • 600 g water
  • 1 heaped tsp vegetable stock paste, homemade, vegan (see tip)
  • 400 g kohlrabi, diced (1 cm)
  • 4 Tbsp plain flour
  • 2 Tbsp cornflour
  • 100 g vegan cream cheese
  • 1 tsp agave syrup
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • sunflower oil, for frying

Preparation

  1. Place Varoma dish on a plate and place aubergine inside. Sprinkle with sugar and ½ tsp salt then set aside for 15 minutes. Meanwhile, continue with recipe.
  2. Place dill in mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place tortilla chips in mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a shallow dish. Rinse mixing bowl.
  4. Place 500 g water and vegetable stock in mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket and weigh in kohlrabi then cook 28 min/100°C/speed 1. Meanwhile, place flour in a shallow bowl. Place remaining 100 g water and cornflour in another shallow bowl then mix. Pat aubergine slices dry with paper towels.
  5. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and transfer cooking liquid to a jug.
  6. Place 100 g cooking liquid, cream cheese, cooked kohlrabi, ¾ reserved chopped dill, agave syrup, ¼ tsp salt and pepper in mixing bowl then mix 20 sec//speed 1.
  7. Heat 5 mm oil in a frying pan over a medium heat. Dip aubergine slices first in flour, then in cornflour mixture and finally in tortilla crumbs. Fry aubergines in hot oil on both sides until golden brown (approx. 2 minutes each side) and drain on paper towels. Arrange crunchy aubergines on creamy kohlrabi, sprinkle with remaining dill and serve.

Useful Items

frying pan, paper towels, jug, hob

Hints & Tricks

  • For a vegan stock paste, omit the Parmesan from the Vegetable Stock Paste in The Basic Cookbook.
