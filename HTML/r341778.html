Citrus quinoa salad with miso ginger dressing

Ingredients

Quinoa

  • 170 g tri-colour quinoa
  • 1000 g filtered water, plus extra for soaking
  • 1 tbsp Vegetable stock paste (see Tips)

Miso ginger dressing

  • 2 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 45 g sunflower seeds
  • 1 tbsp avocado oil
  • 1 tbsp shiro (white) miso paste
  • 20 - 40 g lemon juice, to taste
  • 1 tbsp pure maple syrup
  • 60 g filtered water
  • 1 pinch salt

Salad

  • 60 g fresh baby spinach leaves
  • 60 g rocket
  • 2 blood oranges, peeled and segmented (see Tips)
  • 2 navel oranges, peeled and segmented (see Tips)
  • 3 spring onions/shallots, trimmed and cut into thin slices
  • 1 tbsp pecans (optional)

Preparation

Quinoa

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh quinoa into it. Cover with water and set aside for 30 minutes to soak.
  2. Using simmering basket, thoroughly rinse and drain quinoa. Place water and stock paste into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket with quinoa and cook 15 min/100Â°C/speed 2. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and spread quinoa onto a large plate to cool. Rinse and dry mixing bowl.

Miso ginger dressing

  1. Place ginger and sunflower seeds into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 10.
  2. Add all remaining dressing ingredients into mixing bowl and blend 10 sec/speed 6 or until smooth and well combined. Transfer into a sealable container and place into refrigerator until ready to serve.

Salad

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh spinach and rocket into it. Arrange spinach and rocket onto a serving platter, top with cooled quinoa and orange segments. Garnish with spring onions/shallots and sprinkle with pecans (optional). Drizzle with some of the reserved Miso dressing to serve.

Useful Items

Hints & Tricks

  • Please refer to The Basic Cookbook for the Vegetable stock paste recipe.
  • You can use any type of oranges in this recipe (we recommend using whatever varieties are in season) or replace two of the oranges with two grapefruits.
