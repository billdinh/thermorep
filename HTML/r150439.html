Flat bread

Ingredients

  • 190 g water
  • 50 g hemp seeds (see Tips)
  • 175 g wholemeal spelt flour
  • 175 g white spelt flour
  • 1 tbsp olive oil, plus extra for frying
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • Â½ tsp salt

Preparation

  1. Place a jug onto mixing bowl lid and weigh water into it. Set aside.
  2. Place hemp seeds into mixing bowl and mill 1 min/speed 9.
  3. Add flours, oil, baking powder and salt and mix 5 sec/speed 3.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix 30 sec/speed 4, without measuring cup, slowly adding reserved water through hole in mixing bowl lid, until dough forms.
  5. Transfer dough onto a silicone bread mat or lightly floured work surface and divide into 10 equal portions. Using a rolling pin, roll each portion into a circle (approx. 3 mm thick and 17 cm diameter).
  6. Place a non-stick frying pan over high heat and add a little oil. Working 1 at a time, place flat bread into frying pan and fry for approx. 30 seconds or until small bubbles rise to the surface. Carefully flip bread and fry for a further 30 seconds. Transfer onto a large plate and cover with a kitchen towel to keep warm until ready to serve.

Useful Items

jug, large plate, kitchen towel, silicone bread mat, rolling pin, non-stick frying pan, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • You can find hemp seeds at health food stores or online. Alternatively, replace them with buckwheat groats.
  • These flat breads are great for wraps, burritos and tacos or serve with soups and stews.
