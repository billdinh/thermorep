Smashed roasted potatoes with rosemary salt

Ingredients

Rosemary salt

  • 10 sprigs fresh rosemary, leaves only
  • 250 g sea salt

Potatoes

  • 700 g water
  • 1000 g small potatoes
  • 1 - 2 tbsp olive oil, plus extra to serve
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary, leaves only
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
676 kJ / 161 kcal
Protein
4 g
Carbohydrates
21 g
Fat
6 g

Preparation

Rosemary salt

  1. Line a baking tray (20 x 30 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place rosemary into mixing bowl and mill 1 min/speed 9.
  3. Add salt into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 7. Transfer salt onto prepared baking tray and spread out evenly, then leave to dry out overnight. Transfer salt into a sealable jar or container to store and use as needed.

Potatoes

  1. Line a baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position, then weigh potatoes into it. Secure Varoma lid and steam 25-30 min/Varoma/speed 1, or until potatoes are cooked (see tips).
  3. Preheat oven to 200°C. Transfer potatoes onto prepared baking tray, then place into refrigerator to cool and dry out for 30 minutes.
  4. Using a fork, flatten the potatoes onto baking tray slightly. Drizzle with olive oil, then sprinkle with 1 teaspoon of the reserved rosemary salt and fresh rosemary leaves. Roast for 25-30 minutes (200°C) or until brown and crispy.
  5. Transfer potatoes onto a serving platter. Drizzle with a little extra olive oil, sprinkle with extra rosemary salt and serve immediately.

Useful Items

baking tray (20 x 30 cm), baking paper, sealable storage jar or container, baking tray (30 x 40 cm), serving platter, refrigerator, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • In step 5, potatoes should be cooked through but still firm enough that a knife can pass easily through without mashing.
  • Rosemary salt can be made in advance and stored in a cool, dry place in a sealable storage container until needed.
