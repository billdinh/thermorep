Tin Can Mealie Bread

Ingredients

  • 60 g vegetable oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 200 g milk
  • 2 medium eggs
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 140 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 170 g cornmeal, coarsely ground
  • 20 g sugar
  • 1 ½ tsp baking powder
  • 420 g tinned sweetcorn
  • 50 g Cheddar cheese, grated (optional)
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
1969 kJ / 470 kcal
Protein
13 g
Carbohydrates
55 g
Fat
21 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat barbecue. Brush 6 clean tin cans (approx. 280 g size) with vegetable oil. Dust with flour and set aside.
  2. Place vegetable oil, milk, eggs and salt in mixing bowl then stir 10 sec/speed 6.
  3. Add plain flour, cornmeal, sugar and baking powder then mix 5 sec/speed 4.
  4. Add sweetcorn and mix 5 sec/speed 3. Divide batter between prepared tins. Sprinkle with grated Cheddar cheese then cook on a medium high barbecue for 15 minutes. Serve hot or cold, in the tin.

Useful Items

tins, pastry brush, barbecue

Hints & Tricks

  • The tin can is ideal utensil for cooking the bread on the barbecue and for serving the bread in.
  • Add 30 g tomato purée for a tomato flavoured bread.
  • If tin cans are unavailable, use tin foil portion trays to cook the mealie bread in.
  • You can bake the bread in a preheated oven to 190°C for 15-20 minutes or until breads are firm to touch.
