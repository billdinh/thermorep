Marble Cake

Ingredients

  • 10 ½ oz unsalted butter, cut into pieces, plus extra to grease
  • 13 oz all-purpose flour, plus extra to dust
  • 10 oz sugar, divided
  • 1 tsp homemade vanilla sugar
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 5 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 2 oz whole milk, divided
  • 1 oz cocoa powder (2 tbsp)
Nutrition

1389 kJ / 332 kcal

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease and flour a bundt pan (8.5 in.- 9.5 in.) and set aside.
  2. Place butter into mixing bowl and mix 40 sec/speed 5.
  3. Add 9 oz sugar, vanilla sugar, salt, eggs, flour, baking powder and 1 oz milk into mixing bowl and mix 40 sec/speed 5. Transfer half of cake mixture into prepared bundt cake pan, leaving remainder in mixing bowl.
  4. Add cocoa powder, 1 oz sugar and 1 oz milk into mixing bowl and with aid of spatula mix 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Mix a further 5 sec/speed 5.
  6. Layer cocoa cake mixture into bundt cake pan. To create a marble pattern, swirl through both layers with a fork. Bake for 50-60 minutes (350°F). Allow cake to cool in pan (approx. 15 min.) before transferring to a serving plate and leaving to cool completely. Cut into slices and serve.

Useful Items

bundt cake pan (8.5 in.- 9.5 in.), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • If butter is very soft, omit mixing it by itself and add the butter with the sugar, eggs and flour and proceed with recipe.
  • Dust with confectioners sugar or cover with a chocolate glaze.
  • Butter cake: omit cocoa marbling. Simply transfer the entire cake mixture into prepared bundt cake pan and bake for 50-60 minutes (350°F). Allow cake to cool in pan for 15 minutes before transferring to a serving plate. Cool completely before slicing.
  • Flavor the cocoa mixture with ½ tsp rum extract or with 0.5 oz rum. If using rum, reduce milk from 1 oz milk to 0.5 oz.
