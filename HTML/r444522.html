Spicy Asian Snow Peas

Ingredients

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • ½ in. fresh ginger, peeled
  • 3 oz onions, in half moons
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp chili garlic sauce
  • 1 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp rice wine vinegar, unseasoned
  • 16 oz water
  • 12 oz snow peas, trimmed
  • 2 green onions, cut on the bias, to garnish
  • toasted sesame seeds, to garnish

Preparation

  1. Place garlic and ginger into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add onions, sesame oil, chili garlic sauce, hoisin sauce, soy sauce and rice vinegar and cook 5 min/212°F//speed 1. Transfer to a serving bowl and set aside.
  3. Place water into mixing bowl. Set Varoma into position and weigh in snow peas, secure Varoma lid and steam 13 min/Varoma/speed 2. Remove Varoma, set aside and allow to drain.
  4. Transfer drained snow peas to bowl with reserved sauce and toss to combine. Garnish with green onions and sesame seeds and serve immediately.

Hints & Tricks

  • Replace snow peas with snap peas and cook an additional 2 minutes, or until reaching desired tenderness.
  • Replace snow peas with green beans and cook an additional 5 minutes, or until reaching desired tenderness
