Chiang mai noodles

Ingredients

  • 200 - 250 g dried egg noodles
  • 6 eschalots, cut into halves
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 - 2 fresh red chillies, trimmed and cut into halves, deseeded if preferred
  • 40 g oil
  • 4 - 5 tbsp Thai red curry paste, to taste (see Tips)
  • 500 g boneless skinless chicken thigh, cut into thin strips
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp palm sugar, grated
  • ½ tsp ground turmeric
  • 400 g coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 2 tsp Chicken stock paste (see Tips)
  • 400 g water
  • 5 - 6 stalks pak choy, cut into halves
  • 2 - 4 spring onions/shallots, trimmed and cut into thin slices
  • 4 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only, for garnishing
  • 50 g fresh bean sprouts, for garnishing

Preparation

  1. Cook egg noodles as per packet instructions. Drain, cover and set aside.
  2. Place eschalots, garlic and chilli into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add oil and cook 3 min/100°C/speed 1.
  4. Add curry paste and chicken and cook 3 min/90°C//speed 1.
  5. Add fish sauce, palm sugar, turmeric, coconut milk, lime juice, stock paste and water and cook 5 min/90°C//speed 1.
  6. Add pak choy and cook 4 min/90°C//speed 1.
  7. Divide reserved noodles between serving bowls. Top with chicken and sauce, then garnish with spring onions/shallots, coriander leaves and bean sprouts just before serving.

Useful Items

Hints & Tricks

  • To make your own Thai red curry paste and Chicken stock paste, please refer to The Basic Cookbook or the Everyday Cookbook.
  • The heat and flavour of this dish will depend on the strength of the curry paste you use.
