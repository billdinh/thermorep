Hake and Potato Purée - from 9 months

Ingredients

  • 180 g potatoes, washed, peeled, diced (1-2 cm)
  • 150 g water
  • 50 g hake fillet, skinless, deboned, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 1 egg yolk, from medium egg
Nutrition
per 1 jar
Calories
1487 kJ / 351 kcal
Protein
23 g
Carbohydrates
42 g
Fat
10 g

Preparation

  1. Place potatoes and water in mixing bowl then cook 12 min/100°C/speed .
  2. Ensure all bones are removed from hake then add to mixing bowl and cook 4 min/100°C/speed .
  3. Add egg yolk and blend 30 sec/speed 6. Check food is fully puréed and thoroughly cooked then transfer to a serving bowl. Store remaining purée in an airtight container and refrigerate for up to 24 hours or freeze for up to 2 weeks. Check temperature is suitable for your baby before serving warm.

Useful Items

airtight container, refrigerator, freezer

Hints & Tricks

  • Egg yolks can be introduced from 6 months (egg whites a little later). Here it brings a creaminess to the purée, much like butter, but with more vitamins.
  • To make 6 jars, multiply quantities by 3 and, in step 1, cook 15 min/100°C/speed. Store extra purée in an airtight container and refrigerate for up to 24 hours or freeze for up to 2 weeks.
