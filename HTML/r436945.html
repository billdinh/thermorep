Pinot Noir Poached Pears

Ingredients

Pears

  • 16 oz pinot noir
  • 5 oz pomegranate juice
  • 2 oz sugar
  • ½ oz ginger, peeled, sliced
  • 1 star anise
  • ½ vanilla bean pod
  • 1 Bosc pear, peeled, halved, cored

Reduction

  • 3 oz Roquefort cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tsp chopped pistachios, toasted
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1891 kJ / 452 kcal
Protein
10 g
Carbohydrates
52 g

15 g

Preparation

Pears

  1. Place wine, pomegranate juice, sugar, ginger and star anise into mixing bowl. Split vanilla pod lengthwise and scrape out seeds. Add seeds and vanilla pod into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket, add pear halves and steam 20-25 min/Varoma/speed 2 or until pears have softened.
  2. Transfer steamed pears to a mason jar with a clamp lid (10 oz) and strain wine mixture through a fine mesh sieve. Add strained wine to mason jar with pears and let sit at room temperature for 1 hour.

Reduction

  1. Place wine mixture (without pears) back into mixing bowl and without measuring cup cook 40 min/Varoma/speed 1, until syrupy. Transfer wine reduction back into jar with pears and refrigerate until cool (approx. 1 hr.).
  2. Plate pear halves, drizzle with wine reduction and top with crumbled Roquefort. Garnish with toasted pistachios and serve.

Useful Items

mason jar with clamp lid (10 oz), fine mesh sieve, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Replace Roquefort cheese with two scoops of your favorite vanilla ice cream.
