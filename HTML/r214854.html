Creamy Vegetable Soup, Steamed Chicken with Vegetable Tagliatelle and Mustard Sauce, Milk Bread Loaves, Frozen Fruit Sorbet

Ingredients

Preparation Before Demo

  • 500 g chicken breast fillets, skinless, cut in strips (5 cm x 2 cm)
  • 1 - 2 carrots
  • 1 courgette
  • 450 g mixed vegetables, (e.g. potato, courgette, carrot, leek), cut in pieces

Milk Bread Loaves

  • 300 g milk
  • 50 g butter, diced, plus extra for greasing
  • 2 tsp dried instant yeast
  • 20 - 60 g sugar, to taste
  • 550 g strong white bread flour
  • 1 ½ tsp fine sea salt

Frozen Fruit Sorbet

  • 60 - 100 g granulated sugar, to taste
  • 500 g frozen fruit
  • 2 tsp lemon juice (optional)
  • 1 egg white, from medium or large egg (optional)

Baking

  • 1 egg yolk, beaten, from medium or large egg

Soup, Steamed Chicken and Vegetable Tagliatelle

  • 80 - 100 g onions, halved (1 small onion)
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 400 g water, plus extra for soup
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt, or to taste
  • 20 g butter (optional)

Sauce

  • 50 g crème fraîche
  • 1 Tbsp mustard
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 3 - 4 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 1 egg yolk (optional)

Preparation

Preparation Before Demo

  1. Place chicken in Varoma dish then insert Varoma tray. Use a vegetable peeler to cut carrots and courgette into long thin strips similar to tagliatelle pasta, stop before reaching the core of the carrots or the seeds in the courgette. Place carrot and courgette tagliatelle loosely onto Varoma tray then set Varoma aside. Cut remaining carrot and courgette cores in pieces then set aside in a small bowl. Place mixed vegetables in simmering basket then set aside.

Milk Bread Loaves

  1. Preheat oven to 50°C and grease a baking tray (see tip).
  2. Place milk, butter, yeast and sugar in mixing bowl then warm 3 min/37°C/speed 2.
  3. Add flour and salt then knead 3 min/. Tip out and divide dough into 9 equal-sized pieces. Shape each into a long baton (approx. 30 cm long) then use 3 batons to create each plaited loaf (3 loaves in total). Arrange on prepared tray then place in preheated oven to prove for 20 minutes (50°C, quick rise). Meanwhile, make sorbet.

Frozen Fruit Sorbet

  1. Place sugar in mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 10.
  2. Add frozen fruit, lemon juice (if using) and egg white (if using) then, while stirring with spatula through hole in mixing bowl lid, blend 1 min 30 sec/speed 5.
  3. Insert butterfly whisk then whip 1 min/speed 3. Transfer to serving bowls and enjoy, freezing any leftovers in a freezer-proof container. Clean mixing bowl by covering blade with water then add 1 drop washing up liquid. Set for 1 min/60°C/speed 6. Discard water, rinse and wipe dry then continue with demo.

Baking

  1. Brush loaves with beaten egg yolk then increase oven temperature to 180°C and bake for 22-28 minutes (180°C) or until breads sound hollow when tapped on the underside. Meanwhile, continue with demo.

Soup, Steamed Chicken and Vegetable Tagliatelle

  1. Place onions, garlic clove and olive oil in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add reserved carrot and courgette pieces then, without measuring cup, heat 1 min 30 sec/80°C/speed 1.
  3. Add water and salt, insert prepared simmering basket with vegetables, then place prepared covered Varoma into position. Steam 20 min/Varoma//speed . Once loaves are cooked, remove from oven and transfer to wire racks to cool.
  4. Remove Varoma and keep warm. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and transfer contents to mixing bowl. Add butter and blend 1 min/speed 5-10, increasing speed gradually.
  5. Add water up to the 1 or 1.5 litre mark (depending on type of vegetables and desired soup consistency) and mix 10 sec/speed 5.

Sauce

  1. To make the sauce, transfer almost all the soup to a serving bowl, keeping enough soup in the mixing bowl to cover the lowest blade of the mixing knife. Add crème fraîche, mustard, salt, pepper, parsley and egg yolk, mix 20 sec/speed 7, then heat 2 min/60°C/speed 2.

Serving

  1. Serve creamy vegetable soup hot or allow to cool, cover and refrigerate for later use. Transfer chicken to serving plates, arrange vegetable tagliatelle around the chicken and serve hot with sauce.

Useful Items

vegetable peeler, baking tray, serving bowls, pastry brush, freezer-proof container, wire racks, serving plates, bowl, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • If the oven does not have a 50°C setting, preheat oven to 100°C then turn off before placing loaves in warm oven to prove.
  • Fruits should be frozen in individual pieces or small clusters. Cut fruit in pieces, spread out on a tray, freeze, then use frozen fruit in recipe or transfer to a freezer bag for later use.
  • Bread - for a savoury version, use only 1 tsp sugar.
    For a chocolate bread, add 100 g chocolate chips at the end of step 2 and knead for a further 1 minute.
  • Main course - in step 1, replace olive oil with 20 g butter.
    Replace chicken fillets with fish fillets (1.5 cm thick). For the sauce, replace mustard with freshly squeezed juice from half a lemon and add a pinch of cumin (optional).
    Vary or add vegetables according to the season and your preferences. For instance, add broccoli or turnips, cut in pieces, to the Varoma and steam together with the vegetable tagliatelle.
