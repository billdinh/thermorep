Steamed beetroot with creamy walnut sauce

Ingredients

Steamed beetroot

  • 700 g water
  • 900 g raw baby beetroots, peeled and cut into quarters or eighths
  • 4 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 2 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves only
  • 20 fresh curry leaves, plus extra to serve
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil, plus extra for drizzling
  • sea salt, to season
  • ground black pepper, to season
  • 1 tbsp sherry vinegar

Walnut sauce

  • 80 g walnuts
  • 150 g brown onion, cut into halves
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 125 g plant-based milk of choice
  • 20 - 30 g sherry vinegar
  • ¼ tsp pure maple syrup
  • salt, to taste
  • ground black pepper, to taste
  • pine nuts, roasted, to serve (optional)

Preparation

Steamed beetroot

  1. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position and weigh beetroot into it. Add garlic, thyme and curry leaves into Varoma, drizzle with oil, season with salt and pepper, then stir to combine. Secure Varoma lid and steam 30-40 min/Varoma/speed 2, until tender.
  2. Transfer into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl, toss with 1 tablespoon of the oil, vinegar and season again with salt and pepper, if desired. Cover to keep warm and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Walnut sauce

  1. Place walnuts into mixing bowl and dry roast 5 min/Varoma/speed 1, without measuring cup or until fragrant and slightly darkened. Transfer into a small bowl and set aside.
  2. Place onion into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add oil and sauté 2 min/120°C/speed 2.
  4. Add milk, vinegar, syrup, salt, pepper, reserved walnuts and 1 of the steamed garlic cloves and chop 10 sec/speed 9, then cook 4 min/90°C/speed 2 or until smooth and creamy. Transfer into a separate bowl.
  5. Arrange beetroot mixture onto a serving platter. Sprinkle with pine nuts (optional), garnish with extra curry leaves and serve with Walnut sauce.
