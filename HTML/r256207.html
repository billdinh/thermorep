Brussels sprouts pasta with hazelnut dressing

Ingredients

  • 500 g Brussels sprouts (see Tips)
  • 50 g Parmesan cheese, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 100 g toasted hazelnuts, skinned
  • 20 g sherry vinegar
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 ¼ tsp salt, plus extra to season
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper, plus extra to season
  • 100 g olive oil, plus extra for frying
  • 1000 g boiling water
  • 350 g short dried pasta of choice
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid, weigh Brussels sprouts into it and set aside.
  2. Place Parmesan into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a small bowl and set aside.
  3. Place hazelnuts, vinegar, garlic, ¼ teaspoon of the salt, pepper and oil into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a separate bowl and set aside.
  4. Carefully place boiling water, pasta and remaining 1 teaspoon salt into mixing bowl and cook 12 min/100°C//speed 1 (see Tips), until pasta is cooked to your liking. Meanwhile, trim Brussels sprouts, remove outer leaves and cut into thin slices (5 mm).
  5. Place a large frying pan over medium-high heat and add oil. Add Brussels sprouts to pan and fry for approx. 8 minutes, stirring often, until golden brown and cooked. Season with extra salt and pepper and sprinkle with lemon juice.
  6. Drain pasta using simmering basket, reserving ½ cup of the cooking liquid. Add pasta, cooking liquid and reserved hazelnut dressing to the frying pan with Brussels sprouts and stir to combine. Sprinkle with reserved Parmesan to serve.

Useful Items

large frying pan, bowl, small bowl, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • To save time, you can start frying the Brussels sprouts while the pasta is cooking.
  • If water bubbles up into the mixing bowl lid while pasta is cooking, add 1 tsp of oil to the cooking water to reduce frothing.
