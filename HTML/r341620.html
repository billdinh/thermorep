Aubergine Caviar (Moutabel)

Ingredients

Tahini

  • 300 g sesame seeds
  • 50 g sesame oil

Aubergine Caviar

  • 500 g aubergine (1 large aubergine)
  • 5 mint leaves
  • 1 bunch fresh parsley, cut in pieces
  • 20 g lemon juice
  • 2 Tbsp yoghurt
  • 3 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 2 pinches fine sea salt
  • 40 g tomatoes, diced
  • 80 g green pepper, diced
Nutrition

Preparation

Tahini

  1. Place sesame seeds and sesame oil in mixing bowl, heat 5 min/120°C/speed 2, then chop 30 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Grind 30 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then mix 8 min/speed 2.5. Transfer to an airtight jar and set aside (see tip).

Aubergine Caviar

  1. Preheat oven to 130°C and line a baking tray with baking paper.
  2. Prick aubergine several times with a fork and place on prepared baking tray. Bake for 30 minutes (130°C), turning halfway through cooking time. Leave baked aubergine to cool a little before removing skin then cut in pieces and set aside.
  3. Place mint and parsley in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then chop again 5 sec/speed 5.
  4. Add lemon juice, reserved peeled aubergine, yoghurt, oil, 2 Tbsp reserved tahini, garlic and salt then mix 2 min/speed 6. Transfer to an airtight container and refrigerate. Serve cold, sprinkled with diced tomatoes and green pepper.

Useful Items

baking tray and baking paper, airtight container, airtight jar, oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Store remaining tahini in an airtight container for later use. Use it to stir into soups, dress a salad or dip crudités.
