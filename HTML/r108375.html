Chocolate truffle cake

Ingredients

Cake

  • butter, for greasing
  • 4 eggs
  • 120 g caster sugar
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract
  • 30 g cornflour
  • 80 g self-raising flour
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 2 tbsp coffee liqueur
  • 2 tbsp full cream milk
  • 150 g dark chocolate, broken into pieces
  • 450 g pouring (whipping) cream

Ganache

  • 200 g dark chocolate, broken into pieces
  • 90 g unsalted butter
  • fresh mixed berries, for decorating

Preparation

Cake

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C. Grease a springform cake tin (18 cm) and line with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Place eggs into mixing bowl and whisk 5 min/speed 4, until thick and creamy.
  3. Add sugar and vanilla and whisk 10 min/speed 4. Remove butterfly whisk.
  4. Add cornflour, self-raising flour, cocoa and salt and mix 10 sec//speed 3, or until combined.
  5. Transfer mixture into prepared cake tin and bake for 25 minutes (160°C), or until a wooden skewer inserted into the centre of the cake comes out clean. Transfer onto a wire rack to cool completely. Clean and dry mixing bowl and butterfly whisk.
  6. Place coffee liqueur and milk into a small bowl and stir to combine.
  7. Slice cake horizontally into three equal layers. Brush each slice with liqueur mixture and set aside.
  8. Place dark chocolate into mixing bowl and grate 8 sec/speed 7.
  9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Melt 2 min/50°C/speed 2. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  10. Insert butterfly whisk. Place cream into mixing bowl and whip until soft peaks form/speed 3, watching cream carefully through hole in mixing bowl lid to avoid over whipping. Remove butterfly whisk.
  11. Divide cream between 2 bowls. Add reserved melted chocolate to one of the bowls of cream and gently fold in chocolate. Set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl and butterfly whisk.
  12. Line springform cake tin (18 cm) with plastic wrap. Place 1 reserved cake layer into tin and spread with chocolate cream, top with another layer of cake and spread with reserved plain whipped cream. Top with remaining layer of cake. Press down gently.
  13. Place into refrigerator for 1 hour or until firm.

Ganache

  1. Place chocolate into mixing bowl and grate 8 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add butter and melt 2 min/80°C/speed 2, until smooth. Transfer into a bowl and place into refrigerator (approx. 15 minutes) until mixture firms slightly (ganache should be easily spreadable, but not runny). Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Carefully remove cake from tin and place onto a serving plate. Spread top and sides of cake with cooled ganache.
  4. Garnish with fresh berries (optional). Serve immediately or place into refrigerator until ready to serve. Remove from refrigerator 15 minutes before serving.
