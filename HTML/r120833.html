Cajun Chicken and Sweet Potato Mash

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp black peppercorns
  • 2 tsp coarse sea salt
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp dried onions
  • ¼ tsp ground cayenne pepper
  • ¼ tsp dried oregano
  • ¼ tsp dried thyme
  • ½ tsp dried chilli flakes
  • 700 g water
  • 850 g sweet potatoes, peeled, diced (2 cm)
  • 4 chicken breasts, skinless, butterflied (sliced through horizontally almost to the edge then opened out)
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 40 g sweet chilli sauce
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt

Preparation

  1. Place black peppercorns, coarse salt, garlic powder, dried onions, cayenne pepper, oregano, thyme and chilli flakes in mixing bowl then grind 1 min/speed 10. Transfer cajun spice mix to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Preheat grill to 230°C. Line a baking tray with baking paper or foil.
  3. Place water and sweet potato in mixing bowl then cook 15 min/100°C//speed 0.5. Meanwhile, place butterflied chicken breasts between two pieces of cling film and flatten using a rolling pin. Brush with olive oil and coat each breast with 1 Tbsp reserved cajun spice mix. Grill for 5 minutes (230°C) on each side until browned.
  4. Drain sweet potatoes using simmering basket, discarding liquid then return potatoes to mixing bowl.
  5. Add sweet chilli sauce and salt then mash 10 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then mash again 10 sec/speed 5. Serve mash with grilled cajun chicken.

Useful Items

baking tray and paper, cling film, rolling pin, grill

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with a simple sweetcorn salad.
