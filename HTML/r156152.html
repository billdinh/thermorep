Butternut pumpkin soup, fish with rice and vegetables, apple-pear crumble

Ingredients

  • 100 g long-grain white rice
  • 400 g water plus extra to soak rice
  • 2 skinless, white fish fillets, 150-180 g each
  • 1 tsp salt, plus extra to taste
  • 1 - 2 pinches ground black pepper, plus extra to taste
  • 6 cherry tomato, cut into halves
  • 120 g apples (1 apple), cut into pieces (1.5 cm) and cored
  • 120 g pears (1 pear), cut into pieces (1.5 cm) and cored
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 130 g carrots, cut into matchsticks (1 cm x 5 cm)
  • 4 asparagus spears, cut into halves lengthways if thicker than 1 cm
  • 40 g plain flour
  • 20 g brown sugar
  • 20 g butter
  • 150 g onions, cut into pieces (4 cm)
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 250 - 300 g butternut pumpkin, peeled and cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 20 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 80 g ripe tomatoes, cut into pieces
  • fresh chives, cut into small pieces, for garnishing

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh rice into it. Add extra water to cover rice and set aside to soak.
  2. Sprinkle fish fillets with 1-2 pinches salt and pepper; if fish fillets are thin, roll them up and secure with a toothpick to avoid overcooking. Place fish fillets on a piece of baking paper (30 x 40 cm), add cherry tomatoes and sprinkle again with 1-2 pinches salt. On a seperate piece of baking paper (30 x 40 cm), place apple and pear pieces and drizzle with honey and lemon juice. Close both parcels, folding up baking paper and twisting ends, then place them into Varoma dish. Place Varoma dish into position. Insert Varoma tray and weigh carrots on it, and top with asparagus. Set Varoma aside.
  3. Place flour, brown sugar and butter into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 5. Transfer crumble mixture into a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place onions, garlic clove, pumpkin and olive oil into mixing bowl, chop 2 sec/speed 5, then sauté 8 min/120°C/speed 1.
  5. Add tomatoes to mixing bowl. Drain rice through simmering basket and insert simmering basket into mixing bowl. Add 1 tsp salt, 1 pinch ground pepper and 400g water. Place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and cook 20 min/Varoma/speed 1. Meanwhile, preheat oven to 180°C.
  6. Set covered Varoma aside on a plate to keep warm and remove simmering basket with aid of spatula. Blend soup 1 min/speed 4-9, increasing speed gradually from speed 4 to speed 9.
  7. Divide steamed apple and pear pieces and their cooking juices between 2 ovenproof ceramic dishes (approx. 12 cm), sprinkle with reserved crumble mixture and bake for 15-20 munites (180°C) or until golden.
  8. Serve soup sprinkled with chopped chives as a starter (see Tip), then fish with rice and vegetables as a main dish (remove toothpicks from fish rolls) and finally fruit crumble for dessert.

Useful Items

baking paper, ceramic dish (12cm), ceramic or glass bowl, bowl, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • To keep the main course warm while soup is served, insert simmering basket with rice back into empty but still warm mixing bowl and set covered Varoma containing fish parcel and vegetables in position on mixing bowl lid.
  • Instead of asparagus, use green beans, cut in pieces (2 cm).
    - Use chicken stock or vegetable stock instead of water in step 5 (reduce salt in the same step to ¼ tsp)
