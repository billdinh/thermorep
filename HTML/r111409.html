Teddy Bear Burgers

Ingredients

Ingredients for Teddy Bear Buns

  • 9 oz whole milk
  • 2 oz salted butter
  • 1 oz sugar
  • 1 oz fresh yeast
  • 18 oz bread flour, divided
  • 1 tsp salt
  • sliced black olives, to garnish
  • 4 - 5 cherry tomatoes, to garnish, cut in halves
  • 1 egg yolk, to brush

Ingredients for Burgers

  • 2 ½ oz dry bread roll
  • 2 ½ oz whole milk
  • 1 oz salted butter
  • 3 oz onion
  • 16 oz ground beef
  • 1 tsp yellow mustard
  • ½ tsp salt, to taste
  • ground black pepper, to taste

Ingredients for Sauce

  • 10 ½ oz oil
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp yellow mustard
  • ½ tsp salt, to taste
  • 1 oz lemon juice
  • ½ oz ketchup

Ingredients for Assembly

  • lettuce, to taste
  • potato chips, to taste
  • tomato (sliced), to taste

Preparation

Ingredients for Teddy Bear Buns

  1. Place milk, butter, sugar and yeast into mixing bowl and mix 1 min 30 sec/100°F/speed 2.
  2. Add 9 oz flour and salt and mix 5 sec/speed 5.
  3. Add remaining 9 oz flour and knead 2 min/. Transfer to a bowl, let rise in a warm place about 30 minutes or until doubled in size.
  4. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line baking sheet with parchment paper.
  5. To make teddy bear faces, divide dough into 8 balls (approx. 3 oz) and place on prepared baking sheet about 3 in. apart. Roll 16 smaller balls (approx. 0.5 oz ea) and position at sides of face to form ears (see Tip). Gently press in olive slices for eyes and cherry tomato halves for the nose. Brush with egg yolk and let rise 15 minutes.
  6. Bake 20 minutes (350°F).

Ingredients for Burgers

  1. Place a bowl on top of mixing bowl lid and weigh in bread roll and milk. Set aside.
  2. Place butter and onion into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down mixing bowl with spatula and sauté without measuring cup 5 min/250°F/speed 1. Remove mixing bowl and allow to cool slightly.
  3. Add ground beef and break into pieces with spatula.
  4. Add mustard, salt, pepper and reserved milk-soaked bread, reinsert measuring cup and mix 5 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a clean surface, form into hamburger patties (approx. 2.5 oz) and store in refrigerator until ready to grill. Clean mixing bowl.

Ingredients for Sauce

  1. Place a bowl on top of mixing bowl lid and weigh in oil and set aside.
  2. Place egg, mustard, salt, and lemon juice into mixing bowl. Mix 30 sec/speed 5 while slowly pouring reserved oil onto mixing bowl lid, letting it drizzle around measuring cup in a thin stream onto roating blades to create an emulsion.
  3. Add ketchup and mix 5 sec/speed 5. Refrigerate until ready to use. Meanwhile, grill the burgers.

Ingredients for Assembly

  1. Cut buns in half, being careful not to cut off ears. Spread sauce on the bottom bun and add lettuce. Add potato chips on the top half of the bottom bun to form fur. Add tomato and a grilled burger before adding top half of the bun. Serve hot.

Useful Items

bowl, baking sheet and parchment paper, oven, refrigerator, BBQ grill

Hints & Tricks

  • You can omit the ear step and just make round faces/buns to save time.
  • Brush sides of buns with a little water to the help ears stick.
