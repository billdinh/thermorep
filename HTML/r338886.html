Gazelle Horns (Almond Pastries)

Ingredients

Dough

  • 80 g unsalted butter, diced
  • 200 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 60 g orange blossom water (see tip)
  • 1 tsp lemon zest

Almond Filling and Assembly

  • 250 g blanched almonds
  • 170 g icing sugar
  • 30 g orange blossom water (see tip)
  • 20 g water
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
Nutrition
per 1 piece

359 kJ / 86 kcal

Preparation

Dough

  1. Place butter in mixing bowl and melt 3 min/90°C/speed 2.
  2. Add flour, orange blossom water and lemon zest then knead 30 sec/. Transfer to a bowl, cover with cling film and leave for 15 minutes in fridge. Meanwhile, rinse mixing bowl and continue with recipe.

Almond Filling and Assembly

  1. Place almonds in mixing bowl and grind 30 sec/speed 10.
  2. Add 70 g icing sugar, orange blossom water, water and cinnamon then knead 30 sec/. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Preheat oven to 180°C and line 2 baking trays with baking paper.
  4. Halve chilled dough with spatula and return one half to fridge. Roll out dough half thinly on a floured surface (approx. 2 mm thick). Cut dough circles using a pastry cutter (Ø 6 cm). Make small crescent shapes out of the almond mixture by hand, place on dough circles and wrap filling . Briefly knead leftover dough by hand, re-roll and repeat process until first half of dough has been used up. Place crescents on prepared baking trays and bake for 10-12 minutes (180°C) until lightly golden. Meanwhile, repeat process with second half of dough from fridge then bake for 10-12 minutes (180°C).
  5. After baking, dust pastries with 100 g icing sugar and serve cooled.

Useful Items

cling film, baking trays, baking paper, rolling pin, round pastry cutter, round pastry cutter (Ø 6 cm), refrigerator, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • The taste of orange blossom water is quite intense. Those not used to the taste can replace the orange blossom water with water.
