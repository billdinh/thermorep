Chicken Noodle Bowl

Ingredients

  • 48 oz chicken stock
  • 3 oz red miso (or akamiso)
  • 2 in. fresh ginger, in 4 pieces
  • 2 in. kombu
  • 12 oz chicken breast, skinless, boneless, sliced (½ in)
  • 1 oz ponzu sauce
  • ½ tsp sesame oil
  • 6 ½ oz udon noodles
  • 8 oz bok choy, in pieces (½ in.)
  • 8 oz sweet corn kernels
  • 6 oz cucumber, diced
  • 1 fresh jalapeño chili, sliced
  • 16 mint leaves, to garnish
  • 2 lime, cut into wedges

Preparation

  1. Place chicken stock, miso, ginger and kombu into mixing bowl. Set Varoma dish into position, weigh in chicken, drizzle with ponzu sauce and sesame oil, secure Varoma lid and steam 19 min/Varoma/speed 2. Transfer chicken to a bowl, divide into smaller pieces and keep warm. Remove and discard ginger and kombu from mixing bowl.
  2. Add noodles into mixing bowl. Set Varoma dish into position and weigh in bok choy, insert Varoma tray and weigh in corn, secure Varoma lid and cook 9 min/212°F/speed 1.
  3. Divide steamed noodles and broth between four soup bowls. Top each bowl with equal portions of reserved steamed chicken, corn, cucumber, bok choy, and jalapeño.
  4. Garnish each bowl with mint leaves, a squeeze or wedge of lime and serve.
