Potted Eggs with Salmon and Asparagus

Ingredients

  • 20 g butter, plus extra for greasing
  • 120 g asparagus, woody ends removed
  • 1 small handful fresh chives (approx. 5 g), cut in lengths (2 cm)
  • 1 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 30 g milk
  • 8 medium eggs
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 80 g smoked salmon, cut in thin strips
  • 600 g water, for steaming

Preparation

  1. Grease four ramekins (Ø 8 cm) then set aside.
  2. Cut asparagus spears from stalks and set aside. Cut stalks into pieces.
  3. Place asparagus stalk pieces and chives in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add butter and lemon juice then cook 1 min/100°C/speed 1.
  5. Add milk, eggs, salt and pepper then mix 10 sec/speed 4.
  6. Add smoked salmon and stir in 5 sec/speed 3. Transfer to prepared ramekins then gently arrange asparagus spears on top. Place in Varoma dish.
  7. Place water in mixing bowl then place Varoma into position. Steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 1. Serve immediately in ramekins.

Useful Items

4 ramekins (Ø 8 cm)

Hints & Tricks

  • A wonderfully simple-to-prepare yet impressive brunch dish, steamed in the Varoma. Particularly enjoyable around late spring or early summer when British asparagus is in season. Also makes a nice lunch served with a side salad.

  • To serve 2, halve all ingredients and steam two ramekins above 400 g water in step 7.
