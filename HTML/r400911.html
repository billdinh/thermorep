Caesar Salad

Ingredients

Salad

  • 500 g water
  • 4 large eggs
  • 200 g bread, diced (1.5 cm)
  • 3 Tbsp olive oil
  • 560 g cos lettuce, cut in strips

Dressing

  • 240 g olive oil
  • 150 g Parmesan cheese, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 60 g anchovy fillets, in oil, drained
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 2 tsp mustard
  • 3 Tbsp lime juice
  • 1 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 70 g water

Preparation

Salad

  1. Place water in mixing bowl, insert simmering basket and add eggs. Cook 12 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and place eggs under cold running water to stop them cooking.
  2. Peel eggs, separate egg whites from egg yolks (see tip) then reserve yolks for dressing. Chop egg whites into small cubes and set aside.
  3. Preheat grill on low heat.
  4. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in bread cubes. Transfer bread cubes to a baking tray, drizzle over 3 Tbsp oil and mix with spatula. Place baking tray under grill, turning bread cubes carefully while grilling, until golden brown then set aside.
  5. Place a large salad bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in lettuce. Add croutons (bread cubes) and chopped egg whites then set aside.

Dressing

  1. Place a jug on mixing bowl lid and weigh in 140 g oil. Set aside.
  2. Place Parmesan in mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 7. Set scales to zero, then using a spoon, remove 100 g grated Parmesan (scales will show - 100 g) and reserve for sprinkling on finished salad. Grate remaining cheese again 10 sec/speed 10.
  3. Add 100 g oil, anchovy fillets, garlic, mustard, lime juice, Worcestershire sauce, salt, pepper and reserved egg yolks then mix 20 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Mix 1 min/speed 5 while pouring reserved oil in a slow steady stream onto mixing bowl lid, letting it drizzle around measuring cup onto rotating blades to create an emulsion. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add water and stir 5 sec/speed 3. Pour half the dressing (see tip) over salad, stir and serve immediately with reserved grated Parmesan on the side.

Useful Items

large salad bowl, jug, baking tray, grill

Hints & Tricks

  • Eggs should be soft boiled: egg yolk runny, egg whites just cooked.
  • This recipe makes enough dressing for two salads. Add as much dressing as you like and store the remainder for up to one week in the fridge.
  • Parmesan cheese can be reserved and added to the salad before serving.
