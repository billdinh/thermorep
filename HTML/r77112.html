Meatballs with white wine mushroom sauce

Ingredients

Meatballs

  • 80 g day old bread
  • ½ brown onion
  • 4 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 1 tsp dried mixed herbs
  • 20 g olive oil, plus extra for frying
  • 500 g rump steak, trimmed, cut into pieces (4 cm) and slightly frozen
  • 1 egg
  • 20 g full cream milk
  • ½ tsp sea salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper

White wine mushroom sauce

  • 60 g Parmesan cheese, crust removed and cut into cubes (3 cm)
  • 60 g dry white wine
  • 1 tbsp cornflour
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 eschalot (approx. 30 g)
  • 40 g butter
  • 200 g fresh Swiss brown mushrooms, cut into thin slices
  • 1 pinch sea salt, to taste
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper, to taste
  • 200 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 1 sprig fresh flat-leaf parsley, for garnishing

Preparation

Meatballs

  1. Place bread into mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 10. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place onion, parsley and dried herbs into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add olive oil and sauté 3 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  4. Add rump steak and chop 15 sec/speed 8, or until an evenly-chopped chunky consistency is achieved.
  5. Add egg, milk, salt, pepper and reserved breadcrumbs and combine 5 sec/speed 7.
  6. Form the mince mixture into approx. 20 small meatballs. Place a medium-sized non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add oil. Cook meatballs until browned all over and cooked through (approx. 4-5 minutes). You may need to cook meatballs in 2 batches.
  7. Transfer meatballs into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and set aside to keep warm. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

White wine mushroom sauce

  1. Place Parmesan into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place a ceramic or glass bowl onto mixing bowl, then weigh white wine into it. Add cornflour to ceramic or glass bowl and combine until cornflour has dissolved.
  3. Place garlic and eschalot into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7, or until a finely chopped consistency is achieved. Repeat if necessary. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add butter and sauté 2 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  5. Add mushrooms and cook 2 min/100°C//speed , placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  6. Add reserved cornflour-wine mixture and cook 2 min/100°C//speed , placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid
  7. Add salt, pepper, cream and reserved Parmesan and cook 4 min/90°C//speed , or until sauce is heated and warm, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid
  8. Serve meatballs with mushroom sauce and garnish with flat-leaf parsley before serving.

Useful Items

tongs, medium-sized non-stick frying pan, thermal serving bowl or other large bowl, ceramic or glass bowl, stove

Hints & Tricks

  • Meatballs in mushroom sauce are delicious served with fresh crusty bread and a green salad.
