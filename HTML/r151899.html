Chicken Garam Masala

Ingredients

Garam Masala Spice Mix

  • 2 Tbsp coriander seeds
  • 1 Tbsp cumin seeds
  • 1 Tbsp ground cardamom
  • 2 tsp black peppercorns
  • 1 tsp cloves
  • ½ tsp ground nutmeg
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 dried bay leaf

Chicken Garam Masala

  • 400 g tinned chopped tomatoes
  • 80 g sour cream
  • 50 g unsalted butter, diced
  • 1 tsp sugar (optional)
  • ½ - 1 tsp fine sea salt, to taste
  • 400 g chicken breasts, skinless, cut in cubes (3 cm)

Preparation

Garam Masala Spice Mix

  1. Place coriander seeds, cumin seeds, cardamom, peppercorns, cloves, nutmeg, cinnamon and bay leaf in mixing bowl then grind 40 sec/speed 10. Transfer to an airtight jar and set aside.

Chicken Garam Masala

  1. Place tomatoes, sour cream, butter and sugar (if using) in mixing bowl, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup on mixing bowl lid to help prevent splashing, then cook 12 min/100°C/speed 2.
  2. Add salt and 2 Tbsp reserved garam masala spice mix then, with simmering basket instead of measuring cup on mixing bowl lid, cook 3 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Add chicken and salt to mixing bowl, insert measuring cup then cook 6 min/100°C//speed . Check chicken is thoroughly cooked then season to taste and serve hot.

Hints & Tricks

  • Thermomix® is great for grinding fresh spices and the difference in flavour is enormous. For this ever-popular dish you start by quickly and easily making your own garam masala spice mix before combining with the other ingredients. Making flavoursome curries at home has never been so easy!
  • Serve with basmati or plain rice and sprinkle with parsley.
  • Serve with plain yoghurt if desired, to tone down the spiciness.
  • Reserve the rest of the garam masala spice mix for another curry recipe.
  • For a faster version of this recipe, replace the garam masala spice mix with a shop-bought version.
