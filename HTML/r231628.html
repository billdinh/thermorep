Chipotle Caesar Salad

Ingredients

Chipotle Caesar Dressing

  • 4 oz Parmesan cheese, cut into pieces
  • 1 egg yolk, from large egg
  • 2 oz freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 6 anchovy fillets, preserved in oil, drained, plus extra to garnish
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 3 chipotle chilies, in adobo sauce, canned (approx. 3 tbsp)
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 4 oz extra virgin olive oil

Salad

  • 8 oz bread cubes (1 in.)
  • 1 pinch chili powder
  • 3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 20 oz romaine lettuce, washed and chopped into pieces (1 in.)

Preparation

Chipotle Caesar Dressing

  1. Place Parmesan cheese into mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 7. Remove half the grated Parmesan cheese, transfer to a bowl and set aside to garnish finished salad. Re-grate the remaining cheese 5 sec/speed 10. Transfer to another bowl and set aside.
  2. Place egg yolk and lemon juice into mixing bowl and heat 4 min 30 sec/140°F/speed 3.5.
  3. Add anchovies, garlic, mustard, Worcestershire sauce, chipotle chilies, reserved finely grated Parmesan and salt and blend 10 sec/speed 7.
  4. Place a pitcher onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in olive oil. Set aside.
  5. Mix 1 min/speed 5 while pouring reserved olive oil in a slow steady stream onto mixing bowl lid, letting it drizzle around the measuring cup onto rotating blades to create an emulsion. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.

Salad

  1. Place oven rack in middle of oven. Set oven to broil, low setting.
  2. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in bread cubes, add chili powder and olive oil and toss to coat. Transfer bread cube mixture onto a baking sheet and place under broiler, turn bread cubes halfway through toasting. Watch carefully to toast just until golden brown and set aside.
  3. Place a large salad bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in lettuce. Add reserved croutons and Chipotle Caesar Dressing and toss to combine. Garnish with anchovies and 2 oz reserved grated Parmesan cheese and serve.

Useful Items

pitcher, large salad bowl, baking sheet, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with grilled shrimp, chicken or other lean protein.
