Spicy Sausage Farfalle with Sun-dried Tomatoes and Olives - Farfalle con salsiccia piccante, pomodori secchi e olive

Ingredients

  • 150 g Parmesan cheese, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 100 g kale, tough stalks removed
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 100 g onions, quartered
  • 30 g extra virgin olive oil, plus extra for drizzling
  • 400 g sausage meat, crumbled in pieces
  • 200 g sun-dried tomatoes in oil, drained and roughly chopped
  • 400 g tinned chopped tomatoes
  • 150 g pitted black olives, large
  • ½ - 1 tsp dried chilli flakes, to taste
  • 2 ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1000 g water, for cooking pasta
  • 320 g dried farfalle pasta

Preparation

  1. Place Parmesan in mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place kale in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place garlic, onions and oil in mixing bowl, chop 5 sec/speed 5 then sauté 2 min/100°C/speed 1.
  4. Add sausage meat and sauté 4 min/Varoma//speed 1.
  5. Add sun-dried tomatoes, tinned tomatoes, olives, reserved chopped kale, chilli flakes, 1½ tsp salt and pepper then sauté 10 min/100°C/speed 1, stirring with spatula through hole in mixing bowl lid for 1 minute to combine.
  6. Add 100 g reserved grated Parmesan and stir in with spatula. Transfer to a bowl and keep warm.
  7. Place water in mixing bowl and bring to the boil 10 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  8. Add pasta and remaining 1 tsp salt then cook time indicated on packet/100°C//speed . Drain pasta through simmering basket then transfer to a large serving bowl.
  9. Pour over reserved sauce, sprinkle with remaining 50 g grated Parmesan and a drizzle of olive oil, if desired, then serve immediately.

Hints & Tricks

  • Literally translated as ‘butterfly’ in Italian, farfalle is also known as ‘bow-tie’ pasta. The shapes have a large surface area that thick sauces cling to, making rich cheese or tomato-based sauces a great choice for this pasta type. With this in mind, we’ve created a spicy sausage sauce brimming with the strong flavours of sun-dried tomatoes and olives. With both the sauce and pasta cooked in your Thermomix®, this is a simple dish that comes together quickly.
