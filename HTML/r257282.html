Plaited pork pie

Ingredients

  • 3 spring onions/shallots, trimmed and cut into pieces
  • 500 g pork sausages, removed from casings (see Tips)
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp dried parsley
  • 20 g Worcestershire sauce
  • 375 g puff pastry (25 x 40 cm sheet - see Tips)
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • tomato sauce, to serve

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C. Set aside a baking tray (30 x 40 cm).
  2. Place spring onion/shallots into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add sausage meat, thyme, parsley and Worcestershire sauce and mix 5 sec//speed 4.
  4. Place puff pastry (25 x 40 cm) onto a sheet of baking paper and transfer onto baking tray. Place sausage mixture down the centre of pastry (approx. 8 cm in width and 30 cm in length). Cut pastry on either side of filling into strips (2 cm wide). Fold up top and bottom ends of pastry to enclose ends and sides of filling. Carefully, place one cut strip of pastry over filling, alternating from one side to another, to enclose filling (ensure each end of pastry is tucked under previous strip). Brush with egg and bake for 20 minutes (220°C) or until golden brown. Allow to cool slightly before serving with tomato sauce.

Useful Items

baking tray (30 x 40 cm), pastry brush, baking paper, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Use a good-quality sausage for the best results.
  • To make your own puff pastry, please refer to The Basic Cookbook. Please note this will increase the total time of the recipe.
  • Serve this pie with side of choice.
