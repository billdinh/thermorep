Eggplant and Tomato Pasta

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Parmesan cheese, cut into pieces
  • 1 oz olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 4 oz yellow onions, quartered
  • 21 oz ripe tomatoes, cut into pieces (¾ in.)
  • 1 ½ tsp salt, or to taste
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper, or to taste
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 16 oz eggplant, cut into pieces (¾ in.)
  • 12 ½ oz dried pasta, eg. farfalle (bow tie pasta) or amori
  • 24 ½ oz water
  • 3 sprigs fresh basil leaves (approx. 20 medium leaves)

Preparation

  1. Place Parmesan into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place oil, garlic and onions into mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and sauté 4 min/250°F/speed 1.
  3. Add tomatoes, salt, pepper and sugar and cook 10 min/230°F/speed 1 .
  4. Add eggplant and cook a further 20 min/230°F/speed 1 .
  5. Add pasta and water, mix with aid of spatula, and cook for time indicated on package/212°F//speed 1. Check if pasta is cooked. If not, continue to cook a further 2 min/212°F//speed 1.
  6. Stir in fresh basil leaves and sprinkle with reserved grated Parmesan. Serve immediately.
