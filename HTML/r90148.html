Summer fruits in mulled cider syrup

Ingredients

Mulled cider

  • 50 g brown sugar, to taste
  • 200 g water
  • 3 pieces orange zest (approx. 5 x 1.5 cm), no white pith
  • 60 g orange juice (approx. 1 orange)
  • 2 cinnamon quills, plus extra to serve
  • 6 whole cloves
  • ¼ tsp ground nutmeg
  • 2 cardamom pods, crushed
  • 2 star anise
  • 750 g alcoholic apple cider

Cider fruit

  • 2 vanilla beans, split lengthways, seeds scraped (reserve pods and seeds)
  • 2 cinnamon quills
  • 30 g white balsamic vinegar
  • 100 g caster sugar
  • 1 orange, zest only, no white pith
  • 400 g strawberries, washed and hulled
  • 125 g fresh raspberries
  • 1 peach, flesh only, cut into slices (1 cm)
  • 2 nectarines, flesh only, cut into slices (1 cm)

Preparation

Mulled cider

  1. Place all mulled cider ingredients, except cider, into mixing bowl and cook 5 min/100°C/speed 1.
  2. Add cider and cook 7 min/80°C/speed 1. Strain mulled cider through simmering basket into a large jug and set aside. Discard basket contents.

Cider fruit

  1. Place vanilla seeds, reserved vanilla pods, cinnamon quills, balsamic vinegar, sugar, orange zest and 600 g of the reserved mulled cider into mixing bowl and cook 20-30 min/100°C//speed , or until a syrup consistency is achieved, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  2. Divide strawberries, raspberries, peach slices and nectarine slices evenly between 4 hot-rinsed sealable storage jars (400 mL each).
  3. Strain mulled cider syrup through simmering basket into a large jug, then evenly distribute syrup between the 4 jars.
  4. Add cinnamon quills, orange zest and vanilla bean pods in simmering basket to jars, then seal.
  5. Allow syrup to cool to room temperature, then store in refrigerator until ready to serve.

Useful Items

large jug, 4 sealable storage jars (400 mL each), refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve summer fruits and mulled cider syrup drizzled over desserts such as vanilla ice cream, yoghurt, pancakes, custard etc.
  • These make a beautiful festive gift for friends and family. Tie a gift tag around each one with some serving instructions and a silver spoon to complete the gift.
  • Any leftover Mulled cider makes for a great drink, hot or cold.
