Vegan carrot cake with orange frosting

Ingredients

Carrot cake

  • 60 g extra virgin olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 110 g boiling water
  • 15 g flaxmeal (see Tips)
  • 150 g walnuts
  • 280 g carrots, cut into pieces
  • 60 g sultanas
  • 160 g Rapadura sugar
  • 1 banana, cut into pieces
  • 250 g wholemeal self-raising flour
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste (see Tips)
  • 30 g plant-based milk of choice

Orange frosting

  • 100 g raw cacao butter
  • 1 tbsp arrowroot flour
  • 40 g pure maple syrup
  • 80 g plant-based milk of choice
  • 3 tsp vanilla bean paste
  • 1 - 2 pinches salt
  • 40 g vegan butter (see Tips)
  • 1 orange, zest only, no white pith

Preparation

Carrot cake

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C. Grease and line a round cake tin (23 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and carefully weigh water and flaxmeal into it. Set aside for 5 minutes.
  3. Place walnuts into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 5. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place carrots into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 6.
  5. Add all remaining cake ingredients, including reserved flaxmeal mixture and chopped walnuts, and mix 10 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  6. Transfer mixture into prepared cake tin and bake for 80 minutes (160°C) or until a wooden skewer inserted into the centre of the cake comes out clean. Allow cake to cool for 30 minutes in tin, then transfer onto a wire rack to cool completely. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Orange frosting

  1. Place cacao butter, arrowroot, syrup, milk, vanilla and salt into mixing bowl and heat 1 min 30 sec/100°C/speed 2.
  2. Add butter and orange zest and mix 30 sec/speed 4, until thick. Transfer into a sealable container and allow to cool until mixture is spreadable (approx. 90 minutes). Spread over cake, then decorate as desired before serving.

Useful Items

baking paper, round cake tin (23 cm), wooden skewer, wire rack, bowl, sealable container, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • To mill your own flaxmeal, place 200 g flaxseeds (linseeds) into mixing bowl and mill 30 sec/speed 10. Use as needed. Transfer any leftovers into a sealable container and store in the fridge or freezer for up to 3 months.
  • To make your own vanilla paste, refer to the recipe on Cookidoo® (www.cookidoo.com.au).
  • To make your own vegan butter, refer to the recipe on this recipe chip.
  • To save time, make the Orange frosting while the cake is in the oven.
