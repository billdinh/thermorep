Pomegranate Mojito

Ingredients

  • 900 g ice cubes
  • 30 - 40 fresh mint leaves, (approx. 20 g), plus 4 extra sprigs for garnish
  • 2 limes, quartered
  • 150 g white rum
  • 125 g pomegranate juice
  • 125 g soda water, plus extra for serving if desired
  • 80 g sugar syrup (see tip)
  • 30 g pomegranate seeds

Preparation

  1. Place 600 g ice cubes in mixing bowl and crush 25 sec/speed 4, while stirring with spatula through hole in mixing bowl lid. Divide between 4 glasses.
  2. Place mint leaves and limes in mixing bowl then bruise 20 sec//speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add remaining 300 g ice cubes, white rum, pomegranate juice, soda water and sugar syrup then mix 5 sec//speed 4.
  4. Insert simmering basket, hold in place with spatula and strain cocktail over the crushed ice in glasses. Divide pomegranate seeds between glasses and garnish with sprigs of mint. Stir with a straw before serving, adding more soda water to taste, if desired.

Useful Items

4 glasses, straws

Hints & Tricks

  • Make your own sugar syrup in your Thermomix®. Place 250 g sugar and 250 g water in mixing bowl. Place measuring cup on mixing bowl lid and heat 15 min/Varoma//speed 1. Pour into a sterilised bottle and allow to cool before storing in the fridge.
