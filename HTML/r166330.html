Chicken with yoghurt tahini sauce (fattet d'jaj)

Ingredients

Yoghurt tahini sauce

  • 50 g water, cold
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 40 g lemon juice, freshly squeezed
  • 100 g tahini paste
  • 400 g yoghurt, full fat
  • ½ tsp salt

Chicken and dish assembly

  • 10 garlic cloves
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 1 lemon, zest only
  • 1 tsp salt, plus extra to taste
  • 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper
  • 1000 g chicken pieces (thighs, drumsticks, breasts, wings)
  • 800 g water
  • 2 large pita breads
  • vegetable oil for frying
  • 120 g lemon juice
  • 20 g pine nuts, toasted
  • 5 sprigs fresh coriander, finely chopped

Preparation

Yoghurt tahini sauce

  1. Place a glass onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in 50 g cold water. Set aside.
  2. Place garlic and lemon juice into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add tahini paste and blend 15 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Mix 30 sec/speed 4 while slowly pouring reserved water onto mixing bowl lid, letting it drizzle around measuring cup in a thin stream onto rotating blades.
  5. Add yoghurt and salt and mix gently with spatula. Transfer into a bowl and refrigerate.

Chicken and dish assembly

  1. Place garlic, olive oil, lemon zest, salt and pepper into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl. Use garlic-lemon zest mixture to rub chicken pieces all over, then distribute them in Varoma dish and tray.
  2. Place water into mixing bowl, place Varoma into position and steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Carefully open Varoma lid and mix chicken pieces to ensure even steaming, then continue steaming 20 min/Varoma/speed 1. Meanwhile, prepare pitta croutons. Cut pitta bread into bite-size square pieces and fry in batches in hot oil until they become crispy and golden. Drain on paper towels, sprinkle with salt to taste and set aside.
  4. Remove Varoma and let cool down. Remove all skin and bones and cut chicken into pieces (approx. 2-3 cm) and transfer into a bowl. Mix with lemon juice, adjust seasoning to taste and refrigerate.
  5. To assemble, place pitta croutons in a serving dish (Ø 25 cm, 5 cm height), top with chicken pieces and cover with yoghurt sauce. Garnish with toasted pine nuts and finely chopped fresh coriander before serving.

Useful Items

paper towels, round serving dish (Ø 25 cm, height 5 cm), refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Oven toasted pitta bread croutons: instead of frying pitta bread in step 7, brush the whole pitta breads with olive oil, sprinkle with salt and grill in the oven until crispy and golden. Then break it into small pieces.
  • Save the liquid in the mixing bowl after the chicken is cooked (chicken stock) and use it to make a soup or other preparation which requires stock.
  • For a faster result, replace the chicken pieces with 800 g chicken breasts, cut in 4 pieces each and in step 7 use only 500 g water. In step 8 steam 10 mins/Varoma/speed 1.
