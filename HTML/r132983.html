Amaretti Crème Brulée

Ingredients

  • 16 amaretti biscuits (approx. 100 g)
  • 400 g double cream
  • 50 g granulated sugar
  • 6 medium eggs
  • 8 tsp Demerara sugar, for topping
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1376 kJ / 329 kcal
Protein
2.1 g
Carbohydrates
23 g
Fat
25.4 g

Preparation

  1. Place amaretti biscuits in mixing bowl and chop Turbo/1 sec/2-3 times until fine crumbs. Transfer ⅔ to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Add cream, granulated sugar and eggs to remaining biscuits in mixing bowl then mix 1 min/speed 3. Cook 14 min/90°C/speed 3. Meanwhile, divide reserved biscuit crumbs between 8 ramekins (approx. Ø 8 cm), spreading over the bases.
  3. Pour custard mixture into ramekins. Allow to cool to room temperature then chill in fridge for at least 4 hours or until set.
  4. Before serving, sprinkle over brown sugar and caramelise with a blowtorch or briefly under a very hot grill. Serve chilled.

Useful Items

8 individual ramekins (Ø 8 cm), kitchen blowtorch, refrigerator, grill

Hints & Tricks

  • If mixture curdles in step 2, add 50 g cold milk and blend 30 sec/speed 7 until smooth.
