Baked falafel kofta

Ingredients

  • 100 g white bread, toasted and cut into pieces
  • 500 g water
  • 200 g potatoes, cut into cubes (2-3 cm)
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 200 g red onions, cut into quarters
  • 10 g olive oil, plus extra for greasing and brushing
  • 20 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 20 sprigs fresh coriander, roughly chopped
  • 130 g canned chickpeas, drained
  • 250 g cooked black-eyed beans, drained
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp tahini paste (30 g)
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice

Preparation

  1. Place toasted bread into mixing bowl and grind 9 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place water into mixing bowl, insert simmering basket, add potato cubes in it and steam 25 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula, set aside to cool and empty mixing bowl.
  3. Place garlic, onions and olive oil into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and then sauté 4 min/120°C/speed 1.
  5. Add reserved cooked potatoes, parsley, coriander, chickpeas, black eyed beans, cumin, salt, black pepper, tahini paste and lemon juice and chop 10 sec/speed 6 with aid of spatula, adjust seasoning if needed. Transfer into a bowl and refrigerate for 15 minutes.
  6. Preheat oven to 180ºC. Grease a baking tray with oil and set aside.
  7. With oiled hands form small balls and roll them in reserved bread crumbs to coat. Press gently to form small patties and transfer to prepared baking tray. Brush falafel with olive oil and bake in preheated oven (180°C) for 15 minutes. Turn them over, brush other side with olive oil and continue baking for another 15 minutes. Serve hot or warm.

Useful Items

baking tray, pastry brush, refrigerator, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • For a gluten free alternative replace the bread with 50 g almonds. In step 1 grind the almonds 7 sec/speed 8.
  • Serve with tahini dip (tarator).
