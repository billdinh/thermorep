Egg Liqueur Crème Brûlée

Ingredients

  • 80 g milk
  • 1 vanilla pod, halved lengthways
  • 3 egg yolks, from medium eggs
  • 200 g cream
  • 100 g egg liqueur (e.g. Avocaat)
  • 50 g sugar, plus extra for sprinkling
  • water, hot
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1066 kJ / 255 kcal
Protein
4 g
Carbohydrates
20 g
Fat
16 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 150°C.
  2. Place milk and vanilla pod in mixing bowl then heat 2 min 30 sec/70°C//speed 1. Remove vanilla pod then leave milk to cool in mixing bowl for 10 minutes.
  3. Add egg yolks and cream then mix 10 sec/speed 4.
  4. Add egg liqueur and sugar then mix 10 sec/speed 4.
  5. Divide mixture between 6 ramekins (approx. Ø 10 cm, 4 cm deep), place them in a large baking dish and fill dish with hot water to halfway up sides of ramekins. Bake for 60 minutes (150°C). Carefully remove ramekins from water and set aside to cool to room temperature then chill in fridge until needed.
  6. To serve, sprinkle with a little sugar, caramelise with a cook's blowtorch and serve immediately.

Useful Items

ramekins, large baking dish, cook's blowtorch, oven, refrigerator
