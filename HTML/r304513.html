Peanut Butter Fudge (No Added Sugar)

Ingredients

Fudge

  • 260 g smooth peanut butter, unsweetened
  • 200 g coconut oil
  • 60 g almond milk, unsweetened
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Chocolate Topping

  • 50 g coconut oil
  • 1 Tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 Tbsp agave syrup (approx. 30 g)
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
529 kJ / 128 kcal
Protein
2 g
Carbohydrates
1 g
Fat
12 g

Preparation

Fudge

  1. Line a loaf tin (900 g/2 lb) with baking paper.
  2. Place peanut butter and coconut oil in mixing bowl then heat 2 min 30 sec/60°C/speed 1.
  3. Add almond milk and vanilla extract then blend 10 sec/speed 6.
  4. Transfer to prepared loaf tin and level surface with the back of a spoon. Place in fridge and leave to set for 1-1½ hours. Clean mixing bowl.

Chocolate Topping

  1. After fudge has set, place coconut oil in mixing bowl and melt 1 min 30 sec/80°C/speed 1.
  2. Add cocoa powder and agave syrup then mix 10 sec/speed 5.
  3. Remove fudge from fridge and tip out chocolate topping onto surface. Spread over and level with the back of a spoon. Return to fridge and leave to set for a further 1 hour.
  4. Cut into 2 cm bites and serve immediately or store in fridge until needed.

Useful Items

loaf tin (900 g/2 lb), baking paper, long sharp knife, refrigerator
