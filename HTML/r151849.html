Stir-fried vegetables

Ingredients

  • 10 g garlic cloves
  • 10 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 300 g pak choi or other green vegetables, cut in strips (see tip)
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
151 kJ / 36 kcal
Protein
1 g
Carbohydrates
2 g
Fat
3 g

Preparation

  1. Place garlic cloves and extra virgin olive oil in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Sauté 2 min/120°C/speed .
  3. Add pak choi, salt and black pepper and stir-fry 5 min/120°C//speed . Serve immediately.
