Apple Fritter Bread

Ingredients

  • 10 oz Granny Smith apples (approx. 3), peeled, diced (¼ in.)
  • 2 oz brown sugar
  • 1 ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • &frac18; tsp ground nutmeg
  • 5 oz sugar
  • 4 oz unsalted butter, room temperature, diced (½ in.)
  • 4 ½ oz applesauce, unsweetened
  • 3 oz whole milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 9 oz all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • &frac18; tsp salt

Glaze

  • 2 oz confectioners sugar
  • 1 tbsp whole milk

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease a loaf pan (9 in. x 5 in.), line bottom with a piece of parchment and set aside.
  2. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in apples, brown sugar, cinnamon, ginger and nutmeg. Stir with spatula to combine and set aside.
  3. Place sugar and butter into mixing bowl and mix 1 min/speed 3.
  4. Add applesauce, milk, eggs and vanilla and mix 10 sec/speed 3.
  5. Add flour, baking powder and salt and mix 10 sec/speed 3.5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix a further 10 sec/speed 3.5.
  6. Add reserved apples (reserving 1 cup for topping) to batter, stir through with spatula to combine. Transfer to prepared pan and top with remaining reserved apples.
  7. Bake for 70-75 minutes (350°F) until a skewer inserted in the center comes out clean. Let cool in pan for 10 minutes before transferring to a wire rack. Let cool completely before glazing.

Glaze

  1. Place a small bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in confectioners sugar, add milk and stir to combine. Drizzle glaze over cooled bread, slice and serve.

Useful Items

parchment paper, loaf pan (9 in. x 5 in.), skewer, wire rack, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • For a thinner glaze, reduce the confectioner's sugar to 1 oz.
