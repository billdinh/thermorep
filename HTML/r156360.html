Steamed pears with chocolate cardamom sauce

Ingredients

  • 400 g water
  • 300 - 400 g ripe pears (3-4), peeled, cut into halves and cores removed
  • 100 g dark chocolate, cut into pieces (2-3 cm)
  • 100 g thickened cream
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 1 ½ tsp ground cardamom
  • 50 g flaked almonds, toasted

Preparation

  1. Place water into mixing bowl, place Varoma dish into positon and arrange pears into it. Secure Varoma lid and steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 1. Set covered Varoma onto a plate to keep pears warm. Discard steaming liquid. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  2. Place chocolate, cream, honey and cardamom into mixing bowl and melt 7 min/60°C/speed 1.5.
  3. Divide pears between plates and pour chocolate sauce on top. Serve sprinkled with nuts.

Hints & Tricks

  • Pears with chocolate is a traditional and well-loved combination. Adding cardamom and toasted nuts takes this classic to a new level. You may vary the spices, the nuts or even the presentation: serve as part of a buffet in individual small glasses, as described in the variation.
  • The steamed pears can be prepared in advance. Make the chocolate sauce just before serving and pour over chilled or room temperature pears.
    - Try serving with whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
  • For a different presentation, cut pears into small pieces (0.5-1 cm) after steaming and serve in small glasses, drizzled with chocolate sauce and sprinkled with nuts.
    - Replace cardamom with cinnamon or other spices as desired.
    - Instead of shop-bought flaked almonds, prepare your own nuts: before starting the recipe preheat oven to 160°C, spread whole nuts (e.g. almonds, pistachios, pecans) in a single layer on a baking tray and toast for 10 minutes. Once cooled, place nuts into mixing bowl and chop 5-10 sec/speed 5 and sprinkle over pears.
