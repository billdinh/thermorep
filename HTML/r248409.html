Eggnog Ice Cream

Ingredients

  • 370 g whole milk
  • 350 g double cream
  • 70 g caster sugar
  • 6 egg yolks, from medium eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste
  • 3 Tbsp dark rum
  • 2 cinnamon sticks, broken in pieces
  • ¼ tsp ground nutmeg
  • ice cubes, for cooling
  • whole nutmeg, grated, for garnishing

Preparation

  1. Place milk, cream, sugar, egg yolks, rum, vanilla, cinnamon sticks and nutmeg in mixing bowl then cook 8 min/80°C//speed 4.
  2. Place ice cubes in a large bowl, then rest another large bowl over it. Discard cinnamon then pour mixture through a sieve into the top bowl to cool rapidly, whisking gently for 2 minutes.
  3. Leave mixture in bowl over ice cubes until cool. Cover with cling film, pressed to the surface, then place in fridge until cold.
  4. Pour mixture into a shallow freezer-proof container, cover tightly with lid or cling film then freeze for at least 10 hours.
  5. Before serving, place frozen mixture in fridge for 15-20 minutes to soften, then place a large bowl in freezer. Once mixture has softened, cut into pieces (4 cm x 4 cm) with a sharp knife.
  6. Place half ice cream cubes in mixing bowl and crush 20 sec/speed 6, then mix 10 sec/speed 4, with aid of spatula.
  7. Remove bowl from freezer. Transfer ice cream mixture to bowl, then repeat process with remaining ice cubes. Place ice cream in freezer for 10-15 minutes before serving.
  8. Serve eggnog ice cream garnished with freshly grated nutmeg.

Useful Items

sieve, large bowl, cling film, sharp knife, wire whisk, shallow freezer-proof container, fridge, freezer

Hints & Tricks

  • Enjoy the Christmassy flavours of warming eggnog in this delicious dessert.
