Savoury Crayfish Éclairs

Ingredients

Pastry

  • 30 g Parmesan cheese, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 80 g unsalted butter, diced
  • 150 g water
  • 2 pinches mustard powder
  • 1 - 2 pinches cayenne pepper, to taste
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • 120 g plain flour
  • 3 medium eggs

Assembly

  • 240 g crayfish
  • 110 g mayonnaise
  • 1 lemon, finely grated zest and 25 g juice
  • 1 ½ tsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 pinches ground cayenne pepper
  • 2 pinches fine sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 20 g rocket leaves

Preparation

Pastry

  1. Place Parmesan in mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place butter, water, mustard powder, cayenne pepper and salt in mixing bowl then bring to the boil 5 min/100°C/speed 1.
  3. Add flour and mix 20 sec/speed 4. Remove mixing bowl and set aside to cool for 10 minutes. Meanwhile, preheat oven to 200°C and line 2 baking trays with baking paper.
  4. Place mixing bowl back into position then, without measuring cup, set speed 5 then add eggs, one at a time, through hole in mixing bowl lid.
  5. After adding the last egg, insert measuring cup then mix again 30 sec/speed 5.
  6. Transfer pastry mixture to a large piping bag fitted with a 15 mm plain nozzle then pipe 16 éclairs onto prepared trays, 10 cm in length, leaving 5 cm space between each one. Sprinkle reserved grated Parmesan over each then bake for 15-20 minutes (200°C) until well risen, golden brown and hollow when tapped.
  7. Turn off oven and open oven door slightly, leaving it ajar for 10 minutes to allow éclairs to dry out. Remove trays from oven, transfer éclairs to a wire rack and leave to cool completely. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl.

Assembly

  1. Place a large bowl on mixing bowl lid, weigh in crayfish then set aside.
  2. Place mayonnaise, lemon zest, lemon juice, mustard, cayenne pepper, salt and pepper in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 4. Transfer to bowl with crayfish and stir gently with spatula to coat.
  3. Using a small serrated knife, split éclairs lengthways and place a few rocket leaves inside. Add 1-2 tsp crayfish mixture to each then close and serve.

Useful Items

baking trays, baking paper, wire rack, serrated knife, large bowl, piping bag and plain nozzle, oven
