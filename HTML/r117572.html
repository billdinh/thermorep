Orange Currant Cookies

Ingredients

  • 3 oz sugar
  • 1 orange, organic, thin peel only, no pith, plus 1 tbsp juice
  • 7 oz all-purpose flour, plus extra for dust
  • 5 oz unsalted butter, very cold, cut into pieces
  • 3 oz currants
  • 1 egg yolk, from medium egg
  • all-purpose flour, to dust

Preparation

  1. Place sugar and orange peel into mixing bowl and zest 10 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides and base of mixing bowl with spatula and zest a further 5 sec/speed 8.
  2. Add flour and butter and mix Turbo/1 sec/4 times. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add currants, egg yolk and orange juice and mix 8 sec//speed 4. Tip dough out and bring together into a ball. Wrap in cling wrap and chill in refrigerator for 30 minutes. Meanwhile, preheat oven to 350°F and line two baking sheets with parchment paper.
  4. Divide chilled dough in half and roll each piece out on a lightly floured surface to ⅛ in. thickness. Cut out rounds using a fluted cookie cutter (2½ in.) and transfer to baking sheets. Reroll the trimmings and repeat until all dough is used.
  5. Bake 10-15 minutes (350°F) until lightly golden. Allow to rest on baking sheets for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely. Serve or store in an airtight container.

Useful Items

airtight container, cling wrap, parchment paper, rolling pin, wire rack, baking sheets (approx. 16 in. x 14 in.), cookie cutter (2 in.), refrigerator, oven
