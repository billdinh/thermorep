Greek Bougatsa with Honey and Pistachios

Ingredients

  • 700 g milk
  • 4 egg yolks, from large eggs
  • 60 g maize flour
  • 120 g sugar
  • ½ vanilla pod, seeds only
  • ½ lemon, zest only
  • 130 g unsalted butter, soft
  • 270 g filo pastry (approx. 12 sheets)
  • 2 Tbsp icing sugar
  • 3 Tbsp chopped pistachios
  • 2 Tbsp honey
Nutrition
per 1 slice
Calories
1290 kJ / 308 kcal
Protein
6 g
Carbohydrates
34 g
Fat
16 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Place milk, egg yolks, maize flour, sugar, vanilla seeds and lemon zest in mixing bowl then cook 8 min/90°C/speed 2.5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.
  3. Place butter in mixing bowl and melt 4 min/100°C/speed 1. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Brush a tart tin (Ø 28 cm) with melted butter and place a filo pastry sheet into it. Brush with melted butter and repeat with half of the filo pastry sheets, crossing each layer over the previous one. Discard the lemon zest from the custard then transfer to tart tin. Place a sheet of filo pastry on top of the custard, brush with melted butter and repeat with remaining sheets, taking care to cross each layer over the previous one. Fold filo pastry triangles inside and brush between each layer with melted butter. Brush entire top with melted butter and bake for 25-30 minutes (180°C), until golden brown. Allow to cool to room temperature, then cover and refrigerate for at least 3 hours.
  5. Dust with icing sugar, garnish with chopped pistachio nuts and drizzle with honey before serving.

Useful Items

tart tin (Ø 28 cm), pastry brush, cling film, oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • For an alternative flavour, top this delicious bougatsa with icing sugar and ground cinnamon.
