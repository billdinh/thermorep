Gilthead bream baked in a salt crust

Ingredients

  • 700 g water, for steaming
  • 2000 g coarse salt
  • 1 gilthead bream, gutted, cleaned with its head and scales (800-1000 g)
  • 20 g extra virgin olive oil, (a dash)
  • 1 dash lemon juice
Nutrition
per 1 portion

Preparation

  1. Place water into mixing bowl and heat 8 min/Varoma/speed 1. Meanwhile, set Varoma dish onto Varoma lid, so as not to mark the work top. Cover the bottom of Varoma dish with half of the coarse salt. Carefully wash and dry the bream with a paper towel and place it on the salt, then cover the fish totally with the rest of the salt.
  2. Place Varoma into position and steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 1. Wait 5 minutes until the temperature has dropped before opening the Varoma.
  3. Open Varoma from the side facing away from you. Break the salt and, very carefully, remove the fish (Varoma lid can be used to clean off the salt). Remove the rest of the salt and the skin and separate the four fillets.
  4. Place fillets of bream on the plates and sprinkle with olive oil and lemon juice.

Hints & Tricks
