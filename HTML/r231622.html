Texas Cornbread

Ingredients

  • 2 oz canola oil, plus extra to grease
  • 4 oz cheddar cheese, cut into pieces
  • 2 jalapeño peppers, de-stemmed
  • 8 oz yellow cornmeal
  • 3 oz all-purpose flour
  • 1 oz sugar, or to taste
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • 10 oz whole milk
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 oz frozen corn

Preparation

  1. Grease a round pan (approx. 8 in.-10 in.) (see Tip) and preheat oven to 425°F.
  2. Place cheese into mixing bowl and grate 4 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place jalapeños into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add cornmeal, flour, sugar, salt, baking powder, baking soda, 2 oz canola oil, milk and eggs and mix 10 sec/speed 4.
  5. Add reserved grated cheese and corn and mix 5 sec//speed 4. Transfer batter into prepared pan and bake 20-25 minutes (425°F) or until golden brown. Allow to cool before serving.

Useful Items

round cake pan (approx. 8 in.-10 in.) or cast iron skillet, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • To bake in a cast iron skillet: place 1 tbsp canola oil into a cast iron skillet (approx. 8 in.-10 in.) and place in the oven. Preheat oven to 425°F and continue making the batter. Carefully transfer batter from mixing bowl into hot skillet and bake 20-25 minutes (425°F) or until golden brown.
