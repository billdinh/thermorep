Mustard-dill sauce

Ingredients

  • 200 g rapeseed oil
  • 10 - 15 g fresh dill, leaves only
  • 20 - 30 g caster sugar, to taste
  • 20 g white wine vinegar
  • 20 g lemon juice
  • 25 g Dijon mustard or other medium hot mustard
  • 30 g sweet mustard
  • 1 egg yolk
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper

Preparation

  1. Place a jug on mixing bowl lid, weigh rapeseed oil into jug and set aside.
  2. Place dill in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place sugar, white wine vinegar, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, sweet mustard, egg yolk, salt and black pepper in mixing bowl and mix 5 sec/speed 4.
  4. Mix on speed 3 while very slowly pouring oil onto mixing bowl lid, letting it drizzle around measuring cup in a thin stream onto rotating blades to create an emulsion.
  5. Add reserved chopped dill and mix 8 sec//speed 3, Transfer to a bottle or jar and refrigerate for at least 2 hours. Serve cold.

Useful Items

jug, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve this sauce with salmon (e.g. gravlax) or boiled eggs.
  • Instead of rapeseed oil use another neutral oil (e.g. sunflower oil).
