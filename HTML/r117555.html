Mini Apple and Cinnamon Muffins

Ingredients

  • 150 g salted butter, soft, plus extra for greasing
  • 500 g apples, peeled, cored, quartered
  • 170 g soft brown sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 80 g honey
  • 300 g strong white bread flour
  • 1 ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 Tbsp icing sugar, for dusting
Nutrition
per 1 piece

527 kJ / 126 kcal

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C then grease 30 small muffin moulds with butter.
  2. Place apples in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place sugar, butter and eggs in mixing bowl then beat 3 min/37°C/speed 3.
  4. Add reserved chopped apples, honey, flour, cinnamon and baking powder then mix 20 sec/speed 3. Distribute mixture evenly between moulds then bake for 18-22 minutes until cooked and golden (180°C). Leave in moulds for 5 minutes before removing to a wire rack to cool.
  5. Dust with icing sugar then place in an airtight container ready for taking on your picnic.

Useful Items

muffin moulds, wire rack, airtight container, oven
