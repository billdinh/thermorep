Strawberry Macaroons

Ingredients

  • 7 oz unsweetened coconut chips
  • 1 oz freeze-dried strawberries
  • 6 oz sugar
  • 1 tbsp water
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 2 egg whites, from large eggs
  • 6 oz dark chocolate chips
