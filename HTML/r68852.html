Peanut butter

Ingredients

  • 200 g roasted salted peanuts
  • 30 - 50 g peanut oil, adjust to taste
Nutrition
per 20 gram
Calories
476 kJ / 113 kcal
Protein
3 g
Carbohydrates
1 g
Fat
10 g

Preparation

  1. Place peanuts into mixing bowl and chop 8 sec/speed 4.
  2. Add peanut oil or grapeseed oil and mix 8 sec/speed 4. Check consistency is to your preference.
  3. If you prefer a smoother consistency blend, until desired consistency/speed 4, adding extra oil through hole in mixing bowl lid as needed. Serve immediately or use as needed.

Useful Items

airtight jars

Hints & Tricks

  • Smoother peanut butter: continue chopping in step 1 until peanuts are reduced to a fine powder on speed 3-7, increasing speed gradually. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and repeat as required until desired consistency is achieved.
  • Chunkier peanut butter: halve the quantity of oil and proceed as per recipe.
  • Different flavour: try using different nuts, or a combination of nuts, to create alternate flavours of butter.
  • Peanut butter is great for breakfast or as a decadent addition to desserts.
  • If using unsalted peanuts you may like to add ½ tsp salt in step 2.
  • Store peanut butter in refrigerator until ready to use. If kept refrigerated, peanut butter will keep for 2 - 3 weeks.
