Madeira Cake

Ingredients

  • 55 g blanched almonds
  • 170 g caster sugar
  • 1 lemon, thin peelings of skin and juice
  • 170 g unsalted butter, very soft
  • 3 medium eggs
  • ¼ tsp real vanilla extract
  • 110 g self-raising flour
  • 1 Tbsp milk
  • 1 Tbsp candied lemon and orange peel, in small pieces

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 170°C and line a large loaf tin (900 g/2 lb) with baking paper.
  2. Place almonds in mixing bowl and grind 5 sec/speed 9. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place sugar and lemon peel in mixing bowl then grind 5 sec/speed 10.
  4. Insert butterfly whisk. Add butter and mix 2 min/speed 3.5. Remove butterfly whisk. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add eggs, lemon juice and vanilla extract then mix 30 sec/speed 3.
  6. Add flour, reserved ground almonds and milk then mix 30 sec/speed 3.5. Pour into prepared loaf tin and level the top. Bake for 30 minutes (170°C) then sprinkle candied peel over the centre of cake. Cover with foil then bake for a further 20-30 minutes (170°C) until a skewer inserted in centre comes out clean. Leave in tin to cool for 10 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely. Slice to serve.

Useful Items

900 g loaf tin (2 lb), wire rack, aluminium foil, oven
