Creamy blue cheese with shell pasta

Ingredients

  • 1000 g water
  • 1 - 2 pinches salt
  • 300 g large shell pasta
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 brown onion (approx. 180 g), cut into halves
  • 1 sprig fresh rosemary, leaves only
  • 30 g salted butter
  • 30 g dry white wine
  • 30 g plain flour
  • 400 g full-cream milk
  • 1 - 2 pinches salt, to taste
  • 1 - 2 pinches ground black pepper, to taste, plus extra for serving
  • 100 - 120 g Danish blue cheese, broken into pieces, plus extra for serving
  • 50 g walnuts, cut into pieces, to serve

Preparation

  1. Place water and salt into mixing bowl and heat 8 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  2. Add pasta and cook 8-13 min/90°C//speed  or as per packet instructions, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid. Drain pasta through Varoma dish, then transfer into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl. Add oil and stir through to loosen pasta. Cover to keep warm and set aside. Rinse and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Place onion and rosemary into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and chop for a further 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add butter and wine and cook 4 min/100°C/speed 1.
  6. Add flour, milk, salt and pepper and cook 5 min/90°C/speed 4, then blend 30 sec/speed 3 , increasing speed gradually from speed 3 to speed 8.
  7. Add cheese and cook 2 min/90°C/speed 3. Transfer sauce into bowl with pasta and stir to combine. Sprinkle with extra cheese, pepper and walnuts before serving.

Useful Items

thermal serving bowl or other large bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • You can replace the walnuts with sliced dried figs or slices of fresh pear.
