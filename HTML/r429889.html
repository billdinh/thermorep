Sweet Potato Soup with Star Anise

Ingredients

  • 50 g whole mixed nuts, roasted and salted, vegan (see tip)
  • 1 star anise
  • 1 onion (approx. 100 g), quartered
  • 20 g rapeseed oil
  • 600 g sweet potatoes, cut in pieces (3 cm)
  • 800 g water
  • 1 heaped tsp vegetable stock paste, homemade, vegan (see tip)
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • ½ tsp sweet paprika
  • 75 g soya milk
  • 2 pinches ground nutmeg
  • garden cress, for garnishing
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
862 kJ / 206 kcal
Protein
4 g
Carbohydrates
27 g
Fat
9 g

Preparation

  1. Place nuts in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place star anise in mixing bowl and grind 1 min/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add onion and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add oil and sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  5. Add sweet potatoes and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  6. Add water, vegetable stock, salt, pepper and paprika then cook 20 min/100°C/speed 1.
  7. Add soya milk and nutmeg then purée 1 min/speed 4-9, increasing speed gradually. Divide between serving bowls and serve garnished with cress and reserved chopped nuts.
