Whiskey Cream Liqueur

Ingredients

  • 250 g whisky
  • 20 g milk chocolate, small pieces or callets
  • 20 g dark chocolate, small pieces or callets
  • 160 g sugar
  • 400 g single cream (approx. 18 % fat)
  • 1 medium egg
Nutrition
per 1 litre
Calories
9581 kJ / 2290 kcal
Protein
22 g
Carbohydrates
200.9 g
Fat
93.7 g

Preparation

  1. Place a jug on mixing bowl lid and weigh in whisky then set aside.
  2. Place milk chocolate, dark chocolate, sugar, cream and egg in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 10. Heat 5 min/90°C/speed 3. Heat again 4 min/90°C/speed 3 while slowly pouring reserved whisky onto mixing bowl lid, letting it drizzle around measuring cup onto rotating blades.
  3. Drink while still hot or transfer to bottles, allow to cool, refrigerate and serve cold.
