Orange sesame chicken with broccoli and noodles

Ingredients

Orange hoi sin sauce

  • 2 tbsp almond butter
  • 1 orange, zest (no white pith) and flesh
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 1 tsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 - 3 pitted dates, cut into halves
  • 60 g tamari
  • ½ tsp Chinese five spice
  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • ½ tsp chilli powder
  • 1 pinch salt
  • ½ pinch ground black pepper

Chicken, broccoli and noodles

  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • 700 g skinless chicken thigh fillets, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1600 g water
  • 400 g broccoli, cut into florets (approx. 3 cm)
  • 200 g dried soba noodles
  • 2 spring onions/shallots, trimmed and cut into slices
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds

Preparation

Orange hoi sin sauce

  1. Place all sauce ingredients into mixing bowl and cook 4 min/Varoma/speed 1, then simmer 5 min/90°C/speed 1.
  2. Blend 30 sec/speed 4, increasing speed gradually from speed 4 to speed 9, until smooth.

Chicken, broccoli and noodles

  1. Add ghee, chicken, salt and pepper to mixing bowl and cook 10-15 min/90°C//speed 1, until chicken is cooked. Transfer into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and cover to keep warm. Rinse and dry mixing bowl.
  2. Place water into mixing bowl, place Varoma into position and weigh broccoli into it. Secure Varoma lid and cook 14 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove Varoma.
  3. Add soba noodles to mixing bowl through hole in mixing bowl lid. Place Varoma back into position and cook 3 min/Varoma//speed 2. Divide broccoli, noodles and chicken mixture with sauce between serving bowls, then garnish with spring onions/shallots and sesame seeds to serve.
