Chocolate Puffed Rice Cakes

Ingredients

  • 50 g butter, diced
  • 50 g honey
  • 100 g white chocolate, small pieces or callets
  • 100 g puffed rice
  • 150 g milk chocolate, small pieces or callets
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
952 kJ / 228 kcal
Protein
2.6 g
Carbohydrates
27.5 g
Fat
11.9 g

Preparation

  1. Place 20 g butter in mixing bowl and melt 3 min/90°C/speed 1.
  2. Add 20 g honey and white chocolate then melt 10 min/90°C/speed 1. Meanwhile, wet and wring out a sheet of baking paper and line a rectangular tray.
  3. Add 50 g puffed rice and mix 1 min//speed 1. Transfer mixture to prepared tray.
  4. Using the back of a spoon, level the mixture tightly to a height of 1 cm then place in fridge for 30 minutes while making chocolate layer.
  5. Place remaining 30 g butter in mixing bowl and melt 3 min/90°C/speed 1.
  6. Add remaining 30 g honey and milk chocolate then melt 10 min/100°C/speed 1.
  7. Add remaining puffed rice then mix 1 min//speed 1. Allow mixture to cool in mixing bowl for 10 minutes.
  8. Take milk chocolate mixture and spread onto white chocolate layer, leveling with back of a spoon. Place in fridge to set for 1 hour.
  9. Remove from fridge and leave at room temperature for 10 minutes. Cut into 10 fingers with a large knife.
  10. Pack in an airtight container ready for your picnic.

Useful Items

baking tray and paper, long sharp knife, airtight jar or container, refrigerator
