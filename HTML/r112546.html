Pea and Courgette Tarts

Ingredients

  • 10 g white sandwich bread, sliced, toasted
  • 25 g Parmesan cheese, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 30 g olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 60 g onions, quartered
  • 10 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 2 sprigs fresh mint, leaves only
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 175 g frozen green peas, defrosted
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 150 g courgettes, diced (1 cm)
  • 30 g double cream
  • 2 medium eggs
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Place toast in mixing bowl and grate 15 sec/speed 9. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place Parmesan in mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place oil, onions, parsley, mint and garlic in mixing bowl then chop 2 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Sauté 5 min/100°C/speed 1. Meanwhile, prepare tart moulds by wrapping foil around the bottom of 4 chef rings (Ø 6 cm) to prevent leaking. Grease base and sides with oil.
  5. Add peas, ½ tsp salt and pepper then sauté 3 min/100°C//speed .
  6. Add courgettes and mix well with spatula then sauté 5 min/100°C//speed . Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  7. Place cream, eggs, cayenne pepper, remaining ½ tsp salt and reserved grated Parmesan then mix 15 sec/speed 3. Pour over reserved vegetables and stir.
  8. Coat insides of prepared tart rings with reserved breadcrumbs. Place foil-side down on a baking tray. Pour in mixture then bake for 25-30 minutes (180°C) until golden and set. Serve warm or cold.
