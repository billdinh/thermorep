Chocolate Pecan Pie

Ingredients

  • 8 oz all-purpose flour, plus extra to dust
  • 4 oz sugar, plus 3 tbsp, divided
  • 6 oz unsalted butter, chilled and cut into pieces, divided
  • 6 large eggs (2 whole and 4 separated), divided
  • 2 tbsp whole milk, cold
  • 9 oz whole pecans
  • 4 oz semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 4 oz brown sugar
  • 4 oz maple syrup
  • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste
  • 2 oz bourbon whiskey

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Place flour, 3 tbsp sugar, 5 oz butter, 1 egg yolk and milk into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 6. Transfer pastry to a lightly floured surface, roll into a circle (approx. ¼ in. thick) with a rolling pin and transfer into a pie pan (approx. 10 in.). Line pastry with parchment and pie weights and refrigerate for 10 minutes.
  3. Bake pie crust for 10 minutes (350°F). Remove the parchment and pie weights and bake a further 5 minutes (350°F). Set aside.
  4. Add pecans and chocolate chips to prebaked pie crust.
  5. Place 1 oz butter into mixing bowl and melt 4 min/250°F/speed 1.
  6. Add 4 oz sugar, brown sugar, maple syrup, 2 whole eggs, 3 reserved egg yolks, vanilla bean paste and bourbon and mix 10 sec/speed 4. Pour filling into crust and bake for 35 minutes at (350°F) or until golden brown. Allow to cool before serving.

Useful Items

rolling pin, pie pan (approx. 10 in.), parchment paper, pie weights, oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Replace pie weights with dried beans or rice.
