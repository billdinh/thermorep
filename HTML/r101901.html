Granola

Ingredients

  • 100 g whole mixed nuts (e.g. hazelnuts, almonds, walnuts)
  • 250 g rolled oats
  • 100 g spelt flakes
  • 50 g buckwheat flakes
  • 15 g puffed amaranth
  • 40 g pumpkin seeds
  • 40 g sunflower seeds
  • 10 g linseeds
  • 200 g maple syrup
  • 40 g coconut oil
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C and line a large baking tray with baking paper.
  2. Place nuts in mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a large bowl.
  3. Place bowl with nuts on mixing bowl lid. Weigh into bowl oats, spelt flakes, buckwheat flakes, puffed amaranth, pumpkin seeds, sunflower seeds and linseeds. Mix together with spatula then set aside.
  4. Place maple syrup, coconut oil, cinnamon and salt in mixing bowl then heat 3 min/100°C/speed 2. Pour into dry mixture and mix well with spatula. Transfer to prepared baking tray and spread out evenly.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes (160°C), stirring every 5 minutes with a spoon so granola is evenly browned.
  6. Remove from oven and allow to cool completely on baking tray. Serve granola with soya yoghurt and fresh fruits or store in an airtight container until needed.

Useful Items

baking tray and baking paper, airtight container, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Once cooled, the granola will become crispy and crunchy.
  • Vary the types of cereals, nuts and seeds to what you have available.
  • Dried fruit, such as cranberries, raisins, dates, figs or apricots can be added after baking for a fruity version.
  • Maple syrup can be replaced with 250 g agave syrup.
  • Instead of cinnamon, use other spices such as cardamom or vanilla.
