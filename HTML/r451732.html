Mint Chocolate Chip Ice Cream

Ingredients

  • 5 oz sugar
  • 20 fresh mint leaves
  • 6 oz heavy whipping cream
  • 18 oz whole milk
  • 1 oz creme de menthe
  • 5 egg yolks
  • 3 oz dark chocolate (70% cocoa), broken into pieces
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1222 kJ / 292 kcal
Protein
5 g
Carbohydrates
28 g
Fat
17 g

Preparation

  1. Place sugar and mint into mixing bowl and grind 20 sec/speed 10.
  2. Add whipping cream, milk, creme de menthe and egg yolks and cook 10 min/195°F/speed 2.
  3. Pour mixture into shallow freezable containers up to 1½ in. high and cover with cling wrap. Allow to cool completely before freezing for 8 hours.
  4. Just before serving, place frozen mixture in refrigerator for 15 minutes. Meanwhile chop the chocolate.
  5. Place chocolate into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.
  6. Remove frozen mixture from refrigerator and cut into pieces (1½ in. x 1½ in.) with a sharp knife.
  7. Place ice cream pieces into mixing bowl and crush 15 sec/speed 6. Gently stir in reserved chopped chocolate with spatula. Serve immediately or store in freezer until ready to serve.

Useful Items

sharp knife, shallow freezable containers (e.g. aluminum, silicone), cling wrap, freezer, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • For a firmer consistency return the churned ice cream to the freezer for 15 minutes before serving.
