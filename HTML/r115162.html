Ala hodi (Potato curry)

Ingredients

  • 600 g water
  • 1000 g potatoes, peeled and cut into pieces (2 -3 cm)
  • ½ brown onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 fresh bird's eye chillies, trimmed and cut into halves, deseeded if preferred
  • 1 pandan leaf, tied into a knot
  • ½ tsp fenugreek seeds
  • 1 cinnamon quill
  • ¼ tsp ground turmeric
  • 5 fresh curry leaves
  • 100 g coconut milk
  • 100 g coconut cream
  • 1 tsp salt, to taste
  • 1 - 2 tbsp lime juice, to taste

Preparation

  1. Place water into mixing bowl and place Varoma into position. Weigh potatoes into Varoma dish and onto Varoma tray, ensuring some holes are left uncovered so steam can circulate freely. Secure Varoma lid and steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 1, or until potatoes are cooked. Transfer into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl, cover to keep warm and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  2. Place onion, garlic and chillies into mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add pandan leaf, fenugreek, cinnamon, ground turmeric and curry leaves, then heat 2 min/100°C//speed 2.
  4. Add coconut milk, coconut cream and salt and cook 3 min/100°C//speed .
  5. Add lime juice and combine 10 sec//speed .
  6. Transfer into thermal serving bowl with potatoes and stir to combine. Transfer into serving bowls and serve warm.

Useful Items

thermal serving bowl or other large bowl, serving bowls

Hints & Tricks

  • Pandan leaves can be found in the freezer section of most Asian grocery stores. Fresh curry leaves can be found in most supermarkets and Asian grocery stores.
  • Fenugreek seeds can be found in spice shops or online.
