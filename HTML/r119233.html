Eat your veggie gardens

Ingredients

  • 500 g water
  • 1 bunch broccolini, cut into 12 mini trees
  • 1 garlic clove (small)
  • 1 tbsp hulled tahini
  • 100 g carrot, cut into pieces
  • 400 g canned chickpeas, rinsed and drained (approx. 250 g after draining)
  • 2 tsp lemon juice
  • ½ tsp salt, to taste
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 2 celery stalks, cut into 12 pieces
  • ½ red capsicum, cut into 12 strips
  • 6 snow peas, cut into halves diagonally
  • 12 cherry tomatoes
  • 1 Lebanese cucumber, cut into 12 sticks

Preparation

  1. Place water into mixing bowl and insert simmering basket. Place broccolini into simmering basket and cook 5 min/100°C/speed 1.  Allow to sit in simmering basket for 2-3 minutes, then remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and rinse broccolini under cold running water to cease cooking. Set aside to drain. Rinse and dry mixing bowl.
  2. Place garlic, tahini, carrot, chickpeas, lemon juice and salt into mixing bowl and blend 10 sec/speed 5.
  3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Add oil and blend 2 min/speed 4.5.
  4. Divide hommus equally between 12 small bowls or glasses. Place 1 broccolini tree into each bowl and divide the rest of the chopped vegetables between bowls to create miniature gardens. Insert toothpicks into cherry tomatoes, if desired, for older children. Serve immediately or transfer into refrigerator until ready to serve (see Tips).

Useful Items

tooth picks, small bowls, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • If storing in the refrigerator before the party, remove 10 minutes before serving to allow dip to come to room temperature.
