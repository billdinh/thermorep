Gluten-Free Herbed Pizza Crust

Ingredients

Gluten-Free Dough

  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil, plus extra to grease and brush
  • 6 oz brown rice flour
  • 3 oz cassava flour
  • 2 tbsp potato starch
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp dried parsley
  • ½ tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 package rapid rise yeast
  • 1 large egg
  • 6 oz water

Assembly

  • 3 tbsp pesto sauce
  • fresh mozzarella (approx. 8 oz), sliced, to taste
  • olive oil, to brush
  • 1 plum tomato, sliced
  • fresh basil leaves, to garnish

Preparation

Gluten-Free Dough

  1. Grease a pizza pan with olive oil.
  2. Place rice flour, cassava flour, potato starch, honey, salt, parsley, oregano, basil, yeast, egg, water and olive oil into mixing bowl and knead 1 min/. Transfer dough to prepared pizza pan.
  3. Preheat oven to 425°F. Press dough evenly out from the center with slightly wet hands into a circle (approx. 12 in.). Fold over edges to form a crust. Cover with a damp cloth and let rise in a warm place (approx. 50 min.). Dough is ready when it springs back quickly when touched.

Assembly

  1. Bake for 10 minutes (425°F). Remove crust from oven, top with pesto and cheese. Brush edges with olive oil. Return to oven and bake a further 10-15 minutes (425°F) or until cheese melts. Garnish with sliced tomato and basil. Slice and serve hot.

Useful Items

pizza pan (approx. 12 in.), oven
