Buffalo Chicken Quinoa Fritters

Ingredients

Blue Cheese Dipping Sauce

  • 2 oz mayonnaise
  • 2 oz Greek yogurt
  • 2 oz blue cheese
  • 1 - 2 oz whole milk, as needed

Buffalo Fritters

  • 5 oz hot sauce
  • 2 oz unsalted butter, cut into pieces
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 3 ½ oz quinoa
  • 35 oz water
  • 16 oz chicken thighs, air-chilled, boneless, skinless
  • 2 oz carrots, cut into pieces
  • 2 oz celery, cut into pieces
  • 3 oz panko bread crumbs
  • 1 large egg
  • oil, to fry

Preparation

Blue Cheese Dipping Sauce

  1. Place mayonnaise, Greek yogurt, blue cheese and milk into mixing bowl and mix 15 sec/speed 3. Transfer to a bowl, cover with cling wrap and refrigerate until needed. Clean mixing bowl.

Buffalo Fritters

  1. Place hot sauce, butter and Worcestershire into mixing bowl and cook 4 min/212°F/speed 3. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Insert simmering basket, weigh in quinoa and water and rinse 5 sec/speed 5. Place Varoma into position and weigh in chicken, drizzle 4 tbsp reserved buffalo sauce over chicken, secure Varoma lid and steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  3. Remove Varoma and simmering basket and set aside. Discard water and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Place carrots and celery into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  5. Add reserved steamed chicken, quinoa, reserved buffalo sauce, panko and egg and shred 8 sec//speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  6. Heat oil in a deep pot over medium heat (360°F-370°F). Form 12 patties (approx. 3 oz ea.) and deep fry until browned (approx. 1 min.). Drain on paper towels before serving with reserved Blue Cheese Dipping Sauce.

Useful Items

cling wrap, pot, paper towels, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with carrot and celery sticks.
  • Make an extra batch of buffalo sauce to serve alongside the Blue Cheese Dipping Sauce.
  • Fritters can also be lightly pan fried in batches with 2-3 tablespoons oil over medium heat, or baked (350°F) for 25 minutes.
