Rhubarb and vanilla rhumba

Ingredients

  • 400 g rhubarb, trimmed and cut into pieces (5 cm)
  • 90 g muscovado sugar
  • 1 lime, juice only
  • 1 tsp vanilla paste
  • 50 g water
  • 450 g ice
  • 280 g dark rum
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
301 kJ / 71 kcal
Protein
1.1 g
Carbohydrates
15.8 g
Fat
0.1 g

Preparation

  1. Place rhubarb, sugar, lime juice, vanilla paste and water into mixing bowl and cook 10 min/100°C//speed 1.5. Remove mixing bowl lid and allow to cool for 10 minutes.
  2. Strain mixture through a fine-mesh sieve into a bowl. Transfer solids into a sealable container and place into refrigerator (see Tips). Set rhubarb syrup aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Place rhubarb syrup, ice and rum into mixing bowl and blend 45 sec/speed 9. Transfer into glasses and serve immediately.

Useful Items

fine mesh sieve, sealable container, bowl, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Use reserved rhubarb solids as a topping for muesli, porridge or yoghurt.
  • Adjust rum or ice to achieve desired strength of cocktail.
  • Dark brown sugar can be used instead of muscovado.

  • Virgin rhumba: Omit rum and top up glasses with soda water.
