Butternut mac and cheese

Ingredients

Macadamia crumb

  • 2 slices bread of choice
  • 1 tbsp macadamia nuts
  • 2 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves only

Creamy butternut sauce and pasta

  • 300 g butternut pumpkin, peeled and cut into pieces (approx. 2-3 cm)
  • 1 brown onion (approx. 180 g), cut into quarters
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 tsp stock paste (see Tips)
  • 700 g filtered water
  • 2 tbsp nutritional yeast (see Tips)
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 250 g dried macaroni pasta (egg free)
  • 150 g broccoli, cut into florets (approx. 2 cm)
  • 150 g frozen corn kernels
  • 150 g frozen peas
  • vegan Parmesan cheese, to serve (see Tips)

Preparation

Macadamia crumb

  1. Place bread, macadamias and thyme into mixing bowl and chop 6 sec/speed 8. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.

Creamy butternut sauce and pasta

  1. Place pumpkin, onion and garlic into mixing bowl and cook 10 min/100°C/speed 3, then blend 1 min/speed 4-9, increasing speed gradually from speed 4 to speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add stock paste and water, then cook 15 min/100°C/speed 2.
  3. Preheat oven grill.
  4. Add nutritional yeast, lemon juice and pasta, then cook 5 min/100°C//speed 2.
  5. Add broccoli, corn and peas and cook 4-6 min/100°C//speed 2, until pasta is cooked to your liking. Transfer mixture into a ceramic baking dish (25 x 16 cm) and sprinkle Macadamia crumb over the top. Place under grill to toast, until golden. Sprinkle with vegan Parmesan to serve.

Useful Items

bowl, ceramic or glass baking dish (25 x 16 cm), oven grill

Hints & Tricks

  • Please refer to the Mushroom stock paste recipe on this recipe chip or The Basic Cookbook for the Vegetable stock paste recipe.
  • With a cheesy, umami flavour, nutritional yeast contains all nine essential amino acids, is a great source of B vitamins and is often fortified with vitamin B12 (important for those following a vegan diet). You can find nutritional yeast at health food stores or online.
  • To make your own vegan Parmesan cheese, please refer to the recipe on this recipe chip.
