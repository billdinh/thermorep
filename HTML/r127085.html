Green Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 250 g avocado, cut in pieces
  • 60 g fresh spinach leaves
  • 30 g blanched almonds
  • 80 g yoghurt (probiotic)
  • 300 g soya milk, vanilla flavour
  • 350 - 400 g water, cold, to taste
  • 2 - 3 Tbsp honey, to taste
Nutrition
per 1 glass
Calories
1224 kJ / 292 kcal
Protein
7 g
Carbohydrates
16 g
Fat
21 g

Preparation

  1. Place avocados, spinach, almonds, probiotic yoghurt, vanilla soya milk, water and honey in mixing bowl then blend 1 min/speed 10. Serve immediately.
