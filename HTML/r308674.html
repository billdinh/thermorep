Banana Pancake Bites

Ingredients

Pancakes

  • 140 g plain flour
  • 30 g cocoa powder
  • 30 g caster sugar
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
  • 2 medium eggs
  • 300 g milk
  • unsalted butter, for frying

Chocolate Sauce

  • 200 g milk chocolate, small pieces or callets
  • 150 g double cream
  • 5 bananas

Preparation

Pancakes

  1. Place flour, cocoa powder, sugar and salt in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 7.
  2. Add eggs and 200 g milk then mix 30 sec/speed 3.5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add remaining 100 g milk and mix 30 sec/speed 3.5. Set aside in mixing bowl for 10 minutes. Place 1 knob of butter in a large pancake pan or frying pan and heat. When butter is hot, pour a ladle of dough into pan and spread over entire surface by turning the pan in a circular motion. The pancake must be quite thick. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, then transfer to a plate and set aside. Repeat until all batter has been used. Clean mixing bowl.

Chocolate Sauce

  1. Place chocolate in mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then melt 5-6 min/55°C/speed 1 until melted.
  2. Add cream and mix 30 sec/speed 2.5. Spread 1-2 Tbsp chocolate sauce over a pancake then place a whole banana on top and roll up pancake. Cut into slices (2 cm) and set aside on a serving plate. Repeat until all ingredients have been used. Serve or store in fridge until needed.

Useful Items

ladle, pancake pan or frying pan, hob, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Children can assist with this recipe, weighing ingredients in and setting the controls. Never leave them unattended with Thermomix®, while it is running or between steps.
  • Children can also help with assembly of the pancake bites.
