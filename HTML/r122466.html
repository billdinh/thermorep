Creamy honey mustard chicken

Ingredients

  • 2 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 150 g white wine
  • 1 ½ tbsp Dijon mustard (gluten free)
  • 1 ½ tbsp seeded mustard (gluten free)
  • 900 g boneless skinless chicken thighs, trimmed and cut into cubes (2-3cm)
  • 150 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 1 tsp Chicken stock paste (see Tips)
  • 140 g water
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • ¼ tsp salt, plus extra for seasoning
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper, plus extra for seasoning
  • 2 potatoes (approx. 200 g), cut into thin slices (3 mm)
  • 1 spring onion/shallot, trimmed and cut into thin slices
  • 1 tbsp sorghum
  • 100 g frozen green peas
  • 50 g zucchini, peeled into ribbons
  • 80 g sugar snap peas, trimmed
  • 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed and cut into pieces
  • 2 yellow squash (patty pan squash), cut into thin slices

Preparation

  1. Place parsley into mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a small bowl and set aside.
  2. Place garlic into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add oil and cook 2 min/100°C/speed 1.
  4. Add wine and cook 8 min/100°C/speed 2, without measuring cup.
  5. Add mustards and mix 10 sec/speed 4.
  6. Add chicken, cream, stock paste, 100 g of the water, honey, salt and pepper. Place potatoes into Varoma dish, sprinkle over spring onion/shallot, then season potatoes with salt and pepper. Place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and cook 10 min/Varoma//speed . Remove Varoma and set aside.
  7. Place a small bowl onto mixing bowl lid. Weigh remaining 40 g water into it, then add sorghum and stir until smooth. Add mixture into mixing bowl.
  8. Place Varoma dish into position. Insert Varoma tray and weigh peas, zucchini and sugar snap peas onto tray. Add asparagus and squash onto tray. Secure Varoma lid and steam 10 min/Varoma//speed .
  9. Serve chicken and mustard sauce with potatoes and vegetables on the side, sprinkled with reserved parsley.

Hints & Tricks

  • Always check the label of your mustards to ensure they are gluten free.
  • Please refer to The Basic Cookbook or Everyday Cookbook for the Chicken stock paste recipe.
