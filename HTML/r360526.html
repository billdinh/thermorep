S'more Brownie Cups

Ingredients

Graham Cracker Crust

  • 4 oz graham crackers, broken into pieces
  • 2 ½ oz sugar
  • 2 ½ oz unsalted butter, cubed

Brownie Layer

  • 6 ½ oz sugar
  • 3 ½ oz all-purpose flour
  • 6 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ¼ tsp baking powder
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 3 tbsp water
  • 2 oz vegetable oil
  • 3 oz semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 2 large eggs

Meringue Topping

  • 4 oz water
  • 4 oz sugar
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 3 egg whites, from large eggs
  • 1 pinch salt
  • ¼ tsp cream of tartar

Preparation

Graham Cracker Crust

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line a 12 hole muffin pan with baking cups and set aside.
  2. Place graham crackers, sugar and butter into mixing bowl and grind 5 sec/speed 7. Spoon 2 heaping tablespoons of mixture evenly into each muffin cup and press to form a compact layer. Bake for 10 minutes (350°F). Set aside.

Brownie Layer

  1. Place sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, salt, water, oil, chocolate chips and eggs into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix a further 5 sec/speed 4. Divide evenly into pre-baked crusts and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.

Meringue Topping

  1. Place water, sugar and cornstarch into mixing bowl and heat 6 min/240°F/speed 2. Transfer syrup mixture to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg whites, salt and cream of tartar into mixing bowl and without measuring cup whip 1 min/speed 3.5. Mix 1 min/speed 3.5 meanwhile, slowly add reserved syrup mixture through hole in mixing bowl lid. Insert measuring cup and continue to mix a further 5 min/speed 3.5. Remove butterfly whisk.
  3. Divide meringue over brownie layers, creating peaks and swirls with a spoon ensuring meringue covers brownie batter completely. Bake for 28-30 minutes (350°F), or until lightly browned. Cool 10 minutes before removing from pan. Serve at room temperature.

Useful Items

12 hole muffin pan, muffin liners/baking cups, oven
