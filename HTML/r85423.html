Chocolate and Cream Cheese Tart

Ingredients

Pastry

  • 150 g butter, diced, plus extra for greasing
  • 60 g granulated sugar
  • 210 g plain flour

Filling

  • 150 g dark chocolate, 70% cocoa solids, pieces or callets
  • 180 g granulated sugar
  • 200 g cream cheese
  • 3 large eggs

Preparation

Pastry

  1. Grease a loose-bottomed fluted tart tin (Ø 28 cm) then set aside.
  2. Place sugar in mixing bowl and grind 5 sec/speed 10.
  3. Add butter and melt 3 min/50°C/speed 2.
  4. Add flour and mix 2-4 sec/speed 6 until just clinging together. Press into tart tin using your fingers. Chill in fridge for 30 minutes. Meanwhile, wash and dry mixing bowl and preheat oven to 150°C.
  5. Bake tart shell 15-18 minutes (150°C) until golden. Remove from oven and leave in tin to cool completely.

Filling

  1. Place dark chocolate, sugar, cream cheese and eggs in mixing bowl then heat 10 min/60°C/speed 3. Transfer mixture to baked and cooled pastry shell and allow to cool for 30 minutes before placing in fridge to set (approx. 3-4 hours). Serve chilled.

Useful Items

fluted tart tin (Ø 28 cm), refrigerator, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • This tart can be made a day ahead and stored in the fridge, making it perfect for a dinner party dessert.
