Tomato and Haddock Rice

Ingredients

  • 100 g onions, quartered
  • 50 g red pepper, cut in pieces
  • 50 g olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 40 g tomato purée
  • 1 Tbsp paprika
  • 200 g long grain rice
  • 650 g water
  • 1 teaspoon fish stock paste
  • 400 g haddock fillets, skinless, boneless
  • 120 g frozen peas
  • 50 g green olives, pitted

Preparation

  1. Place onions, red pepper, oil and garlic in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and sauté 5 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  2. Add tomato purée and paprika and cook 2 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  3. Insert simmering basket and weigh in rice. Remove simmering basket then rinse rice under cold running water. Pour contents of mixing bowl into simmering basket then stir with a fork.
  4. Place water and stock in mixing bowl then insert simmering basket. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh in haddock. Cover then cook 20 min/Varoma/speed 4.
  5. Set Varoma aside and add peas to simmering basket. Place Varoma back into position and cook 10 min/Varoma/speed 4. Remove Varoma. With aid of spatula, transfer contents of simmering basket to a serving bowl. Discard cooking water.
  6. Place steamed haddock in mixing bowl and flake 1 sec/speed 3. Transfer to serving bowl then mix together.
  7. Serve decorated with olives.
