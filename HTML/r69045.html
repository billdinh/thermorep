Vitality truffles

Ingredients

  • 150 g dried apricots
  • hot water, to cover apricots, for soaking
  • 100 g hazelnuts
  • 100 g pitted dates
  • 100 g dried figs
  • 100 g rolled oats
  • 50 g raisins
  • 100 g sesame seeds, for coating
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
371 kJ / 88 kcal
Protein
2 g
Carbohydrates
10 g

4 g

Preparation

  1. Place apricots and enough hot water to cover into a small bowl and allow to soak for 15 minutes. Transfer apricots into simmering basket and drain well.
  2. Place soaked and drained apricots and hazelnuts into mixing bowl and chop 30 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add pitted dates, dried figs, rolled oats and raisins and chop 30-40 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Repeat chopping if necessary.
  4. Take 1 tbsp of mixture, form into a ball and coat with sesame seeds. Repeat process until all mixture is used. Transfer into an airtight container to store or serve immediately.

Useful Items

airtight container

Hints & Tricks

  • Vitality truffles are perfect for a sweet snack on the go.
  • Truffles are also delicious rolled in desiccated coconut, cocoa or LSA mix (i.e. milled linseeds, sunflower seeds and almonds) as an alternative to sesame seeds.
  • For different flavours, use different dried fruits and nuts of choice.
  • Nut free truffles: substitute the hazelnuts with pepitas (pumpkin seed kernels) and reduce chopping time in step 2 to 10 sec/speed 9.
