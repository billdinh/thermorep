Broccoli Mozzarella Bites

Ingredients

  • 4 oz mozzarella cheese, cubed
  • 1 oz Parmesan cheese, cubed
  • 35 oz water
  • 12 oz broccoli florets, medium-size
  • 3 oz shallots, halved
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp dried parsley
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • Tabasco sauce, to taste
  • 3 oz panko
  • ketchup, to serve
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
151 kJ / 36 kcal
Protein
2 g
Carbohydrates
3 g
Fat
2 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Prepare two baking sheets with parchment and set aside.
  2. Place mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses into mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position and weigh in broccoli, secure Varoma lid and steam 12 min/Varoma/speed 2. Rinse broccoli under cold water, drain and set aside. Discard steaming water and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Place shallots and garlic into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add reserved broccoli, parsley, basil, salt, eggs, Tabasco, panko and reserved grated cheese and mix 10 sec/speed 5 or until broccoli is chopped. Press to form into 28 balls, transfer to prepared baking sheets and lightly flatten. Bake for 20 minutes (350°F), turn patties over and bake an additional 10 minutes or until golden brown. Serve immediately with ketchup or aioli.

Useful Items

parchment paper, baking sheets, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Broccoli Mozzarella Bites can be made ahead and refrigerated or frozen until ready to use. Heat for 10-15 minutes (350°F) from refrigerated and 15-20 minutes (350°F) from frozen.
