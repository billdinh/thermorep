Bread and tomato soup, rolled spinach omelette and steamed ratatouille vegetables

Ingredients

  • 60 g medium-hard cheese (e.g. Gruyère or Cheddar), cut into pieces
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 50 g extra virgin olive oil, plus extra for serving
  • 50 g day old bread rolls, cut into pieces (1-2 cm)
  • 200 g frozen chopped spinach, thawed
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tsp salt, plus extra to taste
  • 50 g onions, cut into halves
  • 50 g carrots, cut into pieces
  • 700 g ripe tomatoes, cut into pieces
  • 6 - 8 fresh basil leaves
  • 500 g water
  • 1 heaped tsp Vegetable stock paste (refer to The Basic Cookbook)
  • 400 g mixed vegetables (e.g. capsicum, zucchini, eggplant), cut into pieces (2-3 cm)

Preparation

  1. Place cheese into mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place garlic cloves and 10 g extra virgin olive oil into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5, then sauté 2 min/120°C/speed 1.
  3. Add bread and sauté 5 min/120°C//speed 1, without measuring cup. Meanwhile, line Varoma tray with baking paper with enough to go 2 cm up the sides. Transfer sautéed garlic bread to Varoma dish and set aside.
  4. Place spinach, 10 g extra virgin olive oil, eggs and ½ tsp salt into mixing bowl and chop 30 sec/speed 5.5. Transfer spinach-egg mixture onto lined Varoma tray, spread to the borders, sprinkle with reserved grated cheese and insert Varoma tray into Varoma dish.
  5. Place onions, carrots, remaining 30 g olive oil, tomatoes, |1-2 basil leaves, water, stock paste and ½ tsp salt into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  6. Insert simmering basket and add mixed vegetables into it. Place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and cook 30 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove Varoma. With aid of baking paper lining, roll up the spinach omelette, keeping it wrapped in the baking paper. Meanwhile remove simmering basket with aid of spatula, transfer cooked vegetables to a shallow serving dish, season with salt to taste and stir. Unwrap the spinach omelette and cut in slices (approx. 2 cm). Transfer slices to serving dish with vegetables and keep warm. Transfer tomato soup to a serving bowl, add reserved steamed garlic bread to the soup, drizzle with some extra virgin olive oil and garnish with torn basil leaves. Serve the soup hot as a starter and the rolled omelette warm as a main dish with the ratatouille vegetables on the side.

Useful Items

baking paper, bowl, serving dish, serving bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • For a smooth soup, after removing Varoma and simmering basket in step 6, blend 1 min/speed 5-10, increasing speed gradually from speed 5 to speed 10. Add the steamed garlic bread and serve as described in step 6.
  • To make your own bread, refer to recipes in The Basic Cookbook.
  • Replace spinach in the rolled omelette with vegetables of your choice (e.g. cabbage, zucchini).
