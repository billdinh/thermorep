Haupia Pie

Ingredients

Tart shell

  • 130 g salted butter, plus extra for greasing
  • ½ lemon, zest only
  • 60 g soft dark brown sugar
  • 160 g plain flour
  • 1 pinch sea salt
  • 50 g macadamia nuts
  • 50 g shelled salted pistachio nuts
  • 50 g roasted unsalted cashew nuts

Filling

  • 400 g coconut cream
  • 400 g coconut milk
  • 110 g raw sugar
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • 2 tsp vanilla bean extract
  • 80 g cornflour

Topping

  • 600 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 50 g shelled salted pistachio nuts, chopped
  • 20 g shredded coconut, for garnishing

Preparation

Tart shell

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Grease a pie dish (23 cm) and set aside.
  2. Place lemon zest and sugar into mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Mill 10 sec/speed 9, until fine. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add butter and melt 3 min/100°C/speed 1.
  5. Add remaining tart shell ingredients and mix 15 sec/speed 6. Transfer mixture into prepared pie dish and press mixture into the base and sides. Bake for 10-15 minutes (180°C).  Set aside and leave to cool completely. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Filling

  1. Place all filling ingredients into mixing bowl and combine 5 sec/speed 6. Cook 8 min/80°C/speed 4, then cook for a further 2 min/100°C/speed 2.
  2. Pour mixture into cooled tart shell and place into refrigerator to set (approx. 2 hours). Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Topping

  1. Insert butterfly whisk. Place cream into mixing bowl and whip 10-20 sec/speed 4 or until stiff peaks form, carefully watching through hole in mixing bowl lid to avoid over-whipping.
  2. Transfer whipped cream over cooled filling, then garnish with chopped pistachios and shredded coconut. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

pie dish (23 cm), oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • You can make this pie several hours before serving. Proceed as per recipe through to step 6, then store in the refrigerator. Just before serving, continue with steps 7 and 8.
