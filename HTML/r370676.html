Leek quiche

Ingredients

Pie crust

  • 100 g blanched almonds
  • 40 g sesame seeds
  • 200 g rolled oats
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 125 g filtered water

Leek filling

  • 1 brown onion, cut into halves
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 fresh long red chilli, trimmed and deseeded if preferred, cut into halves
  • 3 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 40 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 leeks (approx. 400 g), white part only, cut into thin slices (5 mm)
  • 100 g raw cashews
  • 150 g filtered water
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 40 g cornflour
  • 300 g silken tofu, cut into pieces
  • 1 - 2 pinches paprika

Preparation

Pie crust

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Set aside a loose-based fluted tart tin (23 cm).
  2. Place almonds, sesame seeds, oats and salt into mixing bowl and mill 1 min/speed 9.
  3. Add water and mix 10 sec/speed 6. Transfer mixture into tart tin and evenly press into base and sides. Mixture may be sticky at first but will become easier to work with as it dries. Line pie shell with baking paper, then fill with pie weights, rice or dried beans. Place tart tin onto baking tray and bake for 18 minutes (180°C). Remove from oven, then remove baking paper with pie weights and set aside until ready to use. Reduce oven temperature to (160°C). Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Leek filling

  1. Place onion, garlic, chilli and parsley into mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add oil and leeks and sauté 5 min/120°C//speed 1. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Place cashews into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 9.
  4. Add water and blend 1 min/speed 9.
  5. Add salt, cornflour and tofu and blend 30 sec/speed 9.
  6. Add reserved vegetables and mix 5 sec//speed 3. Transfer into prepared pie crust, sprinkle with paprika and bake for 40-45 min (160°C), until just firm in the centre. Set aside to cool slightly before serving.

Useful Items

baking paper, pie weights, rice or dried beans, bowl, loose-based round fluted tart tin (23 cm), baking tray, oven
