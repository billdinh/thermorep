Broccoli and Stilton Soup

Ingredients

  • 500 g broccoli
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 40 g olive oil
  • 150 g onions, quartered
  • 100 g celery stalks, cut in pieces (3 cm)
  • 120 g potatoes, peeled, cut in pieces (3 cm)
  • 500 g vegetable stock
  • 80 g Stilton cheese, crumbled in pieces
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt, or to taste
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper, or to taste
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1097 kJ / 262 kcal
Protein
11.7 g
Carbohydrates
11.5 g
Fat
18.8 g

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh broccoli into it. Cut broccoli stalks in pieces (3 cm) and place in mixing bowl. Cut remainder of broccoli head into florets and set aside.
  2. Add garlic, oil, onions, celery, potatoes to mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 6, then sauté 5 min/100°C/speed 1.
  3. Add stock and reserved broccoli florets then cook 10 min/100°C/speed 1.
  4. Add Stilton, salt and pepper then blend 2 min/speed 10. Adjust seasoning to taste before serving.
