Sunrise Margarita (non-alcoholic)

Ingredients

  • 2 lemons (approx.140 g), flesh only, halved and pips removed
  • 2 limes (approx. 70 g), flesh only, halved
  • 2 oranges (approx. 210 g), flesh only, halved and pips removed
  • 250 g fresh pineapple pieces
  • 2 - 3 tbsp caster sugar, to taste
  • 250 g ice cubes
  • 150 g water
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
313 kJ / 74 kcal
Protein
0.9 g
Carbohydrates
15 g
Fat
0.2 g

Preparation

  1. Place lemon, lime, orange and pineapple in mixing bowl then mix 15 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add sugar, ice and water then crush 1 min/speed 7. Pour into serving glasses and serve immediately.

Useful Items

6 serving glasses or bowls

Hints & Tricks

  • This non-alcoholic cocktail is a refreshing blend of citrus and pineapple. It makes a great option if you're not drinking. Alternatively, guaranteed to wake you up after a big night out or perfect to kick start a busy day!
