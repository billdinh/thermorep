Apples with Cider Sabayon Sauce

Ingredients

Steamed Apples

  • 4 dessert apples (approx. 150 g each) (e.g. Braeburn, Golden Delicious, Fuji, Royal Gala), peeled and cored
  • 2 - 3 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 500 g water

Cider Sabayon Sauce

  • 50 g sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 160 g dry cider
  • 2 tsp cinnamon sugar, for sprinkling

Preparation

Steamed Apples

  1. Cut apples in eighths and place them in a dish. Sprinkle with lemon juice and mix well to help prevent apples turning brown. Arrange apple pieces in centre of Varoma dish and on Varoma tray and set aside.
  2. Place water in mixing bowl, set Varoma with tray in position and steam 18-22 min/Varoma/speed 1, until apples are soft. Steaming time depends on variety and ripeness of the apples.
  3. Set Varoma aside uncovered and allow apples to cool. Empty and dry mixing bowl.

Cider Sabayon Sauce

  1. Place sugar in mixing bowl and grind 15 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Add egg, egg yolks and cider and heat 9 min/70Â°C/speed 3. Meanwhile, arrange apple pieces on 4 dessert plates. Remove butterfly whisk.
  3. Pour sabayon over apple pieces, sprinkle with cinnamon sugar and serve immediately.
