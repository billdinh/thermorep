Remoulade chicken with prosciutto potatoes

Ingredients

Chicken and potatoes

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 40 g olive oil
  • 40 g lemon juice (approx. 1 lemon)
  • 1 tsp ground sweet paprika
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp salt, plus extra for seasoning
  • 500 - 600 g chicken tenderloins (see Tips)
  • 500 g hot water
  • 400 - 500 g new baby potatoes, unpeeled and cut into quarters
  • 100 g prosciutto, cut into bite-size pieces
  • 2 tbsp pickled capers

Salad with buttermilk dressing

  • 2 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 2 pickled gherkins (approx. 40 g), cut into halves
  • 100 g mayonnaise (see Tips)
  • 100 g buttermilk
  • Â¼ tsp salt
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 1 - 2 baby cos lettuce, trimmed and cut into wedges
  • Parmesan cheese, shaved, to serve (optional)

Preparation

Chicken and potatoes

  1. Place garlic into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 6.
  2. Add 20 g of the oil, lemon juice, paprika, oregano, salt and pepper and mix 4 sec/speed 5.
  3. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh chicken into it. Add marinade and toss to coat.
  4. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position, weigh potatoes into it and season with extra salt. Insert Varoma tray and place coated chicken onto it, secure Varoma lid and steam 15-18 min/Varoma/speed 4 or until potatoes and chicken are cooked. Meanwhile, place a frying pan with remaining oil over high heat. Add prosciutto and capers and fry until crispy (approx. 4-5 minutes).
  5. Remove Varoma tray and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl. Place potatoes, fried prosciutto and capers into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and toss to combine. Set aside.

Salad with buttermilk dressing

  1. Place parsley, gherkins, mayonnaise, buttermilk, salt and pepper into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 6.
  2. Cut reserved chicken into bite-size pieces. Divide lettuce and Parmesan shavings (optional) between serving plates. Arrange chicken and reserved potato mixture onto a serving platter. Drizzle with Buttermilk dressing to serve (see Tips).

Useful Items

bowl, thermal serving bowl or other large bowl, serving plates, frying pan, serving platter, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • You can substitute chicken tenderloins for chicken breasts, but the cooking times will vary.
  • To make your own mayonnaise, please refer to The Basic Cookbook. Making your own mayonnaise will increase the total time of recipe.
  • This dressing makes enough to drizzle over salad, chicken and potatoes.
  • This dish can be served warm or cold.
