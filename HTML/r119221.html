Basque Chicken

Ingredients

  • ½ oz fresh parsley leaves, (approx. 25 sprigs)
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 8 oz yellow onions, quartered (approx. 2 med. onions)
  • ½ oz olive oil
  • 2 tbsp corn starch
  • 10 oz canned crushed tomatoes
  • 4 oz dry white wine
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp dried basil
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 dried bay leaf
  • 24 oz chicken thighs, boneless and skinless, quartered
  • 5 oz red bell pepper, cut into ¼ in. strips (approx. 1 med. pepper)
  • 5 oz yellow peppers, cut into ¼ in. strips (approx. 1 med. pepper)
  • 2 oz pitted Kalamata olives

Preparation

  1. Place parsley leaves into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place garlic, onions and oil into mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and sauté 5 min/250°F/speed 2.
  3. Add corn starch, avoiding the blade, and cook 1 min/212°F/speed 2.
  4. Add tomatoes, wine, paprika, oregano, salt, basil, pepper and bay leaf and cook 8 min/212°F/speed 1.
  5. Place Varoma into position, weigh in chicken pieces, secure Varoma lid and cook 15 min/Varoma/speed 2. Stir chicken pieces with spatula. Insert Varoma tray, weigh in red and yellow peppers, secure Varoma lid and cook 10 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  6. Transfer cooked chicken and peppers to a large serving bowl. Add olives and sauce and stir with spatula. Garnish with reserved parsley. Serve hot.
