Shrimp and Vodka Penne Pasta

Ingredients

  • 4 oz yellow onion, in pieces
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbsp dried basil
  • ¼ tsp dried red pepper flakes, to taste
  • 3 oz vodka
  • 28 oz tomatoes, crushed, canned
  • 16 oz water
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • ½ tsp salt, to taste
  • 10 oz penne pasta, dried
  • 24 oz large peeled shrimp
  • 4 oz heavy whipping cream
  • fresh basil leaves, to serve

Preparation

  1. Place onion, garlic, oil, basil and pepper flakes into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and without measuring cup cook 4 min/250°F/speed 1.
  2. Add vodka and without measuring cup cook 3 min/250°F/speed 1.
  3. Add tomatoes, water, balsamic vinegar and salt. Set Varoma dish (without lid) instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 15 min/212°F/speed 1.
  4. Remove Varoma dish. Add pasta through hole in mixing bowl lid. Set Varoma into position, weigh in shrimp, secure Varoma lid and cook for time indicated on pasta package/Varoma//speed 1.
  5. Remove Varoma and set aside. Add whipping cream to mixing bowl and with aid of spatula mix 2 min/195°F//speed 0.5.
  6. Transfer pasta and shrimp to a serving bowl and toss to combine. Garnish with fresh basil leaves and serve.
