Asian Chicken Noodle Soup

Ingredients

Chicken and Marinade

  • 1 whole uncooked chicken (40-48 oz, approx. 2 ½-3 pound chicken)
  • 1 organic lemon, halved
  • 1 yellow onion, quartered (approx. 5.5 oz)
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • ¾ in. fresh ginger, peeled (approx. 0.5 oz)
  • 3 green onions, trimmed and quartered (approx. 2 oz)
  • 3 sprigs cilantro, stems and leaves
  • 2 tbsp miso
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp mirin

Broth

  • 1 yellow onion (approx. 5.5 oz), halved
  • 1 - 2 fresh chilies (e.g. serrano), deseeded if preferred
  • 3 - 4 shiitake mushroom, dried, sliced
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp chicken stock paste (see Tip)
  • 55 oz water
  • 4 eggs, washed
  • 14 oz udon noodles, fresh (see Tip)
  • 4 oz carrot, halved lengthways and sliced (½ in.) (approx. 1-2 carrots)
  • 3 ½ - 5 ½ oz baby corn, canned, drained and halved
  • 5 ½ - 7 oz broccolini (approx. 1 bunch) trimmed and cut into pieces (1½-2 in.)
  • 1 - 2 tsp salt, to taste
  • 1 - 2 green onions, trimmed and cut into thin slices, to garnish
  • 2 - 3 sprigs cilantro, leaves only, torn, to garnish
  • 1 tbsp ready-made fried onions, to garnish (optional)
  • 1 nori sheet, cut into thin strips, to garnish (optional)

Preparation

Chicken and Marinade

  1. Place chicken into Varoma dish and fill chicken cavity with lemon and yellow onion. Set Varoma with chicken aside.
  2. Place garlic, ginger, green onions and cilantro into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add miso, soy sauce and mirin and mix 5 sec/speed 4. Rub garlic-ginger marinade all over chicken and set Varoma aside.

Broth

  1. Place onion and chili into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Transfer into simmering basket and set aside.
  2. Place mushrooms, oil, stock paste and water into mixing bowl and insert simmering basket with onion and chili. Place Varoma with chicken into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 45 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Add eggs to Varoma dish, arranging them around the chicken, secure Varoma lid and steam 10 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove eggs from Varoma and set cooked eggs aside. Continue to steam chicken a further 5-10 min/Varoma/speed 1 or until chicken has an internal temperature of 165°F. Set Varoma aside and allow chicken and eggs to cool. Remove simmering basket with spatula and discard contents.
  4. Re-insert simmering basket, weigh in noodles and cook 1 min 30 sec/212°F/speed 1. Remove simmering basket with spatula, drain noodles and divide between 4 bowls.
  5. Re-insert simmering basket, weigh in carrot, corn and broccolini and cook 5-8 min/212°F//speed 1. Remove simmering basket with spatula, drain vegetables and divide between the bowls, placing over noodles. Season remaining broth to taste with salt and set aside to keep warm.
  6. Remove chicken skin, shred reserved cooled chicken meat and discard carcass. Divide shredded chicken evenly between bowls.
  7. Peel reserved cooled eggs, cut each egg in half and add 2 halves to each bowl. Pour broth into bowls, garnish with green onions, cilantro leaves, fried onions and nori strips. Serve immediately.

Hints & Tricks

  • Please refer to The Basic Cookbook for the Chicken Stock Paste recipe.
  • Remove noodles from packaging, place into Varoma tray and rinse noodles under warm water to separate. Set aside.
  • Find Asian ingredients at your local Asian specialty store.
  • To shred chicken in Thermomix®: place up to 32 oz chicken meat into mixing bowl and shred 5 sec//speed 3.
