Patty Melt

Ingredients

Caramelized Onions

  • 24 oz yellow onions, halved and thinly sliced
  • ½ oz olive oil
  • ½ oz brown sugar

Patties

  • 20 oz sirloin steak, cut into 1 in. pieces, partially frozen, divided
  • salt, to taste
  • ground black pepper, to taste
  • 4 slices Swiss cheese
  • 8 slices rye bread, toasted

Preparation

Caramelized Onions

  1. Place onions, olive oil and brown sugar into mixing bowl, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 30 min/250°F//speed .

Patties

  1. Scrape bottom of mixing bowl with spatula and cook a further 15 min/250°F//speed . Transfer to a bowl and keep warm.
  2. Place 10 oz beef into mixing bowl and grind 10-12 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place remaining 10 oz beef into mixing bowl and grind 10-12 sec/speed 6. Transfer to bowl with reserved ground beef.
  4. Divide and shape ground beef into four equal-sized oval patties and create a dimple in the center by pressing down with your fingers to prevent them from rising while cooking. Season patties generously with salt and pepper and cook over medium high heat on a grill or stovetop to taste. Flip the patties and add cheese slices.
  5. Serve burgers immediately with Caramelized Onions between toasted rye bread.

Hints & Tricks

  • Partially freeze beef for up to 1 hour before grinding to ensure an even grind and to optimize texture.
  • Shape patties to fit the shape of rye bread slices.
