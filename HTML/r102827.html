Chestnut and Cranberry Stuffing

Ingredients

  • 100 g dried cranberries
  • 50 g ruby port
  • 70 g onions, quartered
  • 50 g unsalted butter, plus extra for greasing
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 450 g sausagemeat, broken in pieces
  • 140 g breadcrumbs
  • 140 g cooked chestnuts, peeled, roughly chopped
  • 2 Tbsp fresh flat-leaf parsley leaves, chopped
  • ½ tsp fresh thyme leaves
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • ¼ tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1 medium egg, lightly beaten

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid then weigh in cranberries and port. Set aside to macerate for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
  2. Place onions, butter and garlic in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then sauté 5 min/120°C/speed 0.5.
  3. Remove mixing bowl and leave to cool for 5 minutes. Meanwhile, preheat oven to 190°C and grease a roasting tin.
  4. Place mixing bowl back in position then add sausagemeat, breadcrumbs, chestnuts, parsley, thyme, salt, pepper, nutmeg, egg, macerated cranberries and port then mix 25 sec/speed 4. Roll into balls (Ø 4 cm) and place in prepared roasting tin. Bake for 30-40 minutes (190°C) until browned and cooked through.
  5. Serve as an accompaniment to roast turkey.

Useful Items

roasting tin, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Mix all the ingredients for this stuffing together in a matter of minutes before shaping into balls and baking in the oven. For this and other Christmas staples, visit Cookidoo®.
  • Fry a teaspoon of stuffing in a little butter to taste and adjust the seasoning.
  • Alternatively, lay balls around the turkey or other roasting joint.
  • The stuffing can also be baked as one. Transfer mixture to a baking dish, forming a layer 4 cm thick then bake as per recipe.
