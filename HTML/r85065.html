Marzipan

Ingredients

  • 240 g granulated sugar
  • 220 g blanched almonds
  • 2 egg yolks, from medium eggs
  • 1 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 Tbsp rose water
  • ¼ tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp natural almond extract

Preparation

  1. Place sugar in mixing bowl and grind 1 min/speed 10. Transfer to a wide flat bowl or a plate and set aside to cool.
  2. Place blanched almonds in mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add 220 g reserved icing sugar and mix 10 sec/speed 3.
  4. Add egg yolks, lemon juice, rose water, vanilla extract and almond extract then knead 1 min/(see tip).
  5. Tip out onto work surface and roll out on remaining 20 g icing sugar, as needed, or wrap well in cling film and store in fridge until ready to use.

Useful Items

large plate, cling film

Hints & Tricks

Homemade marzipan will make your dishes taste far superior, as well as making you feel that little bit more proud of your finished bake. The powerful grinding action of the Thermomix® guarantees a smooth result. Use this recipe in the pistachio almond twists and lemon and poppyseed Battenberg cake.

  • Taste your mixture after step 4; if you would like more almond flavour, add more almond extract to taste then knead again 30 sec/.
  • Marzipan will keep for up to a month in the fridge or for up to 6 months in the freezer, if wrapped up well.
