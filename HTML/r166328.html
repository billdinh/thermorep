Meatballs in a rich tomato sauce (daoud basha)

Ingredients

  • 800 g beef mince
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 pinch freshly ground pepper
  • 1 pinch ground cinnamon
  • extra virgin olive oil, for greasing
  • 200 g onions, cut into quarters
  • 100 g carrots, cut into pieces (optional)
  • 45 g sunflower oil
  • 400 g crushed tomatoes, canned
  • 70 g tomato purée (concentrated)
  • 2 tbsp pomegranate molasses (optional)
  • 150 g water
  • 2 tsp cornflour (starch)

Preparation

  1. Place minced beef, 1 tsp salt, pepper and cinnamon into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 3. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Grease Varoma dish and tray and set aside. With oiled hands shape reserved mince mixture into meatballs (Ø 2 cm) and distribute into Varoma dish and tray. Set Varoma aside.
  3. Place onions, carrots and sunflower oil into mixing bowl, chop 3 sec/speed 4, then remove measuring cup sauté 8 min/120°C/speed 1.
  4. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato purée, pomegranate molasses, 1 tsp salt and 100 g water, place Varoma into position and cook 25 min/Varoma/speed 1. Meanwhile mix in a bowl cornflour and remaining 50 g water, stir with a spoon to dissolve.
  5. Remove Varoma and transfer meatballs into mixing bowl with sauce. Add dissolved corn flour and cook 8 min/100°C//speed . Serve immediately.
