Wholegrain scones

Ingredients

  • 200 g brown rice flour , plus extra for dusting
  • 70 g cornflour
  • 30 g quinoa flour
  • 30 g millet flour
  • 3 ½ tsp gluten free baking powder
  • 2 tsp xanthan gum
  • 1 tsp coconut sugar
  • 50 g butter, chilled and cut into pieces
  • 320 g milk, plus extra for brushing
  • whipped cream, to serve
  • Berry chia jam, to serve (see Tips)

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 170°C. Line a large baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place flours, baking powder, xanthan gum, sugar and butter into mixing bowl and mix 5 sec/speed 5.
  3. Add milk and knead 10-15 sec/, until dough comes together.
  4. Transfer onto a silicone bread mat or lightly floured work surface. Gently shape into a ball, then flatten with your hands into a disc (1.5 cm thickness). Using a scone or biscuit cutter (5 cm), cut dough into rounds and place close together onto prepared baking tray. Shape remaining dough into a ball, flatten into a disc (1.5 cm thickness) and cut into rounds (makes 16 scones in total). Place close together onto prepared baking tray. Brush surface of dough with extra milk.
  5. Bake for 10-15 minutes (170°C), or until lightly golden and cooked through. Serve with whipped cream and jam.

Useful Items

baking tray (30 x 40 cm), baking paper, silicone bread mat, pastry brush, round cookie cutter (5 cm), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • To make your own millet flour, place 250 g millet into mixing bowl and mill 2 min/speed 10. Use as needed.
  • To make the Berry chia jam, please refer to the recipe on this recipe chip.
