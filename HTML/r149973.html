Spiced roasted mixed nuts

Ingredients

  • 2 egg whites
  • 1 tbsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp ground coriander
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp onion powder
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 2 pinches ground cinnamon
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp tamari (see Tips)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 100 g macadamia nuts
  • 100 g cashews
  • 100 g almonds
  • 100 g walnuts
  • 100 g pecans
  • 125 g hazelnuts
  • 125 g pepitas
Nutrition
per 1 portion

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Line a large baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place egg whites into mixing bowl. Insert butterfly whisk and whisk 40 sec/speed 4 or until soft peaks just form.
  3. Add all spices, salt, tamari and oil and mix 3 sec/speed 3.
  4. Add all remaining ingredients and mix 10 sec//speed 1.5. Remove butterfly whisk. Transfer onto prepared baking tray and spread out evenly. Toast for 15 minutes (180°C), stirring occasionally, until cooked to your liking. Set aside to cool completely before serving.

Useful Items

baking paper, baking tray (30 x 40 cm), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • You can replace the tamari with 2 teaspoons gluten free soy sauce.
  • You can store any leftovers in a sealable container in a cool, dry place for up to 4 days.
  • Try using whatever nuts and seeds you have in your pantry.
  • If using soaked (activated) nuts, please adjust baking time accordingly.
