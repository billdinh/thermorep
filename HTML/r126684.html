Hot orange chocolate on a stick

Ingredients

  • 30 g sugar
  • orange zest, 2 strips (1 x 4 cm each)
  • 1 - 2 pinches ground cinnamon
  • 100 g dark chocolate, 70% cocoa solids, in pieces
  • 50 g milk chocolate, in pieces
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
504 kJ / 121 kcal
Protein
1 g
Carbohydrates
11 g
Fat
7 g

Preparation

  1. Place sugar, orange zest and cinnamon in mixing bowl and chop 12 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add dark chocolate and milk chocolate and chop 8 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Then melt 3 min/60°C/speed 2.5.
  4. Transfer chocolate to 8 silicone chocolate moulds (each 15 g) and allow to cool at room temperature for 5 minutes.
  5. Stick 1 wooden skewer or spoon into the centre of each chocolate. Allow the chocolate to cool for a further 30 minutes, then cool completely in the fridge for 2 hours. Carefully remove from the moulds, transfer to gift bags and store dry.

Useful Items

wooden skewer, silicone chocolate moulds, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Alternatively, 6 larger moulds (25–35 g) can be used.
  • Unopened, Hot orange chocolate on a stick keeps for up to 2 months if stored in a cool, dry place.
  • Serve Hot orange chocolate on a stick with mugs of hot milk to make hot chocolate.
