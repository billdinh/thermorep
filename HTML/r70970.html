Honeycomb ice cream slice

Ingredients

  • 100 - 200 g Crunchie Bars® (approx. 2), broken into pieces
  • 100 g raw sugar
  • 30 g cocoa powder
  • 1 egg
  • 600 g pouring (whipping) cream or sweetened condensed milk
  • 250 g malt biscuits (1 packet)

Preparation

  1. Line a slice tin (15 x 25 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place Crunchie Bars® into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 5, until evenly chopped. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place sugar, cocoa and egg into mixing bowl and blend 10 sec/speed 10.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then repeat blending 10 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Insert butterfly whisk. Add cream or sweetened condensed milk and beat 1 min/speed 4.
  6. Add reserved Crunchie Bar® mixture and combine 10 sec//speed 3. Remove butterfly whisk.
  7. Place a layer of malt biscuits into prepared tin and cover with cream mixture. Place another layer of malt biscuits over the top (ensuring the biscuits are facing the same way), placing directly above the biscuits of the previous layer. This will form a giant ice cream sandwich that will be easier to serve.
  8. Place in freezer until set (approx. 4 hours). When you are ready to serve, remove from the freezer and leave for 5 minutes before cutting around the malt biscuits into slices and serving.

Useful Items

slice tin (15 x 25 cm), baking paper, bowl, freezer

Hints & Tricks

  • Choc mint ice cream slice: substitute Crunchie Bars® with Peppermint Crisp®. Proceed as per recipe.
