Crispy rice paper rolls

Ingredients

Asian salad and dressing

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 tsp caster sugar
  • 20 g fish sauce
  • ½ fresh long red chilli, trimmed and cut into halves
  • 1 lime, juice only
  • 6 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only
  • 1 - 2 Lebanese cucumbers, deseeded and cut into pieces (3 cm), to taste

Rice paper rolls

  • 2 spring onions/shallots, trimmed and cut into pieces
  • ½ fresh long red chilli, trimmed and cut into halves
  • 3 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • ¼ tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 ½ tsp garam masala
  • 1 bunch green asparagus, trimmed and cut into pieces (2 cm)
  • 350 g firm tofu, cut into pieces (1 cm)
  • 2 eggs
  • 8 spring roll rice paper wrappers
  • 80 g panko breadcrumbs, for coating
  • peanut oil (approx. 150 g), for shallow frying
  • 40 g roasted salted cashew nuts, roughly chopped to serve
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges to serve

Preparation

Asian salad and dressing

  1. Place garlic, sugar, fish sauce, chilli and juice of 1 lime into mixing bowl and chop 15 sec/speed 8. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place coriander and cucumber into mixing bowl and chop 6 sec/speed 4. Transfer into a separate bowl and set aside.

Rice paper rolls

  1. Place spring onions/shallots, chilli, ginger, turmeric and garam masala into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add asparagus, tofu and 1 of the eggs and mix 6 sec//speed 2.
  3. Prepare rice paper wrappers as per packet instructions. Place 1 rice paper wrapper onto a clean work surface. Place one-eighth of the filling (approx. 3 tablespoons) into centre of wrapper. Carefully fold in left and right sides of wrapper, then fold up bottom of wrapper over filling and roll away from you to tightly and completely enclose filling. Repeat with remaining wrappers and filling to make 8 rolls in total.
  4. Crack remaining egg into a bowl and beat lightly. Place a separate bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh panko breadcrumbs into it. Gently coat rice paper rolls in egg, then coat in panko breadcrumbs.
  5. Line a plate with paper towel and set aside. Place a large frying pan over medium-high heat and add oil. Working in batches, fry rolls for approx. 3-4 minutes, turning frequently, until outside is brown and crispy. Transfer onto prepared plate to drain.
  6. Serve spring rolls with reserved cucumber salad and dressing. Sprinkle with cashews and serve with lime wedges.
