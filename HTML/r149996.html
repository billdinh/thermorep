Crunchy Peanut and Chocolate Cereal Bars

Ingredients

  • 60 g dry-roasted peanuts, unsalted
  • 60 g rolled oats
  • 150 g sweetened condensed milk
  • 20 g barley malt extract
  • 45 g honey
  • 15 g desiccated coconut
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 60 g millet flakes
  • 40 g corn flakes
  • 100 g milk chocolate, small pieces or callets
  • 100 g dark chocolate (50% cocoa solids), small pieces or callets

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C and line a baking tray (20 cm x 30 cm) with baking paper.
  2. Place peanuts in mixing bowl and chop 8 sec/speed 5.
  3. Add oats, condensed milk, barley malt, honey, desiccated coconut and salt then heat 1 min/90°C//speed 1.5.
  4. Add millet flakes and corn flakes then, with aid of spatula, mix 1 min//speed 1.5. Tip out onto prepared baking tray and spread evenly using a soft spatula. Using a sharp knife, cut into bars (approx. 5 cm x 10 cm).
  5. Bake for 12 minutes (180°C). Remove from tin and cut into bars again along same lines, then set aside.
  6. Place milk and dark chocolate in mixing bowl then grate 10 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl.
  7. Place 100 g reserved grated chocolate in mixing bowl and melt 3 min/50°C/speed 2.
  8. Add remaining 100 g reserved grated chocolate and mix 2 min/speed 3. Transfer to a shallow dish. Dip underside of bars halfway in chocolate and place on wire rack with a sheet of baking paper underneath to allow chocolate to set and any excess to drip off.
  9. Drizzle leftover chocolate over the top to decorate and leave to set before serving.

Useful Items

baking paper, shallow bowl or dish, baking tray (20 cm x 30 cm), long sharp knife, wire rack, soft spatula, oven
