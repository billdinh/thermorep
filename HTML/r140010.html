Orange Chicken with Giant Coucous and Pine Nuts

Ingredients

Sauce

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 100 g mayonnaise
  • 1 tsp orange peel, grated, finely, or more to taste
  • ½ tsp smoked paprika
  • 4 - 6 Tbsp orange juice, to taste

Couscous Salad

  • 1000 g water
  • 1 vegetable stock cube (for 0.5 l)
  • 250 g couscous, giant
  • 50 g sultanas (optional)
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 3 - 4 chicken breasts, skinless, cut in 3-4 cm pieces (approx. 450 g)
  • 75 g fresh spinach leaves
  • 1 orange, peeled, cut in segments, white pith removed
  • 30 g pine nuts, toasted

Preparation

  1. Place garlic in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add remaining sauce ingredients and mix 10 sec/speed 4. Stir well with spatula then transfer to a deep bowl and set aside.
  3. Place water and stock cube in mixing bowl then insert simmering basket and weigh in couscous, sultanas (if using), ¼ tsp salt and ¼ tsp black pepper. Rinse 15 sec/speed 5.
  4. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh in chicken pieces, cover then steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 1. Once couscous and chicken are cooked, remove Varoma and set aside. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and transfer cooked couscous to a large serving dish. Loosen grains using spatula then set aside to cool (approx. 45 minutes). Meanwhile, discard cooking liquid and rinse mixing bowl with cold water.
  5. Place reserved cooked chicken in mixing bowl and shred 4 sec//speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and allow to cool.
  6. Add cooled chicken and reserved sauce to large serving dish with couscous and combine using spatula.
  7. Place serving dish on mixing bowl lid and weigh in spinach, orange segments and remaining salt and pepper. Remove then stir well with spatula. Serve immediately with pine nuts scattered on top or store in fridge for up to 2 days (see tip).

Useful Items

deep bowl, serving dish

Hints & Tricks

  • If large pieces of chicken remain after shredding once, simply shred again 4 sec//speed 4.
  • If storing in fridge until needed, omit adding pine nuts until just before serving.
  • The chicken would be great served in a sandwich or with a jacket potato.
