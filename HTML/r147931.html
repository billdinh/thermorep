Quinoa with mixed greens and yoghurt dressing

Ingredients

Yoghurt dressing

  • 200 g Greek-style natural yoghurt
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 avocado, flesh only

Quinoa with mixed greens

  • 200 g white quinoa
  • 600 g water, plus extra for soaking
  • 1 pinch salt, plus extra to taste
  • 300 g broccoli, cut into florets
  • 300 g frozen broad beans
  • cold water, for soaking
  • 60 g rocket
  • 50 g dried cranberries
  • 60 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tbsp slivered almonds, toasted
  • 2 tbsp pepitas, toasted
  • 2 tbsp pine nuts, toasted
  • 2 spring onions/shallots, trimmed and cut into slices
  • 1 tbsp black sesame seeds

Preparation

Yoghurt dressing

  1. Place all dressing ingredients into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 6, until combined. Transfer into a small bowl. Cover with plastic wrap and place into refrigerator until ready to use. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Quinoa with mixed greens

  1. Place simmering basket onto mixing bowl lid and weigh quinoa into it. Rest simmering basket in a large bowl. Cover with water and allow to soak for 5 minutes. Rinse under running water until water runs clear.
  2. Place water and salt into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket with quinoa into mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh broccoli into it. Insert Varoma tray, weigh broad beans into it, secure Varoma lid and steam 15 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove Varoma and transfer broad beans into a bowl of cold water for 2 minutes to cease cooking. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and set quinoa aside.
  3. Place a large bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh rocket, cranberries and oil into it. Peel broad beans, discard pods and add beans to bowl with rocket.
  4. Add all remaining ingredients, including broccoli and quinoa, to bowl with rocket and gently toss to combine. Drizzle with some of the reserved dressing (approx. 40 g per person) to serve.
