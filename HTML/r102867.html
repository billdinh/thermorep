Peking Chicken

Ingredients

  • 40 g spring onions, cut in lengths (3 cm)
  • 20 g fresh root ginger, peeled and cut in round slices (2 mm)
  • 30 g toasted sesame oil
  • 150 g carrots, cut in pieces
  • 130 g water
  • 30 g soy sauce
  • 600 g chicken breasts, cut in pieces (3 cm)
  • 200 g fresh shiitake mushrooms, quartered
  • 100 g fresh beansprouts
  • 1 tsp red curry paste
  • 150 g rice vermicelli noodles, dried

Preparation

  1. Place spring onions, ginger and sesame oil in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then sauté, without measuring cup, 3 min/Varoma/speed .
  2. Add carrots and chop 7 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides and lid of mixing bowl with spatula then chop 3 sec/speed 4.
  3. Add water and soy sauce. Insert simmering basket and weigh in chicken. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh in mushrooms, then cover and steam 15 min/Varoma//speed . Set Varoma aside and remove simmering basket with aid of spatula.
  4. Add cooked chicken, steamed mushrooms, beansprouts and curry paste then cook 6 min/100°C//speed . Meanwhile, place noodles in a large bowl, fill with boiling water and allow to soften as per packet instructions. Drain noodles through a fine sieve then place on a serving dish. Arrange chicken and mushroom mixture over the top and serve immediately.
