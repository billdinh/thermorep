Falafel

Ingredients

  • 270 g dried broad beans, soaked overnight
  • 90 g dried chickpeas, soaked overnight
  • 8 garlic cloves
  • 300 g onions, cut into quarters
  • 5 sprigs fresh coriander, roughly chopped
  • 10 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 2 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tsp flour
  • ½ tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 3 tsp baking powder
  • sunflower oil for frying
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
489 kJ / 117 kcal
Protein
2.8 g
Carbohydrates
6.3 g
Fat
9 g

Preparation

  1. Place soaked broad beans, soaked chickpeas, garlic, onions, fresh coriander, parsley, salt, pepper, flour, cumin, ground coriander, baking soda and baking powder into mixing bowl and chop 30 sec/speed 4-9, increasing speed gradually, then 1 min 30 sec/speed 9 with aid of spatula. Transfer into a bowl and refrigerate for 1 hour.
  2. Pour sunflower oil into a pan to deep fry and heat. Scoop 1 heaped tbsp of mixture and use a second spoon to shape the falafel and push it into the hot oil. Fry falafel until golden brown and crisp. Drain on paper towels. Serve hot.

Useful Items

paper towels, frying pan, stove, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • The amount of oil will depend on the size of the frying pan. The oil should cover the falafel while frying.
  • Serve with tahini dip (tarator).
