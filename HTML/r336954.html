Barley and Vegetables (12-18 months)

Ingredients

  • 2 oz pearl barley
  • 20 oz water
  • 2 oz butternut squash, peeled, cut into pieces (1 in.)
  • 4 oz carrots, peeled, cut into rounds (¼ in.), divided
  • 2 oz broccoli florets
  • 1 oz fresh baby spinach
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
176 kJ / 42 kcal
Protein
1 g
Carbohydrates
9 g
Fat
0 g

Preparation

  1. Place simmering basket onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in barley. Remove simmering basket and rinse barley under cool running water until water runs clear.
  2. Place water and rinsed barley into mixing bowl and cook 20 min/212°F/speed 1.
  3. Add squash and 2 oz carrots. Place Varoma dish into position, weigh in remaining 2 oz carrots and broccoli, secure Varoma lid and steam 10 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove Varoma and set aside.
  4. Add spinach into mixing bowl and cook 2 min/212°F/speed 1. Blend 30 sec/speed 4-7, increasing speed gradually.
  5. Transfer to a bowl and allow to cool to room temperature. Serve with reserved steamed vegetables or freeze in individual portions.
