Quinoa Patties

Ingredients

  • 35 oz water
  • 3 ½ oz quinoa
  • ½ oz parsley, leaves and tender stems only
  • 1 oz olive oil, plus 4 tsp to fry
  • 15 oz canned garbanzo beans rinsed and drained (approx. 1 can)
  • 6 oz fresh portobello mushrooms, sliced (approx. ½ in.)
  • 4 oz yellow onion, quartered
  • 2 oz red bell pepper, cut into pieces
  • 2 oz carrot, cut into pieces
  • 1 oz celery
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 2 oz panko
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 large egg

Preparation

  1. Place water into mixing bowl and insert simmering basket, weigh in quinoa and rinse 10 sec/speed 5. Stir with aid of spatula through hole in mixing bowl lid to ensure a thorough rinsing.
  2. Cook 20 min/Varoma/speed 2. Remove simmering basket with spatula, transfer quinoa to a large bowl and allow to cool. Discard steaming water and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Place parsley into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place olive oil, beans, mushrooms, onion, bell pepper, carrot, celery and garlic into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and chop a further 5 sec/speed 5, then sauté 5 min/250°F/speed 1.
  5. Add reserved quinoa, reserved chopped parsley, panko, salt, pepper and egg and with aid of spatula mix 10 sec//speed 4.
  6. Divide mixture and shape into eight equal-sized patties. Place onto a baking sheet and chill in freezer 10 minutes.
  7. Heat approximately 2 tsp oil in a large frying pan over medium-high heat. Cook four patties at a time, 2-3 minutes each side, or until heated through and browned. Transfer to a plate and keep warm. Cook remaining patties the same way using 2 tsp oil for frying. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

large bowl, plate, baking sheet, freezer, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • For an egg-free version, replace the egg with 1 tbsp flax seed mixed with 3 tbsp warm water. Whisk together and refrigerate 15 minutes before using.
  • To freeze uncooked patties: place patties between sheets of wax paper on a baking sheet and freeze. Wrap individually and store in a freezer bag or plastic container. Fry or grill as needed without thawing first.
  • Patties can be baked for 20 minutes at 400°F until lightly golden.
