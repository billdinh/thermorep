Pecan Pie Tarts

Ingredients

Pastry

  • 70 g butter, unsalted, chilled, cut in pieces, plus extra for greasing
  • 140 g plain flour
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
  • 30 g icing sugar
  • 2 egg yolks, from large eggs
  • 1 Tbsp whole milk

Filling

  • 200 g pecan nuts
  • 60 g soft dark brown sugar
  • 60 g unsalted butter, soft
  • 110 g maple syrup
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 - 3 Tbsp apricot jam

Preparation

Pastry

  1. Grease 20 tartlet tins and set aside.
  2. Place flour, baking powder, salt, butter, sugar, egg yolks and milk in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 6. Wrap dough in cling film and place in fridge for 20 minutes.
  3. Roll dough out between 2 pieces of cling film to 0.5 cm thick. Cut 20 circles using a round biscuit cutter (6 cm). Place dough in tartlet tins and press down. Prick dough with a fork and place in freezer while preparing filling.

Filling

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in pecans. Reserve 20 pecan halves. Place remaining pecans in mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 5.
  2. Add sugar, butter, maple syrup and eggs then mix 15 sec/speed 3. Fill chilled tarts with filling, garnish with reserved pecan halves and bake for 20-25 minutes (180°C). Allow to cool 5 minutes and while still hot brush tops with jam. Cool completely before removing from tartlet tins. Serve immediately or store in an airtight container.

Useful Items

cling film, cookie cutter (Ø 6 cm), rolling pin, tartlet tins, refrigerator, freezer, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Dough should not be overworked. If dough becomes soft, place in fridge (approx. 15 min.) until it can be handled easily again.
  • Replace tartlet tins with a 12-hole muffin tray. Grease generously with butter. Divide dough and roll out 12 circles (8 cm). Place pastry circles in muffin holes and press down. After tarts cool, run a small knife around edges of tarts to loosen for easy removal.
  • Place tarts back in the oven for 5 minutes to caramelise and set the jam.
  • Replace pecan halves with pecan pieces.
  • Orange zest can be added to filling to intensify the flavour.
