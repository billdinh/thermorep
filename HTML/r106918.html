Stuffed Peppers with Herbed Quinoa

Ingredients

  • 1 oz Parmesan cheese, cut into cubes (¾ in.)
  • 8 sprigs fresh mixed herbs (e.g. rosemary, sage, thyme), leaves only, plus extra to garnish
  • 53 oz water
  • 5 ½ oz uncooked quinoa
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 yellow onion (approx. 5.5 oz), cut in half
  • 1 oz sun-dried tomatoes, in oil, lightly drained
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 15 pitted black olives, sliced
  • 1 tbsp capers
  • 2 oz pine nuts, toasted
  • ¼ tsp salt, to taste
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper, to taste
  • 6 mixed bell peppers (small-medium), seeds and tops removed (approx. 6 oz ea)

Preparation

  1. Place Parmesan cheese and herbs into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place 35 oz of water into mixing bowl and insert simmering basket. Weigh quinoa into simmering basket and rinse 10 sec/speed 5.
  3. Cook 12 min/Varoma/speed 2. Remove simmering basket with spatula, transfer quinoa to a large bowl and set aside.
  4. Place garlic, yellow onion, sun-dried tomatoes and oil into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and cook 2 min/212°F/speed 1.
  5. Add reserved Parmesan mixture, reserved quinoa and all remaining ingredients except peppers and combine 3 sec//speed 4. Stuff peppers with quinoa mixture and place upright into Varoma dish.
  6. Place remaining 18 oz water into mixing bowl, place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 15 min/Varoma/speed 1. Garnish with extra chopped herbs to taste and serve immediately.
