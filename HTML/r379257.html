Steamed Carrot Cake

Ingredients

Carrot Cake

  • 7 oz carrots, in pieces (1 in.)
  • 4 ½ oz all-purpose flour
  • 2 ½ oz sugar
  • 2 ½ oz dark brown sugar
  • 4 oz applesauce, unsweetened
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • &frac18; tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 oz chopped pecans, plus extra to garnish
  • 2 oz raisins
  • 45 oz water

Frosting

  • 4 oz cream cheese, room temperature, diced (½ in.)
  • 2 oz butter, room temperature, diced (½ in.)
  • 1 oz confectioners sugar

Preparation

Carrot Cake

  1. Grease and line the bottom of a loaf pan (approx. 9 in. x 5 in.) with parchment and set aside.
  2. Place carrots into mixing bowl and grate 3 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and grate a further 3 sec/speed 8.
  3. Add flour, sugar, brown sugar, applesauce, eggs, cinnamon, baking powder, baking soda, salt, nutmeg and vanilla extract and mix 5 sec/speed 3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix a further 5 sec/speed 3.
  4. Add pecans and raisins and mix 5 sec//speed 3. Transfer batter to prepared pan, cover tightly with foil and place into Varoma dish.
  5. Place water into mixing bowl. Set Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 70 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  6. Remove pan from Varoma and allow cake to cool for 10 minutes before removing from pan. Allow cake to cool completely before frosting. Clean mixing bowl.

Frosting

  1. Insert butterfly whisk. Place cream cheese, butter and sugar into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 4. Remove butterfly whisk. Spread frosting over top of cake and garnish with chopped pecans. Keep cake refrigerated until ready to serve.

Useful Items

parchment paper, foil, loaf pan (approx. 9 in. x 5 in.), refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Ensure the pan you're using fits into Varoma dish and Varoma lid closes properly.
