Steamed Garlic Prawns

Ingredients

  • 100 g Chinese vermicelli
  • 600 g water, plus extra hot water, for soaking
  • 1 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 300 g raw prawns, peeled
  • 6 garlic cloves
  • ½ - 1 fresh red chilli, halved, deseeded, to taste
  • 20 - 30 g fresh ginger, peeled, cut in round slices (2 mm), to taste
  • 20 g rice wine
  • 20 g oyster sauce
  • 2 tsp fish sauce
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 30 g sunflower oil
  • 3 spring onions, finely sliced (approx. 20 g)

Preparation

  1. Wet a large piece of baking paper then wring out and use to line Varoma dish, leaving it slightly wrinkled so that some holes are visible.
  2. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in noodles. Remove from lid and cover with hot water then leave to soak (according to packet instructions). Drain noodles using simmering basket then mix with soy sauce and place on baking paper in Varoma dish.
  3. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh in prawns on top of noodles. Remove Varoma dish and set aside.
  4. Place garlic, chilli, ginger, rice wine, oyster sauce, fish sauce and sugar in mixing bowl then mince 10 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides and lid of mixing bowl with spatula then mince again 10 sec/speed 8. Pour over prawns then cover Varoma dish.
  5. Place water in mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position and steam 12-15 min/Varoma/speed 2 until prawns are cooked. Once prawns are cooked, slide noodles, prawns and sauce into a serving dish. Discard steaming water.
  6. Place oil in mixing bowl and heat 1 min/100°C/speed 2. Pour over prawns, scatter with sliced spring onions and serve.

Useful Items

baking paper, medium mesh sieve, serving dish

Hints & Tricks

  • Use a soy sauce low in salt if preferred.
  • Serves 2 as a main meal, or 4 as a starter.
  • Alternatively, heat oil in a saucepan on the hob while the prawns are still cooking.
  • Use mung bean noodles instead and prepare according to packet instructions.
