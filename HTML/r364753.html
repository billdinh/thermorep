Raw apple pie

Ingredients

Soaking

  • 200 g raw almonds (see Tips)
  • 20 g dried cranberries
  • 280 g pitted dates
  • filtered water, for soaking
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 20 g sultanas (see Tips)

Base

  • extra virgin olive oil, for greasing
  • 100 g pecans

Apple filling

  • 2 green apples (approx. 220 g after coring), peeled, cored and cut into quarters
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • 1 - 2 pinches salt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 10 g psyllium husks
  • 2 red apples (approx. 220 g after coring), cored and cut into pieces (approx. 1 cm), plus an extra apple cut into thin slices, for decorating

Macadamia cream

  • 180 g macadamia nuts
  • 80 g pure maple syrup
  • 100 g filtered water

Preparation

Soaking

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh almonds, cranberries and 80 g of the dates into it. Add salt, cover with water and set aside to soak for 3-6 hours.
  2. Place a separate bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh sultanas and remaining 200 g dates into it. Cover with water and set aside to soak for 3-6 hours.
  3. Using simmering basket, drain and rinse almond mixture and transfer into a bowl.
  4. Using simmering basket, drain and rinse sultana-date mixture and set aside.

Base

  1. Grease a round, fluted loose-based tart tin (23 cm) with oil and set aside.
  2. Place pecans and drained almond mixture into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 8.
  3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and chop for a further 5 sec/speed 8. Transfer mixture into tin and evenly press into base and sides. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Apple filling

  1. Place green apples, cinnamon, nutmeg, salt and drained sultana-date mixture into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add lemon juice and psyllium husks and mix 4 sec/speed 8.
  3. Add chopped red apple and combine 10 sec/î€ƒ/speed 4. Pour mixture over reserved base and smooth surface. Mixture will thicken on standing. Cover with plastic wrap and place into refrigerator until ready to serve (see Tips). Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Macadamia cream

  1. Place macadamias into mixing bowl and chop 7 sec/speed 8.
  2. Add syrup and water and mix 20 sec/speed 8.
  3. Decorate pie with thinly sliced red apple and serve with Macadamia cream.

Useful Items

bowl, plastic wrap, loose-based round fluted tart tin (23 cm), refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • If you don't have time to soak/activate your fruit and nuts, soak them in boiling water for 30 minutes, then drain and use as directed.
  • You can store pie and Macadamia cream in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.
