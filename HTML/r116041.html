Tuna, Courgette and Red Pepper Lasagne

Ingredients

  • 400 g tinned chopped tomatoes
  • ¾ tsp ground cinnamon
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 2 courgettes, peeled in ribbons (approx. 500 g)
  • 1 tsp sunflower oil, for frying
  • 3 red peppers, each pepper cut into 8 pieces
  • 2 tin tinned tuna, in brine, 2 x 160 g (drained weight approx. 220 g)
  • 1 small handful fresh parsley leaves, chopped
  • 80 g grated mozzarella

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Place tomatoes, cinnamon, salt and pepper in mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish into position then weigh in courgette ribbons. Make sure courgettes are loosely placed in Varoma dish to allow steam to circulate.
  2. Cover then steam 7 min/Varoma/speed . Meanwhile, heat oil in a large frying pan over a medium-high heat and fry red peppers until slightly soft and browned in patches. Transfer to a plate and set aside. Remove Varoma and mix courgettes with spatula.
  3. Add tuna and parsley to mixing bowl then place covered Varoma back into position. Steam 3 min/Varoma//speed .
  4. Using tongs, arrange half the cooked courgettes in a layer over base of baking dish (approx. 25 cm x 20 cm). Spread tuna mixture over courgettes then arrange reserved red peppers over tuna mixture. With remaining cooked courgettes, form a layer over red peppers and top with cheese. Bake for 15-20 minutes (180°C) until cheese is golden brown. Serve hot with your choice of salad.

Useful Items

tongs, baking dish (25 cm x 20 cm), frying pan, oven, hob

Hints & Tricks

  • Great with sweet potato wedges.
  • Make individual lasagnes then freeze.
  • The red peppers can be roasted in a hot oven instead of frying on hob. Slice into 8 pieces then roast at 180°C until soft.
