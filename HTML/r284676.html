Beef Short Rib Chili

Ingredients

  • 7 oz white cheddar cheese, cubed
  • 16 oz beef short ribs, boneless, cubed (1 in.)
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 oz jalapeño peppers, deseeded, to taste (approx. 1 pepper)
  • 8 oz yellow onions, quartered
  • 1 oz olive oil
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 ½ tsp salt
  • 5 ½ oz red bell peppers, deseeded, cut into pieces (1 in.)
  • 1 can diced tomatoes (approx. 14.5 oz)
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 tsp beef stock paste
  • 16 oz water
  • 9 oz sweet potatoes, cubed (¾ in.)
  • 1 can cannellini beans (approx. 15 oz), rinsed and drained
  • fresh cilantro leaves, to taste, to garnish

Preparation

  1. Place cheese into mixing bowl and grate 3 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place a large piece of parchment into Varoma dish. Place Varoma dish into position, weigh in short ribs and set aside.
  3. Place garlic, jalapeño, onions, olive oil, chili powder, cumin, coriander, oregano and salt into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7.
  4. Remove half of the onion mixture and toss with reserved meat in Varoma. Fold parchment to close parcel and place seam side up into Varoma dish, tucking the folded ends under the parcel and set aside.
  5. With remaining onion mixture still in mixing bowl, sauté 5 min/250°F/speed 1.
  6. Add red peppers, tomatoes, tomato paste and stock paste and water. Place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 50 min/Varoma//speed 2.
  7. Remove Varoma. Add sweet potatoes into mixing bowl. Place Varoma with beef back into position and insert Varoma tray. Place beans onto Varoma tray, secure Varoma lid and cook a further 10 min/Varoma//speed 2. Remove Varoma and set aside. Transfer chili and beans to covered serving dish.
  8. Drain accumulated liquid from short ribs and discard. Place meat into mixing bowl and shred 8-10 sec//speed 4 until desired consistency is achieved. Transfer to serving dish with chili and stir until combined. Garnish with reserved grated cheese and cilantro leaves. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

parchment paper, covered serving dish

Hints & Tricks

  • Beef stock paste and water can be replaced with 16 oz beef stock.
  • Adjust consistency with additional beef stock or water if a thinner chili is preferred.
