Passionfruit and coconut slice

Ingredients

  • 150 g raw cashews
  • water, for soaking

Passionfruit curd

  • 3 eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 50 g honey
  • 170 g fresh passionfruit pulp (approx. 4 passionfruits), strained if preferred, plus extra for serving
  • 1 lime, zest and juice (no white pith)
  • 50 g coconut oil
  • 1 tsp gelatine powder

Base

  • 40 g coconut oil, plus extra for for greasing
  • 75 g desiccated coconut
  • 75 g macadamia nuts
  • 75 g raw cashews
  • 20 g psyllium husk
  • 40 g rice malt syrup
  • 80 g pitted dates
  • 1 pinch salt

Coconut topping

  • 70 g desiccated coconut
  • 1 tbsp gelatine powder
  • 400 g coconut cream
  • 125 g coconut oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh cashews into it. Cover with water and set aside to soak for 1 hour. Using simmering basket, drain cashews and set aside until required.

Passionfruit curd

  1. Insert butterfly whisk. Place all passionfruit curd ingredients into mixing bowl and cook 15 min/80°C/speed 2. Remove butterfly whisk. Transfer into a sealable container and set aside to cool. Do not clean mixing bowl.

Base

  1. Grease and line a slice tin (16 x 26 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place coconut oil into mixing bowl and melt 1 min/Varoma/speed 1 or until melted.
  3. Add remaining base ingredients and chop 10 sec/speed 8. Transfer into prepared slice tin and press firmly into base of tin. Place into refrigerator for a minimum of 30 minutes or until firm. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Coconut topping

  1. Place desiccated coconut and drained cashews into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 8.
  2. Add gelatine and coconut cream and heat 1 min/100°C/speed 2.
  3. Add coconut oil, vanilla and 60 g of the Passionfruit curd and mix 1 min/speed 10, until smooth. Spread mixture evenly over crust and smooth top with aid of spatula. Place into refrigerator for 2-4 hours, until set.
  4. Spread remaining Passionfruit curd evenly over coconut topping and place back into refrigerator for a minimum of 30 minutes or until set. Cut into squares and drizzle with fresh passionfruit pulp before serving.

Useful Items

bowl, sealable container, slice tin (16 x 26 cm), baking paper, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • You can use soaked, then dehydrated nuts in this recipe.
  • Leftover slice can be stored in a sealable container in the freezer for up to 2 weeks. Allow to thaw slightly before serving.
