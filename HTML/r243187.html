Chocolate Coconut Brownies

Ingredients

  • 4 oz unsalted butter, diced (1 in.) plus extra to grease
  • 3 oz dark chocolate (70% cocoa), broken into pieces
  • 3 large eggs
  • 10 ½ oz sugar
  • 3 oz all-purpose flour
  • 3 oz shredded coconut, unsweetened, divided
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
1268 kJ / 303 kcal
Protein
4 g
Carbohydrates
35 g
Fat
17 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F. Grease a baking pan and line with parchment, leaving a 2 inch overhang on two sides and set aside.
  2. Place chocolate into mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of bowl with spatula.
  3. Add butter and melt 5 min/160°F/speed .
  4. Add eggs, sugar, flour, 1.5 oz coconut and vanilla and mix 30 sec/speed 4. Transfer to prepared pan and sprinkle with remaining 1.5 oz coconut. Bake for 30-35 minutes (325°F). Let cool for 1 hour before using excess parchment to remove brownies from pan. Cut brownies into squares and serve.

Useful Items

square baking pan (8 in. x 8 in.), parchment paper, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Once cut, store brownies in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 2 days. Or freeze uncut brownies tightly wrapped in cling wrap and placed inside a freezer-safe bag for up to 3 months. Thaw at room temperature before serving.
