Vanilla sugar

Ingredients

  • 1 vanilla bean, cut into pieces
  • 200 g sugar

Preparation

  1. Place vanilla bean pieces and sugar into an airtight jar or container. Close jar or container and shake to mix vanilla bean pieces with the sugar. Allow vanilla sugar to dry in the jar or container for 2 - 3 days.
  2. Place jar contents into mixing bowl and mill 20 sec/speed 10. Transfer back into jar or container and store in a dry place or use as needed.

Useful Items

airtight jars or container

Hints & Tricks

  • 1 tsp homemade vanilla sugar is roughly equivalent to 3 tsp (15 g) shop-bought vanilla sugar.
  • Use vanilla beans left over from other recipes (rinse and dry beans after first use). This vanilla sugar will not be as strong and aromatic.
  • Quick version: Without drying them first, place vanilla bean pieces with the sugar into mixing bowl and mill 1 min/speed 10. This vanilla sugar will be flavourful, but may have a tendency to clump together.
