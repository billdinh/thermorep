Colorado Cowboy Beans

Ingredients

  • 8 oz bacon, in pieces (2 in.)
  • 8 oz yellow onions, quartered
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1 heaping tsp ground smoked paprika, hot
  • 1 heaping tsp ground sweet paprika
  • 1 heaping tsp ground cumin
  • 1 jalapeño pepper, de-stemmed, in pieces
  • 2 heaping tsp onion powder
  • 2 oz ketchup
  • 1 oz yellow mustard
  • 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 oz maple syrup
  • 2 oz molasses
  • 1 tsp Tabasco sauce
  • 1 can whole tomatoes (28 oz), drained
  • 14 oz andouille sausage, pre-cooked, cut in half moons
  • 3 cans cannellini beans (15 oz ea.), drained
  • cornbread, to serve
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1933 kJ / 462 kcal
Protein
26 g
Carbohydrates
54 g

17 g

Preparation

  1. Place bacon into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 8.
  2. Add onion, garlic, smoked and sweet paprika, cumin, jalapeño, onion powder and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and cook 20 min/212°F/speed 1.
  3. Add ketchup, mustard, Worcestershire, maple syrup, molasses, Tabasco and tomatoes and chop 10 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add sausage and cook 20 min/212°F//speed 1.
  5. Add beans, stir into sauce with spatula to combine and cook 5 min/212°F//speed 1.
  6. Serve as a side dish with cornbread.
