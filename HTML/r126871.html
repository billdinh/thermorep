Gazelle horns

Ingredients

  • 170 g sugar
  • 80 g unsalted butter, cut into pieces
  • 200 g plain flour
  • 1 lemon, grated zest only
  • 110 g orange blossom water
  • 250 g blanched almonds
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
598 kJ / 143 kcal
Protein
3 g
Carbohydrates
14 g

8 g

Preparation

  1. Place sugar into mixing bowl and grind 20 sec/speed 10. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place butter into mixing bowl and melt 4 min/90°C/speed 2.
  3. Add flour, lemon zest and 60 g orange blossom water and knead 30 sec/. Transfer pastry to a bowl and cover with cling film. Allow to rest for 15 minutes in the refrigerator. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Place almonds in mixing bowl and grind 30 sec/speed 10.
  5. Add 70 g of reserved icing sugar, cinnamon and 50 g orange blossom water and mix 30 sec/. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  6. Preheat the oven to 180°C. Line a baking tray with baking paper and set aside. Divide the pastry in half using the spatula, and keep one of the pieces in refrigerator. Roll the other piece out thinly (2 mm) on a work surface using a rolling pin. Cut out circles using a circular cookie cutter (Ø 8 cm). Make small crescent shapes from the almond paste and lay one on each of the pastry discs. Close each circle like a turnover and shape into a small crescent. Cut off the excess pastry using a pastry wheel. Repeat the operation until there is no more pastry, also using the reserved pastry in the refrigerator.
  7. Lay the crescents out on the prepared baking tray and cook in preheated oven for 10 to 15 minutes (180°C). They should not turn golden. Remove the gazelle horns from the oven, roll them in the remaining icing sugar and serve at room temperature.

Useful Items

baking tray and paper, cling film, baking paper, baking tray, pastry wheel, rolling pin, bowl, cookie cutter (Ø 8 cm), refrigerator, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Oriental cuisine is very rich in almond- and honey-based pastries to accompany the traditional mint tea. Oriental cakes come in shapes inspired by nature or fauna, such as these gazelle horns.
