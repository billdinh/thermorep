Sparkling white sangria punch

Ingredients

  • 150 g caster sugar
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • 40 g brandy
  • 50 g water
  • 120 g orange juice (approx. 2 oranges)
  • 80 g lemon juice (approx. 2 lemons)
  • 1 yellow peach, stone removed and cut into eighths
  • 1 white peach, stone removed and cut into eighths
  • 1 nectarine, stone removed and cut into eighths
  • 1 cinnamon quill
  • 1 Pink Lady apple, core removed and cut into thin slices
  • 500 g lemonade, chilled
  • 750 g sparkling wine, chilled
  • 300 g ice cubes
  • 4 sprigs fresh mint, leaves only, for garnishing

Preparation

  1. Place sugar, ground cinnamon, brandy, water, orange juice and lemon juice into mixing bowl and cook 5 min/90°C/speed 1.
  2. Place stone fruit, cinnamon quill and apple into a sealable container, then transfer brandy mixture in mixing bowl over the top. Allow to cool for approx. 20 minutes to room temperature before placing into refrigerator to chill overnight.
  3. Place a large jug onto mixing bowl lid, then weigh lemonade or soda water and sparkling wine into it. Transfer into a punch bowl with the reserved chilled fruit-brandy mixture.
  4. Add ice cubes to punch bowl, then garnish with mint leaves and serve immediately.

Useful Items

sealable container, punch bowl, large jug, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • If you don't have a punch bowl, you can serve your sangria in the large jug instead.
