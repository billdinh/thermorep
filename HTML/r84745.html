Breast of Chicken in Pizzaiola Sauce

Ingredients

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 tinned anchovy fillets
  • 1 sprig fresh parsley
  • 60 g olive oil
  • 1 Tbsp capers
  • 500 g fresh tomatoes, cut in pieces
  • 1 Tbsp fresh oregano, leaves only
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt, or to taste
  • 2 - 3 pinches ground black pepper, or to taste
  • ¼ tsp sugar
  • 300 g fine green beans
  • 4 chicken breast fillets, skinless (approx. 500 g)

Preparation

  1. Place garlic, anchovy fillets and parsley in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add 30 g oil, capers, tomatoes, oregano, salt, pepper and sugar.
  3. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh in green beans. Cover Varoma and cook beans above sauce 16 min/Varoma//speed 1.5. Meanwhile heat remaining 30 g oil in a large frying pan on hob. Fry the chicken breasts over a medium heat for 8 - 10 minutes on each side until brown and cooked through.
  4. Taste sauce and adjust seasoning if needed then serve chicken with sauce and steamed green beans.
