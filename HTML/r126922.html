Energy bars with cereals and dried fruit

Ingredients

  • 60 g walnuts, shelled
  • 40 g dried fruit (e. g. raisins, blueberries etc.)
  • 250 g muesli
  • 70 g dark chocolate, in pieces (2-3 cm)
  • 125 g blanched almonds
  • 10 g water
  • 100 g honey
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
787 kJ / 188 kcal
Protein
4 g
Carbohydrates
22 g
Fat
10 g

Preparation

  1. Place walnuts and dried fruit into mixing bowl, chop 5 sec/speed 5. Place into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place muesli into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 3. Transfer to bowl with chopped nuts and dried fruit.
  3. Place dark chocolate into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 5. Transfer to bowl with muesli and dried fruit.
  4. Place almonds and 10 g water into mixing bowl and chop 2 min/speed 6 until it achieves a consistency similar to almond paste.
  5. Add honey, and melt 3 min/50Â°C/speed 3. Combine in the bowl with the other ingredients and mix well (see Tip).
  6. Lay out a sheet of baking paper on the work surface, pour on the mixture and smooth out with aid of spatula to a height of approx. 1 cm. Place in refrigerator for 2 hours.
  7. Cut into bars of the required size and serve or store in an air-tight container in the refrigerator.

Useful Items

long sharp knife, baking paper, bowl, air-tight container, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • To best mix the mixture, it is advisable to wear disposable latex gloves and knead the mixture with the hands to achieve an even consistency.
