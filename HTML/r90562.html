Panforte ice cream bombe

Ingredients

  • 500 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 120 g caster sugar
  • 60 g honey
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 1 pinch ground cloves
  • 1 pinch ground ginger
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • 500 g mascarpone cheese
  • 100 g roasted almonds
  • 30 g glacé cherries
  • 200 g mixed dried fruit (e.g. dried cranberries, raisins, apricots, figs)
  • 200 g dark chocolate, broken into pieces

Preparation

  1. Place 250 g of the cream, sugar, honey, egg yolks and spices into mixing bowl and cook 8 min/90°C/speed 3.
  2. Add 250 g of the mascarpone and beat 10 sec/speed 5. Transfer into a shallow freezable container, then place into the freezer for a minimum 8 hours or overnight.
  3. Place almonds into mixing bowl and chop 6 sec/speed 5. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place cherries and mixed dried fruit into mixing bowl and chop 8 sec/speed 5.
  5. Add reserved almonds and combine10 sec//speed 1, or until fruit is completely coated with chopped almonds. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  6. Insert butterfly whisk. Place remaining 250 g of the cream and remaining 250 g of the mascarpone into mixing bowl and whip until soft peaks form/speed 4, watching carefully to avoid over-whipping. Remove butterfly whisk.
  7. Remove ice cream mixture from freezer and cut into cubes (4 cm). Add ice cream cubes and combine 30 sec/speed 8, or until well combined. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  8. Add reserved fruit-almond mixture and combine 5 sec//speed 5.
  9. Transfer into a freezable pudding basin (18-20 cm, 1500 ml capacity) and return to the freezer for 1 hour. Stir with a spoon and ensure that the fruit and nuts are evenly distributed throughout the pudding. Place pudding basin into the freezer to set overnight.
  10. Place chocolate into mixing bowl and melt 2 min/50°C/speed 1. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then melt a further 1-2 min/50°C/speed 1, or until melted.
  11. Remove ice cream from the freezer, then run the outside of the pudding basin under hot water for approx. 30 seconds. Place pudding basin upside down on a serving plate and carefully turn ice cream bombe out.
  12. Drizzle melted chocolate over the top of the pudding, allowing it to drizzle down the sides of the ice cream bombe. Place back into the freezer until chocolate has set (approx. 10 min). Slice ice cream bombe into wedges and serve immediately.

Useful Items

shallow freezeable container, freezable pudding basin (18-20 cm, 1500 ml), freezer

Hints & Tricks

  • If you don't want to make an ice cream bombe you can cut it into wedges or scoop into ice cream cones and drizzle with chocolate instead.
