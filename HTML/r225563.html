Egg Free Pistachio Ice Cream

Ingredients

  • 3 ½ oz unsalted pistachios
  • 9 oz whole milk
  • 9 oz heavy whipping cream
  • 3 ½ oz sugar
  • 1 tbsp corn starch
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
2086 kJ / 499 kcal
Protein
12 g
Carbohydrates
38 g
Fat
34 g

Preparation

  1. Place pistachios into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place milk, whipping cream, sugar and corn starch into mixing bowl and cook 7 min/160°F/speed 4.
  3. Add reserved chopped pistachios and mix 15 sec/speed 4.
  4. Pour mixture up to 1½ inches high into a shallow freezable container and cover with cling wrap. Allow mixture to cool completely before freezing for at least 8 hours or overnight.
  5. Place frozen mixture in the refrigerator for 15 minutes, then cut into pieces (1½ in. x 1½ in.) with a sharp knife. Place ice cream pieces into mixing bowl and crush 30 sec/speed 6, then mix 20 sec/speed 4. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

cling wrap, shallow freezable container (e.g. aluminum, silicone), freezer, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Garnish with chopped pistachios or chopped chocolate.
  • For a firmer consistency return crushed ice cream to the freezer for 15 minutes before serving.
