Pizza Dough with a Trio of Toppings

Ingredients

Pizza Dough

  • 30 g olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 20 g fresh yeast, crumbled
  • 220 g water
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 400 g bread flour
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt

Pizza Bianca with Courgette and Mozzarella

  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary (approx. 10 cm long), leaves only
  • 200 g mozzarella cheese, cut into cubes (3 cm)
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 400 g courgettes, cut into pieces
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper

Caramelised Onion, Ham, Tomato and Mozzarella

  • 200 g mozzarella cheese, cut into cubes (3 cm)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 400 g tinned chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp tomato purée
  • 1 tbsp fresh oregano leaves
  • ¾ tsp fine sea salt
  • 250 g red onions (approx. 1½ onions), cut into halves
  • 20 g balsamic vinegar
  • 50 g raw sugar
  • 100 g cooked ham, cut into thin slices
  • 1 fresh tomato, finely diced

Smoked Salmon, Capers, Rocket and Lemon with Yoghurt Dressing

  • 150 g natural yoghurt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 - 2 tsp whole milk
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt, to taste
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper, to taste
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 200 g smoked salmon, cut into thin slices
  • 2 tbsp pickled capers
  • 40 g rocket leaves, for garnishing

Preparation

Pizza Dough

  1. Lightly grease a ceramic or glass bowl and set aside. Place water, yeast and sugar in mixing bowl then warm 2 min/37°C/speed 1.
  2. Add flour, 30 g olive oil and salt then knead 2 min/. Transfer dough into prepared bowl and work into a ball. Cover bowl with cling film and leave to prove in a warm place until doubled in size (approx. 1 hour).

Pizza Bianca with Courgette and Mozzarella

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C. Line 1 large or 2 smaller baking trays with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place rosemary leaves in mixing bowl and chop 20 sec/speed 8, until a fine consistency is achieved. Repeat chopping if necessary. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place mozzarella in mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 8. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Place garlic in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add courgettes and chop 2 sec/speed 6. Drain off any excess liquid using simmering basket.
  6. Roll out pizza dough into 1 large or 2 smaller pizza bases. Drizzle oil onto pizza base/s and rub it all over the surface. Top pizza/s with reserved mozzarella, reserved rosemary leaves and garlic-courgette mixture in simmering basket. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
  7. Bake for 25 minutes (220°C), or until golden brown and cooked to your liking. Allow to cool slightly before slicing and serving hot.

Caramelised Onion, Ham, Tomato and Mozzarella

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C. Line 1 large or 2 small baking trays with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place mozzarella in mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 8. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Place garlic in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add 20 g  olive oil and sauté 1 min 30 sec/Varoma/speed 1.
  5. Add tinned tomatoes, tomato purée, oregano leaves and ¼ tsp salt then cook 15 min/Varoma/speed 1, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid. Transfer tomato sauce into a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  6. Place red onion in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  7. Add remaining olive oil, balsamic vinegar, raw sugar and remaining ½ tsp salt then cook 15 min/Varoma/speed 1, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  8. Roll out pizza dough into 1 large or 2 smaller pizza bases. Top pizza base/s with reserved tomato sauce, then sprinkle with reserved mozzarella.
  9. Add reserved caramelised onion, ham and fresh tomato, then bake for 25 minutes (220°C), or until golden brown and cooked to your liking. Allow to cool slightly before slicing and serving hot.

Smoked Salmon, Capers, Rocket and Lemon with Yoghurt Dressing

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C. Line 1 large or 2 small baking trays with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place yoghurt, lemon juice, milk, salt and pepper in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 3. Transfer lemon-yoghurt dressing into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Roll out pizza dough into 1 large or 2 smaller pizza bases. Drizzle oil onto pizza base/s and rub it all over the surface.
  4. Bake for 20 minutes (220°C), or until lightly golden and cooked through.
  5. Top pizza/s with smoked salmon, capers and rocket then drizzle with reserved lemon-yoghurt dressing. Slice and serve immediately.

Useful Items

cling film, large bowl, cling wrap, baking paper, 1 large or 2 smaller baking trays, rolling pin, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • This recipe makes one quantity of pizza dough and a choice of three toppings. You can choose which of the toppings you would like to use.
  • To shape pizza, place dough on a baking tray lined with baking paper or greased with extra virgin olive oil. Press it out with your fingertips from the centre to the edges, and gently stretch it into shape. Alternatively you can roll it out with a rolling pin on a floured surface and transfer it to a baking tray lined with baking paper.
