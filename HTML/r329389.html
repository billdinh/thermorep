Vegan gravy

Ingredients

  • 20 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 80 g brown onion, cut into quarters
  • 130 g carrot, cut into pieces (2-3 cm)
  • 1 celery stalk, cut into pieces (2-3 cm)
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 25 g dried porcini mushrooms
  • 1 sprig fresh thyme, leaves only
  • 1 dried bay leaf
  • 1 tbsp arrowroot flour
  • 1 tsp shiro (white) miso paste
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp stock paste (see Tips)
  • 750 g water
  • ½ tsp salt

Preparation

  1. Place oil, onion, carrot, celery, garlic, mushrooms, thyme and bay leaf into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7.
  2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and cook 10 min/100°C/speed 2.
  3. Add arrowroot flour, miso paste, tomato paste, stock paste, water and salt, then cook 10 min/100°C/speed 1.
  4. Scrape down sides and bottom of mixing bowl with spatula and cook 10 min/95°C/speed 1 or until thickened and reduced.
  5. Blend 1 min/speed 4-9, increasing speed gradually from speed 4 to speed 9. Transfer into a large jug and serve warm.
