Chicken and cashews

Ingredients

Chicken and cashews

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 2 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 400 g skinless chicken breast fillet or chicken thigh fillet, cut into strips (approx. 5 x 2 cm)
  • 400 g basmati rice
  • 900 g water
  • 100 - 200 g broccoli, broken into florets
  • 225 g canned bamboo shoots, drained
  • 1 carrot, cut into thin slices diagonally
  • 3 celery stalks, cut into pieces (4 cm)
  • 6 spring onions/shallots, trimmed and cut into pieces (4 cm)
  • ½ capsicum, deseeded and cut into pieces (1 cm)
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds, plus extra for garnishing
  • 50 g unsalted cashew nuts, roasted

Sauce

  • 1 ½ tsp cornflour
  • 2 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 long red chilli, deseeded if preferred (optional)
  • 1 tbsp peanut oil
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 40 g soy sauce or tamari
  • 2 - 3 tsp honey, to taste
  • 1 pinch sea salt, to taste
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper, to taste
  • 20 g lime juice (approx. 1), to serve (optional)

Preparation

Chicken and cashews

  1. Place garlic and ginger into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add chicken and combine 5 sec//speed 1. Once chicken is coated in garlic and ginger mixture transfer onto Varoma tray and set aside.
  3. Insert simmering basket, weigh rice into it, then remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and rinse rice until water runs clear. Place water into mixing bowl and re-insert simmering basket with rice.
  4. Place Varoma with chicken into position, secure Varoma lid and cook 13-15 min/Varoma/speed 1, or until rice is cooked.
  5. Remove Varoma dish, then remove simmering basket with aid of spatula. Transfer cooked rice into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and cover to keep warm. Retain steaming liquid in mixing bowl and place Varoma with chicken back into position.
  6. Weigh broccoli and bamboo shoots into Varoma dish, then add remaining vegetables, if more space is required, on top of chicken on Varoma tray. Sprinkle sesame seeds and cashews over chicken on Varoma tray. Secure Varoma lid and cook 10-15 min/Varoma/speed 1, or until chicken and vegetables are cooked.
  7. Once cooked, set chicken and vegetables aside in a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl to keep warm until ready to serve. Transfer steaming liquid into a jug. Place another jug onto mixing bowl lid and weigh 250 g of the steaming liquid into it. Discard remaining steaming liquid. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Sauce

  1. Place cornflour into the jug with reserved steaming liquid and stir until cornflour has dissolved. Set jug aside.
  2. Place ginger, garlic and chilli (optional) into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and repeat chopping if necessary until a fine consistency is achieved.
  3. Add peanut oil and sauté 2 min/120°C/speed 1.
  4. Add sesame oil, soy sauce or tamari, honey and reserved steaming liquid and cornflour mixture, and cook 4-5 min/100°C/speed 3, or until desired consistency is achieved. Season sauce to taste. Let sauce sit to thicken (approx. 5 minutes).
  5. Add lime juice (optional), then pour sauce over reserved chicken and vegetables and stir through.
  6. Serve with cooked rice and garnish with extra sesame seeds.
