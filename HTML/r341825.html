Peanut butter and cookie ice cream

Ingredients

Soaking

  • 200 g raw cashews (see Tips)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • filtered water, for soaking

Ice cream

  • 50 g coconut sugar
  • 150 g rice malt syrup
  • 100 g peanut butter of choice (see Tips)
  • 380 g coconut cream
  • 1 - 2 pinches salt
  • 2 tsp vanilla bean paste (see Tips)
  • 12 biscuits of choice, broken into pieces (see Tips)

Preparation

Soaking

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh cashews into it. Add salt, cover with water and set aside to soak for 3-6 hours.
  2. Using simmering basket, drain and rinse cashews.

Ice cream

  1. Place coconut sugar into mixing bowl and mill 20 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add syrup, peanut butter, coconut cream, salt, vanilla and drained cashews, then blend 1 min/speed 10. Transfer into freezable container and place into freezer until firm (approx. 6 hours).
  3. Remove ice cream from freezer and set aside for approx. 10 minutes to soften. Cut into pieces (4 x 4 cm), then place half of the ice cream into mixing bowl and crush 1 min/speed 7, mixing with aid of spatula, until smooth. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add remaining ice cream and blend 40 sec/speed 4, mixing with aid of spatula, until smooth and creamy.
  5. Add biscuits and combine 5 sec//speed 4, mixing with aid of spatula, until just combined. Transfer into a freezable container and place into freezer until firm enough to serve (approx. 4 hours).

Useful Items

freezable containers (e.g. plastic, silicone), bowl, freezer

Hints & Tricks

  • If you don't have time to soak/activate your cashews, soak nuts in boiling water for 30 minutes, then drain and use as directed.
  • You can use chunky or smooth peanut butter (or any kind of nut butter) in this recipe.
  • To make your own vanilla paste, refer to the recipe on Cookidoo® (www.cookidoo.com.au).
  • We used the chocolate biscuits from our Chocolate cream cookies (refer to the recipe on this recipe chip), but you can use any type of biscuit in this recipe.
