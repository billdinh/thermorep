Baked Vanilla Cheesecake

Ingredients

Base

  • 350 g strong white bread flour
  • 120 g caster sugar
  • 150 g unsalted butter, diced
  • 1 large egg

Filling

  • 110 g caster sugar
  • 60 g vanilla sugar
  • 5 large eggs, separated
  • 400 g mascarpone
  • 400 g cream cheese
  • 1 Tbsp cornflour
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt

Preparation

Base

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C and line base of a springform tin (Ø 23 cm) with baking paper.
  2. Place flour, sugar, butter and egg in mixing bowl then mix 15 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then mix again 5 sec/speed 6. Transfer to prepared tin, forming an even layer over base, then push mixture evenly up sides of tin to the top. Set aside in fridge. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl thoroughly and make filling.

Filling

  1. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid then weigh in caster sugar and vanilla sugar. Set aside.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg whites in mixing bowl and, without measuring cup, whisk without setting a time/speed 3.5 until soft peaks form, watching carefully to avoid over-whisking.
  3. Without measuring cup, mix 2 min/speed 3 while slowly add reserved sugar, one spoon at a time, through hole in mixing bowl lid. Once all sugar has been incorporated, transfer to a bowl and set aside. Remove butterfly whisk.
  4. Place mascarpone, cream cheese, cornflour, salt and egg yolks in mixing bowl then mix 45 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then mix again 15 sec/speed 5. Fold reserved whisked egg whites into mixture with spatula.
  5. Pour into prepared tin and bake for 60-70 minutes (160°C) until browned with a slight wobble in centre. Leave to cool then place in fridge overnight.
  6. Once set, remove from tin and serve chilled, cut in slices.

Useful Items

baking paper, springform tin (Ø 23 cm), oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • This cheesecake has a pillowy soft, cloud-like filling.
