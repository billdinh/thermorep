Apple Crumble and Custard

Ingredients

Custard

  • 1 gelatine leaf
  • 300 g double cream
  • 100 g whole milk
  • 60 g caster sugar
  • 6 egg yolks, from medium eggs, cold
  • 1 medium egg, cold
  • 2 vanilla pods, seeds only

Apple Purée

  • 200 g cooking apples, peeled, cored, quartered
  • 200 g Golden Delicious apples, peeled, cored, quartered
  • 10 g unsalted butter
  • 35 g caster sugar
  • ½ lemon, juice only

Crumble

  • 125 g plain flour
  • 50 g ground almonds
  • 125 g unsalted butter, soft
  • 75 g Demerara sugar
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • icing sugar, for dusting

Preparation

Custard

  1. Soak gelatine leaf in a bowl of cold water for 5 minutes. Meanwhile, continue with recipe.
  2. Place remaining ingredients in mixing bowl then cook 8 min/85°C/speed 5.
  3. Squeeze out gelatine and add to mixing bowl then stir 20 sec/speed 2. Strain through a fine sieve into a jug, then pour into serving glasses. Leave to cool completely then cover and transfer to fridge.

Apple Purée

  1. Place apples in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add butter and sugar then cook, without measuring cup, 10 min/100°C/speed . Remove bowl and leave to cool. Add lemon juice to taste. When completely cool, spoon cooked apples over custard in glasses. Re-cover and return to fridge. Clean mixing bowl.

Crumble

  1. Line a small tray with greaseproof paper.
  2. Place all crumble ingredients in mixing bowl then pulse Turbo/1 sec/5-6 times until combined. Sprinkle on prepared tray then place in freezer for 2-4 hours until set. Towards the end of this time, preheat oven to 165°C and line a baking tray with baking paper.
  3. Crumble frozen mixture onto prepared baking tray then bake for 15-20 minutes (165°C) until golden. Leave to cool completely.
  4. When cool, break up mixture, keeping lumps as large as possible. Store in an airtight container until ready to use.
  5. To serve, remove custards from fridge 30 minutes before serving to get to room temperature. Sprinkle over some crumble then dust with icing sugar and serve.

Useful Items
