Spooky Spaghetti and Meatballs

Ingredients

Meatballs

  • olive oil, to grease
  • 1000 g water
  • 80 g red quinoa
  • 60 g onions, quartered
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 30 g fresh herbs (mixed, e.g. parsley, thyme, oregano)
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt, to taste
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • ½ tsp dried chilli flakes, optional
  • ¼ tsp dried fennel seeds
  • 550 g turkey meat, boneless and skinless, cut in pieces (3 cm)
  • 1 large egg
  • 25 - 35 pitted black olives, halved

Tomato Sauce

  • 110 g onions, quartered
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 800 g tinned chopped tomatoes (2 x 400 g tins)
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt, to taste
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper

Spooky Spaghetti

  • 1150 g water
  • 1 ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 340 g dried spaghetti

Preparation

Meatballs

  1. Lightly grease Varoma dish and Varoma tray then set aside.
  2. Place water in mixing bowl, insert simmering basket, weigh in quinoa and rinse 10 sec/speed 5. Mix well with spatula and cook 20 min/Varoma/speed 2. Transfer to a bowl and allow to cool slightly. Clean mixing bowl.
  3. Place onions, garlic, herbs, salt, pepper, chili flakes and fennel seeds in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  4. Add turkey and chop Turbo/2 sec/4-5 times until turkey is ground up.
  5. Add egg and reserved cooked quinoa then mix 10 sec/speed 5.
  6. Shape mixture into balls (approx. 30 g each) and place in prepared Varoma dish and tray. Gently press 1 black olive half on top of each ball to form the eye, then set Varoma aside.

Tomato Sauce

  1. Place onions, garlic and olive oil in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  2. Add tomatoes, salt and pepper, place Varoma into position, cover with Varoma lid and cook 15-18 min/Varoma/speed 1. Transfer sauce and eyeballs to a serving plate and keep warm.

Spooky Spaghetti

  1. Place water and salt in mixing bowl then bring to a boil 10 min/100°C/speed 1.
  2. Add pasta through hole in mixing bowl lid and cook without measuring cup for time indicated on packet/100°C//speed 1 or until al dente. Drain pasta through Varoma dish.
  3. Divide pasta, tomato sauce and eyeballs between 6-8 bowls and serve immediately.

Useful Items

serving plate, serving bowls

Hints & Tricks

  • Replace regular spaghetti with squid ink spaghetti for an extra spooky finish.
  • Serve with grated Parmesan cheese or additional black olives.
  • To save time, cook pasta on the hob while the sauce and meatballs are cooking.
  • Using dark turkey meat will ensure that the meatballs remain juicy.
  • Keep a bowl of water nearby to moisten hands and prevent sticking while making meatballs.
