Chilli crab pasta

Ingredients

  • 20 g olive oil, plus extra for drizzling
  • 3 eschalots
  • 2 - 3 garlic cloves, to taste
  • 4 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 2 - 3 fresh long red chillies, trimmed and cut into halves, to taste
  • 50 g white wine
  • 280 g cooked crab meat (see Tips)
  • 200 g cherry tomatoes, cut into halves
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1000 g boiling water
  • 200 g angel hair pasta

Preparation

  1. Place oil, eschalots, garlic, parsley and chillies into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add wine and cook 2 min/120°C/speed 2, without measuring cup.
  3. Place crab meat into centre of a large sheet of baking paper (approx. 60 x 30 cm). Place chilli mixture and cherry tomatoes on top of crab meat. Season with ½ teaspoon of the salt and enclose top and sides to form a sealed parcel. Place seam side up into Varoma dish and set aside.
  4. Carefully place boiling water into mixing bowl and place Varoma dish into position. Secure Varoma lid and steam 8 min/Varoma/speed 4. Remove Varoma.
  5. Add pasta and remaining ½ teaspoon salt into mixing bowl. Place Varoma back into position, secure Varoma lid and cook 3 min/Varoma//speed 1. Remove Varoma and set aside. Drain pasta using simmering basket and transfer into a serving bowl. Add chilli crab mixture and toss gently to combine. Drizzle generously with oil (see Tips) just before serving.

Useful Items

serving bowl, baking paper

Hints & Tricks

  • Please note that the total time for this recipe is based on using cooked crab meat that has already been removed from the shell.
  • For extra flavour, drizzle pasta with a lemon or chilli infused oil.
