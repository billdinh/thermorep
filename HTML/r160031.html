Cheesy Crust Pizza

Ingredients

  • 16 oz low-moisture mozzarella, cubed (approx. 1 in.), divided
  • 1 oz olive oil, plus 1 tsp and extra to grease
  • 8 oz water
  • 2 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 14 oz bread flour
  • 2 tsp fine sea salt, to taste, divided
  • 5 oz yellow onions, quartered (approx. 1 medium)
  • 20 oz canned crushed tomatoes

Preparation

  1. Place 8 oz mozzarella into mixing bowl and grate 15 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Place remaining 8 oz mozzarella into mixing bowl and grate 15 sec/speed 6, set aside with previously grated mozzarella.
  2. Lightly grease a large bowl and set aside. Place 1 oz olive oil, water, yeast and sugar into mixing bowl and mix 20 sec/speed 2.
  3. Add flour and 1 tsp salt and knead 2 min/. Transfer into greased bowl and cover with cling wrap. Let rise until doubled (approx. 1 hour).
  4. Place onions and 1 tsp olive oil into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Add crushed tomatoes and 1 tsp salt. Place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 10 min/250°F/speed 1. Reserve sauce until needed.
  5. Preheat oven to 450°F. Place dough onto center of a pizza pan (14 in.). Flatten into a disk shape and begin stretching dough evenly to edge of pizza pan. Cover and let rest 10 minutes. Stretch dough to hang over pan (approx. 1 in.).
  6. Place half of the reserved mozzarella in a ring around inside edge of the pizza dough. Fold extended edge of dough over cheese and gently pinch all along the edge to firmly seal. Prick the pizza base every inch with a fork while leaving the cheesy crust edges intact. Parbake 10 minutes (450°F).
  7. Evenly spread sauce over bottom of crust and top with remaining grated mozzarella. Bake for 25 minutes (450°F) or until crust has set. Slice and serve immediately.

Useful Items

bowl, large bowl, pizza pan (14 in.), cling wrap, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Place pepperoni or cooked, crumbed sausage on top of the sauce and mozzarella.
  • Add minced garlic or fresh/dried herbs like basil and oregano to the sauce and/or use in the cheesy crust.
  • Replace 8 oz mozzarella with 7-8 string cheese sticks. Cut into pieces (approx. 1 in.) and line the edge of the stretched pizza dough with the string cheese pieces. Fold and seal dough over string cheese. Continue as directed with recipe.
