Asian chicken noodle salad

Ingredients

Ginger-sesame dressing

  • 1 spring onion/shallot, trimmed and cut into halves
  • 4 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 - 2 fresh red chillies, trimmed and deseeded, if preferred
  • 4 strips lime zest, no white pith, and juice
  • 50 g soy sauce
  • 1 tsp palm sugar (approx. 1 cm cube) (optional)
  • 20 g rice wine vinegar
  • 40 g hoi sin sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 20 g peanut oil
  • ½ - 1 tsp salt, to taste

Chicken

  • 500 g water
  • 500 g chicken tenderloins (see Tips)
  • salt, to season
  • ground black pepper, to season

Salad

  • 100 g rice vermicelli noodles
  • boiling water, for soaking
  • 170 g carrot, peeled and cut into pieces (approx. 5 cm)
  • ½ Chinese cabbage, cut into halves lengthways, then cut into thin slices crossways
  • 2 spring onions/shallots, trimmed and cut into thin slices on a diagonal
  • 130 g red capsicum, trimmed and cut into thin strips
  • 70 g snow peas, cut into thin strips
  • 2 sprigs fresh mint, leaves only
  • 4 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only
  • 2 tbsp roasted salted peanuts, roughly chopped, for garnishing
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds, toasted (optional)
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges, to serve

Preparation

Ginger-sesame dressing

  1. Place spring onion/shallot, ginger, garlic, chilli and lime zest into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7.
  2. Add all remaining dressing ingredients and blend 20 sec/speed 6. Transfer dressing into a bowl. Cover with plastic wrap and place into refrigerator until ready to use. Do not clean mixing bowl.

Chicken

  1. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position, insert Varoma tray and weigh chicken onto it. Season chicken with salt and pepper. Secure Varoma lid and steam 10 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  2. Turn chicken pieces over to ensure even cooking. Secure Varoma lid. Steam for a further 4 min/Varoma/speed 1 or until chicken is cooked through. Remove Varoma and set aside to allow chicken to cool for 10 minutes.
  3. Cut chicken into bite-sized pieces and transfer into a large bowl, then set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Salad

  1. Place noodles into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl. Cover with boiling water and set aside for 2 minutes. Using simmering basket drain noodles and set aside to cool.
  2. Place carrots into mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 5. Transfer into bowl with reserved chicken.
  3. Add cabbage, spring onions/shallots, capsicum, snow peas, mint, coriander and reserved cooled noodles and enough reserved dressing to lightly coat salad, then gently toss to combine.
  4. Divide salad between serving plates. Sprinkle with peanuts and sesame seeds (optional) then serve with lime wedges and remaining dressing.

Useful Items

bowl, plastic wrap, large bowl, thermal serving bowl or other large bowl, serving plates, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Chicken breasts can be used as a substitute for tenderloins in this recipe. Simply cut 500 g chicken breasts into halves lengthways and steam 14-16 min/Varoma/speed 1 or until chicken is cooked through.
  • Salad can be made ahead of time (through step 6), then add dressing just before serving. Store dressing and salad in the refrigerator until ready to serve.
