Coconut Milk

Ingredients

  • 32 oz water
  • 10 oz desiccated coconut, unsweetened
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1957 kJ / 468 kcal
Protein
5 g
Carbohydrates
17 g
Fat
46 g

Preparation

  1. Place water into mixing bowl and heat 7 min/140Â°F/speed 1.
  2. Add coconut and mix 10 sec/speed 3. Allow to steep for 10 minutes in mixing bowl.
  3. Mix 30 sec/speed 8 and transfer to a nut milk bag over a large bowl or a fine sieve lined with 4 layers of cheesecloth. Once cool enough to handle, squeeze out as much liquid as possible. Chill before serving.

Useful Items

nut milk bag or fine sieve and cheesecloth, large bowl, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • The leftover pulp can be added to homemade granola or breakfast bars or use it as a topping for oatmeal or smoothies.
  • Store Coconut Milk for up to three days in the refrigerator.
  • If coconut milk and coconut cream separate, simply shake or mix until blended.
  • Serve as an alternative to dairy milk.
  • Add coconut sugar (to taste) for a sweeter Coconut Milk.
