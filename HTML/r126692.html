Spiced nuts

Ingredients

  • 40 g cornflour (starch)
  • 15 g icing sugar
  • 2 tsp fine salt
  • 3 tsp ground sweet paprika
  • 1 ½ tsp ground hot paprika
  • 1 tsp curry powder, mild
  • 400 g peanuts, dry-roasted, unsalted
  • 1 egg
  • ½ tsp ground cumin
  • 1 ½ tbsp sunflower oil

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 150°C. Line baking tray with baking paper.
  2. Place starch, icing sugar, 1 tsp salt, 2 tsp sweet paprika, 1 tsp hot paprika, 1 tsp curry powder in mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place peanuts and egg in mixing bowl and mix 20 sec//speed .
  4. Add spice mixture and mix 30 sec//speed . Arrange peanuts on baking tray. Separate peanuts with a fork and bake for approx. 12–15 minutes (150°C). Meanwhile, place 1 tsp salt, 1 tsp sweet paprika, ½ tsp hot paprika and ½ tsp cumin in a large bowl and mix.
  5. Mix peanuts into the bowl of spices and allow to cool for 10 minutes. Add oil, mix, allow to cool completely and serve in individual portions or store in an air-tight container.

Useful Items

baking tray and paper, large bowl, bowl, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Vary the nuts to taste and season for example with ground rosemary, smoked salt or cayenne pepper.
  • The spiced nuts keep longer if they are kept in airtight packaging in a cool, dry place.
