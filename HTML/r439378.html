Ginger Cabbage Slaw

Ingredients

Ginger Cabbage Slaw

  • 12 oz cabbage, in chunks (2 in.)
  • 3 oz macadamia nuts
  • 1 heaping tsp grated ginger
  • 2 oz dried cherries
  • 5 oz Pink Lady apple, in chunks (1 in.)
  • 1 oz plain yogurt
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp fresh ground pepper, to taste
  • ½ lemon, juice only, freshly squeezed
  • ½ oz extra virgin olive oil
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1146 kJ / 274 kcal
Protein
3 g
Carbohydrates
25 g
Fat
20 g

Preparation

Ginger Cabbage Slaw

  1. Place cabbage, nuts, ginger, dried cherries, apple, yogurt, salt, pepper, lemon juice and olive oil into mixing bowl and chop 3-4 sec/speed 4 until desired consistency is achieved.
  2. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
