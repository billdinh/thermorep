Cookies and cream ice cream with brown sugar biscuit bowls

Ingredients

Biscuit bowls

  • 100 g unsalted butter, cut into pieces (3-4 cm)
  • 120 - 150 g light brown sugar, to taste
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste
  • 180 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • ½ tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 1 pinch salt
  • oil spray, for greasing

Ice cream

  • 150 g plain chocolate biscuits
  • 600 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 200 g sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste
  • chocolate lollies of choice, for decorating

Preparation

Biscuit bowls

  1. Place butter, sugar, egg and vanilla into mixing bowl and mix 2 min/speed 3.
  2. Add flour, bicarbonate of soda and salt and mix 40 sec/speed 3. Transfer dough onto a silicone bread mat or floured work surface. Shape dough into a ball, then wrap in plastic wrap and place into refrigerator for 30 minutes to 1 hour, until firm enough to shape. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Preheat oven to 180°C. Liberally spray 2 x 12-hole muffin trays with oil and set aside.
  4. Divide dough into 18 equal portions (approx. 25 g each portion). On a silicone bread mat or floured work surface, gently flatten each dough portion into a disc large enough to cover the base and sides of each muffin hole (approx. 10 cm). Transfer discs into prepared muffin holes, gently pressing dough into base and sides to form a bowl shape. Line each cookie bowl with cupcake liners and fill with pie weights, rice or dried beans.
  5. Bake for 10 minutes (180°C) or until just starting to brown. Carefully remove pie weights, rice or dried beans and cupcake liners, then bake for a further 3-5 minutes (180°C) or until crisp and brown. Leave to cool in muffin trays.

Ice cream

  1. Place chocolate biscuits into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec//speed 5. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Place cream, condensed milk and vanilla into mixing bowl and whisk until soft peaks form/speed 4, watching carefully through hole in mixing bowl lid to avoid overwhipping.
  3. Add reserved biscuits and mix 10 sec/speed 4.  Remove butterfly whisk.
  4. Place a scoop of ice cream into each biscuit bowl and decorate with chocolate lollies. Place into freezer for 1 hour to set slightly. Remove from muffin tray, transfer into freezable storage containers and place into freezer for up to 6 hours to freeze. Remove from freezer approx. 10 minutes before serving.

Useful Items

silicone bread mat, plastic wrap, 2 x 12-hole muffin trays, 24 cupcake liners, pie weights, rice or dried beans, bowl, freezable storage container, ice cream scoop, refrigerator, oven, freezer

Hints & Tricks

  • Ice cream can be stored in a freezable container in the freezer for up to 1 month.
  • Alternatively, you can make ice cream, freeze as per recipe and scoop into biscuit bowls before serving. However, on warm days, you will need to work quickly as ice cream will melt.
