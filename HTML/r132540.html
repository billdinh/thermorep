Tomato Basil Chicken Quiche

Ingredients

Pastry

  • 270 g plain flour
  • 100 g olive oil
  • 60 g water
  • 8 - 10 fresh basil leaves, to taste
  • 1 tbsp lemon zest
  • ½ tsp salt

Filling

  • 30 g olive oil
  • 150 g onions, quartered
  • 30 g dried tomatoes in oil, drained
  • 300 g chicken breast fillets, skinless, cut in cubes (2 cm)
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 pinch freshly ground pepper
  • 250 g cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 200 g cream, min. 30% fat
  • 2 eggs
  • 60 g feta cheese
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • 10 fresh basil leaves torn in pieces, to garnish

Preparation

Pastry

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C. Line the base of a springform tin (Ø 26 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place plain flour, olive oil, water, basil leaves, lemon zest and salt in mixing bowl then mix 15 sec/speed 5. Remove pastry from mixing bowl and form into a ball. Line prepared springform tin with the pastry using fingertips, prick with a fork to avoid air bubbles and bake for 10 minutes (200°C). Meanwhile prepare the filling.

Filling

  1. Place olive oil, onions and dried tomatoes in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl using spatula.
  2. Sauté 5 min/120°C/speed 1.
  3. Add chicken breast fillets, salt and ground pepper and cook 10 min/100°C//speed 1, then shred 4 sec//speed 4.
  4. Add cherry tomatoes and mix using spatula. Transfer to springform with baked pastry and set aside.
  5. Place cream, eggs, 30 g feta cheese and nutmeg in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 4. Transfer to the springform, sprinkle with remaining 30 g crumbled feta cheese and bake for 30-35 minutes (200°C). Serve warm or cold, garnished with basil.

Useful Items

baking paper, spring form tin (Ø 26 cm), oven
