Beans with beef in tomato sauce (fasouliah)

Ingredients

  • 250 g dried butter beans
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1300 g water
  • 200 g onions, cut into quarters
  • 45 g olive oil
  • 300 g beef shoulder meat, cut into pieces (3-4 cm)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 70 g tomato purée (concentrated)

Preparation

  1. Soak overnight dried white beans in 700 g water with 1 tsp baking soda.
  2. Place onions and oil into mixing bowl, chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and sauté 5 min/120°C/speed 1.
  4. Insert butterfly whisk, add beef, salt and pepper and sauté 10 min/120°C/speed .
  5. Remove butterfly whisk, add water and heat 10 min/100°C/speed .
  6. Add drained and washed pre-soaked beans and tomato puree, cook 50-70 min/100°C//speed , prolong time if white beans are not soft enough (see tip). Transfer into a serving bowl and season to taste before serving.
