Honey lime chicken supremes with Mango chutney

Ingredients

Marinated chicken

  • 30 g honey
  • ½ tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ - 1 tsp ground sweet paprika
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 lime, preferably organic, zest (cut in 1 cm pieces) and juice
  • 4 chicken supremes, skinless (approx. 800 g) (see tip)

Mango chutney and steamed chicken supremes

  • 10 - 15 g fresh coriander
  • 3 - 6 limes, preferably organic, zest (cut in 1 cm pieces) and juice
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 100 g onions, cut in halves
  • 75 g red peppers, cut in pieces
  • 1 - 2 fresh jalapeño chillies, deseeded
  • 500 g ripe mango, flesh only, cut in small pieces (1-2 cm)
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 100 g sugar
  • 250 g water

Preparation

Marinated chicken

  1. Place honey, black pepper, salt, sweet paprika, garlic clove, lime zest and 20 g lime juice in mixing bowl and blend 30 sec/speed 8.
  2. Place chicken in a bowl and drizzle the blended marinade over it, stir to coat then allow to marinate for 30 minutes in refrigerator. Meanwhile line Varoma tray with baking paper with enough to go 2 cm up the sides. Insert Varoma tray in Varoma dish. Clean and dry mixing bowl and continue with recipe.

Mango chutney and steamed chicken supremes

  1. Place coriander and approx. 50 g lime zest in mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 8. Reserve 1-2 tsp coriander-lime zest mixture in a small bowl.
  2. Add garlic cloves, onions, red peppers and jalapeño chillies to mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  3. Add mango, salt, sugar, 70 g lime juice and water. Arrange marinated chicken on lined Varoma tray, set Varoma in position and cook 28 min/Varoma//speed 1. Set covered Varoma aside on a plate to keep warm. Transfer part of the mango chutney to fill a 450 g sterilised jar, cover with a sterilised lid and set aside for another use (see tip). Transfer remaining mango chutney to a wide serving dish and allow to cool for a few minutes until lukewarm. Slice the chicken supremes diagonally and arrange them over the mango chutney in the serving dish, then sprinkle with reserved chopped coriander-lime zest mixture. Serve warm or refrigerate and serve chilled.

Useful Items

baking paper, jam jar with twist-off lid, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • For a complete meal, in step 5 steam in Varoma dish 400-600 g vegetables (e.g. carrots, potatoes, green beans, courgettes, broccoli), cut in bite-size pieces.
  • What is wonderful with this recipe is the perfect cooking point of the chicken, moist and tender, and the surprising and moreish combination with mango chutney. The recipe produces a bonus - an additional jar of mango chutney that you can enjoy another time with steamed meat or fish, your favourite cheese, or a curry.
  • A chicken supreme is a half breast of chicken with the upper part of the wing still attached.
  • For a stronger flavour, marinate the chicken in step 2 for a few hours or overnight.
  • Once it has cooled completely, the mango chutney in the jar can be kept in the refrigerator for up to two weeks or stored in freezable containers in the freezer for up to 6 months.
