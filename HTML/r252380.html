Warm satay chicken salad

Ingredients

Satay sauce

  • 40 g raw unsalted peanuts
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 2 - 3 tsp sambal oelek, to taste
  • 240 g coconut milk
  • 1 ½ tbsp raw sugar
  • 1 ½ tsp tamarind paste (see Tips)
  • 30 g fish sauce

Coconut rice and chicken

  • 300 g jasmine rice
  • 700 g water
  • 1 garlic clove, roughly chopped
  • 160 g coconut milk
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 500 g boneless skinless chicken thighs, cut into pieces (1-2 cm)
  • salt, to season
  • ground black pepper, to season

Salad

  • ¼ red onion, cut into small pieces (5 mm)
  • 2 spring onions/shallots, trimmed and cut into slices
  • ½ tsp salt
  • water, to cover
  • 150 g raw cashews
  • 250 g red cabbage, cut into slices
  • 1 bunch fresh coriander (approx. 30 g), leaves and stalks, roughly chopped
  • 1 carrot, cut into pieces (1-2 cm)
  • ½ red capsicum, deseeded and cut into pieces (1-2 cm)

Preparation

Satay sauce

  1. Place peanuts into mixing bowl and chop 6 sec/speed 5. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place garlic into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with aid of spatula.
  3. Add oil and sambal oelek and sauté 1 min 30 sec/100°C/speed 1.
  4. Add coconut milk, sugar, tamarind paste, fish sauce and reserved peanuts and cook 10 min/100°C/speed , then blend 30 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Do not clean mixing bowl.

Coconut rice and chicken

  1. Insert simmering basket and weigh rice into it. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and rinse rice until water runs clear.
  2. Place water, garlic, coconut milk and salt into mixing bowl. Re-insert simmering basket with rice in it.
  3. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh chicken into it. Season with salt and pepper. Secure Varoma lid and cook 14 min/100°C/speed 4.
  4. Carefully remove Varoma and stir chicken with aid of spatula to ensure even cooking. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and set aside to drain. Place Varoma back into position, secure Varoma lid and cook 7 min/Varoma/speed 2. Set Varoma aside, allowing chicken and rice to cool for 10 minutes.

Salad

  1. Place onion, spring onion/shallots and salt into a bowl. Cover with water and set aside for 10 minutes to soak, then drain.
  2. Place a large bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh cashews and cabbage into it. Add coriander, carrot, capsicum, reserved cooled rice and chicken, drained onions and reserved satay sauce. Toss to combine, then serve.

Hints & Tricks

  • You can find tamarind paste at most Asian food stores.
  • You can prepare all components in advance and store in refrigerator. Just before serving, heat satay sauce slightly to loosen it, then toss together. Great for quick dinners or lunchboxes!
