Vanilla and Honey Toasted Muesli

Ingredients

  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 100 g almonds
  • 80 g pecan nuts
  • 60 g dried apricots, pitted
  • 250 g rolled oats
  • 70 g sunflower seeds
  • 70 g pumpkin seeds
  • 20 g sesame seeds
  • 20 g chia seeds
  • 100 g raisins
  • 40 g dried blueberries (optional)
  • 60 g dried cranberries
  • 60 g desiccated coconut
  • 130 g honey
  • 40 g grapeseed oil
  • 1 - 2 tsp vanilla extract, to taste
  • ½ tsp ground cardamom

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 150°C and line 2 large baking trays with baking paper.
  2. Place cinnamon in mixing bowl and grind 1 min/speed 9.
  3. Add almonds and pecans then chop Turbo/1 sec/2 times.
  4. Add apricots and chop 3 sec/speed 6.
  5. Add all remaining ingredients and, with aid of spatula, mix 2 min/70°C//speed 2. Spread mixture evenly across prepared trays, then bake for 10 minutes (150°C). Stir mixture with spatula to ensure it is evenly toasted, then bake for a further 10-15 minutes (150°C) until golden brown.
  6. Leave mixture to cool on trays, then store in an airtight container until needed.

Useful Items

baking paper, airtight container, large baking trays, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Apple and Cinnamon Toasted Muesli: add 1 additional cinnamon stick in step 2. In step 4, add 90 g pitted dates with apricots and replace honey with 130 g apple juice in step 5.
  • Maple and Orange Toasted Muesli: in step 4, add 1 orange, finely grated zest only, with apricots and replace honey with maple syrup in step 5.
  • Homemade muesli is so easy you’ll never need to buy it again. Make a batch of this nutty, naturally-sweet cereal at the beginning of the week and you'll feel confident that your family is getting a good start to their day.
  • Serve toasted muesli with yoghurt and fresh fruit.
