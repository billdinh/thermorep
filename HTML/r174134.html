Kringle

Ingredients

Dough

  • 2 oz unsalted butter, softened plus extra to grease
  • 4 ½ oz whole milk
  • 1 tbsp active dry yeast
  • 10 ½ oz all-purpose flour, plus extra to dust
  • 2 oz sugar
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 large egg

Filling

  • 2 oz unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 oz sugar
  • 1 tbsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp whole milk
  • 2 tsp sugar, to sprinkle
  • ¼ tsp ground cinnamon, to sprinkle

Preparation

Dough

  1. Grease a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place milk and yeast into mixing bowl and warm 2 min/100°F/speed 1.
  3. Add flour, sugar, salt, egg and butter and knead 2 min/. Transfer to greased bowl and let rise until doubled (approx. 1 hr.).
  4. On a lightly floured surface, roll out dough with a rolling pin into a rectangle (approx. 18 in. x 12 in.).

Filling

  1. Line a baking sheet with parchment and set aside. Place butter, sugar and cinnamon into mixing bowl and warm 1 min/100°F/speed 1.
  2. Spread cinnamon mixture evenly over the entire surface of the dough. Roll up lengthwise into a long roll. Cut roll in half lengthwise with a sharp knife, to within ½ inch of one end (so both dough halves are still connected). Transfer dough to prepared baking sheet and form into a circle by twisting the two halves over each other joining the ends together. Reshape as needed, cover with cling wrap and let rise in a warm place until doubled in size (approx. 30 min.).
  3. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  4. Place egg and milk into a small bowl and whisk with a fork to combine. Brush surface with egg wash and sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes (350°F) until the Kringle becomes golden brown. Let cool slightly. Serve warm.

Useful Items

baking sheet and parchment paper, sharp knife, cling wrap, rolling pin, pastry brush, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Add chopped nuts or dried fruit to dough before rolling.
