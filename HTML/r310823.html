Savory Benne Wafers with Red Pepper Tapenade

Ingredients

Red Pepper Tapenade

  • 2 oz Parmesan cheese, cut into pieces
  • 3 oz tomato, cut into pieces
  • 2 ½ oz red bell pepper, cut into pieces (4 in.)
  • 1 oz fresh basil leaves, plus extra to garnish
  • 3 oz raw cashews
  • 2 oz sun-dried tomatoes
  • 2 oz roasted red peppers, preserved, jarred
  • ½ oz extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 lime, juice only
  • 1 pinch salt

Savory Benne Wafers

  • 10 oz sesame seeds, divided
  • 16 oz all-purpose flour, plus extra to dust
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • 5 ½ oz unsalted butter, cubed
  • 6 oz whole milk
  • sea salt, to sprinkle (optional)

Preparation

Red Pepper Tapenade

  1. Place cheese into mixing bowl and grate 6 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place tomato, red pepper, 1 oz basil, cashews, sun-dried tomatoes, roasted bell peppers, olive oil, lime juice and salt into mixing bowl and chop Turbo/2 sec/2 times, then heat 3 min/212°F/speed 1.
  3. Add reserved grated cheese and stir 5 sec/speed 2. Transfer to a serving bowl and garnish with basil leaves. Clean mixing bowl.

Savory Benne Wafers

  1. Position oven racks to the top and bottom thirds of the oven and preheat oven to 425°F. Line baking sheets with parchment and set aside.
  2. Place 5 oz sesame seeds into mixing bowl and toast 5 min/250°F/speed 1. Set toasted sesame seeds aside.
  3. Place 5 oz sesame seeds into mixing bowl and grind 20 sec/speed 10.
  4. Add flour, salt, baking powder and baking soda and mix 20 sec/speed 8. Add butter and mix 10 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add milk and reserved toasted sesame seeds and mix 15 sec/speed 5.
  6. Transfer dough onto a lightly floured surface and work until smooth (approx. 2 min.). Divide dough into three equal portions and pat each portion into a disk. Loosely wrap two of the portions with cling wrap and refrigerate.
  7. With a lightly floured rolling pin, roll out remaining dough disk until paper thin. Cut 10-12 rounds from dough with a cookie cutter (2 in.) and lightly sprinkle with sea salt.
  8. Transfer wafers to prepared baking sheets. Bake for 8 minutes (425°F) or until lightly golden, halfway through cooking, rotate the trays and swap positions to ensure even baking. Allow to cool (approx. 1 min.) before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
  9. Repeat with remaining dough disks. Knead any leftover dough into another round and repeat process.
  10. Serve Savory Benne Wafers with Red Pepper Tapenade.

Useful Items

parchment paper, cling wrap, cookie cutter (2 in.), wire rack, baking sheets, rolling pin, oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Add a hot chili pepper to Red Pepper Tapenade for a spicy kick.
  • Benne Wafers can be stored in an airtight container for up to five days or frozen for up to a month.
  • For added texture, prick the dough all over with a fork before cutting into rounds.
