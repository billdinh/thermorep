Fatayer with Cheese and Mint

Ingredients

Dough

  • 250 g whole milk
  • 80 g olive oil
  • 15 g fresh yeast
  • 350 g strong white bread flour
  • 15 g sugar
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt

Filling

  • 10 g fresh mint, leaves only
  • 50 g pecorino cheese, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 75 g cream cheese
  • 1 ½ tsp nigella seeds

To make fatayer

  • strong white bread flour, for dusting

Preparation

Dough

  1. Place milk, olive oil and yeast in mixing bowl and warm 2 min/37°C/speed 1.
  2. Add bread flour, sugar and salt and knead 2 min 30 sec/. Transfer dough to a large bowl. Cover with cling film and allow to rise for 1 hour or until doubled in size. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Filling

  1. Place mint and pecorino in mixing bowl and chop 8 sec/speed 8.
  2. Add cream cheese and nigella seeds, then combine using spatula. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.

To make fatayer

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C. Line a baking tray with baking paper.
  2. On a floured surface, divide dough into 12 pieces, and roll into 12 balls, then roll or pressout each ball of dough thinly to a square, approx 15 cm x 15 cm. Place a spoonful of cheese filling in the centre of each square, and roll up the sides to make a box shape. Give the two opposite corners a twist over, to form a neat boat shape.
  3. Place fatayer on prepared baking tray. Bake for 25 minutes (200°C) until golden brown and cooked through. Serve hot or cold.

Useful Items

cling film, rolling pin, baking tray and paper, large bowl, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • To make a meat filling: Place 80 g onion, 1 pinch ground cinnamon and the juice of 1 lemon in mixing bowl and chop 8 sec/speed 8. Add 200 g minced beef, and stir 10 sec/speed 1.
