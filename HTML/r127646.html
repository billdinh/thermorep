Iced Tea Cocktail

Ingredients

  • 1500 g water
  • 8 black tea bags
  • 2 lemons, preferably organic, 1 lemon, cut in slices and 1 lemon, halved
  • 200 g sugar
  • 250 g rum
  • 100 g brandy
  • 150 g Peach Brandy
  • 12 - 16 ice cubes
  • 300 g soda water
Nutrition
per 1 glass
Calories
546 kJ / 130 kcal
Protein
0 g
Carbohydrates
16 g

0 g

Preparation

  1. Place water in mixing bowl and cook 10 min/100°C/speed 1. Transfer to a jug and add tea bags and lemon slices. Allow to steep for 10 minutes, then remove and discard tea bags and lemon slices.
  2. Place 500 g black tea and sugar in mixing bowl then mix 5 sec/speed 4.
  3. Add lemon halves, hold measuring cup in place and crush 1 sec/speed 10.
  4. Add rum, brandy and peach brandy. Insert simmering basket, hold it in place with spatula and strain lemonade tea into the jug with remaining tea. Chill for one hour in refrigerator. Add ice cubes and soda water and serve immediately.

Useful Items

jug, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • For a non-alcoholic version replace the rum with soda water, and the brandy and peach liqueur with peach juice.
  • Serve with sprigs of fresh mint or slices of lemon.
  • Tea can be made up to 1 day before.
