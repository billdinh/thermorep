Green bean bundles, potatoes and thyme butter

Ingredients

  • 500 g water
  • 1 ½ tsp salt
  • 600 g potatoes, cut in cubes (3 cm)
  • 200 g fresh green beans
  • 8 - 16 slices streaky bacon (100 g), smoked, thin slices
  • 80 - 100 g butter
  • 1 - 2 tsp fresh thyme
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1596 kJ / 382 kcal
Protein
8 g
Carbohydrates
26 g

26 g

Preparation

  1. Place water and 1 tsp salt in mixing bowl, insert simmering basket and add potatoes to it. Set Varoma dish in position.
  2. Divide green beans into 8 equal bundles and wrap streaky bacon around each bundle. Place bundles in middle of Varoma dish to ensure even steaming and insert Varoma tray.
  3. Place a small heat-resistant bowl, cup or ramekin on Varoma tray and add butter, remaining ½ tsp salt and thyme. Close Varoma and steam 28 min/Varoma/speed 1. Carefully open Varoma to check whether beans and potatoes are cooked through. If not, steam a further 2-5 min/Varoma/speed 1. Serve green bean bundles with potatoes, drizzled with thyme butter.

Useful Items

small heat-resistant bowl, cup or ramekin

Hints & Tricks

  • These two complementary side dishes can be left to steam in your Thermomix® while a roast is in the oven and you entertain your guests or help the children with their homework. The thyme butter sauce melts on its own in the Varoma while the green bean bundles and potatoes steam. This dish can also be served as a lighter meal on its own, with a simple salad.
  • For a vegetarian version, omit bacon and use spring onion greens or chives to tie the green bean bundles.
  • Serve a cream and chive sauce drizzled over the potatoes instead of the thyme butter.
  • Add some chopped garlic to the thyme butter before steaming.
