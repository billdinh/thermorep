Basic muffin mix

Ingredients

  • 300 g self-raising flour
  • 150 g plain flour
  • ½ tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 200 g brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 370 g milk
  • 180 g vegetable oil

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Line 16 holes of 2 x 12-hole muffin trays with cupcake liners and set aside.
  2. Place flours, bicarbonate of soda and sugar into mixing bowl and mix 5 sec/speed 5.
  3. Add eggs, milk and oil and mix 10 sec/speed 4. (Add flavours of choice here - see Variations)
  4. Divide mixture between prepared muffin trays. Bake for 20 minutes (180°C) or until a wooden skewer inserted into the centre of muffins comes out clean. Transfer onto a wire rack to cool completely.

Useful Items

12-hole muffin tray, wooden skewer, wire rack, cupcake liners, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • These muffins are so versatile; you can put just about anything from your pantry into them!
  • To make gluten free muffins, replace the flours with gluten free varieties.
  • To make wholemeal muffins, replace the white flours with wholemeal flours or a combination of half wholemeal and half white flour.
  • Carrot and sultana: Chop 1 carrot 2 sec/speed 5 and transfer into a bowl before step 1. Proceed as per recipe, adding chopped carrot, 80 g sultanas and 1 tsp mixed spice after adding oil. Proceed as per recipe.
  • Berry and white chocolate: Stir through 100 g fresh or frozen berries of choice and 100 g white chocolate chips with aid of spatula before dividing mixture between muffin trays. Proceed as per recipe.
  • Date and ginger: Chop 160 g pitted dates 2 sec/speed 7 and transfer into a bowl before step 1. Proceed as per recipe, adding chopped dates and 2 tsp ground ginger after adding oil. Proceed as per recipe.
