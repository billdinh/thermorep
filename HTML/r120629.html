Chocolate self-saucing pudding

Ingredients

Cake

  • unsalted butter, for greasing
  • 60 g blanched almonds
  • 100 g gluten free flour mix (see Tips)
  • 2 tsp gluten free baking powder (see Tips)
  • 30 g cocoa powder (pure)
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 100 g soft dark brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 40 g full cream milk
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract

Sauce

  • 100 g soft dark brown sugar
  • 20 g cocoa powder (pure)
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract
  • 150 g water
  • 150 g full cream milk
  • pouring (whipping) cream, to serve

Preparation

Cake

  1. Preheat oven to 170°C. Grease a round ceramic baking dish (18 x 6 cm) and set aside.
  2. Place almonds into mixing bowl and mill 8 sec/speed 7.
  3. Add flour mix, baking powder, cocoa, salt and sugar into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 5.
  4. Add eggs, milk and vanilla and mix 5 sec/speed 4.
  5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix 5 sec/speed 4. Transfer into prepared baking dish and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Sauce

  1. Place sugar, cocoa powder, vanilla, water and milk into mixing bowl and heat 3 min/100°C/speed 2. Pour sauce gently over the back of a spoon to cover pudding and bake for 30-35 minutes (170°C), until pudding is firm to the touch. Serve pudding warm with pouring (whipping) cream.
