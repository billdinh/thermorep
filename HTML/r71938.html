Spicy cauliflower soup

Ingredients

Curry powder

  • 1 tbsp whole black peppercorns
  • 2 ½ tbsp coriander seeds
  • 1 tbsp cumin seeds
  • 2 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • 5 dried chillies
  • 1 tbsp rock salt
  • 2 tsp granulated garlic
  • 4 cardamom pods (see Tips)

Soup

  • 160 g cauliflower, cut into small florets
  • 1 - 2 tsp curry powder, plus extra for garnishing
  • 40 g raw cashew nuts
  • 2 tsp vegetable stock paste (see Tips)
  • 400 g water
  • fresh coriander, leaves only, for garnishing (optional)

Preparation

Curry powder

  1. Place all curry powder ingredients into mixing bowl and dry roast 8 min/120°C/speed 2. Allow to cool then mill 30 sec/speed 10. Transfer into a sealable jar or container to store until ready to use.

Soup

  1. Place all soup ingredients, except coriander leaves (optional), into mixing bowl and cook 15 min/100°C/speed 1.
  2. Then blend 1 min/speed 4, increasing speed gradually from speed 4 to speed 9.
  3. Serve soup hot, garnished with fresh coriander leaves (optional) and extra curry powder to taste.

Useful Items

sealable storage jar or container

Hints & Tricks

  • You can substitute 4 cardamom pods with 1 tsp ground cardamom.
  • Please refer to The Basic Cookbook or the Everyday Cookbook for the Vegetable stock paste recipe.
  • Refrigerate or freeze any leftovers to enjoy later.
  • Crush some Curried almonds in your Thermomix to garnish your soup for added texture and spice.
  • Store leftover curry powder in a cool, dark place.
  • The cashew nuts in this recipe add creaminess to the soup without the need for dairy.
