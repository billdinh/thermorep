Smoky Couscous Salad

Ingredients

  • 800 g boiling water
  • 1 ½ vegetable stock cubes (for 0.5 l each)
  • 200 g pearl couscous (giant)
  • ½ Tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 150 g fresh baby spinach, roughly chopped
  • 200 g feta cheese, diced (1 cm)
  • 150 g roasted red peppers, preserved, drained, cut in strips
  • 40 g extra virgin olive oil, plus ½ Tbsp
  • 20 g fresh flat-leaf parsley, roughly chopped
  • 1 ½ tsp smoked paprika
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1912 kJ / 457 kcal

16.5 g
Carbohydrates

24 g

Preparation

  1. Place water and stock cube in mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket. Weigh in couscous then cook 8 min/98°C/speed 4. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula then stir olive oil through couscous and set aside to cool. Empty mixing bowl.
  2. Weigh spinach, feta and roasted red peppers into a serving bowl.
  3. Place extra virgin olive oil, parsley, paprika, garlic and salt in mixing bowl then blend 3 sec/speed 7. Transfer to serving bowl then toss.
  4. Serve immediately or refrigerate until required.
