Garlic Tomato Beef with Potato and Carrot Mash

Ingredients

  • 700 g floury potatoes, peeled, diced (1.5 cm)
  • 300 g carrots, cut in pieces (1.5 cm)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 100 g onions, quartered
  • 40 g olive oil
  • 400 g tinned chopped tomatoes
  • 1 dried bay leaf
  • 800 g beef meat, sinew free, diced (2.5 cm)
  • 50 g red wine
  • 50 g water
  • 1 beef stock cube (for 0.5 l), crumbled
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 80 g milk
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • fresh parsley leaves, roughly chopped, for garnishing

Preparation

  1. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh in potatoes. Insert Varoma tray then weigh on carrots. Set Varoma aside.
  2. Place garlic, onions and 30 g oil in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  3. Add tomatoes and bay leaf then sauté 5 min/120°C/speed 1.
  4. Add beef, wine, water, beef stock and pepper then cook 10 min/Varoma//speed 1.
  5. Place covered Varoma into position then cook 20 min/Varoma//speed 1. Set Varoma aside. Transfer beef stew to a serving bowl and cover to keep warm. Clean mixing bowl.
  6. Place steamed carrots in mixing bowl and mash 3 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  7. Place steamed potatoes, remaining 10 g oil, milk, salt and nutmeg in mixing bowl then mash 40 sec/speed 3. Transfer to bowl with carrots and mix well with spatula. Garnish beef stew with parsley and serve hot with mash.

Hints & Tricks

  • Potato and carrot mash can be frozen in small batches.
  • Drizzle 1 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil over mashed potatoes for extra flavour.
