Beef with Mushrooms

Ingredients

  • 100 g onions, quartered
  • 15 g olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 2 pinches fine sea salt, or to taste
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper, or to taste
  • 600 g beef rump steak, trimmed, cut in pieces (2-3 cm)
  • 500 g button mushrooms, halved, quartered if large
  • 30 g Dijon mustard
  • 60 g plain yoghurt
  • 30 g cream cheese

Preparation

  1. Place onions, oil and garlic in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add salt and pepper. Insert simmering basket and weigh in beef. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh in mushrooms. Cover Varoma then steam 12 min/Varoma/speed . Set covered Varoma aside then remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and keep warm.
  3. Add mustard, yoghurt and cream cheese to mixing bowl then mix 30 sec/speed 10. Adjust seasoning to taste.
  4. Add steamed beef and mushrooms to sauce then reheat 4 min/70°C//speed 2. Serve warm.

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with mashed potatoes or rice.
  • For vegetarians, replace steak with tofu.
  • Juices from the steak and water from the mushrooms drip down to create the steaming liquid!
