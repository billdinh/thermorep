Asparagus Risotto, Sun-dried Tomato Tear and Share Rolls, Berry Foam

Ingredients

Preparation Before Demo

  • 250 g asparagus, woody ends removed

Sun-dried Tomato Tear and Share Rolls

  • 220 g water
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 tsp dried instant yeast
  • 500 g strong white bread flour
  • 90 g sun-dried tomatoes in oil, drained
  • 30 g olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt, to taste

Berry Foam

  • 80 - 140 g sugar, to taste
  • 500 g mixed berries, frozen, to taste
  • 10 - 30 g lime juice, to taste
  • 1 egg white, from medium or large egg

Baking

  • 1 egg yolk, from medium or large egg

Asparagus Risotto

  • 60 g Parmesan cheese, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 50 g shallots, halved
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 200 g dry white wine
  • 250 g risotto rice
  • 450 g vegetable stock, hot
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 250 g frozen peas
  • 20 g butter

Preparation

Preparation Before Demo

  1. Cut off asparagus tips and set aside. Cut each asparagus stalk into 3 pieces and set aside.

Sun-dried Tomato Tear and Share Rolls

  1. Preheat oven to 50°C and grease a large baking tray (see tip).
  2. Place water, sugar and yeast in mixing bowl then mix 20 sec/speed 2.
  3. Add flour, sun-dried tomatoes, oil and salt then knead 2 min/. Tip out and divide dough into 12 equal-sized pieces, then roll each into a ball. Arrange side by side in rows (6 x 2) on prepared tray so rolls are just touching. Place in preheated oven and leave to prove for 20 minutes (50°C, quick rise). Meanwhile, self-wash mixing bowl by covering blade with water and a drop of washing up liquid. Set for 1 min/60°C/speed 6. Discard water, rinse and wipe dry then make berry foam.

Berry Foam

  1. Place sugar in mixing bowl and grind 15 sec/speed 10.
  2. Add frozen berries, lime juice and egg white then, while stirring with spatula through hole in mixing bowl lid, blend 40 sec/speed 5.
  3. Insert butterfly whisk and whisk 3 min/speed 3.5. Transfer to serving bowls and enjoy, freezing any leftovers in a freezer-proof container. Rinse mixing bowl, then make risotto.

Baking

  1. Brush rolls with reserved egg yolk then increase oven temperature to 180°C and bake for 18-20 minutes (180°C) or until rolls sound hollow when tapped underneath. Meanwhile, continue with recipe.

Asparagus Risotto

  1. Place Parmesan in mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place asparagus stalk pieces, shallots and oil in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  3. Add wine then risotto rice and, without measuring cup, cook 2 min/120°C//speed 1. Scrape base of mixing bowl with spatula to loosen rice.
  4. Add hot stock, salt and pepper. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh in peas. Cover then cook 12-15 min/100°C//speed 1 until rice is cooked al dente (with a bite) - the risotto should be slightly soupy at this stage.
  5. Set Varoma aside. Add reserved asparagus tips, reserved Parmesan and butter then stir in with spatula. Adjust seasoning if desired then replace Varoma and leave to rest for 2 minutes.
  6. Serve with peas on the side accompanied by the tear and share rolls.

Useful Items

bowl, serving bowls, large baking tray, freezer-proof container, pastry brush, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • If the oven does not have a 50°C setting, preheat oven to 100°C then turn off before placing loaves in warm oven to prove.
