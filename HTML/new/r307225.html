Orange Sorbet

 5.0 (3 ratings)

Ingredients

  • 80 g caster sugar
  • 500 g ice cubes
  • 200 g oranges (approx. 2 oranges), without skin or pips, cut in pieces

Preparation

  1. Place caster sugar in mixing bowl, grind 5 sec/speed 10.
  2. Add ice cubes, crush 30 sec/speed 10 while stirring with spatula, moving clockwise and anticlockwise.
  3. Add oranges, mix 30 sec/speed 6 while stirring with spatula, moving closewise and anticlockwise. Serve immediately.
