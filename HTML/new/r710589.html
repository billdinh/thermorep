Almond Pulp Galette with Tomatoes

 4.8 (6 ratings)

Ingredients

Dough

  • 5 oz all-purpose flour
  • 4 oz almond pulp, from homemade almond milk (see Tip)
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 ½ oz unsalted butter, diced, frozen
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 - 3 tbsp ice water, as needed
  • 1 lemon

Herbed Cheese Filling

  • 6 oz goat cheese, crumbled
    or 6 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 oz extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbsp mixed dried herbs, thyme, rosemary, chives
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp salt, to taste
  • ⅛ tsp ground black pepper, to taste

Roasted Tomatoes

  • 16 oz heirloom cherry tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil, plus extra to brush
  • ¼ tsp salt, to taste
  • ⅛ tsp ground black pepper, to taste

Assembly

  • everything bagel seasoning
  • fresh basil leaves, to garnish

Preparation

Dough

  1. Place flour, almond pulp and salt into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 3.5.
  2. Add butter and mix 3 sec/speed 5.
  3. And egg and ice water and mix 4 sec/speed 4. Transfer dough to cling wrap, quickly shape into a disk, wrap and refrigerate 1 hour. Meanwhile clean mixing bowl and make the cheese filling. With a vegetable peeler, thinly peel zest from 1 lemon, avoiding the white pith and set aside. Cut lemon in half, squeeze the juice into a bowl and set aside.

Herbed Cheese Filling

  1. Place goat cheese, reserve lemon zest, reserved lemon juice, olive oil, herbs, garlic powder, salt and pepper and mix 20 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.

Roasted Tomatoes

  1. Preheat oven 400°F. Line a baking sheet with foil. Place tomatoes onto prepared baking sheet and drizzle with olive oil, salt and pepper. Toss to coat and bake 20-25 minutes (400°F), until tomatoes begin to burst. Set aside.

Assembly

  1. On lightly floured parchment, roll out dough into a 12-inch circle.
  2. Spread reserved goat cheese mixture over dough, leaving a 1 inch border, and top with roasted tomatoes.
  3. In a circular direction, gently and tightly fold edges over the tomatoes leaving the very center exposed, pleating the dough as you fold. Brush the exposed dough with olive oil and sprinkle with everything bagel seasoning.
  4. Bake 35-40 minutes (400°F), or until golden brown. Garnish with basil and serve warm or at room temperature.

Hints & Tips

  • Use pulp leftover from making Almond Milk. Search Cookidoo® for Almond Milk recipe in the Superfoods Immunity Boost and Alternative Milks collections.
  • Just before baking galette, instead of brushing exposed dough with olive oil, brush with tomato liquid and oil remaining in pan after roasting tomatoes.
