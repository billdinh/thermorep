Chilli lime sauce

 4.5 (11 ratings)

Ingredients

  • 1 lime, zest only, no white pith
  • 2 spring onions/shallots, trimmed and cut into quarters
  • 20 g lime juice (approx. 1 lime)
  • 80 g sweet chilli sauce
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce

Preparation

  1. Place lime zest and spring onions/shallots into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then repeat chopping 10 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula again.
  2. Add lime juice, sweet chilli sauce and fish sauce and mix 10 sec/speed 5. Transfer into a sealable jar or bottle and refrigerate until ready to serve.
