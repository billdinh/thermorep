Limoncello

 4.5 (2 ratings)

Ingredients

  • 11 lemons, unwaxed, thin peelings of skin only
  • 1000 g vodka
  • 800 g sugar
  • 1000 g water

Preparation

  1. Place lemon peel in a large glass jar (3 l). Place jar on mixing bowl lid and weigh in vodka. Close and leave to infuse in a dark place for 6 days. Shake once a day.
  2. Place sugar and water in mixing bowl then heat 8 min/80°C/speed 2. Transfer to jar with vodka mixture, stir, close and leave for 2 more days.
  3. Pour limoncello through a fine mesh sieve into 6 glass bottles (500 ml each, see tip) and serve or store in a cool, dry place.

Hints & Tips

  • To prepare bottles, thoroughly wash bottles and lids in hot, soapy water. Rinse by pouring over boiling water and leave to drain - do not dry or touch the inside surfaces.
