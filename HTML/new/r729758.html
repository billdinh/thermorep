Red Date Longan Peach Gum Dessert

 4.8 (6 ratings)

Ingredients

  • 1500 g water
  • 3 - 4 pandan leaves, tied into knots
  • 40 g dried longan, rinsed
  • 20 jujube red dates, pitted
  • 50 g peach gum, soaked overnight, cleaned
  • 60 g lump sugar
  • 1 tbsp goji berries, rinsed

Preparation

  1. Place water, pandan leaves, dried longan and red dates in mixing bowl, cook 6 min/100°C//speed 1.
  2. Add peach gum and lump sugar, cook 45 min/90°C/speed . Add goji berries to mixing bowl and let stand for 10 minutes. Serve warm.
