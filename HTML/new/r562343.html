Lemon Rosemary Infused Oil

 5.0 (4 ratings)

Ingredients

  • 8 sprigs rosemary, divided
  • 1 lemon, thin peel only, no pith
  • 30 oz olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove, sliced

Preparation

  1. Wash rosemary sprigs, set aside and let dry thoroughly (see Tip).
  2. Place lemon peel and 2 prepared sprigs rosemary into mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add oil and garlic and heat 20 min/120°F/speed 1. Let cool to room temperature, then strain mixture through a fine sieve. Place 1 prepared rosemary sprig into each of 6 sterilized bottles or glass jars (see Tip). Pour cooled rosemary oil into bottles and seal. Package for gift giving and refrigerate until needed.

Hints & Tips

  • To prepare bottles, place 17.5 oz water into mixing bowl. Place bottles and lids upside down in the center of the Varoma and steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 1. Leave to drain—do not dry or touch the inside surfaces. Alternatively, thoroughly wash bottles and lids in hot, soapy water. Rinse by pouring over boiling water. Prepare for filling, by placing on a clean tea towel in a roasting pan and heating in the oven at 212˚F until required.
  • Some bacteria can grow in water, so wash and thoroughly dry rosemary sprigs and lemon before using. When adding rosemary to bottles, make sure it is washed and dried thoroughly before completely submerging in oil.
