Banana Nut Bundt Cake

 4.6 (9 ratings)

Ingredients

  • 4 oz unsalted butter, diced, plus extra to grease
  • 7 oz dark brown sugar
  • 7 oz ripe bananas, in pieces
  • 2 oz buttermilk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 7 oz all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 3 oz chopped pecans

Caramel Sauce

  • 9 oz sugar
  • 7 oz heavy whipping cream, divided
  • 36 oz water, divided
  • 1 ½ oz unsalted butter, divided, 1 oz cold, in 1 cube (1 in.) and 0.5 oz room temperature
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • chopped pecans, to garnish

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease a Thermomix® bundt pan (8 in.) and set aside.
  2. Place sugar and butter into mixing bowl and mix 30 sec/speed 4.5.
  3. Add bananas, buttermilk and vanilla and without measuring cup mix 45 sec/speed 4.5 while adding eggs one at a time through hole in mixing bowl lid. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add flour, baking soda and salt and with aid of spatula, mix 20 sec/speed 6. Add pecans and fold in with spatula.
  5. Transfer batter to prepared pan and smooth top with spatula. Bake 30-35 minutes (350°F). Let cool 20 minutes before inverting onto a cooling rack to cool completely. Clean mixing bowl.

Caramel Sauce

  1. To ensure the success of recipes with Sugar Stages the ingredients must match exactly in quantity and description given. The recipe should be cooked immediately after placing the sugar with other ingredients in the mixing bowl. The bowl must be filled for Pre-cleaning immediately after the finished caramel is transferred from mixing bowl and the mixing bowl edge cleaned with spatula before Pre-cleaning is started.
  2. Place sugar, 2.5 oz whipping cream, 1 oz water, 1 oz cold butter and lemon juice into mixing bowl, place splash guard instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and caramelize Sugar Stages .
  3. Add remaining 4.5 oz cream and 0.5 oz butter and without measuring cup, mix 2 min/speed 2. Then, without measuring cup, cook 3 min/Varoma/speed 2. Transfer to a heat-proof jar and set aside to cool to room temperature. Immediately clean edge of mixing bowl with spatula to remove any remaining caramel.
  4. Place 35 oz water and vinegar into mixing bowl. Insert measuring cup and start Pre-clean /Fats or Caramel. Rinse and dry mixing bowl and lid.
  5. Invert Banana Nut Bundt Cake onto a cake plate. Drizzle with Caramel Sauce, garnish with chopped pecans and serve with extra Caramel Sauce on the side.

Hints & Tips

  • To ensure success with this recipe the butter must be added as much as possible in one piece. Cut one chunk (approx. 1 in. x 1 in. x 1 in.) and add a bit more if necessary, to achieve 1 oz.
  • Lemon juice prevents crystallization and possible sugar burning and it does not change the taste of the caramel.
  • Bake time will depend on size of pan used. If using a different size bundt pan, adjust baking time accordingly.
