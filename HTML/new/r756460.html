Matcha Hot Chocolate

 4.3 (4 ratings)

Ingredients

  • 14 ½ oz coconut milk
  • 1 tbsp matcha powder
  • 2 oz white chocolate, grated
  • ground white pepper, to taste (optional)
  • mini marshmallows, to garnish

Preparation

  1. Place coconut milk, matcha (see Tip), chocolate and pepper into mixing bowl and start Kettle /195°F.
  2. Froth 10 sec/speed 8. Divide between two cups, garnish with mini marshmallows and serve hot.

Hints & Tips

  • Matcha is green tea that undergoes shading processes before harvesting, then it is ground with stone mills. It is ground so finely that it becomes 90% soluble and when drinking it the whole plant is consumed as opposed to an infusion.
