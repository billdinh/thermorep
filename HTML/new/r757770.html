Preparation

  1. Insert peeler and press down to secure. Place water and ginger into mixing bowl and start Peeler /4 min. Drain ginger through a fine mesh sieve. Remove peeler. Discard peeling water and peels (see Tip).
  2. Rinse ginger and set aside to drain. With the edge of a spoon scrape off any excess peel. Use as needed.

Hints & Tips

  • Only use the TM6® black measuring cup when using Peeler Mode. If not available, place simmering basket instead of clear measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid when using Peeler Mode.
  • Place blade cover & peeler onto mixing knife making sure the upward curved parts are on top of the upper positioned blades. Press down gently to secure it to the mixing knife.
  • When using Peeler Mode, vegetable pieces should fit through hole in mixing bowl lid. For best results, vegetable pieces should be as large as possible. Always place 21 oz water into mixing bowl and a maximum of 28 oz vegetables.
  • Avoid putting peels down the garbage disposal. Ginger peels may be used to fertilize plants, discarded in the trash receptacle or compost bin.
