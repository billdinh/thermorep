Crêpes Suzette

Ingredients

Crêpe Batter

  • 1 oz unsalted butter, cut into pieces
  • 8 ½ oz whole milk
  • 2 oz orange juice
  • ½ oz orange liqueur, e.g. Grand Marnier®
  • 2 large eggs
  • 4 ½ oz all-purpose flour
  • ½ oz sugar (optional)
  • 2 pinches salt

Orange Sauce

  • 1 orange
  • 1 lemon
  • 3 oz sugar
  • 3 oz orange juice
  • 3 oz orange liqueur
  • ⅛ tsp salt
  • 3 ½ oz unsalted butter, diced

Assembly

  • 2 oz unsalted butter, diced

Preparation

Crêpe Batter

  1. Place butter into mixing bowl and melt 2 min/160°F/speed 2.
  2. Add milk, orange juice, orange liqueur, eggs, flour, sugar and salt and mix 10 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl, cover with cling wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before using. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl and make the sauce.

Orange Sauce

  1. With a vegetable peeler, thinly peel the zest from 1 orange and 1 lemon, avoiding the white pith and set aside.
  2. Place sugar, reserved orange peel and reserved lemon peel into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add orange juice, orange liqueur and salt and cook 4 min/212°F/speed 1.
  4. Add butter and mix 20 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl until needed.

Assembly

  1. Lightly grease a non-stick frying pan (9 in.) with butter and heat on medium heat. Pour in ¼ cup of batter, twirling the pan as you pour to cover base of pan completely (crêpe will be approx. 8.5 in. and very thin). Cook for 2-3 minutes or until edges of crêpe are light golden. Turn crêpe over and cook a further 1 minute. Transfer to a plate and cover with a kitchen towel (tea towel) to keep warm. Repeat with remaining butter and batter.
  2. Fold 3 crêpes into quarters, return to frying pan and drizzle with approximately 3 tablespoons reserved Orange Sauce. Cook until heated through and sauce begins to caramelize. Repeat with remaining portions (3 crêpes per serving). Serve warm with extra sauce.
