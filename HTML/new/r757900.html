Green Smoothie Bowls (Matthew Kenney)

 5.0 (4 ratings)

Ingredients

  • 8 oz coconut water
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 tbsp freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 1 tbsp grated ginger
  • ½ tsp spirulina
  • 1 oz fresh spinach leaves
  • 1 ½ oz baby kale
  • 13 ½ oz bananas (approx. 3 med.), cut into pieces and frozen (see Tip)
  • 3 oz frozen mango chunks
  • 3 ice cubes
  • blueberries, to garnish
  • strawberries, sliced, to garnish
  • kiwi, sliced, to garnish
  • hemp seeds, to sprinkle, to garnish

Preparation

  1. Place coconut water, salt, lime juice, ginger, spirulina, spinach, kale, banana, mango and ice into mixing bowl and blend 20 sec/speed 8.
  2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and blend a further 30 sec/speed 8. Divide smoothie mixture between 3 bowls. Garnish each serving with blueberries, sliced strawberries and kiwi. Sprinkle with hemp seeds and serve.

Hints & Tips

  • Other topping options: banana, almond slivers, pumpkin seeds, berries, coconut flakes, pomegranate, granola or any of your favorite toppings.
  • Cut bananas into pieces, spread out on a tray, freeze, then use frozen bananas in recipe or transfer to a freezer bag for later use.
  • Acai Smoothie Bowl: Place 8 oz almond milk, 1 pinch salt, 2 tbsp date paste, 1 packet (3.5 oz) frozen acai, 1 banana, cut into pieces, 4 oz frozen strawberries and 2 oz frozen blueberries into mixing bowl and blend 20 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and blend a further 30 sec/speed 8. Divide between bowls and garnish with your favorite toppings.
  • Dragon Fruit Smoothie Bowl: Place 8 oz coconut water, 1 pinch salt, 1 packet (3.5 oz) frozen dragon fruit, 8 oz frozen pineapple chunks and 4 oz frozen mango chunks into mixing bowl and blend 20 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and blend a further 30 sec/speed 8. Divide between bowls and garnish with your favorite toppings.
