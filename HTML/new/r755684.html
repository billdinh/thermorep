Sautéed Prawns

 5.0 (1 rating)

Ingredients

  • 20 g cooking oil
  • 1 garlic clove
    or 20 g brown onion, cut in wedges
  • 250 g prawn, raw, medium size, peeled, deveined, patted dry with paper towels

Preparation

  1. Place oil and garlic in mixing bowl then, without measuring cup, heat 2 min 30 sec/Varoma/speed .
  2. Add prawns, making sure that they are evenly distributed in mixing bowl, place splash guard on mixing bowl lid and start TM$. Season to taste and serve immediately or use as needed.

Hints & Tips

  • If not serving prawns immediately, cool to room temperature quickly and store in the fridge for up to 2 days.
  • Season to taste and serve as a starter with lime wedges.
