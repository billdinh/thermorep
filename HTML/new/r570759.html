Asian-style Beef Stew

 3.4 (12 ratings)

Ingredients

  • 15 g peanut oil
    or sunflower oil
  • 600 g stewing steak, cut in pieces (3 cm)
  • 5 - 10 g fresh root ginger, finely chopped, to taste
  • 15 g maple syrup
  • 70 g rice wine
    or white wine
  • 100 g soy sauce
  • 80 g water
  • 10 g rice vinegar
  • ¼ tsp dried chilli flakes
  • 3 star anise (see tip)
  • 200 g red peppers (approx. 1 large or 2 small)
  • 100 g fresh shiitake mushrooms
    or mixed fresh mushrooms
  • 300 g broccoli florets
  • 1 spring onion
  • 2 garlic clove
  • 1 ½ tsp cornflour, mixed with 1½ Tbsp water
  • 10 g fresh coriander leaves, roughly chopped
  • sesame seeds, for garnishing

Preparation

  1. Place oil and 300 g beef in mixing bowl, making sure that it is evenly distributed in mixing bowl, place splash guard on mixing bowl lid then brown High Temp. .
  2. Add remaining 300 g beef, ginger, maple syrup, rice wine, soy sauce, water, vinegar and chilli flakes. Insert simmering basket and add star anise then start Slow Cook /1 h 30 min/95°C. Meanwhile, cut red peppers in 1 cm strips, mushrooms in slices and broccoli in small florets. Place vegetables in Varoma dish, making sure some holes remain uncovered for steam to circulate, then cover Varoma. Finely slice spring onion then finely chop garlic (see tip).
  3. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and discard star anise. Add prepared spring onions, prepared garlic and cornflour mixture to mixing bowl, place covered Varoma into position then steam 13 min/Varoma//speed .
  4. Set Varoma aside. Transfer beef stew to a large serving bowl, add steamed vegetables then mix with spatula. Sprinkle with coriander and sesame seeds then serve hot.

Hints & Tips

  • Placing the star anises inside the simmering basket makes it easier to remove them after cooking. They can also be added directly to the mixing bowl though.
  • Serve with cooked soba (buckwheat) noodles, rice or rice noodles. Prepare the side dishes on the hob whilst the stew is cooking in your Thermomix® in step 2.
  • Change or add vegetables according to your preference, e.g. sweet potatoes, courgette or mangetouts. Cut vegetables in small enough pieces so they are cooked through in 13 minutes.
