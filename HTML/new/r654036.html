Artisan Bread (Betty)

 4.9 (60 ratings)

Ingredients

  • 1 packet/envelope dried instant yeast
    or 2 ¼ tsp dried instant yeast
  • 58 ½ oz water, divided, plus extra to sprinkle
  • 21 oz bread flour
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp Rustic Spice Mix for Bread (see Tip) (optional)
  • neutral oil, e.g. vegetable, canola, safflower, to grease
  • 2 oz vinegar
  • 1 oz lemon juice

Preparation

  1. Place yeast and 13 oz water into mixing bowl and warm 3 min/100°F/speed 1.
  2. Add flour, salt and Rustic Spice Mix and knead Dough /3 min. Meanwhile, lightly grease Varoma dish. Transfer dough to prepared Varoma dish, secure Varoma lid and set aside.
  3. Place 35 oz water and vinegar into mixing bowl and start Pre-clean . Discard water and rinse mixing bowl.
  4. Place 10.5 oz water and lemon juice into mixing bowl, set Varoma with dough into position and proof Fermentation /2 h/130°F.
  5. Transfer dough to a floured work surface. Punch down dough to remove all air and form into a ball.
  6. Lightly grease the inside of a stoneware casserole dish with lid Rockstar® "Betty" and carefully place dough into the dish. Let stand 50 minutes. Meanwhile, 15 minutes before end of rising time preheat oven to 400°F.
  7. Use a sharp knife to make three diagonal cuts along the length of the bread and sprinkle a little water over the dough. Cover with lid and bake 30 minutes (400°F) then remove the lid and bake a further 15-20 minutes (400°F) or until golden brown.
  8. Remove from oven and allow to cool in pan 15-20 minutes. Run a silicone spatula around the edge of "Betty" to release the bread, transfer to a wire rack to cool completely before slicing.

Hints & Tips

  • Grease Rockstar® stoneware dishes prior to first use (see instructions). The stoneware forms a natural non-stick layer with use, ensuring food can be easily released.
  • Rockstar® stoneware dishes are not dishwasher safe. To preserve the natural non-stick coating, avoid using cleaning agents and dishwashing liquids. Instead, clean with hot water and a dish sponge or brush. Any lingering odors on the dishes are harmless. For stubborn food residue stuck to the stoneware, soak in warm water before cleaning. After cleaning, allow the stoneware to dry out completely in a place that is well-ventilated, both from above and from below; only store once completely dry.
  • Search Cookidoo® for Rustic Spice Mix for Bread recipe in our Artisan Breads collection. Rustic Spice Mix combines well with any robust bread. Add 1-2 tsp to flour for added flavor and color.
  • Instead of the "Betty" casserole, you can also use another casserole dish with a lid or a Roman pot (8 in. x 12 in.).
