Slow Cooked Navy Beans

 (No ratings)

Ingredients

  • 9 oz dried navy beans
    or 9 oz dried garbanzo beans
  • water, to soak
  • 32 oz broth

Preparation

  1. Place a large bowl onto mixing bowl lid. Weigh in beans, cover with water and allow to soak overnight.
  2. Rinse soaked beans under cold water, set aside and allow to drain.
  3. Insert blade cover and press down to secure. Place drained beans and broth (see Tip) into mixing bowl and start Slow Cook /2 h 30 min/205°F. Drain through Varoma dish and use as needed.

Hints & Tips

  • Place blade cover & peeler onto mixing knife making sure the upward curved parts are on top of the upper positioned blades. Press down gently to secure it to the mixing knife.
  • Do not cook dried kidney beans as a substitute in this recipe, the temperatures reached are not high enough to cook kidney beans.
  • Use these versatile beans in stews and soups, or as a side dish with some seasoning.
  • Add half of an onion, chopped, a small bunch of parsley, chopped, 1 tsp black peppercorn and 1 oz olive oil prior to cooking to season your beans.
