Limau Kasturi Asam Boi (Sour Plum Calamansi Drink)

 5.0 (4 ratings)

Ingredients

  • 90 g rock sugar
  • 3 sour plums, pitted
  • 200 g calamansi
  • 900 g cold water

Preparation

  1. Place rock sugar and sour plums in mixing bowl, grind Turbo/2 sec/2 times.
  2. Add calamansi and water, blend Turbo/2 sec/1 time. Insert simmering basket, hold it in place with spatula and strain calamansi drink into a jug. Serve cold.
