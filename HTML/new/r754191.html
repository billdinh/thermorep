Slow-cooked Aubergine and Split Pea Curry

 2.7 (3 ratings)

Ingredients

  • 150 g yellow split peas
  • 2 garlic cloves, halved
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 200 g onions, cut in wedges
  • 3 tsp ground cumin
  • 3 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • ½ tsp ground cardamom
  • 400 g tinned chopped tomatoes
  • 200 g water, plus extra for soaking
  • 300 - 350 g aubergines, sliced (2 cm), then cut in pieces (3 cm)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 5 g fresh coriander leaves, roughly chopped

Preparation

  1. Please ensure you have a blade cover before starting this recipe (visit our website for details).
  2. Place a large bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in split peas. Cover with plenty of water and leave overnight to soak then drain and set aside.
  3. Place garlic and oil in mixing bowl then sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  4. Add onions, place splash guard on mixing bow lid then sauté High Temp. . Insert measuring cup then chop 4 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add cumin, coriander, garam masala, turmeric and cardamom then cook 3 min/120°C//speed 1. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  6. Carefully insert blade cover. Place reserved drained split peas, tomatoes and water in mixing bowl then cook 20 min/100°C//speed 1.
  7. Add aubergines, reserved sautéed onions, salt and pepper then start Slow Cook /6 h/98°C.
  8. Add coriander, stir in with spatula and serve with rice or naans.
