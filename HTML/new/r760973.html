Avocado Tikka (Matthew Kenney) Metric

Ingredients

  • 225 g cashew nuts
  • water, to soak

Curry Leaf Oil

  • 45 g fresh curry leaves, destemmed
  • 60 g grapeseed oil
  • 1 pinch sea salt

Curry Leaf Yogurt

  • 140 g filtered water
  • 30 g apple cider vinegar
  • 30 g agave syrup
  • 1 tsp sea salt

Tikka Sauce

  • 60 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tsp salt

Potato Breads

  • 850 g water
  • 425 g potatoes, peeled and cubed (5 cm)
  • 30 g fresh dill (approx. 1 bunch)
  • fresh curry leaves, destemmed (approx. 1 handful)
  • 425 g all-purpose flour
  • 450 g extra virgin olive oil, to fry (2 tbsp per batch)
  • 4 avocados, peeled, sliced in wedges
  • microgreens, to garnish
  • edible flowers, to garnish

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid, weigh in cashews, cover with water and soak for at least 4 hours to overnight. Then, drain and rinse soaked nuts through a fine-mesh sieve and discard soaking water.

Curry Leaf Oil

  1. Place curry leaves, oil and salt into mixing bowl and heat 6 min/85°C/speed 1. Strain through a coffee filter into a bowl and set aside. Discard solids.

Curry Leaf Yogurt

  1. Place reserved drained cashews, 140 g filtered water, cider vinegar, agave, 60 g reserved Curry Leaf Oil and salt and start Blend /40 sec. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and start again Blend /40 sec until very smooth. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.

Tikka Sauce

  1. Place olive oil and curry powder into mixing bowl and cook 7 min/100°C/speed 1.
  2. Add cider vinegar and salt and mix 1 min/speed 3. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Rinse mixing bowl.

Potato Breads

  1. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh in potatoes. Top with dill and curry leaves, secure Varoma lid and steam 25 min/Varoma/speed 3. Remove Varoma. Empty mixing bowl. Discard dill and curry leaves.
  2. Place steamed potatoes into mixing bowl and mix 6 sec/speed 7.
  3. Add flour and with aid of spatula, mix 30 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a work surface (dough will be crumbly) and form into 16 balls (approx. 60 g ea.). Use a rolling pin to roll balls into circles (20 cm ea.).
  4. Heat 2 tbsp olive oil in a large frying pan over med-high heat. Once pan is hot, add 1 potato bread and fry until golden (2-3 min. per side), drain on paper towels and repeat with remaining breads.
  5. Spoon approximately 60 g Curry Leaf Yogurt into the centre of each plate and spread into a large circle. Top with 2 fried Potato Breads and sliced avocado, drizzle with reserved Tikka Sauce, garnish with microgreens and edible flowers and serve.

Hints & Tips

  • Not all flowers are edible, some are poisonous. Flowers should be purchased from a reliable source.
  • Those that suffer from pollen allergies should not consume edible flowers.
