Cranberry and rosemary chutney

 4.8 (43 ratings)

Ingredients

  • 1 brown onion (approx. 150 g), cut into halves
  • 220 g raw sugar
  • 125 g red wine vinegar
  • 185 g water
  • 4 sprigs fresh rosemary (approx. 10 cm each), leaves only
  • 1 cinnamon quill
  • 10 whole black peppercorns
  • 1 fresh bay leaf or dried bay leaf
  • ¼ tsp ground nutmeg, plus extra to taste
  • 340 g dried cranberries
  • 1 - 2 pinches sea salt, to taste
  • 1 - 2 pinches ground black pepper, to taste

Preparation

  1. Place onion into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add all remaining ingredients and cook 12 min/90°C/speed 1. Scrape down sides and bottom of mixing bowl with spatula, then cook 5 min/100°C/speed 1. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula again.
  3. Cook 15 min/60°C/speed 1, or until chutney thickens and cranberries are soft, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  4. Season to taste with extra salt, pepper and nutmeg if required, then carefully remove cinnamon quill and bay leaf. Transfer into a jug and serve immediately or transfer into a sealable storage jar to store until use.
