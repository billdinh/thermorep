Tangzhong Milk Bread

 4.8 (142 ratings)

Ingredients

  • 13 oz bread flour, divided
  • 8 oz milk, icy cold, divided (see Tip)
  • 1 egg yolk, from a large egg
  • 1 oz sugar
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 tsp powdered milk
  • 1 tsp dried instant yeast
  • 1 oz unsalted butter, room temperature
  • ice cold water, to spray

Preparation

  1. Place 1 oz bread flour and 3.5 oz milk into mixing bowl and cook 3 min/175°F/speed 3. Let stand for 10-15 minutes.
  2. Add 4.5 oz milk, egg yolk, sugar, salt, powdered milk, 12 oz bread flour and yeast and knead Dough /4 min.
  3. Add butter and knead Dough /1 min. Transfer dough to a silicone mat. Shape into a ball, flatten with a rolling pin and fold to wrap dough loosely into an envelope shape. Let proof in a warm place until doubled in size (approx. 45 min.).
  4. Punch down dough to deflate and divide into 3 equal portions. Form dough into balls and let rest for 10 minutes. Using a rolling pin, flatten each ball into long rectangle, then roll into a log, starting on the shorter edge. Arrange all three dough logs into one loaf pan (9 in. x 5 in. x 4 in.) and let proof until dough fills up 80% of loaf pan (approx. 40 min.). During last 15 minutes, preheat oven to 350°F.
  5. Spray the dough with ice cold water and bake 25 minutes (350°F) until golden brown. Remove milk bread from pan and allow to cool on a wire rack before slicing and serving.

Hints & Tips

  • Before starting the recipe, place milk in the freezer for 20 minutes.
  • Use the Pre-clean mode for easy cleaning. Place 33 oz water into mixing bowl. Add a small drop of dishwashing liquid or 0.5 oz vinegar and start Pre-clean mode.
