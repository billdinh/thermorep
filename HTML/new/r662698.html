Mark Southon's Lamb ragù with watercress and mint pesto (TM6)

 4.8 (85 ratings)

Ingredients

Watercress and mint pesto

  • 120 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 60 g Parmesan cheese, cut into pieces (3 cm), plus extra grated, to serve
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 20 g freshly squeezed lemon juice (approx. ½ lemon)
  • 50 g raw unsalted cashews
  • 100 g watercress, trimmed, leaves and soft stalks only
  • 30 g fresh mint, leaves only
  • sea salt, to taste
  • ground black pepper, to taste

Lamb ragù

  • 20 g olive oil
  • 5 garlic cloves
  • 200 g red onions, cut into wedges
  • 60 g pancetta, cut into pieces
  • 3 sprigs fresh rosemary, leaves only
  • 100 g red wine
  • 400 g canned crushed tomatoes
  • sea salt, to taste
  • ground black pepper, to taste
  • 800 g boneless lamb shoulder, cut into pieces (approx. 2-3 cm)
  • 40 g salted butter
  • cooked pasta of choice, to serve (see Tips)

Preparation

Watercress and mint pesto

  1. Place a jug onto mixing bowl lid, then weigh oil into it. Set jug aside.
  2. Place Parmesan and garlic into mixing bowl, then grate 10 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add all remaining pesto ingredients and chop 4 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Mix until emulsified/speed 3, slowly pouring reserved oil onto mixing bowl lid, letting it trickle into mixing bowl. Pour pesto into a bowl and place in the fridge until ready to serve. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Lamb ragù

  1. Place oil, garlic, onion, pancetta and 2 sprigs of rosemary into mixing bowl, then sauté High Temp. . Next Insert measuring cup and chop 8 sec/speed 4.
  2. Add wine and cook 3 min/120°C/speed 2, without measuring cup. Pour into a bowl.
  3. Insert blade cover. Place tomato, salt, pepper and reserved onion mixture into mixing bowl, then cook 5 min/100°C//speed 1, without measuring cup.
  4. Add lamb and the remaining rosemary leaves, stir with spatula, then start Slow Cook /4 h/98°C or until lamb is tender, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  5. Add butter, then re-insert measuring cup and cook 2 min/100°C//speed 1. Serve ragù with pasta, Watercress and mint pesto and grated Parmesan cheese.
