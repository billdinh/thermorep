Sichuan Chilli Oil

Ingredients

  • 50 g dried bird’s eye chillies
  • 50 g dried chillies
  • 250 g canola oil
  • 4 star anise
  • 3 cinnamon sticks
  • 3 Chinese cardamom pods (cao guo)
  • 4 dried bay leaves
  • 10 g toasted white sesame seeds

Preparation

  1. Place dried bird’s eye chillies and dried chillies in clean and dry mixing bowl, pulverise Turbo/2 sec/4 times. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place canola oil, star anise, cinnamon sticks, cao guo and bay leaves in mixing bowl, cook 20 min/120°C/speed 0.5. Carefully discard spices.
  3. Add reserved chillies, cook 15 min/120°C/speed .
  4. Add sesame seeds, cook 5 min/120°C/speed . Serve as condiment or dipping sauce or pour into a jar and store in refrigerator for up to 4 weeks.

Hints & Tips

  • Replace dried bird’s eye chillies and dried chillies with store-bought chilli powder, adjust spiciness according to your preference.
  • Add some sesame oil and chopped coriander to the chilli oil before serving.
