Fudge Brownies

 4.7 (170 ratings)

Ingredients

  • 8 oz unsalted butter, diced
  • 9 oz sugar
  • 7 oz brown sugar
  • 1 oz vegetable oil
  • 4 large eggs
  • ½ oz vanilla extract
  • 3 ½ oz all-purpose flour
  • 3 ½ oz unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 8 oz semi-sweet chocolate chips

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Lightly grease a baking pan (9 in. x 13 in.) and line with parchment, letting edges hang over sides of pan (excess parchment will serve as handles to lift out the brownie). Set aside.
  2. Place butter into mixing bowl and heat 3 min/120°F/speed 1.
  3. Add sugar, brown sugar and oil and mix 20 sec/speed 5.
  4. Add eggs and vanilla and mix 30 sec/speed 4.
  5. Add flour, cocoa powder, and salt and mix 10 sec/speed 4.
  6. Add chocolate chips and gently fold in with spatula. Pour batter into prepared pan and smooth the top.
  7. Bake 20-25 minutes (350°F) or until just set. Let cool 10 minutes in pan before using parchment to lift out the brownies. Cool to room temperature before slicing into 16 pieces.

Hints & Tips

  • Bake brownies until just set, they continue to cook for a little while when removed from the oven.
  • Add nuts like chopped pecans or walnuts when adding chocolate chips.
