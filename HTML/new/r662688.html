Gail Kordic's Caramel oranges with sabayon (TM6)

Ingredients

Oranges in caramel syrup

  • 4 blood oranges, room temperature (see Tips)
  • 120 g freshly squeezed blood orange juice (approx. 2 oranges)
  • 250 g white sugar
  • 1070 g water
  • 20 g glucose syrup
  • 50 g white vinegar

Sabayon

  • 80 g white sugar
  • 6 egg yolks (see Tips)
  • 60 g dry white wine

Preparation

Oranges in caramel syrup

  1. Line a baking tray (20 x 30 cm) and set aside.
  2. To guarantee the success of recipes using Sugar mode, the ingredients used must match exactly the quantity and description given. The recipe should also be cooked immediately after placing ingredients in the mixing bowl.
  3. Zest half of an orange, no white pith and set zest aside for the Sabayon, then peel 4 oranges and place the peeled oranges into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and set aside.
  4. Place a jug onto mixing bowl lid and weigh orange juice into it, then set aside.
  5. Place sugar, 70 g of the water and glucose syrup into mixing bowl, then start Sugar Stages , without measuring cup.
  6. Immediately pour approximately ¼ of the caramel onto the prepared baking tray in a thin layer. Quickly scrape off any mixture left on rim of mixing bowl using spatula so mixing bowl lid closes securely and immediately re-insert bowl into base.
  7. Insert measuring cup, then heat 5 min/100°C/speed 1, slowly pouring reserved orange juice onto mixing bowl lid, letting it trickle into mixing bowl. Pour hot caramel syrup over the reserved peeled oranges and cover to keep warm.
  8. Place vinegar and remaining 1000 g water into mixing bowl, then start Pre-clean /Fat or caramel. Rinse and thoroughly dry mixing bowl.
  9. Once the caramel on the baking tray has set, gently break it into shards and set aside.

Sabayon

  1. Place sugar and reserved orange zest into mixing bowl, then mill 20 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Add egg yolks, wine and 3 tablespoons of caramel syrup from the reserved oranges, then cook 8-10 min/70°C/speed 3, until egg yolks are cooked and mixture has thickened. Place a generous portion of sabayon in a serving bowl, top with an orange and finish with shards of caramel before serving immediately.
