Hot Cocoa Bundt Cake

Ingredients

Bundt Cake

  • 6 oz unsalted butter, diced, room temperature, plus extra to grease
  • 6 oz all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ oz cocoa powder (70% cocoa)
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 ½ oz chocolate chips
  • 6 oz sugar
  • 4 oz plain Greek yogurt
  • 4 oz whole milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs

Marshmallow Glaze

  • 2 oz unsalted butter, cut into pieces
  • 2 oz mini marshmallows, plus extra to garnish
  • 3 oz confectioners sugar
  • 2 tsp whole milk

Chocolate Glaze

  • 3 oz heavy whipping cream
  • 3 oz chocolate chips

Preparation

Bundt Cake

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease a Thermomix® bundt pan (8 in.) and set aside.
  2. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid, weigh in flour, cocoa powder, baking soda and salt. Whisk to combine and set aside.
  3. Place chocolate chips into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and melt 3 min/110°F/speed 2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add butter and sugar and mix 2 min/speed 4.5.
  5. Add yogurt, milk and vanilla and without measuring cup, mix 30 sec/speed 4.5 while adding eggs one at a time through hole in mixing bowl lid. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  6. Add reserved flour mixture and with aid of spatula, mix 15 sec/speed 6. Transfer batter to prepared pan and smooth top with spatula. Bake 25-30 minutes (350°F) or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Let cool 20 minutes in pan before inverting onto a cooling rack to cool completely.

Marshmallow Glaze

  1. Place butter and marshmallows into mixing bowl, insert measuring cup and melt 3 min/140°F/speed 3. Meanwhile, transfer cooled bundt to a cake plate and set aside.
  2. Add confectioners sugar and milk and mix 20 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix a further 10 sec/speed 5. Drizzle Marshmallow Glaze over cake and set aside.

Chocolate Glaze

  1. Place heavy whipping cream into mixing bowl and heat 3 min/195°F/speed 2.
  2. Add chocolate chips and mix 1 min/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and allow to cool and thicken. Drizzle cooled Chocolate Glaze over Marshmallow Glaze allowing some to drip over the sides. Decorate with marshmallows and serve.

Hints & Tips

  • Bake time will depend on size of pan used. If using a different size bundt pan, adjust baking time accordingly.
  • Replace Thermomix® bundt pan with 6 mini bundt pans (4 oz each).
