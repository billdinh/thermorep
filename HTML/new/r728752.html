Pulled pork burgers

 4.6 (8 ratings)
Difficulty

This is how much time you need to prepare this meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 800 g boneless pork shoulder (5-6 large pieces), trimmed
  • 100 g onion, cut into halves and sliced
  • 1 sprig fresh rosemary, leaves only
  • 225 g water
  • 170 g apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tsp Vegetable stock paste (see Tips)
  • ½ tsp sea salt, plus extra to serve
  • 500 g barbecue sauce, plus extra to serve (see Tips)
  • sea salt, to taste
  • ground black pepper, to taste
  • green lettuce leaves, to serve
  • 6 hamburger buns, to serve
  • red onion, cut into halves and sliced

Preparation

  1. Place garlic into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7.
  2. Insert blade cover. Add pork, onion, rosemary, water, cider vinegar, stock paste, salt and 340 g of the barbecue sauce, then start Slow Cook /4 h 30 min/98°C.
  3. Place Varoma dish over a large bowl and carefully strain pork and onions into it, reserving 60 g of the cooking liquid. Discard remaining liquid.
  4. Carefully remove blade cover and place strained pork and onions back into mixing bowl. Add 60 g reserved cooking liquid and the remaining barbecue sauce, then shred 5-10 sec//speed 3. Season with salt and pepper. Top burger buns with lettuce, pulled pork and red onion slices, then serve with additional barbecue sauce.
