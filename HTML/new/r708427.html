Miso and Honey-roasted Aubergine

 2.5 (2 ratings)

Ingredients

  • 2 aubergines (approx. 550 g)
  • 45 g white miso paste
  • 35 g honey
  • 5 g sesame oil
  • 540 g water
  • spring onions, thinly sliced, for garnishing
  • toasted sesame seeds, for garnishing

Preparation

  1. Cut each aubergine in half lengthways, through the stalk, keeping the stalk intact (see tip). Using a small sharp knife, score the flat side of the aubergine halves about 1 cm deep all the way down the length of the aubergine half at an angle, then repeat across the other way to form a diamond pattern. Repeat with all 4 halves.
  2. Place miso paste, honey, oil and 40 g water in mixing bowl then heat 2 min/40°C/speed 2 until marinade is smooth and combined. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place 2 aubergine halves in Varoma dish and 2 halves in Varoma tray, cut side up, leaving some holes uncovered so steam can circulate. Brush aubergines all over with reserved marinade (use approx. ½ of the marinade at this stage) and set aside remaining marinade.
  4. Place 500 g water in mixing bowl. Place covered Varoma into position and steam 15 min/Varoma/speed 1. Meanwhile, preheat oven to 200°C and line a baking tray with baking paper.
  5. Transfer steamed aubergines to prepared baking tray using tongs and brush all over with remaining marinade. Roast for 20 minutes (200°C) or until deeply brown, soft and sticky.
  6. Serve as a side to other Asian-inspired meat or vegetarian dishes. Delicious with thinly sliced spring onions and toasted sesame seeds scattered over the top.
