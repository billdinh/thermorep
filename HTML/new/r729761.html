Cherry Blossom Steamed Dumplings

4.0 (1 rating)

Ingredients

Prawn filling

  • 180 g medium-sized prawns, deshelled and patted dry with kitchen towel
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¾ tsp sugar
  • ¾ tsp white pepper powder
  • 1 ½ tsp potato starch
  • 1 egg white
  • 2 tsp toasted sesame oil
  • 1 tsp Chinese rice wine (Shaoxing Hua Tiao)

Dumpling skin

  • 80 g wheat starch
  • 80 g potato starch
  • 10 g purple sweet potato powder
  • 670 g water
  • 1 tsp vegetable oil, for greasing

Preparation

Prawn filling

  1. Place prawns, salt, sugar, white pepper powder, potato starch, egg white, sesame oil and rice wine in mixing bowl, mix 3 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and refrigerate for 20 minutes. Rinse mixing bowl.

Dumpling skin

  1. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid, weigh in wheat starch, 40 g potato starch and purple sweet potato powder. Place 170 g water in mixing bowl, boil 2 min/100°C/speed 2.
  2. Slowly pour hot water into bowl with mixed flour and immediately stir until combined. Then, add remaining 40 g starch into it. Transfer dough to a pastry mat. Using hands, knead dough into a ball shape.
  3. Roll dough into an oblong shape and cut in 16 equal portions, cover with cling film to prevent from drying out. Roll each portion into a round shape. Grease rolling pin with some oil, roll out each dough into a round disc (Ø 10 cm).
  4. Place a portion of reserved marinated prawn filling (approx. 10 g) in center of dumpling skin. Pull edge of skin upward and fold into pentagon shape. Using thumb, gently pleat some folds on each side of pentagon and connect with another side of pentagon by sealing edges to form a flower shape. Repeat this step for remaining doughs.
  5. Distribute prawn dumplings on Varoma dish and Varoma tray. Close with Varoma lid.
  6. Place 500 g water in mixing bowl, boil 5 min/Varoma/speed 1. Set Varoma into position, steam 5 min/Varoma/speed 2. Serve hot.
