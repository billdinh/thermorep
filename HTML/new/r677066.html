Pasta e fagioli

 4.7 (11 ratings)

Ingredients

  • 150 g dried borlotti beans (see Tips)
  • 1950 water, plus extra to soak
  • 100 g Parmesan cheese, cut into pieces (3 cm), reserve the rind (optional - see Tips)
  • 1 onion (150 g), cut into pieces
  • 1 tomato, cut into pieces
  • 1 celery stalk, cut into pieces
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 fresh long red chilli, deseeded and cut into halves
  • 200 g speck, cut into cubes (1 cm)
  • 20 g extra virgin olive oil, plus extra to drizzle
  • 1 tbsp Vegetable stock paste or Chicken stock powder (see Tips)
  • 200 g tomato passata
  • sea salt, to taste
  • 200 g ditalini pasta
  • 1 - 2 stalks kale, roughly chopped
  • dried chilli flakes, to serve

Preparation

  1. Start this recipe the night before to allow beans to soak. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh beans into it. Cover with water and set aside to soak overnight. Drain and rinse beans.
  2. Insert blade cover (optional – TM5 and TM6 only). Place 1000 g water and the drained, soaked beans into mixing bowl, then cook 60 min/90°C//speed  or until beans are soft. Drain beans using the Varoma dish. Carefully remove blade cover. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Place Parmesan into mixing bowl, excluding the rind, and grate 10 sec/speed 8. Place in a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Place onion, tomato and celery into mixing bowl, then chop 3 sec/speed 5.
  5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and cook 12 min/120°C/speed  [TM31: 12 min/Varoma/speed ]. Place in a bowl and set aside.
  6. Place garlic and chilli into mixing bowl, then chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and repeat chopping if needed.
  7. Add speck and oil, then cook 5 min/120°C/speed  [TM31: 5 min/Varoma/speed ].
  8. Add reserved drained beans and cooked onion mixture, then cook 2 min/100°C//speed 1.
  9. Add remaining 950 g water, stock paste or powder, passata and Parmesan rind (optional), then cook 15 min/100°C//speed 1.
  10. Add pasta, season with salt to taste, then cook for a further 12 min/100°C//speed 1 or until cooked. Discard Parmesan rind.
  11. Transfer soup into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl, then stir through kale and ½ of the reserved Parmesan cheese. Stand for 5 minutes until kale is wilted. Divide soup between serving bowls. Sprinkle with remaining Parmesan and chilli flakes, then drizzle with a little extra virgin olive oil.

Hints & Tips

  • Adding the Parmesan rind is a traditional Italian way to add intensity of flavour to the dish.
  • To make your own Vegetable stock paste or Chicken stock powder to use in this recipe, search Cookidoo® for the recipes.
  • If you’d rather not use dried beans, substitute the beans in this recipe with 2 x 400 g tinned borlotti beans, rinsed and drained.
