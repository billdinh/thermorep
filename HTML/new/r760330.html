Avocado Tikka (Matthew Kenney)

 1.0 (1 rating)

Ingredients

  • 225 g cashews
  • water, to soak

Curry Leaf Oil

  • 45 g fresh curry leaf, destemmed
  • 60 g grapeseed oil
  • 1 pinch sea salt

Curry Leaf Yogurt

  • 140 g filtered water
  • 30 g apple cider vinegar
  • 30 g agave syrup
  • 1 tsp sea salt

Tikka Sauce

  • 60 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tsp salt

Potato Breads

  • 850 g water
  • 425 g potato, peeled and cut into cubes (5 cm)
  • 30 g fresh dill (approx. 1 bunch)
  • fresh curry leaf, destemmed (approx. 1 handful)
  • 425 g plain flour
  • 450 g extra virgin olive oil, to fry (2 tbsp per batch)
  • 4 avocados, peeled, cut into wedges
  • micro herb, to garnish
  • edible flowers, to garnish

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid, weigh in cashews, cover with water and soak for at least 4 hours to overnight. Then, drain and rinse soaked nuts through a fine-mesh sieve and discard soaking water.

Curry Leaf Oil

  1. Place curry leaves, oil and salt into mixing bowl, then heat 6 min/85°C/speed 1. Strain through a coffee filter into a bowl and set aside. Discard solids.

Curry Leaf Yogurt

  1. Place reserved drained cashews, 140 g filtered water, apple cider vinegar, agave, 60 g reserved Curry Leaf Oil and salt, then start Blend /40 sec. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and start Blend /40 sec, until very smooth. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.

Tikka Sauce

  1. Place olive oil and curry powder into mixing bowl, then cook 7 min/100°C/speed 1.
  2. Add apple cider vinegar and salt, then mix 1 min/speed 3. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Rinse mixing bowl.

Potato Breads

  1. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh in potatoes. Top with dill and curry leaves, secure Varoma lid, then steam 25 min/Varoma/speed 3. Remove Varoma. Empty mixing bowl. Discard dill and curry leaves.
  2. Place steamed potatoes into mixing bowl and mix 6 sec/speed 7.
  3. Add flour and mix 30 sec/speed 7, with aid of spatula. Transfer onto a work surface (dough will be crumbly) and form into 16 balls (approx. 60 g each). Use a rolling pin to roll balls into circles (20 cm each).
  4. Heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a large frying pan over medium-high heat. Once pan is hot, add 1 potato bread and fry until golden (2-3 minute per side), drain on paper towel and repeat with remaining breads.
  5. Spoon approximately 60 g Curry leaf yogurt into the centre of each plate and spread into a large circle. Top with 2 fried Potato Breads and sliced avocado, drizzle with reserved Tikka Sauce, garnish with microgreens and edible flowers and serve.

Hints & Tips

  • Not all flowers are edible, some are poisonous. Flowers should be purchased from a reliable source.
  • Those that suffer from pollen allergies should not consume edible flowers.
