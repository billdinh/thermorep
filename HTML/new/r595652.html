Golden Oatmeal

 4.5 (44 ratings)

Ingredients

  • 13 ½ oz canned coconut milk
  • 1 oz honey
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • ½ tsp ground ginger
  • ¼ tsp salt, to taste
  • 3 oz old fashioned rolled oats
  • 2 tsp ground flaxseed
  • fresh raspberries, to garnish
  • fresh blueberries, to garnish

Preparation

  1. Place coconut milk, honey, turmeric, cinnamon, ginger and salt into mixing bowl and heat 5 min/212°F/speed 1.
  2. Add oats and flaxseed and cook 5 min/200°F//speed 1. Garnish with fresh berries and serve immediately.
