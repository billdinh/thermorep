Panettone

 4.0 (2 ratings)

Ingredients

  • 1 orange, zest only, no white pith
  • 30 g honey
  • 30 g rum
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract
  • 250 g milk
  • 80 g raisins
  • 80 g mixed peel
  • 1 tbsp dried instant yeast
  • 470 g baker's flour
  • 120 g unsalted butter, cut into pieces
  • 100 g sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp bread improver (optional - see Tips)
  • extra virgin olive oil, to grease
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • 1 tbsp flaked almonds, to sprinkle

Preparation

  1. Place orange zest, honey, rum, vanilla and milk into mixing bowl, then heat 2 min/100°C/speed 1.
  2. Add raisins and peel, then transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place yeast, 120 g of the flour and 120 g of the fruit soaking liquid into mixing bowl, then mix 30 sec/speed 1. Set aside in mixing bowl for 1 hour.
  4. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh butter into it. Set aside.
  5. Add sugar, eggs, 250 g of the flour, bread improver (optional) and 150 g of the fruit soaking liquid to mixing bowl, then knead Dough /1 min.
  6. Knead Dough /5 min, adding reserved butter 1 piece at a time through hole in mixing bowl lid. Place dough in a greased bowl and cover with plastic wrap, then place in the fridge overnight.
  7. Remove dough from the fridge and set aside in a warm place to prove for 2 hours or until doubled in size. Place dough, egg yolks, salt and remaining 100 g of flour into mixing bowl, then knead Dough /3 min, adding remaining reserved soaked fruit and liquid through hole in mixing bowl lid.
  8. Line the base of a round springform cake tin (20 cm) with baking paper. Line sides of tin with a baking paper collar 15 cm high.
  9. Place dough in prepared tin. Cover with plastic wrap and set aside in a warm place to prove until risen, approx. 4 cm above edge of tin (approx. 4-6 hours). Sprinkle with flaked almonds.
  10. Preheat oven to 160°C. Bake for 1 hour (160°C) or until a wooden skewer inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean. Set aside to cool in tin for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack to cool completely. Cut into slices to serve.

Hints & Tips

  • Bread improver, though not traditional, will give your home made Panettone extra height, improve the texture and help to keep the Panettone fresh for longer.
  • Panettone will keep covered for 2-3 days. Use leftover Panettone to make your bread and butter pudding. See Cookidoo® for recipe inspiration.
  • For something different slice your panettone into 3 -4 even layers and sandwich together with cream, fresh fruit or custard
