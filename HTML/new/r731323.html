Asparagus and potatoes with hollandaise

 5.0 (2 ratings)

Ingredients

Asparagus and potatoes

  • 600 g water
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • 1 pinch sugar
  • 5 g unsalted butter
  • 700 - 750 g new baby potatoes (see Tips)
  • 1000 - 1500 g white asparagus, trimmed (see Tips)

Hollandaise sauce

  • 20 - 40 g water
  • 10 - 20 g freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 175 - 350 g unsalted butter
  • 4 - 8 egg yolks
  • ½ tsp sea salt
  • 1 - 2 pinches ground black pepper

Preparation

Asparagus and potatoes

  1. Place water, salt, sugar and butter into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket and weigh potatoes into it. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh asparagus into it, secure Varoma lid and steam 30 min/Varoma/speed 1 or until asparagus and potatoes are tender. Remove Varoma and set aside. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula, place asparagus and potatoes into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and cover to keep warm. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Hollandaise sauce

  1. To obtain 250 g hollandaise sauce, use the smallest quantity of ingredients indicated, and to obtain 500 g, use the larger quantity indicated.
  2. Place all sauce ingredients into mixing bowl, then start Thicken /80°C. Season to taste and serve with asparagus and potatoes.

Hints & Tips

  • If your baby potatoes are large, cut them in half before steaming.
  • If the asparagus spears are very thick, turn them over 10 minutes before the end of cooking time for even cooking.
  • Instead of hollandaise sauce, you can serve the asparagus and potatoes with melted butter. Simply place a small bowl of butter into the Varoma dish with the asparagus and proceed as per recipe.
  • You can replace white asparagus with green, if preferred.
