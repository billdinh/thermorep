Sam Wood's Baked salmon with broccoli pesto

 4.6 (5 ratings)

Ingredients

Yoghurt tartare sauce

  • 200 g Greek-style natural yoghurt
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 30 g baby pickled capers, rinsed and drained

Salmon and broccoli pesto

  • 500 g water
  • 200 g broccoli florets
  • 30 g Parmesan cheese, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 spring onion/shallot, trimmed and cut into pieces
  • 30 g olive oil, plus extra to drizzle
  • 20 g freshly squeezed lemon juice (approx. ½ lemon)
  • 4 fresh skinless, boneless salmon fillets
  • 2 bunches asparagus, trimmed, cut into halves widthways if spears are thick
  • sea salt, to season
  • ground black pepper, to season
  • 60 g rocket
  • 180 - 200 g cherry tomatoes, cut into halves or quarters
  • lemon wedges, to serve

Preparation

Yoghurt tartare sauce

  1. Place yoghurt, garlic and capers into mixing bowl, then chop 2 sec/speed 6. Place in a small serving bowl. Rinse mixing bowl.

Salmon and broccoli pesto

  1. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh broccoli florets into it. Secure Varoma lid, then steam 8 min/Varoma/speed 2. Remove Varoma and set aside to cool slightly. Rinse and dry mixing bowl.
  2. Place Parmesan and garlic into mixing bowl, then chop 10 sec/speed 8.
  3. Add spring onion/shallot, oil, lemon juice and cooled broccoli, then chop 5 sec/speed 6.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and chop 5 sec/speed 6 or until smooth and creamy.
  5. Preheat oven to 190°C. Line a baking tray (40 x 35 cm) and place salmon fillets onto it. Divide and spread broccoli pesto over the top of each fillet, then arrange asparagus on the tray with the salmon. Season with salt and pepper and drizzle with oil. Bake for 15 minutes (190°C) or until salmon is cooked to your liking.
  6. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh rocket and tomatoes into it. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Served baked salmon and asparagus with the salad, reserved tartare sauce and lemon wedges.

Hints & Tips

  • To steam salmon and asparagus, in step 6 place 500 g water into mixing bowl. Place salmon with pesto in Varoma dish and asparagus on Varoma tray. Place into position and secure Varoma lid, then steam 15 min/Varoma/speed 2 until salmon is cooked to your liking and asparagus are just tender. Alternatively, you can steam the salmon over a soup recipe of your choosing. Search Cookidoo for your favourite soup recipes.
