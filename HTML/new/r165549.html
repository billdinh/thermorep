Duck, apricot and pine nut pastilla

 5.0 (4 ratings)

Ingredients

  • 100 g onions, cut in halves
  • olive oil, for greasing
  • 1 ½ - 2 tsp ground cumin, to taste
  • ground cinnamon, plus extra for garnish
  • 1 ½ - 2 tsp ground coriander, to taste
  • 2 tsp fennel seeds
  • 1 pinch saffron
  • 600 g duck breast, skinless, boneless, cut in pieces (3 cm)
  • 150 g dried apricot, pitted, cut in quarters
  • 80 g water
  • 1 tsp chicken stock paste, homemade
    or 1 chicken stock cube (for 0.5 l)
  • toasted pine nuts, plus extra for garnish
  • 2 lemons, preferably organic, zest and juice
  • 4 sheets filo pastry (approx. 48 cm x 25 cm)
  • 1 tsp icing sugar

Preparation

  1. Place onions in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl using spatula.
  2. Add olive oil, ground cumin, ground cinnamon, ground coriander, fennel seeds and saffron and sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  3. Add duck breast and 70 g dried apricots and sauté 4 min/120°C//speed .
  4. Add water, chicken stock paste, 20 g toasted pine nuts, lemon zest and juice, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup on mixing bowl lid to help prevent splashing and reduce 5 min/100°C//speed 1. Meanwhile preheat oven to 220°C.
  5. Then chop 5 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl using spatula.
  6. Add 80 g dried apricots and 30 g toasted pine nuts and mix using spatula.
  7. Grease a springform tin (Ø 22-24 cm) with olive oil. Brush a sheet of filo pastry with olive oil, then line the springform tin with it, allowing the pastry to fall over the edge of the tin. Repeat with a second sheet of filo pastry, positioning the corners so that a star shape is formed. Pour the duck filling into it and spread evenly.
  8. Fold edges of filo pastry over to enclose the filling. Cover with the remaining 2 sheets of filo pastry, brushing each with olive oil, and crumpling them attractively. Bake pastilla 20-30 minutes (220°C) or until crisp and golden brown. Serve pastilla garnished with pine nuts and dust with a pinch of cinnamon and icing sugar.
