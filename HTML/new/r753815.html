Potatoes Using Blade Cover & Peeler (TM5)

 3.7 (3 ratings)

Ingredients

Peeling

  • 24 oz baby red potatoes, halved (approx. 2 in. wide) (see Tip)
    or 24 oz baby gold potatoes, halved (approx. 2 in. wide) (see Tip)
  • 21 oz water
  • 1 tsp oil (see Tip) (optional)

Cooking Potatoes

  • ½ tsp salt, to taste
  • 30 oz water (to cover potatoes, but not higher than max fill level 2.2 l)

Preparation

Peeling

  1. Insert peeler and press down to secure. Place potatoes, water and oil into mixing bowl, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and increasing speed gradually, start peeling 4 min/speed 1-4. Rinse and drain potatoes through Varoma dish and set aside. Remove peeler. Discard peeling water and peels (see Tip). Rinse mixing bowl.

Cooking Potatoes

  1. Insert blade cover and press down to secure. Place reserved peeled potatoes and salt into mixing bowl. Add water to cover potatoes (not to exceed max fill level 2.2 l), insert measuring cup and cook 30-40 min/205°F//speed  or until cooked to your liking (see Tip). Using the Varoma dish, drain cooked potatoes and use as desired.

Hints & Tips

  • Place blade cover & peeler onto mixing knife making sure the upward curved parts are on top of the upper positioned blades. Press down gently to secure it to the mixing knife.
  • Only use the TM6® black measuring cup when peeling. If not available, place simmering basket instead of clear measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid when peeling.
  • Add 1 tsp oil to avoid sudsing of potato peels when peeling.
  • When peeling, vegetable pieces should fit through hole in mixing bowl lid. For best results, vegetable pieces should be as large as possible. Always place 21 oz water into mixing bowl and a maximum of 28 oz vegetables.
  • The amount of water for cooking potatoes should be sufficient to cover the weight of potatoes used, and at the same time it should not exceed the maximum capacity of the mixing bowl (2.2 l).
  • The cooking time should be adapted to the potato variety and age of potato. If necessary, extend or reduce cooking time as needed. This will also depend on how the potatoes are being served.
  • Sweet potatoes: replace potato with sweet potato (approx. 21-24 oz), peeled and cut into pieces 1-2 inches and cook for 18-24 min/205°F//speed , until cooked to your preference.
