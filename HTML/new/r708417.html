Crispy Smashed Jersey Royals with Salsa Verde

 5.0 (10 ratings)

Ingredients

Potatoes

  • 500 g water
  • 750 g Jersey Royals
  • 4 Tbsp olive oil
  • fine sea salt, to taste
  • ground black pepper, to taste

Salsa Verde

  • 1 bunch fresh curly parsley, leaves only (approx. 25 g)
  • 1 bunch fresh mint, leaves only (approx. 25 g)
  • 1 tsp pickled capers, drained, rinsed
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 lemon, thin peelings of skin and juice
  • 100 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp caster sugar
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt

Preparation

Potatoes

  1. Place water in mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh in potatoes. Cover Varoma and steam 30 min/Varoma/speed 1 or until tender. Meanwhile, preheat oven to 200°C and pour oil into a deep baking tray. Once the oven is hot, place tray with oil in centre of oven and heat for 5-7 minutes until oil is smoking hot.
  2. Check potatoes are tender using a cutlery knife. Once tender, remove tray of hot oil from oven and carefully transfer steamed potatoes into the tray. Using a fork, crush potatoes lightly until they crack all over but remain whole. Then, gently turn potatoes using tongs until evenly coated in the hot oil. Season with salt and pepper then bake for 30-40 minutes (200°C) until golden brown and crispy. Meanwhile, empty and dry mixing bowl then make salsa verde.

Salsa Verde

  1. Place parsley, mint, capers, garlic and lemon peel in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then chop again 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add lemon juice, oil, salt and sugar then blend 7 sec/speed 6 until combined. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Once potatoes are cooked, transfer to a serving dish and spoon over the salsa verde. Serve with grilled lamb cutlets or roast chicken, for example.
