Nutty Chocolate Caramel Bar

 5.0 (1 rating)

Ingredients

  • 9 oz sugar
  • 37 ½ oz water, divided
  • 1 oz unsalted butter, cold, in 1 cube (approx. 1 in. x 1 in.)
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 9 oz roasted salted peanuts, divided
  • 28 oz milk chocolate, broken into pieces

Preparation

  1. To ensure the success of recipes with Sugar Stages the ingredients must match exactly in quantity and description given. The recipe should be cooked immediately after placing the sugar with other ingredients in the mixing bowl. The bowl must be filled for Pre-cleaning immediately after the finished caramel is transferred from mixing bowl and the mixing bowl edge cleaned with spatula before Pre-cleaning is started.
  2. Line a baking sheet with parchment and set aside.
  3. Place sugar, 2.5 oz water, butter and lemon juice into mixing bowl, place splash guard onto mixing bowl lid and start Sugar Stages . Immediately pour onto prepared baking sheet and evenly spread over parchment. Immediately clean all caramel residues from edge of mixing bowl with spatula, so mixing bowl lid closes properly for Pre-cleaning.
  4. Place 35 oz water and vinegar into mixing bowl, insert measuring cup and Pre-clean /Fats or Caramel. Rinse and dry mixing bowl and lid.
  5. Let caramel cool completely (approx. 20 min.). Then break the cooled caramel into pieces.
  6. Place caramel pieces and 7 oz peanuts into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  7. Place chocolate into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and heat 4 min/120°F/speed 1. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and heat a further 4 min/120°F/speed 2.
  8. Add reserved peanut caramel mixture and mix 5-8 sec//speed 4. Spread the chocolate into silicone chocolate molds (use a bench scraper to scrape off excess chocolate from the molds) or onto parchment (see Tip), press remaining 2 oz peanuts into the warm chocolate and let cool completely. Once set, remove from molds or break into pieces and serve.

Useful Items

Hints & Tips

  • Use silicone praline molds or silicone molds in the shape of a chocolate bars. The mixture can be spread over parchment paper (approx. ½ in. thick), allowed to set and broken into pieces.
  • Garnish Nutty Chocolate Caramel Bars with broken banana chips or coconut chips instead of, or in addition to the peanuts.
