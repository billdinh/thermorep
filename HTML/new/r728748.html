Mole verde with chicken

 3.3 (3 ratings)

Ingredients

  • 100 g pepitas
  • 60 g roasted unsalted peanuts
  • 80 g iceberg lettuce, cut into pieces
  • 100 - 150 g fresh baby spinach leaves, coarsely chopped
  • 1 spring onion/shallot, green part only
  • 1 sprig fresh coriander, leaves only
  • 1 sprig fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 1 serrano chilli (see Tips)
  • 1 pinch ground cumin
  • 2 tsp sea salt, to taste
  • 400 g green tomatillos, cut into halves (see Tips)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 20 g grapeseed oil
  • 100 g onion
  • 280 - 400 g water (see Tips)
  • 8 skinless chicken drumsticks (see Tips)

Preparation

  1. Place pepitas, peanuts, lettuce and spinach into mixing bowl, then blend 30 sec/speed 8, with aid of spatula.
  2. Add spring onion/shallot, coriander, parsley, chilli, cumin, salt, tomatillos, garlic, oil and onion, then blend 2 min/speed 8.
  3. Add water, divide chicken legs between Varoma dish and tray, then place Varoma into position. Secure Varoma lid and cook 45 min/Varoma/speed 1, or until chicken is cooked. Serve chicken covered with the mole verde.

Hints & Tips

  • Adjust quantity of water if needed in order to obtain a thinner sauce. A thick sauce will not generate enough steam to cook the chicken thoroughly.
  • Serrano chillies are similar to jalapeños, but smaller and spicier. You can find them at some major supermarkets. If you can’t find them (or you want a milder heat), replace with a jalapeño.
  • Tomatillos are small, green Mexican tomatoes and you can find them (both fresh and canned) at some major supermarkets or specialty food stores. Alternatively, you can replace them with underripe tomatoes.
  • Serve with rice and beans.
  • Use small chicken drumsticks for this recipe. If using larger pieces, extend cooking time in step 3 by 10-15 minutes.
  • Use this mole in place of a red sauce when making enchiladas.
