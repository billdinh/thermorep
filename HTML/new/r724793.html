Surf and turf with pink peppercorn sauce

Ingredients

Pink peppercorn sauce

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 eschalot, cut into halves
  • 40 g salted butter, cut into cubes
  • 50 g white wine
  • 1 tsp tomato paste
  • 2 tbsp pink peppercorns, lightly crushed (see Tips)
  • 100 g water
  • 1 tsp Chicken stock powder or Chicken stock paste (see Tips)
  • 200 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 4 egg yolks (see Tips)

Surf and turf

  • 500 g water
  • 8 - 12 large raw prawns, deveined and butterflied
  • 4 beef fillet steaks
  • extra virgin olive oil, to drizzle
  • sea salt, to season
  • ground black pepper, to season
  • thick-cut chips, to serve

Preparation

Pink peppercorn sauce

  1. Place garlic and eschalot into mixing bowl, then chop 2 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add butter and cook 5 min/100°C/speed 1.
  3. Add wine, tomato paste and 1½ tablespoons of the crushed pink peppercorns, then cook 4 min/100°C//speed 1, without measuring cup. Reserve remaining pink peppercorns for serving.
  4. Add all remaining sauce ingredients, then cook 7 min/90°C//speed 4. Pour into a thermal serving jug to keep warm. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Surf and turf

  1. Place water into mixing bowl. Divide prawns between Varoma dish and tray, then place Varoma into position. Secure Varoma lid, then steam 10 min/Varoma/speed 2 or until prawns are just cooked. Meanwhile, continue with the recipe.
  2. Heat a grill pan over high heat. Drizzle steaks with oil and season with salt and pepper. If using MEATER+, insert into the middle of the thickest part of the steak, then open the MEATER+ app and follow the prompts to cook to your liking. Cook steaks for 3 minutes on each side for medium-rare or until cooked to your liking or until the MEATER+ app indicates steak is cooked. While prawns finish cooking, transfer the steaks to a plate to rest or until MEATER+ indicates it is ready. Carefully remove the MEATER+ (if using).
  3. Serve steaks and prawns with pink peppercorn sauce and sprinkled with remaining crushed pink peppercorns, and with thick-cut chips.

Hints & Tips

  • Crush peppercorns in a mortar and pestle or with the back of a spoon before starting the recipe.
  • You can replace Chicken stock powder with Vegetable or Chicken stock paste, search Cookidoo® for the recipes.
  • Use the egg whites from this recipe in "To die for" mini pavlovas, Magic bean chocolate cake or Macarons with strawberry cream cheese ganache from this collection. Search Cookidoo® for the recipes.
  • This recipe is also delicious with Sous vide rare beef steak. Search Cookidoo® for how to prepare your steak and serve with this Pink peppercorn sauce.
  • The MEATER+® is a wireless meat thermometer which enables perfect results every time. See TheMix Shop for more details.
  • Sauce will keep for up to 3 days in an airtight container in the fridge.
