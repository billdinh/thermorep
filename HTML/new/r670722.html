Alice Piktija's Cape Malay chicken curry

 4.4 (33 ratings)

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander seeds
  • 3 cardamom pods, crushed
  • 4 whole cloves
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 onions (approx. 300 g), cut into quarters
  • 50 g olive oil
  • 1 ½ tsp ground ginger
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 400 g canned diced tomatoes
  • 2 tsp white sugar
  • 2 potatoes, peeled and cut into pieces (2 cm)
  • 2 tsp sea salt
  • 800 g chicken thigh fillets, cut into pieces (4 cm)

Preparation

  1. Place cumin, coriander, cardamom and cloves into mixing bowl, then dry roast 5 min/120°C/speed 1 (TM31: 6 min/100°C//speed 1). Allow to cool slightly, then mill 1 min/speed 9.
  2. Add garlic, onion, oil, ginger, turmeric, cinnamon, chilli powder and pepper, then chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then sauté 5 min/120°C//speed 1 (TM31: 4 min/100°C//speed 1). Place in a bowl and set aside
  4. Insert blade cover. Place tomatoes, sugar, potatoes, salt and reserved onion spice mixture, mix with spatula, then cook 25 min/100°C//speed 1.
  5. Add chicken and stir with spatula, then cook 35 min/100°C//speed , placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid, until chicken and potatoes are cooked through. Serve curry with rice and condiments of choice (see Tips).

Hints & Tips

  • Serve this aromatic curry with steamed yellow rice and sweet sambals or chutneys. Search Cookidoo® for plenty of sambals, chutneys and rice recipes.
  • This recipe was contributed by Alice Piktija from Thermomix South Africa.
  • For TM31 users, follow the recipe as written with these alterations to some TM settings.
    Step 1: 6 min/100°C//speed 1.
    Step 3: 4 min/100°C//speed 1. Do not remove from bowl or insert blade cover.
    Step 4: 8 min/100°C//speed 1.
    Step 5: Cut chicken into 2 cm pieces. 12 min/100°C//speed 1.
