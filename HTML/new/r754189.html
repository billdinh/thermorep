Miso Fudge

 3.4 (5 ratings)

Ingredients

  • 600 g granulated sugar
  • 300 g whipping cream
  • 1 tsp lemon juice (see tip)
  • 60 g unsalted butter, cut in 2 pieces (6 cm x 4 cm x 1 cm)
  • 50 - 75 g white miso paste, to taste
  • 1000 g water
  • 15 g vinegar

Preparation

  1. To guarantee the success of recipes using Sugar Stages mode, the ingredients used must exactly match the quantities and descriptions specified. The recipe should also be cooked immediately after placing the ingredients in the mixing bowl.
  2. Line a square cake tin (20 cm) with baking paper.
  3. Place sugar, cream, lemon juice and butter in mixing bowl, place splash guard on mixing bowl lid then start Sugar Stages . Remove mixing bowl and leave to cool until temperature drops to 80°C (45-60 mins).
  4. When mixture has cooled, place mixing bowl back into position, add miso paste, insert measuring cup and mix 7 min/speed 1. Transfer to prepared tin and, using spatula, immediately scrape off any mixture left on rim of mixing bowl so mixing bowl lid closes securely for Pre-clean mode. Smooth surface of fudge and leave to cool completely. Meanwhile, continue with recipe.
  5. Place water and vinegar in mixing bowl then start Pre-clean .
  6. Once cool, cut fudge in squares (2 cm) and serve immediately or store in an airtight container in fridge.
