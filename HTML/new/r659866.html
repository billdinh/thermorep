Chilli mussels with risoni

 4.3 (6 ratings)

Ingredients

  • 1000 g fresh mussels, scrubbed and debearded
  • 1 onion (approx. 150 g), cut into quarters
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 fresh long red chilli, deseeded if preferred
  • 2 anchovy fillets
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 400 g canned chopped tomatoes
  • 100 g dry white wine
  • 250 g water
  • 1 tbsp Vegetable stock paste (see Tips)
  • 100 g dried risoni pasta
  • 4 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only, roughly chopped to serve

Preparation

  1. Place Varoma dish onto mixing bowl lid and weigh mussels into it, then set Varoma dish aside.
  2. Place onion, garlic, chilli and anchovies into mixing bowl, then chop 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add oil and sauté 5 min/120°C/speed 1 [TM31: 5 min/100°C/speed 1].
  4. Add tomatoes, wine, water and stock paste, then cook 7 min/100°C/speed 1 or until boiling.
  5. Add risoni and stir with spatula. Place Varoma with mussels into position, secure Varoma lid, then steam 10-12 min/Varoma//speed 1, stirring frequently to ensure even cooking, until pasta is just cooked and mussels have opened (discard any mussels that remain closed after cooking).
  6. Place mussels in a large serving bowl and pour over the sauce. Sprinkle with chopped parsley and serve immediately.
