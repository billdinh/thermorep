Bianca Mazur's Frozen Turkish delight martini

 4.2 (36 ratings)

Ingredients

  • 260 g cranberry juice
  • 160 g gin
  • 40 g freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 50 g rose water
  • 4 egg whites
  • Pashmak (Persian fairy floss), to serve (see Tips)
  • 4 pieces Turkish delight, to serve (see Tips)

Preparation

  1. Place a jug onto mixing bowl lid and weigh cranberry juice into it. Pour juice into ice cube trays and place in the freezer overnight.
  2. When ready to use, place gin, lemon juice, rose water and egg whites into mixing bowl, then mix 10 sec/speed 6.
  3. Add frozen cranberry juice cubes and blend 15 sec/speed 6. Pour into 4 martini glasses.
  4. Top each glass with a tuft of Pashmak (Persian fairy floss). Insert a cocktail skewer through each piece of Turkish delight, then rest a skewer carefully on the rim of each glass. Serve immediately.

Hints & Tips

  • Pashmak, or Persian fairy floss, is a form of fairy floss that is made by pulling rather than spinning sugar syrup and sesame seed paste into delectable thin strands. It is available from selected grocery stores. We’ve used the rose flavoured variety in this recipe.
  • Traditional Turkish delight does not use gelatine. However, to ensure this recipe is vegetarian, be sure to read the label.
  • This recipe was contributed by Bianca Mazur.
