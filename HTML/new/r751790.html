Chocolate Ganache Tart with Caramel

 5.0 (2 ratings)

Ingredients

Caramel Sauce

  • 9 oz sugar
  • 7 oz heavy whipping cream, divided
  • 36 oz water, divided
  • 1 oz unsalted butter, cold, in 1 cube (approx. 1 in. x 1 in.)
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • ½ oz unsalted butter
  • 1 tbsp vinegar

Chocolate Shortcrust Pastry

  • 7 oz all-purpose flour
  • 4 oz sugar
  • 3 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • ⅛ tsp salt
  • 1 vanilla bean
    or 1 ½ tsp vanilla bean paste
  • 4 oz unsalted butter
  • 1 large egg

Dark Chocolate Ganache

  • 7 oz dark chocolate (70% cocoa), broken into pieces
  • 7 oz heavy whipping cream
  • fresh fruit, to garnish

Preparation

Caramel Sauce

  1. To ensure the success of recipes with Sugar Stages the ingredients must match exactly in quantity and description given. The recipe should be cooked immediately after placing the sugar with other ingredients in the mixing bowl. The bowl must be filled for Pre-cleaning immediately after the finished caramel is transferred from mixing bowl and the mixing bowl edge cleaned with spatula before Pre-cleaning is started.
  2. Place sugar, 2.5 oz whipping cream, 1 oz water, 1 oz cold butter and lemon juice into mixing bowl, place splash guard instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and caramelize Sugar Stages .
  3. Add remaining 4.5 oz cream and 0.5 oz butter and without measuring cup, mix 2 min/speed 2. Then, without measuring cup, cook 3 min/Varoma/speed 2. Transfer to a heat-proof jar and set aside to cool to room temperature. Immediately clean edge of mixing bowl with spatula to remove any remaining caramel.
  4. Place 35 oz water and vinegar into mixing bowl. Insert measuring cup and start Pre-clean /Fats or Caramel. Rinse and dry mixing bowl and lid.

Chocolate Shortcrust Pastry

  1. Place flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder and salt into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 4.
  2. Split a vanilla bean lengthwise, scrape out seeds and add to mixing bowl, reserve pod for another use (see Tip). Add butter and egg and mix 15 sec/speed 5. Transfer pastry to a tart pan (8 in.), evenly press into bottom and up the sides of pan. Refrigerate 30 minutes. Meanwhile clean mixing bowl.
  3. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  4. Line chilled dough with parchment and baking weights and blind-bake 20 minutes (350°F). Remove parchment and weights and bake a further 7-8 minutes (350°F). Carefully remove from the oven, allow to cool completely. Meanwhile, place a small bowl onto mixing bowl lid, weigh in 5 oz reserved caramel and set aside.
  5. Once tart shell is cool, spread 5 oz reserved caramel into bottom of tart. Allow caramel to set (approx. 10-20 min.), then unmold tart from pan.

Dark Chocolate Ganache

  1. Place dark chocolate into mixing bowl and chop 6 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add whipping cream and heat 6 min/110°F/speed 1, then mix 10 sec/speed 4 until smooth. Immediately, pour ganache over caramel layer in tart shell and refrigerate until set (approx. 20 min.).
  3. Once tart is set, drizzle with reserved caramel, garnish with fresh fruit and serve.

Hints & Tips

  • To ensure the success of Sugar Stage recipes the butter must be added as much as possible in one piece. Cut one chunk and add a bit more if necessary, to achieve 1 oz.
  • Search Cookidoo® for Vanilla Sugar recipe and use vanilla pods leftover from other recipes (rinse and dry pods after first use). When only using the pods, the vanilla flavor and aroma will not be strong.
  • The lemon juice prevents the sugar from crystallizing and burning, leaving the caramel flavor intact.
