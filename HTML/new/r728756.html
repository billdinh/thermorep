Salmon with lemon hollandaise, asparagus and rice

4.6 (19 ratings)

Ingredients

Hollandaise sauce

  • 15 g water
  • 30 g freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 225 g unsalted butter, cut into pieces
  • 4 egg yolks
  • ½ tsp sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper

Salmon, asparagus and rice

  • 30 g fresh coriander or flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 1 lemon, zested, no white pith, plus 1 tbsp of lemon juice
  • 285 g jasmine rice
  • 1000 g water
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp sea salt, plus extra to season
  • 340 g asparagus or broccoli florets, trimmed
  • 450 g fresh skinless, boneless salmon fillets (approx. 3 cm thick)
  • ground black pepper, to season

Preparation

Hollandaise sauce

  1. Place all sauce ingredients into mixing bowl, then start Thicken /80°C. Pour sauce into a thermal serving bowl or other bowl and cover to keep warm. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Salmon, asparagus and rice

  1. Place coriander and lemon zest into mixing bowl, then chop 20 sec/speed 10. Place in a large bowl and set aside.
  2. Place simmering basket onto mixing bowl lid and weigh rice into it. Rinse rice until water runs clear. Place water into mixing bowl and insert simmering basket with rice. Add onion powder, garlic powder and ½ teaspoon of the salt to the simmering basket, stir to combine with spatula, then mix 10 sec/speed 7.
  3. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh asparagus into it. Insert Varoma tray and weigh salmon onto it. Season with salt and pepper, sprinkle with lemon juice, secure Varoma lid and steam 17 min/Varoma/speed 3 or until the rice is cooked and salmon is cooked to your liking. Remove Varoma and set aside. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula. Transfer rice into bowl with coriander and lemon mixture, then stir to combine using spatula. Divide rice, salmon and asparagus between serving plates and top with hollandaise sauce. Serve any extra sauce on the side.
