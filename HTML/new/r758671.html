Chocolate Budino (Matthew Kenney)

 5.0 (2 ratings)

Ingredients

Budino

  • 3 oz glucose, to taste
  • 7 oz vegan whipping cream, e.g. Silk®
  • 10 ½ oz soy milk
  • non-stick spray oil
  • ½ oz cocoa powder
  • ½ oz sugar
  • ½ tsp agar-agar powder
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 7 oz dark chocolate (70%), broken into pieces

Caramel Sauce

  • 9 oz sugar
  • 4 ½ oz vegan whipping cream, e.g. Silk®, divided
  • 36 oz water, divided
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 oz vegan butter, cold
  • ½ tsp salt
  • edible flowers, to garnish

Preparation

Budino

  1. Place glucose, vegan whipping cream and soy milk into mixing bowl and heat 8 min/212°F/speed 2. Meanwhile, grease 10 Thermomix® custard molds or individual molds (2½ oz ea.) with non-stick spray oil and set aside.
  2. Add cocoa powder, sugar, agar and salt and blend 10 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Place Varoma dish (without lid) instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 5 min/212°F/speed 1.
  3. Add chocolate, allow to sit 1 minute for chocolate to soften, then insert measuring cup and mix 30 sec/speed 5. As quickly as possible, pour into prepared molds and refrigerate 4 hours to overnight until set. Meanwhile, make the caramel.

Caramel Sauce

  1. To ensure the success of recipes with Sugar Stages the ingredients must match exactly in quantity and description given. The recipe should be cooked immediately after placing the sugar with other ingredients in the mixing bowl.
  2. Place sugar, 2.5 oz vegan whipping cream, 1 oz water, lemon juice and cold vegan butter into mixing bowl, place splash guard onto mixing bowl lid and caramelize Sugar Stages .
  3. Add remaining 2 oz whipping cream and ½ tsp salt and without measuring cup mix 2 min/speed  and cook 3 min/Varoma/speed 2. Pour into an airtight jar and set aside. Immediately scrape off any mixture left on rim of mixing bowl so mixing bowl lid closes securely for Pre-clean mode.
  4. Place 35 oz water and 1 drop dish soap into mixing bowl. Insert measuring cup into mixing bowl lid and start Pre-clean /Fats or Caramel.
  5. When ready to serve, invert Chocolate Budinos onto plates and drizzle with Caramel Sauce. Garnish with edible flowers and serve.

Hints & Tips

  • To ensure success with this recipe the vegan butter must be added as much as possible in one piece. Scoop or cut one chunk (approx. 1 in. x 1 in. x 1 in.) and add a bit more if necessary, to achieve 1 oz.
  • Lemon juice prevents crystallization and possible sugar burning and it does not change the taste of the caramel.
  • To store, keep refrigerated in an airtight container. To warm, place in the microwave for 30 seconds to heat through.
  • Heat up leftover caramel to spoon or drizzle over ice cream to create the perfect ice cream sundae.
  • Not all flowers are edible, some are poisonous. Flowers should be purchased from a reliable source.
  • Those that suffer from pollen allergies should not consume edible flowers.
  • Serve with vegan whipped cream.
