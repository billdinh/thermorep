Chai latte parfait

 5.0 (3 ratings)

Ingredients

Chai latte ice cream

  • 180 g white sugar
  • 680 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 1 ½ tbsp black tea leaves (dried)
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp ground cardamom
  • ¾ tsp clove, ground
  • ½ tsp ground ginger
  • ½ tsp nutmeg
  • ½ tsp fennel seeds
  • 8 egg yolks
  • 340 g full cream milk
  • ½ tsp salt

Chocolate sauce

  • 100 g chocolate, broken into pieces
  • 250 g pure cream
  • 50 g unsalted butter, cut into pieces
  • 30 g honey
  • ½ tsp natural vanilla extract
  • 1 - 2 pinches salt
  • 4 - 6 waffle cones (good quality)
  • whipped cream, to serve (optional)

Preparation

Chai latte ice cream

  1. Place sugar into mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place cream, black tea leaves, cinnamon, cardamom, cloves, ginger, nutmeg and fennel into mixing bowl, then heat 10 min/95°C/speed 2.5. Transfer into a jug, cover with plastic wrap and let steep for approx. 10 minutes. Rinse mixing bowl.
  3. Place reserved icing sugar, egg yolks, milk and salt into mixing bowl, then start Thicken /80°C. Meanwhile, strain the reserved cream through a fine mesh sieve into a second jug. Discard the tea leaves and spices.
  4. Add reserved infused cream and heat 3 min/70°C/speed 2.5. Transfer mixture into a shallow freezable container and set aside to cool for 15 minutes, then place into freezer overnight. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Chocolate sauce

  1. Place chocolate into mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add cream, butter, honey, vanilla extract and salt, then heat 6 min/80°C/speed 1. Transfer into a jug and allow to cool completely. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Remove ice cream from freezer and set aside for approx. 10 minutes, until soft enough to cut into pieces (4 x 4 cm). Place half of the frozen ice cream into mixing bowl and crush 20 sec/speed 6, then mix 10 sec/speed 4 with aid of spatula. Transfer back into a freezable container.
  4. Place remaining frozen ice cream pieces into mixing bowl and crush 20 sec/speed 6, then mix 10 sec/speed 4 with aid of spatula. Transfer into container with reserved ice cream and smooth the surface with a spatula. Place in the freezer for a minimum 4 hours or until desired consistency is achieved.
  5. Break waffle cones into pieces, arrange 1-2 tablespoons in each serving glass, cover with 1-2 tablespoons of whipped cream (optional), drizzle with 1 tablespoon of chocolate sauce and finish with 2 scoops of chai latte ice cream. Add a second layer of the waffle pieces whipped cream, chocolate sauce and 2 more scoops of chai latte ice cream. Decorate with whipped cream and chocolate sauce then serve immediately (see Tips).
