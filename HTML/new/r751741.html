Virgin Watermelon Margarita

 5.0 (3 ratings)

Ingredients

  • salt, to rim glasses
  • 1 - 2 lime, cut in wedges, plus extra, sliced, to garnish
  • 450 g ice cubes
  • 900 g watermelon flesh, hulled
  • 225 g fresh strawberries, hulled
  • 170 g lime juice
  • 85 g agave syrup, to taste

Preparation

  1. Place enough salt onto a plate to rim 6 glasses. Rub a lime wedge along the rim of each glass and dip the rim into the salt, rotating the glass until the entire rim is coated and set aside.
  2. Place ice cubes in mixing bowl, grind 3 sec/speed 9.
  3. Add watermelon, strawberries, lime juice and agave, blend 10 sec/speed 9. Divide watermelon mixture between prepared glasses. Garnish with lime slices and watermelon wedges and serve immediately.
