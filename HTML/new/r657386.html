Spiced Polish Christmas cake

 4.8 (18 ratings)

Ingredients

Cake layers

  • 125 g unsalted butter, softened and cut into pieces
  • 220 g dark brown sugar
  • 250 g honey
  • 2 eggs
  • 25 g Christmas spice mix (see Tips)
  • 500 g plain flour, plus extra to dust
  • 2 tsp bicarbonate of soda

Sour cream filling

  • 50 g freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 3 tsp gelatine powder
  • 700 g sour cream
  • 250 g mascarpone cheese
  • 40 g icing sugar

Chocolate ganache

  • 200 g dark chocolate, broken into pieces
  • 160 g pouring (whipping) cream

Preparation

Cake layers

  1. Place butter, sugar, honey, eggs and spice mix into mixing bowl, then mix 10 sec/speed 4.
  2. Add flour and bicarbonate of soda, then knead Dough /2 min. Place dough in a bowl, cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 3 hours or until firm.
  3. Preheat oven to 180°C. Trace a 24 x 30 cm rectangle onto six pieces of baking paper.
  4. Form dough into a log, then cut into 3 x 400 g pieces. Invert a 30 x 40 cm baking tray. Using a rolling pin, roll out a portion of dough between two marked sheets of paper to marked size. Lift dough on paper onto upturned tray. Remove top layer of paper.
  5. Bake for 12 minutes or until cake is lightly browned. Carefully slide cake on paper onto a work surface and set aside to cool. Repeat rolling and baking with remaining paper and portions of dough to make a total of three cake layers. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Sour cream filling

  1. Place lemon juice and gelatine in mixing bowl. Leave for 5 minutes, then melt 2 min/60°C/speed 2. Place in a small bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Place remaining sour cream filling ingredients in mixing bowl and beat 20 sec/speed 3, adding gelatine mixture through hole in mixing bowl lid, until smooth and thickened. Remove butterfly whisk. Pour into a bowl and set aside until needed. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  3. To assemble cake, line a 30 x 40 cm baking tray, top with a cake layer and spread with half the filling. Repeat with another cake layer and filling, then finish with an inverted cake layer. Cover well and refrigerate for a minimum of 4 days (see Tips).

Chocolate ganache

  1. Start making the ganache 1 hour before serving. Place dark chocolate into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add cream and melt 2-3 min/50°C/speed 3. Cool ganache in mixing bowl for 1 hour to thicken slightly. Trim sides of cake to neaten. Place cake on a serving plate, pour ganache over the top and swirl with a spatula. Decorate as desired.
