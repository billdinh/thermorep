Carrot, Feta and Mint Salad

 4.4 (22 ratings)

Ingredients

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 7 sprigs fresh mint, leaves only, torn into pieces, divided
  • 1 ½ oz lemon juice
  • 1 ½ oz extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 ½ tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp ground paprika
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ⅛ tsp ground black pepper
  • 17 ½ oz water
  • 24 ½ oz baby carrots (approx. 3 bunches), peeled and tops trimmed
  • 2 - 3 ½ oz feta cheese, crumbled, to garnish

Preparation

  1. Place garlic and half the torn mint leaves (3½ sprigs) into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add lemon juice, olive oil, cumin, paprika, salt and pepper and mix 3 sec/speed 6. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position and weigh in carrots. Secure Varoma lid and steam 13 min/Varoma/speed 1. Carefully remove Varoma lid. Stir carrots with spatula, secure Varoma lid and cook for a further 2-4 min/Varoma/speed 1, or until carrots are cooked to your liking.
  4. Transfer steamed carrots into a large bowl. Add reserved dressing and gently stir to combine. Set aside to cool completely (approx. 20 min.). Serve carrots garnished with crumbled feta and remaining 3½ sprigs torn mint leaves.
