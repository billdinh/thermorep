Tomato and olive tapenade mini bruschette

 5.0 (1 rating)

Ingredients

  • 120 g pitted black olives, premium quality
  • 1 tsp baby pickled capers
  • 1 anchovy fillet
  • 1 tsp fresh thyme, leaves only
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil, plus extra to brush
  • 1 tsp freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 - 2 pinches ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1 baguette, cut into slices (1 cm thick) and toasted
  • 100 g tomatoes, deseeded and cut into small pieces
  • 5 - 6 fresh basil leaves, torn
  • 80 g feta cheese, crumbled, to serve

Preparation

  1. Place olives, capers, anchovy, thyme, olive oil, lemon juice and pepper into mixing bowl, then blend 5 sec/speed 5. Place in an airtight container and set aside until ready to use.
  2. Preheat oven to 180°C and line a large baking tray.
  3. Brush both sides of baguette slices with extra oil and arrange in a single layer on prepared baking tray. Bake for 3 minutes (180°C) on each side or until golden. Set aside to cool.
  4. To serve, generously spread each baguette slice with the tapenade, then top with tomato, feta and basil leaves.

Hints & Tips

  • Store remaining tapenade in an airtight container in the fridge for 4-5 days.
  • Enjoy tapenade as a dip with crackers, dolloped on pizza or spread over slices of toasted focaccia or ciabatta.
