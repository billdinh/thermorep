Slow Cooked Chili con Carne

 (No ratings)

Ingredients

  • 28 oz stew meat, trimmed and cubed (1 in.)
  • 2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp ground smoked paprika
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • ½ tsp ground cayenne pepper
  • 1 ¼ - 1 ¾ tsp sea salt, to taste, divided
  • 1 - 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • ½ - 1 oz cilantro roots and stalks (leaves reserved to garnish) (see Tip)
  • 1 oz olive oil
  • 7 oz red onion, cut into wedges (approx. 1 in.)
  • 1 fresh jalapeño chili halved, deseeded (optional)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 6 oz red bell peppers, deseeded and cut into pieces (1 in.)
    or 6 oz yellow bell peppers, deseeded and cut into pieces (1 in.)
  • 2 tsp cocoa powder
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 28 oz canned crushed tomatoes
    or 28 oz canned diced tomatoes
  • 1 tsp freeze dried instant coffee granules
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 tbsp water
  • 14 oz canned kidney beans, rinsed and drained
  • 14 oz canned black beans, rinsed and drained, (optional)
  • sour cream, to serve
  • grated cheddar cheese, to serve (see Tips)

Preparation

  1. Place a large bowl onto mixing bowl lid, weigh in meat, oregano, paprika, cumin, coriander, cayenne pepper, ¾ tsp salt and black pepper, toss to combine, massage into meat and set aside.
  2. Place cilantro roots and stalks into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a separate bowl and set aside.
  3. Place olive oil, onion and chili (optional) into mixing bowl, place splash guard onto mixing bowl lid and start High Temp. .
  4. Allow contents in mixing bowl to cool to 250°F. Add garlic, insert measuring cup and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Transfer to bowl with reserved meat and set aside.
  5. Insert blade cover and press down to secure. Place bell pepper, cocoa powder, vinegar, tomatoes, coffee and reserved meat and onions into mixing bowl. Stir with spatula to combine and start Slow Cook /6 h 30 min/195°F (see Tip).
  6. In a small bowl, whisk together cornstarch and water and set aside.
  7. Add kidney beans, black beans, reserved chopped cilantro stems and roots, ½-1 tsp salt, to taste and cornstarch mixture and cook 5 min/212°F//speed 1. Divide between bowls. Garnish with reserved cilantro leaves and serve with sour cream and grated cheese.

Hints & Tips

  • Clean and scrape down the roots of your cilantro with a spoon to remove the tougher, more fibrous bits on the outside of the roots, before adding to the mixing bowl.
  • Grate cheddar cheese in your Thermomix®. Before starting recipe, place 5.5 oz cheese, cut into pieces (1 in.) into mixing bowl and grate 8 sec/speed 7.
  • To ensure the success of slow cooked recipes or adapting other recipes to slow cook, adhere to the following criteria:
    • Cover the ingredients with liquid, such as broth, sauce, etc. Observe the maximum filling mark of 2.2 liters.
    • Use a maximum of 28 oz meat and/or vegetables.
    • If your ingredients have different weight or thickness to those in the recipe, cooking time must be adjusted accordingly.
    • Don’t use slow cookers (or slow cooker mode) to cook dried kidney beans or dishes that contain them. Slow cookers (slow cooking mode) temperatures are not high enough to destroy the high level of Phytohaemagglutinin / natural toxin found in kidney beans.
  • Place blade cover & peeler onto mixing knife making sure the upward curved parts are on top of the upper positioned blades. Press down gently to secure it to the mixing knife.
  • After slow cooking is complete, check to see if cooked to your liking and if necessary continue to cook desired amount of time/195°F//speed 0.5.
  • To serve as a dip with corn chips, thicken the sauce with corn starch before serving. After cooking kidney beans, place a small bowl onto mixing bowl lid, weigh in 2 oz water and add 1 tbsp corn starch. Stir to combine, then add to mixing bowl and cook 5 min/195°F//speed 0.5, until thickened.
