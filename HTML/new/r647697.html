Carrot Walnut Bundt Cake

Ingredients

  • non-stick cooking spray
  • 10 ½ oz carrots, in pieces (1 in.)
  • 4 ½ oz walnut pieces, plus extra to garnish
  • 8 oz sunflower oil
  • 14 oz granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 10 oz all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 2 tbsp ground cinnamon
  • confectioners sugar, to garnish

Preparation

  1. Preheat to oven 350°F. Grease a bundt pan (approx. 10 in.) with non-stick spray and set aside.
  2. Place carrots into mixing bowl and grate 8 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place walnuts into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 5. Transfer to bowl with grated carrots and set aside.
  4. Place sunflower oil and sugar into mixing bowl and mix 15 sec/speed 6.
  5. Without measuring cup, mix 22 sec/speed 5 while slowly adding eggs one at time through hole in mixing bowl lid.
  6. Add flour, reserved carrots and walnuts, baking powder, baking soda, salt and cinnamon, insert measuring cup and mix 35 sec/speed 5.
  7. Transfer batter to prepared bundt pan and bake 40-55 minutes (350°F) or until a toothpick inserted into center of cake comes out clean. Allow to cool in pan for 10 minutes before inverting onto a plate. Garnish with confectioners sugar and walnuts and serve.
