Menu with Pea Ginger Soup, Lemon Salmon with Broccoli and Potato

 (No ratings)

Ingredients

  • 80 g cold pressed olive oil
  • 50 g light soy sauce
  • ½ tsp brown sugar
  • 1 lemon, zest and juice
  • 4 fresh salmon fillets, skinless (150-180 g each)
    or 4 white fish fillets, skinless (150-180 g each)
  • 5 - 10 g fresh ginger, peeled and sliced (optional)
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 100 g shallots, cut in pieces
  • 100 g leek, white part only, cut in pieces
    or 100 g onions, cut in halves
  • 100 g English celery stalks, cut in pieces
  • 350 g frozen green peas
    or 350 g carrots, cut in pieces (see tip)
  • 450 g water
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 250 g potatoes, cut in pieces (1 cm)
  • 400 g broccoli florets
    or 400 g mixed vegetables (e.g. carrots, courgettes, mushrooms), cut in slices (0.5 cm)
  • 2 pinch black pepper, ground
  • 50 - 100 g single cream (approx. 18 % fat), to taste

Preparation

  1. Place 30 g olive oil, soy sauce, brown sugar, lemon zest from half the lemon and 25 g lemon juice in mixing bowl, emulsify 20 sec/speed 5. Place salmon fillets in a deep dish and pour marinade from mixing bowl over the salmon. Refrigerate for 10 minutes and continue with recipe.
  2. Place ginger, garlic clove, shallots, leek and remaining 50 g olive oil in mixing bowl, chop 4 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Sauté 7 min/120°C/speed 1. Meanwhile, line Varoma tray with baking paper.
  4. Add celery stalks and green peas to mixing bowl, chop 8 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add water and 1 tsp salt to mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket and add potatoes to it. Set Varoma dish in position, add broccoli florets to it and season them with ½ tsp salt. Insert prepared Varoma tray, arrange salmon fillets on the tray, season with ½ tsp salt and black pepper and drizzle with marinade. Close Varoma and cook 20 min/Varoma/speed 1. Set covered Varoma aside on a plate to keep warm. Remove simmering basket using spatula, set aside on a plate and cover with a tea towel to keep warm.
  6. Add cream to mixing bowl, insert measuring cup and blend 2 min/speed 5-10, increasing speed gradually. Serve soup for starter (see tip), then salmon fillets, potatoes and broccoli for the main course.

Hints & Tips

  • A quick, healthy meal with bright flavours and colours, starting with a vibrant green pea soup with zingy ginger, followed by lemon-marinated salmon with steamed potatoes and broccoli: this recipe can turn a weeknight dinner into a special treat.
  • If making a carrot soup instead of a pea soup, make sure carrots are cut in small pieces.
  • If the celery stalks have leaves, place a few leaves on top of salmon fillets before steaming to add extra flavour.
  • For a milder taste omit drizzling salmon with marinade in step 3.
  • After serving the soup, set mixing bowl and covered Varoma back in position so the main course keeps warm while the soup is enjoyed.
