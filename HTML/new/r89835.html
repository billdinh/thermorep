Lime glaze

 5.0 (3 ratings)

Ingredients

  • 7 garlic cloves
  • 250 g brown sugar
  • 160 g lime juice (approx. 7 limes)
  • 5 g fresh thyme (approx.15 sprigs)
  • 125 g brandy
  • 2 tsp ground black pepper

Preparation

  1. Place all ingredients into mixing bowl and cook 20-25 min/Varoma/speed 2, or until consistency has thickened, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  2. Strain glaze through a fine mesh sieve into a ceramic or glass bowl, then set aside to cool completely or until ready to use.

Hints & Tips

  • The glaze will thicken as it cools. To test if your glaze is cooked, place a metal spoon into the mixture. The glaze should coat the back of the spoon in a nice thick syrup-consistency.
  • As thyme leaves are very light in weight, we recommend placing all of your thyme leaves into the mixing bowl at once rather than weighing them individually.
  • Use this glaze on your ham as an alternative to the Pineapple and orange glaze. Please refer to the recipe on Cookidoo®.
