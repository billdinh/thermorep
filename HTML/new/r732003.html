Broccoli, Red Pepper and Pine Nut Salad

 4.8 (15 ratings)
Difficulty

Most of our recipes are easy. Those that require a little more time or cooking ability are rated medium or advanced.

This is how much time you need to prepare this meal.

Serving size

This shows how many portions this recipe makes.

Ingredients

  • 300 g broccoli florets
  • 1 red pepper (approx. 200 g), deseeded, cut in pieces
  • 1 apple (approx. 130 g), peeled, cored and quartered
  • 30 g pine nuts
    or 30 g sunflower seeds
  • 25 g olive oil
  • 15 g cider vinegar
    or 15 g white balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 1 ½ tsp mustard
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
    or 1 tsp herb salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper

Preparation

  1. Place broccoli, red pepper, apple, pine nuts, oil, vinegar, honey, mustard, salt and pepper in mixing bowl, then chop 5 sec/speed 4. Serve at room temperature.
