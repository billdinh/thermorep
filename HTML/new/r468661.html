Lemonade

Ingredients

  • 90 g lump sugar, adjust to taste
  • 2 lemons, unpeeled, cut into quarters
  • 800 g cold water

Preparation

  1. Place lump sugar in mixing bowl, grind Turbo/2 sec/2 times.
  2. Add lemons and water to mixing bowl. Hold measuring cup in place, crush Turbo/2 sec/1 time. Insert simmering basket, hold it in place using spatula and strain lemonade into a jug. Serve immediately.

Hints & Tips

  • This refreshing drink is particularly easy to prepare in Thermomix® and is very popular in lemon or lime growing countries such as Italy, Spain, Portugal and Mexico.
  • Choose smaller lemons with smooth shiny peel, as these are juicier and have less white pith.
  • For sweeter or tarter lemonade, adjust sugar to taste.
  • Replace lemons with either 2 small oranges or 1 grapefruit, cut in quarters.
