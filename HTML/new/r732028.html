Corn Pudding - Pastelera de Choclo

 5.0 (2 ratings)

Ingredients

  • 1000 g frozen sweetcorn kernels (see tip)
  • 6 fresh basil leaves
  • 450 g onions, quartered
  • 50 g olive oil
  • 1 - 2 tsp paprika, or to taste
  • 5 g unsalted butter, for greasing
  • 2 tsp fine sea salt
  • 45 g caster sugar, plus extra for sprinkling
  • 100 g milk
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper

Preparation

  1. Place 500 g frozen corn and 3 basil leaves in mixing bowl, then blend 40 sec/speed 5-10, increasing speed gradually. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place remaining 500 g frozen corn and 3 basil leaves in mixing bowl, then blend 40 sec/speed 5-10 increasing speed gradually. Transfer to bowl with reserved ground sweetcorn and set aside.
  3. Place onions in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add oil and paprika, sauté 8 min/120°C/speed 1, then, with simmering basket in place of measuring cup, sauté again 4 min/120°C/speed 1.
  5. Stir mixture with spatula then cook again 15 min/90°C/speed 1. Meanwhile, grease a baking dish (30 cm x 24 cm) and set aside. Preheat oven to 220°C.
  6. Add salt, sugar, milk, reserved ground sweetcorn and pepper to mixing bowl, then cook 7 min/90°C/speed 1.
  7. Transfer sweetcorn mixture to prepared dish and sprinkle with sugar. Bake for 15 minutes (220°C) or until golden brown. Serve hot.

Hints & Tips

  • Using frozen sweetcorn kernels gives the pudding its unique texture. Do not thaw the corn at all before use.
  • Sprinkle the pastelera with merkén spice mix before gratinating.
  • If you prefer a less sweet pastelera, reduce the amount of sugar in step 6.
