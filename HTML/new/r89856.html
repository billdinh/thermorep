Traditional cocktail sauce

Ingredients

  • 250 g grapeseed oil
  • 1 egg, room temperature
  • 2 tsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • ½ tsp sea salt
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 5 drops Tabasco® sauce
  • 35 g tomato sauce
  • 45 g double cream

Preparation

  1. Place a jug onto mixing bowl lid, then weigh grapeseed oil into it. Set jug aside.
  2. Place egg, 1 tsp of the lemon juice, mustard, pepper and salt into mixing bowl and mix 1-2 min/speed 4, slowly pouring oil onto mixing bowl lid, letting it drizzle into mixing bowl to emulsify. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Insert butterfly whisk. Add all remaining ingredients, including remaining 1 tsp lemon juice, and whip 15 sec/speed 3. Remove butterfly whisk. Serve immediately or transfer into a sealable jar or container and place into refrigerator until use.
