Spring greens with grapefruit

Ingredients

  • 500 g water, plus extra for ice bath
  • ice cubes, for cooling
  • 350 g asparagus (approx. 3 bunches), trimmed and cut into pieces (approx. 3 cm)
  • 200 g zucchini, cut into rounds (approx. 1 cm)
  • 2 spring onions/shallots, trimmed and cut into halves
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 2 pink grapefruit, 50 g of juice and remaining segmented
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 2 tsp chia seeds
  • 80 g fresh baby spinach leaves
  • 40 g walnuts, cut into pieces
  • 1 tbsp sunflower seeds
  • 1 tbsp pumpkin seeds
  • 2 tbsp fresh chives, finely chopped

Preparation

  1. Fill a large bowl with water and ice cubes and set aside.
  2. Place 500 g water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh asparagus into it. Place Varoma tray into position, then weigh zucchini onto it. Secure Varoma lid and steam 10-12 min/Varoma/speed 1 or until vegetables are just cooked. Remove Varoma tray and set zucchini aside. Transfer asparagus into reserved iced water for 5 minutes. Using Varoma dish, drain asparagus. Transfer asparagus and zucchini onto paper towel to drain excess moisture. Place asparagus and zucchini into a large serving bowl. Discard cooking water and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Place spring onions/shallots into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add oil, grapefruit juice, salt, pepper and chia seeds and mix 10 sec/speed 3.
  5. Add spinach to serving bowl, drizzle with grapefruit dressing and toss to combine. Top salad with walnuts, seeds, grapefruit segments and chives to serve.

Useful Items

large bowl, large serving bowl, paper towel
