Cod with Olive and Bread Topping, Potatoes and Tenderstem Broccoli

Ingredients

  • 600 - 700 g cod fillets, skinless
  • 120 g cornbread, cut in pieces
  • 50 g green olives, pitted
  • 20 g olive oil, plus extra for brushing and drizzling
  • 125 g onions, quartered
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 30 cherry tomatoes on the vine
  • 10 g orange juice
  • 1 tsp balsamic vinegar
  • ½ tsp sweet paprika
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt, plus extra for sprinkling
  • 750 g water
  • 600 - 650 g new potatoes, halved (2-3 cm)
  • 400 g tenderstem broccoli, rinsed

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C and line a baking tray with baking paper. Place cod on baking tray, brush lightly with oil then set aside.
  2. Place cornbread in mixing bowl and chop 6 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl.
  3. Place olives in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Transfer to bowl with breadcrumbs and set aside.
  4. Place oil, onions and garlic in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then sauté 5 min/Varoma/speed 2. Meanwhile, place tomatoes (still on vines) in a roasting tin, drizzle with oil then roast for 30 minutes (180°C).
  5. Add orange juice, vinegar, paprika, salt, reserved breadcrumbs and chopped olives then mix 5 sec/speed 4. Spread bread paste over cod and set aside.
  6. Place water in mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket then weigh in potatoes. Sprinkle with salt then steam 12 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  7. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh in broccoli. Cover Varoma then steam 10-15 min/Varoma/speed 1 until cooked. Meanwhile, place tray with cod in oven and bake for 12-15 minutes (180°C) until cooked (will depend on thickness of fish).
  8. Slice cod into 6 portions (if not already) then divide steamed broccoli between 6 plates and place cod on top. Serve with steamed potatoes and cherry tomatoes on the side, finishing with a drizzle of oil.

Useful Items

pastry brush, roasting tin, baking paper, baking tray, oven
