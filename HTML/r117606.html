Sardine Sandwich Spread

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Gouda cheese, cut into pieces (approx. 1 ¼ in.)
  • 4 - 5 oz canned sardines in oil, drained (approx. 2.8 oz direct weight)
  • 4 ½ oz salted butter
  • 1 oz shallot, halved
  • 1 oz freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • ¼ tsp paprika
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper

Preparation

  1. Place cheese into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 8.
  2. Add sardines, butter, shallot, lemon juice, paprika, salt and pepper and mix 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix again 10 sec/speed 4. Serve or refrigerate until needed.
