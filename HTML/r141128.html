Perfect party cookies (allergy free)

Ingredients

  • 200 g dairy free spread (e.g. Nuttelex®)
  • 200 g caster sugar
  • 50 g golden syrup
  • 400 g gluten free self-raising flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 3 tsp gluten free baking powder
  • 1 tbsp milk of choice (see Tips)
  • 2 tsp natural vanilla extract
  • water, for brushing
  • gluten free 100s and 1000s, for sprinkling

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C. Line 2 baking trays (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place dairy free spread and sugar into mixing bowl and blend 1 min 30 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add golden syrup, flour, baking powder, milk and vanilla and mix 5 sec/speed 5.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix 5 sec/speed 5.
  5. Transfer onto a silicone bread mat or lightly floured work surface. Divide dough into 2 equal portions. Wrap 1 portion in plastic wrap and place into refrigerator. Roll out other portion to approx. 1 cm thickness. Using a cookie cutter (approx. 6 cm), cut out biscuits and transfer onto prepared trays, leaving enough room between biscuits to spread (approx. 3 cm).
  6. Gather together dough scraps, press together and re-roll to approx. 1 cm thickness. Cut out more biscuits (approx. 6 cm), to create approx. 16 biscuits.
  7. Brush biscuits with water, sprinkle with 100's and 1000's and bake for 7-10 minutes (200°C), until lightly golden. Transfer onto a wire rack to cool.
  8. Roll out remaining portion to approx. 1 cm thickness. Using a cookie cutter (approx. 6 cm), cut out biscuits and transfer onto prepared trays, leaving enough room between biscuits to spread (approx. 3 cm).
  9. Gather together dough scraps, press together and re-roll to approx. 1 cm thickness. Cut out more biscuits (approx. 6 cm), to create approx. 16 biscuits.
  10. Brush biscuits with water, sprinkle with 100s and 1000s and bake for 7-10 minutes (200°C), until lightly golden. Transfer onto a wire rack to cool. Once cooled, transfer into a sealable storage container until ready to serve.

Useful Items

round cookie cutter (6 cm), rolling pin, plastic wrap, silicone bread mat, 2 baking trays (30 x 40 cm), baking paper, cookie cutter, pastry brush, wire rack, sealable container, oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • We used almond milk in our cookies, but you can use your preferred milk of choice.
  • These cookies are best eaten the day they are made.
