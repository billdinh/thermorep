Spelt Flour Pizza with Chicory and Taleggio

Ingredients

Dough

  • 220 g water
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 tsp dried instant yeast
  • 30 g extra virgin olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 200 g strong white bread flour
  • 200 g spelt flour
  • 1 ½ tsp fine sea salt

Topping

  • 2 - 3 pinches fine sea salt, to taste
  • 450 g chicory, quartered
  • 20 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 200 g Taleggio, rind removed, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 10 - 15 g shelled walnuts, in pieces

Preparation

Dough

  1. Place 50 g water, sugar and yeast in mixing bowl then warm 2 min/37°C/speed 2. Meanwhile, grease a large bowl with oil.
  2. Add remaining 170 g water, oil, bread flour, spelt flour and salt then knead 1 min 30 sec/. Transfer dough to prepared bowl, cover with cling film then set aside to rise in a warm place until doubled in size (approx. 1-2 hours).

Topping

  1. Towards end of rising time, preheat oven to 220°C and line a large baking tray with baking paper.
  2. Place salt, chicory and oil in mixing bowl then cook 8 min/Varoma//speed 1. Transfer cooked chicory to simmering basket and set aside to drain and cool.
  3. Transfer risen dough to prepared baking tray. Use fingertips to spread dough out to the size of the tray, with a slightly thicker edge.
  4. Arrange reserved cooked chicory over dough then Taleggio and nuts over the top. Bake for 15-20 minutes (220°C) until edges are golden. Serve hot, cut in slices.

Useful Items

large bowl, cling film, large baking tray, baking paper, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Alternatively, roll dough out on a floured surface then transfer to prepared baking tray.
