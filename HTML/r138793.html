Simple butter biscuits

Ingredients

  • 2 pieces lemon zest (1 x 4 cm), no white pith
  • 130 g sugar
  • 220 g unsalted butter, cut into pieces and softened
  • 2 tsp natural vanilla extract
  • 1 egg
  • 350 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
Nutrition
per 1 portions
Calories
237 kJ / 56 kcal

0.8 g

Preparation

  1. Place lemon zest and sugar into mixing bowl and mill 8 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add butter and beat 30 sec/speed 4.
  3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and beat 30 sec/speed 4.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and beat 30 sec/speed 4.
  5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Add vanilla and egg and mix 1 min/speed 4.
  6. Add flour and mix 15 sec/speed 4.
  7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then mix 15 sec/speed 4. Transfer dough onto a well-floured silicone bread mat or work surface and divide into 2 even portions. Shape each portion into a disc, then wrap in plastic wrap and place into refrigerator for 1-2 hours or until firm enough to roll (see Tips).
  8. Preheat oven to 170°C. Line 2 baking trays (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  9. Working quickly, with one batch at a time, transfer dough onto a well-floured silicone bread mat or work surface and roll out to 5 mm thickness (see Tips). Cut into desired shapes and transfer onto prepared baking trays. Repeat rolling and cutting until all dough has been used. Bake for 8-10 minutes (170°C) or until lightly golden. Allow to cool on baking trays for 10 minutes, then transfer onto wire racks to cool completely. Once cool, decorate biscuits as desired.

Useful Items

plastic wrap, silicone bread mat, 2 baking trays (30 x 40 cm), baking paper, rolling pin, cookie cutter, wire rack, refrigerator, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • To freeze biscuit dough, place into freezer instead of refrigerator after wrapping in plastic wrap. Remove from freezer 1-2 hours before rolling. You may need a little extra flour when rolling. Dough can be frozen for up to 1 month.
  • When rolling out the dough, flour your work surface well; as you roll, lift and rotate your dough to prevent sticking.
  • Gluten free simple butter biscuits: For a gluten free biscuit substitute plain flour with 350 g gluten free plain flour.
