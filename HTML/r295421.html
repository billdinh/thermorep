Steamed Cabbage and Pork Dumplings

Ingredients

Yellow Dumpling Wrappers

  • 2 oz carrots, cut into pieces
  • 3 oz water, divided
  • 2 ½ oz all-purpose flour, plus extra to dust
  • 1 pinch salt

Cabbage Dumplings

  • 5 ½ oz napa cabbage, diced (1 in.)
  • 4 oz carrots, cut into pieces (approx. 2 carrots)
  • ½ tsp salt, to taste
  • 2 ½ oz dried bean curd, broken into pieces
  • ½ oz dried mushrooms
  • ½ oz ginger, peeled, cut into pieces
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp black sesame oil
  • ¼ tsp ground white pepper
  • ½ tsp glutinous rice flour
  • 20 oz water

Pork Dumplings

  • ½ oz green onions, cut into pieces
  • 21 oz water, divided
  • 1 tsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp black sesame oil
  • ½ tsp chicken stock paste, to taste
  • 9 oz ground pork, broken into pieces
  • 20 store-bought dumpling wrappers (round)
  • ponzu sauce, to serve
  • fresh red chilies, sliced, to garnish

Preparation

Yellow Dumpling Wrappers

  1. Lightly grease Varoma dish, Varoma tray, and a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place carrots and 2 oz water into mixing bowl and chop 30 sec/speed 8. Place a fine mesh sieve over a bowl and with aid of spatula strain thoroughly to remove and discard pulp, reserving the carrot water.
  3. Place reserved carrot water into mixing bowl and heat 5 min/212°F/speed 0.5. Allow mixing bowl to cool until screen temperature reaches 140°F, then add flour, 1 oz water and salt and knead 1 min/. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and knead a further 1 min/. Transfer dough to prepared bowl, cover with cling wrap and let rest 15 minutes.
  4. Divide dough into 20 equal pieces and roll out (⅛ in.-¼ in. thick). Dust with flour as needed to prevent sticking. Cover with cling wrap and set aside.

Cabbage Dumplings

  1. Place cabbage, carrots and salt into mixing bowl and chop 20 sec/speed 5. Transfer into simmering basket and set aside to drain.
  2. Place dried bean curd, dried mushrooms and ginger into mixing bowl and chop 6 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Add reserved chopped cabbage mixture, sugar, black sesame oil, pepper and glutinous rice flour and mix 6 sec/speed 6. Transfer vegetable filling to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place 1 tbsp reserved vegetable filling in the center of a reserved dumpling wrapper, run a wet finger along edge of dough to help seal and fold in half. Pick up both corners of the semi-circle and bring them toward each other. Pinch corners together and shape into a round disk. Repeat with remaining ingredients. Transfer dumplings into Varoma dish and Varoma tray.
  4. Place 20 oz water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 1.

Pork Dumplings

  1. Place green onions, 1 oz water, oyster sauce, sesame oil, stock paste and ground pork into mixing bowl and mix 20 sec/speed 4. Transfer pork filling to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place 1 tbsp reserved pork filling in the center of each store-bought wrapper, run a wet finger along edge of dough to help seal and fold in half. Pick up both corners of the semi-circle and bring them toward each other. Pinch corners together and shape into a round disk. Repeat with remaining ingredients. Transfer dumplings into Varoma dish and tray.
  3. Place 20 oz water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  4. Serve steamed dumplings hot with ponzu sauce and sliced chilies.

Useful Items

cling wrap, fine mesh sieve

Hints & Tricks

  • Dumplings can have many shapes, but for a Chinese New Year celebration the shape should be round and disk-shaped to represent gold and silver coins for a prosperous new year.
  • If necessary, add 1-2 tablespoons water to vegetable filling and mix 6 sec/speed 6 until filling comes together.
