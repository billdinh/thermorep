Four-seed honey butter

Ingredients

  • 150 g sesame seeds
  • 50 g pepitas
  • 50 g sunflower seeds
  • 50 g golden linseeds
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 2 tbsp grapeseed oil

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 150°C. Line a large baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh sesame seeds, pepitas, sunflower seeds and linseeds into it. Transfer seeds onto prepared baking tray and bake for 20-25 minutes (150°C) until lightly coloured and aromatic, stirring occasionally.
  3. Place seeds into mixing bowl and chop 20 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add honey and oil and blend 30 sec/speed 7.
  5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and blend 1-3 min/speed 4, until desired smoothness is achieved (see Tip).
  6. Transfer seeded butter into a sealable storage jar and place into refrigerator until ready to serve.

Useful Items

baking paper, bowl, baking tray (30 x 40 cm), sealable storage jar or container, oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • If needed, add an extra 1-2 tsp oil and/or honey and blend 10 sec/speed 4 or until combined. Repeat if needed. The amount of oil required will vary according to the natural oiliness of the seeds and the desired consistency of the butter. More oil will produce a smoother, runnier result.
