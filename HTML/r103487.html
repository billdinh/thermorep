Curry lamb cutlets

Ingredients

Curry powder

  • 30 g coriander seeds
  • 30 g cumin seeds
  • 2 tbsp fennel seeds
  • 10 cardamom pods
  • 5 cloves
  • 1 cinnamon quill
  • 1 tsp whole black peppercorns

Curry sauce, cutlets and rice

  • 50 g vegetable oil, plus extra for frying
  • 1 brown onion (approx. 150 g), cut into halves
  • 1 long red chilli, trimmed
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 20 g fresh coriander, leaves, stalks and roots, plus extra leaves for garnishing
  • 4 cm cube fresh ginger, peeled
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 400 g canned coconut cream
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1200 g water
  • 100 g red lentils
  • 8 - 10 lamb cutlets (approx. 800 g)
  • 6 fresh curry leaves (optional)
  • 300 g white rice

Preparation

Curry powder

  1. Place all curry powder ingredients into mixing bowl and dry roast 10 min/Varoma/speed 2, without measuring cup. Allow to cool slightly in mixing bowl, then insert measuring cup and mill 30 sec/speed 10. Transfer into a sealable container and set aside.

Curry sauce, cutlets and rice

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Place oil, onion, chilli, garlic, coriander and ginger into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 7.
  3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then cook 5 min/Varoma//speed 2.
  4. Add turmeric, coconut cream, salt, 300 g of the water, lentils and 2 tablespoons of the reserved curry spice and cook 10 min/Varoma//speed 2. Set aside in mixing bowl.
  5. Place a large frying pan over high heat and add oil. Place cutlets into pan and sear on each side (approx. 2-3 minutes on each side) until browned. Transfer cutlets into a casserole dish (approx. 25 x 20 cm).
  6. Add curry leaves (optional) to casserole dish. Pour reserved curry sauce over cutlets and stir to combine. Clean and dry mixing bowl. Bake for 15-20 minutes (180°C), or until lamb and lentils are cooked to your liking. Meanwhile, make rice.
  7. Insert simmering basket, then weigh rice into it. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and rinse rice until water runs clear.
  8. Place remaining 900 g water into mixing bowl, then re-insert simmering basket with rice and cook 13-15 min/100°C/speed 4, until cooked to your liking. Divide lamb cutlets, lentils and rice between serving bowls, then garnish with coriander before serving. 

Useful Items

sealable container, large frying pan, casserole dish (25 cm x 20 cm), serving bowls, oven, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with naan bread on the side.
  • Clean and scrape the roots of your coriander to remove the tougher, more fibrous parts on the outside before adding to the mixing bowl.
