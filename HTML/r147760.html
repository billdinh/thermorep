Potato and Cheese Bites

Ingredients

  • 100 g hard cheese, cut in pieces (e.g. Emmental, Cheddar)
  • 1600 g potatoes, peeled, cut in pieces
  • 300 g onions, quartered
  • 5 tsp fine sea salt, or to taste
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 15 g fresh parsley leaves
  • 2 - 3 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves only
  • 15 g fresh chives, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 100 g double cream
  • 50 g cornflour
  • 10 large eggs
  • ¼ tsp ground nutmeg
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
415 kJ / 99 kcal

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C and line a deep baking tray (24 cm x 24 cm x 4 cm) with baking paper.
  2. Place cheese in mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl.
  3. Place 1000 g potatoes, 150 g onions, 2 tsp salt and ¼ tsp pepper in mixing bowl then chop 17 sec/speed 4 while stirring with spatula through hole in mixing bowl lid. Transfer to Varoma dish.
  4. Place 600 g potatoes, 150 g onions, 2 tsp salt and ¼ tsp pepper in mixing bowl then chop 13 sec/speed 4. Transfer to Varoma dish and allow to drain for 15 minutes before squeezing out any excess moisture and transferring to prepared baking tray.
  5. Place garlic, parsley and thyme in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 8.
  6. Add chives, reserved grated cheese, cream, cornflour, eggs, 1 tsp salt and nutmeg then mix 10 sec/speed 4. Pour over chopped vegetables in baking tray and mix together.
  7. Bake for 1 hour - 1 hour 10 minutes (180°C), covering with foil if necessary after 45 minutes to prevent top from browning too much.
  8. Leave for 10 minutes before removing to a chopping board. Peel off the paper then cut into 30 pieces. Serve hot or cold.

Useful Items

baking paper, aluminium foil, deep baking tray (24 cm x 24 cm x 4 cm), oven
