Steamed sticky rice with pork (Rice tube puddings)

Ingredients

Sweet chilli sauce

  • 2 tbsp red chilli hot sauce
  • 3 tsp tomato sauce (to make your own, refer to the recipe in The Basic Cookbook)
  • 30 g sugar
  • 100 g water
  • ½ tsp cornflour

Rice tube puddings

  • 450 g sushi rice
  • 50 g soy sauce
  • 30 g sugar
  • 1 pinch ground white pepper
  • 200 g lean pork fillets, cut in small strips (5 cm x 1 cm x 0.5 cm)
  • 30 g oil
  • 12 - 15 dried shiitake mushrooms (approx. 20 g), soaked to soften, cut in strips
  • 50 g dried prawns (optional)
  • 600 g water
  • 5 sprigs fresh coriander, cut into pieces, for garnishing
  • 10 g roasted unsalted peanuts, cut into pieces, for garnishing

Preparation

Sweet chilli sauce

  1. Place red chilli sauce, tomato sauce, sugar, water and cornflour into mixing bowl and cook 5 min/100°C/speed . Transfer into a small bowl and set aside.

Rice tube puddings

  1. Place simmering basket onto mixing bowl lid and weigh rice into it, then rinse under cold running water. Transfer drained rice to a bowl and add water to cover. Set aside and soak for a minimum 1 hour or place into refrigerator overnight.
  2. Place a medium bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh soy sauce, sugar, pepper and pork into it. Stir to coat pork with sauce and set aside to marinate for 10 minutes. Meanwhile, continue with recipe.
  3. Place oil, shiitake mushrooms and dried small shrimp (optional) into mixing bowl and sauté 5 min/120°C/speed 1.
  4. Add marinated pork along with marinade and stir-fry 5 min/100°C/speed 1.
  5. Add drained rice and stir-fry 5 min/100°C/speed 1, then transfer mixture to 10 dariole moulds (5 cm, 5 cm height). Place filled moulds into Varoma dish.
  6. Place water into mixing bowl, place Varoma into position and steam 40 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove Varoma and unmould rice puddings. Garnish with chopped coriander and chopped roasted peanuts and serve with sweet chilli sauce.

Useful Items

dariole mould (5 x 5 cm), small bowl, bowl, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Dried small shrimp can be purchased in Asian grocers. You can omit them or replace them with shallots, for a different but pleasant result.
    - Instead of dariole moulds, you can also use 7 heat-resistant drinking glasses (7 cm diameter, 6 cm height). In this case, increase water to 900 g and steaming time to 50-55 minutes. If your moulds or glasses are a different size than specified, you may need to adjust the steaming time.
