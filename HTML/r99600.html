Pork fillet with mustard

Ingredients

  • 75 g dry vermouth
  • 100 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 60 g brown onion, cut into halves
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 2 tsp seeded (wholegrain) mustard
  • 1 tsp hot English mustard
  • 2 tsp Chicken stock paste (see Tips)
  • 1 tsp sea salt, to taste
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper, to taste
  • 450 g pork fillet, cut into thin slices (1 cm)
  • 100 g fresh mushrooms, cut into slices
  • 2 tsp crème fraîche
  • 2 - 3 tsp cornflour
  • 1 - 2 sprigs flat-leaf parsley, leaves only, chopped for garnishing

Preparation

  1. Place dry vermouth or Madeira, cream, onion, garlic, seeded mustard, hot English mustard, Chicken stock paste, salt and pepper into mixing bowl. Layer sliced pork fillet and mushrooms in alternating layers into the simmering basket, then insert simmering basket into mixing bowl. Cook 10 min/100°C/speed 1.
  2. Stir pork and mushrooms in simmering basket with aid of spatula to ensure even cooking, then cook 10-13 min/100°C/speed 1, until pork is cooked. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula. Transfer pork and mushrooms into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and set aside to keep warm.
  3. Add crème fraîche and cornflour and cook 3 min/80°C/speed 4.
  4. Place pork and mushrooms onto serving plates, pour mustard sauce over the top and garnish with parsley and serve immediately.

Useful Items

serving plates, thermal serving bowl or other large bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with steamed vegetables or a side salad.
  • Please refer to The Basic Cookbook or Everyday Cookbook for the Chicken stock paste recipe.
  • This recipe can be increased to feed a larger family by cooking the pork and mushrooms in the Varoma. Cooking time will need to be adjusted accordingly.
