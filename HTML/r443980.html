Black Forest Crepes

Ingredients

Batter

  • ½ vanilla bean
  • 2 oz sugar
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 4 oz whole milk
  • 2 oz all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1 oz unsalted butter, diced

Cherry Filling

  • 8 oz frozen cherries, pitted, thawed
  • 2 oz sugar
  • 2 tsp lemon juice

Crepes

  • 2 oz unsalted butter, divided

Whipped Cream

  • 6 oz heavy whipping cream
  • 1 tsp sugar

Assembly

  • chocolate sauce, to drizzle

Preparation

Batter

  1. Split half a vanilla bean lengthwise and scrape out seeds. Place vanilla bean seeds and pod, sugar and salt into mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 10.
  2. Add milk, flour, eggs, cocoa powder and butter and mix 30 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl, cover with cling wrap and refrigerate 30 minutes or up to overnight. Rinse and dry mixing bowl.

Cherry Filling

  1. Place cherries, sugar and lemon juice into mixing bowl and without measuring cup cook 15 min/212°F//speed 1. Transfer to a separate bowl, cover with cling wrap and refrigerate 30 minutes or up to overnight.

Crepes

  1. Heat butter (1 tsp at a time, per crepe) in a skillet (8 in.-10 in.) over medium heat. Add ¼ cup reserved batter to center of pan, swirling while pouring, to cover base of pan completely. Cook until bubbles begin to form and crepe pulls away from the edge (approx. 1-2 min.). Flip and cook a further 1 minute. Transfer to a plate. Repeat with remaining batter. Keep crepes warm while making whipped cream.

Whipped Cream

  1. Ensure mixing bowl is clean and dry and insert butterfly whisk. Place whipping cream and sugar into mixing bowl and without setting a time whip speed 3, stopping when desired stiffness is achieved, watching carefully to avoid overwhipping. Remove butterfly whisk. Transfer whipped cream to a bowl and set aside.

Assembly

  1. To assemble, spread crepes with reserved whipped cream. Loosely fold in quarters and layer (shingled) on a serving platter. Serve warm or at room temperature, topped with reserved Cherry Filling and a drizzle of chocolate sauce, to taste.

Useful Items

cling wrap, serving platter, skillet (10 in.), refrigerator
