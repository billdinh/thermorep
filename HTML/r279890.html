Champagne Jellies

Ingredients

  • 2 oz sugar
  • 6 packets/envelopes gelatin powder, unflavored
  • 1 bottle champagne (750 ml), room temperature
  • 8 oz ginger ale, room temperature
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
121 kJ / 29 kcal
Protein
1 g
Carbohydrates
3 g
Fat
0 g

Preparation

  1. Place sugar, gelatin, champagne and ginger ale into mixing bowl and cook 10 min/212Â°F/speed 2.
  2. Pour champagne mixture into silicone ice cube trays or individual portion cups (approx. 1 oz ea.) and refrigerate until set (approx. 2 hr.).
  3. Run a butter knife around the edges of silicone mold to release the jellies. Transfer to a serving platter and refrigerate until ready to serve.

Useful Items

silicone ice cube trays, butter knife, serving platter, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Mixture can be poured into a baking pan (9 in. x 9 in.). To remove, run a knife around the edges, and use a small spatula to remove from baking pan. Slice into squares and refrigerate until needed.
  • Garnish with colored sprinkles, coarse sugar or gold leaf.
  • Place cubed Champagne Jellies into champagne coupes and top with chilled champagne.
