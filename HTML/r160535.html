Gluten-Free Pizza Dough

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil, plus 2 tsp to grease and brush, divided
  • 8 oz white rice flour, plus extra to dust
  • 4 oz tapioca starch
  • 2 oz potato starch
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp white vinegar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 packet/envelope rapid rise yeast
  • 6 oz water
  • 1 large egg

Preparation

  1. Lightly coat two pizza pans (approx. 10 in.) with 2 tsp olive oil and set aside.
  2. Place rice flour, tapioca starch, potato starch, sugar, vinegar, salt, yeast, 2 tbsp olive oil, water and egg into mixing bowl and mix 20 sec/speed 5, then knead 1 min/. Transfer dough to a surface dusted with white rice flour. Form into a ball, divide in half and transfer to prepared pizza pans.
  3. Preheat oven to 450°F.
  4. Use wet fingertips to spread dough out to the edges of prepared pans and pinch edges to form a slightly thicker edge. Cover with a damp kitchen towel and let rise for 30 minutes or until dough proofs and springs back slightly when touched.
  5. Bake for 12 minutes (450°F) until underside is lightly golden and edges start to brown slightly. Remove from oven and brush edges with olive oil.
  6. Spread with your favorite pizza sauce, cheese and desired toppings. Bake a further 10-12 minutes (450°F) until toppings are warm and bubbly. Cut and serve immediately.

Useful Items

kitchen towel, pastry brush, pizza pans (approx. 10 in.), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • To prepare ahead: after the pizza crust is first baked, allow to cool on pizza tray before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely. Tightly wrap in cling wrap and freeze until needed. The frozen pizza crust does not need to be thawed before baking.
