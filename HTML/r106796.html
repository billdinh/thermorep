Vanilla mascarpone parfait

Ingredients

  • unsalted butter, for greasing
  • 125 g caster sugar
  • 35 g water
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 2 vanilla beans
  • 250 g mascarpone cheese
  • 250 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 1 pomegranate, arils only, for garnishing
  • 75 g blackberries, for garnishing

Preparation

  1. Grease and line a loaf tin (18 x 7 x 6 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place sugar into mixing bowl and mill 30 sec/speed 9.
  3. Add water and heat 7 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  4. Insert butterfly whisk. Add egg yolks and whisk 11 min/speed 4 until thick and pale. Remove butterfly whisk. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  5. Split vanilla beans lengthways, scrape out seeds and discard pods. Place seeds and mascarpone into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 1 or until smooth. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  6. Insert butterfly whisk. Place cream into mixing bowl and whip 10 sec/speed 3.
  7. Add reserved mascarpone mixture and mix 10 sec/speed 3.
  8. Add 40 g of the reserved egg mixture and whisk 5 sec/speed 2. Add remaining egg mixture and mix 5 sec/speed 1.
  9. Transfer into prepared tin and freeze for a minimum of 2 hours or overnight. Remove from freezer and allow to stand for 5 minutes to soften slightly. Garnish with pomegranate arils (seeds) and blackberries before serving.

Useful Items

loaf tin (18 x 7 x 6 cm), baking paper, bowl, freezer

Hints & Tricks

  • If pomegranates or blackberries are not in season, use any fresh fruit of choice.
  • You can use frozen blackberries in this recipe, simply allow them to thaw and drain excess liquid before using.
