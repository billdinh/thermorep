Tapioca Balls with Lemon and Poppy Seed Sauce

Ingredients

Sauce

  • 80 g olive oil
  • 30 g Dijon mustard
  • 10 g agave syrup
  • 10 g poppy seeds
  • 1 lemon, juice only

Tapioca Balls

  • 150 g stale white bread, torn in pieces
  • 10 g fresh parsley leaves
  • 1000 g water
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 680 g cassava, cut in pieces (3 cm)
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 130 g onions, quartered
  • 30 g oil (e.g. sunflower or groundnut), plus extra for frying
  • 60 g plain flour
  • 2 medium eggs, beaten
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
657 kJ / 157 kcal

1 g

Preparation

Sauce

  1. Place all sauce ingredients in mixing bowl and mix 20 sec/speed 3. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Wash and dry mixing bowl.

Tapioca Balls

  1. Place bread and parsley in mixing bowl then grate 15 sec/speed 9. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place water and salt in mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket and weigh in cassava then cook 20 min/100°C/speed 4. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula then set aside and discard cooking water.
  3. Place garlic, onions and oil in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Sauté 5 min/120°C/speed 1.
  4. Add cooked cassava and flour then grind 15 sec/speed 5. Form mixture into 64 balls (approx. Ø 2.5 cm). Dip in beaten eggs then in reserved breadcrumbs.
  5. Heat a little oil in a frying pan over a medium heat. Fry balls for 2-3 minutes until cooked through and golden brown. Drain on paper towel to remove excess oil. Serve with sauce.

Useful Items

frying pan, paper towel, slotted spoon, hob

Hints & Tricks

  • Cassava is a starchy tuberous root. It provides a rich source of carbohydrates as well as containing a significant amount of protein, minerals and vitamins A, B and C.
