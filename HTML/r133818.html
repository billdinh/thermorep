Coconut Macaroons (gluten free)

Ingredients

  • 2 egg whites, from large eggs
  • 380 g sweetened condensed milk
  • 380 g desiccated coconut
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
436 kJ / 104 kcal
Protein
1.7 g
Carbohydrates
6.7 g
Fat
7.8 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C and line 2 large baking trays with baking paper.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg whites in very clean mixing bowl and whisk 2 min/speed 3.5 until stiff peaks form. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Remove butterfly whisk.
  3. Place condensed milk, desiccated coconut and vanilla extract in mixing bowl then mix 20 sec//speed 4.
  4. Add whisked egg whites and mix 20 sec//speed 1.5 while stirring with aid of spatula through hole in mixing bowl lid. Finish mixing by hand until egg is fully incorporated.
  5. Spoon tablespoons of mixture onto prepared baking trays (see tip), leaving 3-4 cm between each macaroon. Bake for 12-15 minutes (160°C) until golden brown. Allow macaroons to cool on trays for 10 minutes before transferring to wire racks to cool completely.
  6. Serve once cool or store in an airtight container until needed.

Useful Items

wire racks, 2 baking trays and baking paper, baking paper, airtight container, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Grease the inside of the tablespoon with a little coconut oil to help mixture drop off more easily onto trays.
  • For a truly allergy free recipe, always check that all your ingredients come from a source free of allergens.
  • For a chocolate variation, dip the bases of the macaroons in 150 g melted dark chocolate. Set aside on a piece of baking paper and allow to set before serving.

This classic gluten free teatime treat is really quick to make…and dipping them in chocolate gives them an extra indulgence!
