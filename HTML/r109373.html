Carrot Croquettes

Ingredients

  • 7 oz carrots (approx. 3-4 carrots) peeled, cut into pieces (approx. ¾ in.)
  • 2 oz white onions, peeled and cut into pieces (approx. ¼ large onion)
  • 1 oz oil
  • 3 ½ oz salted butter, plus extra for greasing
  • 7 oz all-purpose flour, plus extra for breading
  • 17 ½ oz whole milk
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg, to taste
  • ½ tsp salt, to taste
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper, to taste
  • 3 large eggs
  • 5 ½ oz bread crumbs, for breading (see Tip)
  • wheat flour, for breading
  • oil, for frying
  • fresh flat-leaf parsley, to garnish

Preparation

  1. Place carrots, onions, oil, butter and flour into mixing bowl and sauté 10 min/250°F/speed 1.
  2. Add milk, nutmeg, salt and pepper and cook 10 min/212°F/speed 4.
  3. Line a deep baking pan (approx. 12 in. x 7 in. x 2 in.) with parchment paper and pour in the carrot mixture to an even thickness of ¾ inch. Refrigerate for 30 minutes. In a bowl, beat eggs with a whisk.
  4. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in bread crumbs. Set aside.
  5. When the mixture is completely cold, cut into 6 squares and cut each square in half on the diagonal to make 12 carrot shaped triangles. Roll triangles in flour, dip in beaten eggs, and finally the reserved bread crumbs.
  6. Heat oil in frying pan and fry each breaded triangle until lightly browned on both sides (approx. 1 minute each side). Drain on paper towels. Transfer to a serving dish, garnish with parsley leaves to look like carrots and serve.

Useful Items

baking pan and parchment paper, bowl, wire whisk, paper towel, deep frying pan, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Make your own bread crumbs before starting recipe - Place 5.5 oz bread into mixing bowl and chop 15 sec/speed 9. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
