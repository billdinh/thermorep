Quick Christmas Cake

Ingredients

  • 400 g dried mixed fruit (see tip)
  • 120 g brown sugar
  • 125 g unsalted butter, diced, plus extra for greasing
  • 115 g water
  • 115 g brandy, plus optional 2 Tbsp
  • ½ tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • ½ tsp ground mixed spice
  • 1 medium egg
  • 130 g self-raising flour
  • 130 g plain flour
  • 70 g almonds, mixture of blanched and skin on

Preparation

  1. Place dried fruit, sugar, butter, water, 115 g brandy, bicarbonate of soda and mixed spice in mixing bowl then cook 8 min/100°C//speed . Remove mixing bowl and set aside to cool to around 50°C (see tip).
  2. Preheat oven to 180°C. Grease and line a springform tin (Ø 20 cm) with baking paper.
  3. Place cooled mixing bowl back into position. Add egg, self-raising flour and plain flour then mix 40 sec//speed 3 until well combined. Transfer to prepared tin and decorate with almonds. Bake for 60-70 minutes (180°C) until a skewer inserted in centre comes out clean.
  4. Leave in tin to cool completely before pouring over remaining 2 Tbsp brandy (if using). Store in an airtight container, serving when required (see tip).

Useful Items

airtight container, springform tin (Ø 20 cm), baking paper, skewer, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Full of fruit and spice, Thermomix® makes this last-minute cake so easy to prepare. Check out our many other Christmas recipes for even more inspiration when preparing for the big day.
  • If you have time, soak the fruit in brandy for up to 12 hours before using.
  • The temperature of the bowl can be checked by returning it to its position and reading the temperature on the display.
  • The cake can also be frozen then defrosted before serving.
