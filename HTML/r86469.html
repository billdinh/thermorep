Tarte Bourguignonne

Ingredients

Pastry

  • 85 g butter, cold, diced, plus extra for greasing
  • 250 g plain flour, for dusting
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 100 g water, ice cold

Filling

  • 100 g Gruyère cheese, cut in pieces
  • 300 g onions, quartered
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 400 g beef mince
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper

White Sauce

  • 40 g plain flour
  • 40 g butter, diced
  • 400 g milk
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper

Preparation

Pastry

  1. Grease a tart tin (Ø 28 cm) and set aside. Place butter, flour and salt in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  2. Add water and mix 50 sec/. Roll out on a floured surface (2-3 mm thick) and fit into tart tin. Prick base with a fork then cover and chill in fridge for at least 30 minutes. Meanwhile, preheat oven to 200°C.
  3. Line pastry with baking paper and fill with baking beans then bake blind for 15 minutes (200°C). Meanwhile, prepare filling.

Filling

  1. Place cheese in mixing bowl and chop 9 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place onions, oil and ½ tsp salt in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then sauté 5 min/100°C/speed . Spread softened onions onto prepared pastry base, forming a first layer.
  3. Place beef mince, ½ tsp salt and pepper in mixing bowl then sauté 7 min/100°C//speed . Arrange meat on top of onion layer then set aside while preparing white sauce.

White Sauce

  1. Place flour, butter, milk, salt and pepper in mixing bowl then cook 6 min/90°C/speed 4.
  2. Spread sauce evenly over meat layer then sprinkle with reserved grated cheese. Bake for 40 minutes (200°C) until golden on top and bubbling at the edges. Serve warm - works well with a dressed salad.

Useful Items

fluted tart tin (Ø 28 cm), rolling pin, baking beans, baking paper, oven, refrigerator
