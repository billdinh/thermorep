Stuffed Peppers with Herbed Quinoa

Ingredients

  • 30 g vegetarian hard cheese, cut in pieces (3 cm)
  • 8 sprigs fresh mixed herbs (e.g. rosemary, sage, thyme), leaves only, plus extra for garnishing
  • 1500 g water
  • 150 g quinoa, rinsed and drained
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 onion (approx. 150 g), quartered
  • 20 g sun-dried tomatoes, drained
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 15 pitted black olives, sliced
  • 1 Tbsp pickled capers, drained
  • 40 g toasted pine nuts,
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt, or to taste
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper, or to taste
  • 6 mixed peppers (small-medium), tops removed and deseeded

Preparation

  1. Place cheese and herbs in mixing bowl then grate 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place 1000 g water in mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket, weigh in quinoa then cook 12 min/100°C/speed 4. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and set quinoa aside to drain. Rinse and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Place garlic, onion, tomatoes and oil in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then sauté 2 min/100°C/speed 1.
  4. Add reserved cheese mixture, reserved quinoa, olives, capers, pine nuts, salt and pepper then combine 3 sec//speed 4. Spoon mixture into peppers and arrange standing up in Varoma dish.
  5. Place remaining 500 g water in mixing bowl. Place covered Varoma into position then steam 15 min/Varoma/speed 1. Garnish with extra herbs to taste and serve immediately.
