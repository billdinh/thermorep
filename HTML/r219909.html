Mango Blackberry Spritzer

Ingredients

  • 8 oz mango flesh, quartered
  • 6 oz fresh blackberries
  • ½ oz fresh ginger, to taste
  • ½ oz freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 1 tsp brown sugar (optional)
  • 1 bottle white wine, Sauvignon Blanc (750ml)
  • ice cubes, to serve
  • 16 oz club soda
  • fresh mint leaves, to garnish
Nutrition
per 8 portions
Calories
3561 kJ / 851 kcal
Protein
5 g
Carbohydrates
77 g
Fat
2 g

Preparation

  1. Place mango, blackberries, ginger, lime juice and brown sugar into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 6.
  2. Add wine and mix 5 sec/speed 6 Strain through a fine sieve and discard solids.
  3. Serve wine mixture over ice, top off each glass with club soda (approx. 2 oz) just before serving and garnish with mint.
