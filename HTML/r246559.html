Pastitsio

Ingredients

Pasta

  • 140 g Parmesan cheese, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 1 slice bread, torn into pieces
  • 250 g macaroni pasta
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 100 g fresh baby spinach leaves

Meat sauce

  • 2 brown onions, cut into halves
  • 20 g oil
  • 400 g canned tomatoes
  • 100 g tomato paste
  • 2 tsp stock paste (see Tips)
  • 60 g dry white wine
  • 100 g water
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • 500 g beef mince

White sauce

  • 30 g salted butter, cut into pieces
  • 60 g plain flour
  • 500 g milk
  • &frac18; tsp ground nutmeg
  • ¼ tsp ground white pepper
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 2 egg yolks

Preparation

Pasta

  1. Place Parmesan and bread into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 7. Transfer into bowl and set aside.
  2. Prepare pasta according to packet instructions and transfer into a rectangular baking dish (23 x 31 cm). Add eggs and half of the reserved Parmesan mixture and stir to combine with aid of spatula. Press pasta into base of the dish. Top pasta with spinach and set aside.

Meat sauce

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Place onion into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add oil and sauté 4 min/100°C/speed 1.
  3. Add tomatoes, tomato paste, stock paste, wine, water and cinnamon. Insert simmering basket and weigh mince into it. Break up mince with aid of spatula, then cook 15 min/100°C/speed 3.
  4. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula. Transfer meat into mixing bowl and combine 5 sec//speed 3. Carefully pour meat sauce evenly over spinach and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

White sauce

  1. Place butter, flour and milk into mixing bowl and melt 7 min/90°C/speed 4.
  2. Add nutmeg, pepper, salt and egg yolks and combine 3 min/37°C/speed 4. Carefully pour sauce evenly over meat sauce. Smooth surface and sprinkle with remaining Parmesan mixture. Place into oven and bake for 40 minutes (180°C) or until lightly browned. Allow to cool for 10 minutes before serving.

Useful Items

bowl, rectangular baking dish (23 x 31 cm), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • You can use any flavour of stock paste in this recipe. Please refer to The Basic Cookbook or Everyday Cookbook for the stock paste recipes.
  • For a complete meal, serve with a green salad or other vegetables.
  • You can replace the minced beef with minced lamb in this recipe.
