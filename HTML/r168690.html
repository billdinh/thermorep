Mini Bacon and Egg Souffles

Ingredients

  • unsalted butter, to grease
  • 4 oz bacon, cut into pieces (1 in.)
  • 6 large eggs, separated
  • 30 oz water
  • 1 oz green onion, cut on the bias into thin rounds (approx. 1 lg. green onion)
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
506 kJ / 121 kcal
Protein
5 g
Carbohydrates
2 g
Fat
11 g

Preparation

  1. Grease 6 ramekins and set aside.
  2. Place bacon into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 6, then cook 10 min/250°F/speed 1. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Thoroughly clean and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg whites into mixing bowl and whip 4 min/speed 4. Meanwhile boil water on stove top.
  4. Add egg yolks, green onion, reserved bacon, salt and pepper, scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix 20 sec/speed 1. Remove butterfly whisk. Divide mixture into prepared ramekins. Rinse mixing bowl.
  5. Place boiling water into mixing bowl. Place 3 ramekins into Varoma dish, place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 8 min/Varoma/speed 2.5 Check if eggs are cooked to your liking, if needed steam a further 3 min/Varoma/speed 2.5.
  6. Remove cooked souffles and place remaining 3 ramekins into Varoma dish, place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 8 min/Varoma/speed 2.5. Check if eggs are cooked to your liking, if needed steam a further 3 min/speed 2.5.
  7. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

ramekins (approx. 3½ in.)

Hints & Tricks

  • Wash and dry mixing bowl thoroughly after cooking bacon making sure it is free from oil and water before whipping egg whites.
  • Cooking times for eggs will vary slightly, depending on size and temperature of eggs and size of ramekins.
