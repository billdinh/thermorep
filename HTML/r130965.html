Millet flour bread

Ingredients

  • 100 g millet grains
  • 100 g buckwheat grains
  • 330 g water
  • 25 g fresh yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 250 g gluten-free bread flour
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 10 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1247 kJ / 298 kcal

6.5 g

Preparation

  1. Place millet and buckwheat into mixing bowl and mill 1 min/speed 10. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place water, fresh yeast and sugar into mixing bowl and mix 1 min 30 sec/37°C/speed 2.
  3. Add gluten free flour, millet and buckwheat flour, salt, extra virgin olive oil and knead 2 min/.
  4. Lightly grease a sheet of baking paper and line a loaf tin (25 x 10 x 11 cm). With aid of spatula, transfer dough from the mixing bowl into the prepared loaf tin. Sprinkle top of loaf with sesame seeds.
  5. Cover the dough and leave to prove in a warm place for 30-45 min or until it doubles in volume. Preheat oven to 190°C.
  6. Bake the bread in the pre-heated oven (190°C) for 45-50 min. Transfer bread to a colling rack and let it cool down. Serve cut in slices.

Useful Items

cooling rack, baking paper, loaf tin (25 cm long), oven
