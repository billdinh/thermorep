Chilli Tacos

Ingredients

  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 150 g onions, quartered
  • 40 g olive oil
  • 160 g tinned chopped tomatoes
  • 1 Tbsp tomato purée
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 dried bay leaf
  • 1 pinch ground turmeric
  • 1 pinch sweet paprika
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 pinch ground cloves
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper
  • 400 g tinned kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 15 g dark chocolate (70% cocoa solids), small pieces or callets
  • 12 tacos
  • 50 g Cheddar cheese, grated
  • 2 - 3 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only
  • 100 g sour cream
  • 1 lime, quartered

Preparation

  1. Place garlic, onions and oil in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 5. Sauté 8 min/120°C//speed .
  2. Add tomatoes, tomato purée, sugar, salt, bay leaf, turmeric, paprika, oregano, cumin, ground cloves and cayenne. Insert simmering basket and weigh in beans then cook 10 min/100°C//speed . Meanwhile, preheat oven to 180°C. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula.
  3. Add dark chocolate and heat 3 min/100°C//speed . Meanwhile, warm tacos on a baking tray in oven for 3 minutes (180°C).
  4. Transfer chilli to a large bowl and stir in warm kidney beans. Fill tacos with chilli then sprinkle with grated cheese and coriander. Serve accompanied with sour cream and lime.
