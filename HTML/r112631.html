Beetroot, feta and spinach quiche

Ingredients

Pastry

  • 200 g buckwheat
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 100 g unsalted butter, chilled and cut into pieces
  • 45 g water
  • flour, for dusting

Filling

  • 100 g brown onion, cut into halves
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 250 g raw beetroot, peeled and cut into quarters
  • 5 eggs
  • 400 g full cream milk
  • 1 - 2 pinches salt
  • 1 - 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 50 g fresh spinach leaves, torn into pieces
  • 100 g feta cheese, cut into pieces (1 cm)

Preparation

Pastry

  1. Place buckwheat into mixing bowl and mill 1 min/speed 9.
  2. Add salt and butter and mix 5 sec/speed 5, or until crumbly.
  3. Add water and knead 5 sec/. Transfer dough onto a floured silicone bread mat or work surface and shape into a tight ball. Form pastry into a flat disc, wrap in plastic wrap and place into refrigerator to chill for 30 minutes. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Filling

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Line a baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper. Set aside a 22 cm tart tin.
  2. Place onion, garlic and beetroot into mixing bowl and chop 9 sec/speed 4. Transfer onto prepared baking tray and roast for 30 minutes (180°C). Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Dust a silicone bread mat or work surface and a rolling pin with flour. Divide pastry into 3 even portions. Place 2 portions onto dusted silicone mat, or between 2 pieces of baking paper, and roll out to approx. 22 cm. Transfer pastry into base of tart tin (pastry may tear, which can be common).
  4. Roll remaining pastry portion into long thin strips and use to press around the sides of tin and to fill any gaps or breaks in pastry base. Trim any excess pastry from around the edges. Press any leftover pastry to fix any gaps or broken edges. Set pastry case aside.
  5. Place eggs, milk, salt and pepper together into mixing bowl and mix 30 sec/speed 4.
  6. Fill pastry case with roasted beetroot mixture, spinach and finally with egg mixture. Arrange feta on top and bake for 40 minutes (180°C), or until set. Serve warm or cold.

Useful Items

silicone bread mat, plastic wrap, baking tray (30 x 40 cm), baking paper, tart tin (22 cm), rolling pin, refrigerator, oven
