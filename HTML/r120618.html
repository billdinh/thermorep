Pear Pastry with Cream Cheese

Ingredients

  • 300 g pears, quartered (approx. 3 pears)
  • 200 g cream cheese
  • 1 tsp vanilla icing sugar
  • ½ tsp grated lemon zest
  • 500 g puff pastry, ready-made
  • 1 tsp brown sugar
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
542 kcal
Protein
5 g
Carbohydrates
45 g
Fat
38 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C and line 2 baking trays with baking paper.
  2. Place 100 g pears, cream cheese, vanilla sugar and lemon zest in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then mix again 5 sec/speed 5.
  3. Roll puff pastry out to a rectangle (50 cm x 30 cm) then cut out 10 pieces (approx. 15 cm x 10 cm each). Arrange on prepared baking trays, leaving 5 cm between each.
  4. Spread reserved cream cheese mixture over pastry pieces, leaving a 5 mm border around edges. Cut remaining pears into slices (2-3 mm) and arrange on top. Sprinkle with brown sugar and cinnamon then bake for 20-25 minutes (200°C) until puffed and golden.
  5. Serve warm or cold - good with cream or crème fraîche.

Useful Items

2 baking trays and baking paper, rolling pin, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Alternatively, make your own puff pastry using the recipe for Quick Puff Pastry in the Basic Cookbook.
