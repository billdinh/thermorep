Cashew Butter

Ingredients

  • 400 g raw cashew nuts, unsalted
  • 20 g sunflower oil
  • 1 - 2 pinches fine sea salt, to taste
Nutrition
per 1 jar
Calories
10648 kJ / 2545 kcal
Protein
84 g
Carbohydrates
89 g
Fat
208 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C and line a baking tray with baking paper.
  2. Place cashew nuts on prepared baking tray and toast for 5-8 minutes (180°C) until light brown. Set aside to cool for 15 minutes.
  3. Place cooled cashew nuts in mixing bowl and grind 8 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add oil and salt then blend 30 sec/speed 6 until creamy. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Blend again 30 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and serve or place in an airtight jar and refrigerate until needed.

Useful Items

baking tray and baking paper, airtight jar, oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Toasting the nuts brings more depth of flavour to the cashew butter. The recipe can also be done without this step for an even speedier version.
  • Spread on toast or bread, use in smoothies or with yoghurt or muesli.
