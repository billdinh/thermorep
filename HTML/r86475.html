Red Pepper and Apple Dip

Ingredients

  • 60 g sunflower seeds
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 450 g red peppers, quartered
  • 150 g tomatoes, quartered
  • 150 g dessert apples, quartered, cored
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 15 g cider vinegar
  • ¾ tsp salt
  • ¾ tsp sugar
  • ¼ tsp ground cayenne pepper
Nutrition
per 100 gram
Calories
403 kJ / 96 kcal
Protein
2 g
Carbohydrates
7.6 g
Fat
6.4 g

Preparation

  1. Place sunflower seeds in mixing bowl and chop 6 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place garlic, onion, red peppers, tomatoes, apples, oil, vinegar, salt, sugar and cayenne pepper in mixing bowl then chop 6 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Place simmering basket instead of measuring cup on lid of mixing bowl and cook 20 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  4. Add reserved chopped sunflower seeds and blend 10 sec/speed 4 -8, increasing speed gradually. Transfer to a bowl and leave to cool, then serve or refrigerate
