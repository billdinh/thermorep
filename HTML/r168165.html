Cheese pies

Ingredients

Dough

  • 250 g orange juice
  • 90 g sunflower oil
  • 500 g flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder

Cheese filling

  • 200 g Gruyère cheese, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 180 g feta cheese, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp dried mint

Assembly and baking

  • flour, for dusting
  • sesame seeds (optional)

Preparation

Dough

  1. Place orange juice, sunflower oil, flour and baking powder into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 4, then knead 1 min/. Transfer to a bowl, cover with cling film and set aside for at least 30 minutes.

Cheese filling

  1. Place Gruyère cheese into mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 6.
  2. Add feta cheese, egg and mint and mix 5 sec/speed 6. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.

Assembly and baking

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C. Line 2 baking trays with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Transfer reserved dough onto a lightly floured surface and roll-out dough to shape into a log. Divide into 2 equal pieces and with a rolling pin, roll-out each piece into a rectangle (45 x 30 cm). Using a round cookie cutter (Ø 7-8 cm), cut circles and fill each one with 1 tsp of cheese filling. Join two opposite sides over the cheese filling and pinch them together. Prick each side with a fork for steam to escape and sprinkle with sesame seeds. Bake for 20-30 minutes (200°C) or until golden. Place on a cooling rack to cool down and serve warm.

Useful Items

cling film, baking trays, baking paper, rolling pin, cookie cutter (Ø 7.5 cm), cooling rack, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • For a golden colour, brush with milk or orange juice.
  • Cheese pies can also be shaped in crescents.
  • Olive pies: replace cheese filling in step 3 with olive filling. Olive filling: mix in a bowl 100 g chopped onion, 200 g deseeded chopped black olives and 20 sprigs finely chopped coriander.
