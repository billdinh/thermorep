Braised Cod with Thyme and Lemon

Ingredients

  • 200 g courgettes
  • 100 g carrots
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 25 g butter
  • 75 g white wine
  • 10 g lemon juice
  • 2 small pieces lemon zest (2-3 cm)
  • 2 sprigs fresh thyme
  • 1 fresh bay leaf
  • 500 g water, boiling
  • 4 white fish fillets, skinless, boneless (e.g.haddock or cod, approx. 100 g per fillet)
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
Nutrition

Preparation

  1. Using a peeler, cut courgettes and carrots into thin ribbons, cutting from all sides to get an even result. Discard core of each vegetable. Place vegetable ribbons on Varoma tray then set aside.
  2. Place garlic in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8.
  3. Add oil, butter, wine, lemon juice, lemon zest, thyme and bay leaf then heat 5 min/100°C/speed .
  4. Line Varoma dish with greaseproof paper so it comes up sides. Place fish in Varoma dish in a single layer and season with salt and pepper. Pour cooking liquid around fish then set Varoma aside.
  5. Place boiling water in mixing bowl. Place covered Varoma into position and steam 4 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  6. Remove Varoma tray and carefully turn over each fish fillet. Replace tray, cover Varoma and steam again 4 min/Varoma/speed 1. Serve immediately, with cooking liquid drizzled over fish.

Useful Items

peeler, greaseproof paper

Hints & Tricks

  • Boiling water is used so that the vegetables and fish can cook at the same time without any delay.
  • Cooking time depends on thickness of fish fillets - it is possible to remove the vegetables and continue cooking the fish if necessary.
