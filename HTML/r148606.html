Pink scramble

Ingredients

  • olive oil spray, for spraying
  • 60 g raw beetroot, peeled and cut into pieces (approx. 2 cm)
  • 2 eggs
  • 50 g feta cheese, crumbled, plus extra to serve
  • bread of choice, to serve (optional)
  • 1 avocado, flesh only, to serve
  • watercress, for garnishing (optional)
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1857 kJ / 442 kcal
Protein
34.6 g
Carbohydrates
17.6 g

Preparation

  1. Spray inside of mixing bowl generously with oil.
  2. Place beetroot, eggs and feta into mixing bowl and cook 6-8 min/90°C//speed 4, until eggs are cooked to your liking (see Tip). Serve with bread (optional), avocado, crumbled feta and watercress (optional).
