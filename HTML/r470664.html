Vegan Huevos Rancheros

Ingredients

Vegan Sour Cream

  • 5 oz raw cashews
  • 4 oz water, plus extra to soak cashews
  • 3 oz freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 tsp nutritional yeast
  • ¼ tsp salt

Ranchero Sauce

  • 1 oz vegan margarine
  • 3 oz onion, quartered
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 poblano chili, de-seeded, in pieces (1 in.)
  • 5 Roma tomatoes, quartered
  • 6 oz water
  • 1 tsp vegetable stock paste
  • ½ tsp ground cumin
  • ½ tsp ground smoked paprika

Black Beans

  • 15 oz canned black beans, drained, liquid reserved
  • ½ tsp dried Mexican oregano
  • ¼ tsp smoked paprika
  • ½ tsp salt, to taste
  • 4 oz canned Hatch green chilies, diced

Tofu Scramble

  • ½ oz vegan margarine
  • 14 oz tofu, medium firm, drained, cubed (1 in.)
  • ¼ tsp turmeric powder
  • ¼ tsp salt, to taste
  • 4 Mexican tostadas
  • avocado, sliced, to taste
  • cilantro, to garnish, to taste

Preparation

Vegan Sour Cream

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in cashews. Cover cashews with water and soak for at least 4 hours. Drain cashews and discard soaking water.
  2. Place drained cashews, lemon juice, 4 oz water, nutritional yeast and salt into mixing bowl and blend 1 min/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and refrigerate. Clean mixing bowl.

Ranchero Sauce

  1. Place vegan margarine, onion, garlic, and poblano chili into mixing bowl and cook 10 min/212°F/speed 1.
  2. Add tomatoes, water, stock paste, cumin and smoked paprika and cook 15 min/212°F/speed 1 then blend 30 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl, set aside and keep warm.

Black Beans

  1. Drain beans and reserve liquid. Place drained beans, 4 oz reserved bean liquid, oregano, smoked paprika and salt into mixing bowl and cook 7 min/212°F/speed .
  2. Add green chilies and mix 3 sec/speed 2. Transfer to a bowl, set aside and keep warm. Clean mixing bowl.

Tofu Scramble

  1. Place vegan margarine, tofu, turmeric and salt into mixing bowl and cook 4 min/185°F//speed 
  2. To assemble, top each tostada with Black Beans, Tofu Scramble, Ranchero Sauce, avocado and Vegan Sour Cream. Garnish with cilantro and serve immediately.

Hints & Tricks

  • For a spicier version, add ½ tsp chili powder, to taste when adding tomatoes to Ranchero Sauce.
  • Refrigerate any leftover Ranchero Sauce in an airtight container and use as a dip or sauce for enchiladas.
  • If you can't wait (or forgot to soak your cashews), place 5 oz raw cashews and 14 oz water into mixing bowl and heat 8 min/212°F//speed. Let sit covered for 30 minutes. Then drain cashews and discard water.
