Gruyère salmon patties with salad

Ingredients

Gruyère salmon patties

  • 80 g wholemeal bread, torn into pieces (approx. 2 slices)
  • 20 g olive oil, plus extra for drizzling
  • 50 g unsalted butter, cut into pieces
  • 350 g floury potatoes, peeled and cut into cubes (1 cm)
  • 50 g water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 4 spring onions/shallots, cut into pieces
  • 3 fresh boneless, skinless salmon fillets (approx. 150 g each), cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 40 g Gruyère cheese, cut into 8 pieces
  • olive oil spray

Salad

  • 120 g mixed baby salad leaves
  • 100 g cherry tomatoes, cut into halves
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges, to serve (optional)

Preparation

Gruyère salmon patties

  1. Place bread into mixing bowl and chop 15 sec/speed 7. Transfer onto a large plate and set aside.
  2. Place oil, butter, potatoes, water, salt and pepper into mixing bowl and cook 15 min/100°C/speed 1, then mash 30 sec/speed 3. Transfer into a bowl and set aside to cool for 10 minutes.
  3. Place spring onions/shallots into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5.
  4. Add salmon and reserved mashed potatoes and combine 20 sec//speed 3. Spread salmon mixture evenly onto a dinner plate, lightly score with a knife to divide equally into 8 and place into freezer for 15 minutes.
  5. Preheat oven to 180°C. Line a baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  6. With damp hands, take ⅛ of the salmon mixture, place a piece of Gruyère cheese into the centre and shape into a patty (2 cm thickness) to fully enclose cheese. Coat with reserved breadcrumbs and place onto prepared baking tray. Repeat with remaining salmon mixture, cheese and breadcrumbs (makes 8 patties in total). Spray patties with oil and bake for 20 minutes (180°C). Meanwhile, prepare salad.

Salad

  1. Place a serving bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh salad leaves and cherry tomatoes into it. Set bowl aside. Season with salt, pepper, oil and balsamic vinegar and toss to combine. Serve patties with salad and lemon wedges (optional). 

Useful Items

bowl, 1 plate, baking tray (30 x 40 cm), baking paper, large plate, serving bowl, freezer, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • You can make the patties in advance and store in the refrigerator for up to 4 hours, then proceed as per recipe.
