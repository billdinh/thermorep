Orange, Mango and Apple Green Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 dessert apple, (approx. 100-120 g) cored, quartered
  • 100 g ripe mango flesh
  • 1 orange, quartered and peeled, with one strip of peel left on
  • 100 g fresh baby spinach
  • 200 g kefir
  • 100 g liquid minerals (optional)
  • 100 g noni juice
  • 50 g almonds
  • 300 g water plus extra to dilute if desired
  • 200 g ice cubes
Nutrition
per 1 glass
Calories
696 kJ / 166 kcal
Protein
5.8 g
Carbohydrates
16 g
Fat
8.8 g

Preparation

  1. Place all ingredients in mixing bowl and blend 4 min/speed 10.
  2. Add extra water to dilute to a thinner consistency if desired and blend 1 min/speed 5. Enjoy a tall glass of your smoothie for breakfast or as an energizing snack.

Useful Items

refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • This recipe was inspired by Enid Taylor who is passionate about natural ways to health and the benefits of green smoothies and raw food.

  • If you prefer a thicker smoothie, add less water.
  • For a chilled smoothie, add 150 g ice cubes in Step 1, and reduce water by 150 g.
  • Although the liquid minerals are optional, they are well worth adding to smoothies for the extra nutritional benefits.
  • The best nutritional benefits are available when you drink your smoothie within 10 minutes of making it, however you can store leftovers in the fridge for up to 24 hours.

  • Substitute other fruits, nuts or edible green leaves of your choice and create your own favourite smoothies.
  • This amount serves two adults and 4 children or 4 adults.
