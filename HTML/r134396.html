Seitan Pie

Ingredients

Seitan

  • 50 g shelled walnuts
  • 280 g onions, quartered
  • 30 g soy sauce
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 950 g water
  • 175 g vital wheat gluten
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper

Purée

  • 750 g potatoes, cut in small pieces
  • 200 g fresh green peas
  • 200 g coconut milk
  • 350 g water
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp ground nutmeg

Preparation

Seitan

  1. Place walnuts in mixing bowl and grind 8 sec/speed 9.
  2. Add 80 g onions, 10 g soy sauce, 1 garlic clove and salt then chop 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add 150 g water and mix 5 sec/speed 4.
  4. Add vital wheat gluten and mix 5 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Form dough into a log and place in Varoma dish.
  5. Place remaining 800 g water in mixing bowl and place covered Varoma into position then steam 40 min/Varoma/speed 2. Remove seitan and allow to cool before cutting into pieces (4 cm). Clean mixing bowl.
  6. Place olive oil, remaining 200 g onions and 2 garlic cloves in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Sauté 5 min/120°C/speed 1.
  7. Add seitan pieces, remaining 20 g soy sauce and pepper then cook 5 min/100°C/speed 1. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.

Purée

  1. Place potatoes, peas, coconut milk, water, salt, pepper and nutmeg in mixing bowl then cook 40 min/100°C/speed 1. Towards the end of cooking time, preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Transfer half the purée to an oven-proof dish, cover with reserved seitan then top with remaining purée. Bake for 20 minutes (180°C).
  3. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

oven-proof dish, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Seitan (pure gluten) is the protein found in cereals such as wheat. Also known as 'wheat meat', 'wheat protein' or 'wheat gluten' it has a savoury taste which is fairly mild on its own. It can be bought plain or with flavourings already added and is a very versatile ingredient, especially useful for those following a vegetarian diet.
