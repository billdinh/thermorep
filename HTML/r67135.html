Spaghetti carbonara

Ingredients

  • 60 g Parmesan cheese, crust removed, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 30 g Pecorino cheese (optional)
  • 1 eschalot (30 g)
  • 150 g pancetta, cut into cubes (5 mm)
  • 20 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 1200 g water
  • ¾ tsp salt
  • 350 g dried spaghetti pasta
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 egg yolk (optional)
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper

Preparation

  1. Place Parmesan and Pecorino cheese (optional) into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  2. Place eschalot or brown onion into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add pancetta or bacon and extra virgin olive oil and sauté 5 min/120°C/speed 1. Transfer into a ThermoServer or large serving bowl and keep warm.
  4. Place water and ½ tsp of the salt into mixing bowl and bring to the boil 10 min/100°C/speed 1.
  5. Add spaghetti through hole in mixing bowl lid and cook time indicated on package/100°C//speed 1  without measuring cup until al dente. Drain spaghetti through Varoma dish and transfer into ThermoServer or large serving bowl with the pancetta or bacon. Mix well with spatula and keep warm.
  6. Before pasta has a chance to cool, place eggs, egg yolk (optional), reserved grated cheese, remaining ¼ tsp salt and black pepper into mixing bowl and mix 15 sec/speed 4. Transfer egg mixture into ThermoServer or serving bowl with pasta and pancetta or bacon. Combine thoroughly with spatula and serve immediately.

Hints & Tricks

  • If water bubbles onto mixing bowl lid while pasta is cooking, add 1 tsp oil or butter to the cooking water to reduce frothing.
  • Pasta that is cooked al dente is tender but still firm to the bite.
  • The heat of the cooked pasta will partially cook and thicken the egg, so it is important the pasta is still hot when adding the egg mixture to the ThermoServer or serving bowl.
  • Before commencing step 6, rinse the mixing bowl in cold water then dry. This ensures the eggs are not overcooked and the sauce is creamy.
