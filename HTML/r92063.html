Chicken Noodle Soup

Ingredients

  • 14 oz cooked boneless chicken, from pre-cooked roasted chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 heaping tbsp fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 3 ½ oz yellow onions, halved
  • 11 oz mixed vegetables (e.g. carrots, turnips, parsnip), cut into pieces
  • 6 oz celery, cut into pieces (½ in)
  • ½ oz unsalted butter
  • ½ tsp salt, to taste
  • ¼ - ½ tsp ground black pepper, to taste
  • 2 heaping tbsp chicken stock paste, plus 40 oz water
  • 2 oz egg noodles (long fresh egg pasta), e.g. Manischewitz brand

Preparation

  1. Place chicken meat into mixing bowl and shred 2-3 sec//speed 3.5. Transfer to a bowl and refrigerate.
  2. Place garlic, parsley and onions into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7.
  3. Add mixed vegetables and chop with aid of spatula 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add celery and butter and cook 5 min/212°F//speed 0.5.
  5. Add salt, pepper, stock paste, water and cook 25 min/212°F/speed 1.
  6. Add egg noodles and reserved shredded chicken and cook 8 min/212°F//speed 1 or until cooked to desired consistency. Serve immediately.

Hints & Tricks

  • To make your own chicken stock: remove meat from bones. Place bone pieces into mixing bowl. Place 50 oz water, 1 bay leaf, ½ onion, cut into pieces, 1 carrot, sliced (½ in.) and 2 celery sticks, sliced (½ in.) into mixing bowl and cook 30 min/212°F//speed 1.
  • If soup becomes too thick, add additional warmed chicken broth.
  • For a brothier soup: cook pasta in a saucepan on the stove according to package instructions while chicken broth is cooking. Store leftover noodles and chicken broth in separate bowls in the refrigerator.
