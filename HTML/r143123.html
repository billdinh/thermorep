Swiss Chard, Chickpea and Tamarind Stew

Ingredients

  • 350 g Swiss chard, stalks removed
  • 60 g onions, quartered
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 2 tsp caraway seeds
  • 20 g tomato purée
  • 400 g tinned chopped tomatoes
  • 40 g tamarind paste
  • 350 g water
  • 240 g tinned chickpeas (1 x 400 g tin, drained)
  • 220 g tinned butter beans (1 x 400 g tin, drained)
  • ½ Tbsp caster sugar
  • 1 ½ tsp ground coriander
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • ½ tsp dried chilli flakes

Preparation

  1. Blanch chard in a large pot of boiling hot water for 1 minute then transfer to simmering basket. Run under cold water then leave to drain.
  2. Place blanched chard in mixing bowl then chop 6 sec/speed 6. Transfer back to simmering basket and set aside.
  3. Place onions, oil and caraway seeds in mixing bowl then chop 4 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then sautè 4 min/100°C/speed .
  4. Add tomato purèe and stir 1 min/100°C/speed 1.
  5. Add tomatoes, tamarind, water, chickpeas, butterbeans, sugar, coriander, salt and pepper then cook 15 min/100°C//speed 0.5.
  6. Add reserved chopped chard and simmer 15 min/95°C//speed 1. Transfer to bowls and sprinkle with red chilli flakes. Enjoy on its own or with rice.
