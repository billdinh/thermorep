Cranberry and Camembert Stuffed Chicken

Ingredients

Cranberry Pesto Stuffing

  • 1 garlic clove
  • ½ shallot (approx. 60 g)
  • 20 g butter
  • 50 g dried cranberries
  • 30 g hazelnuts
  • 2 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 150 g Camembert, quartered

Chicken, Potatoes and Mustard Sauce

  • 1 garlic clove
  • ½ shallot (approx. 60 g)
  • 10 fresh chives, quartered
  • 20 g butter
  • 500 g water
  • 1 - 2 tsp chicken stock paste (see Tips)
  • 6 potatoes, small, well-washed and cut in pieces (3-4cm)
  • 3 chicken breasts, skinless (approx. 300 g per breast)
  • 150 - 200 g fresh green beans, trimmed
  • 20 g dry white wine
  • 2 Tbsp plain flour
  • 3 tsp English mustard
  • 2 tsp horseradish relish
  • 2 tsp wholegrain mustard
  • 1 - 2 pinches fine sea salt, to taste

Preparation

Cranberry Pesto Stuffing

  1. Place garlic, shallot and butter in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then cook 2 min/100°C/speed 1
  2. Add all remaining stuffing ingredients and chop 10 sec/speed 5. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Chicken, Potatoes and Mustard Sauce

  1. Place garlic, shallot, chives and butter in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then cook 2 min/100°C/speed 1
  2. Add water and stock paste, insert simmering basket, and place potatoes inside.
  3. Place chicken breasts on a work surface, slice each breast horizontally taking care not to cut all the way through. Fill chicken breasts with reserved cranberry stuffing, then insert 1 or 2 cocktail sticks along the edge of the opening in the breasts to enclose the filling.
  4. Place stuffed chicken breasts on Varoma tray. Secure Varoma lid, then place Varoma into position and cook 10 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  5. Turn chicken breasts over to ensure even cooking, then secure Varoma lid and cook for a further 5-10 min/Varoma/speed 2, or until chicken is cooked. Remove Varoma and transfer chicken onto a plate.
  6. Place Varoma dish back into position, then weigh in green beans. Secure Varoma lid and cook 5 min/Varoma/speed 2, or until potatoes and beans are cooked. Meanwhile, place a medium-sized non-stick frying pan over a medium-high heat and add chicken breasts. Cook each breast for 2-3 minutes each side or until golden.
  7. Transfer chicken breasts, potatoes and beans to a large bowl and cover to keep warm. Retain steaming liquid in mixing bowl.
  8. Add all remaining ingredients to mixing bowl and cook 4 min/100°C/speed 3, without measuring cup. Transfer mustard sauce to a serving jug.
  9. Remove cocktail sticks from chicken breasts, then cut each breast in half and serve immediately with potatoes, green beans and mustard sauce.

Useful Items

serving jug, non-stick frying pan, large bowl, cocktail sticks, hob

Hints & Tricks

  • Please refer to The Basic Cookbook for the Chicken stock paste recipe.
  • To serve potato mashed, add cooked potato to mixing bowl after cooking the sauce. Insert buttefly whisk and mash 20-30 sec/speed 3-4.
