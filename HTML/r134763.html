Caramelised Fennel and Ricotta Tarts with Pecorino Crisps

Ingredients

Ricotta

  • 1500 g whole milk
  • 40 g white vinegar
  • 1 lemon, finely grated zest only

Shortcrust Pastry

  • 200 g plain flour
  • 100 g unsalted butter, cold, diced (1 cm)
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • 60 g water, chilled

Filling

  • 200 g fennel, core removed, quartered, fronds reserved for garnish
  • 20 g butter, plus extra for greasing
  • 30 g cider vinegar
  • 1 Tbsp brown sugar
  • 30 - 40 g Swiss chard, stalks removed, thinly sliced
  • 16 green olives, pitted (optional)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 30 g whole milk
  • 30 g whipping cream
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt, or to taste
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper, or to taste

Pecorino Crisps

  • 2 tsp dried fennel seeds
  • 100 g pecorino cheese, cut in pieces (3 cm)

Preparation

Ricotta

  1. Place milk in mixing bowl and warm 10 min/80°C/speed 2.
  2. Add vinegar and heat 1 min/90°C/speed 1. Allow to sit for 1 minute - the milk will separate into curds and whey.
  3. Line simmering basket with two layers of muslin cloth and set over a large bowl. Pour mixture into simmering basket and allow to drain for at least 1 hour. Transfer drained ricotta (approx. 170 g) to a bowl, stir through lemon zest then rest in fridge.

Shortcrust Pastry

  1. Place flour, butter and salt in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 6.
  2. Add water and knead 10-20 sec/, until mixture comes together.
  3. Tip out and form into a ball. Wrap in cling film and place in fridge to rest for 15 minutes.

Filling

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C and grease 8 fluted tart tins (Ø 10 cm).
  2. Place fennel in mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add butter, vinegar and sugar then cook 12 min/120°C/speed 1. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh in Swiss chard then steam 2 min 30 sec/Varoma/speed 1. Transfer Swiss chard to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Using a rolling pin, roll out pastry and use to line prepared tart tins. Line with baking paper and fill with baking beans then blind bake for approx. 15 minutes (180°C) or until pastry cases are golden. Spread fennel mixture on base of each pastry case, then add olives (if using).
  5. Place remaining filling ingredients, reserved Swiss chard and reserved ricotta in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec//speed 3. Spoon mixture evenly over each tart and bake for 15-20 minutes (180°C) or until just set. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl and make crisps.

Pecorino Crisps

  1. Line a baking tray with baking paper.
  2. Place fennel seeds in mixing bowl and toast 2 min/120°C/speed 1. Allow to cool in mixing bowl (approx. 10 minutes).
  3. Add pecorino and grate 15 sec/speed 9.
  4. Spread mixture thinly over prepared baking tray. Bake for 7-10 minutes (180°C) until golden and bubbling. Allow to cool for 5 minutes before roughly breaking into crisps.
  5. Serve tarts warm, garnished with fennel fronds and pecorino crisps.

Useful Items

rolling pin, muslin cloth, large bowl, cling film, baking tray and baking paper, baking paper and baking beans, tart tins (Ø 10 cm), refrigerator, oven
