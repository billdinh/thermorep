Ruby Red Grapefruit Paloma

Ingredients

  • 20 oz grapefruits, peeled, no pith (approx. 2 grapefruits)
  • 4 oz water
  • 1 - 2 oz sugar, to taste
  • 12 oz ice cubes
  • 4 oz tequila, to taste
  • 8 oz club soda
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
649 kJ / 155 kcal
Protein
1 g

22 g
Fat
0 g

Preparation

  1. Place grapefruits, water and sugar into mixing bowl and blend 5 sec/speed 9. Strain juice through simmering basket into a pitcher and set aside. Discard solids.
  2. Place ice cubes into mixing bowl and crush 5 sec/speed 5.
  3. Divide crushed ice, reserved grapefruit juice and tequila between four glasses. Top each glass with 2 oz club soda and serve immediately.

Hints & Tricks

  • Ruby Red Grapefruits from Texas are best eaten when fully ripe as they lose some of their bitterness and can be as sweet as oranges. The pith is extremely bitter, so it's best to remove the skin and pith with a knife.
  • Instead of crushed ice, pour over whole ice cubes.
