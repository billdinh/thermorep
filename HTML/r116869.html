Chorizo and Butternut Squash Soup

Ingredients

  • 140 g onions, quartered
  • 550 g butternut squash, peeled, diced (2 cm)
  • 115 g celery stalks, cut in pieces
  • 70 g ghee
  • 100 g chorizo, sliced (picante chorizo)
  • 220 g passata
  • 400 g water, filtered
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika (hot) plus extra for serving
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 4 Tbsp coconut yoghurt, for serving

Preparation

  1. Place onions, butternut squash, celery and ghee in mixing bowl then chop 10 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then sauté 9 min/100°C/speed 1.
  2. Add chorizo, passata, water, paprika and salt then cook 15 min/100°C/speed 2.
  3. Blend 1 min/speed 1 -4.5, increasing speed gradually. Pour into 4 bowls, add 1 Tbsp yoghurt on top of each bowl and serve garnished with a sprinkle of paprika.

Useful Items

4 heat-proof soup bowls

Hints & Tricks

  • For a child friendly version, omit hot paparika and use a less hot chorizo variety.
  • It's best to use passata or chopped tomatoes from a carton or glass jar rather than a tin. This diminishes the risk of contamination that you would get from tomatoes stored in aluminium tins.
