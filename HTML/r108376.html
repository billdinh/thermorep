Layered chocolate cappucino cups

Ingredients

Ice cream

  • 100 g dark chocolate, broken into pieces
  • 200 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 300 g full cream milk
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 80 g white sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste
  • 2 tbsp Kahlua® (optional)

Chocolate custard

  • 500 g cold water
  • 2 titanium strength gelatine leaves
  • 100 g dark chocolate, broken into pieces
  • 500 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 3 tsp instant granulated coffee
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 4 egg yolks

Crumble

  • 80 g white chocolate, broken into pieces
  • 20 g unsalted butter
  • 20 g caster sugar
  • 20 g flaked almonds
  • 1 tbsp plain flour
  • 1 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1 pinch salt

Chocolate sauce

  • 100 g dark chocolate, broken into pieces
  • 100 g pouring (whipping) cream

Assembly

  • 400 g skim milk (see Tips)
  • cocoa powder, for dusting

Preparation

Ice cream

  1. Place chocolate into mixing bowl and grate 8 sec/speed 8.
  2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Add cream and cook 3 min/50°C/speed 2.
  3. Insert butterfly whisk. Add remaining ice cream ingredients and cook 10 min/90°C/speed 3, until slightly thickened.
  4. Remove butterfly whisk. Transfer mixture into a freezable container and place into freezer for a minimum 8 hours or overnight. Clean and dry mixing bowl and butterfly whisk.
  5. Remove ice cream from freezer and cut into cubes. Place half of the ice cream cubes into mixing bowl and blend 20 sec/speed 9, then mix 10 sec/speed 4. Transfer back into freezeable container. Place remaining ice cream cubes into mixing bowl and blend 20 sec/speed 9, then mix 10 sec/speed 4. Transfer back into freezable container and place into freezer to set (approx. 4-6 hours) or until ready to serve. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Chocolate custard

  1. Place a ceramic or glass bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh water into it. Add gelatine leaves and set aside to soak until softened and swelled (approx. 5-7 minutes). Gently squeeze excess water from gelatine leaves and set aside. Discard soaking water.
  2. Place chocolate into mixing bowl and grate 8 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add cream, coffee and sugar and heat 4 min/60°C/speed 2.
  4. Insert butterfly whisk. Add egg yolks and whip 30 sec/speed 4 then cook 4 min/80°C/speed 2. Remove butterfly whisk.
  5. Add soaked gelatine leaves and mix 30 min/speed 3.
  6. Divide mixture between 8 small serving glasses (approx. 80 g) and place into refrigerator to set for a minimum of 4 hours. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Crumble

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C. Line a baking tray (20 x 30 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place chocolate into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Transfer into a small bowl and set aside.
  3. Place butter into mixing bowl and melt 2 min/80°C/speed 2.
  4. Add remaining crumble ingredients, including reserved chocolate, and mix 5 sec//speed 2. Transfer mixture onto prepared baking tray and spread evenly.
  5. Bake for 15 minutes (160°C). Allow to cool, then crumble into a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Chocolate sauce

  1. Place chocolate into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add cream and heat 2 min/50°C/speed 3. Transfer into a bowl and set aside to cool completely. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Assembly

  1. Place milk into mixing bowl and heat 5 min/70°C/speed 1, or until temperature reaches 70°C.
  2. Blend 20 sec/speed 8, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid, to froth milk.
  3. Remove glasses with custard from refrigerator. Pour a thin layer of reserved chocolate sauce into each glass, then top with reserved ice cream, and sprinkle with reserved crumble. Top each glass with a layer of frothed milk foam and dust with cocoa powder. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

freezable storage container, ceramic or glass bowl, 8 serving glasses, baking tray (20 x 30 cm), baking paper, small bowl , 3 bowls, freezer, refrigerator, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Skim milk creates a better foam than full cream milk.
