Carrot Mash

Ingredients

  • 300 g carrots, cut in pieces
  • 380 g water
  • 15 g rapeseed oil
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
291 kJ / 70 kcal
Protein
1 g
Carbohydrates
5 g
Fat
5 g

Preparation

  1. Place carrots in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Add water then cook 15 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove butterfly whisk.
  3. Add rapeseed oil and blend 30 sec/speed 10. Transfer to ice cube trays, allow to cool then freeze.
  4. Once frozen, run ice cube tray under cold running water, break out frozen cubes and store in a freezer bag in freezer until needed.
  5. To serve, thaw the amount required, then warm before eating.

Useful Items

ice cube tray, freezer bag, freezer
