Hemp and matcha overnight oats

Ingredients

  • 1 - 3 tsp matcha powder, to taste
  • 20 g sultanas
  • 20 g dried apricots
  • 20 g almonds
  • 20 g currants
  • 70 g rolled oats
  • 2 - 3 tbsp hemp seeds (see Tip)
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 300 g plant-based milk of choice
  • pure maple syrup, to serve (optional)
  • fresh fruit, to serve (optional)
  • nut butter, to serve (optional)

Preparation

  1. Place matcha powder, sultanas, apricots, almonds and currants into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7.
  2. Add oats, hemp seeds, chia seeds, salt and milk, then mix 5 sec//speed 3. Divide between 4 serving bowls, cover and place into refrigerator overnight (or a minimum of 6 hours). Serve chilled with syrup (optional), fresh fruit (optional) or nut butter (optional).

Useful Items

serving bowls, plastic wrap, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Small and crunchy, hemp seeds are derived from the hemp plant. They contain all the amino acids (proteins) essential for the body and also are high in omega-3 and omega-6. These seeds can be great for reducing joint pain, improving gut health and reducing sugar cravings. You can find them at some major supermarkets, health food stores and online.
