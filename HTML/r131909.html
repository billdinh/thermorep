Seafood Pizza

Ingredients

Dough

  • 220 g water
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 tsp dried instant yeast
  • 20 g groundnut oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 400 g strong white bread flour, plus extra for flouring
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 tsp squid ink, liquid (optional)

Topping

  • 50 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 - 2 garlic cloves
  • 10 prawns, raw, large, peeled
  • 200 g tinned chopped tomatoes
  • 4 sprigs fresh basil, leaves only
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
  • 150 g fresh mussels, cleaned and debearded
  • 150 g fresh clams
  • 170 g raw squid rings

Preparation

Dough

  1. Place 50 g water, sugar and yeast in mixing bowl then warm 2 min/37°C/speed 2. Meanwhile, grease a large bowl with oil.
  2. Add oil, flour, remaining 170 g water, salt and squid ink (if using) then knead 2 min/. Transfer dough to prepared bowl, cover with cling film then set aside to rise in a warm place until doubled in size (approx. 1-2 hours). Towards end of rising time, prepare topping.

Topping

  1. Place oil and garlic in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 8. Sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  2. Add prawns and cook 3 min/120°C//speed 1. Using a slotted spoon, remove prawns to a bowl and set aside, keeping cooking liquid in mixing bowl.
  3. Add tomatoes, basil and salt to mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket and add mussels and clams. Cook 10 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove simmering basket with the aid of spatula and set aside. Blend sauce in mixing bowl 5 sec/speed 4.
  4. Preheat oven to 230°C and line 2 large baking trays with baking paper.
  5. Divide risen dough into 2 balls then, on a floured work surface, roll out 2 circles (approx. Ø 30 cm each). Transfer pizza bases to prepared baking trays.
  6. Spread tomato sauce, reserved cooked prawns, steamed mussels and clams and raw squid rings equally between pizzas then bake for 15 minutes (230°C). Serve hot, in slices.

Useful Items

large bowl, cling film, slotted spoon, 2 large baking trays, baking paper, rolling pin, oven
