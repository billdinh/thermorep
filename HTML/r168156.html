Fish tagine with tahini

Ingredients

  • 200 g onions, cut into quarters
  • 1000 g white fish fillets, skinless, boneless (e.g. dorade, sea bass)
  • 5 - 7 tsp salt
  • 100 g lemon juice, freshly squeezed
  • 300 g onions, cut into halves
  • 90 g sunflower oil
  • 700 g water
  • ½ tsp ground cumin
  • 180 g tahini paste
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 200 g bitter orange juice (see tip)
  • toasted pine nuts, to garnish
  • fresh parsley leaves, chopped, to garnish

Preparation

  1. Cut 200 g onions in slices and distribute into Varoma dish and tray. Distribute fish filets on top, making sure that some holes remain unobstructed, sprinkle with salt, drizzle with 10 g lemon juice and set aside.
  2. Place remaining onions and sunflower oil into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 3.5, then sauté 18 min/Varoma/speed 1, without measuring cup. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place 500 g water into mixing bowl, set Varoma into position and steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove Varoma and set aside. Empty mixing bowl.
  4. Place 1 tsp salt, 90 g lemon juice, ½ tsp cumin, remaining 200 g water, tahini paste, vinegar and bitter orange juice into mixing bowl and mix 1 min/speed 4.
  5. Add reserved sautéed onions and cook 10 min/100°C/speed 1 without measuring cup. Allow sauce to cool for approx. 20 minutes. Transfer fish filets into a serving dish and break them into chunks, pour tahini sauce onto fish and serve warm or at room temperature. Garnish with pine nuts and chopped parsley.

Hints & Tricks

  • Bitter orange juice can be replaced with a mixture of 50 g lemon juice and 100 g orange juice.
