Marinated White Fish Fillets with Chermoula Sauce and Couscous Salad

Ingredients

Marinated Fish

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 5 g fresh root ginger, peeled, cut in round slices (2 mm)
  • 1 red chilli, fresh, halved and deseeded
  • ½ - 1 tsp dried fennel seeds, to taste
  • 1 pinch ground cinnamon
  • ¼ tsp saffron
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 3 Tbsp olive oil
  • 2 white fish fillets, skinless (approx. 180 g each)

Chermoula Sauce

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 30 g fresh coriander, leaves and stalks, cut in pieces
  • ½ - 1 tsp ground cumin, to taste
  • 1 tsp sweet paprika
  • 1 pinch dried chilli flakes
  • 30 g freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 30 g olive oil

Couscous Salad

  • 100 g couscous
  • 15 g olive oil
  • 110 g water
  • 1 pinch ground cumin
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 5 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only
  • 1 fresh red chilli
  • 1 tsp lemon zest, finely grated
  • 1 carrot (approx. 60 g), cut in pieces
  • 50 g pitted black olives
  • 80 g tinned chick peas, drained

Steaming Marinated Fish

  • 500 g water

Preparation

Marinated Fish

  1. Place garlic cloves, fresh ginger and red chilli in mixing bowl then chop 4 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and add fennel seeds, ground cinnamon, saffron, salt and olive oil then mix together.
  2. Cover white fish fillets with marinade and refrigerate for 1 hour. Meanwhile, continue with recipe. Clean and dry mixing bowl

Chermoula Sauce

  1. Place garlic cloves and coriander in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Then chop again 5 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add ground cumin, sweet paprika, chilli flakes, lemon juice and olive oil then mix 10 sec/speed 2.5. Transfer to a small bowl, cover with cling film and refrigerate until serving. Clean mixing bowl.

Couscous Salad

  1. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in couscous then set aside.
  2. Place olive oil, water, ground cumin and salt in mixing bowl then bring to a boil 4 min/100°C/speed 1. Pour over couscous, mix together with a fork, then cover with cling film and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Place coriander, red chilli and lemon zest in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add carrot and black olives then chop 3 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a salad bowl and add drained chickpeas.
  5. Remove cling film and fluff the couscous with a fork. Add to remaining salad ingredients, mix well then place in fridge until serving.

Steaming Marinated Fish

  1. Arrange marinated fish in Varoma dish, making sure that some holes remain unobstructed so that steam can circulate.
  2. Place water in mixing bowl, set Varoma into postition and steam 15-20 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove Varoma. Serve fish fillets with chermoula sauce and couscous salad.
