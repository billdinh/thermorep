Rice salad

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs, whole, still in shell
  • 280 g zucchini, cut into small cubes
  • 120 g carrots, cut into small cubes
  • 100 g frozen green peas
  • 1200 g water
  • 1 tsp salt, plus extra to taste
  • 250 g brown rice
  • 185 g canned tuna in oil (weight includes oil), drained, broken into pieces
  • 100 g pitted black olives, whole or cut into halves
  • 100 g pickled cucumbers, cut into slices (optional)
  • 1 sprig fresh mint , leaves only
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 - 2 tsp vinegar (optional)
  • 7 cherry tomatoes, cut into halves or quarters

Preparation

  1. Place eggs into Varoma dish and place zucchini, carrots and frozen green peas onto Varoma tray. Set aside.
  2. Place water and salt into mixing bowl, insert simmering basket and weigh brown rice into it. Mix rice with spatula to wet all grains for even cooking. Place Varoma into position and steam 21 min/Varoma/speed 2 . Set Varoma aside and allow eggs and vegetables to cool.  Insert measuring cup and continue to cook rice a further 15-20 min/100°C/speed 4. Quickly rinse rice in simmering basket under cold water to stop cooking then set aside to drain.
  3. Place rice into a large serving bowl, add reserved cooked vegetables, tuna, olives, pickled cucumbers (optional) and mint leaves. Add extra virgin olive oil and vinegar or lemon juice (optional), combine, and adjust seasoning. Peel boiled eggs and cut into slices. Garnish salad with sliced eggs and cherry tomatoes. Serve at room temperature or chilled.

Hints & Tricks

  • Substitute mint with parsley.
  • Add other ingredients to taste (e.g. corn kernels, feta cheese, dried cranberries, fresh dill, fresh coriander, cashew nuts, flaked almonds).
