Chicken Katsu Curry with Rice

Ingredients

Steamed Rice

  • 1000 g water
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 350 g short grain rice

Chicken Katsu Curry

  • 15 g oil, plus extra for baking
  • 5 garlic cloves
  • 120 g onions, cut in large pieces (2 cm)
  • 200 g carrots, sliced (1 cm)
  • 1 ½ Tbsp curry powder
  • 145 g plain flour
  • 500 g chicken stock
  • 20 g brown sugar
  • 15 g Japanese soy sauce
  • ½ tsp garam masala
  • 1 dried bay leaf
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • ¼ tsp ground white pepper
  • 2 eggs, whisked
  • 160 g panko breadcrumbs
  • 4 chicken breasts, skinless (approx. 180 g each)

Preparation

Steamed Rice

  1. Place water and salt in mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket, weigh in rice and cook 18 min/100°C/speed 4. Transfer rice to a bowl and cover to keep warm. Clean mixing bowl.

Chicken Katsu Curry

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C.
  2. Place oil and garlic cloves in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5.
  3. Add onions and carrots and sauté 10 min/120°C//speed 1.
  4. Add curry powder and 45 g plain flour then cook 1 min/100°C//speed 1.
  5. Add stock, sugar, soy sauce, garam masala and bay leaf then with simmering basket instead of measuring cup on mixing bowl lid cook 15 min/Varoma//speed 1. Meanwhile, mix remaining 100 g plain flour with salt and white pepper in a bowl. Put the eggs and breadcrumbs in two separate bowls. Coat chicken breasts in seasoned flour, then dip into whisked eggs then breadcrumbs. Use clean hands to press the breadcrumbs tightly on the chicken breasts, making sure both sides are coated.
  6. Pour oil into a roasting tin to a depth of 0.5 cm. Place coated chicken breasts into oil. Bake for 15 minutes (200°C).
  7. Carefully turn over chicken and bake again for 15 minutes (200°C) or until golden and cooked through. Slice chicken and serve hot with curry sauce and rice.
