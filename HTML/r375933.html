Persian Chicken and Rice

Ingredients

  • 7 oz white basmati rice
  • 3 oz walnuts, raw (halves or whole)
  • 10 oz yellow onions, in pieces
  • ½ tsp ground turmeric
  • ½ tsp salt, to taste
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • ¼ tsp ground cinnamon
  • &frac18; tsp ground nutmeg
  • 28 oz chicken broth, low sodium
  • 1 oz unsalted pistachios, shelled, chopped
  • ½ oz dried barberries
  • 5 cardamom pods
  • 1 pinch saffron (optional)
  • 2 ½ oz pomegranate molasses
  • 1 oz dark brown sugar
  • 32 oz boneless, skinless chicken thighs, trimmed, halved
  • 1 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • pomegranate arils/seeds
  • mixed fresh herbs (mint, cilantro, parsley)

Preparation

  1. Place simmering basket onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in rice. Remove simmering basket, rinse rice under cold running water until water runs clear and set aside to drain.
  2. Place walnuts into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and dry roast 5 min/250°F/speed 0.5.
  3. Add onions, turmeric, salt, pepper, cinnamon and nutmeg and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and sauté 5 min/250°F/speed 1.
  4. Insert simmering basket with rinsed rice into mixing bowl and weigh in chicken broth, pistachios, barberries, cardamom and saffron and steam 30 min/Varoma/speed 2. Remove simmering basket. Transfer steamed rice to a serving bowl, fluff with a fork, set aside and keep warm.
  5. Add pomegranate molasses and brown sugar into mixing bowl. Place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 10 min/Varoma/speed 2. Insert measuring cup and blend 20 sec/speed 3-5, increasing speed gradually.
  6. Add chicken and flour, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 20 min/195°F//speed 1. Transfer chicken to serving platter with reserved rice and pour the sauce over chicken, to taste. Garnish with pomegranate arils and fresh herbs just before serving.
