Hamburgers

Ingredients

  • 16 oz rib eye steak, boneless, do not trim fat, cubed (approx. 1 in.)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 oz red onions
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 egg
  • 2 oz stale bread
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
849 kJ / 203 kcal
Protein
29 g
Carbohydrates
10 g
Fat
5 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and set aside.
  2. Place steak, garlic, onions, Worcestershire sauce, salt, pepper, egg and bread into mixing bowl and grind 20 sec/speed 10.
  3. Shape mixture into 4 patties, 3/4 inch thick. Place on prepared baking sheet and bake for 10-20 minutes (425°F), depending on how rare to well-done you like your burgers.

Useful Items

baking sheet and parchment paper, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Burger mixture can be baked in a muffin pan or patties can be grilled on the BBQ.
  • Foil can be used in place of parchment paper for easy clean up.
