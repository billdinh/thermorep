Gluten Free Carrot and Orange Cake

Ingredients

Sponge cake

  • butter for greasing
  • 200 g almonds
  • 30 g buckwheat grains
  • 250 g carrots, in pieces
  • 3 medium eggs
  • 100 g honey
  • 100 g golden caster sugar
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
  • ½ orange, thin peelings of skin only, plus extra zest for garnish
  • 70 g maize flour
  • ½ tsp baking powder

Topping

  • 250 g double cream
  • 1 - 1 ½ Tbsp orange liqueur
  • 20 g runny honey

Preparation

Sponge cake

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Grease a spring form cake tin (Ø 20 cm).
  2. Place almonds and buckwheat in mixing bowl then grind 10 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a large bowl and set aside.
  3. Place carrots in mixing bowl and chop 8 sec/speed 6. Transfer to bowl with almonds and set aside.
  4. Place eggs, honey, sugar, salt and orange peel in mixing bowl then mix 5 sec/speed 7.
  5. Add maize flour, baking powder and reserved almond-buckwheat-carrot mixture then mix 15 sec/speed 5. Transfer dough to prepared cake tin and bake for 30-35 minutes (180°C) until skewer inserted in centre comes out clean. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl.
  6. Leave cake to cool in tin for 10 minutes then remove and transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.

Topping

  1. Insert butterfly whisk. Place cream in mixing bowl and whisk, without measuring cup, 1 min - 1 min 30 sec/speed 3 until softly whipped.
  2. Add orange liqueur and honey then whisk 30 sec/speed 3. Remove butterfly whisk.
  3. Spread icing over top of cake and scatter with extra orange zest for decoration. Store cake in fridge and serve chilled.

Useful Items

spring form tin (Ø 20 cm), large bowl, wire rack, oven
