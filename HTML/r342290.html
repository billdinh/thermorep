Strawberry Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

Dough

  • 4 oz plain yogurt
  • 2 ½ oz unsalted butter, cubed
  • 2 oz sugar
  • 2 oz water
  • 2 ¼ tsp dried instant yeast
  • 12 oz all-purpose flour, plus extra to dust
  • 5 oz whole wheat flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp salt
  • vegetable oil, to grease

Filling

  • 16 oz strawberries, hulled, halved
  • 4 oz brown sugar
  • 1 oz lemon juice
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract

Assembly

  • 4 oz chopped pecans
  • 4 oz strawberries, hulled, diced

Glaze

  • 8 oz cream cheese, room temperature, cubed
  • 4 oz unsalted butter, room temperature, cubed
  • 5 oz confectioners sugar
  • 1 tsp lemon juice

Preparation

Dough

  1. Place yogurt, butter, sugar, water and yeast into mixing bowl and heat 4 min/110°F/speed 1.
  2. Add all-purpose flour, whole wheat flour, eggs and salt and mix 10 sec/speed 5, then knead 3 min/.
  3. Transfer dough to a lightly floured surface and knead until smooth. Shape into a ball.
  4. Lightly grease a large bowl and place dough into bowl, turning to coat all sides. Cover with cling wrap and allow to rise in a warm place (approx. 1½ hrs.) or until dough has doubled in size. Meanwhile, make filling.

Filling

  1. Place strawberries, brown sugar, lemon juice, cinnamon and vanilla into mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 15 min/Varoma/speed 1. Scrape bottom of mixing bowl with spatula. Place Varoma dish instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook a further 8 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  2. Insert measuring cup and mix 30 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and refrigerate until needed.

Assembly

  1. Lightly grease a baking pan (12 in. x 9 in. x 2 in.) and set aside.
  2. Punch down the risen dough and turn out onto a lightly floured work surface. Roll dough into a rectangle (approx. 24 in. x 12 in.) with a ¼ inch thickness.
  3. Spread filling evenly over dough, leaving a ½ inch border. Sprinkle with chopped pecans and strawberries.
  4. Roll dough into a log shape, pinching at edges to seal. Cut log into 12 equal slices (approx. 1½ in.) and place in prepared baking pan, leaving a ½ inch space between each bun. Cover with cling wrap and allow to rise in a warm place (approx. 1½ hrs.) or until rolls have doubled in size.
  5. Preheat oven to 325°F. Bake for 30-35 minutes (325°F) until golden brown, rotate pan in the last 10 minutes of baking. Set aside and allow to cool. Clean mixing bowl.

Glaze

  1. Place cream cheese, butter, confectioners sugar and lemon juice into mixing bowl and mix 30 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix a further 10 sec/speed 4. Evenly spread glaze over tops of cooled cinnamon rolls. Serve at room temperature or slightly warmed.

Useful Items

cling wrap, baking pan (12 in. x 9 in. x 2 in.), large bowl, rolling pin, oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • If rolls are browning too quickly, loosely tent the pan with foil.
