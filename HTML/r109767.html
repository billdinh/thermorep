Cashew Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 10 g fresh root ginger, peeled, cut in round slices (2 mm)
  • 400 g chicken breasts, skinless, cut into strips
  • 200 g basmati rice
  • 1400 g water
  • 150 g broccoli florets (3-5 cm)
  • 1 carrot, thinly sliced
  • 3 celery stalks, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 6 spring onions, cut in pieces (4 cm)
  • ½ red pepper, cut in pieces (1 cm)
  • 220 g tinned bamboo shoots, drained
  • 1 Tbsp sesame seeds, plus extra to serve
  • 50 g roasted unsalted cashew nuts

Sauce

  • 1 ½ Tbsp cornflour
  • 5 g fresh root ginger, peeled, cut in round slices (2 mm)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 fresh red chilli, deseeded if desired (optional)
  • 1 Tbsp groundnut oil
  • 40 g soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 2 - 3 tsp honey, to taste
  • 20 g lime juice (approx. 1 lime), to serve

Preparation

  1. Place garlic and ginger in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add chicken and mix 5 sec//speed 1. Transfer chicken to Varoma tray and set aside.
  3. Insert simmering basket and weigh in rice.
  4. Add 900 g water and rinse 20 sec/speed 6. Place Varoma into position and cook 15-20 min/Varoma/speed 4, until rice is cooked. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula then transfer cooked rice to a serving dish and cover to keep warm.
  5. Add 500 g water to mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish back into position.
  6. Weigh broccoli, carrot, celery, spring onions, red pepper and bamboo shoots into Varoma dish and, if more space is required, on top of chicken on Varoma tray. Sprinkle over sesame seeds and cashews. Cover Varoma then cook 10-15 min/Varoma/speed 3, or until vegetables are cooked to your preference (see tip). Transfer chicken and vegetables to rice and keep warm. Reserve steaming liquid.

Sauce

  1. Dissolve cornflour in 3 Tbsp reserved steaming liquid in a small bowl and set aside.
  2. Place ginger, garlic and chilli (if using) in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add oil and sauté 2 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  4. Add soy sauce or tamari, sesame oil, honey, reserved cornflour mix and reserved steaming liquid then cook 4 min/100°C/speed 3. Adjust seasoning and check consistency of sauce (it should not be too thin). Cook for a further minute if required.
  5. Add lime juice then pour sauce over reserved chicken and vegetables then stir through. Serve hot, garnished with extra sesame seeds, and with steamed rice on the side.

Useful Items

serving dish, cling film, small bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • Adjust cooking time in step 6 according to how you prefer your vegetables. For crunchy vegetables, cook 10 min/Varoma/speed 3. For softer vegetables, cook 15 min/Varoma/speed 3.
