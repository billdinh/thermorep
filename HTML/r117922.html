Birthday sponge cake

Ingredients

Sponge

  • butter, for greasing
  • 220 g raw sugar
  • 6 eggs, room temperature
  • 2 tsp natural vanilla extract
  • ½ tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 1 ½ tsp cream of tartar
  • 130 g gluten free cornflour

Cream cheese frosting

  • 250 g cream cheese, softened
  • 100 g unsalted butter, cut into pieces, softened
  • 200 g pure icing sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla bean extract
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Preparation

Sponge

  1. Preheat oven to 170°C. Grease and line the base of 2 round cake tins (20 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place sugar into mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Insert butterfly whisk. Add eggs and vanilla and whisk 15 min/speed 4, or until mixture is light and frothy.  Remove butterfly whisk.
  4. Add bicarbonate of soda, cream of tartar and cornflour and combine 50 sec//speed 1, or until fully incorporated. Pour mixture evenly into prepared tins and bake for 25-30 minutes (170°C), or until a wooden skewer inserted into the centre of the cake comes out clean. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  5. Leave sponges in tins to cool for 5 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack to cool completely (approx. 40 minutes). Set aside (see Tips).

Cream cheese frosting

  1. Place all cream cheese frosting ingredients into mixing bowl and mix 15-20 sec/speed 5, or until smooth. Spread a little frosting over base layer of cake. Place other layer on top of frosting. Spread remaining frosting over cake to cover completely. Decorate as desired (see Tips).  Serve immediately or place into refrigerator until ready to serve.

Useful Items

2 round cake tins (20 cm), baking paper, wooden skewer, wire rack, oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Chocolate curls, fresh fruit or gluten free sprinkles can be used to decorate your cake.
  • We used cachous and dragées to decorate our cake. They can be purchased in specialty cake shops and online. To ensure your decorations are gluten free, always check the label.
  • If desired, when cake is cool it can be cut into halves horizontally to form 4 layers. Sandwich each layer together with some frosting before icing.
