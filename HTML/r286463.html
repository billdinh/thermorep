Grilled Pineapple Salsa

Ingredients

  • 14 oz fresh pineapple, cored and sliced in rounds
  • 2 ½ oz red onion, quartered
  • 1 red bell pepper, de-seeded and de-stemmed
  • 1 jalapeño pepper, de-seeded and de-stemmed, halved
  • 1 oz fresh cilantro leaves
  • 1 oz lime juice (approx. 2 limes)
  • 1 oz olive oil
  • 1 ½ tsp salt
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
368 kJ / 88 kcal
Protein
1 g
Carbohydrates
11 g
Fat
5 g

Preparation

  1. Heat a large cast-iron skillet or grill over high heat until smoking. In batches, add pineapple, red onion, red pepper and jalapeño to the dry pan and cook, turning a few times, until charred on all sides but still holding their shapes (approx. 5 min.). Set aside.
  2. Place cilantro leaves into mixing bowl and chop 6 sec/speed 6. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place reserved charred red onion, red pepper and jalapeño into mixing bowl and chop 8 sec/speed 4.5. Transfer into bowl with cilantro and set aside.
  4. Place reserved charred pineapple, lime juice, olive oil and salt into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 5, if necessary scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and chop a further 2 sec/speed 5. Transfer into bowl with cilantro and combine with spatula. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

large cast iron skillet, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • Use Grilled Pineapple Salsa on chicken or fish.
