Chicken pesto pasta

Ingredients

  • 1 brown onion, cut into quarters
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 200 g zucchini, cut into pieces
  • 6 - 8 sprigs fresh basil, leaves only
  • 190 g basil pesto (see Tips)
  • 380 g water
  • 1 tbsp Chicken stock paste (see Tips)
  • 500 g boneless skinless chicken thighs, cut into strips (approx. 5 x 2 cm)
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 350 g dried penne pasta
  • 100 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 150 g frozen peas
  • 50 g Parmesan cheese, shaved, for garnishing

Preparation

  1. Place onion, garlic, zucchini, basil and pesto into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add water, stock paste, chicken and pepper and cook 10 min/100°C//speed .
  3. Add pasta and cream, stir with aid of spatula and cook 8 min/100°C//speed .
  4. Add peas, stir to combine with aid of spatula, then cook 2 min/100°C//speed . Divide between serving bowls and garnish with shaved Parmesan just before serving.

Hints & Tricks

  • Please refer to The Basic Cookbook or the Everyday Cookbook for the Chicken stock paste and Basil pesto recipes.
  • Whenever possible, we recommend making your own pesto in your Thermomix. It's especially cost effective if you have basil growing in your garden or in a window box. If homemade pesto isn't in your budget, we recommend spending a bit more money on a good-quality store bought pesto.
