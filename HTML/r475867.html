Asian-Style Sautéed Rice

Ingredients

Sauce

  • 1 ½ oz tamari sauce
  • 2 oz mirin
  • 1 - 2 tsp grated fresh ginger, to taste

Rice, Veggies and Egg

  • 10 oz long-grain white rice
  • 35 oz water
  • 3 oz carrots, sliced on the bias
  • 2 oz sugar snap peas, cut on the bias
  • 4 large eggs
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp white pepper
  • 4 green onions, cut on a bias
  • ½ oz cilantro leaves, chopped

Sauté

  • 1 oz sesame oil
  • 1 garlic clove, sliced
  • 2 oz Chinese sausage, precooked, diced (⅛ in.) (optional)

Preparation

  1. Cut parchment to fit Varoma tray (see Tip). Scrunch parchment into a ball under running water, squeeze out excess water, lay onto Varoma tray and set aside.

Sauce

  1. Place a small bowl onto mixing bowl lid, weigh in tamari, mirin and ginger, mix well with spatula to combine and set aside.

Rice, Veggies and Egg

  1. Place simmering basket onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in rice. Remove simmering basket and rinse rice under cold running water until water runs clear.
  2. Place water into mixing bowl, insert simmering basket with rice and rinse 7 sec/speed 5. Set Varoma dish into position over rice and weigh in carrots and snap peas. In a separate bowl, use a whisk to combine eggs, salt and pepper.
  3. Insert Varoma tray, pour whisked egg mixture onto parchment and sprinkle with green onions and cilantro, secure Varoma lid and steam 18 min/Varoma/speed 3. Remove Varoma and set aside. Remove simmering basket with spatula, transfer steamed rice into a large bowl and spread up the sides of bowl to cool. Remove omelet from parchment, julienne into strips and set aside. Discard cooking water and dry mixing bowl.

Sauté

  1. Place sesame oil and garlic into mixing bowl and without measuring cup, heat 3 min/250°F/speed 1. Add reserved rice and sausage and without measuring cup, cook 2 min/250°F//speed . Transfer into a large bowl and combine with reserved vegetables, sauce and reserved julienned omelet. Serve hot.

Useful Items

parchment paper, large bowl, wire whisk

Hints & Tricks

  • To cut parchment to fit Varoma tray: tear off a piece of parchment paper wide enough to cover Varoma tray. Place Varoma tray on top of parchment and cut around the tray to form an oval shape.
