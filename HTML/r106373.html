Orange and mango chia pudding

Ingredients

Chia pudding

  • 1 orange, flesh only, deseeded and cut into quarters
  • 6 fresh mint leaves
  • 350 g fresh mango cheeks, cut into pieces (2-3 cm)
  • 400 g coconut milk
  • ½ tsp ground cardamom
  • 1 tbsp pure maple syrup
  • 100 g chia seeds

Topping

  • 50 g macadamia nuts
  • 50 g raw almonds
  • 50 g shredded coconut
  • ¼ - ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • 2 tsp coconut oil
  • 120 - 150 g fresh passionfruit pulp, to serve
  • 100 - 150 g Greek yoghurt, to serve

Preparation

Chia pudding

  1. Place orange, mint and 150 g of the mango into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add all remaining pudding ingredients, except remaining mango, and combine 3 sec/speed 5. Divide mixture between 8 serving glasses or small sealable jars and place into refrigerator for 5 minutes to firm. Layer puddings with remaining 200 g mango pieces. Cover and place into refrigerator for a minimum of 2 hours (see Tips). Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Topping

  1. Place macadamias and almonds into mixing bowl and chop 1 sec/speed 6, or until roughly chopped.
  2. Add coconut, cinnamon and coconut oil and cook 5 min/50°C//speed 1. Allow to cool completely, then transfer into a sealable container until ready to use.
  3. Remove puddings from refrigerator, spoon 1-2 teaspoons passionfruit pulp over each pudding, top with 1-2 tablespoons Greek yoghurt and sprinkle with nut topping to serve.

Useful Items

plastic wrap, 8 serving glasses, sealable container, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • You can replace the fresh mango with frozen, defrosted mango and fresh passionfruit pulp with canned or jarred passionfruit pulp in this recipe.
  • These puddings are a great "on the go" breakfast option. You can make the pudding the night before (through step 2) and store in sealable jars or containers in the refrigerator overnight. Simply add the toppings in the morning before you leave the house.
  • The chia seeds and nuts provide zinc, and the yoghurt and almonds are sources of calcium.
