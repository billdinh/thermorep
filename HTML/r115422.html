Tomato and Vegetable Quiche

Ingredients

Pastry

  • 40 g butter, diced, frozen for 20 minutes, plus extra for greasing
  • 150 g wholemeal flour
  • 2 Tbsp water, ice cold
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt

Filling

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 60 g onions, quartered
  • 50 g red peppers, cut in pieces
  • 25 g olive oil
  • 70 g courgettes, cut in pieces (1 cm)
  • 50 g frozen green peas
  • 50 g leeks, sliced (1 cm)
  • 50 g grated mozzarella
  • 10 cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 120 g soy cream
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 pinches fine sea salt, or to taste
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper, or to taste
  • 2 pinches ground nutmeg, or to taste
  • ¼ tsp dried oregano
  • 8 - 10 fresh basil leaves, to taste, for serving

Preparation

Pastry

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Line base and grease sides of a tart tin (Ø 22 cm).
  2. Place flour and butter in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 6.
  3. Add water and salt then mix Turbo/1 sec/2 times until just combined. Tip out and bring together into a ball then, using your hands press into prepared tin. Place in fridge to chill for 15 minutes.
  4. Line pastry case with baking paper and baking beans then blind bake for 20 minutes (180°C). Remove baking paper and baking beans, prick base of pastry with a fork then bake for a further 5 minutes. Meanwhile, prepare filling.

Filling

  1. Place garlic, onions, red peppers and olive oil in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add courgettes, peas and leeks then, with simmering basket instead of measuring cup on mixing bowl lid, cook 6 min/120°C//speed .
  3. Pour into simmering basket to drain off liquid, pressing vegetables gently with spatula to extract more liquid. Spread drained vegetables out over partially baked pastry case. Sprinkle cheese and and cherry tomatoes onto vegetables then set aside.
  4. Place cream, eggs, salt, pepper, nutmeg and oregano in mixing bowl then mix 5 sec/speed 3. Pour over vegetables in pastry case then bake for 30-40 minutes (180°C) until set and browned. Serve topped with fresh basil leaves.

Useful Items

tart tin (Ø 22 cm), baking paper, baking beans, oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Double cream can also be used instead of soy cream.
