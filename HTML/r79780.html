Pulse and pumpkin curry

Ingredients

  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 4 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 2 brown onions (approx. 300 g), cut into halves
  • 40 g coconut oil or peanut oil
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp ground paprika
  • 2 - 4 tsp ground turmeric, to taste
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 700 g Kent pumpkin, peeled and cut into cubes (4 cm)
  • 400 g canned lentils, rinsed and drained (approx. 250 g after draining)
  • 400 g canned chickpeas, rinsed and drained (approx. 250 g after draining)
  • 180 g celery stalks, cut into pieces (1 cm)
  • 360 g water
  • 2 tsp Vegetable stock paste (see Tips)
  • 150 g fresh green beans, trimmed and cut into pieces (3-4 cm)
  • 40 g lime juice (approx. 2 limes)
  • &frac18; tsp sea salt, to taste
  • 4 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only, plus extra for garnishing
  • natural yoghurt, to serve

Preparation

  1. Place garlic, ginger and onion into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add oil, cumin, paprika, turmeric and curry powder and sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  3. Add pumpkin, canned lentils, canned chickpeas, celery, water and stock paste and cook 10 min/Varoma//speed .
  4. Add green beans and mix well with spatula until well combined, then cook 3-4 min/Varoma//speed 0.5, until beans are cooked through.
  5. Transfer curry into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and stir through lime juice, salt and coriander leaves. Serve immediately with a spoonful of natural yoghurt.

Useful Items

thermal serving bowl or other large bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • Please refer to The Basic Cookbook for the Vegetable stock paste recipe.
  • Serve curry with a side of cooked rice and Naan bread if desired.
  • Vegan pulse and pumpkin curry: omit the yoghurt to serve.
