Sausage Mummies

Ingredients

  • 18 oz water
  • 48 uncooked breakfast sausages, cut into 2 in.-3 in. pieces
  • 1 large egg, lightly beaten
  • 2 tsp whole milk
  • 18 oz puff pastry, thawed
  • yellow mustard, to garnish
  • ketchup or dipping sauce, to serve
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
406 kJ / 97 kcal
Protein
2 g
Carbohydrates
5 g
Fat
8 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Line 2 baking sheets with parchment and set aside.
  2. Place water into mixing bowl, place Varoma dish into position and place in breakfast sausages. Secure Varoma lid and steam 10 min/Varoma/speed 3. Meanwhile, in a small bowl, whisk together egg and milk and set aside.
  3. Roll out puff pastry (approx. ⅛ in. thick) and lightly brush pastry with reserved beaten egg mixture. Cut pastry into ¼ inch strips.
  4. Remove Varoma and allow sausages to cool. Dry sausages with a paper towel and wrap 1 pastry strip (approx. 6 in. long) around each sausage, slightly overlapping pastry to resemble bandages but leaving a gap to allow for placement of the eyes. Place on baking sheet and continue with remaining mummies.
  5. Bake for 15-20 minutes (400°F) or until pastry is golden brown and puffed. Allow to cool slightly before garnishing with yellow mustard for the mummies' eyes (see Tip). Serve warm with ketchup or your favorite dipping sauce.

Useful Items

parchment paper, rolling pin, pastry brush, paper towel, baking sheets, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Use other small sausages (e.g., chipolata).
  • Make your own quick puff pastry in advance using the recipe in The Basic Cookbook. Roll out pastry to ⅛ inch thick square and continue with steps in the recipe.
  • Use the end of a chopstick to help apply mustard "eyes".
