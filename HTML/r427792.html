Tex-Mex Tortillas with Shredded Pork and Black Beans

Ingredients

  • 40 g olive oil
  • 600 g pork meat pieces, sinew free, boneless, cubed (6 cm x 4 cm) (e.g. shoulder)
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 2 pinch ground black pepper
  • 150 g onions, cut in wedges
  • 200 g ripe tomatoes, cut in pieces
  • 20 - 30 g pickled jalapeño chillies, to taste
  • 120 g red peppers, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 120 g green peppers, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 250 g tinned black beans, drained
  • 30 g tomato purée
  • 100 g water
  • ½ tsp chicken stock paste
  • 6 maize tortillas
  • 4 Tbsp coriander leaves
  • 100 g sour cream
  • 2 avocados
  • 1 lime, cut in wedges

Preparation

  1. Place 20 g oil, 300 g pork, ¼ tsp salt and 1 pinch pepper in mixing bowl then brown High Temp. .Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place remaining 20 g oil, remaining 300 g pork, ¼ tsp salt and 1 pinch pepper in mixing bowl then brown High Temp. . Transfer to bowl with reserved browned pork and set aside.
  3. Place 20 g meat juices, onions, tomatoes, jalapeno chilli, red and green pepper in mixing bowl then sauté High Temp. .
  4. Add reserved browned pork, 50 g drained black beans, tomato purée, water and stock paste then cook 20 min/110°C//speed 1.
  5. Place simmering basket instead of measuring cup on mixing bowl lid and cook for a further 10 min/105°C//speed 1, then shred 4 sec//speed 4.
  6. Add 200 g remaining drained black beans, mix gently with spatula then cook 2 min/100°C//speed . Meanwhile, peel and slice avocados. Serve shredded pork and black beans with corn tortillas garnished with sour cream, avocado, fresh coriander and lime wedges on the side.
