Goat's cheese

Ingredients

  • 1000 g goat's milk (see Tips)
  • 55 g lemon juice

Soft goat’s cheese

  • 1 - 2 pinches non-iodised salt, to taste (see Tips)

Rolled goat’s cheese

  • 1 tbsp dried mixed herbs of choice
  • 1 - 2 pinches non-iodised salt

Preparation

  1. Place milk into mixing bowl and heat 10 min/80°C/speed 1.
  2. Add lemon juice and heat 1 min/80°C/speed 1. Transfer into a warmed thermal serving bowl or warmed large ceramic bowl, then wrap bowl in 2 kitchen towels to keep warm and set aside overnight.
  3. Line simmering basket with a nut milk bag or 2 layers muslin cloth and set over a large jug or bowl. Carefully pour curds and whey into lined simmering basket. Set aside to drain for 2 hours, ensuring simmering basket is not submerged in liquid and cheese is able to drain freely.

Soft goat’s cheese

  1. After 2 hours of draining, transfer cheese into a bowl, add salt and stir to combine (see Tips). Transfer into a sealable container and place into refrigerator until ready to serve (see Tips).

Rolled goat’s cheese

  1. After 2 hours of draining, place a weight on top of cheese and transfer into refrigerator to drain for a further 2 hours.
  2. Place dried mixed herbs onto a plate. Add salt to cheese (see Tips) and stir to combine. Using your hands, pat and shape cheese into a small wheel or log, then roll in reserved herbs. Transfer into a sealable container and place into refrigerator until ready to serve (see Tips.)

Useful Items

kitchen towels, thermal serving bowl or large ceramic bowl, plate, sealable container, large jug or bowl, bowl, nut milk bag or 2 muslin cloths, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • We used long life goat's milk in this recipe (it's more readily available than fresh goat's milk). If using fresh goat's milk, ensure that it's at room temperature before starting this recipe.
  • You can find non-iodised salt at most major supermarkets.
  • The cheese will increase in flavour over time. If you prefer a stronger goat's cheese, add more salt after draining.
  • You can store this cheese for up to 1 week in the refrigerator.
