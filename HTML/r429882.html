Apple Crumble with Vanilla Cashew Ice Cream

Ingredients

Apple Crumble

  • 150 g almonds
  • ¼ tsp ground cinnamon
  • 100 g granulated sugar
  • 150 g plain flour
  • 130 g vegan margarine, cold, in pieces, plus extra for greasing
  • 30 g vanilla sugar, homemade
  • 4 - 6 dessert apples (approx. 800 g), peeled, cored and quartered

Vanilla Cashew Ice Cream

  • 2 vanilla pods
  • 60 g granulated sugar
  • 260 g ice cubes
  • 200 g pure cashew paste
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt

Preparation

Apple Crumble

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C and grease a gratin dish (30 cm x 20 cm x 6 cm).
  2. Place almonds in mixing bowl and chop 8 sec/speed 7.
  3. Add cinnamon, granulated sugar, flour and 100 g margarine then mix 10 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place vanilla sugar and apples in mixing bowl then, with aid of spatula, chop 5 sec//speed 4.
  5. Insert measuring cup then heat 5 min/100°C//speed 1. Transfer to prepared gratin dish and distribute remaining 30 g margarine in small pieces over apples. Sprinkle with reserved crumble topping and bake for 25-30 minutes (180°C) until golden brown. Towards the end of this time, rinse mixing bowl and make ice cream.

Vanilla Cashew Ice Cream

  1. Place vanilla pods and sugar in mixing bowl then grind 25 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add ice cubes and chop 6 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add cashew paste and salt then stir 30 sec/speed 5. Serve immediately with warm apple crumble.
