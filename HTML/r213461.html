Potato Rolls

Ingredients

  • non-stick spray oil, to grease
  • 10 oz russet potatoes cut into ¼ in. x 1 in. slices, (approx. 3 md. potatoes)
  • 5 oz water
  • 5 oz whole milk
  • 2 oz vegetable shortening
  • 18 oz all-purpose flour, plus extra to dust
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 ¼ tsp rapid rise yeast
  • 2 tsp salt

Preparation

  1. Line a baking sheet with parchment, lightly grease a large bowl and set aside.
  2. Place potatoes and water into mixing bowl and cook 12-15 min/200°F/speed , then mash 5 sec/speed 4.
  3. Add milk and vegetable shortening and mash a further 5 sec/speed 4. Allow to cool until temperature reaches 110°F (approx. 15 min.).
  4. Add flour, sugar, egg, yeast and salt and knead 4 min/.
  5. Transfer dough to a lightly floured surface and shape into a ball. Place into prepared bowl, cover with cling wrap and let rise in a warm place until doubled in size (approx. 2 hrs.).
  6. Punch down the dough and reshape into a ball. Divide dough into 12 equal pieces. Roll into balls dusting with flour where needed and place on prepared baking sheet 1 inch apart. Cover loosely with oiled cling wrap and let rise in a warm place for 30 minutes. Meanwhile preheat oven to 400°F.
  7. Bake rolls for 12-15 minutes (400°F) until tops are golden. Allow to cool for 5 minutes before serving.

Useful Items

cling wrap, parchment paper, baking sheet, large bowl, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Recipe makes 12 large rolls for burgers or 24 small dinner rolls.
  • This is a wet dough so dust with flour to ease handling.
