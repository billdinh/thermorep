Frozen Chocolate Peanut Butter Bars

Ingredients

Crust

  • 10 ½ oz raw unsalted peanuts, divided
  • 2 ½ oz unsalted butter, in pieces
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 3 tbsp cocoa powder
  • ¼ tsp salt

Chocolate Peanut Butter Filling

  • 9 oz creamy peanut butter
  • 3 oz sugar
  • 3 ½ oz coconut cream
  • 2 oz unsalted butter
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract
  • 12 oz dark chocolate, in pieces
  • 1 oz coconut oil

Preparation

Crust

  1. Line a square pan (9 in. x 9 in.) with foil, letting edges hang over sides of pan. Place a wire rack on a baking sheet and set aside.
  2. Place 2.5 oz peanuts into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place butter into mixing bowl and melt 3 min/170°F/speed 1.
  4. Add 8 oz peanuts, sugar, cocoa powder and salt and grind 5 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and grind a further 5 sec/speed 8. Spread crust mixture into bottom of prepared pan and press firmly into a compact layer. Freeze for 10 minutes. Clean mixing bowl.

Chocolate Peanut Butter Filling

  1. Place peanut butter, sugar, coconut cream, butter and vanilla into mixing bowl and mix 30 sec/speed 4. Spread peanut butter filling into chilled crust and freeze 30 minutes. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  2. Slice into bars (1 in. x 3 in. ea.), transfer to prepared wire rack and place in freezer.
  3. Place dark chocolate and coconut oil into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and heat 6 min/110°F/speed 1. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Pour reserved melted chocolate over frozen peanut butter bars and sprinkle with reserved chopped peanuts. Serve frozen.

Useful Items

square baking pan (9 in. x 9 in.), aluminum foil, wire rack, baking sheet, freezer
