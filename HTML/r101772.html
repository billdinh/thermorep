Salmon Farfalle with Pesto

Ingredients

  • 1500 g water
  • 15 g olive oil
  • 1 ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 400 g fresh salmon fillets
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • ½ lemon, thinly sliced
  • 350 g farfalle
  • 4 Tbsp basil pesto
  • 10 g fresh basil leaves
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
458 kcal
Protein
18 g
Carbohydrates
41 g
Fat
24 g

Preparation

  1. Place water, oil and 1 tsp salt in mixing bowl.
  2. Lay a piece of foil (approx. 30 cm x 15 cm) in Varoma dish then place into position. Weigh salmon fillets into Varoma dish then season with ½ tsp salt and 1 pinch pepper. Place lemon slices on top then wrap foil over tightly forming a parcel. Cover Varoma then steam 25 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove Varoma and set aside.
  3. Add pasta and cook select time indicated on packet/100°C//speed 0.5. Meanwhile, place pesto into a large serving bowl and flake salmon fillets into it.
  4. Drain pasta through simmering basket then add to serving bowl and stir with spatula. Serve warm or cold garnished with fresh basil leaves.

Useful Items

aluminium foil, large serving bowl
