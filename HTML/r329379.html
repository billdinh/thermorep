Kimchi queso dip

Ingredients

Soaking

  • 100 g raw cashews (see Tips)
  • ½ tsp salt
  • filtered water, for soaking

Dip

  • 250 g plant-based milk of choice
  • 20 g nutritional yeast (see Tips)
  • ½ tsp smoked paprika
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp tapioca starch
  • 120 g kimchi (see Tips)
  • 1 garlic clove
  • ½ tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp chipotle chilli in adobo sauce (see Tips)
  • ¼ tsp onion powder
  • 1 jalapeño chilli, cut into thin slices, for garnish
  • red capsicum, finely diced, for garnish
  • tortilla chips or vegetable crudités, to serve

Preparation

Soaking

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh cashews into it. Add salt, cover with water and set aside to soak for 3-6 hours.
  2. Using simmering basket, drain and rinse cashews.

Dip

  1. Place milk, nutritional yeast, paprika, salt, pepper, tapioca starch, kimchi, garlic, cumin, chipotle chilli, onion powder and drained cashews into mixing bowl and blend 1 min/speed 8, until smooth.
  2. Heat 3-5 min/100°C/speed 3, until warm and slightly thickened. Transfer dip into a serving bowl and garnish with slices of jalapeño chilli and finely diced red capsicum. Serve warm with tortilla chips or vegetable crudités.

Hints & Tricks

  • If you don't have time to soak/activate your cashews, soak nuts in boiling water for 30 minutes, then drain and use as directed.
  • With a cheesy, umami flavour, nutritional yeast contains all nine essential amino acids, is a great source of B vitamins and is often fortified with vitamin B12 (important for those following a vegan diet). You can find nutritional yeast at health food stores or online.
  • To make your own kimchi, please refer to the recipe on this recipe chip.
  • You can find chipotle chillies in adobo sauce in the international aisle of most major supermarkets.
