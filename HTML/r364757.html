Nasi lemak

Ingredients

  • 400 g jasmine rice
  • 700 g water
  • 200 g coconut milk
  • 1 - 2 tsp salt, to taste
  • 2 pandan leaves, cut into pieces (10 cm - see Tips)
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1809 kJ / 430.7 kcal

7.1 g
Carbohydrates
80 g
Fat
8.4 g

Preparation

  1. Place simmering basket onto mixing bowl lid and weigh rice into it. Rinse rice until water runs clear.
  2. Place water, coconut milk and salt into mixing bowl. Reinsert simmering basket with rice, add pandan leaves to simmering basket and cook 16 min/100Â°C/speed 4. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula, then serve as desired (see Tips).
