Sweet Chilli Chicken with Slaw

Ingredients

Sweet Chilli Chicken

  • 8 chicken thighs, skin on, bone in (approx. 1 kg)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 20 g fresh root ginger, cut in round slices (2 mm)
  • 1 lime, finely grated zest
  • 40 g light soy sauce
  • 220 g sweet chilli sauce

Slaw

  • 20 g coriander leaves (1 large handful)
  • 5 spring onions, cut in pieces (3 cm)
  • 250 g carrots, cut in pieces
  • 250 g white cabbage, cut in pieces
  • 250 g red cabbage, cut in pieces
  • 30 - 40 g rice vinegar
  • 15 g sesame oil
  • ¾ tsp sugar
  • ¾ tsp fine sea salt, or to taste
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 50 g dry-roasted peanuts, unsalted

Preparation

Sweet Chilli Chicken

  1. Place chicken in a baking dish.
  2. Place garlic, ginger and lime zest in mixing bowl then chop 2 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and chop again 2 sec/speed 8.
  3. Add lime juice, soy sauce and sweet chilli sauce then mix 15 sec/speed 7. Pour over chicken, cover then set aside in fridge to marinate for at least 1 hour (see tip). Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl.
  4. After this time, preheat oven to 180°C and make slaw.

Slaw

  1. Place coriander and spring onions in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 8.
  2. Add carrots, white and red cabbage, rice vinegar, sesame oil, sugar, salt and pepper then, without measuring cup, chop 4-5 sec/speed 4.5 while stirring with spatula through hole in mixing bowl lid. Transfer to a serving bowl and set aside.
  3. Bake marinated chicken for 40-50 minutes (180°C) until cooked through and golden brown.
  4. Scatter slaw with peanuts then serve alongside hot or cold sweet chilli chicken.

Useful Items

baking dish, serving bowl, cling film, refrigerator, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Marinate the chicken for a minimum of 1 hour and up to 24 hours. The flavours will develop the longer it is left.
  • Serves 8 as part of a buffet or 4 as a main course on its own.
