Pistachio and Almond Filo Coil

Ingredients

Filo Coil

  • 100 g blanched almonds
  • 125 g pistachio nuts, unsalted, shelled
  • 1 lemon, finely grated zest only
  • 125 g icing sugar, plus extra for dusting
  • 1 egg white, from medium egg
  • 100 g unsalted butter, diced
  • 8 sheets filo pastry
  • 1 egg yolk, from medium egg
  • 1 pinch ground cinnamon
  • 15 g flaked almonds

Syrup

  • 75 g orange juice
  • 140 g honey

Preparation

Filo Coil

  1. Preheat oven to 170°C.
  2. Place almonds in mixing bowl and grind 8-10 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a small bowl and set aside.
  3. Place 100 g pistachios in mixing bowl and grind 5 sec/speed 9.
  4. Add lemon zest, reserved ground almonds, remaining 25 g pistachios, icing sugar and egg white then mix 10 sec/speed .
  5. Tip out onto work surface, form into a log and divide into 4 equal pieces. Using hands dusted with icing sugar, roll each piece into a log (approx. 35 cm long and 1 cm thick). Place logs in the fridge to rest until required. Clean mixing bowl.
  6. Place butter in mixing bowl and melt 2 min/100°C/speed 1. Transfer to a bowl and clean mixing bowl.
  7. Grease a springform tin (Ø 20 cm) with a little melted butter and line the base of the tin with enough baking paper so that it hangs over the edge.
  8. On a clean work surface, place one sheet of filo and quickly brush with melted butter then place another sheet on top and brush again with butter. Place one nut paste roll on the long side and roll up to form a long log. Repeat with remaining paste and filo sheets to form 4 logs.
  9. Shape the first log into a tight coil. Place coil in centre of springform base, brush the loose end generously with melted butter and attach end of next log. Coil second log around outside of first. Continue with remaining logs until base of tin is covered.
  10. Attach the outside of the springform tin to the base with the coil neatly inside.
  11. In a small bowl, mix egg yolk and cinnamon then brush over filo coil with pastry brush. Sprinkle with flaked almonds and bake for 30-35 minutes (170°C) until pastry is golden brown. Towards the end of this time, make syrup.

Syrup

  1. Place orange juice and honey in mixing bowl then cook 10 min/120°C/speed 1. Remove coil from oven and allow to sit for 5 minutes, then spoon over syrup, leaving to stand a further 20 minutes before removing from tin and serving.

Useful Items

springform tin (Ø 20 cm), pastry brush, baking paper, oven, refrigerator
