Pasta Salad with Vegetables and Trout

Ingredients

  • 1000 g water
  • 200 g white wine
  • 100 g orange juice, freshly squeezed
  • 1 ½ tsp fine sea salt, to taste
  • 400 g fresh trout fillets, skinless
  • 200 g frozen green peas
  • 240 g penne (short, dried pasta) (10 minutes cooking time)
  • 40 g olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 30 g pickled capers, drained and rinsed
  • 80 g black olives, pitted
  • 350 g cherry tomatoes, halved or quartered
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper (optional)
  • 10 g lemon juice, to taste

Preparation

  1. Place water, white wine, orange juice and salt in mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish into position then weigh trout fillets into it. Insert Varoma tray and weigh peas onto it. Steam 12 min/Varoma/speed 1. Set Varoma aside.
  2. Add pasta to mixing bowl then return Varoma to position. Cook 10 min/Varoma//speed 1. Set Varoma aside. Drain pasta using simmering basket then refresh under cold, running water to stop cooking. Transfer to a large serving bowl, add 10 g olive oil and stir well.
  3. Place garlic and 30 g olive oil in mixing bowl then sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1. Discard garlic clove.
  4. Add capers, black olives and reserved cooked peas then cook 3 min/100°C//speed 1. Meanwhile, flake cooked trout fillets into serving bowl then add halved or quartered cherry tomatoes. Transfer capers, olives and peas to serving bowl then mix. Season with pepper and lemon juice, to taste, then serve warm or cold.

Useful Items

large serving bowl, serving bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • Sprinkle with chopped parsley to taste.
  • If you prefer a stronger garlic flavour, keep garlic clove in mixing bowl in step 3, and chop 5 sec/speed 6 before sautéing.
  • You can replace frozen peas with drained tinned peas- add to cooked pasta at the end of step 2.
