Salmon, quinoa, feta and mixed vegetable salad

Ingredients

  • ½ lemon, zest only, no white pith
  • 10 - 12 sprigs fresh mixed herbs (e.g. parsley, basil, chives), leaves only
  • 1 garlic clove
  • ½ fresh chillies
  • 60 g olive oil
  • 1200 g water
  • 1 ½ tsp salt
  • 100 g quinoa
  • 600 g fresh salmon fillets, skinless (2 cm thick)
  • 160 g red capsicum, cut into pieces
  • 50 g red onion, cut into pieces
  • 120 g cucumber, cut into pieces
  • 150 g feta cheese, cut into pieces
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 300 g roma tomatoes, cut into pieces (2 cm)
  • 1 avocado, flesh only (150-200 g), sliced
  • 100 g kale, thinly sliced
  • 1 tbsp pumpkin seeds (optional)
  • 2 tbsp dried cranberries (optional)

Preparation

  1. Place lemon zest and 2-3 sprigs mixed fresh herbs into mixing bowl and chop 7 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place garlic clove, chilli and 30 g olive oil into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7, then sauté 2 min/120°C/speed 1, without measuring cup. Transfer into bowl containing lemon-herb mixture and set aside.
  3. Without rinsing mixing bowl, place water and 1 tsp salt into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket, weigh quinoa into it and stir using spatula to wet the grains. Place Varoma dish and tray into position. Place salmon fillets onto Varoma tray. Sprinkle fillets with reserved lemon-herb mixture, secure Varoma lid and cook 20 min/Varoma/speed 1. Carefully open Varoma to check that salmon is cooked. If necessary, cook for a further 2-5 min/Varoma/speed 1. Set Varoma aside uncovered and allow salmon to cool. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula, transfer quinoa into a large salad bowl and allow to cool. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Place capsicums, onions, cucumber, 100 g of the feta, remaining fresh herbs, Dijon mustard, vinegar, remaining 30 g oil, remaining ½ tsp salt and 2 pinches pepper into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add tomatoes and avocado and combine 10 sec//speed 2. Transfer to salad bowl with cooked quinoa. Using spatula, break steamed salmon into chunks, then add to salad. Add sliced kale or baby spinach and gently toss to combine. Sprinkle with pumpkin seeds (optional), dried cranberries (optional) and remaining 50 g feta. Serve immediately or place into refrigerator until ready to serve.
