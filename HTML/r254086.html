Pumpkin Pancakes with Praline Maple Syrup

Ingredients

Praline Maple Syrup

  • 5 oz pure maple syrup
  • 2 oz heavy whipping cream
  • 2 oz unsalted butter, cut into pieces
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • 2 oz pecans
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Pancakes

  • 2 oz unsalted butter diced (½ in.)
  • 8 oz whole milk
  • 2 ½ oz canned pumpkin purée
  • 2 large eggs
  • 8 oz all-purpose flour
  • 1 oz sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 2 tsp homemade pumpkin spice mix
  • &frac18; tsp salt
  • 3 tsp oil, vegetable, to fry, divided

Preparation

Praline Maple Syrup

  1. Place maple syrup, cream, butter and baking soda into mixing bowl, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 7 min/212°F/speed 1.
  2. Add pecans and vanilla, insert measuring cup and mix 10 sec/speed 5. Transfer into a jar and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.

Pancakes

  1. Place butter and milk into mixing bowl and heat 2 min/160°F/speed 1.
  2. Add pumpkin purée and eggs and mix 10 sec/speed 5.
  3. Add flour, sugar, baking powder, pumpkin spice mix and salt and mix 10 sec/speed 5 (see Tip).
  4. Heat ½ tsp oil in a frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour in a ladle of batter (approx. 4 oz) and cook 1-2 minutes or until bubbles start bursting on pancake surface. Turn pancake over and cook a further 1 minute. Place pancake onto a serving plate and cover to keep warm. Repeat with remaining batter. Serve hot with Praline Maple Syrup and butter.

Useful Items

jar, frying pan, serving plate

Hints & Tricks

  • For light and fluffy pancakes, make the batter first and let rest 10-20 minutes while making the Praline Maple Syrup.
