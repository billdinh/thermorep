Caprese Stuffed Balsamic-Glazed Chicken

Ingredients

  • 6 oz balsamic vinegar
  • 2 chicken breasts, skinless, boneless
  • 1 tbsp dark brown sugar
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 Roma tomato, thinly sliced
  • 2 oz fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • 1 tbsp sun-dried tomatoes
  • 6 fresh basil leaves, plus extra to garnish
  • 20 oz water
  • 4 oz broccoli florets
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
2100 kJ / 502 kcal
Protein
62 g
Carbohydrates
28 g

13 g

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in balsamic vinegar, add chicken and turn to coat. Cover with cling wrap, refrigerate and allow to marinate 30 minutes. Meanwhile, cut two squares of parchment (12 in. x 12 in.) and set aside.
  2. Remove chicken from marinade and set aside. Place marinade and brown sugar into mixing bowl and without measuring cup cook 13 min/Varoma/speed 2. Meanwhile, cut a long pocket ¾ of the way through the thickest side of each chicken breast, being careful not to cut all the way through. Season chicken and pocket with salt and pepper. Stuff pockets with layers of tomato, mozzarella, sun-dried tomatoes and basil and secure with toothpicks.
  3. Place a stuffed chicken breast on one side of each parchment square, fold parchment over and crimp edges together to seal by folding edges in small increments to form tightly sealed half-moon shaped packets. Set aside.
  4. Transfer balsamic glaze to a small bowl.
  5. Place water into mixing bowl. Set Varoma into position, insert Varoma tray and place in chicken packets, secure Varoma lid and steam 22 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  6. Carefully open Varoma lid, add broccoli florets and steam a further 8 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  7. Remove chicken from parchment and brush with balsamic glaze, to taste. Garnish with chopped fresh basil and serve hot with steamed broccoli.

Useful Items

parchment paper, toothpicks, cling wrap, basting brush, refrigerator
