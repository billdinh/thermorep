Smoked Salmon and Brie Quiche

Ingredients

Pastry

  • 6 oz all-purpose flour, plus extra to dust
  • 4 oz unsalted butter, chilled, cut into pieces
  • 2 oz heavy whipping cream
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • ½ tsp salt

Filling

  • 2 oz green onions, cut in pieces, (approx. 2 onions)
  • 5 sprigs dill, leaves only
  • 3 large eggs
  • 8 oz heavy whipping cream
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 1 pinch fresh ground black pepper
  • 3 oz smoked salmon, sliced
  • 3 ½ oz Brie cheese, cut into pieces

Preparation

Pastry

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Place flour, butter, cream, sugar and salt into mixing bowl and mix 20 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a floured surface and form into a ball.
  3. Roll out dough with a rolling pin. Transfer pastry to pie pan, prick dough with a fork to avoid air bubbles and place in refrigerator to chill.

Filling

  1. Place green onions and dill into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5.
  2. Add eggs, cream, salt and pepper and mix 30 sec/speed 4.
  3. Evenly distribute salmon and Brie on pastry, cover with egg mixture and bake for 10 minutes (400°F) then reduce heat to 350°F and bake for 25 minutes or until golden. Serve warm or cold.

Useful Items

pie pan (approx. 10 in.), rolling pin, oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • 10 inch quiche pan can be used instead of a pie pan.
  • Green onions can be replaced with shallots, Brie can be replaced with Gruyère cheese.
