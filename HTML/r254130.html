Pumpkin Scones

Ingredients

  • 8 oz all-purpose flour, plus extra to dust
  • 1 large egg
  • 4 oz canned pumpkin purée
  • 2 oz sugar
  • 1 oz unsalted butter
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 tsp homemade pumpkin spice mix
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • milk, to brush
  • homemade pumpkin butter, to serve
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
958 kJ / 229 kcal
Protein
5 g
Carbohydrates
41 g
Fat
5 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Line a baking sheet with parchment.
  2. Place flour, egg, pumpkin purée, sugar, butter, salt, pumpkin pie spice and baking powder into mixing bowl and mix 15 sec/speed 7. Transfer dough to a lightly floured surface, dust liberally with flour and gently press into a rectangle (9 in. x 3 in. x ½ in.). Cut into six equal triangles and transfer to prepared baking sheet.
  3. Brush tops with milk and bake for 15 minutes (400°F). Serve scones with pumpkin butter.

Useful Items

parchment paper, pastry brush, baking sheet, oven
