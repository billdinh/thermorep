Mediterranean Farro Salad

Ingredients

  • 7 oz farro
  • 52 oz water, divided
  • 1 lemon (approx. 5 oz), thin peel only, no pith, plus 1 oz of juice
  • 1 tsp salt, to taste
  • ¼ tsp freshly ground pepper, to taste
  • ½ oz parsley, leaves and tender stems
  • 2 oz olive oil
  • ½ oz red wine vinegar
  • 6 ½ oz red onions, cut into halves and broken into pieces, divided
  • 2 oz red bell pepper, seeds removed, cut into pieces (approx. ½ pepper)
  • 2 oz green bell pepper, seeds removed, cut into pieces (approx. ½ pepper)
  • 7 oz Roma tomatoes, diced (½ in.) pieces (approx. 2 tomatoes)
  • 5 oz Lebanese cucumbers, diced (½ in.), (approx. ¼ cucumber)
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 2 dried bay leaves
  • 6 stalks fresh thyme, leaves only
  • 3 oz mixed salad greens

Preparation

  1. Place simmering basket onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in farro. Transfer simmering basket into a deep bowl, cover farro with 28 oz water and soak for 1 hour.
  2. Place lemon peel, salt and pepper into mixing bowl and zest 30 sec/speed 9.
  3. Add parsley and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add olive oil, wine vinegar, 3 oz onions, green and red peppers and 1 oz lemon juice and chop 3 sec/speed 4.5. Add diced tomatoes and cucumber. Transfer to a salad bowl and set aside.
  5. Place 3.5 oz onions, garlic, bay leaves, 3 stalks thyme and 24 oz water into mixing bowl. Rinse farro through simmering basket under cold water, insert simmering basket with farro and steam 15 min/Varoma/speed 2.5. Transfer farro to a bowl and allow to cool. Discard cooking water. Add cooled farro to reserved salad and stir through. Serve on a bed of mixed salad greens and garnish with crumbled feta cheese and remaining 3 stalks of thyme.

Hints & Tricks

  • Farro and French green lentils can be soaked overnight.
  • Green French lentils can be replaced with pre-cooked lentils. Omit soaking and cooking lentils from recipe.
  • Thyme can be replaced with oregano.
