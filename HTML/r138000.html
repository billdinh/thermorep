Com ga rau thom (chicken with rice and mint)

Ingredients

Nuoc cham dressing

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 fresh bird's eye chilli, trimmed and deseeded if preferred
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 120 g water
  • 50 g fish sauce
  • 50 g rice wine vinegar
  • 40 g palm sugar

Chicken and rice

  • 250 g jasmine rice
  • 1200 g water, plus extra as needed
  • 2 tbsp Chicken stock paste (see Tips)
  • 40 g fresh ginger, peeled and cut into thin slices
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • 4 skinless chicken breast fillets (approx. 700 g), cut lengthways into quarters
  • 3 spring onions/shallots, cut into thin slices, to serve
  • 20 fresh mint leaves (approx. 2 sprigs), to serve
  • 100 g cucumber, cut into thin slices, to serve

Preparation

Nuoc cham dressing

  1. Place garlic and chilli into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place lime juice, water, fish sauce, vinegar and sugar into mixing bowl and heat 3 min/Varoma/speed 1 or until sugar dissolves. Set aside in mixing bowl to cool for 10 minutes, then add to bowl with chilli mixture and stir to combine. Do not clean mixing bowl.

Chicken and rice

  1. Insert simmering basket and weigh rice into it. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and rinse rice until water runs clear. Transfer rice into a seperate bowl and set aside.
  2. Place water, stock paste, ginger, soy sauce and sesame oil into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket. Place chicken into it and poach 10 min/100°C/speed 2.
  3. Stir chicken with spatula to ensure even cooking and cook 5-7 min/100°C/speed 2 or until chicken is just cooked. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and transfer chicken into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl. Pour half of the stock over the chicken. Cover to keep warm.
  4. If required, top up remaining stock level in mixing bowl with extra water until it reaches 1L mark.
  5. Insert simmering basket back into mixing bowl. Add rinsed rice and cook 10-13 min/100°C/speed 4 or until cooked.
  6. Place mounds of rice into 4 serving bowls. Cut chicken into bite-size pieces and divide between bowls. Drizzle 1-2 tablespoons of the stock over chicken. Garnish with spring onions/shallots, mint, cucumber and serve with reserved Nuoc cham dressing on the side.
