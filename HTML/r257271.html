Seafood en papillote with saffron potatoes

Ingredients

  • 2 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 500 g water
  • 1 ½ tsp salt
  • 300 g new baby potatoes, peeled and cut into halves (approx. 8)
  • 2 pinches saffron
  • 4 firm white fish fillets (see Tips)
  • 40 g unsalted butter, cut into pieces
  • 20 g vermouth (see Tips)
  • 20 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 50 - 80 g frozen peas, to taste
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 20 fresh mussels, debearded and cleaned (see Tips)

Preparation

  1. Place parsley into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 6. Transfer into a small bowl and set aside.
  2. Place water and 1 teaspoon of the salt into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket and weigh potatoes into it and add saffron.
  3. Place Varoma into position and line Varoma dish with a sheet of baking paper (30 x 60 cm). Place fish in a single layer onto baking paper and weigh in butter, vermouth, cream, peas, pepper, remaining ½ teaspoon salt and half of the reserved parsley. Enclose top and sides of paper to form a sealed parcel. Insert Varoma tray and place mussels onto it. Secure Varoma lid and steam 20-23 min/Varoma/speed 2, until fish flakes easily, mussels are open and potatoes are cooked through.
  4. Carefully transfer parcel onto a serving platter. Open parcel and serve with potatoes and mussels, garnished with remaining reserved parsley.

Useful Items

serving platter, baking paper, small bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • For the best result, use fresh fish fillets and fresh mussels in this recipe. Cooking times may vary depending on the type and thickness of fillets used.
  • You can replace the vermouth with white wine, if desired.
