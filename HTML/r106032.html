Lime and chilli corn

Ingredients

  • 3 limes, 2 zest only, no white pith, plus 1 extra, cut into wedges, to serve
  • 6 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves and stems, cut into pieces
  • 1 fresh red chilli, cut into halves and deseeded if preferred
  • 60 g unsalted butter
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Â½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 4 sweet corn cobs, with husks on
  • 500 g water

Preparation

  1. Place lime zest into mixing bowl and chop 15 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add coriander and chilli and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add butter, salt and pepper and mix 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  4. Peel away husks from corn cobs, keeping them attached at the bottom, then remove and discard silky fibres. Discard outer layer of husks, retaining at least 3 internal layers, enough to fully enclose corn. Spread cobs with butter mixture, then wrap cobs in husks to seal.
  5. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish into position and place corn cobs into Varoma dish. Secure Varoma lid and steam 25 min/Varoma/speed 2 or until corn is tender.
  6. Carefully remove and discard corn husks, then serve corn with extra lime wedges.
