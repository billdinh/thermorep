Green mojo

Ingredients

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 20 - 30 sprigs fresh coriander, (only the leaves)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 150 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 15 g vinegar

Preparation

  1. Place garlic and coriander into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add salt, olive oil and vinegar. Blend 3 min/speed 5-10, increasing speed gradually to bind the sauce and obtain a uniform texture. Pour into a bowl and serve with potatoes, seafood or fish, or refrigerate in an air-tight container.

Useful Items

air-tight container, bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • For a more intense colour, add a few leaves of parsley which also soften the taste of the coriander.
