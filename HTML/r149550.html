Allergen Friendly Cranberry Mini Rockbuns

Ingredients

  • 300 g bananas, ripe, cut in pieces
  • 100 g coconut flour
  • 85 g brown sugar
  • 40 g sunflower oil
  • 120 g coconut milk, from a carton (milk alternative)
  • ½ tsp gluten free baking powder
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • ¼ tsp vanilla extract
  • 75 g dried cranberries, or dried fruit of choice
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
247 kJ / 59 kcal
Protein
0.8 g
Carbohydrates
7.8 g
Fat
2.3 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C and line a baking tray with baking paper.
  2. Place bananas in mixing bowl and mash 3 sec/speed 5. Insert butterfly whisk.
  3. Add flour, sugar, oil, coconut milk, baking powder, salt and vanilla extract then mix 10 sec/speed 4.
  4. Remove butterfly whisk. Add cranberries and stir in with spatula.
  5. Using a tablespoon, dollop mixture on baking tray to form approx. 25 small mounds then bake for 18-20 minutes (180°C) until browned. Allow to cool on baking tray for 10 minutes before transferring to wire rack to cool completely before serving.

Useful Items

wire rack, baking tray and paper, baking tray, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • This recipe makes enough mini rockbuns to enjoy some within a couple of days, and to put the rest in the freezer for whenever needed.
