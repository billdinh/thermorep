Strawberry Chiffon Pie

Ingredients

Graham Cracker Pie Crust

  • 6 oz unsalted butter, in pieces
  • 10 oz graham crackers
  • 2 tbsp sugar, (optional)

Filling

  • 3 tbsp water
  • 1 packet/envelope gelatin powder, unflavored
  • 8 oz strawberries
  • 3 oz sugar, divided
  • 3 egg yolks, from large eggs
  • 4 tsp freshly squeezed lemon juice, divided
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 3 egg whites, from large eggs
  • 8 - 10 strawberries, sliced (¼ in.), to garnish

Preparation

Graham Cracker Pie Crust

  1. Place butter into mixing bowl and melt 3 min/160°F/speed 2.
  2. Add graham crackers and sugar and crush 25 sec/speed 5.
  3. Spread crumb mixture into base and up sides of pie pan (9 in.). Press firmly into a compact layer with a spoon or hands. Place in refrigerator while making the filling. Clean mixing bowl.

Filling

  1. Place water and gelatin into a small bowl, stir until combined and set aside.
  2. Place strawberries and 2 oz sugar into mixing bowl and chop 20 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add egg yolks, 2 tsp lemon juice and salt and heat 2 min/195°F/speed 2.
  4. Add reserved gelatin mixture and mix 1 min/speed 5. Transfer to a large bowl and refrigerate. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  5. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg whites, 1 oz sugar and 2 tsp lemon juice into mixing bowl and without measuring cup whip 2 min 10 sec/120°F/speed 3.5 and then whip 1 min 40 sec/speed 3.5. Remove butterfly whisk.
  6. Gently fold whipped egg whites into reserved strawberry mixture with spatula. Transfer strawberry filling to chilled pie crust. Place in refrigerator for 2 hours to overnight.
  7. Garnish with strawberry slices and serve.

Useful Items

pie pan (9 in.), large bowl, refrigerator
