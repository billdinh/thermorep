Grilled eggplant dip

Ingredients

  • 2 eggplants (approx. 900 g total), room temperature
  • 2 tbsp tahini
  • 1 - 2 garlic cloves
  • 20 g lemon juice
  • sea salt, to taste (optional - see Tips)
  • ground black pepper, to taste
  • paprika, to serve
  • 2 - 3 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only, roughly chopped, for garnishing
  • extra virgin olive oil, for drizzling
  • pita bread, toasted, to serve

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven grill. Place eggplants onto a baking tray and grill for approx. 30 minutes, turning occasionally, until skins have blackened and flesh is soft (insert a toothpick or skewer into the tip of the eggplant; it should come out easily when cooked through). Set aside to cool. When cool enough to handle, peel off and discard skins, remove stalks and place eggplant flesh into simmering basket to drain for 1 hour or overnight in the refrigerator (see Tips).
  2. Place tahini, garlic and lemon juice into mixing bowl and blend 10 sec/speed 6 or until smooth.
  3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Add drained eggplant and blend 10 sec/speed 6. Season to taste (see Tips), then transfer into a serving bowl and sprinkle with paprika and parsley. Drizzle with oil and serve with toasted pita bread.

Useful Items

oven grill, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Baby's serve: omit salt (and pepper if preferred) and blend baby's serve until smooth. For an older baby, keep the blended consistency more textured. Wait until your baby is confidently managing finger foods before offering pita toasts.
  • Toddler's serve: serve as is.
  • The longer the eggplant drains, the thicker the dip will be. The drained juice can be discarded.
  • Omit salt if serving to younger children.
