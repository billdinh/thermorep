Coconut and Pistachio Delight

Ingredients

  • ¼ tsp cardamom seeds
  • 40 g pistachio nuts, unsalted, peeled
  • 40 g semolina
  • 30 g margarine, plus extra for greasing
  • 100 g desiccated coconut
  • 250 g milk
  • 50 g sugar
  • 4 medium eggs
  • 250 g sweetened condensed milk
  • 100 g double cream
  • 30 g flaked almonds
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
698 kJ / 167 kcal
Protein
4 g
Carbohydrates
12.2 g

11.3 g

Preparation

  1. Place cardamom seeds and pistachio nuts in mixing bowl then chop 4 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place semolina and margarine in mixing bowl then sauté 3 min/100°C/speed 3.
  3. Add desiccated coconut, milk and sugar then mix 10 sec/speed 3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then cook 6 min/100°C/speed 3. Allow mixture to cool for 20 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile preheat oven to 180°C and grease a rectangular cake tin (30 x 12 x 10 cm).
  5. With aid of spatula, scrape mixture from the bottom of the mixing bowl.
  6. Add eggs then mix 20 sec/speed 3.
  7. Add pistachio nut mixture, condensed milk and cream then mix 10 sec/speed 3. Pour into greased cake tin then sprinkle with almonds.
  8. Half fill a rectangular baking dish (approx. 40 x 24 x 6 cm) with water and place cake tin into it. Bake for 30-35 minutes (180°C). Allow to cool in tin then cut into small squares and serve warm or cold.

Useful Items

rectangular cake tin (30 x 12 x 10 cm), rectangular baking tin (approx. 40 x 24 x 6 cm), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • To extract cardamom seeds from pods, place 1 Tbsp green cardamom pods in mixing bowl then chop 3 secs//speed 6 to break the husks. Discard husks.
