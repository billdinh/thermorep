Christmas Gingerbread (Poland)

Ingredients

Cake

  • 80 g butter, soft, plus extra for greasing
  • 250 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 200 g runny honey
  • 1 ½ - 2 tsp gingerbread seasoning, to taste (see tip)
  • 3 medium eggs
  • ½ tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 200 g cherries in syrup, pitted

Icing

  • 80 g dark chocolate, 70% cocoa, small pieces or callets
  • 60 g butter, diced

Preparation

Cake

  1. Preheat oven to 170°C. Grease a loaf tin (length 28 cm or 2 lb) with butter and sprinkle with flour.
  2. Place butter, honey and gingerbread seasoning in mixing bowl then heat 3 min/50°C/speed 0.5.
  3. Add eggs, bicarbonate of soda and flour then mix 30 sec/speed 4.
  4. Strain cherries through simmering basket then gently squeeze.
  5. Transfer half of dough to prepared tin, place reserved cherries on top, then cover with remaining dough. Bake for 50-55 minutes (170°C) until golden brown and a skewer inserted in middle comes out clean. Meanwhile, clean and dry mixing bowl. Leave gingerbread to cool completely in tin on a wire rack then remove.

Icing

  1. Place chocolate in mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add butter and heat 3 min/50°C/speed 3. Pour over cooled cake.
  3. When icing is set, serve cut in slices.

Useful Items

900 g loaf tin (2 lb), skewer, wire rack, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Gingerbread spice can be made in your Thermomix®: In a clean and dry mixing bowl, place 4 cinnamon sticks, 2 tsp ground ginger, 5 g nutmeg, 10 allspice berries, 10 g cloves, 25 cardamom seeds (shelled), 1 star anise, and 20 black peppercorns then grind 1 min 30 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. If necessary, grind again 30 sec/speed 10. Store in a sealed container.
