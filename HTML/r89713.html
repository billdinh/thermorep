Polish vegetable salad

Ingredients

Salad

  • 300 g carrots, peeled and cut into pieces (4 cm)
  • 200 g parsnip, peeled and cut into pieces (4 cm)
  • 100 g celeriac, peeled and cut into pieces (4 cm)
  • 430 g potatoes, peeled and cut into pieces (4-4.5 cm)
  • 500 g water
  • 4 eggs
  • 70 g leek, white part only, cut into slices (1.5 cm)
  • 250 g dill cucumbers, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 180 g apple, cut into quarters and core removed
  • 220 g frozen green peas, thawed (optional)

Dressing

  • 280 - 300 g mayonnaise (see Tips)
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 pinch sea salt
  • 1 - 2 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only, finely chopped, for garnishing

Preparation

Salad

  1. Place carrot, parsnip and celeriac into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer into Varoma dish and set aside.
  2. Place potatoes into mixing bowl and chop 9 sec/speed 4. Transfer into Varoma dish with carrots and set aside. Rinse mixing bowl.
  3. Place water into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket and place eggs into it. Place Varoma dish with chopped vegetables into position. Secure Varoma lid and steam 14 min/Varoma/speed 1. Set Varoma aside, then remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and carefully transfer eggs into a bowl of cold water.
  4. Place Varoma with vegetables back into position and stir vegetables with spatula to ensure even cooking. Secure Varoma lid and cook 6 min/Varoma/speed 1, or until vegetables are soft.
  5. Remove Varoma and set vegetables aside to cool (approx. 30 min), placing Varoma lid under dish to avoid drips. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  6. Place leek, cucumbers and apple into mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 5. Transfer into a serving bowl and set aside.
  7. Peel cooled eggs, then place peeled eggs into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 4. Transfer chopped egg into serving bowl with apple mixture, then add cooled potatoes and peas (optional).

Dressing

  1. Place all dressing ingredients except parsley into mixing bowl and blend 3 sec/speed 5. Pour dressing over salad in serving bowl and combine well using spatula.
  2. Refrigerate salad until chilled (approx. 30 minutes), then garnish with parsley and serve immediately.

Useful Items

serving bowl, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Make Mayonnaise fresh in your Thermomix to use in this recipe (see The Basic Cookbook or Everyday Cookbook).
  • Substitute ½ of the mayonnaise with sour cream or natural yoghurt for a slightly different flavour.
