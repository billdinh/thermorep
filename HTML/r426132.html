Spam Fritters with Tomato Sauce

Ingredients

Batter

  • 120 g self-raising flour
  • 270 g whole milk, cold
  • 1 medium egg
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt

Tomato Sauce

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 60 g onions, quartered
  • 15 g olive oil
  • 400 g tinned chopped tomatoes
  • ½ tsp dried oregano
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt

Fritters

  • oil, for frying
  • 340 g spam, cut in chunks (2-3 cm)

Preparation

Batter

  1. Place flour, milk, egg and salt in mixing bowl then mix 15 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and set aside to rest. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl and continue with recipe.

Tomato Sauce

  1. Place garlic, onions and oil in mixing bowl, chop 5 sec/speed 5 then sauté 5 min/100°C/speed .
  2. Add tomatoes, oregano and salt then simmer 12 min/100°C/speed . Leave lid on mixing bowl to keep sauce warm.

Fritters

  1. Preheat oil in a deep fat fryer or deep saucepan.
  2. Working in batches of 4-6 pieces, mix chunks of spam in reserved batter then fry in hot oil until crisp and golden (approx. 1-2 minutes). Drain on paper towels.
  3. Serve fritters with tomato sauce on the side.

Useful Items

deep fat fryer, slotted spoon, paper towels

Hints & Tricks

  • Substitute spam with tinned corned beef.
