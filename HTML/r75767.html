Normandy Apple Flan

Ingredients

Shortcrust pastry

  • 220 g plain flour
  • 170 g butter, chilled, diced
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 45 g water, chilled

Tart filling

  • 1 lemon, thin peelings of yellow rind from ½ of the lemon, and juice of the whole lemon
  • 50 g granulated sugar
  • 450 g cooking apples, peeled, quartered, cores removed
  • 400 g dessert apples, peeled, quartered, cores removed
  • 50 - 60 g golden syrup
  • 60 g crabapple jelly

Preparation

Shortcrust pastry

  1. Place flour, butter and salt in mixing bowl and pulse Turbo/0.5 sec/3-5 times until coarse crumbs form.
  2. Add egg yolk and 30 g chilled water, pulse Turbo/0.5 sec/3-5 times, just until pastry comes together.
  3. If it's too dry, add 15 g more chilled water and pulse Turbo/0.5 sec/1-3 times.
  4. Transfer to loose bottom fluted tart tin (Ø 28 cm) and press evenly into the base and sides and press evenly into the base and sides, then chill 30 minutes in refrigerator.
  5. Preheat oven to 220°C. Meanwhile, cover pastry with baking paper and fill with baking beans.
  6. Bake 10 minutes (220 °C) then remove from oven and lift out baking paper and baking beans. Return tart shell to oven for a further 5 minutes (220°C). Meanwhile, clean and dry mixing bowl.

Tart filling

  1. Place lemon peelings and sugar in mixing bowl and grind 15 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add cooking apple pieces and chop 10 sec/speed 5, then cook 7 min/90°C/speed 1 without measuring cup.
  3. Blend 20 sec/speed 8. Leaving any excess juices in mixing bowl, use a spoon to transfer apple purée to baked tart shell. Discard excess juices then rinse and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Cut dessert apple quarters into 1/2 cm slices and arrange in an overlapping flower-shaped pattern on top of the apple purée.
  5. Place golden syrup, lemon juice and jelly into mixing bowl. Blend 2 min/80°C/speed 2 then brush melted mixture over apple slices.
  6. Reduce oven to 200°C and bake 25 minutes. Cool completely then remove from tin and serve on its own or with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

Useful Items

fluted tart tin (Ø 28 cm), baking beans, baking paper, slotted spoon, pastry brush, refrigerator, oven
