Tamales Verdes

Ingredients

  • 21 oz green tomatillos, halved
  • 4 oz white onions, quartered
  • 3 oz jalapeño chilies, stems and seeds removed
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 5 tsp salt, divided
  • 85 oz water, divided
  • 32 oz chicken thighs, boneless and skinless
  • 2 tsp sugar (optional)
  • 4 ½ oz vegetable shortening
  • 17 ½ oz masa harina
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 15 - 30 dried corn husks, rehydrated

Preparation

  1. Place tomatillos, onions, jalapenos, garlic, 2 tsp salt and 7 oz water into mixing bowl and cook 15 min/212°F/speed , then blend 30 sec/speed 5-10 increasing speed gradually.
  2. Add chicken and sugar and cook 18 min/212°F//speed , then shred 4 sec//speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and let cool. Clean mixing bowl.
  3. Insert butterfly whisk, add shortening and whip 3 min/speed 3.
  4. Remove butterfly whisk, add masa harina, 25 oz water, baking powder and 3 tsp salt and mix 2 min/speed 3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix a further 2 min/speed 3. Transfer dough to a bowl and set aside.
  5. With the narrow end of the husk facing towards you, place approximately 3 oz of prepared dough onto the center of 1 softened corn husk and spread outward, evenly covering half the husk on the side facing away from you, leaving about 2 inches of the narrow side clear.
  6. Place 2 tbsp reserved chicken onto center of dough. Wrap the chicken by folding over left and right edges of corn husk and then folding the long narrow end to create a tamale. Place tamales into Varoma open side up and seam side down and repeat with remaining ingredients.
  7. Place 53 oz water into mixing bowl, place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 85 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  8. Let steamed tamales rest 20 minutes inside Varoma. Reheat reserved tomatillo sauce and serve with tamales.
