Besan ki burfi

Ingredients

  • 100 g almonds
  • 300 g dried chickpeas
  • 2 tbsp semolina
  • 200 g ghee (see Tips)
  • 3 tsp ground cardamom
  • 370 g sweetened condensed milk
  • unsalted shelled pistachios, chopped for decorating
  • gold leaf, for decorating (optional)

Preparation

  1. Grease and line a slice tin (28 x 18 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place almonds into mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place chickpeas into mixing bowl and mill 1 min/speed 10.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and roast 8 min/Varoma/speed 2 .
  5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and roast 8 min/Varoma//speed 2.
  6. Add semolina and 100 g of the ghee and mix 20 sec/speed 7, then roast 20 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  7. Add reserved almond meal and remaining 100 g ghee and heat 15 min/Varoma/speed 1, then blend 30 sec/speed 4 -9, increasing speed gradually from speed 4 to speed 9.
  8. Add cardamom and condensed milk and mix 30 sec/speed 3. Transfer into prepared slice tin. Decorate with pistachios and gold leaf (optional) and press down to smooth surface. Cover with plastic wrap and place into refrigerator for 4 hours or until set. Remove from refrigerator and cut into pieces (approx. 2 x 3 cm). Serve immediately or store in the refrigerator in a sealable container until ready to serve.

Useful Items

baking paper, slice tin (28 x 18 cm), bowl, plastic wrap, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • To make your own ghee, please refer to the recipe on this recipe chip.
  • You can store these in the refrigerator in a sealable container for up to 1 month.
