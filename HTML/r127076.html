Peanut Butter Milkshake

Ingredients

  • 30 g peanuts, dry-roasted, unsalted
  • 140 g peanut butter
  • 30 g dark chocolate (50% cocoa), cut in pieces
  • 370 g milk
  • 200 g cream, 35-37 % fat, chilled
  • 1 tbsp icing sugar (optional)
  • 350 g chocolate ice cream
  • 200 g ice cubes
Nutrition
per 1 glass
Calories
2863 kJ / 683 kcal
Protein
18 g
Carbohydrates
35 g
Fat
52 g

Preparation

  1. Place peanuts in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place 20 g peanut butter, dark chocolate and 20 g milk in mixing bowl then melt 3 min/50°C/speed 2. Transfer peanut butter-chocolate sauce to a bowl and allow to cool for 5 minutes. Clean mixing bowl.
  3. Insert butterfly whisk. Place cream and icing sugar in mixing bowl then whip without measuring cup speed 3, without setting a time, until desired stiffness is achieved, watching carefully to avoid overwhipping.  Remove butterfly whisk, transfer whipped cream to an icing bag and cool in refrigerator.
  4. Place 120 g peanut butter, 350 g milk, chocolate ice cream and ice cubes in mixing bowl then blend 1 min 30 sec/speed 10. Garnish 4 glasses with reserved peanut butter-chocolate sauce and transfer milkshake to garnished glasses. Top with reserved whipped cream and chopped peanuts. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

icing bag, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • For a peanut butter cheesecake milkshake, replace the milk and cream with 150 g cream cheese, 250 g greek yogurt and 150 g whole milk.
