Sticky Sriracha tofu bowl

Ingredients

Sticky Sriracha® tofu

  • 350 g firm tofu
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 200 g water
  • 60 g tamari
  • 40 g honey
  • 30 g Sriracha® sauce
  • 25 g gluten free cornflour

Veg and black rice bowl

  • 200 g black rice
  • 1000 g water
  • 1 tsp salt (optional)
  • 2 carrots (approx. 180 g), cut into batons
  • 1 bunch broccolini (approx. 160 g)
  • 100 g red cabbage, shredded
  • 100 g snow peas, trimmed and cut into halves lengthways (diagonally)
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp black sesame seeds

Preparation

Sticky Sriracha® tofu

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh tofu into it. Cut into 24 pieces and set aside.
  2. Place garlic into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add water, tamari, honey, Sriracha® sauce and cornflour and cook 2 min/100°C/speed 1. Pour over tofu and set bowl aside to marinate. Do not clean mixing bowl.

Veg and black rice bowl

  1. Insert simmering basket into mixing bowl and weigh rice into it. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and rinse rice until water runs clear.
  2. Place water and salt (optional) into mixing bowl, then re-insert simmering basket with rice and cook 20 min/100°C/speed 4.
  3. Place Varoma dish into position and place carrots and broccolini into it. Insert Varoma tray, weigh red cabbage and snow peas into it, secure Varoma lid and steam 5-10 min/Varoma/speed 1 or until cooked to your liking.
  4. Meanwhile, place a large frying pan over medium heat and add sesame oil. Drain tofu, reserving marinade. Add tofu to frying pan and fry 2-3 minutes on each side, until warmed through and golden brown. Transfer onto a plate and cover to keep warm.
  5. Remove Varoma and set aside. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula. Divide rice, vegetables and tofu between serving bowls, sprinkle with sesame seeds and serve with reserved marinade.

Useful Items

bowl, large frying pan, large plate, serving bowls, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • We prefer to lightly fry the tofu for added texture and flavour, but it can be steamed on the Varoma tray with the cabbage and snow peas.
