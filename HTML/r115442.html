Sweetcorn fritters with chilli jam and avocado cream

Ingredients

Chilli jam

  • 400 g red capsicum, deseeded and cut into pieces (approx. 3 cm)
  • 3 tsp dried chilli flakes
  • 200 g white sugar
  • 100 g apple cider vinegar
  • 80 g water

Avocado cream

  • 1 avocado, flesh only
  • 50 g sour cream
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper

Sweetcorn fritters

  • 2 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 10 sprigs fresh chives
  • 70 g chickpea flour
  • 30 g gluten free cornflour
  • 2 tsp gluten free baking powder (see Tips)
  • ½ tsp paprika
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 - 2 pinches ground black pepper, to taste
  • 2 eggs, separated
  • 120 g water
  • 400 g canned corn kernels, drained (approx. 250 g after draining)
  • 200 g feta cheese, cut into pieces (approx. 5 mm)
  • olive oil, for frying
  • watercess leaves, for garnishing

Preparation

Chilli jam

  1. Place capsicum into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add dried chilli flakes, sugar, vinegar and water and cook 30 min/Varoma//speed , placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid (see Tips).
  3. Transfer into a sealable sterilised jar, allow to cool and place into refrigerator until ready to serve. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Avocado cream

  1. Place avocado, sour cream, salt and pepper into mixing bowl and purée 15 sec/speed 4. Transfer into a sealable container and place into refrigerator until ready to serve. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Sweetcorn fritters

  1. Place parsley and chives into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7.
  2. Add chickpea flour, cornflour, baking powder, paprika, salt and pepper and mix 5 sec/speed 5.
  3. Add egg yolks and water and mix 10 sec/speed 5 or until smooth.
  4. Add corn and feta and mix 10 sec//speed 2, until combined. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Thoroughly clean and dry mixing bowl.
  5. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg whites into mixing bowl and whisk 1 min/speed 3 or until stiff peaks form.
  6. Transfer egg white mixture into reserved batter and fold in gently using spatula.
  7. Line a large plate with paper towel and set aside. Place a large frying pan over medium heat and add 2-3 tablespoons of the oil. Place large spoonfuls of batter into pan and fry for 2-3 minutes on each side until golden. Transfer cooked fritters onto lined plate to drain. Repeat with remaining batter (makes approx. 20 fritters).
  8. Serve warm fritters topped with Avocado cream, Chilli jam and watercress.

Useful Items

sealable sterilised jar, sealable container, bowl, large frying pan, large plate, paper towel, refrigerator, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • To make your own Gluten free baking powder, please refer to the recipe on this recipe chip.
  • This chilli jam creates strong vapours while cooking. We recommend that your cooking area is well-ventilated by opening a window or turning on an extraction fan.
