Stewed Rhubarb with Ginger Wine Syllabub

Ingredients

Syllabub

  • 1 lemon, thin peelings of skin only
  • 80 g sugar
  • 100 g ginger wine
  • 250 g whipping cream

Stewed Rhubarb

  • 200 g rhubarb, cut in pieces (3 cm)
  • 30 g sugar
  • 30 g water
  • candied ginger, cut in small pieces, for garnishing

Preparation

Syllabub

  1. Place lemon peel and sugar in mixing bowl then grind 10 sec/speed 9.
  2. Add ginger wine and mix 5 sec/speed 3. Strain through a fine mesh sieve into a jug and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.
  3. Insert butterfly whisk. Place cream in mixing bowl and, without setting a time, whip speed 3 until soft peaks form, watching carefully to avoid over-whipping.
  4. Add reserved ginger wine mixture and whip 20-30 sec/speed 3 until soft peaks form, watching carefully to avoid over-whipping. Transfer to a bowl and place in fridge for 1 hour to set. Clean mixing bowl.

Stewed Rhubarb

  1. Place rhubarb, sugar and water in mixing bowl then cook 8 min/100°C//speed . Allow to cool slightly. Divide evenly between individual glasses, then place in fridge until ready to serve.
  2. Remove from fridge, carefully spoon reserved syllabub on top of stewed rhubarb and garnish with candied ginger.

Useful Items

fine mesh sieve, glasses, jug, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Syllabubs can also be made with fruit incorporated into them. First make a thick fruit sauce and allow it to cool, then add whipped cream and a wine liqueur of your choice. Try these:
    Apricot Syllabub
    Place 40 g dried apricots in mixing bowl and chop 20 sec/speed 8. Add 50 g pear cider and 80 g sugar then cook 5 min/100°C/speed 2. Refrigerate until chilled and combine as per step 3 with a further 50 g cider. Garnish with toasted flaked almonds.
    Prune Syllabub
    Place 60 g pitted prunes in mixing bowl and chop 20 sec/speed 8. Add 60 g water and 80 g sugar then cook 5 min/100°C/speed 2. Refrigerate until chilled and combine as per step 3 with a further 50 g fortified wine (e.g. sherry, port). Garnish with grated dark chocolate.
