Pan Pizza

Ingredients

  • 9 ½ oz water, room temperature
  • 1 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 pinch sugar
  • 14 oz bread flour
  • 3 tsp olive oil, divided, plus extra to grease
  • 2 tsp fine sea salt
  • 6 oz low-moisture mozzarella, cubed (approx. 1 in.)
  • 3 ½ oz yellow onion, quartered (approx. 1 medium)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 12 oz canned crushed tomatoes
  • 8 oz white button mushrooms, thinly sliced
  • ¼ tsp dried basil
  • ¼ tsp dried oregano
  • ¼ tsp dried rosemary
  • 1 pinch dried red chili flakes
  • green bell peppers (sliced), to taste (optional)

Preparation

  1. Place water, yeast and sugar into mixing bowl and mix 20 sec/speed 2.
  2. Add flour, 2 tsp olive oil and salt and knead 2 min/. Place in a lightly greased bowl, shape into a ball and cover tightly with cling wrap. Let rise in a warm place for 30 minutes or until doubled.
  3. Place mozzarella into mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 6. Set cheese aside.
  4. Place onion, garlic and 1 tsp olive oil into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down mixing bowl with spatula and cook 4 min/212°F/speed 1.
  5. Add tomatoes, mushrooms, basil, oregano, rosemary and chili flakes. With simmering basket instead of measuring cup on top of mixing bowl lid, cook 5-7 min/212°F//speed 1 until slightly thickened. Set sauce aside.
  6. Transfer dough to a lightly floured surface, divide into 2 equal pieces and shape each piece into a ball. Lightly grease 2 cast iron skillets (approx. 8 in.-10 in.) or cake pans and place dough balls into prepared skillets or pans. Working outwards from the center, evenly push dough with your fingers to the edge of the pan. Cover tightly with cling wrap and set aside for 15 minutes to rest.
  7. Preheat oven to 500°F.
  8. Divide sauce and reserved cheese between the two prepared pizza bases (optional: top pizza with sliced green peppers or pepperoni).
  9. Bake pizza for 15-20 minutes (500°F) or until bottom of crust is golden brown. Allow to rest for 5 minutes before serving.

Useful Items

bowl, cling wrap, cast iron skillets (approx. 8 in.-10 in.) or round cake pans, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • If using cake pans instead of skillets, line them with parchment paper.
