Fall Chopped Salad with Yogurt Dressing

Ingredients

  • 16 oz butternut squash
  • 25 oz water
  • 10 oz raw beets, peeled, quartered
  • 4 oz green apple, quartered
  • 1 oz pomegranate molasses
  • 1 oz honey
  • salt, to taste
  • ground black pepper, to taste
  • 8 oz plain, unsweetened yogurt, to taste, to serve
  • fresh thyme leaves
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
724 kJ / 173 kcal
Protein
4 g
Carbohydrates
38 g
Fat
2 g

Preparation

  1. With a spiralizer, prepare butternut squash. Set Varoma dish into position, place in spiralized squash and set aside.
  2. Place water into mixing bowl, set Varoma with squash into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 13 min/Varoma/speed 2. Transfer to a bowl and set aside to cool.
  3. Place beets, apple, pomegranate molasses, honey, salt and pepper into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Transfer to bowl with reserved squash and combine.
  4. Serve salad with a dollop of yogurt and garnish with thyme.
