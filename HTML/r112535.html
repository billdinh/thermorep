Cranberry and coconut toasted cereal

Ingredients

  • 250 g rolled oats
  • 100 g shredded coconut
  • 160 g buckwheat
  • 125 g raw hazelnuts
  • 150 g dried cranberries
  • 30 g sunflower seeds
  • 30 g chia seeds
  • 1 tbsp ground cinnamon
  • 80 g coconut oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste
  • 130 g pure maple syrup
  • 40 g raw cacao powder
  • 30 g coconut sugar

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 150°C. Line 2 baking trays (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place oats, shredded coconut, buckwheat, hazelnuts, cranberries, sunflower seeds, chia seeds and cinnamon into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec//speed 2. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place remaining ingredients into mixing bowl and mix 2 min/50°C/speed 2.
  4. Add half of the reserved dry mixture and mix 20 sec//speed 4.
  5. Add all remaining dry mixture and mix 40 sec//speed 4, with aid of spatula until combined.
  6. Spread mixture evenly across prepared baking trays, then toast for 20 minutes (150°C). Gently stir mixture with spatula to ensure mixture is evenly toasted and bake for a further 15-20 minutes (150°C), or until golden brown. Allow mixture to cool completely, then serve or transfer into a sealable container until ready to serve.

Useful Items

2 baking trays (30 x 40 cm), baking paper, bowl, sealable container, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with milk or yoghurt for breakfast. You can even pack it into school lunches as a snack to munch on during the day.
