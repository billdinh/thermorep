Turkey roulades with minted pea mash

Ingredients

  • ½ lemon, preferably organic, zest and juice
  • 2 pinches fine salt, plus extra to taste
  • 2 pinches freshly ground black pepper, plus extra to taste
  • 4 - 6 sun-dried tomatoes, in oil, well drained
  • ¼ garlic clove (optional)
  • 400 g turkey breast fillet, skinless (4 slices)
  • 50 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 1000 g water
  • 1 tbsp vegetable stock paste, homemade
  • 400 g fresh green peas, shelled
  • 4 fresh mint leaves, washed and dried

Preparation

  1. Place lemon zest, salt and pepper into mixing bowl and chop 6 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place sun-dried tomatoes and garlic into mixing bowl and chop Turbo/0.5 sec/3 times. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Stretch out turkey slices on the work surface and, if necessary, flatten with a mallet. Season one side of each slice with lemon zest seasoning. Roll each slice into rolls with Ø 4 cm and length 10-12 cm. Tie with kitchen string and brush with 10 g olive oil. Place roulades in Varoma dish.
  4. Place water and stock into mixing bowl, insert simmering basket and add peas. Insert Varoma and cook 20 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  5. Set Varoma aside and keep turkey rolls warm. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and strain contents. Empty mixing bowl. Place cooked peas into mixing bowl, add lemon juice, mint leaves, olive oil, salt and pepper and chop 5 sec/speed 6. Mix with aid of spatula and repeat chopping if required.
  6. Remove string from roulades and slice, top slices with reserved sun-dried tomato paste.
  7. Serve with mint pea mash.
