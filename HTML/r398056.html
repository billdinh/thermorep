Miso Glazed Eggplant with Cucumber Salad

Ingredients

Eggplant

  • 1 garlic clove
  • ½ oz fresh ginger
  • 1 oz sugar
  • 3 oz red miso (or akamiso)
  • 2 oz mirin
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 4 eggplants, Ichiban Japanese
  • vegetable oil, to brush

Cucumber Salad

  • 1 English cucumber (approx. 10 oz)
  • 2 oz red onion
  • 2 oz rice wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 pinch chili flakes
  • ½ oz fresh cilantro, chopped
  • sesame seeds, to garnish

Preparation

Eggplant

  1. Preheat oven 400°F. Line a baking sheet with foil and set aside.
  2. Place garlic, ginger and sugar into mixing bowl and mix 3 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add miso, mirin and sesame oil and mix 5 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Trim stem tops from eggplant and slice in half lengthwise.
  5. Lightly score the flesh of cut eggplants in a cross pattern and brush with oil and half the miso glaze. Bake for 20 minutes (400°F). Brush with remaining miso glaze and bake an additional 5-10 minutes (400°F). Meanwhile make the Cucumber Salad.

Cucumber Salad

  1. With a spiralizer prepare cucumber then, use a vegetable peeler to julienne onions and set aside.
  2. Place rice wine vinegar, sugar, sesame oil and chili flakes into mixing bowl and mix 5 sec/speed 4.
  3. Transfer to a serving dish and toss with reserved spiralized cucumber, julienned onions and cilantro. Garnish with sesame seeds and serve with prepared eggplant.

Useful Items

baking sheet, aluminum foil, basting brush, vegetable spiralizer, vegetable peeler, oven
