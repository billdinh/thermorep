Green Bean Casserole

Ingredients

  • 2 slices stale bread
  • 29 oz water
  • 18 oz fresh green beans, ends trimmed
  • 3 ½ oz red onion, quartered
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 2 oz extra virgin olive oil
  • 9 oz potatoes, cubed (approx. 1 in.)
  • 2 heaping tsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 8 oz fresh mushrooms, sliced
  • 16 oz whole milk
  • 1 tbsp vegetable stock
  • 1 heaping tsp salt
  • ½ tsp white pepper (optional)
  • ½ tsp freshly ground black pepper (optional)
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
615 kJ / 147 kcal
Protein
5 g
Carbohydrates
16 g
Fat
8 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Place bread into mixing bowl and grate 4 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position, weigh in green beans, secure Varoma lid and steam 10 min/Varoma/speed 3. Remove Varoma lid and stir beans to ensure even cooking. Secure Varoma lid and steam a further 5 min/Varoma/speed 3. Transfer cooked beans into a casserole dish and set aside. Discard water.
  4. Place onion, garlic and olive oil into mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 5. Add potatoes and parsley and cook 10 min/250°F/speed 1, then purée 12 sec/speed 8. Add mushrooms and blend 10 sec/speed 5. Add milk, vegetable stock, salt, white pepper and black pepper and cook 16 min/185°F/speed 1.
  5. Pour puréed sauce over reserved beans, sprinkle with reserved bread crumbs and bake for 25 minutes (350°F). Serve hot.

Useful Items

casserole dish (approx. 12 in. x 9½ in. x 2 in.), oven
