Barbecue Beanburger Sliders

Ingredients

Sliders

  • 60 g walnut halves
  • 10 g olive oil, plus extra for brushing
  • 170 g red onions, quartered
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 240 g tinned kidney beans, rinsed, drained (1 x 400 g tin)
  • 100 g tinned black beans, rinsed, drained (drained weight)
  • 140 g quinoa, cooked weight, cooled (see tip)
  • ½ - 1 Tbsp chilli powder, to taste
  • ¾ tsp fine sea salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper

Barbecue Sauce

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 120 g ketchup
  • 2 Tbsp soft dark brown sugar
  • 2 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce (see tip)
  • 1 Tbsp cider vinegar
  • 1 dash chilli sauce (e.g. Tabasco®)
  • ¼ tsp mustard powder
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • 12 slider buns

Preparation

Sliders

  1. Place walnuts in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place oil, onions and garlic in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then sauté 5 min/100°C/speed .
  3. Add kidney beans and black beans then roughly mash 3 sec/speed 4.
  4. Add quinoa, chilli powder, salt and pepper then stir 10 sec//speed 2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Set /speed 2 and add slowly add ground walnuts until a malleable mixture forms. Transfer to a bowl and chill for 15 minutes. Meanwhile, preheat oven to 190°C and line a baking tray with baking paper.
  6. Form mixture into 12 small patties and place on prepared baking tray. Brush tops with olive oil then bake for 35-40 minutes (190°C), turning over halfway through, until browned. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl and make barbecue sauce.

Barbecue Sauce

  1. Place garlic in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add ketchup, dark brown sugar, Worcestershire sauce, cider vinegar, chilli sauce, mustard powder and salt then heat 10 min/98°C/speed 0.5.
  3. Brush cooked patties all over with barbecue sauce then serve in slider buns with desired toppings.

Useful Items

baking tray, baking paper, pastry brush, oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • To cook quinoa, place 1000 g water in mixing bowl, insert simmering basket and weigh in approx. 60 g quinoa. Cook 20 min/100°C/speed 4. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and leave to quinoa to cool.
  • Vegetarians should use a vegetarian Worcestershire sauce.
