Salmon Gravadlax with Buckwheat Blinis

Ingredients

Salmon Gravadlax (prepare 48 hours in advance)

  • 20 g fresh dill leaves
  • 120 g coarse sea salt
  • 80 g caster sugar
  • 15 g vodka
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper, or to taste
  • 1 salmon fillet, fresh, boneless, skin on (approx. 300 g)

Lemon Dill Cream

  • 100 g double cream
  • 20 g lemon juice
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt, or to taste
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper, or to taste
  • 1 small handful fresh dill leaves (approx. 5 g)

Buckwheat Blinis

  • 100 g buckwheat flour
  • 150 g water
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 medium egg
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt, or to taste
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper, or to taste
  • 20 g salted butter, for cooking

Garnish

  • 35 g olive oil
  • 15 g lemon juice
  • 15 g white balsamic vinegar
  • 10 g honey
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt, or to taste
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper, or to taste
  • 20 g rocket salad

Preparation

Salmon Gravadlax (prepare 48 hours in advance)

  1. Place dill in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add salt, sugar, vodka and pepper then mix 20 sec/speed 2. Place salmon skin-side down in a dish and cover with dill and salt mixture. Wrap salmon and marinade tightly in cling film then return to dish and leave to marinate in fridge for 48 hours.

Lemon Dill Cream

  1. Insert butterfly whisk. Place cream, lemon juice, salt and pepper in mixing bowl then whisk 1 min/speed 3. Remove butterfly whisk.
  2. Add dill and mix 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and refrigerate until needed. Clean mixing bowl.

Buckwheat Blinis

  1. Place buckwheat flour, water, baking powder, egg, salt and pepper in mixing bowl then mix 20 sec/speed 6.
  2. Heat a knob of butter in a large pancake or frying pan over a medium heat. Place 3 separate tablespoons batter into pan and cook for 2 minutes on each side. Transfer to a plate, then repeat, adding more butter when necessary, until all batter is used.

Garnish

  1. Place a jug on mixing bowl lid and weigh in oil then set aside.
  2. Place lemon juice, vinegar, honey, salt and pepper in mixing bowl then mix 1 min/speed 3, while very slowly pouring reserved oil onto mixing bowl lid, letting it drizzle around measuring cup in a thin stream onto rotating blades. Dress rocket with vinaigrette.
  3. Unwrap salmon, brush off marinade, and remove skin with a knife then cut salmon into very thin slices. Arrange dressed rocket, salmon slices and cream on a plate and serve chilled with blinis.

Useful Items

dish, cling film, pancake pan or frying pan, jug, pastry brush, refrigerator, hob

Hints & Tricks

  • For a more complete dish, serve with raw vegetables or fruits (tomatoes, grapes, apples), or a potato salad.
  • Fry the slices of salmon lightly for added texture and colour.
