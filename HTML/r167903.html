Middle Eastern eggs

Ingredients

Spinach filling

  • 50 g onions, cut into pieces
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 10 g olive oil
  • 350 g fresh baby spinach leaves
  • 1 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1 tsp sumac
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper

Eggs

  • butter, for greasing
  • 6 eggs
  • ½ tsp salt
  • freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 500 g water
  • 10 g toasted pine nuts, to garnish
  • sumac, to garnish

Preparation

Spinach filling

  1. Place onions, garlic and oil into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  3. Add spinach leaves and sauté 5 min/120°C/speed 1 without measuring cup. Season with nutmeg, sumac, salt, black pepper and mix with spatula.

Eggs

  1. Grease ramekins and place sautéed spinach at the bottom of each ramekin. Crack an egg on top of spinach in each ramekin. Season with salt and ground black pepper and distribute ramekins in Varoma dish and tray. Insert Varoma tray in dish and close.
  2. Place water into mixing bowl, place Varoma into position and steam 15 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove Varoma, carefully remove ramekins from Varoma and garnish with pine nuts and a pinch of sumac before serving.

Useful Items

ramekins (approx. Ø 9 cm, height 5 cm)

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with pitta bread or any type of bread of your choice.
  • For a richer version, in step 6 add a teaspoon labneh onto the egg whites, avoiding to cover the yolk.
