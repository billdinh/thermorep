Feta and Honey Cheesecakes

Ingredients

Pastry Bases

  • 25 g unsalted butter, diced
  • 50 g filo pastry, shredded (see tip)
  • 1 Tbsp caster sugar

Cheesecake Cream

  • 160 g cream cheese
  • 160 g double cream (extra thick - see tip)
  • 40 g icing sugar
  • 40 g honey, thick
  • 50 g feta cheese, smooth, crumbled
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Honey Syrup

  • 50 g runny honey
  • 50 g water

Assembly

  • fresh oregano leaves (optional)
  • 2 Tbsp roasted almonds, roughly chopped
  • 100 g seasonal fruit (e.g. raspberries, blueberries, apricots)

Preparation

Pastry Bases

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Place butter in mixing bowl and melt 3 min/98°C/speed .
  3. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in pastry and sugar. Remove bowl, pour over melted butter then stir to coat. Tip out onto a baking tray and divide into 4 equal amounts. Form into flat nests, approx. Ø 7-8 cm. Bake for 8-12 minutes (180°C) until lightly browned at the edges. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl. Transfer nests to a wire rack to cool.

Cheesecake Cream

  1. Insert butterfly whisk. Place cream cheese, double cream, icing sugar, honey, feta and vanilla extract in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 3. Remove butterfly whisk and finish mixing with spatula if necessary. Transfer to a bowl, cover and place in fridge until required. Clean mixing bowl.

Honey Syrup

  1. Place honey and water in mixing bowl then boil 4 min/100°C/speed 0.5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside to cool completely.

Assembly

  1. When all components are cool, place a pastry nest on each plate. Top with a generous scoop of cheesecake cream. Sprinkle over oregano (if using), almonds and fruit.
  2. Drizzle over honey syrup then serve immediately.

Useful Items

baking tray, wire rack, dessert plates, cling film, oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • This recipe traditionally uses kataifi or kadaif pastry - a finely shredded filo pastry which looks like very fine vermicelli noodles or shredded wheat. It is widely used in Middle Eastern, Greek and Turkish cuisine for making pastries. If you cannot find this pastry, use filo pastry sheets and slice them thinly into strips.
  • Instead of extra thick double cream, just whip normal double cream 30-40 sec/speed 3 before adding the rest of the cheesecake cream ingredients.
