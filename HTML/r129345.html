Potato goulash

Ingredients

  • 100 onions, cut into pieces
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 150 g carrots, cut into pieces
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 100 g spring onions, cut into rings
  • 100 g peppers, red and green, in bite-sized pieces
  • 500 g pork shoulder, boneless, cut into pieces (approx. 2,5 x 2 cm)
  • 350 g potatoes, in bite-sized pieces
  • 400 g water
  • 1 tsp lemon zest, grated
  • 1 tsp tarragon, dried
  • 1 tsp marjoram, dried
  • ½ - 1 tsp hot paprika
  • ½ tsp fine salt
  • ¼ tsp black pepper, freshly ground
  • 4 tbsp crème fraîche

Preparation

  1. Place onion, garlic, and carrot in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add olive oil and sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  3. Add spring onions, paprika and pork goulash and sauté 5 min/120°C//speed , placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid to help prevent splashing.
  4. Add potato, water, lemon zest, tarragon, marjoram, paprika, salt and pepper and cook 30 min/100°C//speed 1. Check if meat is done and prolong cooking time if necessary. Transfer to 4 soup bowls and serve with 1 tbsp crème fraîche on each.

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve goulash with crispy bread on the side.
  • If you can't find crème fraîche or sour cream, instead serve with Greek yoghurt to which a splash of lemon juice has been added.
