Chocolate, Raisin and Hazelnut Biscuits

Ingredients

  • 100 g blanched hazelnuts
  • 130 g unsalted butter, soft
  • 120 g light brown sugar
  • 50 g dark brown muscovado sugar
  • 140 g plain flour
  • 20 g cocoa powder
  • 1 large egg
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
  • 100 g raisins
  • 70 g rolled oats
  • 100 g dark chocolate chips

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C and line 2 large baking trays with baking paper.
  2. Place hazelnuts in mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place butter, light brown sugar and muscovado sugar in mixing bowl then mix 20 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add flour, cocoa powder, egg, baking powder and salt then mix 20 sec/speed 3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add raisins, oats, chocolate chips and reserved chopped nuts then mix 30 sec//speed 3. Finish mixing by hand with spatula, if necessary.
  6. Using wet hands, roll 2 tablespoons dough into balls then flatten each slightly. Place on prepared trays, spaced 5 cm apart. Bake for 15-18 minutes (180°C). Leave on trays for 5 minutes (they will be very soft) then transfer to wire racks to cool.
  7. Serve or store in an airtight container for up to 5 days.

Useful Items

2 baking trays and baking paper, wire racks, oven

Hints & Tricks

These rich, decadent chocolate biscuits are a real treat, with the oats and chopped hazelnuts giving them a lovely texture.
