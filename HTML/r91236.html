Berry Mousse

Ingredients

  • 11 oz fresh mixed red berries (e.g. strawberries, raspberries), plus extra to garnish
  • 7 oz sugar
  • 1 tbsp unflavored gelatin powder
  • 2 oz water
  • 9 oz ricotta cheese
  • 9 oz heavy whipping cream,
  • fresh mint leaves, to garnish
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1184 kJ / 283 kcal
Protein
5 g
Carbohydrates
30 g

17 g

Preparation

  1. Place berries and sugar into mixing bowl and purée 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer approximately 8 oz of puréed berries to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Add gelatin, sprinkling around outside edges avoiding blades and mix 30 sec/speed 2, until completely dissolved. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and heat 2 min/140°F/speed 1.
  3. Add water and heat 2 min/140°F/speed 2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add reserved puréed berries and ricotta and blend 20 sec/speed 8. Transfer mixture into a large bowl and refrigerate until gelatin starts to set and mixture has the consistency of heavy cream (approx. 30 min). Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  5. Insert butterfly whisk. Place cream into mixing bowl and without setting a time whip speed 3, until desired stiffness is achieved, watching carefully to avoid over-whipping. Remove butterfly whisk.
  6. Gently fold whipped cream into fruit mixture with spatula.
  7. Divide fruit mousse into dessert bowls and refrigerate for 4 hours.
  8. Garnish with berries and fresh mint leaves and serve chilled.

Useful Items

dessert bowls, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve fruit mousse with a Berry Coulis.
  • Ricotta cheese can also be replaced with Cloumage, Quark or cream cheese.
