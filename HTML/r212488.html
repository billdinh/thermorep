Coconut Pie

Ingredients

Pastry

  • 250 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 125 g unsalted butter, cold, diced
  • 2 Tbsp desiccated coconut
  • 4 Tbsp water, ice cold

Filling

  • 125 g unsalted butter, diced
  • 150 g desiccated coconut
  • 350 g caster sugar
  • 150 g buttermilk
  • 5 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • whipped cream, for serving

Preparation

Pastry

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C.
  2. Place flour and butter in mixing bowl then chop 7 sec/speed 5.
  3. Add coconut and water then mix 10 sec/speed 5. Tip out onto a lightly-floured surface and roll out to line a fluted tart tin (Ø 26 cm). Line with baking paper and fill with baking beans then blind bake for 15 minutes (200°C). Remove baking paper and beans then bake for a further 10-15 minutes (200°C) until crisp and golden. Set aside. Reduce oven temperature to 180°C and clean mixing bowl.

Filling

  1. Place butter in mixing bowl and melt 4 min/50°C/speed 1.
  2. Add coconut, sugar, buttermilk, eggs and vanilla extract then mix 15 sec/speed 4. Pour into baked pastry case then bake for 35-40 minutes (180°C) until filling is set and firm to the touch. Turn off oven but leave pie to stand in oven, with door ajar, for 15 minutes.
  3. Serve warm, or at room temperature, with whipped cream.

Useful Items

baking paper and baking beans, fluted tart tin (Ø 26 cm), rolling pin, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • This sweet pie is frequently served at special family gatherings, such as weddings.
