Creamy French Dressing

Ingredients

  • 120 g sunflower oil
  • 2 egg yolks, from medium eggs
  • 40 g Dijon mustard
  • 30 g white wine vinegar
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper
  • 1 pinch sugar
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • 250 g single cream
Nutrition
per 1 Total recipe
Calories
8405 kJ / 2007 kcal

15 g
Carbohydrates
13 g

Preparation

  1. Place a jug on mixing bowl lid and weigh in oil. Remove and set aside,
  2. Place egg yolks, mustard, vinegar, cayenne pepper, sugar and salt in mixing bowl then mix 1 min/speed 4 while slowly pouring reserved oil onto mixing bowl lid, letting it drizzle around measuring cup in a thin stream onto rotating blades to create an emulsion.
  3. Place a jug on mixing bowl lid and weigh in cream. Remove measuring cup and mix 15 sec/speed 3 while pouring cream through hole in mixing bowl lid. Serve immediately or refrigerate until needed.

Useful Items

jug, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • This creamy dressing is delicious drizzled over a green salad, or served alongside crudités for dipping.
