Arroz a la Mexicana (Mexican rice)

Ingredients

  • 250 g long grain rice
  • 50 g brown onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 fresh red chilli, trimmed and cut into halves, deseeded if preferred
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 2 tbsp Vegetable stock paste (see Tips)
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp sweet paprika
  • 600 g water
  • 180 g fresh tomatoes, blanched, skins removed, deseeded and cut into cubes (approx. 1 cm - see Tips)
  • 50 g carrot, peeled and cut into cubes (approx. 1 cm)
  • 50 g frozen green peas
  • 2 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only, cut into pieces

Preparation

  1. Place simmering basket onto mixing bowl lid, then weigh rice into it. Rinse rice until water runs clear, then set aside to drain.
  2. Place onion, garlic, chilli and oil into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7.
  3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and sauté 3 min/100°C/speed 1.
  4. Add stock paste, tomato paste, paprika and rinsed rice and cook 3 min/100°C//speed . Transfer rice mixture back into simmering basket.
  5. Place water into mixing bowl and insert simmering basket with rice mixture.
  6. Add tomatoes and carrot to simmering basket, stir through rice and vegetables with spatula and cook 15 min/100°C/speed 4.
  7. Add peas to simmering basket, stir through with spatula and cook 3-6 min/100°C/speed 4, or until rice is cooked.
  8. Carefully remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and transfer rice into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl. Add coriander and stir through rice before serving.

Useful Items

thermal serving bowl or other large bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • Please refer to The Basic Cookbook or Everyday Cookbook for the Vegetable stock paste recipe.
  • Remove tomato skins by using a sharp knife to cut a small cross in base of each tomato and place into bowl. Carefully pour boiling water into bowl to cover tomatoes. Leave until skins split (approx. 1 minute). Remove from water and once cool enough to touch, peel away skins, then cut flesh into quarters.
