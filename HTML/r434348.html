Kale Salad with Carrot and Ginger Dressing

Ingredients

Carrot and Ginger Dressing

  • 5 oz carrots, in pieces (1 in.)
  • 1 in. ginger, peeled
  • 1 ½ oz scallions, white parts only (approx. 5 scallions)
  • 6 oz grapeseed oil
  • 4 oz rice vinegar
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 pinch sugar
  • salt, to taste

Kale Salad

  • 4 oz lacinato kale, center rib removed, thinly sliced, divided
  • 1 oz lemon juice
  • 6 oz rainbow carrots, peeled in ribbons
  • sunflower seeds, to garnish

Preparation

Carrot and Ginger Dressing

  1. Place carrots, ginger, scallions, oil, vinegar, soy sauce, sugar and salt into mixing bowl and purée 50 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.

Kale Salad

  1. Place 3 oz kale and lemon juice into mixing bowl and massage 2 min//speed 2.5.
  2. Add remaining 1 oz kale, carrot ribbons and half the reserved dressing and mix 2 min//speed 2.5. Transfer to a serving platter, garnish with sunflower seeds and serve with remaining dressing, to taste on the side. Store any remaining dressing in the refrigerator for up to one week.
