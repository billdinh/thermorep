Spider Web Cupcakes

Ingredients

Cupcakes

  • 170 g caster sugar
  • 70 g unsalted butter, soft
  • 70 g peanut butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste
  • 230 g plain flour
  • 1 ½ tsp baking powder
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • 260 g whole milk
  • 190 g chocolate chips

Royal Icing

  • 200 g caster sugar
  • 1 egg white, from a large egg
  • 4 - 5 drops lemon juice

Frosting

  • 170 g unsalted butter, soft
  • 320 g icing sugar
  • 60 g cocoa powder
  • 120 g whipping cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 ½ tsp food colouring paste, black (optional)

Preparation

Cupcakes

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Line 2 x 12 hole muffin trays with paper cupcake cases and set aside.
  2. Place sugar, butter, peanut butter, eggs and vanilla paste in mixing bowl then mix 15 sec/speed 5.
  3. Add flour, baking powder, salt and milk then mix 20 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, add chocolate chips and mix 10 sec/speed 5.
  4. Fill cupcake cases ½ full and bake for 18-20 minutes (180°C) or until a cocktail stick inserted in centre comes out clean. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl.

Royal Icing

  1. Place sugar in mixing bowl and grind 20 sec/speed 10.
  2. Add egg white and lemon juice then mix 10 sec/speed 6. Transfer to an icing bag and set aside.

Frosting

  1. Insert butterfly whisk. Place butter in mixing bowl and mix 20 sec/speed 3.
  2. Add icing sugar, cocoa powder, whipping cream, vanilla extract and black food colouring paste then mix 3 min/speed 4.
  3. Apply black frosting with a palette knife. Pipe white royal icing onto top of iced cupcakes in a spiral. Using a cocktail stick, drag through white icing from the centre outwards, creating a spider web.
  4. Serve cupcakes or store in an airtight container until needed.

Useful Items

cocktail stick, piping bag and nozzle, palette knife, 2 x 12 hole muffin tins, airtight container, paper cupcake cases, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Perfect for Halloween and a fun and easy activity to do with kids.
  • Make your own vanilla bean paste: place 6-10 whole vanilla bean pods into mixing bowl and grind 20 sec/speed 10 or until pulverised. Add 3 Tbsp pure vanilla extract and 2 tsp xanthan gum (optional) then stir 3 sec/speed 3. Transfer to a glass jar and use as required.
  • Vanilla bean paste can be replaced with vanilla extract.
