Baked Christmas Pudding Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • 200 g dried mixed fruit
  • 60 g brandy
  • 125 g ginger biscuits
  • 125 g digestive biscuits
  • 70 g butter, diced
  • 150 g Demerara sugar
  • 750 g cream cheese
  • 3 medium eggs
  • 1 ½ tsp mixed spice
  • 1 orange, grated zest only
  • 3 Tbsp dark chocolate, grated

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in dried fruit and brandy. Mix then cover with cling film and set aside to infuse for at least 12 hours, stirring occasionally.
  2. Place ginger biscuits and digestive biscuits in bowl then crush 15 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place butter in mixing bowl lid and melt 2 min/80°C/speed 2.
  4. Add reserved biscuit crumbs then mix 15 sec/speed 3. Press mixture into a spring form tin (Ø 24 cm ), keeping base quite thin and building up the sides. Place in fridge to chill for 15 minutes. Meanwhile, preheat oven to 140°C, clean mixing bowl and make filling.
  5. Drain mixed fruit through a sieve, collecting brandy in a bowl below.
  6. Place sugar in mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 9.
  7. Add cream cheese, eggs, mixed spice, orange zest and reserved brandy from drained fruit then mix 2 min/speed 5.
  8. Add reserved drained fruit and mix 10 sec//speed 3. Pour into prepared base ensuring fruit is distributed evenly. Bake for 1 hour - 1 hour 15 minutes (140°C) until just set. It may be very slightly wobbly in the centre but will set as it cools. Once cool, place in fridge and serve chilled, topped with grated dark chocolate.

Useful Items

cling film, spring form tin (Ø 24 cm), medium mesh sieve, oven, refrigerator
