Beer Battered Cod Bites

Ingredients

  • 400 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 1200 - 1500 g sunflower oil
  • 600 g cod fillets, (skinless) cut in pieces (4 cm)
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • 3 tsp baking powder
  • 550 g ale or lager, sparkling, ice cold
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
257 kJ / 61 kcal
Protein
3.1 g
Carbohydrates
6.9 g
Fat
2.3 g

Preparation

  1. Place a large freezer bag in mixing bowl and weigh flour into it. Place bag in freezer for 15 minutes.
  2. Heat oil to 180°C in a deep saucepan or deep fat fryer. Preheat oven to 180°C and line a baking tray with baking paper.
  3. Season cod with salt and pepper, lightly dust with flour then set aside on a large plate.
  4. Insert butterfly whisk. Place cold flour and baking powder in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 2.
  5. Add ale and whisk 2 min/speed 3 until smooth. Remove butterfly whisk. Batter should be used immediately; dip fish pieces in batter then carefully place in hot oil and deep-fry for 3-5 minutes until golden and cooked through.
  6. Transfer to oven to keep warm while frying remaining fish.
  7. Serve in small cones or bowls with chips if desired.

Useful Items

large food bag, baking tray and paper, deep saucepan or deep fat fryer, large plate, freezer, oven
