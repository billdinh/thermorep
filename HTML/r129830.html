Pear, Rhubarb and Red Fruit Crumble Tart

Ingredients

Pastry

  • 80 g blanched almonds
  • 90 g salted butter, cold, diced
  • 200 g plain flour
  • 100 g caster sugar
  • 1 medium egg
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt

Crumble

  • 100 g salted butter, diced
  • 75 g sugar
  • 130 g plain flour
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon

Filling

  • 400 g pears, peeled, quartered, cored, cut in pieces
  • 350 g rhubarb, cut in pieces
  • 50 g sugar
  • 250 g red fruits, mixed, fresh (e.g. strawberries, raspberries, red currants)

Preparation

Pastry

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Place almonds in mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 7.
  3. Add butter, flour, sugar, egg and salt then mix 20 sec/speed 5. Use to line a square cake tin (22 cm), pressing in well to corners. Bake for 10 minutes (180°C). Meanwhile, make crumble.

Crumble

  1. Place butter, sugar, flour and cinnamon in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Rinse mixing bowl.

Filling

  1. Place pears in mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 5. Strain through simmering basket and discard any juice, then transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place rhubarb, sugar and red fruits in mixing bowl then replace measuring cup with simmering basket and cook 30 sec/120°C/speed 0.5.
  3. Arrange reserved chopped pears over pastry in tin then cover with rhubarb and berry mixture. Sprinkle reserved crumble over the top. Bake for 25-30 minutes (180°C) until golden.
  4. Allow to cool in tin before removing to serve.
