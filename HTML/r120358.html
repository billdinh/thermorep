Carrot gnocchi with zucchini cream

Ingredients

  • 100 g Parmesan cheese, cut into pieces (3 cm), plus extra shaved, to serve
  • 500 g water, plus extra for boiling
  • 500 g carrots, cut into thin rounds (5 mm)
  • 600 g zucchini, cut into cubes (1 cm)
  • 1 egg
  • 2 ½ tsp salt, plus extra to season
  • 250 g plain flour
  • 30 g eschalots
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 1 sprig fresh basil, leaves only, for garnishing

Preparation

  1. Place Parmesan cheese into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  2. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh carrots into it. Secure Varoma lid and steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Place Varoma tray into position, then weigh zucchini onto it, secure Varoma lid and steam 10 min/Varoma/speed 1. Transfer zucchini into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and cover to keep warm. Set carrots aside to cool for 10 minutes. Discard cooking liquid. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Place egg, 1 teaspoon of the salt, reserved carrots and reserved Parmesan into mixing bowl and blend 30 sec/speed 5.
  5. Add flour and mix 20 sec/speed 3.
  6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix 5 sec/speed 3. Transfer carrot mixture into a piping bag fitted with a round nozzle (1 cm) and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  7. Place a large saucepan with water (approx. 2 litres) over high heat and bring to the boil. Meanwhile, continue with next step while water comes to the boil.
  8. Place eschalots and garlic into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  9. Add oil and cook 3 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  10. Add  ½ teaspoon of the salt, 1 pinch of the pepper and two-thirds (approx. 400 g) of the reserved zucchini and cook 2 min/Varoma/speed 1, then mix 20 sec/speed 6.
  11. Add remaining zucchini and and mix 10 sec//speed 1.
  12. Once water is boiling, add remaining 1 teaspoon salt. Pipe gnocchi mixture (3 cm lengths) into saucepan, using scissors to snip gnocchi pieces. Gnocchi will rise to the surface when cooked (approx. 2 minutes). Using a slotted spoon, remove cooked gnocchi and place into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and cover to keep warm.
  13. Divide reserved sauce between 4 serving bowls, top with gnocchi and garnish with basil leaves, Parmesan shavings and remaining pepper before serving.
