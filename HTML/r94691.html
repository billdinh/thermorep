Chicken and Leek Pie

Ingredients

Filling

  • 450 g chicken stock
  • 80 g onions, quartered
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 3 black peppercorns
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 2 sprigs fresh thyme
  • 1 bay leaf, fresh or dried
  • 600 g chicken breasts, sliced (1 cm)
  • 200 g leeks, sliced (1 cm)
  • 200 g carrots, sliced (1 cm) then halved

White Sauce

  • 30 g butter
  • 30 g plain flour
  • 250 g milk
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 50 g white wine

Pastry Lid

  • 375 g puff pastry, ready-made
  • 1 egg yolk

Preparation

  1. Place stock, onions, salt, peppercorns, garlic, thyme and bay leaf in mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh in chicken. Insert Varoma tray and weigh in leeks and carrots then steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  2. Transfer chicken and vegetables to a baking dish (approx. 22 cm x 30 cm). Strain cooking liquid into a bowl, discarding onions and herbs, then set stock aside while making sauce.

White Sauce

  1. Place butter, flour, milk, salt, pepper and Dijon mustard in mixing bowl then cook 4 min/90°C/speed 4.
  2. Add 220 g reserved stock and wine then cook 2 min/90°C/speed 4. Pour sauce over chicken and vegetables in pie dish, mix well to evenly combine then set aside. Preheat oven to 200°C.

Pastry Lid

  1. Roll out puff pastry until large enough to cover top of pie (approx. 5 mm thick). Lay over filling and press down edges to form a tight seal. Brush top with beaten egg yolk then make 3 large slits on top of pie with a sharp knife to allow steam to escape. Bake for 30-40 minutes (200°C) until puffed and golden.
  2. Serve hot with new or mashed potatoes and green vegetables if desired.

Useful Items

baking dish (approx. 22 cm x 30 cm), fine sieve, pastry brush, rolling pin, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • For a Chicken, Ham and Leek Pie, in step 2 add 150 g thickly sliced cooked ham, cut in pieces (2 cm).

  • Flaky puff pastry crumbles under your fork to reveal a steaming, creamy chicken and vegetable filling in this warming and comforting pie. Perfect for family suppers and dinner parties alike as it can be assembled ahead then baked when needed.
