Cevapcici

Ingredients

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 200 g roasted red capsicums (see Tips)
  • 1 - 2 tsp chilli powder
  • 1 - 2 tsp sea salt flakes
  • ½ tsp freshly ground pepper
  • 300 g boneless lamb shoulder, partially frozen, then cut into cubes (2 cm - see Tips)
  • 300 g chuck beef, partially frozen, then cut into cubes (2 cm - see Tips)
  • 300 g pork, partially frozen, then cut into cubes (2 cm - see Tips)
  • olive oil, for brushing
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges, to serve
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
565 kJ / 134 kcal

Preparation

  1. Place garlic into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add capsicum, chilli powder, salt and pepper and mix 15 sec/speed 4. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place lamb into mixing bowl and chop 12 sec/speed 6. Transfer into a separate bowl and set aside.
  4. Carefully remove any stringy lamb from around blades and discard. Place beef into mixing bowl and chop 12 sec/speed 6. Transfer into bowl with lamb mince.
  5. Carefully remove any stringy beef from around blades and discard. Place pork into mixing bowl and chop 12 sec/speed 6. Transfer into bowl with lamb and beef mince. Carefully remove any stringy pork from around blades and discard.
  6. Place capsicum mixture and reserved meats into mixing bowl and mix 1 min/speed 4.
  7. Using damp hands, form mixture into 12 sausage shapes (approx. 3 x 10 cm).
  8. Place a grill pan over medium heat and brush with oil. Add cevapcici to pan and cook on both sides (approx. 3 minutes each side), or until cooked through. Transfer onto a platter and drizzle with lemon juice before serving warm.

Useful Items

pastry brush, bowl, large non-stick high-sided griddle or frying pan, serving platter, large plate, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • Roasted red capsicum can be purchased in jars at the supermarket or from delicatessens. To make your own at home, roast capsicums over a gas flame, turning with tongs until blackened all over. Place into a glass bowl and cover with plastic wrap for 10 minutes or until cooled. Peel and discard skin and deseed before using.
  • Cevapcici will have more flavour when using fatty cuts of meat; consider beef chuck or blade, lamb shoulder and pork shoulder or neck. Be sure to partially freeze the meat first to make cutting easier. When cutting into cubes, cut through the fat so that it minces better. You can also purchase minced meat from your butcher.
  • To partially freeze meat, place in a flat container and freeze for 30 minutes.
