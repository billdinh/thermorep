Tinga de Pollo (shredded chicken)

Ingredients

  • 20 - 30 g chipotle chillies, dried, stems removed, to taste
  • 2 brown onions (approx. 300 g), cut into halves
  • 300 g fresh tomatoes (approx. 4), blanched, skins removed and deseeded (see Tips)
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 2 tbsp Chicken stock paste (see Tips)
  • 1000 g skinless chicken breast fillets, cut into pieces (3-4 cm)
  • 30 g white sugar
  • 2 dried bay leaves

Preparation

  1. Place chillies into mixing bowl and chop 30 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a small bowl and set aside.
  2. Place onion, tomato, oil and stock paste into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then cook 5 min/Varoma/speed 2, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  4. Add chicken, sugar, bay leaves and reserved chillies and cook 18 min/100°C//speed 1, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  5. Shred 5 sec//speed 4, then serve as desired (see Tips).

Hints & Tricks

  • Please refer to The Basic Cookbook or Everyday Cookbook for the Chicken stock paste recipe.
  • Remove tomato skins by using a sharp knife to cut a small cross in base of each tomato and place into bowl. Carefully pour boiling water into bowl to cover tomatoes. Leave until skins split (approx. 1 minute). Remove from water and once cool enough to touch, peel away skins, then cut flesh into quarters.
  • Serve on tortillas, topped with lettuce and sour cream.
  • You can find dried chipotle chillies at specialty food stores, spice stores or online.
