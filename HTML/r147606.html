Currywurst Sauce

Ingredients

  • 75 g pickled gherkins, in pieces
  • 75 g onions, halved
  • 30 g butter, diced
  • 20 g sugar
  • 20 g curry powder
  • 500 g passata
  • 250 g tomato ketchup
  • 35 g tomato purée
  • 10 g cider vinegar
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 tsp sambal oelek
  • ½ vegetable stock cube (for 0.5 l)
Nutrition
per 900 g
Calories
3732 kJ / 892 kcal

Preparation

  1. Place gherkins in mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place onions in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add butter and sugar then sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  4. Add curry powder and sauté 2 min/120°C/speed 2.
  5. Add passata, ketchup, tomato purée, vinegar, salt, sambal oelek, stock cube and reserved chopped gherkins then cook 15 min/100°C//speed 3.5.
  6. Serve with sausages or transfer to warm sterilised jars while hot and seal. Leave to cool then store in fridge for up to 1 week.
