Spiced Beef Sausages

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp pink salt
  • 1 tsp whole black peppercorns
  • 1 tsp yellow mustard seeds
  • 1 heaping tsp coriander seeds
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp dried red pepper flakes
  • 4 whole cloves
  • 5 allspice berries
  • 2 oz ice water
  • 16 oz ground beef, 80/20
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
925 kJ / 221 kcal
Protein
35 g
Carbohydrates
2 g
Fat
8 g

Preparation

  1. Place salt, pepper, mustard seeds, coriander, garlic powder, red pepper flakes, cloves and allspice into mixing bowl and without measuring cup dry roast 6 min/212°F/speed 1.5. Insert measuring cup into mixing bowl lid and grind 30 sec/speed 9. Scrape down lid and sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add ice water and mix 10 sec/speed 3.
  3. Add ground beef and mix 15 sec//speed 3.
  4. Place a piece of parchment on a flat surface and use the parchment to help compress and roll mixture into four sausages (approx. 6.5 in.).
  5. Remove parchment and grill or smoke sausages by cooking to internal temperature of 165°F. Serve hot.

Useful Items

parchment paper, thermometer, bbq grill

Hints & Tricks

  • Instead of grilling sausages, steam in Varoma: place 25 oz water into mixing bowl. Insert Varoma tray into Varoma dish and place sausages onto Varoma tray. Secure Varoma lid, place Varoma into position and steam 17-19 min/Varoma/speed 2 or until sausages are cooked to internal temperature of 165°F.
  • Serve with barbecue sauce.
