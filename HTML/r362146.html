Hawaiian Summer Rolls

Ingredients

  • 36 oz water
  • 3 oz cellophane noodles

Dipping Sauce and Filling

  • 5 garlic cloves
  • 4 oz rice wine vinegar
  • 3 oz sambal oelek
  • 6 oz sugar
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp ground cayenne pepper
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch
  • 33 oz water
  • 16 oz fresh raw medium shrimp, peeled, deveined
  • salt, to taste
  • ground black pepper, to taste
  • 8 oz red cabbage, halved (approx. ¼ head)

Assembly

  • 12 rice paper wrappers
  • 36 mint leaves
  • 1 package matchstick carrots (approx. 4 oz)
  • 1 cucumber, julienned
  • 1 mango, sliced lengthwise in pieces (⅛ in.)
  • 1 avocado (med.), sliced lengthwise in pieces (⅛ in.)
  • 4 green onions white and green parts, cut in pieces (2 in.), sliced lengthwise

Preparation

  1. Place water into mixing bowl and heat 10 min/212°F/speed 1. Transfer to a bowl, add cellophane noodles and set aside to steep fo 15 minutes.

Dipping Sauce and Filling

  1. Place garlic into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  2. Add rice wine vinegar, sambal oelek, sugar, soy sauce, cayenne and cornstarch and mix 4 sec/speed 5, then cook 15 min/212°F/speed 2. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place water into mixing bowl. Set Varoma dish into position and weigh in shrimp. Season with salt and pepper, evenly distribute seasoned shrimp between Varoma dish and tray, secure Varoma lid and steam 12-14 min/Varoma/speed 2, until shrimp are cooked through. Remove Varoma, transfer shrimp to a plate and set aside to cool. Butterfly each shrimp by slicing the body almost in half lengthwise, stopping just before cutting all the way through.
  4. Place cabbage into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  5. Drain reserved cellophane noodles through simmering basket over a pie pan or shallow dish and transfer noodles to a seperate bowl. Reserve water for dipping rice paper wrappers.

Assembly

  1. Line a baking sheet with parchment and set aside.
  2. Immerse one wrapper in reserved water until softened (approx. 30 sec.). Use both hands to pull it up and out of the water, being careful so it doesn't collapse on itself, and immediately lay it flat on a clean work surface.
  3. Place 3 mint leaves in the center of wrapper and top with 3 pieces reserved butterflied shrimp. Add a small handful of reserved noodles, carrots, cucumber and reserved chopped cabbage, top with 2 slices mango, 2 slices avocado and green onions, leaving ½ inch on each side of the wrapper. Roll the wrapper by folding the left and right sides over the filling, then lift the edge closest to you and pull it up and snugly over the toppings and continue rolling tightly until closed and snug. Transfer to prepared baking sheet, seam-side down.
  4. Repeat with remaining wrappers and filling, adding more water to the dipping dish as needed. Cut each Hawaiian Summer Roll in half and serve with dipping sauce.

Useful Items

baking sheet, parchment paper, pie pan or shallow dish

Hints & Tricks

  • Be careful not to overfill your spring roll wrapper because this will cause the rice paper to tear.
