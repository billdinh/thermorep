Banoffee Brazilian Pavé

Ingredients

  • 60 g dark chocolate (50% cocoa solids), small pieces or callets
  • 60 g unsalted butter, diced
  • 250 g oat cakes
  • 400 g whipping cream
  • 1 Tbsp icing sugar
  • 450 g dulce de leche
  • 3 ripe bananas, cut in thin slices

Preparation

  1. Place dark chocolate in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place butter in mixing bowl and melt 3 min/60°C/speed .
  3. Add oat biscuits and chop 8 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.
  4. Insert butterfly whisk. Place 350 g cream and icing sugar in mixing bowl then whip speed 3 without setting a time, until stiff, watching carefully to avoid over whipping. Remove butterfly whisk. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  5. Place dulce de leche and 50 g cream in mixing bowl then mix 15 sec/speed 3.
  6. To make pavé, divide half of the biscuit crumbs between 10 glass cups (or jars) or place in a large serving bowl. Spoon half of the dulce de leche mixture over biscuit crumbs. Divide half of the banana slices between each glass cup and spoon over an even layer of half the whipped cream. Repeat the layers and refrigerate for 1 hour or until ready to serve. Serve Brazilian pavé garnished with reserved chopped chocolate.
