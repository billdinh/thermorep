Jambalaya

Ingredients

  • 1 celery stalk, cut into pieces
  • ½ red capsicum, deseeded and cut into halves
  • ½ green capsicum, deseeded and cut into halves
  • 1 carrot, cut into pieces
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 brown onion, (approx. 150 g), cut into halves
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 200 g chorizo, cut into slices (½ cm)
  • ¼ - ½ tsp cayenne pepper
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 200 g quinoa
  • 450 g water
  • 1 tbsp Vegetable stock paste (see Tips)
  • 400 g raw prawns, peeled and deveined
  • 1 sweet corn cob, kernels only (approx. 130 g)
  • fresh parsley, for garnishing

Preparation

  1. Place celery, red capsicum, green capsicum and carrot into mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 5. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Rinse and dry mixing bowl.
  2. Place garlic and onion into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add oil and chorizo and sauté 7 min/Varoma//speed .
  4. Insert butterfly whisk. Add reserved chopped vegetables, cayenne pepper, black pepper and oregano and cook 2 min/100°C//speed .
  5. Add quinoa, water and stock paste and cook 16 min/100°C//speed .
  6. Add prawns and corn and cook 5 min/100°C//speed . Mix bowl contents well with spatula and cook for a further 2 min/100°C//speed .
  7. Transfer into a serving dish and toss through parsley.  Allow to rest for 5 minutes before serving.

Hints & Tricks

  • Please refer to The Basic Cookbook or Everyday Cookbook for the Vegetable stock paste recipe.
  • For a mild chilli flavour, decrease the cayenne pepper to ¼ teaspoon. For a moderate chilli heat, use ½ teaspoon and for a spicy dish, use 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper.
  • If fresh corn cobs are not in season, use 130 g canned corn kernels, drained.
