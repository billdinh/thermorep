Spinach, Tomato and Goat's Cheese Quiche

Ingredients

Pastry

  • 200 g plain flour
  • 75 g unsalted butter cold, diced
  • 45 g water
  • 2 pinches fine sea salt

Filling

  • 15 g olive oil
  • 150 g onions, quartered
  • 80 g sun-dried tomatoes in oil, drained
  • 150 g fresh spinach leaves
  • 170 g milk
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 - 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 150 g goat's cheese, sliced (1 cm)

Preparation

Pastry

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C and line a spring form tin (Ø 24 cm) with baking paper.
  2. Place flour, oil, water and salt in mixing bowl then mix 15 sec/speed 5. Transfer to prepared tin, pushing in using fingertips, then prick with a fork and bake for 10 minutes (200°C). Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl and prepare filling.

Filling

  1. Place oil, onions and sun-dried tomatoes in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then sauté 5 min/120°C/speed 1.
  2. Add spinach and mix 1 min/100°C//speed 1, stopping to scrape down halfway through if necessary.
  3. Add milk, eggs, nutmeg, salt and pepper then mix 10 sec/speed 3. Pour over baked base then arrange slices of goat's cheese over the top. Bake for 25-30 minutes (200°C).
  4. Serve warm or cold.

Useful Items

spring form tin (Ø 24 cm), baking paper, oven
