Easy Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

Sponge

  • 120 g sunflower oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 120 g caster sugar
  • 120 g self-raising flour
  • 30 g cocoa powder
  • 100 g whole milk
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp real vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs

Icing

  • 125 g dark chocolate (70% cocoa solids), small pieces or callets
  • 125 g sour cream

Preparation

Sponge

  1. Preheat oven to 170°C then grease and line a square cake tin (20 cm) with baking paper.
  2. Place sugar, flour, cocoa powder, oil, milk, baking powder, vanilla extract and eggs in mixing bowl then mix 15 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then mix again 5 sec/speed 5.
  3. Transfer mixture to prepared cake tin. Bake for 25-30 minutes (170°C) until a skewer inserted in centre comes out clean. Allow to cool in tin for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely. Meanwhile, clean and dry mixing bowl.

Icing

  1. When cake is cool, place chocolate in mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then melt 3 min/50°C/speed 2.
  2. Add sour cream and mix 30 sec/speed 2. Finish mixing by hand with spatula.
  3. Immediately ice top of cooled cake. Cut in squares or transfer to an airtight container to store until needed.

Useful Items

baking paper, square cake tin (20 cm), palito ou espeto, wire rack, forno,

Hints & Tricks

  • Any icing can be used such as chocolate buttercream or whipped cream with fresh berries.
  • The chocolate cake can also be baked in a round cake tin (Ø 20 cm) if preferred.
