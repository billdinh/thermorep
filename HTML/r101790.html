Gluten Free Pizza

Ingredients

  • 40 g Cheddar cheese, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 80 g dried chickpeas
  • 20 g milk
  • 20 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • ½ Tbsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 40 g tomato sauce
  • 60 g smoked bacon, cut in thin strips
  • 50 g fresh button mushrooms, sliced
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1928 kJ / 463 kcal
Protein
27 g
Carbohydrates
21 g

30 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat grill to 200°C.
  2. Place Cheddar in mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place chickpeas in mixing bowl and grind 1 min/speed 10.
  4. Add milk, oil, eggs, oregano and salt then mix 15 sec/speed 3. Finish mixing by hand with spatula.
  5. Pour into an oven-proof, non-stick frying pan and cook on hob over a medium heat for 2-3 minutes. Spread over the surface tomato sauce, bacon, mushrooms and reserved grated Cheddar then place under preheated grill for 4-6 minutes (200°C) until cheese is fully melted. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

oven-proof frying pan, grill, hob

Hints & Tricks

  • Substitute topping ingredients with others of choice (e.g. tuna, olives, ham, etc.).
