Traditional hommus

Ingredients

  • 250 g dried chickpeas
  • 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 1000 g water, plus extra for soaking
  • 100 100 - 120 g lemon juice, to taste
  • 100 g tahini
  • 1 1 - 2 garlic cloves, to taste
  • 1 - 1 ½ tsp salt, to taste
  • 80 1 - 2 g ice cubes
  • ½ 1 - 2 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ tsp ground cumin
  • ½ tsp paprika

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh dried chickpeas into it. Add bicarbonate of soda and cover with water. Set aside to soak overnight. Drain and rinse chickpeas.
  2. Place drained chickpeas and water into mixing bowl and cook 40 min/100°C/speed 1. Drain chickpeas and discard cooking liquid.
  3. Place chickpeas into mixing bowl and blend 40 sec/speed 6, with aid of spatula.
  4. Add lemon juice, tahini, garlic and salt and set aside in mixing bowl for 20 minutes to cool.
  5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and blend 20 sec/speed 6, with aid of spatula.
  6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and blend 1 min/speed 6, with aid of spatula.
  7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Add ice cubes and blend 1 min 30 sec/speed 7, with aid of spatula. Transfer hommus into a serving bowl. Garnish with oil, cumin and paprika before serving.

Hints & Tricks

  • Adjust tahini, lemon juice, garlic and salt to taste as desired.
  • Transfer leftovers into a sealable container and store in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.
