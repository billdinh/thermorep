Pistachio Cupcakes

Ingredients

Cupcakes

  • 70 g pistachio nuts, unsalted, plus 1 Tbsp for garnish, roughly chopped
  • 170 g unsalted butter, soft
  • 170 g caster sugar
  • 140 g self-raising flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 3 large eggs
  • 5 - 10 drops pistachio nut extract, to taste

Mascarpone Icing

  • 200 g mascarpone
  • 300 g double cream
  • 120 g icing sugar
  • 8 - 12 drops pistachio nut extract, to taste

Preparation

Cupcakes

  1. Preheat oven to 170°C and line a 12 hole muffin tin with paper muffin cases.
  2. Place pistachio nuts in mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 10. Remove 1 Tbsp to a bowl and set aside for decoration.
  3. Add butter, sugar, flour, baking powder, eggs and pistachio extract then mix 30 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then mix again 30 sec/speed 4. Divide evenly between prepared cases. Bake for 22-25 minutes (170°C) until springy to touch and a skewer inserted in centre comes out clean. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl.
  4. Transfer cupcakes to a wire rack and allow to cool. Once cool, prepare icing.

Mascarpone Icing

  1. Place mascarpone, cream, icing sugar and pistachio extract in mixing bowl then mix 30 sec/speed 3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then mix again 20 sec/speed 3.
  2. Transfer to a piping bag fitted with a fluted nozzle and pipe in a swirling motion over cupcakes. Decorate with roughly chopped pistachios and reserved ground pistachios.
  3. Serve or store in an airtight container in fridge until needed, removing 30 minutes before serving to bring back to room temperature.

Useful Items

12 hole muffin tin, muffin paper cases, skewer, wire rack, piping bag and nozzle, airtight container, oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

These cupcakes work well both as an everyday bake, or to use as celebration cakes. The mascarpone and cream icing is a nice variation on the more common buttercream, and gives a really smooth finish.

  • Colour the icing by adding green food colouring paste if desired.
  • A traditional buttercream icing can also be used if preferred. Flavour with pistachio extract as above.
