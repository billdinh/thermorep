Tortillas

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp flaxseeds (linseeds)
  • 30 g raw almonds
  • 20 g raw cashew nuts
  • 1 tbsp coconut flour
  • 1 tbsp arrowroot
  • ¼ tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp onion salt
  • 8 egg whites (approx. 240 g)
  • 250 g almond milk
  • coconut oil, for greasing
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
273 kJ / 65 kcal
Protein
3.33 g

Preparation

  1. Place flaxseeds (linseeds), almonds and cashews into mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides and bottom of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add all remaining ingredients, except oil, into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then mix a further 10 sec/speed 4. Transfer into a glass or ceramic bowl and set aside to rest and thicken for 30 minutes.
  3. Lightly grease a non-stick frying pan (15 cm) with coconut oil and place over medium heat. Stir batter well, then pour a small ladle of batter into hot frying pan (approx. 2½ tbsp), swirling pan as you pour to cover the base. Fry for 1-2 minutes, or until edges of tortilla are light golden. Flip tortilla over and fry for a further 1-2 minutes. Transfer to a warm serving bowl or a plate and cover to keep warm. Repeat with remaining batter to make 16 tortillas. Serve warm.

Useful Items

non-stick frying pan (15 cm), serving bowl, small ladle, hob

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve tortillas as part of a Mexican dinner, use as wraps for sandwiches or even slice and bake to make tortilla chips.
  • Save your egg yolks to use in a creamy ice cream or in a Hollandaise sauce (refer to the recipes in The Basic Cookbook).
