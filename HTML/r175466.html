Chicken Satay Pizza

Ingredients

Pizza Dough

  • olive oil, to grease
  • 15 oz warm water (100°F-110°F)
  • 16 oz bread flour
  • 6 oz semolina
  • 2 ¼ tsp rapid rise yeast
  • 1 tbsp salt

Satay Sauce

  • 5 oz yellow onion, cut into pieces
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 fresh bird's eye chili, seeds removed
  • 1 tsp shrimp paste
  • 8 oz peanut butter
  • 8 oz coconut milk
  • 2 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 oz ketchup
  • ¼ tsp sesame oil (optional)

For Pizza

  • 12 oz cooked chicken meat, cut into 1 in. x 2 in. pieces
  • 2 tbsp olive oil, to grease
  • bread flour, to dust hands
  • 12 oz provolone cheese, cut into 1 in. pieces, divided
  • 6 oz red bell pepper, cut into ¼ in. strips, divided
  • 6 oz green bell pepper, cut into ¼ in. strips, divided

Preparation

Pizza Dough

  1. Lightly grease a large bowl and set aside.
  2. Place water, bread flour, semolina, yeast and salt into mixing bowl and knead 2 min/. Form dough into a ball and transfer into prepared bowl. Cover with cling wrap and let dough rise until doubled in size (approx. 1 hr.). Rinse and dry mixing bowl.

Satay Sauce

  1. Place onion, garlic and chili into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add shrimp paste, peanut butter, coconut milk, soy sauce, ketchup and sesame oil and cook 10 min/212°F/speed 3. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.

For Pizza

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in chicken and 2 tbsp. reserved satay sauce and mix together coating chicken. Set aside.
  2. Preheat oven to 500°F. Grease two pizza pans with oil (see Tip).
  3. Divide dough in half and place onto prepared pans. Stretch dough out to pan edges, with lightly floured fingers. Cover with clean kitchen towels and let rise 5 minutes.
  4. Place 6 oz provolone into mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Again, place 6 oz provolone into mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 8. Transfer to bowl with previously grated cheese and set aside.
  5. Spread 6 tbsp reserved satay sauce evenly over one pizza base and leave other covered. Arrange half the red and green peppers evenly over pizza and top with half the marinated chicken pieces. Sprinkle with half the grated cheese. Bake for 8-10 minutes (500°F). Repeat with remaining dough. Serve warm.

Useful Items

pizza pans (14 in.), cling wrap, kitchen towels, large bowl, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Satay sauce can be stored in an airtight container for up to one week in the refrigerator.
  • Freeze baked pizza and reheat for 7-9 minutes (500°F).
  • If using a sheet pan divide dough into thirds and make three (12 in.) pizzas. To make four individual (9 in.) pizzas, divide dough into quarters and shape onto a sheet pan with parchment sprinkled with semolina.
  • If using a pizza stone, follow manufacturer's instructions.
  • Replace yellow onion with red onion.
  • Garnish with chopped cilantro.
