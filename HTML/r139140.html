Rava payasam

Ingredients

  • 30 g flaked almonds
  • 30 g sultanas
  • 2 tbsp ghee (see Tips)
  • 70 g semolina
  • 500 g full cream milk
  • 340 g sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 tbsp pouring (whipping) cream
  • 2 egg yolks
  • ¼ tsp natural vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp rosewater
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg, for garnishing

Preparation

  1. Place a small bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh almonds and sultanas into it. Set bowl aside.
  2. Line a plate with paper towel and set aside. Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and 1 tablespoon of the ghee. Allow ghee to melt then add reserved almonds and sultanas and fry until lightly toasted. Transfer onto prepared plate to drain.
  3. Place remaining 1 tablespoon ghee into mixing bowl and melt 1 min/100°C/speed 2.
  4. Add semolina and mix 40 sec/100°C/speed 2.
  5. Add milk and cook 7 min/90°C/speed 4 or until semolina thickens.
  6. Add condensed milk, cream and egg yolks and cook 4 min/90°C/speed 4.
  7. Add vanilla and rosewater and mix 10 sec/speed 4. Divide between serving dishes or glasses and set aside to cool (approx. 30 minutes). Top with nutmeg and reserved toasted almond mixture before serving.

Useful Items

small bowl , non-stick frying pan (15 cm), 1 plate, paper towel, 4 small serving glasses, stove top
