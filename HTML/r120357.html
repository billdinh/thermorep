Zucchini slice

Ingredients

  • 100 g unsalted butter, cut into pieces and softened, plus extra for greasing
  • 80 g sultanas
  • hot water, for soaking
  • 120 g walnuts, plus extra to serve (optional)
  • 80 g raw sugar
  • 1 lemon, zest only, no white pith
  • 250 g zucchini, cut into pieces (approx. 2 cm), plus extra slices to serve (optional)
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 200 g plain flour
  • 1 ½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 100 g pitted dates, cut into pieces, plus extra to serve (optional)

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Grease and line a slice tin (15 x 25 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh sultanas into it. Cover sultanas with hot water and set aside to soak.
  3. Place walnuts into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 4. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place sugar and lemon zest into mixing bowl and mill 30 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add zucchini and chop 20 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  6. Add eggs, butter and cinnamon and mix 15 sec/speed 6.
  7. Add flour, baking powder and salt and mix 4 sec/speed 3.
  8. Using simmering basket, drain sultanas. Add dates, 60 g of the reserved chopped walnuts and drained sultanas to mixing bowl and mix 20 sec/speed 2. Transfer batter into prepared tin, sprinkle with remaining chopped walnuts and bake for 25 minutes (180°C) or until a wooden skewer inserted into the centre of the slice comes out clean. Cool in tin for 5 minutes, then transfer onto a wire rack to cool completely. If desired, garnish with extra zucchini slices (optional), walnuts (optional) and dates (optional) before serving.

Useful Items

slice tin (15 x 25 cm), baking paper, ceramic or glass bowl, bowl, wooden skewer, wire rack, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • A 25 x 10 cm loaf tin can be used instead of a slice tin. Bake for 50 min (180°C) or until a wooden skewer inserted into the centre of the loaf comes out clean.
