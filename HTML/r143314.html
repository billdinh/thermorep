Herb Butter Flatbreads

Ingredients

Herb Butter

  • 60 g butter, cut in pieces
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 sprig fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 2 stalks fresh dill, leaves only
  • 1 sprig fresh rosemary, leaves only
  • 2 sprigs fresh chives, cut in pieces
  • ½ tsp salt (optional)

Flatbread

  • 350 g plain yoghurt
  • 350 g bread flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp sea salt

Preparation

Herb Butter

  1. Place butter in mixing bowl and melt 4 min/75°C/speed 1.
  2. Add garlic cloves, parsley, dill, rosemary, chives and salt in mixing bowl then chop 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.

Flatbread

  1. Place plain yoghurt, flour, baking powder and sea salt in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 6. Transfer dough to a floured surface and cut the dough into 8 equal-sized pieces. Pat and flatten dough pieces with floured hands, dust rolling pin with flour and roll each piece into a flat circle (approx. Ø 14 cm, 3 mm thick). Use a knife to cut 4 lines in the centre of each flat circle, leaving 3 cm at each end.
  2. Preheat hob with a griddle pan on high heat and as soon the pan is hot cook each flat circle 1-2 minutes on each side. When the flatbread is baked, brush it with reserved melted herb butter on each side. Repeat the baking process until all flatbreads are done and serve immediately. 

Useful Items

rolling pin, griddle pan, pastry brush, stove

Hints & Tricks

  • If no grill pan is available, use a heavy based frying pan instead, heated to a high temperature. Alternatively, preheat a heavy baking tray in the oven and bake the breads for 10-12 minutes (200°C).
