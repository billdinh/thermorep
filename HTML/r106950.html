Sweet Potato and Zucchini Frittata

Ingredients

  • ½ oz fresh parsley, leaves only (approx. 25 stems)
  • 3 ½ oz red onions, quartered (approx. ½ med. onion)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil, divided
  • 7 oz sweet potatoes, cut into 1 in. pieces (approx. 1 sm. potato)
  • 3 ½ oz zucchini, cut into 1 in. pieces (approx. 1 zucchini)
  • 3 ½ oz red bell peppers, cut into 1 in. pieces (approx. 1 med. pepper)
  • 6 large eggs
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • ¼ tsp dried chili flakes (optional)
  • 8 oz buffalo mozzarella cut into rounds (¼ in.)

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Place parsley into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place onions and 1 tbsp oil into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and sauté 3 min/250°F/speed 1.
  4. Add sweet potatoes, zucchini and red peppers and chop 10 sec/speed 4.5, then cook 5 min/212°F/speed 1. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  5. Place eggs, salt, pepper and chili flakes into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 3.
  6. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a cast iron frying pan over a medium-low heat. Pour eggs into pan and cook without stirring until eggs are almost set (approx. 5 min.).
  7. Arrange vegetable mixture on top of eggs and bake for 15-20 minutes (350°F) until eggs are well set.
  8. Turn on broiler to high. Arrange mozzarella and reserved chopped parsley over the top of frittata and place under broiler for 2-3 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbling. Serve warm or cold.
