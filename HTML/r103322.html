Mini Chocolate and Apricot Panettone

Ingredients

  • 140 g dried apricots, pitted
  • 100 g dark chocolate, small pieces or callets
  • 200 g milk, plus extra for brushing
  • 2 medium eggs
  • 1 egg yolk, from medium egg
  • 1 tsp natural almond extract
  • 500 g plain flour
  • 120 g caster sugar
  • 2 tsp dried instant yeast
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
  • 200 g butter, soft, cut in pieces
  • 2 Tbsp flaked almonds

Preparation

  1. Place apricots in mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place chocolate in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Transfer to bowl with apricots and set aside.
  3. Place milk in mixing bowl and heat 3 min/37°C/speed 1.
  4. Add eggs, egg yolk and almond extract then heat 30 sec/37°C/speed 1.
  5. Add flour, sugar, yeast and salt then knead 2 min/.  Leave in mixing bowl to rise until doubled in size (approx. 45 minutes). Towards end of rising time, line a 12 hole muffin tin with muffin paper cases.
  6. Push dough down if necessary. Add butter and reserved chopped apricots and chocolate then knead 3 min/ stirring with aid of spatula through hole in mixing bowl lid. Divide dough between muffin cases. Cover with tea towels and leave to rise for 45 minutes. Towards end of rising time, preheat oven to 190°C.
  7. Gently brush tops with a little milk then sprinkle with flaked almonds. Bake for 20-30 minutes (190°C) until skewer inserted in centre comes out clean. Allow to cool then serve.

Useful Items

12 hole muffin tin, muffin paper cases, 2 tea towels, skewer, pastry brush, oven
