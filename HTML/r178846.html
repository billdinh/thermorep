Creamy mushroom soup

Ingredients

  • 400 g button mushrooms, cut in halves or quarters as needed
  • 500 - 600 g water
  • 200 g milk
  • 1 vegetable stock cube (for 0.5 l)
  • 50 g plain flour
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 50 g cream
  • 4 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 40 g cream cheese (optional)
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
550 kJ / 132 kcal
Protein
7 g
Carbohydrates
5 g
Fat
9 g

Preparation

  1. Place button mushrooms in mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 4.
  2. Add water, milk, vegetable stock cube, flour and salt and cook 10 min/100°C/speed 3.
  3. Add cream, parsley and cream cheese (if using) and cook 2 min/90°C/speed 2.
  4. Blend 1 min/speed 4-7, increasing speed gradually. Serve hot.
