Zucchini, lentil and coconut stew

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 ½ cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • ½ red onion, cut into quarters
  • ¼ tsp ground turmeric
  • ½ tsp ground coriander
  • 200 g coconut milk
  • 1 tsp Vegetable stock paste (see Tips)
  • 200 g water
  • 150 g red lentils
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 - 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 1 zucchini, cut into slices (1 cm)
  • ½ red capsicum, cut into pieces (2 cm)
  • 60 g fresh baby spinach leaves
  • 1 lime, cut into eighths, to serve

Preparation

  1. Place oil, garlic, ginger and onion into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5.
  2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then cook 3 min/Varoma/speed 2, or until onion has softened.
  3. Add turmeric and coriander and cook 2 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  4. Add coconut milk, stock paste, water, lentils, salt and pepper and cook 14 min/90°C//speed .
  5. Add zucchini and capsicum and cook 5-10 min/90°C//speed 1, or until lentils and vegetables are tender.
  6. Add spinach and combine 50 sec//speed 1, or until spinach wilts. Divide between 4 serving bowls and serve with lime wedges.
