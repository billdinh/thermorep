Marinated white fish fillets with chermoula sauce and couscous salad

Ingredients

Marinated fish fillets

  • 400 - 450 g boneless, skinless, white fish fillets (approx. 4 fillets)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 1 fresh red chilli, trimmed, cut into halves and deseeded
  • 1 tsp fennel seeds
  • 1 pinch ground cinnamon
  • 2 pinches saffron
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 60 g olive oil

Chermoula sauce

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 bunch coriander, leaves and stalks only
  • ½ tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 pinch dried chilli flakes
  • 30 g lemon juice
  • 30 g olive oil

Couscous salad

  • 200 g couscous
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 700 g water
  • 1 pinch ground cumin
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 5 sprigs coriander, leaves only
  • 1 fresh red chilli, trimmed and cut into halves (optional)
  • 1 tsp grated lemon zest
  • 1 carrot, cut into pieces
  • 50 g pitted black olives
  • 400 g canned chickpeas, rinsed and drained (approx. 250 g after draining)

Preparation

Marinated fish fillets

  1. Place a glass baking dish (23 cm) onto mixing bowl lid and weigh fish fillets into it. Set aside.
  2. Place garlic, ginger, chilli, fennel seeds, cinnamon, saffron and salt into mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add oil and mix 5 sec/speed 5. Pour marinade over reserved fish. Cover with plastic wrap and place into refrigerator for 30 minutes to marinate.

Chermoula sauce

  1. Place garlic and coriander into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add ground cumin, paprika, chilli flakes, lemon juice and oil and mix 10 sec/speed 2. Transfer into a small bowl, cover with plastic wrap and place into refrigerator until ready to serve.

Couscous salad

  1. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid, weigh couscous into it and set aside.
  2. Place oil, 200 g of the water, cumin and salt into mixing bowl and bring to the boil 5 min/100°C/speed 1. Pour mixture over couscous, cover immediately with plastic wrap and set aside for 5 minutes. Fluff with a fork to separate grains. Rinse and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Place coriander, chilli, lemon zest, carrot and olives into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer into bowl with couscous and add chickpeas. Toss to combine, then set aside. Do not clean mixing bowl.
  4. Remove marinated fish from refrigerator and place into Varoma tray. Pour over any remaining marinade.
  5. Place 500 g of water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 15 min/Varoma/speed 1 or until fish is cooked through. To serve, place couscous salad onto a large platter or serving plate, top with fish and spoon over reserved Chermoula sauce. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

small bowl, bowl, serving platter, plastic wrap, ceramic or glass baking dish (23 cm), refrigerator
