Frozen strawberry daiquiri

Ingredients

  • 300 g strawberries, frozen
  • 100 g lime juice
  • 160 g white rum
  • 90 g sugar syrup (see tip)
  • 70 g strawberry syrup
  • 250 g ice cubes
Nutrition
per 1 glass
Calories
935 kJ / 224 kcal
Protein
1 g
Carbohydrates
28 g
Fat
0 g

Preparation

  1. Place strawberries, lime juice, rum, sugar syrup, strawberry syrup and ice cubes in mixing bowl and blend 25 sec/speed 8.
  2. Pour strawberry daiquiri into cocktail glasses and serve immediately.

Useful Items

cocktail glasses

Hints & Tricks

  • To make your own sugar syrup, place 300 g water and 300 g sugar in mixing bowl and cook 18 min/Varoma/speed 1 without measuring cup until sugar has dissolved completely. Allow syrup to cool and transfer to a clean bottle. Sealed well, this colourless syrup will keep a long time in the fridge.
  • Serve with a straw if desired.
