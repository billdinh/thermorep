Caviar Cream Cheese with Cress and Radish Salad

Ingredients

Caviar Cream Cheese

  • 2 gelatine leaves
  • water, for soaking
  • 50 g white wine
  • 300 g cream cheese
  • 15 g caviar
  • ½ bunch fresh chives, finely sliced

Salad and Assembly

  • 8 slices white bread
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 10 g cider vinegar
  • 10 g orange juice
  • ½ tsp Dijon mustard
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • ½ tsp sugar
  • 150 g red radishes, sliced (5 mm)
  • 20 g garden cress
  • 10 g caviar

Preparation

Caviar Cream Cheese

  1. Soak gelatine leaves in a bowl of water for 5 minutes then squeeze out water.
  2. Place wine and squeezed gelatine in mixing bowl then warm 1 min 30 sec/100°C/speed 1. Leave in mixing bowl to cool for 15 minutes.
  3. Add cream cheese and mix 20 sec/speed 2.
  4. Add caviar and chives then mix 10 sec//speed . Transfer to a piping bag and place in fridge until needed. Clean mixing bowl.

Salad and Assembly

  1. Using the measuring cup, cut out 8 discs from bread, toast in a non-stick frying pan on the hob until golden brown then set aside.
  2. Place oil, vinegar, orange juice, mustard, salt and sugar in mixing bowl then mix 3 sec/speed 6.
  3. Add radishes and cress, transfer to a bowl and stir with spatula.
  4. Pipe chilled caviar cream cheese on top of each piece of reserved toasted bread then garnish with caviar. Serve with cress and radish salad.

Useful Items

non-stick frying pan, piping bag and nozzle, hob, refrigerator
