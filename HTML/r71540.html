Citrus margarita

Ingredients

  • salt, for rimming glasses
  • 130 g tequila
  • 50 g Cointreau®
  • 60 g lemon flesh (approx. 1 lemon), peeled, no white pith, cut into quarters and pips removed
  • 30 g lime flesh (approx. 1 lime), peeled, no white pith, cut into quarters and pips removed
  • 90 g orange flesh (approx. ½ orange), peeled, no white pith, cut into quarters and pips removed
  • 1 - 2 tbsp sugar syrup (see Tips) (optional)
  • 500 g ice cubes
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
203 kJ / 48 kcal
Protein
0.5 g
Carbohydrates

Preparation

  1. Place a few centimetres of water into a small bowl or dish. Working one glass at a time, place 6 margarita glasses upside down into the water to wet the rim. Spread salt onto a plate and dip glass rims into salt, then set glasses aside.
  2. Place all remaining ingredients, except ice cubes, into mixing bowl and mix 15 sec/speed 6.
  3. Add ice cubes and crush 1 min/speed 10. Pour margarita mixture into prepared glasses and serve.

Useful Items

6 margarita glasses

Hints & Tricks

  • If you don't have margarita glasses, any glasses will do.
  • Sugar syrup: place 250 g water and 230 g caster sugar into mixing bowl and cook 5 min/100°C/speed 1. Transfer into a jar and place into refrigerator to cool completely (approx. 15 min) before using. Recipe makes approx. 480 g. Leftover syrup can be stored in the refrigerator in a sealed glass jar and used in other cocktails.
