Wholemeal spelt doughnuts with cacao glaze

Ingredients

Cacao glaze

  • 100 g coconut oil
  • 50 g pure maple syrup
  • 60 g raw cacao powder
  • 1 pinch salt

Doughnuts

  • 125 g coconut oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 60 g boiling water
  • 15 g flaxmeal (see Tips)
  • 150 g Rapadura sugar
  • 225 g wholemeal spelt flour
  • 1 ½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 ½ tsp vanilla bean paste (see Tips)
  • 110 g apple sauce
  • 40 g apple cider vinegar

Preparation

Cacao glaze

  1. Place coconut oil into mixing bowl and melt 2 min/37°C/speed 2 or until melted.
  2. Add syrup, cacao powder and salt and mix 10 sec/speed 4. Transfer into a sealable container and set aside until ready to use (see Tips). Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Doughnuts

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Lightly grease a 12-hole doughnut tray with oil and set aside.
  2. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and carefully weigh boiling water and flaxmeal into it, then set aside for 5 minutes to thicken.
  3. Place all remaining ingredients, including flaxmeal mixture, into mixing bowl and mix 30 sec/speed 4, until well combined.
  4. Transfer mixture into prepared doughnut tray, then bake for 18-22 minutes (180°C), until a skewer inserted into each doughnut comes out clean. Allow to cool in tray for 5 minutes, then transfer onto a wire rack to cool completely.
  5. Once cool, spread or drizzle glaze over doughnuts and decorate as desired before serving (see Tips).

Useful Items

12-hole doughnut tray, bowl, sealable container, wire rack, wooden skewer, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • To mill your own flaxmeal, place 200 g flaxseeds (linseeds) into mixing bowl and mill 30 sec/speed 10. Use as needed. Transfer any leftovers into a sealable container and store in the fridge or freezer for up to 3 months.
  • To make your own vanilla paste, refer to the recipe on Cookidoo® (www.cookidoo.com.au).
  • We used the 12-hole doughnut tray from TheMix Shop (www.thermomix.com.au/shop or www.thermomix.co.nz/shop).
  • If needed, reheat the cacao glaze by standing the container in a bowl of hot water until it melts and is pourable.
  • We decorated our doughnuts with shredded coconut, goji berries and pistachios.
