Root vegetables with hazelnut pangrattato

Ingredients

Lemon and hazelnut pangrattato

  • 4 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 3 sprigs fresh lemon thyme, leaves only
  • ½ lemon, zest only, no white pith
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 200 g sourdough bread (day old), torn into pieces (3-4 cm)
  • 50 g toasted hazelnuts, skins removed
  • 25 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 20 g lemon juice (approx. ½ lemon)

Root vegetables

  • 700 g water
  • 300 g raw beetroot (approx. 3), scrubbed clean and cut into eighths lengthways
  • 400 g Dutch carrots, scrubbed clean and cut into halves lengthways
  • 250 g purple carrots, scrubbed clean and cut into quarters lengthways
  • 200 g parsnips, scrubbed clean and cut into quarters lengthways
  • 200 g turnip, scrubbed clean and cut into eighths lengthways
  • 40 g avocado oil
  • ¼ tsp sea salt
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper

Preparation

Lemon and hazelnut pangrattato

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Line a baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place parsley and lemon thyme into mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 6. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place lemon zest and garlic into mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add bread and reserved chopped herbs and chop 10-15 sec/speed 7, or until a roughly chopped consistency is achieved. Transfer onto prepared baking tray and set aside.
  5. Place hazelnuts into mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 6. Transfer onto prepared baking tray with breadcrumb-herb mixture, then drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice. Bake for 13-15 minutes (180°C) until crunchy.
  6. Allow to cool then transfer into a sealable container for up to 3 days.

Root vegetables

  1. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish into position, then weigh beetroot into it. Secure Varoma lid and steam 5 min/Varoma/speed 1. Set Varoma aside.
  2. Place a large serving bowl onto mixing bowl lid, then weigh Dutch carrots, purple carrots, parsnip, turnip or swede and avocado oil into it. Combine well with spatula.
  3. Place Varoma dish back into position and transfer half of the vegetables on top of beetroot. Insert Varoma tray and transfer remaining vegetables onto it. Secure Varoma lid and steam 15-20 min/Varoma/speed 1, or until vegetables are cooked.
  4. Transfer vegetables onto a serving platter, season with salt and pepper, then garnish with reserved pangrattato. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

baking paper, baking tray (30 x 40 cm), sealable container, large bowl, serving platter, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Use pangrattato sprinkled over turkey and other roasted meats.
  • Pangrattato can be premade and frozen for up to 3 months. Simply defrost in a hot oven for a few minutes before using.
  • For flavour purposes, sourdough is best used in this recipe; however any type of bread can be used.
  • Substitute the avocado oil and salt in this recipe with any of the flavoured oils or salts from the homemade gift section.
