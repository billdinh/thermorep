Middle eastern stuffed chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp Arabian spice mix
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 cardamom seeds
  • 1 ½ tsp fennel seeds
  • 1 ½ tsp coriander seeds
  • 25 g olive oil
  • 1 whole chicken (max. 1.3 kg), washed and patted dry, wings clipped
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 2 pinches freshly ground black pepper
  • 100 g Egyptian rice
  • 1900 g water
  • 1 tsp chicken stock paste, homemade
  • 170 g bread, toasted, in pieces
  • 10 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 10 sprigs fresh coriander, roughly chopped
  • 100 g onions, cut into halves
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 5 apricots, fresh or dried, pitted and cut into halves
  • 55 g pitted dates
  • 60 g raisins
  • 50 g pine nuts, toasted
  • 1 tsp sumac

Preparation

  1. Place Arabian spice mix, cumin seeds, cardamom seeds, fennel seeds and coriander seeds into mixing bowl and dry roast 3 min/120°C/speed 4.
  2. Allow spices to cool down for a few minutes and then grind 30 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add olive oil and mix 5 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and use spice mix to rub generously all over skin and inside of chicken. Season with 1 tsp salt and 1 pinch pepper. Allow to marinate for at least half an hour out of refrigerator. Meanwhile, prepare the stuffing.
  4. Place a heat-resistant bowl onto mixing bowl lid, weigh rice into it and set aside.
  5. Place 200 g water and vegetable stock paste into mixing bowl, heat 4 min/100°C/speed 1, transfer to prepared bowl with rice, mix with spatula and allow to rest for 20 minutes. Meanwhile go on with the recipe and clean and dry mixing bowl.
  6. Place toasted bread, parsley and coriander into mixing bowl and grind 5 sec/speed 8. Transfer herbs-bread mixture to the bowl with rice and mix with spatula.
  7. Place onions, garlic, dried apricots and dates into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7.
  8. Add raisins, pine nuts, sumac, 1 tsp salt and 1 pinch pepper and mix 5 sec//speed 2.5. Transfer to bowl with rice and mix with spatula until all ingredients are combined. Stuff chicken with reserved rice mixture, use toothpicks to secure skin around stuffing.
  9. Place 1700 g water into mixing bowl. Set Varoma dish into position and, to ensure steam circulates evenly, lay butterfly whisk flat in the bottom of Varoma dish, place chicken on top, close Varoma and steam 60 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  10. Remove Varoma, turn chicken upside down, place Varoma again in position and steam for another 30 min/Varoma/speed 1, prolong time if needed. Remove Varoma, transfer to a serving platter and serve.

Useful Items

serving platter, toothpicks, heat-resistant bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • Place the chicken for a few minutes under a hot grill if you prefer crispy skin.
