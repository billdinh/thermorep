Salted caramels

Ingredients

  • 120 g unsalted butter, plus extra for greasing
  • 150 g dark brown sugar
  • 120 g glucose syrup
  • ¼ tsp cream of tartar
  • 220 g sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract
  • sea salt flakes, for sprinkling (optional)
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
324.3 kJ / 77.2 kcal
Protein
0.6 g
Carbohydrates
11.4 g

3.7 g

Preparation

  1. Line a square cake tin (20 cm) with baking paper, then grease baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place butter, sugar, glucose syrup and cream of tartar into mixing bowl and cook 10 min/120°C/speed 3, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  3. Add condensed milk and vanilla and cook 15 min/120°C/speed 3, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  4. Transfer mixture into prepared tin and sprinkle with sea salt flakes (optional). Set aside to cool for 30 minutes, then transfer into refrigerator to set (approx. 3 hours). Cut into pieces (approx. 1.5 cm x 5 cm). Wrap individual caramels in greaseproof paper wrappers, twisting edges to seal. Store in the refrigerator until ready to serve or wrap up to give as a gift (see Tip).

Useful Items

square cake tin (20 cm), baking paper, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Salted caramels can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 1 month.
