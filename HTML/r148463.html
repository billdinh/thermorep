Aussie Potato Salad

Ingredients

Salad

  • 500 g water, plus extra for cooling
  • 4 eggs
  • 1000 g potatoes (e.g. Desirée), peeled and cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 150 g bacon, cut in pieces (1-2 cm)
  • 3 spring onions, trimmed and finely sliced (reserve green parts for garnishing)
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil

Dressing

  • 60 g Parmesan cheese, crust removed and cut in pieces (2 cm), plus extra shavings for garnishing
  • 200 g mayonnaise (see tips)
  • 15 g wholegrain mustard
  • 10 g balsamic vinegar

Preparation

Salad

  1. Place water in mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket and place eggs into it. Place Varoma dish into position, then weigh in potato. Arrange potato pieces in Varoma dish so that some holes are exposed for steam to circulate freely. Cover Varoma with lid and steam 14 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  2. Set Varoma with potato aside. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and carefully transfer eggs to a bowl of cold water.
  3. Place Varoma dish with potato back into position. Stir potato pieces with spatula to ensure even cooking, then cover Varoma with lid and cook 8-10 min/Varoma/speed 1 or until potatoes are soft (see tips).
  4. Remove Varoma dish and set potato aside to cool, placing Varoma lid underneath to catch drips. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  5. Place bacon, spring onions and olive oil in mixing bowl then sauté 5 min/Varoma//speed . Transfer to a serving bowl and set aside.
  6. Once eggs have cooled, peel, quarter and slice them, then add to bacon mixture in serving bowl.

Dressing

  1. Place Parmesan cheese in mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place mayonnaise, mustard and balsamic vinegar in mixing bowl then blend 3 sec/speed 5.
  3. Add cooled potatoes and reserved Parmesan cheese to serving bowl. Pour dressing over salad and combine well using spatula.
  4. Garnish salad with reserved green parts of spring onion, finely sliced, and extra Parmesan cheese shavings. Refrigerate salad until chilled (approx. 30  minutes), then serve immediately.

Useful Items

serving bowl, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Potatoes should be cooked through but still firm enough that a knife can pass easily through without mashing.
  • Make mayonnaise fresh in your Thermomix to use in this recipe (see The Basic Cookbook).
