Asparagus and Ham Rotelle

Ingredients

  • 8 oz yellow onions, sliced in half moons (¼ in.)
  • 2 garlic cloves, smashed
  • 1 oz extra virgin olive oil
  • ¼ tsp salt, to taste
  • 32 oz water
  • 10 oz dried rotelle pasta (wagon wheels)
  • 12 oz asparagus, in pieces (2 in.)
  • 4 oz smoked ham steak diced (¼ in.)
  • 1 tbsp capers, drained
  • goat cheese, crumbled, to serve
  • chopped fresh parsley, to serve

Preparation

  1. Place onion, garlic, olive oil and salt into mixing bowl and without measuring cup cook 5 min/250°F//speed 1.
  2. Add water and without measuring cup bring to a boil 10 min/212°F//speed 1.
  3. Add pasta through hole in mixing bowl lid, insert measuring cup and cook for time indicated on pasta package/212°F//speed 1, while adding in the last 5 minutes of cooking: asparagus and ham through hole in mixing bowl lid.
  4. Insert simmering basket, hold it in place with spatula and strain pasta mixture, reserving 8 oz (1 cup) of cooking liquid.
  5. Transfer strained pasta mixture and reserved cooking liquid to a serving bowl and toss to combine. Garnish with capers, fresh crumbled goat cheese and chopped fresh parsley and serve.
