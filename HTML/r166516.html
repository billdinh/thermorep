Asian Cabbage Salad

Ingredients

  • 4 oz slivered almonds
  • 1 oz unsalted sunflower seeds
  • 1 oz sesame seeds
  • 5 oz dried ramen noodles, broken into 4 pieces
  • 10 ½ oz cabbage, cut into 3 in. chunks
  • 4 oz carrots, cut into 1 in. pieces
  • 1 oz green onions, cut on the bias (approx. ⅛ in.)
  • 3 oz rice wine vinegar
  • 2 ½ oz olive oil
  • ½ oz toasted sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp fresh ground black pepper

Preparation

  1. Place almonds, sunflower seeds and sesame seeds into mixing bowl and without measuring cup toast 10 min/Varoma//speed 1 (see Tip). Allow to cool for 20 minutes before adding remaining ingredients.
  2. Add all remaining ingredients and with aid of spatula through hole in mixing bowl lid chop 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a large serving bowl and serve immediately.

Useful Items

large serving bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • Alternatively, you can toast slivered almonds, sunflower and sesame seeds in the oven. Place nuts and seeds on a baking sheet and toast 5 minutes (350°F) watching carefully so they do not burn. Allow to cool 20 minutes before placing in mixing bowl and proceeding with recipe.
  • Toasted sesame oil can be replaced with sesame oil.
