Flatbreads

Ingredients

  • 350 g gluten free flour mix (see Tips)
  • 1 tbsp gluten free baking powder (see Tips)
  • 1 ½ tsp salt
  • 110 g water
  • 50 g full cream milk
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • gluten free cornflour, for dusting
Nutrition
per 1 portions
Calories
896 kJ / 213 kcal

1.7 g
Carbohydrates

Preparation

  1. Place all ingredients, except cornflour, into mixing bowl and mix 30 sec/speed 5, until combined.
  2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and knead 30 sec/. Transfer dough onto a floured silicone bread mat or lightly floured work surface. Divide dough into 8 equal portions (approx. 65 g each), shape into balls and cover with a kitchen towel.
  3. Using a rolling pin dusted with cornflour, roll each ball into a circle (approx. 12-13 cm diameter). Dust dough with more cornflour to prevent sticking, if needed. Cover rolled dough with a kitchen towel to prevent it from drying out.
  4. Place a non-stick frying pan over high heat; ensure pan is very hot before cooking flatbreads. Place 1 flatbread into pan and cook until bubbles begin to form and edges begin to lift (approx. 40 seconds). Turn flatbread over and cook for a further 40 seconds, pressing down gently using an egg flip. Transfer onto a plate and cover to keep warm. Repeat with remaining flatbread. Serve immediately (see Tips).

Useful Items

kitchen towel, silicone bread mat, rolling pin, large non-stick frying pan, egg flip, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • Please refer to the Gluten free flour mix and the Gluten free baking powder recipes on this recipe chip.
  • If not serving flatbread immediately, allow to cool then transfer into a sealable container and place into refrigerator for up to 3 days. To re-heat, sprinkle with water, wrap in aluminium foil and place into a moderate oven for 8-10 minutes (180°C), or until soft and warmed through.
  • Try serving flatbreads with Coconut fish curry and dahl as part of a complete meal. Please refer to the Coconut fish curry on this recipe chip. For the Tomato and lentil dahl recipe, please refer to The Basic Cookbook or Everyday Cookbook.
