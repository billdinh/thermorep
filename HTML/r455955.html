Asian Sloppy Joes

Ingredients

Bao Buns

  • 9 oz all-purpose flour, plus extra to dust
  • ½ oz sugar
  • ½ tsp rapid rise yeast
  • 1 oz whole milk
  • 1 ½ tsp vegetable oil, plus extra to brush
  • ½ tsp rice vinegar
  • 4 oz water
  • ½ tsp baking powder

Sloppy Joes

  • 41 oz water, divided
  • 24 oz ground beef, crumbled
  • 4 oz yellow onion, cut into pieces
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • 4 oz ketchup
  • 3 oz hoisin sauce
  • 1 oz soy sauce
  • 1 oz rice wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • coleslaw salad mix
  • green onions, sliced

Preparation

Bao Buns

  1. Place flour, sugar, yeast, milk, oil, rice vinegar, 4 oz water and baking powder into mixing bowl and mix 5 sec/speed 5. Knead 5 min/. Let rest in mixing bowl for 15 minutes or until dough has risen to 2 inches from top of mixing bowl.
  2. Transfer dough to a lightly dusted surface and divide into 6 equal pieces. Form each piece into a ball and then roll out into an oval (3 in. x 5 in.). Brush dough with oil and fold over. Transfer to a baking sheet leaving space between each bun. Cover with cling wrap, set aside and allow to proof for 30 minutes while preparing Sloppy Joes. Clean mixing bowl.

Sloppy Joes

  1. Place 20 oz water into mixing bowl. Place a large piece of parchment (approx. 25 in.) into Varoma dish, set Varoma into position and weigh ground beef onto parchment. Wrap parchment to seal, arrange in Varoma dish ensuring some holes remain unobstructed to allow even distribution of steam. Secure Varoma lid and steam 15 min/Varoma/speed 2. Remove Varoma, strain meat and discard juices. Set strained meat aside. Discard steaming water.
  2. Place onion, garlic and sesame oil into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and without measuring cup sauté 5 min/250°F/speed 1.
  3. Add ketchup, hoisin sauce, soy sauce, vinegar, 1 oz water and ground ginger and blend 20 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and cook 5 min/195°F/speed 2.
  4. Add reserved ground beef and cook 5 min/195°F//speed 1. Transfer to a bowl, set aside and keep warm while steaming Bao Buns. Clean mixing bowl.
  5. Place 20 oz water into mixing bowl. Divide buns between Varoma dish and tray. Set Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 15 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  6. Open steamed buns and fill with meat. Garnish meat with coleslaw mix and sliced green onions. Serve hot.
