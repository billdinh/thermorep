Pear and ginger purée

Ingredients

  • 500 g pears (approx. 4 pears), peeled, cored and cut into pieces (2-3 cm)
  • 20 g water, plus extra if needed
  • &frac18; tsp ground cinnamon
  • ½ - 1 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled and cut into thin slices
  • natural yoghurt, to serve (optional - see Tips)

Preparation

  1. Place pears and water into mixing bowl and cook 20 min/100°C/speed 1 or until very tender.
  2. Add cinnamon and ginger and blend 15 sec/speed 8, to desired consistency.
  3. Set aside 1 serving, then freeze the remainder in individual portions for future use. If needed, stir through a little more water or your baby's milk to get the consistency just right before serving. Serve with natural yoghurt (optional).

Useful Items

ice cube trays, freezer

Hints & Tricks

  • Adding spice, such as cinnamon and fresh ginger, to your baby's purées is a source of goodness and it also helps your baby become accustomed to new flavours. Exposure to a variety of tastes is particularly crucial in the first year of life when babies are typically more accepting of unfamiliar tastes.
  • You can replace the natural yoghurt with kefir milk in this recipe.
  • Fruit purées like this one also make a lovely addition to your baby's porridge or rice cereal if you're serving one. Just be sure to give your baby plenty of iron-rich, savoury purées too. Fruit is nutritious and it's important that your baby develops a liking for various fruit flavours, but offering less sweet options is crucial too.
