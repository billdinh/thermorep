Colourful quinoa salad

Ingredients

Quinoa

  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 50 g ghee
  • 1000 g water
  • 2 tsp sea salt
  • 150 g white quinoa

Salad

  • 1 spring onion/shallot, white part only
  • 3 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves and stalks, plus extra leaves for garnishing
  • 30 g lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • ¼ tsp sea salt, to taste
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper, to taste
  • 300 g broccoli, cut into large florets
  • 40 g dried cranberries
  • 50 g flaked almonds, toasted

Preparation

Quinoa

  1. Place garlic into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add ghee or butter and sauté 2 min 30 sec/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Add water and salt to mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket, then weigh quinoa into it. Cook 20 min/100°C/speed 4.
  4. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula then transfer quinoa into a large serving bowl and set aside to cool. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Salad

  1. Place spring onion/shallot and coriander into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add lemon juice, oil, salt and pepper and mix 4 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add broccoli and dried cranberries and chop Turbo/0.5 sec/2-3 times, or until desired consistency is achieved. Transfer into serving bowl with quinoa and set aside.
  4. Mix through flaked almonds, garnish with coriander leaves and serve.
