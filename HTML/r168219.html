Sweet cheese rolls (halawet el jebn)

Ingredients

Syrup

  • 300 g sugar
  • 150 g water
  • 1 tsp lemon juice, freshly squeezed
  • 2 tsp blossom water

Kashta cream

  • 1000 g milk
  • 150 g white bread, crust removed, cut into pieces
  • 100 g cornflour (starch)
  • 1 tbsp blossom water
  • 2 tbsp rose water

Dough

  • 200 g medium wheat semolina
  • 50 g fine semolina
  • 500 g akkawi cheese, desalted, cut into pieces (see tip)
  • 200 g mozzarella, rinsed and dried, cut into pieces

Assembly

  • sunflower oil, for greasing
  • unsalted pistachio nuts, chopped, to garnish

Preparation

Syrup

  1. Place sugar, water, lemon juice and blossom water into mixing bowl and heat 10 min/115°C/speed 2. Transfer into a heat-resistant bowl and set aside to cool down. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Kashta cream

  1. Place milk into mixing bowl and heat 5 min/60°C/speed 1.
  2. Add bread, cornflour and heat 4 min/90°C/speed 2.
  3. Add blossom water and rose water and cook 1 min/90°C/speed 2.5. Transfer into a shallow bowl and set aside to cool for approx. 40 minutes. Then refrigerate for 1 hour.

Dough

  1. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid, weigh medium wheat semolina and fine semolina in it and set aside
  2. Place desalted Akkawi cheese into mixing bowl and grate 20 sec/speed 8. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place mozzarella into mixing bowl and grate 15 sec/speed 6.
  4. Add grated Akkawi and 260 g syrup and heat 8 min/80°C/speed 2.
  5. Heat without measuring cup 8 min/80°C/speed 2 while adding reserved semolina-mixture slowly through the opening in mixing bowl lid. Transfer to a bowl and allow to cool.

Assembly

  1. Lightly grease a sheet of baking paper with oil. Transfer dough on a work surface, divide into 4 pieces, cover with cling film and set aside. Place one piece of dough onto prepared baking paper, flatten with hands, place a second sheet of baking paper on top and, with a rolling pin, roll out into a rectangular shape of 2-3 mm thickness. Cut into strips (3 cm x 12 cm). Place 1 tsp of kashta cream on the bottom short edge and roll to form a short cylinder. Press down gently with your fingers to slightly flatten. Repeat with remaining dough and cream. Place a bit of cream in the centre of each roll and garnish with chopped pistachio nuts. Serve with syrup.

Useful Items

shallow bowl or shallow dish, heat-resistant bowl, baking paper, cling film, rolling pin, bowl, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • To desalt Akkawi cheese, soak it for 12 hours or overnight in abundant water, changing the water every half hour in the first hours. Drain and pat dry before use.
