New York Cheesecake

Ingredients

Crust

  • 3 oz unsalted butter, cubed, plus extra to grease
  • 8 oz graham crackers, broken into pieces
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • &frac18; tsp salt

Filling

  • 40 oz cream cheese, room temperature, cubed
  • 9 oz sugar
  • 3 oz sour cream
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • &frac18; tsp salt
  • 6 large eggs
  • 2 egg yolks, from large eggs

Strawberry Topping

  • 16 oz strawberries, stems removed, quartered
  • ½ oz lemon juice
  • 1 oz sugar

Preparation

Crust

  1. Preheat a conventional oven to 325°F (see Tip). Lightly grease a springform pan (approx. 10 in. x 2½ in.) with butter, transfer pan to a baking sheet and set aside.
  2. Place butter into mixing bowl and melt 3 min/212°F/speed . Add graham crackers, sugar and salt and mix 15 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix a further 5 sec/speed 8.
  3. Transfer crumb mixture into base of prepared springform pan. Press to form a firm compact layer. Transfer baking sheet with pan into oven and bake crust for 15 minutes (325°F). Set aside to cool. Meanwhile, wash and dry mixing bowl and increase oven temperature to 450°F.

Filling

  1. Place cream cheese into mixing bowl and mix 30 sec/speed 7 with aid of spatula. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix a further 30 sec/speed 7 with aid of spatula.
  2. Add sugar, sour cream, flour, lemon juice, vanilla and salt and mix 30 sec/speed 7 with aid of spatula.
  3. Without measuring cup, mix 30 sec/speed 5 while adding eggs and egg yolks one at a time through hole in mixing bowl lid. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix a further 30 sec/speed 5.
  4. Pour cheesecake filling over prebaked crust. Let rest five minutes. Bake for 10 minutes (450°F), then reduce heat to 200°F and bake a further 1 hour and 45 minutes (200°F). Clean mixing bowl.
  5. Turn off oven, crack open oven door and allow to cool slowly for one hour.
  6. Run a thin knife around the edge of the pan to loosen cheesecake and allow to cool at room temperature (approx. 2 hr.). Refrigerate until fully set (approx. 4 hr. or overnight).

Strawberry Topping

  1. Place strawberries, lemon juice and sugar into mixing bowl and cook 4 min/140°F//speed 1. Transfer to a bowl and refrigerate until chilled.
  2. Just before serving, remove outer ring of springform pan. Pour Strawberry Topping over cheesecake, slice and serve.

Useful Items

sharp knife, springform pan (approx. 10 in. x 2½ in.), baking sheet, oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Store leftover cheesecake in the refrigerator.
  • New York Cheesecakes bake optimally in a conventional oven. If using a convection oven, turn the fan off if your oven allows it, otherwise turn fan to lowest setting. All ovens heat differently however, so if using a convection oven it is recommended to reduce the temperature by 25°F-30°F and bake per conventional recipe time.
