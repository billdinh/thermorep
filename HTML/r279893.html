Coquito (Puerto Rican Eggnog)

Ingredients

  • 12 oz evaporated milk (approx. 1 can)
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 8 whole cloves
  • 14 oz sweetened condensed milk (approx. 1 can)
  • 13 ½ oz coconut milk (approx. 1 can)
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 4 oz white rum
  • ice cubes, to serve
  • 10 cinnamon sticks, to garnish
  • toasted coconut, to garnish, to taste
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1172 kJ / 280 kcal
Protein
8 g
Carbohydrates
27 g
Fat
14 g

Preparation

  1. Place evaporated milk, cinnamon and cloves into mixing bowl and cook 5 min/212°F/speed 1.
  2. Add condensed milk, coconut milk, egg yolks and vanilla and cook 5 min/170°F/speed 3.
  3. Add rum and mix 15 sec/speed 3. Transfer to a serving pitcher and chill (approx. 2 hrs.). Serve chilled or over ice, garnished with cinnamon sticks and toasted coconut.
