Tuscan Bean Soup

Ingredients

  • 70 g Parmesan cheese, crust removed and cut into cubes (3 cm), plus extra shaved for garnishing
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary (approx. 10 cm long), leaves only
  • 2 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves only
  • ½ brown onion (approx. 75 g), quartered
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 120 g pancetta, diced
  • 400 g water
  • 400 g tinned chopped tomatoes
  • 100 g courgettes, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 400 g canned chickpeas, rinsed and drained (weight is pre-draining)
  • 400 g canned kidney beans, rinsed and drained (weight is pre-draining)
  • 400 g canned cannellini beans, rinsed and drained (weight is pre-draining)
  • 1 tbsp vegetable stock paste, homemade (see Tips)
  • 1 tsp dried chilli flakes
  • 6 slices crusty bread, to serve

Preparation

  1. Place Parmesan in mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place rosemary and thyme in mixing bowl then chop 20 sec/speed 8. Repeat chopping 20 sec/speed 8, or until a fine consistency is achieved. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Place onion and garlic in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add oil and pancetta then sauté 8 min/Varoma/speed 1, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  5. Add water, tomatoes, courgettes, celery, carrot, chickpeas, kidney beans, cannellini beans, stock paste, chilli flakes and reserved chopped herbs then cook 20 min/100°C//speed 1.
  6. Transfer into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and stir through reserved Parmesan. Garnish with extra shaved Parmesan to taste and serve with crusty bread.

Useful Items

Thermo Server or oven proof dish, serving bowls

Hints & Tricks

  • Please refer to The Basic Cookbook for a vegetable stock paste recipe.
  • Vegetarian Tuscan Bean Soup: Omit the pancetta and reduce sautéing time to 3 minutes in step 4. Proceed as per recipe.
