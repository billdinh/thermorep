Egg Free Pasta

Ingredients

  • 250 g semolina, durum wheat
  • 150 g pasta flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 150 g water, cold, plus extra for cooking pasta
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt, plus 1 tsp for cooking pasta
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil

per 1 portion

Preparation

  1. Place semolina in mixing bowl and grind 30 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add flour, water, salt and olive oil then knead 2 min/. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and knead again 30 sec/. Tip out and bring crumbly dough together into a ball. Wrap in cling film and leave to rest for 1-2 hours at room temperature.
  3. Divide rested dough into quarters and roll out with a pasta machine or rolling pin on a floured surface, to thin rectangles (approx. 1 mm thick). Sprinkle with a little flour if necessary to prevent pasta from sticking. Cut dough depending on use: Lasagne or ravioli: pasta sheets, cut (approx . 15 cm x 10 cm). Tagliatelle: cut into strips (1-2 cm x max . 30 cm). Farfalle: cut into small rectangles (2-3 cm x 5 cm) and push together in middle to create a bow shape.
  4. Bring a large pan of salted water to the boil on hob and cook fresh pasta for 4-5 minutes until cooked al dente (lasagne sheets do not require cooking on hob prior to being assembled as they will cook in oven). Serve pasta hot with your choice of sauce.

Useful Items

cling film, pastry cutter, large saucepan, rolling pin or pasta machine, hob

Hints & Tricks

  • Use a fluted pastry cutter when cutting out the pasta to create an attractive edge.
  • When rolling out dough, keep the unused dough wrapped in cling film to prevent it drying out.
