Chickpea and Ham Balls

Ingredients

  • 100 g floury potatoes, peeled, cut in pieces (4 cm)
  • 50 g Parmesan cheese, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 200 g cooked chickpeas, rinsed and drained
  • 2 - 3 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 1 large egg
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground pepper
  • 70 g 00 flour (pasta flour)
  • 100 g dry-cured ham, diced
  • extra virgin olive oil, for frying
  • 120 g carrots, cut in sticks
  • 120 g red peppers, cut in sticks
  • 120 g cucumber, cut in sticks

Preparation

  1. Place potatoes in a saucepan and cover with cold water. Bring to the boil then reduce heat and simmer until cooked. Meanwhile, continue with recipe.
  2. Place Parmesan cheese in mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place chickpeas and parsley in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides and base of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Once potatoes are cooked, drain then run under cold water to cool.
  5. Add cooked potatoes, reserved grated cheese, egg, salt, pepper, 50 g flour and ham then mix 8 sec/speed 5. Shape mixture into 20 balls (approx. Ø 3 cm) then coat in remaining 20 g flour.
  6. Heat oil in a frying pan over a medium heat and fry balls for 10 minutes until golden brown, turning occassionally, then drain on paper towel.
  7. Serve hot or cold with chopped vegetables.

Useful Items

saucepan, frying pan, paper towel, hob
