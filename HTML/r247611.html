Supplì (Roman Rice Croquettes)

Ingredients

Tomato Sauce

  • 80 g onions, quartered
  • 30 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 400 g tinned chopped tomatoes
  • ½ Tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 3 fresh basil leaves, roughly chopped
  • 1 tsp caster sugar
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper

Supplì

  • 100 g bread, torn in pieces
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 600 g risotto rice, any flavour, leftover or freshly made and cooled
  • 100 g fresh mozzarella, cubed (1-2 cm)
  • 2 medium eggs, beaten
  • vegetable oil, for frying

Preparation

Tomato Sauce

  1. Place onions, olive oil and garlic in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then sauté 2 min/100°C/speed 2.
  2. Add tomatoes, balsamic vinegar, basil, sugar, salt and pepper then, with simmering basket in place of measuring cup, cook 15 min/100°C/speed 2.
  3. Blend 5 sec/speed 7 then transfer to a bowl and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.

Supplì

  1. Place bread, salt and pepper in mixing bowl then grate 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Take a small handful of cold leftover risotto (approx. 90 g) and shape into an elongated ball. Press 1-2 cubes of mozzarella in centre and mould rice around the cheese until completely enclosed.
  3. Dip supplì in beaten eggs then roll in reserved breadcrumbs until completely covered. Transfer to a baking tray and repeat until all rice and filling have been used.
  4. Heat vegetable oil in a deep saucepan or deep fat fryer over a medium-low heat.
  5. Fry supplì, a few at a time, turning in the oil, until golden and crisp (approx. 5 minutes). Remove with a slotted spoon and leave to drain on paper towels. Serve warm with tomato sauce.

Useful Items

deep saucepan or deep fat fryer, baking tray, slotted spoon, paper towel, hob

Hints & Tricks

  • Any flavoured leftover risotto or simply plain cooked rice will work well with this recipe.
  • This recipe calls for 600 g leftover risotto or cooked rice, but the breadcrumbs and mozzarella can be easily adjusted if you have more or less rice.
  • If you don't have any leftover rice, try making the 'Risotto with Parmesan Cheese' found in the Basic Cookbook. For best results, prepare the risotto then chill in the fridge for 8 hours or overnight before making supplì.
