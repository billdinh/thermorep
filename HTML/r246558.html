Mexican cottage pie

Ingredients

Mexican mince

  • 1 brown onion, cut into halves
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 carrot, cut into pieces
  • 1 celery stalk, cut into pieces
  • 1 fresh chilli, deseeded if preferred
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 500 g beef mince
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 30 - 40 g taco seasoning
  • 150 g water
  • ½ red capsicum, cut into pieces (1-2 cm)
  • 400 g canned kidney beans, rinsed and drained (approx. 250 g after draining)
  • 1 tbsp pickled jalapeño chillies, drained and roughly chopped
  • 125 g canned sweet corn kernels, rinsed and drained (optional)

Mashed potatoes

  • 40 g tasty cheddar cheese
  • 1000 g potatoes, peeled and cut into pieces (2-3 cm)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 110 g milk
  • 60 g water
  • 20 g salted butter
  • pickled jalapeño chillies, roughly chopped (optional)

Preparation

Mexican mince

  1. Place onion, garlic, carrot, celery, chilli and oil into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5.
  2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then cook 4 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Add mince, tomato paste and taco seasoning and cook 2 min/100°C//speed 1.
  4. Add water, capsicum, beans, jalapeños and corn (optional) and combine 3 min/100°C//speed 2. Transfer into a casserole dish (approx. 23 cm) and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Mashed potatoes

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Place cheese into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place potatoes, salt, pepper, milk and water into mixing bowl and cook 25-30 min/90°C/speed 1.
  3. Insert butterfly whisk. Add butter and mash 30 sec/speed 3. Spoon mashed potato over mince mixture and spread out to cover mince completely. Sprinkle with reserved cheese and pickled jalapeños (optional) and bake for 20-25 minutes (180°C) or until golden. Allow to cool slightly before serving.
