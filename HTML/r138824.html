Wagon wheel slice

Ingredients

  • 70 g sugar
  • 60 g unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 egg
  • 200 g plain flour
  • ¼ tsp baking powder
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 3 - 4 tbsp strawberry jam
  • 170 - 200 g marshmallows, cut into halves lengthways
  • 250 g dark chocolate, broken into pieces
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 170°C. Line bottom and sides of slice tin (20 x 30 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place sugar and butter into mixing bowl and beat 20 sec/speed 5 . Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add egg, flour, baking powder and salt and mix 20 sec/speed 4 .
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix 20 sec/speed 4 or until mixture comes together. Transfer mixture into prepared slice tin and press down with the back of a spoon. Bake for 10 minutes (170°C) or until golden. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  5. While slice is still warm, spread with jam. Cover with marshmallows, cut side down. Return to oven and bake for 8-10 minutes (170°C) or until marshmallow softens. Using the back of a spoon, gently press marshmallows down to create an even surface. Set aside to cool.
  6. Place chocolate and oil into mixing bowl and melt 3 min/50°C/speed 1 .
  7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and melt 1 min/50°C/speed 1. Pour chocolate over marshmallows and spread evenly. Place into refrigerator for 2 hours or until completely set. Cut into 36 squares to serve.

Useful Items

slice tin (20 x 30 cm), baking paper, spoon, oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • We served this dessert as a slice to make it easier and quick to make and serve. However, you can cut biscuit into circles to serve in wheel shapes.
  • This slice can be stored in a sealable container in the refrigerator for up to 1 week.
