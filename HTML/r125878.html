Almond quinoa pastry

Ingredients

  • 100 g butter, chilled and cut into pieces (3 cm), plus extra for greasing
  • 60 g raw almonds (see Tips)
  • 40 g quinoa flakes
  • 80 g plain flour
  • 60 g wholemeal flour
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tsp cold water

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C. Grease a pie dish (22 cm) or loose-bottom tart tin (22 cm) and set aside.
  2. Place almonds and quinoa into mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 7.
  3. Add flours and butter and mix 10 sec/speed 5.
  4. Add egg and water and knead 15 sec/, until mixture just comes together.
  5. Transfer pastry onto a silicone bread mat or large lightly floured sheet of baking paper. Using a rolling pin, roll out pastry (2-3 mm thickness). Drape pastry over rolling pin, gently removing baking paper and ease pastry into dish. Gently press pastry into sides and press to repair any splits in pastry. Trim overhanging edges with a knife. Place dish into refrigerator for 10 minutes to firm.
  6. Line pastry with baking paper and fill with pie weights, rice or dried beans. Place dish onto a baking tray and bake for 10-15 minutes (160°C). Remove baking paper and weights. Bake for a further 10-15 minutes (160°C) or until lightly golden. Set aside to cool completely, and fill as liked.

Useful Items

pie weights, rice or dried beans, pie dish (22 cm), butter knife, silicone bread mat, rolling pin, baking paper, pie weights or dried beans, oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • If you prefer, you can use activated or roasted almonds instead of raw almonds.
  • Pie dish can be substituted with 6-8 smaller individual tart tins.
