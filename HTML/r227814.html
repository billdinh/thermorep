Chicken and Quinoa with Peach Salsa

Ingredients

  • 2 jalapeño peppers, stem and seeds removed
  • ½ tsp salt, to taste
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 can diced tomatoes, with juice (28 oz)
  • 10 oz frozen peaches
  • 35 oz water
  • 7 oz black quinoa
  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (approx. 5-7 oz ea.)

Preparation

  1. Place jalapeños, salt and pepper into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add tomatoes with juice and frozen peaches. Place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 15 min/212°F//speed 1. Transfer to a bowl and keep warm.
  3. Place water into mixing bowl and insert simmering basket. Add quinoa and rinse 10 sec/speed 5.
  4. Line Varoma tray with parchment and place Varoma into position. Arrange chicken on Varoma tray and cover with half the reserved peach salsa. Secure Varoma lid and steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 2, or until chicken is cooked through.
  5. Serve chicken over quinoa and top with remaining peach salsa.

Useful Items

parchment paper

Hints & Tricks

  • For larger chicken breasts, divide the chicken between the Varoma dish and Varoma tray, lined with parchment.
  • Replace black quinoa with red, white or any type of quinoa.
