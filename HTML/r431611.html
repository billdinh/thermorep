Warm Halloumi, Walnut and Pomegranate Salad

Ingredients

  • 3 Tbsp pomegranate molasses
  • 1 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 Tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 Tbsp runny honey
  • 1 Tbsp water
  • 1 pinch dried chilli flakes
  • 250 g kale, tough stems removed, thinly sliced
  • 800 g water, boiling
  • 1 vegetable stock cube (for 0.5 l), crumbled
  • 150 g giant couscous
  • 30 g walnut halves
  • 225 g halloumi cheese, reduced fat, diced (2 cm)
  • fine sea salt, to taste
  • ground black pepper, to taste
  • 100 g pomegranate seeds

Preparation

  1. Place pomegranate molasses, oil, mustard, honey, water and chilli flakes in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 3. Transfer half to a small bowl and set aside.
  2. Add 125 g kale, mix well with spatula then massage 5 min//speed 1.5. Using tongs, transfer kale to a large serving bowl.
  3. Place remaining 125 g kale and reserved dressing in mixing bowl, mix well with spatula then massage 5 min//speed 1.5. Add to serving bowl.
  4. Place water and vegetable stock in mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket, weigh in couscous then cook 8 min/98°C/speed 4. Meanwhile, heat a frying pan over a medium heat and toast walnuts until lightly golden. Roughly chop then transfer to bowl with kale. In the same pan, fry halloumi for 3-4 minutes until golden and crispy on the edges.
  5. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and season couscous to taste. Transfer couscous to bowl with kale and stir gently with spatula to combine.
  6. Top with reserved pan-fried halloumi and sprinkle over pomegranate seeds. Serve immediately.

Useful Items
