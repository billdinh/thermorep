Fry bread

Ingredients

  • 650 g self-raising flour
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 400 g water
  • oil, for deep frying

Preparation

  1. Place flour, salt and water into mixing bowl and knead 1 min/. Transfer onto a floured silicone bread mat or work surface and roll out to a 1.5 cm thickness. Cut dough into squares (approx. 6 x 6 cm, makes 18 in total).
  2. Line a large plate with paper towel and set aside. Place a large saucepan over medium heat and add oil. Bring to 175°C (to check your oil is at 175°C, use a kitchen thermometer or drop a small cube of bread into oil; bread should turn golden brown in 15-20 seconds (keep your oil at this temperature while frying). Working in batches, fry 4 portions of dough at a time for 4-6 minutes, turning halfway, until crispy and golden. Transfer onto prepared plate to drain. Serve while warm (see Tips).

Useful Items

silicone bread mat, large plate, paper towel, large saucepan, kitchen thermometer, rolling pin, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve bread with soups, stews or with jam.
  • This bread is best served on the day it's made.
