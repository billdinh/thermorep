Irish 'Potato' Candy

Ingredients

  • 4 oz cream cheese, room temperature
  • 2 oz unsalted butter, cut into pieces, room temperature
  • 16 oz confectioners sugar
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon, plus 3 tbsp, to dust, divided
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 7 oz unsweetened, desiccated coconut
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
590 kJ / 141 kcal
Protein
1 g
Carbohydrates
19 g
Fat
8 g

Preparation

  1. Place cream cheese and butter into mixing bowl and mix 1 min/speed 3.
  2. Add confectioners sugar, 1 tsp cinnamon and vanilla and mix 1 min/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Add coconut and mix a further 15 sec/speed 4. Transfer mixture to a bowl and refrigerate for 20 minutes.
  3. Shape mixture into 28 slightly oblong, potato shapes (approx. 1 oz ea.). Place 3 tbsp cinnamon in a small bowl. Roll each "potato" in cinnamon. Refrigerate until ready to serve.

Useful Items

refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to five days.
  • Roll potatoes in cinnamon again for darker color.
