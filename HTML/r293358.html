Caprese hasselback chicken

Ingredients

  • 1400 g boiling water
  • 1 tsp salt, plus extra to season
  • 250 g dried risoni pasta
  • 4 chicken breasts (approx. 200 g each), cut deep slits (1 cm apart) into each breast (do not cut all the way through)
  • 18 - 20 semi sun-dried tomato strips in oil (oil reserved for drizzling)
  • 100 g fresh mozzarella, cut into thin slices (5 mm), then cut into halves
  • ground black pepper, to season
  • 2 tbsp basil pesto (see Tip), plus extra to serve (optional)
  • 2 bunches asparagus, trimmed and cut into pieces (4 cm)
  • 200 g cherry tomatoes

Preparation

  1. Place 900 g of the boiling water, salt and risoni into mixing bowl and cook 8-10 min/100°C//speed 1, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid, until risoni is cooked. Meanwhile, working 1 breast at a time, place a sun-dried tomato strip into the first cut. Place a slice of mozzarella into the next cut. Continue filling cuts, alternating with sun-dried tomato and mozzarella.
  2. Place chicken breasts into Varoma dish and onto Varoma tray. Drizzle with reserved sun-dried tomato oil, season with salt and pepper and set Varoma aside.
  3. Drain risoni using simmering basket and transfer into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl. Add pesto to risoni, stir to combine and cover to keep warm.
  4. Place remaining 500 g boiling water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma with chicken into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 12 min/Varoma/speed 4.
  5. Divide asparagus and cherry tomatoes between Varoma dish and tray around chicken. Season with salt and pepper, secure Varoma lid and steam 6 min/Varoma/speed 4 or until vegetables and chicken are cooked to your liking.
  6. Serve chicken and vegetables with reserved risoni and extra basil pesto (optional), if desired.

Useful Items

thermal serving bowl or other large bowl, serving platter
