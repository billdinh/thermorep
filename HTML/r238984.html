Asian-style pork noodles

Ingredients

Pork

  • 50 g vermicelli noodles
  • water, for soaking
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 2 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 1 fresh chilli, deseeded if preferred
  • 6 sprigs fresh coriander stalks only, leaves reserved for garnishing
  • 2 spring onions/shallots, white part only, cut into halves (reserve green part for garnishing)
  • 500 g pork mince
  • 40 g plain flour
  • 225 g canned water chestnuts, drained
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground white pepper
  • 2 tbsp oil

Noodles

  • 1500 g hot water
  • 200 g dried Chinese egg noodles
  • 4 spring onions/shallots, white part only, cut into halves, green part reserved for garnishing
  • 6 garlic cloves
  • 150 g carrots, cut into pieces
  • 1 - 2 fresh chillies, deseeded if preferred
  • 60 g unsalted butter
  • 200 g cabbage, shredded
  • 1 tbsp kecap manis
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • ¼ tsp ground white pepper
  • 40 g water
  • 15 - 20 snow peas, cut into thin slices
  • 1 - 2 tbsp fried shallots, for garnishing

Preparation

Pork

  1. Soak vermicelli noodles as per packet instructions. Drain, cut into short lengths and set aside.
  2. Place garlic, ginger, chilli, coriander stalks and white part of spring onions/shallots into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7.
  3. Add mince, flour, chestnuts, soy sauce, salt and pepper and combine 30 sec//speed 4. Transfer into a bowl, add reserved vermicelli noodles and stir until combined. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Place a large non-stick frying pan over medium-high heat and add oil. Add mince mixture and fry for 20-30 minutes, stirring regularly until crispy and golden brown. Meanwhile, continue with recipe.

Noodles

  1. Place water into mixing bowl and heat 5 min/100°C//speed 1. Add egg noodles and cook 3-4 min/100°C//speed 1. Drain, rinse under warm water and transfer into a large bowl. Set aside.
  2. Place white part of spring onions/shallots, garlic, carrot, chillies and butter into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and cook 2 min/100°C/speed .
  4. Add cabbage, kecap manis, oyster sauce, soy sauce, pepper, water and snow peas and cook 3 min/100°C//speed 1. Toss cabbage mixture through noodles. Top with green part of spring onions/shallots, coriander leaves, fried shallots and reserved mince mixture just before serving.

Useful Items

bowl, frying pan, large bowl, non-stick frying pan, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • Substitute noodles for vegetable zoodles or noodles of choice.
  • You can serve this dish warm or cold (making it great for lunches the next day).
