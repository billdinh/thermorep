Apple Crumble Cake

Ingredients

Crumble Topping

  • 70 g butter, chilled, diced, plus extra for greasing
  • 75 g blanched almonds
  • 75 g plain flour
  • 50 g Demerara sugar

Apple Layer

  • 400 g eating apples, peeled, cored and sliced (0.5 cm slices)
  • 20 g lemon juice
  • 50 g Demerara sugar

Cake

  • 100 g granulated sugar
  • 100 g butter, diced
  • 2 large eggs (60 g size)
  • 100 g self-raising flour

Decoration

  • 40 g flaked almonds to decorate

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Line base of springform tin (Ø 20 cm) with baking paper, grease sides of tin with butter and set tin aside.

Crumble Topping

  1. Place blanched almonds in mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 10 then add plain flour and butter and pulse Turbo/1 sec/3 times. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add sugar and pulse Turbo/1 sec/3 times.  Transfer crumble topping to a small bowl and set aside.

Apple Layer

  1. Place a bowl on top of mixing bowl lid, weigh into it sliced apples, lemon juice and sugar, stir together until apples are coated, then set aside.

Cake

  1. Place sugar, butter and eggs into mixing bowl and mix 30 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Add self-raising flour and mix 20 sec/speed 4. Transfer to prepared tin.
  2. Cover sponge layer evenly with apple layer and juices, then with crumble topping, then sprinkle with flaked almonds for decoration. Bake 45-55 minutes (180°C) or until golden brown. Serve warm or cold - it's lovely with custard or pouring cream.

Useful Items

baking paper, springform tin (Ø 20 cm), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • To make this cake gluten free, substitute GF flour for the plain flour, and instead of the self-raising flour, use GF plain flour and add ½ tsp GF baking powder.
  • To make your own self-raising flour, add ½ tsp baking powder to each 100 g plain flour.
