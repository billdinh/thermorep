Yogurt Cake

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ oz sunflower oil, plus extra to grease
  • 5 ½ oz sugar,
  • 3 large eggs
  • 7 oz all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp homemade vanilla sugar (optional)
  • 4 ½ oz plain yogurt
  • 1 pinch salt
Nutrition
per 1 slice
Calories
1048 kJ / 250 kcal
Protein
4 g
Carbohydrates
34 g

11 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease a bundt pan (8.5 in.- 9.5 in.) and set aside.
  2. Place sugar into mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Transfer 1 tbsp confectioners sugar to a small bowl and set aside to dust.
  3. Add eggs, flour, baking powder, vanilla sugar, yogurt, sunflower oil and salt and mix 30 sec/speed 5. Transfer mixture into prepared bundt pan and bake for 30 minutes (350°F). Allow to cool in pan for 10 minutes before transferring to a serving plate and leaving to cool completely. Dust with reserved confectioners sugar and serve.

Useful Items

bundt cake pan (8.5 in.- 9.5 in.), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Use different yogurt flavors (e.g. coffee, vanilla, berry).
  • For a lemon or orange flavor: add zest of 1 organic lemon or orange to the sugar before grinding.
