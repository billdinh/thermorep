Peach pavlova

Ingredients

Meringue

  • 220 g sugar
  • 1 Tbsp cornflour
  • 4 egg whites, from large eggs
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 tsp lemon juice

Topping

  • 350 g peaches, stoned and cut in slices (1 cm)
  • 20 g honey
  • 150 g water
  • 50 g moscatel wine
  • 400 g whipping cream

Preparation

Meringue

  1. Preheat oven to 150°C. Line a baking tray with baking paper.
  2. Place sugar and cornflour in mixing bowl then grind 5 sec/speed 9. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg whites, salt and lemon juice in mixing bowl then, without measuring cup, whip 4 min/37°C/speed 3 while slowly adding reserved sugar mixture through hole in mixing bowl lid.
  4. With the help of a spoon, place meringue on prepared tray in a circle shape then bake for 10 minutes (150°C). Turn oven down to 120°C and continue baking for 1 hour (120°C), or until set. Turn off oven and allow meringue to cool inside without opening the door (approx. 3-4 hours or overnight). Clean and dry mixing bowl and make topping.

Topping

  1. Place peaches, honey, water and wine in mixing bowl then heat 10 min/100°C//speed 1. Drain through simmering basket, discarding cooking liquid and set aside to cool. Rinse and dry mixing bowl.
  2. Once meringue has cooled, remove from baking paper to a serving plate. Insert butterfly whisk. Place cream in mixing bowl and whip without setting a time/speed 3 until softly whipped, watching carefully to avoid over-whipping. Cover meringue with whipped cream then top with reserved cooled, cooked peaches. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

baking tray and baking paper, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • The meringue and peaches can be made ahead. Whip the cream and assemble the finished dish just before serving.
