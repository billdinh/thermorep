Brioche buns with cheese

Ingredients

Brioche buns

  • 220 g full cream milk
  • 40 g fresh yeast
  • 100 g white sugar
  • 500 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 80 g salted butter, softened
  • 1 egg
  • 1 egg yolk, (reserve egg white for filling)
  • ½ tsp sea salt

Cheese filling

  • 1 egg
  • 80 g white sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla sugar
  • 500 g fresh ricotta cheese, well-drained
  • 100 g sour cream
  • 1 egg white, lightly beaten

Preparation

Brioche buns

  1. Place milk, yeast and sugar into mixing bowl and warm 2 min 30 sec/37°C/speed 1.
  2. Add all remaining brioche ingredients and knead 3 min/. Transfer dough into a large bowl, cover with kitchen towel and leave to prove until doubled in size (approx. 1 hour).
  3. Preheat oven to 200°C. Line 2 large baking trays with baking paper and set aside. Transfer dough onto a silicone bread mat or lightly floured work surface. Roll dough into a log (approx. 35 cm long), then divide into 14 equal portions.
  4. Using a rolling pin, roll each portion into a circle (approx. 8 cm). Transfer onto prepared baking trays and leave to prove for a further 30 minutes. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Cheese filling

  1. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg, sugar and vanilla sugar into mixing bowl and cook 2 min/60°C/speed 3. Remove butterfly whisk.
  2. Add ricotta or quark and sour cream and mix 15 sec/speed 6, with aid of spatula.
  3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then repeat mixing 15 sec/speed 6.
  4. Make an indentation in the centre of each bun with your thumb to create a nest shape. Place 2 tablespoons (approx. 40 g) of the cheese filling into each indentation.
  5. Brush buns with beaten egg white, then bake for 15-20 minutes (200°C) or until golden brown. Leave buns to cool before serving.

Useful Items

large bowl, kitchen towel, silicone bread mat, baking paper, 2 large baking trays, rolling pin, pastry brush, oven
