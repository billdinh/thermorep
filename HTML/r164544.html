Eggnog

Ingredients

  • 40 oz whole milk
  • 20 oz heavy whipping cream
  • 12 egg yolks, from large eggs
  • 4 - 8 oz brandy, optional
  • 2 - 3 oz sugar, to taste
  • 1 pinch salt
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon, plus extra to taste
  • ½ tsp ground nutmeg, to taste
  • 12 egg whites, from large eggs, room temperature

Preparation

  1. Place milk, cream, egg yolks, brandy, sugar, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg into mixing bowl. Place simmering basket instead of measuring cup on mixing bowl lid and cook 20 min/175°F/speed 3. Transfer to a large heat proof bowl and refrigerate 4 hours. Clean mixing bowl.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg whites into mixing bowl and without measuring cup whip 4 min/speed 3.5 or until desired stiffness is achieved, watching carefully to avoid overwhipping. Remove butterfly whisk.
  3. Fold whipped egg whites into chilled mixture with spatula. Sprinkle with cinnamon to taste and serve chilled.

Useful Items

heat proof bowl, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Eggnog will thicken slightly when chilled.
  • The Eggnog can be enjoyed immediately, chilled or over ice. For more complex flavor, allow Eggnog to chill for at least 2 days or up to 2 weeks.
  • Add ½ tsp vanilla bean paste with the spices.
