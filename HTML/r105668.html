Zurich-style veal strips with potatoes

Ingredients

  • 650 g veal scallopini, cut into strips (4 cm) (see Tips)
  • 20 g cornflour
  • 60 g soy sauce
  • 1 brown onion (approx. 150 g), cut into halves
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 1 tbsp Meat stock paste (see Tips)
  • 500 g water
  • 800 g potatoes, peeled and cut into wedges
  • 400 g button mushrooms, cut into thin slices (5 mm)
  • 20 g tomato paste
  • 100 g sour cream (see Tips)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce (optional)

Preparation

  1. Place a large ceramic or glass bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh veal, cornflour and soy sauce into it. Stir to combine, then cover with plastic wrap and place into refrigerator for 2 hours to marinate.
  2. Place onion into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add oil and sauté 2 min/Varoma//speed .
  4. Add stock paste and 450 g of the water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh potatoes into it.
  5. Insert Varoma tray and weigh mushrooms onto it, secure Varoma lid and steam 30 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  6. Carefully remove Varoma tray and set aside. Transfer potatoes into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and cover to keep warm.
  7. Add tomato paste, cream, salt, pepper, Worcestershire sauce (optional) and remaining 50 g of the water into mixing bowl and cook 2 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  8. Add reserved marinated veal and reserved mushrooms into mixing bowl and cook 3 min/Varoma//speed , or until meat is cooked. Serve with reserved steamed potatoes.

Useful Items

plastic wrap or kitchen towel, large ceramic or glass bowl, thermal serving bowl or other large bowl, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Please refer to The Basic Cookbook or Everyday Cookbook for the Meat stock paste recipe.
  • You can use uncrumbed pork schnitzel in place of veal scallopini.
  • You can substitute sour cream with pouring (whipping) cream.
