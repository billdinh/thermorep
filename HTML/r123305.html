Blueberry and Mascarpone Cheesecake

Ingredients

Blueberry Layer

  • 200 g fresh blueberries, plus extra for serving
  • 25 g caster sugar
  • 20 g lemon juice

Biscuit Layer

  • 40 g unsalted butter, soft, plus extra for greasing
  • 200 g rich tea biscuits

Filling

  • 500 g mascarpone
  • 380 g condensed milk
  • 70 g lemon juice

Preparation

Blueberry Layer

  1. Place blueberries, sugar and lemon juice in mixing bowl then heat 5 min/80°C//speed . Meanwhile, grease sides and line base of a springform tin (Ø 20 cm) with baking paper. Transfer blueberries and syrup to a bowl then set aside to cool and thicken. Clean mixing bowl.

Biscuit Layer

  1. Place butter in mixing bowl and melt 2 min/50°C/speed 2.
  2. Add biscuits and crush 10 sec/speed 8. Transfer to prepared tin and press down with back of a spoon forming a firm biscuit layer. Set aside in fridge to chill. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl and make filling.

Filling

  1. Insert butterfly whisk. Place mascarpone, condensed milk and lemon juice in mixing bowl then blend 20 sec/speed 3. Remove butterfly whisk.
  2. Pour half into tin over biscuit layer, spreading out evenly then arrange cooled blueberries on top, reserving syrup for later. Top with remaining cream mixture and smooth out evenly. Chill in fridge for at least 3 hours until firmly set.
  3. Before serving, garnish with extra fresh blueberries and drizzle over reserved blueberry syrup, then slice.

Useful Items

springform tin (Ø 20 cm), baking paper, refrigerator
