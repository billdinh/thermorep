Sesame Chicken Nuggets

Ingredients

  • 7 oz bread, broken into pieces
  • 2 oz sesame seeds
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 3 ½ oz yellow onion
  • ½ oz parsley leaves (approx. 30 leaves)
  • 2 oz Gouda cheese cut in cubes
  • 3 large eggs
  • 16 oz chicken breasts cut in cubes (approx. 1 in.)
  • 1 oz plain yogurt
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 18 oz water
  • 1 oz all-purpose flour, to coat
  • oil, to fry

Preparation

  1. Place 3 ½ oz bread into mixing bowl and chop 15 sec/speed 9. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place remaining bread into mixing bowl and chop 15 sec/speed 9. Transfer to the bowl with reserved bread crumbs.
  3. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in sesame seeds. Transfer to the bowl with reserved bread crumbs and stir through.
  4. Place garlic, onion, parsley, Gouda cheese and 1 egg into mixing bowl and mix 5 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix a further 5 sec/speed 6.
  5. Add chicken, yogurt and salt and mix 10 sec/speed 4. Remove and divide mixture into two equal parts. Place the prepared chicken filling onto parchment paper and form 2 rolls (approx. 1 ½ in. diameter). Place wrapped rolls onto Varoma tray and set aside. Rinse out mixing bowl.
  6. Place water into mixing bowl. Insert Varoma tray, secure lid, place Varoma into position and steam 18 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove Varoma and allow to cool.
  7. Remove parchment paper and slice chicken rolls into nuggets (approx. ½ in.).
  8. In a bowl, lightly beat remaining 2 eggs with a whisk.
  9. Roll each chicken nugget in flour, then lightly beaten egg, and finally reserved bread crumbs and sesame seed mixture. Fry in hot oil. Serve warm.

Useful Items

parchment paper

Hints & Tricks

  • After chicken nuggets are coated with bread crumbs they can be frozen in ziplock bags for up to 3 months, there is no need to defrost before frying.
  • Bread crumbs can be replaced with panko. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh 7 oz panko and 2 oz sesame seeds. Reserve for breading chicken nuggets.
