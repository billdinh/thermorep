Vanilla bean marshmallows

Ingredients

  • 30 g gelatine powder
  • 280 g water
  • 330 g caster sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste
  • cornflour, for dusting (optional)
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
195 kJ / 46 kcal
Protein
0.8 g
Carbohydrates
11.3 g
Fat
0 g

Preparation

  1. Line a baking tin (18 x 26 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place a ceramic or glass bowl onto mixing bowl lid. Weigh gelatine powder and 140 g of the water into ceramic or glass bowl then set aside to soak (approx. 3-5 minutes).
  3. Place sugar into mixing bowl and mill 5 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add remaining 140 g water and heat 15 min/100°C/speed 2.
  5. Add reserved gelatine mixture and combine 2 min/100°C/speed 2. Allow to cool in mixing bowl until the temperature falls below 50°C (approx. 45 minutes). Once cooled, insert butterfly whisk and whip 7 min/speed 3.5.
  6. Add vanilla and combine 10 sec/speed 3. Remove butterfly whisk.
  7. Pour into prepared tin, cover and leave to set at room temperature (approx. 1 hour).
  8. Once set, slice marshmallow into approx. 30 pieces. Dust with cornflour or icing sugar (optional) before serving.

Useful Items

baking paper, baking tin (18 x 26 cm), ceramic or glass bowl, plastic wrap

Hints & Tricks

  • Toasted desiccated coconut can also be used to coat your marshmallows if desired.
  • To check that mixture has fallen below 50°C in step 5, remove mixing bowl from Thermomix base and set aside to cool. When you return the mixing bowl to Thermomix base the lights will indicate the current temperature of the mixture in mixing bowl.
