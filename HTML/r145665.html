Raw chocolate beetroot cake

Ingredients

Cake

  • oil, for greasing
  • 200 g almonds
  • 2 raw beetroots (approx. 350 g), peeled and cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 1 carrot (approx. 135 g), cut into pieces
  • 120 g shredded coconut
  • 1 orange, zest only, no white pith (optional)
  • 2 tbsp psyllium husk
  • 15 pitted dates (approx. 80 g)
  • 140 g pure maple syrup
  • 80 g raw cacao powder
  • 30 g chia seeds
  • 30 g flaxseeds (linseeds)
  • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste
  • ¼ tsp salt

Chocolate icing

  • 250 g raw cacao butter
  • 125 g cashews
  • 40 g chia seeds
  • 220 g pure maple syrup
  • 110 g water
  • 60 g raw cacao powder
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • raw beetroot, peeled and spiralized, for garnishing

Preparation

Cake

  1. Grease and line a springform cake tin (23 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place almonds, beetroot, carrot, shredded coconut and orange zest (optional) into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 9.
  3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Add psyllium husk, dates, syrup, cacao powder, chia seeds, flaxseeds (linseeds), vanilla and salt and combine 25 sec//speed 8, with aid of spatula or until well combined. Transfer into prepared tin and press down evenly using the back of a spoon to form cake base, then place into refrigerator while making icing. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Chocolate icing

  1. Place cacao butter into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7, then melt 3-4 min/37°C/speed 2 or until completely melted.
  2. Add cashews, chia seeds, syrup, water, cacao powder and salt and blend 40 sec/speed 6.
  3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and blend 40 sec/speed 6 or until smooth and creamy. Pour mixture over cake, smooth with the back of a spoon and place into refrigerator to set until firm (approx. 2 hours). Garnish with spiralized beetroot before serving.

Useful Items

springform cake tin (23 cm), baking paper, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Cut cake into squares and place into the freezer to enjoy as a frozen slice.
  • You can store this cake in the refrigerator for up to 3 days or in the freezer for up to 1 month.
