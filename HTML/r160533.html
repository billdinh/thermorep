Strawberry Shortcake

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs, separated, at room temperature
  • unsalted butter, to grease
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp cream of tartar
  • 3 oz corn starch
  • 1 oz all-purpose flour
  • 4 ½ oz caster sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tbsp hot water
  • 16 oz heavy whipping cream
  • 16 oz strawberries, cleaned, hulled and sliced (approx. ¼ in. - ½ in.)

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Lightly grease a nonstick springform pan (9 in.).
  2. Place baking soda, cream of tartar, corn starch and flour into mixing bowl and stir 10 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place sugar into mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer confectioners sugar to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Thoroughly clean and dry mixing bowl and lid.
  5. Insert butterfly whisk. Place 4 egg whites into mixing bowl and without measuring cup whip 4 min/speed 3 until stiff and fluffy.
  6. Mix 4-5 min/speed 3, meanwhile adding one teaspoon at a time of reserved confectioners sugar through hole in mixing bowl lid.
  7. Without setting a time, mix at speed , adding egg yolks one at a time through hole in mixing bowl lid.
  8. Remove butterfly whisk. Add vanilla, water and reserved flour mixture, insert measuring cup and mix 5 sec/speed 3. Gently scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix a further 5 sec/speed 3.
  9. Transfer mixture into prepared cake pan and bake 25-30 minutes (350°F) or until a skewer inserted in the center of the cake comes out clean. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  10. Transfer cake to a wire rack and allow to cool completely.
  11. Insert butterfly whisk. Place cream into mixing bowl and without setting a time whip at speed 3, until desired stiffness is achieved, watching carefully to avoid overwhipping. Remove butterfly whisk.
  12. Using a serrated knife, slice cake in half horizontally and place bottom half on a serving plate, cut side up. Arrange strawberries and whipped cream over bottom layer, cover with top half of cake, cut side down, dust with confectioners sugar and serve immediately.

Useful Items

nonstick springform pan (approx. 9 in.), toothpick or skewer, serving plate, wire rack, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • For a sweeter whipped cream: add 2-3 tsp sugar with cream and without setting a time whip at speed 3, until desired stiffness is achieved.
  • Soak strawberries in a little Grand Marnier, Cointreau or orange juice.
  • To make individual Strawberry Shortcake Parfaits; cut cake into 1 inch chunks and place in individual dessert glasses, fill with strawberries and whipped cream.
