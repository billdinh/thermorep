Seedy Marmalade Flapjacks

Ingredients

  • 150 g unsalted butter, soft
  • 150 g golden syrup
  • 50 g dark brown muscovado sugar
  • 80 g orange marmalade
  • 280 g rolled oats
  • 50 g pecan nuts
  • 50 g pumpkin seeds
  • 50 g sunflower seeds
  • 50 g dried cranberries
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
1428 kJ / 341 kcal

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C. Line base and sides of a square cake tin (20 cm) with baking paper.
  2. Place butter, golden syrup, sugar and marmalade in mixing bowl then heat 3 min/90°C/speed 2.
  3. Add oats, pecans, pumpkin seeds, sunflower seeds and cranberries then mix 20 sec//speed 3. Finish mixing by hand with spatula. Transfer to prepared tin and press down with the back of a spoon to form an even layer. Bake for 25 minutes (190°C) until golden.
  4. Leave to cool in tin before removing. Cut into 12 bars to serve.

Useful Items

square cake tin (20 cm), baking paper, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Vary the types of nuts, seeds and dried fruit as desired. Pistachios, sunflower seeds and dried cherries are another good, colourful combination.

The texture from the seeds and nuts and the addition of marmalade in these flapjacks makes them really stand out from the standard oat recipe. Try different dried fruits, nuts and seeds for a variation.
