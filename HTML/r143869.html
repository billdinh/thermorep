Cacao and Orange bars

Ingredients

  • 160 g cashew nuts, raw
  • 160 g medjool dates, pitted, halved
  • 50 g raisins
  • 4 Tbsp cocoa powder, unsweetened
  • 2 oranges, finely grated zest only
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
591 kJ / 141 kcal
Protein
3.7 g
Carbohydrates
14.6 g
Fat
7.6 g

Preparation

  1. Line a square cake tin (approx. 16 cm) with cling film.
  2. Place cashew nuts in mixing bowl and grind 30 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add dates, raisins, cocoa and orange zest then blend 30 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then blend again 15 sec/speed 7. Tip out into prepared tin and press down firmly with the back of a spoon. Chill in fridge for at least 2 hours.
  4. Remove hardened mixture from tin and place on a chopping board. Slice into 12 bars. Wrap in cling film or greaseproof paper and store in fridge until required. Serve as a healthy, natural snack.

Useful Items

square cake tin (16 cm), cling film, airtight container, greaseproof paper, long sharp knife, chopping board, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • If mixture does not bind, add 1-2 tsp boiling water then mix again 5 sec/speed 4.
