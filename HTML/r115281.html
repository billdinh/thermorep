Black tahini and beetroot hommus

Ingredients

  • 4 beetroots, peeled and cut into pieces (2 x 3 cm) (approx. 500 g)
  • 4 garlic cloves, unpeeled
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 4 tbsp olive oil, plus extra for drizzling
  • 400 g canned chickpeas, rinsed and drained (approx. 250 g after draining)
  • 2 tbsp black tahini
  • 50 g lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tbsp fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only, cut into pieces

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C. Line a baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with aluminium foil.
  2. Place beetroot and garlic onto prepared baking tray, then sprinkle with cumin seeds, salt, pepper and drizzle with 2 tablespoons of the oil. Using your hands, ensure that beetroot and garlic are coated with oil and seasonings. Wrap up to form a parcel. Bake for 35-40 minutes (190°C), or until beetroot is tender. Set aside to cool for 20 minutes, then carefully peel garlic cloves. Discard skins.
  3. Place roasted beetroot, peeled garlic, chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, vinegar and remaining 2 tablespoons oil into mixing bowl and mix 20 sec/speed 7, or until smooth.
  4. Transfer into a serving bowl, drizzle with extra olive oil and garnish with parsley. Serve with crackers of choice.

Useful Items

baking tray (30 x 40 cm), aluminium foil, serving bowl, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Dip can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 1 week.
  • We served this dip with the Rosemary and sea salt crackers. Please refer to the recipe on this recipe chip.
