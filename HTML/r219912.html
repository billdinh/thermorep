Cookie Ice Cream with Figs and Chocolate

Ingredients

  • 7 oz whole milk
  • 10 ½ oz heavy whipping cream
  • 5 oz sugar
  • 3 egg yolks, from large eggs
  • 2 oz digestive biscuits

Assembly

  • 3 ½ oz dried figs, white (stems removed)
  • 3 ½ oz dark chocolate, cut into pieces
  • 3 oz whole milk
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1502 kJ / 359 kcal
Protein
4.8 g
Carbohydrates
39 g
Fat
21.5 g

Preparation

  1. Place 7 oz milk, whipping cream, sugar and egg yolks into mixing bowl and cook 7 min/185°F/speed 4.
  2. Add digestive biscuits and mix 20 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a shallow freezable container, cover with cling wrap and freeze for at least 10 hours or overnight.

Assembly

  1. Place dried figs into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.
  2. Place dark chocolate into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add 3 oz milk and cook 5 min/130°F/speed 2. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Cut ice cream into pieces (1½ in. x 1½ in.) with a sharp knife. Place ice cream pieces into mixing bowl and blend 20 sec/speed 6, then with aid of spatula stir 20 sec/speed 4. Transfer ice cream to dessert bowls and pour chocolate sauce and figs over top. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

cling wrap, shallow freezable container (e.g. aluminum, silicone)

Hints & Tricks

  • Digestive biscuits can be replaced with graham crackers.
