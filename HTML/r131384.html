Orange couscous with steamed salmon

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets, fresh or frozen and thawed
  • 1 lemon, juice only
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 pinch pepper, ground
  • 4 sprigs fresh rosemary, leaves only
  • 1 orange, zest and juice
  • 500 g water
  • 250 g couscous
  • 80 g watercress, leaves only
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
3204 kJ / 767 kcal
Protein
41 g
Carbohydrates
47 g
Fat
45 g

Preparation

  1. Set Varoma dish onto Varoma lid (turned upside down). Place salmon fillets in Varoma dish and season with lemon juice, salt, pepper and rosemary.
  2. Place orange zest into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place water and 1 tsp of salt into mixing bowl and place Varoma into position. Steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 1. Transfer steaming water to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place a large bowl onto mixing bowl lid, weigh 250 g couscous and add 250 g steaming water. Add orange zest and juice into the large bowl, allow to rest for around 5 minutes and then use a fork to fluff up the couscous.
  5. Add watercress and salmon, pulled into flakes, to couscous and mix with spatula. Serve hot or cold.
