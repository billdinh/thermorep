Banana Split

Ingredients

  • 60 g hazelnuts
  • 155 g chocolate, cut in pieces
  • 100 g whole milk
  • 115 g unsalted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 590 g whipping cream
  • 60 g dark brown sugar
  • 60 g caster sugar
  • 60 g maple syrup
  • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste (see tip)
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 8 bananas
  • 8 scoops vanilla ice cream
  • 8 scoops chocolate ice cream
  • 8 candied cherries

Preparation

  1. Place nuts in mixing bowl and chop Turbo/1 sec/1 time. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place chocolate in mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add milk, butter and vanilla extract then cook 4 min/50Â°C/speed 3. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place butter, cream, dark brown sugar, caster sugar, maple syrup, vanilla bean paste and salt in mixing bowl. Place simmering basket in place of measuring cup on mixing bowl lid and cook 12 min/Varoma/speed 2. Set caramel sauce aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl thoroughly.
  5. Insert butterfly whisk. Place whipping cream in mixing bowl and, without setting a time, whip speed 4 for a soft whip until cream is just holding its shape, watching carefully to avoid overwhipping. This can take between a few seconds to 1-3 minutes.
  6. Place whipped cream in a piping bag fitted with a large nozzle and refrigerate until ready to use.
  7. Peel bananas and slice in half lengthwise, then lay 2 banana halves in each serving dish. Place a scoop of vanilla and a scoop of chocolate ice cream into each bowl.
  8. Pipe whipped cream over bananas and ice cream.
  9. Drizzle chocolate and caramel sauce over the whipped cream.
  10. Sprinkle with reserved nuts, top with a cherry and serve immediately.

Useful Items

piping bag and nozzle, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Refrigerate leftover chocolate and caramel sauce in airtight jars for later use.
  • Make your own vanilla bean paste: place 6-10 whole vanilla bean pods into mixing bowl and grind 20 sec/speed 10 or until ground. Add 3 Tbsp vanilla extract and 2 tsp xanthan gum (optional). Stir 3 sec/speed 3. Scrape out bowl with spatula and place in a glass jar to use as required.
  • Vanilla bean paste can be substituted measure-for-measure with vanilla extract.
  • To reduce the number of servings: decrease the amount of bananas and ice cream.
  • Use strawberry sauce instead of, or in addition to the caramel sauce.
  • Experiment with your favourite ice cream flavours - try combinations such as coffee, chocolate, and hazelnut!
