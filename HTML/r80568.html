Carrot Cake

Ingredients

Cake

  • 160 g sunflower oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 300 g carrots, peeled and cut in pieces (3 cm)
  • 3 large eggs
  • 350 g brown sugar
  • 50 g sour cream
  • 250 g wholemeal flour
  • 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • ½ tsp ground nutmeg
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt

Cream cheese icing

  • 1 Tbsp granulated sugar
  • 1 orange, preferably organic, thin peelings of skin only
  • 300 g cream cheese
  • 15 - 30 g honey

Preparation

Cake

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Line a round cake tin (Ø 20 cm) with baking paper and grease the sides.
  2. Place carrots, eggs, sugar, sour cream, oil, flour, bicarbonate of soda, nutmeg, cinnamon and salt into mixing bowl then mix 15 sec/speed 5 with aid of spatula. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix again 5 sec/speed 5.
  3. Transfer batter to prepared cake tin and bake 60-75 minutes (180°C) - cover cake with aluminium foil after 45 minutes if it begins to get too dark. The cake is cooked if it springs back when pressed with a fingertip. Cool in tin for 30 minutes then remove and leave to cool completely on a wire rack. Clean mixing bowl before preparing icing.

Cream cheese icing

  1. Place sugar and orange peelings in mixing bowl then grind 15 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and grind again 15 sec/speed 10.
  2. Add cream cheeese and honey then mix 20 sec/speed 3.
  3. Insert butterfly whisk and whisk 30 sec/speed 2. Once cake is completely cool, spread icing over the top. Cut in slices and serve.

Useful Items

round cake tin (Ø 20 cm), baking paper, cooling rack, aluminium foil, oven

Hints & Tricks

The cake will keep well for up to 4 days. Apply icing on the day of serving.

Butter can be used instead of sunflower oil if preferred.
