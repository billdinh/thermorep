Steamed white chocolate cheesecake

Ingredients

Mango and lime coulis

  • 200 g fresh mango cheeks, flesh only or frozen, cut into pieces
  • 30 g caster sugar
  • 50 g water
  • 2 limes, zest only, no white pith, finely grated
  • 40 g lime juice (approx. 2 limes)

Cheesecake filling

  • 40 g unsalted butter, plus extra for greasing
  • 125 g Granita biscuits (½ packet)
  • 300 g white chocolate, broken into pieces
  • 500 g cream cheese, cut into pieces and softened
  • 50 g caster sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste
  • 4 eggs
  • 1000 g water

Preparation

Mango and lime coulis

  1. Place all coulis ingredients into mixing bowl and cook 4 min/90°C/speed 3. Transfer into a sealable container and allow to cool, then cover and place into refrigerator until ready to use.

Cheesecake filling

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C. Grease and line the bottom of a springform cake tin (19 cm) and set aside.
  2. Place Granita biscuits and butter into mixing bowl and blend 15 sec/speed 8, or until a wet breadcrumb consistency is achieved.
  3. Transfer mixture into the bottom of the prepared tin and press down to form an even base.
  4. Place base into the oven and bake for 10 minutes (160°C), then remove and allow to cool (approx. 30 minutes). Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  5. Place chocolate into mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 8.
  6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then melt 5 min/50°C/speed 1, or until chocolate has melted.
  7. Add cream cheese, sugar and vanilla and blend 25 sec/speed 5.5, or until completely combined. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  8. Crack 4 eggs into a small bowl. Mix until all eggs are combined/speed 4, adding eggs slowly through hole in mixing bowl lid. Transfer cheesecake filling onto cooled biscuit base. Rinse mixing bowl.
  9. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish into position. Place cheesecake into Varoma dish, ensuring it is sitting secure and the Varoma lid closes properly. Place a piece of baking paper on top of the cheesecake, followed by a piece of paper towel, secure Varoma lid and steam 40 min/Varoma/speed 1. Allow cheesecake to cool completely in tin, then place cheesecake in tin into the refrigerator to set overnight.
  10. Serve cheesecake drizzled with mango and lime coulis.

Useful Items

sealable container, springform cake tin (19 cm), baking paper, paper towel, small bowl, refrigerator, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Decorate your cake with thin slices of fresh mango or lime.
  • For a different flavour, replace mango and lime coulis with Kiwi coulis (see recipe on this recipe chip) or Berry coulis (see The Basic Cookbook).
