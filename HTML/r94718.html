Rhubarb Fool

Ingredients

  • 300 g double cream
  • 100 g Greek yoghurt
  • 300 g homemade rhubarb compote, cold or room temperature
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1050 kJ / 251 kcal
Protein
2.3 g
Carbohydrates
2.6 g
Fat
25.8 g

Preparation

  1. Insert butterfly whisk. Place cream in mixing bowl and, without measuring cup, whip speed 3, without setting a time, until lightly whipped, watching carefully to avoid over-whipping.  Remove butterfly whisk and scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add yoghurt and rhubarb compote then mix 15 sec/speed 3.5. Transfer to serving dishes then cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour to set slightly. Serve chilled.

Useful Items

cling film, refrigerator
