Coconut-Lime Crème Brûlée

Ingredients

  • 2 oz sugar
  • ½ oz shredded coconut, unsweetened
  • 1 lime, thin peel only, no pith
  • 14 oz coconut milk
  • 7 oz heavy whipping cream
  • 8 egg yolks, from large eggs
  • 2 oz turbinado sugar (approx. 4 tbsp)
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1519 kJ / 363 kcal
Protein
5 g
Carbohydrates
34 g
Fat
24 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 300°F.
  2. Place sugar, coconut and lime peel into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 10.
  3. Add coconut milk, whipping cream and egg yolks and cook 6 min/175°F/speed 3.
  4. Divide batter between 8 ramekins and bake in a water bath for 40-50 minutes (300°F), until centers are just set but still slightly wobbly. Remove from oven and let cool 15 minutes. Wrap tightly with cling wrap and refrigerate 2 hours, up to overnight.
  5. Before serving, sprinkle tops with turbinado sugar and caramelize with a torch or place under a broiler for 1 minute, watching carefully to avoid burning. Allow to rest for 5 minutes and serve.

Useful Items

kitchen blow torch, cling wrap, ramekins (4 oz each), oven, refrigerator
