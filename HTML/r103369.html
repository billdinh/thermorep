Pancakes with Two Fillings

Ingredients

Pancakes

  • 250 g plain flour
  • 300 g milk
  • 30 g olive oil, plus more for frying
  • 5 medium eggs
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt

Spinach and Cheese Filling

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 50 g onions, halved
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 500 g frozen spinach, thawed, well drained
  • 40 g crème fraîche
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • ½ tsp ground white pepper
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • 60 g pine nuts, for garnish

Mexican Filling

  • 1 - 2 fresh chillies, halved
  • 100 g onions, quartered
  • 30 g olive oil, plus extra for cooking/frying
  • 400 g beef mince, broken in pieces
  • 700 g tinned chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 tsp sweet paprika
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • ½ tsp Tabasco sauce
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 140 g tinned sweetcorn, drained
  • 120 g tinned kidney beans, drained
  • 100 g feta cheese, diced

Preparation

Pancakes

  1. Place flour, milk, oil, eggs and salt in mixing bowl then mix 30 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then mix again 10 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and set aside to rest for 20 minutes. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl and prepare fillings.

Spinach and Cheese Filling

  1. Place garlic, onions and oil in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  2. Add spinach, crème fraîche, salt, pepper and nutmeg then cook 8 min/100°C//speed 1. Meanwhile, toast pine nuts in a dry frying pan then transfer to a large bowl. Drain spinach through a fine sieve, discarding any excess liquid, then set spinach aside in a bowl. Clean mixing bowl.

Mexican Filling

  1. Place chilli, onions and oil in mixing bowl then chop 4 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  2. Add minced beef, tomatoes, salt, paprika, sugar, pepper and Tabasco then cook 10 min/100°C//speed 1.
  3. Add sweetcorn and kidney beans then, without measuring cup, cook 5 min/100°C//speed . Drain off any excess liquid using a sieve then transfer mixture to a bowl.
  4. Heat a little oil in a large pancake or frying pan over a medium heat. Pour a small ladle of batter into the centre then spread out thinly. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side then transfer to a plate. Repeat, adding more oil when necessary until all batter is used.
  5. Place pancakes on a plate, fill with spinach filling or mexican filling, or both, then divide feta cheese between each and wrap. Serve hot.

Useful Items

fine sieve, pancake pan or frying pan, small ladle, hob

Hints & Tricks

  • The stuffed pancakes can also be baked slightly after being rolled.
  • Serve with salad.
