Hot White Wine Punch with Cranberries

Ingredients

  • 50 g dried cranberries
  • 20 g pistachio nuts, unsalted, peeled
  • 500 g white grape juice
  • 30 g sugar
  • 40 g orange juice
  • 2 pinches ground cinnamon
  • 2 cloves
  • 350 g dry white wine
  • 25 g orange liqueur (e.g. Grand Marnier)
Nutrition

Calories

Preparation

  1. Place cranberries in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Divide between four serving glasses.
  2. Place pistachios in mixing bowl and toast, without measuring cup, 5 min/Varoma/speed 1. With measuring cup in place, chop 3 sec/speed 5. Divide between glasses.
  3. Place grape juice, sugar, orange juice, cinnamon and cloves in mixing bowl then boil 5 min/100°C/speed 1.
  4. Add white wine and orange liqueur then heat 7 min/80°C/speed 1. Pour into glasses with cranberries and pistachios and serve hot.
