Baby Peas and Pancetta Risotto

Ingredients

  • 3 oz Parmesan cheese, cut into 1 in. pieces
  • 2 oz shallots, halved
  • 2 oz olive oil, plus extra to fry
  • 7 oz frozen baby peas, defrosted, divided
  • 2 oz dry white wine
  • 7 oz arborio rice
  • 2 tsp chicken stock paste
  • 16 oz water
  • 5 ½ oz pancetta, diced (¼ in.)
  • 1 oz cream
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper, to taste
  • 5 - 10 fresh mint leaves, chopped
  • &frac18; tsp salt, to taste

Preparation

  1. Place Parmesan into mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place shallots and 2 oz olive oil into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5.
  3. Add 3.5 oz baby peas and chop Turbo/1 sec/2 times. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and cook 3 min/212°F/speed 2.
  4. Add wine and rice and without measuring cup cook 2 min/220°F//speed 1.5.
  5. Add stock paste and water and with simmering basket instead of measuring cup on mixing bowl lid cook 7 min/212°F//speed 0.5. Meanwhile sauté pancetta in oil over a high heat on stove top until crisp. Drain on paper towel and set aside.
  6. Add remaining 3.5 oz baby peas, insert measuring cup and cook 5 min/212°F//speed .
  7. Add cream, reserved grated Parmesan, pepper and chopped mint and mix well with spatula. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Let rest for 5 minutes to absorb liquid. Serve sprinkled with reserved fried pancetta.

Useful Items

paper towel, frying pan, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • Chicken stock paste and water can be replaced with 16 oz chicken stock.
