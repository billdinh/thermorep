Spicy Nuts and Popcorn Party Mix

Ingredients

  • 2 oz popcorn (popped), buttered and salted
  • 1 egg white, from a large egg
  • 1 oz honey
  • ¼ tsp salt, to taste
  • 4 oz raw almonds, skin on
  • 4 oz raw pecans
  • 4 oz cashews
  • 1 - 2 tbsp Mexican spice mix, to taste
  • 2 tbsp sesame seeds (optional)
  • ½ tsp cayenne pepper, to taste
  • 1 oz unsalted butter, diced (½ in.)
Nutrition
per 1 portion

23 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 300°F, line a baking sheet with parchment and set aside. Place a large bowl onto mixing bowl lid, weigh in popcorn and set aside.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg white, honey and salt into mixing bowl and without measuring cup whisk 30 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Remove butterfly whisk.
  3. Add almonds, pecans, cashews, Mexican spice, sesame seeds and cayenne and without measuring cup cook 5 min/250°F//speed 1. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Add butter, insert measuring cup and cook a further 30 sec/250°F//speed 1.
  4. Transfer nut mixture to large bowl with reserved popcorn and toss with spatula to combine. Transfer onto prepared baking sheet and bake for 20 minutes (300°F) stirring after 10 minutes to ensure even baking. Do not overbake. Allow to cool before transferring to serving bowls.

Useful Items

baking sheet, parchment paper, large bowl, oven
