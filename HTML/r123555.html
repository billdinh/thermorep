Chocolate Chip Cake

Ingredients

  • 170 g butter, soft, plus extra for greasing
  • 350 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 150 g dark chocolate, small pieces or callets
  • 190 g sugar
  • 1 vanilla pod
  • 3 medium eggs
  • 150 g milk
  • 3 tsp baking powder
Nutrition
per 1 slices
Calories
941 kJ / 225 kcal
Protein
4 g
Carbohydrates
26 g
Fat
12 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C and grease a large loaf tin (900 g/2 lb) then dust with flour.
  2. Place chocolate in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place sugar and vanilla pod in mixing bowl then grind 30 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add butter and eggs then mix 1 min/speed 4.
  5. Add flour, milk and baking powder then mix 20 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  6. Add reserved chopped chocolate then stir in with spatula. Transfer to prepared loaf tin then bake for 45-55 minutes (180°C) until golden brown and a skewer inserted in centre comes out clean, covering with foil after 30 minutes to prevent top from browning too much. Allow to cool in tin for 10 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
  7. Decorate with icing if desired (see tip) then slice to serve.

Useful Items

900 g loaf tin (2 lb), skewer, wire rack, aluminium foil, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • You can make a chocolate ganache to cover the cake if desired: Place 100 g chocolate in mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Add 80 g double cream and melt 2 min/50°C/speed 3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then melt again 1 min/50°C/speed 3. Once cake has cooled, decorate.
