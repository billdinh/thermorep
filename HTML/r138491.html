Cauliflower and sweet potato lasagne

Ingredients

  • 500 g water
  • 400 g cauliflower, cut into bite-size florets
  • 400 g sweet potato, peeled and cut into thin slices (5 mm)
  • 1 brown onion (approx. 120 g), cut into halves
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 40 g salted butter
  • 1 tsp Vegetable stock paste (see Tips)
  • 200 g full cream milk
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper, plus extra to season
  • 2 pinches ground nutmeg
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 375 g fresh lasagne sheets
  • 50 - 70 g fresh baby spinach leaves
  • 3 - 4 sprigs fresh basil, leaves only, finely sliced, plus extra to serve (optional)
  • 300 - 330 g roasted red capsicum, rinsed and drained, torn into pieces
  • 100 g feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1 - 2 pinches paprika

Preparation

  1. Set aside an oven proof baking dish (16 x 25 cm).
  2. Place water into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket and weigh cauliflower into it. Place Varoma into position and divide sweet potato between Varoma dish and tray. Secure Varoma lid and cook 10 min/Varoma/speed 1 .
  3. Set Varoma aside and stir potato to ensure even cooking time. Check if cauliflower is soft. If needed, place Varoma back into position, secure Varoma lid and cook a further 5-10 min/Varoma/speed 1, until sweet potato and cauliflower are cooked.
  4. Remove Varoma and set aside to cool. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and set aside. Rinse mixing bowl.
  5. Place onion, garlic and butter into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5.
  6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and cook 3 min/100°C/speed 1.
  7. Add stock, milk, pepper, nutmeg and reserved cauliflower and cook 4 min/90°C/speed 2.
  8. Add egg yolks and blend 30 sec/speed 3, increasing speed gradually from speed 3 to speed 8.
  9. Season béchamel sauce to taste. Transfer into a jug and set aside.
  10. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  11. Spread 2 tablespoons of sauce into base of reserved baking dish. Place a layer of lasagne sheets on top, then cover with a layer of sauce (approx. 2 tablespoons). Top with approximately one-third of the sweet potato, one-third of the spinach leaves, a few sliced basil leaves and one-third of the roasted capsicum. Top with a layer of lasagne sheets and another layer of sauce (approx. 2 tablespoons) and spread out evenly.
  12. Repeat layering with remaining ingredients (3 layers total), finishing with a layer of lasagne sheets, then spread with remaining sauce. Top with crumbled feta and sprinkle with paprika. Bake for 30-35 minutes (180°C), until golden brown. Set lasagne aside for 5 minutes, and garnish with extra basil (optional) before serving.

Useful Items

baking tray (25 x 16 cm), jug, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Steamed lasagne - This lasagne works well steamed in the Varoma (good to know for those times when you do not have access to an oven).

Place a layer of lasagne sheets on the bottom on the Varoma dish and layer as per the recipe (3 layers total). Cover lasagne with a piece of baking paper and steam 20-25 min/Varoma/speed 1. You will need to stop steaming 1-2 times during the cooking process and, with the aid of spatula, gently lift up the bottom lasagne sheet to remove any pooled water. Cut cooked lasagne into pieces and serve directly from the Varoma dish.

  • Please refer to The Basic Cookbook or Everyday Cookbook for the Vegetable stock paste recipe.
