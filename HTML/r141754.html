Coriander chilli chutney

Ingredients

  • 120 g fresh coriander, leaves, stems and roots, cut into thirds (2-3 bunches) (see Tips)
  • 50 g fresh long green chillies (approx. 3 chillies) trimmed and cut into halves
  • 20 g lemon juice (approx. ½ lemon)
  • 50 g olive oil
  • 1 tsp sea salt
Nutrition
per 1 total recipe
Calories
2163 kJ / 515 kcal

Preparation

  1. Place all ingredients into mixing bowl and blend 30 sec/speed 5.
  2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then blend 1 min/speed 5. Serve immediately, or transfer into a sealable container and place into refrigerator until ready to serve.

Useful Items

sealable container, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Clean and scrape down the roots of your coriander with a spoon to remove the tougher, more fibrous bits on the outside of the roots, before adding to the mixing bowl.
  • You can store chutney in the refrigerator for up to 2 days or in the freezer for up to 1 week.
  • Stir chutney through Greek-style natural yoghurt to create a coriander chilli dip.
