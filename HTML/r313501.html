Pork ribs with black bean sauce

Ingredients

  • 600 - 800 g pork ribs, cut into ribs
  • 800 g water, plus extra for for boiling
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 fresh long red chilli, deseeded and cut in long pieces
  • 1 tbsp potato starch
  • ½ tsp white sugar
  • ¼ tsp ground white pepper
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp Chinese black bean sauce (see Tips)
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp rice wine vinegar
  • 1 - 2 spring onions/shallots, cut into slices, for garnishing

Preparation

  1. Place a large stock pot (2-3 L) with water over high heat and bring to the boil. Carefully add ribs to boiling water and cook, until half cooked (approx. 7 minutes). Carefully remove with tongs, then drain and set aside.
  2. Place garlic and chilli into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add blanched pork ribs, potato starch, sugar, pepper, salt, black bean sauce, oyster sauce, soy sauce and vinegar and mix 10 sec/speed 2. Transfer ribs and marinade into a glass or ceramic bowl, cover with plastic wrap and place into refrigerator for a minimum of 1 hour to marinate (see Tips).
  4. Place water into mixing bowl and place Varoma dish into position. Line Varoma dish with baking paper, poking a few small holes in paper to allow steam to circulate. Place ribs and marinade into Varoma dish, secure Varoma lid and steam 40-50 min/Varoma/speed 1, until ribs are cooked through. Garnish with spring onions/shallots to serve.

Useful Items

plastic wrap, stock pot (3-4 L), ceramic or glass bowl, baking paper, wooden skewer, tongs, refrigerator, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • The ribs can be made ahead of time and allowed to marinate in the refrigerator for up to 8 hours before steaming.
