Vietnamese beef pho

Ingredients

  • 450 g beef bones, cut into pieces (see Tips)
  • 1400 g water
  • 1 brown onion, (approx. 150 g), cut into halves
  • 6 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled and cut into slices
  • 1 stalk fresh lemongrass, white part only, cut into halves
  • 2 star anise
  • 1 cinnamon quill
  • 1 tsp mixed peppercorns
  • 3 whole cloves
  • 1 tsp coriander seeds
  • 1 tsp dried fennel seeds
  • 80 g fish sauce
  • 50 g soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp lime juice (approx. 1 lime)
  • ¼ tsp sea salt, to taste
  • 2 pinches ground white pepper, to taste
  • 300 - 350 g flat rice stick noodles (approx. 6 mm wide)
  • 300 - 350 g beef fillet, partially frozen, cut into thin slices (approx. 3 mm thick)
  • 2 - 3 spring onions/shallots, thinly sliced diagonally, to serve
  • 8 - 10 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only, to serve
  • 6 - 8 sprigs fresh mint, leaves only, to serve
  • 2 sprigs fresh Thai basil, leaves only, to serve
  • 100 g bean sprouts, to serve
  • 1 - 2 long red chillies, deseeded if preferred and finely sliced, to serve
  • 1 - 2 limes, cut into cheeks, to serve

Preparation

  1. Place beef bones, 1200 g of the water, onion, ginger, lemongrass, star anise, cinnamon, peppercorns, cloves, coriander seeds and fennel seeds into mixing bowl. Cook 60 min/100°C/speed , placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  2. Add remaining 200 g water, fish sauce, soy sauce, lime juice, salt and pepper and cook 30 min/100°C//speed , placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  3. Place noodles into a ceramic or glass bowl, cover with boiling water and leave to soak for 15 minutes.
  4. Strain beef stock in mixing bowl through simmering basket into a large jug. Discard contents in simmering basket.
  5. Drain rice noodles through simmering basket and divide evenly between 6 serving bowls.
  6. Place beef fillet slices on top of rice noodles, then pour over hot beef stock (the hot stock will cook the beef slightly).
  7. Garnish with spring onions/shallots, coriander, mint, basil, bean sprouts, chillies and lime cheeks and serve immediately.

Useful Items

ceramic or glass bowl, large bowl, large jug, 6 serving bowls, colander

Hints & Tricks

  • If you cannot buy beef bones pre-cut into pieces please ask your butcher to cut your beef bones into pieces for you. We do not recommend you do this yourself at home.
