Cobb Salad

Ingredients

  • 4 oz bacon, cut into pieces (¼ in.)
  • 35 oz water
  • 4 large eggs
  • 16 oz chicken breast fillet, boneless skinless, cubed (½ in.)
  • ½ tsp salt, divided
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper, divided
  • 3 oz extra virgin olive oil
  • ½ oz shallots
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 2 oz red wine vinegar
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce head, to serve, torn into pieces (approx. 1 lg. head)
  • 1 romaine lettuce head, to serve, torn into pieces (approx. 1 lg. head)
  • 2 tomatoes, cored, seeded and diced (¼ in.), (approx. 9 oz)
  • 2 avocados, diced (½ in.), (approx. 12 oz)
  • blue cheese, crumbled, to serve (approx. 4 oz)
  • fresh chives, to serve, sliced (¼ in.), (approx. ½ oz)

Preparation

  1. Place bacon into mixing bowl and without measuring cup cook 15 min/250°F//speed . Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and without measuring cup cook a further 10 min/250°F//speed . Drain bacon through simmering basket and discard fat. Transfer bacon to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place water into mixing bowl, insert simmering basket and add eggs. Set Varoma into position and weigh in chicken. Sprinkle with ¼ tsp salt and ¼ tsp pepper and toss through, secure Varoma lid and steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  3. Remove Varoma, transfer chicken to a bowl and allow to cool. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and place under cold running water to stop the cooking process. Peel and dice eggs. Set aside diced eggs in a separate bowl.
  4. Place a pitcher onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in oil. Set aside.
  5. Place shallots and garlic into mixing bowl, insert measuring cup and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  6. Add vinegar, lemon juice, mustard, Worcestershire sauce, ¼ tsp salt and ¼ tsp pepper and mix 2 min/speed 4, while slowly pouring reserved oil onto mixing bowl lid, letting it drizzle around measuring cup in a thin stream onto rotating blades to create an emulsion.
  7. Spread lettuce evenly over serving platter. Arrange toppings in rows. Start with a row of chicken then tomatoes, reserved bacon, diced avocado and reserved diced eggs. Add cheese, garnish with chives and drizzle with reserved dressing. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

pitcher, serving platter

Hints & Tricks

  • Make sure steam can circulate when placing chicken in the Varoma. Ensure some holes remain unobstructed for steam to circulate and allow for even cooking.
