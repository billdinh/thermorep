Steamed chocolate and prune puddings

Ingredients

  • 50 g dark chocolate, broken into pieces
  • 250 g pitted prunes
  • 1150 g water
  • 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 125 g butter, softened
  • 200 g brown sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 200 g self-raising flour
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • ¼ tsp ground cardamom
  • ¼ tsp ground cloves
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
779 kJ / 185 kcal
Protein
3 g
Carbohydrates
27 g
Fat
7.5 g

Preparation

  1. Place chocolate into mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place prunes into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add 150 g of the water and bicarbonate of soda and cook 2 min/100°C/speed 1. Transfer into bowl with the chocolate and set aside.
  4. Place butter and sugar into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add eggs, self-raising flour, cinnamon, cardamom, cloves and reserved chocolate-prune mixture and mix 5 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then repeat mixing 5 sec/speed 4.
  6. Transfer batter into 18 silicone cupcake moulds and fill so they are ¾ full. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  7. Place remaining 1000 g of the water into mixing bowl, then place Varoma into position.
  8. Arrange 9 of the filled silicone moulds into Varoma dish and onto Varoma tray. Set remaining 9 silicone moulds aside.
  9. Place a piece of wet and well-wrung baking paper, then a piece of paper towel, on top of the puddings in Varoma dish and tray. Secure Varoma lid and steam 18-20 min/Varoma/speed 1, or until puddings are cooked. Remove Varoma and place puddings into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and cover to keep warm.
  10. Place Varoma back into position and repeat with remaining 9 silicone moulds, covering again with a piece of wet and well-wrung baking paper, followed by a piece of paper towel. Secure Varoma lid, then repeat steaming 18-20 min/Varoma/speed 1, or until puddings are cooked.
  11. Serve puddings warm.

Useful Items

18 silicone cupcake moulds, thermal serving bowl or other large bowl, baking paper, paper towel, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Baked chocolate and prune pudding: In step 6, transfer batter into a round baking dish (1300 mL capacity). Bake in the oven for 30 minutes at 160°C, then cover with aluminium foil and bake for a further 30-45 minutes (160°C) or until a wooden skewer comes out clean.
  • Serve warm puddings with Brandy cream or Brandy butter. For a decadent chocolate dessert make a Chocolate ganache to drizzle over the top (see The Basic Cookbook or Everyday Cookbook).
  • Puddings can be made up to 1-2 weeks prior to Christmas, then frozen. Remove puddings from freezer 1 hour before serving. Place 500 g water into mixing bowl and steam 5 min/Varoma/speed 1 to heat puddings to serve.
