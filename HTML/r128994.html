Chocolate Soufflé with Orange

Ingredients

  • 10 - 15 g butter, for greasing
  • 100 g caster sugar, plus extra for dusting
  • 150 g dark chocolate, small pieces or callets
  • 4 medium eggs, seperated
  • 200 g double cream
  • 15 g cornflour
  • 10 g cocoa powder
  • ¾ - 1 Tbsp orange peel, finely grated, to taste
  • 1 Tbsp icing sugar, for garnish (optional)

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Grease then coat insides of 8 ramekins (Ø 6 cm) with sugar then place on a baking tray.
  2. Place chocolate in mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place egg yolks, cream, cornflour, cocoa and orange peel in mixing bowl then cook 7 min/80°C/speed 4.
  4. Add reserved grated chocolate and mix 15 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a large bowl and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.
  5. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in sugar. Remove bowl then set aside.
  6. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg whites and a pinch of sugar in mixing bowl then, without measuring cup, whisk 4 min/speed 3.5. After 1 minute 30 seconds, begin to very gradually add reserved sugar through hole in mixing bowl lid over the remaining time.  Remove butterfly whisk. Gently fold egg whites into chocolate cream.
  7. Transfer to prepared ramekins on tray and bake for 18 minutes (180°C). Sprinkle with icing sugar (if using) and serve immediately.

Useful Items

8 ramekins (Ø 6 cm), baking tray, large bowl, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Chocolate mixture may appear curdled in step 3, but continue with recipe as it will mix together when grated chocolate is added in step 4.
  • Fold egg whites into chocolate mixture immediately to avoid egg whites deflating.

 

  • Delicious served with orange sorbet.
