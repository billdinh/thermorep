Vegetable pasta soup and pork roll with vegetables, puddings, muffins

Ingredients

Berry muffins and steamed raspberry puddings

  • 4 tsp raspberry jam (40 g)
  • 2 eggs
  • 200 g sugar
  • 60 g butter, cut into pieces
  • 200 g full cream milk
  • ½ tsp natural vanilla extract
  • 200 g plain flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 200 g fresh raspberries
  • 150 g fresh blueberries

Pork roll

  • 500 - 600 g boneless pork shoulder, in one piece, butterflied to approx. 1.5 cm thick
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 4 - 5 slices bacon
  • 20 g sun-dried tomatoes
  • 30 g Parmesan cheese crust removed and cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 5 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 1 eggs

Mediterranean vegetables, Vegetable pasta soup

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 300 g onions, 100 g cut in slices and 200 g cut into pieces
  • 130 g carrots, peeled, cut into slices (5 mm)
  • 70 - 80 g mixed vegetables (e.g. red or green capsicuns, celery), cut into pieces
  • 30 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 50 g white wine (optional)
  • 1 ½ tsp salt
  • 1 heaped tsp Vegetable stock paste (refer to the recipe in The Basic Cookbook)
  • 1000 g water
  • 250 g potatoes, cut into slices (5 mm)
  • 160 g zucchini, cut into slices (1 cm)
  • 150 g ripe tomatoes, cut into thin slices (1 cm)
  • ½ tsp dried oregano
  • 60 g short dried pasta (e.g. elbow macaroni), for soup

Preparation

Berry muffins and steamed raspberry puddings

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Line a 12-hole muffin tray with paper muffin cases and set aside. Distribute jam among 4 individual silicone or aluminium muffin moulds (7 cm diameter, 4 cm height, see Tips) and set aside.
  2. Place eggs, sugar, butter, milk, vanilla extract, flour and baking powder into mixing bowl and mix 30 sec/speed 4. Using spatula, scrape down and stir in any remaining flour on sides of mixing bowl. Pour enough batter over jam in the 4 individual moulds to come up to 5 mm below the rims and set aside on Varoma tray.
  3. Distribute remaining batter between prepared paper muffin cases, filling to 1 cm from the top (yields 7-9 muffins), and arrange raspberries and blueberries over the muffins. Bake for 30 minutes (180°C) (see Tips). While baking, continue with step 4.

Pork roll

  1. Place butterflied meat on a sheet of or baking paper (30 cm x 50 cm), sprinkle with salt and pepper and cover with bacon slices.
  2. Place sun-dried tomatoes, Parmesan cheese and parsley in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add egg and mix 10 sec/speed 3. Spread mixture evenly over bacon slices and roll up the meat, wrapping it tightly with the baking paper. Place pork roll into Varoma dish and set aside.

Mediterranean vegetables, Vegetable pasta soup

  1. Place garlic clove, 200 g onion pieces, 50 g carrots, mixed vegetables and olive oil into mixing bowl, chop 2 sec/speed 5, then sauté 5 min/120°C/speed 1.
  2. Add white wine (optional) and reduce 1 min/Varoma/speed 1, without measuring cup to help alcohol evaporate.
  3. Add 1 tsp salt, vegetable stock paste and water. Insert simmering basket and weigh in potatoes, remaining 80 g carrots, 100 g onion slices, zucchini and tomatoes. Season with oregano and ½ tsp salt, then place Varoma dish into position. Insert Varoma tray with puddings, secure Varoma lid and steam 40 min/Varoma/speed 1. Set covered Varoma aside on a plate to keep puddings and pork roll warm and remove simmering basket with aid of spatula. Transfer vegetables from simmering basket to a serving dish, cover to keep warm and set aside.
  4. Add pasta to mixing bowl and cook time indicated on packet/100°C//speed 1. Transfer soup to a serving bowl.

To serve

  1. Serve soup as a starter. After serving soup, remove baking paper from meat roll, cut roll into slices, arrange on serving dish with vegetables and serve as the main course. Turn out steamed puddings onto plates and serve for dessert. Serve muffins for breakfast or afternoon tea the following day.

Useful Items

paper muffin cases, 12-hole muffin tray, baking paper, silicone or aluminium muffin moulds, serving dish, serving bowl, serving plates, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • If cooking an all-in-one three-course meal isn’t enough of a culinary challenge, why not make some delicious muffins for breakfast while you’re at it? Tasty pasta and vegetable soup, a pork roll filled with flavourful bacon and sun-dried tomato stuffing, served with Mediterranean vegetables, followed by delightful steamed raspberry puddings (when baked instead of steamed, the batter produces completely different but equally wonderful berry muffins). Thermomix® makes it all possible - and easy!
  • To make your own jam, refer to the recipe in The Basic Cookbook.
    - To save time, ask your butcher to butterfly your pork for you.
    - For the puddings, use any heat-resistant individual muffin or pudding moulds that fit 4 on Varoma tray.
    - After baking, allow muffins to cool for 5 minutes in the tin, then transfer to a cooling rack. Once at room temperature, sprinkle with icing sugar, store in a sealable container and serve within 24 hours.
    - For a special treat, serve a spoonful of whipped cream or crème fraîche with the steamed puddings.
    - If you have room in your refrigerator, you can prepare this recipe the night before. Proceed until step 9, stopping just before steaming. Place prepared Varoma, mixing bowl and simmering basket in the refrigerator. When ready to finish, continue recipe from steaming in step 9.
  • Use different berries or small pieces of mango or banana for the muffins.
    - Use other jams or marmalades in the steamed puddings instead of raspberry jam.
