Mini Iced Fingers

Ingredients

Dough

  • 200 g milk
  • 30 g caster sugar
  • 3 tsp dried instant yeast
  • 30 g unsalted butter, plus extra for greasing
  • 400 g strong white bread flour
  • 50 g condensed milk
  • 1 egg yolk, from large egg
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt

Icing

  • 200 g icing sugar
  • 3 - 4 Tbsp water, hot
  • food colouring pastes, as desired

Preparation

Dough

  1. Place milk, sugar and yeast in mixing bowl then warm 3 min/37°C/speed 1. Meanwhile, grease a large bowl.
  2. Add flour, condensed milk, butter, egg yolk and salt then mix 10 sec/speed 7. Knead 7 min/. Transfer to prepared bowl, cover with cling film and leave to rise in a warm place until doubled in size (approx. 1 hour). Meanwhile, line 2 large baking trays with baking paper.
  3. Once dough has doubled in size, tip out onto a clean surface and knock back. Divide into 36 pieces (approx. 20 g each), shape into fingers (approx. 7 cm long) and place on prepared baking trays, 2 cm apart. Cover loosely with greased cling film and leave to rise in a warm place until doubled in size (approx. 30 minutes).
  4. Towards the end of proving time, preheat oven to 160°C.
  5. Bake for 8-10 minutes (160°C) until lightly golden. Transfer to wire racks and allow to cool.

Icing

  1. Once buns have cooled, place a bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in icing sugar. Remove bowl and add water then mix well (see tip). Colour icing as desired and use to decorate buns.
  2. Allow to set before serving - best eaten on the day they are made.

Useful Items

2 large baking trays, baking paper, cling film, large bowl, wire racks, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Icing should be runny but thick enough to stay on finger roll without running over sides. If icing is too runny, add a little extra icing sugar, if too thick, add a little more water.
