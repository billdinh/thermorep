Vegan 'Beefy' Burger

Ingredients

  • 6 oz walnut halves
  • 8 oz portobello mushrooms, stems and gills removed, in pieces
  • 3 oz vegan margarine
  • 13 oz cooked brown rice (see Tip)
  • 3 oz old fashioned rolled oats
  • 1 tbsp red beets, grated
  • 1 oz vegan mayonnaise, plus extra to serve
  • 1 tbsp tamari sauce
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • vegetable oil, to fry
  • 8 hamburger buns, to serve
  • mustard, to serve
  • lettuce, to serve
  • tomatoes, sliced, to serve
  • red onions, sliced, to serve

Preparation

  1. Line a baking sheet with parchment and set aside.
  2. Place walnuts into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place mushrooms and vegan margarine into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  4. Add cooked brown rice (see Tip), rolled oats, grated beets, vegan mayonnaise, tamari, salt, garlic powder, pepper and reserved chopped walnuts and mix 10 sec//speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix a further 10 sec//speed 4. Divide mixture into 8 patties (approx. 4 oz ea.). Place on prepared baking sheet and refrigerate 1 hour.
  5. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Pan fry burgers in batches (approx. 3-4 min per side). Transfer to paper towels to drain.
  6. To serve, build burgers on buns to taste, with vegan mayonnaise, mustard, lettuce, sliced tomatoes and sliced onions.

Useful Items

baking sheet, paper towels, skillet or frying pan, parchment paper, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Cook long grain brown rice before starting recipe. Place 32 oz water into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket and weigh in 7 oz brown rice. Remove simmering basket with spatula and rinse rice under cold running water. Place simmering basket with rice into mixing bowl and rinse 5 sec/speed 5 then cook 50 min/212°F/speed 4. Set aside and start recipe.
