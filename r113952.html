Cinnamon and Date Twist

Ingredients

Dough:

  • 1 oz sugar
  • 1 oz unsalted butter, softened
  • 9 oz all-purpose flour, plus extra to dust
  • 2 ½ oz whole milk
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 large egg

Filling:

  • 2 oz unsalted butter, softened
  • 3 ½ oz pitted dates
  • 3 oz light brown sugar
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon

Preparation

Dough:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Place sugar into mixing bowl and grind 5 sec/speed 9.
  3. Add butter, 9 oz flour, milk, baking powder, and egg into mixing bowl and mix 20 sec/speed 3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and knead 2 min/.
  4. Transfer dough to a floured work surface and roll dough into a rectangle (approx. 14 in x 8 in x ¼ in).

Filling:

  1. Spread butter over dough with spatula.
  2. Place dates, brown sugar and cinnamon into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 5. Sprinkle date mixture over buttered dough. Cut dough in half lengthways and roll each piece into a long, tight, log shape. Transfer to prepared baking sheet.
  3. Twist the 2 log shapes together, keeping seams facing upwards, then pinch together at the ends.
  4. Bake for 25-30 minutes (375° F). Allow to cool for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack. Cut into slices and serve while still warm.

Useful Items

baking sheet and parchment paper, rolling pin, wire rack, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • To rehydrate and soften hard dried dates: place dates in a bowl and pour boiling water over to cover (approx 1 in.). Cover the bowl and let dates soak until they plump up - time needed will depend on the dryness of your dates.
