Koeksisters

Ingredients

Syrup

  • 600 g water
  • 800 g white sugar
  • ½ tsp cream of tartar
  • 3 cm fresh ginger, peeled and cut into slices
  • 2 slices lemon, (5 mm thick)
  • 3 cinnamon quills

Dough

  • 500 g plain flour
  • 20 g baking powder
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 60 g butter, cut into pieces
  • 2 eggs
  • 250 g full cream milk
  • 1500 g vegetable oil, for frying

Preparation

Syrup

  1. Place all syrup ingredients into mixing bowl and cook 15 min/100°C//speed 2. Cook for a further 3 min/100°C//speed 2, without measuring cup. Transfer into a bowl, cover and place into refrigerator until ready to use (see Tips). Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Dough

  1. Place flour, baking powder, salt and butter into mixing bowl and mix 5 sec/speed 5.
  2. Add eggs and milk and mix 8 sec/speed 7, then knead 2 min/. Transfer dough onto a lightly floured silicone bread mat or plastic wrap and shape into a ball. Wrap and place into refrigerater to rest for 30 minutes.
  3. Using silicone bread mat or floured work surface, roll out dough into a rectangle (35 cm wide x 25 cm long, approx. 1 cm thick). Cut rectangle vertically into 4 cm wide strips, then cut each strip horizontally into 7 cm lengths (creating approx. 30 small rectangles in total). Leaving a 1½ cm edge at top end of each piece, make 2 vertical cuts to form 3 even strips. Plait strips, securing ends together by pinching tightly. Place onto a plate and cover with a kitchen towel.
  4. Place a large saucepan over medium heat, add oil and heat oil to 170°C. To test temperature of the oil, use a candy thermometer or drop a small piece of dough into oil; the oil is at 170°C when dough rises to the surface immediately and bubbles gently form around dough.
  5. Remove cold syrup from refrigerator. Carefully drop 4-6 koeksisters into hot oil and fry for 3-5 minutes each side or until golden brown. Carefully remove koeksisters using a large slotted spoon and immediately dip into cold syrup for 30 seconds. Transfer koeksisters to a serving dish and set aside. Repeat frying and dipping with remaining koeksisters. Serve immediately. 

Useful Items

bowl, plastic wrap, silicone bread mat, kitchen towel, rolling pin, thermometer, large saucepan, serving dish, slotted spoon, refrigerator, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • The syrup should be very cold when using in step 7. You may want to make the syrup several hours before or overnight, or place into the freezer for a short time before using.
