Chicken cacciatore

Ingredients

  • 1 brown onion (approx. 180 g), cut into halves
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 2 sprigs fresh basil, leaves only
  • 2 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 40 g olive oil
  • 6 - 8 skinless chicken thigh (depending on size)
  • 400 g canned crushed tomatoes
  • 100 g dry white wine
  • 1 tbsp vegetable stock paste
  • 1 tsp dark brown sugar (optional)
  • 2 pinches salt, to taste
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1 red capsicum (approx. 220 g), deseeded, cut into slices
  • 100 g fresh mushrooms, cut into slices
  • 80 g pitted black olives (Kalamata or Spanish)

Preparation

  1. Place brown onion, garlic clove, fresh basil and fresh parsley into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add olive oil and sauté 5 min/120°C/speed 1.
  3. Add chicken thighs to mixing bowl and cook 5 min/120°C//speed 1.
  4. Add crushed tomatoes, white wine, vegetable stock paste, dark brown sugar (optional), salt and black pepper and cook 15 min/100°C//speed 1, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  5. Add red capsicum, fresh mushrooms and black olives and mix well with spatula. Cook for a further 15 min/100°C//speed , placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  6. Allow to rest 5 minutes before serving.
