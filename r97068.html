Sticky Apple Pork Ribs

Ingredients

  • 400 g apple juice
  • 100 g light brown sugar
  • 25 g fresh root ginger, peeled, cut in round slices (2 mm)
  • 20 g lime juice
  • 2 tsp fine sea salt
  • 2 tsp cider vinegar
  • ¼ tsp ground cardamom
  • ½ tsp black peppercorns
  • ½ tsp dried fennel seeds
  • ½ tsp dried chilli flakes
  • ½ tsp mustard powder
  • 1 Tbsp apple compote
  • 2 kaffir lime leaves
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 2 star anise
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1200 g pork spare ribs

Preparation

  1. Line a large roasting tin with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place all ingredients except pork ribs in mixing bowl. Replace measuring cup with simmering basket and cook 25 min/Varoma/speed 2. Strain sauce through simmering basket into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  4. Place pork ribs in a single layer in lined roasting tin and pour over reserved sauce. Cover with foil and set aside to marinate for 20 minutes.
  5. Cook for 1 hour 15 minutes (180°C). Towards the end of this time, light barbecue if using.
  6. Remove foil and continue to cook in oven or finish cooking on a barbeque for 15-20 minutes until sticky and golden brown. Serve warm.

Useful Items

roasting tin, baking paper, medium mesh sieve, aluminium foil, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • A great recipe for when you have guests over as the ribs can be cooking in the oven in advance and then finished off on the barbecue just before you are ready to sit down to eat.
