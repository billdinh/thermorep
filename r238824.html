Chicken and Pineapple Wraps

Ingredients

  • 28 oz water
  • 1 tsp chicken stock paste
  • 2 tsp salt, divided
  • 1 pinch ground pepper
  • 24 oz boneless, skinless chicken breast, cubed (approx. ½ in.)
  • 8 oz carrots, cut into pieces (approx. 1 in.)
  • 3 ½ oz roasted unsalted almonds
  • 2 limes (approx. 0.5 oz ea.) thin peels only, no pith, plus juice (2 oz total)
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • ½ tsp ground coriander
  • 3 ½ oz mayonnaise
  • 2 oz plain yogurt
  • 8 oz fresh pineapple, diced small (see Tip)
  • ½ oz fresh chives, chopped fine
  • 10 whole wheat tortillas (10 in.)
  • 10 oz iceberg lettuce, shredded

Preparation

  1. Place water, stock paste, 1 tsp salt and pepper into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket, weigh in chicken and cook 10 min/250°F/speed 4. Stir chicken to ensure even cooking and cook a further 10 min/250°F/speed 4. Set chicken and stock aside in separate bowls. Allow to cool to room temperature (approx. 40 min.).
  2. Place reserved chicken into mixing bowl and shred 4 sec//speed 4. Transfer to a large bowl and set aside.
  3. Place carrots and almonds into mixing bowl and chop 8 sec/speed 5. Transfer to bowl with reserved chicken and set aside.
  4. Peel and juice limes, set juice aside. Place lime peels, garlic, turmeric, coriander and 1 tsp salt into mixing bowl and zest 1 min/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add mayonnaise, 2 oz reserved lime juice, yogurt and 2 oz of reserved stock and mix 15 sec/speed 3.
  6. Add pineapple and chives and mix 2 sec//speed 2. Transfer to bowl with reserved carrot, almonds and chicken and mix through with spatula.
  7. Place chicken and pineapple mixture (approx. 4.5 oz) on the center of each tortilla, add lettuce, roll and cut in half. Serve immediately.

Hints & Tricks

  • Fresh pineapple pieces can be replaced with drained canned pineapple pieces/tidbits or crushed (approx. 14 oz can).
