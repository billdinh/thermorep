Pistachio Chocolate Truffles

Ingredients

  • 50 g pistachio nuts, unsalted, shelled
  • 880 g dark chocolate (70% cocoa solids) (includes 400 g for dipping), small pieces or callets
  • 160 g unsalted butter, soft
  • 30 g icing sugar
  • 80 g pistachio paste
  • cocoa powder, for dusting
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
255 kJ / 61 kcal
Protein
0.9 g
Carbohydrates
2.3 g
Fat
5.3 g

Preparation

  1. Line 2 baking trays with greaseproof paper and place in fridge to chill.
  2. Preheat oven to 180°C. Place pistachios on a baking tray and roast for 5-7 minutes (180°C). Leave to cool then place roasted pistachios in mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place 280 g chocolate in mixing bowl and grate 4 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then melt 5-7 min/40°C/speed 1 until smooth and there are no lumps. Transfer to a bowl and keep warm. Clean mixing bowl.
  4. Place butter and icing sugar in mixing bowl then blend 20 sec/speed 5. Insert butterfly whisk and whip 1 min/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and whip again 1 min/speed 4. Remove butterfly whisk. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add pistachio paste and mix speed 1 -4 while slowly adding melted chocolate through hole in mixing bowl lid, gradually increasing to speed 4.
  6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then add reserved chopped pistachios and mix 5 sec/speed 4. Transfer mixture to a piping bag fitted with a 0.5 cm plain nozzle. Pipe truffles in 1.5-2 cm dollops onto chilled trays. Return to fridge and leave to set for 2-3 hours. Clean mixing bowl.
  7. When truffles are completely set, place 600 g chocolate in mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Grate again 6 sec/speed 9. Transfer 150 g grated chocolate to a small bowl and set aside. Melt remainder 3 min/50°C/speed 2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then melt again 3 min/50°C/speed 1. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then melt again 3 min/50°C/speed 1.
  8. Add reserved grated chocolate and mix 4 min/speed 2.
  9. Line another baking tray with greaseproof paper. Using two forks, dip chilled truffles in tempered chocolate, shake off as much as possible then roll in cocoa powder and place on baking tray. You must work as quickly as possible to prevent tempered chocolate from cooling too fast. Once all chocolates have been dipped, set aside at room temperature to set completely. Serve, or keep in an airtight container for up to 3 weeks in a cool, dry, dark cupboard (do not refrigerate).

Useful Items

2 baking trays, greaseproof paper, baking tray, piping bag and nozzle, baking tray and paper, refrigerator, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • The truffles keep for up to three weeks in an airtight container in a cool, dark, dry cupboard - do not refrigerate or store near heating. The size of the truffles is entirely up to you, so the total quantity may vary. Place 8–10 truffles in a clear cellophane bag and tie with colourful ribbon for a homemade edible gift.
  • By the time the melted chocolate is added in step 5, its temperature should have dropped to approx. 32-35°C.
