Yorkshire Pudding

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 150 g plain flour
  • 400 g milk
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 4 tsp lard
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
567 kJ / 136 kcal
Protein
5.3 g
Carbohydrates
17 g
Fat
5.2 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 230°C.
  2. Place eggs, flour, milk, salt and pepper in mixing bowl then mix 15 sec/speed 6. Leave to rest for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Place ½ tsp lard in 8 holes of a Yorkshire pudding or muffin tin then place tin in hot oven for 5 minutes (230°C).
  4. Carefully remove tin from oven then pour batter into holes in tin. Place tin in oven and bake for 15-20 minutes (230°C) until brown and well risen. Serve hot.

Useful Items

Yorkshire pudding tin or muffin tin, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • To make one large Yorkshire pudding rather than individual ones, pour into a large oven-proof dish and bake for 30-40 minutes (230°C) in step 4.
  • This light, fluffy Yorkshire favourite is a must with roast beef. In times of food shortages, it was served with gravy before the meat course to fill everyone up. It’s all whisked up in seconds in the Thermomix® and the batter can even be made up to 24 hours ahead and then set aside until ready to cook.
