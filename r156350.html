Marinated capsicum

Ingredients

  • 500 g water
  • 700 g mixed capsicums (e.g. red, green, yellow, orange)
  • 100 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 35 g white wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 3 pinches salt
Nutrition

Preparation

  1. Place water into mixing bowl and bring to a boil 5 min/Varoma/speed 1. Meanwhile, deseed capsicums and cut into strips. Cut larger strips into halves, if desired.
  2. Place Varoma dish into position, add capsicum, secure Varoma lid and steam 12-14 min/Varoma/speed 1 or until capsicum are tender but still have a slight crunch. Remove Varoma, transfer capsicum into a serving bowl or jar and empty mixing bowl.
  3. Place olive oil, vinegar, cumin and salt into mixing bowl and mix 20 sec/speed 6. Pour dressing over capsicum, combine thoroughly using spatula and allow to cool and marinate for a minimum of 30 minutes. Season to taste and serve as a side dish.

Useful Items

jar

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with fish or grilled meat.
    - Allow to marinate longer to intensify the flavour.
    - You can store the capsicums in a clean, sterilised glass jar in the refrigerator for up to 1 week.
  • Mediterranean style capsicum: After steaming the capsicum, add anchovies, capers, oregano and black olives (all to suit your taste) to the serving bowl.
