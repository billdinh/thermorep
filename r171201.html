Sausage Calzone with Sun-Dried Tomatoes

Ingredients

Dough

  • olive oil, to grease
  • semolina, to sprinkle
  • 15 oz warm water (100°F-110°F)
  • 16 oz bread flour
  • 6 oz semolina
  • 2 ¼ tsp rapid rise yeast
  • 1 tbsp salt

Filling

  • 2 oz Parmesan cheese, cut into 1 in. pieces
  • 2 oz Fontina cheese, cut into 1 in. pieces
  • 4 oz sun-dried tomatoes (in oil), no need to drain
  • 4 oz yellow onions, quartered
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 8 fresh basil leaves (approx. 3 stems)
  • 16 oz Italian sausage, casing removed, cut into 1 in. pieces
  • 1 oz pine nuts
  • 1 large egg, lightly beaten
  • 2 tsp dried oregano (optional)

Preparation

Dough

  1. Lightly grease a large bowl. Line baking sheets with parchment paper, lightly sprinkle with semolina and set aside.
  2. Place water, bread flour, semolina, yeast and salt into mixing bowl and knead 2 min/. Form dough into a ball and transfer into prepared bowl. Cover with cling wrap and let dough rise until doubled in size (approx. 1 hr.). Clean mixing bowl.

Filling

  1. Place Parmesan and Fontina cheeses into mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place sun-dried tomatoes, onions, garlic and basil into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add sausage and pine nuts and cook 8 min/250°F//speed 0.5. Preheat oven to 500°F.
  4. Divide dough into four balls and place two pieces of dough onto each prepared baking sheet. Evenly stretch out each dough into a circle (approx. 6 in.) with lightly floured fingers. Place a fourth of the reserved sausage mixture and grated cheese onto each of the dough rounds, off-centered leaving a ½ inch border. Fold over dough to make a half-circle and pinch to seal.
  5. Brush with egg and sprinkle tops of calzones with dried oregano. Bake for 10-15 minutes (500°F), until golden browned, rotating sheets halfway through baking. Serve warm.

Useful Items

pastry brush, parchment paper, cling wrap, large bowl, baking sheets, oven
