Soufflé omelette with hot smoked salmon

Ingredients

Kewpie mayonnaise

  • 250 g grapeseed oil
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 2 tsp apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tsp rice wine vinegar
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp wasabi paste

Omelette

  • 30 g kale leaves, stems and ribs removed
  • 3 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only (see Tips)
  • 50 g red onion
  • 6 eggs, separated
  • 1 lime, juice only
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 500 g water
  • 150 g hot smoked salmon, cut into pieces or flaked

Preparation

Kewpie mayonnaise

  1. Place a jug onto mixing bowl lid and weigh oil into it. Set jug aside.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg yolks, vinegars, salt and wasabi paste into mixing bowl and whisk 1 min 30 sec/speed 4, gradually pouring reserved oil onto mixing bowl lid and letting it trickle into mixing bowl to emulsify. Remove butterfly whisk. Transfer mayonnaise into a sealable container and place into refrigerator until ready to use (see Tips). Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Omelette

  1. Place kale, parsley and onion into mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 5. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  2. Line Varoma tray with a piece of wet, well-wrung baking paper, making sure paper goes all the way to the edges (to prevent egg from spilling). Set Varoma tray aside.
  3. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg whites, juice of 1 lime, salt and pepper into mixing bowl and whip 1 min 30 sec/speed 3, until light and airy.
  4. Add egg yolks and mix 20 sec/speed . Transfer onto prepared Varoma tray and set aside. Rinse mixing bowl.
  5. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and cook 6 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  6. Remove Varoma lid and top omelette with salmon and reserved kale mixture. Secure Varoma lid and cook 6 min/Varoma/speed 1 or until omelette is cooked to your liking. Transfer onto a serving plate, divide into 4 portions and top with 1 tablespoon of the mayonnaise per serve.

Useful Items

jug, serving plate, sealable container, baking paper, bowl, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • You can use a variety of greens in this recipe. Try replacing the kale with spinach, or the parsley with basil.
  • To save time, you can place both egg whites and yolks into mixing bowl together. However, the omelette will not be as fluffy.
  • Kewpie mayonnaise can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.
