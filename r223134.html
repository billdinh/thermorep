Chicken, leek and corn pie

Ingredients

  • 4 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 1 brown onion, peeled and cut into halves
  • 300 g leeks, white and light green parts only (approx. 2 leeks), cut into pieces
  • 50 g salted butter, cut into pieces
  • 80 g plain flour
  • 250 g water
  • 2 tsp Chicken stock paste (see Tips)
  • 400 g boneless skinless chicken thighs, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 50 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 300 g frozen corn kernels
  • salt, to season
  • ground black pepper, to season
  • 2 - 4 sheets puff pastry (see Tips)

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 170°C.
  2. Place parsley into mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place onion and leeks into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add butter and flour and cook 3 min/90°C//speed 1.
  5. Add water and stock paste and cook 1 min/90°C//speed 1.
  6. Add chicken, cream and corn. Stir to combine, then cook 10 min/100°C//speed 1.
  7. Add salt and pepper and reserved parsley and combine 10 sec//speed 1.
  8. Line a deep pie dish (24 cm) with 1-2 sheets of the puff pastry, then trim off excess. Spoon chicken mixture into dish and cover with remaining pastry sheets (trim off excess). Cut a large cross in the top of the pie to allow steam to escape and bake for 40-45 minutes (170°C) or until golden. Allow to cool slightly before serving.

Useful Items

bowl, pie dish (24 cm), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • To make your own Chicken stock paste and Puff pastry, please refer to The Basic Cookbook or the Everyday Cookbook.
  • You can replace the Puff pastry with Shortcrust pastry, if you prefer. You will need to blind bake Shortcrust pastry for approx. 10 minutes before filling with chicken mixture. To make your own shortcrust pastry, please refer to The Basic Cookbook or the Everyday Cookbook.
