Mulling Spices

Ingredients

Dried Citrus Peels

  • 4 oranges, thin peel only, no pith
  • 4 lemons, thin peel only, no pith

Mulling Spice

  • 3 oz cinnamon sticks (approx. 12 cinnamon sticks)
  • 3 oz allspice berries
  • 2 tbsp whole cloves
  • ¾ tsp vanilla extract
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
75 kJ / 18 kcal
Protein
0 g
Carbohydrates
5 g
Fat
0 g

Preparation

Dried Citrus Peels

  1. Preheat oven to 200°F. Transfer orange and lemon peels onto a baking sheet and bake for 30 minutes (200°F). Turn off oven and allow peels to cool in oven for 1 hour. Transfer to an airtight container until ready to use.

Mulling Spice

  1. Place reserved dried orange and lemon peels and cinnamon into mixing bowl and grind 3 sec/speed 10.
  2. Add allspice, cloves and vanilla extract and toast 4 min/212°F/speed 1.
  3. Place into airtight jars and label with cooking instructions (see Tip).

Useful Items

airtight jars, airtight container, baking sheet, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Stove top cooking instructions: place 32 oz unfiltered apple juice and 2 tbsp Mulling Spices into a saucepan, bring to a boil. Reduce to a low simmer and cook 15-20 minutes. Strain and serve into mugs.
  • Thermomix® cooking instructions: place 32 oz unfiltered apple juice and 2 tbsp Mulling Spices into mixing bowl and heat 10 min/212°F/speed 1, then heat 15 min/140°F/speed 1. Strain and serve into mugs.
