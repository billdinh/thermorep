Smoked Salmon and Zucchini Pasta

Ingredients

  • 35 ½ oz water
  • 1 ¼ tsp salt, divided
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 16 oz pasta, eg. penne or amori
  • 1 organic lemon, (approx. 5 oz) thin peel only (no pith) plus juice from ½ lemon (approx. 1 oz)
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 16 oz zucchini, cubed (1 in.)
  • 8 oz half and half
  • 8 oz smoked salmon, cubed
  • 20 sprigs fresh chives, snipped (¼ in.)
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
2351 kJ / 562 kcal

Preparation

  1. Place water, 1 tsp salt and oil into mixing bowl and bring to a boil 9 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  2. Add pasta and cook for time indicated on package/212°F//speed . Rinse pasta under cold running water, drain well using simmering basket and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.
  3. Place lemon peel, ¼ tsp salt and pepper into mixing bowl and zest 30 sec/speed 9.
  4. Add zucchini and chop 4 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add half and half and cook 4 min/195°F//speed 1.
  6. Add cooked pasta and cook 2 min/205°F//speed 1.
  7. Add salmon, chopped chives and juice from ½ lemon and combine using spatula. Serve immediately.
