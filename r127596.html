Apple crumble meringue slice

Ingredients

Shortcrust pastry

  • 200 g unsalted butter, chilled and cut into pieces, plus extra for greasing
  • 400 g plain flour
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract
  • 50 g icing sugar

Apple mixture

  • 1500 g apples, peeled, cores removed and cut into quarters (approx. 1800 g before preparation)
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon, plus extra for dusting
  • 40 g sugar

Meringue filling

  • 4 egg whites
  • 150 g icing sugar

Preparation

Shortcrust pastry

  1. Place butter, flour, egg yolks, baking powder, vanilla extract and icing sugar into mixing bowl and mix 30 sec/speed 5.
  2. Transfer dough onto a silicone mat or floured work surface and work into a ball. Divide pastry into 2 balls, the first ball weighing 500 g and any remaining dough forming the second. Wrap both balls of pastry in cling film, then place into the refrigerator for 30 minutes to rest.
  3. Preheat oven to 180°C. Grease and line a baking dish (32 x 20 x 5 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  4. Roll out the first 500 g ball of pastry onto a silicone mat or floured work surface to cover the baking dish. Place pastry into the base of baking dish and prick all over with a fork. Bake pastry for 8 minutes (180°C), then set aside to cool. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Apple mixture

  1. Place ½ of the apples into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place remaining apples into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  3. Add reserved chopped apples, cinnamon and sugar and cook 20 min/80°C/speed 2. Drain apples through simmering basket and discard juice. Transfer apple mixture into a bowl and set aside. Thoroughly clean and dry mixing bowl.

Meringue filling

  1. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg whites into mixing bowl and whip 4 min/37°C/speed 3.5.
  2. With butterfly whisk still in place, mix 4 min 30 sec/speed 3, slowly adding icing sugar through hole in mixing bowl lid, 1 spoon at a time, until just combined.
  3. To assemble, transfer apple mixture over cooked pastry base and dust with extra cinnamon.
  4. Top with meringue mixture, spreading evenly over slice. Crumble remaining shortcrust pastry ball into pieces and sprinkle over the top of meringue. Bake for 20-25 minutes (180°C), or until lightly golden brown. Remove from oven and allow to cool completely.
  5. Once cooled, cut into pieces and serve.

Useful Items

rectangular baking dish (32 cm x 20 cm x 5 cm), baking paper, rolling pin, bowl, cling film, silicone mat, refrigerator, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve Apple crumble meringue slice with vanilla ice cream on the side.
