Dukkah

Ingredients

  • 150 g sesame seeds
  • 1 tbsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tbsp coriander seeds
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp dried mint or dried thyme (optional)
  • 100 g toasted hazelnuts or toasted almonds
Nutrition

Preparation

  1. Place sesame seeds, cumin seeds and coriander seeds into mixing bowl and dry roast 8 min/120°C/speed 1, without measuring cup. Allow to cool (approx. 30 minutes).
  2. Once cooled, add salt, pepper and mint or thyme (optional) and chop Turbo/0.5 sec/1-2 times or until a fine yet even consistency is achieved. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place nuts into mixing bowl and chop Turbo/0.5 sec/3-4 times or until a chunky yet even consistency is achieved. Transfer into bowl with seeds and combine with spatula.
  4. Serve immediately or transfer into a sealable container and place into refrigerator until ready to use.

Useful Items

sealable container, bowl, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with sourdough bread and good quality extra virgin olive oil or use dukkah to coat chicken or lamb.
  • Add ½ tsp ground turmeric for added colour and flavour.
