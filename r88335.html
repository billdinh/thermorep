Smoked Haddock and Spinach Tart

Ingredients

Pastry

  • 200 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
  • 100 g butter, cold, diced
  • 50 g milk, cold

Filling

  • 50 g Cheddar cheese, cut in pieces
  • 300 - 350 g smoked haddock
  • 1300 g whole milk
  • 150 g onions, finely sliced (2 mm)
  • 10 g olive oil
  • 10 g unsalted butter
  • 50 g water
  • 300 g fresh spinach leaves

Cooking

  • 3 medium eggs, 1 egg separated
  • 100 g double cream
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper

Preparation

Pastry

  1. Place flour, salt and butter in mixing bowl then chop 10 sec/speed 8.
  2. Add milk and pulse Turbo/1 sec/2-4 times until just clinging together. Tip out, wrap in cling film and chill in fridge for 30-40 minutes.
  3. On a lightly floured surface, roll pastry out thinly (3 mm), then place pastry in a loose bottomed tart tin (Ø 25 cm). Chill in fridge for 20 minutes. Meanwhile preheat oven to 160°C.
  4. Line tart with baking paper and fill with baking beans. Bake for 15 minutes (160°C). Remove baking paper and beans and return to oven for 10 minutes (160°C). Meanwhile prepare filling.

Filling

  1. Place cheese in mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Insert simmering basket and weigh in haddock. Add milk, pouring it over to just cover fish, then poach 12 min/98°C/speed 1.
  3. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula then set aside. Strain milk through a sieve into a bowl and set aside to cool. Clean mixing bowl.
  4. Place onions, oil and butter in mixing bowl and sauté 8 min/120°C//speed 0.5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  5. Place water and spinach in mixing bowl then cook 3 min/100°C/speed 2, stirring with spatula through hole in mixing bowl lid.  Drain water from mixing bowl and discard, then chop spinach 1 sec/speed 5. Transfer spinach to a sieve and set aside to drain.

Cooking

  1. Lightly beat 1 egg white and brush all over part-baked pastry case then bake for further 5 minutes (160°C).
  2. Flake cooked haddock into pastry case. Add reserved cheese, onions and spinach and spread mixture out evenly.
  3. Place 100 g poaching milk, eggs and remaining egg yolk, cream, salt and pepper in mixing bowl then mix 7 sec/speed 5. Pour into tart case to just below tart rim.
  4. Bake in oven for 45-55 minutes (160°C) until golden and lightly set in the middle. Serve warm or cold with a dressed salad.

Useful Items

cling film, quiche or tart tin (Ø 25 cm), rolling pin, baking paper, baking beans, fine sieve, pastry brush, refrigerator, oven
