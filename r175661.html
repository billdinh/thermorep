Lemon Tiramisu

Ingredients

  • 2 lemons, unpeeled, quartered
  • 350 g water
  • 75 g limoncello
  • 210 g sugar
  • ½ lemon, zest only
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 egg yolk, from large egg
  • 500 g mascarpone
  • 300 g sponge finger biscuits
  • 1 Tbsp lemon zest, to garnish
Nutrition
per 1 portion

2374 kJ / 567 kcal

Preparation

  1. Place 2 lemons and water in mixing bowl. Hold measuring cup in place and mix 2 sec/speed 10. Insert simmering basket, hold in place with spatula then strain lemonade into a bowl. Clean mixing bowl.
  2. Place reserved lemonade, limoncello and 80 g sugar in mixing bowl then cook 10 min/100°C/speed 1. Transfer to a bowl and allow to cool for 30 minutes then refrigerate for |20 minutes. Meanwhile, clean and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Place 130 g sugar and zest of ½ lemon in mixing bowl then grind 10 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Insert butterfly whisk. Add eggs and egg yolk to mixing bowl then whip 4 min/speed 3.5. Remove butterfly whisk.
  5. Mix 2 min/speed 3 while adding mascarpone in spoonfuls through hole in mixing bowl lid onto rotating blades.
  6. Briefly soak sponge fingers in lemon syrup until they soak up just enough liquid without falling apart (approx. 3-4 seconds). Arrange half of the soaked sponge fingers in the bottom of a rectangular dish (30 cm x 20 cm x 6 cm). Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the sponge fingers base. Cover with remaining soaked sponge fingers and spread remaining mascarpone mixture on top. Cover with cling film and refrigerate for 5 hours.
  7. Garnish with lemon zest just before serving.

Useful Items

rectangular dish (30 cm x 20 cm x 6 cm), cling film, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Add an extra layer of flavour to this lemon tiramisu by garnishing with toasted flaked almonds or toasted flaked coconut.
