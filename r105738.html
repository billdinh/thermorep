Horchata

Ingredients

  • 160 g blanched almonds (see Tips)
  • 1000 g water
  • 100 g long grain white rice
  • 1 cinnamon quill
  • 50 g raw sugar
  • ice cubes, to serve
  • ground cinnamon, for dusting

Preparation

  1. Place a large bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh almonds and 500 g of the water into it. Set aside.
  2. Place rice and cinnamon quill into mixing bowl and mill 25 sec/speed 10. Transfer into bowl with almonds and cover with plastic wrap. Place into refrigerator for 10-12 hours. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Place sugar, remaining 500 g water and reserved soaked almonds into mixing bowl and blend 1 min/speed 6, until completely smooth.
  4. Line a fine-mesh sieve with two layers of muslin cloth or a nut milk bag and place over a jug. Transfer mixture into lined sieve and squeeze any remaining pulp to extract as much liquid as possible. Discard leftover pulp.
  5. Divide horchata between 4 glasses and add ice. Dust with ground cinnamon before serving immediately.

Useful Items

fine-mesh sieve, glasses, large bowl, plastic wrap, large jug, nut milk bag or 2 muslin cloths, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • You can substitute almonds with whole or slivered almonds which have already been blanched.
  • If not serving immediately, place into refrigerator. Slight separation may occur, so stir prior to serving.
