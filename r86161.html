Butternut Squash Macaroni Cheese

Ingredients

  • 200 g Cheddar cheese, cut in pieces
  • 800 g water
  • 400 g butternut squash, diced (1 cm)
  • 350 g macaroni pasta
  • 40 g plain flour
  • 40 g butter
  • 400 g milk
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt, or more to taste
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper, or more to taste
  • 2 pinches ground nutmeg

Preparation

  1. Place Cheddar in mixing bowl and grate 8 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place water in mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket and weigh in butternut squash. Steam 22 min/Varoma/speed 2. Remove simmering basket, discard steaming water then transfer squash to a large bowl and cover to keep warm. Rinse and dry mixing bowl.
  3. On hob, cook macaroni according to packet instructions then add to bowl with butternut squash. Meanwhile, preheat oven to 190°C and begin making cheese sauce.
  4. Avoiding blade, place flour, butter, 150 g reserved grated cheese, milk, salt, pepper and nutmeg in mixing bowl then cook 7 min/90°C/speed 3. Check and adjust seasoning to taste.
  5. Pour sauce over pasta and butternut squash and mix well to combine. Transfer to Thermo Server or ovenproof dish and sprinkle with remaining cheese then bake 20-25 minutes (190°C) until bubbling and brown on top. Allow to stand for 10 minutes before serving.

Useful Items

Thermo Server or ovenproof dish, saucepan, Thermo Server, oven, hob
