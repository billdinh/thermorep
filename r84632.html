Chocolate Ice Cream

Ingredients

  • 200 g dark chocolate, 70% cocoa solids, pieces or callets
  • 1 pinch instant coffee granules
  • 500 g milk
  • 6 egg yolks, from large eggs
  • 250 g single cream
  • 100 g caster sugar
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1189 kJ / 284 kcal
Protein
6.6 g
Carbohydrates
21.7 g
Fat
19 g

Preparation

  1. Place chocolate and coffee in mixing bowl then grind 10 sec/speed 10.
  2. Add milk and egg yolks then heat 4 min/80°C/speed 4.
  3. Add cream and sugar then heat 2 min/80°C/speed 4. Transfer to shallow freezer-proof box and leave to cool for 30 minutes. Refrigerate for 24 hours to allow mixture to mature then place in freezer until solid (6 hours or overnight).
  4. Place frozen mixture in refrigerator for 15 minutes, then cut in pieces (3 cm) with a long, sharp knife. Place half the frozen mixture pieces in mixing bowl and blend 30 sec/speed 9. Add remaining frozen mixture pieces and, while stirring with spatula through hole in mixing bowl lid and stopping to scrape down sides of mixing bowl as neccessary, churn for 1 min/speed 6 -9, increasing speed gradually.
  5. Insert butterfly whisk and mix 20 sec/speed 4. Serve immediately, for example in cones, as an accompaniment to a dessert or with chocolate sauce and fresh berries.

Useful Items

freezer-proof box, long sharp knife, refrigerator, freezer
