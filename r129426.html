Chilli Cheese Nachos

Ingredients

  • 150 g onions, quartered
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 40 g oil
  • 200 g beef mince, broken in pieces
  • 160 g tinned chopped tomatoes
  • 1 Tbsp tomato purée
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 dried bay leaf
  • 1 pinch ground turmeric
  • 1 pinch sweet paprika
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 pinch ground cloves
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper
  • 200 g tinned kidney beans, rinsed
  • 15 g dark chocolate (50% cocoa), in small pieces or callets
  • 200 g tortilla chips
  • 50 g pickled jalapeño chillies
  • 200 g Cheddar cheese, grated

Preparation

  1. Place onions, garlic and oil in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 5. Sauté 8 min/120°C//speed .
  2. Add mince and cook 4 min/120°C//speed .
  3. Preheat grill to 200°C.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes, tomato purée, sugar, salt, cumin, oregano, bay leaf, turmeric, paprika, clove and cayenne pepper. Insert simmering basket and weigh in kidney beans, then cook 8 min/100°C//speed . Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and set aside.
  5. Remove bay leaf. Add chocolate and stir 3 min/100°C//speed .
  6. Add cooked kidney beans to mixing bowl and mix well with spatula.
  7. Sprinkle tortilla chips over a baking tray, then pour chilli mixture over. Top with jalapeño chillies and grated cheese. Grill for 3-5 minutes (200°C) until cheese is melted. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

baking tray, barbecue grill rack, grill

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with crème fraîche, guacamole and/or salsa.
