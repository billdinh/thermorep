Mushroom pastry

Ingredients

  • 200 g puff pastry, rolled out (1-2 mm)
  • 50 g mozzarella
  • 10 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 400 g fresh mushrooms, cut into quarters
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 30 g port wine
  • 100 g cheese, in slices (e.g. Gruyère, Gouda etc.)

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C. Line a pie tray with removable bottom (21 x 31 cm approx.) with baking paper. Line prepared pie tray with pastry.
  2. Place mozzarella cheese in mixing bowl and grate 2 sec/speed 9. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place parsley in mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 7.
  4. Add olive oil and garlic, chop 2 sec/speed 8 and sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  5. Place 200 g mushrooms in mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 4.
  6. Add 200g mushrooms, salt and wine and cook 5 min/120°C/speed .
  7. Add cheese and cook 1 min/100°C/speed . Spread over pastry, sprinkle with grated reserved mozzarella and bake at 200°C for about 25 minutes. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

bowl, baking paper, loose-based rectangular tart tin (21 cm x 31 cm), oven
