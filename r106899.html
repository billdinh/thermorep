Citrus Margarita

Ingredients

  • water, to rim glasses
  • salt, to rim glasses
  • 5 oz tequila
  • 2 oz orange liqueur (e.g. Cointreau®)
  • 2 oz lemon, flesh only (approx. 1 lemon), peeled, no white pith, quartered and seeds removed
  • 1 oz lime, flesh only (approx. 1 lime), peeled, no white pith, quartered and seeds removed
  • 3 oz orange, flesh only (approx. ½ orange), peeled, no white pith, quartered and seeds removed
  • 1 - 2 tbsp simple syrup (see Tip)
  • 18 oz ice cubes

Preparation

  1. Place approximately 1 inch of water into a small bowl or dish. Working one glass at a time, place 6 margarita glasses upside down into the water to wet the rim. Spread salt onto a plate and dip glass rims into salt. Set glasses aside.
  2. Place all remaining ingredients, except ice cubes, into mixing bowl and mix 15 sec/speed 6.
  3. Add ice cubes and crush 1 min/speed 10. Pour margarita mixture into prepared glasses and serve.

Hints & Tricks

  • To make your own simple syrup place 9 oz water and 8 oz caster sugar into mixing bowl and cook 5 min/212°F/speed 1. Transfer into a jar and place in refrigerator to cool completely (approx. 15 min) before using. Leftover syrup can be stored in the refrigerator in a sealed glass jar and used in other cocktails.
  • If you don't have margarita glasses, any glasses will do.
