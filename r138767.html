Vietnamese mixed meat pho

Ingredients

  • 450 g beef bones, pre-cut into pieces (see Tips)
  • 1400 g water
  • 1 brown onion (approx. 120 g), cut into halves
  • 6 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled and cut into slices
  • 1 stalk fresh lemongrass, white part only, cut into halves
  • 2 star anise
  • 1 cinnamon quill
  • 1 tsp mixed peppercorns
  • 3 whole cloves
  • 1 tsp coriander seeds
  • 1 tsp dried fennel seeds
  • 60 g fish sauce
  • 50 g soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp lime juice (approx. 1 lime)
  • ¼ tsp sea salt, to taste
  • 2 pinches ground white pepper, to taste
  • 300 - 350 g flat rice stick noodles (approx. 6 mm wide)
  • boiling water, for soaking
  • 100 g beef fillet, partially frozen, cut into thin slices (approx. 3 mm thick)
  • 100 g pork fillet, partially frozen, cut into thin slices (approx. 3 mm thick)
  • 100 g skinless chicken breast fillet, partially frozen, cut into thin slices (approx. 3 mm thick)
  • 2 - 3 spring onions/shallots, trimmed and cut into thin slices on the diagonal, to serve
  • 8 - 10 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only, to serve
  • 6 - 8 sprigs fresh mint, leaves only, to serve
  • 2 sprigs fresh Thai basil, leaves only, to serve
  • 100 g bean sprouts, to serve
  • 1 - 2 long red chillies, deseeded if preferred and cut into thin slices, to serve
  • 1 - 2 limes, cut into wedges, to serve

Preparation

  1. Place beef bones, 1200 g of the water, onion, ginger, lemongrass, star anise, cinnamon, peppercorns, cloves, coriander seeds and fennel seeds into mixing bowl and cook 60 min/100°C/speed , placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  2. Add fish sauce, soy sauce, lime juice, salt, pepper and remaining 200 g water and cook 30 min/100°C//speed , placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  3. Place a ceramic or glass bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh noodles into it. Cover with boiling water and set aside to soak for 15 minutes.
  4. Using simmering basket, strain beef stock into a large jug. Discard contents in simmering basket (see Tips). Clean and dry mixing bowl and simmering basket.
  5. Place stock into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket and weigh beef, pork and chicken into it, then cook 1-2 min/100°C/speed 1 or until just cooked.
  6. Drain noodles and divide evenly between 6 serving bowls. Place meat on top of rice noodles, then pour over reserved hot beef stock. Garnish with spring onions/shallots, coriander, mint, Thai basil, bean sprouts, chillies and lime cheeks before serving.

Useful Items

ceramic or glass bowl, glass bowl or large jug, serving bowls

Hints & Tricks

  • If you cannot buy beef bones pre-cut into pieces, ask your butcher to cut them into pieces for you. We do not recommend you do this yourself at home.
  • After step 4, if your stock is thick and grainy, you can strain stock through a fine-mesh sieve lined with 2 layers of paper towel for a smoother stock.
  • To save time cook the stock the day before.
