Apricot liqueur

Ingredients

  • 700 g apricot juice
  • 100 g sugar
  • 50 g almond liqueur (e.g. Amaretto)
  • 300 g white rum

Preparation

  1. Place apricot nectar and sugar in mixing bowl, place simmering basket on mixing bowl lid as a splash guard instead of measuring cup and bring to boil 15 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  2. Keep simmering basket inserted and allow to cool to 70°C 5 min 30 sec/speed 3.
  3. Add almond liqueur and rum, and stir 15 sec/speed 4. Transfer to 2 clean bottles (0,5 l each) while still hot (see tip). Allow to cool and store in a cool, dry place (see tip).

Useful Items

bottles (500 ml)

Hints & Tricks

  • To ensure the liqueur keeps for as long as possible, wash the bottles thoroughly and dry in a warm oven or wash in the dishwasher. Transfer the liqueur to the bottles while they are still warm.
  • Unopened, the apricot liqueur can keep up to 6 months if stored in a cool, dry place.
  • Use the liqueur to prepare a fruity sparkling wine cocktail. Serve ⅓ apricot liqueur and ⅔ sparkling wine in a champagne glass.
