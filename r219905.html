Chimichurri Steak Pita Sandwich

Ingredients

Chimichurri Sauce

  • 3 ½ oz extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 oz fresh parsley, leaves and tender stems
  • 10 fresh basil leaves
  • 10 sprigs fresh oregano, leaves only
  • 1 oz shallots, quartered
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 fresh Fresno chili, stem and seeds removed
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 3 - 4 tsp red wine vinegar

Steak

  • 24 oz flank steak
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ tsp salt, to taste
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper, to taste
  • 4 pita breads, whole
  • 32 arugula leaves, to taste (optional)
  • 4 heirloom tomatoes, sliced (¼ in.)

Preparation

Chimichurri Sauce

  1. Place a pitcher on top of mixing bowl lid, weigh olive oil into pitcher and set aside.
  2. Place parsley, basil, oregano, shallots, garlic, Fresno chili, salt and pepper into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and chop a further 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add vinegar and mix 3 min/speed 4 while slowly pouring olive oil onto mixing bowl lid, letting it drizzle around measuring cup in a thin stream onto rotating blades to create an emulsion. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.

Steak

  1. Preheat grill. Rub flank steak with olive oil, salt and pepper. Grill steak to medium rare, approximately 3-4 minutes each side or to desired doneness. Cover with foil and let rest 10 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile lightly grill pita breads and then cut in half.
  3. Slice steak thinly on the bias and drizzle Chimichurri Sauce over steak. Fill pitas with arugula, tomato slices and steak. Serve with additional Chimichurri Sauce.

Useful Items

pitcher, bbq grill, bread knife, aluminum foil, grill

Hints & Tricks

  • Double the Chimichurri Sauce recipe and use half to marinate the flank steak for 30-60 minutes.
  • Serve Chimichurri Sauce with grilled meat, including chicken, pork chops, sausages and hamburgers.
