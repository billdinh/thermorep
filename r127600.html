Layered chocolate orange slice

Ingredients

Orange chocolate brownie slice

  • 160 g unsalted butter, plus extra for greasing
  • 70 g chocolate, orange flavoured, broken into pieces
  • 120 g dark chocolate, 70% cocoa solids, broken into pieces
  • 300 g sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract
  • ½ tsp fine salt
  • 120 g plain flour

Orange curd

  • 1 orange, zest only, no white pith
  • 110 g caster sugar
  • 30 g orange juice (approx. ½ orange)
  • 20 g orange liqueur (e.g. Cointreau®)
  • 80 g unsalted butter
  • 2 eggs

Preparation

Orange chocolate brownie slice

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Grease and line a square baking tin (20 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place orange flavoured chocolate into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place 160 g butter and dark chocolate into mixing bowl and melt 3 min/60°C/speed 2, or until melted.
  4. Add sugar and eggs and mix 15 sec/speed 3.
  5. Add vanilla extract, salt and flour and mix 20 sec/speed 3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then mix for a further 5 sec/speed 3. Transfer half of the brownie mixture into a large bowl and set aside.
  6. Transfer remaining brownie mixture in mixing bowl into the bowl with the crushed orange flavoured chocolate. Combine with spatula, then pour into prepared baking tin.
  7. Bake for 10-15 minutes (180°C) or until just set. Set aside to cool in tin. Keep oven temperature at 180°C.

Orange curd

  1. Place orange zest and sugar into mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then mill for a further 10 sec/speed 9.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Add orange juice, orange liqueur, unsalted butter and eggs and cook 9 min/80°C/speed 3, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid. Transfer orange curd into a bowl and set aside to cool completely.
  3. Spoon cooled orange curd over cooled brownie layer in baking tin and smooth out leaving a ½ cm gap around the edges.
  4. Carefully spoon the remaining brownie mixture over the curd in baking tin, then bake for 15-20 minutes (180°C).
  5. Allow to cool completely in tin, then place into refrigerator for 1 hour. Cut into small pieces and serve.
