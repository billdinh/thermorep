Indian mustard

Ingredients

  • 250 g mustard seeds, white or yellow
  • 250 g orange juice
  • 250 g white wine vinegar
  • 100 g sugar
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp curry powder, mild
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • ½ tsp ground turmeric
  • ½ tsp fine salt

Preparation

  1. Place mustard seeds in mixing bowl, grind 1 min/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place orange juice, vinegar and sugar in mixing bowl and bring to boil 8 min/100°C/speed 2.
  3. Add cumin, curry powder, ginger, cinnamon, turmeric, salt and mustard flour and stir 10 sec/speed 4. Transfer to 2 jam jars with twist-off lids (each 500 g) and close firmly. Store Indian mustard in a cool, dry place.

Useful Items

jars with twist-off lids, bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • To ensure the mustard keeps for as long as possible, wash the jars thoroughly and dry in a warm oven or wash in the dishwasher. Transfer the mustard to the jars while they are still warm.
  • Unopened, the mustard can keep up to 6 months if stored in a cool, dry place.
  • The mustard tastes best if allowed to steep for 2 weeks before use.
  • Store in the refrigerator after opening.
