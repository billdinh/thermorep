Florentine Meatballs and Linguine

Ingredients

Florentine Meatballs

  • 10 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 5 oz fresh baby spinach, de-stemmed
  • 4 garlic cloves, divided
  • 2 oz milk
  • 1 tbsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp oregano, fresh or dried
  • 16 oz ground beef, crumbled
  • 1 oz grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 3 oz bread crumbs
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp fresh ground black pepper
  • 3 oz yellow onions, in pieces
  • 2 oz extra virgin olive oil, plus extra to grease

Pasta and Sauce

  • 30 oz canned crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 8 - 10 fresh basil leaves, torn, divided
  • 43 oz water
  • 12 oz spinach linguine, dried
  • Parmesan cheese, shaved, to garnish

Preparation

Florentine Meatballs

  1. Place parsley, spinach, and 2 garlic cloves into mixing bowl and with aid of spatula, chop 10 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and chop a further 5 sec/speed 6.
  2. Add milk, onion powder, oregano, ground beef, Parmesan, egg, bread crumbs, salt and pepper and with aid of spatula mix 15 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place 2 garlic cloves, onion and olive oil into mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and cook 5 min/212°F/speed 2. Meanwhile, lightly grease Varoma dish and tray and shape small meatballs (1½ in.) with oiled hands. Distribute meatballs ½ inch apart into prepared Varoma dish and tray. Secure Varoma lid and set aside.

Pasta and Sauce

  1. Add tomatoes, salt, sugar and 4-5 torn basil leaves into mixing bowl. Set Varoma with meatballs into position and cook 25 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove Varoma and set aside. Pour sauce into a large bowl, cover and set aside to keep warm. Rinse mixing bowl.
  2. Place water into mixing bowl and without measuring cup, bring to a boil 10 min/212°F/speed 1.
  3. Add linguine through hole in mixing bowl lid, insert measuring cup and cook for time indicated on package/212°F//speed 1, or until al dente. Drain pasta.
  4. Add drained pasta to bowl with reserved sauce and toss to coat. Serve Pasta and Sauce with Florentine Meatballs, garnish with torn fresh basil leaves and shaved Parmesan cheese and serve immediately.

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with 4 oz grated cheddar or Parmesan cheese.
  • Serve Florentine Meatballs with rice, pasta or steamed potatoes.
