Orange Buttermilk Chess Pie

Ingredients

Pie Crust

  • 1 homemade pie crust, chilled (9 in.)

Filling

  • 2 oranges, thin peel only, no pith, divided, plus 2 tbsp juice
  • 7 oz sugar
  • 2 oz yellow cornmeal
  • 1 vanilla bean, cut into pieces
  • 1 oz unsalted butter, chilled, cut into pieces (½ in.)
  • 3 oz buttermilk
  • 4 large eggs
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • confectioners sugar, to garnish
Nutrition
per 1 slice
Calories
1335 kJ / 319 kcal
Protein
5 g

Preparation

Pie Crust

  1. Adjust oven rack to lower middle position. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. Line chilled pie crust with parchment and baking weights and blind-bake for 10 minutes (375°F), remove parchment and weights and bake a further 10 minutes (375°F) until lightly golden. Transfer pie pan to a wire rack to cool. Meanwhile make filling. Reduce oven temperature to 350°F.

Filling

  1. Place half the orange peels, sugar, cornmeal and vanilla bean into mixing bowl and grind 2 sec/speed 9.
  2. Add butter and mix 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add buttermilk, orange juice, eggs and salt and mix 10 sec/speed 3.5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix 10 sec/speed 4.
  4. Transfer filling into pie crust and bake for 35-40 minutes (350°F) until filling is golden. Dust with confectioners sugar and garnish with remaining orange peel. Serve warm or chilled.

Useful Items

parchment paper, baking weights, wire rack, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Baking weights can be replaced with 16 oz dry beans.
