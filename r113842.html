Sweet Corn, Pecan and Vanilla Ice-Cream

Ingredients

  • 2 vanilla pods
  • 7 oz milk
  • 5 ½ oz organic heavy whipping cream
  • 5 ½ oz brown sugar
  • 3 ½ oz pecans, or other nuts (e.g. walnuts, almonds, hazelnuts)
  • 15 ½ oz canned sweet corn kernels, drained
  • 3 egg yolks, from large eggs
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1712 kJ / 409 kcal
Protein
6 g
Carbohydrates
43 g

Preparation

  1. Split vanilla pods lengthwise and scrape out seeds. Place seeds and vanilla pods, milk, cream, brown sugar, pecans and corn into mixing bowl and cook 10 min/212°F/speed 1.
  2. Remove vanilla pods and mix 1 min/speed 10. Drain mixture from mixing bowl through a fine sieve into a bowl and strain. Using the spatula, press the pulp into the sieve removing any remaining liquid.
  3. Transfer mixture back into mixing bowl and mix 1 min/speed 3 while adding egg yolks one at a time through hole in mixing bowl lid onto rotating blades.
  4. Cook 4 min/175°F/speed 2. Pour mixture up to 1½ inches high into shallow freezable containers and cover with cling wrap. Allow to cool completely before freezing for at least 10 hours.
  5. Place frozen mixture in refrigerator for 15 minutes then cut into cubes (1 in.) with a sharp knife.
  6. Place half the ice cream cubes into mixing bowl and crush 15 sec/speed 9, and mix 30 sec/speed 6 with aid of spatula. Transfer ice cream to a bowl.
  7. Place remaining frozen ice cream cubes into mixing bowl and crush 15 sec/speed 9 then mix 30 sec/speed 6 with aid of spatula. Transfer into bowl with ice cream and serve immediately.

Useful Items

fine mesh strainer, freezable containers (e.g. aluminum, silicone), cling wrap, freezer, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • The pecan nut, one of the most popular gourmet nuts in the world, is very easily found in Mexico. These nuts are used in several recipes, but one of the most famous is the pecan nut ice cream recipe, made with vanilla and corn kernels, which give a smooth texture and a strong addictive flavor to this dessert.
  • Be creative and decorate with some caramelized pecan nuts, roasted pine nuts or roasted and sliced almonds.
