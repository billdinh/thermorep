Pork and lentils with onion and radish pickle

Ingredients

Onion and radish pickle

  • ½ red onion, cut into thin slices (see Tip)
  • 2 radishes, trimmed and cut into thin slices (see Tip)
  • 2 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tbsp caster sugar

Pork with lentils

  • 4 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 40 g olive oil
  • 30 g French mustard
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 800 g pork fillets, trimmed and cut into halves
  • 200 g crème fraîche
  • 150 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 1000 g hot water
  • 800 g canned lentils, rinsed and drained (approx. 500 g after draining)
  • 2 dried bay leaves

Preparation

Onion and radish pickle

  1. Place onion, radishes, vinegar and sugar into a ceramic or glass bowl, stir to combine and set aside to pickle while continuing with recipe.

Pork with lentils

  1. Place parsley into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add oil, mustard, salt and pepper and mix 5 sec/speed 7. Place a plate onto mixing bowl lid and weigh pork fillets onto it. Rub pork with half of the mustard mixture and set pork aside. Keep remaining mustard mixture in mixing bowl.
  3. Add cremé fraîche and cream to mixing bowl with remaining mustard mixture and mix 5 sec/speed 4. Transfer into a frying pan and set aside. Rinse mixing bowl.
  4. Place hot water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position, divide reserved pork fillets between Varoma dish and tray, secure Varoma lid and steam 15-20 min/Varoma/speed 4, until pork is cooked to your liking. Remove pork and cover to keep warm. If needed, contine to cook pork over lentils in next step.
  5. Insert simmering basket and weigh lentils into it. Add bay leaves, then cook 5 min/100°C/speed 4. Meanwhile, place frying pan with reserved sauce over medium heat and cook, stirring frequently, until sauce has thickened slightly (approx. 5 minutes).
  6. Divide lentils between serving plates. Slice pork and serve with lentils. Spoon over sauce and serve with reserved pickled vegetables.

Useful Items

plate, serving plates, frying pan, ceramic or glass bowl, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • We used a mandoline to thinly slice our onion and radishes.
