Turkey and Wild Rice Stuffed Peppers

Ingredients

  • 3 oz Monterey Jack cheese, cubed
  • 2 oz Parmesan cheese, cubed
  • 1 oz panko
  • 10 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves only
  • 32 oz water
  • 1 ½ tsp salt, to taste, divided
  • 6 oz wild rice
  • 6 bell peppers, mixed (red and yellow)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil, divided
  • 4 oz red onions, in pieces
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 8 oz cremini mushrooms, sliced (¼ in.)
  • 1 oz unsalted butter
  • 1 oz all-purpose flour
  • 10 oz breakfast turkey sausages, quartered
  • 2 oz cream cheese, cubed
  • 3 oz baby spinach

Preparation

  1. Place Monterey Jack, Parmesan cheese, panko and thyme into mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place water and 1 tsp salt into mixing bowl. Set simmering basket onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in rice. Remove simmering basket and rinse rice under cold running water until water runs clear. Insert simmering basket with rice into mixing bowl and mix 5 sec/speed 4, then cook 50 min/212°F/speed 4. Remove simmering basket, transfer cooked rice to a bowl and keep warm. Discard water and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Preheat oven to 375°F. Slice and reserve tops from bell peppers. Remove and discard seeds and rub peppers with 1 tbsp oil. Place peppers upright into a baking dish (approx. 9 in. x 9 in. x 4 in.) and set aside. Thinly slice reserved bell pepper tops and set aside.
  4. Place onion, garlic and 1 tbsp oil into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Add mushrooms and ½ tsp salt, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and sauté 8 min/250°F//speed .
  5. Add butter and flour (avoiding blades), place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 2 min/250°F//speed .
  6. Add turkey sausage, cream cheese and spinach and with aid of spatula cook 5 min/250°F//speed . Scrape down sides and bottom of mixing bowl with spatula. Add reserved sliced bell pepper tops and with aid of spatula cook a further 5 min/250°F//speed . Transfer turkey mixture, reserved rice and half the reserved grated cheese into a large bowl and toss to combine.
  7. Spoon turkey-rice mixture into prepared bell peppers and top with remaining grated cheese mixture. Bake 35-40 minutes (375°F), until cheese has browned and peppers have softened. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

baking dish (approx. 9 in. x 9 in. x 4 in.), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Choose peppers with a flatter bottom that can stand easily on their own.
  • If cheese topping begins to brown too quickly, cover the dish loosely with foil.
