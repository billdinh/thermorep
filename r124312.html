Nachos with Chilli and Cheese

Ingredients

  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 150 g onions, quartered
  • 40 g olive oil
  • 200 g beef mince, broken in pieces
  • 160 g tinned chopped tomatoes
  • 1 Tbsp tomato purée
  • 1 tsp caster sugar
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 dried bay leaf
  • 1 pinch ground turmeric
  • 1 pinch sweet paprika
  • 1 pinch ground cloves
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper
  • 240 g tinned kidney beans, rinsed, drained (1 x 400 g tin)
  • 15 g dark chocolate (70% cocoa solids), small pieces or callets
  • 200 g tortilla chips
  • 150 g Cheddar cheese, grated (see tip)

Preparation

  1. Place garlic, onions and oil in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then sauté 8 min/120°C//speed .
  2. Add beef mince and fry 4 min/120°C//speed .
  3. Add tomatoes, tomato purée, sugar, salt, oregano, cumin, bay leaf, turmeric, paprika, cloves and cayenne pepper. Insert simmering basket, weigh in kidney beans then cook 10 min/100°C//speed . Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and set aside.
  4. Add chocolate and cook 3 min/100°C//speed . Meanwhile, preheat grill to 200°C and spread tortilla chips in a baking dish.
  5. Remove bay leaf from chilli then add reserved kidney beans and mix well with spatula. Pour chilli over tortilla chips in baking dish then sprinkle over grated Cheddar. Grill for 3-5 minutes (200°C) until cheese has melted.
  6. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

baking dish, grill

Hints & Tricks

  • To grate your own Cheddar at the beginning of the recipe, cut cheese in 2 cm pieces and grate 4-5 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside then continue as per recipe.
  • For your next at-home movie night with the kids, try whipping up a batch of these cheesy nachos. A classic beef chilli bursting with mild Tex-Mex flavours is ladled over crunchy tortilla chips, topped with grated Cheddar then grilled until melted. Serve in bowls with sour cream and enjoy whilst watching a fun family blockbuster.
