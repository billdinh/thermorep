Bloody Mary and Spiced Citrus Dust

Ingredients

Spiced Citrus Dust

  • 1 lemon, peel only, dehydrated
  • 1 orange, peel only, dehydrated
  • 1 mandarin, peel only, dehydrated
  • 1 lime, peel only, dehydrated
  • 1 dried red chili
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 tsp sugar

Bloody Mary

  • ½ oz lemon peel, thin peel only, no pith (approx. 1 lemon)
  • 1 tsp black peppercorns
  • 3 in. fresh lemongrass (optional)
  • 28 oz tomato juice
  • 12 fresh basil leaves
  • 1 oz lemon juice (approx. 1 lemon)
  • ¼ - ½ jalapeño pepper, deseeded
  • ½ tsp white pepper
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp celery salt
  • 8 oz vodka
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 8 ice cubes, plus extra to serve
  • 4 5 - 7 slices lime, to rim glasses and garnish
  • 4 stalks celery, to garnish
  • 8 green olives, to garnish

Preparation

Spiced Citrus Dust

  1. Place all ingredients into mixing bowl and grind 20 sec/speed 10. Transfer to an airtight container and set aside.

Bloody Mary

  1. Place lemon peel, peppercorns and lemongrass into mixing bowl and grind 15 sec/speed 10.
  2. Add tomato juice, basil, lemon juice, jalapeno, white pepper, sugar, celery salt, vodka, Worcestershire sauce and 8 ice cubes and blend 1 min/speed 10.
  3. Place reserved Spiced Citrus Dust onto a plate and spread out wider than the rim of the glasses. Moisten each rim with a slice of lime. Dip glass rims into Spiced Citrus Dust, rotate to coat entire rim and fill prepared glasses with ice. Pour Bloody Mary mixture over ice and garnish with celery, lime slices and green olives. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

glasses, airtight container

Hints & Tricks

  • Use lime juice and peel instead of lemon.
  • Use 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper instead of jalapeño.
  • Use jalapeño peppers sparingly as some are very hot.
