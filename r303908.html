Plant-based parmesan

Ingredients

  • 50 g walnuts
  • 50 g sunflower seeds
  • 2 tbsp hemp seeds (see Tips)
  • 2 tbsp nutritional yeast (see Tips)
  • ¼ tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp salt

Preparation

  1. Place all ingredients into mixing bowl and blend 5 sec/speed 8. Transfer into a sealable container, place into refrigerator and use as required (see Tips).

sealable container, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Small and crunchy, hemp seeds are derived from the hemp plant. They contain all the amino acids (proteins) essential for the body and also are high in omega-3 and omega-6. These seeds can be great for reducing joint pain, improving gut health and reducing sugar cravings. You can find them at some major supermarkets, health food stores and online.
  • With a cheesy, umami flavour, nutritional yeast contains all nine essential amino acids, is a great source of B vitamins and is often fortified with vitamin B12 (important for those following a vegan diet). You can find nutritional yeast at health food stores or online.
  • You can store this cheese in the refrigerator for up to 1 week.
