Achcharu (pickle)

Ingredients

  • 2 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 10 - 15 pickling onions, peeled
  • 600 g white wine vinegar
  • 2 - 3 carrots (approx. 250 g), cut into matchsticks
  • 8 - 12 fresh bird's eye chillies green, trimmed and deseeded if preferred
  • 6 garlic cloves, cut into thin slices
  • 1 - 2 tbsp black mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp white sugar
  • 2 pinches ground turmeric
  • ¼ tsp salt

Preparation

  1. Place simmering basket into a large jug or bowl and set aside.
  2. Place ginger into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add onions and 300 g of the vinegar and heat 8 min/Varoma//speed . Transfer onions into simmering basket to drain. Transfer drained onions into a separate large bowl and set aside. Transfer vinegar back into mixing bowl. Place simmering basket back into large jug or bowl.
  4. Add carrots into mixing bowl and heat 2 min/100°C//speed .
  5. Add chillies and heat 2 min/100°C//speed  . Transfer carrots and chillies into simmering basket to drain. Transfer drained carrots and chillies into bowl with onions. Transfer vinegar back into mixing bowl.
  6. Add remaining 300 g vinegar and all remaining ingredients into mixing bowl and boil 4 min/Varoma/speed 2. Pour hot vinegar mixture over reserved onion mixture and stir well to combine. Transfer into a sterilised jar (approx. 1.5L capacity; see Tips) with sealable lid and set aside for 1-2 days to pickle. Serve as a condiment with your meal.

Useful Items

glass bowl or large jug, large bowl, sealable sterilised jar

Hints & Tricks

  • Black mustard seeds can be found in spice shops or online.
  • This pickle is spicy, so adjust the amount of chillies used to your preference.
  • To sterilise jars, wash on the hottest cycle in a dishwasher or hot-rinse the jars and dry in a hot oven for 15 minutes. Allow jars to cool before using. 
  • You can store this pickle in the refrigerator for up to 4 weeks.
