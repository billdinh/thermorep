Stir-fried Prawns with Spring Onions

Ingredients

  • 2 spring onions, cut in pieces (4 cm)
  • 10 g fresh root ginger, peeled, cut in round slices (2 mm)
  • 10 g vegetable oil
  • 300 g raw prawns, peeled, trimmed (see tip)
  • 1 Tbsp rice wine
  • 1 fresh red chilli, thinly sliced (optional)
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
841 kJ / 231 kcal
Protein
33 g
Carbohydrates
2 g
Fat
6 g

Preparation

  1. Place white parts of spring onions, ginger and oil in mixing bowl then sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  2. Add prawns and stir-fry 3 min/100°C//speed 1.
  3. Add rice wine and stir-fry 3 min/Varoma//speed 1.
  4. Add green parts of spring onions, chilli (if using) and salt then stir-fry 2 min/Varoma//speed 1. Transfer to a serving dish and serve hot.
