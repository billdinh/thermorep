Chai Tea Powder

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp cardamom seeds
  • 1 whole nutmeg
  • 2 star anise
  • 6 whole cloves
  • 1 tsp black peppercorns
  • 1 oz cinnamon sticks
  • 9 oz nonfat powdered milk
  • 2 tsp ground ginger
  • 2 ½ oz black tea leaves
  • 4 ½ oz sugar, to taste
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
138 kJ / 33 kcal
Protein
2 g
Carbohydrates
6 g
Fat
0 g

Preparation

  1. Place cardamom seeds, nutmeg, star anise, cloves, peppercorns and cinnamon sticks into mixing bowl and grind 30 sec/speed 10.
  2. Add powdered milk, ginger, tea leaves and sugar and grind 30 sec/speed 9.
  3. Transfer into an airtight jar and label with cooking instructions (see Tip).

Useful Items

airtight jar

Hints & Tricks

  • Stove top cooking instructions: place 2 tsp Chai Tea Powder in a tea ball or a tea bag. Heat 8 oz water until boiling. Allow tea ball to steep for 5-8 minutes. Serve immediately.
  • Thermomix® cooking instructions: place 8 oz water and 2 tsp Chai Tea Powder into mixing bowl and cook 5 min/195°F/speed 3. Strain and serve.
  • Allow to steep longer for a stronger tea.
