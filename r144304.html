Courgette and Dried Fruit Scones

Ingredients

  • 180 g spelt flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 180 g courgettes, cut in pieces
  • 40 g unsalted butter, cold, diced
  • 1 ½ tsp baking powder
  • 2 pinches fine sea salt
  • 20 g honey
  • 80 g dried apricots, pitted, cut in pieces (1 cm)
  • 1 egg, beaten, for brushing

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C. Lightly dust a baking tray with flour then set aside.
  2. Place courgettes in mixing bowl and grate 3 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place flour, butter, baking powder and salt in mixing bowl then mix 5 sec/speed 6.
  4. Add reserved grated courgettes, honey and apricots then mix 40 sec/. Tip out onto a floured surface and bring together into a disc (approx. 3 cm thick), being careful not to overwork the dough.
  5. Use a sharp knife to cut into six triangular segments. Arrange on prepared baking tray and brush with beaten egg. Bake for 12-15 minutes (210°C) until lightly golden then transfer to a wire rack to cool. Serve warm or at room temperature.

Useful Items

baking tray, wire rack, pastry brush, long sharp knife, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Scones are best eaten on the day they are made.
