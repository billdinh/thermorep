Coconut quinoa with sticky mango

Ingredients

Coconut quinoa

  • 160 g white quinoa
  • 4 Medjool dates, pitted
  • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • 600 g canned coconut milk
  • 150 g fresh mango cheeks, diced

Sticky mango

  • 60 g coconut sugar
  • 4 fresh mango cheeks
  • 1 lime, juice only
  • 80 g canned coconut milk

Preparation

Coconut quinoa

  1. Place quinoa, dates, vanilla, cinnamon and coconut milk into mixing bowl and cook 16 min/100°C//speed 1, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid. Transfer into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and stir through diced mango.

Sticky mango

  1. Place a medium-sized non-stick frying pan over high heat.
  2. Place coconut sugar into a shallow bowl or onto a plate. Press cut sides of mango cheeks into sugar, then place mango, sugar side down, into frying pan and cook until caramelised (approx. 3-5 minutes). Remove mango cheeks from frying pan and set aside to keep warm.
  3. With frying pan still on high heat, add lime juice and coconut milk, then stir to combine (approx. 30 seconds), before transferring to a serving jug.
  4. Serve coconut quinoa immediately, topped with sticky mango and a drizzle of the coconut milk mixture.

Useful Items

thermal serving bowl or other large bowl, medium-sized non-stick frying pan, serving jug, stove top
