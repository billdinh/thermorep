Apricot amaretti

Ingredients

  • 220 g sugar
  • 180 g almonds
  • 100 g dried apricots, pitted
  • 2 egg whites
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 2 tsp honey
  • 100 g mixed nuts, whole (e.g. pistachios, almonds and hazelnuts)
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
515 kJ / 123 kcal
Protein
3 g
Carbohydrates
12 g
Fat
6 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C. Line a baking tray with baking paper.
  2. Place sugar in mixing bowl and pulverise 30 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place almonds in mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 7.
  4. Add apricots, chop 5 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  5. Rinse mixing bowl.
  6. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg white, salt and honey in mixing bowl and whisk until stiff 2 min/speed 3.5. Remove butterfly whisk
  7. Add almond and apricot mixture, nuts and 150 g icing sugar and stir 20 sec/speed 2.
  8. With wet hands, shape 24 little balls (Ø approx. 3 cm), roll in remaining icing sugar and place on baking tray approx. 1 cm apart. Let the amaretti rest for 10-15 minutes to let the surface dry. Bake for 6-10 minutes (190°C), allow to cool and store cool and dry in jars.

Useful Items

baking tray and paper, bowl, oven
