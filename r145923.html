Amaranth and Chickpea Patties

Ingredients

Patties

  • 60 g rice
  • 60 g amaranth grains
  • 500 g water
  • 190 g potatoes, peeled, cut in pieces (4 cm)
  • 170 g onions, quartered
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 10 g fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 100 g chick peas, tinned, rinsed, drained
  • 20 g tomato purée
  • 1 Tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 Tbsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt, or to taste
  • ½ tsp ground cumin
  • ½ tsp ground pepper
  • 1 - 2 pinches ground cayenne pepper, to taste

Preparation

Patties

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C and line a baking tray with baking paper.
  2. Place rice in mixing bowl and grind 1 min/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place amaranth and water in mixing bowl then cook according to packet instructions/100°C/speed 0.5. Drain through a fine sieve then add to bowl with ground rice.
  4. Place potatoes in mixing bowl and grate 3 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a fine sieve and strain, pressing potato against sieve with a spatula to remove excess water. Set aside.
  5. Place onions, oil and garlic in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  6. Add reserved strained potato and fry 4 min/120°C/speed 1.
  7. Add parsley, chickpeas, tomato purée, mustard, reserved rice flour and amaranth, smoked paprika, salt, cumin, pepper and cayenne then mix 10 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then mix again 3 sec/speed 5.
  8. Spoon mixture onto baking tray to form 6 patties (Ø 8 cm) then bake for 20 minutes (180°C), turn over and bake for a further 10 minutes (180°C). Serve topped with your favourite sauce and choice of salad.

Useful Items

baking tray and paper, fine sieve, soft spatula, turner / fish slice, oven
