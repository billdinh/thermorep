Carrot and Parsnip Mash

Ingredients

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 oz shallot, quartered
  • 16 oz carrots, cut into 1 in. pieces
  • 10 ½ oz parsnips, cut into 1 in. pieces
  • ½ tsp vegetable stock paste
  • 8 oz water
  • ¼ tsp salt, to taste
  • ¼ tsp fresh ground black pepper, to taste
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
310 kJ / 74 kcal
Protein
1 g
Carbohydrates
18 g
Fat
0 g

Preparation

  1. Place garlic and shallot into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7.
  2. Add carrots and parsnips, vegetable stock paste, water, salt and pepper and cook 25 min/212°F/speed 1. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Purée 20 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a serving bowl and serve.

Useful Items

serving bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • For a creamier version: add 2 oz butter or coconut oil.
  • For a smoother mash: after puréeing, scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and purée a further 5 sec/speed 4 or until desired texture is achieved.
  • To keep this recipe gluten-free, ensure your stock paste is truly gluten-free or use the recipe in The Basic Cookbook to make your own.
