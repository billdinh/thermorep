Chicken in Red Pepper Sauce with Courgette and Feta Couscous

Ingredients

Chicken in Red Pepper Sauce

  • 6 - 8 chicken drumsticks, skin on
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp freshly ground pepper
  • 180 g red peppers, deseeded
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 300 g tomatoes, halved
  • 30 g olive oil

Courgette and Feta Couscous

  • 200 g couscous
  • 15 g fresh flat-leaf parsley
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 30 g shallots
  • 150 g courgettes
  • 200 g water
  • 1 heaped tbsp chicken stock paste, homemade
  • 100 g feta cheese

Preparation

Chicken in Red Pepper Sauce

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C.
  2. Place chicken drumsticks into a casserole dish, season with salt and pepper and set aside.
  3. Place red peppers, garlic clove, tomatoes and olive oil in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5, then sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 2.
  4. Blend 30 sec/speed 7. Spoon the sauce over the chicken and bake for 40-45 minutes (200°C). Meanwhile clean and dry mixing bowl, then continue with recipe.

Courgette and Feta Couscous

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid, weigh couscous into it and set aside.
  2. Place parsley in mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place olive oil, garlic clove and shallots in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5, then sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  4. Add courgettes and chop 8 sec/speed 4, then sauté 4 min/120°C/speed 1. Transfer to bowl with chopped parsley and set aside.
  5. Place water and chicken stock paste in mixing bowl then heat 5 min/100°C/speed 1. Transfer to bowl with couscous, stir with spatula to moisten the couscous, cover with cling film and allow to sit for 10 minutes.
  6. Gently break apart and fluff the cooked couscous with a fork. Add reserved chopped parsley-courgette mixture to couscous, mix gently and crumble feta over the courgette couscous. Serve couscous hot with the red pepper chicken.
