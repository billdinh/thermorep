Pea Quiche

Ingredients

  • 450 g plain flour
  • 220 g salted butter, cold, diced
  • 100 g water
  • 2 ¾ tsp fine sea salt
  • 400 g fresh green peas
  • 20 g cornflour
  • 400 g sour cream
  • 150 g milk
  • 4 medium eggs
  • ¾ tsp ground black pepper
  • ½ tsp ground nutmeg
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C. Line a brownie tin (approx. 32 cm x 18 cm) with baking paper.
  2. Place flour, butter, water and ½ tsp salt in mixing bowl then knead for 1 min/. Transfer to prepared tin, spread out to form an even layer over base and 3 cm up the sides of tin. Prick base with a fork. Scatter peas over base then set aside.
  3. Place cornflour, sour cream, milk, eggs, 1 tsp salt, pepper and nutmeg in mixing bowl then mix 20 sec/speed 4. Pour over peas then bake for 35-40 minutes (200°C). Allow to stand for 5 minutes then cut bite sized pieces to add to your buffet table - to enjoy hot or cold.

Useful Items

baking paper, brownie tin (32 cm x 18 cm), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • If the dough is too soft to work with, wrap in cling film and chill in fridge for 15-30 minutes.
  • You can also cut the pea quiche into slices and serve as a lunch or light supper option.
  • Pea quiche with ham: Add 200 g ham, cut in pieces at the beginning of the recipe then chop 5 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Sprinkle over base at same time as peas.
