Aussie Meat Pies

Ingredients

Shortcrust Pastry

  • 200 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 100 g unsalted butter, chilled, diced, plus extra for greasing
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • 50 - 60 g water, chilled

Iced Puff Pastry

  • 90 g ice cubes
  • 180 g unsalted butter, diced, frozen
  • 200 g plain flour
  • 1 ½ tsp fine sea salt

Filling

  • 2 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 180 g onions, quartered
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 500 g beef fillet steak, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 100 - 120 g button mushrooms, thinly sliced, to taste
  • 1 Tbsp cornflour
  • 2 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 Tbsp yeast extract
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper

Preparation

Shortcrust Pastry

  1. Place flour, butter and salt in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 6.
  2. Add 50 g water and knead 20 sec/ until dough comes together. If necessary, add up to an extra 10 g water and knead again 10 sec/ or until dough comes together. Tip dough out onto a floured work surface and shape into a ball. Wrap in cling film and leave to chill in fridge for 30 minutes. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl and continue with recipe.

Iced Puff Pastry

  1. Place ice cubes and butter in mixing bowl then chop 15 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add flour and salt then mix 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Knead 2 min/ then tip dough out and wrap in cling film. Leave to chill in fridge for 30 minutes. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl and continue with recipe.

Filling

  1. Grease a large 6-hole jumbo muffin tray with butter.
  2. Place parsley in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place garlic and onions in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add oil and sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  5. Add beef, mushrooms, cornflour, Worcestershire sauce, soy sauce, yeast extract, pepper and reserved chopped parsley then cook 10 min/100°C//speed 1. Transfer to a bowl and leave to cool completely.
  6. Using a rolling pin, roll out shortcrust pastry on a floured work surface to 2 mm thick. Line prepared muffin tray with pastry, cutting away any excess.
  7. Bring remaining pastry into a ball and roll out again. Cut out 6 discs large enough to double line the base of each pie. Place 1 disc in the base of each pie and press down gently. Place lined tray in freezer for 15 minutes to chill. Meanwhile, preheat oven to 200°C.
  8. Once chilled, fill each pastry case with cooled beef mixture.
  9. Using a rolling pin, roll out iced puff pastry on a floured work surface to 5 mm thick. Cut into 6 discs, large enough to form the top of each pie. Place 1 disc on top of each pie and seal by pressing around the edges.
  10. Use a small knife to cut an opening on the top of each pie. Bake for 30-35 minutes (200°C) until pastry is golden and puffy. Leave to cool slightly before removing from tray. Serve pies warm.

Useful Items

6-hole jumbo muffin tray, rolling pin, cling film, refrigerator, oven, freezer

Hints & Tricks

  • For a mince meat pie, mince your meat by chopping Turbo/0.5 sec/2-4 times and continue as per recipe.
  • Serve meat pies with tomato ketchup.
  • Roll out, wrap and freeze any leftover pastry to use at a later date.
  • To freeze cooked pies, leave to cool to room temperature, and freeze separately in freezer bags. Alternatively, put a piece of baking paper in between each pie and freeze in one freezer bag.
