Beef and Beer Pie

Ingredients

Beef and Beer Stew

  • 40 g olive oil
  • 600 g stewing steak, cubed (2 cm)
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 2 pinch ground black pepper
  • 150 g onions, quartered
  • 15 g cornflour
  • 250 g water
  • 150 g leeks, sliced
  • 330 g dark beer
  • 1 tsp fresh thyme leaves
  • 1 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • ½ tsp meat stock paste

Pastry and Assembly

  • 150 g plain flour
  • 65 g unsalted butter, cold, diced
  • 1 medium egg
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 egg yolk, lightly whisked, for glazing

Preparation

Beef and Beer Stew

  1. Place 20 g oil, 300 g beef, ½ tsp salt and 1 pinch pepper in mixing bowl then brown High Temp. . Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place remaining 20 g oil, remaining 300 g beef, ½ tsp salt and 1 pinch pepper in mixing bowl then brown High Temp. . Transfer to bowl with browned beef and set aside.
  3. Drain beef juices from reserved browned beef into a small bowl. Place 20 g beef juices in mixing bowl, discarding the rest. Add onions and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in cornflour and water. Remove from lid, mix until dissolved then set aside. Add leeks to mixing bowl and sauté 5 min/120°C//speed 1.
  5. Add reserved browned beef, beer, reserved cornflour mixture, thyme, Worcestershire sauce and stock paste then cook 20 min/110°C//speed 1.
  6. Place simmering basket in place of measuring cup on mixing bowl lid and cook a further 15 min/105°C//speed 1. Transfer to a rectangular baking dish (20 cm x 15 cm) and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.

Pastry and Assembly

  1. Place flour, butter, egg and salt in mixing bowl then mix 15 sec/speed 4. Remove pastry from mixing bowl and form into a flattened ball. Wrap in cling film and refrigerate for 10-15 minutes.
  2. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  3. Using a rolling pin, roll out pastry between two sheets of baking paper. Brush edge of baking dish with egg yolk then cover dish with pastry. Gently press the edges with fingertips or a fork to seal, trimming any excess with a sharp knife. Brush surface with egg yolk and make a small hole (5 mm) in the middle for steam to escape. Bake for 35-40 minutes (180°C) or until pastry is golden brown and crisp. Serve hot.

Useful Items

cling film, rolling pin, baking paper, pastry brush, oven-proof dish (approx. 20 cm x 15 cm), small bowl, refrigerator, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • This warming and cosy pie is perfect for relaxed lunches. Browning the meat before stewing gives a richer flavour to the pie.
  • Serve with mashed potatoes and steamed green vegetables.
  • This pie can be also baked in a pie dish (Ø 20 cm) or in a square baking dish (17 cm).
  • Add 150 g carrots, cubed, along with the beer in step 5.
