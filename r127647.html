Cucumber Gin Granita

Ingredients

  • 150 g sugar
  • 250 g water
  • 150 g cucumber, cut in pieces
  • 500 g ice cubes
  • 120 - 150 g gin, to taste
  • 13 sprigs fresh mint

Preparation

  1. Place sugar and water in mixing bowl then heat 8 min/100Â°C/speed 1.
  2. Add 4 sprigs mint and transfer to a bowl. Leave syrup to cool to room temperature for about 30 minutes then discard mint sprigs (see tip).
  3. Place cooled mint syrup and cucumber in mixing bowl then blend 30 sec/speed 10.
  4. Add ice cubes, gin and leaves of 1 sprig mint then crush 5-10 sec/speed 6 until finely crushed. Divide between 8 glasses and serve immediately, each garnished with 1 sprig mint.
