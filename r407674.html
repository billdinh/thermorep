Beef and Apple Cabbage Rolls

Ingredients

Rice and Cabbage

  • 30 oz water
  • 4 oz long-grain rice
  • 16 savoy cabbage leaves, divided

Filling

  • 4 oz yellow onion, in pieces
  • 3 ½ oz fennel bulb, quartered (approx. 1 sm.)
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp oil
  • ¾ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp ground cumin, to taste
  • ½ tsp paprika
  • 4 oz fresh cremini mushrooms, thinly sliced
  • 8 oz ground beef, broken into pieces
  • 4 oz Granny Smith apple, cored, peeled, diced (½ in.), plus extra to garnish

Sauce

  • 16 oz tomatoes, quartered (approx. 4)
  • 4 oz water
  • 2 oz brown sugar, to taste
  • 1 oz apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp salt

Preparation

Rice and Cabbage

  1. Place water into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket and weigh in rice. Remove simmering basket and rinse rice under cold running water until water runs clear. Insert simmering basket with rinsed rice into mixing bowl.
  2. Set Varoma dish into position and add 8 cabbage leaves, secure Varoma lid and steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 2. Remove Varoma. Set steamed cabbage aside and keep warm. Transfer steamed rice to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Set Varoma dish into position and add remaining 8 cabbage leaves, secure Varoma lid and steam 15 min/Varoma/speed 2. Remove Varoma and set steamed cabbage aside to keep warm. Clean mixing bowl.

Filling

  1. Place onion, fennel, garlic, oil, salt, cumin and paprika into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add mushrooms, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 5 min/212°F/speed .
  3. Add beef and apples, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 5 min/212°F//speed .
  4. Add reserved steamed rice, insert measuring cup and mix 1 min//speed 0.5. Stir through with spatula, transfer filling to a bowl and set aside.

Sauce

  1. Place tomatoes, water, brown sugar, apple cider vinegar, tomato paste, Worcestershire and salt into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 6. Cook 10 min/212°F/speed 1. Meanwhile preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Line bottom of a baking dish (12 in. x 9 in.) with 4 steamed cabbage leaves. Divide the beef-apple mixture evenly between remaining 12 steamed cabbage leaves (approx. a heaping ¼ cup ea.) and roll into a cylinder, tucking in the edges as you go. Place filled cabbage rolls seam side down over the cabbage leaves in baking dish. Pour sauce evenly over the cabbage rolls.
  3. Cover with foil and bake for 45 minutes (350°F). Garnish with julienned apples and serve hot.
