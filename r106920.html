Jalapeño Chicken Lettuce Wraps with Guacamole

Ingredients

Guacamole

  • 3 sprigs cilantro, leaves and stems (approx. 0.5 oz)
  • ¼ red onion (approx. 0.5 oz)
  • 2 ripe avocados, peeled and pit removed
  • 1 tomato, quartered and de-seeded (approx. 6-8 oz)
  • 1 tbsp jalapeño chilies, fresh, plus extra sliced to serve (optional)
  • 1 - 2 tsp freshly squeezed lime juice, (approx. 1 lime), to taste
  • 1 pinch salt, to taste
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper, to taste

Cilantro and Jalapeño Chicken

  • ¼ tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 dried chili, whole
  • ½ tsp cumin seeds
  • ½ tsp dried oregano
  • ½ tsp paprika
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • 24 oz chicken tenderloins
  • 5 sprigs cilantro, leaves and stems
  • 1 tbsp jalapeño chilies, pickled, drained
  • 2 oz freshly squeezed lime juice, (approx. 2 limes)
  • 2 oz honey
  • ¼ tsp onion salt

Warm Freekeh Salad

  • 9 oz freekeh
  • 35 oz water
  • 1 tbsp vegetable stock paste (see Tip)
  • 3 - 3 ½ oz red bell pepper, de-seeded, in pieces (½-1 in.)
  • 3 ½ oz macadamia nuts, halved (optional)
  • ½ red onion, thinly sliced (approx. 2 oz)
  • 2 sweet corn cobs, kernels only (approx. 9-9.5 oz)
  • 3 sprigs cilantro, leaves only, roughly chopped
  • 1 pinch onion salt, to taste
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1 romaine lettuce, leaves separated, to serve
  • 3 ½ oz sour cream, to serve (optional)

Preparation

Guacamole

  1. Place cilantro and onion into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add all remaining guacamole ingredients and mix 2 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl, cover and place in refrigerator until ready to use. Clean mixing bowl.

Cilantro and Jalapeño Chicken

  1. Place cayenne pepper, dried chili, cumin, oregano, paprika, garlic and cinnamon into mixing bowl and grind 30 sec/speed 10.
  2. Place Varoma into position, insert Varoma tray and weigh in chicken tenderloins. Remove Varoma and rub spice mixture from mixing bowl over the chicken. Set Varoma with chicken aside.
  3. Without cleaning mixing bowl, place cilantro and jalapeños into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add lime juice, honey and onion salt and cook 3 min/212°F/speed 3. Transfer jalapeño sauce into a pitcher and set aside.

Warm Freekeh Salad

  1. Without cleaning mixing bowl, insert simmering basket and weigh in freekeh. Remove simmering basket with spatula and rinse freekeh until water runs clear.
  2. Place water and stock paste into mixing bowl and re-insert simmering basket with freekeh.
  3. Place Varoma with chicken into position and drizzle half the reserved jalapeño sauce over the chicken tenderloins. Secure Varoma lid and steam 15 min/Varoma/speed 2. Remove Varoma and turn over chicken pieces on Varoma tray to ensure even cooking. Stir freekeh in simmering basket with spatula.
  4. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh in red bell pepper and macadamia nuts. Add red onion and corn kernels to Varoma dish, re-insert Varoma tray with chicken, secure Varoma lid and cook 3-5 min/Varoma/speed 2, or until chicken is fully cooked.
  5. Remove Varoma and transfer chicken onto a serving platter. Transfer steamed vegetables into a serving bowl and add cilantro leaves and remaining jalapeño sauce.
  6. Remove simmering basket with spatula and allow freekeh to drain. Transfer drained freekeh into serving bowl with the vegetables and season to taste with onion salt and pepper.
  7. Fill a lettuce leaf with the freekeh salad, top with a spiced chicken tenderloin, a spoonful of reserved guacamole, extra jalapeño chilies and sour cream to serve.
