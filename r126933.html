Chicken ball kebabs with rice

Ingredients

  • 200 g chicken breast fillet, sliced
  • 30 g shallot, sliced (approx. 1 shallot)
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary, needles washed and dried
  • 1 sprig fresh parsley
  • 1 sprig fresh marjoram
  • 30 g white wine
  • 50 g stale white bread, crust removed, in pieces
  • 30 - 50 g milk, for soaking the bread
  • 30 g Parmesan cheese, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 30 g mixed fresh herbs, (e.g. thyme, marjoram, basil, oregano)
  • 1 pinch fine salt
  • 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 courgette, medium-sized, cut into 16 slices
  • 1 pepper (vegetable), any colour, cut into 16 square pieces
  • 700 g water
  • ½ tsp vegetable stock paste, homemade
  • 150 g risotto rice, e.g. Arborio or Carnaroli
  • 20 g extra virgin olive oil

Preparation

  1. Place chicken breast fillets into a bowl with shallot, one sprig of rosemary, parsley, marjoram and white wine. Marinate for 1 hour. Meanwhile, soak breadcrumbs in milk in a bowl.
  2. Place Parmesan cheese into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place the mixed herbs into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Gently squeeze out marinade from meat and, with mixing knife moving at speed 5, add meat through hole in mixing bowl lid. Then chop 10 sec/speed 8.
  5. Add strained bread, Parmesan cheese, salt and pepper and chop 10 sec/speed 4. Remove mixture from mixing bowl and, with slightly wet hands, form 16 meatballs.
  6. Assemble kebabs by placing 1 slice of courgette, 1 meatball, 1 piece of pepper and so on onto the skewers until all ingredients are used up. Moisten and wring a sheet of baking paper then line Varoma tray, making sure to keep some holes unobstructed. Arrange kebabs onto Varoma tray. Clean mixing bowl.
  7. Place water and stock paste into mixing bowl, place simmering basket into position with the rice, place Varoma into position and cook: 20 min/Varoma/speed 1. At the end of the cooking time, remove Varoma and simmering basket with aid of spatula, strain rice and set aside.
  8. Empty mixing bowl and inside it place olive oil and remaining rosemary and sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1. Season rice with rosemary-flavoured oil.
  9. Serve kebabs accompanied by the rice.

Useful Items

wood skewers, bowl, baking paper

Hints & Tricks

  • If preferred, substitute the marjoram with fresh sage leaves.
  • At step 7, if preferred, add a pinch of chilli powder.
  • After cooking in the Varoma, the kebabs can be transferred to a hot frying pan with a dash of oil for several minutes or grilled in the oven.
