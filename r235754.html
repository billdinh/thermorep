Fish and Herb Parcel

Ingredients

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 2 Tbsp fresh parsley leaves
  • 2 Tbsp fresh basil leaves
  • 1 Tbsp oil
  • 1 Â¼ tsp fine sea salt, plus extra to taste
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 1 fish fillet (approx. 150-200 g)
  • ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1 courgette, in slices (3 mm)
  • 2 - 3 slices lemon, unwaxed
  • 1 Tbsp pine nuts
  • 1000 g water
  • 60 g long grain rice

Preparation

  1. Place garlic, parsley, basil, oil, salt and pepper in mixing bowl then blend 3-4 sec/speed 8.
  2. Pat fish dry with paper towels then place on a large enough piece of foil and spread fish with herb mixture.
  3. Place half of the courgette slices on the fish, lightly season with salt and pepper, cover with lemon slices, sprinkle over pine nuts, wrap up parcel and place in Varoma dish.
  4. Place water and salt in mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket then weigh in rice and remaining courgette slices. Place covered Varoma into position then steam 25 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  5. Serve fish and herb parcel with courgette rice.

Useful Items

aluminium foil, paper towels

Hints & Tricks

  • The cooking time may need altering depending on the type and thickness of the fish used.
