Zucchini Fritters with Gravlax and Lemon Aioli

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Parmesan cheese, cubed
  • 1 green onion, trimmed, in pieces
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black peppercorns
  • 16 oz zucchini, diced
  • 5 oz mozzarella, diced
  • 3 ½ oz panko bread crumbs
  • 1 large egg
  • olive oil spray

Lemon Aioli

  • 1 lemon, thin peel only, no pith, plus 2 tsp lemon juice
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 7 oz mayonnaise
  • 2 tsp mustard
  • 6 oz gravlax, sliced, to serve
  • fresh dill, to garnish

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Line a baking sheet with parchment and set aside.
  2. Place Parmesan cheese, green onion, garlic, salt and peppercorns into mixing bowl and grind 5 sec/speed 10.
  3. Add zucchini and mozzarella and chop 5 sec/speed 10.
  4. Add panko and egg and mix 5 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix a further 5 sec/speed 4.
  5. Evenly divide zucchini mixture into 30 mounds (approx. 1 oz ea.), flatten slightly and place on prepared baking sheet. Lightly spray with olive oil and bake for 20 minutes (400°F), flip zucchini bites and bake a further 5-10 minutes (400°F) until golden brown. Clean mixing bowl.

Lemon Aioli

  1. Place lemon peel, garlic and salt into mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add mayonnaise, 2 tsp lemon juice and mustard and mix 30 sec/speed 3.
  3. Top Zucchini Fritters with a dollop of Lemon Aioli and a slice of gravlax. Transfer to a platter, garnish with fresh dill and serve immediately.

Useful Items

baking sheet, parchment paper, serving platter, oven
