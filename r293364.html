Quick beetroot relish

Ingredients

  • 1 red onion (approx. 150 g), cut into thin slices
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 20 g dark brown sugar
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 300 g raw beetroots, peeled and cut into quarters (see Tips)
  • 1 green apple, peeled cored and cut into quarters (see Tips)
  • 3 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 20 g currants
  • 40 g apple cider vinegar (see Tips)
  • ½ tsp ground allspice

Preparation

  1. Place onion, oil, sugar, salt and pepper into mixing bowl and sauté 5 min/120°C//speed 1. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Do not clean mixing bowl.
  2. Place beetroot, apple and ginger into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add currants, vinegar, ground allspice and reserved onion mixture and cook 20 min/100°C//speed 1 or until beetroot is soft. Transfer into a bowl and set aside to cool for 5 minutes before serving (see Tips).

Hints & Tricks

  • To save time, peel and cut the beetroot and apple while the onion sautés in step 1.
  • For best results use a tart cooking apple.
  • You can replace the apple cider vinegar with balsamic or red wine vinegar.
  • You can store the relish in a sealable container in the refrigerator for up to 2 weeks.
  • Serve with grilled meats, on a burger or sandwich or as an accompaniment to the Country-style chicken liver fritters, Fresh tomato tart or Pear and burghul salad (please refer to the recipes on this recipe chip).
