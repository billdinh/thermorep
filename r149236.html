Lebanese Lamb Puff Pastry Tart

Ingredients

  • 280 g onions, thinly sliced
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 40 g pomegranate molasses
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 500 g lamb mince, broken in pieces
  • 30 g tomato purée
  • 2 pinches chilli flakes
  • 1 tsp ground allspice
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 chicken stock cube (for 0.5 l)
  • 130 g water
  • 30 g pine nuts
  • 500 g puff pastry
  • 3 Tbsp fresh parsley leaves, roughly chopped
  • 2 Tbsp fresh mint leaves, roughly chopped
  • 100 g pomegranate seeds

Preparation

  1. Place onions and oil in mixing bowl then sauté 10 min/120°C//speed 1.
  2. Add 30 g pomegranate molasses and cook again 2 min/120°C//speed 1. Drain through a sieve then transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Preheat oven to 200°C and line a baking tray with baking paper.
  4. Place garlic in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7.
  5. Add lamb mince, tomato purée, chilli flakes, allspice, cumin, coriander, cinnamon and salt then cook 5 min/100°C//speed .
  6. Add stock cube, water, 10 g pomegranate molasses and pine nuts then, with simmering basket instead of measuring cup on mixing bowl lid, cook 12 min/100°C//speed . Meanwhile, roll pastry out into a rectangle approx. 35 cm x 25 cm x 3 mm and place on prepared baking tray. Score a border (2 cm) then bake for 10 minutes (200°C)
  7. Spread reserved onions over pastry base.
  8. Add 2 Tbsp parsley and mint to meat mixutre and stir in with spatula. Drain meat mixture through a sieve to remove excess liquid, then spread over onion layer. Bake for 12-15 minutes (200°C) until golden.
  9. Scatter tart with pomegranate seeds and remaining 1 Tbsp chopped parsley then serve warm.

Useful Items

baking tray and paper, sieve, oven
