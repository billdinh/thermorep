White Chocolate and Raspberry Cheesecake

Ingredients

Base

  • 200 g ginger biscuits, halved
  • 80 g butter, diced

Filling

  • 5 gelatine leaves
  • water, for soaking gelatine
  • 180 g white chocolate, pieces or callets
  • 200 g light evaporated milk
  • 60 g caster sugar
  • 1 tsp real vanilla extract
  • 500 g cream cheese
  • 250 g raspberries

Preparation

Base

  1. Line base of a spring form cake tin (Ø 20 cm) with baking paper.
  2. Place biscuits in mixing bowl and grind 8 sec/speed 10.
  3. Add butter and mix 5 sec/speed 5. Spread mixture into prepared cake tin. Use a spoon or your hands to press crumb mixture down firmly to make a compact layer. Place in fridge while making filling.

Filling

  1. Soak gelatine leaves in a bowl of cold water.
  2. Place white chocolate, evaporated milk and caster sugar in mixing bowl then melt 7 min/70°C/speed 1.5.
  3. Squeeze gelatine leaves to remove excess water then add softened gelatine to mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 4.
  4. Add vanilla extract and cream cheese then mix 10 sec/speed 4.
  5. Add raspberries and gently stir in with spatula, then pour filling into cake tin over biscuit base. Cover with cling film and chill in fridge for at least 6 hours or overnight until set. Serve in slices.

Useful Items

spring form tin (Ø 20 cm), baking paper, cling film, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • This cheesecake has to be made ahead to allow time for the filling to set. This makes it a perfect option for when you have guests as everything is done before they arrive!
