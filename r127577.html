Chewy pistachio biscuits

Ingredients

  • 80 g sugar
  • 90 g pistachio nuts, shelled, unsalted
  • 30 g rice flour
  • 2 egg whites
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract
  • 2 tbsp icing sugar, for dusting
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
222 kJ / 52 kcal
Protein
1.4 g
Carbohydrates
6.8 g
Fat
2.3 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Line 2 baking trays with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place white sugar into mixing bowl and mill 4 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place 30 g of the pistachio nuts into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 6. Transfer into a bowl and set aside (omit this step when using almonds).
  4. Place remaining pistachio nuts and 30 g of the reserved sugar into mixing bowl and mill 15 sec/speed 8.
  5. Add rice flour and mix 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Thoroughly clean and dry mixing bowl.
  6. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg whites into mixing bowl and whisk 2 min/37°C/speed 3.5.
  7. With butterfly whisk still in place, whip 3 min/37°C/speed 3, slowly add remaining reserved sugar through hole in mixing bowl lid, 1 spoon at a time, until soft peaks form and meringue is glossy. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  8. Add vanilla and reserved pistachio and sugar mixture and combine 4 sec//speed 2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then repeat 4 sec//speed 2.
  9. Place a teaspoonful of biscuit mixture onto 1 of the prepared baking trays, then top with a sprinkle of the reserved crushed pistachios (or flaked almonds). Repeat with remaining biscuit mixture to form approx. 20 biscuits and garnish with remaining crushed pistachio nuts (or almonds).
  10. Bake for 9 minutes (180°C) or until browned around the edges. Dust with icing sugar while still warm, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely (approx. 20 minutes) before serving.

Useful Items

baking trays, baking paper, wire rack, oven
