Herbed Goat Cheese Crostini with Tapenade

Ingredients

Herbed Goat Cheese

  • 5 fresh basil leaves
  • 3 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only, plus extra to garnish
  • 1 sprig fresh tarragon, leaves only
  • 8 oz goat cheese, crumbled
  • 2 oz cream cheese, cubed

Olive Tapenade and Assembly

  • 5 fresh basil leaves
  • 1 sprig fresh thyme, leaves only
  • 1 - 2 garlic cloves
  • 6 oz pitted Kalamata olives, drained well
  • 1 tbsp capers
  • 1 oz sun-dried tomatoes, in oil, drained
  • 1 ½ oz extra virgin olive oil
  • ½ oz balsamic vinegar
  • 1 pinch dried red pepper flakes
  • 2 oz unsalted butter, diced
  • 1 baguette

Preparation

Herbed Goat Cheese

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Place 5 basil leaves, parsley and tarragon into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add goat cheese and cream cheese and blend 20 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and blend a further 20 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.

Olive Tapenade and Assembly

  1. Place 5 basil leaves, thyme and garlic into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add olives, capers, sundried tomatoes, olive oil, balsamic vinegar and red pepper flakes and chop 10 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and chop a further 10 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and blend 10 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.
  3. Place butter into mixing bowl and melt 2 min/160°F/speed 1. Meanwhile, slice baguette into ½ inch slices and arrange on baking sheets. Brush bread slices with melted butter and toast in oven for 10 minutes (400°F) or until golden brown. Remove and let cool.
  4. Use a spoon or piping bag fitted with a round tip to top each toast with ½ tbsp reserved Herbed Goat Cheese then top with 1 tsp Olive Tapenade.
  5. Arrange Herbed Goat Cheese and Olive Tapenade Crostini on a serving platter. Garnish with parsley and serve immediately.

Useful Items

pastry brush, piping bag and tip, serving platter, baking sheets, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Herbed Goat Cheese and Olive Tapenade can be made up to two days in advance.
