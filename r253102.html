Roast beef and mushroom pizza

Ingredients

Pizza dough

  • 250 g spelt flour
  • 250 g plain flour
  • 280 g warm water
  • 2 tsp dried instant yeast
  • 20 g olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 1 ¼ tsp salt
  • 1 pinch white sugar

Roast beef and mushroom topping

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 300 g sour cream
  • 1 ¼ tsp salt
  • ¾ tsp ground black pepper
  • 350 g roast beef, cut into thin strips (approx. 2 x 5 cm)
  • 30 g whiskey
  • 25 g honey
  • 2 tsp sweet paprika
  • ½ tsp hot paprika
  • 250 g fresh brown mushrooms, cut into slices
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 ½ tsp dried thyme
  • ¾ tsp grated lemon zest, no white pith
  • 2 spring onions/shallots, trimmed and cut into very thin slices
  • 50 g Parmesan cheese, shaved

Preparation

Pizza dough

  1. Place flours, water, yeast, oil, salt and sugar into mixing bowl and knead 2 min/. Transfer into a large bowl, cover and set aside in a warm place to prove until doubled in size (approx. 1 hour).
  2. Preheat oven to 220°C. Grease and line 2 baking trays (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  3. Divide dough into two portions. On a silicone bread mat or lightly floured work surface, roll out each piece of dough into an oval shape (approx. 35 x 25 cm), then transfer onto prepared baking trays. Pinch edges of dough to create a border, to help contain toppings during baking. Set aside while making toppings.

Roast beef and mushroom topping

  1. Place garlic into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add sour cream, ½ tsp of the salt and ¼ tsp of the pepper and mix 10 sec/speed 10. Spread mixture evenly over pizza bases.
  3. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh roast beef, whiskey and honey into it. Add ½ tsp salt, ¼ tsp pepper, sweet paprika and hot paprika and stir to combine well. Spread mixture evenly over pizza bases.
  4. Place a separate bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh mushrooms into it. Add oil, ¼ tsp salt, ¼ tsp pepper, thyme and lemon zest, then stir to combine. Spread mixture evenly over pizza bases. Bake for 20-25 minutes (220°C). Sprinkle with spring onions/shallots and shaved Parmesan cheese, before slicing and serving.

Useful Items

baking trays (30 x 40 cm), rolling pin, large bowl, kitchen towel, silicone bread mat, bowl, baking paper, oven
