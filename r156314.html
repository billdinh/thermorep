Chicken galantine with dill yoghurt

Ingredients

Chicken galantine

  • 150 g mixed capsicum (e.g. red, green, yellow), cut into pieces
  • 300 g skinless chicken breast fillets, cut into pieces (3-4 cm)
  • 300 g boneless chicken thighs, cut into pieces
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp dried mixed herbs
  • 500 g water

Dill yoghurt sauce

  • 10 - 15 sprigs fresh dill, leaves only
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 300 g plain yoghurt (see Tips)
  • 50 g mayonnaise (see Tips)
  • 2 pinches salt

Preparation

Chicken galantine

  1. Place mixed capsicums into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Drain capsicums through simmering basket, transfer them into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place chicken breasts into mixing bowl and mince 10 sec/speed 7. Transfer into bowl with chopped capsicum and set aside.
  3. Place chicken thighs into mixing bowl and mince 10 sec/speed 7.
  4. Add pepper, salt, dried mixed herbs, reserved minced chicken breasts and chopped capsicum into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 3.
  5. Divide mixture between 2 large pieces of baking paper (30 cm x 60 cm each), wrap and form each into a sausage shape. Close ends of baking paper tightly and place galantine rolls into Varoma dish.
  6. Place water into mixing bowl, place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 30 min/Varoma/speed 1. Set Varoma aside and clean and dry mixing bowl. Allow galantine rolls to cool for 30 minutes, then place into refrigerator for 2 hours.

Dill yoghurt sauce

  1. Place dill and garlic clove into mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 7.
  2. Add yoghurt, mayonnaise and salt and mix 10 sec/speed 4. Transfer sauce into a bowl. Unwrap galantine rolls, cut into slices (1 cm) and serve with dill yoghurt sauce

Useful Items

baking paper, refrigerator
