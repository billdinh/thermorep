Easy Amaretto Fudge

Ingredients

  • 4 oz milk chocolate, in pieces
  • 20 oz white chocolate, in pieces
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk (approx. 14 oz)
  • 1 oz amaretto liqueur
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 2 oz slivered almonds
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
351 kJ / 84 kcal
Protein
1 g
Carbohydrates
10 g
Fat
4 g

Preparation

  1. Line a square baking pan (9 in. x 9 in.) with foil and set aside.
  2. Place milk chocolate into mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside (use a dry pastry brush to clean as much chocolate out of mixing bowl without wetting it).
  3. Place white chocolate and cinnamon into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 7.
  4. Add sweetened condensed milk and amaretto and heat 4 min/212°F/speed 1. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add vanilla and salt and cook 2 min/212°F/speed 1. Mix 10 sec/speed 4. Transfer to prepared pan.
  6. Place reserved grated milk chocolate into mixing bowl and melt 3 min/120°F/speed 1. Evenly drop teaspoons of melted milk chocolate onto the white chocolate layer. Drag a skewer through chocolate to create swirls and top with almonds. Refrigerate until set (approx. 2 hrs.).
  7. Remove from pan using the foil to lift out the fudge and place on a clean surface. Peel off foil and cut fudge into cubes (1 in.). Store in airtight containers or package for gift giving.

Useful Items

aluminum foil, airtight containers, skewer, square baking pan (9 in. x 9 in.), pastry brush, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • The fudge can be eaten right away, but for a firmer, dryer fudge, allow to sit overnight or store in the refrigerator.
