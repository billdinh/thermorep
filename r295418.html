Firecracker Pearl Rice Meatballs

Ingredients

  • 7 oz glutinous rice
  • 28 oz water, divided, plus extra to soak rice
  • 1 oz carrots, in pieces
  • ½ oz dried mushrooms
  • ½ oz dried shrimp
  • ½ oz ginger, peeled, sliced
  • 1 - 2 red chili peppers, stem removed, to taste
  • 2 green onions, in pieces
  • 1 tsp rice flour
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp sugar
  • &frac18; tsp ground white pepper
  • 16 oz ground chicken, crumbled
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
146 kJ / 35 kcal
Protein
2 g

4 g

1 g

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in glutinous rice. Cover rice with water and set aside to soak for 1½ hours.
  2. Place carrots, mushrooms, shrimp, ginger, red chili, green onions, rice flour, salt, sugar and white pepper into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 6.
  3. Add ground chicken and 3 oz water and mix 15 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl.
  4. Form tablespoon sized meatballs (approx. 42 meatballs) and set aside.
  5. Strain soaking rice and transfer to a plate. Roll each meatball in strained rice to coat. Place 15 meatballs in Varoma dish, insert Varoma tray and arrange 27 meatballs onto Varoma tray.
  6. Place 25 oz water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 25-30 min/Varoma/speed 1, until rice is cooked. Serve hot.

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve Firecracker Pearl Rice Meatballs on their own or with soy sauce or chili oil and chilies, sliced in rings.
