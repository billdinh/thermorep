Mini Banana and Oat Bakes

Ingredients

  • 60 g coconut oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 10 pitted dates (approx. 60 g)
  • 200 g bananas, cut in pieces
  • 185 g rolled oats
  • 1 tsp real vanilla extract
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
272 kJ / 65 kcal
Protein
1.1 g
Carbohydrates
8 g
Fat
3.2 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C and grease a 24 hole mini muffin tin with coconut oil.
  2. Place coconut oil in mixing bowl and melt 2 min/50°C/speed 1.
  3. Add dates and bananas then blend 20 sec/speed 8.
  4. Add oats and vanilla then mix 20 sec//speed 3. Divide between muffin holes.
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes (180°C) until golden.
  6. Remove to a wire rack and allow to cool. Serve at room temperature or store in an airtight container until needed.

Useful Items

mini muffin tin (24 holes), wire rack, airtight container, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Frozen banana pieces can also be used. Blend for longer in step 3 until smooth. For easy blending, freeze bananas in individual slices on a flat baking tray.
  • Best eaten fresh.
