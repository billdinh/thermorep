Hawaiian Bread Rolls

Ingredients

  • 4 oz pineapple juice
  • 4 oz unsalted butter, cubed, plus extra to grease
  • 3 oz dark brown sugar
  • 1 packet/envelope dried instant yeast
  • 17 oz all-purpose flour
  • 4 large eggs, divided
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ½ tsp ground ginger
  • 1 tbsp water
Nutrition

Preparation

  1. Place pineapple juice, butter, sugar and yeast into mixing bowl and heat 3 min/100°F/speed 2.
  2. Add flour, 3 eggs, salt, vanilla extract and ginger and mix 5 sec/speed 5, then knead 3 min/. Meanwhile, lightly grease a large bowl and set aside.
  3. Transfer dough to a lightly floured surface and quickly shape into a ball. Transfer into prepared bowl, cover with cling wrap and let rise until doubled in size (approx. 90 min.).
  4. Grease two round cake pans (9 in.). Punch down dough and reshape into a ball. Divide dough into 16 equal pieces (approx. 2 oz ea.). Roll into balls and place into prepared cake pans, almost touching. Cover with cling wrap and let proof a second time until doubled in size (approx. 30 min.). Meanwhile preheat oven to 350°F.
  5. Make an egg wash by whisking together 1 egg and water in a small bowl. Brush tops of rolls with egg wash.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes (350°F) until tops are dark brown. Allow to cool for 5 minutes before serving.

Useful Items

cling wrap, round cake pans (9 in.), pastry brush, large bowl, oven
