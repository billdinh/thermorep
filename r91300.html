Brownies

Ingredients

  • 8 oz unsalted butter, cut into pieces, plus extra to grease
  • 9 oz dark chocolate, 70% cocoa, cut into pieces
  • 13 ½ oz sugar,
  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 5 ½ oz all-purpose flour
  • 5 ½ oz walnut halves (optional)
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
987 kJ / 236 kcal
Protein
3 g
Carbohydrates
24 g
Fat
14 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 340°F. Grease a rectangular cake pan (approx. 12 in. x 8 in.) and line with parchment, letting edges of paper hang over sides of pan (the excess parchment will serve as handles to lift out the brownies).
  2. Place chocolate into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7.
  3. Add butter and melt 4 min/160°F/speed 2.
  4. Add sugar, eggs, salt and flour and mix 1 min/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add walnuts and mix 10 sec/speed 4.
  6. Transfer into prepared cake pan, distributing batter evenly. Bake for 23-25 minutes (340°F), or until a toothpick or skewer inserted in the center comes out with a few damp crumbs. Remove from cake pan using the parchment to lift out the brownies. Allow to cool and serve brownies cut into squares.

Useful Items

rectangular cake pan (approx. 12 in. x 8 in.), parchment paper, toothpick or skewer, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • If using chocolate with a lower cocoa percentage, increase chocolate and decrease sugar amount. For instance, for chocolate with 50% cocoa, increase chocolate to 13 oz and decrease sugar to 9 oz.
