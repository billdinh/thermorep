Potato and Taleggio Roly-polies with Chive Dip

Ingredients

Potato Roly-polies

  • 500 g water
  • 2 tsp fine sea salt
  • 330 g floury potatoes, cut in pieces (3 cm)
  • 60 g shallots, halved
  • 15 g dried morel mushrooms, soaked in boiling water for 30 minutes, then squeezed
  • 20 g unsalted butter, diced
  • 1 medium egg
  • 1 Tbsp cornflour
  • 100 g Taleggio cheese, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 275 g puff pastry, ready-rolled
  • plain flour, for dusting

Chive Dip

  • 200 g crème fraîche
  • 2 dashes Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 Tbsp lemon juice
  • ½ bunch fresh chives, finely sliced, plus extra for garnishing (optional)
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper

Preparation

Potato Roly-polies

  1. Place water and 1 tsp salt in mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket, weigh in potatoes then steam 30 min/100°C/speed 1. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula, mash potatoes in simmering basket with a fork then leave to cool for 20 minutes. Discard steaming water.
  2. After cooling time, preheat oven to 200°C and line a baking tray with baking paper.
  3. Place shallots and morels in mixing bowl then chop 6 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add butter and sauté 2 min/120°C/speed 1.
  5. Add reserved mashed potatoes, egg, cornflour, Taleggio and 1 tsp salt then knead 45 sec/.
  6. On a lightly-floured surface, unroll puff pastry and cover evenly with potato mixture, leaving 3 cm on one long edge, then starting from the other long edge, roll up into a log. Cut the roll into 8 pieces (approx. 4 cm) then place flat on prepared tray.
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes (200°C) until golden. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl and make dip.

Chive Dip

  1. Place all chive dip ingredients in mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a serving bowl and garnish with extra chives (if using). Serve dip with warm potato roly-polies.

Useful Items
