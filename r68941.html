Crème brûlée

Ingredients

  • 400 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 70 g white sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 275 g eggs (approx. 5), room temperature
  • caster sugar, for caramelising top
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1631 kJ / 388 kcal
Protein
7 g
Carbohydrates
30 g
Fat
28 g

Preparation

  1. Place pouring (whipping) cream , white sugar, vanilla extract and eggs into mixing bowl and mix 1 min/speed 3, then cook  13-15 min/85°C/speed 3 . Crème should thicken while cooking.
  2. Once thickened, divide crème mixture among 6 individual ramekins. Allow to cool to room temperature then place into the refrigerator to chill.
  3. Once chilled, dust the top of each crème with a thick layer of caster sugar then caramelise by heating with a kitchen blow torch until golden brown and crisp. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

6 ramekins (8 cm, 140 ml), kitchen blow torch, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • The time it takes for your crème to set will depend on the type of cream used.
  • If you don't have a kitchen blow torch, place the ramekins filled with crème under a hot grill until the sugar caramelises. Allow the crème to cool before serving.
  • Chocolate crème brûlée: add 100 g chocolate, broken into pieces after cooking in step 1 and blend 10 sec/speed 3. Proceed as per recipe.
  • Chai crème brûlée: add 1 tsp ground cinnamon or 2 tsp chai tea powder in step 1 and proceed as per recipe.
