Peanut Butter Bark

Ingredients

  • 12 oz bittersweet chocolate, broken into pieces
  • 3 oz peanut butter chips
  • 30 candy coated chocolates, e.g., holiday M&M's® (approx. 3 oz)
  • 20 mini salted pretzels (approx. 1 oz)
Nutrition
per 12 pieces
Calories
10954 kJ / 2618 kcal

Preparation

  1. Place bittersweet chocolate into mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and melt 3 min/120°F/speed 1.
  2. Line a baking sheet with parchment. Pour the melted chocolate onto prepared tray and spread to approximately ¼ inch thickness with spatula.
  3. Place peanut butter chips into mixing bowl and melt 3 min/120°F/speed 1.
  4. Spoon melted peanut butter in random dollops onto prepared melted chocolate. To make swirls, drag a skewer through both.
  5. Sprinkle the top evenly with candies and pretzels, to taste. Let set until firm. Break into pieces before packaging or serving.

Useful Items

baking sheet, parchment paper, skewer

Hints & Tricks

  • Chocolate can be refrigerated for a few minutes to help set faster. Do not refrigerator any longer than necessary as it may collect condensation, which will affect the end result.
  • Replace candies and pretzels with dried fruits and seeds.
