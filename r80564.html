Chicken Provencale

Ingredients

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary
  • 80 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 20 g red wine vinegar
  • ¾ tsp fine sea salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 750 g chicken thighs, boneless and skinless, cut in pieces (3 cm)
  • 250 g cherry tomatoes
  • 5 juniper berries
  • 20 black olives
  • 300 g courgette, cut in pieces (1.5 cm)
  • 200 g aubergine, cut in pieces (1.5 cm)
  • 150 g red pepper, cut in pieces (1.5 cm)
  • 1000 g water
  • 20 g fresh basil leaves (optional)
  • 1 lemon, sliced

Preparation

  1. Place garlic and rosemary in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add oil, vinegar, salt and pepper then mix 5 sec/speed 4. Transfer dressing mixture to a large bowl, then place this bowl on mixing bowl lid. Weigh chicken into bowl and mix well with dressing.
  3. Wet and wring out a large piece of baking paper then use it to line Varoma dish. Place into lined Varoma dish prepared chicken (use a slotted spoon), leaving dressing in bowl. Place Varoma dish into position. Add to Varoma dish cherry tomatoes, juniper berries and olives. Set Varoma dish aside.
  4. Set bowl containing dressing on mixing bowl lid. Weigh into bowl courgette, aubergine and red pepper. Toss vegetables in the dressing. Insert Varoma tray into Varoma dish and transfer vegetables and dressing to tray.
  5. Place water in mixing bowl. Place covered Varoma into postion. Steam 40 min/Varoma/speed 1. Set Varoma aside and discard steaming water, then carefully transfer chicken, vegetables and juices to a large serving plate. Garnish with basil leaves and lemon slices before serving.

Useful Items

slotted spoon, large serving plate, baking paper, bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • To serve 8, cook up to 350 g brown rice in the simmering basket as well during Step 5, and use speed 4 instead of speed 1. Serve with a simple green salad as well.
  • Delicious served with good, crusty French bread to mop up the juices.
