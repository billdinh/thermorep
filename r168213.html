Spiced rice pudding (meghli)

Ingredients

  • 200 g Egyptian rice
  • 1 tbsp ground cinnamon
  • 2 tbsp ground caraway
  • 1300 g water or meghli water (see tip)
  • 400 g sugar
  • 1 tbsp desiccated coconut, to garnish (optional)
  • mixed nuts, to garnish (optional)
  • blanched almonds, to garnish (optional)
  • unsalted pistachio nuts, chopped, to garnish (optional)

Preparation

  1. Place rice into mixing bowl and grind 2 min/speed 10.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk, add cinnamon, caraway, water, mix 10 sec/speed 2.5, and then cook 30 min/100°C/speed 2.
  3. Add sugar and cook 5 min/100°C/speed 1. While the mixture is still hot, pour into small individual bowls and set aside to cool down. Sprinkle with 1 tbsp desiccated coconut and garnish with mixed nuts, almonds and pistachio nuts before serving.

Hints & Tricks

  • For extra flavour, replace water with Meghli water: place 2000 g water, 5 cinnamon sticks, 2 whole nutmeg seeds, 2 pieces of fresh ginger and 10 cloves into mixing bowl and cook 60 min/100ºC/speed 1. Add 1 tbsp aniseed, 1 tbsp ground cinnamon, 1 tbsp ground caraway, stir with spatula and leave it to infuse. When cooled down, strain it. Omit the extra tablespoon ground cinnamon in the main recipe if using meghli water.
