Nut Cupcakes

Ingredients

  • 100 g walnuts
  • 100 g hazelnuts
  • 200 g brown sugar
  • 150 g butter, soft, plus extra for greasing
  • 6 medium eggs
  • 125 g rice milk, plus extra for sprinkling
  • 350 g rice flour, plus extra for sprinkling
  • 100 g cornflour
  • 1 lemon, finely grated zest only
  • 2 Tbsp baking powder
  • 60 g raisins
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
950 kJ / 227 kcal
Protein
4 g
Carbohydrates
28 g
Fat
12 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Grease 2 x 12 hole muffin tins then sprinkle with rice flour.
  2. Place walnuts and hazelnuts in mixing bowl then chop 2 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Insert butterfly whisk. Place sugar, butter and eggs in mixing bowl then heat 3 min/37°C/speed 3.
  4. Add rice milk, rice flour, cornflour, lemon zest and baking powder then mix 10 sec/speed 3.
  5. Remove butterfly whisk. Add raisins, reserved chopped walnuts and hazelnuts then mix 2 sec/speed 4. Divide between muffin holes and bake for 20 minutes (180°C). Serve warm or cold.
