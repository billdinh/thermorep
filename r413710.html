Herb Stuffing with Butternut Squash and Brussels Sprouts

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ oz extra virgin olive oil, divided, plus extra to grease
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary (leaves only) (approx. 2 tsp)
  • 1 tsp fresh sage leaves (approx. 4 leaves)
  • 1 tsp fresh thyme (leaves only)
  • 16 oz butternut squash, peeled and cubed (1 in.)
  • 3 oz shallots, finely sliced (approx. 2 shallots)
  • 16 oz Brussels sprouts, trimmed, halved
  • 5 oz apple, diced
  • salt, to taste
  • fresh ground black pepper, to taste
  • 6 oz onion, quartered
  • 4 ½ oz celery, diced
  • 14 oz bread slices, cubed (1 in.)
  • 12 oz vegetable stock
  • 1 oz dried cranberries
  • 1 oz chopped walnuts

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F and grease a casserole dish (13 in. x 9 in.).
  2. Place fresh rosemary, sage and thyme into mixing bowl and chop 6 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place a large bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in butternut squash, shallots, Brussels sprouts, apple and 1 oz olive oil. Season with salt and pepper, to taste and toss to coat. Transfer to a baking sheet and bake for 30 minutes (400°F) until tender. Reduce heat to 350°F.
  4. Meanwhile, place 0.5 oz olive oil, onion and celery into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and sauté 5 min/212°F//speed 1.
  5. Add bread and sauté a further 2 min/212°F//speed 1. Add stock, cranberries, walnuts and reserved chopped herbs and stir through with spatula. Transfer stuffing mixture and vegetable mixture to prepared casserole dish, season with salt and pepper, to taste and stir well to combine.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes (350°F). Serve immediately.

Useful Items
