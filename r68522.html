Beef stroganoff

Ingredients

  • 4 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 1 brown onion (approx. 180 g), cut into halves
  • 30 g butter
  • 1 tbsp ground paprika
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper, to taste
  • 700 g beef tenderloin fillet, cut into strips (6 x 2 cm)
  • 1 pinch salt, to taste
  • 200 g button mushrooms, cut into quarters
  • 50 g tomato paste
  • 100 g white wine
  • 1 tbsp meat stock paste
  • 1 tbsp cornflour
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 120 g sour cream

Preparation

  1. Place fresh flat-leaf parsley into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place brown onion into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add butter, ground paprika and cayenne pepper and sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  4. Add tenderloin and salt and cook 10 min/100°C//speed 1, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  5. Add button mushrooms, tomato paste, white wine, meat stock paste, cornflour, Dijon mustard and sour cream and cook 10 min/100°C//speed 1, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  6. Allow to rest 5 minutes, then garnish with parsley and serve hot.
