Salt Cod with Spinach and Chickpeas

Ingredients

  • 200 g cornbread, broken up
  • 220 g olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 150 g onions, quartered
  • 500 g salt cod, presoaked, desalted and shredded (see tip)
  • 500 g frozen spinach, defrosted, water squeezed out
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 800 g tinned chickpeas, rinsed, drained

Preparation

  1. Place cornbread and 50 g oil in mixing bowl then grind 5 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place 2 garlic cloves, onions and 70 g oil in mixing bowl, chop 5 sec/speed 5 then sauté 5 min/120°C/speed 1.
  3. Add cod, spinach, salt and pepper then sauté 7 min/120°C//speed .
  4. Preheat oven to 200°C. Place half the chickpeas in a baking dish, cover with cod mixture and top with remaining chickpeas. Clean mixing bowl.
  5. Place remaining 1 garlic clove and remaining 100 g oil in mixing bowl, chop 3 sec/speed 8 then heat 3 min/120°C/speed 1. Pour over chickpeas, cover with reserved ground cornbread and bake for 20 minutes (200°C) or until golden brown.
  6. Serve warm.

Useful Items

baking dish, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • For homemade salt cod: Place 200 g table salt in a baking dish and place cod on top, skin-side down. Cover cod with remaining 300 g table salt then cover dish tightly with cling film. Refrigerate for 24-48 hours. After this time, wash all the salt off. Cod can be used immediately but will taste quite salty. Alternatively, soak in water for 12 hours before using.
