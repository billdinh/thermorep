Waffles with maple bacon

Ingredients

Maple bacon

  • 400 g streaky bacon
  • 2 tbsp pure maple syrup

Waffles

  • 280 g gluten free flour mix (see Tips)
  • 2 tsp gluten free baking powder (see Tips)
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 60 g white sugar
  • 320 g almond milk
  • 100 g vegetable oil
  • 3 eggs, separated
  • pure maple syrup, to serve

Preparation

Maple bacon

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C. Line a baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper.
  2. Place bacon rashers in a single layer onto prepared tray and bake for 10 minutes (190°C). Remove tray from oven and brush one side of bacon with syrup.
  3. Preheat grill. Grill bacon until crispy or as desired. Transfer into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and cover to keep warm.

Waffles

  1. Place flour mix, baking powder, salt and sugar into mixing bowl and mix 5 sec/speed 8. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place milk, oil and egg yolks into mixing bowl and mix 8 sec/speed 4.
  3. Add reserved flour mixture and mix 8 sec/speed 8. Transfer batter into a large bowl and set aside. Thoroughly clean and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg whites into mixing bowl and whip 2 min/speed 3 or until stiff peaks form. Remove butterfly whisk. Fold egg whites gently through reserved batter mixture.
  5. Preheat waffle iron. Pour batter (approx. 3 tablespoons per waffle) into waffle iron and cook according to manufacturer's instructions or until golden and crispy. Transfer waffle onto a plate and cover with kitchen towel to keep warm. Repeat with remaining batter (to make approx. 10 waffles in total).
  6. Serve waffles warm with Maple bacon and drizzled with extra maple syrup.
