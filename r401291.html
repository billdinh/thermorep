Chicken Crêpes with Poblano Chilli Salsa

Ingredients

Chicken

  • 1000 g water
  • 500 g chicken breasts, skinless, cut in pieces

Crêpes

  • 120 g wholemeal flour
  • 175 g almond milk
  • 125 g water
  • 2 medium eggs
  • 3 Tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Chilli Salsa

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 150 g onions, quartered
  • 250 g poblano chillies, halved, deseeded (see tip)
  • 30 g olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 1 chicken stock cube (for 0.5 l), crumbled
  • 300 g avocados, quartered
  • 600 g water

Preparation

Chicken

  1. Place water in mixing bowl, place Varoma dish into position and weigh in chicken. Cover Varoma and steam 25 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove Varoma and discard cooking water.
  2. Place steamed chicken in mixing bowl and shred 5 sec//speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.

Crêpes

  1. Place flour, almond milk, water, eggs, honey and baking powder in mixing bowl then mix 20 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and place in fridge for 30 minutes to prevent any bubbles forming. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl.
  2. Lightly grease a non-stick frying pan with oil and heat over a medium heat. Pour in 2 Tbsp crêpe mixture and cook for 1 minute on each side. Transfer to a plate then repeat with remaining batter.

Chilli Salsa

  1. Place garlic, onions, chillies and oil in mixing bowl, chop 5 sec/speed 5, then sauté 10 min/120°C/speed 1.
  2. Add chicken stock, avocados and water, blend 1 min/speed 10, then cook 10 min/100°C/speed 1. Fill each crêpe with reserved shredded chicken then pour over chilli salsa and serve hot.

Useful Items

non-stick frying pan, refrigerator, hob

Hints & Tricks

  • The poblano chilli is a mild chilli pepper from Puebla, Mexico. They are available from Mexican supermarkets or online. If not available, substitute with mild green chilli of choice.
  • Sprinkle with grated panela cheese or cheese of choice and garnish with coriander leaves before serving.
