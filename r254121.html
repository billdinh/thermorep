Salmon and Lemon Rice Salad

Ingredients

Dill Sauce

  • 6 oz sour cream
  • 1 oz freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp fresh dill, leaves only
  • 1 tsp capers
  • ¼ tsp salt

Rice and Salmon

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 ½ tsp salt, divided
  • 1 tsp peppercorns
  • 2 lemons, thin peel only, no pith
  • 2 oz green onions, trimmed and cut into pieces (2 in.)
  • 1 oz parsley, leaves and tender stems
  • 2 oz lemon juice, divided
  • ½ tsp ground turmeric
  • 9 oz long-grain white rice
  • 42 oz water
  • 9 oz asparagus, cut into pieces (1 in.)
  • 1 lemon, sliced (¼ in.)
  • 24 oz salmon fillets
  • 1 tbsp fresh dill, leaves only

Preparation

Dill Sauce

  1. Place sour cream, lemon juice, dill, capers and salt into mixing bowl and mix 5 sec/speed 3. Transfer to a bowl, cover with cling wrap and refrigerate until needed. Clean mixing bowl.

Rice and Salmon

  1. Place garlic, 1 tsp salt, peppercorns and lemon peel into mixing bowl and grind 3 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and grind a further 3 sec/speed 8.
  2. Add green onions, parsley, 1 oz lemon juice and turmeric and mix 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Insert simmering basket, weigh in rice and water and rinse 30 sec/speed 7. Add reserved parsley mixture to rice and mix through with spatula.
  4. Place Varoma into position and weigh in asparagus. Insert Varoma tray and arrange lemon slices and top with salmon. Season with ½ tsp salt and dill, secure Varoma lid and steam 20-25 min/Varoma/speed 4, until salmon is cooked through.
  5. Remove Varoma and simmering basket. Transfer steamed rice to a serving bowl and mix through asparagus and 1 oz lemon juice. Flake salmon into rice and toss through gently with spatula. Serve warm with reserved Dill Sauce.

Useful Items
