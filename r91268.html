Stuffed Peppers with Rice and Tomato Sauce

Ingredients

Stuffed Peppers with Rice

  • 2 ½ oz yellow onions, halved
  • 1 garlic clove (optional)
  • 8 - 12 oz ground beef
  • 2 slices white bread, cut into pieces
  • 1 oz mustard
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sweet paprika
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 large egg
  • 4 bell peppers, red, green or yellow
  • 43 oz water
  • 2 heaping tsp homemade vegetable stock paste
  • 8 oz basmati rice

Tomato Sauce

  • 1 ½ oz unsalted butter, cut into pieces
  • 1 ½ oz all-purpose flour
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes (approx. 15 oz)
  • 2 oz tomato purée (concentrated)
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp lemon juice

Preparation

Stuffed Peppers with Rice

  1. Place onions and garlic into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5.
  2. Add ground beef, bread, mustard, salt, paprika, black pepper and egg and knead 1 min 30 sec/.
  3. Cut the tops off the peppers and set aside. Remove all seeds and fill peppers with ground beef mixture and replace tops. Place stuffed peppers into Varoma dish.
  4. Place water and vegetable stock cubes into mixing bowl, insert simmering basket and weigh in rice. Place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 30 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove Varoma and remove simmering basket with spatula. Transfer rice and stuffed peppers into a serving dish and keep warm. Transfer cooking liquid into a bowl and set aside.

Tomato Sauce

  1. Place butter and flour into mixing bowl and sauté 3 min/212°F/speed 2.
  2. Add 7 oz reserved cooking liquid, tomatoes, tomato purée, salt, pepper, sugar and lemon juice and cook 5 min/212°F/speed 4. Serve Stuffed Peppers with rice and tomato sauce.

Hints & Tricks

  • Instead of ground beef use ground lamb or a mixture of ground pork and beef.
  • Depending on size of peppers, there may be leftover ground beef mixture. Form it into small balls and place with the peppers in Varoma.
  • The recipe can be halved to make two portions, but do not reduce the 43 oz water for steaming. Cooking time for the tomato sauce can be reduced by 1 minute.
