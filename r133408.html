Butterscotch Banana Sponge Pudding

Ingredients

Sponge

  • 85 g unsalted butter, soft, plus extra for greasing
  • 140 g caster sugar
  • 175 g self-raising flour
  • 2 bananas (approx. 240 g)
  • 150 g milk
  • 1 large egg
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Butterscotch Sauce

  • 50 g unsalted butter, diced
  • 130 g light brown sugar
  • 30 g golden syrup
  • 250 g water

Preparation

Sponge

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C and grease a deep oven-proof serving dish (approx. 30 cm x 20 cm).
  2. Place butter and sugar in mixing bowl then cream 30 sec/speed 4.
  3. Add flour, 1 banana, milk, egg, salt, cinnamon and vanilla extract then mix 15 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then mix again 15 sec/speed 4. Transfer to prepared dish then set aside. Clean mixing bowl and make sauce.

Butterscotch Sauce

  1. Place butter, sugar, golden syrup and water in mixing bowl then, with simmering basket in place of measuring cup, cook 10 min/110°C/speed 2. Meanwhile, cut remaining banana lengthways into 5 thin slices then gently lay on top of sponge mixture in dish.
  2. Pour sauce over banana and sponge in dish then bake for 35-40 minutes (180°C) until a skewer inserted in centre comes out clean.
  3. Serve hot - works well with cream or vanilla ice cream.

Useful Items

oven-proof dish (approx. 30 cm x 20 cm), skewer, oven

Hints & Tricks

Banana and butterscotch are a match made in heaven in this warming pudding. The sponge reveals a hidden pool of rich sauce making it perfect for a cold evening!
