Lentil, Cauliflower and Kale Salad

Ingredients

  • 10 oz cauliflower, cut into florets (approx. 6 florets)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 5 oz lacinato kale, cut into 2 in. pieces, stems removed (approx. 1 bunch)
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 16 oz Roma tomatoes, halved
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar, plus extra to garnish
  • 1 oz olive oil, plus extra to garnish
  • ½ oz fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tsp salt, to taste
  • 4 oz black lentils
  • 24 oz water
  • 3 oz feta cheese, drained and crumbled
  • freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Line baking sheet with foil and set aside.
  2. Toss cauliflower with 2 tbsp oil and place onto prepared baking sheet. Bake for 15 minutes (400°F) and set aside.
  3. Place kale into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a large serving bowl and set aside.
  4. Place garlic into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Add tomatoes, vinegar, 1 oz oil, basil and salt and chop 4 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a separate bowl and set aside.
  5. Place reserved cauliflower into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec//speed 4.5. Transfer to serving bowl with reserved kale and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.
  6. Insert simmering basket into mixing bowl and weigh in lentils. Rinse lentils under cold running water. Place lentils from simmering basket into mixing bowl. Add water and with simmering basket instead of measuring cup on mixing bowl lid cook 30 min/212°F//speed 1.
  7. Drain lentils through simmering basket. Pass under cold water to stop the cooking. Drain again. Transfer to large serving bowl and toss with reserved chopped kale, cauliflower and tomato mixture. Garnish with feta, a drizzle of oil, vinegar and pepper. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

aluminum foil, large serving bowl, baking sheet, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Lacinato kale is also known as bumpy-leaf, better known by its Italian translation cavolo nero, and also known as Tuscan Cabbage, Tuscan Kale, black cabbage and dinosaur kale.
