Aubergine dip (moutabal/baba ganoush)

Ingredients

  • 1000 g water
  • 1000 g aubergines, peeled, cut into cubes (3-4 cm)
  • 60 g lemon juice, freshly squeezed
  • 70 g tahini paste
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only

Preparation

  1. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish into position, weigh 700 g aubergines into Varoma dish. Insert Varoma tray and weigh remaining 300 g aubergines in it. Drizzle with 10 g lemon juice, close Varoma lid and cook 50 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove Varoma and set aside, remove Varoma lid and allow aubergines to cool down for 15 minutes. Empty mixing bowl.
  2. Place remaining 50 g lemon juice, tahini paste, garlic and salt into mixing bowl and mix 1 min/speed 6.
  3. Gently press the aubergines with a spoon to drain excess moisture, add aubergines into mixing bowl and blend 30 sec/speed 4. Transfer into a serving bowl and garnish with olive oil and parsley leaves.
