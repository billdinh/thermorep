Spelt Salad with Burmese Tofu

Ingredients

Tofu

  • 300 g dried chickpeas
  • 930 g water
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • olive oil, for sealing

Salad

  • 150 g pearled spelt, rinsed
  • 800 g water
  • 2 tsp fine sea salt
  • 70 g sun-dried tomatoes in oil, drained
  • 15 g fresh basil leaves
  • 10 g fresh parsley leaves
  • 1 Tbsp pickled capers
  • 1 pinch ground white pepper
  • 70 g olive oil
  • 1 Tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 100 g yellow cherry tomatoes, quartered
  • 50 g rocket salad
  • 100 g mixed lettuce
  • 50 g fresh baby spinach

Preparation

Tofu

  1. Place chickpeas in mixing bowl and grind 1 min/speed 10.
  2. Add 330 g water, salt and turmeric then mix 15 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then mix again 5 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Rinse mixing bowl.
  3. Place remaining 600 g water in mixing bowl and heat 8 min/120°C/speed 1.
  4. Add reserved ground chickpea mix and cook 8 min/100°C/speed 4. Meanwhile, line a large loaf tin (900 g/2 lb) with baking paper. Transfer mixture to prepared tin then pour a layer of olive oil over the top. Allow to cool then cover and place in fridge for 8 hours or until set. Clean mixing bowl.

Salad

  1. Place pearled spelt, water and 1 tsp salt in mixing bowl then cook 20-25 min/100°C//speed . Drain using simmering basket then transfer to a serving bowl. Rinse mixing bowl.
  2. Place sun-dried tomatoes, basil, parsley, capers, remaining 1 tsp salt and pepper in mixing bowl then chop 10 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add olive oil and balsamic vinegar then mix 5 sec/speed 7. Transfer to serving bowl with spelt and mix together well.
  4. Unmould set tofu and dice. Place serving bowl on mixing bowl lid. Weigh in 300 g diced tofu, cherry tomatoes, rocket, lettuce and spinach. Remove from lid and mix together gently then serve immediately.

Useful Items

loaf tin (900 g/2 lb), baking paper, cling film, serving bowl, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • The remaining tofu will keep in the fridge for 3-4 days.
