Strawberry Lemonade Cupcakes

Ingredients

Strawberry Cupcakes

  • 7 oz granulated sugar
  • 7 oz strawberries, stems removed and quartered
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 oz canola oil
  • 1 tsp freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • 7 oz all-purpose flour
  • 2 ¼ tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt

Lemonade Frosting

  • 2 lemons, peeled, thin peel only, no pith and juiced (2 oz), divided
  • 15 oz confectioners sugar
  • 6 oz unsalted butter, softened and cut into pieces

Preparation

Strawberry Cupcakes

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line a muffin pan with cupcake liners and set aside.
  2. Place sugar, strawberries, eggs, oil, lemon juice, nutmeg, flour, baking powder and salt into mixing bowl and mix 20 sec/speed 4.
  3. Transfer batter into prepared muffin pan, filling each liner ¾ full. Bake cupcakes for 18-20 minutes (350°F) or until lightly golden. Allow cupcakes to cool for 10 minutes before removing from muffin pan.

Lemonade Frosting

  1. Place lemon peel into mixing bowl and grate 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and grate a further 3 sec/speed 7. Add confectioners sugar, butter, and 2 oz lemon juice and mix 1 min/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix a further 20 sec/speed 4. Insert butterfly whisk and whip 1 min/speed 4. Remove butterfly whisk. Transfer frosting to a piping bag fitted with star tip. Pipe frosting onto cupcakes in swirl pattern, decorate to your liking and serve.

Useful Items

12 hole muffin pan, cupcake liners, piping bag with star tip, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • If the icing is too soft to pipe: refrigerate for a few minutes before frosting.
  • Refrigerate frosting if not using immediately, but bring it back to room temperature before using.
