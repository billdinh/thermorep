Neapolitan ice cream cake

Ingredients

Chocolate ice cream

  • 80 - 100 g dark chocolate (70% cocoa), cut into pieces (1-2 cm)
  • 20 - 50 g cocoa powder
  • 400 g full cream milk
  • 200 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 150 g sugar
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 1 pinch salt (optional)

Vanilla ice cream

  • 1 vanilla bean
  • 600 g full cream milk
  • 200 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 180 g white sugar
  • 6 egg yolks
  • 1 pinch salt, (optional)

Strawberry ice cream

  • 60 g sugar, to taste
  • 500 g strawberries, frozen and cut into pieces
  • 120 g pouring (whipping) cream

Cake topping

  • 125 g white chocolate, cut into pieces (1-2 cm)
  • 250 g strawberries, cut into halves

Preparation

Chocolate ice cream

  1. Place chocolate into mixing bowl and grate 8 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add remaining chocolate ice cream ingredients and cook 8 min/90°C/speed 2, then blend 5 sec/speed 4 -7, increasing speed gradually from speed 4 to speed 7. Pour mixture, up to 4 cm high, into shallow freezable containers and cover with plastic wrap. Allow mixture to cool completely, then place into freezer for 10 hours. Thoroughly clean and dry mixing bowl. Continue with vanilla ice cream.

Vanilla ice cream

  1. Split vanilla bean in half lengthways and scrape seeds. Place seeds, vanilla bean and all remaining vanilla ice cream ingredients into mixing bowl and cook 8 min/90°C/speed 2. Carefully remove vanilla bean pod.
  2. Pour mixture, up to 4 cm high, into shallow freezable containers and cover with plastic wrap. Allow mixture to cool completely, then place into freezer for a minimum of 10 hours. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Assembly

  1. Line the bottom of a springform cake tin (23 cm) with baking paper and place into freezer.
  2. Place chocolate and vanilla ice cream into refrigerator for 15 minutes.
  3. Cut chocolate ice cream into pieces (4 cm). Place half the chocolate pieces into mixing bowl and crush 20 sec/speed 6, then mix 10 sec/speed 4, with aid of spatula.
  4. Remove prepared tin from freezer and transfer chocolate ice cream into base of prepared tin.
  5. Place remaining chocolate pieces into mixing bowl and crush 20 sec/speed 6, then mix 10 sec/speed 4, with aid of spatula. Transfer into prepared tin, ensuring ice cream is level. Place into freezer for 15 minutes. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  6. Cut vanilla ice cream into pieces (4 cm). Place half the vanilla pieces into mixing bowl and crush 20 sec/speed 6, then mix 10 sec/speed 4, with aid of spatula. Transfer ice cream into a bowl and set aside.
  7. Place remaining vanilla pieces into mixing bowl and crush 20 sec/speed 6, then mix 10 sec/speed 4, with aid of spatula.
  8. Remove reserved chocolate ice cream from freezer. Spread vanilla ice cream over chocolate ice cream, ensuring ice cream is level. Place back into freezer. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Strawberry ice cream

  1. Place sugar into mixing bowl and mill 15 sec/speed 10.
  2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Add frozen strawberries and crush 15 sec/speed 8.
  3. Add cream and mix 40 sec/speed 5, with aid of spatula. Transfer into cake tin over vanilla ice cream and place back into freezer to set overnight. Thoroughly clean and dry mixing bowl.

Cake topping

  1. Place white chocolate into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 8.
  2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then melt 3 min/50°C/speed 1.
  3. Working quickly, place ice cream cake onto a serving plate, then release cake from tin. Pour white chocolate over cake, allowing it to drizzle over edges.
  4. Decorate with strawberries and serve.

Useful Items

plastic wrap, shallow freezable containers (e.g. plastic, silicone), springform cake tin (23cm), baking paper, bowl, serving plate, freezer, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Save egg whites to make meringues. You can also freeze them to use at a later date.
  • Replace white chocolate topping with dark or milk chocolate.
  • You can decorate the cake with any ingredients you have on hand. Try using chocolate curls, chocolate buttons, other types of berries or lollies.
