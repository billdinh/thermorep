Welsh Rarebit with Branston Pickle

Ingredients

  • 6 slices bread, cut thickly (1.5 cm), then toasted
  • 350 g Cheddar cheese, mature, diced (1.5 cm)
  • 1 tsp plain flour
  • 4 egg yolks, from medium eggs
  • 2 tsp English mustard powder
  • 1 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 90 g Branston Pickle, or similar chunky pickle
  • 75 g ale
  • 20 g butter

Preparation

  1. Preheat grill to 200°C. Line a baking tray with baking paper and arrange toasted bread slices on it.
  2. Place Cheddar and flour in mixing bowl and grate 3 sec/speed 8.
  3. Add egg yolks, mustard powder, Worcestershire sauce, pickle and ale then mix 5 sec//speed 4.
  4. Butter the toast then spread cheese mixture evenly over each slice. Grill for 5 minutes (200°C) until brown and bubbling. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

baking tray and paper, grill

Hints & Tricks

  • The bread can be toasted using the grill. Toast for 5 minutes (200°C) or until brown, turning halfway through.
  • Serve with a side salad.
  • Few things say British more than Branston Pickle, and it's most popular partner is that other British classic - Cheddar cheese. We've paired them up for you in this quick and tasty lunch.
  • The pickle can be left out for a smoother result.
