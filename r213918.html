Toum (garlic dip)

Ingredients

  • 400 g grapeseed oil
  • 40 g lemon juice
  • 75 g garlic cloves (approx. 25 cloves)
  • 1 tsp salt

Preparation

  1. Place oil and lemon juice into mixing bowl and emulsify 1 min/speed 4. Transfer into a jug and set aside.
  2. Place garlic cloves, salt and 50 g of the reserved oil emulsion into mixing bowl and blend 1 min 30 sec/speed 5.
  3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and blend 1 min 30 sec/speed 5.
  4. Scrape down base and sides of mixing bowl with spatula and blend for a further 1 min/speed 6. Repeat if needed to achieve a paste consistency.

Useful Items

jug, sealable container, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • You can serve this dip with a variety of meat or vegetable dishes.
  • This dip can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.
