Brandy butter

Ingredients

  • 40 g brandy
  • 300 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 250 g water, chilled
  • 120 g icing sugar
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
312 kJ / 74 kcal
Protein
0.3 g
Carbohydrates
6.3 g
Fat
5 g

Preparation

  1. Place a jug onto mixing bowl lid, then weigh brandy into it. Set jug with brandy aside.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Place cream into mixing bowl and whip 1-3 min/speed 4, or until solids and liquids have separated. Remove butterfly whisk. Strain liquids from butter solids through simmering basket.
  3. Place water and butter solids into mixing bowl and mix 5-10 sec/speed 4. Liquid and solids should again separate.
  4. Strain liquid from butter solids through simmering basket a second time so that only butter remains. Squeeze as much liquid out of the butter as possible.
  5. Place butter and icing sugar into mixing bowl and mix 30 sec/speed 3.
  6. Mix 1 min/speed 3, pouring brandy through hole in mixing bowl lid 1 teaspoon at a time. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl, then mix 20 sec/speed 4.
  7. Transfer into a sealable container or roll into desired shape (see Tips) and place into refrigerator until ready to use.

Useful Items

large jug, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Roll butter into a sausage shape to make butter rounds or form into a rectangle shape to store in the refrigerator or freezer.
  • For butter that stays fresher for longer, wash a second time using 500 g chilled water in step 4 to ensure butter is completely clean.
  • The fresher your cream, the longer it will take for the liquids and solids to separate. Cream closer to its expiry date will separate faster.
  • This butter is great served on slices of the Christollen or melted over the Sago plum pudding.
