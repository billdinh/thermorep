Curry Bread Rolls

Ingredients

  • 15 g sugar
  • 350 g strong white bread flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 115 g water
  • 1 tsp dried instant yeast
  • 20 g butter, plus extra for greasing
  • 25 g milk, plus extra for glazing
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 1 - 2 Tbsp sunflower seeds

Preparation

  1. Place 5  g sugar, 50  g flour, 50 g water and yeast in mixing bowl then warm 1 min/37°C/speed 2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add remaining 10 g sugar, remaining 300 g flour, remaining 65 g water, butter, milk, egg, salt and curry powder then knead 4 min/. Meanwhile, grease a large bowl. Transfer dough to prepared bowl, cover with cling film and leave to rise in a warm place until doubled in size (approx. 1-2 hours).
  3. Line a baking tray with baking paper. Divide dough into 4 balls and place on prepared baking tray, slightly flatten then cover and leave in a warm place for a further 30 minutes. Towards end of resting time, preheat oven to 180°C.
  4. Brush buns with milk and sprinkle tops with sunflower seeds. Bake for 20-25 minutes (180°C) until golden on top. Transfer to a wire rack and allow to cool. Serve with burger of choice.

Useful Items

cling film, large bowl, baking tray and paper, wire rack, pastry brush, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Store rolls in an airtight container after cooling if not eating the same day as baking.
