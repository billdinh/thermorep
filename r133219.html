Spiced Autumn Fruit Cobbler

Ingredients

Fruit Filling

  • ½ orange, thin peelings of skin
  • 100 g vanilla sugar
  • 400 g cooking apples (e.g. Bramley) peeled, cored, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 400 g pears, peeled, cored, cut in pieces (4 cm)
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2 cloves
  • 2 star anise
  • 200 g blackberries

Topping

  • 80 g unsalted butter, diced, plus extra for greasing
  • 35 g crystallised ginger, diced
  • 170 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 75 g granulated sugar
  • ¾ tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • ½ tsp coarse salt
  • 60 g buttermilk, plus extra for brushing
  • 1 Tbsp Demerara sugar, for sprinkling

Preparation

Fruit Filling

  1. Place orange peelings and vanilla sugar in mixing bowl then grind 10 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add apples, pears, cinnamon stick, cloves and star anise then cook 10 min/100°C//speed . Meanwhile, grease a rectangular baking dish (approx. 26 cm x 24 cm x 5 cm). Transfer contents of mixing bowl to prepared dish then scatter blackberries over surface. Clean mixing bowl and make topping.

Topping

  1. Preheat oven to 170°C. Place ginger in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add flour, sugar, baking powder, bicarbonate of soda and salt then mix 15 sec/speed 3.
  3. Add butter and mix 5 sec/speed 7.
  4. Add buttermilk and mix 10 sec/speed 3. Tip out onto a generously floured work surface and flatten to 1.5 cm thick. Using a cookie cutter (5 cm), cut out preferred shapes of dough and place on fruit, with approx. 5 cm between each. Re-roll any trimmings to cut further shapes.
  5. Brush shapes lightly with buttermilk then sprinkle with Demerara sugar. Bake for 35-45 minutes (170°C) until cobbler topping is cooked and well browned.
  6. Serve hot - delicious with cream, custard or ice cream.

Useful Items

rectangular baking dish (26 cm x 24 cm x 5 cm), cookie cutter, rolling pin, pastry brush, oven

Hints & Tricks

This is the ultimate warming, autumnal dessert, with the cinnamon, cloves and ginger adding extra warmth. You can also try it with different fruits or berries depending on what you have available.
