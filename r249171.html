Barley, bacon and chorizo soup

Ingredients

  • 200 g bacon, cut into cubes (2 cm)
  • 200 g chorizo, cut into slices (1 cm)
  • 1 brown onion, cut into halves
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 2 - 4 tsp dried oregano, to taste
  • 3 celery stalks, cut into pieces (1 cm)
  • 2 carrots, cut into pieces (1 cm)
  • 80 g tomato paste
  • 1 - 2 tbsp Chicken stock paste (see Tips)
  • 1100 g water
  • 200 g pearl barley
  • salt, to taste
  • ground black pepper, to taste
  • 3 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, for garnishing

Preparation

  1. Place bacon and chorizo into mixing bowl and cook 5 min/Varoma//speed 1. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place onion, garlic and oregano into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 5.
  3. Add celery, carrot, tomato paste, stock paste, water and barley and cook 30 min/100°C//speed 1.
  4. Add reserved bacon and chorizo, salt and pepper, then stir through with aid of spatula and cook 10 min/100°C//speed 1. Divide between serving bowls and garnish with parsley to serve.

Useful Items

bowl, serving bowls

Hints & Tricks

  • Please refer to The Basic Cookbook or the Everyday Cookbook for the Chicken stock paste recipe.
  • Serve with crusty bread, if desired.
