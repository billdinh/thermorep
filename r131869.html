Pizza with Courgettes

Ingredients

Dough

  • 220 g water
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 tsp dried instant yeast
  • 30 g extra virgin olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 400 g strong white bread flour, plus extra for flouring
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt

Topping

  • 250 g mozzarella, drained, cut in pieces
  • 60 g spring onions, white part only, cut in pieces (3 cm)
  • 40 g extra virgin olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 300 g courgettes, diced (1 cm)
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 250 g tinned chopped tomatoes
  • 6 - 8 leaves fresh basil
  • 1 tsp dried marjoram

Preparation

Dough

  1. Place 50 g water, sugar and yeast in mixing bowl then warm 2 min/37°C/speed 2. Meanwhile, grease a large bowl with oil.
  2. Add remaining 170 g water, flour, oil and salt then knead 2 min/. Transfer dough to prepared bowl, cover with cling film then set aside to rise in a warm place until doubled in size (approx. 1-2 hours). Meanwhile, make filling.

Topping

  1. Place mozzarella in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place spring onions in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add 20 g oil, courgettes, ½ tsp salt and pepper then cook 10 min/Varoma/speed . Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Towards end of rising time, preheat oven to 220°C and generously grease a large baking tray with oil.
  5. On a floured work surface, roll out dough to a circle (approx. Ø 30 cm). Transfer pizza base to prepared baking tray.
  6. Place tomatoes, ½ tsp salt, basil, marjoram, and remaining 20 g oil in mixing bowl then chop 6 sec/speed 4. Spread sauce over base then add reserved cooked courgettes and reserved chopped mozzarella.
  7. Bake for 15-20 minutes (220°C) until crisp and golden. Serve hot, cut in slices.

Useful Items

large bowl, cling film, large baking tray, rolling pin, oven
