Sirloin Steak with New Potatoes and Chimichurri

Ingredients

Chimichurri Sauce

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 50 g shallots
  • 10 g fresh coriander
  • 10 g fresh parsley
  • 1 Tbsp dried oregano
  • 100 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 30 g white wine vinegar
  • ½ tsp dried chilli flakes
  • ½ lemon, juice only

Sirloin Steak with New Potatoes

  • 1200 g water
  • 2 ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 700 g new potatoes, unpeeled, quartered
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • ½ tsp ground cumin
  • ½ tsp ground coriander
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper
  • 1000 g beef sirloin steaks

Preparation

Chimichurri Sauce

  1. Place garlic cloves, shallots, coriander, parsley and oregano in mixing bowl then chop 2 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add oil, vinegar, chilli flakes and lemon juice then mix 8 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.

Sirloin Steak with New Potatoes

  1. Place water and 1½ tsp salt in mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket and add new potatoes to it then cook 25 min/100°C/speed 4. Meanwhile, place olive oil, ground cumin, ground coriander, 1 tsp salt and cayenne pepper in a small bowl and mix well. Rub both sides of the steaks with mixture. Heat a griddle pan and cook steaks for 3-4 minutes on each side or until cooked as desired. Remove to a board and leave to rest for a couple of minutes.
  2. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and transfer new potatoes to a serving dish. Slice steaks on the diagonal in thin pieces and place in a serving dish. Serve steaks and new potatoes immediately with chimichurri sauce to spoon over.
