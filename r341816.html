Thai green broccoli and spinach soup

Ingredients

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 eschalot, cut into halves
  • 1 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 1 stalk lemongrass, tough outer edge removed, core bruised and cut into thirds
  • 1 fresh long green chilli, trimmed, deseeded if preferred and cut into halves
  • ½ tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tbsp tamari, gluten free (see Tips)
  • 6 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves and stalks only, cut into thirds
  • 1 broccoli, broken into florets and stalks cut into thin slices
  • 180 g fresh baby spinach leaves
  • 400 g coconut milk
  • 500 g filtered water
  • 1 tsp stock paste (see Tips)
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • fresh coriander leaves, for garnishing
  • 1 spring onion/shallot, cut into slices, for garnishing

Preparation

  1. Place garlic, eschalot, ginger, lemongrass, chilli, cumin, tamari and coriander into mixing bowl and chop 1 min/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add broccoli and blend 5 sec/speed 5, mixing with aid of spatula.
  3. Add spinach, coconut milk, water and stock paste and cook 15 min/100°C/speed 2. Blend 1 min/speed 4-9, increasing speed gradually from speed 4 to speed 9. Stir through lime juice, with aid of spatula. Garnish with coriander leaves and spring onion/shallot to serve.
