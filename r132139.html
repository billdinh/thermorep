Cucumber and mint raita

Ingredients

  • 2 Lebanese cucumbers, cut into halves lengthways, deseeded if preferred, and cut into thirds
  • 1 tsp salt, plus extra to taste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 sprigs fresh mint (approx. 20 g), leaves only
  • 400 g Greek-style natural yoghurt
  • 1 tsp lemon juice (optional)
  • 1 - 2 pinches ground black pepper, to taste
  • ¼ tsp Kashmiri chilli powder
Nutrition

3.2 g

Preparation

  1. Place cucumber into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 6. Sprinkle with salt and transfer into simmering basket to drain for 20 minutes. Rinse and dry mixing bowl.
  2. Rinse and drain cucumber mixture, then wrap in paper towel or kitchen towel and squeeze to remove excess liquid. Set cucumber aside.
  3. Place cumin seeds into mixing bowl and roast 2 min/Varoma/speed 1. Allow to cool in mixing bowl for 5 minutes.
  4. Add mint and chop 10 sec/speed 7.
  5. Add yoghurt and lemon juice (optional) and mix 15 sec/speed 4.
  6. Add salt, to taste, pepper, chilli powder and reserved cucumber and mix 10 sec/speed 2. Serve immediately or transfer into refrigerator until ready to serve. 

Useful Items

paper towel, kitchen towel

Hints & Tricks

  • You can make this raita up to 3 days before serving; it's best served chilled.
