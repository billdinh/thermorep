Moroccan chicken with couscous

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ tbsp garam masala
  • 3 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 ½ tsp salt
  • ¾ tsp ground black pepper
  • 800 g skinless chicken thigh fillets, cut into pieces (3-4 cm)
  • 250 g couscous
  • 50 g sunflower oil
  • 500 g water
  • 10 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 80 g brown onion, cut into halves
  • 60 g pitted green olives
  • 1 tsp Chicken stock paste (see Tip)
  • 1 lemon, zest only, no white pith
  • 60 g lemon juice

Preparation

  1. Place garam masala, turmeric, salt and pepper into a bowl and stir to combine.
  2. Place half of the spice mixture onto a plate and toss chicken pieces in mixture until completely coated. Place chicken into the Varoma dish and set aside. Set remaining spice mixture aside.
  3. Place a separate bowl onto the mixing bowl lid and weigh couscous, 30 g of the oil and 250 g of the water into it. Stir to combine using a fork and set aside.
  4. Place parsley into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  5. Place garlic and onion into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  6. Add remaining 20 g oil and remaining spice mixture and cook 4 min/Varoma/speed 1 . Meanwhile, place soaked couscous onto the Varoma tray, then use a fork to fluff and separate grains.
  7. Add remaining 250 g water, green olives, stock paste and lemon zest and juice into mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish and tray into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 23-25 min/Varoma/speed 1, until chicken is cooked through.
  8. Remove Varoma and carefully transfer cooked chicken into the sauce in mixing bowl. Stir with spatula to combine. Divide couscous between serving bowls, then top with chicken and sauce. Garnish with reserved parsley before serving.
