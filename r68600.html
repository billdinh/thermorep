Asian-style fish fillets

Ingredients

  • 2 cm cube fresh ginger, peeled
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 3 sprigs fresh coriander, stems and leaves separated
  • 2 ½ cm fresh lemongrass, white part only
  • 1 long green chilli, deseeded if preferred
  • 600 g white fish fillets
  • 400 g jasmine rice
  • 1000 g water
  • 500 g mixed vegetables (e.g. zucchini, broccoli, capsicum), cut into slices
  • fresh bamboo shoots (optional)
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
2369 kJ / 564 kcal
Protein
39 g
Carbohydrates
88 g
Fat
3 g

Preparation

  1. Place fresh fresh ginger, garlic clove, coriander stems, lemongrass and long green chilli into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then lightly sauté 2 min/100°C/speed 2. Place fish fillets onto Varoma tray and use mixture in mixing bowl to coat each fillet liberally. Set Varoma tray with coated fish aside.
  2. Place jasmine rice into simmering basket and rinse until water runs clear. Set rice aside to drain.
  3. Place water into mixing bowl and insert simmering basket with rice. Place mixed vegetables into Varoma dish. Insert Varoma tray with fish into Varoma dish. Cook 16-18 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove simmering basket and set rice aside.
  4. Replace Varoma and steam a further 3-5 min/Varoma/speed 1, until fish and vegetables are cooked to your preference.
  5. Serve fish, rice and vegetables immediately, garnished with fresh bamboo shoots (optional).
