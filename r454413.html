Gluten-Free Seed Bread

Ingredients

  • non-stick cooking spray
  • 4 oz long-grain brown rice
  • 4 oz white basmati rice
  • 2 ½ oz millet
  • 5 oz cassava flour
  • 1 ½ tsp salt
  • 2 tsp xanthan gum
  • 1 packet/envelope dried instant yeast
  • 1 oz brown sugar
  • 1 ½ oz mixed seeds, (flax, sesame , pepitas)
  • 34 oz water, divided
  • 1 oz extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 2 large eggs
Nutrition
per 1 portion

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven 350°F. Grease a loaf pan (8 in. x 4 in.) with non-stick cooking spray and set aside.
  2. Place brown rice, basmati rice and millet into mixing bowl and grind 2 min/speed 9.
  3. Add cassava flour, salt, xanthan gum, yeast, brown sugar, mixed seeds, 14 oz water, olive oil, vinegar and eggs and mix 15 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix a further 15 sec/speed 4. Transfer dough to prepared loaf pan and smooth top with spatula. Place into Varoma dish (see Tip).
  4. Place 20 oz water into mixing bowl. Set Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and proof 35 min/150°F/speed 2, until almost doubled in size. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  5. Bake for 40 minutes (350°F). Rotate pan and bake an additional 20 minutes (350°F). Allow to cool in pan 5 minutes then transfer to a wire rack. Serve warm or allow to cool completely before transferring to an airtight container until ready to serve.

Useful Items

loaf pan (8 in. x 4 in.), wire rack, airtight container, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Grease your spatula to help smooth top of dough.
  • All pans differ in size and shape. Ensure the pan you're using fits into Varoma dish and Varoma lid closes properly
