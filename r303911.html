Cashew cheddar cheese

Ingredients

  • extra virgin olive oil, for greasing
  • 3 tsp agar agar (see Tips)
  • 170 g filtered water
  • 100 g plant-based milk of choice
  • 100 g raw cashews
  • 2 ½ tbsp nutritional yeast (see Tips)
  • 2 tsp apple cider vinegar
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp onion powder
  • ¾ tsp salt
  • 40 g shiro (white) miso paste
  • 1 - 2 pinches ground turmeric
  • 1 pinch paprika
  • 2 tsp dried chives

Preparation

  1. Grease a small loaf tin (13 x 7 cm) or 2 ramekins (8 cm) and set aside.
  2. Place agar agar and water into mixing bowl and heat 5 min/100°C/speed 3.
  3. Add all remaining ingredients and cook 8 min/98°C/speed 4, then blend 1 min/speed 4-9, increasing speed gradually from speed 4 to speed 9 or until smooth. Transfer into prepared tin or ramekins and set aside to cool (approx. 45 minutes). Cover and place into refrigerator until firm (approx. 2 hours), then serve as desired.

Useful Items

small loaf tin (13 x 7 cm), ramekins (8 cm), plastic wrap, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Smoked cashew cheddar cheese: replace paprika with smoked paprika, add chives (optional) and add 1 teaspoon liquid smoke (available at gourmet or specialist food stores).
  • Pepper Jack cashew cheese: replace apple cider vinegar with pickled jalapeño liquid. Before transferring into loaf tin, add 1-2 tablespoons finely chopped pickled red and green jalapeños, chives (optional) and 1-2 pinches of chilli flakes and blend 4 sec/speed 4. Proceed as per recipe.
  • With a cheesy, umami flavour, nutritional yeast contains all nine essential amino acids, is a great source of B vitamins and is often fortified with vitamin B12 (important for those following a vegan diet). You can find nutritional yeast at health food stores or online.
  • Agar agar yields the best results when combined with hot liquids, then transferred into the refrigerator to set. You can find agar agar powder at health food stores, some Asian food stores and online.
  • You can store this cheese in the refrigerator for up to 1 week.
