Pear leathers

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe pears, peeled, cored and cut into cubes (3 cm) (approx. 500 g pear flesh)
  • 2 tsp lemon juice
  • &frac18; tsp ground cinnamon (optional)
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
154 kJ / 36 kcal
Protein
0.2 g
Carbohydrates
6.2 g
Fat
0 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 50°C. Line 2 large baking trays (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place pears and lemon juice into mixing bowl and cook 5 min/100°C/speed 3.
  3. Add cinnamon (optional) and blend 30 sec/speed 8.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then cook 20 min/90°C/speed 2, without measuring cup.
  5. Transfer mixture onto prepared baking trays. Using an offset spatula, spread mixture evenly across baking paper (2-3 mm thickness). Mixture will thin out during baking. Bake for 6-8 hours (50°C) or until pureé is dry to the touch and pliable (see Tips). Trim ends and, using scissors, cut dried pear mixture into 8 strips (8 cm length), then roll up to serve.

Useful Items

baking tray (30 x 40 cm), baking paper, offset spatula, scissors, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Cooking times will vary depending on your oven and the thickness of your pureé. If after 5 hours of baking, your pureé is still quite wet to touch, increase your oven temperature to 60°C and continue drying until dry to the touch.
  • These pear leathers are delicious served with a little blue cheese or as part of a cheese platter.
