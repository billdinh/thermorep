Basic vanilla cake

Ingredients

  • 100 g unsalted butter, cut into pieces and softened, plus extra for greasing
  • 340 g plain flour
  • 270 g sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 250 g full cream milk
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs, room temperature

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Grease and line 2 round cake tins (18-20 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place butter, flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, milk and vanilla into mixing bowl and beat 1 min 30 sec/speed 4.
  3. Scrape down sides and base of mixing bowl with spatula and mix 1 min 30 sec/speed 4.
  4. Add eggs and beat 3 min/speed 3.5. Divide batter evenly between prepared cake tins, tap cakes 2-3 times on work surface to release any air bubbles, then bake for 30-35 minutes (180°C) or until golden brown and a wooden skewer inserted into the centre of the cakes comes out clean. Allow to cool in tins for 5 minutes, then transfer onto wire racks to cool completely. Frost and decorate as desired (see Tips).

Useful Items

2 round cake tins (20 cm), baking paper, wooden skewer, wire rack, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Gluten free vanilla cake: For a gluten free cake, replace 340 g plain flour with 340 g gluten free plain flour.
  • Basic chocolate cake: For a chocolate cake, reduce plain flour by 40 g and add 40 g Dutch cocoa powder with flour.
  • You can frost your cake with Buttercream icing or Marshmallow icing (please refer to the recipes on this recipe chip).
  • This cake can be made up to 3 days in advance. Wrap well in plastic wrap and store in a cool   place or in the refrigerator.
  • This cake can be frozen for up to 1 month. Thaw completely before frosting and decorating.
  • If using this cake for a layered cake, 3D cake or a novelty cake, it is best to store the cake in the refrigerator, then shape and assemble the cake when it's cold and firm.
