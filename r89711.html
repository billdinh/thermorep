Pancetta-wrapped apricot stuffing roll

Ingredients

  • 26 slices flat pancetta (approx. 350 g), thick cut and rind removed
  • 250 g day old bread, torn into pieces (3 cm)
  • 8 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley (approx. 15 g), leaves only
  • 3 fresh sage leaves
  • 2 brown onions, cut into halves
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 80 g butter
  • 12 dried apricots (approx. 70 g), cut into pieces
  • 50 g dried cranberries
  • ¼ tsp sea salt, to taste
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper, to taste
  • ½ tsp ground allspice
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp apricot jam

Preparation

  1. Cut 6 of the pancetta slices into pieces, then transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Line 2 baking trays with 2 large rectangular pieces of aluminium foil (approx. 30 x 40 cm). Placing the longer edge of the aluminium foil in front of you, lay 10 of the pancetta slices vertically down the piece of foil, overlapping each slice of pancetta slightly with the next. Repeat with remaining piece of aluminium foil and remaining pancetta slices. Set pancetta-lined foil pieces aside.
  3. Place bread into mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place parsley, sage, onion, garlic, butter and reserved pancetta pieces into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then repeat chopping 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula again, then sauté 5 min/Varoma/speed 1. Set mixing bowl aside and allow to cool slightly (approx. 10-15 minutes).
  5. Add all remaining ingredients, including reserved breadcrumbs, and combine 10 sec//speed 4. Transfer into a ceramic or glass bowl to cool completely (15 minutes).
  6. Preheat oven to 180°C. Once stuffing mixture has cooled, place half of the stuffing mixture horizontally down the middle of the pancetta on 1 of the aluminium sheets. Wrap the pancetta ends over the stuffing to enclose, then wrap aluminium foil around the pancetta to form a sausage shape. Repeat with remaining stuffing and second sheet of pancetta-lined aluminium foil.
  7. Bake for 40 minutes (180°C), or until pancetta is crispy and coloured. Serve warm.

Useful Items

2 baking trays (40 x 35 cm), aluminium foil, ceramic or glass bowl, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • This recipe can be made in advance and frozen. Place the stuffing roll into the freezer after rolling and wrapping in aluminium foil in step 6. Allow to thaw before proceeding as per recipe to bake in step 7.
  • Bacon-wrapped apricot stuffing roll: substitute the pancetta slices with 13 middle bacon rashers. Cut 3 of the bacon rashers into pieces and set aside in step 1. Use 5 bacon rashers to line each sheet of aluminium foil in step 2 and proceed as per recipe.
