Chocolate tofu mousse pots

Ingredients

  • 120 g dairy free chocolate, broken into pieces
  • 30 g cocoa powder
  • 70 g almond milk
  • 100 g caster sugar
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 300 g silken tofu, drained
  • 50 g frozen raspberries, for decorating (optional)
  • 1 sprig fresh mint leaves, for decorating (optional)

Preparation

  1. Place chocolate, cocoa powder, almond milk, sugar and salt into mixing bowl and cook 2 min/100°C/speed 2, or until chocolate melts. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place tofu into mixing bowl and blend 15 sec/speed 4, until smooth. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add reserved chocolate mixture and mix 10 sec/speed 4.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix 5 sec/speed 4.
  5. Divide mixture between 4 small serving glasses (approx. 150 g) and place into refrigerator to chill (approx. 1 hour). Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  6. Place frozen raspberries (optional) into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec//speed 4.
  7. Top each chocolate mousse with raspberry caviar and decorate with mint leaves (optional). Serve immediately.
