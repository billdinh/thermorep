Breakfast Sausage Pizzas

Ingredients

Sauce

  • ½ oz dried mushrooms
  • 4 oz red onions, cut into pieces
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 9 oz tomatoes, cut into pieces
  • ¼ tsp salt

Toppings

  • 2 oz mozzarella, cubed (1 in.)
  • 2 oz cheddar cheese, cubed (1 in.)
  • 8 oz uncooked breakfast sausage, casing removed, crumbled or cut into pieces

Dough

  • 11 ½ oz all-purpose flour, plus extra to dust
  • 7 ½ oz water
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 4 oz red onions, thinly sliced half-moons, divided
  • 6 eggs, divided
  • Italian parsley, to garnish

Preparation

Sauce

  1. Place mushrooms into mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 8.
  2. Add onions, garlic and oil and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and cook 2 min/250°F/speed 1.
  3. Add tomatoes and salt, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 10 min/250°F/speed 1. Remove simmering basket, insert measuring cup and blend 4 sec/speed 8. Transfer sauce to a bowl and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.

Toppings

  1. Place mozzarella and cheddar cheese into mixing bowl and grate 3 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place sausage into mixing bowl and cook 4 min/250°F//speed 0.5. Mix well with spatula and cook a further 4 min/250°F//speed 1. Line a bowl with paper towels. Transfer cooked sausage into bowl and let drain. Clean mixing bowl.

Dough

  1. Place flour, water, baking powder and salt into mixing bowl and mix 5 sec/speed 5, then knead 2 min/.
  2. Transfer dough to a lightly floured surface. Cut dough into six equal pieces and shape each piece into a ball. Cover with a damp paper towel and let rest 10 minutes. Meanwhile preheat oven to 425°F and line two baking sheets with parchment.
  3. Roll out each piece of dough into a circle (approx. 6 in.). Transfer dough circles onto prepared baking sheets and brush with oil. Prebake pizza dough for 5 minutes (425°F) before adding toppings.
  4. Evenly divide and spread reserved sauce over each pizza dough, leaving a ½ inch border. Top with onions, reserved cheese and reserved sausage. Crack one egg directly onto each pizza, nestled into toppings. Bake for 5-7 minutes (425°F) until eggs are set to taste and pizza edges are puffed and lightly golden. Let rest 3-5 minutes before slicing. Garnish with chopped parsley and serve hot.

Useful Items

paper towels, parchment paper, rolling pin, baking sheets, pastry brush, oven
