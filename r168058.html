Lime Yogurt Berry Mousse

Ingredients

Mixed Berry Sauce

  • 9 oz fresh mixed berries
  • 4 oz sugar
  • ½ oz freshly squeezed lemon juice

Lime Yogurt Mousse

  • 1 lime, thin peel only, no pith, plus juice, divided
  • 3 oz ultrafine sugar, divided
  • &frac18; tsp salt
  • 2 oz lime juice
  • 1 tsp unflavored gelatin powder
  • 4 oz plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 oz heavy whipping cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 egg whites, from large eggs
  • ¼ tsp cream of tartar
  • fresh mixed berries, to garnish

Preparation

Mixed Berry Sauce

  1. Place berries, sugar and lemon juice into mixing bowl and increasing speed gradually blend 30 sec/speed 5-10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Cook 10 min/195°F/speed 2. Strain through a sieve to remove and discard seeds. Evenly divide Mixed Berry Sauce between 8 ramekins, cover with cling wrap and freeze 2 hours until set. Clean and dry mixing bowl thoroughly.

Lime Yogurt Mousse

  1. Place lime peel, 0.5 oz sugar and salt into mixing bowl and zest 1 min/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add lime juice and gelatin by sprinkling around edge of mixing bowl avoiding the blade. Cook 1 min/195°F/speed 3. Allow to cool 5 minutes or until temperature reads 100°F.
  3. Insert butterfly whisk. Add yogurt, cream and vanilla and mix 30 sec/speed 4. Remove butterfly whisk. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl and butterfly whisk.
  4. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg whites, 2.5 oz sugar and cream of tartar into mixing bowl and without measuring cup whisk 8 min/160°F/speed 3.
  5. Add reserved yogurt mixture and whip 30 sec/speed 3. Divide Lime Yogurt Mousse evenly over Mixed Berry Sauce in 8 chilled ramekins. Cover with cling wrap and chill 6-8 hours until set. Garnish with mixed berries before serving.

Useful Items

cling wrap, ramekins, fine mesh sieve, freezer

Hints & Tricks

  • Mixed berries can be fresh or frozen e.g. 3 oz strawberries, 1 oz blackberries, 3.5 oz blueberries, 0.5 oz pomegranate arils and 1 oz cherries (pits removed).
