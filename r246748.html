Nasi goreng

Ingredients

  • 2 400 - 500 g chicken thigh fillets, cut into strips (1 cm)
  • 2 ½ tbsp soy sauce, (see Tips)
  • 3 tbsp fish sauce
  • 50 1 - 2 g cornflour, (see Tips)
  • 2 garlic cloves,
  • 2 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • ¼ tsp ground turmeric,
  • 200 1 - 2 fresh long red chillies, deseeded if preferred
  • ½ 400 - 500 red capsicum, cut into pieces
  • 2 carrots, cut into pieces
  • 2 celery stalks, cut into pieces
  • 200 g cabbage, cut into pieces
  • 300 g jasmine rice
  • 500 g water
  • 400 g coconut milk
  • 1 ½ tbsp oil, for frying
  • 6 eggs
  • ½ - 1 tsp chilli powder
  • 2 spring onions/shallots, cut into thin slices

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh chicken into it. Add 1½ tablespoons of the soy sauce and 1 tablespoon of the fish sauce to bowl and stir to combine. Weigh cornflour into bowl and toss to coat. Set aside.
  2. Place garlic, ginger, turmeric and fresh chillies into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7.
  3. Add capsicum, carrot, celery and cabbage and chop 5 sec/speed 5, with aid of spatula. Transfer mixture into Varoma dish and set aside.
  4. Place simmering basket onto mixing bowl lid and weigh rice into it. Rinse rice until water runs clear. Place water and coconut milk into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket with rice into mixing bowl and cook 7 min/100°C/speed 4. Stir rice with aid of spatula.
  5. Place Varoma dish with vegetables into position, secure Varoma lid and cook 7-9 min/Varoma/speed 2 or until vegetables and rice are cooked to your liking (see Tips).
  6. Place a large non-stick frying pan over medium-high heat and add 1 tablespoon of the oil. Add reserved chicken mixture to pan and cook 8-10 minutes, turning often, until chicken is cooked through. Transfer into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and cover to keep warm. Add remaining 2 teaspoons oil to pan. Crack eggs into pan and fry to your liking. Transfer onto a plate and cover to keep warm.
  7. Transfer vegetables and rice into thermal serving bowl with chicken. Add chilli powder, remaining 1 tablespoon soy sauce, remaining 2 tablespoons fish sauce and spring onions/shallots, then stir to combine. Divide rice mixture between serving bowls and top each bowl with a fried egg to serve.

Useful Items

large non-stick frying pan, bowl, plate, thermal serving bowl or other large bowl, serving bowls, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • To make this dish gluten free, use gluten free cornflour and gluten free soy sauce.
  • To save time, fry the chicken and eggs while your vegetables and rice are cooking.
  • To steam your chicken (instead of frying), place chicken, soy sauce and fish sauce into a bowl and set aside (omit cornflour). After cooking rice and vegetables, transfer into a thermal serving bowl and cover to keep warm. Place chicken into Varoma dish, secure Varoma lid and steam 10–15 min/Varoma/speed 2, or until cooked through. Add to rice mixture and proceed as per recipe.
