Meatloaf

Ingredients

  • 2 oz white bread, fresh or stale, cut into pieces
  • 3 ½ oz whole milk
  • 1 oz brown sugar
  • 1 oz Dijon mustard
  • 3 ½ oz ketchup
  • ½ oz oil, plus extra to grease
  • 3 ½ oz yellow onions
  • 16 oz ground beef, crumbled
  • 16 oz ground pork, crumbled
  • 2 jumbo eggs
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • ½ tsp sweet paprika
Nutrition
per 1 slice
Calories
844 kJ / 208 kcal

11 g

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh bread and milk into bowl. Set aside for bread to absorb milk.
  2. Place brown sugar, mustard and ketchup into mixing bowl and mix 30 sec/speed 3.5. Transfer sauce to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Preheat oven to 350°F and grease a loaf pan (approx. 9 in. x 5 in.) and set aside.
  4. Place oil and onions into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5.
  5. Squeeze milk out of bread and discard milk. Add bread, ground beef, pork, eggs, salt, black pepper and sweet paprika. Knead with aid of spatula 1 min/.
  6. Transfer the mixture into prepared loaf pan. Press together firmly and smooth the surface, baste or pour reserved sauce over the meatloaf. Bake 40-45 minutes (350°F). Serve warm or cold.

Useful Items

bowl, loaf pan (approx. 9 in. x 5 in.), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Vary the spices and adjust their amounts according to preference.
  • To grind your own meat, freeze pieces (1¼ in.) of sinew-free meat in a single layer for 30 minutes. Before starting the recipe place partially-frozen meat into the mixing bowl and grind 10-15 sec/speed 6.
