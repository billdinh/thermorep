Breakfast Scones

Ingredients

  • 12 oz all-purpose flour
  • 2 ½ oz baker's sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 oz unsalted butter, plus extra to serve
  • 4 oz whole milk, plus extra to brush
  • 1 large egg

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Place flour, sugar, baking powder, salt and butter into mixing bowl and mix 5 sec/speed 8.
  3. Add milk and egg and mix 5 sec/speed 3.5, then knead 20 sec/.
  4. Transfer dough to prepared baking sheet and bring together to form a ball. Gently push out dough into a circle (approx. ¾ in. thick) and cut into 12 equal triangles. Separate the triangles on baking sheet leaving room to expand.
  5. Brush scones with milk and bake for 12-15 minutes (425°F) until they have risen and are golden brown.
  6. Serve warm with butter.

Useful Items

parchment paper, baking sheet (approx. 16 in. x 14 in.), pastry brush, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with butter, jam and whipped cream.
  • Be careful not to over-handle the dough, as this will make scones tough and crumbly.
  • For savory scones: omit sugar and before starting recipe place 2 oz cheese into mixing bowl and grate 3 sec/speed 8. Continue with recipe and add ¼ tsp mustard powder, and ⅛ tsp cayenne pepper or paprika when adding flour.
