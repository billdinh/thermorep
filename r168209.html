Hummus

Ingredients

  • 250 g chickpeas, cooked
  • 150 g water
  • 50 - 70 g lemon juice, freshly squeezed
  • 60 - 140 g tahini paste
  • 1 - 2 garlic cloves (optional)
  • 1 - 1 ½ tsp salt
  • 1 - 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 - 3 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only (optional)
  • ½ tsp ground cumin
  • ½ tsp ground paprika

Preparation

  1. Place cooked chickpeas, water, lemon juice, tahini paste, garlic and salt into mixing bowl and mix with aid of spatula 1 min/speed 7.
  2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix 30 sec/speed 7. Transfer hummus to a serving bowl and garnish with olive oil, parsley, cumin and paprika.
