One pot pasta with chorizo

Ingredients

  • 100 g fresh baby spinach leaves
  • 50 g Parmesan cheese, crusts removed and cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 1 brown onion (approx. 120 g), cut into halves
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • ½ carrot, cut into halves
  • 2 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 400 g canned crushed tomatoes
  • 650 g water
  • 1 - 1 ½ tbsp Vegetable stock paste (see Tips)
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 1 chorizo sausage, cut into slices
  • 350 g dried spaghetti
  • 2 sprigs fresh basil, leaves only, for garnishing
  • 12 pitted Kalamata olives, cut into slices (optional)

Preparation

  1. Place a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh spinach into it. Set aside.
  2. Place Parmesan cheese into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a separate bowl and set aside.
  3. Place onion, garlic, carrot, parsley and tomatoes into mixing bowl and blend 10 sec/speed 7.
  4. Add water, stock paste, pepper and chorizo into mixing bowl and cook 5 min/100°C//speed , placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  5. Add spaghetti through hole in mixing bowl lid and cook 15 min/90°C//speed , until al dente, using spatula to submerge pasta into sauce, then place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid once pasta is completely covered in sauce.
  6. Transfer pasta and sauce into bowl with reserved spinach and stir well to combine.
  7. Divide between serving bowls. Top with basil leaves and olives (optional), then serve warm with reserved Parmesan cheese.
