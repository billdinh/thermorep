Tomatoes stuffed with mushrooms and nuts

Ingredients

  • 50 g mixed nuts (e.g. almonds, walnuts)
  • 30 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 50 g brown onion, cut into halves
  • 1300 g ripe tomato (5-6 cm) (see Tip)
  • 100 g fresh mushrooms (e.g. button, oyster)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 10 - 12 leaves fresh mint
  • 4 - 5 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only, plus extra to garnish
  • 75 g long grain white rice
  • 20 g freshly squeezed lemon juice (approx. ½ lemon)

Preparation

  1. Place mixed nuts into mixing bowl and chop 8 sec/speed 5. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place 20 g of the oil and onions into mixing bowl, chop 5 sec/speed 5, then sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1, without measuring cup. Meanwhile, cut a slice (approx. 2 cm thick) off the bottom (opposite the stem) of each tomato, making sure tomatoes fit under Varoma lid when on Varoma tray. Reserve tomato slices.
  3. Add 125 g of the reserved tomato slices, mushrooms, ½ teaspoon of the salt, black pepper, 6-8 of the mint leaves (or ½ tsp dried mint) and parsley into mixing bowl, chop 5 sec/speed 5, then cook 10 min/Varoma/speed 1. Meanwhile, scoop out the tomatoes with a melon baller or spoon, reserving seeds and pulp and setting them aside with remaining reserved tomato slices. Arrange hollowed tomatoes into Varoma dish and tray with openings facing up.
  4. Add rice, reserved ground nuts and lemon juice into mixing bowl and mix 5 sec//speed 3. Fill tomatoes with rice mixture.
  5. Place reserved tomato centres and any remaining slices (approx. 500 g), remaining 4 mint leaves (or ½ tsp dried mint), ½ tsp salt and 10 g oil into mixing bowl and blend 5 sec/speed 5. Set Varoma in position, secure Varoma lid and steam 30 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove Varoma. Transfer sauce into a serving dish, arrange stuffed tomatoes on the sauce and garnish with fresh parsley leaves. Serve warm or cold.

Useful Items

melon baller, serving dish

Hints & Tricks

  • This recipe from Cyprus is bursting with the fresh flavours of tomato, lemon and mint. The rice mixed with nuts and mushrooms has a crunchy and almost meaty texture. Serve as a satisfying main course or as an impressive side dish.
  • Choose tomatoes that are large enough to fill, yet small enough to fit under the lid on the Varoma tray (5-6 cm). The number of tomatoes may vary between 8 and 12 depending on their size and shape.
  • The same mixture can be used to stuff vine leaves. Vine leaves can be bought preserved or fresh. In either case, soak leaves for 1 minute in boiling water, then drain and pat dry before filling. To fill vine leaves, you will need only 600-650 g tomatoes. Use 125 g chopped tomatoes instead of tomato slices and the remaining chopped tomatoes instead of reserved tomato centres and slices. Fill vine leaves and proceed as directed by the recipe.
    - Use the same mixture to stuff kale leaves, zucchinis, capsicums, onions, or other vegetables.
