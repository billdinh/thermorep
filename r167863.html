Gluten-Free Monkey Bread

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ oz olive oil, divided, plus extra to grease
  • 8 oz brown rice flour
  • 3 oz potato starch
  • 2 oz tapioca starch
  • 1 packet/envelope dried instant yeast
  • 1 oz sugar
  • 2 tsp dried chopped rosemary
  • 1 ½ tsp xanthan gum
  • 6 oz warm water
  • ½ oz apple cider vinegar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp salt
Nutrition
per 1 piece

916 kJ / 219 kcal

Preparation

  1. Grease a bundt pan with olive oil. Place brown rice flour, potato starch, tapioca starch, yeast, sugar, rosemary, xanthan gum, water, vinegar, 1 oz olive oil, eggs and salt into mixing bowl and knead 1 min/.
  2. Transfer to a large bowl. Scoop 8 equal portions dough into prepared bundt pan. Cover with plastic wrap or a damp kitchen towel and let rest in a warm place for 1 hour.
  3. Preheat oven to 400°F. Bake for 20-22 minutes (400°F). Remove from oven, brush with 0.5 oz olive oil and allow to cool on a wire rack. Serve warm.

Useful Items

bundt cake pan (8.5 in.- 9.5 in.), plastic wrap or damp kitchen towel, pastry brush, large bowl, wire rack, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Replace rosemary with your favorite herb. For a sweet version, omit rosemary and brush with butter instead of olive oil and sprinkle with cinnamon sugar.
