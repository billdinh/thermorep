Low Carb Breakfast Frittata

Ingredients

  • 80 g Cheddar cheese, cut in pieces
  • 6 medium eggs
  • 2 Tbsp double cream
  • 2 spring onions, cut in pieces (approx. 30 g)
  • ½ tsp dried parsley
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 6 slices bacon, cut into 1 cm strips
  • 80 g fresh spinach
Nutrition
per 6 portions
Calories
5580 kJ / 1344 kcal
Protein
94.8 g
Carbohydrates
2.4 g

104.4 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Place Cheddar cheese in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place eggs, cream, spring onions, dried parsley, salt and pepper in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 3. Add 60 g grated Cheddar cheese and mix well with spatula.
  3. Heat oil in a frying pan with heat-resistant handle (approx. Ø 26 cm) over a medium-low heat. Add bacon and cook until crispy (approx. 5 minutes.) Add spinach and cook until wilted, (approx. 1 minute.)
  4. Pour egg mixture into frying pan over bacon and spinach, then cook without stirring for approx. 5 minutes or until eggs are almost set.
  5. Sprinkle reserved cheddar cheese over frittata and place frying pan in oven for 15 minutes (180°C) until eggs are set.
  6. Serve warm or cold.

Useful Items

frying pan, frying pan with heat-resistant handle, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • This versatile frittata is delicious served warm straight from the oven, or as a cold, 'on-the-go' breakfast option for busy weekday mornings.
  • Experiment with different vegetables such as cherry tomatoes and chopped red peppers to vary the flavour of the frittata.
