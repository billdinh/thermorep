Sloppy Joes with slaw

Ingredients

Mince

  • 20 g olive oil
  • 150 g brown onion, cut into halves
  • 100 g red capsicum, cut into pieces
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 500 g beef mince
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 100 g tomato ketchup (see Tips)
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp hot mustard
  • 40 g barbecue sauce
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper

Slaw

  • 2 - 3 cornichons, cut into thin slices, to taste
  • ¼ white cabbage, thinly shredded
  • ¼ red onion, cut into thin slices
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise (see Tips)
  • 2 tbsp sour cream (see Tips)
  • 4 round bread rolls

Preparation

Mince

  1. Place oil, onion, capsicum and garlic into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 6.
  2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and sauté 2 min/120°C/speed 2.
  3. Add mince and cook 3 min/120°C//speed 1. Scrape base and blades of mixing bowl with spatula to break up any lumps.
  4. Add oregano, ketchup, Worcestershire sauce, mustard, barbecue sauce, salt and pepper and cook 15-20 min/120°C//speed , placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid, until mince is cooked (see Tips).

Slaw

  1. Place cornichons, cabbage, red onion, mayonnaise and sour cream into a bowl and stir to combine.
  2. Slice rolls into halves. Fill with mince and serve with slaw.

Hints & Tricks

  • To make your own tomato ketchup and mayonnaise, please refer to the recipes in The Basic Cookbook.
  • For a more subtle flavour, you can replace the sour cream with mayonnaise in the salad. Use 4 tablespoons of mayonnaise in total.
  • To save time, prepare the slaw while the mince mixture is cooking.
  • To mince your own meat, please refer to the guide in the front of The Basic Cookbook. Please note that it may increase the total time of the recipe.
