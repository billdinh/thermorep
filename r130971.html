Neapolitan

Ingredients

White fondant (see tip)

  • 300 g sugar
  • 100 g water

Vanilla biscuit

  • 4 eggs
  • 120 g sugar
  • 10 g natural vanilla extract
  • 70 g plain flour
  • 30 g cornflour (starch)

Chocolate biscuit

  • 4 eggs
  • 120 g sugar
  • 50 g plain flour
  • 30 g cornflour (starch)
  • 20 g cocoa powder, unsweetened

Ganache

  • 250 g dark chocolate, 70% cocoa solids
  • 200 g cream, min. 30% fat

To finish

  • 30 g water, to be adjusted according to weight of fondant
  • 50 g chocolate sprinkles

Preparation

White fondant (see tip)

  1. Place sugar and water into mixing bowl, then heat 25 min/120°C/speed 2 without measuring cup. Then let temperature fall to 70°C.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk and whip 2 min/speed 4, then again 2 min/speed 2. Transfer onto a work surface and knead until paste is white and uniform. Transfer to a bowl, cover with cling film and leave to mature for 2-3 days in refrigerator.

Vanilla biscuit

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C. Line a baking tray with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Place eggs, sugar and vanilla essence into mixing bowl and whip 15 min/65°C/speed 3.5.
  3. Add flour and cornstarch, then whip 10 sec/speed 1. Finish off by gently incorporating flour with aid of spatula if necessary (see tip). Empty mixture out onto prepared tray and bake in preheated oven for 15 minutes (180°C). Clean mixing bowl. Remove cake from the oven, allow it to cool and cut it across into three equal parts.

Chocolate biscuit

  1. Line a baking tray with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Place eggsand sugar into mixing bowl and whip 15 min/65°C/speed 3.5.
  3. Add flour, cornstarch and cocoa, then whip 10 sec/speed 1. Finish off by gently incorporating flour with aid of spatula if necessary (see tip). Empty mixture out onto prepared tray and bake in preheated oven for 15 minutes (180°C). Clean mixing bowl. Remove cake from the oven, allow it to cool and cut it accross into three equal parts.

Ganache

  1. Place chocolate into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 10. Then melt 3 min/55°C/speed 1.
  2. Add cream and mix 30 sec/speed 4. Cover one third of chocolate biscuit with ganache, place one third of vanilla biscuit on top, also cover it with ganache, then place one third of chocolate biscuit on top. Repeat operation with remaining biscuit, alternating 2 parts vanilla biscuit and 1 part chocolate biscuit. Clean mixing bowl.

To finish

  1. Place white fondant, cut into pieces, and 10% of its weight in water into mixing bowl, then melt 6 min/37°C/speed 1. Coat each of the two cakes with white icing. Sprinkle with chocolate or coloured sprinkles and trim to get a clean edge with a sharp knife. Cut each cake into slices and keep cool until ready to serve.

Useful Items

cling film, baking tray, baking paper, bowl, refrigerator, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • For best results, prepare the white fondant 2-3 days in advance.
  • It is best to finish incorporating the flour using the spatula so that the mixture does not collapse and keeps its lightness.
