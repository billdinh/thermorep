Beetroot soup with herb dumplings

Ingredients

Herb dumplings

  • 10 - 15 g fresh mixed herbs (e.g. basil, flat-leaf parsley, coriander, sage, chervil), leaves only
  • 200 g full cream milk
  • 60 g unsalted butter, cut into pieces
  • ¾ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground nutmeg (optional)
  • 150 g coarse semolina
  • unsalted butter, plus extra for greasing
  • 1 egg

Beetroot soup

  • 500 g raw beetroot, peeled, cut into pieces
  • 700 g water
  • 150 g white wine
  • 2 heaped tsp Vegetable stock paste (refer to the recipe in The Basic Cookbook)
  • 25 g plain flour
  • ½ - 1 tsp salt
  • 2 pinches ground nutmeg
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 100 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 1 tbsp white wine vinegar (optional)

Preparation

Herb dumplings

  1. Place herbs into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 8. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place milk, butter, salt and nutmeg (optional) into mixing bowl and heat 3 min/80°C/speed 1.
  3. Add semolina and cook 2 min 30 sec/100°C/speed 3. Remove mixing bowl and allow semolina mixture to cool for approx. 10 minutes. Meanwhile, grease Varoma dish and tray.
  4. Place mixing bowl back into position, add egg and reserved chopped herbs and stir 15 sec/speed 3. Transfer onto a plate and using wet hands or two teaspoons, form 30 dumplings (approx. 2.5 cm). Arrange dumplings into Varoma dish and onto Varoma tray and set aside.

Beetroot soup

  1. Place beetroot into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 4.5.
  2. Add water, white wine, stock paste, flour, salt, nutmeg and pepper and mix 5 sec/speed 3.
  3. Place Varoma dish and tray in position, secure Varoma lid and cook 25 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  4. Set Varoma aside. Add cream and vinegar (optional) to mixing bowl, insert measuring cup and blend 1 min 30 sec/speed 5-10, increasing speed gradually from speed 5 to speed 10. Season to taste. Divide soup and dumplings between serving bowls and serve immediately.

Useful Items

bowl, plate, serving bowls

Hints & Tricks

  • For a thicker soup, add a little beurre manié (5-10 g each of butter and flour, kneaded together in a small bowl) after the blending in step 8, and cook 2 min/95°C/speed 2.
  • Wasabi dumplings: Replace chopped herbs with 30-40 g wasabi paste.
    - Horseradish dumplings: Prepare the dumpling dough with only 180 g milk. Replace the herbs with 80 g preserved grated horseradish.
