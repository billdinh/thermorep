Goi muc (squid salad)

Ingredients

Nuoc cham dressing

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 fresh bird's eye chilli, trimmed and deseeded if preferred
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 120 g water
  • 50 g fish sauce
  • 50 g rice wine vinegar
  • 40 g palm sugar

Salad

  • 50 g roasted salted peanuts
  • 80 g bean shoots
  • 80 - 100 g Chinese cabbage, finely shredded
  • 1 carrot, cut into matchsticks
  • ½ red capsicum, cut into thin slices
  • 3 baby radishes, trimmed and cut into thin slices
  • 10 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only
  • 2 sprigs fresh mint, leaves only
  • 500 g calamari tubes, cleaned
  • 40 g peanut oil
  • 2 tbsp fried shallots

Preparation

Nuoc cham dressing

  1. Place garlic and chilli into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place lime juice, water, fish sauce, vinegar and sugar into mixing bowl and heat 3 min/Varoma/speed 1 or until sugar dissolves. Set aside in mixing bowl to cool for 10 minutes, then add to reserved chilli mixture and stir to combine. Set bowl aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Salad

  1. Place peanuts into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place a large bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh bean shoots and cabbage into it. Add carrot, capsicum, radishes, coriander and mint, then set aside.
  3. Place a seperate bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh calamari into it. Transfer onto a clean work surface and cut each calamari tube into halves lengthways. With the inner side facing up, taking care not to pierce all the way through, score a lattice pattern over the entire surface, then cut into bite-size pieces and set aside.
  4. Place 20 g of the oil into mixing bowl and heat 3 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  5. Add 250 g of the calamari pieces and cook 1-2 min/Varoma//speed  or until cooked to your liking. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Discard any cooking liquid.
  6. Place remaining 20 g oil into mixing bowl and heat 3 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  7. Add remaining 250 g calamari pieces and cook 1-2 min/Varoma//speed  or until cooked to your liking. Transfer into bowl with reserved calamari. Add ⅓ of the reserved dressing into bowl with calamari and toss to coat.
  8. Add remaining dressing into bowl with reserved salad and toss to combine.
  9. Divide salad between serving plates. Top with calamari, then sprinkle with fried shallots and reserved peanuts before serving.
