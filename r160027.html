Banana Split

Ingredients

  • 2 oz hazelnuts
  • 5 ½ oz chocolate, broken into pieces
  • 3 ½ oz whole milk
  • 4 oz unsalted butter, divided
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 21 oz heavy whipping cream, divided
  • 2 oz dark brown sugar
  • 2 oz sugar
  • 2 oz maple syrup
  • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste (see Tip)
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 8 bananas
  • 8 scoops vanilla ice cream
  • 8 scoops chocolate ice cream
  • 8 candied cherries

Preparation

  1. Insert large tip into icing bag and set aside.
  2. Place nuts into mixing bowl and chop Turbo/1 sec/0 times. Set nuts aside.
  3. Place chocolate into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add milk, 1 oz butter and vanilla extract and cook 4 min/120°F/speed 3. Set chocolate sauce aside.
  5. Place 3 oz butter, 5 oz cream, dark brown sugar, sugar, maple syrup, vanilla bean paste and salt into mixing bowl. Place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 12 min/Varoma/speed 2. Set caramel sauce aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl thoroughly.
  6. Insert butterfly whisk. Place 16 oz cream into mixing bowl and without setting a time, whip speed 4 for a soft whip until cream is just holding its shape. Watching carefully to avoid overwhipping. This can take a few seconds to 1-3 minutes.
  7. Place whipped cream into icing bag and refrigerate until ready to use.
  8. Peel bananas and slice in half lengthwise. Lay two banana halves in each serving dish. Place one scoop of vanilla and one scoop of chocolate ice cream into each bowl.
  9. Pipe whipped cream over bananas and ice cream.
  10. Drizzle chocolate and caramel sauce over the whipped cream.
  11. Sprinkle with reserved nuts, top with a cherry and serve immediately.

Useful Items

serving dish, icing bag and large tip, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • To reduce the number of servings: decrease the amount of bananas and ice cream.
  • Use strawberry sauce instead of or in addition to the caramel sauce.
  • Use your favorite ice cream flavors. Try combinations such as coffee, chocolate, and hazelnut ice creams.
  • Refrigerate leftover chocolate and caramel sauce in airtight jars for later use.
  • Make your own Vanilla Bean Paste: place 6-10 whole vanilla bean pods into mixing bowl and grind 20 sec/speed 10 or until pulverized. Add 3 tbsp pure vanilla extract and 2 tsp xanthan gum (optional). Stir 3 sec/speed 3. Scrape out bowl with spatula and place in a glass jar to use as required.
  • Vanilla Bean Paste can be substituted measure-for-measure with vanilla extract.
