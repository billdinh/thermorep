Lemon Sorbet

Ingredients

  • 650 g water
  • 350 g sugar
  • 200 g lemon juice (5-7 lemons), freshly squeezed
  • 1 egg white (see tip)
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
767 kJ / 183 kcal

Preparation

  1. Place 400 g water and sugar in mixing bowl then cook 5 min/100Â°C/speed 3.
  2. Add remaining 250 g water and lemon juice then mix 3 sec/speed 3.
  3. Add egg white (see tip) and mix 3 sec/speed 4. Pour mixture up to 4 cm deep into shallow freezer-proof containers and cover with lid or cling film. Cool mixture completely before freezing for at least 8 hours or until solid.
  4. Cut frozen mixture into pieces (approx. 4 cm) with a long sharp knife. Place pieces in mixing bowl and crush 30 sec/speed 9 then, with aid of spatula, mix 30 sec/speed 4. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

cling film, long sharp knife, shallow freezer-proof containers, freezer

Hints & Tricks
