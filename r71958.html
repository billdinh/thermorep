Indulgent hot chocolate

Ingredients

  • 200 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 300 g full cream milk
  • 1 vanilla bean, split lengthways
  • 8 whole black peppercorns
  • 100 g dark chocolate (70% cocoa), broken into pieces
  • 1 - 2 tsp raw sugar, to taste (optional)
  • chocolate, shaved, to serve (optional)

Preparation

  1. Place a glass bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh cream and milk into it. Add vanilla bean and peppercorns to glass bowl and leave to infuse for 15 minutes.
  2. Place chocolate into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add sugar (optional) and infused cream and milk mixture, including vanilla bean and peppercorns, and heat 5 min/80°C/speed 2.
  4. Strain through simmering basket into 2 mugs or a serving jug. Garnish with chocolate shavings (optional) and serve immediately.

Useful Items

glass bowl, 2 mugs or a serving jug

Hints & Tricks

  • Recipe for one: reduce all ingredients by half and proceed as per recipe.
  • The pepper adds to the rich creaminess of this hot chocolate. Adding a pinch of salt will enhance the chocolate flavours even further.
  • Serve this hot chocolate with Baked chocolate ricotta cakes with blueberries as a delicious afternoon tea.
  • Mix your hot chocolate 5 sec/speed 8 to froth just before serving.
