Gluten-Free Bread

Ingredients

  • 4 oz white basmati rice
  • 4 oz brown rice, long grain
  • 3 oz millet grain
  • unsalted butter, to grease
  • 1 oz garbanzo flour
  • 5 oz arrowroot flour
  • 1 oz chia seed, plus extra to sprinkle (optional)
  • 2 oz pumpkin seeds, plus extra to sprinkle (optional)
  • 2 tsp xanthan gum
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 14 oz water, plus extra to brush
  • 2 tsp dried instant yeast
  • 1 oz macadamia oil
  • 1 tsp apple cider vinegar
  • 2 large eggs

Preparation

  1. Place basmati rice, brown rice and millet into mixing bowl and grind 1 min/speed 9. Meanwhile, grease a deep-sided bread or loaf pan and set aside.
  2. Add garbanzo flour, arrowroot flour, chia seeds, pumpkin seeds, xanthan gum and salt and mix 10 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place honey, water and yeast into mixing bowl and warm 2 min/100°F/speed 1.
  4. Add oil, vinegar, eggs and reserved flour mixture and mix 15 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix 5 sec/speed 5.
  5. Transfer mixture to prepared loaf pan and leave to proof in a warm place until doubled in size (approx. 30 min.). Preheat oven to 350°F.
  6. Brush top of loaf with water and sprinkle with extra chia and pumpkin seeds. Bake for 40-45 minutes (350°F). Allow to cool in pan for 5 minutes then transfer to a wire rack. Serve warm or allow to cool completely before transferring to an airtight container until ready to serve.

Useful Items

airtight container, bowl, deep-sided loaf pan, pastry brush, wire rack, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve fresh or toasted with your favorite jelly, butter or spread.
  • Proofing time may vary depending on time of year and room temperature. If dough hasn't doubled in size after 30 minutes, cover with a kitchen towel (tea towel) and allow to rise a further 15-30 minutes. (Use your oven's proof function if available)
  • Use bread pan when available.
  • Grind 1.5 oz dried garbanzo beans with rice and millet to make your own garbanzo flour.
  • This bread can be stored in individually wrapped slices in freezer. Use as required for toast or defrost (approx. 20 min.) for sandwiches.
  • Can be used to make gluten-free bread crumbs.
  • To save time when making gluten free-bread: make up 5 to 10 zip lock bags in advance with pre-measured dry ingredients: 4 oz ground white basmati rice, 4 oz ground long grain brown rice, 3 oz millet grain, 1 oz garbanzo bean flour, 5 oz arrowroot flour, 1 oz chia seeds, 2 oz pumpkin seeds, 2 tsp xanthan gum and 1 tsp of salt. Store in freezer until required. Omit first step and continue as directed by recipe.
