Turkey, Asparagus and Goat Cheese Penne

Ingredients

  • 60 oz water
  • 1 tsp salt, plus extra to season
  • 12 oz penne pasta, dried
  • 8 oz cooked turkey meat, cubed or pulled
  • 8 oz asparagus, cut in pieces (1 in.), woody ends removed
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 lemon, thin peel only, no pith
  • ¼ tsp dried chili flakes (optional)
  • ½ oz fresh parsley (leaves only)
  • 2 oz olive oil
  • 6 oz cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 2 oz goat cheese, crumbled
  • 2 oz baby arugula
  • lemon zest, to garnish

Preparation

  1. Place water into mixing bowl and bring to a boil 15 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  2. Add salt and dried pasta through hole in mixing bowl lid, place Varoma dish (dish only, no lid) instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 5 min/Varoma//speed .
  3. Weigh turkey and asparagus into Varoma dish, secure Varoma lid and steam 6 min/Varoma//speed , or until pasta is cooked to your liking. Remove Varoma, set aside and keep turkey and asparagus warm. Drain pasta, transfer to a large bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Place garlic, lemon peel, chili flakes and parsley into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and chop a further 5 sec/speed 8.
  5. Transfer to bowl with reserved pasta, drizzle with olive oil, season with salt and add reserved turkey and asparagus. Top with tomatoes, goat cheese and arugula. Stir with spatula to combine, garnish with lemon zest and serve.

Hints & Tricks

  • Before serving, top with 4 oz green or black olives and/or 4 oz roasted red peppers, cut into strips. Stir with spatula to combine.
