Za'atar pesto

Ingredients

  • 130 g olive oil, plus extra for topping
  • 150 g blanched almonds, toasted
  • 1 - 2 garlic cloves
  • 40 g fresh za’atar leaves, washed and dried
  • 5 sprigs parsley, leaves only, washed and dried
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sumac
  • 50 g lemon juice

Preparation

  1. Place a jug onto mixing bowl lid, weigh olive oil into jug and set aside.
  2. Place toasted almonds in the mixing bowl and grind 5 sec/speed 9
  3. Add garlic, za’atar leaves, parsley leaves, salt, sumac and lemon juice and chop 10 sec/speed 7.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and chop 5 sec/speed 6.
  5. Mix speed 4, without setting a time, while very slowly pouring olive oil onto mixing bowl lid, letting it drizzle around measuring cup in a thin stream onto rotating blades. Transfer pesto into a jar with a twist-off lid, cover with a layer of olive oil and store in the refrigerator until ready to serve.

Useful Items

jug, jam jar with twist-off lid, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Weigh the herbs before you wash them as the weight changes if the herbs are wet.
  • Serve pesto with pitta bread or any other bread of your choice. It also accompanies well raw vegetables or can be used to dress pasta or as a base for salad dressing.
