Gin and cucumber cooler

Ingredients

  • 50 g white sugar
  • 50 g water
  • 200 g ice cubes, plus extra to serve
  • 300 g cucumber, peeled and cut into pieces (2 cm), plus extra slices for garnishing
  • 2 limes, juice only
  • 120 g gin
  • 550 g soda water
Nutrition
per 1 portions
Calories
262 kJ / 62 kcal
Protein
0.2 g
Carbohydrates
7.2 g
Fat
0.1 g

Preparation

  1. Place sugar and water into mixing bowl and heat 4 min/100°C/speed 2, until sugar has dissolved.
  2. Add ice, cucumber and lime juice and blend 30 sec/speed 9.
  3. Add gin and soda water and mix 10 sec/speed 4.
  4. Divide cooler between serving glasses, add ice and garnish with extra cucumber slices before serving.

Useful Items

8 serving glasses

Hints & Tricks

  • Soak cucumber slices in gin for a spiked garnish.
  • Cucumber purée can be made in advance and stored in the refrigerator for 1 day. Pour gin over ice, top with cucumber purée and soda.
  • Omit gin for a non-alcoholic drink.
