Cassoulet with Duck and Extra Veg

Ingredients

  • 4 Tbsp olive oil
  • 2 duck breasts, skinless (approx. 180 g each)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 leek, white part only, sliced 2 cm (approx. 150 g)
  • 2 carrots, sliced 1 cm (approx. 100 g)
  • 1 celery stalk, sliced 1 cm (approx. 60 g)
  • 1 courgette, sliced 1 cm (approx. 200 g)
  • ¾ tsp fine sea salt, plus extra to taste
  • &frac13; tsp ground black pepper, plus extra to taste
  • 400 g tinned chopped tomatoes
  • 10 g fresh parsley leaves
  • 1 Tbsp fresh thyme leaves
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 480 g tinned cannellini beans (2 x 400 g tins, drained)
  • 1 vegetable stock cube (for 0.5 l)
  • 300 g water, hot

Preparation

  1. Heat 2 Tbsp olive oil in a frying pan over medium-high heat then brown the duck breasts all over for approximately 5 minutes. Remove duck breasts and set aside.
  2. Place 2 Tbsp olive oil and garlic in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 7.
  3. Add leek, carrots, celery, courgette, salt and pepper then cook 5 min/100°C/speed .
  4. Add tomatoes, reserved browned duck breasts, parsley, thyme and bay leaves then heat 3 min/100°C//speed 0.5.
  5. Add beans, stock cube and water (no higher than maximum mark in mixing bowl) then, with simmering basket in place of measuring cup, cook 25 min/98°C//speed 0.5.
  6. Remove duck breasts with tongs and slice thinly. Return meat to mixing bowl and stir with spatula.
  7. Adjust seasoning to taste before serving.

Useful Items

tongs, frying pan, hob

Hints & Tricks

  • A filling dish for after a run with protein from the duck and beans but without too many calories.
  • Serve with crusty bread.
