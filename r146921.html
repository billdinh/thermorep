Prawn and Fish Ball Soup with Ramen Noodles

Ingredients

Prawn and Fish Balls

  • olive oil, for greasing
  • 3 cm ginger, fresh root, peeled, cut in round slices (2 mm)
  • 2 spring onions, trimmed and quartered
  • 6 sprigs fresh coriander
  • ½ - 1 long red chilli, deseeded and halved
  • 3 dried shiitake mushrooms, halved
  • 400 g white fish fillets, cut in pieces (4-5 cm)
  • 200 g large raw prawns, peeled
  • 230 g water chestnuts, whole, tinned, drained
  • 2 Tbsp crispy, fried shallots
  • 2 pinches ground white pepper
  • 2 pinches fine sea salt
  • 2 tsp soy sauce

Broth

  • 300 - 400 g ramen noodles, fresh
  • 1500 g water
  • 2 Tbsp chicken stock paste (see Tips)
  • 2 Tbsp chinese cooking wine
  • 2 Tbsp soy sauce
  • ½ tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 tsp raw sugar
  • ¼ tsp shrimp paste (belachan)
  • 1 star anise
  • 200 g Asian greens (e.g. choy sum, bok choy), trimmed and cut in pieces (3 cm)
  • 2 spring onions, trimmed and thinly sliced
  • 4 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only

Preparation

Prawn and Fish Balls

  1. Grease Varoma dish and tray with oil and set aside. Place ginger, spring onions, coriander and chilli in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add all remaining fish ball ingredients and chop 10 sec/speed 7. With damp hands, shape 2 teaspoons of mixture into walnut-sized balls to make approx. 30 fish and prawn balls. Transfer into greased Varoma and set aside. Rinse and dry mixing bowl.

Broth

  1. Insert simmering basket, then weigh in noodles. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and set aside.
  2. Place water, stock paste, wine, soy sauce, sesame oil, salt, sugar, shrimp paste (belachan) and star anise in mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position and cook 17 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  3. Weigh Asian greens into Varoma dish and tray. Secure Varoma lid and cook 2 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  4. Rinse noodles in simmering basket under warm water, until water runs clear and noodles separate.
  5. Remove Varoma, insert simmering basket with noodles, then place Varoma back into position and cook 1 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  6. Divide noodles between 6 serving bowls, top with prawn and fish balls and Asian greens. Carefully remove star anise, then distribute broth between serving bowls. Garnish with spring onions and coriander before serving.
