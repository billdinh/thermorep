Portuguese Fish and Potato Stew

Ingredients

  • 4 - 6 cod fillets (approx. 28 oz)
  • salt, to taste
  • 24 oz tomatoes, diced (approx. ¼ in.), divided
  • 3 ½ oz green bell peppers, thinly sliced strips or rounds, divided
  • 3 ½ oz red bell peppers, thinly sliced strips or rounds, divided
  • 10 oz yellow onion, cut into pieces (approx. ½ in.), divided
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 oz olive oil
  • 35 oz red potatoes, sliced in rounds (approx. ¼ in.)
  • 6 oz white wine
  • 2 oz water
  • 1 ½ tbsp clam juice
  • &frac18; tsp chili powder
  • 1 dried bay leaf
  • 6 - 8 sprigs cilantro, leaves only

Preparation

  1. Place Varoma into position. Season fillets with salt and place into Varoma, cover with 10 oz tomatoes, 2 oz green peppers and 2 oz red peppers. Invert Varoma lid and set Varoma dish on top of lid to catch drippings and set aside.
  2. Place 14 oz tomatoes, 5 oz onion, garlic and oil into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5
  3. Add remaining 5 oz onion, 1.5 oz green pepper and 1.5 oz red pepper and sauté 5 min/250°F//speed 
  4. Add potatoes, wine, water, clam juice, chili powder and bay leaf, place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 25 min/Varoma//speed .
  5. Remove Varoma, place fish into a large serving bowl and carefully pour over the potato mixture. Gently stir with spatula to mix through. Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves and serve immediately.

Hints & Tricks

  • You can use any firm white fish such as hake, pollack or corvina.
