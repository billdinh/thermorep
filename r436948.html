Steak with Peppered Parmesan Sauce and Roquefort Potatoes

Ingredients

Steak

  • 2 filet mignon medallions (approx. 4-6 oz ea.)
  • salt, to season

Potatoes

  • 20 oz water
  • 8 oz baby red potatoes or mixed baby potatoes
  • 1 sprig rosemary
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ tsp salt

Peppered Parmesan Sauce

  • 2 oz Parmesan cheese, in pieces
  • 1 tbsp whole black peppercorns
  • 5 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves only
  • 2 oz shallots, in pieces
  • 2 oz unsalted butter, in pieces
  • 5 oz chicken stock
  • 1 oz brandy
  • 1 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 oz heavy whipping cream

Assembly

  • 1 tbsp canola oil
  • 1 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 2 oz Roquefort cheese, crumbled
  • fresh rosemary, to garnish (optional)

Preparation

Steak

  1. Season steaks with salt and set aside while steaming potatoes.

Potatoes

  1. Place water into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket, weigh in potatoes, top with rosemary and steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 3. Line a baking sheet with foil and set aside.
  2. Remove simmering basket with spatula. Transfer steamed potatoes to prepared baking sheet, toss with oil and salt and set aside. Rinse and dry mixing bowl.

Peppered Parmesan Sauce

  1. Place Parmesan, peppercorns and thyme into mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place shallots and butter into mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and cook 5 min/212°F/speed 1.
  3. Add chicken stock, brandy and flour and without measuring cup cook 10 min/212°F/speed 2.
  4. Add reserved Parmesan mixture and whipping cream and cook 3 min/195°F/speed 2.5. Meanwhile, preheat oven to broil. Transfer sauce to a bowl and set aside to keep warm.

Assembly

  1. Place reserved steamed potatoes into oven and broil until crispy (approx. 6-10 min.). Meanwhile, cook steaks.
  2. In a skillet over medium-high heat, add oil and cook reserved seasoned steaks. Sear 2½ minutes and flip, add butter and sear other side 2½ minutes or to your liking. Turn and sear edges (approx. 1 min. per edge).
  3. When steaks are done, cover with foil and let rest 5 minutes. When potatoes are done, smash them with spatula and sprinkle with Roquefort cheese and rosemary.
  4. Serve steaks with Peppered Parmesan Sauce and smashed Roquefort Potatoes on the side.

Useful Items

baking sheet, aluminum foil, skillet or frying pan, oven broiler, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • For a complete meal, serve with a fresh garden salad.
