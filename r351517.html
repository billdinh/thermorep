Mushroom-filled Buns

Ingredients

Dough

  • 40 g icing sugar
  • 500 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 50 g olive oil
  • 240 g water
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt

Filling

  • 400 g celery stalks, cut in pieces
  • 60 g fresh bamboo shoots
  • 500 g fresh king oyster mushrooms
  • 50 g olive oil
  • 40 g soy sauce
  • 10 g sugar
  • ½ tsp ground allspice
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • 20 g cornflour

Assembly

  • honey, for coating
  • sesame seeds, for sprinkling

Preparation

Dough

  1. Place sugar, flour, oil, water and salt in mixing bowl then knead 3 min/. Transfer to a bowl and leave to rest in a warm place for approx. 20 minutes.

Filling

  1. Place celery in mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place bamboo shoots and mushrooms in mixing bowl then chop 10 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add oil, soy sauce, sugar and allspice then heat 3 min/120°C/speed .
  4. Add pepper and cornflour then heat 2 min/120°C/speed .
  5. Add reserved chopped celery and mix 10 sec/speed 3. Transfer to a bowl and set aside to cool.

Assembly

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C and line a baking tray with baking paper.
  2. Place risen dough on a work surface and dust with flour. Divide into 18 equal-sized pieces and shape each into a circle (approx. Ø 10 cm). Place 2 Tbsp filling in centre of each dough circle and seal by pulling all sides into the top. Coat each with honey, sprinkle with sesame seeds and arrange on prepared tray.
  3. Bake for 10 minutes (200°C) then place a sheet of baking paper loosely over the top. Bake for a further 5 minutes (200°C) or until golden then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely before serving.

Useful Items

baking tray, baking paper, wire rack, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Substitute sesame seeds with poppy seeds.
  • If unavailable, substitute fresh king oyster mushrooms with button mushrooms.
