Zucchini fritters

Ingredients

  • 2 zucchinis (approx. 450 g)
  • 180 g sweet potato, peeled
  • ½ red onion
  • 6 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 3 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 80 g wholemeal self-raising flour
  • olive oil, for shallow frying
  • avocado, mashed, to serve (optional)
  • Greek-style natural yoghurt, to serve (optional)

Preparation

  1. Using a julienne peeler, peel zucchini and sweet potato into thin strips, then set aside.
  2. Place onion and parsley into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add eggs, flour and reserved zucchini and sweet potato, then mix 15-20 sec//speed 3, mixing with aid of spatula, until well combined.
  4. Place a large frying pan over medium heat and add oil. Working in batches, place heaped tablespoons of the zucchini mixture into pan and fry 3-4 minutes each side, until golden and cooked through. Transfer onto a plate lined with paper towel and cover to keep warm (makes approx. 25 fritters in total). Serve warm with mashed avocado (optional) and natural yoghurt (optional).

Useful Items

large frying pan, plate, paper towel, julienne peeler, stove top
