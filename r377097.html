Iced four-spice latte

Ingredients

Macadamia and hemp milk

  • 140 g macadamia nuts
  • 1000 g filtered water, plus extra for soaking
  • 100 g hemp seeds (see Tips)
  • 60 g pitted Medjool dates
  • 1 pinch sea salt
  • ½ tsp natural vanilla extract (optional)

Four-spice latte

  • 2 - 3 cm piece fresh turmeric, peeled (see Tips)
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon, plus extra for dusting
  • &frac18; tsp ground black pepper
  • 2 - 3 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract
  • 2 tsp honey (see Tips)
  • 130 - 150 g ice cubes

Preparation

Macadamia and hemp milk

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh macadamias into it. Cover with water and set aside to soak for 2 hours. Using simmering basket, drain and rinse nuts.
  2. Place hemp seeds, dates, salt, vanilla (optional) and drained nuts into mixing bowl and chop 15 sec/speed 6 or until finely chopped.
  3. Add 400 g of the water and blend 1 min/speed 8.
  4. Add remaining 600 g water and blend 1 min/speed 8 or until smooth (see Tips). Transfer into a sterilised sealable container and place into refrigerator until ready to serve (see Tips).

Four-spice latte

  1. Place 500 g of the Macadamia and hemp milk (chilled) into mixing bowl. Add all remaining latte ingredients and blend 2-3 min/speed 9, until smooth and frothy.
  2. Divide between serving glasses and dust with extra ground cinnamon to serve.

Useful Items

bowl, serving glasses, sealable container, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • You can use a mix of macadamias and cashews (140 g nuts in total) in this recipe.
  • Hemp seeds are from the same species as cannabis, but they don't have any psychoactive effects. On the contrary, they're exceptionally nutritious and a rich source of protein, omega fats and various vitamins and minerals.
  • Honey is not suitable for children under 1 year of age.
  • For an even smoother texture, place a nut milk bag over a large bowl. Pour mixture into nut milk bag and squeeze firmly to extract milk (however, you will lose some of the fibre).
  • You can store the Macadamia and hemp milk in the refrigerator for up to 5 days. The contents may settle, so be sure to give it a shake before using.
  • You can replace the fresh turmeric with 1½ tsp ground turmeric in this recipe, but the flavour will be earthier (the fresh turmeric has a fresher, more pleasant flavour).
