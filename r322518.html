Meyer Lemon Bars

Ingredients

Crust

  • 1 oz sugar
  • 1 oz coconut flakes
  • 3 oz all-purpose flour
  • 3 oz whole wheat flour
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 4 oz unsalted butter, cubed

Filling

  • 7 oz sugar
  • 1 Meyer lemon, thin peel only, no pith, plus extra lemon, sliced and quartered, to garnish
  • 2 oz all-purpose flour
  • 4 large eggs
  • 5 oz Meyer lemon juice, freshly squeezed (approx. 4 lemons)
  • 1 pinch salt
  • ¼ tsp baking soda
  • confectioners sugar, to garnish
  • fresh mint leaves, to garnish

Preparation

Crust

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease and line a baking pan (9 in. x 9 in.) with parchment and set aside.
  2. Place sugar and coconut into mixing bowl and grind 20 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add all-purpose flour, whole wheat flour, salt and butter and mix 15 sec/speed 4. Press mixture into prepared baking pan and bake for 15 minutes (350°F). Remove from oven and set aside.

Filling

  1. Place sugar and lemon peel into mixing bowl and grind 20 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add flour, eggs, lemon juice, salt, and baking soda and mix 15 sec/speed 4. Pour onto reserved crust and bake for 18-20 minutes (350°F). Cool 10 minutes before cutting into squares. Serve chilled or at room temperature, garnished with confectioners sugar, sliced lemon quarters and mint.

Useful Items

square baking pan (9 in. x 9 in.), parchment paper, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Use regular lemons instead of Meyer and add an additional 1 oz sugar.
