Grain salad lettuce cups

Ingredients

  • 20 g dried shiitake mushrooms
  • 800 g hot water
  • 160 g farro
  • 1 tbsp Vegetable stock paste (see Tips)
  • 1200 g water
  • 140 g puy lentils
  • 100 g white quinoa
  • 10 raw peeled prawns
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp sea salt flakes, plus extra to taste
  • ½ tsp freshly ground black pepper, plus extra to taste
  • 2 kale leaves, stalks removed and leaves cut into thin slices
  • 6 sprigs fresh basil, leaves only, cut into thin strips
  • 2 tsp lemon juice
  • 2 baby cos lettuce, leaves separated
  • 150 g feta cheese, crumbled

Preparation

  1. Place a ceramic or glass bowl onto mixing bowl lid, weigh shiitake mushrooms and hot water into it, then set aside to soak (approx. 30 minutes) while cooking farro.
  2. Place farro into mixing bowl and toast 5 min/Varoma/speed 1. Transfer toasted farro into simmering basket.
  3. Place stock paste and 600 g of the water into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket with farro and cook 10 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  4. Add lentils into simmering basket and cook for a further 15 min/Varoma/speed 1. Stir contents of simmering basket.
  5. Drain reserved mushrooms, reserving soaking liquid. Remove and discard woody stalks from mushrooms, then cut mushroom caps into pieces.
  6. Add mushrooms and quinoa to simmering basket and pour soaking liquid over grains in simmering basket and cook 17 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  7. Pour remaining 600 g water over grains in simmering basket, stir grains and cook for a further 6 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  8. Place Varoma dish into position, place prawns into it, secure Varoma lid and steam 8 min/Varoma/speed 1, or until grains and prawns are just cooked. Remove Varoma dish and cut prawns into pieces (approx. 2 cm).
  9. Transfer grains into a large bowl and toss with olive oil, salt, pepper, kale, basil and lemon juice. Set aside to cool slightly (approx. 10 minutes).
  10. Spoon grain salad into 12 baby cos leaves. Top with prawns and crumbled feta, and season with extra salt and pepper before serving.

Useful Items

bowl, jug, ceramic or glass bowl, large bowl, serving platter

Hints & Tricks

  • Please refer to The Basic Cookbook or Everyday Cookbook for the Vegetable stock paste recipe.
  • You can substitute mint leaves for basil leaves, if desired.

  • Vegetarian: omit prawns in step 8 and proceed as per recipe, without using Varoma dish.
