Italian Savory Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • 4 ½ oz salted crackers
  • 3 oz unsalted butter, softened, cut into small pieces
  • 7 oz whole milk
  • 4 ½ tsp unflavored gelatin powder
  • 2 oz green olives, pitted, plus 15-16 extra, to garnish
  • 9 oz ricotta cheese, well drained
  • 7 oz Gorgonzola, creamy, or other blue cheese
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 pinch freshly ground pepper
Nutrition
per 1 slice
Calories
902 kJ / 216 kcal
Protein
8 g
Carbohydrates
9 g
Fat
16 g

Preparation

  1. Line a springform pan with parchment paper and set aside.
  2. Place crackers into mixing bowl and crush 7 sec/speed 7.
  3. Add butter and mix 25 sec/speed 4.
  4. Spread mixture in base of springform pan. Press crumb mixture firmly using a spoon or your hands to make an even and firm base. Place in refrigerator to chill while making cheese topping. Clean mixing bowl.
  5. Place milk into mixing bowl and warm 3 min/160°F/speed 3.
  6. Add gelatin by sprinkling into mixing bowl while avoiding blades and edges and mix 30 sec/speed 4.
  7. Add olives, (reserving an additional 15 or 16 for decoration), ricotta , Gorgonzola, salt and pepper and mix 30 sec/speed 4. Pour mixture over base and spread out evenly.
  8. Cover with cling wrap and allow to cool in refrigerator for at least 4 hours. Garnish with additional olives halfway through cooling time, or immediately before serving.

Useful Items

springform pan (10 in.), parchment paper, cling wrap, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Ricotta cheese can be replaced with quark, Cloumage Cheese®, Fromage Frais, mascarpone, sour cream or crème fraiche.
  • Italian Savory Cheescake compliments a charcuterie platter.
  • Serve smaller slices as a starter with proscuitto or with bread, fruit, crackers and wine at a party.
  • Pitted olives can be replaced with 3 oz walnuts.
  • Gelatin powder can be replaced with 8 gelatin sheets: follow package instructions to hydrate gelatin and add in step 6.
