Chocolate Caramel Bake with Walnuts

Ingredients

  • 100 g halved walnuts, plus extra for decorating

Dough

  • 200 g butter, diced
  • 350 g strong white bread flour
  • 100 g granulated sugar
  • 2 egg yolks, from medium eggs
  • 1 Tbsp double cream

Chocolate Layer

  • 200 g dark chocolate, small pieces or callets
  • 50 g butter, diced
  • 130 g double cream
  • 400 g sweetened condensed milk (caramel)
  • 50 g almonds

Preparation

  1. Place walnuts in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Preheat oven to 180°C. Line a brownie tin (approx. 32 cm x 18 cm) with enough baking paper to overhang sides of tin.

Dough

  1. Place butter, flour, sugar, egg yolks and cream in mixing bowl then mix 20 sec/speed 6. Transfer to tin and press into base and 1 cm up sides.
  2. Bake for 15-20 minutes until golden (180°C). Remove from oven and place on a wire rack to cool. Clean mixing bowl and make chocolate layer.

Chocolate Layer

  1. Place chocolate in mixing bowl then chop 6 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add butter and cream then melt 5 min/80°C/speed 1.
  3. Add reserved chopped nuts and mix 5 sec//speed 3. Remove mixing bowl and allow to cool for 30 minutes.
  4. Spread caramel condensed milk on cooled cake base then pour over cooled chocolate walnut filling. Decorate with walnut halves and almonds then place in fridge to set for at least 3 hours. Cut in pieces and serve.

Useful Items

baking paper, brownie tin (32 cm x 18 cm), wire rack, oven, refrigerator
