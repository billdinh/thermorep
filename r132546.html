Tapenade Baked Chicken and Mashed Potatoes with Caramelized Onions

Ingredients

Tapenade

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 120 g black olives, pitted
  • 30 g capers, pickled
  • 5 g lemon juice, freshly squeezed
  • 2 anchovy fillets, preserved in salt rinsed with water to remove salt
  • 1 sprig fresh thyme, leaves only
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 - 2 pinches ground black pepper, to taste
  • 30 g olive oil

Chicken Filling

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 - 3 sprigs fresh rosemary, leaves only, to taste
  • 1 lemon, preferably organic, small, quartered
  • 1 level tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper

Chicken Assembly

  • 1200 - 1500 g chicken, gutted
  • salt, for seasoning
  • ground black pepper, for seasoning
  • hot paprika, for seasoning
  • 200 g chicken stock
  • 1400 g floury potatoes

Mashed Potatoes

  • 300 g onions, halved
  • 15 g olive oil
  • 20 g butter
  • 20 g liquid caramel
  • ½ tsp meat stock paste, homemade
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 350 g milk

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.

Tapenade

  1. Place garlic clove in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8.
  2. Add black olives, capers, lemon juice, anchovy fillets, thyme, salt and black pepper then chop 10 sec/speed 4.5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add olive oil and mix 10 sec/speed 4.5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Chicken filling

  1. Place garlic cloves, rosemary, lemon, preferably organic, salt and black pepper in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.

Chicken assembly

  1. Gently slide fingers under skin from back of the chicken to the front, to loosen skin (be careful not to tear it). Using a butter knife, loosen the skin at the front of the chicken. Add reserved tapenade with a teaspoon between loosened skin and chicken breast. Press the skin with your fingers to spread the tapenade from the back to the front of the chicken. Stuff the chicken with its filling and bundle the chicken legs together with a kitchen string. Season skin with salt, black pepper and paprika. Then place the prepared chicken in a casserole dish and add chicken stock to it. Bake for 1 hour (180°C), then increase oven temperature to 200°C and bake a further 30 minutes. After the first 30 minutes baking time, start with the peeling of the potatoes and continue with preparation of the mashed potatoes.

Mashed potatoes

  1. Place onions in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl using spatula.
  2. Add olive oil and butter then sauté 8 min/120°C/speed 1. Meanwhile cut peeled potatoes in 1-2 cm cubes.
  3. Add liquid caramel and meat stock paste then sauté 2 min/120°C/speed 1.
  4. Insert butterfly whisk. Add 1000 g potatoes pieces, salt and milk then cook without measuring cup 20-25 min/95°C/speed 1.
  5. Insert measuring cup and mash 30 sec/speed 3. Transfer to a serving bowl and set aside.
  6. Remove cooking string and serve baked chicken hot with mashed potatoes and reduced chicken stock with chicken juices as a sauce.

Useful Items

casserole dish, kitchen string, oven
