Cheddar and Stilton Soufflé Omelette

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs, separated
  • 30 g Cheddar cheese, cut in pieces
  • 30 g Stilton, cut in pieces
  • 20 g spring onions, sliced (1 cm)
  • 30 g milk
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 1 Tbsp butter, for frying
  • 2 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only, chopped, for serving

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk into a very clean mixing bowl. Place egg whites in mixing bowl and whisk, without measuring cup, 1.5-3 min/speed 3 until stiff peaks. Remove butterfly whisk then transfer whisked egg whites to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place egg yolks, Cheddar, Stilton, spring onions, milk, salt and pepper in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 6.
  4. Add a quarter of the whisked egg whites and mix 5 sec/speed 2.
  5. Add remaining whisked egg whites and gently mix 10 sec/speed 2, then finish folding in with spatula.
  6. Heat butter in an oven-proof frying pan over a medium heat until melted. Add omelette mixture and cook for about 5 minutes then transfer to oven for 5 minutes (180°C) to finish cooking. Ease around the edges of omelette with a spatula then slide onto a warm plate and serve immediately with a scattering of chopped fresh parsley leaves.

Useful Items

oven-proof frying pan, oven, hob

Hints & Tricks

  • To serve 2, double all ingredients.

  • A twist on the classic three-egg omelette, here we have jazzed it up a bit by incorporating whisked egg whites to the mix. This creates a light, soufflé-like omelette, with a wonderful flavour of two very English cheeses.
