Pepper and Salami Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 onion, halved (approx. 80 g)
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 fresh red chilli, deseeded if desired
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 600 g peppers, pickled, , for serving, if desired
  • 30 g tomato purée
  • 100 g water
  • 1 tsp vegetable stock paste
  • 1 tsp balsamic vinegar
  • ¾ tsp fine sea salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • ½ tsp sweet paprika
  • 1 pinch sugar
  • 30 g crème fraîche
  • 80 g salami, cut in strips (1 cm)
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
926 kJ / 222 kcal

Preparation

  1. Place onion, garlic and chilli in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add olive oil and sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  3. Add peppers and heat 5 min/100°C//speed 1. Remove approximately ¼ pepper strips and set aside.
  4. Add tomato purée and heat  2 min/100°C/speed 1.
  5. Add water, vegetable stock, balsamic vinegar, salt, pepper, paprika and sugar then cook 6 min/100°C/speed 1.
  6. Add crème fraîche then purée 30 sec/speed 4 -8  increasing speed gradually.
  7. Add salami and reserved pepper strips then heat 2 min/100°C//speed 1. Serve with pasta.
