Garlic Chicken Sauté with Bok Choy

Ingredients

Glaze

  • 2 tbsp corn starch
  • 1 tbsp dark brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp fermented black bean paste
  • 4 oz chicken stock
  • 1 ½ - 2 oz soy sauce, to taste
  • 1 oz black vinegar
  • ½ oz oyster sauce
  • ½ oz sesame oil

Sauté

  • 15 garlic cloves
  • 4 green onions, cut into pieces
  • ½ oz sesame oil
  • 32 oz boneless, skinless chicken thighs, sliced (1 in. x 2 in.)
  • 10 oz baby bok choy, halved
  • 1 fresh Fresno chili, red, sliced, to serve

Preparation

Glaze

  1. Place corn starch, brown sugar, black bean paste, chicken stock, soy sauce, black vinegar, oyster sauce and sesame oil into mixing bowl and without measuring cup cook 8 min/Varoma/speed 2. Transfer glaze to a bowl and set aside.

Sauté

  1. Place garlic, green onions and sesame oil into mixing bowl, insert measuring cup and chop 2 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and without measuring cup sauté 5 min/250°F/speed 1.
  2. Add chicken, insert measuring cup and cook 6 min/212°F//speed . Drain chicken through simmering basket, discard liquid and return drained chicken to mixing bowl.
  3. Add half the reserved glaze, stir through with spatula and cook 3 min/Varoma//speed . Transfer to a serving dish.
  4. Place bok choy and remaining glaze into mixing bowl and cook 4 min/Varoma//speed . Transfer to serving dish with chicken mixture, garnish with red Fresno chili slices and serve hot.
