Chia coconut bread

Ingredients

  • 100 g water
  • 2 tbsp chia seeds
  • coconut oil, for greasing
  • 235 g desiccated coconut
  • 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • ½ tsp ground ginger
  • ¼ tsp ground cloves
  • ¼ tsp ground cardamom
  • 3 eggs
  • 170 g natural yoghurt
  • 2 tbsp pure maple syrup
  • ½ tsp natural vanilla extract

Preparation

  1. Place a glass or ceramic bowl onto mixing bowl lid, weigh water into bowl, then add chia seeds and set aside to soak for 1 hour.
  2. Preheat oven to 160°C. Grease and line a loaf tin (18 x 7 x 6 cm) with baking paper, leaving a 2 cm overhang and set aside.
  3. Place desiccated coconut into mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 9.
  4. Add all remaining ingredients, including reserved chia and water mixture, and combine 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then mix 5 sec/speed 4.
  5. Transfer into prepared tin and bake for 50-55 minutes (160°C), or until a wooden skewer inserted into the centre of the bread comes out clean.
  6. Leave to cool in tin for 10 minutes, then using overhanging baking paper, remove from tin and transfer onto a wire rack to cool completely before serving.

Useful Items

wooden skewer, ceramic or glass bowl, loaf tin (18 x 7 x 6 cm), baking paper, wire rack, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Wrap bread in plastic wrap or place into a sealable storage container and store in refrigerator for up to 1 week.
  • Serve toasted for breakfast with your favourite compote and yoghurt, or spread with a little butter or jam and enjoy for afternoon tea.
