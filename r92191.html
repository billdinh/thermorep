Kashmiri Rice

Ingredients

  • 350 g basmati rice
  • 3 cardamom pods
  • 3 cloves
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 pinch saffron threads
  • 30 g oil
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 800 g water
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 100 g onions, quartered
  • 20 g hazelnuts, with skin
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
279 kcal
Protein
5 g
Carbohydrates
47 g
Fat
7 g

Preparation

  1. Insert simmering basket and weigh in rice, cardamom pods, cloves, cinnamon stick, ½ tsp turmeric and saffron threads then set aside on a plate.
  2. Place oil and garlic in mixing bowl then chop 8 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then sauté 3 min/Varoma/speed 1. Tip out and add to reserved rice.
  3. Place water, ½ tsp turmeric and salt in mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket and steam 22 min/Varoma/speed 4. Remove simmering basket, set aside and keep warm. Discard water then clean and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Place onions and hazelnuts in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then toast 5 min/Varoma/speed 1. Serve the rice garnished with the onion and hazelnut mixture.
