Barley and Lentil Radicchio Salad

Ingredients

  • 3 oz dry lentils
  • 3 oz pearl barley
  • 55 oz water, divided
  • 7 oz bacon, cut into pieces (¼ in.)
  • 3 oz extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 oz shallots, cut into pieces
  • 5 sprigs thyme, leaves only
  • ¼ tsp salt, to taste
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 2 oz lemon juice
  • 1 oz stone ground mustard
  • ½ oz honey
  • 5 oz red onions, cut into pieces
  • 5 oz radicchio, cored and cut into pieces (3 in.)
  • ½ oz parsley leaves
  • 6 oz radicchio, cut into pieces (3 in.)
  • 1 pomegranate, arils/seeds only
  • 8 tbsp slivered almonds

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in lentils, barley and 20 oz water. Let soak 1 hour. Meanwhile make bacon dressing.
  2. Place bacon into mixing bowl, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 30 min/250°F/speed . Drain bacon into simmering basket over a bowl, reserve drippings and set aside.
  3. Place a pitcher onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in oil. Set aside.
  4. Place shallots, thyme, salt and pepper into mixing bowl, insert measuring cup and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add 2 oz reserved bacon fat, lemon juice, stone ground mustard and honey and mix 1 min 30 sec/speed 4, while slowly pouring reserved oil onto mixing bowl lid, letting it drizzle around measuring cup in a thin stream onto rotating blades to create an emulsion. Return dressing to pitcher and set aside.
  6. Rinse lentils and barley through simmering basket, insert simmering basket with grains into mixing bowl, add 35 oz water and cook 20 min/212°F//speed 1. Remove simmering basket with spatula, set aside and allow to drain. Clean mixing bowl.
  7. Place onions into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Add 5 oz radicchio and parsley and shred 8 sec//speed 4. Transfer to a serving bowl.
  8. Place 6 oz radicchio into mixing bowl and shred 8 sec//speed 4. Transfer to serving bowl with shredded radicchio-parsley mixture.
  9. Add pomegranate seeds and almonds to serving bowl. Add reserved grains, reserved bacon and reserved bacon dressing and toss to combine. Serve warm.

Useful Items

serving bowl, pitcher

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with sliced apples for added freshness.
  • Serve with flaked smoked salmon or steamed fresh salmon with lemons: place salmon into Varoma over a bed of lemon slices, season with salt and pepper and steam 12-50 minutes/Varoma/speed 2, until cooked to your liking.
