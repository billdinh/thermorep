Shortcrust pastry

Ingredients

  • 100 g white glutinous rice (e.g. sushi rice)
  • 1 tbsp white chia seeds
  • 40 g blanched almonds
  • 50 g arrowroot flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 20 g potato starch
  • 2 pinches salt
  • 80 g unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 tbsp cold water

Preparation

  1. Place rice and chia seeds into mixing bowl and mill 1 min 30 sec/speed 9.
  2. Add almonds and mill 20 sec/speed 9.
  3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Add arrowroot flour, potato starch, salt and butter and mix 10 sec/speed 3.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Add egg yolks and water and knead 40 sec/. Transfer pastry onto a silicone bread mat or work surface. Flatten pastry into a disc (1 cm thickness), wrap in plastic wrap and place into refrigerator to chill for a minimum of 1 hour. The pastry should be firm enough to roll.
  5. Place pastry between 2 pieces of baking paper and dust with arrowroot. Roll out pastry to slightly larger than tart tins being used (approx. 4 mm thickness) (see Tips).
  6. Carefully remove top layer of baking paper and line tins carefully with pastry. Prick base with a fork and place into refrigerator for 10 minutes. Bake for 15 minutes (170°C), or until lightly brown and cooked. Use tart shells as needed.

Useful Items

silicone bread mat, plastic wrap, baking paper, rolling pin, individual tart tins, refrigerator, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • This pastry will line a 20 cm tart tin or 8 individual tart tins (8 cm).

  • Sweet shortcrust pastry: Before step 1, place 20 g raw sugar into mixing bowl and mill 20 sec/speed 8, then add rice and chia seeds and proceed as per recipe.
