Couscous with chicken, lamb and spicy sausages

Ingredients

  • 4 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only
  • 4 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 200 g brown onions, cut into halves
  • 400 g lamb shoulder, preferably bone in (see tip), cut into pieces (4 cm x 4 cm)
  • 3 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp ground pepper
  • 40 g olive oil
  • 400 g carrots, cut into pieces (approx. 1.5 cm)
  • 250 g turnips, cut into 6 or 8 pieces (depending on size)
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • 900 g water
  • 300 g skinless chicken breast fillets, cut into fine strips (approx. 2 cm long)
  • 6 beef or lamb spicy sausages (optional)
  • 400 g zucchinis, unpeeled, cut into slices (approx. 1.5 cm)
  • 350 g couscous, medium grain
  • 70 g raisins
  • 350 g canned chickpeas, rinsed and drained
  • 1 - 2 tsp harissa paste

Preparation

  1. Place coriander and parsley into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a small bowl and set aside.
  2. Place onions into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add lamb meat, 2 tsp of the salt, pepper and 20 g of the oil and sauté 10 min/Varoma//speed .
  4. Add carrots, turnips, cinnamon, ginger, 550 g water and reserved chopped herbs. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh chicken breast fillets into it, making sure that some holes remain unobstructed. Arrange spicy sausages (optional) and zucchinis on top of chicken. Secure Varoma lid and steam 15 min/Varoma//speed . Meanwhile, place couscous, remaining 1 tsp salt and remaining 20 g oil into a bowl. Rub the couscous in a circular motion between your hands to moisten the grains evenly and remove any lumps that may form, cover with 350 g water and stir using spatula to moisten the couscous evenly. Cover the bowl and set aside.
  5. Gently fluff the couscous with a fork to separate the grains and transfer it onto Varoma tray. Carefully remove Varoma lid, insert prepared Varoma tray, secure Varoma lid and steam a further 10 min/Varoma//speed . Remove Varoma.
  6. Add raisins and chickpeas into mixing bowl, place Varoma back into position and heat 5 min/Varoma//speed . Remove Varoma and transfer contents of mixing bowl into a large serving dish. Add chicken, sausages and zucchinis and combine gently using spatula. Spoon out 100 g of the broth from the serving dish into a gravy boat, add harissa and stir with a fork. Transfer couscous to another serving dish. Serve couscous, vegetables, meat and sausages (optional) hot, with harissa sauce on the side.

Hints & Tricks

  • This dish easily serves 6. If you serve a starter first, it can serve up to 8 people.
  • Couscous is a traditional dish from northwest Africa. Merguez are fresh, spicy sausages made from mutton and/or beef, and are typical of the region. Replace them with any other spicy fresh sausage, or omit them altogether. For a more traditional flavour, replace the cinnamon and ginger with 2 tablespoons ras-el-hanout, a North African spice blend that can contain up to 40 ingredients.#
  • The bone in the lamb shoulder meat adds flavour. However if you are cutting the meat yourself, the dish will also be excellent with boneless meat.
    - If you have some couscous left over, use it to prepare a traditional Moroccan dessert. Place couscous onto a plate, sprinkle with 50 g sugar and 1 tsp ground cinnamon. Serve warm or cold.
