Mexican chicken soup

Ingredients

  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 3 sprigs fresh oregano, leaves only
  • 4 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves, stems and roots, plus extra leaves for garnishing (see Tips)
  • 1 long red chilli, deseeded if preferred
  • 1 brown onion, (approx. 150 g), cut into halves
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 limes, cut into halves
  • 350 g skinless chicken thigh fillets, cut into cubes (2 cm)
  • 750 g water
  • 2 tbsp Chicken stock paste (see Tips)
  • 400 g canned corn kernels, rinsed and drained (weight is pre-draining)
  • 400 g canned kidney beans, rinsed and drained (weight is pre-draining)
  • 190 g fresh tomatoes, finely diced
  • 2 spring onions/shallots, trimmed and sliced
  • 140 g red capsicum, deseeded and diced
  • 240 g avocado, flesh only, sliced
  • 180 g cabbage, shredded
  • corn chips, to serve
  • sour cream, to serve
  • marinated jalapeño chillies (jarred), to serve

Preparation

  1. Place garlic, oregano, coriander (leaves, stems and roots), chilli, onion, cumin and olive oil into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then sauté 5 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  2. Squeeze the juice from lime halves into a bowl, then add lime juice to mixing bowl with the squeezed lime halves.
  3. Add chicken, water and stock paste and cook 10 min/100°C//speed . Remove squeezed lime halves with a slotted spoon, then squeeze well before discarding.
  4. Add corn and kidney beans and cook 4 min/100°C//speed .
  5. Divide soup between 6 large serving bowls, then top with tomato, spring onion/shallot, capsicum, avocado and cabbage. Serve soup immediately with corn chips, sour cream, jalapeños and extra coriander leaves.

Useful Items

slotted spoon, 6 large soup bowls

Hints & Tricks

  • To prepare coriander for use in this recipe, remove the fibrous brown off-shoots from the main root by scraping down with a teaspoon.
  • Please refer to The Basic Cookbook or the Everyday Cookbook for the Chicken stock paste recipe.
