Crunchy quinoa patties with avocado smash

Ingredients

Quinoa patties

  • 130 g tri-colour quinoa
  • 850 g water, plus extra for soaking
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 80 g Gruyère cheese, cut into pieces (2 cm)
  • 40 g chickpea (besan) flour
  • 3 eggs
  • 30 g white chia seeds
  • 2 spring onions/shallots, cut into thin slices (3 mm)
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • olive oil, for frying

Smashed avocado

  • 2 avocados, flesh only
  • ½ lime, juice only
  • 1 pinch salt

Poached eggs

  • 1600 g water
  • 30 g white vinegar
  • oil, for brushing
  • 4 eggs
  • balsamic glaze, to serve
  • fresh flat-leaf parsley, to serve

Preparation

Quinoa patties

  1. Place simmering basket onto mixing bowl lid and weigh quinoa into it. Rest simmering basket in a large bowl. Cover with water and allow to soak for 5 minutes. Rinse under running water until water runs clear.
  2. Place water and ¼ teaspoon of the salt into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket with quinoa into mixing bowl and cook 15-20 min/Varoma/speed 1, until quinoa is cooked. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and set quinoa aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Place cheese into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add flour, eggs, chia seeds, spring onions/shallots, pepper, remaining ¼ teaspoon salt and cooked quinoa and combine 10 sec//speed 3. Set aside for 15 minutes to allow mixture to firm.
  5. Place a large frying pan over medium heat and add oil. Divide mixture into 8 portions (approx. 2 tablespoons each) and shape into patties. Working in batches, fry patties for 7-8 minutes on each side or until golden. Transfer into a thermal serving bowl or onto a plate and cover to keep warm. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Smashed avocado

  1. Place avocado, lime and salt into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 4. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Rinse mixing bowl.

Poached eggs

  1. Place a paper towel on a plate and set aside. Place water and vinegar into mixing bowl. Brush simmering basket with oil, insert simmering basket and boil 12 min/100°C/speed 1 or until boiling.
  2. Crack 2 eggs into two separate bowls and set aside. Poach 3 min/100°C/speed 3, adding eggs 1 at a time through hole in mixing bowl lid into simmering basket. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and lay poached eggs onto paper towel to drain well.
  3. Crack another 2 eggs into separate bowls. Re-oil simmering basket and insert into mixing bowl. Reheat water 3 min/100°C/speed 1 or until boiling. Poach 3 min/100°C/speed 3, adding eggs 1 at a time through hole in mixing bowl lid into simmering basket. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and lay poached eggs onto paper towel.
  4. Divide patties, avocado and poached eggs between serving plates. Garnish with balsamic glaze and parsley to serve.
