Super Quick Broccoli Salad

Ingredients

  • 350 g broccoli florets
  • 1 eating apple, (approx. 150 g), quartered, stem removed
  • 20 g onion
  • 100 g courgettes, cut in pieces
  • 20 g cider vinegar, preferably organic
  • 60 g extra virgin olive oil
  • ½ tsp sea salt
  • ¼ tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 40 g raisins (optional)
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
911 kJ / 218 kcal
Protein
4.7 g
Carbohydrates
13.8 g

16 g

Preparation

  1. Place broccoli, apple, onion, courgette, vinegar, oil, salt and pepper in mixing bowl then chop 7 sec/speed 4.
  2. Add raisins if using and stir 5 sec//speed 2. Transfer to a serving bowl and serve straight away or chill to serve later.

Useful Items

refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Make half the quantities to serve two (or serve one portion at dinner and chill the other portion for lunch the next day).
  • Serves 6-8 as a side salad.

  • If making ahead, cover and chill in the fridge for up to 4 hours. Stir well and check seasoning - you may need more when it's cold.
  • Super quick salads, or instant salads as they are sometimes known, are a wonderful fresh addition to meals, and help increase your intake of fruit and vegetables easily.
  • Salads chopped with their dressing ingredients always taste better because the fruits and vegetables are immediately coated as they are chopped, so all the natural juices become part of the dressing.

  • Use freshly squeezed lemon juice instead of cider vinegar.
  • Replace 30 g oil with 30 g apple juice if desired.
  • Substitute your favourite vegetables and fruits to make different salads.
  • Add 30-50 g nuts and/or seeds if desired.
