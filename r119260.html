Spinach salad with crunchy quinoa and green goddess dressing

Ingredients

Green goddess dressing

  • 60 g olive oil
  • 30 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 eschalot
  • 2 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 4 sprigs fresh tarragon, leaves only
  • 2 sprigs fresh basil, leaves only
  • 250 g avocados (approx. 2 medium avocados), flesh only
  • 40 g apple cider vinegar
  • 2 anchovy fillets
  • 2 tsp lime juice
  • salt, to taste
  • freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Spinach salad

  • 120 g white quinoa
  • 500 g water, plus extra for soaking
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp pomegranate molasses
  • 200 g fresh baby spinach leaves
  • 1 pomegranate, arils only

Preparation

Green goddess dressing

  1. Place a jug onto mixing bowl lid and weigh oil into it. Set jug aside.
  2. Place a small bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh cream into it. Set bowl aside.
  3. Place garlic, eschalot and herbs into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 6.
  4. Add avocados, apple cider vinegar, anchovies and lime juice and blend 20 sec/speed 5.
  5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix 1 min/speed 3, slowly pouring oil, then cream through hole in mixing bowl lid until combined.
  6. Add salt and pepper and mix 10 sec/speed 3. Transfer into a bowl and cover with plastic wrap on surface of dressing to prevent a skin forming. Place into refrigerator for a minimum of 2 hours before serving. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Spinach salad

  1. Place a ceramic or glass bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh quinoa into it. Cover with water and set aside to soak for 5 minutes. Drain quinoa using simmering basket, then rinse quinoa until water runs clear. Transfer half of the quinoa into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place water into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket with remaining quinoa in it and steam 15 min/Varoma/speed 1. Transfer into a large bowl and toss with oil and pomegranate molasses. Set aside to cool.
  3. Place a non-stick frying pan over medium-high heat and add reserved quinoa, stirring to evaporate liquid. Continue stirring until quinoa grains toast and pop (approx. 2-3 minutes). Transfer into a separate bowl and set aside. Once cooled, add to large bowl with steamed quinoa.
  4. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh spinach into it. Add spinach to large bowl and toss gently to combine. Transfer into a serving bowl and spoon over Green goddess dressing as desired. Top with pomegranate arils and serve with extra dressing on the side. 

Useful Items

jug, small bowl , bowl, plastic wrap, ceramic or glass bowl, large bowl, large non-stick frying pan, serving bowl, refrigerator, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • You can replace white quinoa with any colour quinoa in this recipe.
  • You can store the Green goddess dressing in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Serve any leftover dressing with roast potatoes.
