Tamarind and Chilli Barbecued Lamb Chops

Ingredients

  • 70 g onions, quartered
  • 10 g fresh root ginger, peeled, cut in round slices (2 mm)
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 jalapeño chilli, fresh, halved, deseeded
  • 1 ripe mango, large, cut in pieces
  • 3 Tbsp tamarind paste
  • 4 Tbsp treacle
  • ¼ tsp ground allspice
  • 90 g red wine
  • 180 g orange juice
  • 2 Tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 24 lamb chops

Preparation

  1. Light barbecue or preheat grill (220°C).
  2. Place onions, ginger, garlic and chilli in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 7.
  3. Add mango, tamarind paste, treacle, allspice, red wine and orange juice then cook 25 min/98°C/speed . Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add vinegar and, without measuring cup, cook 10 min/100°C/speed . Insert measuring cup then blend 1 min/speed 10.
  5. Place lamb chops on barbecue or under a hot grill (220°C). When charred, flip over and char other side.
  6. Brush each chop generously with tamarind and mango sauce then move to cooler part of barbecue, or lower under the grill. Cook for 5-8 minutes, turning over halfway to baste other side. Serve immediately or keep warm.

Useful Items

pastry brush, grill

Hints & Tricks

  • Add extra jalapeños or hotter chillies for more heat.
