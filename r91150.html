Chocolate Lava Cake

Ingredients

  • 5 ½ oz unsalted butter, cut into pieces, plus extra to grease
  • 6 large eggs, separated
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 7 oz baker's sugar, divided
  • 9 oz dark chocolate, cut into small pieces
  • 2 oz all-purpose flour,

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Grease a springform pan (approx. 8½ in.) and set aside.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg whites and salt into mixing bowl and whisk 6 min/speed 3.5 or until stiff.
  3. Add 1 oz sugar and mix 20 sec/speed 3.5. Remove butterfly whisk. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place remaining 6 oz sugar, egg yolks and butter into mixing bowl and mix 6 min/160°F/speed 4.
  5. Add chocolate, wait a few seconds for chocolate to soften, then mix 20 sec/speed 5.
  6. Add flour and mix 10 sec/speed 5.
  7. Insert butterfly whisk. Add reserved whisked egg whites and mix 15 sec/speed 2.5. Remove butterfly whisk and transfer cake batter into prepared springform pan.
  8. Bake for 10-15 minutes (400°F). Allow cake to cool in pan for 5 minutes and serve warm or cold.

Useful Items

springform pan (approx. 8½ in.), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Quick Chocolate Cake: place chocolate into mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 7. Add butter and melt 4 min/160°F/speed 2. Add flour, sugar, salt and eggs and mix 15 sec/speed 5. Transfer into prepared springform pan and bake for approximately 20 minutes (350°F).
  • f using chocolate with 70% cocoa or more, reduce amount to 7 oz.
  • The mixing method and short baking time produce a mousse-like center. If you prefer a firm cake, bake for 20-25 minutes (350°F).
  • The cake can be served warm with vanilla ice cream.
