Hazelnut hot chocolate

Ingredients

Hazelnut chocolate spread

  • 150 g raw hazelnuts, skins on
  • 40 g raw cacao powder
  • 100 g pure maple syrup
  • 100 g milk

Hazelnut hot chocolate

  • 500 g milk

Preparation

Hazelnut chocolate spread

  1. Place hazelnuts into mixing bowl and mill 35 sec/speed 9 or until finely ground. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add cacao, maple syrup and milk and blend 30 sec/speed 4.
  3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and blend 1 min/speed 6 or until smooth. Transfer into a sealable jar or container and place into refrigerator until ready to use (spread will thicken slightly in refrigerator).

Hazelnut hot chocolate

  1. Place milk into mixing bowl and warm 6 min/90°C/speed 2.
  2. Add 100 g of the reserved Hazelnut chocolate spread and blend 1 min/speed 8 or until well combined and frothy. Transfer into cups or mugs to serve.

Useful Items

sealable storage jar or container, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Hazelnut chocolate spread can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 2-3 weeks, but no longer than the expiration date of your ingredients.
  • If you don’t have any raw cacao on hand, you can use regular cocoa powder instead.
  • If you would prefer a lower fructose option, you can substitute the maple syrup with rice malt syrup.
  • Dairy-free hazelnut hot chocolate: You can substitute the cow’s milk with almond milk or soy milk.
