Smoky beetroot and black bean sliders with soused cucumber

Ingredients

Soused cucumber

  • 50 g apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tbsp white sugar
  • 1 long red chilli, cut into slices
  • 1 tbsp poppy seeds
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 100 g Lebanese cucumber, peeled or sliced into ribbons (3 mm thick)

Patties

  • 90 g brown onion
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 tsp olive oil, plus extra for frying
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 100 g raw beetroots, peeled and cut into pieces (approx. 3 cm)
  • 1 tbsp pepitas
  • 400 g canned black beans, rinsed and drained (approx. 250 g after draining)
  • 2 tbsp chickpea (besan) flour
  • 1 - 2 tsp ground smoked paprika (optional)
  • 1 tsp liquid smoke seasoning (optional - see Tips)

Assembly

  • 180 g sour cream
  • 2 - 3 tsp horseradish cream
  • 6 slider buns (see Tips)

Preparation

Soused cucumber

  1. Place vinegar and sugar into mixing bowl and heat 3 min/Varoma/speed 2 or until sugar dissolves.
  2. Add chilli, poppy seeds, salt and pepper and mix 5 sec/speed 2. Set aside to cool (approx. 30 minutes).
  3. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh cucumber into it. Pour mixture over cucumber, cover and set aside to marinate. Clean and dry mixing bowl. Meanwhile, proceed with recipe.

Patties

  1. Place onion, garlic and oil into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add chia seeds and cook 3 min/Varoma/speed 2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add beetroot and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add pumpkin seeds, black beans, flour, paprika (optional) and liquid smoke (optional) and combine 30 sec/speed 4. Shape mixture into 6 patties (approx. 6 cm in diameter), transfer onto a plate and set aside to rest for 15 minutes.
  5. Place a large frying pan over medium heat and add a little of the extra oil. Working in batches, cook patties for 3-5 minutes on each side or until cooked to your liking.

Assembly

  1. Place sour cream and horseradish into a small bowl and stir to combine.
  2. Cut slider buns into halves horizontally and toast (if desired). Top each bun with a patty, Soused cucumbers and sour cream mixture to serve.

Useful Items

bowl, small bowl, large non-stick frying pan, large plate, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • You can find liquid smoke at specialty food stores, some supermarkets and online. It adds a delicious smoky flavour to foods (minus the fire!).
  • We used our Buckwheat and almond slider buns (please refer to the recipe on this recipe chip), but you can use any type of roll or bun you like.
