Spanish Tortilla - Tortilla Española

Ingredients

  • 3 ½ oz olive oil, plus extra to fry
  • 3 ½ oz water
  • 7 oz yellow onions, cut in half moons, sliced thinly (approx. 1/16 in.) (see Tip)
  • 17 ½ oz waxy potatoes, e.g. Yukon Gold, cut in half moons peeled and sliced (approx. ¼ in.) (see Tip)
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 6 large eggs
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper

Preparation

  1. Place oil and water into mixing bowl and heat 5 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  2. Add onions and cook 5 min/Varoma//speed .
  3. Add potatoes and salt and cook 14 min/212°F//speed . Check if potatoes are cooked but not starting to fall apart and if necessary, cook again 3 min/Varoma//speed .
  4. In a large bowl mix together eggs and pepper, stirring well with a whisk. Combine potato mixture from mixing bowl with eggs and gently stir together.
  5. Heat a frying pan with a generous amount of oil over medium heat. Gently pour egg and potato mixture into hot pan and cook for 10 minutes or until egg is set. Invert pan with tortilla onto a large plate, then slide tortilla back into frying pan to cook the other side (approx. 5_minutes). Once cooked turn tortilla out onto a large serving plate. Serve hot, warm or cold.

Useful Items

wire whisk, large bowl, frying pan, stove

Hints & Tricks

  • Serves 4-6 for lunch or as a main course or 12 as tapas.
  • It is important to use waxy potatoes such as Yukon Gold potatoes so that the potato slices retain their shape during cooking. If the potatoes do begin to fall apart, don't worry, the tortilla will still taste delicious.
  • How to cut in half moons: Start at one of the flat ends (with onion half lying flat on the cutting board) and slice horizontally to create half moon-shaped pieces.
  • Replace onion with leeks, well washed and sliced (¾ in.).
  • For a lower GI version, replace potatoes with sweet potatoes and reduce cooking time to 10 minutes in Step 3.
