Seafood rice

Ingredients

  • 400 g clams
  • 300 g whole unpeeled prawns
  • 800 g water
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 200 g chopped tomatoes, canned
  • 150 g red pepper
  • 150 g green pepper
  • 70 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 350 g rice
  • 2 tbsp parsley, fresh, chopped
  • 1 pinch saffron threads
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 150 g white wine
  • 50 g orange juice

Preparation

  1. Place clams in salted water so that they release all of the sand (approx. 30 minutes).
  2. Shell prawns (set the meat aside) and place the shells, the heads and the water into mixing bowl. Wash the clams with hot water. Put the Varoma into position, insert the clams and cover with the Varoma lid. Steam 14 min/Varoma/speed 2. Set Varoma aside without removing the Varoma lid. Strain the stock with a fine mesh strainer and set aside. Rinse the mixing bowl and mixing bowl lid well.
  3. Place garlic, chopped tomatoes, red and green pepper and olive oil in the mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Sauté 10 min/120°C/speed 1.
  4. Add rice and sauté 2 min/120°C//speed .
  5. Add half of the parsley, saffron, salt, white wine, orange juice and set aside stock, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid to aid evaporation and avoid spattering and cook 13 min/100°C//speed 1.
  6. When the cooking is finished add the meat of the prawns into mixing bowl and stir with spatula. Transfer the clams to a serving bowl. Pour the rice over the clams and stir lightly with the spatula. Garnish with the remaining chopped parsley and serve immediately.
