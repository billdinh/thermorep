Focaccia

Ingredients

  • 350 g water
  • 2 tsp dried instant yeast
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 500 g baker's flour
  • 1 tsp salt, plus extra for sprinkling
  • 2 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves only, for sprinkling
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
681 kJ / 162 kcal
Protein
5 g

30 g

Preparation

  1. Place water and yeast into mixing bowl and heat 2 min/37°C/speed 1. Meanwhile, line a baking tray with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Add extra virgin olive oil, baker's flour and salt and mix 20 sec/speed 6, then knead  2 min/.
  3. Transfer dough into a greased bowl, cover with plastic wrap or a lightly dampened tea towel and leave to prove in a warm place until doubled in size (approx. 1 hour).
  4. Preheat oven to 200°C. Knock down dough, then transfer onto prepared baking tray and form into desired shape. Leave to prove for a further 30 minutes.
  5. Sprinkle salt and thyme or rosemary leaves onto dough. Bake for 20-30 minutes (200°C) until golden brown.
  6. Allow to cool on tray for 5 minutes then transfer to a wire rack. Serve warm or allow to cool completely before transferring to an airtight container until ready to use.

Useful Items

plastic wrap or kitchen towel, baking tray and paper, wire rack, airtight container, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve focaccia with dips or with your choice of topping such as caramelised onion and goat's cheese or marinated red capsicum, fresh tomato and basil.
