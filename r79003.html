Fish pie with mashed potato topping

Ingredients

Fish pie filling

  • 30 g olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 500 g boneless white fish fillet, cut into pieces (3-4 cm)
  • 120 g smoked salmon, cut into strips (6 x 2 cm)
  • 250 g cherry tomatoes (1 punnet), cut into halves
  • 2 pinches sea salt
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 1 brown onion, cut into halves
  • 1 leek (approx. 110 g), trimmed and cut into pieces
  • 120 g bacon, cut into pieces

Velouté sauce

  • 3 - 5 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 30 g unsalted butter
  • 30 g plain flour
  • 130 g water
  • 1 tsp Vegetable stock paste (see Tips)
  • 70 g sour cream or pouring (whipping) cream

Mashed potato

  • 50 g Parmesan cheese, crust removed and cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 1000 g floury potatoes (e.g. Red Pontiac, Royal Blue), peeled and cut into pieces (2-3 cm)
  • ½ - 1 tsp salt, to taste
  • 250 g full cream milk
  • 30 g butter, plus extra for shaving
  • 1 - 2 pinches ground nutmeg (optional)

Preparation

Fish pie filling

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Lightly grease a baking dish (22 x 30 cm) and set aside.
  2. Place fish fillets, smoked salmon and cherry tomatoes into baking dish. Season with salt and pepper, then set baking dish aside.
  3. Place onion and leek into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 6.
  4. Add bacon and chop 2-4 sec/speed 4, until evenly chopped. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add olive oil and sauté 5 min/120°C/speed 1. Spread onion, leek and bacon mixture over fish and salmon in baking dish, then set baking dish aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Velouté sauce

  1. Place parsley into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add all remaining sauce ingredients and cook 5 min/90°C/speed 4. Transfer sauce into baking dish over onion and bacon mixture. Set baking dish aside in the refrigerator. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Mashed potato

  1. Place Parmesan into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Rinse and dry mixing bowl.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Place potatoes, salt and milk into mixing bowl and cook 25 min/95°C/speed 1, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  3. Add butter, nutmeg (optional) and reserved grated Parmesan, insert measuring cup and mash 30 sec/speed 3.
  4. Transfer mashed potato into baking dish and spread over evenly using spatula. Scatter extra butter shavings on top and bake for 40 minutes (180°C). Leave to stand for 5 minutes in baking dish before serving.

Useful Items

baking dish (22 x 30 cm), bowl, oven, refrigerator
