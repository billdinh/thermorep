Berry and cream filo stacks

Ingredients

Filo stacks

  • 80 g shelled Brazil nuts (see Tips)
  • 60 g unsalted butter, cut into pieces
  • 4 sheets filo pastry
  • 5 tbsp icing sugar

Berry and cream filling

  • 200 g thickened cream
  • 100 g mascarpone cheese
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract
  • 2 tbsp icing sugar
  • 120 g strawberries, trimmed and cut into thin slices (see Tips)
  • fresh mint leaves only, for garnishing (optional)

Preparation

Filo stacks

  1. Place Brazil nuts into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 6. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place butter into mixing bowl and melt 2 min/90°C/speed 2. Transfer into a separate bowl and set aside.
  3. Preheat oven to 190°C. Line a baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  4. Place 1 sheet of the filo pastry onto a silicone bread mat or clean work surface and brush with reserved melted butter. Scatter with one-quarter of the reserved Brazil nuts and, using a fine-mesh sieve, dust with 1 tablespoon of the icing sugar. Place a second sheet of the filo pastry on top and brush with butter, scatter with another one-quarter of the Brazil nuts and dust with another 1 tablespoon of the icing sugar. Repeat the process twice more until you have 4 stacked sheets, finishing in a sprinkling of nuts and icing sugar. Cut into 16 squares (approx. 8 x 7 cm) and place onto prepared baking tray. Bake for 8-10 minutes (190°C) or until golden brown. Remove filo stacks from oven and dust immediately with remaining 1 tablespoon icing sugar. Set aside to cool. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Berry and cream filling

  1. Insert butterfly whisk. Place cream, mascarpone, vanilla and icing sugar into mixing bowl and whip 30 sec/speed 3 until firm, watching carefully through hole in mixing bowl lid to avoid over whipping. Remove butterfly whisk.
  2. To assemble, place 8 of the filo squares onto a serving platter or individual serving plates. Top each square with 2 tablespoons of the cream mixture and half of the sliced strawberries. Place remaining 8 filo squares on top of strawberries and cream, pushing down gently. Top filo stacks with remaining cream and strawberries, then garnish with mint leaves (optional) to serve (see Tips).

Useful Items

bowl, baking tray (30 x 40 cm), baking paper, silicone bread mat, pastry brush, fine-mesh sieve, serving platter, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • You can use nuts and fresh fruit of choice in this recipe. We used Brazil nuts for the unique flavour and texture.
  • You can assemble this dish up to 1 hour before serving.
