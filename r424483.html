Fig and walnut ice cream

Ingredients

  • 200 g walnuts
  • 250 g dried figs
  • 220 g white sugar
  • 380 g water
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 500 g pouring (whipping) cream

Preparation

  1. Place walnuts into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 6. Transfer into a large bowl and set aside.
  2. Place figs, sugar and water into mixing bowl and heat 25 min/98Â°C/speed 2, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  3. Insert measuring cup. Blend 1 min/speed 4-9, increasing speed gradually from speed 4 to speed 9 or until smooth. Transfer into bowl with walnuts. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg yolks into mixing bowl and mix 1 min/speed 4, until thick and pale. Remove butterfly whisk. Transfer into bowl with fig mixture.
  5. Re-insert butterfly whisk. Place cream into mixing bowl and whisk without setting time/speed 3, until soft peaks form, watching carefully through hole in mixing bowl lid to avoid overwhipping. Remove butterfly whisk. Transfer into bowl with fig mixture and stir to combine with spatula. Transfer into a freezable container and place into freezer for 6-8 hours, until firm or ready to serve.
