Lamb Tagine with Apricots and Honey

Ingredients

  • 250 g couscous
  • 400 g water
  • 1 tsp ras el hanout (see tip)
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 100 g dried apricots, pitted
  • 60 g blanched almonds
  • 400 g onions, halved
  • 200 g carrots, cut in pieces
  • 20 g olive oil, plus 2 Tbsp
  • 500 g lamb meat
  • 200 g aubergines
  • 100 g tinned chopped tomatoes
  • 40 - 50 g honey, to taste
  • ½ vegetable stock cube (for 0.5 l)
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • 2 pinches saffron threads
  • 1 - 2 tsp lemon juice, to taste
  • ½ tsp ground cumin
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • ½ bunch fresh coriander, leaves only, chopped (see tip)

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid then weigh in couscous and 250 g water. Remove bowl, add ras el hanout, 2 Tbsp oil and ½ tsp salt then mix and leave to soak. Meanwhile, continue with recipe.
  2. Place apricots and almonds in mixing bowl then chop 4 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place onions and carrots in mixing bowl then chop 7 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add 20 g oil then, without measuring cup, cook 6 min/120°C//speed . Meanwhile, cut lamb in 2.5 cm cubes and aubergines in 1.5 cm cubes.
  5. Add tomatoes, lamb cubes, aubergine cubes, honey, 150 g water, vegetable stock, ginger, cinnamon, saffron, lemon juice, cumin, ½ tsp salt and pepper then cook 60 min/80°C//speed . Towards the end of cooking time, spread soaked couscous in Varoma dish, making sure that some holes remain uncovered to allow steam to circulate.
  6. Add reserved chopped apricots and almonds to mixing bowl. Place covered Varoma into position then cook 15 min/Varoma//speed . Remove Varoma.
  7. Arrange couscous with lamb tagine on serving dish and serve sprinkled with chopped coriander.
