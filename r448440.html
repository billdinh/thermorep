TM6

Red Pepper and Tomato Soup

Ingredients

  • 40 g lentils
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 50 g onions, quartered
  • 200 g red peppers, quartered (2 peppers)
  • 50 g olive oil
  • 400 g ripe tomatoes, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 400 g water
  • 1 vegetable stock cube (for 0.5 l), crumbled
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt, or to taste
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper, plus extra for sprinkling
  • ¼ tsp ground cayenne pepper (optional)
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1002 kJ / 241 kcal
Protein
5 g
Carbohydrates
13 g
Fat
18 g

Preparation

  1. Place lentils in mixing bowl and grind 20 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place garlic, onions, red peppers and oil in mixing bowl then chop 2 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then sauté 5 min/100°C/speed 1.
  3. Add tomatoes, water, vegetable stock, reserved ground lentils, salt, pepper and cayenne pepper (if using), cook 14 min/100°C/speed 2.5 then start Blend/1 min/speed 10.
  4. Divide between serving bowls and serve sprinkled with pepper.
