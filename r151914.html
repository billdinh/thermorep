Three Course Meal for Two: Vegetable Soup, Baked Chicken Casserole with Rice, Steamed Cinnamon Apple

Ingredients

Flavoured Breadcrumbs

  • 50 g bread roll, torn in pieces
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 5 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only

Three Course Meal

  • 250 g carrots, cut in pieces (2-3 cm)
  • 250 g courgettes, cut in pieces (2-3 cm)
  • 100 g leeks, white part only, sliced
  • 500 g chicken breasts, skinless, cut in strips (10 cm x 1 cm)
  • 2 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 2 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves only (optional)
  • 1 apple, cut in eighths
  • 1 Tbsp sugar
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 lemon, finely grated zest only
  • 2 garlic cloves,
  • 150 g onions, quartered
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 1 dried bay leaf
  • 650 g water
  • 120 g long grain rice

Béchamel Sauce

  • 250 g milk
  • 20 g plain flour
  • 10 g unsalted butter
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg

Preparation

Flavoured Breadcrumbs

  1. Place bread , garlic and coriander in mixing bowl then grate 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Rinse mixing bowl.

Three Course Meal

  1. Place 100 g carrots, 100 g courgettes and 50 g leeks in mixing bowl then chop 2 sec/speed 4. Transfer to Varoma dish.
  2. Place chicken in Varoma dish on top of vegetables and season with 1 tsp salt, pepper and thyme (if using) then set aside.
  3. Place apple, sugar, cinnamon and lemon zest on a sheet of baking paper, seal parcel well and place on Varoma tray. Set aside.
  4. Place garlic, onions and oil in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  5. Add bay leaf and sauté 5 min/120°C//speed .
  6. Remove bay leaf, add remaining 150 g carrots, remaining 150 g courgettes, remaining 50 g leeks, water and 1 tsp salt. Insert simmering basket, weigh in rice and mix well with spatula to wet all grains. Insert Varoma tray into Varoma dish, place covered Varoma into position and cook 20 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove covered Varoma, remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and set aside. Keep rice warm until ready to serve.
  7. Insert measuring cup and blend 1 min/speed 5-9, increasing speed gradually. Transfer soup to a bowl and keep warm.

Béchamel Sauce

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Place milk, flour, butter, salt, pepper and nutmeg in mixing bowl then cook 5 min/90°C/speed 4. Place chicken with reserved vegetables in a baking dish, pour over béchamel sauce and sprinkle with reserved flavoured breadcrumbs. Bake for approx. 20 minutes (180°C) or until brown and chicken is thoroughly cooked. Serve soup as a starter, followed by baked chicken and vegetables with rice, and finish with steamed apple for dessert.

Useful Items

baking paper, baking dish, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • A full three course meal for two, easily created in your Thermomix® using the mixing bowl for soup, the simmering basket to cook rice, and the Varoma® dish and tray for separating the main course from a tasty dessert.
  • Steamed apple can be served with ice cream.
  • Courgettes, carrots and leeks can be replaced with the same weight of other vegetables, such as broccoli.
  • Replace chicken breasts with white fish fillets.
