Mini Pizzas

Ingredients

  • 30 g extra virgin olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 220 g water, lukewarm
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 tsp dried yeast
  • 450 g strong white bread flour
  • 100 g tomato purée
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 150 g passata
  • 300 g mozzarella, grated

Preparation

  1. Lightly grease a large bowl with oil and set aside. Place water, sugar and yeast in mixing bowl then mix 20 sec/speed 2.
  2. Add bread flour, tomato purée, oil and salt then knead 2 min/. Transfer dough to greased bowl, cover with cling film then leave to prove in a warm place until doubled in size (approx. 1-2 hours).
  3. Preheat oven to 230°C and line 2 baking trays with baking paper.
  4. Once dough has doubled in size, use spatula to divide dough into 16 equal-sized-pieces. Form each one into a ball then roll into a circle (Ø 10 cm). Transfer to baking trays.
  5. Spread passata evenly over dough bases then sprinkle mozzarella evenly over the top. Bake for 10-15 minutes (230°C) then serve warm.

Useful Items

large bowl, cling film, 2 baking trays and baking paper, rolling pin, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Many different varieties of pizza can be made. Top with your choosen ingredients at step 5, finishing with a scattering of grated mozzarella. Ham and mushroom, or ham and pineapple, olives, goats cheese, peperonni or fresh basil leaves are examples of great additions to the cheese and tomato pizza in the recipe.
