Texas BBQ Pulled Pork

Ingredients

  • 5 oz thick cut smoked bacon, cut into pieces (½ in.)
  • 6 ½ oz fresh Roma tomatoes, quartered, deseeded
  • 5 oz white onions, quartered
  • 2 chipotle chilies, in adobo sauce, canned
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • ½ tsp paprika
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 2 oz apple cider vinegar
  • 1 oz brown sugar
  • 1 oz molasses
  • 1 oz Worcestershire sauce
  • ½ oz Dijon mustard
  • 32 oz boneless pork loin, cut into pieces (1 in.)
  • cilantro, to garnish

Preparation

  1. Place bacon into mixing bowl, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and sauté 15 min/250°F/speed . Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add tomatoes, onion, chipotle, garlic, paprika, salt and pepper and chop 15 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add vinegar, sugar, molasses, Worcestershire sauce and mustard and mix 5 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add pork (carefully arranged around blades) and cook 30 min/212°F//speed .
  5. Turn pork over into the sauce, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook a further 30 min/212°F//speed .
  6. Strain meat with simmering basket into a large serving bowl and reserve sauce. Return strained pork to mixing bowl, insert measuring cup and shred 6-8 sec//speed 4. Transfer to a serving bowl.
  7. Place reserved strained sauce into mixing bowl, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and heat 15 min/Varoma/speed 2, then insert measuring cup and blend 15 sec/speed 10. Transfer sauce into serving bowl and combine with shredded pork.
  8. Garnish with cilantro and serve immediately.
