Teriyaki Chicken and Rice

Ingredients

  • 3 oz soy sauce
  • 47 oz water, divided
  • 1 piece ginger (½ in.)
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 2 oz honey
  • 2 tbsp corn starch
  • 16 oz chicken thighs, boneless and skinless, cut into pieces (1 in.)
  • 1 heaping tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp salt, to taste
  • 8 oz long-grain white rice
  • 3 ½ oz frozen peas and carrots
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 pinch salt
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1992 kJ / 476 kcal
Protein
33 g
Carbohydrates
64 g
Fat
9 g

Preparation

  1. Place soy sauce, 12 oz water, ginger, garlic honey and corn starch into mixing bowl and blend 8 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and cook 10 min/250°F/speed 2. Transfer sauce to a bowl.
  2. Place a large bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in chicken and 8 oz of the reserved teriyaki sauce and combine with spatula. Set aside remaining sauce. Place 35 oz water, sesame oil and salt into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket and weigh in rice, peas and carrots.
  3. Place Varoma dish into position and place in reserved chicken.
  4. Line Varoma tray with damp parchment. Whisk eggs and salt in a small bowl and pour onto parchment. Insert Varoma tray, secure Varoma lid and steam 22 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  5. Cut the omelet into pieces with spatula and distribute over rice. Combine chicken with reserved sauce and serve over rice.

Useful Items

parchment paper, large bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • Replace soy sauce with coconut aminos.
