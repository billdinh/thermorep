Gluten-Free Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • 3 oz old fashioned rolled oats
  • 4 oz vegetable oil
  • 3 oz applesauce, unsweetened
  • 3 bananas, ripe (approx. 10 oz), quartered
  • 3 large eggs
  • 6 oz sugar
  • 2 oz brown sugar
  • 2 oz tapioca flour
  • 4 oz rice flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1167 kJ / 279 kcal

4 g

11 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease a loaf pan (9 in. x 5 in.) and set aside.
  2. Place oats into mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside
  3. Place oil, applesauce, bananas, eggs, sugar and brown sugar into mixing bowl and mix 5 sec/speed 4.
  4. Add tapioca flour, rice flour, reserved oat flour, baking powder, salt and vanilla and mix 10 sec/speed 4. Pour batter into prepared pan and bake for 50 minutes (350°F) or until toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  5. Let bread cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack. Allow to cool completely before slicing and serving.

Useful Items

loaf pan (9 in. x 5 in.), wire rack, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Rolled oats can be replaced with oat flour.
