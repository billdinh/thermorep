Broccoli and blue cheese soup

Ingredients

Macadamia and Parmesan biscuits

  • 70 g Parmesan cheese, crust removed and cut into cubes (3 cm)
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary, (approx. 10 cm long), leaves only
  • 70 g roasted salted macadamia nuts
  • 50 g butter, softened
  • 100 g plain flour
  • &frac18; tsp ground black pepper, to taste

Broccoli and blue cheese soup

  • 50 g cheddar cheese, cut into cubes (3 cm)
  • 250 g broccoli, cut into florets
  • 300 g water
  • 1 tbsp Vegetable stock paste (see Tips)
  • 300 g full cream milk
  • 1 tbsp plain flour
  • 50 - 60 g blue cheese, crumbled
  • 1 - 2 pinches ground black pepper, to serve

Preparation

Macadamia and Parmesan biscuits

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Line a baking tray (20 x 30 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place Parmesan and rosemary into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place macadamias into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 6.
  4. Add all remaining biscuit ingredients, including reserved Parmesan mixture, and blend 7 sec/speed 7.
  5. Transfer dough onto a floured silicone bread mat or work surface and knead lightly.
  6. Roll out dough, then cut into 20 rounds using a cookie cutter.
  7. Place rounds onto prepared baking tray and bake for 8 minutes (180°C), or until biscuits are lightly golden brown. Set aside to cool. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Broccoli and blue cheese soup

  1. Place cheddar cheese into mixing bowl and grate 3 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  2. Place broccoli into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add water and stock paste and cook 12 min/90°C/speed 1.
  4. Add milk and flour and blend 15 sec/speed 6.
  5. Add blue cheese and reserved grated cheddar cheese and cook 3 min 30 sec/90°C/speed 1, then blend 10 sec/speed 4, increasing speed gradually to speed 8.
  6. Season with pepper and serve with Macadamia and Parmesan biscuits.

Useful Items

baking paper, baking tray (20 x 30 cm), silicone bread mat, rolling pin, cookie cutter, oven
