Chicken Pad Thai

Ingredients

  • 200 g rice noodles, dried
  • water, boiling
  • 3 Tbsp groundnut oil, for soaking and frying
  • 40 g peanuts, dry-roasted, unsalted
  • 1 Tbsp palm sugar
  • 1 Tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 Tbsp tamarind paste
  • 150 g water, plus 2 Tbsp
  • ½ tsp chilli powder
  • 80 g carrots, cut in pieces
  • 60 g shallots, halved
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 200 g chicken breasts, cut in strips (1 cm)
  • 200 g firm tofu, diced (1 cm)
  • 200 g fresh bean sprouts
  • 2 medium eggs, beaten
  • 2 limes, cut in wedges, for garnish
  • dried chilli flakes, for garnish

Preparation

  1. Place dried rice noodles in a large, wide bowl or container (so that they are spread out) then cover with boiling water and 1 Tbsp groundnut oil. Leave to stand for 10 minutes.
  2. Place peanuts in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a small bowl and set aside.
  3. Place sugar, fish sauce, soy sauce, tamarind paste, 2 Tbsp water and chilli powder in mixing bowl then heat 5 min/95°C/speed 0.5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place carrots in mixing bowl and grate 3 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  5. Place shallots and garlic in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 5.
  6. Pre-weigh ingredients ready for stir-frying; place a bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in chicken and tofu. Set bowl aside. Place a jug on mixing bowl lid and weigh in water. Set jug aside. Place bowl with reserved chopped carrots on mixing bowl lid and weigh in bean sprouts then set bowl aside.
  7. Heat remaining 2 Tbsp oil in a wok over a high heat, add chopped shallots and garlic then fry for 1 minute until fragrant, stirring occasionally. Add chicken and tofu then stir fry for 3 minutes.
  8. Push chicken and tofu to one side of wok then add eggs to other side of wok and stir briskly until scrambled.
  9. Add to wok soaked and drained rice noodles, 3 Tbsp reserved pad thai sauce and water from jug then stir fry until noodles become tender.
  10. Add to wok reserved bean sprouts and chopped carrots then stir fry for 1 minute over a low heat. Add more reserved pad thai sauce to taste then serve immediately with lime wedges, reserved chopped peanuts and dried chilli flakes.
