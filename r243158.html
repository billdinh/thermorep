Pozole Blanco

Ingredients

  • 5 oz white onion, cut into pieces
  • 5 oz radishes, trimmed, halved
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 2 dried bay leaves
  • 1 sprig fresh thyme
  • 1 sprig fresh marjoram
  • 2 tsp salt, divided
  • 43 oz water, divided
  • 42 oz pork loin, cubed (1 in.)
  • 1 can hominy (approx. 14.5 oz) drained
  • 8 oz romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1 - 2 limes, cut into wedges, to serve
  • 2 tsp dried Mexican oregano
  • dried bird's eye chilies, to taste
  • corn tostadas, to serve

Preparation

  1. Place onions into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place radishes into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place garlic, bay leaves, thyme, marjoram and 26 oz water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh in pork, season with 1 tsp salt, secure Varoma lid and cook 80 min/Varoma//speed . Remove Varoma, transfer cooked pork to a bowl and keep warm.
  4. Add 17 oz water, hominy and 1 tsp salt into mixing bowl and cook 10 min/212°F//speed .
  5. Remove bay leaves, thyme and marjoram (see Tip).
  6. Distribute pozole and reserved pork into deep bowls and serve with reserved chopped onion and radishes, lettuce, lime wedges, oregano, bird's eye chilies and tostadas.
