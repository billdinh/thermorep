Light and Fresh Beet Pasta

Ingredients

  • 9 oz red beets, washed and peeled, halved (approx. 3 beets), divided
  • 30 oz water
  • 6 oz linguine
  • 3 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 2 oz pistachios
  • 1 oz extra virgin olive oil
  • ½ tsp salt
  • &frac18; tsp ground black pepper
  • ½ oz goat cheese, crumbled

Preparation

  1. Place 6 oz beets and 30 oz water into mixing bowl and blend 30 sec/speed 10, then bring to a boil 10 min/212°F/speed 1.
  2. Add pasta through hole in mixing bowl lid. Place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid once pasta falls through and cook for time indicated on package/212°F//speed 1 or until al dente. Meanwhile, place butter and nuts into a frying pan over medium heat, until butter is a light nutty color, stirring constantly to prevent burning. Set butter-nut mixture aside.
  3. Drain pasta through simmering basket, transfer to frying pan with butter-nut mixture and stir through to combine. Rinse mixing bowl.
  4. Place 3 oz beets, olive oil, salt and pepper into mixing bowl, insert measuring cup and chop 4 sec/speed 5, then cook 5 min/212°F/speed 1. Meanwhile, transfer pasta onto two plates.
  5. Spoon chopped cooked beets over pasta, sprinkle with goat cheese and serve.
