Roasted Sweet Potato and Broccoli Spelt Salad

Ingredients

  • 1500 g sweet potatoes, peeled, cut in pieces
  • 2 - 4 sprigs fresh thyme, to taste
  • 100 g olive oil, plus 2 Tbsp
  • 50 g balsamic vinegar
  • 1 Tbsp wholegrain mustard
  • 2 - 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 ½ tsp fine sea salt, or to taste
  • ¾ tsp ground black pepper, or to taste
  • 800 g water
  • 250 g pearled spelt, rinsed
  • 350 g tenderstem broccoli, cut in pieces (4 cm)
  • 100 g red onions, thinly sliced
  • 100 g black olives
  • 4 Tbsp pumpkin seeds
  • 15 g fresh parsley leaves, roughly chopped, for serving

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C. Place 500 g sweet potatoes in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Tip out into a large roasting tin.
  2. Place remaining 500 g sweet potato in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Add to roasting dish. Return any large pieces to mixing bowl and chop again 2 sec/speed 5, then add to dish.
  3. Drizzle with 2 Tbsp oil, scatter with thyme then roast for 30-35 minutes (190°C), stirring occasionally, until cooked and browned. Meanwhile, rinse mixing bowl and continue with recipe.
  4. Place oil, balsamic vinegar, mustard, garlic, salt and pepper in mixing bowl then mix 15 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a large salad bowl and set aside.
  5. Place water and spelt in mixing bowl then cook 16 min/Varoma//speed . Meanwhile, prepare broccoli and place in Varoma dish.
  6. Place covered Varoma dish into position then steam above spelt 6 min/Varoma//speed  or until cooked. Meanwhile, add onions and olives to dressing in salad bowl.
  7. Remove Varoma and set aside. Drain cooked spelt then add to salad bowl.
  8. Add roasted sweet potato and mix together well.
  9. Add pumpkin seeds, chopped parsley and steamed broccoli then gently stir together. Serve warm or cold.

Useful Items

roasting dish, large salad bowl, large roasting tin, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Serves 14-16 as part of a buffet selection, or 10 as a main meal.
  • Try using different grains such as pearl barley instead of spelt, if desired.
