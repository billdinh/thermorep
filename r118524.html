Almond thins

Ingredients

  • 300 g raw almonds
  • 1 tsp salt flakes
  • ½ tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 sprig fresh rosemary, leaves only
  • 40 g olive oil
  • 30 g water
  • 2 tbsp black sesame seeds
  • cheese, to serve

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Set aside a baking tray (30 x 40 cm) and 2 sheets of baking paper (30 x 40 cm each).
  2. Place almonds, salt, cayenne pepper and rosemary into mixing bowl and mill 15 sec/speed 9.
  3. Add oil, water and sesame seeds and combine 15 sec//speed 3.
  4. Transfer mixture onto 1 sheet of baking paper. Cover with other sheet of baking paper and roll out (approx. 3 mm thickness).
  5. Peel off top sheet and transfer baking paper with mixture onto baking tray. Bake for 15 minutes (180°C). Allow to cool, then break into shards and serve with cheese.

Useful Items

baking tray (30 x 40 cm), baking paper, rolling pin, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve these Almond Thins with Romesco and Roasted Carrot Hummus (please refer to the recipes on this recipe chip).
  • Replace black sesame seeds with linseeds or 30 g sunflower seeds.
  • You can store these crackers in a sealable container in a cool, dark place for up to 3 days.
