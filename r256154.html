Crab linguine

Ingredients

  • 1200 g water
  • 1 tsp salt, plus extra for seasoning
  • 350 g linguine pasta
  • 1 tsp oil
  • 50 g salted butter
  • 1 brown onion (approx. 120 g), cut into halves
  • 1 - 2 fresh red chillies, cut into halves and deseeded if preferred
  • 1 tsp fennel seeds
  • 2 bunches asparagus, cut into thirds
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper, plus extra for seasoning
  • 280 g crab meat
  • 6 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 12 sprigs fresh chives, finely chopped
  • 3 tbsp lemon juice

Preparation

  1. Place water and ½ teaspoon of the salt into mixing bowl and boil 10 min/100°C/speed 1.
  2. Add linguine through hole in mixing bowl lid and cook time indicated on package/100°C//speed 1, without measuring cup or until al dente. Drain linguine using Varoma dish and transfer into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl. Add oil, stir to combine, then cover to keep warm.
  3. Place butter, onion, chillies and fennel seeds into mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 5.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and cook 3 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  5. Add asparagus, pepper and remaining ½ teaspoon salt and cook 4 min/Varoma//speed 1.
  6. Add crab and cook 1 min/100°C//speed . Transfer into bowl with reserved linguine. Add parsley, chives and lemon juice and stir to combine. Season with salt and pepper as needed, then serve.
