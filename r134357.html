Grain Bread with Cashew Butter

Ingredients

Bread

  • 270 g strong wholemeal bread flour
  • 150 g strong white bread flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 20 g oat flakes
  • ½ Tbsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 200 g milk, plus 1 Tbsp for brushing
  • 30 g sugar beet molasses
  • 25 g oil
  • 1 medium egg
  • 2 tsp fine sea salt
  • 50 g raisins
  • 1 egg yolk, from medium egg
  • sesame seeds, for sprinkling
  • pumpkin seeds, for sprinkling

Cashew Butter

  • 250 g cashew nuts
  • 40 g groundnut oil
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt

Preparation

Bread

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C and line a baking tray with baking paper.
  2. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid then weigh in wholemeal bread flour, white bread flour and oats. Remove from lid, add bicarbonate of soda then mix together well with spatula.
  3. Place milk, molasses, oil, whole egg and salt in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 5.
  4. Add reserved flour mixture and knead 2 min/.
  5. Add raisins and knead 30 sec/. Tip out onto a lightly floured surface, bring together into an oval loaf shape then place on prepared tray.
  6. In a small bowl, mix together egg yolk and 1 Tbsp milk then brush over surface of bread. Using a knife make two cuts in bread then sprinkle with sesame and pumpkin seeds. Bake for 40 minutes (180°C). Allow to cool on a wire rack. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl and make cashew butter.

Cashew Butter

  1. Place all cashew butter ingredients in mixing bowl and grind 5 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then grind again 5 sec/speed 6. Serve bread with cashew butter.

Useful Items

baking tray and baking paper, pastry brush, long sharp knife, wire rack, oven
