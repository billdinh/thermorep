Mustard-Dill Sauce

Ingredients

  • 7 oz canola oil
  • ½ oz fresh dill, leaves only
  • ½ - 1 oz caster sugar, to taste
  • ½ oz white wine vinegar
  • ½ oz lemon juice
  • 1 oz Dijon mustard or other medium hot mustard
  • 1 oz Bavarian style sweet mustard
  • 1 egg yolk, from a large egg
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper

Preparation

  1. Place a pitcher onto mixing bowl lid, weigh in canola oil and set aside.
  2. Place dill into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place sugar, vinegar, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, sweet mustard, egg yolk, salt and pepper into mixing bowl and mix 5 sec/speed 4.
  4. Mix on speed 3 while very slowly pouring oil onto mixing bowl lid, letting it drizzle around measuring cup in a thin stream onto rotating blades to create an emulsion.
  5. Add reserved chopped dill and mix 8 sec//speed 3. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours and serve cold.

Useful Items

pitcher, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve this sauce with salmon (e.g. gravlax, a Scandinavian dish of dry-cured salmon marinated in herbs) or boiled eggs.
  • Replace canola oil with another neutral oil (e.g. safflower oil, sunflower oil).
