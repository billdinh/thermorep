Mozzarella Stuffed Flatbread

Ingredients

Topping

  • 15 g Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tbsp fresh parsley leaves

Filling

  • 40 g Parmesan cheese, cut in pieces
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 150 g mozzarella, cut in slices
  • 40 g crème fraîche
  • 1 egg

Dough

  • 130 g curd cheese, 40 % fat (quark)
  • 80 g butter, cut in pieces
  • 270 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 1 egg
  • 1 pinch sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 egg yolk, lightly whisked, for glazing
  • 1 tbsp water

Preparation

Topping

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C. Line a baking tray with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place Parmesan and parsley in mixing bowl then grate 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.

Filling

  1. Place Parmesan and garlic cloves in mixing bowl then grate 10 sec/speed 10.
  2. Add mozzarella, crème fraîche and egg then mix 3 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.

Dough

  1. Place curd cheese, butter, plain flour, egg, sugar and salt in mixing bowl then knead 1 min 30 sec/. Transfer dough to a floured surface, bring together, then divide into two equal-sized pieces. Roll one dough piece into a circle (Ø 28 cm). Place it on prepared baking tray and spread the filling over the dough, leaving a 2 cm border.
  2. Roll the second piece of dough into a circle (Ø 28 cm), place it over the filling and press the edges together, then fold edges over forming a firm seal.
  3. Whisk egg yolk with water using a fork and brush over surface of bread. Use a sharp knife to make small slits over top of bread and bake for 20-25 minutes (200°C) until golden. Serve warm, garnished with reserved Parmesan cheese-parsley mixture.

Useful Items

baking tray and paper, rolling pin, pastry brush, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • This bread is delicious warm and fresh from the oven, while the cheese is still soft.
  • To reheat the bread, simply wrap in greaseproof paper or baking paper, and warm in the oven 140°C for 10 minutes.
  • The combination of Parmesan and mozzarella keeps the cheese soft and gooey, with the full flavour from the Parmesan.
  • Any kind of brewed beer would go well with this satisfying bread.
