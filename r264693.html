Roasted Cauliflower Soup with Chilli and Garlic Oil

Ingredients

Roasted Cauliflower

  • 550 g cauliflower, cut in florets
  • 3 Tbsp olive oil
  • ½ Tbsp ground turmeric
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper, plus extra for seasoning

Chilli & Garlic Oil

  • 2 fresh red chillies, halved
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 125 g olive oil

Soup

  • 180 g onions, quartered
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 1 vegetable stock cube (for 0.5 l)
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 600 g water
  • 3 Tbsp pumpkin seeds

Preparation

Roasted Cauliflower

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C.
  2. Place cauliflower on a baking tray, drizzle with 3 Tbsp oil then sprinkle with turmeric, 2 pinches salt and 2 pinches pepper. Mix well then roast for 30 minutes (200°C). Meanwhile, continue with recipe.

Chilli & Garlic Oil

  1. Place chillies, garlic and 1 tsp salt in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and add 125 g oil then cook 20 min/90°C/speed .
  2. Leave to cool a little then decant into a sterilised glass bottle. Clean mixing bowl.

Soup

  1. Place onions and 30 g oil in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then cook 5 min/90°C/speed 1.
  2. Add roasted cauliflower, stock cube, salt and water then cook 10 min/90°C/speed . Meanwhile, toast pumpkin seeds in a dry frying pan over a medium heat until slightly golden. Set aside for serving.
  3. Blend 30 sec/speed 5. Season to taste.
  4. Divide between serving bowls, drizzle with reserved chilli and garlic oil and top with reserved toasted pumpkin seeds to serve.

Useful Items

baking tray, sterilised glass bottle, serving bowls, frying pan, hob, oven
