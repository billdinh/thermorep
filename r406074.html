Sweet potato and chickpea pizzas

Ingredients

  • 250 g sweet potato, peeled and cut into pieces
  • 230 g filtered water
  • 120 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 150 g chickpea (besan) flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 250 - 300 g fresh tomatoes, cut into slices
  • 200 - 250 g mozzarella, cut into slices
  • olive oil spray
  • rocket, to serve
  • shaved Parmesan cheese, to serve

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Line 2 large baking trays with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place sweet potato into mixing bowl and grate 6 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add 130 g of the water and cook 8 min/95°C/speed 2.5, then blend 20 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add olive oil and 100 g of the chickpea (besan) flour and mix 10 sec/speed 6.
  5. Add remaining 100 g water, remaining 50 g chickpea (besan) flour, egg, chia seeds and salt, then mix 20 sec/speed 5 or until smooth and well combined.
  6. Divide the mixture into 12 equal portions (approx. 2 heaped tablespoons each). Place onto the prepared baking trays and using an offset spatula, thinly spread out each pizza to approx. 5 mm thickness (14-15 cm diameter). Allow room between each pizza for mixture to spread. Top each pizza with fresh tomato and mozzarella slices and lightly spray with oil (especially the edges). Bake for 20 minutes (180°C) or until golden and crisp. Top with rocket and shaved Parmesan just before serving.

Useful Items

baking paper, large baking trays, offset spatula, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • If the mixture becomes too stiff or sticky to spread into pizza rounds, simply blend through another 1 tablespoon water and 2 teaspoons extra virgin olive oil to soften it.
