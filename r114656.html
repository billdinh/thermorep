Lemon tart

Ingredients

Crust

  • 30 g blanched almonds
  • 100 g coconut oil
  • 90 g pure maple syrup
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract
  • 90 g coconut flour
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp gluten free baking powder (see Tips)

Filling

  • 110 g unsalted butter
  • 4 eggs
  • 150 g caster sugar
  • 100 g lemon juice (approx. 3 lemons)
  • 50 g orange juice (approx. 1 large orange)
  • pouring (whipping) cream, to serve (optional)

Preparation

Crust

  1. Preheat oven to 170°C. Set aside a rectangular fluted tart tin (34 x 13 cm).
  2. Place almonds into mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place coconut oil into mixing bowl and melt 2 min/50°C/speed 2.
  4. Add syrup, eggs and vanilla and mix 5 sec/speed 4.
  5. Add coconut flour, salt, baking powder and reserved almonds and mix 5 sec/speed 7 until dough forms.
  6. Press dough into bottom and sides of prepared tin then prick base with a fork. Bake for 13-15 minutes (170°C) until lightly golden. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  7. Set aside tart case to cool. Reduce oven to 140°C.

Filling

  1. Place butter into mixing bowl and melt 2 min/50°C/speed 2. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  2. Place eggs and sugar into mixing bowl and mix 1 min/speed 4.
  3. Add lemon juice, orange juice and melted butter and mix 1 min/speed 4.
  4. Set aside for 10 minutes, then carefully skim off and discard any foam that has formed on surface.
  5. Pour mixture into reserved crust and bake for 25-30 minutes (140°C) until set (filling will still have slight wobble in the centre). Allow to cool completely in tin (approx. 1 hour). Remove from tin and place onto a serving plate, before serving with cream (optional).

Useful Items

rectangular fluted tart tin (34 x 13 cm), bowl, serving platter, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Place baked tart into preheated oven (170°C) on a baking tray before carefully pouring filling into it to avoid spilling.
