Turkey Chili

Ingredients

  • 8 oz Pepper Jack cheese, cubed
  • 1 fresh jalapeño chili, de-stemmed (approx. 1 oz)
  • 2 serrano chilies, de-stemmed (approx. 1 oz)
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 7 oz yellow onions, quartered
  • 4 oz green bell pepper, halved
  • 4 oz red bell pepper, halved
  • 2 oz olive oil
  • 4 oz celery stalk, in pieces
  • 16 oz salsa, (tomato, md. heat)
  • 1 can tomato sauce, (approx. 15 oz)
  • 16 oz ground turkey, crumbled
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 oz lemon juice, (approx. 1 md. lemon) (optional)
  • 1 can pinto beans, (approx. 15 oz), drained
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
2207 kJ / 526 kcal

Preparation

  1. Place cheese into mixing bowl and grate 15 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place jalapeño, serrano chilies and garlic into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7.
  3. Add onion, green and red peppers and chop 5 sec/speed 4.
  4. Add oil and celery and sauté 3 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  5. Add salsa, tomato sauce, ground turkey, chili powder, cumin, coriander and lemon juice and cook 15 min/212°F/speed 0.5.
  6. Add pinto beans and cook 15 min/212°F//speed . Serve hot with reserved grated cheese.
