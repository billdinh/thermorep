Lemon and white chocolate lava cakes

Ingredients

  • 100 g unsalted butter, softened and cut into pieces, plus extra for greasing
  • 230 g white chocolate, broken into small pieces (see Tips)
  • 130 g icing sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste
  • 1 lemon, zested (no white pith) and juiced
  • 80 g plain flour
  • 1 pinch salt
  • fresh blueberries, to serve
  • crème fraîche, to serve (optional - see Tips)

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C.
  2. Place butter and chocolate into mixing bowl and melt 5 min/100°C/speed 1. Meanwhile, grease 8 silicone dariole moulds (see Tips) well with butter and place onto a baking tray. Set aside.
  3. Add sugar, eggs, egg yolks, vanilla, lemon zest and juice to mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add flour and salt and mix 5 sec/speed 3 or until just combined. Divide batter between prepared dariole moulds (they should be almost full) and bake for 12 minutes (220°C). Cool in moulds for a maximum of 2 minutes. Carefully unmould cakes onto serving plates and serve immediately with blueberries and créme fraîche (optional).

Useful Items

dariole moulds, baking tray, serving plates, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • We recommend using good-quality chocolate for the best results in this recipe.
  • We used the Thermomix silicone dariole moulds (available in The Mix Shop™) for our lava cakes. Cooking times will vary if using metal or ceramic moulds.
  • You can serve this dish with thickened cream, if desired.
