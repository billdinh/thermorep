Pizza Dough

Ingredients

  • 1 oz extra virgin olive oil, plus extra to grease
  • 8 oz water, room temperature
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 tsp dried instant yeast,
  • 14 oz bread flour
  • 1 tsp salt,

Preparation

  1. Lightly grease a large bowl and set aside. Place water, sugar and yeast into mixing bowl and mix 20 sec/speed 2.
  2. Add flour, olive oil and salt and knead 2 min/. Place dough into prepared bowl and form into a ball. Cover bowl with cling wrap and let dough rise until doubled in size (approx. 1 hr.). Use pizza dough as needed.

cling wrap

Hints & Tricks

  • The cling wrap can be lightly oiled to prevent it from sticking to the rising dough.
  • Use dough to make 1 large rectangular pizza or 4 individual round pizzas.
  • To shape pizza: place dough on a baking sheet (approx. 16 in. x 14 in.) lined with parchment or greased with olive oil. Press it out with fingertips from the center to the edges, and gently stretch into shape. Alternatively you can roll it out with a rolling pin on a floured surface and transfer to a baking sheet lined with parchment.
  • To bake pizza: form a small ridge around the edge, and spread lightly with desired toppings. Bake for 20-25 minutes in a preheated oven (450°F).
