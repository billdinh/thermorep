Smashed Plum Tomato Bruschetta with Poached Eggs

Ingredients

  • 30 g extra virgin olive oil, plus extra for drizzling
  • 8 plum tomatoes, large, halved
  • 2 garlic cloves, sliced (optional)
  • 3 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves only
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt, plus extra for sprinkling and seasoning
  • 4 slices sourdough bread
  • 1300 g water
  • 4 large eggs, room temperature
  • ground black pepper, to taste

Preparation

  1. Place oil, tomatoes, garlic (if using), thyme and salt in mixing bowl then pulse Turbo/0.5 sec/1 time. Cook 12 min/98°C/speed . Meanwhile, heat a griddle pan until hot, drizzle bread slices with oil and sprinkle with salt then grill bread on both sides. Set aside.
  2. Transfer cooked tomato sauce to a bowl and cover to keep warm.
  3. Without cleaning mixing bowl, insert simmering basket then place water in mixing bowl.
  4. Place eggs in simmering basket and cook 16 min/70°C/speed 4 (see tip).
  5. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula. Top each slice of toast with some tomato sauce. Crack the tip of each egg carefully and peel back the shell. Gently tip out the whole egg onto the toast.
  6. Season to taste and serve immediately.

Useful Items

griddle pan, aluminium foil, hob

Hints & Tricks

  • A perfect weekend brunch with hassle-free poached eggs thanks to your Thermomix®. Prepare the tomato sauce in advance then gently reheat, before spreading on griddled sourdough and topping with perfectly runny eggs. One for the whole family to enjoy together.
  • The cooking time for the eggs is based on large eggs at room temperature. Any variation to this may produce a different result.
