Prawn dumplings (xia jiao)

Ingredients

Dipping sauce

  • 100 g soy sauce
  • 25 g rice wine vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • ½ - 1 tsp dried chilli flakes, to taste
  • 1 tsp toasted sesame seeds

Dumpling dough

  • 350 g water
  • 215 g wheat starch, plus extra for dusting (see Tips)
  • 20 g potato starch
  • 90 g tapioca flour

Filling

  • 5 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 3 pieces dried shiitake mushrooms, cut into slices
  • 50 g spring onions/shallots, trimmed and cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 4 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only
  • 170 g shrimp (small), peeled (see Tips)
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp sake
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 pinch ground white pepper

Assembly and cooking

  • 1 carrot (large), cut in 20 thin slices
  • oil, for greasing
  • wheat starch, for dusting
  • 600 g water

Preparation

Dipping sauce

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh soy sauce and vinegar into it. Add oil, chilli flakes and sesame seeds, then stir to combine and set aside until ready to serve.

Dumpling dough

  1. Place water into mixing bowl and heat 3 min/80°C/speed 1.
  2. Add wheat starch, potato starch and tapioca flour and mix 15 sec/speed 3, then knead 1 min/. Transfer dough onto a work surface dusted with wheat starch. Divide dough into 2 equal portions and roll each portion into a log (approx. 4 cm). Wrap in plastic wrap and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Filling

  1. Place ginger and mushrooms into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7.
  2. Add spring onions/shallots and coriander leaves and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add shrimp, oil, sake, soy sauce and pepper and mix 15 sec/speed 3. Transfer into a bowl. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Assembly and cooking

  1. Divide carrot slices between Varoma dish and tray. Using oiled hands, cut each log into 10 slices (approx. 1 cm). Keep dough covered with plastic wrap while making dumplings to prevent it from drying out. Flatten each slice with the palm of your hand into a disc. Using a rolling pin, roll out each disc into a thin circle (2 mm thickness, 10 cm diameter), dusting with a little wheat starch as needed to prevent dough from sticking. Place 1 heaped teaspoon of the filling in the centre of each disc. Seal dumplings by by folding in half, then pinching and pleating the edges to seal, trimming away any excess dough, if needed. Arrange a dumpling on each carrot slice.
  2. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma in position, secure Varoma lid and steam 12 min/Varoma/speed 1. Serve dumplings warm with dipping sauce.

Useful Items

plastic wrap, rolling pin, bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • You can find wheat starch at most Asian grocers or online.
  • You can replace shrimp with peeled prawns, cut into small pieces, if preferred.
  • You can make these dumplings ahead of time and store them in the refrigerator for up to 2 days. Steam as per recipe, but you may need to add up to 5 minutes of additional cooking time if they are very cold.
  • Don't worry if your dumplings don't look perfect on your first batch. Chefs train for years to master the technique used to fold and create these dumplings!
