Cloud Eggs

Ingredients

  • olive oil spray
  • 4 large eggs
  • &frac18; tsp salt, plus extra to taste
  • freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • chopped parsley, to garnish
Nutrition
per 1 portions
Calories
347 kJ / 83 kcal
Protein
7 g
Carbohydrates
1 g
Fat
6 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F. Line a baking sheet with parchment, spray with olive oil and set aside.
  2. Separate eggs. Divide egg yolks gently into separate cups, cover with cling wrap and set aside. Place egg whites and salt into mixing bowl. Insert butterfly whisk and whip 3-5 min/100°F/speed 3 until desired stiffness is achieved. Remove butterfly whisk.
  3. Divide whipped egg whites into 4 cloud circles on prepared baking sheet. Flatten to approximately 1 inch thick and make an indentation for yolks in each cloud with the back of a spoon.
  4. Garnish with salt and pepper and carefully add egg yolks to indentations.
  5. Bake for 6 minutes (450°F) until egg whites are lightly golden and egg yolks are cooked to your liking.
  6. Garnish with chopped parsley. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

cling wrap, parchment paper, baking sheet, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Egg yolks can be replaced with roasted pumpkin or roasted sweet potato. If using roasted vegetables egg whites will only need 5 minutes cooking time.
  • Mixing bowl and butterfly whisk must be clean and dry before starting recipe.
  • To cook on stove top: place olive oil in a non-stick frying pan and heat to medium-low. Transfer whipped egg whites to frying pan and flatten to a circle (approx. 1 in. thick) ensuring edges of egg whites are not touching edges of frying pan. Make identations into the egg white clouds with the back of the spoon and carefully add a yolk into it. Cover frying pan with lid and cook for 5-8 minutes, until the egg whites are lightly golden and egg yolks are cooked to your liking. If the egg whites stick to the frying pan lid use a silicone spatula to gently slide around inside edge to dislodge.
  • For runny eggs: precook clouds for 3 minutes, then add yolks and cook a further 3 minutes.
