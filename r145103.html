Whole cauliflower with pea purée

Ingredients

  • 30 g Parmesan cheese, crust removed and cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 20 g tomato paste
  • 4 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves only
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 2 ½ tsp salt
  • 3 pinches ground black pepper
  • 700 - 800 g cauliflower (whole)
  • 1030 g water
  • 300 g frozen green peas
  • 40 g unsalted butter
  • 20 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • fresh chives, finely chopped, for garnishing

Preparation

  1. Place Parmesan into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place 2 of the garlic cloves into mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add paprika, tomato paste, thyme, oil, oregano, ½ teaspoon of the salt and 2 pinches of the pepper and mix 10 sec/speed 7. Set aside in mixing bowl.
  4. Remove any leaves from cauliflower and trim stalk so it sits flat. Place cauliflower into Varoma dish, then rub with reserved tomato paste mixture.
  5. Place 1000 g water into mixing bowl, then place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  6. Remove Varoma. Insert simmering basket and weigh peas into it. Add remaining garlic clove to simmering basket, then place Varoma back into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 5-7 min/Varoma/speed 1 or until peas and cauliflower are tender (see Tip). Remove Varoma and set aside. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula. Discard cooking liquid.
  7. Place cooked peas and garlic into mixing bowl. Add butter, cream, remaining salt, remaining pepper and remaining 30 g water and purée 25 sec/speed 7.
  8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then purée 20 sec/speed 7 or until smooth. Transfer purée into a serving bowl.
  9. Transfer cauliflower onto a serving plate. Sprinkle with reserved Parmesan cheese and chives. Serve immediately, cut into wedges with pea purée.

Useful Items

bowl, serving bowl, serving plate

Hints & Tricks

  • Cooking time will vary according to the size of your cauliflower.
