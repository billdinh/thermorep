Anti-inflammatory pineapple and turmeric smoothie

Ingredients

  • 200 g pineapple pieces (fresh or frozen), cut into pieces (2 cm)
  • 200 g banana, frozen and cut into pieces (2 cm)
  • 200 g ice
  • 4 - 6 cm piece fresh turmeric, peeled
  • 2 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 3 sprigs fresh mint, leaves only, plus extra for garnishing
  • 1 lime, flesh only
  • 100 g fresh baby spinach leaves
  • 100 g walnuts (soaked or activated)
  • 300 g coconut water, plus up to 200 g extra

Preparation

  1. Place pineapple, banana, ice, turmeric, ginger, mint, lime, spinach, walnuts and coconut water into mixing bowl and blend 2 min/speed 10 . Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add up to 200 g more coconut water to desired consistency and blend 10 sec/speed 8 . Transfer into serving glasses and garnish with extra mint leaves before serving.

Useful Items

2 serving glasses

Hints & Tricks

  • This smoothie will keep in the refrigerator for 1 day. Be sure to thoroughly mix before serving (5-10 sec/speed 8).
  • To make this recipe nut free, replace the walnuts with sunflower seeds.

Pineapple and turmeric ice cream: Transfer smoothie into ice cube trays and freeze for up to 3 months. Place 400 g of the frozen smoothie cubes into mixing bowl and crush 25 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, add 30 g coconut water and blend 20-30 sec/speed 4 until smooth.
