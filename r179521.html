Meatloaf with onion gravy

Ingredients

Meatloaf

  • 80 g day old bread, torn into pieces
  • 180 g brown onion, cut into quarters
  • 100 g carrots, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 1 celery stalk (approx. 50 g), cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 - 2 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves only
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 1 egg
  • 250 g pork mince
  • 250 g beef mince
  • 1 tbsp Sriracha® sauce
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 100 g roasted whole red pepper strips (or roasted red pepper)
  • 3 hard-boiled eggs, peeled (see Tips)
  • 1250 g water
  • 200 g broccoli, cut into florets
  • 200 g carrots, cut into batons

Onion gravy

  • 380 g brown onion, cut into eighths
  • 1 ½ tbsp olive oil
  • ¼ tsp Chicken stock paste (see Tips)
  • 1 ½ tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp Sriracha® sauce
  • 2 tsp soy sauce
  • ground black pepper, to taste

Preparation

Meatloaf

  1. Line a terrine or loaf tin (18 x 7 x 6 cm - ensure it fits comfortably into the Varoma dish) with wet, well-wrung baking paper, leaving an 8-10 cm overhang on the long sides. Set aside.
  2. Place bread into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place onion, carrot, celery, garlic and thyme into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add oil and cook 3 min/100°C/speed 2. Set aside in mixing bowl to cool (approx. 15 minutes).
  5. Add raw egg, minces, Sriracha® sauce, Worcestershire sauce, soy sauce, salt, pepper and reserved bread and mix 10 sec/speed 5. Place one-third of the meat mixture into the bottom of prepared tin. Use spatula to smooth and level mixture, then make an oblong indent lengthways down the centre of mixture. Place half of the roasted capsicum into the indent and place peeled eggs on top. Cover eggs with remaining roasted capsicum, so eggs are wrapped. Top with remaining meat mixture ensuring eggs are completely covered. Fold overhanging baking paper over meatloaf, then cover with aluminium foil (or terrine lid), ensuring foil is not in contact with meat. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  6. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position and place meatloaf into Varoma dish. Secure Varoma lid and steam 60 min/Varoma/speed 1
  7. Carefully remove Varoma. Insert simmering basket and weigh broccoli and carrot batons into it. Place Varoma back into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 8 min/Varoma/speed 1 or until vegetables are cooked to your liking. Remove Varoma and set aside. Transfer vegetables into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and cover to keep warm. Transfer steaming liquid into a jug. Rinse and dry mixing bowl.

Onion gravy

  1. Place onion and oil into mixing bowl and cook 10 min/Varoma/speed 2, without measuring cup.
  2. Scrape down sides and bottom of mixing bowl and cook 10 min/Varoma/speed 2, without measuring cup.
  3. Add stock paste, Worcestershire sauce, Sriracha® sauce, soy sauce, pepper, any excess liquid from the loaf tin (approx. 80 g) and 100 g of the reserved steaming liquid and cook 3 min/Varoma/speed 2, without measuring cup. Spread onion gravy over top of meatloaf, then serve with steamed vegetables.

Useful Items

loaf tin (18 x 7 x 6 cm), baking paper, bowl, aluminium foil, thermal serving bowl or other large bowl, jug

Hints & Tricks

  • To make hard-boiled eggs and Chicken stock paste, please refer to The Basic Cookbook or Everyday Cookbook.
  • Serve meatloaf with any other sides of choice; we served ours with Parsnip and bean mash (please refer to the recipe on this recipe chip).
