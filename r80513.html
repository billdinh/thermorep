Millet-stuffed cabbage rolls with mushroom sauce

Ingredients

Millet-stuffed cabbage rolls

  • 250 g millet grains
  • 1200 g water
  • 6 - 12 savoy cabbage leaves (6 large leaves or 12 small leaves)
  • 180 g onions, cut in halves
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 200 g mixed fresh mushrooms (e.g. button, wild, shiitake)
  • 20 g butter
  • 1 - 1 ½ tsp salt
  • ½ - ¾ tsp ground black pepper

Sauce

  • 30 g butter
  • 80 g cream, min. 30% fat
  • ½ - ¾ tsp salt
  • ¼ - ½ tsp ground black pepper

Preparation

Millet-stuffed cabbage rolls

  1. Insert simmering basket in mixing bowl and add millet to it. Add water, then use spatula to stir and wet the millet. Prepare cabbage leaves, removing thick hard central part of larger leaves and place leaves in Varoma dish. Set Varoma in position and steam 16 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  2. Set Varoma dish aside on Varoma lid to allow leaves to drain and cool. Remove simmering basket using spatula and set aside. Empty cooking liquid into a jug and set aside.
  3. Place onions and garlic cloves in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  4. Add mushrooms and chop 4 sec/speed 4.5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl using spatula. Set scale to zero and using a spoon, transfer 100 g chopped onion-garlic-mushroom mixture (scale will show '-100 g') to a small bowl and set aside for sauce.
  5. Add butter to remaining chopped mixture in mixing bowl and sauté without measuring cup 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  6. Add reserved steamed millet, salt and pepper and mix with aid of spatula 10 sec//speed 4. Adjust seasoning to taste. Place 2-3 tbsp of stuffing on each steamed cabbage leaf and roll up. Place rolls in Varoma dish and tray, making sure overlapping ends of cabbage leaves are tucked under the rolls.
  7. Place 500 g reserved cooking liquid in mixing bowl, set Varoma in position and steam 30 min/Varoma/speed 1. Set covered Varoma aside on a plate to keep warm. Empty cooking liquid into a jug and set aside this second cooking liquid for the mushroom sauce.

Sauce

  1. Place butter and reserved chopped onion-garlic-mushroom mixture in mixing bowl and sauté 5 min/120°C/speed 1.
  2. Add cream and 200 g of the reserved second cooking liquid and cook 5 min/98°C/speed 1.
  3. Add salt and pepper and blend 20 sec/speed 9, then adjust seasoning to taste. Serve stuffed cabbage rolls hot, drizzled with mushroom sauce.
