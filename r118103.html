Duck and cherry salad

Ingredients

Herb dressing

  • 2 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 2 sprigs fresh mint, leaves only
  • 2 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves only
  • 2 tsp white wine vinegar
  • 40 g extra virgin olive oil
  • salt, to season
  • freshly ground pepper, to season
  • 200 g jar Morello cherries, pitted

Duck breasts

  • 4 duck breasts, skin on (approx. 800 g)
  • salt, to season
  • freshly ground black pepper, to season

Tarragon cream sauce

  • 3 egg yolks
  • 50 g caster sugar
  • 30 g tarragon vinegar (see Tips)
  • 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper
  • 120 g pouring (whipping) cream

Assembly

  • 1 bunch lettuce, of choice, leaves separated

Preparation

Herb dressing

  1. Place herbs into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a bowl.
  2. Place bowl with herbs onto mixing bowl lid and weigh vinegar and oil into it. Stir to combine, season to taste and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Place cherries into simmering basket and set aside to drain.

Duck breasts

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C.
  2. Place an ovenproof frying pan over high heat. Season duck breasts with salt and pepper. Place duck breasts skin side down into hot pan and press to flatten. Reduce heat to medium and cook for 4-6 minutes, until skin is crispy. Turn duck skin side up and transfer into oven to roast for 10 minutes (200°C), or until cooked to your liking.
  3. Transfer breasts onto a plate, cover with aluminium foil and set aside to rest.

Tarragon cream sauce

  1. Insert buttefly whisk. Place egg yolks and sugar into mixing bowl and beat 4 min/37°C/speed 3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add vinegar and pepper and cook 7 min/70°C/speed 3. Remove butterfly whisk. Transfer into a bowl and set aside to cool. Clean and dry mixing bowl and butterfly whisk.
  3. Insert butterfly whisk. Place cream into mixing bowl and beat speed 3, until just thickened and holding its shape. Fold gently into cooled dressing.

Assembly

  1. Arrange lettuce onto a large serving platter. Slice cooked duck and arrange on top of lettuce. Drizzle lettuce and duck with reserved herb dressing. Add drained cherries to salad. Spoon over creamy tarragon dressing, as desired. Serve immediately with remaining creamy dressing on the side.

Useful Items

bowl, ovenproof frying pan (26 cm), 1 plate, aluminium foil, serving platter, oven, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • You can find tarragon vinegar at specialty food stores and delicatessans. You can also make your own by infusing fresh tarragon in white wine vinegar for 3-4 days.
