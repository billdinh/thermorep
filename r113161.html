Chicken Involtini with Sun-Dried Tomato Pesto

Ingredients

Sun-Dried Tomato Pesto

  • 70 g olive oil
  • 40 g Parmesan cheese, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 160 g pine nuts
  • 100 g sun-dried tomatoes in oil, drained

Chicken Involtini

  • 70 g mozzarella cheese, cut in pieces (4 cm)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only, plus extra for garnishing
  • 2 medium eggs
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt, or to taste
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper, or to taste
  • 70 g breadcrumbs
  • 15 chicken breast fillets
  • 30 fresh spinach leaves, large stalks removed

Sauce

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 75 g onions, quartered
  • 50 g olive oil
  • 650 - 700 g tomato passata

Preparation

Sun-Dried Tomato Pesto

  1. Place a jug on mixing bowl lid and weigh in olive oil. Set jug aside.
  2. Place Parmesan and garlic in mixing bowl then grate 10 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add pine nuts and sun-dried tomatoes then chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Mix 30 sec/speed 4, while slowly pouring reserved oil onto mixing bowl lid, letting it drizzle around measuring cup in a thin stream onto rotating blades. Transfer pesto to an airtight container and refrigerate until ready to use. Clean mixing bowl.

Chicken Involtini

  1. Place mozzarella in mixing bowl and grate 3 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place garlic and parsley in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add eggs, salt, pepper,  4 Tbsp of reserved sun-dried tomato pesto and breadcrumbs then mix 30 sec/speed 5.
  4. Using a meat mallet or rolling pin, pound chicken breast fillets to tenderise and flatten. Distribute pesto mixture evenly over fillets, then top each with two spinach leaves and a sprinkle of reserved mozzarella. Roll fillets up to enclose filling and secure with cocktail sticks. Arrange chicken involtini in simmering basket and Varoma dish, then set aside until ready to cook. Clean mixing bowl.

Sauce

  1. Place garlic and onions in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add olive oil and sauté 3 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Add tomato passata and 1 Tbsp reserved sun-dried tomato pesto. Insert simmering basket, then place Varoma into position. Steam involtini 20-25 min/Varoma/speed 2, until chicken is cooked through. Set Varoma aside and remove simmering basket with aid of spatula. Place sauce in a serving dish, top with chicken involtini and serve immediately garnished with fresh parsley.

Useful Items

jug, airtight jar or container, cocktail stick, meat mallet or rolling pin, serving dish, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Vegetables of choice can be steamed on the Varoma tray at the same time as chicken involtini if desired. This may affect cooking time for the chicken so check it is cooked through and extend cooking time accordingly if required.
  • To store leftover sun-dried tomato pesto, transfer to an airtight container, then top with a thin layer of olive oil until pesto is covered. Place in fridge until required.
