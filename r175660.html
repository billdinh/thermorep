Almond Honey Semolina Cake

Ingredients

Cake

  • unsalted butter, for greasing
  • 230 g almonds
  • 250 g unsalted butter, diced
  • 300 g fine semolina
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tsp almond extract
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Syrup

  • 140 g sugar
  • 150 g water
  • 175 g honey
  • 25 g unsalted butter
  • 1 ½ tsp orange blossom water (optional)
  • 50 g flaked almonds, toasted

Preparation

Cake

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Grease sides and line base of a round cake tin (Ø 22 cm) with baking paper then set aside.
  2. Place almonds in mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place butter in mixing bowl and melt 2 min 30 sec/55°C/speed 2.
  4. Add reserved ground almonds, semolina, baking powder, salt, eggs, almond extract and vanilla extract then beat 20 sec/speed 4. Transfer to prepared tin and spread out using spatula. Bake for 35 minutes (180°C). Remove cake from tin, place on a serving plate and allow to cool until lukewarm. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl and make syrup.

Syrup

  1. Place sugar, water, honey, butter and orange blossom water in mixing bowl. Place simmering basket instead of measuring cup on mixing bowl lid to help prevent splashing and cook 9 min/120°C/speed 2.
  2. Prick top of cake all over with a cocktail stick. Spoon syrup over while it is still warm, allowing syrup to sink into cake before adding more. Set aside for at least 15 minutes to allow cake to absorb all the syrup. Sprinkle toasted flaked almonds over just before serving.

Useful Items

baking paper, round cake tin (Ø 22 cm), cocktail stick, serving plate, oven
