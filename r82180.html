Sunday morning breakky bake

Ingredients

  • 20 - 40 g butter, plus extra for greasing
  • 420 g ciabatta bread, cut into slices (1.5 cm)
  • 100 g Parmesan cheese, crust removed and cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 100 g cheddar cheese, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • ½ long red chilli, deseeded if preferred (optional)
  • 3 spring onions/shallots, trimmed and cut into quarters
  • 20 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 100 g red capsicum, deseeded and cut into quarters
  • 120 g prosciutto, cut into strips (1-2 cm)
  • 2 fresh tomatoes, deseeded and cut into slices (0.5-1 cm)
  • 2 pinches ground cayenne pepper
  • ½ tsp dried oregano
  • ½ tsp sea salt, to taste
  • 6 eggs
  • 70 g full cream milk

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C. Grease a deep-sided baking dish (32 x 20 x 5 cm) and set aside.
  2. Spread butter over bread slices, then place slices into prepared baking dish overlapping the slices slightly to ensure the dish is filled and the bottom is covered.
  3. Place Parmesan and cheddar cheese into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place garlic, chilli (optional) and spring onions/shallots into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add olive oil and sauté 2 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  6. Add capsicum and chop 3 sec/speed 5.
  7. Add prosciutto, tomatoes, cayenne pepper, dried oregano and salt and mix 10 sec//speed 2. Spread mixture evenly over and around bread slices in baking dish and set aside.
  8. Without cleaning mixing bowl, place eggs and milk into mixing bowl and mix 5 sec/speed 5. Pour mixture over bread in baking dish, then sprinkle with reserved grated cheese.
  9. Bake for 45-50 minutes (160°C) or until top is golden brown and just firm to the touch. Allow to cool for 5 minutes before serving.

Useful Items

deep-sided baking dish (32 x 20 x 5 cm), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • This is a great breakfast casserole to take to your next brunch or picnic. It can be refrigerated and served cold or room temperature so it travels well and combines the best of breakky ingredients.
