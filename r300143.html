Tuna Pasta Bake

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ tsp fine sea salt, plus extra to taste
  • 350 g dried pasta (e.g. penne, fusilli)
  • 100 g bread, torn in pieces
  • 5 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 100 g Cheddar cheese, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 150 g mozzarella cheese, drained, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 110 g onions, quartered
  • 30 g olive oil, plus 1 Tbsp for pasta
  • 500 g passata
  • 240 g tinned tuna in oil, drained (2 x 160 g tins)
  • 200 g single cream
  • 100 g water, plus extra for boiling
  • 1 Tbsp dried oregano
  • 1 Tbsp tomato purée
  • 1 pinch dried chilli flakes (optional)
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper, plus extra to taste

Preparation

  1. Bring a large saucepan, about ⅔ full with water and 1 tsp salt added, to the boil. Cook pasta according to time indicated on packet, less 2 minutes. Meanwhile, continue with recipe.
  2. Place bread and 3 sprigs parsley in mixing bowl then chop 10 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Preheat oven to 200°C. Place Cheddar and mozzarella in mixing bowl then grate 8 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place garlic, onions and 30 g oil in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then sauté 3 min/110°C/speed 1.
  5. Drain pasta in Varoma dish then transfer to a baking dish (approx. 30 cm x 22 cm). Stir through 1 Tbsp oil then set aside.
  6. Add passata, tuna, cream, water, 2 sprigs parsley, oregano, tomato purée, chilli flakes (if using), 1½ tsp salt and pepper to mixing bowl then cook 10 min/120°C//speed . Adjust seasoning to taste then pour over cooked pasta in baking dish. Add ⅓ reserved grated cheese and stir gently with spatula until just combined.
  7. Top with remaining grated cheese and reserved breadcrumbs then bake for 15-20 minutes (200°C) until bubbling and golden. Serve immediately with a green salad.

Useful Items

large saucepan, baking dish (approx. 30 cm x 22 cm), oven, hob

Hints & Tricks

  • A true dinnertime classic, this ever-popular bake appeals to kids and adults alike. Using mostly store-cupboard ingredients, it’s a great option when you need to throw something together quickly. There are so many versions around - our recipe features a creamy tomato sauce, plenty of grated cheese and a crunchy breadcrumb topping. Delicious served with a fresh green salad or steamed vegetables for a complete meal.
