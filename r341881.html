Thai tofu and sweet potato cakes

Ingredients

Lime mayo

  • 4 fresh Kaffir lime leaves
  • 1 piece lime peel (approx. 4 x 2 cm), no white pith
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 - 2 pinches salt
  • 200 g vegan mayonnaise (see Tips)

Tofu and sweet potato cakes

  • 275 g sweet potato (approx. 1 sweet potato), peeled and cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 300 - 400 g firm tofu, pressed and cut into pieces (3 cm - see Tips)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 2 spring onions/shallots, trimmed and cut into thirds
  • 2 fresh Kaffir lime leaves
  • 1 fresh long green chilli, trimmed, deseeded and cut into halves
  • 10 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only
  • 2 nori sheets, torn into pieces
  • 25 g tamari, gluten free (see Tips)
  • 30 g lime juice (approx. 2 limes)
  • 30 g plant-based milk of choice
  • 45 g chickpea (besan) flour
  • ½ tsp salt, to taste
  • 1 - 2 pinches ground black pepper, to taste
  • extra virgin olive oil, for frying

Preparation

Lime mayo

  1. Place Kaffir lime leaves, lime peel, garlic and salt into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add mayonnaise and mix 10 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a sealable container and place into refrigerator until ready to serve. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Tofu and sweet potato cakes

  1. Place sweet potato into mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a nut milk bag or 2 muslin cloths and squeeze firmly over a bowl to extract as much liquid as possible. Discard liquid. Transfer sweet potato pulp into a separate bowl.
  2. Place tofu into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 4. Transfer into bowl with the sweet potato pulp and set aside.
  3. Place garlic, ginger, spring onions/shallots, Kaffir lime leaves, chilli, coriander and nori into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add tamari, lime juice, milk, flour, salt and pepper and mix 5 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add reserved tofu-sweet potato mixture and combine 5 sec//speed 3 or until just combined.
  6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then combine 10 sec//speed 3
  7. Place a large frying pan over medium-high heat and add oil. Divide mixture into 20 portions (approx. 1 heaped tablespoon each) and gently shape into patties. Working in batches, fry patties for 3-4 minutes on each side, until golden brown and cooked to your liking. Serve Thai tofu and sweet potato cakes with Lime mayo.

Useful Items

bowl, large frying pan, sealable container, nut milk bag or 2 muslin cloths, refrigerator, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • To make your own vegan mayonnaise, refer to the recipe on this recipe chip.
  • We recommend using organic tofu whenever possible.
  • To press tofu, use a tofu press or line a plate with paper towels. Place tofu block on the paper towels and cover with another layer of paper towel. Place a plate or a cutting board on top of the paper towels and then weigh it down with heavy books or cans. Set aside for several hours until liquid has been pressed out.
  • Be sure to check the label of your tamari, as not all brands are gluten free.
  • You can store the Lime mayo in the refrigerator for up to 2 days.
