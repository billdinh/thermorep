Banana Nut Bliss Cookies

Ingredients

  • 5 oz pitted dates
  • 3 ½ oz ripe bananas, sliced
  • 3 oz cashew butter
  • 4 tbsp coconut flour
  • 1 oz cashews, roasted, unsalted, chopped
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 2 tbsp toasted desiccated coconut, to garnish (optional)
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
502 kJ / 120 kcal
Protein
2 g
Carbohydrates
15 g
Fat
7 g

Preparation

  1. Place dates into mixing bowl and chop 7 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and chop a further 7 sec/speed 6.
  2. Add banana, cashew butter, coconut flour, cashews, vanilla, cinnamon and salt and mix 10 sec/speed 3.5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix a further 10 sec/speed 3.5.
  3. Shape cookie dough into 12 evenly sized balls (approx. 1 oz) and refrigerate 15 minutes.
  4. Flatten dough balls with the bottom of a glass (approx. 1½-2 in.). Coat cookies in coconut, cover and refrigerate until ready to serve.

Useful Items

flat bottom glass (1½-2 in. round), refrigerator
