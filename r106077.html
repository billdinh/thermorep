Dirt and Worms Chocolate Ice Cream

Ingredients

  • 100 g chocolate bourbons
  • 200 g dark chocolate, (70% or higher) in small pieces or callets
  • 500 g milk
  • 6 egg yolks, from medium eggs
  • 250 g single cream (approx. 18 % fat)
  • 100 g caster sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 100 g gummy worms

Preparation

  1. Place bourbons in mixing bowl and grind 5 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a small bowl and set aside.
  2. Place chocolate in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 8.
  3. Add milk and egg yolks then cook 4 min/80°C/speed 4.
  4. Add cream, sugar and vanilla extract then cook again 2 min/80°C/speed 4.
  5. Transfer to shallow freezer-proof containers, set aside to cool then freeze for at least 6 hours or until solid.
  6. Remove ice cream from freezer. Tip out and cut into squares (3 cm) then place half the frozen pieces in mixing bowl. Blend 30 sec/speed 9 increasing speed gradually. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  7. Place remaining frozen pieces in mixing bowl and blend 30 sec/speed 9 increasing speed gradually.
  8. Insert butterfly whisk and mix 20 sec/speed 4. Spoon ice cream into serving bowls then scatter reserved ground bourbons over the top.
  9. Decorate with gummy worms then serve.

Useful Items

freezer proof containers, long sharp knife, serving bowls, freezer
