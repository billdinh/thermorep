Seven Spice Chicken with Rice

Ingredients

Seven Spice Mixture

  • ½ tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1 Tbsp ground black pepper
  • 1 Tbsp coriander seeds
  • 1 Tbsp cumin seeds
  • 1 Tbsp cloves
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 10 green cardamom pods

Chicken

  • 200 g onions, quartered
  • 50 g olive oil
  • 900 g water
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 300 g rice, rinsed
  • 700 - 800 g chicken breasts, skinless, cubed (2 cm)
  • 30 g pine nuts
  • 30 g blanched almonds
  • 30 g pistachio nuts, unsalted, shelled
  • 4 - 5 sprigs fresh coriander leaves, chopped, for garnishing

Preparation

Seven Spice Mixture

  1. Place nutmeg, pepper, coriander seeds, cumin seeds, cloves, cinnamon and cardamom in mixing bowl then grind 1 min/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.

Chicken

  1. Place onions in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add 15 g oil and, without measuring cup, sauté 5 min/120°C/speed .
  3. Add water, 1 Tbsp reserved seven spice mixture and ½ tsp salt. Insert simmering basket and weigh in rice.
  4. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid, weigh in chicken and 15 g oil. Remove bowl from lid, add remaining seven spice mixture and ½ tsp salt, then mix with spatula (see tip). Transfer chicken to Varoma dish, place covered Varoma into position and steam 20 min/Varoma/speed . Meanwhile, heat remaining 20 g oil in a frying pan and brown pine nuts, almonds and pistachios for 2-3 minutes, then drain on paper towels.
  5. Remove Varoma and remove simmering basket with aid of spatula. Transfer rice to a serving dish then add cooked chicken and browned nuts.
  6. Sprinkle with chopped coriander and serve hot.

Useful Items

frying pan, paper towels, hob

Hints & Tricks

  • For extra flavour, make the spice mix ahead and leave chicken to marinate for up to 2 hours.
  • Replace spice mix with korma curry powder for a different flavour.
