Curried Sausages and Lentils

Ingredients

  • ½ tsp fennel seeds
  • ½ tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander seeds
  • 2 tsp ground turmeric
  • ¾ in. fresh ginger, peeled
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1 - 2 fresh chilies, (e.g. serrano), deseeded if preferred
  • 4 sprigs cilantro
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 ½ oz carrot, cut into pieces (approx. ¾ in.)
  • 2 celery stalks, cut into pieces (approx. ¾ in.)
  • 3 ½ oz red bell peppers, deseeded and cut into pieces (approx. 1 in.)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp tamarind paste
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 31 ½ oz water
  • 10 ½ oz French green lentils, dry (see Tip)
  • 24 oz sausages, uncooked (e.g. good quality Angus beef)
  • 9 ½ oz canned coconut cream
  • 2 oz peanut butter
  • 5 ½ oz broccoli, broken into florets (approx. 1 in.)
  • 5 ½ oz cauliflower, broken into florets (approx. 1 in.)
  • 3 ½ oz fresh green beans, trimmed and cut into thirds

Preparation

  1. Place fennel, cumin and coriander seeds into mixing bowl and dry roast 5 min/Varoma/speed 1, then grind 30 sec/speed 10.
  2. Add turmeric, ginger, garlic, chilies and cilantro and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add olive oil and sauté 3 min/212°F/speed 1.
  4. Add carrot, celery and red bell peppers and chop 3 sec/speed 5.
  5. Add soy sauce, tamarind paste, oyster sauce, brown sugar, water and lentils and cook 20 min/Varoma//speed .
  6. Place Varoma into position, insert Varoma tray and weigh in sausages. Secure Varoma lid and steam 15-20 min/Varoma//speed , or until sausages are cooked through. Remove Varoma tray and set sausages aside to cool.
  7. Add coconut cream and peanut butter into mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh in broccoli, cauliflower and green beans. Secure Varoma lid and steam 8-10 min/Varoma//speed , or until vegetables are cooked.
  8. Remove Varoma and set aside. Transfer curried lentils and steamed vegetables into a serving bowl or other large bowl and cover to keep warm. Peel casings from reserved cooled sausages and cut into slices (½-¾ in.). Stir sausage slices through lentil and vegetable mixture and serve.

Hints & Tricks

  • French green lentils retain their shape and take less time to cook than regular green lentils.
  • Vegetarian option: omit sausages and increase vegetables by 17.5 oz. Steam additional vegetables on the Varoma tray while other vegetables cook in the Varoma dish. Adjust cooking time accordingly. You may want to rotate your vegetables in the Varoma dish and tray halfway through cooking time to ensure even cooking.
