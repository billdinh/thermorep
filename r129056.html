Jacket potato mice

Ingredients

  • 4 baking potatoes (large), scrubbed
  • 60 g medium-hard cheese, cut into pieces (e. g. Cheddar)
  • 20 g butter
  • 6 fresh chives, snipped (1 cm)
  • 30 g carrots, sliced (4 mm)
  • 12 frozen green peas, defrosted
  • 6 spaghetti strands, broken into 3 cm lengths, for garnish

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C and line a baking tray with foil.
  2. Slice approx. 5 mm off the top of each potato then cut a cross into potato flesh without cutting the skin. Place prepared potatoes onto baking tray and bake for 50-60 minutes (200°C).
  3. Place cheese in mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 8.
  4. Using an oven glove to hold potato, spoon out flesh, leaving about 1 cm flesh on skin so potato holds its shape, then transfer potato flesh to mixing bowl.
  5. Add butter and chives then mix 20 sec/speed 4.
  6. Spoon potato mixture back into skins then garnish with carrots for ears, peas for eyes and nose and spaghetti for whiskers.
  7. Return to oven to warm through for 10 minutes (200°C). Serve hot.

Useful Items
