Salmon Fillets with Salsa Verde

Ingredients

Salsa Verde

  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 5 anchovy fillets, in oil
  • 1 lemon, thin peelings of skin and juice
  • 25 g fresh parsley, tough stems removed
  • 25 g fresh basil, tough stems removed
  • 50 g pickled baby capers, drained
  • 40 g extra virgin olive oil

Salmon and Assembly

  • 500 g water
  • 1 ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 250 g new potatoes, quartered
  • 4 fresh salmon fillets (approx. 120 g each)
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 lemon, sliced (5 mm)
  • 200 g mangetout

Preparation

Salsa Verde

  1. Place garlic, anchovies, lemon peel, lemon juice, parsley, basil, capers and oil in mixing bowl then blend 10 sec/speed 10, stopping to scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula if needed. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.

Salmon and Assembly

  1. Place water and 1 tsp salt in mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket, weigh in potatoes then steam 8 min/Varoma/speed 1. Meanwhile, arrange salmon in Varoma dish and on Varoma tray then season with ½ tsp salt and pepper (see tip). Top with lemon slices.
  2. Place covered Varoma into position then steam 8 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Remove Varoma lid then remove Varoma tray. Arrange mangetout around salmon fillets in Varoma dish and on Varoma tray, then insert Varoma tray. Cover Varoma and steam 3-4 min/Varoma/speed 1 until salmon is cooked (see tip).
  4. Serve salmon, potatoes and mangetout with reserved salsa verde.
