Carrot coriander soup and tikka salmon with steamed vegetables

Ingredients

  • 450 g carrots, 150 g cut into slices (approx. |5 mm) and 300 g cut into chunks
  • 200 g broccoli florets
  • 4 fresh salmon fillets (150-180 g each)
  • ½ - 1 tsp salt, plus extra to taste
  • 2 pinches freshly ground black pepper
  • 4 tsp tikka paste
  • 50 g red lentils
  • ½ lemon, zest only
  • 2 cm fresh ginger, sliced
  • 100 g onions, cut into quarters
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 50 g butter, cut in pieces
  • 700 g water
  • 1 heaped tsp vegetable stock paste, homemade
  • 2 sprigs fresh coriander, plus some extra leaves to garnish
  • 100 g milk
  • 4 tsp cream, to garnish (optional)

Preparation

  1. Weigh 150 g carrots slices and broccoli florets in Varoma dish.
  2. Insert Varoma tray in Varoma dish, line Varoma tray with baking paper and arrange salmon fillets on Varoma tray. Season with 1 pinch salt and ground pepper, spread 1 tsp tikka paste on top of each salmon fillet and set Varoma aside.
  3. Place lentils, lemon zest and ginger into mixing bowl, chop 10 sec/speed 10.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Chop again 10 sec/speed 10.
  5. Add 300 g carrots chunks, onions, garlic, and butter, chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  6. Add water, stock paste, ½-1 tsp salt and 1 pinch pepper, place Varoma into position and steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 1. Set covered Varoma aside on a plate to keep warm.
  7. Add coriander and milk and blend soup 1 min/speed 5-10, increasing speed gradually. Serve the soup hot, garnished with some coriander leaves and a swirl of cream. Garnish the salmon tikka with remaining coriander leaves, finely cut, and serve with steamed vegetables on side.

Hints & Tricks

  • Replace vegetables with any other vegetables of your choice that cook in the same time. Replace salmon with other fish fillets of your choice.
