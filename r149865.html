Steamed Lamb with Vegetables

Ingredients

  • 1 lemon, finely grated zest only
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary, leaves only
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt, or to taste
  • 4 pinches ground black pepper, or to taste
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 900 g leg of lamb, boneless
  • 200 g fresh green beans, trimmed
  • 400 g potatoes, peeled, cut in pieces (4 cm)
  • 100 g turnips, quartered
  • 200 g carrots, sliced (5 mm)
  • 1000 g water
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1698 kJ / 406 kcal

31.2 g

24 g

Preparation

  1. Place grated lemon zest, garlic, rosemary leaves, salt, pepper and oil in mixing bowl then mix 15 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Line Varoma with 2 large sheets of heat-resistant cling film by crossing them. Place leg of lamb in Varoma and brush with oil mixture in mixing bowl. Fold cling film over meat tightly.
  3. Weigh green beans into Varoma tray then set aside.
  4. Place simmering basket on mixing bowl lid and weigh potatoes, turnips and carrots into simmering basket and set aside.
  5. Place water in mixing bowl. Place covered Varoma dish into position (keeping Varoma tray aside) and steam 45 min/Varoma/speed 2. Carefully remove Varoma, then insert simmering basket and Varoma tray then steam again 25 min/Varoma/speed 2. Set Varoma aside and remove simmering basket with aid of spatula.
  6. Place cooked lamb on a chopping board, cover with foil to keep warm, and leave to stand for 10 minutes. Make a hole in cling film and collect cooking juices in a gravy boat. Slice and arrange meat on serving plates with vegetables, then serve immediately with cooking juices.

Useful Items

cling film, pastry brush, aluminium foil, gravy boat, chopping board

Hints & Tricks

  • To make a gravy, empty mixing bowl and pour in cooking juice. Add 300 g lamb stock, 2 Tbsp Cognac and 2 tsp cornflour, then heat 5 min/90°C/speed 3.
