Bubble and squeak soup

Ingredients

  • 150 g speck, cut into cubes (1 cm - see Tips)
  • 150 g brown onion, cut into quarters
  • 1 leek, trimmed, white part only cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 40 g salted butter
  • 250 g potatoes, unpeeled and cut into pieces (approx.1 cm)
  • 1 tsp dried parsley
  • 1000 g water
  • 1 tbsp Chicken stock paste (see Tips)
  • 2 pinches ground nutmeg
  • ¼ - ½ tsp ground black pepper, to taste
  • 200 g Savoy cabbage, finely shredded (see Tips)

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh speck into it. Set aside.
  2. Place onion and leek into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add butter and sauté 2 min/120°C/speed 2.
  4. Add potatoes, parsley, water, stock paste, nutmeg and pepper and cook 20 min/120°C/speed 2 or until potatoes are cooked. Meanwhile, place a frying pan over medium-high heat and fry speck until crisp. Transfer onto a plate lined with paper towel and set aside to drain.
  5. Blend soup 40 sec/speed 4-9, increasing speed gradually from speed 4 to speed 9.
  6. Add cabbage and cook 2 min/120°C//speed 1.
  7. Divide soup between bowls and top with speck to serve.

Useful Items

plate, paper towel, frying pan, soup bowls, bowl, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • You can replace the speck with bacon, if desired.
  • Please refer to The Basic Cookbook for the Chicken stock paste recipe.
  • You can use any variety of white cabbage in this recipe, however the cooking times may vary. Cook cabbage until just tender.
