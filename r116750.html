Dill and feta flatbread

Ingredients

  • 20 g fresh dill
  • 2 tsp dried instant yeast (8 g)
  • 150 g water
  • 100 g olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 350 g bread flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 200 g feta cheese, cut into pieces
Nutrition
per 1 piece

1491 kJ / 356 kcal

Preparation

  1. Place dill in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place dried instant yeast, water and olive oil in mixing bowl and warm 2 min/37°C/speed 2.
  3. Add bread flour and knead 3 min/. Allow to rise in mixing bowl, covered, for 30 minutes.
  4. Preheat oven to 220°C. Line a baking tray with baking paper, grease with olive oil and set aside.
  5. Add reserved chopped dill and feta to mixing bowl and knead 1 min/. Transfer dough to a lightly floured surface, shape into a ball, divide into 8 and shape into flatbreads (Ø 10-12 cm). Place onto prepared baking tray and allow to rise in a warm place for 15 minutes. Bake for 15 minutes (220°C). Serve immediately.

Useful Items

baking tray and paper, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • This is a tasty and filling flatbread, perfect for a lunch with salad, or for an appetiser.
  • If feta is not available, substitute with a fresh cheese such as ricotta or paneer.
