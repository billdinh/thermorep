Globe artichokes with lemon garlic butter

Ingredients

Artichokes

  • 6 - 8 globe artichokes (see Tips)
  • ½ lemon, juice only
  • 1000 g water

Lemon garlic butter

  • 2 lemons, zest only of 1 lemon, plus 80 g of lemon juice (approx. 2 lemons)
  • 2 - 3 garlic cloves
  • 300 g vegan butter (see Tips)

Preparation

Artichokes

  1. Prepare artichokes by removing stalk from bottom of each artichoke to make a flat base and removing the outer layer of leaves (approx. 4 leaves). Cut off the top one-quarter and discard. Brush cut tops with lemon juice. Place artichokes upright into Varoma dish.
  2. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish with artichokes into position. Secure Varoma lid and steam 35-40 min/Varoma/speed 2 or until leaves pull off easily. Remove Varoma and set aside. Rinse and dry mixing bowl.

Lemon garlic butter

  1. Place lemon zest and garlic into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add butter and lemon juice and melt 3 min/50°C/speed 3. Transfer into a small dipping bowl and serve with artichokes.

Useful Items

pastry brush, small bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • Depending on the size of your artichokes, you may be able to fit up to 8 artichokes into the Varoma dish. Ensure that the Varoma lid can close securely.
  • To make your own vegan butter, refer to the recipe on this recipe chip.
