Cheese Balls Three Ways

Ingredients

  • 5 oz sharp cheddar cheese, cubed
  • 1 lemon, thin peel only, no pith
  • 10 oz goat cheese, in pieces
  • 5 oz cream cheese, cubed
  • Â½ tsp dried red chili flakes (optional)
  • 1 bunch fresh chives, finely chopped
  • 2 oz sliced almonds
  • 2 oz pomegranate seeds
  • crackers, to serve

Preparation

  1. Place cheddar cheese and lemon peel into mixing bowl and grate 6 sec/speed 7.
  2. Add goat cheese, cream cheese and chili flakes and mix 1 min/speed 6.
  3. Divide cheese mixture into three equal-sized balls, place on a serving tray and refrigerate to firm. Meanwhile, finely chop chives, transfer to bowl and set aside.
  4. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid, weigh in almonds and set aside. Place another bowl onto mixing bowl lid, weigh in pomegranate seeds and set aside.
  5. Roll to coat each cheese ball in either the reserved chopped chives, sliced almonds or pomegranate seeds and serve with crackers.
