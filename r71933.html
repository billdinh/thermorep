Pork san choy bau

Ingredients

  • 4 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 1 garlic clove
  • ½ - 1 fresh red chilli, deseeded if preferred, plus extra finely sliced to serve
  • 300 g sinew free pork fillet, diced
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp rice wine
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp raw sugar
  • 1 tbsp cornflour
  • 40 g carrot, cut into pieces
  • 2 dried shiitake mushrooms, sliced
  • 140 g canned water chestnuts, drained
  • 3 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only, plus extra to serve
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 - 2 spring onions/shallots, trimmed and finely sliced, to serve
  • 4 - 6 iceberg lettuce cups
  • fresh lime, cut into wedges, to serve (optional)

Preparation

  1. Place ginger, garlic and chilli into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add pork and mince 3 sec/speed 7.
  3. Add soy sauce, oyster sauce, rice wine, sesame oil, sugar and cornflour and combine 5 sec//speed 2. Transfer mince mixture into a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place carrot, shiitake mushrooms, water chestnuts and coriander into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 5. Transfer water chestnut mixture into a separate bowl and set aside .
  5. Place olive oil into mixing bowl and heat 1 min/100°C/speed 1.
  6. Add reserved mince mixture and cook 3 min/100°C//speed 1.
  7. Mix well with spatula scraping around the bottom of the bowl and blades and cook 3 min/100°C//speed 1.
  8. Add reserved water chestnut mixture and cook 1 min/100°C//speed 1.
  9. Transfer into lettuce cups and serve with extra finely sliced spring onion/shallot, coriander leaves, finely sliced red chilli and fresh lime wedges (optional).
