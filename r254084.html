Chicken Roulade with Prosciutto and Cheese

Ingredients

Filling

  • 1 tbsp dried tarragon
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ oz lemon peel, thin peel only, no pith
  • 6 oz mascarpone cheese

Roulade

  • 4 chicken breasts, air-chilled, boneless and skinless
  • 8 slices prosciutto
  • 2 oz baby spinach
  • 3 oz shallots
  • 1 oz unsalted butter
  • 6 oz white wine
  • 10 oz chicken stock, low sodium
  • 1 tsp dried tarragon
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 12 oz fingerling potatoes
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 3 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 oz heavy whipping cream

Preparation

Filling

  1. Place tarragon, pepper, salt and lemon peel into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 7.
  2. Add mascarpone and mix 5 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.

Roulade

  1. Carefully butterfly chicken breasts, making sure not to cut all the way through. Gently flatten each chicken breast with a rolling pin and set aside.
  2. Lay out a piece of cling wrap (approx. 10 in.) and place two slices of proscuitto in the center.
  3. Place one reserved chicken breast onto proscuitto, spread chicken with ¼ of the reserved mascarpone mixture and top with ¼ of the spinach leaves. Roll chicken in cling wrap, twist ends and tie the two ends together. Repeat with remaining chicken, place chicken parcels into Varoma dish and tray and set aside.
  4. Place shallots and butter into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5, then sauté 5 min/250°F/speed 1.
  5. Add wine, stock, tarragon and mustard. Insert simmering basket, weigh in potatoes and sprinkle with salt.
  6. Place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 25 min/Varoma/speed 2. Remove simmering basket and Varoma and set aside.
  7. Add flour and cream into mixing bowl and cook 5 min/212°F/speed 2, then blend 30 sec/speed 8. Meanwhile, slice chicken roulades into ½ in.-1 in. slices. Transfer to a serving platter, pour a little sauce over the top and serve immediately with steamed potatoes.

Useful Items

rolling pin, serving platter, cling wrap

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with mashed potatoes and steamed green beans.
