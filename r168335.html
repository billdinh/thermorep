Pimento Cheese Puffs

Ingredients

  • 16 oz cheddar cheese, cubed (1 in.)
  • 8 oz cream cheese, room temperature
  • 7 oz pimentos, jarred with juice
  • 4 oz mayonnaise
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp hot sauce, vinegar based
  • 16 slices white sandwich bread
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
280 kJ / 67 kcal
Protein
3 g
Carbohydrates
4 g
Fat
5 g

Preparation

  1. Place cheddar cheese into mixing bowl and grate 6 sec/speed 7.
  2. Add cream cheese, pimentos with juice, mayonnaise, garlic, Worcestershire sauce, pepper and hot sauce and mix 15 sec/speed 7.
  3. Preheat oven to 400°F. Line 2 baking sheets with parchment. Place bread on baking sheets and lightly toast (400°F) (approx. 5 min.).
  4. Cut 4 rounds out of each piece of toast using a round cookie cutter. Spread pimento cheese on toast rounds. Place on baking trays and bake 12-15 minutes (400°F), until melted and slightly puffed. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

baking sheets, parchment paper, round cookie cutter (approx. 2½ in.), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • The pimento spread can be served as a dip with crudités or crackers.
