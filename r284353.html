Chicken and Chickpea Tagine

Ingredients

  • 3 - 5 garlic cloves, to taste
  • 120 g onions, quartered
  • 80 g celery stalks, cut in pieces
  • 30 g olive oil, plus 1 Tbsp
  • 8 chicken thighs, boneless and skinless, halved
  • 100 - 120 g carrots (2 carrots), cut in batons (1 cm x 5 cm)
  • 1 Tbsp 00 flour
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 chicken stock cube (for 0.5 l), crumbled
  • 450 g water, just boiled
  • 150 g dry white wine
  • fine sea salt, to taste
  • ground black pepper, to taste
  • 480 g tinned chickpeas, rinsed, drained (2 x 400 g tins)
  • 300 g couscous
  • fresh coriander leaves, roughly chopped, for garnishing

Preparation

  1. Place garlic, onions, celery and 30 g oil in mixing bowl, chop 5 sec/speed 5 then sauté 5 min/100°C/speed 0.5. Meanwhile, heat 1 Tbsp oil in a frying pan over a high heat and brown chicken.
  2. Add carrots and cook 5 min/100°C//speed 0.5. Meanwhile, mix flour with cinnamon, cumin and turmeric in a small bowl then set aside. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add reserved spiced flour and cook 2 min/100°C//speed 0.5.
  4. Add reserved browned chicken thighs, chicken stock, water, wine, salt and pepper then cook 30 min/95°C//speed .
  5. Add chickpeas and cook 15 min/95°C//speed . Meanwhile, prepare couscous according to packet instructions.
  6. Adjust seasoning to taste then serve chicken with couscous garnished with fresh coriander leaves.
