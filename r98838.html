Parmesan gelato

Ingredients

Parmesan gelato

  • 150 g Parmesan cheese, crust removed and cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 350 g double cream
  • 1 pinch ground paprika (optional)

Balsamic reduction

  • 250 g balsamic vinegar
  • 30 g honey

Assembly

  • 1 ciabatta baguette, cut into thin slices
  • 4 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, cut into halves lengthways

Preparation

Parmesan gelato

  1. Place Parmesan into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add cream and paprika (optional) and heat 6 min/60°C/speed 3.
  3. Blend 30 sec/speed 9 or until Parmesan is fully incorporated.
  4. Pour mixture into a slice tin (18 cm x 22 cm) and place into refrigerator until set (approx. 4 hours). Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Balsamic reduction

  1. Place vinegar and honey into mixing bowl and cook 12-15 min/Varoma/speed 1, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid, until thickened and reduced. Set aside.

Assembly

  1. Preheat grill. Drizzle ciabatta slices with oil and place under grill until toasted on both sides (approx. 1-2 minutes). While still warm, rub garlic cloves over toasted ciabatta slices. Spoon a small scoop of Parmesan gelato onto each slice and drizzle with balsamic reduction. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

slice tin (18 x 22 cm), refrigerator, oven grill

Hints & Tricks

Recipe contributed by Mark England, Thermomix Recipe Community member

  • Balsamic reduction can create quite a strong aroma while cooking. Turn on an extraction fan or open a window to avoid any strong fumes.
  • Use any remaining glaze to drizzle over salads, serve with bread and olive oil or use with dessert dishes featuring berries.
  • Cut your ciabatta into thick or thin slices, depending on how many people you are serving.
