Thai chicken balls with sweet chilli dipping sauce

Ingredients

Sweet chilli dipping sauce

  • 3 long red chillies, cut into halves and deseeded, if preferred
  • 60 g water
  • 60 g rice wine vinegar
  • 60 g raw sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 garlic cloves

Chicken balls

  • 1 fresh red chilli, cut into halves and deseeded, if preferred
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 6 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only
  • 2 spring onions/shallots, trimmed and cut into halves
  • 20 g oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp self-raising flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 30 g red curry paste (see Tip)
  • 80 g coconut milk
  • 500 g chicken mince
  • 500 g water
  • 1 cucumber, peeled into ribbons (optional)
  • 20 g alfalfa sprouts (optional)
  • 1 tsp black sesame seeds (optional)

Preparation

Sweet chilli dipping sauce

  1. Place all sauce ingredients into mixing bowl and purée 20 sec/speed 8.
  2. Scrape down lid and sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then cook 20 min/90°C//speed 1, without measuring cup. Transfer into a serving bowl and allow to cool before serving, or cover with plastic wrap and place into refrigerator until ready to use. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Chicken balls

  1. Place chilli, garlic, coriander and spring onions/shallots into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add oyster sauce, flour, salt, curry paste and coconut milk and mix 6 sec/speed 7.
  3. Add chicken mince and mix 5 sec//speed 4, or until all ingredients are incorporated. Transfer into a bowl. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Place water into mixing bowl. Line Varoma dish and tray with wet, well-wrung baking paper. Place rounded tablespoons of chicken mixture (approx. 24) into Varoma dish and tray. Place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 15-18 min/Varoma/speed 2, or until chicken is cooked through. Set Varoma aside to allow chicken balls to cool.
  5. If desired, wrap cooled chicken balls in cucumber ribbons and top with alfalfa sprouts and sesame seeds, then serve with reserved Sweet chilli dipping sauce.
