Easy apple pie

Ingredients

  • butter, unsalted, for greasing
  • 4 sheets filo pastry
  • 400 g milk
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 80 g sugar
  • 30 g cornflour (starch)
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 400 g apples (e.g. Reinette), peeled, cored and cut into quarters
  • 1 lemon, grated zest and juice
  • icing sugar to taste, for sprinkling
Nutrition

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Grease a pie tray with removable bottom (Ø 24 cm approx.) with butter. Line with 4 overlapping sheets of filo pastry or one sheet of puff pastry.
  2. Place milk, egg yolks, sugar and cornflour in mixing bowl and cook 8 min/90°C/speed 3. Transfer to a bowl, let cool and pour over pastry. Sprinkle with cinnamon and set aside.
  3. Place apples, lemon zest and lemon juice in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 4. Remove, pour over cream and bake at 180°C for about 25 minutes or until golden. Serve warm sprinkled with icing sugar.

Useful Items

springform tin (Ø 24-26 cm), bowl, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve apple pie accompanied with a scoop of ice cream of your choice.
