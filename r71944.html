Steamed salmon with broccoli pesto

Ingredients

  • 600 g water
  • 150 g broccoli, broken into florets
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 2 skinless salmon fillets (approx. 125 g each)
  • 1 pinch sea salt, to taste
  • 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 50 g Parmesan cheese, crust removed and cut into cubes (3 cm)
  • 6 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 60 g raw almonds
  • 20 g rocket leaves
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 2 tsp lemon juice (approx. ½ lemon)
  • 4 snow peas, thinly sliced, to serve

Preparation

  1. Place water into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket and weigh broccoli into it. Add garlic to simmering basket with broccoli.
  2. Place Varoma into position. Place salmon onto Varoma tray and season to taste with salt and pepper. Secure Varoma lid and steam 12 min/Varoma/speed 1. Set Varoma aside. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and run broccoli and garlic briefly under cold water to cease cooking. Set broccoli and garlic aside to drain.
  3. Return Varoma to position and steam for a further 4-6 min/Varoma/speed 1, or until salmon is cooked through. Transfer salmon into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and cover to keep warm. Rinse and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Place Parmesan and parsley into mixing bowl and chop 7 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add almonds, rocket and reserved broccoli and garlic and chop 4 sec/speed 6
  6. Add olive oil and lemon juice and combine 10 sec//speed 2.
  7. Serve salmon straight away with pesto on the side and snow pea slices to garnish.

Useful Items

thermal serving bowl or other large bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • Recipe for one: reduce all ingredients, except water quantity, by half and proceed as per recipe. The water quantity needs to remain the same for steaming.
  • Great for your brain and heart - the salmon and broccoli combo provides the perfect nutritional boost for the middle of your week.
  • Serve this dish with Lemon and caper aioli.
  • Pesto will keep in a sealable container in the refrigerator for several days.  Serve with other meals or return pesto to the mixing bowl and add more oil through hole in mixing bowl lid to turn into a dip.
  • Steam some eggs, rice or additional vegetables in the simmering basket while you cook the salmon in step 3 to enjoy with any leftovers the next day.
