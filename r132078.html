Durban naan

Ingredients

  • 30 g olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 330 g full cream milk, plus extra for brushing
  • 2 tsp dried instant yeast
  • 60 g unsalted butter
  • 600 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 2 tsp fennel seeds
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • 30 g white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • poppy seeds (see Tips), for sprinkling

Preparation

  1. Grease a round springform baking tin (23 cm) with oil and set aside. Grease a large ceramic or glass bowl with oil and set aside.
  2. Place milk, yeast and butter into mixing bowl and heat 3 min/37°C/speed 2.
  3. Add flour, fennel seeds, salt, sugar, oil and egg and knead 4 min/.  If dough is sticky, add 1 tablespoon flour and knead for a further 30 sec/. Transfer dough into prepared bowl and cover with plastic wrap. Leave to prove in a warm place until doubled in size (approx. 1 hour).
  4. Preheat oven to 180°C. Transfer dough onto a silicone bread mat or floured work surface. Divide dough into 16 equal portions (approx. 60 g each) and roll into balls. Place balls into prepared baking tin, placing first roll into the centre of the tin and the remaining balls around it (creating a flower shape).
  5. Brush rolls with milk, sprinkle with poppy seeds and bake for 20-25 minutes (180°C), until golden brown. Serve warm.

Useful Items

large ceramic or glass bowl, springform cake tin (23 cm), plastic wrap, silicone bread mat, pastry brush, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • You can replace the poppy seeds with sesame seeds in this recipe.
