Cod with citrus butter

Ingredients

  • 1 orange, zest and juice
  • 1 lemon, zest and juice
  • 700 g water
  • 400 g potatoes, cut in pieces
  • 400 g courgettes, cut in slices (5 mm)
  • butter, for greasing
  • 4 cod fillets (120-200 g each)
  • ½ tsp salt, adjust to taste
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper, adjust to taste
  • 80 g butter
  • 1 tsp dried fennel seeds
  • 3 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only, chopped
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1694 kJ / 405 kcal
Protein
33 g
Carbohydrates
23 g
Fat
19 g

Preparation

  1. Place orange and lemon zest in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a small bowl and set aside.
  2. Place water in mixing bowl, insert simmering basket and weigh potatoes into it. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh courgettes into it. Lightly grease Varoma tray. Season both sides of cod fillets with salt and black pepper, place on Varoma tray and sprinkle with ¼ of reserved chopped zest. Insert Varoma tray into Varoma dish, close Varoma lid and steam 25 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Remove Varoma and remove simmering basket with spatula. Transfer courgettes, fish and potatoes to a large dish, set aside and keep warm. Empty mixing bowl.
  4. Place butter, fennel seeds and remaining citrus zest in mixing bowl and heat 4 min/60°C/speed 1.
  5. Add orange and lemon juice to mixing bowl and mix 30 sec/speed 3. Place cod, courgettes and potatoes on plates. Pour over warm citrus butter, sprinkle with parsley and serve immediately.
