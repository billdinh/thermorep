Meatballs and rice noodle salad with spring rolls

Ingredients

Meatballs

  • 40 g bread (approx. 1 slice), torn into pieces
  • 40 g full cream milk
  • 1 stalk fresh lemongrass, white part only
  • 3 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 1 fresh long red chilli, trimmed and deseeded if preferred
  • 2 tsp fish sauce
  • 500 g beef mince
  • 500 g water

Glaze

  • 80 g honey
  • 40 g soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 lime, zest only

Nuoc cham dressing

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 fresh bird's eye chilli, trimmed and deseeded if preferred
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 120 g water
  • 50 g fish sauce
  • 50 g rice wine vinegar
  • 40 g palm sugar

Salad

  • 150 g vermicelli noodles
  • boiling water, for soaking
  • 4 green lettuce leaves
  • 1 cucumber, deseeded and cut into slices on the diagonal
  • 4 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only
  • 4 sprigs fresh Thai basil, leaves only
  • 1 fresh long red chilli, cut into thin slices (optional)
  • 4 spring rolls, cut into 3 pieces (optional - see Tips)

Preparation

Meatballs

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh bread and milk into it. Stir to ensure even soaking of the bread. Set aside.
  2. Place lemongrass, ginger and chilli into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7.
  3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and chop 5 sec/speed 7.
  4. Add fish sauce, mince and reserved bread mixture and combine 30 sec//speed 3.
  5. Using damp hands, roll 1 tablespoon of mince into a ball and place into Varoma dish. Repeat rolling of remaining mixture (approx. 18 balls) and divide meatballs evenly between Varoma dish and tray.
  6. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish and tray into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 15 min/Varoma/speed 1 or until meatballs are cooked through. Transfer into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and cover to keep warm. Discard cooking liquid. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Glaze

  1. Place honey, soy sauce, fish sauce and lime into mixing bowl and heat 5 min/100°C/speed 2, without measuring cup. Transfer into thermal serving bowl with meatballs and gently stir to coat meatballs. Cover to keep warm. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Nuoc cham dressing

  1. Place garlic and chilli into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place lime juice, water, fish sauce, vinegar and sugar into mixing bowl and heat 3 min/Varoma/speed 1 or until sugar dissolves. Set aside in mixing bowl to cool for 10 minutes, then transfer into bowl with reserved chilli mixture and stir to combine. Set aside until ready to use.

Salad

  1. Place a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh vermicelli noodles into it. Cover with boiling water and set aside to soak for 2 minutes. Drain noodles using simmering basket. Transfer noodles into a bowl. Add half of the reserved Nuoc cham dressing and toss to coat.
  2. Divide noodles between serving bowls. Top noodles with reserved meatballs, lettuce, cucumber, coriander, Thai basil, chilli (optional) and spring rolls (optional). Serve with remaining reserved dressing on the side.

Useful Items

bowl, thermal serving bowl or other large bowl, serving bowls

Hints & Tricks

  • This recipe can be served with or without spring rolls. To make your own spring rolls, refer to the recipe in this collection.
  • To develop a more intense flavour, transfer mince mixture into a bowl and cover with plastic wrap. Transfer into refrigerator for a minimum of 6 hours or overnight before cooking.
