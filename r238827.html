Chicken and Spinach Salad with Bulgur Wheat

Ingredients

  • 5 ½ oz cream cheese
  • ½ oz fresh basil leaves
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 oz lemon juice
  • 2 tsp salt, divided
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 16 oz chicken breast, cut into strips (½ in. x 2 in.)
  • 32 oz water
  • 7 oz bulgur wheat (see Tip)
  • 4 oz fresh baby spinach
  • 1 oz olive oil
  • ½ oz white vinegar

Preparation

  1. Place cream cheese, basil, garlic, lemon juice, 1 tsp salt and pepper into mixing bowl and blend 5 sec/speed 5. Add chicken and mix 10 sec//speed 2. Place chicken and sauce on a long piece of parchment (approx. 25 in.) and fold paper into a parcel. Place parcel into Varoma dish, seam side down, ensuring some holes remain unobstructed to allow even distribution of steam and set aside.
  2. Place water and 1 tsp salt into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket and weigh in bulgur wheat. Place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and steam for time indicated on package/Varoma/speed 1. Remove Varoma, remove simmering basket with spatula and leave water in mixing bowl. Transfer bulgur wheat into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place Varoma into position, turn chicken parcel over to ensure even cooking and steam a further 10 min/Varoma/speed 2. Transfer cooked chicken into bowl with reserved bulgur wheat. Place bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in spinach leaves, olive oil and vinegar. Remove bowl from mixing bowl lid, stir through with spatula to combine and serve immediately.

Useful Items

parchment paper

Hints & Tricks

  • Replace cream cheese with yogurt and replace bulgur wheat with brown basmati rice. Rinse brown basmati rice before cooking. Cook rice for an extra 5 minutes more than chicken.
  • Save time by making the chicken parcel the night before, refrigerate and let marinate overnight.
  • Bulgur wheat comes in different varieties. Please read package directions for cooking times and rinsing if required before cooking. If your bulgur requires 30 minutes just leave it in mixing bowl after turning over the chicken and continue for the extra 10 minutes.
