Baked Falafel with Jalapeño Tahini Sauce

Ingredients

Falafel

  • non-stick spray oil
  • 4 oz red onions, cut into pieces
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 oz parsley leaves
  • 1 oz cilantro leaves, plus extra to garnish
  • 10 sprigs fresh mint, leaves only
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 can chickpeas (approx. 15 oz), rinsed, drained
  • 2 oz bread crumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 oz lemon juice
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • ¼ tsp ground turmeric
  • &frac18; tsp ground cinnamon
  • &frac18; tsp ground cumin
  • 1 pinch ground cardamom

Jalapeño Tahini Sauce

  • 1 jalapeño pepper, stem and seeds removed
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 10 sprigs cilantro, leaves only
  • 5 oz tahini
  • 4 oz water
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground cumin
  • 6 mini pita breads, toasted, sliced in half
  • 1 cucumber, sliced, to serve
  • fresh baby kale leaves, chopped, to serve
  • 1 tomato, sliced, to serve
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges

Preparation

Falafel

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Spray two 12-hole muffin pans with non-stick spray and set aside.
  2. Place onion, garlic, parsley, cilantro, mint and salt into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 6.
  3. Add chickpeas, bread crumbs, egg, flour, lemon juice, pepper, turmeric, cinnamon, cumin and cardamom and mix 5 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl.
  4. Shape falafel mixture into 24 balls (approx. 1 oz ea.), place in prepared muffin pans and flatten slightly with back of a spoon. Bake for 10-15 minutes (425°F) until crispy and browned. Let cool 3 minutes before unmolding. Meanwhile, rinse and dry mixing bowl and make tahini sauce.

Jalapeño Tahini Sauce

  1. Place jalapeño, garlic and cilantro into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and chop a further 3 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add tahini, water, lemon juice, salt and cumin and mix 15 sec/speed 3. Transfer to a bowl.
  3. Stuff two hot Baked Falafel into each toasted pita bread, add cucumber slices, chopped kale and tomato slices. Serve with lemon wedges and Jalapeño Tahini Sauce.
