Gluten free plain flour

Ingredients

  • 200 g quinoa
  • 100 g chia seeds
  • 100 g arrowroot flour (see tip)
  • 200 g cornflour (starch)
Nutrition
per 100 g
Calories
1598 kJ / 380 kcal
Protein
7.5 g
Carbohydrates
63.3 g
Fat
9.2 g

Preparation

  1. Place quinoa and chia seeds into mixing bowl and mill 1 min/speed 9.
  2. Add arrowroot flour and cornflour and blend 20 sec/speed 9. Store in sealable jar or container until ready to use.

Useful Items

air-tight container

Hints & Tricks

  • Try using different flours in this recipe such as soy flour or chickpea flour. You can also try different grains to mill such as brown rice, amaranth or millet.
  • To ensure gluten free recipes are gluten free, always read the labels of your products carefully to ensure no wheat or gluten has been used as part of the production process.
