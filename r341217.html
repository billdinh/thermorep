Rainbow cauliflower sushi

Ingredients

Rainbow cauliflower sushi

  • 530 g cauliflower, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 50 g raw cashews
  • 1 avocado, flesh only
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 ½ tsp wasabi paste
  • 6 nori sheets
  • ½ red capsicum, cut into fine julienne
  • 50 g red cabbage, shredded
  • 1 - 2 spring onions/shallots, cut into fine julienne
  • filtered water, for brushing

Peanut dipping sauce

  • 100 g roasted unsalted peanuts
  • 1 - 2 tbsp filtered water
  • 1 - 2 tbsp pure maple syrup
  • 1 - 2 tbsp tamari, gluten free (see Tip)
  • 3 - 4 tsp Sriracha® sauce

Preparation

Rainbow cauliflower sushi

  1. Place cauliflower into mixing bowl and chop 8 sec//speed 5, mixing with aid of spatula. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place cashews into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7.
  3. Add avocado, sesame oil and wasabi and blend 6 sec/speed 10.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and blend for a further 6 sec/speed 10. Transfer into a bowl, then clean and dry mixing bowl.
  5. Place 1 nori sheet, shiny side down, onto a bamboo sushi mat. Spread 6 compacted tablespoons of the reserved cauliflower over the bottom two-thirds of the nori sheet, leaving top one-third free. Spread 1 heaped tablespoon of the avocado mixture in a line across cauliflower at the end closest to you. Layer 4 pieces of the capsicum, 4 pieces of the cabbage and 4 pieces of the spring onions/shallots onto avocado mixture. Brush the top edge of nori sheet with water. Using the bamboo sushi mat, begin rolling tightly from the bottom up to form a roll. Allow to sit for 1-2 minutes, seam side down, then slice into 6 pieces. Repeat with remaining nori sheets, cauliflower, avocado mixture and vegetables (makes 6 rolls in total).

Peanut dipping sauce

  1. Place peanuts into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then transfer 2 teaspoons of the chopped peanuts into a small bowl and set aside.
  2. Add water, syrup, tamari and Sriracha® and blend 1 min/speed 10. Transfer into a small dipping bowl, sprinkle with reserved chopped peanuts and serve with sushi.

Useful Items

small bowl, bowl, bamboo sushi mat, pastry brush

Hints & Tricks

  • Be sure to check the label of your tamari, as not all brands are gluten free.
