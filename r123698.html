Vanilla nut milk

Ingredients

  • 240 g raw almonds
  • water, for soaking
  • 1 vanilla bean, cut into halves lengthways
  • 80 g pitted dates
  • 1000 g filtered water

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh almonds into it and cover with water. Cover with plastic wrap and set aside to soak overnight.
  2. Drain almonds well. Place vanilla bean, dates and drained almonds into mixing bowl and chop 15 sec/speed 6, until finely chopped.
  3. Add 500 g of the filtered water and mix 15 sec/speed 6, until almost smooth.
  4. Add remaining 500 g filtered water and mix 15 sec/speed 4. Allow to sit in mixing bowl for 15 minutes to infuse.
  5. Place a nut milk bag or muslin cloth over a large jug or glass bowl. Pour mixture into nut milk bag and squeeze firmly to extract milk. Transfer into sterilised sealable bottles and place into refrigerator until ready to use.

Useful Items

colander , bowl, plastic wrap, sealable sterilised jar, nut milk bag or muslin cloth, glass bowl or large jug, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Vanilla nut milk can be stored in sterilised or hot-rinsed sealable bottles in the refrigerator for up to 1 week.
  • You can substitute almonds with raw cashews.
