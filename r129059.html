Green pancakes with ham and cheese

Ingredients

Pancake batter

  • 250 g plain flour
  • 500 g milk
  • 15 g olive oil, plus extra for frying
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 pinch fine salt
  • 200 g frozen spinach leaves, thawed and squeezed

Filling

  • 200 g medium-hard cheese, cut into pieces (e. g. Cheddar)
  • 180 g cooked ham in slices, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 2 pinches black pepper, freshly ground

Preparation

Pancake batter

  1. Place flour, milk, oil, eggs and salt in mixing bowl then mix 5 sec/speed 6.
  2. Add thawed spinach and mix 20 sec/speed 7.
  3. Heat a little olive oil in a frying pan over a medium heat. Pour a small ladle of batter into the centre then spread out thinly. Cook for 2-3 minutes, flip over and cook for a further minute then transfer to a plate. Repeat with remaining batter until batter is used up. Clean mixing bowl then prepare filling.

Filling

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C and line a baking tray with baking paper.
  2. Place cheese in mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 8. Remove 3 tbsp grated cheese and set aside for topping.
  3. Add ham and pepper then chop 10 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Place a pancake on a clean surface then fill with 2½ tbsp ham and cheese mixture then roll and place on lined baking tray. Repeat with remaining pancakes. Sprinkle 3 tbsp reserved grated cheese over pancakes then bake for 15 minutes (180°C) until edges are golden and cheese has melted. Serve hot.

Useful Items
