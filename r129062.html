Hot dogs

Ingredients

  • 80 g medium-hard cheese, cut into pieces (e. g. Cheddar)
  • 500 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 300 g plain yoghurt
  • 50 g milk
  • 50 g olive oil
  • 40 g butter
  • 1 tsp fine salt
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 8 vienna sausages
  • 1 spring onions, for eyes, sliced (2 mm)
  • 1 olive, black, pitted, for eyes and nose, cut in tiny pieces

Preparation

  1. Place cheese in mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a small bowl and set aside.
  2. Preheat oven to 180°C and line a large baking tray with baking paper.
  3. Place flour, yoghurt, milk, oil, butter, salt and baking powder in mixing bowl then knead 1 min/. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then knead again 15 sec/.
  4. Tip dough out onto a lightly floured surface and divide into 12 equal-sized pieces. Take one piece and roll out to a rectangle (approx. 28 cm x 10 cm) then place a Vienna sausage in the centre along the length leaving a 2 cm border at both ends. Sprinkle 1 Tbsp reserved grated cheese along the sausage then roll up dough, encasing sausage and cheese filling. Squeeze a tail shape from 2 cm excess, shape a head with excess on the other side then place on baking tray. Repeat with remaining dough, sausages and cheese.
  5. Bring remaining pieces of dough together and use to make ears and legs for dogs. Use slices of spring onion and tiny pieces of olive for eyes and nose.
  6. Bake for 15-20 minutes (180°C) until golden. Allow to cool slightly before serving warm.

Useful Items

baking tray and paper, rolling pin, oven
