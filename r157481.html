Poached Eggs

Ingredients

  • 58 oz water
  • ½ oz white vinegar
  • oil, for brushing simmering basket
  • 2 large eggs
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
669 kJ / 160 kcal
Protein
14 g
Carbohydrates
1 g
Fat
11 g

Preparation

  1. Place water and vinegar into mixing bowl. Brush simmering basket with oil, insert simmering basket into mixing bowl and bring to a boil 11 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  2. Crack eggs into two separate bowls and set aside.
  3. Add eggs one at a time, sliding carefully through hole in mixing bowl lid to opposite sides of simmering basket. Poach 3 min/Varoma/speed 2. Remove simmering basket with spatula and lay poached eggs onto paper towel to drain well. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

paper towel, pastry brush, bowls

Hints & Tricks

  • For each additional portion: crack 2 eggs into separate bowls. Oil the simmering basket and re-insert it into mixing bowl. Reheat water 3 min/Varoma/speed 1. Once water has returned to a boil, proceed as directed in the recipe.
  • The water must be boiling (212°F) before adding the eggs.
  • Take care, this recipe contains raw or lightly cooked eggs.
  • Serve poached eggs on toast.
  • Make Eggs Benedict by topping an English muffin with Canadian bacon or ham, then add a poached egg and hollandaise sauce. Refer to The Basic Cookbook for the Hollandaise Sauce recipe.
