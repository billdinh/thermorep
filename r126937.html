Chicken breasts with courgette topping

Ingredients

  • 50 g onions, cut into pieces
  • 100 g carrots, cut into pieces (2-3 cm)
  • 300 g courgettes, cut into slices
  • 50 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 400 g chicken breast fillets, sliced horizontally
  • 500 g water
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1004 kJ / 240 kcal
Protein
25 g
Carbohydrates
4 g
Fat
13.9 g

Preparation

  1. Place onion, carrots and 200 g courgettes into mixing bowl, chop 10 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add olive oil and sauté 5 min/100°C/speed 1, then 5 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Place chicken breast slices and remaining courgettes into Varoma dish and tray. Top each piece of chicken with courgette purée.
  4. Place water into mixing bowl, insert the Varoma and cook 25 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  5. Serve chicken fillets hot with courgette slices.
