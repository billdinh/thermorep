Raspberry Jam

Ingredients

  • 250 g frozen raspberries
  • 250 g jam sugar
  • 20 g lemon juice
Nutrition

4443 kJ / 1062 kcal

Preparation

  1. Place raspberries, jam sugar and lemon juice in mixing bowl then, with simmering basket in place of measuring cup, cook 25 min/105°C/speed 1. Meanwhile, sterilise jam jars (see tip).
  2. Test setting point of jam (see tip). Transfer to the warm sterilised jars and seal immediately. Label before storing in a cool, dry place. Once opened, store in fridge.

Useful Items

jam funnel, sterilised jam jars

Hints & Tricks

  • To sterilise jam jars, preheat oven to 120°C then wash jars well. Place clean jars on a baking tray then heat for approx. 20 minutes (120°C). Always pour hot jam into still warm jars.
  • Setting point test: place a saucer in the freezer for 5-10 minutes. Place 1 tsp jam onto cold saucer. When cool, push jam with your finger. If it wrinkles, it is ready. If not, boil jam for a further 2 min/105°C/speed 3, then test again.
  • To make twice as much jam, double the ingredients and cook for the same time, 25 min/105°C/speed 1.
