Soft butter rolls

Ingredients

  • 220 g buttermilk
  • 50 g water
  • 2 tsp dried instant yeast
  • 70 g sugar
  • 50 g butter, softened, plus extra melted for brushing
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 egg
  • 680 g baker's flour, plus extra for dusting

Preparation

  1. Place buttermilk, water and yeast into mixing bowl and heat 3 min/37°C/speed 1. Meanwhile, line a baking tray with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Add sugar, butter, salt, egg and baker's flour and mix 10 sec/speed 6, then knead 2 min/.
  3. Preheat oven to 200°C. Transfer dough onto ThermoMat or floured work surface and work into a ball. Wrap in the ThermoMat or place into a bowl and cover with plastic wrap or kitchen towel. Leave to prove in a warm place until doubled in size (approx. 1 hour).
  4. Knock down dough. Divide dough into 20 equal-sized pieces (approx. the size of a lime) and form into rolls. Transfer rolls onto prepared baking tray then leave to prove for a further 10-15 minutes. Brush with additional melted butter then bake for 12-15 minutes (200°C) or until lightly golden.
  5. Allow to cool on tray for 5 minutes then transfer to a wire rack. Serve warm or allow to cool completely before transferring to an airtight container until ready to use.

Useful Items

plastic wrap or kitchen towel, ThermoMat, pastry brush, baking tray and paper, wire rack, airtight container, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve these slightly sweet rolls at breakfast or brunch with your favourite spreads.
