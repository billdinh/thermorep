Home-style sponge cake

Ingredients

  • butter, for greasing
  • ½ tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 1 tsp cream of tartar
  • 80 g cornflour
  • 30 g plain flour
  • 130 g caster sugar
  • 4 eggs, separated, room temperature
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract
  • 2 tbsp water, hot
  • 400 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 500 g strawberries, hulled, sliced lengthways, plus extra for decorating
  • icing sugar, for dusting

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Grease 2 springform cake tins (20 cm) and set aside.
  2. Place bicarbonate of soda, cream of tartar, cornflour and plain flour into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 5. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Thoroughly clean and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Place caster sugar into mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a separate bowl and set aside. Thoroughly clean and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg whites into mixing bowl and whip  2-4 min/speed 3, without measuring cup, until stiff peaks form.
  5. Mix 4-5 min/speed 3, adding reserved icing sugar 1 tsp at a time through hole in mixing bowl lid. It is important not to rush this process.
  6. Mix without setting a time/speed , adding egg yolks 1 at a time through hole in mixing bowl lid.  Remove butterfly whisk.
  7. Add vanilla extract, water and reserved flour mixture and mix 5 sec/speed 3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix a further 2-3 sec/speed 3, or until fully combined. Divide mixture between prepared cake tins and bake for 25-30 minutes (180°C) or until a wooden skewer inserted into the centre of the cakes comes out clean. Transfer cakes on to wire racks and allow to cool completely.
  8. Insert butterfly whisk. Once cakes are fully cooled, place cream into mixing bowl and whip without setting a time/speed 3, until soft peaks form, watching carefully to avoid overwhipping. Place one sponge cake onto a serving platter and spread whipped cream evenly over the top. Cover with sliced strawberries then place the second sponge on top. Dust with icing sugar, decorate with extra strawberries and serve.

Useful Items

2 springform cake tins (20 cm), wire rack, wooden skewer, oven, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • The strawberries in this recipe can be replaced with fresh fruit of choice, or fruit jam.
  • For best results, start this recipe with a thoroughly clean and dry mixing bowl.
