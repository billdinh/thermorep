Cannelloni with artichoke filling and tomato sauce

Ingredients

  • 140 g Parmesan cheese, crust removed and cut into pieces (2-3 cm)
  • 200 g smoked cheese (e.g. smoked scamorza, smoked Edam cheese), cut into pieces (4-5 cm)

Filling

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 20 g extra virgin olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 300 g fresh artichoke hearts
  • 100 g white wine
  • 100 g ricotta cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 pinches freshly ground black pepper
  • 16 cannelloni shells (approx. 180 g)

Tomato sauce

  • 40 g celery stalk, cut into pieces
  • 30 g brown onion
  • 40 g carrot, cut into pieces
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 400 g canned tomatoes
  • 200 g water
  • 1 pinch sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper

Preparation

  1. Place Parmesan cheese and smoked cheese into mixing bowl and grate 15 sec/speed 10. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.

Filling

  1. Place garlic and oil into mixing bowl and sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  2. Add artichokes and white wine and cook 15 min/100°C/speed 1, without measuring cup. Meanwhile, grease Varoma dish and tray.
  3. Insert measuring cup and mix 40 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl using spatula.
  4. Add ricotta cheese, 300 g reserved grated cheese, eggs, salt and pepper and mix 40 sec/speed 4. Transfer into a piping bag and allow to cool for a few minutes.
  5. Pipe filling mixture into cannelloni tubes. Arrange filled cannelloni into greased Varoma dish and tray, leaving a little space between them and lightly brush the cannelloni with oil to avoid sticking. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Tomato sauce

  1. Place celery stalks, onions, carrots, garlic, tomatoes, water, sugar, salt and black pepper into mixing bowl and chop 15 sec/speed 5.
  2. Place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and cook 30 min/Varoma/speed 1. Serve cannelloni with sauce and sprinkled with remaining grated cheese.

Useful Items

pastry brush, bowl, piping bag

Hints & Tricks

  • Frozen artichoke hearts can be used instead of fresh or jarred.
  • Mushroom cannelloni: replace artichokes with 400 g sliced fresh mushrooms. In add mushrooms with white wine and cook 15 min/100°C/speed 1, without measuring cup. Drain mushroom mixture through simmering basket, transfer drained mixture into mixing bowl and continue with recipe.
    - For meat-lovers, add 100 g pancetta or bacon after cooking for 15 minutes.
