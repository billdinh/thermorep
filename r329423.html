Five seed energy bars

Ingredients

  • extra virgin olive oil, for greasing
  • 30 g flaxseeds (linseeds)
  • 50 g white chia seeds
  • 100 g pepitas
  • 100 g sunflower seeds
  • 50 g sesame seeds
  • 80 g dried cranberries
  • 40 g shredded coconut
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste (see Tips)
  • 60 g pure maple syrup (see Tips)
  • 50 g rice malt syrup
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
545.8 kJ / 130 kcal

4.3 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C. Grease and line a square cake tin (20 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place flaxseeds (linseeds) into mixing bowl and mill 30 sec/speed 10.
  3. Add all remaining ingredients and mix 20 sec/speed 4. Transfer into prepared tin and firmly press down. Bake for 30-35 minutes (160°C) or until golden.
  4. Allow to cool to room temperature, then place into refrigerator until firm (approx. 1 hour). Slice into long strips (2.5 cm wide), then cut into halves to serve (makes 20 bars in total).

Useful Items

square cake tin (20 cm), baking paper, oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • You can store these bars in a sealable container in the pantry for up to 10 days.
  • To make your own vanilla paste, refer to the recipe on Cookidoo® (www.cookidoo.com.au).
  • You can replace the maple syrup with rice malt syrup (using 110 g rice malt syrup total).
  • Cacao energy bars: Add 1 tablespoon cacao powder when adding all other ingredients. Proceed as per recipe.
