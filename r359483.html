Mini Roast Beef, Beetroot Chutney and Horseradish Rolls

Ingredients

Beetroot Chutney

  • 400 g cooked beetroot, peeled, cut in pieces (3 cm)
  • 100 g red onions, quartered
  • 180 g apples, peeled, cored and quartered
  • 200 g soft brown sugar
  • 100 g red wine vinegar
  • 120 g balsamic vinegar

Horseradish Cream

  • 60 g crème fraîche
  • 50 g mayonnaise
  • ½ Tbsp creamed horseradish
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 12 slider buns
  • 6 slices roast beef, halved
  • 50 g rocket leaves

Preparation

Beetroot Chutney

  1. Place beetroot, onions and apples in mixing bowl then chop 8 sec/speed 4.
  2. Add sugar, red wine vinegar and balsamic vinegar then, with simmering basket in place of measuring cup, cook 30-40 min/Varoma//speed 1 until mixture is sticky and liquid has reduced.
  3. Transfer to a bowl and set aside to cool (see tip). Clean mixing bowl.

Horseradish Cream

  1. Place crème fraîche, mayonnaise, creamed horseradish, salt and pepper in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 4.
  2. To assemble, halve slider buns and set tops aside. Place on each bun ½ slice roast beef, 1 Tbsp reserved beetroot chutney and 1-2 tsp horseradish cream. Add a little rocket to each then replace tops. Transfer to a serving plate and serve immediately.
