Kaffir lime granita

Ingredients

  • 80 g lime juice
  • 60 g raw sugar
  • 8 Kaffir lime leaves, 2 leaves cut into very thin slices
  • 300 g ice cubes
Nutrition
per 1 portions
Calories
238 kJ / 56 kcal
Protein
0.2 g
Carbohydrates
12.7 g
Fat
0.1 g

Preparation

  1. Place lime juice, sugar and 6 of the whole Kaffir lime leaves into mixing bowl and cook 2 min 30 sec/Varoma/speed 2 , without measuring cup.
  2. Remove lid from mixing bowl and set aside to infuse and cool for 20 minutes.
  3. Using a fine-mesh sieve, strain infused syrup into a bowl. Rinse mixing bowl to remove any residue from leaves, then pour strained syrup back into mixing bowl.
  4. Add ice cubes and remaining 2 sliced Kaffir lime leaves and blend 20 sec/speed 1 -9, increasing speed gradually from speed 1 to speed 9.
  5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and blend 15 sec/speed 6.
  6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and blend 15 sec/speed 6 or until smooth and Kaffir leaves are finely chopped.
  7. Serve immediately or transfer granita into a freezable container and place into freezer until firm or until ready to serve. 

Useful Items

bowl, freezable storage container, fine mesh sieve, freezer

Hints & Tricks

  • If you have a stevia plant growing in your garden, you might like to add 10-15 leaves to this granita and reduce the sugar (to taste). Add fresh or dried stevia leaves along with the whole Kaffir lime leaves, then strain out as per the recipe.
  • If the granita becomes too firm to serve, place into the refrigerator or leave on the kitchen bench until thawed to your desired softness before serving. Scrape across the surface with a fork or spoon to serve. You may wish to store all frozen desserts in individual portions, as refreezing thawed leftovers can be a safety hazard.
