Beet Hummus

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ oz walnut halves or pieces
  • ½ oz sesame seeds
  • 10 ½ oz cooked beets, peeled, cut into pieces (approx. 1¼ in.)
  • 3 oz cream cheese
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 oz stale bread, roughly torn (approx. 1 slice)
  • &frac18; tsp ground cumin
  • ½ oz lemon juice
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt, to taste
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper, to taste
  • ¼ tsp salt, to taste
  • 1 tbsp olive oil, to drizzle

Preparation

  1. Place walnuts and sesame seeds into mixing bowl and without measuring cup toast 10 min/250°F/speed 1. Set mixing bowl aside and allow to cool15 minutes.
  2. Once cooled, insert measuring cup into mixing bowl lid and grind 12 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and blend 8 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and blend a further 8 sec/speed 8.
  3. Add cooked beets, cream cheese, garlic, bread, cumin, lemon juice, salt and pepper and mix 15 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix a further 15 sec/speed 8.
  4. Check and adjust seasoning and transfer to a serving bowl, drizzle with olive oil and serve.

Hints & Tricks

  • Roast your own beets or use vacuum packed pre-cooked beets, but not pickled beets. Raw beets can also be used for a different flavor.
  • How to roast your own beets: peel and cut into ¾ in. cubes and lightly rub with 1 tsp olive oil. Place beets in a baking pan and bake 25-30 minutes (400°F). Remove from oven and allow to cool.
  • Steam your beets: peel and cut beets into ¾ in. cubes and place into simmering basket. Add 20 oz water into mixing bowl and insert simmering basket with beets. Steam 30 min/Varoma/speed 3.
  • Serve with pita bread or assorted sliced or whole raw vegetables.
