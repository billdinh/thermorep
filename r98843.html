Pistachio cake

Ingredients

Syrup

  • 330 g raw sugar
  • 370 g water
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 cinnamon quill
  • 1 tsp ground cardamom
  • 1 tsp dried fennel seeds
  • 1 orange, zest only, no white pith

Cake

  • 190 g salted butter, softened, plus extra for greasing
  • 280 g shelled unsalted pistachios, plus 2 tbsp for decorating
  • 170 g raw sugar
  • 1 orange, zest only, no white pith
  • 3 eggs
  • 60 g full cream milk
  • 1 tbsp white vinegar
  • 220 g self-raising flour
  • 120 g plain flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Preparation

Syrup

  1. Place all syrup ingredients into mixing bowl and cook 9 min/Varoma/speed 2, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid. Leave to cool in mixing bowl, then strain through a fine-mesh sieve into a small jug and set aside until ready to serve. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Cake

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Grease and line a springform cake tin (23 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place pistachios into mixing bowl and chop 7 sec/speed 5. Transfer and press into base of prepared tin.
  3. Place sugar and zest into mixing bowl and mill 20 sec/speed 9.
  4. Add butter and eggs and mix 30 sec/speed 5.
  5. Repeat mixing speed 5, adding milk and vinegar through hole in mixing bowl lid until just combined.
  6. Add 200 g of the reserved syrup and mix 10 sec/speed 5.
  7. Add self-raising flour, plain flour and baking powder and pulse Turbo/1 sec/4 times. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  8. Pulse Turbo/1 sec/4 times, if required until mixture is fully combined.
  9. Pour mixture over pistachio base and bake for 45-55 minutes (180°C), or until a wooden skewer inserted into the centre of the cake comes out clean.
  10. Leave cake to cool in tin for 5 minutes, then invert onto a serving platter and release cake from tin (pistachio base will now be on top). Scatter with 2 tbsp pistachios, then pour over the remaining syrup, to taste. Serve cake warm or cool.

Useful Items

small jug, fine mesh sieve, baking paper, springform cake tin (23 cm), wooden skewer, serving platter, oven
