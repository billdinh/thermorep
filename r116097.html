Whole Steamed Chicken with Spicy Cajun Rub

Ingredients

Lemon and Herb Stuffing

  • 5 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 2 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves only
  • 1 - 2 sprigs fresh sage leaves, to taste
  • ½ lemon, thin peelings of skin and juice (approx. 30 g)
  • 30 g butter, diced
  • 70 g onions, quartered
  • 1 - 2 pinches fine sea salt, to taste
  • 1 - 2 pinches ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1 Tbsp sunflower seeds
  • 100 g breadcrumbs

Spicy Cajun Rub

  • 160 g coarse sea salt
  • 1 Tbsp chilli powder
  • 1 Tbsp ground smoked paprika
  • 1 Tbsp dried onion flakes
  • 1 Tbsp coriander seeds
  • 1 Tbsp granulated garlic
  • 1 ½ Tbsp dried thyme
  • 1 ½ Tbsp dried oregano
  • 2 tsp ground cayenne pepper
  • 2 tsp ground black pepper
  • ½ tsp ground cumin
  • ½ tsp dark brown muscovado sugar

Whole Chicken

  • 1 whole uncooked chicken (1.2-1.5 kg)
  • 1000 g water

Preparation

Lemon and Herb Stuffing

  1. Place parsley, thyme, sage and lemon peel in mixing bowl then chop 10 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then chop again 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place butter and onions in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then sauté 4 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Add reserved chopped herbs and peel, salt, pepper, sunflower seeds, lemon juice and breadcrumbs then mix 10 sec/speed 3. Transfer to a bowl and set aside to cool. Clean and dry mixing bowl then prepare rub.

Spicy Cajun Rub

  1. Place all spice rub ingredients in mixing bowl and combine 5 sec/speed 7. Transfer to an airtight jar and set aside until needed.

Whole Chicken

  1. Place whole chicken into Varoma dish and fill chicken cavity with reserved cooled stuffing. Rub 1-2 Tbsp of reserved rub into chicken skin.
  2. Place water in mixing bowl and place covered Varoma into position then steam 60 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Check to see if your chicken is cooked through (see tip). If chicken is not cooked, cover Varoma and steam for a further 10 min/Varoma/speed 1, or until chicken is cooked. Transfer chicken to a serving platter, then carve and serve immediately.

Useful Items

airtight jar, serving platter

Hints & Tricks

  • The rub on its own makes a great gift idea for friends and family.
  • Ensure your stuffing is completely cool before using.
  • To check your chicken is cooked, slice the chicken behind it's leg near to the chest. If the juices run clear, it is cooked.
  • If you don't want to use a stuffing in your chicken or time is limited, fill the chicken cavity with 2 garlic cloves, sliced, 1 whole lemon, halved and 2 sprigs fresh rosemary in step 6 and proceed as per recipe.
