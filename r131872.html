Smoked Salmon Pizza

Ingredients

Dough

  • 270 g water
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp dried instant yeast
  • 60 g extra virgin olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 500 g spelt flour, plus extra for flouring
  • 2 tsp fine sea salt

Topping

  • 100 g mozzarella, drained, cut in pieces
  • 250 g cherry tomatoes
  • 80 g tomato purée
  • 20 g balsamic vinegar
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 3 - 4 sprigs fresh basil, leaves only
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 3 pinches fine sea salt
  • ½ fresh chilli, deseeded, sliced (5 mm)
  • 190 g smoked salmon, sliced (1 cm)
  • 2 - 3 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 60 g red onions, quartered
  • 30 g extra virgin olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 20 g lemon juice
  • 100 g pitted black olives
  • 100 g baby leaf salad, mixed

Preparation

Dough

  1. Place 50 g water, sugar and yeast in mixing bowl then warm 2 min/37°C/speed 2. Meanwhile, grease a large bowl with oil.
  2. Add remaining 220 g water, oil, flour and salt then knead 1 min 30 sec/. Transfer dough to prepared bowl, cover with cling film then set aside to rise in a warm place until doubled in size (approx. 1-2 hours). Towards end of rising time, prepare topping.

Topping

  1. Place mozzarella in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place tomatoes, tomato purée, vinegar, garlic, basil, oregano, sugar, 2 pinches salt and chilli in mixing bowl then chop 4 sec/speed 6.
  3. Preheat oven to 230°C and generously grease 2 large baking trays with oil.
  4. Divide risen dough in half then, on a floured work surface, roll out 2 circles of dough (approx. Ø 30 cm each). Transfer each pizza base to a prepared baking tray.
  5. Spread tomato sauce over bases, leaving approx. 3 Tbsp in mixing bowl. Spread salmon and chopped mozzarella over bases then bake for 12-15 minutes (230°C) until crisp and golden.
  6. Towards end of baking time, add remaining pinch of salt, parsley, onions, oil and lemon juice to mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 4.
  7. Add olives and mix 4 sec//speed 2. Pour mixture onto baked pizzas then serve hot with salad.

Useful Items

large bowl, cling film, 2 large baking trays, rolling pin, oven
