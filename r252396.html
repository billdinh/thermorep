Chicken and almond stir-fry

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp Chinese cooking wine
  • 2 tbsp kecap manis
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • 600 - 700 g boneless skinless chicken thighs, cut into strips (1 x 5 cm)
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • 2 tsp Chinese five spice
  • salt, to season
  • ground black pepper, to season
  • 3 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 150 g raw almonds
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 500 g water
  • 200 g broccoli, cut into small florets
  • 200 g cauliflower, cut into small florets
  • 1 carrot, cut into matchsticks
  • 200 g cabbage, shredded
  • 150 g snow peas, trimmed and cut into halves
  • 3 spring onions/shallots, trimmed and cut into slices

Preparation

  1. Place Chinese cooking wine, kecap manis, soy sauce and 1 teaspoon of the sesame oil into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and set aside.
  2. Place a large snaplock bag onto mixing bowl lid and weigh chicken into it. Add cornflour, Chinese five spice, salt and pepper into bag. Seal bag and toss to coat, then set aside.
  3. Place ginger into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add remaining 1 teaspoon sesame oil and cook 1 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  5. Add almonds and cook 4 min/Varoma/î€ƒ/speed 1. Transfer into thermal serving bowl with sauce. Do not clean mixing bowl.
  6. Place a large frying pan over high heat and add oil. Shake off excess seasoning from reserved chicken, then add to pan and fry for 10 minutes or until chicken is golden and cooked through. Meanwhile, continue with recipe.
  7. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh broccoli and cauliflower into it. Add carrot, secure Varoma lid and steam 8 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  8. Weigh cabbage and snowpeas into Varoma. Add spring onions/shallots and toss vegetables together with aid of spatula. Secure Varoma lid and cook 2-3 min/Varoma/speed 1 or until vegetables are cooked to your liking (vegetables should still be a little crispy). Transfer into thermal serving bowl with sauce and almonds and stir to combine. Divide between serving bowls and top with chicken to serve.
