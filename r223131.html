Lemon delicious

Ingredients

  • 125 g unsalted butter, plus extra for greasing
  • 1 lemon, zest only, no white pith
  • 220 g raw sugar
  • 4 eggs, separated
  • 75 g plain flour
  • 80 g freshly squeezed lemon juice (see Tip)
  • 310 g milk
  • boiling water
  • icing sugar, for dusting
  • thickened cream, to serve (optional)

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Grease a deep-sided casserole dish (approx. 20 cm) and set aside.
  2. Place lemon peel and sugar into mixing bowl and mill 20 sec/speed 9.
  3. Add butter and mix 20 sec/speed 4.
  4. Insert butterfly whisk. Beat 10-20 sec/speed 3, until well combined. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Mix 30 sec/speed 3, slowly adding 1 egg yolk at a time through hole in mixing bowl lid. Remove butterfly whisk.
  6. Add flour, lemon juice and milk and combine 10 sec/speed 2.
  7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and combine 5 sec/speed 2. Pour mixture into prepared casserole dish.
  8. Thoroughly wash and dry mixing bowl and butterfly whisk. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg whites into mixing bowl and whisk 2-3 min/speed 4, without measuring cup, until soft peaks form. Remove butterfly whisk.
  9. Gently fold egg whites through lemon mixture. Place casserole dish into a large baking dish and fill up baking dish halfway with boiling water. Bake for 40-45 minutes (180°C), until golden. Dust with icing sugar and serve while warm with cream (optional).

Useful Items

casserole dish, large baking dish, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • You may need 2-3 lemons to produce 80g of lemon juice.
