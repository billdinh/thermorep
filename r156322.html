Steamed eggplant and ricotta lasagne

Ingredients

  • 50 g Parmesan cheese, crust removed and cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 5 - 10 g fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 1 - 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 1100 g water
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 160 g dried lasagne sheets
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 50 g brown onion, cut into pieces
  • 50 g carrot, cut into pieces
  • 30 g celery stalk, cut into pieces
  • 30 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 300 g eggplant, cut into cubes (approx. 1 cm)
  • 500 g canned crushed tomatoes
  • 250 g ricotta cheese, drained

Preparation

  1. Choose a rectangular dish (approx. 23 x 15 x 7 cm) that fits into Varoma dish (see Tip) and set aside. Place Parmesan cheese, parsley and pepper into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer 2 teaspoons into a small bowl and the remaining mixture into another small bowl, and set both aside.
  2. Place 500 g of the water and 1 teaspoon of the salt into mixing bowl and boil 5 min/100°C/speed 1. Pour boiling water into the rectangular dish and add one pasta sheet at a time to soak. Stir gently to keep pasta sheets separate during the first few minutes of soaking.
  3. Place garlic, onion, carrot, celery and oil into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 6, then sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1, without measuring cup .
  4. Add eggplant, remaining 1 tsp salt and crushed tomatoes and cook 15 min/Varoma//speed 1.
  5. Drain pasta sheets by pouring onto Varoma tray and letting the water drain away. Cover base of the rectangular dish with a thin layer of eggplant mixture. Place a layer of pasta sheets over the sauce, cover with another thin layer of eggplant mixture, sprinkle with 1/3 of the larger amount of reserved Parmesan mixture and distribute 4-5 heaped teaspoons ricotta on top without spreading it. Repeat to make 4 pasta layers, finishing with a layer of eggplant mixture. Cover lasagne dish with a piece of wet, well-wrung baking paper and place into Varoma dish.
  6. Place remaining 600 g water into mixing bowl, place Varoma into position and steam 30 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove Varoma and baking paper, then sprinkle lasagne with remaining Parmesan mixture. Cut lasagne into squares and serve warm.

Useful Items

rectangular baking dish (23 cm x 15 cm x 7 cm), baking paper, small bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • Use any kind of rectangular or oval container that fits into the Varoma dish while leaving some holes unobstructed for steam to circulate. You can also fold a disposable aluminimum tin if it is too large. Or use the Varoma tray (see Variations).
  • Mix 1 tsp grated lemon zest into the ricotta before using it.
    - Artichoke lasagne: replace eggplant with sliced artichoke hearts (fresh or defrosted).
    - Zucchini lasagne: replace eggplant with sliced zucchini.
    - If you cannot find a suitable container, the lasagne can be steamed in the Varoma tray: line the Varoma tray with a large sheet of baking paper (approx. 40 cm x 35 cm), with the excess hanging over the edges. Assemble the lasagne as directed by the recipe, then fold the overhanging baking paper over the lasagne. Place Varoma tray into Varoma dish and proceed as directed by the recipe. To serve, cut lasagne and plate each portion individually.
