Baked kibbeh (kibbeh bil sanieh)

Ingredients

Filling

  • 150 g onions, cut into quarters
  • 40 g sunflower oil
  • 70 g pine nuts
  • 400 g lamb meat, finely minced
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp freshly ground pepper
  • 1 pinch ground cinnamon

Kibbeh

  • 500 g lamb fillet sinew free, cut into cubes (3 cm)
  • 4 ice cubes
  • 200 g onions, cut into quarters
  • 300 g fine bulgur wheat, rinsed with cold water and strained well (see tip)
  • 2 level tsp salt
  • 2 level tsp ground cinnamon

Assembly and baking

  • 10 g sunflower oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 1 level tsp freshly ground pepper (optional)
  • 1 level tsp Arabian spice mix (optional)

Preparation

Filling

  1. Place onions and sunflower oil into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5, then sauté 5 min/120°C/speed 1.
  2. Add pine nuts, minced lamb and sauté 7 min/120°C/speed 1.
  3. Add salt, pepper and ground cinnamon and mix with spatula. Transfer into a deep bowl and set aside.

Kibbeh

  1. Place lamb cubes and 2 ice cubes into mixing bowl and chop 3 min/speed 6. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place onions, 2 ice cubes and bulgur into mixing bowl and chop 30 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add reserved minced lamb, salt, ground cinnamon and mix 20 sec/speed 6 with aid of spatula.
  4. Insert measuring cup and knead 2 min/. Transfer into a bowl.

Assembly and baking

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Grease a round baking dish (Ø 28 cm) and set aside.
  2. Spread half of kibbeh mixture in prepared baking dish and lightly press with wet hands. Form a smooth and flat surface. Layer on top with reserved mince filling and cover with remaining kibbeh mixture. Score with a sharp knife for decoration and brush with remaining 10 g sunflower oil. Sprinkle with pepper and spice mix (optional).
  3. Bake for 55 minutes (180°C) or until golden brown. Serve hot.

Useful Items

deep bowl, pastry brush, round baking dish (Ø 28 cm), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Do not let rinsed bulgur rest for more than 30 minutes, as it will soak too much.
  • Serve with Yoghurt and cucumber salad.
