Mexican Quinoa Stuffed Aubergine

Ingredients

  • 100 g Cheddar cheese, diced (1-2 cm)
  • fresh coriander (approx. 10 g)
  • 2 aubergines, large (approx. 800 g)
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 120 g onions, quartered
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 - 2 fresh jalapeño chillies, to taste
  • 275 g water
  • 400 g tinned chopped tomatoes (1 x 400 g tin)
  • 1 ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 120 g quinoa, rinsed
  • 100 g tinned kidney beans, drained
  • 100 g tinned sweetcorn, drained
  • 1 vegetable stock cube (for 0.5 l)
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • ½ tsp chilli powder

Preparation

  1. Place cheese in mixing bowl and grate 3 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place fresh coriander in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8. Transfer to another bowl and set aside.
  3. Cut aubergines in half lengthways then cut around the inside edge (leaving ½ cm border) and scoop out flesh.
  4. Place pieces of aubergine flesh, oil, onions, garlic and chillies in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then sauté 4 min/120°C/speed 1. Meanwhile, mix 200 g tinned tomatoes, 200 g water and ½ tsp salt together in a bowl then transfer to a large oven-proof dish. Arrange aubergine shells on top, skin-side down.
  5. Add quinoa, remaining 200 g tinned tomatoes, 75 g water, kidney beans, sweetcorn, stock cube, ground coriander, cumin, paprika, 1 tsp salt and chilli powder. Stir together with spatula then cook 15-20 min/100°C//speed  until quinoa is cooked.
  6. Preheat oven to 190°C.
  7. Add reserved chopped coriander and stir in with spatula. Divide filling evenly between aubergine shells in oven-proof dish then sprinkle with reserved grated cheese. Bake for 30-40 minutes (190°C) until browned and bubbling. Serve hot.

Useful Items

oven-proof dish, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Four smaller aubergines can be used instead of two large ones. Serve two halves per portion.
  • Works well served with a side salad.
