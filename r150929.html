Steamed Wholemeal Bread

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp butter, for greasing
  • 2 Tbsp semolina, for dusting
  • 250 g spelt grains
  • 1250 g water
  • 10 g dark treacle (optional)
  • 20 g fresh yeast, crumbled
  • 50 g bread flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 10 g balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 100 g sunflower seeds

Preparation

  1. Choose a rectangular loaf tin (metal, 20 cm long) that fits in the Varoma dish, grease with butter and dust with semolina, then set aside.
  2. Place spelt grains in mixing bowl and grind 1 min/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place 250 g water, treacle (if using) and yeast in mixing bowl and warm 2 min/37Â°C/speed 1.
  4. Add reserved ground spelt, bread flour, balsamic vinegar, salt and sunflower seeds and mix 30 sec/speed 3. Transfer dough to prepared loaf tin, dust surface with flour and cover with heat-resistant cling film. Place loaf tin in Varoma dish and allow dough to rise for 15 minutes. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl.
  5. Place 1000 g water in mixing bowl, set Varoma in position and steam 50 min/Varoma/speed 1 (for silicone baking moulds see tip).
  6. Remove Varoma, take out loaf tin and remove cling film then leave to cool in tin for 10 minutes. Transfer bread from tin to a wire rack and allow to cool before slicing and serving.

Useful Items

loaf tin (metal, 20 cm long), heat-resistant cling film, wire rack

Hints & Tricks

  • This bread is made without an oven! The result is moist and full of flavour, and the whole grains add extra nutrition. If you are not in a hurry, reduce the yeast and give the bread more time to rise, as described in the variation.
  • If using a silicone bread mould, extend steaming time by 10 minutes.
  • For a longer rise, the amount of fresh yeast can be halved in step 4 allow covered bread dough to rise for 2-3 hours in a warm place or overnight in the fridge, then continue with step 5.
    - Nut Bread: Instead of sunflower seeds, add 80 g nuts (hazelnuts or walnuts).
    - Carrot Bread: After step 2, place 100 g carrots, cut in pieces, in mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 5. Leave carrots in mixing bowl and continue with recipe.
    - Carrot Walnut Bread: After step 2, place 80 g carrots, cut in pieces, in mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 5. Leave carrots in mixing bowl and continue with recipe. In step 4 replace the 100 g sunflower seeds with 30 g walnuts.
