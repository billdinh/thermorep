English Breakfast Popovers

Ingredients

  • 6 tsp olive oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 5 ½ oz all-purpose flour
  • 14 oz whole milk
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 12 small uncooked breakfast sausages, (approx. 8 oz package)
  • 6 slices Canadian bacon, each slice cut in half
  • 12 cherry tomatoes, cut in quarters
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
667 kJ / 159 kcal
Protein
6.4 g
Carbohydrates
12.6 g

9.3 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F. Place ½ tsp olive oil in each hole of a 12 hole muffin pan and place in oven to preheat.
  2. Place eggs, flour, milk, salt and pepper into mixing bowl and mix 15 sec/speed 6. Allow batter to rest. Meanwhile, fry sausages and Canadian bacon in a frying pan on the stove until browned.
  3. Mix batter 5 sec/speed 5 and carefully pour into hot muffin pan, (approx. ⅔ full). Top each with a piece of Canadian bacon, a sausage and 4 cherry tomato quarters. Immediately return to hot oven and bake 20 minutes (450°F) until well risen. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

frying pan, 12 hole muffin pan, oven, stove

Hints & Tricks

  • Pre-cooked breakfast sausages and ham could replace breakfast sausages and Canadian bacon to make this recipe even quicker.
