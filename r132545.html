Mediterranean Chicken Wings with Herb-Orange Rice

Ingredients

Chicken Wings

  • 2 tbsp sweet paprika
  • 1 tbsp mixed herbes de Provence
  • 1 ½ tsp salt
  • ¾ tsp ground white pepper
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • ½ - 1 fresh chilli, to taste
  • 2 tsp lemon zest
  • 12 - 16 chicken wings

Herb-Orange Rice

  • 50 g onions
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary, leaves only
  • 2 sprigs fresh oregano, leaves only
  • 15 g olive oil
  • 1000 g water
  • 1 ½ tsp salt
  • 200 g long grain rice
  • 1 orange, peeled, white pith and pips removed, cut in small pieces

Preparation

Chicken Wings

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Place in a bowl paprika powder, herbs de Provence, salt and white pepper then set aside.
  3. Place garlic cloves, chilli and lemon zest in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl using spatula.
  4. Chop again 5 sec/speed 8. Transfer to bowl with spices, mix together and set aside.
  5. Place chicken wings in a casserole dish and rub them with reserved spice mixture. Bake for 15 minutes (180°C). Meanwhile prepare herb-orange rice.

Herb-Orange Rice

  1. Place onions, garlic clove, rosemary and oregano in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Chop again 3 sec/speed 8.
  3. Add olive oil and sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place water and salt in mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket and add rice to it. Pour reserved onion-herb mixture over rice and mix with spatula, then cook 20 min/100°C/speed 4. Meanwhile, increase oven temperature after 15 minutes (180°C) baking time to 200°C and bake chicken wings for a further 15-20 minutes (200°C).
  5. Remove simmering basket using spatula and transfer rice to serving dish, add orange pieces, then mix using spatula. Serve herb-orange rice hot with Mediterranean chicken wings.
