Chunky Beef Stew

Ingredients

  • 8 oz yellow onions, in pieces
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • ½ oz all-purpose flour
  • ½ oz extra virgin olive oil
  • 24 oz beef meat (stew cut)
  • 16 oz water
  • 3 oz tomato paste
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 12 oz baby gold potatoes, quartered
  • 9 oz carrots, peeled, sliced (¾ in.)
  • chopped fresh parsley, to garnish
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1071 kJ / 256 kcal
Protein
27 g
Carbohydrates
22 g
Fat
7 g

Preparation

  1. Place onion, garlic, flour and olive oil into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and without measuring cup sauté 5 min/250°F/speed 1.
  2. Add beef, water, tomato paste, salt and pepper, insert measuring cup and cook 70 min/195°F//speed 1.
  3. Add potatoes and carrots and cook 40 min/195°F//speed 1.
  4. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley and serve hot.
