Potato Pizza with Vegetables - Pizza di patate con verdure

Ingredients

Dough

  • 100 g potatoes, peeled, cut in pieces (2-3 cm)
  • 320 g water
  • 2 tsp fine sea salt
  • 20 g extra virgin olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 600 g durum wheat flour
  • 1 tsp dried instant yeast

Topping

  • 10 g extra virgin olive oil, plus extra for drizzling
  • 80 g yellow peppers, cut in pieces (1-2 cm)
  • 80 g red peppers, cut in pieces (1-2 cm)
  • 50 g courgettes, cut in pieces (1-2 cm)
  • 100 g aubergines, cut in pieces (1-2 cm)
  • 300 g white button mushrooms, fresh, sliced
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 4 - 6 fresh basil leaves, shredded, plus extra for garnish

Preparation

Dough

  1. Grease a large baking tray with plenty of oil.
  2. Place potatoes in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 4.5. Scrape down sides and base of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add 150 g water and cook 10 min/100°C/speed 1.
  4. Add 150 g water, salt, oil, flour and yeast. Wait for temperature to cool to 60°C then knead 3 min/.
  5. Add remaining 20 g water and knead again 1 min/ (the dough should be quite soft).
  6. Tip dough out onto prepared baking tray and spread into a rectangle over the whole tray using the tips of your fingers. Cover with cling film (see tip) and leave to rise for 1 hour.

Topping

  1. Towards the end of this time, preheat oven to 200°C.
  2. Place oil, vegetables and salt in mixing bowl then cook 6 min/100°C//speed .
  3. Drain cooked vegetables through simmering basket then arrange over pizza. Sprinkle with shredded basil leaves and bake for 20-30 minutes (200°C) until cooked.
  4. Drizzle with a little extra oil and scatter with extra basil leaves, before serving hot, cut in slices.

Useful Items

cling film, baking tray (approx. 40 cm x 35 cm), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • The humble potato is a popular pizza topping across Italy. A great example of traditional peasant food, many recipes combine thinly sliced potatoes with fresh herbs and a drizzle of olive oil for a filling and inexpensive family meal. In southern Italy, potatoes are used in the dough itself. Cooked, mashed potatoes are combined with yeast and flour to make a tender crust that can be topped with any ingredients.
  • Sprinkle over mozzarella cubes or other cheese before baking in step 8.
  • Lightly grease cling film to prevent it from sticking to the dough during the resting and leavening stages.
