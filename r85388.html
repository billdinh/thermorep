Torta Caprese

Ingredients

  • 200 g butter, diced, plus extra for greasing
  • 250 g blanched almonds
  • 200 g dark chocolate, 70% cocoa solids, pieces or callets
  • 180 g caster sugar
  • 1 tsp real vanilla extract
  • 4 medium eggs
  • 2 tsp icing sugar, for serving

2769 kJ / 662 kcal

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 170°C and grease and line base of a cake tin (Ø 20 cm) with baking paper.
  2. Place almonds in mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 10.
  3. Add chocolate and chop Turbo/1 sec/5 times. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place butter, sugar and vanilla extract in mixing bowl then mix 20 sec/speed 5.  Insert butterfly whisk and cream 1 min/speed 4.
  5. Add eggs and mix 30 sec/speed 4.  Remove butterfly whisk and scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  6. Add chopped nuts and chocolate then mix 20 sec/speed 3.5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl and mix again 10 sec/speed 3.5.
  7. Transfer to prepared cake tin and bake for 45-50 minutes (170°C) until firm to touch. Leave to cool in tin before turning out. Serve warm or cold with a dusting of icing sugar.

Useful Items

baking paper, round cake tin (Ø 20 cm), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • This is a rich, decadent cake that will satisfy any chocolate lover. Ground almonds are used in place of flour, making it a great option for those on gluten-free diets. Torta Caprese is a very traditional Italian cake named after the island of Capri from which it originates.
