Petit Gateaux of Nuts and Chocolate

Ingredients

  • 20 g hazelnuts, toasted, plus extra ground for sprinkling
  • 20 g blanched almonds, toasted
  • 20 g butter, plus extra for greasing
  • 30 g plain flour
  • 15 g brown sugar
  • 45 g milk
  • 1 medium egg
  • ½ orange (zest and 10 g juice)
  • 20 g chocolate (2 squares from a bar)
  • crème fraîche, to serve
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
413 kcal
Protein
11 g
Carbohydrates
31 g

27 g

Preparation

  1. Place hazelnuts and almonds in mixing bowl then grind 1 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl.
  2. Preheat oven to 200°C. Butter 2 ramekins (approx. Ø 7 cm) then sprinkle each ramekin with ½ Tbsp ground nuts.
  3. Place butter in mixing bowl and melt 1 min/100°C/speed 1.
  4. Add flour, sugar, milk, egg, orange zest and 10 g orange juice then mix 10 sec/speed 4.
  5. Add reserved ground nuts and mix 2 sec/speed 2. Divide between prepared ramekins and place a chocolate square in the centre of each (which will sink to form a melting middle). Bake for 18 minutes (200°C) or until cakes rise.
  6. Sprinkle with ground hazelnuts and serve with crème fraîche.

Useful Items

ramekins (approx. Ø 7 cm, height 4 cm), oven
