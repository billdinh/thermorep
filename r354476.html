Herby Steaks with French Potato Salad

Ingredients

Herb Butter

  • ½ shallot (approx. 10 g)
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 5 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves only
  • 4 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 80 g unsalted butter, diced
  • 2 pinches fine sea salt
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper

French Bistro Potato Salad

  • 4 fresh chives, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 4 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves only
  • 3 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 2 spring onions (approx. 35 g), cut in pieces
  • 60 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 30 g red wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt, plus extra to taste
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper, plus extra to taste
  • 500 g water, boiling
  • 700 g new potatoes, quartered
  • vegetable oil, for frying
  • 4 rump steaks

Preparation

Herb Butter

  1. Place shallot, garlic, thyme and parsley in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then chop again 3 sec/speed 5.
  2. Add butter, salt and pepper then mix 10 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a piece of cling film, roll up tightly into a log, twisting ends to seal, then place in freezer until needed. Clean mixing bowl.

French Bistro Potato Salad

  1. Place chives, thyme, parsley and spring onions in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add oil, vinegar, mustard, salt and pepper then mix 20 sec//speed 4. Transfer dressing to a large bowl and set aside.
  3. Place water in mixing bowl then insert simmering basket. Place potatoes in simmering basket then steam 12 min/Varoma/speed 2. Meanwhile, heat a lightly-greased griddle pan or frying pan over a high heat, season steaks with salt and pepper to taste then fry for 2-3 minutes each side, until cooked as desired. Transfer to a plate, cover with foil and leave to rest.
  4. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula, transfer potatoes to bowl with dressing then stir gently to combine.
  5. Divide potato salad between 4 serving plates and place a steak on each. Slice reserved herb butter into rounds and place on each steak. Serve immediately.
