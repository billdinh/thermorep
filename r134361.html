Buckwheat and Cacao Granola

Ingredients

  • 250 g buckwheat grains
  • water, for soaking
  • 125 g hazelnuts
  • 300 g oat flakes
  • 80 g honey
  • 50 g coconut oil
  • 25 g cacao powder
  • 50 g goji berries
Nutrition
per 100 g
Calories
1954 kJ / 467 kcal
Protein
12 g
Carbohydrates
56 g
Fat
21 g

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid. Weigh in buckwheat then cover with water. Leave to soak for 2 hours. Drain then set aside.
  2. Place hazelnuts in mixing bowl and chop 1 sec/speed 7. Transfer to bowl with drained buckwheat.
  3. Place bowl with nuts and buckwheat on mixing bowl lid. Weigh in oats, remove from lid, mix together then set aside.
  4. Preheat oven to 180°C and line a baking tray with baking paper.
  5. Place honey, coconut oil and cacao powder in mixing bowl then heat 3 min/70°C/speed 1. Pour over reserved mixture, stirring well with spatula to coat evenly. Spread on prepared tray and bake for 20 minutes (180°C). Leave to cool then add goji berries.
  6. Store in an airtight container until ready to serve.

Useful Items

medium mesh sieve, airtight container, baking tray and baking paper, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Despite its name, buckwheat contains no wheat and is naturally gluten-free, making it a good option for those with an intolerance to wheat or gluten. It is also suitable for diabetics as it helps to maintain good blood glucose levels.
