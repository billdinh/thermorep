Triple Chocolate Layer Cake

Ingredients

Chocolate Cakes

  • 3 medium eggs
  • 150 g caster sugar
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • 200 g unsalted butter, soft, plus extra for greasing
  • 150 g dark chocolate, small pieces or callets
  • 50 g water
  • 100 g brown sugar
  • 80 g cream (whipping or double)
  • 200 g self-raising flour
  • 70 g cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda

Chocolate Filling

  • 100 g dark chocolate, small pieces or callets
  • 20 g cocoa powder
  • 80 g unsalted butter, diced
  • 100 g cream (whipping or double)
  • 350 g sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt

Chocolate Ganache

  • 150 g dark chocolate, small pieces or callets
  • 250 g fresh strawberries
  • 80 g cream (whipping or double)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Preparation

Chocolate Cakes

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Line bases of 3 sandwich cake tins (Ø 18 cm) with baking paper and grease with butter.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Place eggs, caster sugar and salt in mixing bowl then whisk 5 min 30 sec/37°C/speed 3.5. Remove butterfly whisk. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Without cleaning mixing bowl, place butter, chocolate and water in mixing bowl then warm 5 min/50°C/speed 1.
  4. Insert butterfly whisk. Add brown sugar and cream then whisk 15 sec/speed 3.5.
  5. Around butterfly whisk, add reserved egg mixture, flour, cocoa powder, baking powder and bicarbonate of soda then mix 10 sec/speed 3.5. Remove butterfly whisk.
  6. Divide cake batter between prepared tins (approx. 320 g per tin, see tip). Place cake tins in oven, two on the top rack and one on the middle rack then bake immediately for 18-20 minutes (180°C) until a skewer inserted in centre comes out clean. About halfway through baking, rotate the cake from the middle rack to the top rack and move one from the top to the middle. Leave cakes to cool for 5 minutes before removing from tins. Leave to cool completely on a wire rack before filling (approx. 50 minutes). Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl.

Chocolate Filling

  1. Place chocolate and cocoa powder in mixing bowl then grind 10 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add butter, cream, condensed milk and salt then cook 8 min/120°C/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl, leave to cool for 10 minutes then refrigerate for 50 minutes.
  3. Remove chilled chocolate filling from fridge and cut into pieces. Insert butterfly whisk. Place chocolate pieces in mixing bowl and whisk 1 min/speed 3.5. Remove butterfly whisk. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.

Chocolate Ganache

  1. Without cleaning mixing bowl, place chocolate in mixing bowl, chop 8 sec/speed 5 then melt 5 min/50°C/speed 1. Dip strawberries into melted chocolate and set aside on baking paper.
  2. Add cream and vanilla extract then heat 2 min/80°C/speed 1.
  3. Place one cake on serving plate, spread ½ reserved chocolate filling over it, place second cake on top, then spread with remaining chocolate filling. Place last cake on top and cover with chocolate ganache. Decorate with reserved chocolate-covered strawberries and serve, cut in slices.

Useful Items

sandwich cake tins (Ø 18 cm), baking paper, wire rack, skewer, oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Use the built-in Thermomix scales to measure out the exact quantity of cake batter for each cake tin. At the end of the recipe, turn scales on, lift out mixing bowl and pour some cake batter into the prepared tin. When you return the mixing bowl to the base, it will show a minus figure indicating the weight removed (- 320 g).
  • Dark chocolate is a blend of cocoa solids and cocoa fats. The percentage figure indicates the amount of cocoa solids in the chocolate. 70% is the right percentage to use for cooking, but chocolates with higher or lower percentages can be used.
