Banoffee Cream Pie

Ingredients

Pie Crust

  • 1 homemade pie crust, chilled (9 in.)

Filling

  • 8 oz unsalted butter, cut into pieces (½ in.)
  • 6 oz dark brown sugar
  • 6 ½ oz heavy whipping cream
  • 2 tbsp corn syrup
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 1 packet/envelope gelatin powder, unflavored

Topping

  • 1 - 2 tbsp sugar, to taste
  • 16 oz heavy whipping cream
  • 2 - 3 bananas, sliced (¼ in.)
  • chocolate, grated, to garnish

Preparation

Pie Crust

  1. Adjust oven rack to lower middle position. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. Line chilled pie crust with parchment and baking weights and blind-bake for 15 minutes (375°F), remove parchment and weights and bake a further 10 minutes (375°F) until lightly golden. Transfer pie pan to a wire rack to cool. Meanwhile make filling.

Filling

  1. Place butter, brown sugar, cream, corn syrup, vanilla extract and salt into mixing bowl. Place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 20 min/250°F/speed 1. Transfer into a bowl and stir in gelatin until combined. Cool in refrigerator until thickened (approx. 1-2 hr.) then transfer into cooled pie crust. Meanwhile clean mixing bowl and make topping.

Topping

  1. Place sugar into mixing bowl, insert measuring cup and grind 10 sec/speed 10.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Add whipping cream and without setting a time, whip speed 3 until desired stiffness is achieved, watching carefully to avoid overwhipping. Remove butterfly whisk and refrigerate whipped cream until ready to serve.
  3. To serve, arrange banana slices onto firm caramel, top with whipped cream and swirl evenly over surface of pie to form peaks. Garnish with grated chocolate. Slice and serve immediately.

Useful Items

wire rack, parchment paper, baking weights, oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Baking weights can be replaced with 16 oz dry beans.
  • Pie crust with caramel filling may be stored in refrigerator; add sliced bananas and whipped cream when ready to serve.
