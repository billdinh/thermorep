Almond Dip

Ingredients

  • ½ tsp ground cumin
  • ½ tsp chili powder
  • ¼ tsp ground coriander
  • ¼ tsp sweet paprika
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 4 oz raw almonds
  • 2 oz nutritional yeast (approx. 4 tbsp)
  • 6 oz water
  • 4 oz grapeseed oil
  • 3 ½ oz lemon juice (approx. 4 lemons)
  • ½ oz liquid all purpose seasoning, e.g. Bragg® liquid aminos
  • 1 tsp salt
Nutrition

Preparation

  1. Place all ingredients into mixing bowl and blend 1 min/speed 9. Transfer to a bowl and serve with chips or over steamed vegetables.
