Silverbeet and mushroom calzone

Ingredients

Pizza dough

  • 30 g olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 220 g warm water
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 tsp dried instant yeast
  • 400 g baker's flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 1 tsp salt

Spinach filling

  • 100 g mozzarella cheese, cut into pieces (2-3 cm)
  • 50 g Parmesan cheese, crust removed and cut into pieces (1-2 cm)
  • 50 g firm feta cheese, cut into pieces (1-2cm)
  • 50 g roasted red peppers, cut into pieces
  • 20 g pitted black olives, cut into slices
  • 3 sprigs fresh basil, leaves only
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 brown onion, cut into halves
  • 300 g fresh silverbeet (approx. 2 bunches), trimmed and cut into pieces
  • 100 g fresh Swiss brown mushrooms, cut into quarters
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp freshly ground black pepper

Preparation

Pizza dough

  1. Lightly grease a large bowl with oil and set aside.
  2. Place water, sugar and yeast into mixing bowl and mix 20 sec/speed 2.
  3. Add flour, oil and salt and knead 2 min/. Transfer dough into prepared bowl and shape into a ball. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and leave to prove in a warm place until doubled in size (approx. 1 hour). Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Spinach filling

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C. Line a baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place mozzarella into mixing bowl and grate 6 sec/speed 5. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place Parmesan cheese into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer into bowl with mozzarella and set aside.
  4. Place bowl containing cheeses onto mixing bowl lid and weigh feta, roasted peppers and olives into it. Remove bowl, add basil and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  5. Place oil, garlic and onion into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7.
  6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and sauté 5 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  7. Add silverbeet, mushrooms, salt and pepper and cook 5 min/Varoma//speed , mixing with aid of spatula. Transfer into simmering basket and set aside over a bowl to drain excess liquid. Allow to cool to room temperature (approx. 10 min), then press down to remove any remaining excess liquid from silverbeet mixture.
  8. Transfer silverbeet mixture to reserved cheese mixture and stir to combine.
  9. Transfer dough onto a silicone bread mat or floured work surface. Divide dough into 6 portions. Roll out each dough portion into a 20 cm circle. Spread one-sixth of the silverbeet mixture over half of each dough circle, leaving a 2 cm border. Carefully fold remaining half of dough over filling to enclose. Using your fingers or a fork, crimp edges to seal calzones.
  10. Place calzones onto prepared baking tray and bake for 15 minutes (190°C) until golden. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

large bowl, plastic wrap, baking tray (30 x 40 cm), baking paper, bowl, silicone bread mat, rolling pin, oven
