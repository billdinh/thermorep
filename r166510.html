Roasted Cauliflower and Kale Soup

Ingredients

  • 20 oz cauliflower florets (approx. 1 med. head)
  • 8 oz fennel bulb, cut into pieces (approx. 1 med. bulb)
  • 4 oz yellow onions, cut into pieces (approx. ½ med. onion)
  • 2 oz carrots, cut into pieces (approx. 1 med. carrot)
  • 2 oz celery, cut into pieces (approx. 1 med. stalk)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 4 tbsp olive oil, divided
  • 1 - 2 tsp ground cumin, to taste
  • 10 sprigs fresh lemon thyme, leaves and tender stems
  • 32 oz vegetable broth
  • 3 oz kale, stems removed (approx. ½ bunch)
  • 1 oz lemon juice, to taste
  • 1 tsp salt, to taste
  • ½ tsp fresh ground black pepper
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
690 kJ / 165 kcal
Protein
6 g
Carbohydrates
14 g

11 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Place a large bowl on top of mixing bowl lid and weigh in cauliflower, fennel, onions, carrots, celery and garlic with 3 tbsp oil and cumin, toss the vegetables and transfer onto baking sheet and roast (425ºF) until vegetables turn golden or crispy (approx. 30 min.).
  2. Place roasted vegetables, lemon thyme and 1 tbsp oil into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of bowl with spatula.
  3. Add vegetable broth and cook 10 min/212°F/speed 1.
  4. Add kale, lemon juice, salt and pepper, stir through with spatula and cook 2 min/212°F/speed 1, then increasing speed gradually blend 1 min/speed 9 (watching carefully as Thermomix® will bounce). Serve hot.

Useful Items

baking sheet, large bowl, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Garnish soup with chopped raw kale, a drizzle of cream, toasted pumpkin seeds or your favorite roasted nuts.
  • Kale is an excellent source of vitamin K, vitamin A, vitamin C, fiber, carotenoids and contains 45 different flavonoids with a variety of antioxidant and anti-inflammatory effects.
  • Cauliflower is an excellent source of vitamin C, vitamin K, folate, pantothenic acid, vitamin B6 and is a good source of choline, dietary fiber, omega-3 fatty acids, manganese, phosphorus, and biotin.
