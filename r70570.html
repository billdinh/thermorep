Pad Thai noodles with prawns

Ingredients

  • 20 g olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 200 g flat rice stick noodles
  • boiling water, for soaking
  • 80 g roasted unsalted peanuts, for garnishing
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 red chilli, deseeded if preferred
  • 6 sprigs fresh coriander, roots and stems (reserve leaves for garnishing)
  • 1 ½ tbsp fish sauce
  • 40 g lime juice (approx. 2-3 limes)
  • 1 tbsp tamarind paste
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • ¼ tsp shrimp paste
  • 3 tsp palm sugar (approx. 1 cm cube), grated
  • 2 eggs
  • 500 g water
  • 350 g raw prawns, peeled
  • 70 g bean shoots, for garnishing
  • 10 fresh chives, finely chopped, for garnishing
  • 3 tbsp fried shallots, for garnishing
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges, to serve

Preparation

  1. Line both Varoma dish and tray with a piece of wet, well-wrung baking paper. Lightly grease the baking paper on Varoma tray and set aside.
  2. Soak rice stick noodles in boiling water as per packet instructions. Drain noodles and place into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl to keep warm.
  3. Place peanuts into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 4. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Rinse and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Place garlic, chilli and coriander roots and stems into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add 20 g olive oil and cook 1 min/100°C/speed 1. Transfer half of the garlic-herb mixture into Varoma dish and set aside. Retain other half in mixing bowl.
  6. Add fish sauce, lime juice, tamarind paste, soy sauce, shrimp paste and palm sugar and mix 10 sec/speed 5. Transfer sauce into thermal serving bowl or other large bowl with the drained noodles and combine with spatula until noodles are coated. Re-cover noodles and set aside to keep warm.
  7. Without cleaning mixing bowl, place eggs into mixing bowl and beat 3 sec/speed 5.
  8. Transfer egg onto lined Varoma tray, and spread evenly to create a thin layer. Set Varoma tray carefully aside.
  9. Place water into mixing bowl and place Varoma dish with garlic-herb mixture into position. Weigh prawns into Varoma dish, then stir gently stir to combine with spatula.
  10. Carefully insert Varoma tray with egg, then secure Varoma lid and steam 6 min/Varoma/speed 1. Check prawns and transfer any cooked prawns to thermal serving bowl or other large bowl.
  11. Secure Varoma lid, then steam for a further 5-7 min/Varoma/speed 1, or until both egg and prawns are cooked to your liking.
  12. Remove Varoma tray with omelette. Slice omelette into thin strips then add omelette strips and prawns to thermal serving bowl or other large bowl with the noodles and combine.
  13. Top Pad Thai with bean shoots, chives, coriander leaves, fried shallots and reserved chopped peanuts. Serve immediately with lime wedges on the side.

Useful Items

baking paper, thermal serving bowl or other large bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • Vegetarian option: substitute prawns with 350 g mushrooms, sliced in step 8 and remove shrimp paste and fish sauce from recipe. Proceed as per recipe.
  • Shrimp paste, also known as belachan, can be found in Asian supermarkets.
