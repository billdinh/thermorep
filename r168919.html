Dutch Baby Pancake

Ingredients

  • 1 oz unsalted butter
  • 1 oz sugar
  • ½ oz lemon peel, thin peel only, no pith (approx. 1 lemon)
  • 3 large eggs
  • 4 oz whole milk
  • 2 oz all-purpose flour
  • ½ tsp salt
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
841 kJ / 201 kcal
Protein
7 g
Carbohydrates
20 g
Fat
11 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F.
  2. Place butter into frying pan and place into preheated oven until butter has melted (approx. 5 min.).
  3. Place sugar and lemon peel into mixing bowl and zest 30 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add eggs and milk and mix 2 sec/speed 5.
  5. Add flour and salt and mix 5 sec/speed 5.
  6. Carefully swirl melted butter in pan, using caution with the hot handle. Pour all batter into pan. Bake for 12-15 minutes (450°F) or until puffed in the middle and browned around the edges. Serve hot.

Useful Items

cast iron frying pan (10 in.), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with a squeeze of lemon or fresh mixed berries dusted with confectioner's sugar.
  • Dutch Baby Pancake will rise in the oven and then fall, much like a popover, this is normal. The consistency is that of a thicker crepe, not a fluffy buttermilk type of pancake.
