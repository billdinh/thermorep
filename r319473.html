Almond Ice Cream (No Added Sugar)

Ingredients

  • 140 g blanched almonds
  • 350 g milk
  • 150 g double cream
  • 1 tsp cornflour
  • 1 tsp almond extract
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 Tbsp honey
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1029 kJ / 248 kcal
Protein
6.1 g
Carbohydrates
8 g
Fat
20.6 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in almonds. Transfer to a baking tray and roast for 10 minutes (180°C), stirring half way through, then leave to cool (approx. 20 minutes).
  3. Place roasted almonds in mixing bowl and grind 30 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add milk, cream, cornflour, almond extract, vanilla extract and honey then cook 7 min/70°C/speed 4. Transfer to a shallow freezer-proof container and leave mixture to cool completely before covering and freezing for 10 hours or overnight.
  5. Before serving, place frozen mixture in fridge for 15 minutes. Once mixture has softened, cut into 4 cm cubes with a sharp knife. Place half ice cream cubes in mixing bowl and crush 15 sec/speed 6 then churn 10 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  6. Place remaining half ice cream cubes in mixing bowl and crush 15 sec/speed 6 then churn 10 sec/speed 4. Add to bowl with ice cream. Serve immediately or return to freezer until needed.

Useful Items

baking tray, freezer-proof container, oven, freezer, refrigerator
