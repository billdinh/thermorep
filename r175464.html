Roasted Vegetable Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 oz olive oil, plus extra to grease
  • 15 oz bread flour
  • 2 ½ oz whole wheat flour
  • 9 oz warm water (100°F-110°F)
  • ½ oz sugar
  • 2 ¼ tsp rapid rise yeast
  • 2 tsp salt

Topping

  • 12 oz eggplant, cut into ¼ in. rounds (approx. 1 lg. eggplant)
  • 8 oz red onion, cut into 2 in. strips (approx. 1 lg. onion)
  • 1 garlic clove, halved
  • 6 oz green bell pepper, cut into 2 in. pieces (approx. 1 lg. green pepper)
  • 6 oz red pepper, cut into 2 in. strips
  • olive oil, to drizzle
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • ½ tsp salt
  • &frac18; tsp fresh ground black pepper
  • 10 oz Fontina cheese, cut into 1 in. pieces

For Pizza

  • 2 tbsp olive oil, plus extra to grease pan
  • bread flour, to dust hands
  • 2 tbsp capers (optional)

Preparation

  1. Lightly grease a large bowl and set aside.
  2. Place bread flour, whole wheat flour, water, sugar, yeast, 1 oz olive oil and salt into mixing bowl and knead 2 min/.
  3. Form dough into a ball and transfer into prepared bowl. Cover with cling wrap and let dough rise until doubled in size (approx. 1 hr.).

Topping

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Place eggplant, onion, garlic, red and green pepper on prepared baking sheet, drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with oregano, salt and pepper. Roast vegetables for 25 minutes (400°F), turning after 10 minutes. Set aside to cool.
  2. Place 5 oz cheese into mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place remaining 5 oz cheese into mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 8. Transfer to bowl with reserved grated cheese and set aside.

For Pizza

  1. Increase oven temperature to 500°F. Grease two pizza pans with olive oil.
  2. Divide dough in half and place onto prepared pans. Stretch each dough out to pan edges, with lightly floured fingers. Cover with clean kitchen towels and let rise 5 minutes.
  3. Spread ½ roasted garlic clove over one pizza base and brush with1 tbsp of olive oil. Distribute half the roasted vegetables evenly on pizza and sprinkle with half the reserved grated cheese and 1 tbsp capers. Bake for 10-12 minutes (500°F). Repeat with remaining dough. Serve warm.

Useful Items

parchment paper, baking sheet, cling wrap, pizza pans (14 in.), kitchen towels, large bowl, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Eggplant can be steamed in the Varoma if you halve the recipe and only do one pizza. Add sliced eggplant to Varoma dish and tray and steam 8 min /250°F// speed 1 while cooking the onions and green pepper. The eggplant will still be firm and not as tender in this version. If using this version you can also add red bell pepper when adding green bell pepper, this will give a chopped version of both peppers or alternatively replace fresh bell pepper with canned or jar of drained roasted red pepper strips.
  • Add 1 tsp Italian herbs to pizza dough when adding salt.
