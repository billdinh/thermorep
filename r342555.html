Potato Base Pizza

Ingredients

Dough

  • olive oil, for greasing
  • 50 g Parmesan cheese, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 300 g potatoes, cut in pieces (3 cm)
  • 10 g cornflour (2 Tbsp)
  • 50 g cream
  • 200 g plain flour
  • 5 medium eggs
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper

Filling and Assembly

  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 100 g chorizo, sliced
  • 50 g olive oil
  • 50 g tomato purée
  • 50 g water
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • ½ vegetable stock cube (for 0.5 l)
  • 200 g frozen spinach, thawed, drained
  • 200 g tomatoes, sliced (1 cm)
  • ¼ tsp ground nutmeg (optional)
  • 200 g Gouda, diced (2 cm)

Preparation

Dough

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C and grease a springform tin (Ø 28 cm).
  2. Place Parmesan in mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 10.
  3. Add potatoes, cornflour, cream, flour, eggs, salt and pepper, then with aid of spatula, chop 6 sec/speed 5.
  4. Transfer dough to prepared tin and prebake base for 20 minutes (200°C). Meanwhile, rinse mixing bowl and continue with the recipe.

Filling and Assembly

  1. Place onion and chorizo in mixing bowl then chop 8 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add oil and sauté 4 min/120°C/speed 1.
  3. Add tomato purée, water, pepper and stock cube, cook 4 min/120°C/speed 1 then blend 20 sec/speed 8.
  4. Spread tomato and chorizo ​​sauce over potato base. Cover with spinach and slices of tomato, season with nutmeg (if using), sprinkle with Gouda and bake for 20 minutes (200°C), covering with baking paper if necessary after 10 minutes.
  5. Cut pizza in 12 slices and serve hot or cold.

Useful Items

springform tin (Ø 28 cm), baking paper, oven
