Cannelloni

Ingredients

  • 100 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 200 g onion, chopped into segments
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 150 g chopped tomatoes, canned or fresh
  • 200 g chicken breast, diced
  • 200 g beef, diced
  • 200 g pork meat, lean, sinew free, diced
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 pinch pepper, ground
  • 1 bay leaf, dried
  • 30 g cognac or brandy
  • 50 g flour
  • 200 g milk
  • 24 - 30 dried cannelloni tubes

Béchamel sauce

  • 60 g butter
  • 60 g flour
  • 750 g milk
  • 1 ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper, ground
  • 3 pinches nutmeg, ground

To brown

  • 50 g grated cheese

Preparation

  1. Place olive oil, onion and garlic in the mixing bowl and sauté 8 min/120°C/speed 1.
  2. Add tomatoes and sauté 2 min/120°C/speed 1.
  3. Add chicken breasts, beef, pork, salt, pepper, bay leaf and cognac and cook 28 min/120°C/speed 1.
  4. Add flour and blend 1 min/100°C/speed 2.
  5. Remove bay leaf and chop 8 sec/speed 5.
  6. Add milk and blend 5 min/100°C/speed 2.
  7. Pour the contents into a piping bag – to fill the cannelloni tubes more easily – and let the mixture cool. Boil dried cannelloni in a pot. Drain the tubes well, place them on a tea towel and set aside. Once cold, fill the tubes with the mixture, place the tubes into a gratin dish and set aside.
  8. Pre-heat oven/grill to 220ºC.

Béchamel sauce

  1. Place butter into mixing bowl and heat 4 min/100°C/speed 1.
  2. Add flour and sauté 5 min/120°C/speed 3.
  3. Add milk, salt, pepper and nutmeg and blend 8 min/90°C/speed 4.

To brown

  1. Pour the béchamel over the cannelloni tubes, sprinkle with grated cheese and grill 5 minutes (220ºC) or until golden brown. Serve hot.

Useful Items

gratin dish (40 cm x 25 cm x 6 cm), tea towel, piping bag and Ø 10 mm nozzle, oven
