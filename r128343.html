Vegetable and hazelnut cheese muffins

Ingredients

  • 100 g sunflower oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 60 g medium-hard cheese (e.g. cheddar, gruyère etc.), cut into pieces (1,5 cm)
  • 100 g carrots, cut into pieces (2.5 cm)
  • 70 g hazelnuts, skin on
  • 180 g milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 250 g plain flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 ½ tsp fine salt
  • ½ tsp ground cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 250 g frozen green peas, thawed

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Grease or line a 12 hole muffin pan with paper baking cups and set aside.
  2. Place cheese into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8.
  3. Add carrots and hazelnuts and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add oil, milk and eggs and mix 8 sec/speed 3.
  5. Add flour, baking powder, salt and cayenne pepper and mix 10 sec/speed 5.
  6. Add peas and mix 10 sec//speed 3 with aid of spatula. Transfer to prepared muffin pan, fill baking cups to nearly full and bake for 20-25 minutes (180°C). Serve warm or at room temperature.

Useful Items

paper muffin cases, 12-hole muffin tin, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Vegetable cheese muffins with pancetta:
    Reduce salt to 1 tsp in step 5, then add 100 g pancetta or ham pieces with peas in step 6 and mix 10 seconds longer.
