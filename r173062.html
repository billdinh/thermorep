Thin Crust Pizza Dough

Ingredients

  • olive oil, to grease
  • 15 oz warm water (100°F-110°F)
  • 16 oz bread flour
  • 6 oz fine semolina
  • 2 ¼ tsp rapid rise yeast
  • 1 tbsp salt
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
2385 kJ / 570 kcal
Protein
20 g
Carbohydrates
114 g
Fat
3 g

Preparation

  1. Lightly grease a large bowl and set aside.
  2. Place water, flour, semolina, yeast and salt into mixing bowl and knead 2 min/. Form dough into a ball and transfer into prepared bowl. Cover with cling wrap and let dough rise until doubled in size (approx. 1 hr.). Use as needed.

Useful Items

cling wrap, large bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • To test for proofing: put a small ball of dough in a glass of water and when it rises to the top the dough has finished proofing. Use pizza dough as required by recipe.
  • Makes two 14 inch, three 12 inch or four individual 9 inch round pizzas.
    To shape pizza, place dough on oiled pizza pan or a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Press it out from the center to the edges with your fingertips, and gently stretch into shape. Let rest 5 minutes. Add favorite sauce and toppings and bake 10-15 minutes (500°F) until crispy and golden.
