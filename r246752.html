Pork meatballs with couscous

Ingredients

Meatballs

  • 6 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • ½ lemon, zest only, no white pith
  • ½ tsp dried chilli flakes (optional)
  • 2 tsp dried oregano
  • 80 g Parmesan cheese, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 60 g bread of choice, cut into pieces
  • 4 slices prosciutto, cut into pieces
  • ½ brown onion, cut into halves
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 500 g pork mince
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • olive oil, for greasing

Tomato sauce

  • 100 g tasty cheese, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 50 - 80 g brown onion, cut into halves
  • 30 g olive oil, plus extra oil for greasing
  • 800 g canned crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper

Herbed couscous

  • 400 g couscous
  • 400 g boiling water
  • ½ lemon, juice only
  • 1 tbsp salted butter

Preparation

Meatballs

  1. Place parsley, lemon zest, chilli flakes (optional) and oregano into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place Parmesan, bread and prosciutto into mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a separate bowl and set aside.
  3. Place onion and garlic into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7.
  4. Add mince, egg, salt, pepper, reserved prosciutto mixture and 1½ tablespoons of the reserved herb mixture and combine 10 sec/speed 5.
  5. Lightly grease Varoma dish. With oiled hands, shape mixture into 6 big meatballs and place into prepared Varoma dish. Set Varoma aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Tomato sauce

  1. Place cheese into mixing bowl and grate 4 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place garlic, onion and oil into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5, then sauté 3 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Add tomatoes, salt and pepper. Place Varoma with meatballs into position, secure Varoma lid and cook 20 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  4. Preheat oven to 180°C. Lightly grease a baking dish (23 x 21 cm).
  5. Transfer meatballs into prepared baking dish and pour over sauce. Top with reserved grated cheese and bake for 15 minutes (180°C) or until golden. Meanwhile, continue with recipe.

Herbed couscous

  1. Place a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl onto mixing bowl lid and carefully weigh couscous and boiling water into it. Set bowl aside, add lemon juice and remaining herb mixture, then stir to combine. Cover for 10 minutes to allow water to absorb.
  2. Add butter and stir until butter melts and is mixed through. Serve meatballs on top of herbed couscous.

Useful Items

bowl, baking dish, thermal serving bowl or other large bowl, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with salad or steamed vegetables, if desired.
