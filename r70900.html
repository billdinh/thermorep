Spelt bread rolls

Ingredients

  • 375 g water
  • 3 tsp dried instant yeast or 30 g fresh yeast
  • 1 - 1 ½ tsp sea salt
  • 300 g spelt flour
  • 450 g baker's flour
  • 45 g extra virgin olive oil
  • sesame seeds, for sprinkling

Preparation

  1. Line a baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place water and yeast into mixing bowl and warm 2 min/37°C/speed 1.
  3. Add all remaining ingredients, except sesame seeds, and mix 6 sec/speed 6, then knead 2 min/.
  4. Transfer dough onto a silicone bread mat or floured work surface and work into a ball. Wrap in silicone bread mat, or place into a ceramic or glass bowl and cover with a kitchen towel. Leave to prove in a warm place until doubled in size (approx. 30 minutes).
  5. Knock down dough and roll into a sausage, then cut into 20 equal-sized pieces. Shape each piece into a roll and place onto prepared baking tray, leaving enough room between each roll to allow for proving.
  6. Using wet hands, flatten rolls slightly and sprinkle with sesame seeds. Preheat oven to 200°C. Leave buns to prove again in a warm place for a further 30 minutes.
  7. Bake for 15-20 minutes (200°C), or until golden brown. Serve warm or allow to cool completely before placing into a sealable container to store.

Useful Items

baking paper, sealable container, kitchen towel, ceramic or glass bowl, baking tray (30 x 40 cm), silicone bread mat, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Use other seeds of choice to sprinkle over the rolls before baking (e.g. poppy seeds, pepitas).
  • If you have leftover rolls, you can store them in the freezer.
