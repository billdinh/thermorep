Mixed Nut Cereal Bars

Ingredients

  • 100 g whole mixed nuts (e.g. hazelnuts, almonds, walnuts)
  • 250 g muesli
  • 220 g dark chocolate, small pieces or callets
  • 100 g honey
  • 130 g unsalted butter, diced, plus extra for greasing
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
1109 kJ / 265 kcal

Preparation

  1. Grease and line a baking tray (20 cm x 30 cm).
  2. Place nuts in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a large bowl and set aside.
  3. Place muesli in mixing bowl and chop 3 min/speed 3. Transfer to bowl with nuts.
  4. Place 70 g chocolate in mixing bowl and grate 5-10 sec/speed 5, until no large pieces of chocolate remain. Transfer to bowl with muesli and nuts.
  5. Place honey and 80 g butter in mixing bowl then melt 5 min/50°C/speed 3. Pour into bowl with other ingredients and mix well with spatula.
  6. Tip mixture out onto lined baking tray and level surface with a spatula, to a height of approx. 1 cm. Chill in fridge for 1 hour. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl.
  7. Place remaining 150 g dark chocolate and 50 g butter in mixing bowl then melt 5 min/50°C/speed 3. If mixture is not completely melted, repeat 5 min/50°C/speed 3. Pour chocolate over nut and muesli mixture and refrigerate for a further 1 hour.
  8. Once set, cut into bars of desired size and serve or store in an airtight container.

Useful Items

long sharp knife, large bowl, airtight container, baking paper, baking tray (20 cm x 30 cm), refrigerator
