Sunflower and nori filling

Ingredients

Soaking

  • 100 g sunflower seeds (see Tips)
  • 50 g raw almonds
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • filtered water, for soaking

Sandwich filling

  • 1 celery stalk, cut into pieces
  • 1 nori sheet, cut into pieces
  • 1 tbsp pickled capers
  • 60 g dill pickled cucumbers
  • 1 tsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 3 - 4 tbsp vegan mayonnaise (see Tips)
  • 2 sprigs fresh dill, leaves only
  • 2 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only

Preparation

Soaking

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh sunflower seeds and almonds into it. Add salt, cover with water and set aside to soak for 3-6 hours.
  2. Using simmering basket, drain and rinse soaked seeds and almonds.

Sandwich filling

  1. Place celery and nori sheet into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5.
  2. Add capers, pickled cucumbers, vinegar, lemon juice, mayonnaise, dill, parsley and drained seeds and almonds, then blend 8 sec/speed 10.
  3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and chop 8 sec/speed 10, until a chunky paste. Serve as desired or transfer into a sealable container and place into refrigerator until ready to use (see Tips).

Useful Items

bowl, sealable container, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • To make your own vegan mayonnaise, please refer to the recipe on this recipe chip.
  • If you don't have time to soak/activate your seeds and nuts, soak in boiling water for 30 minutes, then drain and use as directed.
  • You can serve this filling in sandwiches, in wraps or pitas, as a topping for baked potatoes or as a dip with crudités. We served ours with Vegie crackers, please see recipe on this recipe chip.
  • You can store this filling in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.
