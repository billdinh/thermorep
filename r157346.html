Lentil Loaf

Ingredients

  • 5 oz dried green lentils
  • 32 oz water, divided
  • 2 oz fine sea salt, plus ¼ tsp, to taste, divided
  • 5 oz cremini mushrooms, halved
  • 3 oz yellow onions, quartered (approx. ½ medium onion)
  • 2 oz carrots, cut into pieces (approx. 1 carrot)
  • 2 oz celery, cut into pieces (approx. 2 stalks)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 oz olive oil
  • 1 oz bread crumbs
  • 3 oz old fashioned rolled oats
  • 1 oz brown rice flour
  • 1 oz sunflower seeds
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 ½ tsp dried oregano
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1 pinch dried red chili flakes
  • 1 heaping tsp vegetable stock paste
  • 4 ½ oz ketchup
  • 1 oz balsamic vinegar
  • ½ oz maple syrup

Preparation

  1. Place a large bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in lentils. Add 20 oz water and 2 oz salt and set aside to soak 4 hours or overnight. Drain soaked lentils through a fine mesh sieve. Rinse under cold running water and set aside to drain.
  2. Place mushrooms, onions, carrots, celery, garlic and olive oil into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and sauté 5 min/212°F//speed 1.
  3. Add bread crumbs, oats, flour and sunflower seeds and mix 5 sec/speed 3.
  4. Add reserved lentils, tomato paste, Worcestershire sauce, oregano, ¼ tsp salt, black pepper, chili flakes, stock paste and 12 oz water and without measuring cup, cook 20 min/212°F//speed 1.
  5. Preheat oven to 375°F. Line a loaf pan (approx. 9 in. x 5 in.) with parchment paper and set aside.
  6. Transfer lentil mixture into prepared loaf pan. Fold over flaps of parchment paper or use your hands to press down and compress the loaf. Bake 35 minutes (375°F). Clean mixing bowl.
  7. Place ketchup, vinegar and maple syrup into mixing bowl and mix 30 sec/speed 9. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  8. After the first 35 minutes of baking, pour half the reserved sauce over Lentil Loaf and spread evenly. Bake a further 20 minutes (375°F). Allow the loaf to rest for 5-10 minutes and serve hot with additional sauce.

Useful Items

large bowl, loaf pan (approx. 9 in. x 5 in.), parchment paper, fine mesh sieve, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Vegetable Stock Paste recipe can be found in the US Basic Cookbook.
  • For more flavor, water can be replaced with vegetable stock.
  • To make your own brown rice flour: place rice into mixing bowl grind 20 sec/speed 10.
  • To make your own bread crumbs: place bread into mixing bowl and grind 6 sec/speed 8.
