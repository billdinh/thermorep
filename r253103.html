Mushroom and sun-dried tomato fettuccine

Ingredients

  • 60 g Parmesan cheese, cut into pieces
  • 20 fresh basil leaves
  • 1200 g water
  • 1 tsp Vegetable stock paste (see Tip)
  • 350 g fettuccine
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 55 g olive oil
  • 500 g fresh button mushrooms, cut into quarters
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 80 g semi sun-dried tomatoes, cut into pieces
  • 120 g pouring (whipping) cream

Preparation

  1. Place Parmesan into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer into a small bowl and set aside. Rinse mixing bowl.
  2. Place basil into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a separate small bowl and set aside.
  3. Place water and stock paste into mixing bowl and heat 10 min/100°C/speed 1.
  4. Add fettuccine through hole in mixing bowl lid and cook as per packet instructions/100°C//speed 1, without measuring cup. Drain pasta using Varoma dish and transfer into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl. Add 1 tablespoon of the oil to pasta, stir to combine, then cover and set aside to keep warm. Rinse mixing bowl.
  5. Place garlic and remaining 35 g of the oil into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8.
  6. Add mushrooms, salt and tomatoes and sauté 8 min/Varoma/speed  .
  7. Add cream and cook 3 min/90°C/speed .
  8. Add reserved chopped basil and 40 g of the grated Parmesan to mixing bowl and stir with aid of spatula to combine. Divide pasta between serving bowls, top with mushroom sauce and sprinkle with remaining Parmesan cheese to serve.
