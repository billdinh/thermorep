Pork with Black Bean Sauce

Ingredients

Marinade

  • 80 g ginger wine
  • 500 - 600 g pork fillet, trimmed and cut in strips (1 x 10 cm)
  • 2 Tbsp hoi sin sauce
  • 1 tsp olive oil

Black bean sauce

  • ½ - 1 long red chilli, deseeded if preferred (optional)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 400 g tinned black beans
  • 60 g soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 Tbsp Shaoxing rice wine
  • 80 g water
  • 1 tsp palm sugar (1 cm cube), grated

Rice and vegetables

  • 300 g white rice
  • 900 g water
  • 1 bunch tenderstem broccoli (approx. 180 g), trimmed and cut in thirds
  • 230 g tinned bamboo shoots, rinsed and drained (weight is pre-draining)
  • 1 spring onion, trimmed and thinly sliced, for garnishing
  • 1 - 2 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only, for garnishing

Preparation

Marinade

  1. Place a ceramic or glass bowl on mixing bowl lid, then weigh ginger wine and pork into it. Add hoi sin sauce and oil to bowl, stir to combine and set aside.

Black bean sauce

  1. Place chilli (optional) and garlic in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Drain black beans through simmering basket, rinse well and allow to drain. Add 150 g of drained black beans and all remaining sauce ingredients and cook 3 min/100°C/speed 5. Transfer to a large serving bowl to keep warm. Rinse and dry mixing bowl.

Rice and vegetables

  1. Insert simmering basket, weigh rice into it, then rinse rice until water runs clear.
  2. Place water into mixing bowl, insert simmering basket with rice and cook 5 min/Varoma/speed 2. Place Varoma into position and place pork and marinade into Varoma dish. Cover with Varoma lid and cook 8 min/Varoma/speed 2. Set Varoma aside. Stir rice in simmering basket with spatula, then stir pork in Varoma dish to ensure even cooking. Place Varoma dish back into position.
  3. Insert Varoma tray and place broccoli, bamboo shoots and remaining drained black beans onto it. Cover with Varoma lid and cook 4-6 min/Varoma/speed 2, or until all pork is cooked.
  4. Combine pork with black bean sauce, vegetables and black beans. Serve pork over rice, garnished with spring onion and coriander.
