Aromatic chicken pilaf

Ingredients

  • 400 g basmati rice
  • 10 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 6 garlic cloves
  • 1 brown onion (approx. 150 g), peeled and cut into halves
  • 50 g olive oil
  • 2 cinnamon quills
  • 10 cardamom pods, crushed
  • 2 dried bay leaves
  • 1 tbsp ground coriander
  • ½ tsp ground cloves
  • 2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp ground nutmeg
  • ¼ - ½ tsp chilli powder, to taste
  • 500 g boneless skinless chicken thighs, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 400 g canned chopped tomatoes
  • 100 g dry white wine
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 800 g water
  • 3 fresh curry leaves
  • 350 g broad beans (fresh or frozen), unpodded
  • 5 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only, for garnishing

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Insert simmering basket, then weigh rice into it. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and rinse rice until water runs clear. Transfer rice into a casserole dish (28 cm round x 9 cm deep).
  2. Place ginger, garlic, onion and oil into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add cinnamon, cardamom, bay leaves, ground coriander, ground cloves, cumin seeds, nutmeg and chilli powder and sauté 5 min/Varoma//speed 1.
  4. Add chicken and cook 3 min/100°C//speed 1.
  5. Add tomatoes, wine, turmeric, salt, pepper and 500 g of the water and cook 10 min/100°C//speed 1.
  6. Carefully transfer chicken mixture into casserole dish. Add curry leaves and stir well to combine. Cover with a lid or aluminium foil and bake for 25 minutes (180°C). Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  7. Place remaining 300 g water into mixing bowl, insert simmering basket, then weigh broad beans into it and cook 10 min/Varoma/speed 2. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and set beans aside to drain and cool slightly. When cool enough to handle, peel pods and set podded beans aside. Discard pods.
  8. Remove casserole dish from oven and add reserved broad beans. Gently stir to combine and scrape rice from edges of casserole dish. Stand, covered, for 15 minutes, fluffing rice every 5 minutes to ensure even absorption. Remove cinnamon quills and whole spices, if desired. Garnish with coriander leaves before serving.

Useful Items

casserole dish (28 cm round x 9 cm deep), aluminium foil, oven

Hints & Tricks

Aromatic chicken pilaf with octopus: Try adding 250 g octopus tentacles (2-3 cm pieces) in step 9 when adding broad beans. The residual heat from the pilaf will gently cook the octopus.
