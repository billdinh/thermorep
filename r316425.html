Spicy Cheese Straws

Ingredients

  • 14 oz sharp cheddar cheese, cut into pieces, divided
  • 6 ½ oz all-purpose flour, plus extra to dust
  • 4 oz unsalted butter, cut into pieces
  • ¼ tsp ground mustard powder
  • ¼ tsp sea salt
  • &frac18; tsp sugar
  • 3 tsp cayenne pepper, to taste
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
234 kJ / 56 kcal
Protein
2 g
Carbohydrates
3 g
Fat
4 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F and line two baking sheets with parchment.
  2. Place 7 oz cheese into mixing bowl and grate 7 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Place remaining 7 oz cheese into mixing bowl and grate 7 sec/speed 7.
  3. Add reserved grated cheese, flour, butter, mustard, salt, sugar and cayenne and mix 14 sec/speed 5.
  4. Transfer mixture to a floured work surface and roll dough into a rectangle (14 in. x 12 in. x ¼ in.), cut dough in half horizontally and then into strips (½ in. wide).
  5. Twist each strip, transfer to prepared baking sheets and bake 10-12 minutes (350°F) until golden. Set aside and allow to cool completely.
  6. Once completely cool, store in an airtight container until ready to serve.

Useful Items

baking sheets, parchment paper, airtight container, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Scoop dough into a cookie press fitted with a flat ridged tip. Pipe the dough in 4 inch strips onto prepared baking sheet and bake 10-12 minutes (350°F) until golden.
