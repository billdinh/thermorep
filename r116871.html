Lamb Koftas

Ingredients

  • 15 g fresh coriander leaves
  • 140 g onions, quartered
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1000 g lamb mince, broken in pieces
  • 40 g tomato purée
  • 1 Tbsp raw honey
  • ½ tsp dried mint
  • 3 tsp harissa paste
  • 2 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
907 kJ / 217 kcal
Protein
14.5 g
Carbohydrates
2.8 g
Fat
16.4 g

Preparation

  1. Place coriander in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8.
  2. Add onions and garlic then chop 2 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then chop again 2 sec/speed 8.
  3. Add lamb mince, tomato purée, honey, mint, harissa paste, salt and pepper then mix 2 min//speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatulal then mix again 1 min//speed 4.
  4. Shape mixture into 12 kofta shapes (like long-sausages) around 12 metal skewers and place in fridge for 30 minutes.
  5. Towards end of this time, preheat grill to 190°C (medium-hot) then grill for 12-15 minutes until cooked through, turning occasionally to ensure even cooking. Serve hot with your choice of roasted vegetables.

Useful Items

metal skewers, refrigerator, grill

Hints & Tricks

  • Recipe developed by Jenny Tschiesche BSc(Hons) Dip(ION) FdSc BANT. For more information see www.jennytschiesche.com.

  • Wooden skewers can also be used. Soak them in warm water for at least 30 minutes before beginning recipe to prevent them from burning during cooking.
