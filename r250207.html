Thai red pork curry

Ingredients

  • 1 brown onion, cut into halves
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 3 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 4 fresh Kaffir lime leaves
  • 3 - 4 tbsp Thai red curry paste (see Tips)
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 400 g coconut cream
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter (see Tips)
  • 400 g sweet potato, peeled and cut into cubes (2.5 cm)
  • 500 - 600 g pork loin, cut into cubes (3 cm) and fat trimmed
  • 200 g frozen green beans, cut into pieces (2-3 cm)
  • fresh coriander, leaves only, for garnishing
  • fresh red chilli, cut into thin slices, for garnishing (optional)
  • cooked rice, to serve

Preparation

  1. Place onion, garlic, ginger, Kaffir lime leaves and curry paste into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Mixture should be a paste consistency. If needed, chop for a further 10 sec/speed 9.
  2. Add fish sauce, coconut cream, peanut butter and sweet potato and cook 6 min/100°C//speed 1.
  3. Add pork and cook 8 min/90°C//speed 1.
  4. Add green beans and cook for 5-8 min/80°C//speed 1 or until pork and potato are cooked. Transfer into a serving bowl. Garnish with coriander and chilli (optional) and serve with rice.

Hints & Tricks

  • To make your own Thai red curry paste and peanut butter, please refer to the recipes in The Basic Cookbook or the Everyday Cookbook.
  • If using store-bought Thai red curry paste, adjust amount of curry paste used based on label recommendations and personal preference.
