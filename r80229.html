Lemon cupcakes with citrus syrup

Ingredients

Cupcakes

  • 60 g white sugar
  • 1 lemon, zest only, no white pith
  • 130 g butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 170 g self-raising flour
  • 180 g coconut milk
  • 1000 g water

Citrus dessert syrup

  • 170 g white sugar
  • 15 g water
  • 60 g lemon juice (approx. 2 lemons)
  • desiccated coconut, toasted or untoasted, for garnishing

Preparation

Cupcakes

  1. Place 12 silicone cupcake moulds into Varoma dish and onto Varoma tray, then set Varoma aside.
  2. Place sugar and lemon zest into mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 9.
  3. Add butter and mix 8 sec/speed 5.
  4. Add eggs, egg yolk, flour and coconut milk and mix 8 sec/speed 5.
  5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then mix a further 4 sec/speed 5. Fill cupcake moulds half-way, then set Varoma aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  6. Place water into mixing bowl. Set Varoma with cupcakes into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 22 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  7. Remove cupcakes and allow to cool. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Citrus dessert syrup

  1. Place all syrup ingredients, except desiccated coconut, into mixing bowl and cook 3 min/90°C/speed 2. Pour hot syrup over puddings, garnish with coconut and serve.

Useful Items

silicone cupcake moulds

Hints & Tricks

  • Gluten free option: replace self-raising flour with 100 g almonds and 85 g coconut. Place almonds and coconut into mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 7. Add 20 g cornflour in step 4 with the eggs. Proceed as per recipe. Steaming time in step 5 may need to be extended by up to 5 minutes.
  • Jam pudding: Place ½ -1 tsp jam into the bottom of each mould before adding cupcake batter. Proceed as per recipe.
