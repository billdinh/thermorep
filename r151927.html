Mango chutney

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp Sichuan peppercorns
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 10 g fresh root ginger, cut in round slices (2 mm)
  • 80 g onions, cut in halves
  • 20 g water
  • 250 g raw cane sugar
  • 700 g mangoes, flesh only, cut in pieces
  • 120 g vinegar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp ground turmeric
  • 100 g raisins

Preparation

  1. Place Sichuan peppercorns in mixing bowl and toast 6 min/Varoma//speed 1, then grind 25 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place garlic cloves and ginger in mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add onions and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place water and raw cane sugar in mixing bowl and cook 5 min/100°C/speed 1.
  5. Add reserved ground Sichuan pepper, reserved ginger-onion mixture, mangoes, vinegar, salt, turmeric and raisins and cook 45 min/100°C/speed 1, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup on mixing bowl lid to help prevent splashing.
  6. Remove simmering basket, insert measuring cup and mix 10 sec/speed 4. Pour into 2 sterilised jam jars with lids and close immediately. Turn jam jars upside down for 10 minutes before turning right side up. Allow to cool and serve or store in a cool, dry place.

Useful Items

jars with screw-top lids

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with cheese or as a condiment for meat or fish or with an Indian curry meal.
  • After opening, store jar in the fridge.
  • Replace mangoes with apples, pears, plums, apricots or papayas.
