Smoked Trout Dip

Ingredients

  • 1 lime, thin peel only, no pith, plus 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 10 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 3 sprigs fresh dill, leaves only, plus extra to garnish
  • ¼ jalapeño pepper (optional)
  • 5 sprigs fresh tarragon, leaves only
  • 1 avocado, quartered
  • 10 oz cream cheese, room temperature, cut into pieces
  • 10 oz sour cream
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper
  • 10 oz smoked trout, bones removed, cut into pieces
  • cucumbers, sliced, to serve
  • baby carrots, to serve
Nutrition
per 1 portion

20 g

Preparation

  1. Place lime peel, parsley, dill, jalapeño and tarragon into mixing bowl and chop 6 sec/speed 10.
  2. Add lime juice, avocado, cream cheese, sour cream and cayenne and mix 10 sec/speed 6.
  3. Add trout and mix 5 sec/speed 3. Transfer to a serving dish and garnish with dill.
  4. Serve with cucumber slices and baby carrots.

Useful Items

serving dish

Hints & Tricks

  • Replace sour cream with plain, unsweetened yogurt.
  • Serve with toast or fresh baguette.
