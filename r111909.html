Steamed rice balls with spicy lime dipping sauce

Ingredients

Spicy lime dipping sauce

  • 1 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 1 lime, zested and juiced
  • 2 tbsp tamari
  • ½ tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 60 g water
  • 1 spring onion/shallot, trimmed and cut into thin slices
  • 1 fresh red chilli, deseeded, if preferred and cut into thin slices, plus extra for garnishing

Steamed rice balls

  • 150 g jasmine rice
  • 1000 g water, plus extra for soaking
  • 4 spring onions/shallots, trimmed and cut into pieces, plus green tops for garnishing, cut into thin slices
  • 50 g canned water chestnuts
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 3 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 500 g pork mince
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp fish sauce (gluten free)

Preparation

Spicy lime dipping sauce

  1. Place ginger and lime zest into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 8.
  2. Add tamari, sesame oil, honey, water and lime juice and mix 10 sec/speed 5.
  3. Add spring onion/shallot and chilli and combine 5 sec//speed 2. Transfer into a bowl or sealable container and place into refrigerator until ready to serve.

Steamed rice balls

  1. Insert simmering basket into mixing bowl and weigh rice into it. Transfer into a bowl and soak rice in cold water for 30 minutes, then drain well with aid of simmering basket.
  2. Place spring onion/shallots, water chestnuts, garlic and ginger into mixing bowl and chop 7 sec/speed 8.
  3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and chop 7 sec/speed 8.
  4. Add mince, salt and fish sauce and combine 10 sec//speed 3, or until combined.
  5. Roll tablespoonfuls of mixture (approx. 18 balls in total) and coat each ball well in soaked rice. Place rice balls onto Varoma tray. If they don't fit comfortably on Varoma tray, divide balls between Varoma tray and Varoma dish.
  6. Place water into mixing bowl. Insert Varoma tray into Varoma, place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 2, or until rice is tender.
  7. Garnish balls with chillies and spring onion tops then serve warm with Spicy lime dipping sauce.
