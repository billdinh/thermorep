Porcini and Pancetta Risotto - Risotto con porcini e pancetta

Ingredients

  • 30 g dried porcini mushrooms, in small pieces
  • 70 g Parmesan cheese, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 5 g fresh parsley leaves (1 small handful)
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 100 g onions, quartered
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 150 g pancetta, smoked, diced (2 cm)
  • 150 g button mushrooms, sliced
  • 400 g risotto rice
  • 500 g water, boiling, plus extra for soaking
  • 1 vegetable stock cube (for 0.5 l)
  • 50 g unsalted butter, diced

Preparation

  1. Place porcini mushrooms in a bowl, cover with boiling water then leave to soak for 15 minutes. Meanwhile, continue with recipe.
  2. Place Parmesan in mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place parsley in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place garlic, onions and oil in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add pancetta and button mushrooms then sauté 4 min/120°C//speed .
  6. Add rice then cook 2 min/120°C//speed 1. Scrape base of mixing bowl with spatula to loosen rice.
  7. Add reserved porcini mushrooms and soaking liquid, boiling water and stock cube then cook 12 min/100°C//speed 1.
  8. Add butter, reserved chopped parsley and half of the reserved grated Parmesan then stir in gently with spatula. Leave to stand for 2 minutes before serving, with remaining grated Parmesan for sprinkling.
