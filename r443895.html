Pollock with Potato and Squash Crust with Chablis Sauce

Ingredients

Pollock with Potato and Squash Crust

  • 150 g red kuri squash, peeled, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 150 g potatoes, cut in pieces
  • 40 g onions, halved
  • 2 dash lemon juice
  • 1 medium egg
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt, plus extra to taste
  • 4 pollock fillets (approx. 120 g each)
  • ground black pepper, to taste

Chablis Sauce

  • 30 g shallot, halved
  • 30 g spring onions, cut in pieces
  • 2 sprigs fresh dill, leaves only, plus extra for garnishing (optional)
  • 10 g unsalted butter
  • 50 g dry white wine (Chablis)
  • 200 g cream
  • 10 g plain flour
  • 1 vegetable stock cube (for 0.5 l), crumbled
  • fine sea salt, to taste
  • ground black pepper, to taste

Preparation

Pollock with Potato and Squash Crust

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C and line a baking tray with baking paper.
  2. Place squash in mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a damp cheese cloth or tea towel and set aside.
  3. Place potatoes, onions and 1 dash lemon juice in mixing bowl then chop 10 sec/speed 5. Transfer to cheese cloth with squash, squeeze out excess moisture then transfer to a large bowl. Add egg, salt and remaining 1 dash lemon juice then mix gently with spatula.
  4. Season pollock with salt and pepper, top each with 80 g potato mixture and place on prepared tray. Bake for 20 minutes (180°C). Halfway through baking time, clean mixing bowl and make sauce.

Chablis Sauce

  1. Place shallot, spring onions and dill in mixing bowl then chop 10 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add butter and sauté 2 min/120°C/speed 1.
  3. Add wine, cream, flour and vegetable stock, cook 6 min/100°C/speed 1 then blend 10 sec/speed 8. Season to taste and serve with pollock, garnished with extra dill (if using).

Useful Items

cheese cloth, baking tray, baking paper, large bowl, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Substitute red kuri squash with squash of choice, peeled, cut in pieces (2 cm).
