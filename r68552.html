Butter chicken

Ingredients

  • 2 - 3 tbsp cumin seeds
  • 2 - 3 tbsp fennel seeds
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 cm cube fresh ginger, peeled
  • 1 brown onion (approx. 180 g), cut into halves
  • 100 g butter, cut into pieces, plus extra to serve
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 440 g ripe tomatoes, cut into halves
  • 150 g raw cashew nuts
  • 100 g water
  • 750 g skinless chicken thigh fillet, cut into cubes (4 cm)
  • 200 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 1 tsp chilli powder (optional)
  • 1 - 2 tsp garam masala, plus extra to serve
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 pinch salt, to taste

Preparation

  1. Place cumin seeds and fennel seeds into mixing bowl and dry roast 2 min/120°C/speed 1. Allow to cool slightly then  insert measuring cup and mill 1 min/speed 10. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place garlic cloves, fresh ginger and brown onion into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7.  Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add butter and olive oil and sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  4. Add tomatoes, cashew nuts and water into mixing bowl and purée 1 min/speed 8.
  5. Add chicken pieces, pouring (whipping) cream, chilli powder (optional), garam masala, ground turmeric, salt and reserved cumin and fennel mixture and cook 17 min/100°C//speed 1.
  6. Add extra butter and extra garam masala to taste and combine before serving warm.
