Pasta Primavera

Ingredients

Pangritata

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 40 g ciabatta bread, cut into 2 cm pieces (approx. 1 slice)
  • 1 sprig fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 30 g pine nuts

Vegetable pasta

  • 100 g Parmesan cheese, crust removed and cut into pieces (approx. 2 cm)
  • 2000 g water
  • 100 g broccoli, broken into florets
  • 100 g zucchinis, cut into thin slices (approx. 5 mm)
  • 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed and cut into pieces (approx. 3 cm)
  • 50 g sugar snap peas, cut into slices
  • 2 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 1 sprig fresh basil, leaves only, plus extra for garnishing
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 100 g fresh mushrooms, cut into quarters
  • ½ tsp dried chilli flakes
  • 60 g olive oil
  • 250 g cherry tomatoes, cut into halves
  • 1 tsp salt, plus extra to season
  • 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper, plus extra to season
  • 500 g fresh pasta (see Tips)
  • 60 g salted butter
  • 180 g double cream

Preparation

Pangritata

  1. Place garlic into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7.
  2. Add bread, parsley and oil and chop 10 sec/speed 5.
  3. Place a frying pan over medium heat. Place pine nuts and bread mixture to pan and toast for 5 minutes, or until golden brown and crispy. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.

Vegetable pasta

  1. Place Parmesan cheese into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place 1000 g of the water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh broccoli, zucchini and asparagus into it. Secure Varoma lid and steam 10 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Place Varoma tray into position and weigh peas onto it. Secure Varoma lid and steam 2 min/Varoma/speed 1. Transfer cooked vegetables into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and cover to keep warm. Rinse and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Place parsley and basil into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a separate bowl and set aside.
  5. Place garlic into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  6. Add mushrooms, chilli and 40 g of the oil and cook 6 min/100°C//speed , without measuring cup. Transfer into bowl with vegetables.
  7. Place tomatoes, reserved herbs and remaining 20 g oil and cook 2 min/100°C//speed . Transfer into a separate thermal serving bowl or other large bowl, season with salt and pepper and cover to keep warm.
  8. Place remaining 1000 g water into mixing bowl and heat 10 min/100°C/speed 1.
  9. Add salt and pasta into mixing bowl and cook 5 min/100°C//speed 1 or until pasta is al dente. Drain pasta using simmering basket. Transfer into bowl with vegetables. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  10. Place butter, cream and pepper into mixing bowl and heat 3 min/100°C/speed 2.
  11. Add reserved Parmesan and heat 2 min/100°C/speed 2. Add to bowl with pasta and vegetables and toss to combine. Serve pasta and vegetables topped with reserved tomatoes, pangritata and basil leaves.

Useful Items

non-stick frying pan, bowl, thermal serving bowl or other large bowl, stove top
