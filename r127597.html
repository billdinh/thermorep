Baked cheesecake

Ingredients

Pastry

  • 70 g unsalted butter, cut into pieces, plus extra for greasing
  • 40 g sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 100 g plain flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Filling

  • 250 g sugar
  • 1 lemon, zest only, no white pith
  • 100 g unsalted butter, softened
  • 250 g cream cheese, cut into pieces
  • 7 eggs, separated
  • 40 g lemon juice
  • 1 tsp lemon essence
  • 1 tsp natural almond extract
  • ¼ tsp fine salt
  • 1000 g ricotta cheese
  • 70 g cornflour (starch)

Lemon icing

  • 100 g icing sugar
  • 30 g lemon juice
  • fresh blueberries, for garnishing

Preparation

Pastry

  1. Preheat oven to 150°C. Grease and line a baking dish (30 x 24 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place butter, sugar, eggs, flour and baking powder into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then mix for a further 10 sec/speed 4. Transfer pastry mixture into prepared baking dish, press down to form a base and set aside.

Filling

  1. Place sugar and lemon zest into mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then repeat milling 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Place butter and cream cheese into mixing bowl and mix 30 sec/speed 3.5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add reserved lemon sugar and combine 20 sec/speed 3.
  4. Mix 1 min/speed 2, slowly adding egg yolks through hole in mixing bowl lid, until well combined.
  5. Add lemon juice, lemon essence, almond essence and salt and mix 5 sec/speed 3.
  6. Mix 3 min/speed 1, slowly adding ricotta cheese through hole in mixing bowl lid until combined. Remove butterfly whisk.
  7. Add cornflour and blend 20 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a large bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl and butterfly whisk.
  8. Re-insert butterfly whisk. Add egg whites and whip 5 min/37°C/speed 3.5. Remove butterfly whisk.
  9. Transfer half of the egg white mixture into the large bowl with the ricotta mixture and gently fold using the spatula. Transfer remaining egg white mixture into large bowl and fold again using spatula. Pour mixture into baking dish on top of prepared base and bake for 1 hour 30 minutes (150°C). Set aside to cool completely (approx. 1 hour).
  10. Clean and dry mixing bowl and butterfly whisk.

Lemon icing

  1. Re-insert butterfly whisk. Place icing sugar and lemon juice into mixing bowl and mix 30 sec/speed 3.
  2. Drizzle lemon icing over cooled cheesecake before garnishing with fresh blueberries, slicing and serving.

Useful Items

rectangular baking dish (30 cm x 24 cm), baking paper, large bowl, oven
