Pumpkin Spice Jam

Ingredients

  • 1 oz lemon peel, thin peel only, no pith , plus juice (2 oz), (approx. 2 lemons)
  • 1 oz orange peel, thin peel only, no pith, plus juice (2 oz), (approx. 2 orange)
  • ½ oz fresh ginger, peeled
  • 15 oz sugar
  • 1 tbsp pumpkin pie spice or homemade Pumpkin Spice Mix
  • 2 cans pumpkin purée (approx. 15 oz ea.)
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
121 kJ / 29 kcal
Protein
0 g

8 g

0 g

Preparation

  1. Place lemon peel and orange peels, ginger, sugar and pumpkin spice into mixing bowl and grate 20 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and grate a further 20 sec/speed 9.
  2. Add lemon and orange juices and pumpkin and mix 30 sec/speed 3.5. Place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 60 min/240°F/speed 1. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, replace simmering basket onto mixing bowl lid and cook a further 30 min/240°F/speed 1.
  3. Allow to cool before transferring into 4 sterile airtight jars (8 oz) until needed.

Useful Items

airtight jars (8 oz)

Hints & Tricks

  • Fresh ginger can be replaced with 2 tsp ground ginger.
  • Transfer to decorative jars or containers and label for gift-giving.
  • Make the homemade Pumpkin Spice Mix from recipe in Pumpkin and Spice Collection.
