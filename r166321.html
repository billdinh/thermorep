Chicken with rice

Ingredients

Rice and chicken

  • 400 g Egyptian rice
  • 2 tbsp Arabian spice mix
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 2 pinch pepper, freshly ground
  • 1000 g chicken breasts, cut into strips
  • 50 g olive oil, plus extra for drizzling
  • 100 g onions, cut in halves
  • 200 g beef mince
  • 1000 g water
  • ½ tsp ground nutmeg
  • 3 dried bay leaves

Sauce

  • 40 g butter, cut in pieces
  • 60 g flour

Garnish and serving

  • 20 g almonds, roasted
  • 20 g pine nuts, roasted
  • 20 g unsalted pistachio nuts, roasted

Preparation

Rice and chicken

  1. Place simmering basket onto mixing bowl lid. Weigh in rice, rinse under running water and set aside to drain.
  2. Place into a bowl 1 tbsp Arabian spice mix, ½ tsp cinnamon, 1 tsp salt, 1 pinch pepper, mix and set aside.
  3. Distribute chicken strips on Varoma dish and tray, coat with the reserved spice mix, drizzle with some olive oil and set aside.
  4. Place onions and 50 g olive oil into mixing bowl, chop 5 sec/speed 5 and then sauté 5 min/120°C/speed 1.
  5. Add minced beef and sauté 10 min/120°C/speed 1. Transfer into simmering basket with rice and mix with spatula.
  6. Place water, 1 tbsp Arabic spice mix, ½ tsp cinnamon, 1 tsp salt, 1 pinch pepper, nutmeg and bay leaves into mixing bowl, insert simmering basket, set Varoma into positon and steam 20-25 min/Varoma/speed 4.
  7. Remove Varoma and set aside, mix chicken strips with spatula. Remove simmering basket and set aside. Place Varoma back into position and steam 20-25 min/Varoma/speed 4.

Sauce

  1. Remove Varoma and set aside. Remove bay leaves, add butter and flour into mixing bowl and cook 7 min/90°C/speed 4.

Garnish and serving

  1. Transfer rice-beef mince mixture into a deep serving bowl, add chicken strips on top, pour some sauce and garnish with almonds, pine nuts and pistachio nuts. Serve remaining sauce separately.
