Pearl rice meatballs

Ingredients

  • 200 g sushi rice
  • 350 g pork belly, cut into pieces (5 cm) (see Variation)
  • 3 - 4 fresh shiitake mushrooms (approx. 50 g)
  • 25 g carrot
  • 20 g canned water chestnuts, drained and cut into halves
  • 5 - 10 g piece fresh ginger, peeled and cut into thin slices
  • 20 g spring onion/shallot, cut into pieces (2 cm)
  • 2 pinches ground white pepper
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp cornflour
  • 20 g soy sauce
  • 600 g water

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid, weigh rice into it, cover with water and set aside to soak. Place a plate onto mixing bowl lid and weigh pork belly onto it, then place into freezer. Soak rice and freeze pork belly for 1 hour.
  2. Place semi-frozen pork belly into mixing bowl and chop 25 sec/speed 6.
  3. Add shiitake mushrooms, carrot, water chestnuts, ginger, spring onion/shallots, white pepper, salt, sugar, cornflour and soy sauce and chop 10 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Knead 1 min/. Transfer mixture onto a plate. Using spatula, divide into 4 equal portions, then from each portion, shape 8 small balls (approx. 3 cm). Strain soaked rice through simmering basket then roll balls in the soaked rice, to cover each ball all over with rice. Arrange 14 balls into Varoma dish, insert Varoma tray and arrange 18 balls onto it.
  5. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 30 min/Varoma/speed 1. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

bowl, plate, freezer

Hints & Tricks

  • If using dried mushrooms, cover them with hot water and soak for 30 minutes, then drain and set aside.
    - Pearl rice meatballs are usually served without a dip. However, sweet chilli sauce can be served as a dip for this dish, if desired.
    - You can find glutinous rice in Asian supermarkets, or replace it with any other sticky rice, such as sushi rice.
  • You can replace pork belly with pork mince, provided the pork mince is fatty (30% fat), as pork fat is necessary to give the meatballs their smooth and soft texture. If using pork mince, omit freezing and initial steps.
