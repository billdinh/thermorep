Sliders with sunflower sauce

Ingredients

Sunflower sauce

  • 50 g sunflower seeds
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 200 g natural yoghurt
  • ¼ tsp mustard powder
  • ½ tsp sea salt
  • ground black pepper, to taste

Turkey patties

  • 30 g day-old bread, crusts removed
  • 1 brown onion (approx. 160 g), cut into quarters
  • 20 g olive oil, plus extra for frying
  • 500 g turkey mince
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 20 g water
  • 40 g tomato sauce, plus extra to serve (see Tips)
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 12 slider buns (or small bread rolls)
  • iceberg lettuce, shredded (optional)
  • cheese, sliced (optional)

Preparation

Sunflower sauce

  1. Place sunflower seeds into mixing bowl and mill 25 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a small bowl and set aside.
  2. Place garlic into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7.
  3. Add all remaining sauce ingredients, including reserved sunflower seeds, and mix 10 sec/speed 3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Insert butterfly whisk. Mix 15 sec/speed 3. Remove butterfly whisk. Transfer into a bowl, cover with plastic wrap and place into refrigerator until ready to use (see Tips). Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Turkey patties

  1. Place bread into mixing bowl and mill 5 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place onion into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7.
  3. Add oil and cook 3 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  4. Add mince, dried oregano, water, tomato sauce, salt, pepper and reserved breadcrumbs and combine 20 sec//speed 3. Form mixture into 12 patties (approx. 50 g each).
  5. Place a non-stick frying pan over medium-high heat and add oil. Working in batches, fry patties for approx. 3-4 minutes on each side or until golden brown and cooked through. Transfer patties into a thermal serving bowl or onto a plate and cover to keep warm.

Assembly

  1. Slice slider buns into halves. Spread bottom half of rolls with extra tomato sauce and reserved Sunflower sauce. Top with lettuce (optional), a patty and a slice of cheese (optional). Top with remaining half of roll to serve.

Useful Items

small bowl , plastic wrap, bowl, non-stick frying pan, thermal serving bowl or plate, refrigerator, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • To make your own tomato sauce, please refer to the recipe in The Basic Cookbook or Everyday Cookbook.
  • Sunflower sauce and turkey patties can be made a day in advance. Store in the refrigerator until ready to pan fry and serve.
