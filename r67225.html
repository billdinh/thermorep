Chicken with creamy vegetable sauce

Ingredients

  • 400 g water
  • 100 g carrots, cut into pieces
  • 60 g celeriac, cut into pieces
  • 60 g celery leaves
  • ½ - 1 tbsp chicken stock paste
  • 600 g chicken breast fillets, cut into strips (7 x 3 cm)
  • ½ tsp salt, plus extra to taste
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper, plus extra to taste
  • 100 g button mushrooms, cut into slices
  • 30 g plain flour
  • 60 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce (optional)
  • 1 tsp lemon juice (approx. 1/2 lemon)

Preparation

  1. Place water, carrots, celeriac, celery or parsley leaves and chicken stock paste into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position. Weigh chicken fillet strips into Varoma dish, season with salt and pepper and steam 15 min/Varoma/speed 1. Meanwhile, place button mushrooms onto Varoma tray and set aside.
  2. Carefully open Varoma lid away from you and flip chicken strips with spatula to ensure chicken cooks evenly. Insert Varoma tray with button mushrooms, and steam for a further 5 min/Varoma/speed 1. Set Varoma aside.
  3. Add plain flour, pouring (whipping) cream, Worcestershire sauce (optional) and lemon juice into mixing bowl and cook 1 min/100°C/speed 5.5.
  4. Add cooked button mushrooms and cook 1 min/80°C//speed 2. Adjust seasoning to taste and serve chicken and sauce separately, or cover chicken with sauce before serving.
