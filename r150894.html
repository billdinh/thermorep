Cannelloni with Artichoke Filling and Tomato Sauce

Ingredients

  • 140 g Parmesan cheese, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 200 g smoked cheese (e.g. smoked mozzarella, smoked Edam), cut in pieces (4-5 cm)

Filling

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 20 g extra virgin olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 300 g artichoke hearts
  • 100 g white wine
  • 100 g ricotta cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 16 cannelloni (large, dried, cylindrical egg pasta) (approx. 180 g)

Tomato Sauce

  • 40 g celery stalk, cut in pieces
  • 30 g onion, halved
  • 40 g carrot, cut in pieces
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 400 g tinned tomatoes
  • 200 g water
  • 1 pinch sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper

Preparation

  1. Place Parmesan cheese and smoked cheese in mixing bowl and grate 15 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.

Filling

  1. Place garlic and extra virgin olive oil in mixing bowl and sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  2. Add artichokes and white wine and, without measuring cup, cook 15 min/100°C/speed 1. Meanwhile, grease Varoma dish and tray.
  3. Insert measuring cup and mix 40 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add ricotta cheese, 300 g reserved grated cheese, eggs, salt and black pepper and mix 40 sec/speed 4. Transfer to a disposable piping bag or freezer bag and allow to cool for a few minutes.
  5. Cut a hole in the piping bag to the same diameter as dried cannelloni tubes and fill tubes with stuffing mixture. Arrange filled cannelloni in greased Varoma dish and tray, leaving a little space between them and lightly brush with extra virgin olive oil to avoid sticking. Clean mixing bowl.

Tomato Sauce

  1. Place celery, onion, carrot, garlic, tomatoes, water, sugar, salt and black pepper in mixing bowl and chop 15 sec/speed 5.
  2. Set Varoma in position and cook 30 min/Varoma/speed 1. Serve cannelloni hot, covered in sauce and sprinkled with remaining grated cheese.

Useful Items

disposable piping bag or freezer bag, pastry brush

Hints & Tricks

  • Cooking cannelloni in your Varoma means the steam tenderises the pasta beautifully. Use this recipe as a starting point to create your own version – see our variations to help get started.
  • Frozen artichoke hearts can be used instead of fresh or tinned.
  • Mushroom cannelloni: replace artichokes with 400 g sliced fresh mushrooms. In step 3 add mushrooms and white wine and, without measuring cup, cook 15 min/100°C/speed 1. Drain mushroom mixture through simmering basket, return drained mixture to mixing bowl and continue recipe from step 4.
    - For meat-lovers, add 100 g pancetta or bacon cubes in step 4.
