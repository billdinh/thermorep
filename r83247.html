Cranberry and Camembert stuffed chicken

Ingredients

Cranberry pesto stuffing

  • 1 garlic clove
  • ½ eschalot (approx. 60 g)
  • 20 g butter
  • 50 g dried cranberries
  • 30 g raw hazelnuts
  • 2 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 1 pinch sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 150 g Camembert cheese, cut into quarters

Chicken, potatoes and mustard sauce

  • 1 garlic clove
  • ½ eschalot (approx. 60 g)
  • 10 fresh chives, cut into quarters
  • 20 g butter
  • 500 g water
  • 1 - 2 tsp Chicken stock paste (see Tips)
  • 6 small potatoes, well-washed and cut into pieces (3-4cm)
  • 3 skinless chicken breast fillets (approx. 300 g per breast)
  • 150 - 200 g fresh green beans, trimmed
  • 20 g dry white wine
  • 2 tbsp plain flour
  • 3 tsp Hot English mustard
  • 2 tsp jarred horseradish
  • 2 tsp seeded mustard
  • 1 - 2 pinches sea salt, to taste

Preparation

Cranberry pesto stuffing

  1. Place garlic, eschalot and butter into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then cook 2 min/100°C/speed 1
  2. Add all remaining stuffing ingredients and chop 10 sec/speed 5. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Chicken, potatoes and mustard sauce

  1. Place garlic, eschalot, chives and butter into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then cook 2 min/100°C/speed 1
  2. Add water and stock paste. Insert simmering basket, then place potatoes into it.
  3. Place chicken breasts onto a work surface, then slice each breast horizontally taking care not to cut all the way through. Fill chicken breasts with reserved cranberry stuffing, then insert 1 or 2 toothpicks along the edge of the opening in the breasts to enclose the filling.
  4. Place stuffed chicken breasts onto Varoma tray. Secure Varoma lid, then place Varoma into position and cook 10 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  5. Turn chicken breasts over to ensure even cooking, then secure Varoma lid and cook for a further 5-10 min/Varoma/speed 2, or until chicken is cooked. Remove Varoma and transfer chicken onto a plate.
  6. Place Varoma dish back into position, then weigh green beans into it. Secure Varoma lid and cook 5 min/Varoma/speed 2, or until potatoes and beans are cooked to your liking. Meanwhile, place a medium-sized non-stick frying pan over a medium-high heat and add chicken breasts. Cook each breast for 2-3 minutes each side or until chicken is golden.
  7. Transfer chicken breasts, potatoes and beans into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and cover to keep warm. Retain steaming liquid in mixing bowl.
  8. Add all remaining ingredients to mixing bowl and cook 4 min/100°C/speed 3, without measuring cup. Transfer mustard sauce into a serving jug.
  9. Remove toothpicks from chicken breasts, then cut each breast into half and serve immediately with potatoes, green beans and mustard sauce.

Useful Items

toothpick, thermal serving bowl or other large bowl, serving jug, medium-sized non-stick frying pan, tongs, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • Please refer to The Basic Cookbook or Everyday Cookbook for the Chicken stock paste recipe.
  • To serve potato mashed, add cooked potato into mixing bowl after cooking the sauce. Insert buttefly whisk and mash 20-30 sec/speed 3-4.
