Thai Coconut Lemongrass Chicken Soup

Ingredients

  • 5 - 15 dried red chilies, e.g. chile de arbol, stems removed, to taste
  • 30 oz coconut milk
  • 14 oz chicken stock
  • 4 oz shallots, thinly sliced
  • 2 oz fish sauce
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 oz ginger, thinly sliced
  • 2 oz fresh galangal (Thai ginger), thinly sliced
  • 2 oz fresh lemongrass, cut into thin rounds, white part only
  • 1 - 2 oz tamarind paste, to taste
  • 6 oz fresh oyster mushrooms, hand torn into pieces
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 16 oz chicken breast fillet, boneless skinless, cut into thin strips (approx. ¼ in.)
  • 10 - 15 sprigs cilantro, leaves only
Nutrition

Preparation

  1. Place dried chilies into mixing bowl and toast 5 min/212°F/speed 2.
  2. Add coconut milk, chicken stock, shallots, fish sauce and sugar. Insert simmering basket and add ginger, galangal, lemongrass and tamarind paste into simmering basket and cook 15 min/200°F/speed 1.
  3. Remove simmering basket with spatula and discard solids.
  4. Add mushrooms and lime juice and cook 5 min/185°F//speed 1.
  5. Add chicken and cook 5-8 min/185°F//speed 1. Stir in cilantro with spatula. Serve hot.
