Nut Balls with Tomato Sauce

Ingredients

Nut Balls

  • 400 g shelled walnuts
  • 180 g bread, torn in pieces
  • 15 g fresh coriander, leaves only
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 240 g onions, quartered
  • 50 g olive oil
  • 4 medium eggs
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt

Sauce

  • 600 g tomatoes, cut in pieces
  • 150 g onions, quartered
  • 80 g carrots, cut in pieces
  • 50 g olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 dried bay leaf
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt

Preparation

Nut Balls

  1. Place walnuts in mixing bowl and chop 15 sec/speed 9. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place bread, coriander and 1 garlic clove in mixing bowl then grate 20 sec/speed 9. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place onions, olive oil and remaining 3 garlic cloves in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Sauté 5 min/120°C/speed 1.
  4. Add reserved chopped walnuts, eggs and salt then mix 6 sec/speed 4.
  5. Add reserved breadcrumbs and mix 6 sec/speed 4. With wet hands, form 40 walnut-sized balls then arrange on Varoma dish and tray.

Sauce

  1. Place tomatoes, onions, carrots, olive oil and garlic in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  2. Add bay leaf and salt. Place covered Varoma into position and steam 20 min/Varoma//speed 1. Remove Varoma and set aside then discard bay leaf from sauce.
  3. With measuring cup in place, blend 30 sec/speed 5-7, increasing speed gradually. Serve nut balls with sauce (see tip).
