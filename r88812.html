Chicken Curry with Spinach

Ingredients

Curry

  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 20 g fresh root ginger, peeled, cut in round slices (2 mm)
  • 300 g onions, quartered
  • 50 g olive oil
  • 700 - 800 g chicken thighs, boneless and skinless, cut in pieces (4 cm)
  • 1 - 2 tsp fine sea salt, to taste
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 ½ tsp garam masala
  • 1 ½ tsp ground cumin
  • ½ tsp chilli powder
  • 1 Tbsp plain flour
  • 400 g tinned chopped tomatoes, drained (1 x 400 g tin)
  • 400 g coconut milk
  • 250 g fresh spinach leaves

Rice

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 50 g olive oil
  • 350 g basmati rice
  • 1000 g water
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 250 g plain yoghurt, to serve

Preparation

Curry

  1. Place garlic and ginger in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 7.
  2. Add onions and oil then chop 3 sec/speed 5. Sauté 10 min/120°C/speed 1.
  3. Add chicken, salt, turmeric, garam masala, cumin, chilli powder and flour then sauté 3 min/100°C//speed 1.
  4. Add tomatoes and coconut milk then cook 15 min/100°C//speed 1.
  5. Add spinach and cook 5 min/100°C//speed 1, while stirring with spatula through hole in mixing bowl lid. Pour into a serving bowl and cover with cling film to keep warm.

Rice

  1. Place garlic and oil in mixing bowl then sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 2.
  2. Insert simmering basket and weigh in rice, water and add salt then cook 22 min/Varoma/speed 4.
  3. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and transfer rice to a serving bowl. Serve curry with rice and accompanied by plain yoghurt.

Useful Items

cling film, serving bowls

Hints & Tricks

  • If all the spinach does not fit, add two-thirds then stir for 2 minutes until wilted, then add the remaining spinach leaves.
