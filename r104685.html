Prawn and mushroom crepes

Ingredients

Chilli sauce

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 long red chilli, deseeded
  • 1 stalk fresh lemongrass, white part only, cut into pieces (2 cm)
  • 2 tsp palm sugar
  • 40 g fish sauce
  • 40 g lime juice (approx. 2 limes)
  • 2 tbsp rice wine vinegar

Crepe batter

  • 125 g plain flour
  • ¼ tsp ground turmeric
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 250 g coconut milk
  • 1 egg
  • 120 g water
  • oil, for frying

Prawn filling

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 3 spring onions/shallots, trimmed and cut into quarters
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 60 g water
  • 100 g fresh shiitake mushrooms, cut into thin slices (approx. 5 mm)
  • 250 g raw peeled prawns (approx. 24 prawns)
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 100 g bean sprouts
  • 6 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only, for garnishing

Preparation

Chilli sauce

  1. Place garlic, chilli and lemon grass into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add palm sugar, fish sauce, lime juice and rice wine vinegar and stir 15 sec/speed 4. Transfer into a small serving bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Crepe batter

  1. Place all crepe batter ingredients, except oil, into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 4. Transfer into a jug and place into refrigerator for 30 minutes. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  2. Place a 20 cm frying pan over medium-high heat and add oil. Pour approx. 3 tablespoons of batter into pan, swirling pan to cover entire base. Cook for 2-3 minutes or until edges of crepe are light golden. Using a fish slice, turn crepe and cook for a further 1 minute. Transfer onto a plate and cover with kitchen towel to keep warm. Repeat with remaining batter to make a total of 8 crepes.

Prawn filling

  1. Place garlic and spring onions/shallots into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add sesame oil, water and fresh shiitake mushrooms and cook 4 min/100°C//speed .
  3. Add prawn meat, salt and pepper and cook 1 min 30 sec/100°C//speed 1,  without measuring cup or until prawns are just cooked.
  4. Place crepes onto serving plates. Spoon reserved prawn mixture onto one half of each crepe, then top with bean sprouts and coriander, and serve with reserved Chilli sauce.

Useful Items
