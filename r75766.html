Applesauce Cake

Ingredients

  • 120 g butter, soft, diced, plus extra for greasing
  • 160 g granulated sugar
  • 1 heaped tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp ground cloves
  • 1 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 300 g cooking apples, quartered, cores removed
  • 2 tsp lemon juice
  • 300 g plain flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 110 g raisins
  • 70 g walnut pieces

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 170°C. Line a deep cake tin (Ø 23 cm) with baking paper and grease the sides with butter.
  2. Place sugar, cinnamon, cloves and nutmeg in mixing bowl and grind 20 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place apple quarters and lemon juice in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and chop again 5 sec/speed 6.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and cook 5 min/100°C/speed 2.
  5. Add reserved spiced sugar, flour, butter, eggs, salt and bicarbonate of soda and mix 40 sec/speed 5.
  6. Add raisins and walnuts, mix 40 sec//speed 2. Transfer mixture to prepared cake tin and bake 50 minutes-1 hour (170°C) or until a skewer inserted in the centre comes out clean.
  7. Leave in tin for 10-15 minutes then run a knife around inside edge of tin before transferring cake to a wire rack to cool. Delicious served warm or cold, perfect with a cup of tea.

Useful Items

deep cake tin (Ø 23 cm), baking paper, skewer, cooling rack, oven

Hints & Tricks

Grinding pre-ground spices with a dry ingredient from your recipe refreshes and intensifies their aromas and flavours. If you prefer to use whole spices, use 3 cm cinnamon stick, 1 tsp whole cloves and 1 whole nutmeg - grind these with the sugar 30 sec/speed 10 in step 2.

For a quick version, add all ingredients at once and mix 40 sec/speed 6 with the aid of the spatula.
