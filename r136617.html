Balsamic salmon and zucchini noodles

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 500 g zucchini, trimmed and peeled into "spaghetti" (see Tips)
  • 70 g balsamic vinegar
  • 80 g white wine
  • 60 g honey
  • 20 g Dijon mustard
  • 1 tbsp fresh rosemary, leaves only
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 4 fresh boneless, skinless salmon fillets (approx. 150 g each)
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 - 2 pinches ground black pepper

Preparation

  1. Grease Varoma dish and tray and set aside.
  2. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh zucchini into it. Add oil and toss to combine. Set bowl aside.
  3. Place balsamic vinegar, wine, honey, mustard, rosemary and garlic into mixing bowl and blend 8 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Place Varoma into position. Insert Varoma tray and weigh salmon fillets onto it. Season salmon with salt and pepper. Secure Varoma lid and steam 10 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  5. Set aside Varoma lid and tray with salmon. Place reserved zucchini into Varoma dish and insert Varoma tray with salmon back into position. Secure Varoma lid and steam 3-6 min/Varoma/speed 1 or until zucchini and salmon are cooked to your preference.
  6. Remove Varoma and divide zucchini between serving bowls. Place salmon on top of noodles and drizzle with sauce to serve.

Useful Items

bowl, 4 serving bowls

Hints & Tricks

  • We recommend using a vegetable peeler with a spiral or spaghetti attachment to peel zucchini. Alternatively, peel zucchini and cut into very thin strips.
