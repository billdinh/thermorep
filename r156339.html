Sardines in parcels with green bean salad

Ingredients

Sardines in parcels

  • 8 fresh whole sardines, gutted (each 70-80 g) (see Variations)
  • extra virgin olive oil, for greasing
  • 4 pinches salt

Green bean salad

  • 2 eggs
  • 500 g water
  • 500 g fresh green beans
  • 250 g cherry tomato, cut into halves
  • 80 g spring onions/shallots, cut into thin strips (julienne)
  • 40 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 15 g vinegar
  • 2 - 3 pinches salt

Preparation

Sardines in parcels

  1. Rinse sardines and pat dry with paper towels. Grease 4 rectangles of baking paper (30 cm x 40 cm each) with oil and place 2 sardines on each rectangle. Season each pair with 1 pinch salt. Fold up baking paper lengthwise, then twist the ends to form parcels. Place parcels seam side up, 2 into Varoma dish and 2 onto Varoma tray.

Green bean salad

  1. Wrap eggs in wet, well-wrung baking paper and place into Varoma dish along with the parcels. Insert Varoma tray into Varoma dish.
  2. Place water into mixing bowl, insert simmering basket and weigh green beans into it. Place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 25 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove Varoma. Carefully open one parcel, insert the point of a knife in the middle of a sardine and check that the flesh separates easily from the bone, indicating sardines are cooked through. If not, place Varoma back into position, secure Varoma lid and cook for a further 3-4 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove Varoma. Unwrap the eggs and cool them under running water.
  3. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and transfer cooked beans into a serving bowl, along with cherry tomatoes, spring onions, extra virgin olive oil, vinegar and salt. Peel cooked eggs, cut into wedges, add into the serving bowl and gently toss the salad. Open parcels and serve fish immediately with warm bean salad.

Useful Items

paper towel, baking paper, pastry brush, serving bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • Replace fresh sardines with defrosted sardines or with other small oily fish of the same size and weight (e.g. mackerel).
    - Replace sardines with 2 whole sea bass, gutted (approx. 350-380 g each): Use two 50 cm x 30 cm rectangles of oiled baking paper, make 1 parcel for each seabass and proceed with step 2. Before serving, remove skin and bones and serve half a fish per portion.
    - For a different flavour, add a bay leaf and thin lemon slices to each parcel, or sprinkle fish with ground sweet paprika and drizzle with extra virgin olive oil before serving.
