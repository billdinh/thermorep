Lemon Tart

Ingredients

Pastry

  • 120 g unsalted butter, cold, diced
  • 175 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 50 g icing sugar, plus extra for sprinkling
  • 3 egg yolks, from medium eggs, 1 beaten

Filling

  • 130 g lemon juice
  • 200 g caster sugar
  • 200 g unsalted butter, diced
  • 5 lemons, finely grated zest only
  • 4 medium eggs
  • 6 egg yolks, from medium eggs

Preparation

Pastry

  1. Place butter, flour and icing sugar in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 6.
  2. Add 2 egg yolks and pulse Turbo/2 sec/5 times. Tip out and bring together into a flattened ball, then wrap in cling film and chill in fridge for 1 hour.
  3. Roll out chilled pastry between two pieces of cling film, or on a lightly floured surface, and use to line a tart tin (Ø 22 cm). Prick base with a fork then set aside in fridge for 30 minutes. Meanwhile, preheat oven to 190°C and clean mixing bowl.
  4. Line rested pastry shell with baking paper and fill with baking beans then blind bake for 15-20 minutes (190°C) until golden. Remove baking beans and paper then bake for a further 5 minutes (190°C). Set aside on a wire rack and brush all over with remaining beaten egg yolk.

Filling

  1. Place lemon juice, sugar, butter and lemon zest in mixing bowl then heat 5 min/50°C/speed 1.
  2. Without measuring cup, mix 2 min/80°C/speed 3 while adding eggs, followed by egg yolks, one at a time through hole in mixing bowl lid. Insert measuring cup then continue heating 4 min/80°C/speed 3 until mixture thickens like lemon curd. Pour into prepared pastry shell. Set aside in fridge to chill for at least 2 hours.
  3. Remove from tin, then dust with icing sugar and serve.

Useful Items

cling film, tart tin (Ø 22 cm), rolling pin, wire rack, pastry brush, baking beans, baking paper, serving plate, refrigerator, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • For a bruléed finish, before serving, place chilled tart dusted with icing sugar under a very hot grill for up to a minute until small brown spots appear on surface (do not let pastry burn). Chill before serving.
