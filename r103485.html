Spinach and artichoke penne

Ingredients

  • 80 g butter, plus extra for greasing
  • 100 g Parmesan cheese, cut into pieces (2-3 cm)
  • 100 g mozzarella cheese, cut into pieces (2-3 cm)
  • 1450 g water
  • 320 g penne pasta
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 brown onion (approx. 150 g), cut into eighths and layers separated
  • 340 g marinated artichoke hearts, drained and cut into quarters
  • 120 g fresh baby spinach leaves
  • 2 sprigs fresh mint, leaves only, cut into pieces
  • 40 g plain flour
  • 2 tsp Vegetable stock paste (see Tips)
  • 1 tsp onion salt
  • ½ tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • ¼ tsp ground cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 200 g sour cream
  • 100 g frozen green peas, thawed

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Grease a casserole dish (23 cm round x 9 cm deep) with butter and set aside.
  2. Place Parmesan into mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place mozzarella into mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a separate bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Place 1200g of water into mixing bowl and bring to the boil 8 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  5. Add pasta and cook 9 min/100°C//speed 1. Drain pasta and transfer into a large bowl. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  6. Place garlic into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  7. Add onion and 40g of the butter and cook 4 min/100°C//speed 1.
  8. Add artichoke hearts and cook 1 min/100°C/speed 2.
  9. Add spinach and mint and cook 1 min/100°C//speed 1. Transfer mixture into bowl with pasta. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  10. Add flour, stock paste, onion salt, pepper, cayenne pepper (optional), remaining 40 g of butter and remaining 250 g of water and cook 5 min/90°C/speed 4.
  11. Add sour cream and reserved Parmesan and cook 1 min/90°C/speed 3. Transfer into bowl with pasta. Add peas to pasta mixture and stir well to combine. Transfer all ingredients into prepared casserole dish, sprinkle with reserved mozzarella and bake for 20 minutes (180°C) or until golden brown. Serve immediately.
