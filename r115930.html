Mixed Grain Pilaf with Beetroot

Ingredients

  • 800 g water
  • 1 ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 tsp sunflower oil
  • 200 g mixed grains (e.g. bulgar wheat, red rice and millet)
  • 1 dried bay leaf
  • ½ cucumber, deseeded, cut in pieces (approx. 150 g)
  • 100 g spring onions, sliced (2-3 cm)
  • 75 g red peppers, cut in pieces
  • 20 g lemon juice
  • 60 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 small handful fresh coriander leaves (approx. 10 g)
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 150 g cooked beetroot, diced (1 cm)

Preparation

  1. Place water, ½ tsp salt and oil in mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket then weigh in mixed grains and add bay leaf. Steam 17 min/100°C/speed 4.
  2. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and transfer grains to a serving bowl then stir to loosen. Empty mixing bowl.
  3. Place cucumber, onions, red peppers, lemon juice, oil, coriander, 1 tsp salt and pepper in mixing bowl then chop 2 sec/speed 5. Pour over grains in serving bowl.
  4. Place serving bowl with grains on mixing bowl lid and weigh in beetroot. Remove from lid and mix together well with spatula. Serve immediately.

Hints & Tricks

  • Buy a packet of mixed grains that all have the same cooking time. We used a mix of bulgar wheat, red rice and millet. Adjust cooking time accordingly.
