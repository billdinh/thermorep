Rocky road with vanilla bean marshmallow

Ingredients

Vanilla bean marshmallow

  • butter, for greasing
  • 30 g gelatine powder
  • 280 g water
  • 330 g white sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste

Rocky road

  • 100 g slivered almonds
  • 30 g desiccated coconut
  • 150 g Turkish delight, cut into pieces (2 cm)
  • 500 g dark chocolate

Preparation

Vanilla bean marshmallow

  1. Grease and line a baking tin (18 x 26 cm) with baking paper, allowing a 2 cm overhang at each end. Set tin aside.
  2. Place a ceramic or glass bowl onto mixing bowl lid, then weigh gelatine powder  and 140 g of the water into it. Set gelatine aside to soak.
  3. Place sugar into mixing bowl and mill 5 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add remaining 140 g of the water and heat 15 min/100°C/speed 2.
  5. Add reserved gelatine mixture and heat 2 min/100°C/speed 2. Set mixing bowl aside and allow mixture to cool until the temperature falls below 50°C (approx. 30 minutes).
  6. Insert butterfly whisk and beat 7 min/speed 3.5.
  7. Add vanilla bean paste and combine 10 sec/speed 3. Remove butterfly whisk.
  8. Transfer into prepared baking tin, then cover with plastic wrap and set aside until marshmallow has set (approx. 1 hour). Once set, cut into small pieces (2 cm) and set aside until use. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Rocky road

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C and line 2 baking trays with baking paper.
  2. Place almonds onto 1 of the baking trays and the coconut onto the other, then evenly spread with spatula. Bake coconut for 2 minutes and almonds for 6 minutes (180°C), or until lightly browned. Set aside to cool.
  3. Place a large bowl onto mixing bowl lid, then weigh 100 g of the reserved marshmallows into it. Add cooled toasted coconut to bowl and combine until marshmallows are covered. Discard any excess coconut in the bowl.
  4. Place large bowl back onto mixing bowl lid and weigh Turkish delight into it. Add slivered almonds, then set bowl aside.
  5. Line a baking tin (18 x 18 cm) with baking paper, allowing a 2 cm overhang at each end. Set baking tin aside.
  6. Place chocolate into mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then melt 7 min/60°C/speed 2, or until chocolate has melted.
  7. Transfer melted chocolate into the large bowl with all other ingredients and combine well using spatula.
  8. Pour rocky road mixture into prepared baking tin and set aside to set. Once set, cut into pieces and serve.

Useful Items

baking tin (18 x 26 cm), baking paper, ceramic or glass bowl, plastic wrap, 2 baking trays, large bowl, baking tin (18 x 18 cm) , oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Store leftover marshmallows in a sealable container.
