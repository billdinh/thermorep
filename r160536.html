Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

Dough

  • 2 oz water
  • 2 ½ oz unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 4 oz sour cream
  • 2 ½ oz sugar
  • 17 oz bread flour, plus extra to dust
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 tbsp active dry yeast
  • 2 large eggs
  • vegetable oil, to grease

Filling

  • 2 oz unsalted butter, melted
  • 6 oz light brown sugar
  • ¼ tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • &frac18; fine sea salt
  • 4 oz raisins (optional)
  • 2 oz chopped pecans

Glaze

  • 5 oz sugar
  • 4 oz cream cheese, room temperature
  • &frac18; tsp ground cinnamon
  • ¼ tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tsp heavy whipping cream

Preparation

Dough

  1. Preheat oven to proof (see Tip).
  2. Place water, butter, sour cream and sugar into mixing bowl and heat 4 min/110°F/speed 1.
  3. Add flour, salt, yeast and eggs and mix 10 sec/speed 5 then knead 3 min/.
  4. Transfer dough to a lightly floured surface and knead until smooth. Dough should be buttery but no longer sticky.
  5. Lightly oil a large bowl with vegetable oil and place dough into bowl, turning dough to coat all sides. Cover with cling wrap, place in oven to proof or in a warm place for 1½ hours or until the dough has doubled in size.
  6. Lightly oil a baking pan (approx. 9 in. x 13 in.) or line with parchment paper. Set aside.
  7. Punch down the risen dough and turn out onto a lightly floured work surface. Roll the dough out into a rectangle (approx. 24 in. x 12 in.) with a ¼ inch thickness. Let dough rest while you make the filling.

Filling

  1. Place butter, brown sugar, vanilla, cinnamon, salt and raisins (if using) into mixing bowl and cook 5 min/212°F/speed 0.5.
  2. Spread filling evenly over dough leaving a ½ inch border and sprinkle with chopped pecans. Lightly press pecans into dough.
  3. Roll the dough into a log shape, pinching at edges to seal. Cut the log into 12 equal slices (approx. 1½ in.) and place them on the baking pan, leaving a ½ inch space between each bun. Cover with cling wrap and set in oven to proof or a warm place to rise for 1½ hours or until rolls have doubled in size.
  4. Preheat oven to 375°F. Bake for 20-25 minutes until golden brown. Set aside and allow to cool.

Glaze

  1. Place sugar into mixing bowl grind 20 sec/speed 10.
  2. Add cream cheese, cinnamon, vanilla and whipping cream and mix 30 sec/speed 4. Drizzle glaze over cinnamon rolls or use an icing spatula to spread out evenly. Transfer to a serving dish and serve immediately.

Useful Items

large bowl, cling wrap, parchment paper, icing spatula, serving dish, baking pan (approx. 9 in. x 13 in.), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • If your oven does not have a Proof Mode setting: instead of placing in oven, cover with cling wrap and leave to rest at room temperature until doubled in size (approx. 2 hours).
  • Add more spices, a little nutmeg, ground ginger, allspice or zested orange or lemon peel for a different taste.
