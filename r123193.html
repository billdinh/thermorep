Healthy chocolate mousse

Ingredients

  • 100 g dark chocolate, broken into pieces
  • 400 g avocado (approx. 3 avocados), flesh only
  • 120 g coconut cream
  • 100 g pure maple syrup
  • 30 g raw cacao powder (see Tips)
  • fresh raspberries, to serve
  • whipped cream, to serve (optional)
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1647 kJ / 392 kcal
Protein
4.1 g
Carbohydrates
19.1 g

33 g

Preparation

  1. Place chocolate into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 8.
  2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then melt 3 min/60°C/speed 2 or until melted.
  3. Add avocado, coconut cream, syrup and cacao and blend 10 sec/speed 5.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and blend 20 sec/speed 6.
  5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and blend 15 sec/speed 6 or until smooth and creamy. Transfer into 6 serving glasses and serve topped with raspberries and whipped cream (optional).

Useful Items

6 serving bowls or glasses

Hints & Tricks

  • If you don’t have any raw cacao on hand, you can use regular cocoa powder instead.
