Passionfruit bavarois (Jude Blereau)

Ingredients

Pastry

  • 70 g rolled oats
  • 20 g desiccated coconut
  • 30 g macadamia nuts
  • 30 g almond oil
  • 40 g pure maple syrup

Bavarois

  • 200 g fresh passionfruit pulp, strained to remove seeds (approx. 25 passionfruits)
  • 1 ½ tsp agar agar powder
  • 80 g blanched almonds
  • 430 g water
  • 60 g cornflour
  • 2 tsp natural vanilla extract
  • 50 g pure maple syrup
  • 70 g raw sugar
  • 140 g coconut milk

Passionfruit jelly

  • 30 g water
  • ¼ tsp agar agar powder
  • 1 tbsp raw sugar
  • 140 g fresh passionfruit pulp (approx. 10 passionfruits)

Preparation

Pastry

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Line a large baking tray with baking paper. Place 6 pastry rings (7 cm) onto lined tray and line sides of each ring with baking paper. Set aside.
  2. Place oats, coconut and macadamias into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add oil and syrup and pulse Turbo/1 sec/3-4 times or until mixture sticks together when pressed. Divide mixture evenly between prepared pastry rings and press into base. Bake for 8-10 minutes (180°C) or until lightly golden. Set aside to cool. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Bavarois

  1. Place strained passionfruit juice and agar agar into a bowl and stand for 10 minutes. Meanwhile...
  2. Place almonds into mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add water and blend 20 sec/speed 9. Strain almond milk mixture through a nut milk bag or muslin cloth into a large jug. Discard any pulp.
  4. Place almond milk, cornflour, vanilla and syrup into mixing bowl and cook 6 min/90°C/speed 3. Transfer almond custard into a bowl, cover with plastic wrap and place into refrigerator to set for 45 minutes. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  5. Place reserved passionfruit juice mixture and sugar into mixing bowl and cook 8 min/90°C/speed 2. Transfer into a bowl to cool, stirring occassionally.
  6. Place coconut milk and cooled almond custard into mixing bowl and blend 5 sec/speed 9, until smooth.
  7. Add cooled passionfruit juice mixture and blend 8 sec/speed 9, until well combined. Pour mixture onto cooled pastry bases and place into refrigerator for 15 minutes. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Passionfruit jelly

  1. Place water, agar agar, sugar and passionfruit pulp into mixing bowl and cook 10 min/90°C//speed 2. Allow to cool slightly.
  2. Pour spoonfuls of jelly onto set bavarois. Place bavarois into refrigerator to set for a minimum of 1 hour or overnight.
  3. Place bavarois onto serving plates and gently remove pastry rings and baking paper. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

large baking tray, baking paper, 6 pastry rings, bowl, jug, nut milk bag or muslin cloth, plastic wrap, serving plates, oven, refrigerator
