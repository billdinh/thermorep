Pasta Salad with Cod and Tartare Sauce

Ingredients

Salad

  • 1500 g water
  • 2 ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 3 tsp olive oil
  • 300 g carrots, cut in pieces (1 cm)
  • 200 g courgettes, cut in pieces (1 cm)
  • 100 g frozen peas, defrosted
  • 400 g cod fillets, skinless, boneless
  • 20 g lemon juice
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 350 g dry pasta, multi-coloured fusilli

Tartare Sauce

  • 60 g pickles
  • 1 Tbsp pickled capers, drained
  • 100 g sunflower oil
  • 1 large egg
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • &frac18; tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp lemon juice

Preparation

Salad

  1. Place water, 2 tsp salt and 1 tsp oil in mixing bowl then place Varoma dish into position.
  2. Place carrots, courgettes and peas in Varoma dish then insert Varoma tray. Weigh in cod, lemon juice, season with ½ tsp salt, pepper, and 1 tsp oil then steam 22 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove Varoma, separate cod into 3 cm pieces then transfer cod and vegetables to a serving bowl and allow to cool.
  3. Add pasta and cook time according to packet instructions/100°C//speed . Drain pasta using Varoma dish. Stir through 1 tsp oil then allow to cool. Once pasta is cool add to cooked cod and vegetables in serving bowl then set aside in fridge. Meanwhile, make sauce.

Tartare Sauce

  1. Place pickles and capers in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a small bowl and set aside.
  2. Place a jug on mixing bowl lid. Weigh in oil then set aside.
  3. Place egg, salt, pepper, mustard and lemon juice in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 5. Mix again 1 min 30 sec/speed 5 while very slowly pouring reserved oil onto mixing bowl lid, letting it drizzle around measuring cup in a thin stream onto rotating blades. Add reserved chopped pickles and capers and stir with spatula. Pour onto salad and stir until evenly distributed.
  4. Return to fridge to chill for at least 1 hour and serve when ready.
