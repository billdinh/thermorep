Tofu Meatballs with Rice and Vegan Quindim

Ingredients

  • 70 g stale white bread, torn in pieces
  • 300 g onions, quartered
  • 150 g carrots, cut in pieces
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 20 g pitted black olives
  • 20 g celery stalk, cut in pieces
  • 10 g fresh coriander
  • 55 g olive oil
  • 2 tsp fine sea salt
  • 200 g tofu, diced
  • 50 g plain flour
  • 400 g cassava, cut in pieces
  • 260 g coconut milk
  • 180 g sugar, plus extra for sprinkling
  • 15 g sunflower oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 30 g cornflour
  • 100 g desiccated coconut
  • 1200 g water
  • 250 g brown rice
  • 150 g frozen spinach, defrosted, drained
  • 100 g frozen green peas
  • 100 g baby carrots, sliced

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C and line a baking tray with baking paper. Grease 6 dariole moulds (Ø 7 cm) with oil and sprinkle with sugar then set aside.
  2. Place bread in mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place 200 g onions, carrots, 3 garlic cloves, olives, celery, coriander, 25 g olive oil and 1 tsp salt in mixing bowl then chop 7 sec/speed 5.
  4. Add tofu, flour and reserved breadcrumbs then mix 3 sec/speed 5. Form into 20 balls then place on prepared baking tray and cook for 20-30 minutes (200°C) until browned, covering and setting aside once done. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl and continue with recipe.
  5. Place cassava in mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 7.
  6. Add coconut milk, sugar, oil and cornflour then mix 10 sec/speed 7.
  7. Add coconut and mix 10 sec/speed 3. Divide mixture between prepared moulds and place in Varoma dish then cover with Varoma lid and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  8. Place water and remaining 1 tsp salt in mixing bowl.
  9. Insert simmering basket and weigh in rice. Place Varoma into position then steam 40 min/Varoma/speed 2. Remove Varoma and unmould quindim. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula. Reserve 190 g cooking water and discard rest. Transfer cooked rice to a serving bowl. Pour over reserved cooking water and stir with spatula. Cover to keep warm.
  10. Place remaining 1 garlic clove, 100 g onions and 30 g olive oil in mixing bowl, chop 5 sec/speed 5 then sauté 5 min/120°C/speed 1.
  11. Add spinach, peas and carrots then sauté 7 min/100°C//speed . Transfer to serving bowl with rice and stir together with spatula. Serve vegetable rice with tofu meatballs. Enjoy quindims for dessert.

Useful Items

baking tray and baking paper, jug, serving bowl, cling film, dariole moulds, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Quindim is a classic Brazilian dessert made of set coconut and custard. Typically presented as an upturned cup, it is dense with a glistening appearance and is intensely sweet and addictive!
