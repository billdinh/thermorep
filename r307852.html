Moroccan Chicken Bowl

Ingredients

Couscous

  • 8 ½ oz whole wheat pearl couscous
  • 15 oz water
  • ½ tsp salt

Moroccan Chicken

  • 26 oz chicken thighs, boneless and skinless, cubed (2 in.)
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 8 oz plain yogurt
  • ½ tsp ground ginger
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • &frac18; tsp ground black pepper
  • 4 ½ oz red onions, cut into pieces, plus extra, sliced, to garnish
  • 3 oz dried apricots, pitted, plus extra, chopped, to garnish
  • 2 oz raisins
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 10 sprigs cilantro, leaves only, plus extra, chopped, to garnish
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • slivered almonds, to garnish

Preparation

Couscous

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid, weigh in couscous and set aside.
  2. Place water and ½ tsp salt into mixing bowl and bring to a boil 6 min/212°F/speed 1.
  3. Pour boiling water over reserved couscous. Cover bowl with cling wrap and let absorb for 10 minutes. Meanwhile, dry mixing bowl and prepare chicken.

Moroccan Chicken

  1. Place a large bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in chicken, garam masala, yogurt, ground ginger, ¼ tsp salt and pepper. Set bowl with chicken mixture aside.
  2. Place onions, dried apricots, raisins, garlic, cilantro and oil into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and sauté 3 min/212°F/speed 1.
  3. Add reserved chicken mixture and cook 10 min/212°F//speed 1.
  4. Pour Moroccan Chicken over couscous and garnish with chopped cilantro, apricots, slivered almonds and red onions. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

large bowl, cling wrap

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with lemon wedges for a fresh, citrus note.
