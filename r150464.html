Melanzane parmigiana

Ingredients

  • 2 eggplants, cut lengthways into thin slices (approx. 3-5 mm)
  • salt, for sprinkling
  • 20 g olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 40 g Parmesan cheese, crust removed and cut into pieces (approx. 2- 3 cm)
  • 40 g pine nuts
  • 40 g bread of choice, torn into pieces
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 3 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves only
  • 8 fresh sage leaves
  • 800 g canned whole tomatoes
  • 20 - 25 fresh basil leaves, plus extra to serve
  • 100 g fresh mozzarella, cut into thin slices (approx. 5 mm)
  • mixed leaf salad, to serve

Preparation

  1. Layer eggplant slices into Varoma dish, sprinkling salt between each layer. Set aside for 1 hour to extract bitter juices (see Tips). Rinse and pat dry on both sides with paper towel and set aside.
  2. Preheat oven 180°C. Lightly grease a baking dish (30 x 23 cm) with oil and set aside.
  3. Place Parmesan, pine nuts and bread into mixing bowl and mill 5 sec/speed 8. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place oil, garlic, thyme and sage into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7.
  5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and cook 3 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  6. Add tomatoes and cook 20 min/100°C/speed 2.
  7. Add 10 of the basil leaves and 40 g of the breadcrumb mixture and mix 5 sec/speed 5.
  8. Cover prepared baking dish with a thin layer of sauce (approx. 5-6 tablespoons). Top with one-third of the eggplant, slightly overlapping. Spoon 4-5 tablespoons of the tomato sauce over eggplant, scatter with 4-5 torn basil leaves and half of the mozzarella. Place another one-third of the eggplant over mozzarella, slightly overlapping. Spoon 4-5 tablespoons of the tomato sauce over eggplant, scatter with 4-5 torn basil leaves and half of the breadcrumb mixture. Top with remaining eggplant, pour over remaining tomato sauce, scatter with remaining mozzarella and sprinkle with remaining breadcrumb mixture. Bake for 45-55 minutes (180°C) or until eggplant is cooked and the top is golden. Allow to cool for 10 minutes, then garnish with extra basil leaves and serve with a mixed leaf salad.

Useful Items

paper towel, baking dish (30 x 23 x 5 cm), bowl, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • To save time, you can prepare the sauce while the eggplant is resting.
  • You can prepare and assemble this dish up to 24 hours in advance. Simply bake as per recipe, until eggplant is cooked and top is golden.
  • To make this recipe gluten free, simply use gluten free bread and proceed as per recipe.
