Soy bread with seeds

Ingredients

  • 120 g plain soy yoghurt
  • 140 g water
  • 1 tbsp molasses
  • 2 tsp dried instant yeast
  • 200 g soy flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 30 g extra virgin olive oil, plus extra for brushing
  • 300 g Italian 00 flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp white sesame seeds, plus extra for garnishing
  • 2 tsp poppy seeds

Preparation

  1. Place yoghurt, water, molasses and yeast into mixing bowl and heat 3 min/37°C/speed 2. Lightly flour a large bowl with soy flour and set aside.
  2. Add oil, soy flour, Italian 00 flour and salt and knead 2 min 30 sec/.
  3. Add seeds and knead 30 sec/. Transfer mixture into prepared floured bowl. Cover with plastic wrap and set aside in a warm place until doubled in size (approx. 2 hours).
  4. Line a baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  5. Transfer dough onto a lightly floured silicone bread mat or work surface. Divide dough into halves. Using your hands, gently roll each portion into a long sausage shape. Place lengths of dough next to each other, then criss-cross over each other repeatedly to create a two-piece plait. Seal ends of plait by pressing ends of dough pieces together, then bring the two ends together to create a wreath. Press down ends of the wreath together to seal and transfer onto prepared baking tray. Cover with a kitchen towel and set aside to prove (approx. 1 hour).
  6. Preheat oven to 180°C. Brush bread with a little oil, then sprinkle with extra sesame seeds. Bake for 25-30 minutes (180°C) or until golden brown on top. Carefully remove from oven and transfer onto a wire rack to cool before serving.

Useful Items

large bowl, plastic wrap, baking tray (30 x 40 cm), baking paper, silicone bread mat, kitchen towel, pastry brush, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • You can store this bread for 2-3 days in the refrigerator in plastic bags. Before eating, heat the bread in the oven to regain crispiness.
  • You can freeze this bread. Cut into slices and place into freezer bags. Thaw and heat in a hot oven.
