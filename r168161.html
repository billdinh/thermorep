Lentil puree (moujadarra)

Ingredients

  • 200 g onions, cut into quarters
  • 40 g sunflower oil, plus extra for frying
  • 300 g lentils
  • 1600 g water
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 pinch ground white pepper
  • 50 g Egyptian rice, rinsed
  • 10 g olive oil
  • 200 g onions, cut into halves
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
2477 kJ / 592 kcal
Protein
18.9 g

Preparation

  1. Place 200 g onions and 40 g sunflower oil into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 5, then sauté 10 min/120°C/speed 1. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place lentils, 1500 g water, salt and pepper into mixing bowl and cook 40 min/Varoma/speed 1.5.
  3. Insert butterfly whisk. Add remaining 100 g water, rice, olive oil, and reserved sautéed onions and cook 20 min/Varoma/speed 1.5.
  4. Adjust seasoning to taste and cook 20 min/Varoma/speed 1.5, meanwhile heat oil in a frying pan, slice 200 g onions lengthwise and fry them until golden brown. Drain the onion on paper towels. Remove butterfly whisk. Transfer lentil puree into a serving bowl and garnish with fried onions before serving.

Useful Items

bowl, paper towels, serving bowl, frying pan, stove

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve Lentil puree with a salad and bread on the side.
