Chilli mussels with thyme and tomatoes

Ingredients

  • 1000 g fresh mussels , scrubbed, debearded
  • 2 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves only
  • 2 sprigs fresh basil, leaves only
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 fresh bird's eye chilli, deseeded if preferred
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 400 g canned chopped tomatoes
  • 100 g white wine
  • 40 g lemon juice (approx. 1 lemon)
  • 2 tbsp vegetable stock paste
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1618 kJ / 385 kcal
Protein
48 g

15 g

Preparation

  1. Place mussels into Varoma dish then spread thyme and basil leaves evenly over the top of the mussels. Set Varoma dish with mussels aside.
  2. Place garlic cloves and bird's eye chilli into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add olive oil and sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  4. Add canned chopped tomatoes, white wine, lemon juice and vegetable stock paste into mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish into position and cook 12-15 min/Varoma/speed 1, until all mussels have opened. Discard any mussels that remain closed after cooking.
  5. Transfer mussels into a large serving bowl, pour over sauce and serve.
