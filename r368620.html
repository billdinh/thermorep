Gougères

Ingredients

  • 100 g Gruyère cheese, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 60 g Parmesan, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 190 g water
  • 80 g unsalted butter, cut into pieces
  • ½ - 1 tsp salt, to taste
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 125 g plain flour
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper
  • 4 eggs
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
203.2 kJ / 48.4 kcal

2.1 g

Preparation

  1. Place cheeses into mixing bowl and grate 7 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  2. Place water, butter, salt and pepper into mixing bowl and cook 4 min/100°C/speed 1.
  3. Add flour and cayenne pepper and mix 1 min/speed 3.
  4. Place a sheet of baking paper onto kitchen bench and transfer pastry onto it to cool for 10 minutes.
  5. Preheat oven to 200°C. Line 2 baking trays (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  6. Place reserved cooled pastry back into mixing bowl and mix 1 min/speed 5, adding 1 egg at a time through hole in mixing bowl lid, allowing eggs to mix for 10-15 seconds before adding the next one. After adding the last egg, mix for a further 20-30 sec/speed 5. Mixture should hold its shape.
  7. Add reserved cheeses and mix 10 sec/speed 3 or until combined. Place pastry into a piping bag fitted with a plain nozzle (10 mm). Pipe onto prepared trays (approx. 40 apricot-sized balls), leaving a 3 cm space between each ball. Bake for 20-25 minutes (200°C) until golden brown and cooked through. Serve warm (see Tips).

Useful Items

bowl, baking paper, baking trays (30 x 40 cm), piping bag (10 mm nozzle), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • You can place trays of unbaked gougères into the freezer. Once frozen, place into snaplock bags. To bake from frozen, place onto lined baking trays. Bake 220°C for 8 minutes, then reduce temperature to 190°C and continue to bake for 15-20 minutes until golden brown and cooked through.
  • Gougères are best served warm but can be stored for up to 2 days in a sealable container.
  • Gougères can be filled with Leek and smoked salmon dip (please refer to the recipe in this recipe collection).
