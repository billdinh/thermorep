Bulgur Salad with Rocket, Peach and Avocado

Ingredients

  • 800 g water
  • 120 g bulgur wheat
  • 100 g rocket leaves
  • 3 peaches, cut in pieces (1 cm)
  • 1 avocado, cut in pieces (1 cm)
  • 50 g almonds, toasted
  • 30 g lemon juice
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 10 g honey
  • ½ tsp cumin
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt

Preparation

  1. Place water in mixing bowl then insert simmering basket. Weigh in bulgur wheat then cook 15 min/100°C/speed 4. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula, stir with a fork then set aside to cool for 10 minutes. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl.
  2. Place a serving bowl on mixing bowl lid then weigh in rocket, add peaches and avocado then set bowl aside.
  3. Place almonds in mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 7. Transfer to serving bowl.
  4. Place lemon juice, oil, honey, cumin and salt in mixing bowl then mix 5 sec/speed 5. Pour over salad in serving bowl, add cooked bulgur then combine with spatula. Serve warm or place in fridge to chill until needed.
