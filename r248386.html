Quenelles with Watercress Sauce

Ingredients

Quenelles

  • 375 g white fish fillets, skinless, cut in pieces
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • ¼ tsp ground white pepper
  • ½ tsp ground nutmeg
  • 100 g unsalted butter, diced

Panade

  • 30 g unsalted butter, diced
  • 100 g milk
  • 50 g plain flour
  • 1 medium egg
  • 1 egg yolk, from medium egg
  • 2 egg whites, from medium eggs
  • 600 g water

Sauce

  • 1 Tbsp cornflour
  • 100 g single cream
  • 2 shallots, halved
  • 30 g unsalted butter, diced
  • 150 g watercress, plus extra for garnish
  • 200 g water
  • 100 g natural yoghurt
  • 1 Tbsp chicken stock paste
  • ½ lemon, juice only
  • ground black pepper, to taste

Preparation

Quenelles

  1. Place fish in mixing bowl and chop 20 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then chop again 10 sec/speed 6.
  2. Add salt, pepper, nutmeg and butter then mix 30 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl, cover with cling film and refrigerate for 1 hour. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl and make panade.

Panade

  1. Place butter and milk in mixing bowl then cook 3 min/90°C/speed 1.
  2. Add flour and cook 1 min 30 sec/80°C/speed 3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then cool for 3 minutes.
  3. Set to 2 min/speed 3 then after the first 30 seconds, drop egg and egg yolk, one at a time, through hole in mixing bowl lid, at 30 second intervals. Transfer to a bowl and cover with cling film placed directly on the panade to prevent a skin from forming. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
  4. Return fish mixture and panade to mixing bowl and blend 20 sec/speed 5. Set to 1 min/speed 5 and add egg whites (from quenelle ingredients), one at a time, through hole in mixing bowl lid, at 15 second intervals.
  5. Using 2 tablespoons, dipped frequently in cold water, shape mixture into 16-18 evenly sized quenelles. Place in lightly greased Varoma dish and tray then insert Varoma tray and cover with Varoma lid.
  6. Place water in mixing bowl, place Varoma into position and steam quenelles for 12 min/Varoma/speed 2. Remove Varoma and transfer to a pre-warmed serving plate, discard cooking water then immediately make sauce.

Sauce

  1. Place cornflour in a small bowl. Place bowl on mixing bowl lid, weigh in cream, remove and stir together then set aside.
  2. Place shallots in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add butter then sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  4. Add cornflour mixture, watercress, water, yoghurt, stock paste (see tip), lemon juice and pepper then mix 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and cook 6 min/100°C//speed 2. Taste and adjust seasoning as desired.
  5. Pour sauce over quenelles, garnish with extra watercress and serve with rice.
