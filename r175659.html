Baked Peaches with Meringue Topping

Ingredients

Baked Peaches

  • 4 peaches
  • 50 g almonds
  • 25 g granulated sugar

Swiss Meringue

  • 3 egg whites, from medium eggs
  • 140 g caster sugar
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1253 kJ / 299 kcal
Protein
7 g
Carbohydrates
51 g
Fat
7 g

Preparation

Baked Peaches

  1. Preheat oven to 170°C. Line a baking tray with baking paper.
  2. Halve peaches and remove stones, then arrange peach halves on prepared baking tray.
  3. Place almonds and sugar in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 10. Divide almond and sugar filling between peach halves. Bake for 12-15 minutes (170°C), until just cooked, depending on ripeness of peaches. Meanwhile, clean and dry mixing bowl thoroughly.

Swiss Meringue

  1. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg whites, caster sugar and salt in mixing bowl then heat 4 min/70°C/speed 2. Remove measuring cup and whisk 5 min/speed 3.5. Remove butterfly whisk. Transfer to a disposable piping bag, cut a 1 cm opening and pipe meringue onto baked peach halves. Use a cook’s blowtorch to brown the outside of the meringue lightly. Spoon over any syrup from the baking tray and serve immediately.

Useful Items

cook's blowtorch, baking tray and baking paper, disposable piping bag or freezer bag, oven
