Rhubarb and strawberry fool

Ingredients

Fruit

  • 1 orange, juice and 2 strips of zest (1 x 4 cm), no white pith
  • 200 g rhubarb, trimmed and cut into pieces (4 cm)
  • 1 green apple, peeled, cored and cut into quarters
  • 60 g raw sugar
  • 150 g strawberries, hulled, plus extra for decorating

Custard

  • 30 g raw sugar
  • 20 g gluten free cornflour
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 300 g full cream milk

Whipped cream

  • 300 g pouring (whipping) cream

Preparation

Fruit

  1. Place orange zest into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add orange juice, rhubarb, apple and sugar and cook 12 min/100°C/speed 1.
  3. Add strawberries and mix 2 min/100°C/speed 1, then purée 15 sec/speed 4. Transfer into a bowl. Cover with plastic wrap and place into refrigerator (approx. 1 hour) until chilled. Meanwhile, continue with custard. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Custard

  1. Place sugar and cornflour into mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 9.
  2. Add vanilla, eggs and milk and cook 10 min/90°C/speed 3, or until thickened. Transfer into a large bowl. Cover with plastic wrap and leave to cool to room temperature (approx. 1 hour). Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Whipped cream

  1. Insert butterfly whisk. Place cream into mixing bowl and whip until soft peaks form/speed 3.5, watching carefully through hole in mixing bowl lid to avoid overwhipping.  Remove butterfly whisk.
  2. Transfer cream into bowl with reserved custard and gently fold together until just combined. Place spoonfuls of custard into the base of 6 serving glasses. Top with spoonfuls of rhubarb purée. Continue layering until glasses are filled. Place into refrigerator until ready to serve. Decorate with strawberries just before serving.

Useful Items

bowl, plastic wrap, large bowl, 6 serving bowls or glasses, refrigerator
