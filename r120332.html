Citrus and spice spider web cheesecake

Ingredients

Cheesecake

  • 150 g pumpkin, peeled and cut into pieces (3-4 cm)
  • 70 g water
  • 60 g unsalted butter, cut into pieces, plus extra for greasing
  • 1 orange, zest (no white pith) and juice
  • 150 g Granita® biscuits
  • 300 g cream cheese, softened
  • 70 g caster sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 pinch grated nutmeg
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract

Spider web

  • 50 g dark chocolate, broken into pieces

Preparation

Cheesecake

  1. Place pumpkin and water into mixing bowl and cook 10 min/100°C/speed 2.
  2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then blend 10 sec/speed 4, or until smooth. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Grease and line a springform cake tin (20 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  4. Place orange zest into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add biscuits and mill 10 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  6. Place butter into mixing bowl and melt 2 min/50°C/speed 2.
  7. Add reserved biscuit mixture and mix 10 sec/speed 2. Transfer into prepared cake tin and press evenly into base. Place into refrigerator to set (approx. 10 minutes). Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  8. Preheat oven to 160°C.
  9. Place cream cheese and sugar into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 3. Scrape down sides and bottom of mixing bowl with spatula.
  10. Add eggs, orange juice, reserved pumpkin purée, cinnamon, nutmeg and vanilla and mix 1 min/speed 4, or until well combined. Transfer into cake tin and bake for 35-40 minutes (160°C), until firm but with a slight wobble in the centre. Turn off oven and open door to let cheesecake cool (approx. 1 hour). Place into refrigerator for a minimum of 4 hours. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Spider web

  1. Place chocolate into mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 10.
  2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then melt 1 min 30 sec/50°C/speed 1. Transfer melted chocolate into a piping bag with a thin nozzle. Remove cake from tin and transfer onto a serving plate. Decorate cake with chocolate into a spider web pattern. Place into refrigerator until ready to serve.

Useful Items

bowl, springform cake tin (20 cm), baking paper, serving plates, serving platter, piping bag (thin nozzle), refrigerator, oven
