Peanut and Soba Noodle Slaw

Ingredients

Slaw

  • 1600 g water
  • 180 g dried soba noodles, broken in half
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 2 spring onions, trimmed and cut in pieces (approx. 30 g)
  • ¼ red cabbage, cut in pieces (approx. 330 g)
  • ¼ white cabbage, cut in pieces (approx. 430 g)
  • 2 carrots, cut in pieces (4 cm) (approx. 200 g)

Peanut Dressing

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled, cut in round slices (2 mm)
  • 100 g peanut butter
  • 60 g rice wine vinegar
  • 45 g sesame oil
  • 60 g soy sauce
  • 2 Tbsp honey

Garnishes

  • 80 g roasted salted peanuts
  • 3 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only
  • 1 Tbsp sesame seeds

Preparation

Slaw

  1. Place water in mixing bowl and boil 12 min/100°C/speed 1 or until water is boiling.
  2. Insert simmering basket. Weigh in soba noodles and cook 3-5 min/100°C/speed 1 until noodles are cooked. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and rinse noodles under cold water to cease cooking.
  3. Allow noodles to drain, then transfer to a large bowl and drizzle with olive oil. Gently toss to ensure oil covers all of the noodles, then set aside to cool completely. Rinse and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Place spring onions in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7.
  5. Add red cabbage and chop 6 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then chop 2-3 sec/speed 5, until an evenly chopped consistency is achieved. Transfer to a serving bowl and set aside.
  6. Place white cabbage in mixing bowl and chop 6 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then chop 2-3 sec/speed 5, until an evenly chopped consistency is achieved. Transfer to serving bowl with red cabbage and set aside.
  7. Place carrots in mixing bowl and chop 3-5 sec/speed 5, until an evenly chopped consistency is achieved. Transfer to serving bowl with cabbage and set aside.

Peanut Dressing

  1. Place garlic and ginger in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add all remaining dressing ingredients and blend 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer to serving bowl with slaw and combine well with spatula. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Garnishes

  1. Place peanuts in mixing bowl and chop 1-3 sec/speed 6, until a roughly chopped consistency is achieved.
  2. Add cooled soba noodles to slaw in serving bowl and gently combine with spatula. Garnish slaw with reserved chopped peanuts in mixing bowl, coriander leaves and sesame seeds, then serve immediately.

Useful Items

large bowl, serving bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • All components of this recipe can easily be made well in advance and stored in the fridge. Dress and assemble salad, as per recipe, just before serving.
