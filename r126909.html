Lentil and spinach soup

Ingredients

  • 50 g onions, cut into quarters
  • 50 g carrots, peeled and cut into slices (0.5 cm)
  • 50 g celery leaves, washed and dried
  • 250 g dried lentils
  • 1 dried bay leaf
  • 1 tbsp vegetable stock paste, homemade
  • 1000 g water
  • 50 g olive oil
  • 200 g fresh spinach leaves, washed and dried
  • 160 g rice (cooking time 16 minutes)
  • ½ tsp salt (optional)

Preparation

  1. Place onion, carrot and celery into mixing bowl, chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add lentils, bay leaves, stock cube and water and cook 20 min/100°C//speed 1, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid to help prevent splashing.
  3. Add olive oil, spinach, rice and season if necessary, cook 16 min/100°C//speed 1, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup. Check if rice is cooked and prolong cooking time if necessary.
  4. Transfer to a soup tureen or large serving bowl and serve hot.
