Chicken Tagine with Prunes, Figs and Honey

Ingredients

  • 100 g onions, quartered
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 600 g water
  • 550 g chicken thighs, boneless and skinless, cut in pieces (3-4 cm)
  • 12 prunes, pitted (approx. 120 g)
  • 8 dried figs, halved if large (approx. 120 g) (optional)
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt, or to taste
  • ¼ tsp ground pepper, or to taste
  • ¼ tsp ground coriander
  • ¼ tsp ground cumin
  • 250 g couscous
  • 100 g raisins
  • 60 g honey, or to taste
  • 50 g blanched almonds

Preparation

  1. Place onions, garlic and 1 Tbsp oil in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then, without measuring cup, sauté 3 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  2. Add 300 g water. Insert simmering basket and weigh in chicken, prunes, figs, ¼ tsp salt, pepper, coriander and cumin. Mix well by hand with spatula then steam 20 min/Varoma/speed . Meanwhile, place couscous, remaining 1 Tbsp olive oil and remaining ¼ tsp salt in a bowl and mix with a fork. Add raisins and remaining 300 g water then allow to stand for a few minutes.
  3. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula. Add honey and almonds to contents of mixing bowl. Reinsert simmering basket then mix contents together. Place Varoma dish into position, insert Varoma tray then transfer couscous to tray. Cover and steam 10-15 min/Varoma/speed 1 until chicken is cooked.
  4. Break up couscous with a fork and place in a serving dish. Arrange chicken and fruit around couscous, transfer honey sauce to a serving jug then serve warm with sauce on the side.

Useful Items

serving jug, serving dish

Hints & Tricks

  • If you would like a less sweet sauce, decrease honey and add a little lemon juice to taste.
  • Add dried apricots instead of prunes or figs.
