Pollock with Potatoes, Savoy Cabbage and Citrus Sauce

Ingredients

  • 500 g vegetable stock
  • 1 tsp oil
  • 600 - 700 g potatoes, cut in cubes (3 cm)
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • ¾ tsp ground black pepper
  • ½ tsp paprika, sweet
  • ½ tsp curry powder
  • 600 g pollock, cut in cubes (3 cm)
  • 250 g savoy cabbage, shredded (1 cm)
  • 100 g cream cheese
  • 2 - 3 oranges, juice (200 g) plus thin peelings of ½ orange
  • 15 g cornflour (starch)
  • ½ tsp ground turmeric

Preparation

  1. Line Varoma dish with baking paper, leaving some holes uncovered for steam to circulate, then set aside.
  2. Place stock and oil in mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket and weigh potatoes into it, then cook 8 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. On a plate mix together ½ tsp salt, ½ tsp pepper, paprika and curry powder.  Place plate on mixing bowl lid, then coat fish in spice mix.
  4. Transfer fish to prepared Varoma dish, cover and place Varoma into position and steam 4 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  5. Insert Varoma tray and weigh in cabbage. Cover Varoma and steam again 13 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  6. Set Varoma and simmering basket aside. Empty mixing bowl, reserving steaming liquid.
  7. Place 200 g reserved steaming liquid, cream cheese, peel from ½ orange, orange juice, cornflour, ground turmeric, ½ tsp salt, ¼ tsp pepper in mixing bowl then mix 5 sec/speed 5. Cook 4 min/100°C/speed 2. Adjust seasoning to taste then serve sauce with fish, potatoes and cabbage.
