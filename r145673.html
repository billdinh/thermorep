Rice milk

Ingredients

  • 100 g brown rice
  • 60 g raw almonds (see Tips)
  • 1400 g water, plus extra for soaking
  • 45 g coconut oil (optional)
  • 50 g honey (optional)
  • ½ tsp salt

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh rice and almonds into it. Cover with warm water and set aside to soak overnight.
  2. Drain rice and almonds. Place 1000 g of the water, soaked rice and almonds into mixing bowl and blend 1 min/speed 9.
  3. Add coconut oil (optional), honey (optional) and salt and cook 5 min/60°C/speed 4, then blend 1 min/speed 9.
  4. Add remaining 400 g water and mix 5 sec/speed 5. Place a nut milk bag or muslin cloth over a jug and strain milk through it. Discard solids and set milk aside to cool, then use as needed.

Useful Items

bowl, nut milk bag or muslin cloth, jug

Hints & Tricks

  • For a nut free alternative, omit the almonds and increase the brown rice to 160 g.
  • You can replace the almonds with cashews in this recipe - it will yield a smoother, creamier milk.
  • For a quicker version, soak the almonds and rice in boiling water for 30 minutes (your milk will have a milder flavour compared to soaking it overnight).
