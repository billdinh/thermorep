Turkey Lasagna Rolls with Pumpkin Sauce

Ingredients

  • 12 dried lasagna noodles

Pumpkin Sauce

  • 3 ½ oz Parmesan cheese, cubed
  • 3 ½ oz mozzarella cheese, cubed
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 oz all-purpose flour
  • 1 oz unsalted butter, cubed
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 16 oz small sugar pumpkin, peeled, de-seeded and cubed (1 in.)
  • 10 oz chicken stock
  • 3 oz heavy whipping cream
  • 1 oz maple syrup

Turkey Filling

  • 4 oz yellow onion, in pieces
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 3 fresh sage leaves, large
  • ½ oz extra virgin olive oil
  • ½ tsp salt, to taste
  • 20 oz ground turkey, broken in pieces
  • 12 oz ricotta cheese

Lasagna

  • 2 oz fresh baby spinach

Preparation

  1. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions in a large saucepan on stove top. Meanwhile, make Pumpkin Sauce.

Pumpkin Sauce

  1. Place Parmesan and mozzarella cheeses into mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place garlic, flour, butter, salt and pepper into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and sauté 3 min/250°F/speed .
  3. Add pumpkin and stock and cook 12 min/212°F/speed 1.
  4. Add whipping cream, maple syrup and half the reserved grated cheese and blend 1 min/speed 4-8 increasing speed gradually. Transfer a quarter of the sauce to a square baking pan (9 in. x 9 in.) transfer remaining sauce to a bowl and set aside. Cover with cling wrap touching the surface to prevent a skin from forming. Clean mixing bowl and preheat oven to 375°F.

Turkey Filling

  1. Place onion, garlic, sage, olive oil and salt into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and sauté 3 min/212°F/speed 1.
  2. Add turkey and cook 5 min/212°F//speed . Strain mixture through simmering basket and discard liquid. Place drained turkey mixture back into mixing bowl, add ricotta and mix 1 min//speed 1. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.

Lasagna

  1. Lay cooked noodles on a work surface. Divide Turkey Filling onto lasagna noodles with a spoon, spreading along entire surface. Lay spinach leaves over top of filling. Roll up noodles with filling and place in prepared baking pan, standing upright. Pour half the remaining sauce over noodle rolls. Sprinkle with remaining grated cheese and bake 35 minutes (375°F). Serve warm with remaining sauce on the side.

Useful Items

square baking pan (9 in. x 9 in.), large saucepan, cling wrap, oven, stove top

Hints & Tricks

  • Garnish with pomegranate arils, pumpkin seeds or pine nuts.
