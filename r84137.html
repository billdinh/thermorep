Sea Bass with Lemon & Herb Couscous

Ingredients

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 25 g spring onion, cut in pieces
  • 5 g fresh parsley
  • 5 g fresh mint
  • 1 lemon, plus thin peelings of ½ lemon
  • 100 g couscous
  • 120 g water, boiling
  • 100 g cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 2 pinches sea salt
  • 2 pinches ground pepper
  • 2 sea bass fillets
  • ½ tsp olive oil
  • 1000 g water, for steaming
  • 150 g tenderstem broccoli

Preparation

  1. Place garlic, spring onion, parsley, mint and lemon zest in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then chop again 3 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add couscous and boiling water then mix 2 min//speed .
  3. Add tomatoes, salt and pepper then mix well with spatula.
  4. Cut out 2 sheets of baking paper (approx. 40 cm length) and divide couscous between them. Place a sea bass fillet on each, drizzle with olive oil then a pinch of salt and pepper and place 3 slices of lemon on top. Bring paper together to create a parcel, leaving room for steam to circulate, then transfer to Varoma dish.
  5. Place water in mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish into position and insert Varoma tray. Weigh tenderstem broccoli into Varoma tray, then cover and steam 20 min/Varoma/speed 2. Serve fish and couscous in the parcel accompanied by the broccoli.

Useful Items

baking paper

Hints & Tricks

  • Other types of fish can be used; the parcels may need cooking for longer depending on thickness of fish fillets used.

  • The fish and couscous parcels can be prepared ahead, kept in the fridge and then cooked just before eating.
