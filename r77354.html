Crunchy Granola

Ingredients

  • 600 g jumbo oats
  • 60 g desiccated coconut
  • 100 g wheat germ
  • 50 g sunflower seeds
  • 70 g sesame seeds
  • 30 g pumpkin seeds
  • 200 g mixed nuts (e.g. hazelnuts, pecans, walnuts, cashews, pistachios, almonds, peanuts)
  • 75 g linseeds
  • 250 g raisins
  • 180 g spelt flakes
  • 200 g soft dried apricots
  • 100 g honey, runny
  • 220 g coconut oil
  • 60 g milk
  • 100 g barley malt extract
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
697 kJ / 167 kcal
Protein
4.1 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 170°C and line two large baking trays with baking paper.
  2. Place a large bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh into it rolled oats, desiccated coconut, wheat germ, sunflower seeds, sesame seeds, pumpkin seeds, mixed nuts, linseeds, raisins and spelt flakes then set bowl aside.
  3. Place apricots in mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 6. Transfer to bowl of dry ingredients.
  4. Place honey, coconut oil, milk, barley malt extract and salt in mixing bowl then heat 5 min/37°C/speed 2. Pour over dry ingredients in bowl and stir well with spatula.
  5. Spread out granola on prepared baking trays. Bake one baking tray after another, each for 20-25 minutes (170°C), stirring gently two or three times until dried out. Set aside and let cool completely. Once cold transfer to airtight jars or containers and store until needed.

Useful Items

2 baking trays and baking paper, large bowl, air-tight jars or containers, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Perfect served on its own or with milk or yoghurt and fresh fruit.
  • Sprinkle over vanilla ice cream for a quick dessert.

  • This recipe is very flexible, so feel free to adapt it depending on what ingredients you have to hand.
  • To make a smaller quantity, halve the ingredients.
