Tuna with Tomato Sauce, Capers and Olives

Ingredients

  • 40 g extra virgin olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 400 g tuna, cut in pieces (3 cm x 3 cm)
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
  • 1 Tbsp dried oregano, plus 1 pinch for sprinkling
  • 350 g ripe tomatoes, peeled and quartered
  • 50 g water
  • 15 g pickled capers, drained
  • 20 green olives, pitted

Preparation

  1. Grease simmering basket with oil. Place basket on mixing bowl lid and weigh in tuna pieces then sprinkle with salt and 1 pinch oregano. Remove basket from mixing bowl lid and set aside.
  2. Place oil and 1 Tbsp oregano in mixing bowl then fry 5 min/120°C/speed 1.
  3. Add tomatoes, water, capers and olives. Insert prepared simmering basket and cook 15 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula. Place tuna in a serving dish and pour over sauce. Serve immediately.

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with chips or boiled potatoes.
  • You can make the recipe with other fish such as cod, swordfish or monkfish.
