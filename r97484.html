Mini Vegetable Frittatas

Ingredients

  • 40 g olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 15 g fresh flat-leaf parsley leaves
  • 50 g Parmesan cheese
  • 100 g shallots, halved
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 150 g aubergines, cut in pieces
  • 150 g courgettes, cut in pieces
  • 200 g red peppers, cut in pieces
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 125 g double cream
  • 8 medium eggs
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • ½ tsp Chinese five spice
Nutrition
per 1 piece

165 kJ / 40 kcal

1.7 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Grease 2 mini muffin tins (24 holes each).
  2. Place parsley in mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a small bowl and set aside.
  3. Place Parmesan in mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a small bowl and set aside.
  4. Place oil, shallots and garlic in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add aubergines, courgettes and peppers then chop 6 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then sauté 6 min/Varoma//speed 2. Strain through a sieve then place vegetables back in mixing bowl.
  6. Add reserved chopped parsley and ½ tsp salt then mix 5 sec//speed 3. Divide vegetables between tart holes.
  7. Place cream, eggs, ½ tsp salt, pepper, five spice and reserved ground Parmesan in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 4.
  8. Pour egg mixture into tart holes over vegetables. Bake for 15-20 minutes (180°C). Cool for 5 minutes before turning out onto a plate to serve.

Useful Items

2 mini muffin tins (24 holes each), fine sieve, oven
