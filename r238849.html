Veggie and Fruit Muffins

Ingredients

  • 5 oz zucchini, cut into pieces (1 in.)
  • 3 oz carrot, cut into pieces (1 in.), (approx. 1 carrot)
  • 3 oz apple, quartered (approx. 1 sm.)
  • 2 ½ oz coconut oil
  • 6 oz coconut sugar
  • 1 oz water
  • 4 oz banana, (approx. 1 banana)
  • 7 oz whole wheat flour
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • ½ tsp ground nutmeg
  • ½ tsp ground ginger
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 ¼ tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
761 kJ / 182 kcal
Protein
3 g
Carbohydrates
31 g
Fat
7 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F. Line a muffin pan with muffin liners.
  2. Place zucchini, carrot and apple into mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place coconut oil and coconut sugar into mixing bowl and blend 30 sec/speed 4.
  4. Add water, banana, flour, vanilla, nutmeg, ginger, cinnamon, baking powder and salt and mix 10 sec/speed 4.
  5. Add reserved vegetable mixture and mix 5 sec//speed 4. Fill muffin liners ¾ full and bake for 20-25 minutes (375°F) or until golden brown. Store in an airtight container before serving or packing lunches.

Useful Items

12 hole muffin pan, muffin liners/baking cups, airtight container, oven
