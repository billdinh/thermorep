Root Vegetable Hotpot with Harissa

Ingredients

  • 25 g olive oil
  • 80 g onions, quartered
  • ½ Tbsp fresh thyme, leaves only
  • 1 - 2 tsp harissa paste, to taste
  • 1 vegetable stock cube (for 0.5 l), crumbled
  • 600 g mixed root vegetables, peeled, cut in pieces (3-4 cm) (e.g. carrots, parsnips, swede, sweet potatoes, see tip)
  • 400 g water
  • 240 g tinned haricot beans, drained, rinsed (1 x 400 g tin)
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 5 g fresh parsley leaves, roughly chopped

Preparation

  1. Place oil and onions in mixing bowl, chop 3 sec/speed 5 then sauté 8 min/100°C/speed 0.5.
  2. Add thyme, harissa, vegetable stock, root vegetables and water then stir 3 min/100°C//speed 0.5. Simmer 15 min/98°C//speed  until vegetables are just tender.
  3. Add beans, salt and pepper then simmer 5 min/95°C//speed . Sprinkle with parsley and serve.
