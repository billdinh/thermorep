Asian Steamed Side of Salmon

Ingredients

Marinade

  • 40 g sesame oil
  • 40 g light soy sauce
  • 25 g lime juice, freshly squeezed
  • 30 g fresh root ginger, peeled, cut in round slices (2 mm)
  • 15 g fresh coriander, plus extra for garnish
  • 3 spring onions, cut in pieces
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 fresh red chilli, deseeded if desired
  • 800 g fresh salmon fillet (1 large fillet)

Cooking

  • 1000 g water

Preparation

Marinade

  1. Place oil, soy sauce, ginger, coriander, spring onions, garlic and red chilli in mixing bowl then chop 10 sec/speed 8.
  2. Place salmon in a shallow bowl then pour marinade over. Coat well, then cover and set aside in fridge to marinate for 1-4 hours.

Cooking

  1. After this time, place marinated salmon and all marinade on a large sheet of baking paper, fold and seal to form a parcel. Arrange chopsticks or a trivet in Varoma dish then place salmon parcel on top.
  2. Place water in mixing bowl. Place covered Varoma into position then steam 25-30 min/Varoma/speed 2 until fish is cooked through.
  3. Remove salmon and juices from parcel and transfer to a serving plate. Set aside to cool then place in fridge until needed.
  4. Scatter with fresh coriander leaves and serve cold.

Useful Items

baking paper, chopsticks, serving plate, cling film, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Check salmon is cooked through by making a cut with a knife in the centre of the fish - it should be light pink and no longer translucent.
  • Serves 8 as part of a large buffet, or 4 as a main course with sides.
  • Can also be enjoyed hot.
