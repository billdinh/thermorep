Mini Crab cakes with Cilantro Pesto

Ingredients

Crab Cakes

  • 2 ½ oz yellow bell peppers, stem and seeds removed, cut into pieces
  • 2 oz green onions, cut into pieces (1 in.)
  • 10 sprigs thyme, leaves only
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 12 oz canned crab meat (approx. 3 x 6.5 oz), drained
  • 3 oz bread crumbs
  • 1 oz sour cream
  • 1 large egg

Cilantro Sauce

  • ½ oz cilantro leaves
  • ½ oz mint leaves
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 jalapeño pepper, stem and seeds removed
  • 2 ½ oz avocado
  • 2 ½ oz coconut cream
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • ¼ tsp ground cumin
  • ¼ tsp sugar (optional)
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 3 - 4 tbsp vegetable oil, to fry

Preparation

Crab Cakes

  1. Line a baking sheet with parchment and set aside.
  2. Place peppers, onions, thyme, salt and pepper into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 6.
  3. Add crab, bread crumbs, sour cream and egg and mix 10 sec/speed 3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix 3 sec/speed 4.
  4. Form crab mixture into 30 flat rounds (approx. 1 tbsp) and place onto prepared baking sheet. Refrigerate 30 minutes. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl and make Cilantro Sauce.

Cilantro Sauce

  1. Place cilantro, mint, garlic and jalapeño into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add avocado, coconut cream, lime juice, cumin, sugar and salt and mix 10 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl, cover with cling wrap and refrigerate until needed.
  3. Heat oil in a large non-stick pan over medium heat. Working in batches, fry crab cakes until golden brown (approx. 2-3 min. per side). Garnish each Crab Cake with ½ tsp Cilantro Sauce and serve warm.

Useful Items

baking sheet, parchment paper, cling wrap, non-stick pan, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Replace canned crab with fresh lump crab meat when available.
