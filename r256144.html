Salmon with yoghurt dressing

Ingredients

Yoghurt dressing

  • 1 garlic clove
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 200 g yoghurt
  • 50 g tahini
  • ½ lemon, juice only

Salmon

  • 1000 g water
  • 1 fresh salmon fillet (approx. 1 kg), skin on
  • 1 - 2 pinches salt
  • 1 - 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Walnut crumble

  • 80 g walnuts
  • 20 g fresh coriander, leaves only (approx. 1 bunch)
  • 2 sprigs fresh mint, leaves only
  • 1 - 2 fresh red chillies, deseeded if preferred and cut into halves
  • ¼ brown onion
  • 30 g lemon juice
  • 1 tsp sumac
  • 2 tsp oil
  • salt, to taste
  • ground black pepper, to taste

Preparation

Yoghurt dressing

  1. Place garlic into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add salt, yoghurt and tahini and blend 10 sec/speed 4, adding lemon juice through hole in mixing bowl lid. Transfer into a sealable container and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Salmon

  1. Place water into mixing bowl and place Varoma into position. Place salmon fillet onto a large piece of wet, well-wrung baking paper. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and drizzle with oil. Tightly wrap salmon in damp baking paper and place into Varoma dish. Secure Varoma lid and steam 20-30 min/Varoma/speed 2, until cooked to your liking (see Tips). Remove Varoma, unwrap salmon and place, skin side up, onto a plate. Peel away skin, gently scrape off grey flesh and discard. Rinse and dry mixing bowl.

Walnut crumble

  1. Place walnuts into mixing bowl and dry roast 8-10 min/Varoma/speed 1. Transfer into a small bowl and set aside.
  2. Place coriander, mint and chillies into mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add onion, lemon juice, sumac, oil and reserved walnuts and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Season as necessary with extra salt and pepper.
  4. To serve, spread half of the yoghurt dressing over salmon, then spoon walnut crumble over the dressing. Serve with extra yoghurt on the side.

Useful Items

baking paper, sealable container, plate, small bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • Cooking times in the Varoma may vary depending on the size and thickness of your salmon side (or if you are using salmon steaks or fillets).
  • You can serve this dish warm or chilled.
