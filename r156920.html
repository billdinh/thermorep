White BBQ Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp black peppercorns
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 11 oz mayonnaise
  • 2 oz water
  • 2 oz rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp Creole mustard
  • 2 tsp prepared horseradish
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
Nutrition
per 1 Total recipe
Calories
4866 kJ / 1163 kcal
Protein
5 g
Carbohydrates
61 g

Preparation

  1. Place peppercorns and garlic into mixing bowl and chop 8 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add mayonnaise, water, vinegar, mustard, horseradish, salt and sugar and mix 1 min/speed 4. Transfer sauce to a bowl and serve immediately or transfer into an airtight jar and store in refrigerator.

Useful Items

bowl, airtight jar, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • If you prefer a thicker sauce omit or reduce the water.
  • To use when grilling chicken: brush lightly over chicken during the last few minutes of grilling. This sauce can also be used to dip or to pour over roasted or grilled chicken.
  • This delicious White BBQ Sauce can also be used on pork or fish, drizzled on baked potatoes, or as a condiment for burgers.
