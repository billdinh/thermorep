English Muffin Bread

Ingredients

  • unsalted butter, to grease and serve
  • 3 tsp cornmeal, divided
  • 12 ½ oz whole milk
  • 1 packet/envelope dried instant yeast
  • 13 ½ oz bread flour
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 pinch baking soda
  • 20 oz water
  • jam, to serve
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
623 kJ / 149 kcal
Protein
5 g
Carbohydrates
26 g
Fat
3 g

Preparation

  1. Lightly grease a loaf pan (approx. 9 in. x 5 in.) and line the bottom with parchment. Sprinkle pan with 2 tsp cornmeal and set aside.
  2. Place milk and yeast into mixing bowl and heat 3 min/120°F/speed 1.
  3. Add flour, sugar, salt and baking soda and mix 10 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix a further 10 sec/speed 5. Transfer dough to prepared pan and smooth top with spatula. Sprinkle top of dough with remaining 1 tsp cornmeal. Place pan into Varoma (see Tip).
  4. Place water into mixing bowl, set Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and proof 35 min/150°F/speed 2, until almost doubled in size. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  5. Bake for 60-70 minutes (350°F), rotating pan after 40 minutes of baking. Cool in pan for 10 minutes before transferring to a wire rack. Cool completely. Slice, toast and serve with butter and jam.

Useful Items

parchment paper, wire rack, loaf pan (approx. 9 in. x 5 in.), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Grease your spatula to help smooth top of dough.
  • All pans differ in size and shape. Ensure the pan you're using fits into Varoma dish and Varoma lid closes properly.
