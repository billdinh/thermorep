Broccoli and Cheese Stromboli

Ingredients

Dough

  • 1 oz olive oil, plus extra to grease
  • 15 oz bread flour
  • 2 ½ oz whole wheat flour
  • 9 oz warm water (100°F-110°F)
  • ½ oz sugar
  • 2 ¼ tsp rapid rise yeast
  • 2 tsp salt

Filling

  • 4 oz provolone cheese, cut into 1 in. pieces
  • 1 oz fresh basil leaves
  • 20 oz water
  • 8 oz broccoli florets, cut into 2 in. pieces
  • 6 oz ricotta cheese
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • &frac18; tsp fresh ground pepper
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried basil (optional)

Preparation

Dough

  1. Line baking sheets with parchment paper and lightly grease a large bowl and set aside.
  2. Place bread flour, whole wheat flour, water, sugar, yeast, 1 oz olive oil and salt into mixing bowl and knead 2 min/.
  3. Form dough into a ball and transfer into prepared bowl. Cover with cling wrap and let dough rise until doubled in size (approx. 1 hr.). Clean mixing bowl.

Filling

  1. Place provolone cheese and basil into mixing bowl and grate 3 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place water into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket, add broccoli and steam 10 min/Varoma/speed 3. Remove simmering basket and set aside to cool slightly. Discard water and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Place, ricotta, reserved provolone and basil mixture, steamed broccoli, salt and pepper into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 4.5. Preheat the oven to 450°F.
  4. Divide dough into four pieces and place two pieces of dough onto each prepared baking sheet. Evenly stretch out dough into four ovals (approx. 7 in. x 8 in.) with lightly floured fingertips. Place a fourth of the reserved broccoli mixture down the center of each dough in a log shape, leaving a 2 inch border all around. Fold short sides of dough over the filling, then bring long sides together and pinch to seal.
  5. Place each stromboli seam side down on baking sheets. Cut 3 small slits in top of dough to vent, brush with olive oil and sprinkle with basil. Bake stromboli 12-15 minutes (450°F) or until golden brown. Serve warm.

Useful Items

pastry brush, parchment paper, cling wrap, large bowl, baking sheets, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with Marinara Dipping Sauce.
