Beef and coconut soup

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • 2 eschalots
  • 1 carrot, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 3 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 1 fresh bird's eye chilli, trimmed
  • 200 g fresh shiitake mushrooms, cut into slices
  • 400 g coconut cream
  • 10 g palm sugar
  • 2 tsp fish sauce
  • 2 tsp soy sauce
  • 500 g water
  • 1 tbsp Vegetable stock paste (see Tips)
  • 100 g bok choy, trimmed and cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 300 g beef fillet steak, trimmed and cut into thin slices (5 mm - see Tips)
  • 1 lime, zested and juiced
  • fresh bean sprouts, to serve
  • 2 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only, to serve
  • fried shallots, to serve

Preparation

  1. Place oil, eschalots, carrot, ginger and chilli into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add mushrooms and sauté 2 min/120°C//speed 1.
  3. Add coconut cream, palm sugar, fish sauce, soy sauce, water and stock paste and cook 5 min/120°C//speed 2.
  4. Add bok choy and cook 2 min/120°C//speed 2.
  5. Add steak and cook 2 min/120°C//speed 2.
  6. Add lime zest and juice and stir 20 sec//speed 2. Divide soup between bowls, then top with bean sprouts, coriander leaves and fried shallots to serve.

Hints & Tricks

  • Please refer to The Basic Cookbook for the Vegetable stock paste recipe.
  • You can replace the fillet steak with rump steak, if desired.
