Carrot Cake with Cream Cheese Frosting

Ingredients

Carrot Cake

  • unsalted butter, to grease
  • 2 oz pecans
  • 12 oz carrots, cut into pieces (approx. 6 carrots)
  • 9 oz all-purpose flour
  • 2 ½ oz brown sugar
  • 9 oz sugar
  • 1 tbsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • 1 tsp ground nutmeg
  • ¼ tsp ground cloves
  • &frac18; tsp ground cardamom
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 3 large eggs
  • 4 oz grapeseed oil
  • 1 can pineapple chunks (approx. 14 oz), drained
  • 6 oz buttermilk
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • ½ tsp salt

Cream Cheese Frosting

  • 5 ½ oz unsalted butter, cut into pieces
  • 8 oz cream cheese, cut into pieces
  • 8 oz confectioners sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla

Preparation

Carrot Cake

  1. Preheat oven 350°F. Grease and line 2 round cake pans with parchment and set aside.
  2. Place nuts into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place carrots into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  4. Add flour, brown sugar, sugar, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, cloves, cardamom, baking soda, baking powder, eggs, oil, pineapple, buttermilk, vanilla and salt and mix 10 sec/speed 6.
  5. Divide batter into prepared cake pans and bake 40-50 minutes (350°F) or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Set cakes aside to cool while making frosting.

Cream Cheese Frosting

  1. Place butter, cream cheese, confectioners sugar and vanilla into mixing bowl and mix 20 sec/speed 5.
  2. Place one cake layer on a serving plate. Frost the top of the base cake with an icing spatula. Add second cake layer and frost top and sides. Carefully press reserved chopped nuts up the sides of the cake. Refrigerate in an airtight container until ready to serve.

Useful Items

round cake pans (9 in.), parchment paper, serving plate, airtight container, icing spatula, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Use a 9 inch springform pan if two 9 inch cake pans are not available.
  • This recipe can also be made into a 3 layer cake if you have access to three 9 inch cake pans. Frosting will need to be increased by half when adding a third layer to the cake.
  • Replace canned pineapple with 9 oz ripe fresh pineapple cut into chunks (approx. ¾ in.).
  • Add 3.5 oz chopped walnuts or pecans to batter with the carrots.
