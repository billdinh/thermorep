Varoma Pizza

Ingredients

  • 90 g Gouda cheese, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • ½ tsp dried instant yeast
  • 125 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 60 g milk
  • 5 g olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 100 g ajvar (see tip)
  • ½ tsp pizza herb mix
  • 700 g water

Preparation

  1. Generously grease 6 Thermomix® ramekins size 1.
  2. Place Gouda in mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place yeast, flour, milk, oil, baking powder and salt in mixing bowl then knead 1 min 30 sec/. Tip crumbly dough out onto a floured surface and shape into a ball. Divide into 6 equal-sized pieces (approx. 30 g each), place one piece in each prepared ramekin and press down with fingers.
  4. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in ajvar. Add pizza herb mix and stir to combine. Top each dough piece with ajvar mixture and sprinkle with reserved grated Gouda. Cover each ramekin with heat-resistant cling film and place in Varoma dish. Clean mixing bowl.
  5. Place water in mixing bowl, place covered Varoma into position and steam 35 min/Varoma/speed 2. Remove Varoma, carefully remove cling film, unmould pizzas with a table knife and serve warm.

Useful Items

heat-resistant cling film

Hints & Tricks

  • Before steaming, add your favourite toppings (e.g. vegetables, salami, ham).
  • Ajvar is a relish made principally from roasted red peppers, with aubergine, garlic and chilli pepper. It is available from large supermarkets or online.
  • Serve Varoma pizza with a salad.
