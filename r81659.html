Chorizo and chicken pasta

Ingredients

  • 700 g skinless chicken breast fillets, cut into strips (approx. 5 x 2 cm)
  • 250 g chorizo sausage (approx. 2), cut into slices (5 mm)
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 1 brown onion (approx.150 g), cut into halves
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 - 2 long red chillies, deseeded if preferred
  • ½ red capsicum (approx. 90 g), deseeded and cut into pieces
  • 1 carrot (approx. 160 g), cut into pieces
  • 150 g zucchini, cut into pieces
  • 90 g tomato paste
  • 400 g canned diced tomatoes
  • ½ tsp sea salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 180 g shell pasta
  • 200 g water
  • 2 tsp Chicken stock paste (see Tips)
  • 90 g red wine

Preparation

  1. Place Varoma dish and tray into position, then weigh chicken into dish and onto tray. Set Varoma aside.
  2. Place chorizo and oil into mixing bowl and cook 6 min/120°C//speed , placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place onion, garlic, chilli, capsicum, carrot and zucchini into mixing bowl and chop 3-4 sec/speed 7, until an evenly chopped consistency is achieved. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add tomato paste, canned tomatoes, salt and pepper to mixing bowl. Place Varoma with chicken into position, secure Varoma lid and cook 10 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  5. Stir chicken in Varoma with spatula to ensure even cooking, then secure Varoma lid and cook for a further 5 min/Varoma/speed 1. Stir chicken with spatula again. Set Varoma aside.
  6. Add pasta, water, stock paste, wine and reserved chorizo to mixing bowl. Place Varoma with chicken back into position, secure Varoma lid and cook 10 min/100°C//speed .
  7. Transfer pasta and chicken into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and gently stir using spatula to combine. Cover pasta and allow to rest for 5 minutes, then serve hot.
