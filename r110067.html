Layered Caramel Flan

Ingredients

  • 2 oz liquid caramel, room temperature
  • 7 oz sweetened condensed milk
  • 7 oz evaporated milk
  • 4 eggs, divided
  • 2 tsp natural vanilla extract, divided
  • 2 oz sugar
  • ½ oz cocoa powder (approx. 2 tbsp)
  • 1 oz all-purpose flour
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 53 oz water

per 1 portion

Preparation

  1. Place a small Bundt pan into the Varoma (be sure it fits). Pour liquid caramel into Bundt pan and set aside Varoma with Bundt pan.
  2. Place sweetened condensed milk, evaporated milk, 2 eggs and 1 tsp vanilla into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 5. Pour mixture into Bundt pan over the liquid caramel and set aside.
  3. Place 2 remaining eggs, sugar and 1 tsp vanilla into the mixing bowl and mix 1 min/100°F/speed 4.
  4. Add cocoa powder, flour, salt and mix 10 sec/speed 3. Pour mixture carefully into Bundt pan over the flan mixture, cover the pan with foil and secure Varoma lid. Clean mixing bowl.
  5. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position and steam 80 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  6. Remove Varoma and allow flan to cool to room temperature (approx. 45 minutes) then remove foil and refrigerate 4 hours. Unmold onto cake plate and serve cold.

Useful Items

cake plate, aluminum foil, cup Bundt pan, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • To make your own liquid caramel, melt 7 oz sugar with 2 tbsp water and 2 tsp lemon juice in a medium-size, very clean saucepan over medium-high heat. Do not stir, but occasionally swirl the saucepan until sugar becomes a fairly dark amber. At this point, very carefully pour in approx. 3.5 oz hot water (it will bubble up and spatter). Stir over medium heat until all caramel dissolves and thickens slightly. This caramel will keep for weeks refrigerated in a sealed jar.
