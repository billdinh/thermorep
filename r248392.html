Pasta with Anchovies and Breadcrumbs

Ingredients

  • 6 sprigs mixed fresh herbs (e.g. basil, parsley, coriander)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 30 g olive oil, plus extra for frying
  • 180 g breadcrumbs
  • 50 g tomato purée
  • 1235 g water
  • 15 g tinned anchovies
  • 350 g dried spaghetti

Preparation

  1. Place mixed herbs in mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place garlic in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add 30 g oil and cook 8 min/120°C/speed 1. Meanwhile, heat 1 Tbsp olive oil in a frying pan and toast breadcrumbs until golden brown then set aside.
  4. Add tomato purée, 235 g water and anchovies to mixing bowl then cook 10 min/80°C/speed 1. Blend 10 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  5. Place 1000 g water in mixing bowl and, without measuring cup, bring to the boil 4 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  6. Add pasta through hole in mixing bowl lid and, without measuring cup, cook for time indicated on packet/100°C//speed 1. Drain through Varoma dish.
  7. Place cooked pasta and reserved anchovy sauce in mixing bowl then cook 1 min/100°C/speed . Transfer to serving bowls, garnish with toasted breadcrumbs and reserved chopped herbs then serve with the extra breadcrumbs on the side.

Hints & Tricks

  • This is an ancient Sicilian recipe, created at a time when Parmesan or similar hard cheese was not readily available. Grated Parmesan can be used instead of breadcrumbs.
