Lemon Poppy Seed Loaf

Ingredients

Bread

  • 4 oz unsalted butter, room temperature, plus extra to grease
  • 7 oz sugar
  • 1 lemon, thin peel only, no pith, plus 1 oz juice
  • 8 oz all-purpose flour
  • 5 oz sour cream
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp poppy seeds

Glaze

  • 2 oz confectioners sugar
  • 1 tbsp whole milk

Preparation

Bread

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Lightly grease loaf pan (approx. 9 in. x 5 in.) and line the bottom with parchment.
  2. Place sugar and lemon peel into mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 10.
  3. Add butter and mix 1 min/speed 3.5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add flour, sour cream, eggs, baking soda and salt and mix 15 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add 1 oz lemon juice and poppy seeds and mix 15 sec/speed 5. Transfer batter to prepared pan and smooth top with spatula. Bake for 55-60 minutes (350°F), rotating pan after 40 minutes. Let rest in pan for 10 minutes before transferring to a wire rack. Allow to cool before glazing.

Glaze

  1. Place a small bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in confectioners sugar, add milk and stir to combine. Drizzle glaze over cooled loaf, slice and serve.

Useful Items

parchment paper, wire rack, loaf pan (approx. 9 in. x 5 in.), oven
