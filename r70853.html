Tartare sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 pickled gherkins (approx. 20 g)
  • 1 sprig fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 3 - 4 fresh chives or 1 tsp dried chives
  • 1 egg
  • ½ tsp mustard powder
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • ½ tsp fresh tarragon, leaves only or 1/4 tsp dried tarragon
  • ½ tsp sea salt
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • 2 tsp white wine vinegar
  • 200 g grapeseed oil
  • 2 tsp pickled capers, drained

Preparation

  1. Place gherkin into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place parsley and chives into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add egg, mustard powder, lemon juice, tarragon, salt, pepper and vinegar to mixing bowl. Place a jug onto mixing bowl lid and weigh oil into it and set aside.
  4. Mix 2-3 min/speed 5, slowly pouring oil onto mixing bowl lid, letting it drizzle into mixing bowl to emulsify. Take care not to add oil too quickly, as this may cause the sauce to split. Scrape down sides of bowl with spatula.
  5. Add capers and reserved gherkin and combine 5 sec//speed 3.
  6. Use immediately or transfer into a sealable jar or container and refrigerate until use.
