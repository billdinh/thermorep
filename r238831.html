Hummus with Peas and Beans

Ingredients

  • 2 oz sesame seeds
  • 2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 11 oz frozen peas
  • 3 oz olive oil, divided, plus extra to garnish
  • 2 oz water
  • 2 tsp sugar (optional)
  • 1 oz freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 oz Greek yogurt
  • 1 can cannellini beans (approx. 15 oz) drained and rinsed, reserve a few beans to garnish
  • salt, to taste

Preparation

  1. Place sesame and cumin seeds into mixing bowl and without measuring cup toast 4 min/250°F/speed 1. Remove ½ tsp toasted seed mixture for garnish and set aside. With remaining toasted mixture, grind 50 sec/speed 10. Set aside in a separate bowl.
  2. Place peas, 2 oz olive oil, water and sugar into mixing bowl and blend 5 sec/speed 10, then cook 3 min/160°F/speed 2. Allow to cool in mixing bowl (approx. 30 min.).
  3. Add 1 oz olive oil, reserved ground seed mixture, lemon juice, garlic, yogurt, cannellini beans and salt and blend 30 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and blend 30 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a serving bowl and garnish with reserved beans, drizzle of olive oil and reserved whole seed mixture. Serve chilled or at room temperature.

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve as an appetizer or dip, with pita bread, crackers or crudités.
