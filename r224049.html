Tuna Noodle Bake

Ingredients

  • 1235 g water
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 225 - 255 g Chinese egg noodles (see tip)
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 200 g Cheddar cheese, cut in pieces
  • 70 g bread, torn in pieces
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 15 g fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 100 g onions, quartered
  • 100 g red peppers, cut in pieces (approx. ½ large red pepper)
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 485 g milk
  • 30 g plain flour
  • 70 g dry white wine (e.g. Chardonnay)
  • 1 Tbsp chicken stock paste
  • 225 g button mushrooms, halved
  • 340 g tinned tuna, in brine (drained weight)

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Grease a casserole dish (20 cm x 30 cm x 6 cm) with oil and set aside.
  2. Place water and salt in mixing bowl then heat 10 min/100°C/speed 1. Add noodles, place simmering basket in place of measuring cup on mixing bowl lid and cook time indicated on package/100°C//speed 1. Drain cooked noodles through simmering basket. Combine noodles and oil in prepared casserole dish then stir through to combine, spreading out pasta with spatula. Clean mixing bowl.
  3. Place cheese in mixing bowl and grate 3 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place bread in mixing bowl and chop 15-20 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  5. Place garlic and parsley in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 7.
  6. Add onions, peppers and oil then chop 3 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then sauté 3 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  7. Add milk, flour, wine, stock paste and water then cook 6 min/100°C/speed 3.
  8. Add mushrooms, tuna and reserved cheese then cook 5 min/90°C//speed 2.
  9. Pour sauce over noodles in casserole dish and mix through with spatula.
  10. Combine remaining cheese with reserved breadcrumbs and sprinkle over top of tuna bake.
  11. Bake for 25 minutes (180°C) until golden. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

casserole dish (20 cm x 30 cm x 6 cm), bowl, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Breadcrumbs can be replaced with crushed crackers or potato crisps.
  • Mushrooms can be replaced with sweetcorn and peas.
  • Egg noodles can be replaced with pasta (e.g. fusilli, macaroni etc.)
  • Save time by boiling egg noodles according to package directions on the hob while making sauce.
