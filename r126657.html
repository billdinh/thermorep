Broccoli burgers with radish salsa

Ingredients

Radish salsa

  • 160 g red radishes, cleaned
  • 1 red onion (approx. 60 g), cut into halves
  • 10 g mustard (approx. 1 tsp)
  • 25 g white wine vinegar
  • 35 g olive oil
  • ½ tsp fine salt
  • 2 pinches freshly ground black pepper
  • 15 g capers, pickled, drained

Broccoli fritters

  • 460 g water
  • 500 g broccoli, in florets
  • 70 g almonds, whole
  • 30 g soy flour, full fat, as egg replacement
  • 20 g breadcrumbs, vegan
  • 30 g oat flakes
  • 1 tsp fine salt
  • ¼ tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • vegetable oil, for frying

Preparation

Radish salsa

  1. Place radishes, onion, mustard, vinegar, olive oil, salt and pepper in mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 4.5.
  2. Add capers and stir 5 sec//speed 2. Transfer salsa to a bowl and allow to steep in fridge. Rinse mixing bowl.

Broccoli fritters

  1. Add 400 g water to mixing bowl, place Varoma into position, weigh broccoli, close Varoma and cook 16 min/Varoma/speed 1. Set Varoma aside. Remove Varoma lid and allow broccoli to cool for approx. 20 minutes. Empty and dry mixing bowl.
  2. Place almonds into mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 8.
  3. Place soy flour in a bowl and mix to a smooth paste with 60 g water
  4. Add half of broccoli florets, soy flour, breadcrumbs, oat flakes, salt and pepper to mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5 with aid of spatula.
  5. Add remaining broccoli florets and use spatula to stir 5 sec//speed 3.5. Shape 8 round patties (Ø approx. 7 cm) from broccoli mixture. Heat a little oil in a frying pan and fry patties on either side until golden brown. Allow to drain on a paper towel. Serve the broccoli burgers warm with radish salsa.

Useful Items

frying pan, bowl, refrigerator, stove

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with mashed potatoes, quinoa or buckwheat.
  • Soy flour is available in health food shops. A vegan egg replacement can alternatively be used.
