Hearty seafood chowder

Ingredients

  • 4 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only, plus extra for garnishing
  • ½ bunch fresh chives, cut into thirds
  • 1 brown onion, cut into halves
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 200 g carrot, cut into pieces (approx. 2 cm)
  • 80 g celery, cut into pieces (approx. 2 cm)
  • 200 g unsalted butter
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 2 tsp fresh thyme, leaves only
  • 50 g plain flour
  • 150 g white wine
  • 2 potatoes (approx. 200 g), peeled and cut into pieces (approx. 2 cm)
  • 200 g fish stock (see Tip)
  • 150 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 200 g firm white fish fillets, cut into pieces (approx. 2-3 cm)
  • Tabasco® sauce, to taste (optional)
  • 150 g large raw prawns, peeled and deveined
  • 150 g scallops
  • 100 g smoked salmon, cut into pieces

Preparation

  1. Place parsley and chives into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a small bowl and set aside.
  2. Place onion and garlic into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add carrot and celery and chop 5 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add 50 g of the butter and cook 5 min/100°C/speed 2.
  5. Add remaining 150 g butter, curry powder, salt, pepper, thyme and flour and cook 3 min/100°C/speed 2.
  6. Add wine, potatoes, stock and cream and cook 20-25 min/100°C/speed 2, until potatoes are tender.
  7. Add fish and Tabasco® (optional) and cook 3 min/100°C//speed 1.
  8. Add prawns, scallops and reserved parsley mixture and cook 3 min/100°C//speed 1. Place a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh smoked salmon into it. Transfer chowder into thermal serving bowl and carefully stir to combine salmon. Serve garnished with extra parsley.

Useful Items
