Sesame logs

Ingredients

  • 10 ½ oz all-purpose flour
  • 4 oz self-rising flour
  • 6 ½ oz unsalted butter, cold, cut into pieces
  • 4 oz caster sugar
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 2 - 4 tbsp whole milk
  • 1 ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 oz sesame seeds
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
800 kJ / 191 kcal
Protein
2.8 g

Preparation

  1. Place all purpose flour, self-rising flour, butter, caster sugar and salt into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 6.
  2. Add egg yolk, milk and vanilla and mix 25-30 sec/speed 5. Tip dough out and bring together to form a large log. Wrap in cling wrap and leave to rest in refrigerator for 30 minutes. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl.
  3. Place sesame seeds into mixing bowl without measuring cup and toast 8 min/Varoma/speed 2. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Preheat oven to 350°F and line two baking sheets with parchment paper.
  5. Remove chilled dough from refrigerator. Cut dough into 20 pieces and roll out into logs (3 in. x 1 in.). Roll each log in toasted sesame seeds and place on prepared baking sheets (approx. 1 ¼ in. apart).
  6. Bake for 12-15 minutes (350°F) until lightly golden. Allow to rest on baking sheets for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely. Serve or store in an airtight container for up to 5 days.

Useful Items

airtight container, cling wrap, parchment paper, wire rack, baking sheets (approx. 16 in. x 14 in.), refrigerator, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • These cookies taste better after the first day when vanilla has time to soak in.
