Roasted Aubergine Salad with Tahini Dressing

Ingredients

Green Tahini Dressing

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 50 g light tahini
  • 60 g freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 60 g water
  • 20 g fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 20 g fresh coriander, leaves only
  • ½ tsp ground cumin
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt

Aubergine Salad

  • 2 aubergines, sliced lengthways (1 cm)
  • 2 Tbsp za'atar, mixed with 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 800 g water
  • 120 g bulgar wheat
  • 100 g fresh spinach leaves
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 120 g tinned chickpeas (drained weight)
  • 100 g cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 50 g pitted black olives
  • 50 g red onions, thinly sliced
  • 80 g low fat feta cheese
  • 50 g pomegranate seeds

Preparation

Green Tahini Dressing

  1. Place garlic, tahini, lemon juice, water, parsley, coriander, cumin and salt in mixing bowl then blend 6 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then blend again 6 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside, then clean mixing bowl.

Aubergine Salad

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Place aubergines on a baking tray lined with baking paper and use a sharp knife to make diagonal incisions in flesh. Using a pastry brush, brush za'atar oil mixture over aubergines. Roast for 20-25 minutes (180°C) until soft and browned on edges. Meanwhile, cook bulgar wheat.
  2. Place water in mixing bowl then insert simmering basket. Weigh in bulgar wheat and cook 15 min/100°C/speed 4. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula then stir bulgar wheat with a fork.
  3. Place spinach on a large serving platter. Season cooked bulgar wheat with salt and pepper, scatter over spinach, then top with roasted aubergine slices.
  4. Add chickpeas, cherry tomatoes, olives and red onion. Top with feta and pomegranate, then drizzle with green tahini dressing. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

serving platter, baking tray and baking paper, pastry brush, sharp knife, oven
