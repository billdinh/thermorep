Sticky meatball subs

Ingredients

  • 60 g day-old bread (approx. 2 slices), torn into pieces
  • 1 tsp dried mixed herbs
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 320 g water
  • 500 g beef mince
  • 1 brown onion (approx. 150 g), cut into quarters
  • 30 g unsalted butter
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 100 g tomato sauce (see Tips)
  • 20 g white vinegar
  • 1 tsp English mustard
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 24 mini dinner rolls
  • 24 cheese slices (approx. 6 x 3 cm)

Preparation

  1. Place bread into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 8.
  2. Add dried herbs, egg, salt and 100 g of the water and blend 5 sec/speed 5.
  3. Add mince and combine 10 sec//speed 4.
  4. Roll 2 teaspoons of the mince mixture into a ball and place into Varoma dish. Repeat rolling with remaining mixture (makes approx. 50 meatballs) and divide between Varoma dish and tray. Set Varoma aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  5. Place onion into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  6. Add butter and cook 5 min/100°C/speed 1.
  7. Add sugar, tomato sauce, vinegar, mustard, Worcestershire sauce and remaining 220 g water. Place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and cook 10-12 min/Varoma/speed 2 or until meatballs are cooked through. Remove Varoma and transfer meatballs into a bowl and set aside.
  8. Insert measuring cup into mixing bowl lid and continue to cook sauce 8 min/100°C/speed 2, then blend 20 sec/speed 6. Transfer sauce into bowl with meatballs and gently stir to combine. Set aside and allow to cool completely (see Tips).
  9. Preheat oven to 180°C. Line a baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  10. Cut a slit ¾ down the middle of each roll and press in the sides to create a space for the meatballs. Place 2 meatballs and sauce into each dinner roll and top with a slice of cheese. Place onto prepared baking tray and bake for 5 minutes (180°C) or until cheese is bubbling and rolls are golden brown. Allow to cool slightly before serving.

Useful Items

bowl, baking tray (30 x 40 cm), baking paper, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • To make your own tomato sauce, please refer to the recipe in The Basic Cookbook or Everyday Cookbook.
  • It is easier to assemble the subs when the meatballs and sauce are completely cool. The meatballs can be made up to 2 days in advance and stored in the refrigerator in a sealable container.
  • Subs can be assembled and placed onto baking trays, covered with plastic wrap and stored in the refrigerator for up to 2 hours before baking. Bake for 6-8 minutes (180°C) or until cheese is bubbling and rolls are golden brown.
