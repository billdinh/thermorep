Tomato soup with ham and cheese wands

Ingredients

Ham and cheese wands

  • 100 g unsalted butter, cut into cubes and frozen
  • 100 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 1 pinch fine salt
  • 50 g chilled water
  • 40 g cheddar cheese, cut into pieces (2 cm)
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • 30 g ham, cut into pieces
  • 1 tbsp celery seeds

Tomato soup

  • 100 g brown onion, cut into halves
  • 130 g carrots, cut into pieces
  • 110 g celery stalks, cut into pieces
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 50 g unsalted butter
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 dried bay leaf
  • salt flakes, to taste
  • ¼ tsp white pepper
  • 100 g tomato paste
  • 800 g fresh tomatoes, cut into halves
  • 1 tbsp Vegetable stock paste (see Tips)
  • 400 g water

Preparation

Ham and cheese wands

  1. Place butter, flour and salt into mixing bowl and mix 5 sec/speed 5.
  2. Add water and mix 5 sec/speed 6. Transfer dough onto a lightly floured work surface, dust with extra flour and shape into a ball. Flatten into a rough square, then wrap in plastic wrap and place into refrigerator for 15 minutes. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Place cheese into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 8. Transfer into a small bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Place dough onto a lightly floured work surface. Using a rolling pin, roll into a rectangle (approx. 1 cm thickness). Fold dough lengthways into thirds, then turn folded dough a quarter turn to the left. Roll into a rectangle (approx. 1 cm thickness) again and fold into thirds. Turn dough a quarter turn to the left and repeat rolling and folding once more. Wrap dough in plastic wrap and place into freezer for 10 minutes.
  5. Preheat oven to 200°C. Line a baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  6. Place dough onto a lightly floured work surface and roll into a rectangle (approx. 5 mm thickness) and brush with beaten egg.
  7. Sprinkle cheese and ham over half of the dough (lengthways). Fold remaining half of dough over filling, dust with flour and gently roll out into a rectangle (approx. 5 mm thickness). Brush with beaten egg and sprinkle with celery seeds.
  8. Cut dough into wide strips (1 cm). Place onto prepared baking tray. Place a sheet of baking paper over wands and weigh down with another baking tray. Bake for 10 minutes (200°C) then remove top tray and baking paper. Bake for a further 3-5 minutes (200°C), or until golden. Transfer onto a wire rack to cool.

Tomato soup

  1. Place onion, carrots, celery and garlic into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add butter, oil, bay leaf, salt and pepper and cook 10 min/100°C/speed , without measuring cup. Carefully remove bay leaf.
  3. Add tomato paste and tomatoes and chop 8 sec/speed 6, then cook 5 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  4. Add stock paste and water and cook 15 min/100°C//speed , then blend 1 min/speed 5 -10 , increasing speed gradually from speed 5 to speed 10. Serve soup with Ham and cheese wands.
