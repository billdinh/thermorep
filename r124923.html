Bloody Mary

Ingredients

  • 500 g tomato juice
  • 100 g vodka
  • 40 g lemon juice
  • 200 g ice cubes, plus extra for serving, to taste
  • 1 - 2 pinches celery salt, to taste (optional)
  • ¼ - ½ tsp fine sea salt, to taste
  • 1 - 2 pinches ground black pepper, to taste, plus extra for serving

To Serve

  • 1 - 4 drops Tabasco, to taste
  • 1 - 4 drops Worcestershire sauce, to taste
  • 2 celery stalks, washed, cut in half
Nutrition

Preparation

  1. Place tomato juice, vodka, lemon juice, ice cubes, celery salt (if using), salt and pepper in mixing bowl then mix 30 sec/speed 2. Place a few ice cubes in each serving glass.
  2. Insert simmering basket, hold in place with spatula then strain over ice in glasses. Season with black pepper, Tabasco and Worcestershire sauce then serve garnished with celery stalks.

Useful Items

4 glasses

Hints & Tricks

  • Add more vodka for a stronger cocktail.
  • Use your celery stick to stir your drink once all the spices and sauces have been added. You can then enjoy it during or after your cocktail!
  • This cocktail is thought to be named after the Hollywood actress Mary Pickford.
