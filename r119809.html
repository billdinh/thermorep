Cheddar Cheese and Lamb Shepherd's Pie

Ingredients

  • 5 oz cheddar cheese, cut into pieces (approx. 1 in.)
  • 24 oz lamb meat, sinew free, cut into pieces (approx. 1 in.)
  • 3 ½ oz yellow onions, cut in halves
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 7 oz carrots, cut into pieces (approx. 1 ½ in.)
  • 1 oz olive oil
  • 15 oz canned diced tomatoes
  • 8 oz button mushrooms, sliced
  • 5 oz white wine
  • 1 ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 oz Worcestershire sauce
  • 35 oz russet potatoes, peeled, cut into pieces (approx. ¾ in.)
  • 5 ½ oz whole milk
  • 1 ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 3 oz unsalted butter

Preparation

  1. Place cheese into mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 5. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place lamb into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 6.5. Transfer into a separate bowl and set aside.
  3. Place onions, garlic, carrots and olive oil into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add tomatoes and ground lamb (break up meat with spatula) and sauté 5 min/Varoma/speed 1 and then without measuring cup stir 3 min/Varoma//speed 2.
  5. Add mushrooms, wine, 1½ tsp salt, pepper and Worcestershire into mixing bowl. Place potatoes in Varoma dish, secure Varoma lid, set Varoma in position and steam 30 min/Varoma//speed 1.
  6. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  7. Carefully remove Varoma lid, stir potatoes in Varoma with spatula, secure Varoma lid and steam 5 min/Varoma//speed 1. Set Varoma aside and transfer lamb mixture to a casserole dish (approx. 12 in. x 9½ in. x 2 in.).| Clean mixing bowl.
  8. Place milk and 1½ tsp salt into mixing bowl and cook 3 min/212°F/speed 1.
  9. Add steamed potatoes and 1 oz butter and with aid of spatula mash 15-20 sec/speed 4.
  10. Spread mashed potatoes over top of lamb mixture, sprinkle with reserved grated cheddar cheese and dot with remaining 2 oz butter. Bake for 25-30 minutes (400°F) or until top is golden brown. Allow to rest for a few minutes to firm and serve.

Useful Items

bowl, casserole dish (approx. 12 in. x 9 ½ in. x 2 in.), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • For best results, be sure to steam potatoes until tender.
  • To prepare mashed potatoes in advance pour 4 oz water,7 oz milk,1½ tsp salt| and potatoes into mixing bowl and cook 20-25 min/212°F/speed 1. Add 1 oz butter and mash 20-30 sec/speed 3.
  • Save time by cooking ground lamb a day before.

This term is known to have been in use in 1791, when potato was being introduced as an edible affordable crop for the poor ("cottage" meaning a modest dwelling for rural workers).
