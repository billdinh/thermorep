Pumpkin Pie

Ingredients

Pie Crust

  • 1 pie crust, homemade and pre-cooked (see Tip)

Pie Filling

  • 15 oz canned pumpkin purée
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 egg yolk, from a large egg
  • 14 oz canned sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • ¼ tsp ground nutmeg
  • &frac18; tsp ground cloves
  • &frac18; tsp ground black pepper (optional)
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • whipped cream, to serve

Preparation

Pie Crust

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Cover a pre-cooked pie crust with foil and warm for 10 minutes (425°F). Crust should be warm when adding pie filling.

Pie Filling

  1. Place pumpkin purée, eggs and yolk, condensed milk, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, cloves (see Tip), pepper, salt and vanilla into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 3.5. Pour filling into warmed pie crust.
  2. Reduce oven temperature to 400°F and bake for 10 minutes (400°F) then reduce oven temperature to 300°F and bake 40-50 minutes (300°F) until middle is just set (see Tip). Allow to cool completely on a wire rack (approx. 4 hr.) before serving.
  3. Cut pie into 8 slices and serve with whipped cream.

Useful Items

aluminum foil, thermometer, wire rack, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • For a perfect homemade pie crust, refer to the Two Crust Pie Pastry recipe in the Thanksgiving Collection.
  • Spices can be replaced with 1 tbsp of pumpkin pie spice blend.
  • Pie can be rotated half way through baking if necessary.
  • To save time: a store-bought 9 inch unbaked pie crust can replace homemade pie crust.
  • Pie is done when center reaches 175°F when tested with a thermometer. If you do not have a thermometer you can also test pie readiness by inserting a paring knife 1 inch from edge of crust, it should come out clean.
  • To whip cream: Insert butterfly whisk. Place 18 oz cream and 1 tsp sugar into mixing bowl and without setting a time whip speed 3 until desired stiffness is achieved, while watching carefully to avoid overwhipping. Remove butterfly whisk and store whipped cream in an airtight container in the refrigerator until needed.
  • Slices can be cut in half for 16 servings.
