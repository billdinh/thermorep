Apple, Pineapple and Lime Yoghurt Drink

Ingredients

  • 200 g fresh pineapple, peeled, cut in pieces
  • 150 g apples, cut in pieces
  • 80 g soft brown sugar
  • 1 lime, finely grated zest and juice
  • 1500 g whole milk
  • 120 g plain yoghurt (full fat with live cultures)

Preparation

  1. Place pineapple, apples, sugar, lime zest and juice in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then cook 5 min/Varoma/speed 2. Transfer to a bowl, cover and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.
  2. Place milk and yoghurt in mixing bowl then heat 4 min/50°C/speed 3. Transfer to an airtight container, wrap in tea towels and set aside in a warm place for 8 hours.
  3. Place reserved yoghurt and reserved fruit purée in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 3. Transfer to sterilised bottles, seal then set aside in fridge until ready to serve.

Useful Items

cling film, tea towels, airtight container, sterilised glass bottles, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • The ideal temperature to ferment yoghurt is about 43°C. If you have an airing cupboard, this is the perfect place to leave the yoghurt to set.
