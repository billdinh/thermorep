Spinach and Artichoke Dip

Ingredients

  • 1 can artichoke hearts, (approx 14 oz), drained
  • 4 oz Parmesan cheese, cut into pieces
  • 4 oz mozzarella cheese, cut into pieces
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • ½ oz shallot, halved
  • 1 oz olive oil
  • 10 oz frozen spinach leaves, defrosted and drained
  • 8 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 3 oz sour cream
  • ½ tsp red pepper flakes
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • ½ tsp Tabasco sauce

Preparation

  1. Drain artichoke hearts and set aside.
  2. Place Parmesan and mozzarella cheese into mixing bowl and chop 8 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place garlic, shallot and olive oil into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then sauté 2 min/250°F/speed 1.
  4. Add drained artichoke hearts and spinach and chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  5. Add cream cheese, sour cream, pepper flakes, salt, Worcestershire and Tabasco and mix 10 sec/speed 4.
  6. Add reserved chopped cheese and cook 8 min/175°F//speed 1. Transfer dip to a heatproof serving bowl. Serve hot.

heatproof serving bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with artisan breads, crackers, tortilla chips, crostini or veggies.
  • This dish can be frozen. Reheat in an oven at 350°F until warmed through.
  • To brown dip: bake 15-30 minutes (400°F) or until golden brown.
  • Broil setting can be used to brown top, watch carefully to avoid burning.
