Fish soup

Ingredients

  • 1000 g water
  • 250 g unpeeled raw prawns
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 150 g brown onion, cut into pieces
  • 100 g green capsicum, deseeded and cut into pieces
  • 100 g red capsicum, deseeded and cut into pieces
  • 3 sprigs fresh coriander, plus extra leaves for garnishing
  • 200 g tomato passata
  • 50 g olive oil
  • 2 tsp Vegetable stock paste (see Tips)
  • 1 dried bay leaf
  • 1 long red chilli, thinly sliced and deseeded if preferred
  • 60 g basmati rice
  • 500 g fresh mussels, scrubbed and debearded
  • 300 g skinless, firm white fish fillets
  • 1 pinch salt, to taste
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper, to taste

Preparation

  1. Place 500 g of the water and raw prawns into mixing bowl and cook 5 min/100°C/speed 1. Strain stock through simmering basket into a jug and set aside. Reserve prawns and set aside to cool. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  2. Place garlic, onion, green capsicum, red capsicum and coriander into mixing bowl and chop 6 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add tomato passata (purée) and blend 15 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add olive oil and cook 7 min/100°C/speed 1.
  5. Add remaining 500 g of the water, stock paste, bay leaf, chilli and reserved prawn stock and cook 7 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  6. Add rice to mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh mussels into it. Insert Varoma tray and weigh fish onto it. Secure Varoma lid and cook 15 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove Varoma. Break fish fillet up into pieces and set aside with mussels.
  7. Peel prawns, cut flesh into halves and add prawn flesh to the soup. Leave soup to stand in mixing bowl with the lid on and the measuring cup in place for 2 minutes. Discard prawn shells.
  8. Distribute mussels and fish fillet pieces between 6 serving bowls. Pour over soup and garnish with chopped coriander. Season soup to taste with salt and pepper and serve immediately.

Useful Items

large jug, serving bowls

Hints & Tricks

  • Please refer to The Basic Cookbook for the Vegetable stock paste recipe.
  • Serve soup with fresh crusty bread on the side.
