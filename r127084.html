Muesli Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 8 dried apricots, pitted
  • 50 g muesli with fruit, plus extra to garnish
  • 400 g skimmed milk
  • 1 apple (approx. 120 g), cut in quarters
  • 80 g frozen strawberries
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
Nutrition
per 1 glass
Calories
1432 kJ / 343 kcal
Protein
13 g
Carbohydrates
58 g
Fat
2 g

Preparation

  1. Place dried apricots in a bowl. Place bowl containing apricots on mixing bowl lid and weigh 25 g fruit muesli and 200 g milk into it. Set bowl aside and allow to soak overnight in refrigerator.
  2. Place soaked mixture, 25 g fruit muesli, 200 g milk, apple, strawberries and cinnamon in mixing bowl and blend 1 min 30 sec/speed 10. Serve immediately garnished with fruit muesli.

refrigerator
