Asparagus Stem Soup

Ingredients

  • 300 g asparagus stems, woody ends, cut in pieces (5 cm)
  • 600 g water, boiling
  • 1 lemon, thin peelings of skin only
  • 150 g onions, quartered
  • 30 g olive oil, plus extra for serving
  • 200 g potatoes, peeled and diced (2 cm)
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt, plus extra for seasoning
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper, plus extra for serving
  • 50 g double cream, plus extra for serving

Preparation

  1. Place asparagus stems in mixing bowl (see tip) and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, add water and lemon peelings then, with simmering basket in place of measuring cup on mixing bowl lid, boil 15 min/100°C/speed 1.
  2. Blend 30 sec/speed 5-7, increasing speed gradually then pour asparagus stock through a fine sieve into a large bowl, pressing with the back of a spoon to extract all the liquid. Set stock aside and discard asparagus pulp. Rinse mixing bowl.
  3. Place onions and oil in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then sauté 4 min/100°C/speed 2.
  4. Add potatoes, reserved asparagus stock, salt and pepper then cook 20-25 min/100°C/speed 1 or until potatoes are cooked.
  5. Add cream and blend 1 min/speed 5-7, increasing speed gradually. Adjust seasoning to taste then divide soup between 4 bowls and garnish with an extra swirl of cream, a drizzle of olive oil and a sprinkle of black pepper. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

fine sieve, large bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • When using asparagus in recipes, freeze the stems and store until you have enough to make this soup.
