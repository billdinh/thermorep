Ginger bundt cake

Ingredients

  • 120 g butter, cut into pieces (3-4 cm), plus extra for greasing
  • 100 g raw almonds
  • 2 - 3 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 100 g brown sugar (see Tips)
  • 190 g buttermilk
  • 2 tsp natural vanilla extract
  • 3 eggs
  • 210 g spelt flour (see Tips)
  • 1 tbsp ground ginger
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C. Grease a bundt tin or fluted ring cake tin (20 cm) and set aside.
  2. Place almonds and fresh ginger into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 7, or until finely chopped. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Without cleaning mixing bowl, place butter into mixing bowl and melt 2 min/90°C/speed 1.
  4. Add all remaining ingredients, including reserved almond mixture, and mix 6 sec/speed 4.
  5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix 4 sec/speed 3, until combined.
  6. Transfer mixture into prepared tin, smooth surface and bake for 30-35 minutes (160°C), or until a skewer inserted into the centre of the cake comes out clean. Allow cake to cool in tin for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack to cool completely before serving.

Useful Items

bowl, bundt cake tin (20 cm), wooden or metal skewer, wire rack, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • You can use your preferred brown sugar in this cake – either regular brown sugar or a less refined option such as Rapadura sugar (Panela) or coconut sugar.
  • The spelt flour can be substituted with regular plain flour if you prefer.
