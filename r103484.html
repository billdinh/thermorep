Chicken and mushroom lasagne

Ingredients

  • butter, for greasing
  • 100 g Parmesan cheese, crust removed and cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 100 g cheddar cheese, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 100 g mozzarella cheese, cut into pieces (3 cm)

Chicken mince

  • 10 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 4 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves only
  • 1000 g skinless chicken thigh fillets, partially frozen and cut into pieces (4-5 cm)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 50 g olive oil
  • 200 g white wine
  • 250 g fresh Portobello mushrooms, cut into thin slices (5 mm)
  • 200 g fresh Swiss brown mushrooms, cut into quarters
  • 2 tsp Chicken stock paste (see Tips)
  • 50 g plain flour
  • ½ tsp freshly ground black pepper

Sauce

  • 800 g milk
  • 100 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 80 g butter
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 100 g plain flour

Assembly

  • 12 sheets fresh lasagne sheets (375g)

Preparation

  1. Grease a casserole dish (30 x 25 x 6 cm) with butter and set aside.
  2. Place Parmesan, cheddar  and mozzarella cheese into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.

Chicken mince

  1. Place parsley and thyme leaves into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Transfer into a large bowl and set aside.
  2. Without cleaning mixing bowl, place 500 g of the chicken into mixing bowl and mince 10 sec/speed 6. Transfer into bowl with herbs.
  3. Place remaining 500 g chicken into mixing bowl and mince 10 sec/speed 6. Transfer into bowl with chicken and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Place garlic into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add oil and wine. Place Varoma into position, then weigh Portobello mushrooms into it. Insert Varoma tray, then weigh Swiss brown mushrooms onto it. Secure Varoma lid and cook 10 min/Varoma/speed 1. Transfer Swiss brown mushrooms into Varoma dish, stir to combine and set aside.
  6. Add stock paste, flour, pepper and reserved chicken mixture to mixing bowl and cook 5 min/Varoma//speed 1.
  7. Mix with spatula to break up any lumps and cook for a further 5 min/Varoma//speed 1.
  8. Add 200g reserved mixed mushrooms, then mix 5 sec//speed 3. Transfer mince mixture into a large bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Sauce

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Place milk, cream, butter, salt, pepper and flour into mixing bowl and cook 8 min/100°C/speed 3.
  2. Add 150g of the reserved grated cheeses and cook 2 min/100°C/speed 3.

Assembly

  1. Spread a thin layer of sauce over base of prepared casserole dish. Place 4 lasagne sheets over sauce. Spread one-third of the mince mixture over lasagne sheets, spread with another thin layer of sauce, and sprinkle with one-third of the cheese mixture. Top with 4 lasagne sheets, then one-third of the mince mixture, a thin layer of sauce and one-third of the cheese mixture. Top with final 4 lasagne sheets, a thin layer of sauce, the final one-third of the mince mixture and the remaining sauce. Top with remaining reserved mushroom mixture and sprinkle with remaining cheese mixture.
  2. Bake for 30-40 minutes (180°C) until browned on top. Allow lasagne to rest for 15 minutes before serving.

Useful Items

casserole dish (30 x 25 x 6 cm), bowl, large bowl, oven
