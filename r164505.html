Blood Orange Margarita

Ingredients

  • salt, to rim glasses
  • 3 limes (divided), 2 limes to juice, 1 quartered to garnish and rim glasses
  • 4 oz Tequila
  • 1 oz orange liqueur (e.g. Cointreau®)
  • 1 oz blood orange liqueur (e.g. Solerno®)
  • 5 oz blood orange juice (approx. 5 blood oranges if using fresh)
  • 1 oz agave
  • 12 - 14 ice cubes
Nutrition
per 1 portion

0 g

Preparation

  1. Place enough salt or sugar onto a plate to just cover it. Rub a lime wedge along the rim of each glass and dip the rim into the salt, rotating the glass until the entire rim is coated and set aside.
  2. Place tequila, orange liqueur, blood orange liqueur, blood orange juice, 2 oz lime juice, agave and ice into mixing bowl and blend 1 min/speed 10. Pour into prepared glasses and garnish with a thin slice of lime or blood orange. Serve immediately.
