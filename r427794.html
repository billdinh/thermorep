TM6

Chinese Pork with Vegetables

Ingredients

  • 600 g pork fillet, sliced (0.5 cm x 1 cm x 5 cm)
  • 50 g soy sauce
  • 300 g dried rice noodles
  • 50 g oil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp fresh root ginger, freshly grated
  • 150 g red onions, cut in wedges
  • 150 g red peppers, sliced (0.5 cm x 5 cm)
  • 150 g carrots, sliced (0.5 cm x 5 cm)
  • 1280 g water
  • 1 tsp Chinese five spice
  • 1 tsp brown sugar
  • 70 g oyster sauce
  • 100 g mangetout, trimmed
  • 150 g baby pak choi, roughly chopped
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 fresh red chilli, sliced diagonally
  • 2 Tbsp roasted, unsalted cashew nuts
  • 2 spring onions, sliced diagonally

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid, weigh in pork fillet and 30 g soy sauce. Remove bowl from lid, mix well with spatula then cover and set aside in fridge to marinate for 15-20 minutes.
  2. Place a large bowl on mixing bowl lid, weigh in rice noodles then set aside. Drain pork through simmering basket.
  3. Place 15 g oil and 300 g drained pork in mixing bowl then brown High Temp.. Transfer to a large bowl and set aside.
  4. Again place 15 g oil and remaining drained pork in mixing bowl.then brown High Temp. . Transfer to bowl with browned pork, set aside to keep warm.
  5. Place remaining 20 g oil and garlic in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  6. Add grated ginger, red onions, red peppers and carrots then sauté High Temp. .
  7. Add 80 g water, Chinese five spice, sugar, remaining 20 g soy sauce, oyster sauce, mangetout and baby pak choi, mix well using spatula then cook 5 min/120°C//speed . Transfer to bowl with browned pork, mix gently with spatula then set aside to keep warm.
  8. Place 1200 g water and 1 tsp salt in mixing bowl then boil Kettle . Pour over rice noodles in large bowl and allow to soak for the time indicated on packet. Drain through Varoma dish then transfer to a large serving bowl. Arrange reserved meat and vegetables over rice noodles and serve garnished with red chilli, roasted cashew nuts and spring onions.
