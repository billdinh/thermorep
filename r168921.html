Chocolate Beet Cake

Ingredients

Cake

  • coconut oil, to grease
  • 12 oz red beets, peeled, cut into 1 in. cubes (approx. 3 beets)
  • 24 oz water
  • 4 oz coconut oil, melted
  • 1 oz brewed coffee
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 9 ½ oz all-purpose flour
  • 10 oz sugar
  • 1 oz unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 ½ tsp baking soda
  • 1 pinch salt

Chocolate Avocado Frosting

  • 10 oz ripe avocado, flesh only, (approx. 2 avocados)
  • 3 ½ oz maple syrup (optional)
  • 1 oz unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 pinch salt

Preparation

Cake

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Lightly grease and line a springform pan with parchment paper and set aside.
  2. Insert simmering basket and weigh in beets, add water and steam 25 min/Varoma/speed 2. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and let beets cool 5 minutes. Reserve 4 oz water and set aside. Discard remaining water.
  3. Place cooled beets into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and chop a further 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add 4 oz reserved beet water, coconut oil, coffee, eggs, vanilla, flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda and salt and mix 10 sec/speed 6.
  5. Pour into prepared pan and bake 45 minutes (350°F) or until a toothpick inserted into center comes out clean. Cool completely before frosting.

Chocolate Avocado Frosting

  1. Place avocado, maple syrup, cocoa powder, vanilla and salt into mixing bowl and mix 20 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix a further 30 sec/speed 5.
  2. Evenly spread frosting over top and sides of cake with an icing spatula. Store in an airtight container and refrigerate until ready to serve.

Useful Items

toothpick, springform pan (9 in.), airtight container, parchment paper, icing spatula, oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Allow cake to come to room temperature before serving.
