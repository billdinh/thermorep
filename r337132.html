Mushroom pâté

Ingredients

  • 20 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 eschalots (approx. 60 g)
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 300 g fresh mixed mushrooms, cut into halves (e.g. portobello, shiitake, oyster)
  • 6 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 3 - 4 sprigs fresh rosemary, leaves only
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 3 - 4 drops Tabasco® sauce
  • 100 g walnuts, toasted

Preparation

  1. Place oil, eschalots and garlic into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5, then cook 3 min/120°C/speed 2.
  2. Add all remaining ingredients, except walnuts, and cook 8 min/98°C/speed 2.
  3. Add walnuts and chop 1 min/speed 4-9, increasing speed gradually from speed 4 speed 9 (see Tip). Transfer into serving dish and smooth top using spatula. Cover and allow to cool, place into refrigerator to chill (approx. 1 hour), then serve.

Useful Items

serving dish, plastic wrap, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • For a chunkier pâté, blend 1 min/speed 4 (without increasing speed) after adding walnuts.
