Pumpkin Curry

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp ground coriander
  • 2 tsp cumin seeds
  • ½ tsp cardamom seeds
  • 1 tsp dried ground turmeric
  • 1 pinch ground cloves
  • 2 in. fresh ginger, peeled, in pieces
  • 4 garlic cloves, peeled
  • 3 dried red chilies
  • 2 dried bay leaves
  • 1 cinnamon stick (2 in.)
  • 15 curry leaves (optional)
  • 2 oz coconut oil
  • 12 oz yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 8 oz vegetable stock
  • 4 oz coconut milk
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 30 oz small sugar pumpkin, peeled and cubed (1 in.)
  • 6 oz raw cashews, unsalted
  • steamed rice, to serve
  • dried cranberries, to serve
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1481 kJ / 354 kcal
Protein
8 g
Carbohydrates
27 g
Fat
27 g

Preparation

  1. Place coriander, cumin, cardamom, turmeric and cloves into mixing bowl and without measuring cup, dry roast 6 min/212°F/speed 1.
  2. Add ginger and garlic, insert measuring cup and chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  3. Add chilies, bay leaf, cinnamon, curry leaves, coconut oil, onions, stock, coconut milk and salt. Set Varoma into position and weigh pumpkin into Varoma dish and tray, secure Varoma lid and steam 20-25 min/Varoma/speed 1, until cooked to your liking. Remove Varoma. Remove and discard bay leaves, chilies and cinnamon stick from curry sauce. Stir in cashews with spatula to combine.
  4. Serve steamed pumpkin over rice, top with curry sauce and garnish with cranberries.
