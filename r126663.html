Asian vegetable soup with tofu

Ingredients

  • 15 g fresh coriander leaves
  • 5 tbsp soy sauce
  • 30 g light sesame oil
  • 100 g tofu, plain, drained and diced (2 cm)
  • 150 g carrots, cut into pieces
  • 100 g daikon radish, cut into pieces
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 30 g fresh ginger root, thinly sliced
  • 1000 g water
  • 2 tbsp vegetable stock paste, homemade, vegan (see tip)
  • 100 g spring onions, cut into diagonal rings
  • 100 g snow peas, cut in half
  • 100 g thin wheat noodles, broken into pieces
  • 2 - 3 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 - 2 tbsp agave syrup
  • 1 pinch fine salt
  • 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper

Preparation

  1. Place coriander in mixing bowl, chop 3 sec/speed 7 and transfer to a bowl.
  2. Mix coriander in bowl with 2 tbsp soy sauce and 10 g sesame oil. Add tofu and allow to marinate for at least 1 hour.
  3. Place carrots and daikon into mixing bowl, chop 8 sec/speed 4.5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place garlic and ginger into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add 20 g sesame oil and sauté 2 min/120°C/speed 1.
  6. Add water, stock paste, carrot and daikon mixture and spring onions, and cook 15 min/100°C//speed 1.
  7. Add snow peas and noodles and cook for time indicated on packet/100°C//speed 1.
  8. Season soup to taste with remaining 3 tbsp soy sauce, vinegar, agave syrup, salt and pepper. Transfer to 6 soup bowls, garnish with marinated tofu and serve.

Useful Items

Hints & Tricks

  • Prepare the vegetable stock paste featured in The Basic Cook Book without parmesan and with vegan wine for a vegan variation of this paste.
  • Use rice vinegar to create a particularly exotic dish.
