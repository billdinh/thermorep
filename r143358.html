Sweet Potato Doughnuts

Ingredients

Doughnuts

  • 150 g sweet potatoes, peeled, cut in pieces
  • 150 g water
  • 40 g olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 120 g granulated sugar
  • 200 g whole milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 200 g plain flour
  • 1 ½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • ½ tsp ground ginger
  • ¼ tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt

Topping

  • 30 g pistachio nuts, unsalted, shelled
  • 4 Tbsp icing sugar
  • 2 tsp water, boiling

Preparation

  1. Place sweet potatoes in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add water then cook 12 min/100°C/speed 0.5. Meanwhile, preheat oven to 170°C and lightly grease 2 x 6-hole doughnut tins. Drain through a sieve then return to mixing bowl.
  3. Add oil, sugar, milk, egg and vanilla to mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 5.
  4. Add flour, baking powder, ground cinnamon, ground ginger, ground nutmeg and salt then mix 7 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then mix again 2 sec/speed 4. Divide between prepared doughnut holes. Bake for 20-24 minutes (170°C) until cooked and golden. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl.
  5. Allow doughnuts to cool in tins for a few minutes, then loosen with a knife and carefully transfer to a wire rack. Meanwhile, make topping.

Topping

  1. Place pisatchios in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Tip out onto a plate and set aside.
  2. Mix icing sugar and water together in a small bowl. Brush tops of doughnuts with icing then dip in chopped nuts.
  3. Serve warm or at room temperature.

Useful Items

fine sieve, 2 x 6 hole doughnut tins (Ø 8 cm), wire rack, pastry brush, oven
