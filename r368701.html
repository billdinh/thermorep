Savoury potato and onion bake

Ingredients

  • 250 g raw cashews
  • 500 g filtered water
  • 1 tsp stock paste (see Tips)
  • 1 tsp paprika, plus extra for sprinkling
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp nutritional yeast (see Tips)
  • 30 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 brown onions (approx. 540 g), cut into quarters
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 800 g potatoes, peeled and cut into thin slices

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C. Set aside a baking dish (28 cm).
  2. Place cashews, water, stock paste, paprika, salt, pepper and nutritional yeast into mixing bowl and blend 2 min/speed 10 or until smooth and creamy. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Place oil, onion and garlic into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 4.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and sauté 10 min/120°C//speed 2, without measuring cup.
  5. Cover base of dish with one-third of the potato slices. Cover with one-third of the onions, then half of the cashew mixture. Layer with another one-third of the potatoes, then another one-third of the onions. Cover with remaining potatoes and onions, then top with remaining cashew mixture. Sprinkle with paprika, cover with aluminium foil and bake for 45 minutes (190°C). Remove foil and bake for a further 15-20 minutes (190°C), until potatoes are tender. Allow to rest for 10 minutes before serving.

Useful Items

bowl, aluminium foil, baking dish, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Please refer to the Mushroom stock paste recipe on this recipe chip or The Basic Cookbook for the Vegetable stock paste recipe.
  • With a cheesy, umami flavour, nutritional yeast contains all nine essential amino acids, is a great source of B vitamins and is often fortified with vitamin B12 (important for those following a vegan diet). You can find nutritional yeast at health food stores or online.
