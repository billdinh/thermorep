Orange and passionfruit Eton mess

Ingredients

Meringue

  • 100 g caster sugar
  • 3 egg whites

Passionfruit curd

  • 80 g passionfruit pulp
  • 40 g orange juice (approx. 1 orange)
  • 50 g caster sugar
  • 80 g unsalted butter, softened
  • 3 eggs

Assembly

  • 200 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 2 oranges, segmented
  • 10 sprigs fresh mint, leaves only

Preparation

Meringue

  1. Preheat oven to 100°C. Line a baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place a ceramic or glass bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh caster sugar into it. Set aside.
  3. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg whites into mixing bowl and beat 2 min/37°C/speed 3.5, without measuring cup, or until soft peaks form.
  4. Beat 3-5 min/37°C/speed 3, gradually adding reserved caster sugar through hole in mixing bowl lid one spoonful at a time, until sugar dissolves and stiff peaks form. Remove butterfly whisk. 
  5. Transfer meringue mixture onto prepared baking tray, spreading out to a 2 cm thickness. Bake for 80 minutes (100°C). Turn off oven and leave meringue in oven to cool for 1 hour. Clean and dry mixing bowl and butterfly whisk.

Passionfruit curd

  1. Strain passionfruit pulp through a fine-mesh sieve into a jug. Reserve any remaining pulp and seeds.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Place passionfruit juice, orange juice, sugar, butter and eggs into mixing bowl and cook 8 min/80°C/speed 3.
  3. Check to see if mixture has reached setting point by inserting a spoon into curd. Curd should coat the back of the spoon in a thick layer. If curd is not set, cook for a further 2-3 min/80°C/speed 3, or until thickened. Remove butterfly whisk.
  4. Transfer curd into a sealable jar or container and set aside to cool to room temperature, then place into refrigerator until cold. Clean and dry mixing bowl and butterfly whisk.

Assembly

  1. Insert butterfly whisk. Place cream into mixing bowl and whip 30-40 sec/speed 4, or until soft peaks form. Remove butterfly whisk.
  2. To assemble, dollop  2-3 teaspoons passionfruit curd and 2-3 teaspoons whipped cream onto 6 serving plates in any pattern you like. Break meringue into shards and decorate each plate with 1-2 meringue pieces. Garnish with orange segments, mint leaves and reserved passionfruit pulp and seeds. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

baking tray (30 x 40 cm), baking paper, ceramic or glass bowl, fine mesh sieve, jug, sealable storage jar or container, 6 serving plates, oven, refrigerator
