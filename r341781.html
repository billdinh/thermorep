Roasted eggplant

Ingredients

  • 2 eggplants, cut into halves lengthways
  • salt, for sprinkling
  • 70 g harissa paste (see Tips)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 65 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 70 g pistachio nuts
  • 2 slices bread (approx. 70 g), torn into pieces
  • 1 ½ tsp sumac
  • pomegranate arils, for garnishing (see Tips)

Preparation

  1. Line a baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper. Score a crisscross pattern into each cut side of the eggplants, then sprinkle with salt and set aside for 30 minutes. Rinse salt from eggplants, dry well with a paper towel and place onto baking tray.
  2. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  3. Place harissa, garlic, turmeric, oregano, vinegar and 40 g of the oil into mixing bowl and blend 10 sec/speed 8.
  4. Brush paste over eggplant flesh, ensuring paste gets into cuts. Bake for 50 minutes (180°C). Meanwhile, proceed with recipe. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  5. Place pistachios, bread and sumac into mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  6. Add remaining 25 g oil and cook 3 min/120°C/speed 2.
  7. Remove eggplant from oven and sprinkle 2 tablespoons of the pistachio crumb over each eggplant. Bake for 10 minutes (180°C), until tender. Carefully transfer onto a serving platter, garnish with pomegranate arils just before serving.

Useful Items

baking tray (30 x 40 cm), baking paper, pastry brush, serving platter, paper towel, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • You can find harissa in the international aisle of most major supermarkets. To make your own harissa, refer to the recipe on Cookidoo® (www.cookidoo.com.au).
  • For a milder version, replace the harissa with tomato paste.
  • Did you know you can use your Thermomix® to easily remove pomegranate arils? Go to the Thermomix in Australia YouTube channel to watch the video.
