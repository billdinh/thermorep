Seafood Stew with Coconut Milk

Ingredients

  • 250 g rice
  • 50 g onions, halved
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 3 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only
  • 3 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 2 sprigs fresh tarragon, leaves only
  • 50 g olive oil
  • 200 g tinned chopped tomatoes
  • 80 g green peppers, cut in pieces
  • 1 fish stock cube (for 0.5 l)
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 200 g coconut milk
  • 500 g peeled, raw prawns, medium
  • 400 g fish fillets, e.g. Hake, sea bass, etc. cut in pieces (5 cm)
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper
  • 1 Tbsp fresh chives, finely chopped

Preparation

  1. Cook rice on hob according to packet instructions. Meanwhile, prepare stew.
  2. Place onions, garlic, coriander, parsley and tarragon in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add oil, tomatoes, peppers, fish stock and salt then blend 15 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Sauté 5 min/Varoma/speed 1.5.
  4. Add coconut milk and cook 5 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  5. Add prawns and cook 4 min/100°C//speed 
  6. Add fish fillet pieces and cayenne pepper then cook 4 min/100°C//speed . Sprinkle with chives and serve immediately with rice.
