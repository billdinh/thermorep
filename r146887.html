Spanish Green Lentil And Chorizo Soup

Ingredients

Soup

  • 1 onion, halved
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 red pepper, deseeded and cut in pieces
  • ½ tsp smoked paprika
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 1 carrot, cut in pieces
  • 2 celery stalks, cut in pieces
  • 100 g pumpkin, cubed (2-3 cm)
  • 150 g French green lentils, soaked overnight and drained
  • 1 dried bay leaf
  • 600 g water
  • 2 Tbsp vegetable stock paste (see Tips)
  • 250 g chorizo, sliced (1 cm)

Pancetta Croutons

  • 4 slices bread (example sourdough), each slice cut into 3 strips
  • 12 slices pancetta

Preparation

Soup

  1. Place onion, garlic, red pepper and paprika in mixing then chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add olive oil and, with simmering basket in place of measuring cup on mixing bowl lid, sauté 5 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Add carrot and celery then chop 4 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add all remaining soup ingredients and cook 30 min/100°C//speed .

Pancetta Croutons

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C and line a baking tray with baking paper.
  2. Wrap 1 pancetta slice around each of the bread slices and place on prepared baking tray. Bake for 5-10 minutes (180°C) or until crispy. Serve soup hot with pancetta croutons.

Useful Items
