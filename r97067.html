Beef Koftas with Saffron Yoghurt

Ingredients

  • 15 g fresh coriander, leaves only
  • 10 g fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 75 g onions, quartered
  • 500 g beef mince, broken in pieces
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt, plus extra, to taste
  • ½ tsp ground allspice
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper, plus extra, to taste
  • 1 pinch saffron threads
  • 1 Tbsp milk, hot
  • 200 g plain yoghurt
  • 2 tsp olive oil, for brushing

Preparation

  1. Place 12 wooden skewers in a bowl of water to soak for at least 30 minutes. If cooking under a grill (rather than on a barbecue), line a large baking tray with foil.
  2. Place coriander in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Remove 2 Tbsp and set aside.
  3. Add parsley and onions then chop 5 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add beef mince, ground coriander, cumin, paprika, salt, allspice and pepper then mix 10 sec/speed 5. Shape mixture into 12 sausages (10 cm long). Cover with cling film and set aside in fridge for 30 minutes. Meanwhile, heat grill to high (250°C) or light barbecue.
  5. Place 1 pinch saffron threads and 1 Tbsp hot milk in a small bowl and set aside.
  6. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh yoghurt into it. Remove bowl, add salt and pepper, to taste, stir in reserved coriander, then set bowl aside.
  7. Thread soaked skewers through koftas and brush with oil. Cook on barbecue, or place on lined baking tray and cook under grill (250°C), for 6-10 minutes, turning regularly, until browned and cooked through.
  8. Stir reserved saffron milk into yoghurt mixture. Serve alongside warm koftas.

Useful Items

baking tray, aluminium foil, wooden skewers, large bowl, cling film, bowl, pastry brush, grill, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • If skewers start to burn during cooking, cover the ends with foil. Metal skewers, which will not need soaking, can also be used.
