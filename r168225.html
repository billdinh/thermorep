Soft date cake with walnuts

Ingredients

  • 220 g butter, cut in pieces, plus extra for greasing
  • 220 g flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 100 g walnuts, shelled
  • 190 g pitted dates
  • 190 g water
  • 165 g sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract
  • 140 g date syrup
Nutrition

1720 kJ / 441 kcal

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180ºC. Grease a bundt cake tin (Ø 22-24 cm) and sprinkle with flour. Set aside.
  2. Place walnuts into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 5. Transfer into a bowl and reserve.
  3. Place dates and water into mixing bowl and heat 3 min/70°C/speed 3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, salt, butter, egg and vanilla and mix 40 sec/speed 6.
  5. Add 60 g reserved chopped walnuts and mix with spatula. Transfer mixture into prepared cake tin and bake for 45 minutes (180 ºC) or until a skewer inserted in the centre comes out clean. Allow cake to cool in tin for approx. 20 minutes before transferring to a serving plate.
  6. Pour date syrup on top of cake and sprinkle with remaining 40 g chopped walnuts before serving.

Useful Items

bundt cake tin (Ø 22-24 cm), serving plate, skewer, oven
