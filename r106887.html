Gluten and Grain Free Dinner Rolls

Ingredients

  • 7 oz raw almonds
  • 1 oz psyllium husks
  • 2 tsp gluten-free baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3 egg whites, from large eggs
  • 1 oz apple cider vinegar
  • 9 oz water, boiled
Nutrition
per 1 piece

11.2 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line a large baking sheet with parchment paper and set aside.
  2. Place almonds and psyllium into mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 9.
  3. Add baking powder and salt and combine Turbo/0.5 sec/4 times.
  4. Add egg whites and vinegar and knead 20 sec/.
  5. Add boiling water and knead 1 min/. Dough will be sticky but workable with spatula.
  6. Divide dough into 5 equal-sized pieces, form into rolls and place onto prepared baking sheet. Bake 50-55 minutes (350ºF), or until lightly golden.
  7. Allow to cool 5 minutes before transferring to a cooling rack. Serve warm or allow to cool completely (approx. 20 minutes) before transferring to an airtight container until ready to serve.

Useful Items

parchment paper, airtight container, cooling rack, large baking sheet, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • To ensure recipe results are truly gluten-free, always check your ingredient labels to ensure traces of wheat or gluten have not been used in the production process.
  • This recipe will work in a convection oven, but the rolls will not rise as much. A conventional oven setting is recommended.
