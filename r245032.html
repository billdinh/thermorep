Sea Bass with Castelluccio Lentils - Spigola con lenticchie di Castelluccio

Ingredients

Lentils

  • 200 g lentils, type Castelluccio
  • water, cold, for soaking

Citrus and Herb Topping

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 lemon, thin peelings of skin and juice
  • 1 fresh red chilli, deseeded
  • 15 g fresh parsley leaves
  • 15 g fresh basil leaves
  • 15 g fresh mint leaves
  • 50 g sun-dried tomatoes in oil, drained
  • 1 Tbsp pickled capers, drained
  • 45 g extra virgin olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 4 sea bass fillets, with skin

Cooking

  • 1000 g water
  • 1 vegetable stock cube (for 0.5 l)
  • 1 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil, plus extra for drizzling
  • 1 Tbsp lemon juice
  • fine sea salt, to taste
  • ground black pepper, to taste

Preparation

Lentils

  1. Soak lentils in a bowl of cold water for 30 minutes. Meanwhile, prepare topping.

Citrus and Herb Topping

  1. Place garlic, lemon peel and chilli in mixing bowl then grate 3 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add parsley, basil, mint, sun-dried tomatoes, capers, oil and lemon juice then chop Turbo/2 sec/1 time. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then chop again Turbo/2 sec/1 time.
  3. Arrange sea bass fillets, skin side down, in greased Varoma dish and tray, leaving some holes uncovered for steam to circulate. Spread topping evenly over fish, then cover with Varoma lid and set aside.

Cooking

  1. Drain soaked lentils then place in mixing bowl. Add water and stock cube then cook 16 min/100°C//speed 1.
  2. Place covered Varoma into position then steam above lentils 6-10 min/Varoma//speed 1 until fish and lentils are cooked.
  3. Drain cooked lentils through simmering basket then place in a bowl. Stir through 1 Tbsp oil and 1 Tbsp lemon juice then season with salt and pepper. Serve lentils scattered over a plate with sea bass on top, drizzled with extra oil.
