Carrot poppy seed muffins

Ingredients

Carrot muffins

  • 250 g raw almonds
  • 50 g brown sugar
  • 1 orange, zest only, no white pith
  • 300 g carrots, cut into pieces (approx. 3)
  • 4 eggs
  • 80 g gluten free cornflour
  • 1 tsp gluten free baking powder (see Tips)
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 tbsp poppy seeds
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • ½ tsp ground nutmeg
  • 40 g vegetable oil
  • 20 g sultanas
  • 50 g desiccated coconut

Topping

  • 6 tbsp crème fraîche
  • 1 carrot, peeled into thin ribbons, for decorating
  • pure maple syrup, for drizzling

Preparation

Carrot muffins

  1. Preheat oven to 170°C. Line a 12-hole muffin tray with muffin wrap papers and set aside.
  2. Place almonds into mixing bowl and mill 2 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place sugar and orange peel into mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 10.
  4. Add carrot and chop 8 sec/speed 5.
  5. Add all remaining muffin ingredients, including reserved almond meal, and combine 5 sec//speed 6, with aid of spatula.
  6. Spoon muffin mixture evenly into prepared muffin tray and bake for 25-30 minutes (170°C), or until a wooden skewer inserted into the centre of the muffin comes out clean. Transfer onto a wire rack to cool completely (approx. 30 minutes).

Topping

  1. Place 2 teaspoons crème fraîche on top of each muffin. Decorate with carrot ribbons and drizzle with syrup before serving.

Useful Items

12-hole muffin tray, muffin wrap papers, bowl, wooden skewer, wire rack, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • To make your own Gluten free baking powder, please refer to the recipe on this recipe chip.
  • Muffin wrap papers are available at most supermarkets. You can also use standard cupcake liners.
