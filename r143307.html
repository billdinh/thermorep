Four Cheese Sharing Flatbread

Ingredients

  • 50 g olive oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 50 g Parmesan cheese, cut in pieces
  • 80 g mozzarella, cut in slices
  • 120 g water
  • 270 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 80 g ricotta cheese
  • 70 g Gorgonzola

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C. Line a baking tray with baking paper, grease with olive oil and set aside.
  2. Place garlic cloves and Parmesan cheese in mixing bowl then grate 10 sec/speed 10.
  3. Add mozzarella and chop 3 sec/speed 4. Transfer cheese mixture to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place olive oil, water, plain flour, baking powder and salt in mixing bowl then knead 1 min 30 sec/. Transfer dough to a floured surface, form into a ball, divide into two pieces and with a rolling pin roll each piece into a desired shape (oval or rectangular) 3 mm thick. Place on prepared baking tray and bake for 10 minutes (220°C).
  5. Remove flatbreads from the oven, spread ricotta cheese and cheese mixture over the dough, leaving a 2 cm border, then cover the surface of the cheeses with small pieces of Gorgonzola. Bake for further 10-15 minutes (220°C), or until the cheese is completely melted and golden. Cut hot in small pieces and serve warm or cold.

Useful Items

baking tray and paper, rolling pin, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • This delicious bread is perfect for sharing; serve fresh from the oven for the best flavour and texture.
  • To reheat, wrap the bread in greaseproof paper or baking paper, and warm in the oven at 150°C for 15 minutes.
  • Replace the Gorgonzola with another blue cheese such as Stilton, Dolcelatte or Cambozola.
