Red Cabbage and Chicken Salad

Ingredients

  • 1000 g water
  • 400 g chicken breasts, skinless, diced (2 cm)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 50 g balsamic vinegar
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 15 g wholegrain mustard
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 40 g onions, quartered
  • 250 g red cabbage, core removed, cut in pieces
  • 120 g carrots, cut in pieces
  • 1 round lettuce leaves
  • 1 pear, cored, sliced lengthways (1 cm)
  • 150 g Brie, cut in pieces
  • 100 g walnut pieces

Preparation

  1. Place water in mixing bowl. Place Varoma dish in position and weigh in chicken. Cover then steam 18 min/Varoma/speed 2. Set Varoma aside and keep warm. Clean mixing bowl.
  2. Place garlic in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add balsamic vinegar, oil, mustard, salt and pepper then mix 3 sec/speed 5. Transfer dressing to a small jug and set aside.
  4. Place onions, red cabbage and carrots in mixing bowl then chop 15 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add 30 g reserved dressing and cook 5 min/120°C//speed 1. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Cook again 5 min/120°C//speed 1. Arrange lettuce leaves on a large serving plate. Scatter chopped salad over then add reserved steamed chicken, sliced pear, Brie and walnuts. Pour remaining dressing over then serve immediately.

Useful Items

jug, serving plate

Hints & Tricks

  • Camembert could be used instead of Brie.
  • The ratio of carrots to cabbage can be changed according to taste.
  • For a vegetarian version, omit chicken and increase amounts of cheese and nuts.
  • Enjoy with fresh bread.
