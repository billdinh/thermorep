Taiwanese sticky rice

Ingredients

  • 400 g glutinous rice
  • 500 g water, plus extra for soaking
  • 10 g dried shiitake mushrooms
  • 40 g oil
  • 10 g sesame oil
  • 20 g small peeled shrimp (see Tip)
  • 200 g boned pork belly meat, rind removed and flesh cut into thin strips (5 mm)
  • 60 g soy sauce
  • ½ tsp icing sugar
  • ½ tsp salt (optional)
  • 1 sprig fresh coriander, leaves, for garnishing

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh rice into it. Cover with water and set aside to soak for 1 hour. Using the simmering basket, drain rice and set aside.
  2. Place a separate bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh mushrooms into it, cover with water and set aside to soak, until needed.
  3. Place oils, shrimp and reserved drained mushrooms into mixing bowl and cook 3 min/100°C/speed .
  4. Add pork and cook 3 min/100°C//speed .
  5. Add soy sauce, icing sugar, salt (optional) and reserved rice and cook 3 min/100°C//speed .
  6. Line Varoma dish with baking paper and poke a few holes into base to ensure steam can circulate freely. Transfer meat and rice mixture into lined Varoma dish. Without cleaning mixing bowl, place 500 g water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 25 min/Varoma/speed 1, until rice and pork are cooked. Serve warm, garnished with coriander leaves.

Useful Items

bowl, baking paper

Hints & Tricks

  • Small peeled shrimp are dried and can be found in the refrigerated section in Asian food stores. They are eaten whole.
