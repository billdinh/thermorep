Green smoothie

Ingredients

  • 150 g cucumbers, cut in pieces
  • 60 g fresh spinach leaves
  • 3 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 3 sprigs fresh mint, leaves only
  • 1 lemon, peeled, white pith and pips removed
  • 1 cm fresh root ginger, cut in round slices (2 mm)
  • Â¼ tsp sea salt (optional)
  • 1 orange (approx. 100 g), peeled, white pith and pips removed, cut in quarters
  • 1 apple (approx. 100 g), cut in quarters
  • 50 - 100 g ice cubes
  • 50 - 100 g water

Preparation

  1. Place cucumbers, spinach leaves, parsley, mint, lemon, ginger, salt, orange, apple, ice cubes and water in mixing bowl and blend 1-2 min/speed 9 until desired consistency. Serve immediately.
