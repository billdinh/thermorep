Mac and Cheese Fritters

Ingredients

Mac and Cheese

  • 2 oz Gruyère cheese, cubed
  • 2 oz aged white cheddar cheese, cubed
  • 1 oz Parmesan cheese, cubed
  • 35 oz water
  • 8 oz elbow macaroni
  • 1 oz unsalted butter, diced
  • 1 oz all-purpose flour
  • 9 oz whole milk
  • ¼ tsp salt, to taste
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • ½ oz panko

Dredging

  • 2 ½ oz all-purpose flour
  • 4 oz panko
  • 3 large eggs
  • vegetable oil, to fry

Preparation

Mac and Cheese

  1. Place Gruyère, cheddar and Parmesan into mixing bowl and grate 7 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place water into mixing bowl, place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and boil 9 min/212°F/speed 1.
  3. Add pasta and with simmering basket on mixing bowl lid, cook for time indicated on package instructions/212°F//speed 1 until al dente. Drain pasta and transfer to a large bowl.
  4. Place butter and flour into mixing bowl, insert measuring cup into mixing bowl lid and cook 5 min/250°F/speed 1. Add milk, salt, pepper and nutmeg and cook 6 min/195°F/speed 4.
  5. Add reserved grated cheese and panko and mix 30 sec/speed 3. Transfer into bowl with cooked pasta and stir though with spatula. Cover with cling wrap and chill in the refrigerator (approx. 1 hr.).
  6. Scoop chilled mac and cheese into balls (approx. 1 oz ea.) and place on a baking sheet.

Dredging

  1. Place a shallow bowl onto mixing bowl lid, weigh in flour and set aside. Place another shallow bowl onto mixing bowl lid, weigh in panko and set aside. In a separate bowl, lightly whisk eggs.
  2. Dredge each mac and cheese ball in flour, then egg and finally panko. Gently shake off excess and return to baking sheet.
  3. In a deep frying pan or saucepot, heat 2-3 inches of oil to 360°F-370°F (use a thermometer). Working in batches, use a slotted spoon to fry breaded mac and cheese balls until golden (approx. 1 min.). Drain on paper towels and serve warm.

Useful Items

cling wrap, baking sheet, shallow bowls or dishes, slotted spoon, paper towels, large bowl, thermometer, deep frying pan or saucepot, wire whisk, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with Tabasco sauce.
  • Fried Mac and Cheese Fritters can be held in a warming oven (200°F) until ready to serve.
  • When adding the grated cheese, add in minced garlic, green onions, red pepper or diced ham.
  • Replace Gruyère cheese with a medium cheddar or mild white cheddar.
