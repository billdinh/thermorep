Sweet or Savoury Buckwheat Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp chia seeds
  • 3 Tbsp oat milk
  • 150 g buckwheat flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Â¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • 230 g oat milk
  • 2 Tbsp maple syrup (optional)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 Tbsp oil, for frying

Preparation

  1. Place chia seeds in mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and add 3 Tbsp oat milk. Set aside for 20 minutes.
  2. Place flour, baking powder and salt in mixing bowl then mix 5 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add oat milk, maple syrup (if using), vanilla extract and reserved chia mixture then mix 15 sec/speed 5.
  4. Heat oil in frying pan over medium heat. Using a tablespoon, drop pancake mixture into frying pan. Once it begins to bubble, flip over cooking for 1-2 minutes on both sides. Set aside to keep warm while cooking remaining pancakes in batches. Serve in stacks of 3-4 with chosen toppings.

Hints & Tricks

  • For savoury pancakes, omit maple syrup and vanilla. Serve as an alternative to bread, muffins or crumpets, topped with hummous, guacamole, beans or allergy-free cheese.
