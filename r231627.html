Rustic Potatoes with Chorizo

Ingredients

  • 5 oz Pepper Jack cheese, cubed (½ in.), divided
  • 3 oz white onion, quartered
  • 1 - 2 oz fresh jalapeño chili (approx. 3 chilies) halved, de-seeded optional, plus extra to garnish
  • 12 oz chorizo, cut into pieces (¼ in.)
  • 32 - 33 oz red potatoes, cut into pieces (½ in.)
  • 15 oz water
  • 2 oz unsalted butter, cubed (½ in.)
  • 1 oz whole milk
  • 2 ½ oz green onions, cut on the bias (¼ in.), divided
  • 1 pinch salt, to taste

Preparation

  1. Place cheese into mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place onion and jalapeño into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 6.
  3. Add chorizo and sauté 5 min/212°F/speed 1. Transfer to a plate lined with paper towels and allow to drain.
  4. Place potatoes and water into mixing bowl and cook 20 min/212°F//speed .
  5. Add butter, milk, 2 oz green onions, salt, reserved chorizo and 4 oz reserved grated cheese and combine with spatula. Transfer to a serving bowl with a lid and let sit for 5 minutes. Garnish with remaining grated cheese, green onions and jalapeño. Serve immediately.

Useful Items

serving bowl, paper towels

Hints & Tricks

  • Red potatoes can be replaced with Yukon gold. Cooking times will differ depending on variety of potato; check Yukon gold potatoes after 17 minutes. Test with a knife before continuing with next step.
