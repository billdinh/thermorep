Chocolate Sandwich Cookies with Vanilla Cream

Ingredients

Cookie Dough

  • 3 ½ oz black sesame seeds
  • 6 oz cake flour
  • 3 oz unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • 7 oz dark chocolate (50% cacao), in pieces
  • 8 oz unsalted butter, room temperature, in pieces
  • 5 oz sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract

Vanilla Cream Filling

  • 10 ½ oz bakers sugar
  • 3 ½ oz unsalted butter, room temperature, in pieces
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 tbsp whole milk
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract

Preparation

Cookie Dough

  1. Place black sesame seeds into mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 10.
  2. Add cake flour, cocoa, salt and baking soda and mix 10 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place chocolate into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Without measuring cup melt 1 min/120°F/speed 2.
  4. Add butter and melt 1 min/140°F/speed 2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and melt a further 1 min/140°F/speed 2.
  5. Add sugar, egg and vanilla and mix 5 sec/speed 4.
  6. Add reserved cocoa and flour mixture and mix with aid of spatula through hole in mixing bowl lid 15 sec/speed 6. Remove cookie dough from mixing bowl and form a rectangle (approx. 5 in. x 9 in.). Wrap in cling wrap and chill 30 minutes. Clean mixing bowl.
  7. Line a baking sheet with parchment and set aside. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  8. Cut dough into 3 parts and work with ⅓ of the dough at a time. Make sure you keep the reserved dough chilled until required. Place ⅓ of the dough between 2 sheets of cling wrap. Roll out chilled dough with a rolling pin to ⅛ in. thickness. With a round cookie cutter (approx. 2 in.), cut circles and place on prepared baking sheet.
  9. Bake (350°F) in 3 batches for 9 minutes each, watching carefully (cookies must be firm to the touch). Meanwhile prepare the filling.

Vanilla Cream Filling

  1. Place 5 oz sugar into mixing bowl and grind into confectioners sugar 1 min/speed 10, transfer to a bowl and set aside. Place remaining 5 ½ oz sugar into mixing bowl and grind into confectioners sugar 1 min/speed 10.
  2. Add reserved sugar, butter, salt, milk and vanilla and mix 10 sec/speed 3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and mix a further 10 sec/speed 3 to obtain an even texture. Place into icing bag and set aside.

Assembly

  1. When cookies are cool, sandwich 2 cookies together with (approx. ½ tsp) vanilla cream filling from the icing bag. Serve or store in an airtight container in the refrigerator.

Useful Items

cling wrap, rolling pin, icing bag, baking sheet and parchment paper, cookie cutter (2 in.), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Add a personal touch to your cookies with a cookie stamp. Before baking, choose your stamp, make your mark and pop them into the oven.
  • Bakers sugar is also labeled as superfine or caster sugar.
