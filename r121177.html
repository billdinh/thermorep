Nougat

Ingredients

  • 2 sheets rice paper
  • 400 g honey
  • 1 egg white
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 50 g dried cranberries (see Tips)
  • 100 g shelled and unsalted pistachios
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
626 kJ / 149 kcal
Protein
2 g
Carbohydrates
28.3 g
Fat
3.5 g

Preparation

  1. Cut 1 sheet of the rice paper to fit a loaf tin (9 x 25 cm). Set aside.
  2. Place a jug onto mixing bowl lid and weigh honey into it. Set jug aside.
  3. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg white and salt into mixing bowl and beat 3 min/37°C/speed 4,  without measuring cup.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula. Mix speed 3, slowly adding honey through hole in mixing bowl lid, then cook 50 min/100°C/speed 2, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  5. Leave to cool in mixing bowl for a maximum of 5 minutes, then cook for a further 25 min/100°C/speed 2, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid. Mixture should be thick and hold its shape. To test if your nougat will set, place a spoonful of mixture onto a plate and leave mixture to cool slightly (approx. 2-3 minutes). Mixture should be firm, easily formed into a ball and hold its shape when left to stand. If not, cook for an extra 3-5 min/100°C/speed 2, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid, taking care not to over cook. Remove butterfly whisk.
  6. Add cranberries, nuts and vanilla and stir with aid of spatula, then combine 20 sec//speed 2, until well incorporated.
  7. Working quickly, transfer mixture into prepared tin and smooth into corners using spatula. Cover with remaining piece of rice paper and using your fingertips smooth out nougat surface. Place into refrigerator for 2 hours. Clean and dry mixing bowl (see Tips).
  8. Run a knife along edge of tin to loosen edges and turn out nougat onto a chopping board. Set aside for 10 minutes to soften. Using a serrated knife, cut into slices or squares. Serve immediately or place into a sealable container and store in refrigerator until ready to serve. 

Useful Items

1 plate, jug, serrated bread knife, sealable container, loaf tin (9 x 25 cm), refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • The type of honey you use may affect the flavour and consistency. We recommend using a honey with a mild, delicate flavour.
  • To assist with cleaning the mixing bowl, place 500 g water into mixing bowl and boil 10 min/100°C/speed 1, then immediately discard water contents and thoroughly clean and dry mixing bowl.
  • Nougat must be stored in the refrigerator until ready to serve.
  • Cranberries and pistachios can be substituted with other dried fruit or nuts of choice.
