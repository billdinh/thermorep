Mango and cayenne juice

Ingredients

  • 120 g carrot (approx. 1), cut into pieces (3-4 cm)
  • 200 g ripe mango, flesh only, cut into pieces (3-4 cm)
  • 320 g freshly squeezed orange juice (chilled)
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper
  • 1 pinch ground turmeric
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
312 kJ / 74 kcal
Protein
1.54 g
Carbohydrates
14.62 g
Fat
0.23 g

Preparation

  1. Place carrot into mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add mango, orange juice, cayenne pepper and turmeric and blend 1 min 30 sec/speed 10. Transfer into a jug and place into refrigerator to chill for 20 minutes.
  3. Divide between 4 glasses and serve.

Useful Items

jug, 4 glasses, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Turmeric has long been used as a powerful anti-inflamatory agent in both Chinese and Indian cultures.
  • Oranges and mango are a rich source of Vitamin C, which has antioxidant properties that help protect cells from oxidative damage..

  • For a frozen treat in summer, pour juice into icy pole moulds and freeze.
  • You can store this juice overnight in the refrigerator.
