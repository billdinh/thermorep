Vegetarian sausage rolls

Ingredients

  • 1 brown onion (approx. 180 g), cut into halves
  • 1 tbsp dried oregano
  • 100 g walnuts
  • 90 g rolled oats
  • 3 eggs
  • 150 g feta cheese, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 1 tbsp tamari
  • 50 g breadcrumbs
  • 3 sheets ready-made puff pastry, slightly thawed
  • milk, for brushing
  • sesame seeds, for sprinkling (optional)
Nutrition
per 1 portion

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C. Line a baking tray with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place brown onion into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add dried oregano, walnuts and rolled oats and chop 3 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add eggs, feta cheese, tamari or soy sauce and breadcrumbs and combine 20 sec/speed 5.
  5. Cut puff pastry sheets into halves and transfer to ThermoMat or floured work surface.
  6. Place 1/6 of the mixture onto the long edge of 1 pastry sheet half and roll. Cut into four equal portions and place onto prepared baking tray. Brush pastry with milk and sprinkle with sesame seeds (optional). Repeat process with remaining mix and pastry sheet halves until all used.
  7. Bake for 20 minutes (200°C) until crisp and golden. Allow to cool slightly before serving warm.

Useful Items

baking tray and paper, ThermoMat, pastry brush, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve vegetarian sausage rolls with tomato ketchup. These are also ideal for lunch boxes or snacks.
  • If you are not serving sausage rolls immediately, allow them to cool slightly before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely. Store in an airtight container until use or freeze uncooked sausage rolls in an airtight container. Allow to thaw thoroughly before baking as per recipe.
  • Herbs of choice can be used in place of the dried oregano.
