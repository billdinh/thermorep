Shrimp Cakes

Ingredients

  • 18 oz large raw shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 oz pork belly, cut into pieces (optional)
  • 2 green onions, cut into pieces
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 12 sprigs cilantro, leaves and tender stems
  • ½ oz ginger, peeled (optional)
  • 1 tbsp tapioca flour
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • &frac18; tsp ground white pepper
  • vegetable oil, to fry
Nutrition
per 1 piece
Calories
406 kJ / 97 kcal
Protein
10 g
Carbohydrates
2 g
Fat
5 g

Preparation

  1. Place shrimp, pork belly, green onions, garlic, cilantro, ginger, tapioca flour, oil, fish sauce, sugar, salt and pepper into mixing bowl and chop Turbo/2 sec/3-4 times until well blended.
  2. Shape into 12 equal sized patties.
  3. Heat a large frying pan over medium heat and brush with oil. Working in batches, fry shrimp cakes until cooked through and golden brown (approx. 2 minutes each side). Drain on paper towels and serve hot.

Useful Items

frying pan

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with Ginger Dipping Sauce, soy sauce or a squeeze of lime.
