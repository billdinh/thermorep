Steak tartare

Ingredients

  • 500 g beef sirloin, cut into cubes (approx. 4 x 4 cm)
  • 250 g spring onions, cut into halves (approx. 3 stalks)
  • 100 g pickled gherkins, drained
  • 2 eggs, hard-boiled
  • 1 onion, cut into pieces (approx. 150-200 g)
  • 2 sprigs fresh parsley (leaves only)
  • 2 sprigs fresh chervil (leaves only)
  • 4 fresh tarragon leaves
  • 20 g lemon juice, freshly squeezed
  • 80 g ketchup
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce (optional)
  • 3 tbsp capers
  • 1 tsp fine salt
  • 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper

Preparation

  1. Place meat in a single layer on a tray and keep in freezer for 20-30 minutes.
  2. Place spring onions in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Remove from mixing bowl and set aside in a bowl.
  3. Place gherkins in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Remove from mixing bowl and set aside in another bowl.
  4. Place hard boiled eggs in mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 4.Remove from mixing bowl and set aside in another bowl.
  5. Chop onion, parsley, chervil and tarragon 5 sec/speed 5. Remove from mixing bowl and set aside in a large bowl.
  6. Place half of semi-frozen meat in mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 5-10, increasing speed gradually. Remove from mixing bowl and set aside in bowl with onion and herbs.
  7. Place remaining semi-frozen meat in mixing bowl and chop 10 sec/speed 5-10, increasing speed gradually. Remove from mixing bowl and set aside in same bowl.
  8. Add lemon juice, ketchup, Worcestershire sauce, capers, salt and pepper to bowl and mix with spatula. Divide meat into 6 portions and form steak tartare (you can do so with the help of a ring mould). Serve steak tartare with spring onions, gherkins and chopped boiled eggs .
