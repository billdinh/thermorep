Chicken and Pear Pie

Ingredients

Gluten Free Pastry

  • unsalted butter, to grease
  • 5 oz rice flour
  • 2 ½ oz arrowroot flour
  • 3 oz corn starch, plus extra to dust
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 2 tsp xanthan gum
  • 5 ½ oz unsalted butter, chilled
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 ½ oz water, chilled

Chicken and Pear Filling

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 leek, white part only, cut into pieces
  • 2 oz unsalted butter
  • 24 oz chicken thigh, boneless and skinless, cut into cubes (1 in.)
  • 3 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves only
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 2 oz water
  • 2 - 3 tbsp corn starch
  • 1 tbsp vegetable stock paste (see Tips)
  • 2 oz white wine
  • 5 ½ oz pear (approx. 1 pear) peeled, cored and cut into cubes (¾ in.)
  • 2 oz heavy whipping cream (35% fat)

Preparation

Gluten Free Pastry

  1. Grease a deep-dish pie pan and set aside. Place rice flour, arrowroot flour, corn starch, salt and xanthan gum into mixing bowl and mix 5 sec/speed 4.
  2. Add butter and combine 10 sec/speed 6.
  3. Add egg, vinegar and water and mix 10 sec/speed 6 and knead 30 sec/.
  4. Transfer dough onto a silicone baking mat or floured work surface and work into a tight ball. Wrap in cling wrap and place in refrigerator to chill for a minimum of 30 minutes.
  5. Preheat oven to 350°F. Roll out 2/3 of the pastry between a silicone mat and a sheet of parchment paper (or two sheets of parchment) into a circle (10 in.).
  6. Line pie pan with rolled out pastry and chill in refrigerator for 15 minutes. Wrap remaining pastry in cling wrap and refrigerate until ready to use.
  7. Blind bake pastry crust for 20 minutes (350°F) or until lightly golden. Set pastry crust aside to cool while you prepare the filling.

Chicken and Pear Filling

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Place garlic and leek into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add butter and sauté 3 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  4. Add chicken, thyme and pepper and cook 5 min/195°F//speed 1. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in water. Add corn starch to bowl and stir until dissolved.
  6. Add corn starch and water mixture, stock paste and wine and cook 5 min/195°F//speed 1.
  7. Add pear and cook 8 min/195°F//speed 1.
  8. Add cream and combine 20 sec//speed 1.
  9. Transfer chicken and pear filling into pastry crust. Roll out remaining refrigerated pastry on a silicone bread mat or floured work surface into a circle (9 in.).
  10. Place pastry over pie and seal edges by pressing down with fingertips or a fork. Make two slits in center of the pastry to allow steam to escape and bake 40-50 minutes (400°F) or until golden brown. Serve hot.

Useful Items

silicone baking mat, cling wrap, parchment paper, rolling pin, ceramic or glass bowl, deep pie pan (approx. 9 in.), refrigerator, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Refer to the Basic Cookbook for the Vegetable Stock Paste recipe.
  • Serve Chicken and Pear Pie as a hearty meal with your favorite steamed vegetables or salad on the side.
  • Leftovers store well in a sealable container in the refrigerator for lunches the next day.
  • To ensure recipe results are truly gluten-free, always check your ingredient labels to ensure traces of wheat or gluten have not been used in the production process.
  • For tips on how to blind bake your pastry, refer to any of the pastry recipes in the Basic Cookbook.
