Cranberry and Camembert stuffed chicken

Ingredients

Cranberry pesto stuffing

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 60 g shallot (approx. 1/2 shallot)
  • 20 g butter
  • 50 g dried cranberries
  • 30 g hazelnuts
  • 2 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley, leaves only
  • 1 pinch fine salt
  • 1 pinch black pepper, freshly ground
  • 150 g Camembert cheese, cut into pieces

Chicken, potatoes and mustard sauce

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 60 g shallot (approx 1/2 shallot)
  • 10 fresh chives, cut into pieces
  • 20 g butter
  • 500 g water
  • 1 - 2 tsp chicken stock paste, homemade
  • 6 potatoes, medium-sized, well-washed and cut into pieces (3-4cm)
  • 900 g chicken breast fillets, skinless (3 fillets, approx. 300 g each)
  • 150 - 200 g fresh green beans, trimmed
  • 20 g dry white wine
  • 2 tbsp plain flour
  • 3 tsp hot mustard
  • 2 tsp jarred horseradish
  • 2 tsp wholegrain mustard
  • 1 - 2 pinches fine salt, to taste

Preparation

Cranberry pesto stuffing

  1. Place garlic, shallot and butter into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then cook 2 min/100°C/speed 1
  2. Add dried cranberries, hazelnuts, parsley, salt, pepper and Camembert cheese and chop 10 sec/speed 5. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Chicken, potatoes and mustard sauce

  1. Place garlic, eschalot, chives and butter into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then cook 2 min/100°C/speed 1
  2. Add water and stock paste. Insert simmering basket, then place potatoes into it.
  3. Place chicken breasts onto a work surface, then slice each breast horizontally taking care not to cut all the way through. Fill chicken breasts with reserved cranberry stuffing, then insert 1 or 2 toothpicks along the edge of the opening in the breasts to enclose the filling.
  4. Place stuffed chicken breasts onto Varoma tray. Close Varoma lid, then place Varoma into position and cook 10 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  5. Turn chicken breasts over to ensure even cooking, then close Varoma lid and cook for a further 5-10 min/Varoma/speed 2, or until chicken is cooked. Remove Varoma and transfer chicken onto a plate.
  6. Place Varoma dish back into position, then weigh green beans into it. Close Varoma lid and cook 5 min/Varoma/speed 2, or until potatoes and beans are cooked to your liking. Meanwhile, place a non-stick frying pan over a medium-high heat and add chicken breasts. Cook each breast for 2-3 minutes each side or until chicken is golden.
  7. Transfer chicken breasts, potatoes and beans into a large bowl and cover to keep warm. Retain steaming liquid in mixing bowl.
  8. Add white wine, flour, hot mustard, horseradish, wholegrain mustard and salt to mixing bowl and cook 4 min/100°C/speed 3, without measuring cup. Transfer mustard sauce into a sauce boat.
  9. Remove toothpicks from chicken breasts, then cut each breast into half and serve immediately with potatoes, green beans and mustard sauce.
