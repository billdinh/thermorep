Mango Cheesecake

Ingredients

Almond and Macadamia Base

  • 40 g butter, plus extra for greasing
  • 120 g blanched almonds
  • 120 g macadamia nuts
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
  • 1 Tbsp maple syrup
  • 40 g desiccated coconut

Cheesecake

  • 5 gelatine leaves
  • 120 g coconut sugar
  • 1 mangoes, flesh only (approx. 260 g)
  • 500 g cream cheese
  • 300 g pouring (whipping) cream

Mango Coulis

  • 20 g coconut sugar
  • 200 g mango, flesh only, plus extra finely sliced for garnish
  • 1 Tbsp lemon juice

Preparation

Almond and Macadamia Base

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Grease and line a spring form tin (Ø 20 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place almonds and macadamias in mixing bowl then chop 4-6 sec/speed 6, or until evenly chopped.
  3. Add salt, maple syrup, coconut and 40 g butter then mix 20 sec/speed 4, or until combined. Transfer mixture to prepared cake tin, then using the back of a spoon, press down firmly to form a base.
  4. Bake for 12-16 minutes (180°C) or until lightly brown. Set base aside in tin and allow to cool. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Cheesecake

  1. Place gelatine leaves in a small bowl of cold water and set aside to soak for 5 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, place coconut sugar in mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 9.
  3. Add mango and cream cheese then mix 30 sec/speed 5, with aid of spatula.
  4. Squeeze excess water from softened gelatine leaves then melt in a saucepan over a gentle heat until just dissolved. Add to mixing bowl.
  5. Add cream then combine 40 sec/speed 5, stopping to scrape down sides of mixing bowl if necessary. Pour mixture evenly over base in tin and set aside in fridge to set (approx. 5 hours). Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl.

Mango Coulis

  1. After this time, place coconut sugar in mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 9.
  2. Add mango and lemon juice then blend 15 sec/speed 7. Spread mango coulis over cheesecake and place in fridge to set for 30 minutes.
  3. Garnish with extra finely sliced mango and serve chilled.

Useful Items

spring form tin (Ø 20 cm), baking paper, saucepan, oven, hob, refrigerator
