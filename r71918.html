Cloudy apple cider jelly

Ingredients

  • 6 titanium strength gelatine leaves (sheets)
  • water, chilled, to cover gelatine
  • 20 g raw sugar
  • ¼ tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 red apple, core removed and cut into quarters, plus extra, finely sliced for garnishing
  • 330 g alcoholic apple cider
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
668 kJ / 159 kcal
Protein
3 g
Carbohydrates
32 g
Fat
0.09 g

Preparation

  1. Place gelatine leaves into a glass bowl and cover with chilled water. Leave to soak for 5-7 minutes or until swelled and softened.
  2. Place sugar and cinnamon into mixing bowl and mill 10 sec/speed 10.
  3. Add apple and blend 3 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add 100 g of the alcoholic apple cider and blend 2 min/speed 8, then cook 3 min/100°C/speed 2.
  5. Squeeze gelatine leaves to remove excess water, add squeezed gelatine leaves to mixing bowl and combine 5 sec/speed 2. Discard soak water.
  6. Pour mixture into a bowl and place into refrigerator to chill for 30 minutes or until jelly is just starting to set. Add remaining cider and mix well with spatula. Pour into serving dishes then refrigerate until set (approx. 3-4 hours or overnight) prior to serving.
