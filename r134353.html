Green Tea Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 10 g linseeds
  • 300 g water
  • 1 green tea teabag
  • 20 g fresh spinach leaves
  • 20 g kale, roughly chopped
  • 10 g fresh root ginger, peeled, cut in round slices (2 mm)
  • 300 g Granny Smith apples, quartered, plus extra slices for garnish
  • 100 g green grapes, plus extra for garnish
  • 20 - 50 g honey, to taste
  • ice cubes, for serving

Preparation

  1. Place linseeds in mixing bowl and grind 20 sec/speed 9. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place water in mixing bowl and heat 3 min/100Â°C/speed 1.
  3. Add teabag then transfer to a bowl and leave to infuse and cool completely. Discard teabag.
  4. Place spinach, kale, ginger, apples, grapes, honey, reserved ground linseeds and cooled tea in mixing bowl then blend 1-2 min/speed 9, until desired texture. Serve over ice, garnished with apple slices and grapes.
