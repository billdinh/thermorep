Crab Cakes

Ingredients

  • 150 g bread, torn in pieces
  • 30 g shallots, halved
  • 30 g celery stalks, cut in pieces (5 mm)
  • 5 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only (approx. 5 g)
  • 60 g red pepper, cut in pieces (5 mm)
  • 2 Tbsp mayonnaise
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp Old Bay Seasoning® (see tip)
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper, or to taste
  • 450 - 500 g crab meat
  • vegetable oil, for frying

Preparation

  1. Line a baking tray with baking paper or foil.
  2. Place bread in mixing bowl and grate 30 sec/speed 9. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place shallots, celery and parsley in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 6.
  4. Add red pepper and chop 6 sec/speed 4.
  5. Add mayonnaise, eggs, mustard, Worcestershire sauce, Old Bay Seasoning®, salt and pepper then mix 8 sec/speed 3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  6. Add crab meat and 4 Tbsp reserved breadcrumbs then mix 5 sec//speed .
  7. Shape into 6 crab cakes. Place remaining breadcrumbs on a plate, lightly coat crab cakes with breadcrumbs and transfer to prepared baking tray. Cover with cling film and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
  8. Heat oil in a large frying pan over a medium-high heat. When oil is hot, place crab cakes in pan (in batches if necessary) and fry until golden brown (approx. 3-5 minutes each side). Drain on paper towels then serve immediately.

Useful Items

baking paper, aluminium foil, paper towels, cling film, baking tray, large frying pan, refrigerator, hob

Hints & Tricks

  • Add 1-2 tsp fresh coriander or dill leaves (5-6 sprigs, leaves only) to the mixture.
  • Crab cakes can be grilled instead of fried. Grill on a high temperature for 3-4 minutes each side or until golden brown.
  • Serve crab cakes with sweet chilli dipping sauce, lemon wedges, or tartar sauce.
  • Sprinkle crab cakes with fresh coriander leaves, chopped chives or the green part of spring onions, sliced thinly.
  • If using shop-bought breadcrumbs or Panko, simply omit grating of bread and add with shallots .
  • To make your own Old Bay Seasoning®, mix together 1 Tbsp celery salt, ¼ tsp paprika, ⅛ tsp ground black pepper, ⅛ tsp cayenne pepper, 1 pinch mustard powder and 1 pinch ground nutmeg.
