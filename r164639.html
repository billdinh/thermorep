Pork Stuffing Balls

Ingredients

  • 1 orange, thin peel only, no pith
  • 4 oz stale bread
  • 1 tsp ground nutmeg
  • ½ tsp ground cloves
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp freshly ground pepper
  • 2 oz fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 2 oz yellow onion, quartered
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 oz bacon, diced
  • ½ oz extra virgin olive oil, plus extra to grease
  • 2 oz pine nuts
  • 32 oz ground pork
  • 3 oz heavy whipping cream
  • 2 large eggs
  • 25 oz water
Nutrition

Preparation

  1. Place orange peel, bread, nutmeg, cloves, salt, pepper and parsley into mixing bowl and chop 15 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place onion, garlic, bacon and olive oil into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add pine nuts and cook 6 min/212°F/speed 1.
  4. Add pork, cream, reserved spiced breadcrumbs and eggs and with aid of spatula, mix 10 sec/speed 4. Shape the mixture into 18-20 balls (approx. 2 in.). Grease Varoma tray and dish and arrange with evenly spaced stuffing balls.
  5. Place water into mixing bowl, place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 20-25 min/Varoma/speed 3. Serve Pork Stuffing Balls as an appetizer or side dish.
