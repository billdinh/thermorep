Barbecue Sauce

Ingredients

Dry Rub

  • 3 oz salt
  • 1 ½ heaping tbsp black peppercorns
  • 1 cinnamon stick, in pieces
  • 1 heaping tsp coffee beans
  • 1 heaping tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 10 oz brown sugar

Barbecue Sauce

  • 5 ½ oz yellow onions, halved (approx. 1 md. onion)
  • ½ oz garlic cloves (approx. 5 cloves)
  • ½ oz canola oil
  • 1 tsp mustard
  • 3 oz apple cider vinegar
  • 3 oz Worcestershire sauce
  • 3 oz molasses
  • 9 oz ketchup

Preparation

Dry Rub

  1. Place salt, pepper, cinnamon stick, coffee, cumin seeds and chili powder into mixing bowl and grind 45 sec/speed 9.
  2. Add brown sugar and grind 20 sec/speed 9. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.

Barbecue Sauce

  1. Place onions, garlic and oil into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and sauté 5 min/250°F/speed 1.
  2. Add 1 oz Dry Rub and mustard and cook 30 sec/250°F/speed 1.
  3. Add vinegar, Worcestershire and molasses and cook 10 min/250°F/speed 1.
  4. Add ketchup and blend 30 sec/speed 9. Transfer to a jar and serve or refrigerate until needed.

Useful Items

airtight container, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • To ensure preservation, clean jars thoroughly in hot soapy water. Rinse by pouring boiling water over them. Prepare for filling by placing clean jars in a baking pan and heat in oven (212°F) until required. Pour into jars while jars are still warm and proceed as directed by the recipe.
  • Serve with grilled meat.
