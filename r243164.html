Mexican Creamed Corn

Ingredients

  • 3 oz yellow onions, cut into pieces
  • 2 dried 'chile de árbol' chilies
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 oz vegetable oil
  • 23 oz fresh corn kernels
  • 10 ½ oz water
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 2 leaves fresh epazote, shredded
  • 8 tsp mayonnaise
  • 2 limes, quartered
  • 8 tsp panela cheese, crumbled
  • fresh bird's eye chilies or fresh pequin chilies, to taste
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
686 kJ / 164 kcal
Protein
4 g
Carbohydrates
19 g
Fat
10 g

Preparation

  1. Place onions, chilies, garlic and oil into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  2. Add corn and cook 10 min/250°F//speed 1.
  3. Add water, salt and epazote and cook 6 min/195°F//speed 2.
  4. Divide mixture into 8 serving glasses and add 1 tsp mayonnaise, 1 tsp cheese and ¼ lime to each glass. Serve with chilies on the side, to taste.
