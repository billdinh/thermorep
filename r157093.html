Quinoa Salad with Brazil Nut and Cilantro Pesto

Ingredients

  • 5 oz Brazil nuts
  • 35 oz water
  • 7 oz quinoa
  • 10 ½ - 12 oz butternut squash, diced (approx. ½-¾ in. pieces)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 lime peels, to make zest, no pith
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt, to taste
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1 - 2 oz fresh cilantro leaves, to taste, divided
  • 1 oz brown rice vinegar
  • ½ oz lime juice
  • 6 ½ oz olive oil
  • 3 ½ oz radishes, stems removed, halved
  • 1 oz shallots, halved (approx. ¼ in.)
  • 3 ½ oz Granny Smith apples, stems removed, quartered
  • 3 ½ oz jicama, peeled cut into pieces (approx. 1 in.)
  • 3 oz scallions, hand sliced on the bias (approx. ¼ in.)

Preparation

  1. Place nuts into mixing bowl and dry roast without measuring cup 6 min/250°F/speed . Transfer to a bowl and set aside (see Tip).
  2. Place water into mixing bowl and insert simmering basket. Weigh quinoa into simmering basket and rinse 10 sec/speed 5. Stir quinoa with aid of spatula through hole in mixing bowl lid to ensure a thorough rinsing and steam 10 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  3. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh in squash, secure Varoma lid and cook 10 min/Varoma/speed 2. Remove Varoma and transfer cooked squash to a large serving bowl. Remove simmering basket with spatula, transfer quinoa to serving bowl with squash and allow to cool. Discard steaming water and dry mixing bowl.
  4. Place garlic, lime peel, salt and pepper into mixing bowl and zest 30 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add roasted nuts and 0.5-1 oz cilantro and grind 10 sec/speed 8. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and grind a further 5 sec/speed 8.
  6. Add vinegar, lime juice and oil and mix 20 sec/speed 7. Transfer 6.5 oz of dressing into a bowl with lid and set aside.
  7. Add radishes, shallots, apples, jicama and remaining 0.5-1 oz cilantro leaves into mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 4.5.
  8. Add scallions, transfer chopped vegetables to bowl with quinoa and gently toss though. Serve Quinoa Salad with Brazil Nut and Cilantro Pesto.

Hints & Tricks

  • Quinoa can be steamed in advance and stored in the refrigerator, ready for use. Just omit the quinoa cooking step and continue as directed.
  • Quinoa can be replaced with quinoa and ancient grain mix.
  • Prepping ingredients while Thermomix® cooks saves time: prepare butternut squash while Brazil nuts roast and prepare radishes, shallots, scallions, green apple, jicama and cilantro while quinoa cooks.
