Cornbread

Ingredients

  • 210 g cornmeal, finely ground (see tip)
  • 140 g plain flour
  • 20 g caster sugar
  • 1 Tbsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 150 g unsalted butter, diced, plus extra for greasing
  • 400 g milk
  • 2 medium eggs
Nutrition
per 1 slice

1178 kJ / 282 kcal

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C and grease a round cake tin (Ø 20 cm).
  2. Place cornmeal, flour, sugar, baking powder and salt in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place butter in mixing bowl and melt 2 min/90°C/speed 3.
  4. Add milk and eggs then mix 15 sec/speed 5.
  5. Add reserved cornmeal mixture then mix 10 sec/speed 4. Transfer batter to prepared tin and bake for 40-45 minutes (200°C) until a skewer inserted in centre comes out clean.
  6. Leave to cool in tin for 5 minutes, then remove and cool completely on a wire rack. Slice to serve.

Useful Items

round cake tin (Ø 20 cm), skewer, wire rack, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Cornbread, popular in Southern cooking, is a bread with a cake-like texture and slightly sweet flavour. Whilst our recipe is delicious plain, why not try experimenting by adding fresh herbs, cheese, or even sliced jalapeños.
  • Cornmeal is sometimes sold as polenta meal, or fine polenta can be used.
  • The bread can be baked in a loaf tin (450 g/1 lb) for the same time, or for cornbread muffins in individual muffin tins for 18-20 minutes (200°C).
