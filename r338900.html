Hot Wings with Blue Cheese Dip

Ingredients

Wings

  • 75 g plain flour
  • ¼ tsp paprika
  • ¼ tsp cayenne pepper
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • 10 chicken wings

Blue Cheese Dip

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 120 g sour cream
  • 60 g mayonnaise
  • 70 g blue cheese, crumbled
  • 15 g lemon juice
  • 1 Tbsp milk
  • fine sea salt, to taste
  • ground black pepper, to taste

Hot Sauce

  • sunflower oil, for deep frying
  • 110 g unsalted butter, diced
  • 120 g chilli sauce
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • ¼ tsp garlic powder

Preparation

Wings

  1. Place flour, paprika, cayenne and salt in mixing bowl then mix 10 sec/speed 5.
  2. Place chicken wings in a large bowl, pour over flour mixture, then stir until evenly coated. Cover and refrigerate for 1 hour minimum.

Blue Cheese Dip

  1. Place garlic in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add sour cream, mayonnaise, blue cheese, lemon juice and milk in mixing bowl then mix 5-10 sec/speed 5 until combined but still with small pieces of cheese. Adjust seasoning to taste then transfer to a serving bowl, cover and refrigerate until required. Clean mixing bowl.

Hot Sauce

  1. Preheat oil in a deep fat fryer to 190°C. Preheat oven to 100°C.
  2. Place butter, chilli sauce, pepper and garlic powder in mixing bowl then heat 10 min/95°C/speed 0.5. Meanwhile, fry wings in batches in hot oil for 10-15 minutes or until browned. Transfer to a baking dish lined with paper towels and keep warm in oven while cooking remaining wings.
  3. Remove paper towels from baking dish and pour hot sauce over wings, stirring to coat.
  4. Serve immediately with blue cheese dip.

Useful Items

large bowl, cling film, baking dish, paper towels, deep fat fryer, serving bowl, oven, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Do not be tempted to skip the refrigeration step on this recipe - this makes the flour sticky and results in superbly crispy wings!
  • Any strong blue cheese should work in the dip, but Danish Blue or Gorgonzola Piccante are best.
