Courgette and cod lasagne

Ingredients

  • 600 g courgettes, cut lengthwise into slices (approx. 0,5 cm)
  • 500 g cod fillet, skinless, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 150 g Parmesan cheese, cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 150 g peeled shrimps
  • 400 g water
  • 800 g milk
  • 80 g flour
  • 1 pinch fine salt, to taste
  • 1 pinch black pepper, freshly ground, to taste
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg, to taste

Preparation

  1. Place courgette in Varoma dish leaving some holes uncovered for steam to circulate. Place cod on Varoma tray. Insert Varoma tray into Varoma dish and set aside.
  2. Place Parmesan cheese into mixing bowl and grate 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place garlic and olive oil in mixing bowl and chop 8 sec/speed 5 and sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  4. Add peeled shrimp and sauté 2 min/120°C//speed . Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  5. Place water into mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position and steam 15 min/Varoma/speed 2. Remove Varoma and set aside.
  6. Empty mixing bowl. Place milk, flour, salt, pepper and nutmeg into mixing bowl and cook 8 min/90°C/speed 4.
  7. Preheat oven to 200° C.
  8. Transfer cooked cod fillets to a plate and flake with aid of two forks.
  9. In a heat-resistant casserole dish greased with olive oil, arrange a layer of courgette slices, cover with some cod and shrimp, sprinkle with a little grated cheese and cover with a layer of sauce. Continue forming layers until all ingredients are used up, the last layers being courgette, sauce and grated cheese. Bake for 15 minutes (200°C) until golden brown.

Useful Items

casserole dish (20 cm x 30 cm x 6 cm), oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve Courgette and cod lasagne accompanied with a salad.
