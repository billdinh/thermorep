Churros

Ingredients

Cinnamon sugar

  • 100 g raw sugar
  • 1 cinnamon quill

Chocolate dipping sauce

  • 200 g dark chocolate, broken into pieces
  • 90 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 1 tsp chilli powder (optional)
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon

Churros

  • 300 g water
  • 100 g salted butter
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 180 g plain flour
  • 2 eggs
  • vegetable oil, for deep frying

Preparation

Cinnamon sugar

  1. Place sugar and cinnamon quill into mixing bowl and mill 30 sec/speed 10. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.

Chocolate dipping sauce

  1. Place chocolate into mixing bowl and grate 8 sec/speed 8.
  2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then heat 3 min/60°C/speed 2.
  3. Add cream, chilli powder (optional) and ground cinnamon and mix 1 min/60°C/speed 3. Transfer into a small thermal serving bowl or other small bowl and cover to keep warm. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Churros

  1. Place water, butter and salt into mixing bowl and cook 4 min/80°C/speed 3.
  2. Add flour and cook 4 min/80°C/speed 3.
  3. Mix 1 min/speed 4, adding eggs 1 at a time through hole in mixing bowl lid, ensuring the first is incorporated before adding the next. Mixture should be smooth and glossy. Transfer mixture into a piping bag with a star-shaped nozzle (2 cm).
  4. Place a saucepan or deep frying pan over medium-high heat and add oil (5 cm high). Heat to 180°C; to check temperature use a kitchen thermometer or until a teaspoon of batter turns golden in 15 seconds. Working in batches, pipe lengths of dough (15 cm) into hot oil and cook for 4-5 minutes, stirring with tongs occasionally, until deep golden brown. Remove from oil with tongs and drain on kitchen towel (makes approx. 16).
  5. Dust with reserved cinnamon sugar and serve warm with chocolate dipping sauce.

Useful Items

bowl, small thermal serving bowl or other small bowl, piping bag with star nozzle (2 cm), kitchen towel, large frying pan, stove top
