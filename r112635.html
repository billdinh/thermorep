Sun-dried tomato and pepita bread

Ingredients

  • 40 g Parmesan cheese, crust removed and cut into pieces (3 cm)
  • 80 g sun-dried tomatoes
  • 140 g buckwheat
  • 200 g brown rice
  • 140 g arrowroot flour
  • 40 g chia seeds
  • 40 g pepitas, plus extra for sprinkling
  • 1 tsp xanthan gum
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 400 g water, plus extra for sprinkling
  • 1 tbsp coconut sugar
  • 2 tsp dried instant yeast
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp tomato paste
  • poppy seeds, for sprinkling

Preparation

  1. Place Parmesan cheese into mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 9. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place sun-dried tomatoes into mixing bowl and chop 2 sec/speed 5. Transfer into a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Place buckwheat and rice into mixing bowl and mill 1 min 30 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add arrowroot, chia seeds, pepitas, xanthan gum and salt and mix 6 sec/speed 6. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  5. Place water, coconut sugar, yeast, oil, vinegar and eggs into mixing bowl and heat 2 min/37°C/speed 2, then mix 15 sec/speed 6. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  6. Add tomato paste and reserved buckwheat flour mixture and mix 5 sec/speed 6.
  7. Add reserved sun-dried tomatoes and half of the grated Parmesan cheese, then knead 1 min/. Pour batter into a loaf tin (25 x 9 x 11 cm), cover with plastic wrap and leave to prove (approx. 1 hour).
  8. Preheat oven to 210°C. Sprinkle loaf with water, poppy seeds and remaining grated Parmesan cheese and set aside for 10 minutes. Bake for 35 minutes (210°C), or until golden. Serve warm or cold.

Useful Items

bowl, loaf tin (25 x 9 x 11 cm), plastic wrap, oven
