Wild Salmon Fishcakes

Ingredients

  • 2 slices sourdough bread, roughly torn
  • 1 lemon, thin peelings of skin, plus slices for serving
  • 15 g fresh flat-leaf parsley
  • 520 g fresh salmon fillets, skinless, cut in pieces
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 Tbsp ghee
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
1061 kJ / 254 kcal
Protein
22 g
Carbohydrates
7.2 g
Fat
15.2 g

Preparation

  1. Place bread, lemon peel and parsley in mixing bowl then chop 10 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add salmon, egg and salt then mince 7 sec/speed 5.
  3. Shape salmon mixture into 6 fishcakes (Ø 7 cm).
  4. Melt ghee in a frying pan over a medium heat. Fry fishcakes for 4-5 minutes on each side until cooked through.
  5. Serve with sliced lemon. Sweet potato wedges and green vegetables make a great accompaniment.

Useful Items

frying pan, hob

Hints & Tricks

  • Lean protein is required to help repair injury and to build muscle. These wild salmon fishcakes also provide a healthy dose of omega 3 fatty acids, also known as ‘essential’ fatty acids (that means we do not make these fats ourselves). Omega 3 is anti-inflammatory which is even more important for those exercising on a regular and intense basis.
