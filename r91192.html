Tagliatelle with Porcini Mushrooms

Ingredients

  • 1 oz fresh parsley, leaves only
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 2 oz extra virgin olive oil, divided
  • 4 ½ oz fresh porcini mushrooms, sliced
  • 4 oz fresh mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 ¼ tsp salt, divided
  • 43 oz water
  • 10 ½ 1 - 2 oz tagliatelle (long dried egg pasta),
  • 1 - 2 pinches freshly ground black pepper,

Preparation

  1. Place parsley into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7. Transfer 1 tbsp chopped parsley into a bowl and set aside. Leave remainder in mixing bowl.
  2. Add garlic clove and 1.5 oz olive oil and sauté 3 min/250°F/speed 1. Remove garlic clove if desired.
  3. Add porcini mushrooms, fresh mushrooms and ¼ tsp salt and cook 12 min/212°F//speed . Transfer to a serving bowl, keep warm and set aside.
  4. Place water into mixing bowl and bring to a boil 10 min/212°F/speed 1.
  5. Add tagliatelle and remaining 1 tsp salt and without measuring cup cook for time indicated on package/212°F//speed 1 or until al dente. Drain pasta through Varoma dish and transfer into serving bowl with reserved porcini mushrooms. Add remaining 0.5 oz olive oil, black pepper and reserved chopped parsley, combine with spatula and serve hot.

Hints & Tricks

  • This recipe calls for two types of mushrooms but you can adapt to taste.
  • The quantity of salt can be increased to taste depending on the quality of the mushrooms.
  • Serve with grated Parmesan cheese.
  • Porcini mushrooms are also known as ceps.
  • This dish can also be made with homemade pasta. Cook pasta in a saucepan while sautéing mushrooms in the Thermomix®.
