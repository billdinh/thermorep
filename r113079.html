Cheat's mascarpone

Ingredients

  • 500 g double cream
  • ¼ tsp citric acid
  • 2 tbsp boiling water

Preparation

  1. Place cream into mixing bowl and cook 40 min/90°C/speed 1.
  2. Place citric acid and boiling water into a small bowl and stir to combine. Add mixture to mixing bowl and cook 5 min/90°C/speed 2. Transfer mixture into a warmed thermal serving bowl or warmed large ceramic bowl (see Tips). Cover and set aside for 12 hours (room temperature).
  3. Transfer bowl with cheese into refrigerator for a further 8 hours or until cheese is thick. Place simmering basket over a bowl. Transfer mascarpone into simmering basket and drain for 10 minutes. Transfer mascarpone into a sealable container and store in the refrigerator for up to 4 days.

Useful Items

sealable container, thermal serving bowl or large ceramic bowl, bowl, small bowl, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Yield will vary depending on brand of cream used. For an increased yield, you can double this recipe.
  • To warm a thermal serving bowl or ceramic bowl, fill with hot water, let stand for a few minutes, then discard water and dry bowl.
  • We recommend using this mascarpone in cold dishes only, as it may split when heated. Use it in tiramisu, parfaits, dolloped on cakes or simply spread on toast with fresh fruit.
