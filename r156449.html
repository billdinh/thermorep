Huevos Rancheros

Ingredients

  • 2 oz cotija cheese, cut into pieces (approx. 1 in.)
  • ½ oz fresh cilantro leaves (approx. ½ bunch)
  • 1 ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • ½ - 1 fresh jalapeño chili, stem and seeds removed and cut in half, to taste
  • 3 ½ oz yellow onion, quartered (approx. ½ medium onion)
  • 1 oz olive oil, plus extra to fry tortillas
  • 28 oz canned diced tomatoes, undrained (see Variation)
  • 1 oz tomato paste
  • ½ tsp sugar
  • 6 large eggs
  • 16 oz canned refried pinto beans
  • 6 corn tortillas (approx. 6 in.)
  • 1 ripe avocado, cut into pieces (approx. ½ in.)
  • sour cream, to serve

Preparation

  1. Place cheese into mixing bowl and grate 5 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place cilantro into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Place salt, pepper, cumin, garlic, jalapeño and onion into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  4. Add oil and cook 5 min/212°F/speed 1. Meanwhile, cut parchment paper to fit Varoma tray (see Tip). Scrunch parchment paper into a ball under running water, squeeze out excess water, lay onto Varoma tray and set aside.
  5. Add tomatoes, tomato paste, sugar and half of the reserved chopped cilantro into mixing bowl. Place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 10 min/212°F/speed 1.
  6. Remove simmering basket and scrape sauce from lid into mixing bowl. Pour half the sauce (approx. 10.5 oz) from mixing bowl onto parchment lined Varoma tray. Spread sauce evenly over parchment and crack 6 eggs over the sauce. Gently drag a fork through the egg whites, so they combine with sauce, without disturbing egg yolks.
  7. Insert Varoma tray carefully into Varoma dish, secure Varoma lid and place Varoma into position. With remaining sauce in mixing bowl below, cook 16 min/212°F/speed 1. Meanwhile, begin heating refried beans in a saucepan over low heat, stirring occasionally.
  8. Heat a frying pan on medium heat and add a teaspoon of oil at a time to fry tortillas (see Tip). Transfer to a plate and keep warm.
  9. Once eggs are cooked to your liking, top with a sprinkling of remaining chopped cilantro and cheese.
  10. Carefully remove Varoma dish and Varoma tray. Transfer sauce from mixing bowl to a serving plate. Cut eggs into sections with spatula and place on top of sauce. Serve immediately with warm corn tortillas spread with beans and topped with tomato sauce and egg. Garnish with avocado and sour cream.

Useful Items

serving plate, plate, parchment paper, fork, frying pan, bowl, saucepan

Hints & Tricks

  • Each tortilla takes approx. 45-60 sec. to cook each side.
  • For large groups, Huevos Rancheros can be baked in the oven on a baking sheet. Heat oven to 375°F. Make two batches of tomato sauce and spread evenly on a baking sheet before adding a dozen eggs.
  • To cut parchment to fit Varoma tray: tear off a piece of parchment paper wide enough to cover Varoma tray. Place Varoma tray on top of parchment and cut around the tray to form an oval shape.
  • Canned diced tomatoes can be replaced with fresh tomatoes (stems removed and cut in half). Chop tomatoes 10 sec/speed 9 for smooth version or 5 secs/speed 5 for chunky version. Continue with recipe steps. Canned diced tomatoes give a chunky texture and fresh will give a smoother textured sauce.
  • When adding spices, add extra spices for more flavor; e.g. 1/2 tsp of turmeric powder, 1 tsp of smoked paprika when adding other spices.
  • Grind your own peppercorns and cumin seeds. Place salt, black peppercorns and 1 tsp cumin seeds into mixing bowl and grind 30 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl and continue with recipe.
