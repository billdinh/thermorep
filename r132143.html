Peanut chutney

Ingredients

  • 200 g fresh tomatoes, cut into quarters and deseeded
  • 90 g roasted unsalted peanuts (see Tips)
  • 20 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only (approx. 20 g)
  • 15 g fresh long green chillies, trimmed and cut into halves (approx. 1 chilli)
  • 10 g tamarind paste (see Tips)
  • ¼ tsp garlic paste (see Tips)
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • 1 tsp brown sugar (optional)
  • 30 g shredded coconut

Preparation

  1. Place all ingredients into mixing bowl and blend 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer into a bowl to serve (see Tips).

bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • You can replace the roasted unsalted peanuts with raw peanuts.
  • To make your own tamarind paste and garlic paste, please refer to the recipes on this recipe chip.
  • Serve this sauce cold or at room temperature with finger foods. You can also add yoghurt, tamarind or lemon juice to taste to create a variety of different sauces and dips. Alternatively, use it as a base for cooking curries to add an extra depth of flavour.
