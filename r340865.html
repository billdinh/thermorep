Persian Love Cake

Ingredients

Sponge

  • 60 g pistachio nuts, unsalted, shelled
  • ½ tsp ground cardamom
  • 250 g blanched almonds
  • 1 lemon, thin peelings of skin
  • 150 g light brown sugar
  • 200 g unsalted butter, soft, plus extra for greasing
  • 4 medium eggs
  • 100 g plain flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 2 tsp rose water
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt

Syrup

  • 40 g lemon juice
  • 30 g light brown sugar

Icing and Decoration

  • 200 g mascarpone
  • 40 g icing sugar
  • ½ tsp rose water
  • edible rose petals (optional)

Preparation

Sponge

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C. Grease and line base of a round cake tin (Ø 20 cm).
  2. Place pistachios in mixing bowl then roughly chop 2 sec/speed 6. Transfer to a bowl, remove 2 Tbsp of larger chunks to a smaller bowl then set both aside.
  3. Place cardamom and almonds in mixing bowl then grind 20 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Place lemon peel and 30 g sugar in mixing bowl then grind 15 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  5. Add remaining 120 g sugar and butter then cream 30 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  6. Add eggs and mix 20 sec/speed 4.
  7. Add flour, reserved almond mixture, baking powder, rose water and salt then mix 20 sec/speed 4.
  8. Add large bowl of reserved chopped pistachios and mix 20 sec//speed 3. Transfer to prepared tin then bake for 40-50 minutes (170°C) until a skewer inserted in centre comes out clean. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl.
  9. Leave cake to stand for 5 minutes in tin. Meanwhile, make syrup

Syrup

  1. Place lemon juice and sugar in mixing bowl then heat 2 min/90°C/speed 1.5. Meanwhile, poke holes in the cake surface using a cocktail stick. Drizzle syrup over cake, allowing it to seep into the holes then leave to cool. Clean mixing bowl.
  2. Once cool, remove from tin and transfer to a serving plate or cake stand.

Icing and Decoration

  1. Place mascarpone, icing sugar and rose water in mixing bowl then mix 15 sec/speed 4. Insert butterfly whisk then, without setting a time, and without measuring cup, whip speed 3 until softly whipped, watching carefully to avoid over-whipping. Spoon over cake surface.
  2. Garnish with a sprinkling of reserved chopped pistachios and edible rose petals (if using) before serving.

Useful Items

round cake tin (Ø 20 cm), baking paper, skewer, serving plate, cocktail stick, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • A version of this floral scented cake was originally made by a Persian woman in the hope that the Prince would fall in love with her. With beautiful colours and rich flavours, its hard not to love!
  • For an even more intense flavour, make your own ground cardamon by freshly grinding cardamom pods.
