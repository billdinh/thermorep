Apricot Jam

Ingredients

  • 1000 g apricots, fresh, stoned and quartered
  • 600 g jam sugar
  • 50 g lemon juice (approx. 2 lemons)

Preparation

  1. Place apricots, jam sugar and lemon juice in mixing bowl then, with simmering basket in place of measuring cup, cook 25 min/Varoma/speed 1. Meanwhile, sterilise jam jars (see tip).
  2. Test setting point of jam (see tip). Transfer to the warm sterilised jars and seal immediately. Label before storing in a cool, dry place. Once opened, store in fridge.

Useful Items

jam funnel, sterilised jam jars

Hints & Tricks

  • To sterilise jam jars, preheat oven to 120°C then wash jars well. Place clean jars on a baking tray then heat for approx. 20 minutes (120°C). Always pour hot jam into still warm jars.
  • Setting point test: place a saucer in the freezer for 5-10 minutes. Place 1 tsp jam onto cold saucer. When cool, push jam with your finger. If it wrinkles, it is ready. If not, boil jam for a further 2 min/110°C/speed 1, then test again.
  • To make a smaller amount of jam, halve the recipe to 500 g fruit, 300 g sugar and 25 g lemon juice then cook for the same time, 25 min/Varoma/speed 1.

A great recipe to keep the flavours of summer around that little bit longer. This apricot jam recipe makes a delicious preserve as well as doubling up as a glaze to give your bakes an extra special finish — use for the apple rose tart or the pear frangipane.
