Cod with Garlic Caramel Sauce

Ingredients

  • 4 cod fillets, boneless (approx. 100 g each)
  • 450 g water
  • 200 g extra virgin olive oil
  • 15 garlic cloves
  • 3 pinches ground cayenne pepper
  • 300 g fresh spinach leaves
  • &frac18; tsp fine sea salt
  • 20 g liquid caramel
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
2437 kJ / 583 kcal

Preparation

  1. Place cod fillets skin-side down in a saucepan and add 150 g of water. Cover and simmer until fillets begin to bleach and foam, then turn off heat and keep covered for 2 minutes. Transfer fillets to an oven-proof dish (approx. 30 cm x 20 cm) and reserve cooking liquid.
  2. Place oil in mixing bowl and heat 8 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Add garlic and cayenne pepper then sauté 10 min/Varoma/speed 1. Pour through a fine sieve, reserve oil and set aside.
  4. Preheat oven to 200°C.
  5. Place half of sautéed garlic and 50 g of reserved cooking liquid from saucepan in mixing bowl then blend 2 min/speed 8. Meanwhile, place remaining sautéed garlic over cod fillets. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then stir 2 min/speed 4 while slowly pouring reserved oil onto mixing bowl lid. Once combined, pour over cod.
  6. Place remaining 300 g water in mixing bowl. Place Varoma into position, weigh in spinach, sprinkle with salt then cook 10 min/Varoma/speed 2.
  7. Bake cod for 8 minutes (200°C).
  8. Arrange steamed spinach on serving plates, place cod fillets on top and drizzle with liquid caramel.

Useful Items

oven-proof dish (approx. 30 cm x 20 cm), slotted spoon, saucepan, fine sieve, hob, oven
