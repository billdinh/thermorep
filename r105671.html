Classic Flammkuchen

Ingredients

Dough

  • 250 g baker's flour
  • 120 g water
  • 30 g olive oil
  • ½ tsp salt

Topping

  • 1 spring onion/shallot, trimmed and cut into halves
  • 200 g sour cream
  • 100 g ham, cut into cubes (1-2 cm)
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 2 pinches ground nutmeg
  • 3 pinches ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp fresh chives, finely chopped for garnishing

Preparation

Dough

  1. Place flour, water, olive oil and salt into mixing bowl and knead 2 min/. Transfer dough onto silicone bread mat or plastic wrap and shape into a ball. Wrap in plastic wrap and place into refrigerator to rest for 30 minutes. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  2. Preheat oven to 250°C. Line a baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  3. Transfer dough onto a silicone bread mat or lightly floured work surface and roll out into a rectangle (25 x 35 cm, 3 mm thickness). Place dough onto prepared baking tray and set aside.

Topping

  1. Place spring onion/shallot into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add sour cream, ham, salt, nutmeg and pepper and mix 10 sec//speed 3.
  3. Transfer mixture onto the middle of the dough and spread evenly, leaving a 1 cm border around all edges.
  4. Bake for 15 minutes (250°C) or until crispy. Cut into 16 pieces, garnish with chives and serve immediately.

Useful Items

plastic wrap, silicone bread mat, baking tray (30 x 40 cm), baking paper, rolling pin, refrigerator, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve Flammkuchen fresh out of the oven while the crust is crispy.
  • Ham can be substituted with bacon or speck.
