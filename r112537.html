Spelt and buckwheat tortillas with chilli con carne

Ingredients

Dough

  • 12 sprigs fresh coriander, leaves only
  • 125 g buckwheat
  • 275 g spelt flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 1 tbsp olive oil, plus extra for cooking
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • 190 g water

Chilli con carne

  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • ½ tsp smoked paprika
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • ¼ tsp ground cayenne pepper
  • 1 brown onion, cut into halves (approx. 150 g)
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 - 2 fresh red chilli, deseeded if preferred
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 500 g beef mince
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 400 g canned chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tsp Meat stock paste (see Tips)
  • 160 g water
  • 1 red capsicum, cut into cubes (approx. 2 cm)
  • 1 green capsicum, cut into cubes (approx. 2 cm)
  • 400 g canned kidney beans, rinsed and drained (approx. 250 g after draining)
  • 1 tbsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp salt

Buckwheat 'rice'

  • 1000 g water
  • 1 - 2 pinches salt
  • 350 g buckwheat

Assembly

  • 1 avocado, flesh only, cut into slices
  • 100 g sour cream, to serve
  • fresh coriander, leaves only

Preparation

Dough

  1. Place coriander into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place buckwheat into mixing bowl and mill 1 min/speed 9.
  3. Add spelt flour, oil, baking powder, salt and reserved coriander and mix 5 sec/speed 3.
  4. Mix 20 sec/speed 5, slowly adding water through hole in mixing bowl lid until dough forms. Transfer dough onto a silcone bread mat or lightly floured work surface.
  5. Shape dough into a ball and wrap in silcone bread mat or transfer into a lightly greased bowl and cover with kitchen towel. Set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Chilli con carne

  1. Place spices into mixing bowl and dry roast 2 min/Varoma/speed 1. Transfer into a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place onion, garlic, chilli and oil into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5.
  3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then cook 3 min/100°C//speed 2.
  4. Insert butterfly whisk. Add mince and reserved spices and cook 3 min/Varoma//speed 1.
  5. Add tomato paste and mix 20 sec//speed 1.
  6. Add all remaining chilli ingredients and cook 25 min/90°C//speed 1. Transfer into a thermal serving bowl or other large bowl and cover to keep warm. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Buckwheat 'rice'

  1. Place water and salt into mixing bowl. Insert simmering basket, then weigh buckwheat into it. Cook 12 min/Varoma/speed 2. Meanwhile, divide tortilla dough into 8 equal portions. Using a rolling pin, roll each portion into a thin circle (approx. 1 mm thick and 26 cm in diameter).

Assembly

  1. Place a non-stick frying pan over high heat and add a little oil. Place one tortilla into frying pan and cook for 4-5 seconds, or until small bubbles rise to the surface. Carefully flip tortilla and cook for a further 4- 5 seconds. Transfer onto a large plate and cover with a kitchen towel to keep warm until ready to serve. Repeat frying with remaining dough portions.
  2. Serve tortillas warm with chilli con carne, buckwheat 'rice',  sliced avocado, sour cream and coriander.

Useful Items

large plate, non-stick frying pan, bowl, silicone bread mat, kitchen towel, thermal serving bowl or other large bowl, rolling pin, stove top
