Peppadew® and Feta Stuffed Chicken with Lemon Couscous

Ingredients

  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 10 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves only
  • 80 g feta cheese, crumbled
  • 80 g preserved red peppers (Peppadew®), drained
  • 4 chicken breasts (approx. 140 g each)
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt, plus 2 pinches
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper, plus 1 pinch
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 600 g water
  • 150 g couscous
  • 1 lemon, thin peelings of skin only
  • 50 g lemon juice
  • 80 g olive oil

Preparation

  1. Line Varoma tray with baking paper, leaving some holes uncovered for steam to circulate, then insert Varoma tray into Varoma dish.
  2. Place garlic and 5 sprigs thyme leaves in mixing bowl then chop 10 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add feta and peppers then chop 1-2 sec//speed 4 until roughly chopped.
  4. Make a small but deep horizontal slit on a long side of each chicken breast to make a pocket, taking care not to cut through. Using a teaspoon, fill each breast with feta mixture then place on prepared Varoma tray. Sprinkle chicken breasts with ½ tsp salt, ¼ tsp pepper and paprika.
  5. Place water in mixing bowl then place covered Varoma into position and steam 20-30 min/Varoma/speed 1 until chicken is cooked (see tip). Set covered Varoma aside to keep warm and reserve cooking liquid.
  6. Place a large bowl on mixing bowl lid then weigh in couscous and 150 g reserved cooking liquid. Remove bowl, cover with cling film and set aside for 5 minutes. Meanwhile, discard remaining cooking liquid and clean mixing bowl.
  7. Place lemon peel and 5 sprigs thyme leaves in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula then chop again 3 sec/speed 10.
  8. Add lemon juice, oil, 2 pinches salt and 1 pinch pepper then mix 5 sec/speed 2.
  9. Gently fluff couscous with a fork to separate grains, then pour over dressing and mix well. Slice chicken and serve warm with couscous.

Useful Items

baking paper, cling film

Hints & Tricks

  • Cooking time will vary depending on the thickness of chicken breasts.
