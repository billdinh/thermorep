Bread Pudding

Ingredients

  • 2 oz golden raisins,
  • boiling water, to soak raisins (approx. 8 - 10 oz)
  • 8 slices brioche bread, sliced ½ in - 1 in. (approx. 14 - 15 oz loaf)
  • 1 oz unsalted butter, room temperature for buttering, plus extra to grease
  • 2 oz sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 16 oz heavy whipping cream
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • confectioners sugar, to garnish

Preparation

  1. Place a small bowl onto mixing bowl lid. Weigh in raisins, cover raisins with boiling water and set aside to soak for 15 minutes.
  2. Turn on broiler to high heat. Place brioche slices on baking sheet and toast lightly 30 seconds each side (watch carefully as all broilers work differently) and set aside.
  3. Preheat oven to 325°F. Lightly butter a casserole dish (8 in. x 11.5 in. x 2 in.).
  4. Butter toasted brioche on one side, then cut each slice in half diagonally to make two triangles. Arrange half the triangles, buttered side up, in the prepared dish. Sprinkle half the soaked raisins over brioche. Layer remaining triangles, point side up, across layer of brioche in base of casserole. Sprinkle with remaining raisins and set aside.
  5. Place sugar into mixing bowl and grind 5 sec/speed 10.
  6. Add eggs, whipping cream, cinnamon and vanilla and mix 10 sec/speed 5.
  7. Pour over brioche and bake 30-35 minutes (325°F) until just set.
  8. Garnish with a sprinkling of confectioners sugar and serve immediately.

Useful Items

casserole dish (8 in. x 11.5 in. x 2 in.), small bowl, baking sheet, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • Serve with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
  • Add almond extract and orange zest while adding the vanilla and cinnamon.
  • Add toasted nuts while adding raisins.
  • For a lighter dessert, replace half the cream (8 oz) with 8 oz of milk.
