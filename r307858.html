Tofu Ramen Bowl

Ingredients

  • 6 scallions, white parts, halved, green parts, thinly sliced, to garnish
  • 1 oz ginger, peeled, in pieces
  • 1 oz sesame oil, plus extra to brush
  • 6 oz fresh cremini mushrooms, sliced (¼ in.)
  • 1 oz soy sauce, plus extra to brush
  • 48 oz vegetable broth
  • 16 oz tofu, firm, sliced in planks (½ in.)
  • 4 whole eggs
  • 2 packages dried ramen noodles
  • 3 oz carrots peeled, julienned
  • sesame seeds, to garnish

Preparation

  1. Place scallions (white parts only) ginger and sesame oil into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add mushrooms and soy sauce and without measuring cup cook 5 min/250°F//speed 1.
  3. Add broth, insert measuring cup and cook 12 min/212°F//speed 2. Meanwhile pan-sear tofu. Brush or coat tofu with soy sauce and sesame oil. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat and pan fry until crispy (approx. 5-8 min. per side). Cut into ¼ inch strips and set aside.
  4. Set Varoma dish into position, add eggs, secure Varoma lid and steam 8-10 min/Varoma//speed 2. Remove Varoma and let eggs cool slightly.
  5. Add ramen and carrots and cook for time indicated on package/212°F//speed 1.
  6. Divide ramen, broth and carrots between 4 bowls. Top with reserved tofu. Peel reserved cooled eggs, cut each egg in half and add 2 halves to each bowl. Garnish with reserved sliced green scallions and sesame seeds and serve hot.

Useful Items

skillet or frying pan, basting brush
