British spice rub

Ingredients

  • ½ orange, zest only, no white pith
  • 1 lemon, zest only, no white pith
  • 4 fresh bay leaves
  • 4 allspice berries
  • 2 tbsp dried fennel seeds
  • 150 g sea salt
  • 1 cinnamon quill
  • 3 pinches ground cloves
  • 4 sprigs fresh rosemary (approx. 10 cm each), leaves only
  • 4 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves only
  • ½ whole nutmeg, finely grated

Preparation

  1. Place orange zest, lemon zest, bay leaves, allspice, fennel, 20 g of the salt, cinnamon quill and ground cloves and mill 10 sec/speed 9. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then repeat milling 10 sec/speed 9, or until a fine powdery consistency is achieved. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add rosemary, thyme, nutmeg and remaining 130 g salt, and mill 10 sec/speed 9. Transfer spice rub into a sealable storage jar and set aside until use.

Hints & Tricks

  • Place British spice rub into small glass jars with labels to give as gifts to friends and family. These make fantastic table gifts if you are having a sit-down lunch or dinner.
  • Try using this spice rub on the Whole steamed chicken.
  • Use 2-3 tablespoons of this spice rub on any roasted poultry or meats.
