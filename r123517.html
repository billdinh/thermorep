Patatas Bravas with Chorizo

Ingredients

  • 60 g red onions
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • ½ - 1 fresh red chilli, deseeded if preferred and halved
  • 400 g tinned chopped tomatoes
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper
  • 1 pinch ground smoked paprika
  • ¼ - ½ tsp fine sea salt, to taste
  • 800 - 850 g small potatoes, peeled and cut in pieces (2-3 cm)
  • 500 g chorizo, thinly sliced
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
828 kJ / 197 kcal

11 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C. Line a baking tray (30 x 40 cm) with baking paper and set aside.
  2. Place onions, oil, garlic and chilli in mixing bowl then chop 3 sec/speed 7. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then sauté 3 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Add tinned tomatoes, cayenne pepper, paprika and salt. Place Varoma dish into position and weigh potatoes into it. Secure Varoma lid and cook 20 min/100°C/speed 1. Set Varoma aside and insert measuring cup, then blend 30 sec/speed 5. Transfer sauce into a bowl and cover to keep warm.
  4. Place potatoes onto prepared baking tray and top with sliced chorizo. Bake for 15 minutes (200°C). Remove from oven and stir potatoes and chorizo to ensure even cooking. Return tray to the oven and bake for a further 25-30 minutes (200°C), or until potatoes are golden and chorizo is sizzling.
  5. Transfer potatoes and chorizo into a serving bowl and serve sauce warm on the side.

Useful Items

baking tray (30 x 40 cm), baking paper, silicone spatula, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • This is a classic tapas dish served throughout Spain. Enjoy as the start to a Tapas themed menu or simply as a nibble at your next party.
