Spinach and Parmesan Soup

Ingredients

  • 40 g Parmesan cheese, cut in pieces (2 cm)
  • 70 g leeks, sliced (5 cm)
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 30 g unsalted butter, diced
  • 250 g potatoes, peeled, diced (1 cm)
  • 650 g water
  • 1 vegetable stock cube (for 0.5 l), crumbled
  • 250 g fresh spinach leaves
  • 40 g double cream
  • ½ tsp fine sea salt
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • extra virgin olive oil, for drizzling
  • baguette, sliced, toasted, for serving
Nutrition
per 1 portion

Preparation

  1. Place Parmesan in mixing bowl and grate 8 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place leeks and garlic in mixing bowl then chop 5 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add butter and sauté 3 min/120°C/speed 1.
  4. Add potatoes, water and vegetable stock then cook 10 min/100°C/speed 1.
  5. Add 125 g spinach and cook 10 min/100°C/speed 1.
  6. Add remaining 125 g spinach and cook 10 min/100°C/speed 1.
  7. Add reserved grated Parmesan, cream, salt and pepper then blend 1 min/speed 4-9, increasing speed gradually.
  8. Serve immediately with a drizzle of oil and toasted baguette slices.
