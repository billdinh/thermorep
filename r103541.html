Rhubarb and Ginger Tart

Ingredients

  • 200 g ginger cake, sliced (1 cm)
  • 300 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 150 g butter, cold, diced
  • 20 - 40 g water
  • 1 tsp caster sugar
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
  • 150 g brown sugar
  • 500 g rhubarb, peeled, cut in pieces (2 cm)

Preparation

  1. Toast sliced ginger cake then set aside to cool.
  2. Place flour, butter, water, sugar and salt in mixing bowl then mix 20 sec/speed 4. Tip dough out and bring together into a ball then wrap in cling film and set aside in fridge to rest for 30 minutes. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl.
  3. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  4. Roll out rested dough on a lightly floured surface until large enough to line a Ø 26 cm fluted tart tin.
  5. Place toasted ginger cake slices and brown sugar in mixing bowl then chop 10 sec/speed 8. Spread half the cake crumbs over base of tart tin then arrange rhubarb pieces on top. Finish with remaining cake crumbs. Bake for 35-40 minutes (180°C) until rhubarb is cooked and topping is crunchy. Serve warm or cold.

Useful Items

toaster, cling film, rolling pin, fluted tart tin (Ø 26 cm), refrigerator, oven

Hints & Tricks

  • This is a great recipe to use up leftover ginger cake.
