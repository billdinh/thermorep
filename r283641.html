Savory Turnip Cake

Ingredients

  • 10 ½ oz long-grain white rice (soaked for 2 hours then drained)
  • 45 ½ oz water, divided
  • 35 oz Chinese white radishes/turnips, peeled, cubed (1 in.)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil, plus extra to grease and pan fry
  • ½ tsp salt, to taste
  • ¼ tsp ground white pepper, to taste
  • scallions, to garnish
  • fresh red chilies, chopped, to garnish
  • Chinese chives, to garnish
Nutrition
per 1 slice

331 kJ / 79 kcal

Preparation

  1. Place prepared rice and 10.5 oz water into mixing bowl and blend 10 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Clean mixing bowl.
  2. Place radishes, oil, salt and pepper into mixing bowl and grate 20 sec/speed 10. Place simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid and cook 15 min/212°F/speed 3.
  3. Remove mixing bowl lid and allow radishes to cool in mixing bowl until screen temperature reaches 160°F (approx. 30 min.) then blend 20 sec/speed 10.
  4. Without measuring cup, mix 2 min/speed 3 while adding reserved rice mixture through hole in mixing bowl lid.
  5. Transfer radish mixture into a lightly greased loaf pan (see Tip) and cover with foil. Place into Varoma and set aside. Rinse mixing bowl.
  6. Place remaining 35 oz water into mixing bowl, place Varoma into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 60 min/Varoma/speed 1. Remove loaf pan, transfer to a wire rack and allow cake to cool in pan (approx. 1 hr.). Cover loosely and refrigerate overnight.
  7. Remove turnip cake and slice into ¼-½ inch slices.
  8. Heat oil in a frying pan and sear both sides of cake slices until browned (approx. 2 min. per side). Garnish with scallions, chopped chilies and Chinese chives. Serve warm.

Useful Items

aluminum foil, loaf pan (approx. 9 in. x 5 in. x 3 in.), frying pan, wire rack, aluminum foil or cling wrap, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • Loaf pans differ in sizes and shapes. Ensure pan fits in Varoma and lid closes properly before steaming.
  • Serve with light soy sauce and chopped scallions.
  • To make scallion curls: cut scallions into sections and thinly slice lengthwise. Place sliced scallions into a bowl of iced water and evenly submerge in water for 5-7 minutes or until scallions curl. Remove from water and dry with paper towel. Use to garnish as desired.
  • Before transferring radish mixture to prepared loaf pan, add fillings such as minced sautéed mushrooms, dried shrimp or minced dry-cured ham. Mix well with spatula to combine and continue as per recipe.
