Sweet and Spiced Nuts

Ingredients

  • 1 egg white, from a large egg
  • 5 oz sugar
  • 6 oz raw almonds, skin on
  • 6 oz raw pecans
  • 2 tsp pumpkin pie spice
  • 2 tsp curry powder
  • ¼ tsp ground cayenne pepper, to taste
  • ¼ tsp salt
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
640 kJ / 153 kcal
Protein
3 g
Carbohydrates
11 g
Fat
12 g

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 300°F. Line a large baking sheet with parchment and set aside.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Place egg white and sugar into mixing bowl and without measuring cup whisk 30 sec/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  3. Add almonds, pecans, pumpkin pie spice, curry, cayenne and salt and without measuring cup mix 10 min/250°F//speed 1. Remove butterfly whisk.
  4. Transfer nuts to prepared baking sheet and spread out evenly with spatula. Bake for 25 minutes (300°F) stirring halfway to ensure even cooking. Do not over bake. Allow to fully cool before storing in an airtight container or packaging for gift giving.

Useful Items

large baking sheet, parchment paper, airtight container, oven
