Plum sauce

Ingredients

  • 800 g canned plums, drained (approx. 420 g after draining - see Tips)
  • 400 g apples, peeled, cored and cut into pieces (approx. 1-2 cm)
  • 40 g raisins
  • 9 garlic cloves
  • 2 ½ cm piece fresh ginger, peeled and cut into thin slices (1 cm)
  • 20 g curry powder (mild)
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp caraway seeds
  • ¼ tsp ground cayenne pepper
  • 400 g white sugar
  • 200 g white wine vinegar

Preparation

  1. Place plums, apples, raisins, garlic, ginger, curry powder, salt, pepper, caraway seeds and cayenne pepper into mixing bowl and chop 15 sec/speed 5, with aid of spatula.
  2. Add sugar and vinegar and mix 5 sec/speed 5, then cook 10 min/100°C/speed 2.
  3. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then cook 30 min/90°C/speed 2, placing simmering basket instead of measuring cup onto mixing bowl lid.
  4. Insert measuring cup. Blend 1 min/speed 5-9, gradually increasing speed from speed 5 to speed 9. Transfer into sealable storage jars, allow to cool, then place into refrigerator until ready to use (see Tips).

Useful Items

sealable storage jars, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • When in season, you can use fresh plums. You will need 500-600 g fresh plums, quartered and stones removed. Proceed as per recipe.
  • Plum sauce will keep for 2 weeks when stored in the refrigerator. It's delicious with pork, beef and chicken.
