Pilau Rice

Ingredients

Steamed Rice

  • 1000 g water
  • 200 g basmati rice
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt

Tarka

  • 100 g onions, quartered
  • 20 g ghee
  • 2 cm cinnamon stick
  • 6 cardamom pods
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander seeds
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
985 kJ / 236 kcal

4.2 g

Preparation

Steamed Rice

  1. Place water in mixing bowl and insert simmering basket. Weigh in rice and add salt. Rinse rice 10 sec/speed 5 then steam 20 min/100°C/speed 4, or until rice is fully cooked.
  2. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula. Place rice in a warm serving bowl then cover to keep warm.

Tarka

  1. Rinse and dry mixing bowl. Place onions in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  2. Add ghee, cinnamon stick, cardamom, cumin and coriander seeds then cook 8 min/Varoma/speed 1.
  3. Add tarka to rice and stir together gently until evenly mixed then serve hot.

Useful Items

cling film, serving bowl

Hints & Tricks

  • The amount of rice can be adapted. The maximum weight of dry rice you can cook is 400 g.  The amount of water and salt stays always the same. Allow 50-70 g dry rice per person.
