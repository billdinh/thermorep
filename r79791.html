Sushi salad

Ingredients

  • 400 g sushi rice
  • 900 g water
  • 1 tsp peanut oil
  • 2 - 3 spring onions/shallots, trimmed and cut into quarters
  • 2 carrots (210 g), cut into quarters
  • 150 g sashimi grade tuna or sashimi grade salmon, cut into cubes (2 cm)
  • 3 - 4 tbsp mirin
  • ½ tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp white sesame seeds or black sesame seeds
  • 1 avocado, flesh only, cut into cubes
  • 1 Lebanese cucumber, peeled and cut into cubes
  • 15 cooked prawns (approx. 120 g), peeled
  • 3 - 4 tbsp soy sauce
  • 3 - 4 tbsp rice wine vinegar
  • 1 nori sheet, cut into thin strips, for garnishing
  • wasabi paste, to serve (optional)

Preparation

  1. Insert simmering basket and weigh sushi rice into it. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and rinse rice thoroughly (water should run very clear). Allow rice to drain in simmering basket. Once rice has drained, place water into mixing bowl and re-insert simmering basket with rice. Pour peanut oil over rice and cook 15-17 min/100°C/speed 4, or until rice is cooked.
  2. Remove simmering basket with aid of spatula and set rice aside to cool. Once cooled, transfer rice into a large serving bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.
  3. Place spring onions/shallots and carrot into mixing bowl and chop 3-6 sec/speed 6, or until carrot is evenly chopped. Transfer into large serving bowl with the rice.
  4. Place serving bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh tuna or salmon into it. Add mirin, sesame oil, sesame seeds, avocado, cucumber, prawns, soy sauce and vinegar to serving bowl and combine gently using the spatula.
  5. Garnish salad with nori strips and serve immediately with wasabi paste on the side (optional).
