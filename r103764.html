Parfait au chocolat with summer berries (Nico Moretti)

Ingredients

Parfait au chocolat

  • 300 g pouring (whipping) cream
  • 200 g dark chocolate (70% cocoa), broken into pieces (approx. 2 cm)
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 2 eggs
  • 100 g caster sugar
  • 1 tsp natural vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp orange liqueur (see Tips)

Raspberry coulis

  • 200 g fresh raspberries (see Tips)
  • 100 g caster sugar
  • ½ lemon, juice only

Macerated summer berries

  • 300 g fresh mixed berries (e.g strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1 tbsp caster sugar
  • 1 tbsp orange liqueur (see Tips)

Preparation

Parfait au chocolat

  1. Line a small loaf tin (18 x 7 x 6 cm) with plastic wrap and set aside.
  2. Insert butterfly whisk. Place cream into mixing bowl and whip 20 sec/speed 4, until firm peaks form. Remove butterfly whisk. Transfer cream into a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl and butterfly whisk.
  3. Place chocolate into mixing bowl and chop 4 sec/speed 7.
  4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula, then melt 3 min/50°C/speed 1.
  5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula and repeat melting 3 min/50°C/speed 1 or until chocolate has just melted.
  6. Insert butterfly whisk. Add egg yolk, eggs and sugar and whisk 4 min/speed 4. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.
  7. Add vanilla and orange liqueur and mix 15 sec/speed 3. Remove butterfly whisk.
  8. Add reserved whipped cream and fold 10 sec//speed 4, until just combined. Pour mixture into prepared loaf tin, cover with plastic wrap and place into freezer for a minimum of 8 hours or until set. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Raspberry coulis

  1. Place all coulis ingredients into mixing bowl and cook 4 min/90°C/speed 4, then purée 20 sec/speed 5 -9, increasing speed gradually from speed 5 to speed 9. Transfer into a jug and set aside to cool.

Macerated summer berries

  1. Place a ceramic or glass bowl onto mixing bowl lid, then weigh mixed berries into it. Add sugar and orange liqueur. Gently stir to combine, cover and transfer into refrigerator for 30 minutes, or until sugar has dissolved. If needed, gently stir ingredients to ensure sugar dissolves.
  2. Remove parfait from freezer 5 minutes before serving. Unmould and place onto a serving platter. Serve with Macerated berries and Raspberry coulis.

Useful Items

plastic wrap, loaf tin (18 x 7 x 6 cm), bowl, jug, ceramic or glass bowl, serving platter, freezer, refrigerator

Hints & Tricks

  • We used Grand Marnier® for our orange liqueur, but any type is suitable.
  • You can use fresh or frozen raspberries in this recipe.
  • Raspberry coulis can be made in advance and frozen, then defrosted, before serving.
  • Before serving, transfer raspberry coulis into a squeezable sauce bottle and drizzle over serving plates to create designs or patterns.
