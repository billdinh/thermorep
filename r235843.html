Mexican Black Bean Dip with Healthy Tortilla Chips

Ingredients

  • 20 g fresh coriander, leaves only
  • 80 g tomatoes, deseeded, diced (1 cm)
  • 50 g onions, diced (1 cm)
  • ½ tsp cider vinegar
  • 4 wheat flour tortillas
  • oil (low calorie oil spray)
  • 1 ¼ tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 230 g tinned black beans, rinsed, drained (drained weight)
  • 180 g chipotle chilli salsa
  • 30 g freshly squeezed lime juice
  • ¼ tsp cayenne pepper
  • ¼ tsp ground cumin
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 2 Tbsp sour cream, low fat (optional)

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Place coriander in mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 7, then transfer to a bowl.
  2. Place a bowl on mixing bowl lid and weigh in tomatoes, onions, vinegar and 10 g reserved coriander. Stir with spatula to combine then set aside.
  3. Cut each tortilla into 8 equal triangles and place on a baking tray lined with baking paper.
  4. Spray triangles with oil then sprinkle with 1 tsp garlic powder and smoked paprika. Bake for 10-15 minutes or until crisp, then leave to cool on baking trays and make dip.
  5. Place black beans, salsa, lime juice, cayenne pepper, cumin, ¼ tsp garlic powder, remaining chopped coriander, salt and pepper then mix 3 sec/speed 5.
  6. Transfer dip to a serving bowl then top with tomato-onion mixture and sour cream (if using). Serve immediately with tortilla chips.
