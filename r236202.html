Spicy Pork with Tomatoes

Ingredients

  • 16 oz pork tenderloin, sliced ¼ in. thick, then halved
  • 2 tsp Italian herb mix
  • ½ tsp salt, to taste
  • ½ tsp freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 5 oz yellow onion, quartered
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 2 cans diced tomatoes, fire roasted with green chilies (approx. 14.5 oz ea.)
  • 4 oz water
Nutrition
per 1 portion
Calories
824 kJ / 197 kcal
Protein
26 g
Carbohydrates
14 g
Fat
4 g

Preparation

  1. Place a bowl onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in pork. Add Italian herbs, salt and pepper, mix well to coat pork and refrigerate.
  2. Place onion and garlic into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 5 then sauté 2 min/250°F/speed .
  3. Add tomatoes with green chilies and water and boil 8 min/212°F/speed 1.
  4. Add reserved marinated pork and cook 10 min/212°F//speed . Serve immediately.
